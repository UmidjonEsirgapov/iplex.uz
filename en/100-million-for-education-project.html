General information:

1. The project "100 million for education" is a popular project in which everyone who is a citizen of the Republic of Uzbekistan can participate;

2. The purpose of this project is to popularize learning in our country and discover knowledgeable people;

3. The project will be conducted in two stages: the first stage will be conducted online (from April 1 to August 20), and the second stage will be conducted offline (after August 20, on the eve of the Independence Day);

4. The first round of the Olympiad will start on April 1 on @bilimtestbot Telegram bot and will last until August 20. Participants work on the questions presented in the bot and collect points;

5. To use @bilimtestbot, users must be fully registered and enter their phone number. The participation of users who do not register fully or enter incorrect information will be considered invalid. Therefore, you are asked to be careful when registering;

6. @bilimtestbot Telegram bot presents new questions every week. Users can work on questions at any time during the week. But one participant can participate only once in the questions presented every week;

7. The number of weekly questions is 40. Questions are formulated by qualified experts. A question received by one user will not be repeated by another user. A sufficient database of questions has been formed for this.

8. @bilimtestbot will have 1 hour to complete 40 questions. In order to solve the questions, it is required to have at least an intermediate level of knowledge in all directions.

9. Every week, users work on the questions presented and collect points until August 20. TOP 50 participants with the highest results will be announced every week through social networks and My Country TV channel;

10. 100 participants who scored the highest points from the questions presented by @bilimtestbot until August 20 will be invited to the second stage of the Olympiad in Tashkent;

11. In the second round of the Olympiad, the final Olympiad will be held offline among the 100 participants who scored the highest points in @bilimtestbot. In order to ensure the criteria of justice in the final, the finalists will be strictly controlled and the winner will be determined. — More information
