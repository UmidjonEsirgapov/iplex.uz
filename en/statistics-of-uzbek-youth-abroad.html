The number of young Uzbeks going to study or work in near and far foreign countries is increasing significantly.

According to the information of the World Association of Youth of Uzbekistan, 181,500 foreigners went to study in Russia in the first half of 2020, of which 24,000 are Uzbeks.

According to information received by Tajikistan, the number of Uzbek students in the republic has reached 3,000. This indicator is 30,000 in neighboring Kyrgyzstan, 26,500 in Kazakhstan, 10,000 in South Korea, 5,000 in China, 2,000 in Germany, 1,500 in Latvia, 3,500 in Turkey, 2,000 in Belarus, 4,000 in Ukraine, 750 in Japan, and more than 550 in the Arab Republic of Egypt.

When summarizing the youth of other foreign countries, more than 112,850 young people from Uzbekistan are currently studying abroad (the numbers may be higher - the World Association of Youth of Uzbekistan). — Read on infoedu.uz
