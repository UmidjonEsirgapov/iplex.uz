The schedule of entrance tests for academic lyceums for the 2024-2025 academic year

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
For the academic year 2024-2025, entrance tests for academic lyceums will be held on July 25 in the second shift (only in Tashkent city) and on July 26 in two shifts (in all regions).
In this: 1st shift — starts at 09:00 2nd shift — starts at 15:00 Tests are held to determine 2 subjects and intellectual ability level (IQ) suitable for the field of study.
In this, applicants are given a total of 60 test tasks, 20 of each subject, and they have 2 hours to complete them, along with filling out the answer sheet.

For the academic year 2024-2025, entrance tests for academic lyceums will be held on July 25 in the second shift (only in the city of Tashkent) and on July 26 in two shifts (in all regions). In this case:

  • 1st shift - starts at 09:00
  • 2nd shift - starts at 15:00

Tests are held to determine the level of intellectual ability (IQ) and 2 subjects suitable for the educational field. Applicants will be given a total of 60 test tasks, 20 from each subject, and 2 hours will be allocated to complete them, along with filling out the answer sheet.

Applicants can find out when and where they will take part in the test through my.edu.uz website and download the permit from their personal office. They should arrive 1 hour before with an identity document and permission to participate in the test.

Through this new opportunity, parents and the general public can actively participate in ensuring the fair and transparent conduct of the tests. This measure, aimed at increasing the transparency of the process by monitoring the tests online, will provide great convenience for applicants and their families. — Official Data
