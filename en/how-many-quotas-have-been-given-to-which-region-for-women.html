Additional allocated quotas for women for the 2025/2026 academic year

In the 2025/2026 academic year, an additional 4,000 state grants were allocated for women in full-time undergraduate education. This figure has doubled compared to last year's quota of 2,000.

These grants are divided across regions and a separate amount is set for each region.
