A new order of production practice in vocational training organizations

Useful information for applicants and students:

From January 1, 2025, a new procedure for production practice will be introduced in vocational training organizations. This will be implemented based on the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers on December 28, 2024.

The main aspects of the new procedure

  • Instead of production and pre-diploma practice, a single production practice system will be introduced. acquired skills are evaluated together with employers.

The main clauses of the regulation approved in the decision

  • Internship programs are developed and approved in agreement with the enterprises of the relevant sector and industry.
  • Internship is organized in enterprises and organizations.
  • Vocational education organizations and employers are required to fulfill their duties.

Advantages of the new system

  • Students will get to know real production processes during practice.
  • Under the guidance of qualified teachers, students will acquire high-quality practical knowledge and skills.
  • As a result of the practical skills assessment system together with employers, students will be ready for the labor market.

Conclusion

This decision will help students aimed at improving their skills in the practice process and ensuring their employment. The new procedure will improve the quality of professional education and prepare personnel for production requirements. — Source
