The admission process is carried out in two stages according to the principle "First the test, then the selection". This innovation expands opportunities for applicants and opens the way for selection based on test results.

Phase 1: Registration for the test (June 5-25)

Applicants will register to participate in the test from June 5 to June 25 (including June 25). At this stage, the following information is selected:

  • Set of subjects to be tested

  • Language of education

  • Teaching area

  • If a professional (creative) exam is required, only the university that conducts this exam is selected

Note: At this stage, it is still university or institute is not selected. For example, the phrases "I applied to the Law University" will be incorrect before the test.

Tests are held in July-August.

Stage 2: Selection stage - choosing a university and course

After the tests are fully completed, applicants, depending on their results:

  • Higher chooses an educational institution

  • Bachelor's field of education

  • Form of education (full-time, part-time, evening)

for 15 days. During this period, applicants can analyze their scores and make a choice according to their real possibilities.

Opportunities for those who could not enter the university

If the applicant cannot enter the university, based on the test results:

  • College

  • Technical School

can be chosen. For this purpose, HEIs will be given another 10 days after the announcement of admission results (mandate).

Conclusion

The new system will allow applicants to plan more accurately, make realistic choices based on scores, and have alternative directions. Therefore, in-depth acquaintance with each stage of the admission process is the key to success. Get the information in time and don't be mistaken when making a decision! — Source
