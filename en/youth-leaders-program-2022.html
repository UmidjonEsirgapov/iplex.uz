Applications are now being accepted for the 2022 Summer Youth Leaders Program (YLP)!

YLP is the world's first international virtual leadership development course with unlimited scholarships offered by the World Youth Leaders Organization (WYLO) on July 16-17, 2022.
Worldwide The Youth Leaders Organization (WYLO) is a passionate volunteer community of young leaders committed to ensuring equal and equitable access to quality education. The YLP Scholarship is a full need-based scholarship that helps young people from underprivileged backgrounds participate in the Youth Leaders Program without worrying about financing this amazing opportunity.

Throughout the program, you'll expand your professional network, participate in fun virtual classes, workshops, games, and chats, and make friends from different backgrounds. In addition, you will have the opportunity to meet directly with world-class speakers.

Address: Online.

Benefits:
- Through this course, you will learn leadership skills and how to effectively apply them; you will make friends from representatives;
-All participants and finishers will receive a certificate;
-If you win an award in the program, you can additionally receive an Award certificate and other interesting opportunities;
-In addition, you will have the opportunity to meet world-class speakers directly;
-Additional exciting opportunities for prize winners.

Eligibility Criteria:
Your legal age must be between 16 and 29 as of the time of submission of this application.
Eligible Regions: For all countries worldwide.

Apply submission:
STEP 1.
Submit the questionnaire through the given link.

STEP 2.
If you are shortlisted, you will be contacted for an interview via WhatsApp.

STEP 3.
Successfully selected applicants will receive an invitation letter. Then pay the $10 fee or use the YLP scholarship waiver code.

STEP 4.
Fill out the registration form and you're ready to join this amazing experience!
Application deadline: May 14, 2022.
