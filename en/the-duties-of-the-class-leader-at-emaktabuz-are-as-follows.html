The duties of the class leader at Emaktab.uz are as follows

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
create a profile on the Platform for the parent (legal representative of the student) and distribute a temporary Login and Password; to be responsible for the procedure for recovering a forgotten Login and Password for parents and teachers, and for the transfer of personal data of the student and parent (legal representative of the student); to constantly monitor the activity of the parent (legal representative of the student) on the Platform, to contact them when necessary and conduct a preventive interview. If the preventive interview does not give a positive result, to provide information to the school director; marking students who missed classes due to illness or family circumstances with special symbols (s, dq, k, kq, m, ch, q, ns, tq) in the class journal; if the student misses classes for 3 hours without reason, collect a set of necessary documents and present them to the head of the institution; in the activities of general secondary educational institutions, it ensures the fulfillment of the tasks set in accordance with the regulation on the procedure for working with the automated information system and the archiving procedure.
