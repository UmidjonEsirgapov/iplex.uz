The prestigious Hamdan International Photography competition was founded by Sheikh Hamdan Bin Mohammed Al Maktoum, and every year the best photographers from around the world are determined on various subjects. In this year's season of the contest, participants can participate in nature-themed photos and win a big cash prize. All talented photographers of the world can participate in this competition.

The competition will be held in the following directions:

  • Nature - nature
  • Portfolio (Story telling) - portfolio
  • General - general. Black/white and color photos are accepted.
  • Portrait - portrait

    • 3rd place - $16,000
    • 4th place - $10,000
    • 5th place - $6,000
