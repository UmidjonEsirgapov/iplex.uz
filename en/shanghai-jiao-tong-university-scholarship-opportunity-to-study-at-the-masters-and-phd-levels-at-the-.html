General Information:

Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) is considered one of the top universities in China. According to the QS 2021 world ranking, it currently ranks 47th in the world. More than 7,000 international students from more than 100 countries study at SJTU every year. The duration of the master's degree is 2-3 years, and Phd programs are 4 years.

Requirements for candidates:

  • All foreign candidates can apply (Chinese citizens cannot apply);
  • Must be physically and mentally healthy;
  • Must have excellent academic performance;
  • A bachelor's degree is required for master's degrees and a master's degree is required for doctoral degrees;
  • Fluent knowledge of Chinese or English is required (Learn more about language requirements);

Important dates:

  • October 1, 2021- Admission for the program opens;
  • December 15, 2021- Admission for the program ends;
  • March 31, 2022- 2nd admission for the program opens;
  • 31-May 2022- 2nd admission for the program ends;

Registration for the program:

  • Application online fill in;
  • Pay the registration fee-$120;

Required Documents;

  • Diploma and its grades (if you have not yet graduated from the university, you need an official statement from the university about when you will graduate);
  • All grades (Transcripts are required- Bachelor's grades for master's degree; bachelor's degree and master's transcripts are also required for doctoral degree);
  • Language certificates;
  • Passport size photo;
  • Personal statement and study plan;
  • 2 academic letters of recommendation;
  • All documents must be submitted in English or Chinese;

Contract fees;

  • -$6,600 USD for PhD studies;
  • Master's fees vary depending on the field (learn more here)

There are 2 ways to win a grant under this program:

1) SJTU apply for scholarships through;

From 2022, scholarships for doctoral programs will be: SJTU scholarship, CSC/SGS scholarship, SJTU scholarship. Scholarships for Master's programs are: CSC/SGS Scholarship, SJTU Scholarship, School/College Scholarship. Details of each scholarship are available in the online application system.

When applying on this site, candidates should select "Apply for Scholarship through SJTU" and follow the online steps. Applicants must complete the scholarship application process in a timely manner according to the university's requirements.

The earlier the application is completed, the sooner the applicant will receive an offer of admission and scholarship, if the result of the university's review is positive. Applicants can check the scholarship results through the application system.

2) By applying for the scholarship through the Chinese Embassies/Consulates in your country:

Applicants can also apply to the Chinese Embassies/Consulates in their home countries to apply for the scholarship. The application schedule varies from country to country (usually from November to April). Get the grant requirements, procedures, documents, agency number, program category type, etc. by contacting the Chinese Embassy/Consulate;

To contact the Chinese Embassies/Consulates, visit the official website of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.


Important Notes: The scholarship period cannot be extended. All scholarship recipients are required to participate in an annual scholarship review in April and/or May, which determines whether they will receive a scholarship for the following academic year.
