On July 24, 2023, by the Knowledge Assessment Agency under the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation Entrance tests to academic lyceums were held for the 2023/2024 academic year.

The tests were organized in 2 shifts in 29 large-capacity buildings in the republic, and more than 28,000 applicants participated.

The tests were conducted to determine the level of intellectual ability (IQ) and 2 subjects suitable for the field of study.

Applicants were given a total of 60 test tasks, 20 of each subject, and 1 hour and 50 minutes to work on them.

The results of the test are calculated by the agency within 5 days and announced after they are submitted to the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation. — Details here
