Scholarships for students at Boston University, USA

  • Where: Boston, USA
  • Accepting applications deadline: December 1, 2021
  • Scholarship: tuition fee
  • When: annually

Boston University offers Trustee Scholarship to top students. These grants are awarded to students pursuing a degree in the US to provide academic excellence and leadership opportunities.

The grant covers the entire period of study (4 years) and all university fees. In order to receive the scholarship in the following years, the student must have good academic performance.

Requirements for candidates:
• The scholarship can be received by a student admitted to the same university;

• Candidates must have leadership skills, achievements in school;

• Applicants must be 5-10% of the best students of the course and have a 4.0 GPA. (with full marks);

• They should know English well. ( IELTS 7/ TOEFL iBT 80-100) DETAILS

  • The application must be filled out in the CommonApplication system.
