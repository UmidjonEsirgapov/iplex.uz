German language teachers will improve their skills in Germany

According to the decision of the government, in order to further improve the German language teaching system in Uzbekistan and to direct young people to professional education in Germany, 27 German language teachers from professional educational institutions were sent to the training course at the "TELC" language training center in Germany.

On October 16 with the teachers selected for training a meeting was held, the purpose of the trip and the expected results were exchanged. For 8 weeks, teachers will improve their language levels and professional skills at the "TELC" center.

During the course, teachers are expected to participate in intensive training courses based on modern language teaching methods and master the latest methods of teaching German.
