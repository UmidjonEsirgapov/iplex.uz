Qualification tests will start on October 29

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Proficiency tests for teachers of preschool, general education and non-school educational institutions will begin from October 29, 2024 and will last until November 16. for biology, physics — 120 minutes For other subjects — 90 minutes Stages of qualification tests: First stage: 40 tests (35 from specialization, 5 from professional standards) are given to determine the level of knowledge in the specialty subject and professional standards. qualification category tests for pedagogues of general education and non-school educational institutions will begin on October 29 and last until November 16.

Remarks on the time of the test:

  • Mathematics, informatics and information technologies, chemistry, biology, physics for subjects — 120 minutes
  • For other subjects — 90 minutes

Stages of qualification tests:

  1. First stage: 40 tests to determine the level of knowledge in the specialty subject and professional standards (35 from the specialty, from professional standards 5) are given. Correct answers are evaluated with 2 points, and a total of 80 points is given.

  2. Second stage: 10 tasks are given using advanced methods and technologies to determine pedagogical skills. Correct answers are evaluated with 2 points and a total of 20 points can be scored.
