Some state universities are planned to be privatized

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In the concept of development of the higher education system of the Republic of Uzbekistan until 2030, expropriation and privatization of some state higher education institutions (with the exception of higher military educational institutions) and the introduction of corporate management principles, while retaining at least 51 percent of the state's share as an experiment
In addition, the existing capacity of institute status universities will be gradually increased and changed to university status.
