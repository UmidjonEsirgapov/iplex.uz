Students who do not get enough points in the exams can, according to their wishes, study on the basis of a super-contract. You can calculate its amount as follows.

  • If it does not reach from 0.1 points to 1.0 points, it pays 1.5 times the normal contract;
  • If it does not reach 1.1 points to 2.0 points, it pays 2 times the normal contract;

  • If it does not reach 3.1 points to 4.0 points, it will pay 3 times of the normal contract;
