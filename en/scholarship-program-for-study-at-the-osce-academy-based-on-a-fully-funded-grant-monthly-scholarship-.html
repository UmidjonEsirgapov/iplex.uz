General Information:

OSCE Academy is a public foundation in Bishkek, established in 2002 by the Government of Kyrgyzstan and the Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe. Also, this OSCE Academy has now announced a fully funded grant program for advanced, leading young people from the countries of Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan. Every year, up to 60 young people with a strong interest in political science, conflict resolution of international relations, international development studies, economics and management are accepted on a competitive basis.

All candidates can continue their master's courses at the OSCE Academy in these 2 areas:

  • "Economic management and development"
  • "Politics and Security"

Important: Master's courses will start on September 1, 2022 and will last until February 29, 2024. Candidates who successfully participate in the program will take part in the program, which will be held on August 1-26, 2022. they should participate in summer school. All courses within this program are taught in English only.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Educated at the 4-year undergraduate level ("Economic Management and Development or Politics and Security" fields or related fields)must be English;
  • excellent knowledge is required;
  • Must be a citizen of one of the countries of Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan, or a citizen of any other OSCE academy member state;

Stakeholders of the grant:

  • They can be transported on the basis of a full grant;
  • Monthly stipend of 360 Euros;
  • Operation expenses to travel to Bishkek (Economy class);
  • Medical Insurance expenses are covered;
  • The best students with high GPA are sent to paid internship programs in Europe;

To register for the program:

  • Candidates who want to apply for the "Economic Management and Development" course should register through this link;

Note: Only those who have successfully passed the first stage only candidates will be invited to an interview;
