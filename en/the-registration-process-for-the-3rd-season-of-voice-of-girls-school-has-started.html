General information:

In recent years, great conditions have been created for the development of young people. it's no secret to any of us.
At the same time, a number of opportunities have opened wide for our girls who have strong potential in various fields. The "Girls' Voice" club under the Youth Affairs Agency of the Republic of Uzbekistan started its activity 2 years ago, in 2019. One of the highest goals of the club was to gather active girls working in different regions of our country on one platform, to create a space for them to exchange ideas and skills. It is no exaggeration to say that the first election was held in an open and transparent manner and the club achieved its first goal. In this way, coordinators were elected to 14 regions of our republic, and after the elections, 14 coordinators voted for themselves the coordinator of the Republic. It should be mentioned that the term of the elected coordinators was set for one year. Now, even after handing over their tasks to new coordinators, these girls continue their activities in various fields and achieve great achievements. Looking at the recent history, we can say that "Voice of Girls" club was the first big step in the career of many girls.
