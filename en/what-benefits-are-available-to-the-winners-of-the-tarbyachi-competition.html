What benefits are available to the winners of the "Tarbyachi" competition?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In accordance with the Regulation approved by the order of the Ministry of Pre-School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 47 dated March 19, 2019, the winners of the first, second, and third place in each category at the Republican stage of the competition with secondary special, vocational education In addition, in accordance with the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 393 of June 20, 2017, the winners of the Republican competition "Teacher" according to the presentation of the Ministry of Preschool Education, according to the decision of the State Commission they are admitted to higher educational institutions and suitable educational fields on the basis of state grants without non-competition entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam). The winners with secondary special, vocational education will have the privilege of entering the "Pre-school education" field of higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan. The winners of the examination will be admitted to the relevant higher education institutions, according to the decision of the State Commission, in accordance with the presentation of the Ministry of Preschool Education, to the appropriate educational fields, without competitive entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam) on the basis of state grants.
