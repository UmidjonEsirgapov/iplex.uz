An evaluation center for managerial personnel will be established

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Evaluation center for managerial personnel will be established.
The main tasks of the center are as follows: Development of competency models and indicators for state civil servants based on socio-psychological research.
Development and introduction of new assessment methods, modernization of the competency assessment process. With the decision "On the establishment of the Center for the Evaluation of Managerial Personnel" (No. 629), the Center for the Evaluation of Managerial Personnel is established under the authority of the State Service Development Agency. This center performs a number of tasks aimed at improving the process of selection and evaluation of leading personnel.

The main tasks of the center are as follows:

  • Development of competency model and indicators for state civil servants based on social-psychological research.
  • Personal-professional diagnosis of state civil servants, comprehensive assessment of them based on the competency model.
  • Development and implementation of new assessment methods, modernization of the competency assessment process.
  • Development and implementation of a personal-professional development trajectory for managers based on the results of the assessment.
  • Conduct tests and psychological questionnaires to determine the professional knowledge, values and mental potential of candidates for the position of manager.
  • Introduction of digital technologies in the processes of evaluation and monitoring of management personnel, their selection and placement.
  • Conducting scientific researches and expert-analytical work related to the activity of the center.

The organization of this center will help to improve the quality of management personnel, to use fair and modern methods in their evaluation. — Source
