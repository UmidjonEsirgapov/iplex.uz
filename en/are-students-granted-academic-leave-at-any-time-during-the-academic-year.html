Are students granted academic leave at any time during the academic year?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Students are granted academic leave at any time of the academic year for the following periods: for military service - for the period of military service specified in the legislation; for pregnancy and childbirth, as well as child care - for the period of pregnancy and childbirth, as well as child care leave established by legislation (Articles 233-234 of the Civil Code); to restore health and take care of a sick member of his family - for the period until the beginning of the semester of the next year that has not been fully used by the student (if, in accordance with paragraph 11 of this Regulation, the student is transferred to the existing correspondence, distance or externship form of education during the academic leave, for the period until the beginning of the next semester).
In this case, one of the mentioned periods It is determined by the rector (director) of the higher education institution according to the student's application.
Stipends are not paid to the student during the period of academic leave.
