Entry to OTM without an exam: Which Olympiad Winners Have Opportunities?

According to the Presidential Decree No. PQ-4306 dated May 3, 2019, some international Olympiad winners are admitted to the state higher education institutions of Uzbekistan. accepted without test and additional exam. This privilege was introduced in order to support high-potential young people and encourage talented students.

Which international Olympiads offer a privilege?

The winners of the following 5 international Olympiads have the right to enter the OTM without an exam:

1. International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO)

International Mathematical Olympiad

2. International Physics Olympiad (IPhO)

International Physics Olympiad

3. International Chemistry Olympiad (IChO)

International Chemistry Olympiad

4. International Biology Olympiad (IBO)

International Biology Olympiad

5. International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI)

International Olympiad in Informatics

Olympiad winners included in this list are directly accepted as students without passing a test to higher education institutions.

What are the privileges of other Olympiad winners?

Except for the 5 Olympiads listed above, no international or local does not give the right to enter the OTM without an exam.

For winners of the Science Olympiad of the Republic:

  • 1st, 2nd, 3rd place winners will be awarded maximum points in the specialty subject.

  • These points are valid for 3 years does.

  • For example:

    • A laureate in chemistry - if it is a major subject -> 93 points

    • If there is a second specialty subject -> 63 points

In other subjects, the applicant takes the test himself.

Special procedure for IT subject

  • The winners of the republican Olympiad in the subject of informatics and information technology have the right to receive maximum score in the subject of mathematics.

Conclusion

Only those who won the international stage of IMO, IPhO, IChO, IBO and IOI olympiads will be admitted to the HEIs of Uzbekistan without an exam. For the winners of other Olympiads, the privilege is given only in the form of maximum score in the specialty.
