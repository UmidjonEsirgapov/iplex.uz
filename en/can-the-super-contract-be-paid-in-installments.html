Are super-contract education loans available?

According to current legislation, super-contract education loans are not available. This means that preferential educational loans provided by the state are given only to students studying on the basis of a regular contract. However, some commercial banks may offer their own loan products under market conditions. This means that the interest rates and terms are likely to be high.
