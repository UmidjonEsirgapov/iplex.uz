The Berlin Chamber of Deputies established a research fund in 1994 in gratitude to the Allies of the Second World War, who were an integral part of Berlin public life for almost 50 years.

The purpose of the research fund - Friendly with France, Great Britain, USA and Russia and other successor states of the Soviet Union. is to maintain and expand relations.

Who can participate in the program?

Bachelor graduates, masters and doctoral students, candidates of science can participate every year.

Privileges:

  • Graduates and doctoral students 1100 euros per month;
  • For candidates of science Stipends in the amount of 1630 euros.
  • 330 € financial support for additional living expenses for all.

These benefits are allocated 15 scholarships for a period of 10 months.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must have at least a bachelor’s degree;
  • Candidates have a maximum Must be 35 years old and have completed a bachelor's degree by the age of 25;
  • Must be academically and socially active.

Required documents:

  • CV;
  • Research plan;
  • Motivational letter;
  • Information about connection with the University of Berlin;
  • 2 letter of recommendation;
  • university diploma and grades;
