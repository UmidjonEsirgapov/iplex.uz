A new institute will be opened in Andijan

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The decision of the President "On measures to organize the activities of the Andijan State Institute of Foreign Languages" was announced for discussion.
It is noted that the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education will ensure that 10 foreign language teachers working in foreign higher education institutions are involved in the educational process of the Institute until 2024.

According to the document, the educational process at the new institute will start from the 2022/2023 academic year.

It is noted that the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education will ensure the involvement of 10 foreign language teachers working in foreign higher education institutions until 2024 in the educational process of the Institute.

Also, the Agency for the Popularization of Foreign Language Learning under the Cabinet of Ministers, from the 2022/2023 academic year, to the Institute:

- organizing training courses (or intensive training) for professors and specialists in the field of professional translation at the Institute, inviting local and international experts on a fee-contract basis;

- in Andijan region in order to obtain a foreign language level certificate for foreign language teachers at the end of their professional development courses;

- organizing TEFL/TESOL courses with the support of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, the "El-Yurt Umid" fund, sponsors and local authorities; in allocating additional grant places at the expense of local budget funds on the basis of the contract signed between the local state authority and the applicant with the condition of production in this area in the next 5 years;

- Assist in the organization of preparation courses for international qualification certificates of foreign language teaching (TKT, CELTA, DELTA, etc.) at the master's level of education. — Official Data
