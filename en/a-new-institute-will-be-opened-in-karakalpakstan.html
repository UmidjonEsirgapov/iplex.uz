A new institute will be opened in Karakalpakstan

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
The Institute of Agriculture and Agro-Technology of Karakalpakstan will be established on the basis of the Nukus branch of the Tashkent State Agrarian University. It is determined: training of personnel with modern knowledge and skills for the application of innovative resource-saving advanced technologies in agriculture for the northern region of the republic, introduction of "Smart agriculture" and digitized agro-technologies; modernization of the training-laboratory base, reflection of knowledge on innovative and modern production processes related to the field, high-performance and resource-saving advanced technologies in science programs; practice through the development of modern technologies and interactive educational methods (E-Minbar electronic platform), students' independent educational and practical activities (case studies, coaching, etc.), as well as wide introduction of a modular, object-oriented and dynamic educational environment (Moodle) designed to ensure mutual distance cooperation between teachers and students (webinar technologies), develop innovative and analytical thinking in them, and others.

On the basis of the Nukus branch of the Tashkent State Agrarian University, the Institute of Agriculture and Agrotechnologies of Karakalpakstan will be established. The draft of the relevant decision of the President was announced for discussion.
