South Korean Government Scholarship

The South Korean Government Scholarship Program is organized to support future leaders and promote international cooperation in the field of education by inviting talented foreign students to pursue higher education at South Korean universities.

The following expenses are covered by the scholarship:

For those admitted to the Bachelor program:

  • Economy class air ticket for round trip;
  • A stipend of 900,000 Korean won per month;
  • Accommodation fee: 200 upon arrival. 000 Korean Won;
  • Language course fee: full coverage;
  • Tuition fee: All admission fees are waived by the host institution (university). Tuition is covered by NIIED and the university.
  • Medical insurance: 20,000 Korean won per month (limited coverage);
  • Grants for Korean language proficiency: 100,000 Korean won per month for candidates with TOPIK level 5 or 6.

Admission to master's and doctoral programs for those who have:

  • Economy class air ticket for round trip;
  • 1,000,000 Korean won per month for research, 210,000 Korean won for scholars in the field of humanities and social sciences; 240,000 KRW for natural and mechanical science scholars;
  • Accommodation fee: 200,000 KRW upon arrival;
  • Language course fee: full coverage;
  • Tuition fee: All admission fees are waived by the host institution (university). Tuition is covered by NIIED and the university.
  • Dissertation printing costs: 500,000 ~ 800,000 KRW, depending on prices;
  • Medical insurance: 20,000 KRW per month (limited coverage);
  • Grants for Korean speakers: For candidates with TOPIK 5 or 6 100,000 Korean won per month.

Deadline for submission of documents:

Documents of undergraduate students are accepted in September-October-November (studies start from the next academic year). The results of the competition will be known in January.

Documents of students entering the master's or doctoral program will be accepted in February-March-April (studying starts from the next academic year). The results of the competition will be known in June.
