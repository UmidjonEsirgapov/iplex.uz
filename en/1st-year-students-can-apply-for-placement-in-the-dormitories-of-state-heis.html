1st-year students can apply for placement in the dormitories of state HEIs

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, the work of uploading information about those recommended for studentship to the "Student Accommodation Information System" has been completed. they can send an electronic application through the service center or the Unified interactive state services portal - my.gov.uz.
Also, the following categories of students are admitted to student accommodation on the basis of priority: pupils of "Mehribanlik Houses", orphans and students deprived of parental care; Children of families in need of social protection included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection", "Iron Register" and "Women's Register" and students in the "Youth Register"; Students with group I and II disabilities; Children of families from the same family studying bachelor's degree in the form of full-time education on the basis of two or more payment contracts; International science Olympiads, students who won the national science Olympiads held by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation; Students who won the republican stage of the "Student of the Year" and "Student Theater Studios" contests; Students who won the republican stage of the intellectual game "Zakovat" held among students of higher education institutions.

The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovations informed that the work of uploading information about those recommended to students to the "Student Residence Information System" has been completed. Now, 1st-year students can submit an electronic application for placement in the dormitories of state higher education institutions through the State Services Center or Single interactive state services portal - my.gov.uz.

Also, the following categories of students are admitted to student accommodation on a priority basis:

  • "Kindness "houses", orphans and students deprived of parental care;
  • children of families in need of social protection included in the "Single Register of Social Protection", "Iron Register" and "Women's Register", and students who are in the "Youth Register";
  • with group I and II disabilities students;
  • Children of families from one family studying bachelor's degree in full-time education on the basis of two or more fee-contracts;
  • Students who won the international science Olympiads, republican science Olympiads held by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation;
  • "Student of the Year" and "Students of Theater students who won the republican stage of the "studios" competition;
  • Students who won the republican stage of the intellectual game "Zakovat" held among students of higher education institutions.

In addition, some students may be partially or completely exempted from the dormitory fee.

The following dormitories are fully exempted from payment:

  • People with group 1-2 disability according to eye diseases;
  • Chinese orphans (both parents died) and orphans (one parent died);
  • Children of the Mercy Home;
  • Students in need of social protection.

  • People with group 2 disability according to common diseases;
  • Student Council chairman, floor captain and Students responsible for the dormitory library.
