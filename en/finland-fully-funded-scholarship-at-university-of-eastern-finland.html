Finland: Fully funded scholarship at University of Eastern Finland

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Grant amount.
Grant covers 100% or 80% of tuition fees.
Date: January 20, 2021 Place of study: University of Eastern Finland - Finland Requirements: • scholarships students admitted to the master's program in the field of information technologies can receive; • the scholarship is intended for international students who do not have the citizenship of the EU/EEZ countries; • students must have a bachelor's degree (or the equivalent of three years of full-time study) in one of the following disciplines: Computer Science, Informatics, Communications Engineering, or a related discipline; • students must know English (TOEFL PBT/iBT: 580/90, IELTS – 5).

University of Eastern Finland has campuses in Kuopio and Joensuu. The University offers scholarships to international students who are not citizens of EU/EEA countries. Scholarships are awarded to graduate students enrolled in the Information Technology program.

The amount of the grant. The scholarship covers 100% or 80% of tuition fees.

80% of students receive this scholarship. Study - in English, duration of study - 2 years.

Date: January 20, 2021

Place of study: University of Eastern Finland - Finland

Requirements:

• scholarships can be received by students admitted to the master's program in information technology;

• the scholarship is intended for international students who do not have citizenship of the EU / EEA countries;

• students must have a bachelor's degree (or equivalent to three years of full-time study), in one of the subjects: computer science, informatics, communication engineering or related subjects;

• students must know English (TOEFL PBT/iBT: 580/90, IELTS – 5).

How can I get a scholarship?

The application must include the following documents:

• official account statement with official English translation;

• certified bachelor degree;

• motivation letter;

• video to be uploaded to one of the platforms: youtube/vimeo/tudou you must provide a link to the video. The video should consist of an introduction, a brief description of the student's background and the student's possible services (the duration of the video should not exceed 2 minutes);

• a letter of recommendation;

• should provide the result of one of the English language proficiency tests;

• a copy of the passport;

• a sample of work or a link to information written in English (research, lecture, essay, fiction, literature review). This article should be translated into English or Finnish.
