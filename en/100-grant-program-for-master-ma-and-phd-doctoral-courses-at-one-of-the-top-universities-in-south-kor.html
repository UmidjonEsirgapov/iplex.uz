General Information:

One of the top universities in South Korea-Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST) University administration (MA) and master's degree (PhD) has announced a 100% all-expenses-paid grant program for doctoral studies. This program is mainly for students studying (IT) information technology. The total number of allocated grants is about 300.

Benefits of the grant:

  • The contract fee is covered 100%;
  • A monthly stipend of 140,000 Won($120) is assigned for master's degree students;
  • A monthly stipend of 295,000 Won) ($210) will be assigned for doctoral students;
  • A separate $85 will be allocated for food expenses (Students must have 9 credits in the previous semester to receive this grant); students will be given an additional $100.;

For students conducting scientific projects:

  1. A one-time amount of $4,100 for master's students;
  2. Average of $10,500 for doctoral students. one-time funding;
  • A one-time arrival ticket to South Korea will be covered;
  • 80% medical insurance will be covered;
  • One-time medical examination every year;

Documents required for the program:

  1. Initially, it is necessary to submit an online application for this program
  2. Diploma and grade book (must be certified by a notary);
  3. Copy of passport;
  4. Language certificate (TOEFL (BT) 80 / TOEFL (PBT) 550 / IELTS 6.5 / TOEIC 750 / NEW TEPS 327);

Important: Candidates accepted into the program must pay a one-time fee of 680,000 KRW ($571). Financially needy students must meet other requirements stating that they cannot afford to pay this amount. — More information
