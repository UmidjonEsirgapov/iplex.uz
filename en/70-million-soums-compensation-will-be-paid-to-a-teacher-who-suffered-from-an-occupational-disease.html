70 million soums will be paid as compensation to the teacher who suffered from an occupational disease

A teacher from Syrdarya worked in the same school for 20 years and contracted chronic laryngitis. Although it was determined that the illness was related to professional activity, the local education department did not pay him the amount due to him on time.

What was the court decision?

Federation of Trade Unions of Uzbekistan Syrdarya Regional Council filed a lawsuit on behalf of the teacher. application.

  • The district pre-school and school education department did not pay the compensation of a teacher who retired due to an occupational disease.
  • Although instructions were given by the Federation of Trade Unions in this regard, they were not followed.
  • According to the court decision, the teacher was awarded 70 million soums in damages and compensation.

Attention to occupational diseases is increasing

This situation once again put the issue of protecting the rights of pedagogues suffering from occupational diseases and creating suitable conditions for them on the agenda. It is emphasized that the compensation system for teachers' health problems and the compensation for damages caused by diseases should work effectively. — Source
