Private university students can work legally from the 3rd year

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the government's decision "On measures to attract qualified teachers-pedagogues to schools with a low level of education" (15.10.2021 No. 646), in the 3rd year of day-evening part-time departments in pedagogy studying students are given the right to teach in schools.
This procedure applies equally to students of state and non-state higher education institutions.
According to the previous procedure, students could teach in schools starting from the 4th year.
