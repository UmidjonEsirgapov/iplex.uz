Now only those who study well will study on the grant!

Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year grant places will be redistributed annually based on GPA. According to the new regulation, only students with a high learning rate will remain on the grant or win a new grant.

Who does it apply to?

  • ✅ Bachelors and masters students who are students for the first time in the 2024/2025 academic year

  • ❌ 2nd year and above students are excluded from this procedure

About masters:

  • 1st-year masters grants are also redistributed in the summer

  • ❌ But the following are excluded:

    • Women studying at a master's degree

    • Specializations with a full state grant

    • 1-year master's programs

For the holders of privileges:

  • ❌ For those who entered the grant based on the privilege (winners of the international Olympiad, women who received recommendations, etc.) this procedure does not apply

  • For them, the grant is guaranteed for a full 4 years

Those who entered with targeted admission:

  • ❌ The new procedure will not be applied to those who have a grant through targeted admission

  • For them, too, the grant is given for 4 years

What if the student is deprived of the grant?

  • ✅ If the student has GPA 3.5 or higher in the next academic year, can apply for the grant again

  • Every year in July-August, the competition is held again, taking into account the previous results not received

Conclusion:

The new system introduces criteria of competition and justice into the grant process. Each student must maintain a high rate of continuous study and achievement to remain on the scholarship. — More information
