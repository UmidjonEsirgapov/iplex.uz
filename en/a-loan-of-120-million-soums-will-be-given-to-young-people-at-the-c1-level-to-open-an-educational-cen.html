A loan of 120 million soums will be given to young people at the "C1" level to open an educational center in remote areas.

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
A door of great opportunities is opening in Uzbekistan for young people who know foreign languages.
Now, young people who have the "C1" level, if they open an educational center specializing in foreign languages in remote and remote areas, they will receive an interest-free loan of up to 120 million soums
Through this, the state not only encourages language learning, but also aims to create equality in education across regions. Now young people with C1 level, if they open an educational center specializing in foreign languages in remote and remote areas, they will be allocated 120 million soums interest-free loan.

Who can use this opportunity?

  • Young people with C1 level international language certificate;

  • Those who want to open a foreign language training center on their own initiative in remote and remote areas;

  • It is required that the activity of the training center specializes in foreign languages.

The state not only encourages language learning through this, but also in regional education aims to create equality.

"2+6" system: financial relief for language learners

  • For young people learning a foreign language in private educational centers:

    • The first 2 months of education will be paid by themselves

    • Next 6 months of education will be paid by the state;

  • Through this system, more young people are expected to enter the labor market in a ready state.

Why is it important?

  • Knowing foreign languages is the key to finding a job, developing entrepreneurship and entering foreign markets in the modern world.

  • Through these initiatives, international labor migration and export potential will increase.

  • Youth initiative and equal opportunities in education will expand. — Source
