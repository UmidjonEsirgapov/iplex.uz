The "Career" electronic platform from the 2026/2027 academic year will be launched

In order to digitize the processes of providing employment to students and graduates of higher and professional educational organizations of the Republic, it is planned to launch the electronic platform "Career" starting from the 2026/2027 academic year. This initiative serves to increase the employment of young people, direct them to professions that are in high demand in the labor market, and establish effective cooperation with employers.

Initiative to introduce the "Career" platform

Regarding the discussion portal In the year "Neighborhood Development and Upliftment of Society", reform programs on priority directions and implementation of the "Uzbekistan - 2030" strategy The draft of the Decree on the State Program aimed at increasing the number of students is available for public discussion. According to the project, it is planned to manage and monitor the processes of providing employment to students and graduates through a single system through the new platform. How does the "Career" electronic platform work? operates as a modern digital system that helps to provide.

Online labor market will be created

A single online labor market connecting students, graduates and employers will be formed through the "Career" platform. Through this system, job seekers and employers will have the opportunity to establish quick communication with each other.

Suitable jobs are recommended on the basis of artificial intelligence

It is planned to use artificial intelligence technologies on the platform. Through it:

  • Students and graduates' abilities, mastery level and interests are analyzed;

  • Suitable vacancies are automatically recommended to them;

  • Qualified and suitable candidates are offered to employers.

Online preparation for job interviews is created

Through the platform students and graduates will have the opportunity to remotely prepare for the interview process with employers. This will further increase the employment opportunities of young people.

Employment processes will be automatically monitored

A system of automatic monitoring of the employment processes of students and graduates will be introduced on the "Career" platform. Through this, the employment indicators will be analyzed and relevant decisions will be made easier.

What opportunities will be created as a result of the introduction of the platform?

If this project is implemented, it is planned to achieve the following results:

New opportunities for 1.6 million students and graduates

The opportunity for 1.6 million students and graduates to choose a profession that matches their interests and skills through the platform will be created.

A convenient personnel base for employers will be formed

More than 50,000 employers will be integrated into the platform, and an opportunity will be created for them to quickly and efficiently find qualified personnel.

Employment of technical school graduates will increase

The processes of finding suitable jobs for 150,000 technical school graduates will be simplified and their employment will increase.

Summary

The introduction of the "Career" electronic platform is expected to be an important step in ensuring the employment of students and graduates in Uzbekistan, digitizing the labor market and establishing effective cooperation with employers.
