Australia: Charles Darwin University $7,500 scholarship program for undergraduate and graduate courses

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Application: Candidates do not need to submit a separate application for the grant.
The admission for the grant program will continue until the end of the acceptance of documents for the course you have chosen.
You can apply to the university here.

University:

Charles Darwin University, established in 2003 as an Australian public university. This university was formed as a result of the merger with the University of the Northern Territory and was named after the famous English naturalist Charles Darwin. Since then, knowledge has been shared with thousands of national and international students.

Destination Australia grant program:

This grant is international and candidates from all countries can apply. The grant is intended to cover the student's $7,500 tuition fee.

Application:

Candidates do not need to submit a separate application for the grant. By submitting an application to one of the university's bachelor's or master's courses here, they will have the right to apply for the above grant. You can see the study programs offered by the university here.

Expiration period:

The admission for study in the 2nd semester of the university is currently open and the last date of admission is different in different areas. Admission for the scholarship program will also continue until the end of the acceptance of documents for the course of your choice. You can apply to the university here.
