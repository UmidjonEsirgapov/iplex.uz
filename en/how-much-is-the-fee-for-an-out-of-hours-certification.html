How much is the fee for out-of-order attestation?

Clause 21 of the Regulation on the Certification of Pedagogical Personnel stipulates that a fee of one times the basic calculation amount shall be collected for providing public service for the extraordinary certification of pedagogic personnel.

The amount of fees is as follows:

  1. When applying through state service centers - 100% of the basic calculation amount (currently 340 thousand soums, 375 thousand soums from October 1st).

  2. When applying through the https://pedkadr.uzedu.uz/ platform - of the basic calculation amount 90 percent (currently 306 thousand soums, starting from October 1 337 thousand 500 soums).

  3. When applying through the single interactive public services portal - 90 percent of the base calculation amount (currently 306 thousand soums, From October 1 337 thousand 500 soums).

Payments are made at the time of registration and application for participation in the certification. That is, when the type of "extraordinary certification" is selected, a notice (receipt) with all the information about the payment will be sent to each teacher.
