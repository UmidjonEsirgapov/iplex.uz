1319 candidates for the directorship were trained in the "Management training course" by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education, and 834 of them were given a certificate. This was announced at the meeting of the Board dedicated to the summary of the work carried out in the field in the first half of this year and the implementation of future tasks under the leadership of the Minister of Preschool and School Education Hilola Umarova. 5. 7,949 trainee-teachers were involved in professional certification, 190,000 teachers were involved in tests for awarding qualification categories based on the new procedure.

In the meeting held in a critical-analytical spirit, the existing shortcomings in the system and the work that needs to be done were discussed in detail. In particular, it was criticized that in some regions of the republic, including Andijan, Jizzakh, Samarkand regions, the indicators of coverage of children's preschool education dropped significantly compared to the beginning of the year.

Therefore, by the end of the year, it is necessary to reach the level of coverage of preschool education to 76%, to effectively start the activities of 1,300 basic schools starting from the new academic year, at the same time Specific tasks have been set for those responsible for organizing Professional Development Hour and Professional Development Day events and proper training of mentors. — Source
