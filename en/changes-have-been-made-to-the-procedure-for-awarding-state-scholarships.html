Changes have been made to the procedure for awarding state scholarships

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
By the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, a number of changes have been made to the procedure for appointing State Scholarships of the President of Uzbekistan and State Scholarships for students of Master's and Bachelor's degrees.
According to the decision, information technology, history of Uzbekistan and knowledge and skills of foreign languages are determined to be assessed through special tests at the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency (by region).
According to the new regulation: the lower limit of the passing score for the tests is set at 55% of the highest possible score; Applicants who scored below 55 percent and did not participate in the tests are not recommended for presidential and named state scholarships; An appeals commission consisting of at least 5 members will be established to consider the complaints of applicants who are dissatisfied with the evaluation results.

By the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, a number of changes were made to the procedure for appointing the President and State Scholarships of Uzbekistan for students of Master's and Bachelor's degrees. technologies, history of Uzbekistan and knowledge and skills in foreign languages will be evaluated in the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency (by region) through special tests.

According to the new regulation:

  • the lower limit of the passing score for the tests is set at 55 percent of the highest possible score;
  • score below 55 percent Candidates who have passed the tests and failed to participate in the tests will not be recommended for the presidential and named state scholarships. These criteria include:
    • research works (up to 40 points), achievements (up to 20 points) and results of agency tests (up to 30 points) for master's degree candidates;
    • research works (up to 30 points), achievements (up to 20 points), language certificate (up to 10 points) and results of agency tests (up to 30 points) for bachelor's degree candidates are included as part of the evaluation.

    It is planned that the applicants' learning indicators will be formed through the HEMIS information system of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

    In addition, it was decided that students who won the named state scholarships will not be allowed to participate again in the named state scholarship contests at a certain stage of education. — Read on infoedu.uz
