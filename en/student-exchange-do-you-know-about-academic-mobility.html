Student exchange: do you know about academic mobility?

Academic mobility is an opportunity for students or teachers to study or teach for a certain period of time in higher education institutions within the country or abroad.

There are two main types of student exchange

  1. Free movers - a student on his own initiative studies at a university in another region or country.
  2. Students within the program - in this case, the university or faculty makes an agreement with another HEI and implements exchange programs. Programs can be at national or international level.
