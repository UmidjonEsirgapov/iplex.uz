How much will the entrance exam fee be in 2025? It was officially announced

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The amount of this payment is determined annually on the basis of the basic calculation amount (BHM).
Applicants who have graduated from school in previous years, those who want to take part in tests for admission to higher education. The amount of this payment is determined annually on the basis of base calculation amount (BHM).

In 2025:

  • BHM - 340,000 soums

  • The payment is half of the BHM, i.e. 170,000 soums

This procedure is formalized on the basis of the regulation "On education in the Republic of Uzbekistan" and applies to applicants who have completed secondary education in previous years.

Who will pay?

  • Applicants who have graduated from school in previous years

  • Those who want to take the test for admission to higher education

Who are exempt from the fee?

The following categories of applicants are exempt from the test fee of 170 thousand soums:

  • Graduates who graduated from school this year

  • Persons with disabilities

  • Children of orphanages and orphanages

  • Those listed in the "Unified Register of Social Protection" and their children

  • Young people in the "Youth register"

Note: Test fee in the exam is a necessary document for participation. If the payment is not made, the applicant will not be admitted to the test. — Official Data
