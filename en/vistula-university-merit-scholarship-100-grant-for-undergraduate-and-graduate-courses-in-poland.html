Vistula University, located in Warsaw, Poland, offers the Vistula University Merit Scholarship program to foreign students who wish to study at the undergraduate and graduate level. This grant will be awarded to a total of 70 students for studies starting in October 2023 or March 2024.

The application dates for this grant are as follows:

  • For studies starting in October 2023 – until August 1, 2021;
  • For studies starting in March 2024 - until January 15, 2024.

Grant to study at the Vistula University or the Vistula School of Hospitality (Vistula School of Hospitality) on a full-time basis (full-time) in undergraduate or graduate courses taught in English and to act as ambassadors of these universities.

Who is the grant for?

For those who want to study at bachelor's and master's degrees
