About the program:

Harvard University MBA (Masters of Business Administration) courses are one of the world's leading business programs taught at one of the world's most prestigious universities. The Boustany MBA Harvard Scholarship program is offered every two years for students with high knowledge and skills who want to study in a two-year course at Harvard Business School.

Grant offers the following provides:
• Financial assistance of $102,200 in tuition fees ($51,100 per year);
• Internship-related travel and living expenses.

This grant is available for an MBA program beginning in Fall 2022.

Eligibility requirements:

Candidates have excellent academic standing. they need to have information and set themselves the goal of achieving high results in the future. Although the scholarship is open to candidates of any nationality, priority is given to candidates from the country of Lebanon. But candidates from all countries are welcome to apply.

Note: Candidates can only apply for the scholarship after receiving an offer for Harvard MBA program.

You can apply for the MBA program here.

Application process:
Candidates apply for the scholarship by sending their CV (with photo), GMAT scores and MBA acceptance letter from the university to the address below to:

  • admissions@boustany-foundation.org

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview and based on the results of the interview, one candidate will be awarded a grant.

Application deadline: May 31, 2022.

Note: The grant will be awarded in June 2022.
