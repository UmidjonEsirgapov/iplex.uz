To whom is an educational loan granted and what is the procedure for obtaining it?

Brief content:

The educational loan agreement is concluded for an amount equal to the one-year payment amount specified in the payment-contract agreement and it is allocated in the amount of the main rate of the Central Bank.
Of these, 11 banks will pay 100% of the amount specified in the university agreement.

This the procedure does not apply to foreign citizens and stateless persons.

Educational loans are given to students who are citizens of Uzbekistan who have been recommended or accepted to study under a full-time, part-time and evening education in the form of full-time, part-time and evening education, as well as those who have transferred their studies. family members with a permanent source of income (parents, siblings, husband or wife) or guardians can participate as joint borrowers.

The principal debt of the education loan is repaid for 7 years starting from the seventh month after the end of the student's official study period.

The following documents are submitted to commercial banks published on the official website of the Ministry of Finance in order to receive an educational loan:

  • application;
  • payment contract agreement and the attached statement/invoice (if available). The contract must specify the deadline for graduation and the amount of payment for one year;
  • a document on the provision of repayment of an educational loan. In this case, no collateral or guarantor is required for the education loan allocated to the children of the families included in the "Uniform Social Protection Register";
  • Certificate for children of the families included in the "Uniform Social Protection Register";
  • information about the joint borrower.

Documents will be reviewed in 5 working days.
