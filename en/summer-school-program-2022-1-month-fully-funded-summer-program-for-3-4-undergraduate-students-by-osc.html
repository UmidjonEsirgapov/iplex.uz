General Information:

OSCE Academy is a public foundation in Bishkek, established in 2002 by the Government of Kyrgyzstan and the Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe. Also, a 1-month summer school in Bishkek/Issyk-Kol, Kyrgyzstan, fully funded by this OSCE Academy for advanced, leading young people from Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan currently studying at the undergraduate level. (will take place between August 1-26, 2022) announced the program.

In particular, this Summer School is designed to provide participants with knowledge and skills in the following 2 categories of courses:

  • Mathematics for Economists(Mathematics for Economists);
  • In the social sphere research methods (Research Methods in Social Sciences);

Requirements:

Candidates must meet the following criteria: Bachelor 3-4 year students in economics, international relations, political science or related fields;
• High level of English;
• Development and international studies with a focus on the wider Central Asian region interest in relations;
• Citizens of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan;

Financial support/scholarship:

• Stipend of 360 Euros for four weeks;
• Visa and registration support;
• Travel across the Central Asia region to Bishkek travel expenses are also fully covered;

Note: The Academy does not cover medical insurance and expenses of family members. The Academy will help you in all matters related to getting to Bishkek and finding housing in the city.
