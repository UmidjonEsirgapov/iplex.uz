Exact dates for entrance tests to higher education institutions for the 2024-2025 academic year have been announced. The tests are scheduled to be held from July 14 to July 28, 2024. The Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, Kongratbay Sharipov, said this at the "Government Hour" in the Legislative Chamber.

Details of Entrance Tests

This year, the specific days for entrance exams to higher education institutions are July 14-28, and applicants will pass their tests during this period. This news is important for applicants and their parents, because the process of preparing for the exams will be planned and organized.

Speech of Minister Sharipov

Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation Kongratbay Sharipov informed about the planned days of entrance exams in "Government hour". He also spoke about the work being done to ensure the fair and transparent conduct of this year's exams. All the necessary conditions will be created for the applicants and all measures will be taken to ensure the fair conduct of the exams.
