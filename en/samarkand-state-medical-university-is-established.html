Samarkand State Medical University is established

The draft of the President's decision "On the fundamental improvement of the personnel training system at the Samarkand State Medical Institute" was announced. establishment of the Samarkand State Medical University; Establishment of the Research Institute of Rehabilitation and Sports Medicine under the university on the basis of the health restoration hospital of the Samarkand region, located in the village of Ortashiq, Samarkand district, Samarkand region; On the basis of the first and second clinics of the Samarkand State Medical Institute, the establishment of a multidisciplinary clinic of the Samarkand State Medical University and a specialized children's minimally invasive surgical clinic of the Samarkand State Medical University, respectively; Renaming the lyceum of the Samarkand State Medical Institute as a university lyceum; Establishment of a scientific research institute of microbiology, virology, infectious and parasitic diseases under the university on the basis of the Samarkand branch of the Republican Scientific and Practical Center of Microbiology, Virology, Infectious and Parasitic Diseases named after L.M. It is determined that the university: is the legal successor of the Samarkand State Medical Institute in terms of all rights, obligations and contracts; From January 1, 2022, it will be transferred to the self-financing system; university clinics, lyceum, specialized scientific-practical center of neurosurgery, scientific center of immunology and human genomics, and research institute of rehabilitation and sports medicine are the founders of the university and their activities are coordinated by the university; structural structures and the university itself are state institutions with the status of a legal entity, they have personal treasury accounts in the treasury department of the Ministry of Finance; the first heads of the structural structures of the university are appointed and dismissed by the Minister of Health according to the presentation of the academic council of the university; Samarkand region is working with public health technical schools and medical colleges named after Abu Ali ibn Sino in the following directions: creating the material and technical, educational and methodological base necessary for the educational process and attracting experienced specialists to the educational process; to enrich the funds of the information-resource center of technical schools and colleges with modern educational and teaching-methodical literature, to improve the "electronic library" system, and to ensure their effective use, to establish a hub of innovative education and teaching-methodical resources in the information-resource center of the university; in order to develop scientific activity in technical schools and colleges, to provide scientific and methodological support to teachers working in them.
