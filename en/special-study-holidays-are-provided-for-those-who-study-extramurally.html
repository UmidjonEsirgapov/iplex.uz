Special study holidays are provided for those who study extramurally

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Employers are writing to part-time employees that they require them to take work leave during the study period.
Pursuant to Article 385 of the Labor Code, employees studying in the form of evening or part-time education in higher and secondary special, vocational education organizations, educational holidays for the period of participation in laboratory-examination sessions: for first- and second-year students studying in the form of evening education in higher education organizations - at least twenty calendar days, secondary in special, vocational education organizations - at least ten calendar days, and for those studying in the form of part-time education in higher and secondary special education organizations - at least thirty days; To those studying in the third and higher courses in the form of evening education in higher education institutions - at least thirty calendar days, in organizations of secondary special and vocational education - at least twenty calendar days, and to those studying in the form of part-time education in organizations of higher and secondary special education, vocational education - at least forty calendar days are given annually, with the average salary maintained.
