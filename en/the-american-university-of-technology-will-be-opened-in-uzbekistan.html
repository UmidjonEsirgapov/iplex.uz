The American University of Technology will be opened in Uzbekistan

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
The presidential decree has been signed and the university will start its activities from the 2024/2025 academic year.
It was reported by Uza.
Educational areas: Engineering Health Information technologies Architecture and design Economy and finance International relations The American University of Technology is expected to bring new standards and opportunities to the educational system of Uzbekistan. The presidential decree on this was signed and the university will start its activities from the 2024/2025 academic year.

Highly educated personnel in the fields of engineering, healthcare, information technologies, architecture and design, economy and finance, and international relations will be trained at the level of international standards at the new university. This was reported by Uza.

University graduates receive a diploma from Arizona State University or a partner foreign higher education institution recognized in the United States of America. In this case, the diploma awarded to graduates is recognized in the Republic of Uzbekistan. It offers great opportunities for students and the opportunity to receive an internationally recognized education.
