The "President's gifts" will be delivered to the 1st graders by August 20

The processes of sending the "President's gifts" to the regions and preparing for the new academic year, which will be given to the 1st graders today by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education A press tour dedicated to the promotion was organized.

According to the Information Service of the Ministry, in the 2024-2025 academic year in 10,182 schools, more than 525 thousand teachers will teach 6.5 million. it is expected to provide education to more than 100 students.

In the new academic year, 719,366 children of school age are planned to be admitted to the 1st grade. The "President's gift" consisting of 12 educational materials, given by the enterprise "Supplement and Logistics Service", for students admitted to the 1st grade, will be fully delivered to the regions by August 10, and to all schools according to the number of admitted students by August 20.

These 12 educational materials - portfolio, It consists of a set of notebook, sketchbook, watercolor paints, colored paper, pencil case, ballpoint pen, colored pencil, regular pencil, plasticine, glue, eraser. is being introduced.

As it was noted during the press tour, in the new academic year, the main focus will be on quality education. For this purpose, in the 2023/2024 academic year, the system of teaching and evaluation of Presidential and specialized schools was pilot-tested in 500 schools of the republic, and positive results were achieved. This experiment will be implemented in 1,000 additional schools starting from the new academic year.

In addition, 500 foreign experts were attracted to schools in the last academic year, and effective results were achieved in teaching foreign languages to students, and this process will be continued in the new academic year.

In 2024, the state Based on the program, more than 180,000 new student places will be created as a result of construction and repair works in 608 schools (building 30 new schools, reconstruction of 518 schools and complete repair of 60 schools). Also, 95 new sports hall buildings will be built.

Preschool education was also highlighted. In the 2024-2025 academic year 37,548 (6,867 state, 835 non-state, 1,462 PPP and 28,384 family) preschool education organizations will cover more than 2.3 million (74.6 percent) children aged 3-7 years.
