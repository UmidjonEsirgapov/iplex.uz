Note to applicants: Admission to educational programs where a foreign language is the first subject is based on a national or international certificate.

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to the law (PF-81, 2024.24.05), starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, admission to the fields of study where a foreign language is the first subject in the complex of subjects is carried out on the basis of a national or international certificate specifying the level of knowledge of a foreign language.
In this case, the test of this subject will not be conducted during the admission to the education courses where foreign language is the first subject.
Applicants are required to present a certificate of the specified level.
