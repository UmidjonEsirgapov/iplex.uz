Rent compensation is provided to highly rated private educational centers

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Private educational centers with a high rating will be compensated for rent. In accordance with the relevant decision of the President, the rating of private educational centers in the republic will be formed and continuously maintained by the Youth Affairs Agency in September-November 2024.
Rating based on the results, the rental costs for the buildings and structures of the private educational centers that have achieved the highest results, especially in remote and remote areas, will be compensated.
The results of this rating will be published on the official website of the Youth Affairs Agency.
