On October 17, President Shavkat Mirziyoyev held a meeting to discuss expected results in the economy in 2024 and the main macroeconomic indicators intended for 2025. This was reported by the press service of the head of state.

At the meeting, the main directions of the State budget project for 2025 were defined. Based on the principle of the welfare state, it is planned to increase the funds allocated to education and health care by 20 percent. According to the law, this project and measures to ensure its implementation are discussed in local councils and finally submitted to the Legislative Chamber of the Supreme Assembly.
