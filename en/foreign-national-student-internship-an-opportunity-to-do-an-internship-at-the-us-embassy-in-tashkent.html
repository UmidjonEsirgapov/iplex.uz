General information:

U.S. Embassy in Tashkent Public Relations Department "Foreign National We are looking for suitable and qualified candidates for the Student Internship Program. The internship can be extended from 6 months to 1 year. The internship is 20 hours per week from Monday to Friday. Students are not paid during the internship.

Benefits:

The internship provides an excellent opportunity for students to gain work experience in an international organization; by actively participating in various programming activities, they can improve their English language skills and further develop their skills in planning events and program management.

The main tasks that interns should perform:

  • Helping employees to keep the ACT package in the right order and organize explanatory work;
  • Preparing book gifts (making book lists, stickers for books pasting etc.);
  • Assist clients with registration and registration of computers, TVs, ACT resources, books;
  • Assist with phone calls and ACT visitor registration;
  • Assist staff with managing ACT social media channels;
  • Assist staff with maintaining client and application databases
  • Downloading, printing and making copies of information from designated websites;

Students must meet the following criteria to participate in the program:

  • Be at least 18 years old;
  • Citizens who are legal residents of Uzbekistan outside of the United States;
  • Educated from a high school or secondary special education institution graduated;
  • Currently studying at the undergraduate level as a 1-3 year student of a higher education institution in Uzbekistan;
  • Has a good academic standing at the current educational institution;
  • Must have good computer, internet and customer service skills;
  • Fluent knowledge of English, Russian and/or Uzbek languages;

Note: "Student must be studying at a high school, trade school, technical or vocational institute, college, university or other accredited institution of higher learning and be able to complete at least 20 hours of practice per week.

How to apply:

Candidates must fill out the application form and submit the application along with the following documents In order to be considered in the program by April 15, 2022, they should submit to the email address TashkentRecruitment@state.gov:

  • Completed application form of the foreign national student internship program;
  • Copy of passport;
  • Certificate confirming that the candidates are currently registered at the indicated institution;
  • Current study transcript;
  • Recommendations and certificates (optional);

Selection criteria:

  • Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview;
  • The selected candidate must provide written permission from the educational institution and other necessary documents;
