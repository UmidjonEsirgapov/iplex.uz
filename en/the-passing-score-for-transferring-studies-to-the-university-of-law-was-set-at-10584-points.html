The passing scores for transferring to the Tashkent State University of Law have been announced

To Tashkent State University of Law from abroad, 56 percent (105.84 points) of the maximum score for transferring studies from foreign HEIs branches and non-state HEIs was set. This indicator is important for students who want to continue their studies in the field of law.

What is the minimum score for transferring studies to HEIs?

For transferring studies to other higher education institutions under the supervision of the Ministry of Higher Education, the passing score is 35% of the maximum score (66.1 points). At the same time, the Ministry of Health has not clarified yet for higher educational institutions in the field of medicine.
