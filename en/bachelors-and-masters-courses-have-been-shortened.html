Bachelor's and master's courses have been reduced: Changes in the new academic year

Starting with the new 2024-2025 academic year, higher education courses have been optimized by up to 40% based on the educational programs of TOP-300 universities. Nazokat Abdukunduzova, secretary of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, announced this at a press briefing on the preparation of the republic's higher and professional educational institutions for the new academic year. As a result, 306 undergraduate courses were reduced to 183 and 645 master's specialties were reduced to 546. qualification requirements were developed, approved and delivered to all universities according to these optimized directions.
