The procedure for granting interest-free education loans to women

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to the President's decision No. PQ-323 of July 18, 2022, a system of interest-free (0%) education loans was introduced for women studying on a payment contract.
To whom
✅ To students of all higher education institutions in Uzbekistan (Uzbekistan branches of state, private and foreign HEIs); ✅ To students of secondary special and professional educational institutions (technicum, vocational college, etc.).

According to the decision of the President No. A system of interest-free (0%) educational loans has been introduced to women studying under a payment contract.

Who are they granted to?

Which courses are given?

✅ It is given to all students, whether it is 1st year or 3rd year, it does not matter.

Which form of education is given?

  • Daytime;
  • External;
  • Evening.

To the following not granted:

  • super-contract;
  • joint education;
  • distance education;
  • second specialization;
  • residency;
  • to HEIs abroad.

When does the payment start?

The main part of the education loan is the student's official study period. it will be returned for 7 years starting from the seventh month (i.e. after six months) after completion. For example, if an educational loan of 7 million soums is taken, after graduation, 83 thousand soums will be paid monthly for 7 years after 6 months.
