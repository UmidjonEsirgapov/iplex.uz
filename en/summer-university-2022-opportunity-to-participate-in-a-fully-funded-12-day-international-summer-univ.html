General Information:

XVIII International Summer University at DKU in Almaty: Kazakhstan-German University (DKU) in cooperation with the German Academic Exchange Fund (DAAD) invites students from Germany and Central Asia to participate in the 18th International Summer University in 2022!

The goal of the Summer University is, on the one hand, to assess the development of social movements after independence, and on the other hand, to critically review theoretical approaches that explain social activism in Central Asia and the South Caucasus.

Summer University 2022 offers 2 main modules on Central Asia and South Caucasus. While the Central Asia course deals with political science and sociology, the South Caucasus course is more anthropological. Students only need to apply for one module. Each module consists of groups of 10-15 students. Also, lessons are taught in English by an experienced teacher. Course work consists of group work, seminars, presentations.

Course

Topic

1. Central Asia

Civic activism in Central Asia

(political science and sociology)

2. South Caucasus

Civil activism (anthropology) in the South Caucasus

Note: Also, because the Summer University intends to create an impression of the Central Asian region, Kazakhstan and the city of Almaty, this program includes trips to exhibitions and trips to the mountains, sightseeing. , will also receive a variety of activities such as interactive and guided visits. Most of the costs are covered by the tuition fee.

Important: Candidates are currently studying political science and sociology or related fields at the undergraduate or graduate level, or students who have demonstrated a strong interest in these fields can apply. will have opportunities:

Participants from Central Asia:

Students and young academics from Central Asian countries (except Almaty) can apply directly to DKU.DKU will provide scholarships to cover travel expenses, accommodation in shared rooms (including breakfast) and tuition fees. Meals will also be provided during the conference days.

Note: Dinners are not included (dinner costs must be covered by the candidate).

Note: In fact, the total cost of this 12-day program is 650 euros for German students. For students from Central Asia, it is 35,000 tenge (average $80). It must be paid on the first day of the summer university. You can participate even without winning a grant by paying this amount of money.

Application documents:

  • Motivation letter (maximum 300 words);

  • A document confirming sufficient knowledge of the English language (if available, attach IELTS\TOEFL certificates);

  • If available, university or document confirming admission to the university of applied sciences;

  • Questionnaire form ( download here ).

Language of instruction: English

Certificate: After successful completion of the summer school, participants will receive a certificate indicating the subject of the course and the number of 3 ECTS obtained.

Deadline: Send application documents to May 15 to int_office@dku.kz. — More information
