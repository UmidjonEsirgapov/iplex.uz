Extramural education: in which areas is 5 years of work experience required?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the decision No. PQ-289 of June 22, 2022, persons with at least five years of work experience related to pedagogical activities in the education system are required to be admitted to the education courses in the field of pedagogy in the form of correspondence education.

Other areas of education: For other areas of education, experience related to pedagogical activities is not required, and any applicant can register for them online.

According to the decision PQ-289 dated June 22, 2022, when accepting part-time education for educational areas in the field of pedagogy persons with at least five years of work experience related to pedagogical activity in the educational system are required.

Directions in which at least 5 years of work experience are required for external education:

  • Primary education
  • Fine arts and engineering graphics
  • Music education
  • Physical culture
  • Technological education

Pedagogical staff who have at least 5 years of work experience in the public education system and are currently working in this system can register as applicants for the part-time education of these educational areas.

Those who have at least five years of work experience and are currently working information about the incoming teaching staff is obtained from the Ministry of Pre-school and School Education.

Other educational areas:

For other educational areas, experience related to pedagogical activities is not required and any applicant can register for them online. — Read on infoedu.uz
