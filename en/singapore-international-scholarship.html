Singapore International Scholarship

Date: 01.12.2020
For whom: Doctorate applicants
Stipend: $1440-1780

Singapore International Graduate Award (SINGA) is an international graduate award aimed at producing doctors of science at the following research institutes: "The Nanyang Technological University", "National University of Singapore" and "Singapore University of Technology and Design". Students who successfully complete their studies will receive a doctorate degree from one of these three universities.

Requirements:
1) Excellent academic results
2) High level of English
3) Positive evaluations by academic judges

Benefits:
$1440 monthly stipend, after passing the qualifying exam $1,780 increase and $1,080 in airfare.
