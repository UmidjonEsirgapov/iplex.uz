The procedure for obtaining a license is changing: it is not necessary to hire teachers in advance!

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
A new procedure for obtaining a license will be introduced. Amendments have been made to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 80 dated 21.02.2022.
The following important points are defined as a new procedure: The procedure for hiring teachers is being canceled: a licensed non-governmental educational organization must enter all information about the educational organization, students and pedagogues in the relevant information systems of state bodies within one month.

A new procedure for obtaining a license will be introduced

Decision No. 80 dated 21.02.2022 was amended. These changes are aimed at improving the process of licensing activities in the field of private education. The following important points are defined as a new procedure:

  1. The procedure for hiring teachers is canceled:A non-governmental educational organization that has received a license, an educational organization within one month, must enter all information about students and pedagogues into the relevant information systems of state bodies. This information is constantly updated.

  2. Obligation to integrate into information systems:Higher education organizations must have an information system that manages their educational, scientific and administrative processes. Also, this system should be integrated into the HEMIS system based on the requirements set by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

These changes are aimed at developing the private education sector and improving the quality of education. Earlier, in a meeting with the President, Doctor of Legal Sciences Akhtam Yakubov proposed to cancel the condition of hiring teachers before obtaining a license to open a private school. This proposal has been approved by the President and is being formalized in the form of a decision. — Read on infoedu.uz
