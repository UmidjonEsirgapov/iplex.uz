Now students are given 2 different grants

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Socially Incentive Education Grant: It is allocated to those who have been accepted as a student under the state grant and within the framework of targeted admission based on the benefits defined in the legislation. they can keep it until they finish their studies.

Starting from the new academic year, the grants given to students are divided into two types: social incentive and educational grant.

  1. Social Incentive Education Grant:

  • According to the state grant and targeted admission based on the benefits established by the law
  • This grant is also given to holders of grants from sectors and industries, state bodies and organizations.
  • Students can keep this grant until they finish their studies.

  1. Educational Grant:

  • For one year, young people who are accepted as students within the general quota
  • Starting next year, the grant will be redistributed based on the learning results of educated students.

The new grant system is aimed at encouraging students and creating additional opportunities for them. — Official Data
