Free resources for learning a foreign language

There are many free and quality resources for learning languages on the Internet. Below are the most effective online courses, podcasts, apps and dictionaries.

Online courses and tutorials

  • Duolingo – A free game-based language learning app.
  • BBC Languages – Free language courses.
  • Coursera – Some courses free (no certification).
  • Open Culture – Free lessons in more than 40 languages.

Audio and podcasts

  • BBC 6 Minute English – Short and effective podcasts in English.
  • Coffee Break Languages – Podcasts in different languages.
  • LingQ – Language learning through text audio materials.

YouTube channels

  • English Addict with Mr Steve – Learning English through live lessons.
  • Français Authentique – Learning French the natural way.
  • SpanishDict – Spanish grammar and strengthen vocabulary.

Apps and websites

  • Anki – Effective flashcards for memorizing words.
  • Memrise – Interactive activities and memorization techniques.
  • Tandem – Practice another language by teaching your mother tongue.

Online dictionaries and translators

  • WordReference – The perfect dictionary for English, French, Spanish and other languages.
  • DeepL – A tool for high-quality translation.

Language learning can be free and effective. With the help of these resources, you can learn a foreign language independently and develop your speaking skills. — Official Data
