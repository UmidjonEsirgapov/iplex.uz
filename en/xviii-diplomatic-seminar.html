XVIII Diplomatic Seminar

The seminar will be devoted to global security, reform of the system of international relations and integration processes in the post-Soviet region.

Days of the seminar: May 29 - June 2.
Place of holding: Yekaterinburg.
Language: Russian. language.
End date: April 29, 23:59 Moscow time.

Requirements for participants:
-Being between 18 and 30 years old.
-Bachelor and master degree in international relations, political science, journalism, economics and law students;
-Representatives of public organizations, student associations and youth movements;
-Young scientists and historians from Russia, CIS countries, European and Asian regions can participate.

Funding:

-Accommodation, transportation, and food expenses in Yekaterinburg during the seminar will be covered. He will have to pay for it himself.

How to participate?
After registering on the website of the foundation, you will need to fill out the Gorchakov Foundation form.

An essay (no more than 5000 characters) on one of the topics on the agenda of the seminar should be attached to the application.

Seminar delegates will be selected based on the selection process. — Official Data
