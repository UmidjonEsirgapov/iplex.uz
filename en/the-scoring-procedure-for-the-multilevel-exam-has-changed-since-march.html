Changes to the evaluation criteria for the multi-level system of foreign language exams introduced

Changes have been made to the evaluation criteria for the multi-level system of the foreign language proficiency test conducted by the Education Evaluation Agency. These changes were discussed and approved at the meeting of the Agency's Scientific and Methodological Council held on March 16, 2023, and are scheduled to be put into practice from March 2023.
