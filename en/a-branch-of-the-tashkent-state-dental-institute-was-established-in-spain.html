A branch of the Tashkent State Dental Institute was established in Spain

On December 18, this agreement was signed at a conference organized in cooperation with the management of the Spanish province of Palma de Mallorca, the Spanish Ministry of Health, Balearic University and the Ministry of Health of Uzbekistan.

Within the framework of this cooperation, 30 pedagogues will be involved in classes by both HEIs every year, and Spanish specialists will be retrained in 6 areas of dentistry. and it was agreed to teach in courses to improve their qualifications.

Furthermore, from 2021, 100 Spanish students will be admitted to qualification courses in the field of dentistry, and directions such as creating conditions for Spanish patients to use the potential of the medical tourism system in Uzbekistan have been determined. Today, more than 18,000 students receive education.
