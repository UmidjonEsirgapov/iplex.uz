Sambhram University was established in the city of Jizzakh by Government Decree (No. 401, 29.06.2021).

Sambhram University (India) is a non-governmental higher education organization.

Education at the university is carried out in the form of full-time education in English, Russian and the state language on a fee-contract basis.

Admission parameters for undergraduate education and master's specialties are determined by the University.

University graduates are awarded a diploma recognized as a document of higher education in Uzbekistan and India.

According to the decision, preferential educational grants will be allocated for the education of 20% of the students admitted to the University every year. In this case, 5% of students will receive free education, 5% will receive 50%, and 10% will receive 25% of the contract amount. — Details here
