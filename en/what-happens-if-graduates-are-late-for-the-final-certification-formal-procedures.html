Lateness is regarded as a violation of order

According to the "Regulation on the final state certification of students of general secondary education", if a graduate comes to the exam late, this situation is considered a violation of order.

If he:

  • Verbal or If he is late for the written exam,

  • Disorderly or

  • He commits other indiscipline during the exam process,

then he will be deprived of the right to continue with the final attestation.

Official entry in the report will be included

If the graduate is excluded from the exam, this situation will be separately noted in the final attestation report. This decision is taken by the attestation commission.

Based on the decision of the school pedagogical board, the graduate may have the right to take the final attestation again 2 weeks after the end of the exam.
