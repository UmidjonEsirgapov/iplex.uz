Ghent University Top-up Grants: grant program for master's degree courses in Belgium - 1000 Euro scholarship

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Requirements: For students who wish to pursue a master's or 2nd master's degree at Ghent University; When applying for a master's program, you must have a bachelor's degree; When applying for the Master-after-Master program, you must have a master's degree; You must be a citizen of one of the countries on the long list of OESO-DAC countries (Uzbekistan is included).
Selection procedure: The coordinator of each English-taught master's program will invite their top 3 newly admitted students to the "Committee for Development Cooperation (CDC)". The decision will be made by the CDC in May 2022 .
In order for the candidates to apply for the grant, more attention should be paid to the following aspects: There is no age limit, but preference is given to applicants under 40 years of age; Relevance of the course of the master's degree to the student's country; It is better if the candidate has high academic performance; Selected students must do their dissertation at Ghent University.

General Information:

Ghent University offers its "Top-up grants" for candidates from all countries on the OESO-DAC list who wish to pursue a master's degree at Ghent University. This grant is only available to new students applying to an English-taught Master's or 2nd Master's program. You are not required to apply separately for this grant program. This grant is awarded only to 10 students with the highest academic performance and talent. However, you are requested to register by March 1st via this link.

Note: If you have not applied to Ghent University by March 1st, your documents will not be considered for the Top-up grant.

The grant includes:

  • €1000 per month stipend;
  • Full insurance (health, civil liability, repatriation).

Note: The grant does not cover airfare and visa costs.

Please note that as Ghent is an expensive city and considering the high standard of living in Belgium, it is recommended to have additional funds. Here you can get acquainted with the average prices for living in Belgium. you will also be able to get acquainted here in detail with the courses available at the university.

  • For students who want to get a master's or 2nd master's degree at Ghent University;
  • When applying for a Master's program, you must have a bachelor's degree;
  • When applying for a Master-after-Master's program, you you must have a master's degree;
  • You must be a citizen of one of the countries on the long list of OESO-DAC country list (Uzbekistan is included).

Selection procedure:
The coordinator of each master's program taught in English will nominate their best 3 newly admitted students to the "Development Cooperation Committee" (CDC)" offers.
The decision will be made by the CDC in May 2022.

  • The Development Cooperation Committee will select applications based on the recommendations received from this committee's working group.
  • The committee will then proceed to develop reasonable recommendations to the Executive Committee of Ghent University.
