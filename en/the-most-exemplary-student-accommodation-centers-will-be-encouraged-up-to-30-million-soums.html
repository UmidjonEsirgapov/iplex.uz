The most exemplary "Student Accommodation" centers will be encouraged up to 30 million soums

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The most exemplary "Student accommodation" centers will be encouraged up to 30 million soums. On August 1, under the leadership of the Deputy Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation Sarvarkhan Buzrukkhanov, students will be provided with accommodation in higher education institutions. a meeting dedicated to the measures was held.
Taking into account the opinions of the officials, from the 2024/2025 academic year, the necessary measures were determined for the accommodation of students of all levels of state higher education institutions in dormitory and rental apartments based on the sequence of established criteria. It is envisaged to start the activity of assistance centers (infokiosk) and attach experienced volunteers to it, as well as to encourage the most exemplary functioning "Assistance centers for placement in student accommodation" with monetary awards from 20 million to 30 million soums.
