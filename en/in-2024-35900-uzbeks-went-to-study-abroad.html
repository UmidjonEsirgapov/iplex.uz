A total of 35.9 thousand citizens of Uzbekistan went abroad for education in January-December 2024. The State Statistics Committee of Uzbekistan informed about this.

  6. Kazakhstan - 1.6 thousand people.
  7. Great Britain - 792 people.

  9. USA - 576 people.
