About the expected changes in the education system:

Presidential candidate Shavkat Mirziyoyev introduces his election program:

First of all, by 2030, the need for preschool education will be fully covered and coverage will reach 100%.

Kindergartens and schools will be fully supplied with filtered drinking water.

In the next 7 years, it is necessary to create additional 2.5 million student places in schools. For this purpose, the program "500,000 places for students per year" will be implemented.

Additional concessions will be given to private education.

Building schools based on public-private partnership will be widely introduced.

Elementary students will be fully provided with educational tablets.

School buses will travel free of charge in our remote areas.

In the next seven years, teachers who are "linguists" from abroad will be brought to all schools. — Source
