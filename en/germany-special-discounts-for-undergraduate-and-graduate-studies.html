About the university and the grant program:

University of Passau founded in 1973, is a public research university located in Passau, Lower Bavaria, Germany. It is the youngest university in Bavaria and therefore has the most modern campus in the state. It has four faculties and 39 different undergraduate and graduate programs.

The University of Passau offers special discounts on undergraduate and graduate program fees for foreign students.

Application process:

Candidates must first apply online HERE for their chosen undergraduate or graduate program. After that, candidates can apply for the international student financial aid program HERE.

Basic documents:

  • Diploma and transcripts (with translation);
  • CV;
  • Copy of passport.

Admission requirements:

Because the requirements for entering the university are different for each field. First, you choose your major, and then you can familiarize yourself with the admission requirements. You can find out the requirements HERE.

Benefits of the grant:

  • Discounts are provided by the university in the form of 25%, 50%, 75%, 100% of the tuition fee based on the academic performance of eligible candidates.
