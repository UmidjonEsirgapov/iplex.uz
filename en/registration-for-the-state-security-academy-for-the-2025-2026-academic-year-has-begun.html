Registration for the State Security Academy for the 2025-2026 academic year has begun

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
the registration of candidates for the 2025-2026 academic year for the National Security Service Academy will begin. The registration of applicants will begin to select candidates for full-time education.
Including, male graduates of general secondary, secondary special and professional educational institutions who have completed military service in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan to study full-time undergraduate education in the academy. Citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan are accepted.
In this case, candidates who have successfully passed all stages of selection will start studying at the Academy from the first year.

registration of candidates for the 2025-2026 academic year at the National Security Service Academy will begin

On October 10 of this year In the 2025-2026 academic year, the registration of applicants for the Amir Temur Academy of the State Security Service of undergraduate education will begin in the full-time form of education. They can register online through the my.uzbmb.uz website until November 10 of this year.

The selection of candidates is carried out in accordance with the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 241 dated April 27, 2021.

Including, undergraduate education at the academy According to the rules, male citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan who have completed military service in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan and are graduates of general secondary, secondary special and professional educational institutions are accepted for full-time study in the field.

Main requirements for candidates:

  • To be deemed fit due to the state of health;
  • To be not less than 170 cm in height;
  • From seventeen to twenty-three years old, graduates of general secondary, secondary special and professional educational institutions;
  • From eighteen to twenty-five years old military personnel who do not have military ranks and are forced to serve in the reserve;
  • servicemen who are serving in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan on a fixed-term and contract basis;
  • 1st and 2nd-year students who are under twenty-six years of age and who have expressed a desire to study at the Academy in the field of undergraduate education.

In this case, the candidates who have successfully passed all stages of the selection start their studies at the Academy from the first year. — More information
