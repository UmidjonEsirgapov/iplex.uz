Will all teachers be given bonuses on the occasion of the "October 1" holiday?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:

It is known that in previous years, pedagogic employees could be encouraged with a bonus amount equal to two position salaries during the year for their personal contributions to the development of general education institutions and high performance in their activities.
