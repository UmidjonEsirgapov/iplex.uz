When and what documents are submitted to the Presidential School?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
In June of this year, the parents of a student who expressed a desire to participate in the competition of the President's school, submit the following documents in electronic form through the Agency's special portal (ariza.piima.uz): information about the applicant; applicant's photo taken during the last 2 months; a copy of the applicant's grades (electronic) according to the results of the academic year, approved by the school director; information on documents confirming the identity of the applicant and his parents or legal representatives; the contact phone number is entered into the platform.
Documents are submitted only to the Presidential school in the area (province) where the general education institution where the candidate is studying is located.

Parents of students who expressed a desire to participate in the selection of the presidential school this year In June, they will submit the following documents in electronic form through the special portal of the Agency (ariza.piima.uz):

  • information about the applicant;
  • a photograph of the applicant taken during the last 2 months;
  • a copy of the (electronic) list of the applicant's grades according to the results of the academic year approved by the school director;
  • the applicant and his parents or legal guardian the identity document information of the representative;
  • the contact phone number is entered into the platform.

The documents are submitted only to the Presidential school in the region (province) where the general education institution is located.
