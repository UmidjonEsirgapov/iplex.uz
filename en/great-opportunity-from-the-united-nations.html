Attention young leaders: The UN Secretary-General's Ambassador for Youth is seeking a generation of young leaders for the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)!

Applications are open. and invites all young leaders between the ages of 15-29.

Over a two-year period, these 17 young leaders will engage young people in the 2030 Agenda not only through their existing initiatives, platforms and networks, but also through advocacy opportunities with the United Nations and its partners.

This is to be recognized for your leadership in enabling change in communities to help achieve the global goals.

Candidates are selected based on the following criteria:
-Leadership - the ability to influence and motivate others;
-Innovation - originality and ingenuity;
-Scalability - the ability to benefit others beyond the current sphere of influence;
-Impact - directly related to their work results;
-Presentation - to inspire and motivate others through a story.
