What is the Canadian Student Visa Application Process?

Canada is one of the ideal countries for many students to live and study around the world. The government of Canada, which is deeply aware of this, has been providing many opportunities for international students to study in Canada. Of course, in order to take full advantage of such opportunities, students must first enter Canada as a student. This is done by obtaining a Canadian student visa.

Through today's article, the OLIYGOH.UZ team will try to explain the process of applying for a Canadian student visa.

What is a Study Permit?

Study Permit - for international students who want to study in Canada student visa. It is issued for the designated educational institution (DLI) where the student has applied and been accepted to study, and there is an application fee of CAD 150 (approximately US$123).

You are not allowed to enter Canada with your Study Permit alone. To do this, you must be issued with an Electronic Travel Authorization along with the permit.

Study Permit process:

  • Get a letter of acceptance from the designated educational institution (DLI);
  • Apply for a Study Permit online or download the application package;
  • Pay your application fee and mail the receipt to the desired address send it;
  • Get a medical examination from a panel of doctors registered in your country;
  • Get a decision on your submitted application.

Canadian student visa requirements:
You will need to prepare some documents to get a visa. Your financial situation is an important factor in deciding whether or not you will be allowed to study. The Canadian government needs to see that you can support yourself financially during your studies, either through savings or through a sponsor like a parent.

Usually, to get a study permit in Canada, you will need:

  • Letter accepted by DLI;
  • Results of a medical examination and police check;
  • Two passport size photographs of yourself;
  • Proof that you can financially support yourself during your studies;
  • You may also be required to submit biometrics. You will need to do this at an approved Visa Application Center in your country.

If you receive a REJECTION for a student visa:

If your visa application is refused, you will be informed of the reasons why your application was refused. Re-applying for a study permit can be done at any time, but you will need to provide additional information to resolve the reason for your rejection.
