General Information:

Holland Scholarship is an excellent opportunity for young people from all non-EU countries to continue their studies at the master's level at Maastricht University . The duration of the master's degree can vary depending on the directions:

  • 1-year courses-consist of 13 months;
  • 2-year courses-consist of 25 months;

You can study at the master's degree in the following directions:

  • Arts and social sciences;
  • Medicine, health and life sciences;
  • Engineering;
  • Law, Business and Economics;
  • Psychology;

Benefits of the scholarship;

  • €12,350 stipend for living expenses for 1-year courses;
  • €23,750 stipend for 2-year courses;
  • Medical Insurance - 700 Euros;
  • Visa expenses - 192 Euros;
  • Contract payments (varies depending on the directions) - 13,800, 16,000, 16,800 Euros are paid;
  • And the costs of other training courses are covered;

Program admission process:

  • Register from one of these Master's programs at Maastricht University and also read the detailed information about your program, and then register here and you will get an ID code;
  • The following documents are also required:

1) CV;

2) Motivation letter;

3) Your undergraduate grades;

4) Contact information of the recommender;

5) Copy of Passport;

Requirements for candidates:

  • Euro Citizens of countries that are members of the Union and citizens of Suriname and Switzerland cannot apply;
  • The candidate must be suitable for the field he is applying for;
  • The candidate must not have previously studied at the undergraduate level in the Netherlands;
  • Preference is given to students who came through the student exchange program;
  • Those over 35 years old cannot apply;
  • Bachelor grades must be excellent;

Before registering for the program, check out the official page of the program!!!
