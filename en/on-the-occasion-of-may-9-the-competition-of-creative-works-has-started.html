General information: On the occasion of May 9, the Day of Remembrance and Appreciation, the Ministry of Defense announces a competition of creative works called "War Memories". It is being held in order to educate in the spirit and to encourage people not to forget the complications of war.

The competition is held in the following areas of creativity:

  • Painting
  • Graphics
  • Sculpture
  • Folk art
  • Description
  • Essay
  • Essay
  • Stories

Students, teachers of higher educational institutions, teachers of art schools, Ministry of Culture, Academy of Arts of Uzbekistan, Union of Artists of Uzbekistan, Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, Ministry of Public Education, Academy of Sciences of Uzbekistan are participating in the competition. Members of the Institute of Art Studies and the "Hunarmand" Association can participate.

Starting from 2021, this contest will be held every year on May 9, the Day of Remembrance and Appreciation, at the Shon-Sharaf State Museum in the "Victory Garden" memorial complex. must submit the following to the complex:

  • Certificate (resume) for participation in the competition;
  • Copy of passport (ID card);
  • Copy of educational document (diploma) (if available);
  • Creative description and reference from the place of study or work;
  • 1 3x4 size and 1 color photo of size 9x12;
  • A sample of creation (work) created by a participant in the competition, artistically, artistically and logically completed.

The book "History from Memories" is planned to be created based on the materials submitted by volunteers to this competition and the exhibits in the "Museum of Fame".

Competition the winners will be awarded valuable prizes.

Those who want to participate in the competition are asked to bring or send their creative works to the following address with the sign "to the competition" and a formalized application:

100069, Tashkent city, Almazor district, Sagbon street, 382.

Phones for contact +998 97 744 21 77, +998 99 860-18-87, +998 94 296 74 71
