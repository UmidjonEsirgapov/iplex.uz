The number of non-state secondary educational organizations will be increased to 600

The most relevant news in the field of education today was:

Brief content:
The main directions of the project: Increasing the number of non-state secondary education organizations: the number of non-state general secondary education organizations will be increased to 600 by 2025.
14 based on international education programs training of middle-level personnel will be launched at the technical school.
Training programs for technical school teachers will be implemented.

The state program draft for 2025 was announced and presented for discussion. According to the project, it is planned to expand the coverage of non-state general secondary education organizations and implement a number of other educational system innovations.

The main directions of the project:

Increase the number of non-state general secondary education organizations:

  • The number of non-state general secondary education organizations by 2025 will be delivered to 600. It aims to increase the share of the non-state sector in the education system and expand the opportunities for quality education.

Development of professional education:

  • The share of qualified pedagogues in professional educational institutions will increase.
  • Training of middle-level personnel in 14 technical schools based on international education programs will be launched.
  • Professional training programs for technical school teachers will be implemented.

Attestation processes:

  • Attestation processes for pedagogues will be carried out through the platform "pedagog.edu.uz". and they are given qualification categories.

Introduction of international education programs:

  • It is planned to introduce international education programs in 14 technical schools.
  • These programs will be translated and delivered to technical schools.
  • Organization of a process aimed at improving the quality of staff and students' education according to international programs.

Significance of the project

These initiatives are aimed at improving the quality of education, training personnel in accordance with international standards, and strengthening the role of the non-state sector in the education system.
