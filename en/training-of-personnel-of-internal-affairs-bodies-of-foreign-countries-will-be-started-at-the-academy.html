Training of personnel of internal affairs bodies of foreign countries will be started at the Academy of the Ministry of Internal Affairs

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The distribution of the annual quota, specialization and type of education among foreign countries is carried out by the Ministry of Internal Affairs in agreement with the apparatus of the Security Council under the President.
Foreign personnel are admitted to the academy without entrance exams. The foreign personnel who have mastered it will be given a qualification in their specialty, a diploma and a badge of the specified state model. 18.08.2023) was adopted.

According to the decision:

  • From the 2024/2025 academic year A system of training, retraining and advanced training of personnel of internal affairs bodies (police) of foreign countries will be established at the academy;
  • A Faculty of working with foreign personnel will be established at the academy.

By decision the regulation was approved.

According to the regulation, education of foreign personnel at the Academy is carried out in accordance with bilateral and multilateral international agreements.

Annual admission quotas for foreign personnel at the Academy Determined by the President.

The distribution of the annual quota, specialization and type of education among foreign countries is carried out by the Ministry of Internal Affairs in agreement with the apparatus of the Security Council under the President.

Foreign personnel are admitted to the Academy without entrance exams .

Foreign personnel are taught in Uzbek, Russian and English .

Training of foreign personnel in bachelor, master's and post-higher education programs full-time education, in retraining and professional development programs full-time and it is carried out in remote forms.

Foreign personnel who have mastered undergraduate educational programs are awarded a qualification in their specialty, a diploma and a badge of the specified state model.

Foreign personnel who have mastered master's education programs are awarded a master's degree and a diploma and badges of the specified state pattern.

Foreign personnel who mastered the basic doctoral program and defended their dissertation in the prescribed manner are awarded the degree of doctor of philosophy (PhD) in relevant disciplines.

Short-term certificates (certificates) of the prescribed model are issued to foreign personnel who have completed the courses.
