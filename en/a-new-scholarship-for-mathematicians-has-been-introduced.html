A scholarship named after Academician Tashmuhammad Aliyevich Sarimsakov was introduced for students and researchers studying mathematics.

According to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, the scholarship is used in higher educational institutions of the republic Mathematics Education It is assigned to undergraduates who have completed the 2nd year, master's students who have completed the 1st year, and basic doctoral students who have completed the 1st year.

For the academic year 2020-2021 academician T.A. Sarimsokov scholarship quota:

  • - for 5 undergraduate students (750 per month for 10 months thousand soums);
  • - for 5 master students (from 1 million soums per month for 10 months);
  • - for 5 basic doctoral students (for 2021 from 1.5 million soums per month for 10 months).

The scholarship was established by the Foundation for the Development of Mathematics Science and Education under the Institute of Mathematics based on the Decision No. PQ-4708 of May 7, 2020 on "Measures to improve the quality of education in the field of mathematics and develop scientific research". available:
