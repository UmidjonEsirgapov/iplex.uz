The procedure for appointing presidential and state scholarships for students was approved

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
By the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, changes and additions were made to some government decisions.
This is reported by the Ministry of Justice.
Stipends will be appointed from September 1.

Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers amendments and additions were made to some decisions of the government. This is reported by the Ministry of Justice.

According to the additions, the procedure for awarding presidential and state scholarships for undergraduate and graduate students has been separately approved.

The presidential scholarship is awarded to 1 graduate student of each undergraduate student of Higher Education Institution. Other state scholarships are paid to students studying in the last two years.

Nomination of candidates for scholarships is carried out by the Higher Education Councils on the basis of a public discussion of the applicants' educational and scientific activities.

Based on the decision of the Higher Education Council, candidates recommended for the next stage of the competition are entered into the "stipendiya.edu.uz" system from August 1-15 every year. Scholarship recommendations will be considered on September 10-30. Scholarships will be awarded from September 1.
