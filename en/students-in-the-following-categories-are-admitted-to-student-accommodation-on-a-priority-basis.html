Students in the following categories are admitted to student accommodation on a priority basis

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Children of "Mercy Houses", orphans and students deprived of parental care; Children of families in need of social protection included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection", "Iron Register" and "Women's Register" and students in the "Youth Register"; Students with group I and II disabilities; children of families from the same family studying bachelor's degree in the form of full-time education on the basis of two or more payment contracts; students who won the international science Olympiads, republican science Olympiads held by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation; Students who won the republican stage of the "Student of the Year" and "Student Theater Studios" contests; students who won the republican stage of the "Zakovat" intellectual game held among students of higher educational institutions; students who are the chairman of the student council, floor captain in the student residence.
  • Children of "Mercy Houses", orphans and students deprived of parental care;
  • Children of families in need of social protection included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection", "Iron Register" and "Women's Register" and students who are in the "Youth Register";
  • Group I and II students with disabilities;
  • children from the same family who are studying at the bachelor's level in the form of full-time education on the basis of two or more fee-contracts;
  • students who have won international science Olympiads, republican science Olympiads held by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation;
  • "Student of the Year" and "Student Theater Studios" students who won the republican stage of examinations;
  • students who won the republican stage of the intellectual game "Zakovat" held among students of higher education institutions;
  • students who became the chairman of the student council, floor captain in the student residence.
