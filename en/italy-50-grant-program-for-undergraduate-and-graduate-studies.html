Attending a study course at this university means acquiring the basic tools to start a career in hospitality, tourism and culinary arts with an international approach, acquiring the skills most in demand by the market and having a good command of the English language.

The grant program offered by the university provides financial support to international students.

Who is the program for?

  • The program is for international students from all countries.

For which fields of study:

The grant program is valid for all undergraduate and graduate programs offered by the university.

Application process:

Students must first apply and be admitted to one of the university's undergraduate or graduate programs HERE. For the scholarship, it is required to apply together with the following documents:

Bachelor:

  • send the following documents to the e-mail address segreteria@vish.it with the "bachelor scholarship request" attached:
    • A copy of the application for a grant (completely filled out and signed);
    • Curriculum vitae;
    • Motivation letter;
    • Copy of passport;

Master’s degree:

  • send the following documents to segreteria@vish.it with the “master scholarship request” attached:
    • Copy of bachelor’s or master’s degree
    • Copy of high school diploma/certificate;
    • Curriculum vitae;
    • Any foreign language certificates;
    • Copy of passport;

Benefits of the grant:

Vesuvio International School provides 50% of tuition fees to cover the cost of studying in Italy.
