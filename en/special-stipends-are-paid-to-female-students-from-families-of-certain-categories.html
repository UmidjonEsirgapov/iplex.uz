New benefits for poor families: Female students scholarships and support measures for children

During 2025-2026, the government of Uzbekistan is introducing new benefits and support measures for children of families in need of social protection. Below are the main directions of this news.

Special scholarship for female students from "Red" category families

Starting from June 1, 2025, female students from "red" category families studying with "excellent" grades will be paid a special scholarship in the amount of 2 times of the basic scholarship. This step is aimed at material and moral support for educated and diligent young people who need social protection.

School quotas for children from poor families

From September 1, 2025, in the amount of 15% of the total number of children attending extended day groups of general secondary education institutions for children from poor families separately quota will be allocated. They are exempted from parental fees.

Opportunity to rest in camps for free from 2026

Starting from January 1, 2026, children of poor families who study with "excellent" grades will have free rest and health care in camps under the system of the Federation of Trade Unions of Uzbekistan. Expenses are fully reimbursed.

State body coordinating social protection

All the above benefits and social services are coordinated by the National Social Protection Agency. This agency provides assistance to needy families through the Register of Poor Families and the Single Register of Social Protection.

Conclusion

On the basis of the new decree, great opportunities have been created for children of families in need of social protection. This is not only financial support, but also a great incentive for them to get education, have fun and find their place in society. — Read on infoedu.uz
