Can the applicant take the test at a place other than the one he chooses?

Applicant Can he take the test in a place other than the region he has chosen?

Applicants often ask the following question: "Is it possible to take the test in a place other than the province he has chosen?" The answer to this question is clear: No, it is not possible.

Why can he take the test only in the region he has chosen?

Which province the applicant has chosen during the online registration process, it is exactly that. participates in the test exam organized in the region. The reason is as follows:

  • The test materials for each applicant are allocated according to the region of his choice.

  • The materials are specially prepared and distributed, and it is not possible to move them to another region.

  • Such a rule is established for the fair and orderly passage of the test process.

The possibility of changing to another region is there?

After the registration is completed, it is not possible to change the region. For this reason, applicants should be very careful when choosing a region.

Conclusion

Applicants can take the exam only in the test center of their chosen region. Since the test materials are pre-allocated for this region, it is not possible to participate elsewhere. Pay special attention to choosing a region when registering! — Read on infoedu.uz
