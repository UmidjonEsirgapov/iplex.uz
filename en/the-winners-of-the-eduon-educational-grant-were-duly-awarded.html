The winners of the EduOn - educational grant were duly awarded

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
On August 27, about 12,000 talented young people who registered for the 1st stage tested their mutual knowledge, and 2,001 candidates who showed the best results were sent to the second stage.
Final stage test results EduOn.uz education platform, "Mfaktor" business school and OLIYGOH.UZ teams were reviewed by a jury consisting of 3 people, and the most worthy participants were ceremoniously presented with QR-code diplomas and grant vouchers at the "EduON educational forum" organized on September 12 at the "Mfaktor" business school. Baymirzaev and Saburyazov Islambek, a student of the Faculty of Public Law of Tashkent State Law University, were awarded special commemorative diplomas, as well as grant vouchers worth 1 million soums to each of these candidates to use the courses of the EduOn.uz educational platform. Also, the most worthy 98 participants selected from the candidates who actively participated in the final stage were awarded 400,000 soums to use the courses of the EduOn.uz educational platform online. Grant vouchers and special QR-code commemorative certificates were presented to all participants who participated in the 3 stages.

In order to support the education of young people and further develop knowledge and skills such as business, IT, foreign languages, personal development among young people, EduOn.uz educational platform, "Mfaktor" business school and In cooperation with the OLIYGOH.UZ team, the Republic-wide general prize fund announced the grant competition for educational with special achievements consisting of grant-vouchers of 50 million soums.

More than 40,000 talented young people from different regions of our Republic registered online and became contestants for this competition.

The first and second stages of the competition were organized in the form of an online exam on the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform. About 12,000 talented young people who registered on the 1st stage on August 27 tested their knowledge, and 2,001 candidates who showed the best results qualified for the second stage. In the 1st stage of the competition, the participants tested their knowledge on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform through questions of different levels about their IQ (logical analysis). Among the participants of the 1st stage

  • 6 participants had a 100% result;
  • 30 participants showed a 97% result, while the average result of all participants who took the test was 51%

The 2nd stage of the competition is on September 3 In the 1st stage, among the 2001 candidates who showed the best results, an exam was organized on the basis of basic business-related questions, and the 413 participants who showed high results won the ticket to the final stage.

At the final stage of the competition, special motivational letters were written by the participants. Motivational letters consist of 3 main questions, questions were asked about the changes taking place in the field of online education in Uzbekistan, EduOn.uz educational platform conveniences and opportunities, and why they are worthy of winning the EduOn - educational grant and 317 motivational letters were received at this Final stage.

The results of the final stage trial exam were reviewed by a jury of 3 people in cooperation with the EduOn.uz educational platform, "Mfaktor" business school and OLIYGOH.UZ teams, and the most worthy participants were selected at the EduON educational forum, which will be held on September 12 at the "Mfaktor" business school. QR-code diplomas and grant vouchers were ceremoniously presented.

The absolute winner of the competition was Shokirova Gulrukhbonu, a 2nd-level student of the University of World Economy and Diplomacy, and the winner of the competition was awarded a 1st-level diploma and a 5 million soum grant voucher to use the courses of the EduOn.uz educational platform

A grant voucher of 2.5 million soums was presented for using the courses of the EduOn.uz educational platform.

The 3rd place winners Akbarjon Baymirzaev, a doctoral student of the Andijan Mechanical Engineering Institute, and Saburyazov Islambek, a student of the Faculty of Public Law of the Tashkent State Law University, were awarded special commemorative diplomas, as well as 1 million soums grant vouchers for using the courses of the EduOn.uz educational platform.

In addition, the most suitable 98 participants selected from the candidates who actively participated in the final stage will receive 400,000 soum grant vouchers to use the online courses of the EduOn.uz educational platform and all participants who participated in the 3 stages special QR-code commemorative certificates were presented.

Special certificates of participating candidateswere sent on behalf of the EduOn.uz team OLIYGOH.UZ platform.
