How to concentrate to study well?

Concentration during studying is one of the main factors of successful learning. Below are 5 important recommendations for concentration.

1. Choose a quiet place free of distractions

Start studying in a quiet environment away from distractions to focus. Distracting sounds and external factors can distract and reduce efficiency, so study in a quiet and peaceful place.

2. Take short breaks

Prolonged reading can tire the brain. So take short breaks and rest. For example, take a 5-10 minute break every 45 minutes. This allows the brain to rest and focus.

3. Turn off your phone and notifications

It's important to turn off notifications on your phone or computer while you study. Notifications from various social networks can be distracting and an obstacle to studying. Leave the phone aside and devote yourself only to the lesson.

4. Hydrate your body

Hydrating your body is important for brain performance. Keep a water bottle with you and drink regularly to reduce any fatigue.

5. Review and Summarize What You've Learned

Make summaries of the material learned or explain them to others to reinforce memory and focus. It will not only strengthen your knowledge, but also help you to understand the topic more deeply. — More information
