Preferences given to teachers: full information

A number of benefits for pedagogues have been established in the Republic of Uzbekistan, which are aimed at appreciating the work of teachers and supporting their activities. Below we will discuss the main benefits in detail.

Rest and medical benefits for teachers

  • October 1 - "Teachers and Coaches Day" is considered a holiday and is designated as a day off.

  • Pedagogical staff regularly undergo free medical examination.

  • Teachers are granted 56 days of annual paid extended leave.

Working hours and working conditions

  • Working hours for pedagogic employees do not exceed 36 hours per week.

  • Pedagogues with many years of experience are involved in compulsory certification. .

Salary and allowances

  • According to the performance results, school teachers will receive a 10-40% bonus, librarians and psychologists will receive a 10-20% bonus.

  • Up to 40% bonus will be given to inclusive class teachers

  • Up to 50% bonus is given to teachers working in remote areas.

  • An additional 50% bonus is given to teachers with international certificates.

  • Special bonuses are given to teachers of foreign languages.

Incentives and additional opportunities

  • Teachers who have trained science Olympiad winners will be encouraged.

  • Teachers who have trained national or international Olympiad winners will be given cash prizes and benefits in the qualification category.

  • Teachers who have won Olympiads themselves. Up to 150% bonus will be determined.

  • The winner of the competition "The best school for teaching foreign languages" will receive a prize of half a billion soums.

  • The professors of the medical field will be additional fee for treatment-diagnostic work.

Teachers' rights

  • Responsibility measures will be applied to persons who unreasonably interfere in the activities of teachers.

Conclusion

Preferences created for teachers of Uzbekistan are aimed at appreciating their work, financial incentives and improving working conditions. This not only strengthens the social protection of pedagogues, but also serves to increase the quality of education. — Read on infoedu.uz
