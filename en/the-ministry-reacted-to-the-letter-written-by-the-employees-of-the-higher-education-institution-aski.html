The Ministry reacted to the letter written by the employees of the Higher Education Institution asking them to provide information in Cyrillic script

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation announced that cases such as the request from employees of higher education institutions to fill in travel information in Cyrillic script will no longer be allowed.
Furthermore, the ministry announced that the work in system organizations and higher education institutions is conducted entirely in the state language, that is, in the Uzbek alphabet based on the Latin script. The statement of the ministry said that the responsible executor of the letter circulated on social networks had been informed and given the necessary recommendations. On March 27, 2024, the letter sent to the rectors and branch directors of Higher Education, Science and Innovations was distributed During the years 2021-2024, the rector, vice-rectors, heads of departments and departments, faculty deans and heads of departments of each higher education institution were asked to provide information on trips made in Uzbek Cyrillic alphabet.

For information, In Uzbekistan, the law "On the introduction of the Uzbek alphabet based on the Latin script" was adopted on September 2, 1993. — Read on infoedu.uz
