In what cases will the state pay 50 percent of the contract money to the student?

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 132 of March 11, 2021, the procedure for partially covering the contract fee in order to support young people in need of social protection is established. Below you will find detailed information about the conditions and procedures for this relief.

Who has the right to receive 50% of the contract fee?

Students included in the "Iron Register"

Contract relief from the state:

  • professional educational institutions;

  • higher education institutions (state, private and foreign HEIs branches);

  • It is given to children of families included in the "Iron Register".

Students included in the "Youth Register"

Also, young people included in the "Youth Register" can also use this relief.

How much assistance will be provided?

Covered by the state amount:

  • up to 50 percent of the contract amount;

  • but it should not exceed 50 times of the basic calculation amount (BHM).

For example:

BHM from August 1, 2025 - 412,000 soums. 50 × BHM = 20,600,000 soums. That is, the maximum assistance can be up to 20,600,000 soums.

What documents are needed to receive relief?

Documents confirming that young people and their families are on the relevant list and information about the contract payment from the educational institution must be submitted. Local authorities and relevant agencies control this process.

Conclusion

The state will cover up to half of the contract fee, creating a great opportunity for young people in need of social protection. For this, you need to be included in the "Iron Register" or "Youth Register" and submit documents. — Official Data
