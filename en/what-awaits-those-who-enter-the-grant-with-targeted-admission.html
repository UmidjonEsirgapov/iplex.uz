Who can participate in Targeted Admission?

Once the quota is approved, Targeted Admission seats will be allocated by region. In order to use this quota, the applicant must be permanent resident (have a residence permit) in that area. The applicant must also have a certificate of completion of general secondary or secondary education.

How are the quotas for targeted admissions allocated?

Quotas are allocated on a cross-section of areas, for example, health care or public education. The places in the cross-section of the courses are divided by region.

Will those who do not get enough points in the targeted admission drop out of school?

If the applicant who participated in the targeted admission contest collects the required points, he will be a student on the basis of a grant. If the score is not enough, the applicant will participate in the general competition together with other applicants.

How is targeted admission selected when applying for studies?

If the applicant applying online for admission to OTM chooses targeted admission, he will participate in both targeted and general competition. For example, if an applicant is allocated 2 places in the field of treatment of Tashkent Medical University in the area where he lives, he will participate in the competition for both these 2 places and for the general quota.
