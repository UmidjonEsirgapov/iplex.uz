The development of a national agreement for the field of education will allow not only to strengthen coordination between various stakeholders involved in the field of education, but also to expand the most cost-effective solutions to avoid duplication of projects and inefficient use of resources.

The new project under the GPE "Multiplier" grant is expected to focus on improving infrastructure and improving the quality of education. The systemic transformation of the sector will be additionally financed by GPE.

It is worth noting that, with the technical support of UNICEF and other partners, significant progress was made in the field of education at the preschool and general secondary levels last year.

Including:

  • new public-private partnership models were launched.
  • the state program of preschool education was revised based on the requirements for the development of children of primary and preschool age;
  • the first preschool online platforms for educators and students were launched;
  • introduction of new textbooks on mathematics, mother tongue and natural sciences at the primary level in general secondary education and important results in curriculum reform achieved;
  • opportunities for innovation and expansion of digital education were created.
