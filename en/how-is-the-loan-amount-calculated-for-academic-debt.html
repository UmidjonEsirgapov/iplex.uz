How is the amount of credit for academic debt calculated?

If a student fails to pass the final examination of a subject during the session, he must make an additional payment to retake this subject. The payment amount is calculated based on the following formula:

QTM = (BTKM ÷ JKM) × FKM

Here:

  • QTM is the amount to be paid for redevelopment
  • BTKM is the basic payment-contract amount
  • JKM – total loan amount
  • FKM – loan amount allocated to the subject in which debt is owed

Example: payment for debt of 5 credits

  • Amount of the annual base contract – 6,412,000 soums
