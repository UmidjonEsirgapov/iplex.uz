How to get back 12% of the contract paid by students

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to Article 378, Clause 16 of the Tax Code, the salary and other taxable income for education in professional and higher education institutions of Uzbekistan (education of one's own or children under the age of 26 or spouse) must be taxed. incomes are not taxed, Khushnudbek Khudoyberdiyev writes.
Income tax is not deducted from the funds paid for the following 3 categories of contracts: if a citizen transfers it to his own contract; if parents transfer to the contract of their child under 26 years old; if a husband or wife transfers to the contract of a spouse under the age of 26.
This privilege applies to which educational institutions: students of professional education (vocational schools, colleges and technical schools); to students of higher education.

According to paragraph 16 of Article 378 of the Tax Code, taxes are not imposed on wages and other incomes that should be taxed for education in professional and higher education organizations of Uzbekistan writes Khushnudbek Khudoyberdiyev.

Income tax is not deducted from the funds paid for the contract of the following 3 categories:

  • if a citizen transfers to his own contract;
  • parents transfer to the contract of a child under 26 years of age;
  • husband or wife under 26 years of age is married. transfer to his friend's contract.

This privilege applies to which educational institutions:

  • students of professional education (vocational schools, colleges and technical institutes);
  • students of higher education.

Tax discount is provided when the payment is made from: are:

  • own funds;
  • educational loans given to individuals by commercial banks;
  • funds (loans) given by legal entities to individuals with the condition of repayment.

If the payment is made independently and not from salary, a tax deduction can be used by submitting an annual income declaration.

Note: if payment is made for the contract of others from the above 3 categories, for example, brother, sister, son-in-law or daughter-in-law or other persons, no benefits will be applied. — Source
