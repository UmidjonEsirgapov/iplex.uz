From which banks are preferential educational loans available?

Possibility of obtaining a loan through the educational loan information system

Educational loans are issued through the official talim-krediti.mf.uz information system. In this system, it is possible to get a preferential loan through several banks. Students can choose a convenient bank and apply for a loan based on a payment agreement.

Educational loan conditions

Loan amount

  • For first-year students — determined on the basis of the contract amount.

  • For second- and upper-year students — the average of mastering subjects

Loan term

  • Study period + 7.5 years.

Interest rate

  • General rate - 14%.

  • For women - 0% (interest-free education loan).

Required documents for obtaining an education loan

  • A formalized contract between the university and the student;

  • Identity documents when applying to the bank.

Conclusion

An education loan is a great opportunity for students and helps to cover the costs of education under favorable conditions. Especially for women, interest-free credit is a great support for continuing education. The possibility of obtaining a loan online through talim-krediti.mf.uz makes the process even easier. — Source
