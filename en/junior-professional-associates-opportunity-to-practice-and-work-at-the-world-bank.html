Summary:
General Information: The World Bank's Junior Professional Associates Program (JAP) aims to empower young people by providing entry-level professional opportunities.
This international Junior Professional Associates program provides opportunities for university graduates and young professionals to use their qualitative and analytical skills and technical knowledge while working with experienced World Bank members. aims to expand its capabilities. In the Junior Professional Associates (JAP) program of the World Bank, young professionals gain experience in the development of the modern world with new and modern problems. This international Junior Professional Associates program provides an opportunity for graduates and young professionals to use their qualitative and analytical skills and technical knowledge while working with experienced World Bank members.

Program Requirements:

  • Be 28 years old or younger;
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent;
  • Proficient in English;
  • Proficiency in other official languages of the World Bank (Arabic, Chinese, French, Russian, Portuguese and Spanish) preferred;

Documentation submission;

  • The application is submitted online
 on the official website of the World Bank;
  • Be careful to provide all the necessary information. Inaccurate information may lead to disqualification;

    • Required Documents:

    • Resume(CV);
    • Certificate of experience (if experienced in the field);
    • Certificate of education and reference;
    • Certificate of language proficiency;
    • Attached letter (Cover letter);

    Note: You can apply for the program throughout the year!
