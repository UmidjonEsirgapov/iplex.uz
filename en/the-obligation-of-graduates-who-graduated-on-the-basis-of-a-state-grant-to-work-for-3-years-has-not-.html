In accordance with the news circulating in social networks that the mandatory requirement to work for at least 3 years for graduates of higher education institutions who studied on the basis of a grant has been canceled.

In paragraph 6 of the President's decision of June 2, 2005, together with the ministries and agencies of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education The regulation on targeted training of personnel on the basis of state grants was assigned.

In accordance with this task, the regulation on the procedure of targeted training of personnel on the basis of state grants was approved by the decision No. 58, 154-1, 19 and 02/2658 of July 7, 2005. It stipulates the obligation of graduates who graduated from higher education institutions on the basis of a state grant to work for three years.

In accordance with Annex 3 of the Head of State's Decision No. 279 of June 15, 2022, some decisions of the President, including Decision No. 92 of June 2, 2005, were deemed to have lost their validity. However, according to the implementation of this decision, the regulation on the procedure for targeted training of personnel based on state grants has not lost its validity.
