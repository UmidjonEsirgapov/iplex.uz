The South Korean institute is accepting applications for the grant program

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
It is open to candidates for master's and PhD degrees from all over the world.
Photo: AllTerra Education is accepting applications for the grant program of South Korea's Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology.
Also, monthly stipends of $100 for masters and $225 for Ph.D.

Candidates from all over the world for Master's and PhD degrees can participate.

Photo: AllTerra Education

Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology of South Korea is accepting applications for the grant program.

Candidates from all over the world can participate in it for master's and PhD degrees.

Master's study period is 2 years, Ph.D. and the degree is 4 years.

The full tuition fee of the admitted students is covered. In particular, a tuition fee of $2,500 will be allocated for each semester.

Also, monthly stipends will be provided to master students in the amount of $100, and for PhD students in the amount of $225.

In addition, 60% of student insurance costs and one-way flights to Korea will be covered. Financial incentives in the amount of $75 per month for food expenses.

Applications can be sent until April 15 of this year. More information here.
