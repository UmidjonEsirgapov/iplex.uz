100% scholarship for master's degree studies at Doha University in Qatar

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:

Opportunity to study at one of the top universities in Qatar, the master's degree, on the basis of a 100% grant. All foreign students can apply for this grant program. All expenses will be covered by this grant. This university has announced 2 different grant programs:

1) Sanad Scholarship- all foreign students and Qatari students can apply. includes the following expenses:

  • Contract fees are fully covered
  • Free accommodation is provided;
  • Monthly stipend;
  • Medical insurance;
  • One-time travel expenses to home country;

2) Tamim Scholarship - It is intended for all international students in Arab countries and Qataris and includes the following expenses:

  • Full contract fees are covered
  • Free accommodation is provided;
  • Monthly stipend;
  • Medical insurance;
  • One-time travel expenses to home country;

Requirements for candidates:

  • Ministers of all nationalities can apply;
  • All candidates must have bachelor's degrees or final year undergraduate students can also apply;
  • No age limit;

Required Documents:

  • Passport copy;
  • Bachelor's diploma;
  • Transcript grades;
  • 2 academic letters of recommendation;
  • Language certificates - IELTS 6.5
  • Personal statement;
  • Academic essay;
  • CV;

Document submission process- Create an account and online fill out the application form completely!

Deadline: 20.01.2022.
