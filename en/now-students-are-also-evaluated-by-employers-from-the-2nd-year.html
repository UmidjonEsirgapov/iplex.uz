Now students are also evaluated by employers from the 2nd year

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Now, based on the need for large projects, students are evaluated by employers starting from the 2nd year.
Training based on large projects. the need for specialists will be clearly indicated.
Additional modules will be included in the curriculum. Rectors will introduce additional educational modules for students studying on the basis of orders. Now, based on the needs of large projects, students are evaluated by employers starting from the 2nd year.

Training of personnel based on large projects

According to it, the need for specialists in the field and directions is clearly indicated on the technical and economic basis of projects with a value of more than 1 million dollars and the number of employees is more than 50. The heads of the responsible ministry and sector will place specific orders to higher education institutions and targeted training of personnel will be launched.

Additional modules will be included in the curriculum

Rectors will introduce additional educational modules for students studying based on the order. Students who have successfully mastered these modules will receive credit points.

Investor participation in the student evaluation system

After the second year, students' knowledge and skills are evaluated annually with the participation of an investor interested in hiring them. Also, students prepare and defend their thesis based on the issues within the scope of the project.

Conclusion

Using the new system, universities prepare qualified specialists who are suitable for the labor market and meet the demands of investors.
