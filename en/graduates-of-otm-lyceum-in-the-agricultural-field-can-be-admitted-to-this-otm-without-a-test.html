A draft of the order of the Minister of Agriculture "On the procedure for admission of graduates of academic lyceums of higher educational institutions in the agricultural sector to these higher educational institutions through an individual interview" has been posted on the portal for discussion of regulatory and legal documents.

According to the project, all subjects of academic lyceums of higher educational institutions in the agrarian sector graduates with high results in these higher educational institutions are admitted to these higher educational institutions without entrance tests, through individual interviews, on the basis of a fee-contract, outside of the admission parameters. According to the results of the interview, the admission is made on the basis of the points collected according to the rating system.

This procedure will be applied starting from the 2024-2025 academic year for the admission of graduates to the higher educational institution without entrance exams, through an individual interview, to enter a higher education institution once within two years from the year of graduation from the academic lyceum. possible. — Official Data
