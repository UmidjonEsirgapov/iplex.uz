The OSCE Academy in Bishkek, in cooperation with the German CRISP organization, is organizing a 5-day training on the topic of Peace and Conflicts in Kyrgyzstan. During the training, the participants will consider ways to resolve disputes between countries in easy ways and will receive a special certificate. Detailed information about the training is available at this link.

Benefits:

During the program, candidates

  • Can participate in the training for free;
  • Travel expenses to and from Kyrgyzstan will be covered;
  • They will be provided with accommodation;
  • They will be awarded with a special certificate.

Requirements:

  • Candidates Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Must be a citizen of Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Kyrgyzstan, Mongolia and Afghanistan;
  • Professionals from non-governmental non-profit organizations and government organizations, as well as researchers, activists and experts can apply;
  • Social must have completed a bachelor's or master's degree in social sciences or related fields;
  • must be able to actively participate in 5-day training, i.e. actively participate in lectures, morning, afternoon and evening assignments.

Those who want to participate in the program should send their CV and a typed letter about their interest in the training and the results they expect from it, with 1 PDF file to the responsible expert employee Kanykei Temirbekova via the e-mail address training@osce-academy.net.
