There is no additional fee for resubmitting the application after correcting the errors in the application

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was this:

Brief content:
In the event that deficiencies or errors are detected during the process of submitting documents for participation in the certification of teaching staff, the following procedure applies: Notification procedure: If the documents are incomplete, a notification will be sent to the teaching staff by the competent ministries through the State Services Center, the Unified Interactive State Services Portal, or official websites.
The notification provides information about the deficiencies and indicates the deadline for correction.
Possibility of resubmitting documents: Pedagogical personnel can resubmit their documents after correcting the errors before the completion of the application process.

If deficiencies or errors are discovered during the application process for participation in the attestation, the following procedure applies:

  1. Notification procedure:

    • If the documents are incomplete, the competent ministries will send a notification to the pedagogic personnel through the center of public services, the Unified interactive public services portal or official websites.
    • The notification will provide information about the deficiencies and indicate the deadline for correction.

  2. Possibility to resubmit the documents:

    • Pedagogical personnel before the end of the document submission process, they can resubmit their documents after correcting the errors.
    • In this process, repeated fee is not charged, i.e. no additional payment is required for resubmission.

  3. Legal basis:

    • This procedure is approved by the Cabinet of Ministers in 2021 It will be carried out in accordance with the decision No. 572 of September 17 (paragraph 30).

Educators should carefully monitor the application process and, if possible, correct all deficiencies within the specified period. — Read on infoedu.uz
