Open Doors is an international online Olympiad that provides a great opportunity to study for free at one of Russia's leading universities. If you are planning to study master's or doctorate in Russia, don't miss the opportunity to become a student without any exams or fees. By participating in this international online Olympiad, you can become a student of the best Russian university and receive a monthly scholarship. The language of instruction will be English or Russian, depending on the chosen course and direction.

Olympiad will be held in 2 stages for those applying for master's degree. In step 1, you will be asked to register and fill out a portfolio. In the 2nd stage, you will be asked to complete tasks in the selected direction placed on the platform. The 3rd stage is only for candidates who want to study at the doctoral level, and the selected candidates will have to pass an interview.

Participation in the Olympiad is possible in 14 areas:

  • Biology&biotechnology
  • Computer&Information
  • Mathematics&Artificial Intelligence
  • Business&Management
  • Political Science&International Relations
  • Neurology&Psychology
  • Physics
  • Linguistics&Modern Languages
  • Chemistry given in Russian and English

    Final results will be announced 3 days after the end of the Olympiad.
