Training procedure for students of primary education

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Question: If a student studying in primary education works as a teacher in a school, will he go to professional development courses?
Answer: No, pedagogues who conduct their work while studying at a higher education institution are involved in professional development.
Basic information: from January 1, 2025: According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 867 of December 20, 2024, pre-school and school education employees must take professional development courses lasting at least 100 hours (for teachers) or 144 hours (for leaders) every 5 years.

Question: Does a student studying in primary education, who works as a teacher in a school, go to professional development courses?

Answer: No, pedagogues who conduct their work while studying at a higher education institution are not involved in professional development.

Basic information:

  1. Starting from January 1, 2025:According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 867 of December 20, 2024, it is mandatory for pre-school and school education employees to take training courses lasting at least 100 hours (for teachers) or 144 hours (for leaders) every 5 years.

  2. Exceptional case:The letter of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education No. 03-33-3453 dated November 22, 2024, Appendix 2, Item 11 states that pedagogues who conduct their work while studying at a higher education institution should not be involved in professional development.

Conclusion:

If if a student studying in primary education works as a teacher in a school, he does not participate in training programs because he is studying in higher education.
