697 additional grants were allocated for children of customs officers

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Additional quota: This preferential quota applies only to the first route.
Additional information on the preferential quota: Amount of additional quota: A total of 697 additional quotas have been allocated.
Competition: Customs authorities employees' children will compete with the points they have earned.

An additional state grant-based quota has been introduced for the children of customs officials. This privilege is provided on the basis of a 2% grant, which makes up a total of 697 additional quotas.

Conditions for using the privilege:

  • Minimum score: The applicant must have scored at least 68 points. Those who score less than 68 are not eligible for this concession.
  • Additional Quota: This concession quota is valid for the first stream only. Second, third, etc. does not apply to directions. But they can also choose five courses, only if they cannot get into the preferential quota, they will compete with their score in the general quota.

Additional information on the preferential quota:

  • Additional quota amount: A total of 697 additional quotas have been allocated.
  • Competition: Children of customs officials they will compete with their points.

Note:

This preferential quota is valid only for the first course. If the applicant is unable to enter the first course, he will compete with his score within the general quota.
