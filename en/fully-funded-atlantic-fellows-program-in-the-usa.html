The Atlantic Fellows for Health Equity program for all candidates in the US at George Washington University on health-related topics provides an excellent opportunity to expand and deepen their understanding and knowledge.

Benefits:

  • All travel expenses related to the program are covered by the program;
  • Opportunity to participate in 4 conferences during the year;
  • Going to America 2 times in 1 year all expenses are covered;

Myhim dates:

March 1, 2022: Application opens. Application closes on May 1, 2022. First round of applicant interviews June 2022. Second round of applicant interviews July 2022: Applicant acceptance notices will be sent by August 2022.

Applicant requirements and required documents:

  • All countries citizens can apply;
  • Entrepreneurs and heads of organizations can apply;
  • Must be fluent in English;
  • Candidates must be able to visit Washington 2 times a year;
  • You must include all information about your field and place of work as well as your residence address;
  • Statement of interest (Max: 500 words you must show your interest in this program without being blind);

  • Employer support form (Employer support form);
  • 2 letters of recommendation;
