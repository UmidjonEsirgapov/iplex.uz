The Ministry of Innovative Development of the Republic of Uzbekistan announces the contest "Future scientist". The main goal of the competition is to support the results and initiatives of innovative activities of talented young scientists by the state, to create the necessary conditions for their scientific research and to attract potential young people to science.

on" will be held in accordance with the requirements set forth in the regulation of the "Procedure for holding the Future Scientist" contest, Appendix 2 of the Resolution No. 222 of April 19, 2021.

In this contest, applications will be accepted for projects in three directions:

  • Scientific ideas;
  • Startup projects;
  • Scientific projects.

Within the "Future scientist" contest, young scientists':

  • Scientific ideas are encouraged;
  • Scientific and start-up projects are financed.

I. A scientific idea - is one of the forms of reflecting reality such as a scientific proof, problem, hypothesis, theory, which appears as a result of scientific progress, a result of scientific discoveries, a project developed on the basis of the basic principles and priority rules of various scientific fields. conditions:

1. The age of applicants should not exceed 40 regardless of their academic level, title, position and workplace;

2. Participation of 1 participant with 1 idea;

3. The amount of funds required for the implementation of the submitted scientific idea should not exceed the amount in the Regulation approved by Appendix 2 of the Resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers dated April 19, 2021 No. 222;

4. The maximum period of creating a mock-up and (or) prototype, software product, services and social products based on scientific ideas is up to one year. documents www.yoshlarakademiyasi.uz should be uploaded to the selection column of the web page:

1. A copy of the applicant's passport or ID card, 2 color photographs (.jpeg) with a size of 3x4 cm on a white background.

2. The text of the scientific idea. In this, the mechanisms of implementation of the scientific idea, the period and preliminary results should be indicated. The original copy is scanned and submitted in pdf and word format.

3. Reference and cost estimate on the funds required for the implementation of the scientific idea.

4. Presentation of a scientific idea. (no more than 5 slides, the content of the scientific idea is fully covered).

II. Startup project is a new business project that is characterized by the use of innovative developments that create a new product or service type in conditions of high risk of implementation, scale and rapid growth.

Terms of the submitted startup project:

1. The age of project executors should not exceed 40 years, regardless of their academic level, rank, position and workplace;

2. Participation of 1 team with 1 project (the number of team members should not exceed 5);

3. The amount of the awarded start-up project implementation amount should not exceed the amount in the Regulation approved by the Annex 2 of the Cabinet of Ministers Resolution No. 222 dated April 19, 2021.

4. The maximum period of implementation of startup projects is up to one year.

As part of the competition, proposals for startup projects will be accepted until 18:00 on May 31 of this year.

In this case, the following documents based on the forms provided by the ministry www.yoshlarakademiyasiya.uz The web page should be loaded through the options column:

1. Application for participation in the competition (.pdf), a copy of the national passport or ID card of each member of the team (.pdf), 2 color photographs on a white background (.jpeg) size 3x4 cm;

2. Startup project reference (.pdf);

3. Cost estimate for startup project implementation;

4. Startup project business plan (.pdf);

5. Startup project presentation. (not more than 7 slides, the content of the startup project is fully covered) (.pdf).

The presented scientific idea and startup projects will be subjected to preliminary technical examination on the strict compliance with the selection requirements, the reliability and authenticity of the project materials, the absence of copying (plagiarism), and the formation based on the established requirements.

Projects that have not passed the initial technical examination are not recommended for the next stage of the competition.

The authors of the scientific idea and startup project are personally responsible for the truthfulness and reliability of the information in the submitted projects, the full and timely implementation of the expected results specified in the tasks of the competition programs.

Applications for the competitions It will be accepted from May 1, 2021. — Official Data
