He hit the student. the teacher was imprisoned for 7 days for another offense

A probationary teacher who hit a student at school 188 in Chilonzor district of Tashkent was punished for another offense. This was reported by the Children's Ombudsman.

On September 24, 2024, a 44-year-old woman (the woman who hit the student) went to secondary school 204 in Yashnabad district, where her 14-year-old daughter studies, had an argument with the school management and used physical force against the deputy director of the school. When the woman was asked to observe order in a public place, the National Guard and IIO behaved anti-socially towards the employees and showed strong resistance without fulfilling their legal demands. The woman committed these illegal actions in front of many schoolchildren.

On September 25, this case was considered in Yashnabad district court on criminal cases. Article 183 (Petty bullying), Part 1 of Article 194 (Failure to fulfill the legal requirements of a military serviceman (employee) of the National Guard) and Article 197-5 of the Code of Administrative Responsibility by a female court (Unlawfully interfering with the professional activity of a teacher of an educational organization or preventing him from performing his official duties) was found guilty and sentenced to 7 days of administrative imprisonment, 3 times of the BHM (1,020,000 soums) and a fine of 7 times of the BHM (2,380,000) soums. It is reported that the action he committed against the 6th grade student is currently under investigation pending investigation.
