A state scholarship named after Zahiriddin Muhammad Babur will be established

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan on January 25, 2023 to widely celebrate the 540th anniversary of the birth of the great poet and scientist, famous statesman Zahiriddin Muhammad Babur.
According to paragraph 8 of the decision: Starting from the academic year 2023/2024, the state scholarship named after Zahiriddin Muhammad Babur should be established for students of higher education institutions in the fields of history and geography, and the Cabinet of Ministers should approve the regulation on the procedure for appointing this state scholarship within three months.
