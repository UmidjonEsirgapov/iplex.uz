Do pedagogues participating in the certification for the first time also pay?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
After graduating from a higher education institution, pedagogues who have less than three years of work experience can voluntarily participate in the extraordinary certification.
This is their first pedagogic certification.
All personnel undergo mandatory certification every five years.

Educators who have completed the higher education institution and have less than three years of work experience can take part in extraordinary attestation of their own free will. This is their first certification. All pedagogic personnel undergo mandatory certification every five years.

If pedagogues are newly hired or have graduated from higher education organizations and entered the workforce, they will undergo mandatory certification after three years of professional activity. The payment for out-of-order attestation is one times the amount of the basic calculation.

Also, the amount of payment depends on the type of attestation, regardless of the number of times one participates in the attestation. — Official Data
