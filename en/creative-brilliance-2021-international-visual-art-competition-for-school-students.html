Creatove Brilliance 2021 competition has been announced by the Picasso Art site in order to support and encourage young people with extraordinary talent in the field of visual arts.

Who is the competition for? Amateur artists between the ages of 6 and 19 who are interested in visual arts can participate in the contest.

Requirements for painting:

  • Creative works can be on any topic and size;
  • It is advisable to use the following media and colors:
    • Water color
    • Poster color
    • Acrylic paint
    • Oil paint
    • Graphite
    • Pencil painting
    • Colored pencils;
    • Pen and ink
    • Mixed media
    • Ball Point pencil, etc.
  • Computer work will not be accepted.

Prizes:

  • Winners will receive an Art Recognition Pack, which includes:
    • Winners' work will be posted on the Art Picasso website for free;
    • A portrait of the artist will be posted in the gallery as a winner;
    • Winners will receive an official certificate.
