1. DEFINE YOUR GOALS

A goal is a great event that reminds us why we are breathing. Live with purpose. Let your great future goals stand before you, not abstractions. Then you know exactly where you are going, and you can take your next step with confidence. Remove the word "dream" from your vocabulary and turn your dreams into goals and achieve them all.

2. GET ENOUGH SLEEP, BUT WAKE UP EARLY

Many people think that it is useful to do work or study instead of sleep, but this is a mistake. A person who does not have enough rest will be less productive at work. This leads to a loss of self-confidence. Avoid it. Get enough sleep, midnight 18+ movies and videos aren't worth a dime to your future. Instead, go to bed earlier and get in the habit of waking up before the sun rises in the morning. Then your day will be blessed and your work will be fruitful. I can't do that, advice for those who say they are late learners, get used to waking up 5 minutes earlier every day than the day before. Then you will become a person who wakes up 35 minutes earlier than before in a week.

3. PLAN YOUR DAY

Every day before you go to sleep, decide what you need to do the next day, keep a diary as much as possible and write down the things you need to do in it. Then your next step will be determined. Don't try to write it down. Because the record doesn't go away, and it clearly shows that what you need to do has been done or not. If you do this in your mind, you won't know what you have done and what you have left behind, and your productivity will suffer. id="4. START YOUR DAY WITH THE HARDEST THING YOU HAVE TO DO">4. START YOUR DAY WITH THE HARDEST THING YOU NEED TO DO

Procrastination can lead to depression and low self-confidence without even knowing it. So start your day by doing the hardest thing you have to do (the one that usually gets put off). Stop procrastinating. Doing this will leave you with that "I have to do that" feeling of whining and tiredness throughout the day, and you'll be in a good mood for the rest of the day.

5. MOTIVATE YOURSELF AND STRENGTHEN YOUR WILL

We often live waiting for someone to motivate us. But no one comes. So learn to motivate yourself. The best way to motivate yourself is to think about your goals. Visualize yourself after achieving your goals, and you will realize that your current pain is nothing compared to the future pleasure, and you will have a special energy to move towards that goal faster. In this way, you will become a strong-willed person who can go for success, not one who turns back halfway.
