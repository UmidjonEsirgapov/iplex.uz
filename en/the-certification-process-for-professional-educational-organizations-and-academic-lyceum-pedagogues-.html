The certification process for professional educational organizations and academic lyceum pedagogues has begun

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Processes of attestation and obtaining qualification categories for pedagogues working in professional educational organizations and academic lyceums have begun.
Through this process, pedagogues will have the opportunity to improve their qualification level. It is accepted through the platform pedagog.edu.uz.

Processes of attestation and qualification category acquisition have started for pedagogues working in vocational education organizations and academic lyceums. Through this process, pedagogues will have the opportunity to improve their qualifications.

Document submission procedure:

  • Platform: Documents are accepted through the pedagog.edu.uz platform.
  • Registration: To submit documents on the platform, registration is required through the One ID system (One ID)
  • Date of document acceptance: Acceptance of documents will last until March 1 of this year.

Important note:

  • Documents will not be accepted after the specified period. Therefore, pedagogues should not delay the application process.
  • In the process of applying for a qualification, make sure that all necessary documents are completed and completed correctly.

Please note: This process greatly contributes to the professional development of pedagogues and serves to increase their efficiency in educational processes.
