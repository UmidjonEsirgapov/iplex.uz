The written exam for admission to lyceums is cancelled

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The procedure for transferring educational organizations from attestation and state accreditation will be canceled and a complex and special state accreditation system will be introduced.
From January 1, 2024: the rating will be determined in general for state and non-state higher education organizations; higher education organizations that have taken the first 10 places in the ranking for the last 5 years will be exempted from the next comprehensive state accreditation.
From the 2023/2024 academic year: the preliminary selection stage based on a written exam will be canceled for the admission of students to academic lyceums; admission rates to educational programs of colleges and technical institutes based on state grants will be doubled; applicants are given the right to choose one of the priorities of "state grant" or "educational field" and participate in the competition in the sequence of undergraduate educational fields according to the chosen priority; a start-up project suitable for the student's educational direction and the practice of defending the idea of ​​rationalization as a graduation thesis and a master's thesis are introduced; paid short-term target production internships of students of higher education institutions will be organized in enterprises of economic sectors.

"On measures to effectively organize state management in the field of higher education, science and innovation within the framework of administrative reforms" (PQ-200, 03.07.2023) was adopted

According to the decision, higher education programs will be redeveloped on the basis of the programs of foreign higher education organizations, which are included in the top 200 list in the international ranking.

The procedure for the certification and state accreditation of educational organizations will be canceled, and a system of complex and special state accreditation will be introduced. Here:

  • complex state accreditation is conducted every 4 years based on the results of external evaluation of educational organizations;
  • the right to transfer students studying in an educational organization whose license has been revoked or in a closed educational program to suitable and similar educational areas and specialties of state and non-state educational organizations

Also, the practice of international accreditation of educational programs will be introduced. Here:

Higher education organizations that are among the first 1,000 of the international rating organizations recognized by the Ministry are exempted from the next complex state accreditation.

The rating of higher education organizations is carried out without interfering with the activities of the educational organization and the educational process by obtaining data through the information system.

From January 1, 2024:

  • the rating will be generally determined by state and non-state higher education organizations;
  • higher education organizations that have taken the first 10 places in the rating during the last 5 years will be exempted from the next comprehensive state accreditation.

2025 From January 1: from:

  • preliminary selection stage based on written exam will be canceled for admission of students to academic lyceums;
  • admission rates for educational programs of colleges and technical schools based on state grant will be doubled;
  • applicants will receive "state grant" or "education direction" priorities and the right to participate in the competition in the sequence of undergraduate educational directions according to the chosen priority;
  • the practice of defending the startup project and rationalization idea as a graduation qualification work and master's thesis will be introduced;
  • economics students of higher educational institutions will be introduced paid short-term targeted production internships will be organized at the enterprises of the branches of the industry.

From the 2024/2025 academic year: — More information

  • The entrance exams to the higher education institution will be organized on the basis of the principle of "test first, then the selection" and applicants will be awarded a higher education institution, an opportunity to choose the direction and form of education will be created;
  • certificates based on the results of international exams in general education subjects will be taken into account for admission to the state OTM.
