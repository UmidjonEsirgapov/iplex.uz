When is the admission to the 2nd specialty will be?

According to the current regulations, in 2025, the admission process for the second specialty will last from July 10 to August 10. This admission is online only. Candidates submit their documents through a special platform.

Online application procedure

To apply for the 2nd specialty, the applicant must complete the following steps:

  • Correctly enter passport data and JSHSHIR number into the system;

  • Scan the diploma and its application uploading the electronic copies made;

  • Selecting a higher education institution and field of study according to the desire to study.

Through this process, candidates will have the opportunity to receive a second higher education in the desired field.

Study conditions for the 2nd specialty

  • Second and subsequent higher education is carried out on a fee-contract basis.

  • The form of study is usually part-time, evening or distance education.

  • To participate in the admission, the applicant must have a diploma of previous higher education.

The process of admission to the 2nd specialty is simplified and implemented online.
