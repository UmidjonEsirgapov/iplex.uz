The form of correspondence education has been canceled: what changes are expected now?

According to the Decree of the President of May 5, 2025, important changes have begun in the higher education system of Uzbekistan. In particular, correspondence education will be phased out, and distance and approved (part-time) education will be introduced instead.

Correspondence education for the second specialty has been canceled

  • Now correspondence education for the second specialty closed. That is, new students will not be accepted in correspondence form in this direction.

  • This decision was made in order to improve the quality of education and modernize the educational process.

Current students will complete their studies

  • Students currently studying in correspondence form will complete their studies based on the current procedure. completes.

  • In the end, they will be awarded an officially recognized state model diploma.

  • If students want, they can change the form of study from part-time to another form according to the established procedure.

Part-time education will be launched

  • A new form of education - approved "Part-time education" is planned to be introduced.

  • This model is especially convenient for working people, classes are held on the basis of a shortened and flexible schedule.

Conclusion

Closing of extramural education is a continuation of reforms in the education system, which will be replaced by modern and in-demand forms of education - distance and part-time courses.
