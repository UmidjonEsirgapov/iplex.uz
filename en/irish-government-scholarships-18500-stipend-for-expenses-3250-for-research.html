The Government of Ireland Postgraduate Scholarship Programme is a program for studying at the best universities in Ireland for master's, doctorate and doctoral programs. During the program, the participants are financially supported.

Duration of the program:

The program lasts from 1 to 4 years depending on the chosen course and directions.

Benefits:

  • Stipend in the amount of 18500 euros;
  • 3250 EUR funding for research;
  • Salary for doctoral programs: first year 38,632 euros, second year 39,186 euros etc.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must have a bachelor’s degree;
  • Proficient in English must;
  • Must have excellent grades;
  • Must meet the requirements given here;
  • Must be a leader in his field;
