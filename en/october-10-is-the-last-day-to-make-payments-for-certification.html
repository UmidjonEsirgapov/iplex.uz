Today is the last day to make payments for the attestation

Today, 2024 October 10 is the last day for making payments for the attestation process for teaching staff. For your information, applications for the attestation of teaching staff to be held in the second half of 2024 were accepted from August 1 to October 1. some pedagogues who applied for the grant did not have time to make the payments. For this reason, they appealed to the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.

In order to support teachers and make them comfortable, the payment terms were extended and they were given the opportunity to make payments until October 10. — Read on infoedu.uz
