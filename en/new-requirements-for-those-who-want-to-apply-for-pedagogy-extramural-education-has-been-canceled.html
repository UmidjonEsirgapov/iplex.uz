New requirements for those who want to apply for pedagogy: Extramural education has been canceled

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
A number of important changes have been introduced for applicants planning to study in the field of pedagogy starting from the 2025/2026 academic year.
External education will be canceled. Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, pedagogical education will be conducted only in the day and evening form. Based on the presidential decree, the pedagogical education system is being fundamentally revised. Check out the main changes below:

1. The form of external education will be canceled

From the 2025/2026 academic year, pedagogical education will be conducted only in the daytime and evening form. According to the decree, part-time training of pedagogues will be stopped. This change is an important step aimed at strengthening the quality of education and practical skills.

2. A new pedagogical university will be established

On the basis of Tashkent State Pedagogical University named after Nizomi, National Pedagogical University of Uzbekistan named after Nizomi will be established. The new university operates as the leading pedagogical institution in the republic.

3. Courses with a low level of knowledge will be closed

Ped courses whose students have a low average level of knowledge will be gradually closed

. Through this, the quality of education and criteria will be dramatically strengthened.

4. 13 Higher Education Institutions were transferred to the Ministry of Preschool and School Education

13 higher education institutions were transferred to the system of the Ministry of Education in order to manage and develop the quality of education. This ensures direct integration with school education in the training of pedagogic personnel.

Applicants planning to apply for the Ped department should make their decisions consciously based on these news. Education form, quality requirements and university selection are now more important. — Read on infoedu.uz
