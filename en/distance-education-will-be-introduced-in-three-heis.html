Distance education will be introduced in three HEIs

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Starting from the 2020-2021 academic year, distance learning will be introduced in three higher education institutions as an experiment, the press service of the State Test Center reported. Al-Khorazmi Tashkent University of Information Technologies, Tashkent State University of Law and Tashkent State Pedagogical University are planned to introduce a form of distance learning as an experiment.
According to the report, the Ministry of Information Technology and Communications Development, Justice and Higher and Secondary Special Education will determine the procedure for the implementation of the form of distance learning by August 1, create a software-hardware complex, and implement the educational process of relevant HEIs. a distance learning platform will be introduced and equipped with the necessary equipment.
