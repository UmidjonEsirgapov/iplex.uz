Important guide to fill the answer sheet correctly

Filling the answer sheet correctly is extremely important for the applicants who are preparing for the tests. Getting a high score on the test depends not only on knowledge, but also on a careful approach to the technique. Below we provide detailed information on how to fill out the answer sheet correctly.

‚ùå The most common mistakes

1. Paint outside the circle

The painted circle should be clean and clear. Going beyond the edge of the circle is considered an error by the scanner.

2. Not completely painting the inside of the circle

Just a light drawing or not completely painting is also considered wrong. It is required to paint the inside of the circle in full, clear and uniform dark color.

3. Fixing a mistake by "punching" it

Many people try to fix a wrong character by "punching" it, but the scanner can't read the character. Puncturing the answer sheet is strictly prohibited.

4. Marking multiple answers at the same time

If you mark two or more circles for one test question, this answer is also considered an error. Mark only one answer for each question.

5. Losing a title due to carelessness

Every technical error can deprive you of a high score and even a title. So, caution is the key to success.
