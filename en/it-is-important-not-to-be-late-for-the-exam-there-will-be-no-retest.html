This year, the Education Evaluation Agency also strictly announced: there will be no re-testing for applicants who are late or unable to come to the exam.

The rules are strict:

Applicants must have a passport to the test. (ID card) and applicant's permit must arrive before the time specified. Applicants who come with a copy of their passport or other documents, as well as those who arrive late, will not be admitted to the tests. The rules of this year's tests remain unchanged and will be strictly controlled.

Important note:

Furthermore, no retest will be conducted for an applicant who fails to appear for the exam, even if there is a serious reason. Therefore, it is very important that each applicant arrives on time and with the correct documents. Arriving late to the exam or with incorrect documents will result in the loss of the opportunity to pass the exam.

Applicants must fully comply with the rules and requirements in the process of preparing for the test. Compliance with these requirements is necessary for the successful completion of the examination process. — Source
