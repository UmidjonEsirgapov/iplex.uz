On June 24-26, 2022, the governments of Portugal and Kenya and the UN Global Compact CEiiA, Cascais, Nova Business and Economics School (Nova SBE), SOA are organizing the UN Ocean Conference Youth and Innovation Forum.
Forum SDG 14 will be a platform to implement ocean actions and solutions proposed by youth in achieving the goals.
The objectives of the forum are: -To provide youth-led solutions that help implement the 2020 SDG 14 goals and the 2030 Agenda.-To provide training, mentoring, investor relations and networking in the public and private sectors to help young entrepreneurs and innovators expand their ideas and maximize impact -Engaging young leaders as key agents of change.-Enabling youth to contribute to the political process of the UN Ocean Conference by participating in eight interactive dialogues. The UN Ocean Conference is organizing a youth and innovation forum.

The forum will be a platform for implementing ocean actions and youth-proposed solutions to achieve the SDG 14 goals.

The objectives of the forum are:
-To provide youth-led solutions that help implement the 2020 SDG 14 goals and the 2030 agenda.
-Yosh provide training, mentoring, investor relations and networking in the public and private sectors to help entrepreneurs and innovators expand their ideas and maximize impact. It will be held on June 24-26 at the Nova School of Business and Economics in Cascais, Portugal. Applicants must be 18-35 years old to be eligible to participate. The forum gives priority to accepting and funding candidates from developing and middle-income countries.

Participants are evaluated based on the following criteria:

-How well the candidate's professional, academic field matches the goals of SDG14.
-How well the candidate's current work matches the goals and values ​​of the youth forum.
-The amount of impact of the candidate's current and previous work on the ocean sector area. This will be evaluated in terms of scope, geographic area covered, scale of operations, legislative impact, etc.
-Can the candidate's work be expanded to new audiences, geographies or sectors?
-Applicant will need to demonstrate leadership through projects or initiatives and/or participation in other major events and forums.
