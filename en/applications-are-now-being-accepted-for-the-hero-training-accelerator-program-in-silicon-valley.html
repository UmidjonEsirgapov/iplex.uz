Applications are now being accepted for the Hero Training accelerator program in Silicon Valley

About the expected changes in the educational system:

Brief content:
The program will be implemented in two stages - online and offline, and the best 20 teams will be involved in a 5-week program held in the USA.
The 80 most promising projects will have the opportunity to participate in the two-week "Astana Hub" online program.
In it, international Insights into attracting investments from venture funds, product development and scaling will be provided.

The program will be implemented in two stages - online and offline, and the best 20 teams will be invited to a 5-week program in the USA.

Photo: Spot

"Astana Hub" for IT startups will be held in Silicon Valley is accepting applications for the acceleration program. This was reported by the press service of the technopark to "Spot".

The program to accelerate the training of participants was organized by Draper University in San Mateo, California.

Technological start-ups from the countries that are members of the Organization of Turkic States - Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Turkmenistan, Azerbaijan, and Turkey can participate in it.

Program two.

  • online pre-acceleration;
  • offline acceleration in Silicon Valley itself.

80 projects considered the most promising will have the opportunity to participate in the two-week Astana Hub online program.

At the next stage, the best 20 teams will be invited to Hero Training in California.

The accelerator will last 5 weeks, and the startups will also be provided with accommodation and work for 6 months to enter the US market.

Members of the Draper innovation ecosystem will help the finalist startups attract investment and launch products in foreign markets.

Also, the participants will have the opportunity to attract investments from the venture fund "My Ventures" starting from $30,000.

It includes experienced entrepreneurs, experts, corporate partners and investors.

Startups wishing to participate in the program can apply through the link until April 7.
