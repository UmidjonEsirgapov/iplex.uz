What should applicants who are dissatisfied with the exam results do?

Higher When the exams for educational institutions start, the test questions, especially the questions from the science of law, are the reason for the discussions of the applicants and the general public.

According to the law, the applicants who participated in the bachelor's entrance tests of higher education institutions and are dissatisfied with the results, can apply to the appeals commission in electronic or written form within 10 days after the announcement of the final selection results.

Electronic form the appeal is sent through the special portal of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency "applicant's personal cabinet" and a notification about the review of the appeal is sent to him. The applicant who filed a complaint must come to the building of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency with his/her passport and applicant's permit at the time specified in the notification.

In this process, the applicant will be given a copy of the answer sheet, a list of incorrectly marked answers to test tasks, and a book of questions submitted on the day of the test. The applicant writes down the answers to the questions that he considers to be incorrect and submits them to the appeal commission. The appeals commission will consider the appeal based on written answers only in the presence of the applicant.

If the objections are unfounded, the results will remain unchanged. Also, at the applicant's request, a copy of the commission's decision will be provided.

If the objections are found to be justified and accepted by the commission, the results will be submitted to the state admissions commission within 5 working days after the end of the appeal process.

Important! If the answer sheet is torn or the applicant does not answer the questions in writing, the applicant's complaints will be considered. does not come out.
