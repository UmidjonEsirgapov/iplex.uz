The requirement for a B2 certificate for foreign language teachers was postponed for one year

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers on May 19, 2021, starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, all foreign languages teachers were required to have at least a B2 level certificate. However, taking into account the public opinion, the deadline for the introduction of this requirement was moved to the 2025/2026 school year.

Details and grounds of the decision

üîπ Decision number: Decision No. 322 of May 19, 2021 (https://lex.uz/docs/-5431845) üîπ Original plan: from the 2024/2025 school year, it was mandatory for foreign language teachers to have a B2 certificate. üîπ Change: This requirement was delayed for one year due to appeals received from teachers and the public. üîπ Date of new entry into force: 2025/2026 academic year.

Who does it apply to?

This requirement applies to foreign language teachers working at the following levels of education:

  • General secondary schools

  • Special secondary education institutions

  • Professional educational institutions
