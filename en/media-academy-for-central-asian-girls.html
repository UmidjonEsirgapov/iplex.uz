Central Asian Girls Media academy!

Duration: 2 weeks.

Blogging, writing articles, video editing and podcasting are taught at the academy.

Girls between the ages of 14 and 25 can participate.

The language of instruction is Russian.

The academy starts in mid-April.

At the end of the course, there will be a final project defense, where successful candidates can get funding to start their own initiative (on gender equality, combating violence and stereotypes).

Application deadline:

April 7, 2022.
