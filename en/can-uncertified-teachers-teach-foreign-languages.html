Without a certificate teachers can also teach a foreign language

According to the order of the Prime Minister of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 27/9-183 of November 28, 2023, until the 2025/2026 school year, specialists without a B2 level certificate can also teach a foreign language to public secondary schools in some regions can be accepted as a teacher. This decision was introduced in order to cover the demand for foreign language teachers.

Conditions for employment without a certificate

  • Uncertified specialists may be temporarily employed in schools in areas approved by the Cabinet of Ministers Resolution No. 471 1.
  • Only for the 2025/2026 school year. until this privilege is valid.

Obligation to obtain a certificate

These specialists must meet the following requirements within one year of employment:

✔ Have a certificate of at least B2 level according to national or international assessment systems.

✔ If this requirement is not met, the teacher the employment contract concluded with is terminated.

Purpose and impact

This decision was adopted as a temporary measure to eliminate the shortage of foreign language teachers. At the same time, obligation to obtain a certificate for quality education in the future is preserved. — Source
