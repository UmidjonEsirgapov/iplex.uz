A number of reliefs are provided for applicants who wish to receive higher education

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the results of the bachelor's entrance exams for the 2023/2024 academic year, the following are selected: applicants who are not recommended for studentship, the opportunity to study in one of all bachelor's education fields on the basis of a differentiated fee-contract upon registration; Applicants who are recommended to one of the 2nd, 3rd, 4th or 5th bachelor's education courses selected during registration, but whose score is lower than the fee-contract-based passing score in the first bachelor's education course selected, the opportunity to study in the first bachelor's education course on a differentiated fee-contract basis
In this case, the points scored by the applicants according to the results of the entrance exams are taken as the basis.
