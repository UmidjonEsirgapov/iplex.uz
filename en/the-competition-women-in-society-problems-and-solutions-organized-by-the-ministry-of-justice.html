The competition "Women in society: problems and solutions" organized by the Ministry of Justice

General information:

The Ministry of Justice has announced the "Woman in society: problems and solutions" contest as part of "Current two months on women's rights".

Competition in the following categories. will be held:

  • "For the best journalistic material";
  • "For the best blog post";
  • "Recognition of justice".

Who can participate:

  • All citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan, especially journalists and bloggers, can participate in the contest.

Competition conditions:

The materials submitted to the competition must be published in print or electronic media, television and radio channels or personal blogs from January 1, 2022 to March 20, 2022.

It is necessary to cover the issues of women's rights, the privileges and opportunities being created, as well as existing problems and proposals for their solution.

Scientific works, seminars, Lectures, slides, large-scale works of art (short stories, novels, epics) prepared for training, conferences, roundtable discussions are not accepted.

Up to 3 materials are accepted from each participant.

To participate in the competition, submit the following documents and materials from March 11 to March 25, 2022 (on the same day) It is required to send to @tanlovadliyabot telegram bot:

1. Participant Information

2. Participant's photo

3. Material participating in the competition (text or audio/video/photo file written in Word, the file itself or a link to the site/file source where it is placed)

Note: All materials must be confirmed with a screenshot, pdf-copy, link. It will be evaluated by the jury on the basis of criteria such as effectiveness, relevance, as well as simplicity and comprehensibility, appropriate and effective use of the facts on the topic.

1 participant in each nomination will be declared the winner, announced on the eve of the completion of the "Current Two Months on Women's Rights" project, and awarded with a diploma and the following souvenirs:

  • For the best journalistic material - television;
  • For the best blogpost - blender kit;
  • Recognition of justice - multivarka.
