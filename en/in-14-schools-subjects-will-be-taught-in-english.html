In 14 schools, subjects will be taught in English.

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
This program will be integrated with the program of the "New Uzbekistan" University.
Based on the final results of the participants in the international Olympiads, a system will be established to encourage the leader of the national team. gave instructions on strengthening its correlation with higher education.

President Shavkat Mirziyoyev got acquainted with the presentation of plans for the development and improvement of the quality of school education.

In the "Uzbekistan - 2030" strategy, education system reforms are defined as the first task. Accordingly, this field is being developed in both general and specialized ways. In particular, there are 14 presidential schools, 9 schools of creativity and 182 specialized schools operating in our country.

Based on the experience of the past years and the demands of life, it is envisaged to improve the directions in them.

In particular, in the specialized school named after Mirzo Ulugbek, in cooperation with the Technical University of Munich, it is proposed to introduce engineering programs as an experiment. This program will be integrated with the program of the "New Uzbekistan" University.

In order to widely use the opportunities of highly qualified pedagogues, "Science Councils" will be established on a public basis under the Presidential Educational Institutions Agency. These councils are involved in developing educational programs, improving the assessment system, and improving the professional skills of teachers.

Plans to start teaching subjects in English in 14 schools from the next academic year, and to build 8 new specialized schools in 2025-2026.

There are also proposals for creative schools. In particular, they organize master classes with the participation of well-known writers and public figures. Creative works of students will be published at the expense of the Writers' Union. Talented young people are supported by conducting various examinations.

Science Olympiads are important in improving the quality of education. In recent years, as a result of the introduction of a suitable incentive system for the winners, the desire for knowledge has increased. Aspirant students, the medals won in the "big five" science Olympiads have doubled compared to before. International Olympiads named after Al-Farghani, Beruni, Mendeleev were held in our country.

In order to raise these works to a new level, it is proposed to establish a center for preparing students for science Olympiads. Advanced scientists and foreign specialists will be involved in it, and each student will be specially prepared through the development program.

Based on the final results of the participants in the international Olympiads, a system of motivating the national team leader will be established. A single Olympiad calendar and preparation system will be created, reflecting all science Olympiads. Along with the main training, psychological trainings and joint meetings with other teams will be held.

In the presentation, the issue of introducing a transparent and impartial system for evaluating students' knowledge was also considered.

Currently, the evaluation of knowledge is different in different schools. The level of questions in the exam papers is also different. According to the requirements of international testology, the level of difficulty of the questions in all options should be equal.

Therefore, in the final state certification, i.e. in the school graduation exams, it is proposed to evaluate the knowledge of students based on uniform control materials. Exam assignments will be written and practical, the practice of checking answers through a special platform will be introduced, questions will be constantly updated.

Another important innovation is the opportunity to choose a subject suitable for the entrance exams to a higher education institution in the school graduation exams. gave instructions.

In the presentation, attention was also paid to the issues of spreading culture in education and other fields. It was emphasized that it is necessary to form love for art in the young generation from school and university.

For this purpose, it is proposed to introduce the subject of "Art" instead of the subject of "Fine Art and Drawing" in creative schools. Not only painting, but also painting, crafts, cinematography, sculpture and design skills are taught.

In higher educational institutions, the advisory system was connected with the Ministry of Culture, and the task of conveying status, charity, and theater heritage to young people was set.
