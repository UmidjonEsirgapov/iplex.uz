From which category of students the grant will not be withdrawn until graduation?

Students enrolled on a contract basis during their education in the Republic of Uzbekistan sometimes have the opportunity to study on the basis of a state grant during their studies. However, in some cases, the grant may be retained or withdrawn. Below is detailed information on the grant benefits for students in need of social protection.

Students in need of social protection

The following categories of students enrolled on a public grant can keep the grant until graduation, even if their grades drop or they have academic debt:

  1. Has a disability persons (Group I and II, or those with disabilities from childhood);

  2. Social orphans (those without parents or left without parental care);

  3. Those who are on the lists of "Iron Register", "Women's Register", "Youth Register";

  4. Children of poor families, children of families in need of support;

  5. Students with difficult family conditions (for example, a child of a single mother, from a family with many children, from an unemployed family).

Conditions for Retaining the Grant

The following important conditions apply to students of this category. does:

  • If enrolled on the basis of a state grant, the grant will remain until graduation.

  • The grant will not decrease depending on the student's academic status (even if grades drop).

  • Protected against losing the grant even if there is academic debt.

  • For these students, the contract value can be paid without a scholarship.

Conclusion

Thus, for students who need social protection, the state grant will be preserved and benefits will be available until the end of their studies.
