According to the order of the Minister of Pre-school and School Education No. 51 dated February 22, 2024, trainee teachers who are employed for the first time as pedagogues in educational organizations will be involved in professional certification tests. go or register at YAIDXP (my.gov.uz). Heads of educational institutions will take measures to attract highly educated pedagogues who have been hired for the first time to the teaching profession in the state general secondary education organization to professional certification and control tests in time.

Control tests are given 120 minutes for the sciences and pedagogical skills of mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, informatics and information technologies, and 90 minutes for humanities and pedagogical skills.

The applicant will not be charged a fee for participating in the control test for obtaining a professional certificate for the first time.

The applicant who has scored 56 or more points according to the result of the control test is considered to have passed the control test, and the electronic certificate will be sent to his personal office. An employment contract concluded with an applicant who failed to get enough points by participating in the out-of-hours control test will be canceled in accordance with the procedure established by the labor law. — Official Data
