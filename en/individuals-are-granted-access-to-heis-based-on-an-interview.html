Government Resolution (No. 675, 04.11.2021) approved the regulation on the procedure for admission of citizens with at least five years of work experience in the economic sectors to part-time and evening education on the basis of a fee-contract, differentiated according to the results of the interview. This is reported by the "Legal Information" channel.

According to the regulations, the list of ministries, agencies and large business associations that issue recommendations to citizens with at least 5 years of work experience to study on the basis of a differentiated fee-contract based on the results of the interview is approved and published in the media by July 1 every year.

Ministry, office and large business associations for the academic year, central and regional structures and system organizations can give recommendations to up to 100 applicants.

Citizens with appropriate recommendations are registered through the special electronic system of the State Test Center every year from July 20 to August 10.

The interview process is organized every year from August 20 to August 30.

According to the results, 100 applicants who scored 55 points and above in the scoring criteria are recommended for studentship on the basis of a differentiated fee-contract by order of the rector (director) of the Higher Education Institution.

The recommendations are valid only for the given academic year.

The regulation does not apply to higher military and paramilitary educational institutions, foreign and non-governmental higher education organizations in Uzbekistan and their branches.

This procedure will come into force on May 1, 2022. — Details here
