    Admission of candidates for clinical residency and master's degree

    Medical specialists with a basic higher medical education are accepted for clinical residency.

    Admission to clinical residency is carried out once a year on the basis of selection. Medical service officers who have served in the army for at least 3 years, who have shown themselves positively during military service, and are qualified for any type of medical specialty are involved in the competition. first place holders in the field;

    a certificate of invention for innovations created in the field of medicine.

    Documents - a statement on the specialty chosen by the candidate, a service description issued by the head of the medical supply service of the military district (union), an extract from the service card will be sent to the admissions committee by July 31 of this year.

    At the end of the competition, tests will be held. The test includes questions on the field of specialization. Questions related to military medicine are published on the website www.mudofaa.uz every year until April 1. Candidates with the highest scores based on the test results are admitted to the clinical residency. The Armed Forces and other forces are allocated a quota separately.

    Applicants to study according to the referral of other ministries, departments and organizations or according to their own wishes are admitted to study on the basis of a payment contract after successfully passing the examination (test) in the field of specialization.

    Directions, number of places on the website www.mudofaa.uz will be announced.

    Educational processes will be allowed after the citizens who have passed the competition have paid the specified fee-contract amount.

    The following documents will be submitted to study on the basis of the fee-contract: an identity document (passport or ID card), a diploma of higher medical education.

    The list of those admitted to the clinical residency is the Minister of Defense is announced by the order.

    Organization of training in clinical residency

    Training in clinical residency is carried out in full-time form, separated from the main place of service.

    The duration of training in clinical residency is in accordance with the law, vacation time for pregnancy, childbirth and baby care, as well as extension to the period of illness if there are relevant conclusions of medical institutions. It is possible.

    The report of the clinical registrar is submitted for semi-annual, annual and final attestation after approval at the department meeting.

    In the training process, the current evaluation system is used to assess the knowledge and skills of clinical registrars.

    The commission makes a decision on the results of the attestation and it is read out in the Scientific Council of the Academy.

    Responsibility for the quality of training of specialists in the clinical registrar. heads of departments, treatment units, laboratories and leading specialists of the Ministry of Defense are responsible.

    Clinical internship graduates are given a document of a single model established for the Republic of Uzbekistan on compliance with the requirements of specialization and practical activity. This document gives the right to engage in professional activities in the specialty of medicine.

    Organization of education at the master's degree

    Education is carried out in full-time form, separated from the main place of work.

    The activities of the master's student in terms of educational-methodical, scientific-research, pedagogical work, practice, internship and preparation of the master's thesis are the preparation of masters. in accordance with the organization scheme, it is carried out according to the calendar work plan of the master's student.

    The content of the calendar work plan of the master's student is developed together with the academic supervisor and approved by the deputy head of the academy for educational affairs. The graduate student reports on the implementation of his calendar work plan every month at the department meeting.

    At the end of each academic semester, the head of the department reports on the implementation of the master student's calendar work plan at the Scientific Council meeting.
