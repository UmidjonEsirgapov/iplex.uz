A new rector was appointed to the Nukus State Pedagogical Institute

A new appointment was made in the leadership of the Nukus State Pedagogical Institute named after Ajiniyaz. Alisher Ubbiyev was appointed the new rector of the institute.

Who is Alisher Ubbiyev?

Alisher Ubbiyev is an expert with extensive experience in the education system. He worked in many important positions. Before being appointed to the post of rector, he:

  • Worked as the director of the Agency of Specialized Educational Institutions under the Ministry of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan;

  • Actively participated in educational reforms in the Republic;

  • Contributed to the introduction of new methods and best practices.

Expected results from the appointment

With the appointment of a new rector, the institute is expected to:

  • further increase in the quality of education,

  • expansion of scientific activities,

  • improvement of management based on modern approaches,

  • strengthening of international cooperation relations.

Conclusion

The appointment of Alisher Ubbiyev as the rector of the Nukus State Pedagogical Institute is expected to further improve the quality of education. The projects implemented during his leadership can serve as a basis for this. — More information
