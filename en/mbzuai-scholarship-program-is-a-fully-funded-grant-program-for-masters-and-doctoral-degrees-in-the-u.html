United Arab Emirates (UAE) Master and Doctorate levels "Machine Learning (ML), Computer Vision (CV), Natural Language Processing (NLP)" The opportunity to study through the fully funded "MBZUAI Scholarship Program" grant program was created. This grant program is an excellent opportunity for young people who have advanced, specific knowledge and skills in artificial intelligence.

Duration of the grant:

  • For the master's degree: 2 years;
  • For the doctoral degree (PhD): 4 years;

Benefits of the grant:

  • The program will be provided with a monthly stipend during:
  • 100% contract fees will be covered;
  • Free accommodation will be provided;
  • Medical insurance expenses will also be covered;

Requirements for candidates for the master's degree:

  • Must have completed 4 years of bachelor's degree;
  • Academic performance should also be excellent, overall GPA should be at least 3.2 out of 4.0;
  • Language certificate-min IELTS-6.0 or TOEFL IBT -90 or 1550 EmSAT;
  • The presence of GRE certificate will also give priority in the admission process;
  • Personal statement (must be 800 words);
  • At least 2 letter of recommendation;
  • Entrance test;
  • Admission interview;

Requirements for candidates for doctoral studies:

  • Bachelor and master's degree must be completed with good grades with an overall GPA of at least 3.2 out of 4.0;
  • Language certificate-min IELTS-6.0 or TOEFL IBT-90 or 1550 EmSAT;
  • GRE certificate (a minimum quantitative score of 150 in verbal reasoning, 150 in quantitative reasoning, and 3.0 in analytical reasoning);
  • Personal statement (must be 800 words);
  • Research statement;
  • At least 2 letters of recommendation;
  • Entry test;
  • Admission interview;

Required documents:

  • Online application form;
  • Passport details;
  • Detailed CV;
  • Passport size photo with white background;

Note: Registration for the program last date 31.01.2022.
