Fully funded MATSUMAE International Foundation Research Fellowship Program 2023

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
-Applicants must obtain an invitation (acceptance) letter from the host institution in Japan before applying.-Candidates must have a PhD degree.
Successful candidates for the MATSUMAE International Foundation Research Scholarship Program will receive the following benefits: -In Japan A monthly stipend of ¥220,000 will be provided to cover research activities (including materials) and living expenses.-¥120,000 will be provided to cover a portion of initial living expenses in Japan.-Round-trip economy class air tickets will be provided between the nearest international airport from the grantee's residence and Tokyo, Japan.-Overseas travel insurance with medical treatment benefits will be provided. invites you to participate in the study tour.
Applicants should submit application documents to MIF via the following e-mail: application@mif-japan.org Applications will not be accepted for any reason after the deadline.

Grants are only available to successful applicants and are intended for necessary living expenses and research activities in Japan.

Matsumae International Scholarship will not be involved in any way with immigration procedures, airfare or accommodation expenses for the applicant's family, companions, etc.

Eligibility Criteria.
To be eligible for the MATSUMAE International Foundation Research Fellowship Program 2023, you must meet the following conditions:

Non-Japanese applicants who meet all of the following requirements must submit their application documents can apply.

-Applicants must obtain an invitation (acceptance) letter from the host institution in Japan before applying.
-Candidates must have a Ph.D. (Doctoral Degree).
-Applicants must be 49 years of age or younger at the time of application.
-Candidates must be proficient in English or Japanese.
-Applicants must not have been to Japan before (except for sightseeing or conference visits).
-Applicants must have a recognized profession in their home country, return there after completing the scholarship period, and must contribute to the development of their countries.

Benefits.

Successful candidates for the MATSUMAE International Foundation Research Fellowship Program will receive the following benefits:

-A monthly allowance of 220,000 yen to cover research activities (including materials) and living expenses in Japan
-120,000 yen will be awarded to cover part of the initial cost of living in Japan.
-Two-way economy class air tickets will be provided between the nearest international airport from the grantee's residence and Tokyo, Japan.

How to Apply.
How to Apply: Please download the application form and fill accordingly. A photo ID taken within the last three months must be attached.
Please make sure the application is filled out clearly.

Applicants should submit application documents to MIF via the following email: application@mif-japan.org
Applications will not be accepted after the deadline for any reason.

Application deadline: 2022 June 20.
