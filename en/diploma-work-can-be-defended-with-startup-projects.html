Diploma work can be defended with startup projects

An important innovation related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
This innovation creates a great opportunity not only for students, but also for the approach to higher education.
Now students can prove their knowledge in practice, not on paper, and get an assessment.
Advantages of a diploma work based on a startup project Practical experience will be increased.

Can startup projects replace a diploma thesis?

According to the President's decision (https://t.me/lawyakhakhborot/14037), now a student's startup project or rationalization work suitable for the field of study can be protected as a diploma thesis or master's thesis possible.

This innovation creates a great opportunity not only for students, but also for the approach to higher education. Now students can prove their knowledge in practice and get an assessment.

Advantages of diploma work based on a startup project

  • Practical experience is gained.

  • The created project works in real life and is profitable.

  • It is possible to have a good portfolio when finding a job.

  • Get real business feedback from teachers and experts.

Who is this opportunity for?

This opportunity is open to all undergraduate and graduate students. Only start-up project or rationalization work should be suitable to the specialization you are studying. For example, programmers can work on IT products, engineers can work on technical solutions, and marketers can work on advertising platforms.

Conclusion

In short, it is no longer necessary to fill out papers and write 60-70 pages of text to get a diploma. A student can demonstrate his knowledge in practice, defend his startup and get a diploma. And this will help to direct not only time, but also work effectively. — Source
