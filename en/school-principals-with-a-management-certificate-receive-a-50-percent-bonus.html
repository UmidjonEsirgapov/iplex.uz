School principals with a management certificate receive a 50 percent bonus

According to the government decision (No. 681, 25.12.2023), from January 1, 2024 at the national research institute for professional development of pedagogues and training in new methods named after A. Avloni, candidates for the position of school director will be trained in management training courses and a management certificate will be issued to them electronically through a special portal. the system of giving will be introduced.

According to the regulation, the principals working in the schools are required to receive a management certificate once every 5 years. The duration of training courses is 144 hours and will be taught for 1 month.

During the training period of the directors, their position at the main place of work and the current average salary will be preserved.
