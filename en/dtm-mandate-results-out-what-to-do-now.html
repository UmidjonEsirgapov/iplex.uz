DTM mandate results are one of the most exciting and fateful days of the year for applicants. Someone enters the university of their dreams, and someone gets an unexpected result. But this is not the end of the road. Below we give you a list of specific steps and options after the mandate.

1. Check Your Result Carefully

When the mandate is released, do not rush:

  • See that your name and choices are correctly indicated.
  • Find out on which quota (grant or contract) you passed.
  • If there is an error, contact DTM within the specified period do.

2. If you passed the study

Congratulations! What to do now:

  • Go to the university admissions office and submit the main documents (certificate, passport, photo, application for a contract).
  • Pay the contract on time (if there is a contract).
  • Get ready for student life: get information about the dormitory, transportation, class schedule.

3. If You Failed to Study

There is no reason to be disappointed - you still have many opportunities. Most importantly, Mentalaba.uz will open a new path for you.

Mentalaba.uz is a platform that allows you to apply to partner universities in Uzbekistan and abroad at any time of the year without an exam.

  • Even if your DTM result is not sufficient, applicants with 75+ points will be admitted to some HEIs without an exam through the Mentalaba certificate.
  • For foreign universities it is possible to submit documents online.
  • The platform clearly shows all admission conditions, directions and contract amounts of partner higher education institutions.

Also, through Mentalaba.uz you can:

  • Submit documents without leaving your city.
  • You can monitor the admission process online.
  • You will get the right to be a student in a short time.

So, don't let DTM stop you — take a step towards your dreams with Mentalaba!

4. Opportunities to Submit Abroad

After the mandate, many applicants begin exploring overseas education options. The reasons for this are clear:

  • The admission process is independent of DTM results.
  • Ability to study in many countries at any time of the year.
  • Availability of various grant and scholarship programs.

iApply.org is an international platform that allows you to apply online to universities around the world.

  • Up to 100% grant is available in Turkey, Poland, Hungary, Latvia, UAE and other countries.
  • The application process is completely online - just upload your passport, diploma/certificate and necessary files.
  • iApply.org team will help you from choosing a university to the visa application process.

So if your DTM result is as expected even if not, through iApply.org you can enter the foreign university of your dreams.

5. Don't Waste Your Time

Many applicants spend several months wondering "what to do" after the mandate. And you take action immediately:

  • Determine the deadlines for submitting documents.
  • If necessary, start translating and confirming your documents.
  • Choose the educational option that suits you.
