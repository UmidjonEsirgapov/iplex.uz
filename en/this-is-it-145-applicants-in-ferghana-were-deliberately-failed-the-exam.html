This is it: 145 applicants in Ferghana were deliberately failed the exam

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
This is reported by the General Prosecutor's Office of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
In particular, the results of the professional (creative) exam held in the Fergana branch of the Uzbekistan State University of Physical Education and Sports in the 2022/2023 academic year were studied, and 356 people (therefore, 322 were added, 34 were reduced) by making false entries in the actual scores of applicants, it was determined that applicants with high scores were not recommended for studentship.
On 10.12.2022, the regional prosecutor's office sent a letter to the chairman of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan through the General Prosecutor's Office, in which an interagency working group was formed.

When examining the results of the professional (creative) exam held at the Fergana branch of the Uzbekistan State University of Physical Education and Sports , the actual scores of 356 applicants falsified. This is reported by the General Prosecutor's Office of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

In particular, in the 2022/2023 academic year, the results of the professional (creative) examination held at the Fergana branch of the Uzbekistan State University of Physical Education and Sports were studied and 356 (of which 322 were added, 34 were reduced) entrants were falsely entered into the scores that they actually scored. it was determined that the applicants who scored were not recommended for studentship.

In particular, the 105.2 points actually scored by the applicant D. Bakirov in the field of cycling were falsified and the reduced 67.4 points were entered into the electronic database of the State Test Center. through the prosecutor's office, a letter was submitted to the chairman of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan, in which an interdepartmental working group was formed and asked to review the results of the professional (creative) examination held at the Fergana branch of the State University of Physical Education and Sports of Uzbekistan and make a proposal on the result. was revised and it was determined that 145 people were unreasonably not accepted for studentship.

The results of the study were discussed at the Commission meeting on 14.02.2023, and a decision was made to recommend the above 145 (3 state grants and 142 payment - contract) applicants for studentship.

Based on this decision, the branch director According to orders No. 10, 31 and 32 dated 16.02.2023, the above 145 applicants were accepted as students and their violated rights were restored. with other articles, a criminal case was initiated and the inevitability of liability was ensured.
