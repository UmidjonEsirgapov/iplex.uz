To determine the level of knowledge of a foreign language, special tests for blind (group I and II) applicants will be held in January, March and May 2025. Below is detailed information about this process and benefits.

Test schedule

  • January:

    • Registration: January 2-30
    • Payment period: January 31
    • Test date: February 15

  • March:

    • Registration: February 1-27
    • Payment period: February 28
    • Test date: March 14

  • May:

    • Registration: April 1-29
    • Payment deadline: April 30
    • Test date: May 14

Registration and testing process

  1. Applicants register online through the my.gov.uz platform.
  2. When registering, they have the opportunity to choose an area convenient for them and take the exam in this area.
  3. The tests are conducted in one stage, using a computer.

Special benefits

  • Persons with group I and II disabilities, as well as those with disabilities 50% discount is applied to the payment price for children.
    • Original price: 506,250 soums
    • With discount: 253,125 soums

Note

  • Tests are intended only for applicants belonging to the above categories.
  • Depending on government assignments and the number of applicants. test dates are subject to change.
