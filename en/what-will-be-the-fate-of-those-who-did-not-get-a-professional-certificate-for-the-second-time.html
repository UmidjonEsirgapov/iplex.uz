What will be the fate of those who did not get a professional certificate for the second time?

Brief content:
What will happen to those who did not get a professional certificate for the second time?
A candidate who scored 56.7 points or higher according to the result of the control test is considered to have passed the control test. The employment contract concluded with the applicant who failed to collect will be canceled in accordance with the procedure established by the labor legislation.

What will happen to those who did not get a professional certificate for the second time?

According to the relevant decision of the Cabinet of Ministers (12.02.2024 No. 87), those who could not participate in the control test for valid reasons and participated in the control test for the first time For those applicants who failed to pass it, extraordinary control test is organized every year until the beginning of the new academic year. However, the employment contract concluded with the candidate who failed to score enough points after participating in the extraordinary control test will be canceled in accordance with the procedure established by the labor law.

Also, the employer will send a complaint to the higher education institution from which the candidate whose employment contract was terminated has graduated, about the applicant's failure to pass professional certification.

This decision requires serious attention to professional qualifications and certificates adopted for the purpose of personnel training. — Read on infoedu.uz
