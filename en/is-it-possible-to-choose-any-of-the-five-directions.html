Is it possible to choose any of the five directions?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Many applicants think that after the test, they will choose any one of the five courses if they pass the test.
So, you can't change anything now.

Is it possible to choose any one of the five directions?

ANSWER: No, it is not possible to study by choosing any one of the five directions.

Most of the applicants have scored all five directions after the test. if there is, they think that they will choose any one and read it. This is a wrong idea. After the test, the applicants selected five courses in a row and set their priority.

Now, after the final answers are out, the applicants cannot change their chosen courses. The computer itself determines which direction it entered according to Priority. Choosing a priority according to state grant or payment-contract cannot change which direction you entered.

When viewing the results of the mandate, on the mandat.uzbmb.uz website, if the top of the direction is colored green (grant) or blue or yellow (contract), you will enter that direction. This cannot be changed. It is not possible to go up or down in the sequence of directions.

I am on budget, can I change to a contract for another direction?ANSWER: No, you can't.
