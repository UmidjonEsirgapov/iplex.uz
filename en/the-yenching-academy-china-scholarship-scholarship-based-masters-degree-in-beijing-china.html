The Yenching Academy China Scholarship: Scholarship-based Master's degree in Beijing, China

The Yenching Academy: China's prestigious Beijing University provides grants to foreigners for the master's degree .

Benefits of the grant:

  • All living, study and travel expenses are covered.
  • Number of seats. 125.

Fields where documents can be submitted:

  • Society and Law;
  • Literature and Culture;
  • Politics and International Relations;
  • Economy and Management;
  • History and Archeology;
  • Religion and Philosophy;

Requirements for the candidate:

- Bachelor's degree;
- Good grades;
- Good knowledge of English;

Documents to be submitted:

- Bachelor/appendix of grades/diploma (with notarized translation);
- Two letters of recommendation;
- Personal statement of 750 words;
- English language test (IELTS (Academic): Overall 7.0; TOEFL (iBT): Overall 100)

IMPORTANT: The official results of the English language test must be sent directly to the university. Please write "Yenching Academy of Peking University" to the shipping address when taking the exam. Use code C488 for TOEFL.
