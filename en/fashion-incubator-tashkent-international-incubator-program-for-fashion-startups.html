Fashion Incubator Tashkent: International incubator program for fashion startups

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Fashion Incubator Tashkent: International incubator program for startups in the field of fashion. For citizens of Uzbekistan who have an idea or an operating business.
Requirements for candidates:-Have an idea for a project in the fashion industry or have been running a business in this field for no more than 24 months. international incubator program for start-ups

Fashion Incubator Tashkent (FIT): a project implemented by the German International Cooperation Society GmmbH (GIZ), the association "Uztoqimalichiksanoat". requirements:
-Have an idea for a project in the fashion industry or have been running a business in this field for no more than 24 months.
-Must have graduated from the field of design or be studying in the last semester.

15 participants will be selected for the program.

For participants opportunities:
-Participation in program activities (seminar, camp, meetings with local and international experts) is absolutely free.
-Development of social (soft) and professional (hard) skills.
-Opportunity to turn a business idea into a ready-made business plan.

Registration deadline: April 30, 2022.
