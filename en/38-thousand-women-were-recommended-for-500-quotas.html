3,856 women have been recommended for 500 quotas

For whom preferential quotas have been allocated?

Having a higher education A quota of 500 state grants has been allocated this year for women who are not registered, but have at least 5 years of work experience in their specialty. This opportunity plays an important role in women's access to higher education.

Number and presentation of those who received recommendations

In 2025, 3,856 women were given recommendations for these quotas. Their list was submitted to the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency in the prescribed manner.

The most recommended areas

The following areas were in high demand:

  • Primary education

  • Therapy

  • Pre-school education

  • Accounting

  • Audit

Due to the large number of women who want to study in these areas, relatively more recommendations are allocated.
