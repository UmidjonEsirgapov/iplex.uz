Why are the results of the school stage of the Science Olympiad delayed? The Ministry commented

The Ministry of Preschool and School Education commented on the situation related to the delay of the information about the winners of the school stage of the basic science Olympiad.

It was reported that there was a technical failure on the data entry site.

"Currently IT specialists are conducting work and will complete it soon. If the technical problem does not find its solution or the malfunction continues, then the list will be allowed to be made in paper form," the ministry said.
