How will the teaching hours be distributed this year?

According to clause 15 of the regulation "On the procedure for completing classes in general education institutions and forming tariff lists", teaching hours will be allocated to teachers based on the following priority sequence:

  • Teachers who have trained winners of international olympiads
  • Winners of international olympiads working as teachers
  • Teachers with international certificates specializing in foreign languages and STEAM subjects (TESOL Level 5, CEFR B2, IELTS 6.0 and above, as well as by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan other certificates to be determined)
  • Teachers who graduated from the world's leading universities included in the ranking of TOP-1000 universities
  • Teachers who trained the winners of the republican stage of the main Olympiads
  • Winners of the republican stage of the main Olympiads who work as teachers
  • At the republican stage of the examination contests (teacher of the year) Teachers with prizes
  • Teachers with the highest qualification category
  • Teachers with the first qualification category
  • Teachers of the winners of the regional and district stages of the main Olympiads
  • Teachers with the second qualification category
  • Teachers with a master's degree in the field of pedagogy
  • Teachers with excellent grades according to the results of theoretical and practical tests conducted in the specified order
  • Teachers with secondary special, professional education until they are provided with teachers with higher education
  • Leaders in the relevant specialty of the general education institution until they are provided with teachers with higher education

The maximum amount of teaching hours for teachers who have priority based on the above indicators It is recommended to set.

With the written consent of the teacher who has the opportunity to receive the maximum lesson hours, the lesson hours that can be allocated to him can be given to other teachers. — Official Data
