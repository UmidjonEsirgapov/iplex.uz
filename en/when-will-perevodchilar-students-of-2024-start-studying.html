When will "Perevodchilar" students of 2024 start studying?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
Students transferring their studies from foreign and private educational institutions to public higher education institutions must first obtain a contact letter from the transferring higher education institution. students who are transferring their studies must do the same.
Students who received the communication letter must bring the necessary documents from the previous place of study within 10 days.

Students transferring their studies from foreign and non-state educational institutions to state higher education institutions must first of all transfer from the higher education institution to which they are transferring They should receive a contact letter. At the same time, students who are transferring their studies from a state university to another university should do the same.

Students who received a contact letter should bring the necessary documents from their previous place of study within 10 days. Based on the documents, subject differences are issued and their educational stages are determined.

After that, students must pay 25% of the payment contract until October 15. If the payment is not made, they will not be admitted to the ranks of students.
