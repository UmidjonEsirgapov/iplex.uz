From 2025, higher military students will receive the "Commander-in-Chief" scholarship

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the decree of the President, from the 2025/2026 school year, a number of new benefits and incentive programs for young people will be introduced in Uzbekistan. "Commander-in-Chief" state scholarship will be introduced. According to the new regulations, the "Commander-in-Chief" state scholarship will be established for exemplary cadets and trainees of higher military educational institutions. This is being carried out within the framework of the tasks defined in the President's open dialogue with the youth.

The State Scholarship "Commander-in-Chief" will be introduced

According to the new regulations:

  • The "Commander-in-Chief" state scholarship will be established for exemplary cadets and trainees of higher military educational institutions.

  • This scholarship provides material and moral support to the most exemplary cadets and trainees.

Engineering-invention Olympiads and preferential grants

Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year:

  • Holding engineering-invention Olympiads among students will be launched.

  • The winning graduates will be admitted to the state HEIs without exams on the basis of an additional state grant.

  • These will only follow the Olympiad direction and related educational directions.

Encourage interest in science and the environment

From this except:

  • Grants of up to 100 million soums will be allocated for the creation of national media contents that interest young people in science, educational and form national values.

  • A "Green Initiatives" support fund will be established and up to 10 billion soums will be allocated to it.

  • This fund serves to finance projects to protect the environment and instill love for nature. — Details here
