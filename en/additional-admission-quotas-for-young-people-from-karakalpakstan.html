Additional admission quotas for young people from Karakalpakstan

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PF-213 of August 31, 2022 allocates 3,000 additional quotas for Karakalpak youth on the basis of a state grant and 6,500 on the basis of a payment contract
In the next report of the State Commission on the implementation of this Decree, taking into account the need for highly educated personnel in the fields of the districts for the youth of Karakalpakstan, in the field of undergraduate education: 3,000 to the state higher education institutions of the republic on the basis of a state grant; 6,500 additional admission parameters for state higher education institutions in the regions were approved on the basis of a basic fee-contract.
Here: admission parameters based on a state grant - at the expense of applicants who scored 68.0 points and above in the entrance exams, but were not recommended for studentship on the basis of a state grant; admission parameters based on the basic fee-contract - were formed at the expense of applicants who scored 56.7 points and above in the entrance exams, but were not recommended for studentship. 500 additional quotas are set to be allocated.

In the next report of the State Commission on the implementation of this Decree, taking into account the need for highly educated personnel in the fields of industry for the youth of Karakalpakstan in the field of undergraduate education:

  • 3,000 to the state higher education institutions of the republic - on the basis of a state grant ta;
  • 6,500 additional admission parameters were approved for state higher education institutions in the regions - based on the basic fee-contract.

In this:

  • admission parameters based on the state grant - at the expense of applicants who scored 68.0 points and above in the entrance exams, but were not recommended for studentship based on the state grant;
  • the parameters of admission based on the basic fee-contract - were formed at the expense of applicants who scored 56.7 points and above in the entrance exams, but were not recommended for studentship.

Young people from Karakalpakstan who were recommended for studentship within the framework of additional admission parameters can find out the status of their recommendation through the website my.uzbmb.uz from their personal office.
