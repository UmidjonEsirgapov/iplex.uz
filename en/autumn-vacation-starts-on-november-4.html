Autumn vacation starts on November 4

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
School vacation periods are determined as follows: Autumn vacation: 6 days from November 4; Winter vacation: 14 days from December 28; Spring break: 7 days from March 21; Summer vacation: from the date of the final control test to September 1.
If the last day of the vacation falls on a holiday, classes will be resumed from the next working day.
An additional week of vacation is scheduled for students of the 1st grade in February.
