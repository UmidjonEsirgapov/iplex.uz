As reported, at the end of extended negotiations between the leaders of Uzbekistan and Kazakhstan, an agreement on cooperation in the field of higher education and further education was signed between the two countries, among other agreements and contracts in various fields.

The document was signed by the leaders responsible for the higher education system of the two countries and it was agreed to continue mutually beneficial cooperation.

The agreement provides for the education of 20 citizens of the two countries in higher education institutions every year, the establishment of Kazakh language and literature in Uzbekistan, and the Uzbek language and literature in Kazakhstan, the implementation of projects in the field of education and science, programs, organize conferences, seminars, master classes, olympiads, conduct scientific research, and publish articles in scientific journals of the two countries.
