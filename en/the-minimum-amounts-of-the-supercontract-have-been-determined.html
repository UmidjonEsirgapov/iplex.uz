The minimum amounts of the supercontract have been determined

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
These prices are calculated for applicants who did not reach the passing score by more than 4.05 points and scored higher than 56.7 points. For the 2021/2022 academic year, it is decided to apply the cost of teaching one student in the form of a fee-contract for the basic fee-contract without a scholarship, and to give a 10 percent discount to the minimum amount of the differentiated fee-contract for the applicants who have been vaccinated against the coronavirus infection.

It is considered for applicants who have not reached more than 4.05 points and scored more than 56.7 points. When calculating the super contract, the amount of the base contract without the scholarship is considered.

The press service of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education reported that the minimum amounts of the payment contract (super contract) differentiated by undergraduate education areas were approved (PDF). According to the Ministry's message, the points obtained by the entrance exams did not reach the limit of the acceptance parameters within the framework of the approved payment contract by more than 4.05 points, and not less than 56.7 points, compared to the basic payment contract amounts actually determined for the first academic year of the applicants who expressed their desire to study additionally.

Also, when calculating the minimum amounts of super contracts For the 2021/2022 academic year, it is decided to apply the cost of teaching one student in the form of a payment-contract under a basic payment-contract without a scholarship, and a 10 percent discount to the minimum amount of the differentiated payment-contract is given to the applicants who have been vaccinated against the coronavirus infection.

Relief for 0.05 points in the super-contract granted

  • from 1.06 to 2.05 points - 2.0 times;
  • from 2.06 to 3.05 points - 2.5 times;
  • from 3.06 to 4.05 points - 3.0 times.
