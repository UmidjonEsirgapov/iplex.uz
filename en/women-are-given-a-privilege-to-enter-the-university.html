Women are given a privilege to enter the university

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Groups of "Followers of Tomaris" will be formed in Uzbekistan.
Members of this group will be given the right to study based on a special recommendation The Government's decision was adopted.
This is reported by the Telegram channel "Legal Information". The members of this group are given the right to study on the basis of a special recommendation "On" was adopted by the Government. This is reported by the "Legal Information" channel on Telegram.

According to it, from June 1, 2023, the activities of "Followers of Tomaris" collective groups will be organized on a voluntary basis by involving women who are not younger than 18 and not older than 30, have a general secondary or secondary specialized education, who live permanently in the assigned area and who are communicative and have organizational skills.

The main tasks of these groups are to assist in identifying issues that have a negative impact on the behavior of girls studying in secondary, secondary special and professional educational institutions and are related to the violation of their rights and interests. will be formed in the presence of divisions on women's issues. It was reported that the members of the group will work for a total of 360 hours for 1 year without negatively affecting the study and work process.

Also, based on the recommendation of the head of the Department of Public Security of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, the members of the collective groups "Followers of Tomaris" who made a significant contribution to the prevention of crimes against women and the protection of the rights and freedoms of women. After successfully passing all the stages of selection, the OTM of the law enforcement agencies is granted the right to study on the basis of quota without individual interview (without tests).

In addition, if the number of recommended candidates exceeds the specified quota, their admission to study is carried out based on the points collected according to the results of the competition, and is distributed in a sequential order within the allocated admission quotas. — Details here
