The University of Military Security and Defense will be established

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
A new system will be introduced in the training of sergeants. provides in-depth knowledge of modern weapons and tactical methods; prepares them comprehensively as the backbone of the army.
The "2+2" system for narrow specialties will be established. Specialists in 18 fields, such as psychologists, communicators, storekeepers, suppliers, will be selected from other higher education institutions and trained on the basis of 2 years of general and 2 years of military education.
Conclusion The newly established Military Security and Defense University is preparing military personnel. ensures a unified, modern and systematic approach.

Important reforms are being implemented in the military education system

The president, having familiarized himself with the presentation on the improvement of military education, approved the implementation of fundamental reforms based on the new system.

A new university will be established instead of the Armed Forces Academy

According to the new model, the Armed Forces The Academy will be reformed and a Military Security and Defense University will be established in its place. This university prepares highly qualified personnel for the defense system of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

All military higher education institutions will be integrated into a single system

The following institutions of the Ministry of Defense will be included in this university:

  • 5 military institutes;

  • Higher training school for sergeants;

  • Aviation and UAV specialists training center.

Through this approach, military education is conducted on the basis of a unified and modern system.

A new system is introduced in the training of sergeants

The higher school of training of sergeants based on the new system:

  • forms leadership skills in sergeants;

  • deep knowledge of modern weapons and tactical methods

  • comprehensively prepares them as the backbone of the army.

The "2+2" system for narrow specialties will be launched

Specialists in 18 fields, such as psychologist, communicator, warehouseman, supplier, are selected from other higher education institutions, 2 years general, 2 years military is prepared on the basis of education. In this system:

  • experts are formed according to specific needs;

  • the system of higher military education works more efficiently.

A new opportunity for talented cadets: the "Supreme Commander" scholarship

Graduates who won this scholarship, which is provided on a competitive basis:

  • within 5 years after the bachelor's stage, they will be admitted to a master's degree without an exam;

  • will have the opportunity to deepen their higher military education.

Graduates with two diplomas

Graduates of 36 branches of higher military education will have the following:

  • diploma of military specialty;

  • diploma of civilian higher education.

This will expand their opportunities to work even after military service.

Conclusion

The newly established University of Military Security and Defense will provide a unified, modern and systematic approach to the training of military personnel. From the training of sergeants to narrow specialties, scholarships and the dual diploma system, the innovations are aimed at increasing the defense potential of Uzbekistan. As a result of these reforms, it is expected that the quality of education will increase and the formation of internationally competitive military specialists. — Details here
