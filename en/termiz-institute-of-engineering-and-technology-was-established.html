Termiz Institute of Engineering and Technology was established

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:

By the decision of the President of Tashkent State Technical University The Termiz Institute of Engineering and Technology was established on the basis of the Termiz branch. According to the decision signed on December 3, 2021, training, retraining and upgrading of specialists with in-depth knowledge of modern technologies is considered one of the main activities of the institute. continues to study at the institute in the fields of undergraduate education and master's degree.

The institute, among other things:

  • forms its educational programs based on the program of a foreign higher education institution ranked among the first 1,000 in the international ranking;
  • based on the demands of employers, reducing the amount of practical training in the theoretical education part of the curriculum
  • establishes research and innovation service enterprises for the effective implementation of consulting and expert-analytical services in the areas of the institute's activities.

It is noted that, in order to increase the scientific potential of the institute, from the 2022/2023 academic year, measures will be taken to train relevant master's specialties in the leading HEIs, and from 2023, in basic doctoral studies. — Official Data
