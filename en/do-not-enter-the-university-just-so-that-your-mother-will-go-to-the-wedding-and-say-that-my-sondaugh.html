Only 2-3 people from one village or neighborhood (usually not even that much) studied at the Higher Education Institution, naturally, their fame spread to seven neighborhoods and they were admired by everyone. How they got into education is another matter, because it was a time when corruption was rampant and education was almost ignored. In its place, universities should prepare students for the next stage - the job market.
