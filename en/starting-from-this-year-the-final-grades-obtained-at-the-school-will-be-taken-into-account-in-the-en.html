Starting from this year, the final grades obtained at the school will be taken into account in the entrance exams

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to it, starting from the 2022-2023 academic year, the average of the last 6 years of compulsory 11-year education will be included in the certificate (diploma appendix) of graduates of general secondary, secondary special educational institutions and vocational schools, and this indicator will be taken into account in the entrance exams to the higher education institution starting from the 2023-2024 academic year.
Grades are calculated through electronic platforms and sent to the "ABITUR" program of DTM.
The average indicator of the grades is the total sum of the annual grades in grades 6-11 or grades 6-9 and stages 1-2 it is issued by dividing it into taught subjects.

The regulation on the procedure for calculating the average score of graduates of educational organizations and taking it into account in the entrance exams to higher education organizations has been approved. According to it,

starting from the 2022-2023 academic year, the average score of the last 6 years of compulsory 11-year education will be included in the certificate (diploma appendix) of graduates of general secondary, secondary special educational institutions and vocational schools, and this indicator will be higher starting from the 2023-2024 academic year. taken into account in the entrance exams to the educational organization.

According to the regulation, the grades of the last 6 years of the 11th year of education in the certificate of school graduates are added to the score for entering the OTM. Grades are calculated through electronic platforms and sent to the "ABITUR" program of DTM.

The average of grades is obtained by dividing the total sum of annual grades in grades 6-11 or grades 6-9 and 1-2 by the subjects taught.

  • 71-85% – 4 marks;
  • 56-70% – 3 marks and up to 56% – 2 marks.
