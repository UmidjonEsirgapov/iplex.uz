The state pays for foreign language learning for technical school students

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the new decision of the government, concessions are being given to students of professional educational institutions who want to learn a foreign language.
Content of the decision: In the event that students enrolled in professional educational institutions learn a foreign language independently and present an international or equivalent certificate, 50% of the expenses incurred by the learner for learning a foreign language, but the part not exceeding 10 times the basic calculation amount, will be reimbursed from the State budget funds.
Example: 10 times of the BHM is equal to 3.4 million soums.
