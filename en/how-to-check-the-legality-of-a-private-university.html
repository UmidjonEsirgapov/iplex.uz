How to check the legality of a private university?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The validity of the license of non-state HEIs can be checked by visiting the website license.gov.uz. According to Article 31 of the Law on Education, non-state HEIs that provide educational services in accordance with state education standards and state educational requirements issue educational documents (diplomas) of the model approved by the state to graduates. If the license number is entered in the "Document number" section of the website, it will be displayed.

At the same time, according to Article 31 of the Law on Education, graduates of non-state HEIs that provide educational services in accordance with state education standards and state education requirements are provided with a model education approved by the state issues documents (diploma). — More information
