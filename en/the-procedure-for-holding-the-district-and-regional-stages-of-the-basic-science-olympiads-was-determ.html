The procedure for holding the district and regional stages of the basic science Olympiads was determined

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Order No. 331 of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education dated October 11, 2024 was signed on the procedures for conducting the second (district) and third (regional) stages of the Basic Science Olympiad.
According to it, In the 2024-2025 academic year, the second district (city) and third - regional stages of the main Olympiad in general education subjects, as well as the first stage of selection of candidates for the national team of Uzbekistan, which will participate in international Olympiads, will be held.
The Ministry of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, heads of preschool and school education departments of Tashkent city and regions: the second - district (city) of the main Olympiad and approval of the regional organizing committee for the organization and conduct of the third - regional stages; development of a program of measures and cost estimates related to the organization of the Olympiad within three days; at the meeting of the organizing committee, to analyze the results of the first - school stage of the main Olympiad, to approve the list of participants of the second - district (city) and third - regional stages of the main Olympiad; formation of the composition of the jury and appeal commission, audience leaders, supervisors; attracting experienced teachers from higher educational institutions of the region, educational institutions under the jurisdiction of the Agency of Specialized Educational Institutions, academic lyceums, state and non-state (private) comprehensive schools to the jury; by October 20 of this year, to determine a place that has sufficient material and technical base and is convenient for coming and going from all districts (cities); in order to ensure open, fair and transparent conduct of the Olympiad, to install surveillance cameras in the auditoriums and places where the Olympiad will be held, to attract impartial supervisors and observers to each auditorium; organizing placement of students and teachers coming from distant regions to the third - regional stage of the main Olympiad in the dormitory of regional pedagogical skill centers; after the completion of the Olympiad, organizing a ceremony of awarding the winners of the Olympiad within three days; The tasks of ensuring the correct and high-quality formalization of relevant documents (records, decisions, reports) related to the organization and holding of the Olympiad by the members of the jury and presenting the analytical report on the results to the Republican Organizing Committee
