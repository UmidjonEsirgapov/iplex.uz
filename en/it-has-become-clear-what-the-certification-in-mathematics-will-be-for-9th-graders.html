It has become clear what the certification in mathematics will be for 9th graders

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The Ministry of Preschool and School Education has drawn lots through a special site to determine test options for mathematics for 9th grade graduates. 2 tickets consisting of questions are presented.
In the ticket, the student is asked 10 questions (6 algebra, 4 geometry). 3 questions (2 algebra, 1 geometry) are related to knowledge, 5 questions (3 algebra, 2 geometry) are related to reasoning, 2 questions (1 algebra, 1 geometry) are related to reasoning. they write on prepared (school stamped) A4 format papers.
  • The Ministry of Preschool and School Education has drawn lots to determine the test options for mathematics for 9th grade graduates through a special website
  • It was revealed that students will take the exam based on 5th and 7th options.

Students will be provided with 2 tickets consisting of suggested basic questions. The student is asked 10 questions (6 algebra, 4 geometry) in the ticket. 3 of the questions (2 algebra, 1 geometry) are related to knowledge, 5 questions (3 algebra, 2 geometry) are related to application, 2 (1 algebra, 1 geometry) are related to reasoning. For writing the answers to the questions, the school has prepared (with a school stamp) A4 format. they write on papers.
