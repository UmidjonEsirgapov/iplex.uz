Brief about the university and the grant program:

University of Coimbra (Coimbra University) was founded in 1290, and it is considered one of the oldest universities not only in Portugal but also in the world. The university is divided into three major campuses and has eight faculties offering a wide range of academic programs. It ranks #601-800 in the ranking of world universities published by THE (Times Higher Education).

Presented by the university The Strategic Support program provides financial incentives to outstanding international students admitted to certain undergraduate programs of the university.

Program details:

  • University: University of Coimbra;
  • Course level: Bachelor's degree;
  • Grant amount: 2000 euros;
  • Application procedure: Online;
  • Grant type: International.
