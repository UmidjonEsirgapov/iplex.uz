General Information:

The U.S. Embassy in Tashkent is pleased to announce the launch of the final phase of the Alumni English Project in Bukhara. Graduates of exchange courses in the USA conduct two different English courses three times a week, each lesson lasts an hour and a half. "TOEFLiBT Preparation Course" is intended for 14 years and older who are above the intermediate level. The TOEFL preparation course is also designed to help students who are planning to apply to US colleges and universities.

These programs are designed to help develop practical communication skills with an emphasis on conversation, pronunciation and listening'. In addition to practicing and improving your English, you will also learn about American culture to better understand life in the United States.

If you are interested in enrolling in one of these English courses in Bukhara, register for the exam on April 8th. Enrollment requirements will be provided during testing.
