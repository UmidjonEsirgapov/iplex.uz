In what cases is recognition of a foreign diploma carried out directly (without exams)?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Recognition of documents on education in foreign countries is carried out directly (without tests) in the following cases: education in foreign countries of applicants who received general secondary, secondary, secondary special, vocational and professional education in foreign countries receipt documents; documents of applicants who have studied in foreign educational institutions with separate divisions (branches) and joint educational institutions operating in the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan; Documents of applicants who have studied in foreign institutions of higher education, whose list is approved annually by the joint decision of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the State Inspectorate (Quacquarelli Symonds World University Rankings, Times Nearer Education, Academic Ranking of World Universities); documents of applicants who received education at the expense of quotas (grants) allocated by foreign countries for targeted training of specialists on the basis of referrals from the ministries and agencies of the Republic of Uzbekistan, which have higher education institutions in the system; Documents of applicants who entered foreign educational institutions before January 1, 1992; full members of the European Association for Quality Assurance in Higher Education - documents, lists of applicants who have studied in educational institutions accredited by authorized organizations, except for areas, specialties and professions determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan; Documents and lists of applicants who studied in educational institutions accredited by the authorized organizations of the United States of America, the Australian Union, the State of Israel, Canada, the Republic of Singapore, the Republic of Korea, and Japan, with the exception of the directions, specialties and professions determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan; documents of applicants who received education at the expense of the funds allocated by the ministries and agencies of the Republic of Uzbekistan for the targeted training of specialists in foreign higher education institutions; Documents on education in the cases stipulated by international agreements of the Republic of Uzbekistan, decisions of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
