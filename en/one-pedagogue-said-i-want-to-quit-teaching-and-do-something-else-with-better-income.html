The teacher writes on his Twitter page that he is thinking about leaving the teaching profession.

"Even though you receive a monthly salary, you have a headache: what will I spend this money on? Your child hurts, you feel sick, you cry, but you think about going to the doctor, you can't afford it, my conscience is in front of my children. It's difficult.

I'm also forgetting the habits of women: I stopped dreaming about beautiful clothes and jewelry, I just said, "Go ahead, I'll wear this all the time." I can't stop telling my children to be patient.

Thanks to the treatment, I reduced my expenses again and managed to pay off debt. Sometimes my child rebels: "when will our house be finished, when will we invite guests, my friends, that's it, that's it." On that day, an acquaintance of mine who works in a bank said: "My husband earns 14 million monthly, excluding bonuses." I am a high-class teacher and I work for 4 months for this salary. Is this a respect for the teacher, a response to his work?" - he sighs.

How can you expect results from a person who lives in so many shortcomings?

"Have you ever taught a 6-7 year old child? Do you know how hard it is to teach and educate a child? We give our love, soul, all our knowledge, our nerves, our health to several children.

But in the end, we hear a grateful saying, "You should think about improving the quality of education, not about increasing the salary." Where are we lacking from other industry workers? Is our work so worthless? How can you expect results from a person who lives in so many shortcomings?

If you talk a lot, your heart hurts only... What is the percentage of teachers who receive a high salary out of the total number of teachers? Why isn't everyone's salary increased at once?
