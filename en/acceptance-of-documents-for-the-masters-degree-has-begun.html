Acceptance of documents for the master's degree has begun

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Today, on July 1, 2020, the process of online registration of documents for admission to master's degrees of higher education institutions of the republic began. For the 2020/2021 academic year, the process of submitting documents for admission to master's specialties of the state higher education institution is carried out through the official website of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.
Test questions to determine the scientific-pedagogical ability (or learning ability) of applicants in the fields of specializations related to the entrance exams for the master's degree (in particular, the entrance exams for specialized subjects)

The process of submitting documents for admission to master's majors is carried out through the official website of the Ministry of Higher Education.

Today, on July 1, 2020, the process of online registration of documents for admission to master's majors of the republic's higher education institutions began.

President's decree of June 12, 2020 In accordance with Decision No. PQ-4749, the process of submitting documents for admission to the master's specialties of the state higher education institution for the 2020/2021 academic year will be carried out through the official website of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

As mentioned in the Ministry's message, admission to the master's degree is a state grant determined by the presidential decree by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education by conducting an online test based on the conditions of the coronavirus pandemic. and is carried out in accordance with the admission parameters on the basis of a payment contract.

Test questions to determine the scientific-pedagogical ability (or learning ability) of applicants in the relevant fields of specialization are also included in the entrance exams for the master's degree (in particular, in the entrance exams for specialized subjects). — Official Data
