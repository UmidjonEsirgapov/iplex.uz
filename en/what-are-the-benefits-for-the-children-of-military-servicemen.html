What are the benefits for the children of military servicemen?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-3726 of May 14, 2018 provides privileges for children of military personnel of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan to enter higher education institutions.
Died while performing the duties of military service. Children of military personnel of the ministries (departments) of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan, who were disabled during the performance of military duties due to injuries, contusions, injuries or diseases, are recommended to study on the basis of a state grant without selection, except for the admission criteria set for entering higher education institutions. Basis: the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated May 14, 2018 Decision No. PQ-3726.

Decision No. PQ-3726 of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated May 14, 2018 provides for privileges for children of military personnel of the Republic of Uzbekistan to enter higher education institutions.

According to this decision:

  • To strengthen the country's defense power Children of military servicemen serving in the ministries (departments) of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan, who have made a worthy contribution, achieved certain results in combat service and scientific activity, will participate in the competition within the framework of the five percent acceptance rates allocated for admission to higher education institutions. The children of employees are recommended to study on the basis of a non-competitive state grant in addition to the admission criteria set for entering higher education institutions. Resolution No. PQ-3726 of the President of the Republic of May 14, 2018.

    Regulation approved by Resolution No. 462 of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated June 22, 2018. — Source
