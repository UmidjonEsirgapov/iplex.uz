Monthly Paid Summer Internship Program in Natural Language Processing in Southern California

Important news for students and applicants.

Summary:
USC University of Southern California is recruiting qualified and experienced undergraduate and graduate students to help develop projects in natural language processing, machine learning, statistical modeling, computational linguistics, and machine translation.
This internship is a paid internship.
Interns will have the opportunity to complete a three-month (12-week) internship during the summer of 2022.

USC University of Southern California is seeking qualified and knowledgeable undergraduate and graduate students to assist with project development in natural language processing, machine learning, statistical modeling, computational linguistics, and machine translation. is doing.

This practice is one of the paid internships. Interns will have the opportunity to conduct an internship in the summer of 2022 for a period of three months (12 weeks). Practices will be held in Marina del Rey when possible, but may be virtual practices due to COVID-19 restrictions. Even if virtual, interns must live in the US for the duration of the internship.

Participants are required to have good programming skills, but no prior natural language processing experience is required. Training manuals on relevant topics will be provided for practitioners at the beginning of the summer.

Program Areas:

Natural Language Processing;

Knowledge Technologies;

Machine Learning and Data Science;

Internet and Networked Systems;

Cybersecurity Research and Experimentation

Requirements:

  • Candidate must be a university undergraduate or graduate student;
  • Good programming skills are required;
  • Must be fluent in English.

Benefits:

  • Interns will receive a monthly salary sufficient to cover living expenses.
