The State Duma of Russia offered to pay money to students who received excellent grades. This was reported by "Lenta.ru".

It was reported that this initiative was given by Anna Skroznikova, a deputy of the "New People" party. He sent his appeal to the Prime Minister of Russia, Mikhail Mishustin, about payment of fees to students who received "five" grades. The deputy said that in order to increase the educational efficiency, financial incentives should be given to the students who studied with excellent grades. According to Skroznikova, high school students with high academic performance should be rewarded with the experience of transferring money to the "Pushkin Card". — Official Data
