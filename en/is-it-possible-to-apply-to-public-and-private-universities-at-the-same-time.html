Applicants often face the question is it possible to apply to public and private higher education institutions at the same time?. The official procedures in this regard are as follows:

  • Foreign and private universities – No limit, you can apply to any OTM.

⚠️ Important!The application is not limited. however, in the end you only need to choose one HEI. Because it is not possible to study at two different educational institutions or in two fields at the same time.
