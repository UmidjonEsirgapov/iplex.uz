An Ecology scholarship will be established for students

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The scholarship of the Minister of Ecology, Environmental Protection and Climate Change will be established starting September 1, 2023 for higher education institutions in ecology, environmental protection, forestry, "green" economy and hydrometeorology and other areas of high demand. scholarship of the Minister of Ecology, Environmental Protection and Climate Change will be established for the most talented students.
In this case, students studying on a fee-contract basis will receive scholarships in the amount of 3 times the basic calculation amount per month, and 6 times the basic calculation amount per month for those who are studying on the basis of a state grant, from the funds of the Ecology Fund.
