What are the additional quotas for children of military personnel?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Are the additional quotas for children of military servicemen valid for 5 competitions? Five percent additional admission quotas for those who have completed military service in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan and have a recommendation letter from the command of the military unit, five percent additional state grant for children of employees of ministries and agencies that are part of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan, and two percent additional state grant for children of National Guard employees according to the decision No. PQ-3726 of May 14, 2018. According to the decision PQ-4212 dated February 16, 2019, an additional five percent state grant will be given to the children of the current employees of the internal affairs bodies who have made a significant contribution to the prevention of crime and the fight against crime, and who have achieved high results in their service or academic activities.
Allocation of additional quotas The first higher education institution where additional quotas have been selected for the children of military personnel is a full-time day education institution. is valid for the form of education.

Are additional quotas of this type for children of military personnel also valid for 5 competitions?

Additional quotas are determined by the following relevant decisions of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan:

  • Decision No. PQ-3394 dated November 17, 2017 of the Republic of Uzbekistan Five percent additional admission quotas for those who have completed military service in the Armed Forces and have a recommendation letter from the command of the military unit, five percent additional admission quotas for the children of employees of ministries and agencies that are part of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan, two percent for the children of National Guard employees according to the decision of May 14, 2018 No. PQ-3726 grant,
  • Decision No. PQ-4212 dated February 16, 2019 provides an additional five percent state grant for the children of current employees of internal affairs bodies who have made a significant contribution to crime prevention and fighting crime, and who have achieved high results in their service or academic activities.

Applicants must choose additional contracts and grant quotas of this type. does not apply to all 5 options. — Read on infoedu.uz

Distribution of additional quotas

  • Additional quotas for children of military personnel applies to the first higher education institution selected for full-time education.
  • Additional quota for children of internal affairs officers full-time and part-time education to the first higher education institution valid for forms.
  • Additional quota for those with a military recommendation valid for the first higher education institution selected.
