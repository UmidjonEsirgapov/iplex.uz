List of HEIs included in the TOP-1000 whose diplomas are directly recognized

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation has approved the list of foreign higher education institutions that occupy the first 1,000 places among higher education institutions in international rankings for 2025.
This list is Quacquarelli Symonds World University Rankings. (QS) and Academic Ranking of World Universities (ARWU) are based on the ratings of internationally recognized organizations.
This list is important for young people who plan to study abroad, because the diplomas obtained from these universities are recognized in Uzbekistan without any additional nostrification. approved the list of foreign higher education institutions that took the first 1,000 places among institutions. This list is based on the ratings of internationally recognized organizations such as Quacquarelli Symonds World University Rankings (QS) and Academic Ranking of World Universities (ARWU).

This list is important for young people planning to study abroad, because the diplomas obtained from these universities are recognized in Uzbekistan without any additional nostrification. Below is a summary of these two main international ranking organizations:

  • Quacquarelli Symonds World University Rankings (QS) The QS ranking focuses on criteria such as academic quality, research, and international performance when evaluating universities around the world. It is updated annually and includes thousands of universities.

  • Academic Ranking of World Universities (ARWU) ARWU, also known as the "Shanghai Ranking", mainly focuses on academic research of universities. It presents a list of the world's best universities every year.

This list approved for 2025 will greatly help students in choosing foreign higher education institutions. The possibility of automatic recognition of diplomas obtained from these universities in Uzbekistan guarantees the quality of education and international recognition. — Read on infoedu.uz
