Will the scholarship not be given if the student does not study for "5"?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
It is known that the standard scholarship of 517,880 soums is paid to students enrolled on the basis of a grant. stipends will not be paid during the next semester.
Therefore, if those who study on a grant want to receive a scholarship, they must master at least 30 percent of the program above 71 percent.

However, at the end of the academic semester, students with 30% or more of the "satisfactory" grade (below 71%) will not receive a scholarship during the next semester.

Therefore, if students on the grant wish to receive a scholarship, they must master at least <30% of the program above 71%.

❕ Students who study on the basis of a contract will receive a scholarship only if they pay the contract money as a scholarship. However, stipends of a little more than 100,000 soums per month are paid to contract students who finish the semester with excellent grades. — Read on infoedu.uz
