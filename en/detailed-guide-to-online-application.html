Detailed guide to online application

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Entry to the system Applicants are registered through the site abitur.dtm.uz.
To enter the system, a phone number and a secret password must be entered. after entering, the applicant can choose HEIs.

The information service of the Ministry of Higher Education reported that applicants can submit documents for admission to study online. This made it possible for applicants to register online using a mobile phone or computer without leaving home.

Oliygoh.uz team presents a guide on registration through the official website of the State Test Center abitur.dtm.uz. You can also watch the video guide at the end of the article to register via the My.gov.uz website.

Log in

Applicants are registered via the abitur.dtm.uz website. To log in to the system, you need to enter a phone number and a secret password.

Enter the phone number in seven-digit format, enter a password of at least eight characters, and click the "Enter" button. After that, a special SMS code will be sent to the entered phone number, which must be entered and confirmed within five minutes.

Input of personal data

Passport after logging in you will be asked to fill in your data.

In the first line, the applicant must enter his identification number. The identification number is located at the bottom of the passport and consists of 14 digits.

Location of the identification number in the passport.

Passport series, passport number and nationality of the applicant are entered in the following lines. Confirm the section "I agree to the use of my personal data" and click "Save". year of graduation, document series and number.

If the applicant has privileges

If the applicant has privileges, after entering personal data, he can enter information about his privileges in the "Privileges 2020" section located on the home page.

In the privileges section, persons with disabilities, military units Applicants who have a recommendation letter from the command, are blind, and have a foreign language certificate enter their privileges. Other types of benefits are filled out on the basis of information received from relevant agencies.

Example of entering a foreign language certificate:

If the applicant has a foreign language certificate, he must fill in the following information: document series and number, date of document issuance, validity period, type of foreign language, type of certificate, level and document photo. Then click on the Save button.

Select HEIs

After entering the personal details and eligibility details, if any, the applicant can select HEIs.

The photo shows only three directions. Applicants can choose five courses.

Choose a course as follows:

  1. Choose the form of education: full-time, part-time or evening.

  2. Choose the language of education: Uzbek, Russian, Karakalpak.

  3. Choice of the graphics for taking the test: Latin or Cyrillic.

  4. Depending on the first selected OET and direction, it is possible to choose four more OET and their direction.

  5. Testing region: the applicant chooses the region convenient for him to take the test.

  6. "Save" button is clicked.

Then a special SMS code will be sent to the phone number indicated during registration, and it should be entered in the following section:

After that, the applicant a registration form will be created.

If the applicant has lost his login password, he can restore or change his password through the password recovery section.

Invoice payment

Applicants who graduated from educational institutions in the years before the academic year in which the tests are being organized, will have to pay half the amount of the basic calculation, (currently 111,500 soums) to participate in the tests. After the application is filled out, an invoice will be generated at the bottom of the applicant's registration form and payment must be made within the specified period. Payment can be made through electronic payment systems (click, payme, upay, paynet, etc.) or bank cash registers. does not increase.

After the data is entered completely and correctly, an SMS message will be sent to the phone number with the content "You are registered".

The applicant can change his benefits, the chosen fields of study, the address of permanent residence and the educational institution he graduated from before the end of the registration period (until July 20).
