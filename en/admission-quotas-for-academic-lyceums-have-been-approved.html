Admission quotas for academic lyceums have been approved

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
By the relevant order of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, the parameters of admission to academic lyceums under the jurisdiction of higher education institutions for the academic year 2024/2025 have been approved in terms of educational directions and languages of instruction.
Admission parameters-2024.pdf According to it, a total of 15,496 quotas were allocated to 71 academic lyceums, including 12,324 Uzbek, 2,730 Russian and 442 Karakalpak language quotas.
