The process of professional certification of teachers is being misinterpreted - the ministry

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
The Ministry of Preschool and School Education has informed about the wrong interpretations regarding the involvement of teachers in the process of professional certification.
In the instruction letter sent by the Ministry to the Agency of Special Education Institutions and the Regional Departments of Preschool and School Education, which category it is clearly defined that teachers should be involved in the process of professional certification.
In this instruction letter, it is noted that it is allowed to hire candidates who have pedagogical work experience and who are changing their workplace for various reasons to the position of trainee teacher, as well as to involve pedagogues with higher, first and second qualification categories to professional certification tests.

Pre-school and the Ministry of School Education reported on misinterpretations regarding the involvement of teachers in the professional certification process. The instruction letter sent by the Ministry to the Agency of Specialized Educational Institutions and regional pre-school and school education departments clearly defines which categories of teachers should be involved in the process of professional certification.

In this instruction letter, candidates who have pedagogical work experience and are changing their workplace for various reasons, as well as those who have higher, first and second qualification categories it is noted that it is allowed to involve pedagogues in professional certificate tests.

In the letter of the Ministry dated August 9, 2023, starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, applicants with higher education who will be employed for the first time in teaching professions should start working as trainee teachers and they should obtain a professional certificate within one year.
