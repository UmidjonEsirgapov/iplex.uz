What privileges are available to members of the Youth Union for admission to higher education institutions?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Decision No. 914 of the Cabinet of Ministers of November 14, 2017 stipulates that active members of the Youth Union of Uzbekistan will be given letters of recommendation and preferential admission to educational institutions.
According to this decision: - letters of recommendation It is given to captains in the system of the youth union of Uzbekistan, department leaders in primary organizations, selfless young people with two years of work experience; - young people who have letters of recommendation are admitted to pedagogic education courses of higher educational institutions at their place of residence based on the decision of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan without non-competition tests and professional (creative) exams on the basis of state grants; - letters of recommendation are presented to one person (14 people in total) from the Republic of Karakalpakstan, the regions and the city of Tashkent.
The 3rd stage is the final stage and is carried out by the republican commission. and educational institutions.

According to this decision:

- letters of recommendation are given to leaders of the system of the youth union of Uzbekistan, department leaders in primary organizations, selfless young people with two years of work experience;

- young people with letters of recommendation to the Republic of Uzbekistan Education based on the decision of the state commission for admission to institutions of higher education, they are accepted on the basis of state grants without non-selective tests and professional (creative) examinations;

- letters of recommendation from one person (total of 14) from the Republic of Karakalpakstan, regions and the city of Tashkent. presented to young people.

Candidates are selected in the following three stages

 in order to receive a letter of recommendation:

1st stage — It is carried out by the district (city) commission in districts and cities of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

2nd stage — It is carried out by the territorial commission of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, regional centers and the city of Tashkent.

The 3rd stage is the final stage and is carried out by the republican commission.

The republican commission approves the list of the most deserving young people from the Republic of Karakalpakstan, the regions and the city of Tashkent according to the results of the competition, and Within 10 days, he submits a proposal to the state commission for admission to educational institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

✅ The recommendation is valid only for the presented year.

The recommendations do not apply to foreign higher education institutions located in the Republic of Uzbekistan.
