Artificial intelligence and robotics course was organized for schoolchildren with the participation of Korean experts

Artificial intelligence and robotics course for students in school 129 of Uchtepa district was established. In this course, 32 students of the school's graduating class are learning about artificial intelligence and robotics from Korean experts.

For the course Based on the grant of the Ministry of Education of Korea, the material and technical base of the Robotics Laboratory was formed. Arduino kit and PIBO robot were brought for this lab. With the help of these devices, students will have the opportunity to test their theoretical knowledge of programming on robotic devices. It is planned to apply positive experiences to other schools. — Source
