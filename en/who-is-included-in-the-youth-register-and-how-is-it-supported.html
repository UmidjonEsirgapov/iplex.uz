Who is included in the "Youth Register" and how is it supported?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
Conditions for inclusion in the "Youth register" Age category: 14 to 30 years old (must not be under 31 years old).
Form of support: Support is provided in various areas at the expense of the fund.
Types of support Solving socio-economic problems: Ensuring youth employment.

According to the legislation of Uzbekistan, citizens who are 14 years old and under 30 years old (under 31 years old) have the right to be included in the "Youth Register". Citizens of this age group are supported from the funds of the fund. This initiative is aimed at solving the socio-economic problems of young people and improving their quality of life.

Conditions for inclusion in the "Youth Register"

  • Age category: 14 to 30 years old (must not be 31 years old).
  • Citizenship: It is required to be a citizen of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
  • Form of support: Support is provided in various areas at the expense of the fund.

Types of support

Solution of socio-economic problems:

  • Ensuring youth employment.
  • Involvement in entrepreneurship.
  • Organizing safe labor migration abroad

Vocational training:

  • Employment of young people through vocational training.
  • Support of entrepreneurial initiatives.

Recovery of education costs:

  • Allocation of subsidy to cover education costs.
  • Driving covering the costs of training courses.

Financial support:

  • Providing one-time assistance to young people in difficult financial situations.

Supporting talented young people:

  • Subsidies for the development of initiatives in the fields of science, sports, art and culture.

Conclusion

"Youth Notebook" is an important initiative aimed at supporting young people. Through this, young people can increase their social and economic opportunities, use additional support in vocational training and education.
