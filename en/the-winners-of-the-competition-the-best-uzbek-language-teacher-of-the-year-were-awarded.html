year 2024 On October 18, an awarding ceremony for the winners of the republican contest "The best Uzbek language teacher of the year" was held at the 5th school in Tashkent. Representatives of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education, officials of the State Language Development Department of the Cabinet of Ministers and teachers took part in this event.

Competition results:

  • 2nd place: II degree diploma and monobloc.
  • 3rd place: III degree diploma and laptop.
