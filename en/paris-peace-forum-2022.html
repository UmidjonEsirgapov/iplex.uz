2022 In November, the Paris Peace Forum aims to promote a more comprehensive recovery and propose initiatives that address global and common challenges.

Each year, governance projects that offer concrete solutions to global challenges become part of the Forum's Solutions Space and discussions. The solutions highlighted at the Forum go through the concept stage and are characterized by international and collaborative dimensions, as well as the ability to create sustainable changes in global governance.

By participating in the Paris Peace Forum, project leaders will have a unique opportunity to demonstrate their innovative solutions and promote their project.
