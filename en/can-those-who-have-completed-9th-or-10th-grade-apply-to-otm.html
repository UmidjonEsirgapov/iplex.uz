Can those who have completed the 9th or 10th grade apply to the OTM?

In the Republic of Uzbekistan, the procedure for entering higher education institutions (HEIs) is determined on the basis of the "Law on Education". This law, which was updated in 2020, clearly specified the levels of education and the criteria for entering higher education.

Who can enter the Higher Education Institution according to the "Law on Education"?

üìö According to Article 11 of the "Law on Education" adopted in 2020, the following persons have the right to apply for the Higher Education Institution:

  • General secondary education - that is, students who have completed 11 years of school.

  • Secondary special education - 9 years of school + 2 years of college or technical school graduates.

  • Primary professional education - 9 years of school + 2 years of vocational school graduates.

  • Persons with a diploma of secondary special or vocational education in previous years.
