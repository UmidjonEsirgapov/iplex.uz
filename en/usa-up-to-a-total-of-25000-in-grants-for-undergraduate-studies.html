About the University and Grant Program:

Transylvania University is a private university located in Lexington, Kentucky. It was founded in 1780 and was the first university in Kentucky. It offers 36 major programs, as well as two engineering degrees, and is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools.

The grant program offered by the university provides financial support to talented international students.
