A new educational direction will be opened at the Andijan State Medical Institute

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In the Andijan State Medical Institute, starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, bachelor's training in the field of "Industrial Pharmacy" will be launched. the head of state noted at the meeting.
"Department heads of the Andijan State Medical Institute will be assigned to be responsible for providing practical support to the activities of district and city medical associations.

In the Andijan State Medical Institute, starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, bachelor's training in the field of "Industrial Pharmacy" will be launched. This was stated by the head of state at a meeting chaired by President Shavkat Mirziyoyev with the activists of Andijan region. In addition, within two months, an angiograph machine will be installed in the clinic of the Andijan State Medical Institute, and the type and number of surgeries performed on the cardiovascular system will be increased," said the head of state.
