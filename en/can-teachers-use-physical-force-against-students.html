Can teachers use physical force or discriminate against a student?
The "Improved General Secondary Education Institution" approved by Order No. 282 of the Minister of Preschool and School Education dated August 31, 2023 according to the "model code of conduct", the requirements for pedagogues include the following: Avoiding the use of physical force or psychological pressure on students; Not to discuss personal information of students in front of classmates; Do not follow the student in public; If the students go abroad, inform the management of the institution about it in time; Not to engage in personal enmity with the student's parents or their legal representatives; Not to use information about the educational institution to harm its interests and reputation or for one's own interests; Be an example to students, show a personal example, be friendly with them; Refrain from actions contrary to the interests of the educational institution and prevent such situations; In case of any unpleasant incident or event, immediately notify the management of the educational institution; Widespread promotion of a healthy lifestyle among young people, warning them of harmful habits and religious extremist influences alien to our society; Explain these rules to the students during the "Class Hour" classes held on Fridays of the week.
