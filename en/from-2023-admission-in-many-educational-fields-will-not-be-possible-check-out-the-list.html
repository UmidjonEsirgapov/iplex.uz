From 2023, admission in many educational fields will not be possible. Check out the list

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In the next academic year, 14 courses will not be accepted for part-time education. According to the decision of the President No. PQ-289 dated June 21, 2022, admission to correspondence education in the following 14 educational fields of higher education institutions training pedagogical staff will not be carried out from the admission of the 2023/2024 academic year: 60110600 — Mathematics and informatics 60110700 — Physics and astronomy 60110800 — Chemistry 60110900 — Biology 60111000 — Basics of geography and economic knowledge 60111400 — Uzbek language and literature 60111500 — Mother tongue and literature (by language) 60111600 — Uzbek language in foreign language groups 60111700 — Other Russian language in linguistic groups 60111800 — Foreign language and literature (by languages) 60112000 — Military education before conscription 60112500 — School management 60112600 — Foreign language in pre-school and primary education (by languages) 60112700 — Special pedagogy (by types of activity) Above Persons who have at least five years of pedagogical work experience in the education system are admitted to study in the non-specified fields of pedagogy in the form of correspondence education.
In this case, the persons who wish to study in the fields of education in the field of pedagogy in the form of correspondence education must have at least five years of pedagogical work experience in the educational system of the "Uniform National Labor System" interagency software-hardware complex is determined based on the data.
