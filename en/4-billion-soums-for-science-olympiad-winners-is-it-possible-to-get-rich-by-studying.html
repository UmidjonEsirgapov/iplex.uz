4 billion soums for Science Olympiad winners - is it possible to get rich by studying?

Brief content:
The number of Uzbek students participating in international science Olympiads and returning as winners is increasing year by year.
According to the information provided by preschool and school education, for students and their teachers Since 2019, monetary awards of 4.1 billion soums have been allocated.
From 2022, a special training program has been put into practice in order to prepare students for prestigious international Olympiads and teach them the necessary knowledge and skills. Prizes of up to 150 million soums were allocated to the winners, and up to 135 million soums to the teacher who prepared them. Kun.uz has compiled the prizes and privileges given to the winners of science Olympiads.

Photo: KUN.UZ

International science Olympiads are mainly held in the field of exact and natural sciences. Since the beginning of giving larger prizes to the winners of such Olympiads, the number of international Olympiads from Uzbekistan has also increased. According to the information provided by preschool and school education, monetary awards of 4.1 billion soums have been allocated since 2019 for students and their teachers who won prizes in prestigious international Olympiads. Last year alone, 39 students returned as winners of regional and international Olympiads.

37 medals

According to the Ministry of Preschool and School Education, schoolchildren from Uzbekistan have been participating in international competitions such as IMO, ICHO, IBO, IPHO, IOI since 2017. During this time, students won 4 gold, 13 silver and 20 bronze medals - a total of 37 medals. In 2017, a total of 37 students participated in regional international Olympiads, and in 2022, this number reached 308.

From 2022, a special training program was implemented to prepare students for prestigious international Olympiads and to teach them the necessary knowledge and skills. Hotel "Uzbekistan" was selected. Members of the national team of Uzbekistan consisting of 17 students and 50 students of the 9th-10th grade who recorded high results in the qualifying Olympiads, who were included in the reserve for the next years, participated in this training.

Prizes and benefits for winners

According to the decision of the President "On measures to identify talented young people and organize a continuous system of highly qualified personnel training", the following benefits will be given to students who took high places in international Olympiads:

- International Mathematics Olympiad, International Physics Olympiad, International Chemistry Olympiad, International Biology Olympiad winners are admitted to state higher education institutions without tests and additional exams;

- participants who took 1st - 3rd places in the republican stage of the main Olympiads are awarded with a certificate that gives the right to receive the maximum score in entrance exams to state higher education institutions in their specialty and is valid for a period of three years."

- students awarded with gold medals - 500 times of BHM (150 million) and their teachers - 450 times of BHM (135 million);

- students awarded with silver medals - 300 times of BHM (90 million) and their teachers - 250 times of BHM (75 million);

- awarded with bronze medals students will be awarded 200 times (60 million) BMH and their teachers will be awarded 150 times (45 million) BMH.

A special opportunity for teachers of international winners

Teachers who trained winners of international Olympiads and directors of educational institutions will also receive 200, 175 and The practice of paying a bonus of 150 percent has been introduced.

Also, winners of international Olympiads working as teachers in educational institutions are paid 150 percent of their salary every month, and winners of the republican stage of the main Olympiads are paid a monthly bonus of 100 percent.

Rewarding at the regional level

Ministry According to the information given, starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, the winners of the regional stage of the basic science Olympiad will be awarded monetary prizes. Last year, 2.4 billion soums were allocated to the winners, and in the 2022/2023 academic year, nearly 3 billion soums were allocated.

2.9 billion soums have been allocated to encourage students and their teachers. It is important that money is allocated not only to students and teachers, but also to judges. This practice has not been observed in Uzbekistan before.

One-time monetary awards for the winners:

- 50 times the BHM (15 million) for the 1st place;

- For the 2nd place - 35 times the BHM (10.5 million);

- for the 3rd place - 30 times the BHM (9 million). — Official Data
