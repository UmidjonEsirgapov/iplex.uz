That's it! Some public universities will be converted into private ones

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
In the concept of development of the higher education system of the Republic of Uzbekistan until 2030, which is planned to be privatized, some state higher education institutions (with the exception of higher military educational institutions) will be expropriated and privatized, while retaining at least 51 percent of the state share as an experiment. introduction of management principles is defined.

Privatization of some state HEIs is planned

In the concept of development of the higher education system of the Republic of Uzbekistan until 2030, as an experiment, it is determined to expropriate and privatize some state higher education institutions (except for higher military educational institutions), while retaining at least 51 percent of the state's share, and introduce the principles of corporate management in them. — Official Data
