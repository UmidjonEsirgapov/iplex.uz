To receive a recommendation, the applicant must meet all of the following criteria: have a specialty in secondary special or professional education; that he has not received or is not receiving higher education before receiving the letter of recommendation; have five years of work experience in secondary special or professional education specialty.

According to the government's decision, applications for recommendation must be received every year from May 1 to June 1.

According to the committee, the following document is attached to the application:

  • Citizen passport or ID card copy;
  • Excerpt from work record book (electronic work book);
  • Reference-lens;
  • Diploma in secondary special or professional education.

To receive a recommendation, the applicant must meet all of the following criteria:

  • have a specialty in secondary special or professional education
  • not having or not receiving higher education before receiving the recommendation;
  • having five years of work experience in the specialty of secondary special or professional education.

Information about women who have a recommendation will be included in the special program of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency, indicating the directions suitable for their work.

Even with a recommendation, women participate in the entrance exams to the OTM. According to the results of the entrance exams, women who were not recommended as a student based on the points scored, but who have a letter of recommendation who scored 30% of the highest points possible and not less are selected according to the selection within separate admission parameters (total 500 quotas) on the basis of the quotas distributed by the Admissions Committee, strictly observing the sequence of points. it is recommended to study on a fee-contract basis. — Official Data
