KAUST fellowship - an opportunity for those who plan to study at the master's and doctoral levels of the King Abdullah University of Science and Technology. Expenses are fully covered, they can study and research for free.

BENEFITS

  • Exempt from contract fee;
  • $20,000 to $30,000 per month per year;
  • Provided with accommodation and health insurance;
  • Help with transportation.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must be admitted to KAUST;
  • English language proficiency level (IELTS 6/TOEFL iBT 79). If the candidate has studied in countries such as the USA, Canada, Great Britain, Ireland, Australia or New Zealand, a language certificate is not required;
  • GRE exam result (optional);
  • Document confirming the last degree (diploma) and its grades;
  • 3 letters of recommendation;
  • CV (curriculum vitae).

For applications and offers: admissions@kaust.edu.sa
