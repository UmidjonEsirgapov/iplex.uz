Who can participate in the targeted admission?

After the quota is approved, the targeted admission places will be divided by region. In order to use the target admission quota allocated to one region, an applicant who permanently resides (has a residence permit) in that region uses targeted admission (source). The applicant must also have a certificate of completion of general secondary or secondary education.

How are the quotas for targeted admissions distributed?

Quotas are allocated according to the directions and the places in the directions are allocated according to the regions (source). For example, areas such as health care and public education are separated.

Will those who do not score enough points in the targeted admission drop out of school?

If the applicant who participated in the targeted admission competition collects the required points, he will become a student on the basis of a grant. But if he does not pass, he will participate in the general competition together with other applicants. If he gets the score, he will definitely be a student.
