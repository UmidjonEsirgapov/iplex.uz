The certification of pedagogues in the system of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education will begin on October 29, 2024. This process is carried out in real time test through proficiency tests. These tests are conducted in the subjects of physical education and foreign language.

Attestation is conducted in two stages, one day and at the same time using computer programs. The time allocated for the test varies depending on the subjects:

  • For mathematics, informatics, information technology, chemistry, biology and physics - 120 minutes.
  • For all other subjects - 90 minutes.

Composition of qualification tests:

1. Stage: Determination of specialization and professional knowledge

At the first stage, the pedagogue is required to show the level of knowledge in specialty and professional standard. At this stage, 40 test questions will be given (35 from the specialty subject, 5 from pedagogical professional standards). 2 points are given for each correct answer, and a total of 80 points can be collected.

2. Stage: Determination of pedagogical skills

In the second stage, 10 tasks are given on the application of advanced pedagogical methods. These methods include "case study", project method, interactive learning and others. Each correct answer is valued at 2 points, for a total of 20 points. — Official Data
