Students' knowledge is now assessed through an artificial intelligence-based platform

Ministry of Preschool and School Education and Singapore Education organization, a system for assessing students' knowledge of mathematics and natural sciences through the artificial intelligence-based Cerebry platform was launched.

This platform can be used by school teachers and students anywhere using a computer, tablet.

The platform is based on artificial intelligence (AI) is based, and the questions asked in it are flexible to the students' knowledge.

Teachers also give assignments to students through the platform and can monitor the results.

All school students can use this platform and analyze the results. Registration is required for this. Assignments are posted in Uzbek, Russian and English languages.

At the moment, the registration processes of students and teachers of schools in our Republic are continuing through the cerebry.co platform. — Official Data
