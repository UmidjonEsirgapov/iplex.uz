In what cases is the contract of women studying at the master's degree not paid by the state?

The Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan According to the decision (15.08.2022 No. 447), the contract fees of all women studying at the master's level of public higher education institutions are reimbursed from the state budget funds without the condition of return. apply to the rector (branch director) of the higher educational institution where they receive their education for reimbursement by September 15 of the academic year. The applications will be reviewed within three days and a list will be formed.

In what cases will the contract not be paid?

The contract of women studying at the master's degree will not be paid by the state in the following cases:

  • The applicant must submit the application to the rector (branch director) of the higher education institution after September 15 of the academic year. if
  • He was admitted to study on the basis of a differentiated (super) payment-contract

These conditions are set in order to ensure the correct and targeted spending of funds allocated by the state. — Read on infoedu.uz
