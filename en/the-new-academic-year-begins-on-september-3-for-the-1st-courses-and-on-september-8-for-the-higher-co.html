The new academic year begins on September 3 for the 1st courses, and on September 8 for the higher courses.

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The dates of the beginning of the new 2025/2026 academic year have been approved. According to the order of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, the dates of the beginning of the new academic year have been determined in higher education organizations and academic lyceums.
Senior year students and students of the 2nd and higher stages of higher education institutions will start the new academic year on September 8.
The week "Introduction to the credit education system" and "Dedication to students" will be organized as a special week for the students of the first stage admitted to higher education institutions.

Starting dates of the new 2025/2026 academic year approved

According to the order of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, new academic year start dates were set in higher education organizations and academic lyceums.

For academic lyceum and 1st stage students

In the 2025/2026 academic year:

  • All stages of academic lyceums students;

  • 1st-level students of higher education institutions

classes will start on September 3, 2025.

For senior-year students

2nd and higher-level students of higher education organizations will begin the new academic year They will start from September 8.

"Entering the credit education system" and "Dedication to students" week

A special week will be organized for first-year students admitted to higher education institutions.

Events within the week:

  • Introducing students to the activities of the higher education organization;

  • Explaining the content, essence and advantages of the credit-module system;

  • Giving recommendations on the purposeful selection of elective subjects;

  • Forming independent learning skills;

  • Promoting work on academic integrity, professional and personal development.

Conclusion

The 2025/2026 academic year will begin with new reforms and traditional events. 1st courses start on September 3rd, and senior courses start on September 8th.
