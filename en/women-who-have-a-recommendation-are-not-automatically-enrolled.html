Women who have a recommendation are not automatically enrolled

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Among some applicants, a misconception is forming that "women with a letter of recommendation will become a student without an exam." there is a letter of recommendation: a letter of recommendation for girls from needy families - this type of privilege is 4% of the total quota.
A letter of recommendation for women with 5 years of work experience - a separate quota of 500 is allocated for this category.

Among some applicants, there is a misconception that "women with a letter of recommendation will be students without an exam". In fact, this situation is absolutely wrong.

Having a recommendation does not mean that you automatically become a student

There are two types of recommendation for women in the higher education system of Uzbekistan:

  1. Recommendation for girls from needy families - this type of privilege is general. is 4% of the quota.

  2. Recommendation for women with 5 years of work experience - 500 quotas are allocated separately for this category.

What is the procedure for those who receive a recommendation?

  • Both categories of women take part in the exam condition.

  • They will participate in the competition within a separate quota.

  • However, those who scored less than 56.7 points will not be recommended for studentship even on the basis of a recommendation.

For this reason, the recommendation gives a privilege, but automatic studentship does not guarantee.

Important note:

  • Applications for a quota of 500 based on 5 years of experience will be accepted from May 1.
