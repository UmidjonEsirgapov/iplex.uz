A new procedure for issuing educational loans to students has been approved

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, 14% educational loans will be allocated to students for a period of 7 years.
According to it: commercial banks will allocate educational loans at the main rate of the Central Bank (14%).
Note: now, it is envisaged that not only families with two or more children studying on a fee-contract basis, but also all students studying on a fee-contract basis will be given the same preferential educational loan. a new system of allocating loans for education on the basis of payment-contract payment will be launched.

Starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, students will be allocated 14% educational loans for a period of 7 years.

According to it:

  • commercial banks the main rate of the Central Bank (14%) allocates an educational loan. The resource will be placed by the Ministry of Finance;
  • the student will repay the loan for seven years, starting from the seventh month after officially completing his studies.
  • family members of the student with a permanent source of income are allowed to participate as joint borrowers in the loan process.
  • In order to provide commercial banks with financial resources for educational loans, the Education Loan Financing Fund will be established under the Ministry of Finance (it will not be a legal entity). At least 1 trillion soums will be allocated to the fund in 2021, based on the real demand from the following years. If the main rate decreases, the loan and resource rate will also decrease, if it increases, it will remain unchanged.
