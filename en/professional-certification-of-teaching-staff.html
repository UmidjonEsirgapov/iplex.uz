Professional certification of teaching staff

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
In this: applicants with higher education, who are employed for the first time in the teaching profession, work as trainee teachers and are required to have a professional certificate for a period of one year from the time of their employment; After receiving a professional certificate, pedagogues must have a professional certificate for one year from the time of resuming their activities, which have been interrupted for five or more years; As a rule, the control test for obtaining a professional certificate is held in the form of a test every year in March-May; According to the results of the control test, the applicant who scored 56 points or higher is considered to have passed the control test, and the electronic certificate is sent to his personal office.
