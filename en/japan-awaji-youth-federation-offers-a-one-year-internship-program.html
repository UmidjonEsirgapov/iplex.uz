Japan: Awaji Youth Federation offers a one-year internship program

Awaji Youth Federation is one of the new and promising projects of Pasona Group. The project was established in 2017 on Avaji Island. The organizers invite young leaders who want to develop to be a part of this project and do not miss this opportunity.

The organizers offer:

  • Working with Pasona Group Inc for 1 year under contract;
  • You will need to work 5 days a week, 8 hours a day;
  • Salary - monthly 250,000JPY;
  • Medical insurance, paid vacation (10 days).

Application starts September 1 and lasts until November 30. Interviews will be held in December-January. The internship program will start in April, 2022.

To apply, go through the online list and fill in the necessary information.

During the program, participants will not only have the opportunity to work with qualified professionals and exchange experience, but may also establish new partnerships and even be hired in the future.
