Qualifying tests for the State Security Service Academy will be held on November 18, 2023

Tests in the subjects of mother tongue (Uzbek, Russian, Karakalpak), mathematics, history of Uzbekistan and foreign languages will be organized in 1 shift and will be held from 14:00 to 17:00.
