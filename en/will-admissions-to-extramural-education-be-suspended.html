Will admissions to extramural education be suspended?

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Acceptance for correspondence education in other areas has not been stopped.
Secondly, there are many directions in the field of pedagogy, can everyone apply for correspondence in other directions than the above 14?
In the field of pedagogy Any person will not be admitted to other courses either.

Following the messages on social networks, "has admission to part-time education been stopped?" more and more questions. We would like to clarify such questions.

First of all, this decision applies only to 14 PEDAGOGICAL areas. The list is given in the picture below. ❌ admission to these 14 courses has been suspended since this year.

Acceptance in other courses by correspondence education has not been stopped.

Secondly, PEDAGOGY there are many courses in the field of education, can everyone apply for courses other than the 14 above?

❗️No, they cannot. Only persons who have at least 5 years of work experience related to pedagogical activities in the educational system are admitted to SIRTSKI.

✅ This applies only to the field of PEDAGOGY. This decision is not related to directions in other fields.

Also, there are no restrictions for any direction in the field of pedagogy in the form of day and evening education, any applicant can submit an application.
