Applicants are given the right to participate in the competition for up to 5 undergraduate courses

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The President signed the decision "On the parameters of the state order for admission to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan in the 2021/2022 academic year." registration of applicants will be carried out from June 22 to July 22 of this year (also on July 22) through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal and the official website of the State Test Center; For women who have a recommendation letter from the Ministry of Neighborhood and Family Support, an additional 2,000 state grant admission rates will be allocated in comparison to the generally established state grant admission rates for full-time undergraduate education; In order to socio-economically develop the Urgut district of Samarkand region and to reduce the shortage of personnel in the education system, 100 additional targeted admission rates based on state grants will be allocated for this district in comparison to the admission rates based on the generally established state grant in the field of pedagogy in the form of full-time undergraduate education; applicants are given the right to participate in the competition for up to five undergraduate educational fields with the same set of subjects and the same sequence of subjects; applicants will be given an additional opportunity to participate in the competition based on a state grant, and then a fee-contract based on the priority of the bachelor's education courses chosen during the registration process. signed.
