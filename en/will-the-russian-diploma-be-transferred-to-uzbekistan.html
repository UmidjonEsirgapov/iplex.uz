Will the Russian diploma be transferred to Uzbekistan?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
On May 30, 2019, the government of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the government of the Russian Federation signed an agreement on the mutual recognition of education, qualifications and scientific degrees. higher education of no less than a year, confirmed by a full-time bachelor's diploma, is recognized in both countries and gives the right to carry out specialist activities.
Also, it gives their holders the right to continue studying for a master's degree in both countries.

On May 30, 2019 between the Government of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the Government of the Russian Federation education, qualification and an agreement on mutual recognition of academic degrees was signed. According to it, educational programs with state accreditation, higher education with a duration of not less than four years and a full-time bachelor's degree are recognized in both countries and give the right to carry out specialist activities. It also gives their holders the right to continue their master's studies in both countries.

You can check whether the higher education institution has a license or state accreditation through the website of the Federal Service for Supervision of Science and Education (Rosobrnadzor) of Russia (https://obrnadzor.gov.ru/). — More information
