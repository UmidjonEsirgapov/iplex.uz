What subjects are the entrance exams for religious educational institutions?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The Department of Education and Scientific Research of the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan has announced which subjects are planned to be used for the entrance exams for the 2021/22 academic year. taken: Fiqh and Aqeed (test) 30 x 3.1 - maximum 93 points; Arabic language (oral, creative exam) 30 x 2.1 - 63 points; History of Uzbekistan (test) 30 x 1.1 - 33 points.
School of Hadith: Hadith and Mustalahul Hadith (oral, creative exam) 30 x 3.1 - 93 points; Arabic language (oral, creative exam) 30 x 2.1 - 63 points; History of Uzbekistan (test) 30 x 1.1 - 33 points.

The Department of Education and Scientific Research of the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan announced on which subjects the entrance exams are planned for the 2021/22 academic year. at the entrance, an exam is taken in the following subjects:

  • Fiqh and Aqeed (test) 30 x 3.1 - maximum 93 points;
  • Arabic language (oral, creative exam) 30 x 2.1 - 63 points;
  • Uzbekistan history (test) 30 x 1.1 - 33 points.

Hadith science school:

  • Hadith and Mustalahul hadith (oral, creative exam) 30 x 3.1 - 93 points;
  • Arabic language (oral, creative exam) 30 x 2.1 - 63 points;
  • Uzbekistan history (test) 30 x 1.1 - 33 points.

Secondary special religious educational institutions:

  • Foreign language (test) 30 x 3.1 – 93 points;
  • History (test) 30 x 2.1 – 63 points;
  • Spirituality and enlightenment (test) 30 x 1.1 – 33 points.

For information, last academic year due to the pandemic, entrance exams in higher and secondary special religious educational institutions under the authority of the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan were reduced from 3 subjects to two. "Hadith" subjects, as well as "History" and "Foreign language" tests were conducted for special Islamic educational institutions. — More information
