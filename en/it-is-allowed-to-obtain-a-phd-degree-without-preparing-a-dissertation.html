It is allowed to obtain a PhD degree without preparing a dissertation

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
It is possible to get a PhD degree without preparing a dissertation.
The draft of the President's relevant decision on this matter has been published.
According to the document, the following proposals are allowed: - a single publication published by the decision of the councils of higher education and scientific organizations without preparing a dissertation awarding the scientific degree of doctor of philosophy (PhD) according to the decision of the academic council that awards scientific degrees based on the discussion of the author's monograph presentation; - awarding the doctor of philosophy (PhD) academic degree based on the recommendation of the Council of the Youth Academy to the author of scientific developments on solving specific tasks, advanced technological developments that are in high demand in domestic and foreign markets without preparing a dissertation (except for medical sciences); - presentation of at least four scientific innovations on the results of research in a specific historical, political, architectural and art history field without preparing a dissertation and on the presentation on the TV "History of Uzbekistan" and scientific innovations being used in the scripts of fiction or documentary films and awarding the scientific degree of Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in history, politics, architecture and art science sciences to the author of the research in the established order based on the decision of the scientific council awarding academic degrees; - inclusion of a candidate of science, a doctor of philosophy (PhD) with the scientific title of a highly qualified and experienced professor with a Hirsh index of at least two into the scientific council awarding the scientific degree of Doctor of Science (DSc); - cancellation of the procedure for additional discussion of theses in the leading organization; - establishment of the accounting commission that conducts the voting process in the academic council, establishing the practice of approving the approval of applicants at the academic level by means of "open" voting in the academic council granting scientific degrees, and canceling the procedure of "closed" voting; - introduction of the practice of monitoring the discussion and open defense of dissertations in scientific seminars in all disciplines in real time via the Internet. The relevant draft of the President's decision in this regard has been published.

According to the document, the following proposals are allowed:

- to award the degree of Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) according to the decision of the academic council that awards scientific degrees based on the discussion of the presentation of a monograph of the sole author published by the decision of the councils of higher education and scientific organizations without preparing a dissertation;

- awarding the Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) academic degree based on the recommendation of the Council of the Youth Academy to the author of scientific developments on solving specific tasks, advanced technological developments that are in high demand in domestic and foreign markets without preparing a dissertation (except for medical sciences); four scientific innovations were presented on the TV channel "History of Uzbekistan" and on the basis of the presentation and the decision of the scientific council awarding scientific degrees on the use of scientific innovations in the scripts of feature films or documentaries, awarding the author of the study the scientific degree of doctor of philosophy (PhD) in the disciplines of history, politics, architecture and art studies in accordance with the established procedure; admission of a candidate of science with the scientific title of an experienced professor, a doctor of philosophy (PhD);

- cancellation of the procedure for conducting additional discussion of dissertations in the leading organization;

- establishing the practice of approving applicants at the academic level by "open" voting in the scientific council granting scientific degrees, establishing the accounting commission that conducts the voting process in the scientific council and canceling the "closed" voting procedure

- introducing the practice of monitoring the process of discussion and open defense of dissertations in scientific seminars in all disciplines in real time via the Internet.

- the practice of accepting qualifying exams in order to increase the quality of assessing the level of preparation of applicants to post-higher education institutions and focus their time after admission to these institutions on the quality of research. Starting from January 1:

authority to independently approve the Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) and Doctor of Science (DSc) scientific degrees of applicants in the fields of history, philosophy, philology, law, architecture, art science, pedagogy, and psychology, which have become important in improving the quality of the educational and educational process;
