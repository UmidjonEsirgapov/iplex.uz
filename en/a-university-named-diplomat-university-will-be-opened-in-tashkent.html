A university named "Diplomat University" will be opened in Tashkent.

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Education is provided in the fields of economy, information technology, law, international relations.
The document stipulates the establishment of the higher education institution "Diplomat University".

Education will be provided in the areas of economics, information technology, law, international relations.

Draft of the government's decision on the establishment of the activities of the non-governmental higher education organization "Diplomat University". was put up for public discussion.

The document stipulates the establishment of the higher educational institution "Diplomat University".

According to the draft decision, the university educational program will be developed in harmony with the educational programs of leading foreign higher educational institutions of Uzbekistan and the world. Professors and teachers of leading foreign universities, scientists and specialists are involved in the educational process. University teachers are sent for internships to prestigious foreign higher educational institutions and scientific institutions for qualification improvement and retraining.

Accordingly, "Diplomat university" will be given the right to open a branch in Uzbekistan of foreign universities included in "The times higher education" TOP-1000 rating.

Teaching at the university is full-time, distance (online) in Uzbek, Russian and English and evening forms based on the credit system.

According to the draft decision, the university will:

  • provide education in the areas of economics, information technology, law, international relations;
  • give its graduates a diploma recognized as a document of higher education in the republic;
  • a student who has fulfilled certain requirements and conditions of the curriculum it is confirmed that after graduating from the university, he can get a diploma in two majors (double major) or major and related majors (major+minor).

According to the decision, the university operates in Tashkent city. It will be exempted from paying all kinds of taxes until August 1, 2022.

Earlier, it was reported that the draft decision on the establishment of the Tashkent University of Applied Sciences in the capital was put to public discussion, and a non-state Japanese digital university was established in the city.
