Islamic Cooperation Youth Forum: ICYF announced its ICYF Internship online internship program. Through this program, candidates will gain knowledge, skills and experience in various fields. They will have the skills to apply the theoretical knowledge they have acquired in the university in real life. At the end of the program, certificates for CV portfolios will be presented to fully participating and active candidates:

Program benefits:

  • Opportunity to learn about the structure of ICYF;
  • Develop political knowledge at the international level;
  • Acquire business, communication and management skills;
  • Increase self-confidence growth;

Note: The program will be held in 2 forms: Offline (ICYF Head Office: Istanbul World Trade Center, A3 Block 7th floor, Yeşilköy/Istanbul) and online; The program will take place between 01.10.2021-31.02.2021;

Requirements for candidates:

  • Age 18-35;
  • Being in higher education;
  • The level of English must be excellent and knowledge of Arabic and French languages is preferred;
  • knowledge of MS office programs;

Important: Deadline: 19.09.2021. Only candidates who passed the 2nd stage will be interviewed;
