According to the decision, from January 1, 2025:

  • compensation is provided to cover the part of the interest rate that exceeds the main rate of the Central Bank, but not more than 1.5 times, for loans of up to 20 billion soums, allocated in the national currency at market interest rates, from the own funds of commercial banks for the establishment of the activities of non-state general secondary education organizations;

  • until January 1, 2030, legal entities will be exempted from the profit tax on expenses related to the construction of non-state general secondary education buildings at their own expense. the right to deduct in equal shares during the calculation for 2 years.

Also, for the establishment of new non-governmental general secondary education organizations and the construction of buildings, every year by local governments:

  • acceptable land plots are determined by March 1;
  • by April 1, a list of addresses with a total area of not less than 50 hectares of land in the republic is submitted to the government for approval
  • Land plots based on the address list approved by May 1 will be put up for "E-auction" auctions on the basis of ownership or lease.

In this case, land plots that have not been sold in auctions for more than 3 months will be put up for repeated auctions using the mechanism of step-by-step reduction of the starting price up to 50%. — Official Data
