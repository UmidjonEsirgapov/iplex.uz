From the next academic year, the training of bachelor pedagogues will be transferred to a full state grant. This was announced by President Mirziyoyev at a meeting with the people of Syrdarya.

"We will develop universities in the field of pedagogy based on a special approach.

From the next academic year, we will transfer the training of bachelor pedagogues to a full state grant. In the master's degree, the quotas will be doubled and will be fully funded by the state.

Schools with a shortage of staff and poor quality of education will be selected in advance, and a targeted state order will be introduced for them. In this regard, we will set up an attractive incentive system for young people who have completed their studies to go and work in these schools," said the head of state.
