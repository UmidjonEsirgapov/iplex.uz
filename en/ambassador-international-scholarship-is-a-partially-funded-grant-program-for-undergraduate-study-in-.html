Grant Description:
AIU Ambassador International Scholarship Awards opportunity for international students to study in Japan. Scholarships allow for undergraduate studies in all subjects taught at Akita International University. Application deadline - November 4, 2021.

Successful students will be awarded 50,000 JPY through the Akita International University Ambassador International Awards Program.

How to Apply:
How to Apply: Applicants undergraduate application form should be downloaded and submitted to the admissions office, Akita International University Yuwa, Akita City 010-1292 Japan-88-TEL-59-1831 ugrad@aiu.ac.jp.

Required documents:

  • statement of purpose (information about why they are applying for this particular grant);
