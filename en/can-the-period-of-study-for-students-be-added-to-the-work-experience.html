Can the period of study for students be added to the work experience?

Which study periods are included in the work experience?

One of the questions that interest many students is is the study period included in the work experience? Republic of Uzbekistan According to paragraph "e" of Article 37 of the Law "On State Pension Provision of Citizens", full-time education for those who received it, certain stages of education are added to the work experience.

Which periods of study are included in the work experience?

The following types of education are counted as work activities in the pension calculation:

  • Education at higher education institutions
  • Master's degree and clinical residency
  • Internship-researcher institute
  • Senior researcher-researcher institute
  • Basic doctoral studies
  • Doctoral studies
  • Periods of study abroad

For whom is the study period added to the internship?

Only full-time students have the opportunity to count these years as work experience at the time of retirement.

Why is this important?

Adding the period of study to work experience provides the opportunity to have more work experience and increase the pension amount when reaching retirement age.
