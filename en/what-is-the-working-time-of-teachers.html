What is the working time of teachers?

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
If the weekly lesson load of a high school teacher is set at 20 hours, according to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 275 of December 21, 2005, the weekly workload of the teacher is increased by a factor of 0.8 for each teaching hour. as a result of multiplication, it is set to 36 hours by adding 16 hours.
According to Article 115 of the Labor Code, the normal working time for an employee cannot be more than 40 hours per week.
Working hours for teachers should not exceed 6 hours in upper grades and 7.2 hours in elementary grades.

Working time distribution

  • If a teacher works with students for 900 minutes or 15 (astronomical) hours, the remaining 21 hours should be spent on other tasks specified in the workload and assignments given by the school management.
  • According to Article 115 of the Labor Code, the normal working time for an employee cannot exceed 40 hours per week.
  • For teachers, every 6-day work week the duration of daily work should not exceed 7 hours, and in a 5-day work week should not exceed 8 hours.

Legal procedures

  • An employee cannot be used for more than 8 hours, this is considered forced labor, and the guilty persons will be held accountable based on the relevant articles of the code of administrative responsibility.
  • With the consent of the employer due to the increase in the volume of work or important assignments. can involve him in work even during overtime. In this case, double wages should be paid for overtime hours. This time should not exceed 120 hours per year.

Special conditions

  • According to Article 118 of the Labor Code, for work related to a high level of excitement, mental stress, nervous tension, i.e. for work with a special tone, for certain categories of employees (medical workers, pedagogues, etc.), the working time period is 36 hours per week.
  • The list of such employees and their working hours are determined by the Government of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Advice

  • Heads of schools, follow the rules of reduced working hours.
  • Working hours for teachers should not exceed 6 hours in upper grades and 7.2 hours in elementary grades.
