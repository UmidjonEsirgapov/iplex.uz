About the university and the grant program:

Lingnan University, founded in 1967, was the first in Hong Kong to establish a service-learning group that gave students the opportunity to volunteer and share their knowledge and experience with charities and communities. Lingnan University ranks #571-580 in the ranking of international universities published by QS.

The grant program offered by the university is being offered for Lingnan University 2021-2022 academic year academic programs to enable students to study in Hong Kong for a bachelor's degree program.

Who is the grant program for?

  • All citizens of the country, including local Hong Kong residents, can apply for the program;
  • To apply, students must first be enrolled in the university's bachelor's degree programs. must be admitted to one.

Application process:

  1. First, candidates must register online HERE and apply to one of the undergraduate programs of their choice;
  2. No separate application is required for the grant program. All students admitted to the bachelor's program are automatically eligible for scholarship;
  3. The university selects the students who will be offered the grant based on the academic status of the candidates.

Required documents:

  • Basic documents required for candidates:
    • High school, college or high school diploma;
    • Copy of passport;
    • Resume;
    • Language certificate - from English language proficiency certificates - IELTS (Academic) 6.5 or higher, TOEFL at least 550 (paper-based test) or 79 (Internet-based test).

Benefits of the grant:

Local students of Lingnan University for HK$110,000 (approx. US$14,168) and a full tuition fee grant of HK$175,000 (approx. US$22,540) for non-local students and an additional scholarship of HK$50,000 (approx. US$6,440).
