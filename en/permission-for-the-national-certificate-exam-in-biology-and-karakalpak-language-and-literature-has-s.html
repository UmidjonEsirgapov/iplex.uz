The issuing of permits for the national certificate exam in biology and Karakalpak language and literature, which will be held on November 19, 2023 (Sunday), has begun.

Applicants participating in the test can obtain the permit from the "National Certificate" section of the my.uzbmb.uz site.

The permit indicates when and where the applicants will take part in the test, and applicants are requested to arrive before the specified time.

Applicants must arrive at the premises where the test will be held from 08:30 on the basis of an identity document (passport or ID card) (test at 10:00) will be entered.

An additional exam will not be held for applicants who are late or absent from the test. — Official Data
