Application to lyceums will last from June 1 to June 30 - draft decision

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The draft of the Cabinet of Ministers decision on "Improving the procedure for admission, transfer, restoration and expulsion of students to academic lyceums" was published on the portal for discussion of regulatory and legal documents projects.
Photo: Kun.uz According to the regulation to be approved by the decision: registration for admission of applicants to academic lyceums is carried out free of charge from June 1 to June 30 every year through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal or the official website of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation; the applicant is prohibited from making changes and additions to the information provided about him after June 30. Also: exams (initial written exam and entrance tests) will be held in the language of education at the academic lyceum (Uzbek, Russian, Karakalpak language); materials (assignments) of the exam (preliminary written exam and entrance test) are prepared based on the programs of general education subjects (grades 5-9); applicants whose preliminary written exam results are satisfactory or higher are allowed to participate in entrance tests conducted by the Knowledge Assessment Agency; the points obtained by the applicants in the preliminary written exam will not be taken into account when announcing the results of the competition.
