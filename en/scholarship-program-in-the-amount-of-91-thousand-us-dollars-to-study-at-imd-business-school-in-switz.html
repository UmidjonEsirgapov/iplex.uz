General information:

IMD business school, located in Switzerland, which is considered one of the most prestigious universities in Europe, has announced a fully funded grant program for all international students to study at the master's level. This grant is valid for students admitted to the master's degree in Business Administration. You can apply for this grant program at any time of the year, as it is accepted 4 times a year (by February 1; by May 1; by July 1; by September 1;).

Requirements for candidates:

  • Citizens of any state can apply for this grant;
  • Must have completed a bachelor's degree;
  • Must have at least 3 years of work experience. (Full-time);
  • 2 letters of recommendation;
  • GMAT\GRE minimum 630 but average 680 is acceptable;
  • TOEFL minimum 105+ or IELTS minimum 7.5 is acceptable;
