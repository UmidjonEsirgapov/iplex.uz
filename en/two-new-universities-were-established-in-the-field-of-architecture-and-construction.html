The Presidential Decree on measures to further improve the personnel training system in the field of architecture and construction (PQ-416, 08.11.2022) was adopted.

According to the decision:

- Tashkent Institute of Architecture and Construction will be reorganized as Tashkent University of Architecture and Construction, specializing in engineering, architecture, construction and construction materials technologies.

- Samarkand State Institute of Architecture and Construction named after Mirzo Ulugbek Samarkand State University of Architecture and Construction named after Mirzo Ulugbek will be reorganized as a university and will be specialized in restoration of cultural heritage objects, design, architecture, construction, urbanization and engineering.

Educational processes in universities will be conducted in the state, English and other foreign languages based on the credit-module system, and a Higher Science School will be established to train scientific and scientific-pedagogical personnel.

From January 1, 2023, universities will have academic, organizational and financial independence.

The position of the first vice-rector for strategic development will be introduced in the structure of universities.

Until March 1, 2023, bachelor's degrees and master's specialties will be approved according to international education and professional classifications. — Source
