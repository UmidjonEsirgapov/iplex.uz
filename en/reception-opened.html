Registration for applicants has begun - apply through my.uzbmb.uz submit

The process of online registration of applicants for the 2025-2026 academic year began on June 5. Now all applicants can apply to participate in the tests through the official platform my.uzbmb.uz until June 25.

Registration dates and process

Applicants can register to participate in the tests from June 5 to June 25 (including June 25). During the application process, only the following information is selected:

  • The complex of subjects for which the test will be taken

  • The language of education

  • The region of the test

At the same time, if the applicant's entrance exams include a professional (creative) exam in that case, he will have to choose the HEI where the creative exam will be taken.

News on taking the professional (creative) exam

One of the important aspects is that applicants can take the creative exam in any region. For example, if someone wants to enter the University of Journalism and Mass Communication of Uzbekistan, he can take the creative exam in Andijan, Bukhara or other regions. This creates regional convenience for applicants.

Teaching area: Special attention to Sokh and Denov

Another new possibility is that Sokh and Denov districts can be selected as a separate test area. Sokh people don't have to go to Ferghana, and Denov people don't have to go to Termiz. This saves travel expenses and time.

Payment procedure: For those who graduated in previous years

If the applicant graduated from school in previous years, he must pay 187,500 soums to participate in the test. This payment must be made before June 25. It is also possible to pay in installments, but if it is late, it will not be included in the test.

Possibility to change the application

Applicants who change their mind can resubmit the application until June 25. This process is important for changing test subjects, language of instruction, or test area. No changes will be made after June 25.

Exam Dates

Entrance exams will be held in July-August. Specific dates will be announced separately. It is important to properly plan the time to prepare for the exam.

Conclusion

The process of registering applicants for the new academic year has begun. Use the opportunities wisely: choose a test site near your area, take the professional exam at any HEI, update the application on time and do not delay payment.
