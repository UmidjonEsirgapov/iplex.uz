Foreign language teachers without a B2 certificate were given the opportunity until the end of the 2024/2025 academic year

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Teachers in schools who do not have a B2 certificate were given an opportunity until the end of the academic year.
What about those who are entering a new job? In accordance with the decision of the Ministry of Innovation and Preschool and School Education (29.05.2024, No. 3515), the list of national or international certificates that must have B2 level was published. What about new hires?

According to the letter signed by the first deputy minister of preschool and school education Usman Sharifkhojayev, the requirement for working foreign language teachers to have at least a level B2 national or international certificate has been extended until the 2025/2026 school year (in this regard, the Ministers

For this reason, employment contracts concluded with foreign language teachers working in schools that do not have a B2 level certificate before the 2024/2025 academic year will not be canceled. Regional administrations will monitor whether they receive the specified certificates by the end of the 2024/2025 academic year.

According to the law, new employees are required to have level B2 certificates. Personnel without certificates will not be hired.

Until the 2024/2025 academic year all foreign language teachers must have B2 level national or international certificates according to the decision of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and Preschool and School Education (29.05.2024, #3515) list has been announced.

Important! The following certificates have been removed from the list compared to the decision approved in 2021:

  • APTIS for teachers
  • Linguaskill
  • Test of English for International Communication (TOEIC)

If teachers before the decision comes into force (until May 29, 2024) who have received these certificates, benefits and allowances will be applied until their expiration date (no more than three years for unlimited certificates).

The following certificates are used to determine qualifications and skills, not the level of knowledge of foreign languages, and therefore are not included in the list:

  • Teaching Knowledge Test (TKT)
  • Teaching English for Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL)
  • Teaching English as a Foreign Language (TEFL)
  • Teaching English as a Second Language (TESL)
  • Certificate in English Language Teaching to Adults (CELTA) (DELTA)

Note! After May 29, 2024, only the certificates specified in the new decision will be valid. Privileges and discounts are not applied to certificates obtained remotely (Remote, Home Edition and other types) without visiting test centers officially registered by authorized international organizations providing services in the field of education. — Read on infoedu.uz
