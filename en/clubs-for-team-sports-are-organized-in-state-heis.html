Brief content:

In 2024-2027, sports clubs that do not have the status of a legal entity for team sports will be established in all state higher education institutions. It is defined in the draft decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan (PF-37 of 21.02.2024) posted on the discussion portal. According to it: maintenance of sports clubs of higher education institutions, providing them with sports equipment and equipment is carried out at the expense of the funds of higher education institutions from teaching on a fee-contract basis; the extra-budgetary income and expenditure estimates of higher education institutions provide for the costs of maintaining sports clubs; The "Student League" will be held between sports clubs of higher education institutions.
