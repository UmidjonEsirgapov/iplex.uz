Important news for students who received higher education in a foreign country: on September 5, 2024, next tests on nostrification will be held. These tests are an important step in the process of recognition of higher education documents obtained abroad.

The list of candidates participating in the test, detailed information about the subjects to be tested and the test region can be found in the pdf file above. This information can be found on the my.gov.uz site or through the State Service Centers through the application number submitted during registration.

For the convenience of the applicants, the tests will be organized in 13 regions of the republic, in places close to the permanent residence of the applicants. This simplifies the process of participating in the exams and saves the time of the applicants.
