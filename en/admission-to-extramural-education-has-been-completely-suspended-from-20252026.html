Admission to extramural education has been completely suspended from 2025/2026

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Higher education institutions will continue teaching through full-time, distance or optimized (part-time) forms of education.
Part-time admissions are closed only for new students. It should not exceed 50%.

According to the decree of the President, from the 2025/2026 academic year, part-time study in the higher education system of Uzbekistan has been completely suspended. This decision applies equally to all state and non-state universities.

What will change now?

  • Part-time education is not accepted in any direction. This form of education is completely closed.

  • External education quotas will not be allocated for new applicants.

  • Higher education institutions will continue teaching through full-time, distance or approved (part-time) forms of education.

  • All changes will come into effect from the 2025/2026 academic year.

Who will continue their studies?

  • Students currently studying in part-time education (those at the upper level) will continue their studies as before.

  • Only for new students correspondence admission is closed.
