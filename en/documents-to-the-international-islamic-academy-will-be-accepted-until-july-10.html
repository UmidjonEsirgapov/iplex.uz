Documents to the International Islamic Academy will be accepted until July 10

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
"According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On measures to further improve the procedure for admission to the International Islamic Academy of Uzbekistan", the procedure for passing the preliminary examination for applicants who have applied for admission to the bachelor's degree program of the academy is current.
Therefore, applicants applying to the International Islamic Academy of Uzbekistan can register their documents in the prescribed manner through the official website of the academy until July 10, 2020.
You can be informed about the complete and correct submission of documents the next day through the official website," says Bahadir, head of the academy's press service. Jovliyev.

Documents from applicants to the International Islamic Academy of Uzbekistan will be accepted until July 10, 2020. Those who pass the preliminary test in the form of an intellectual test are allowed to register through DTM. This is reported by the press service of the academy.

"According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On measures to further improve the procedure for admission to the International Islamic Academy of Uzbekistan", the procedure for passing the preliminary examination has been introduced for applicants who have applied for admission to the bachelor's degree program of the academy. Based on the results of the preliminary examination, an appropriate decision is made on whether to transfer the applicants to the next stages of the entrance exams (professional (creative) exams and tests) or reject them. Applicants who apply to the Academy can register their documents in the prescribed manner through the official website of the Academy until July 10, 2020.

In this process, the applicant registers by uploading the documents specified in this link in PDF format or high-quality photos and uploading them to the site using a computer or mobile communication device while at home. must be issued according to the passport. If a document with a name, surname, date or other name is uploaded incorrectly, the computer system will automatically reject all information. You can be informed about the complete and correct submission of the documents the next day through the official website," says the head of the academy's press service, Bahadir Jovliyev. preliminary test in the form of an intellectual test will be held on July 11-13, 2020. These tests will be simplified as much as possible and will be organized online.

"At the time of registration, applicants must enter their mobile phone number in the designated place of the questionnaire. Because the initial exam time, internet link and personal LOGIN and PASSWORD for entering the exam will be sent to this mobile number via SMS. You can find out about the procedure and assessment criteria for the intellectual potential test on the academy's website and official channel on Telegram.

Applicants who successfully passed the preliminary exam

On July 15-20, 2020, the Unified Interactive State Services Portal my.gov.uz, its regional departments or the State Test Center Through the website, in addition to the Academy, students can choose 4 other higher educational institutions with the same set of subjects and the same sequence of tests, and submit documents in the prescribed manner," says Bahadir Jovliyev.
