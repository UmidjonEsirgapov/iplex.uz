According to the latest news of 2024, comprehensive reforms are being implemented in the system of higher engineering educational institutions (HEIs) of the Republic of Uzbekistan. Within the framework of these reforms, it is planned to gradually expand the existing 36 engineering universities and their branches in the country, and a total of 20 technical universities will be established. This innovation aims to ensure the existence of at least 1 technical university in each region of Uzbekistan.

Reforms in the Engineering Higher Education System

The main goal of the reforms is to develop the level of education in the field of technology and engineering in Uzbekistan in accordance with international standards. In this regard, the following main changes will be implemented:

  1. Enlarging HEIs: 36 engineering universities and their branches will be enlarged throughout the country, and a total of 20 technical universities will be established. This approach is aimed at increasing the efficiency of universities and improving the quality of education.

  2. At least 1 Technical University in Each Region: At least one technical university operates in each region of Uzbekistan. It creates an opportunity for local students to get quality education and supports regional development.

International Cooperation

Along with the process of expansion, each technical university should establish cooperation with prestigious technical universities of developed partner countries. This gives the opportunity to introduce students to modern technologies and international experience. Cooperation with the following countries is planned:

  • Germany: Has strong experience in innovative technologies and engineering education.
  • Japan: World leader in the field of robotics and automation.
  • China: Famous for comprehensive technological and technical development.
  • Russia: Strong in fundamental science and technology education. has a position.
  • Italy: A leader in design and engineering.
  • Turkey: One of the leading countries in engineering education and industrial development.
  • South Korea: Recognized worldwide in IT and electronics.
  • Singapore: Famous for technological and educational achievements.

Through these partnerships, it is aimed to create an opportunity for students of Uzbekistan's technical universities to receive education at international standards and accelerate the technological development of the country. — Official Data
