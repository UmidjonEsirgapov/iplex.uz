22 sanatorium-type boarding schools in the republic will be converted into general education schools

Brief content:
22 sanatorium-type boarding schools in the republic will be converted into general education schools. According to the sources of "National Education Info", 22 sanatorium-type specialized boarding schools have been converted into general education schools.
National Social Protection Agency, the Ministry of Preschool and School Education and the Ministry of Health conducted an inventory of the schools.

according to the sources of "Halq Talim Info", 22 sanatorium-type specialized boarding schools have been turned into general education schools.

Schools by the Working Group consisting of experts from the National Agency for Social Protection, Ministry of Preschool and School Education and Ministry of Health inventory conducted.

According to the results of the investigation, a number of deficiencies were found during the inventory of 22 sanatorium-type specialized boarding schools. Therefore, it is proposed to turn these specialized boarding schools into general education schools.

This includes:

  • organizing 166 "extended day groups" in each general education institution for more than 4,000 children and maintaining free meals for children of low-income families, orphans and temporary children living with their relatives;
  • Organization of inclusive classes and attracting 42 children who are required to be taught in special conditions to these inclusive classes;
  • Filling the tutors allocated to these classes (assistant pedagogical staff) at the expense of the vacancies due to the transformation of sanatorium-type specialized schools into general education schools;
  • Proposals to continue work in inclusive classes of general secondary schools operating in the republic, including those close to the child's area of permanent residence.
