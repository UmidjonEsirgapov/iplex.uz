Uzbekistan prepares specialists for German railways

Agreement on training specialists for German railways between the Ministry of Poverty Reduction and Employment and the German company "DB Engineering Consulting Operations Group" signed.

Deutsche Bahn is the main railway operator of Germany and is a joint-stock company with 100% state participation. The total working capital of the company is 76 billion 303 million euros.

Now, our compatriots who want to work in the transport and engineering fields in Germany will be trained in the language and profession by German experts in vocational training institutions under the Poverty Reduction and Employment and Ministry of Transport based on the standards of this state.

"We are in great need of specialists for the German railway transport, bus, metro system, and we are ready to train them in Uzbekistan based on German standards and employ candidates who have successfully passed professional and language exams in the Deutsche Bahn network," said Nico Varbanoff. — Details here
