School graduates will be able to work in a kindergarten

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the government's decision, starting from the 2024/2025 school year in the Republic of Karakalpakstan, the city of Tashkent and one in each region, pedagogical classes will be organized on a pilot basis for the systematic preparation of 10-11th grade students for the profession of pedagogue.
On the basis of advanced foreign experiences, a separate curriculum for pedagogical classes will be developed.
Graduates who have completed pedagogical classes will be given a certificate and a state model certificate entitling them to work as a teacher's assistant in preschool educational organizations at the 2nd level of the National Qualifications Framework.

A separate curriculum for pedagogical classes will be developed based on advanced foreign experiences.
