Hilola Umarova was born on August 9, 1988 in Weimer, Germany. On December 30, 2022, he was appointed to the position of Minister of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan. His activities and reforms in the field of education are aimed at improving the quality of the education system.

Work activities

The field of education and diplomacy:

  • He graduated from the University of World Economy and Diplomacy with a bachelor's degree in 2010, and a master's degree in 2013.
  • From a leading specialist to the position of vice-rector at Inha University.
  • Worked as president, director of the agency for the development of creativity and specialized schools.

Pedagogy and management:

  • Worked as a director at the IT school named after Muhammad al-Khorazmi.
  • Worked as a coordinator and mentor in the "Technovation Girls" program.

In the position of minister reforms (2022-2023 years)

  • Electronic certificate: From the 2022-2023 school year, certificates of school graduates are issued in electronic form.
  • Vazir platform: The vazir.uzedu.uz platform has been launched to work with teachers' appeals.
  • Informatics lesson: In the 1st grades, the subject "Informatics and information technologies" was introduced.
  • Appointment of directors: Appointment to the position of directors is carried out based on the recommendation of the "Council of Directors" on the basis of an open competition.
  • Management training courses: Applicants who wish to become directors are required to have a management certificate.

News in the education system

  • Pedagogical certification: A professional certification procedure has been introduced for new teachers working in schools.
  • International qualification certificate: 70% additional salary will be paid to pedagogues with a qualification certificate.
  • Summative evaluation: The new evaluation system will be gradually implemented in all schools. is being increased.
  • Foreign experts: Foreign experts were recruited to teach foreign languages.
  • PISA and TALIS studies: It was decided to conduct PISA and TALIS studies in 83 educational institutions and 210 institutions in the republic.

Results of school development

  • Elective subjects were introduced in 135 schools.
