Not being able to manage your time properly affects productivity. The best tool against this is time management using mobile apps. Below we provide information on TOP-7 mobile applications that help from daily planning to concentration.

The best applications for time management:

  1. Todoist A convenient platform for managing tasks and daily planning. You can create lists, set reminders, and set priorities.

  2. Trello visual project management application for teamwork. Work processes can be controlled using cards and whiteboards.

  3. Forest An application that forces you to use your phone less. It works on the principle of virtual tree planting, putting the phone aside to concentrate.

  4. RescueTime Tracks time spent on computers and mobile devices. Helps Analyze performance and identify excess distractions.

  5. Google Calendar A simple yet powerful calendar app. One of the most convenient tools for Meetings, Reminders and Scheduling.

  6. Notion The universal platform to manage your personal and professional life. Allows you to manage your database, notes, tasks and schedules in one place.

  7. Focus Booster A time management application based on the Pomodoro technique. Increases productivity through cycles of 25 minutes of work and 5 minutes of rest.

Why are these apps useful?

  • Clearly set a daily plan

  • Reduce distractions

  • Optimize and control time

  • Improve work and study efficiency

These apps can radically change your time management habits. — Source
