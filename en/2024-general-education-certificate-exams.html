National Certificate Examinations in General Education 2024: Registration and Dates

In October, November and December 2024 Don't miss the chance to get national certificate in general education subjects! These exams allow you to assess and confirm your knowledge of subjects at an official level.

Registration process

Applicants can register online at my.gov.uz. Exams are held in an area convenient for them, and students are given the opportunity to take tests in this area. The registration period, subjects and test dates are clearly indicated in the table.

Subjects and test dates

  • In October: Mathematics, physics, history, geography.
  • In November: Mother tongue and literature, Russian language and literature, Karakalpak language and literature.
  • In November-December: Mathematics, physics, history, geography.
  • In December: Mother tongue and literature, Russian language and literature, Karakalpak language and literature.

The amount of payment for each test is 506,250 soums, which is 90% of the amount of one and a half basic calculations.

Changes in chemistry and biology

Attention! The national certificate exams in Chemistry and Biology are planned to be held in 2025 in a new format. The approval process for these subjects will be held in September-October 2024. — Source
