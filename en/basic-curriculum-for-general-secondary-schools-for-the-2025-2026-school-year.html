For foreign language main changes

lesson hours for 7th grade

In the new curriculum, foreign language lesson hours in 7th grade have been increased. Now in the 7th grade, foreign language is set to 4 hours a week. Previously, this indicator was 3 hours.

Lesson hours in 10th grade

In return, foreign language lesson hours in 10th grade were reduced from 3 hours to 2 hours. Lesson hours in the remaining classes have remained as before.

Conclusion

The basic curriculum for the 2025-2026 school year was developed to organize the school education process more efficiently. Changes in foreign language lessons are aimed at strengthening students' language skills.
