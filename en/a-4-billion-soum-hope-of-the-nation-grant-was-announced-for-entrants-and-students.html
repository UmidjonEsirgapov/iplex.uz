A 4 billion soum "Hope of the Nation" grant was announced for entrants and students

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The winners of the contest will be awarded with a certificate of the winner of the "Millat Umidi Granti" competition and a special grant of Millat Umidi University.
All documents will be submitted by the candidates online through the platform from June 8 to June 15.
Active participation in the final stage The next 30-40 candidates whose results are recognized as high will receive a special 50% educational grant of Millat Umidi University (this grant provides for the payment of the student's half-year academic year contract) and a certificate of the winner of the "Millat Umidi Grant" competition.

Millat Umidi University IDP IELTS, Cambridge International College, Result School and In cooperation with ISystem, announces the "Millat Umidi Grant" competition, the total amount of which is 4 billion soums for applicants at the level of the Republic.

The organization and conduct of the competition is carried out by a special commission composed of international class professors and specialists of Millat Umidi University.

For information, Millat Umidi University is a higher education institution in Uzbekistan that provides education based on the Pearson BTEC international program of Great Britain. All information about the university can be obtained from the website millatumidi.uz. The winners of the competition will be awarded with a certificate of the winner of the "Millat Umidi Grant" competition and a special grant of Millat Umidi University. Also, special QR-code certificates will be given to candidates who participated in the competition. And the winning students will be able to gain experience based on interviews at partner enterprises.

Contest organizers: Millat Umidi University, OLIYGOH.UZ team, IDP IELTS, Cambridge International College, Result School and ISystem. Quota for:
  • The number of grant winners is set for more than 100 students this academic year.

Registration deadline:

  • June 7, 2024, 10:00 p.m.

How to register will be held?

Applicants who wish to participate in the "Millat Umidi Grant" competition should register through the special Telegram bot of the competition. Through this address, the participants provide their personal information, and through this, a special "Personal cabinet" of candidates is created on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.

Participants of the competition should pay attention to the fact that their personal information is filled in correctly and accurately during registration.

Stages of the competition

The "Millat Umidi Grant" competition consists of 3 stages.

The first stage

Test-exam participating in the competition Candidates take an online exam on the national level through the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.

Exam type: 50 test questions on different levels of IQ and English.

Exam language: Uzbek and English

Duration: 1st stage of the competition June 6 starts at 18:00 and ends on June 7 at 23:59. During this period of time, all candidates can answer the questions of the test exam for 60 minutes in the exam window of the first stage through a private office. Please note - Participant who started the test of the 1st stage after 23:00 on June 6 will be given a total of 23:59 remaining time to complete the test.

After the completion of the 1st stage, the general results mandate will be formed and 5000 (five thousand) participants who have obtained the highest result will go to the 2nd stage. will be held.

The second stage

This stage is the stage of submitting documents, and the candidates participating in the grant competition have created on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform "Personal "cabinet" in the prescribed manner.

Candidates selected from the exams present the following documents online through a special platform window:

  • Identity document (passport or ID);
  • International exam certificates (if available: IELTS, SAT);
  • Achievements in various contests, competitions, Olympiads and projects;
  • Motivational letter (The subject of the motivational letter: "why I deserve Millat Umidi scholarship?" A special motivational letter written about why the candidates consider themselves worthy of winning the "Hope of the Nation Grant" competition and the goals they have set for them. The candidates' independent opinion, the consistency and fluency of the ideas expressed in the letter, as well as the uniqueness of the conclusion are of particular importance. The motivational letter is submitted in Uzbek or English (a letter written in English increases the reader's chances).

All documents are submitted online by the candidates from June 8 to June 15. The participants will be given online and offline webinars by University professors and teachers on writing letters in English.

After the completion of the 2nd stage, the mandate of the overall results will be formed, and 2000 (two thousand) participants who will get the highest result will get a ticket to the final (3rd stage).

Third stage

This stage is the most important stage of the "Hope of the Nation Grant" competition, and at this stage the participants pass a special written exam.

Time and address: stage 3 of the competition 23-30 June days among selected candidates will be held in a special examination building of Nation Umidi University .

The exam will consist of 2 parts:

  • An exam to determine the level of the English language (Listening, Reading, Writing);
  • Mathematics and logical analysis test.

The English language test will have a total of 100 points, so:

  • Listening – 30 questions, Part 1, Part 2, Part 3 – maximum 30 points;
  • Reading – 30 questions, Passage 1, Passage 2 – maximum 30 points;
  • Writing – Essay, 250 word - maximum score 40 points.

Mathematics + Logical-analytical exam:

Mathematics and Logical-analytical exam consists of 60 points in total, so it consists of 30 questions in total. 2 points are given for each question answer.

  • 60% Mathematical questions;
  • 40% logical-analytical questions.

After the date of the final stage, within 14 days the exam results of the participants will be checked transparently by a special commission and the results will be announced. The candidates deemed the most worthy will be announced as absolute winners of the competition.

Encouragement of the competition winners

The winners of the competition will be solemnly awarded in the special event hall of Millat Umidi University.

Young people who took part in the contest and took prizes are encouraged as follows:

  • The 20-30 candidates with the highest results National Umidi University's 100% special education grant (This grant provides for the payment of the student's 1-year academic year contract ) and the winner's certificate of the "Millat Umidi Granti" contest. a special 50% educational grant (This grant provides for the payment of the student's half-year academic year contract) and the winner's certificate of the "Hope of the Nation Grant" contest. grants are planned.

Special QR-code certificates will be given online to all candidates who participated in the competition. Certificates will be sent to the participants through "Personal Cabinet" on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.
