Teachers who fail to pass certification: Will the employment contract be terminated?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
If the teacher fails to pass the certification for the second time, then the employment contract is terminated.
This termination is carried out in accordance with Article 100, Clause 2 of the Labor Code.
If the teacher receives a low score on any of the evaluation criteria, the decision "did not pass the certification" is made.

Will the teacher be fired if he fails to pass the certification?

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 392 of May 13, 2019, the procedure for passing the certification of teaching staff is established. According to this regulation, if the teacher does not get enough points, the decision "Did not pass the certification" will be issued to him. However, in this case, the teacher will not be fired immediately.

What will happen to teachers who fail to pass the certification?

If the teacher fails to pass the certification, he will be sent to additional professional development courses and will be involved in re-certification the following year. If the teacher does not pass the certification for the second time, the employment contract will be terminated. This cancellation is carried out in accordance with Clause 2 of Article 100 of the Labor Code.

Evaluation criteria

Evaluation is carried out according to each criterion during the attestation. If a teacher receives a low score on any of the evaluation criteria, the decision "failed certification" is made. An average is not released.

Summary

If you fail the certification, you will not be fired immediately. You will be sent to extraordinary training courses and re-certification next year. If you fail a second time, the employment contract may be terminated. — Source
