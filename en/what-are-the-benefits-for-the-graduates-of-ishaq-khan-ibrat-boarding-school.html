What are the benefits for the graduates of Ishaq Khan Ibrat boarding school?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the Resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 698 of September 5, 2017, a boarding school specialized in foreign languages named after Ishaq Khan Ibrat was established in Torakorgon district of Namangan region.
According to the Regulation approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 393 dated June 20, 2017, on the recommendation of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the graduates of the boarding school specialized in foreign languages named after Ishaqkhan Ibrat in Torakorgan District, Namangan Region. basically, by the decision of the State Commission, they are admitted to study in the bachelor's degree courses of foreign language and literature, philology and language teaching of higher education institutions without competitive tests and without professional (creative) exams on the basis of additional state grants.
