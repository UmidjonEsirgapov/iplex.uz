Procedure of awarding points to applicants based on national certificate

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 646 dated October 12, 2020 and decision No. 17 of January 10, 2024, the procedure for awarding additional points to applicants based on the national certificate is established. This procedure serves to make the process of admission to higher education more transparent and fair, taking into account the level of knowledge of the applicants.

Regarding certificates issued before March 1, 2024

üìå National certificate obtained before March 1, 2024 (within the validity period of the certificate) will be awarded in the following order:

  • 86 points and above: the applicant will be given the maximum score set for this subject in the tests.

  • between 60-85 points: the applicant will be given proportional (percentage) points compared to the maximum score.

  • Compulsory subjects: the applicant with a national certificate will be given the maximum score from the relevant compulsory subjects.

Regarding certificates issued after March 1, 2024

üìå For national certificates issued starting from March 1, 2024, the following procedure applies:

  • A+, A level: the applicant is given the maximum score in this subject in the tests.

  • B+, B, C+, C levels: the applicant is given a proportional score compared to the maximum score.

  • Compulsory subjects: for those who have a national certificate, the maximum score is given in compulsory subjects.

Conclusion

With the national certificate, the applicants will have the opportunity to prove their knowledge of general education subjects and get the maximum or proportional score in the tests. This system creates an additional incentive for applicants and simplifies the process of admission to higher education. ‚Äî Source
