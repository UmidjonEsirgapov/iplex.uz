Check National Certificate (Plural Level)

To confirm the national certificate, use the services of the Knowledge Assessment Agency of Uzbekistan through their verification page. Or scan the QR code below the certificate. If you upload the certificate to the app.uzbmb.uz or app.dtm.uz sites, you will ensure its authenticity.

Checking the APTIS Certificate

Confirmation of the APTIS certificate can be done in two ways:

a) send an e-mail to the address info@britishcouncil.uz, indicate the full name of the certificate holder, the date of receipt of the certificate and the special code provided in the official letter. b) If the APTIS certificates are issued by the ASSESSMENT CENTER, you can confirm it through the official verification page.

Preparation for Checking the LINGUASKILL Certificate

Before checking the LINGUASKILL certificate, recognition must be obtained by male examination centers in Uzbekistan. The results of organizations registered with codes UZ050, UZ026, UZ004, and UZ059 are considered reliable. To verify, enter the information from the certificate on the verification page.
