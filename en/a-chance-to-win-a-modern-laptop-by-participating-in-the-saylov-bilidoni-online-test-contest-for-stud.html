A chance to win a modern laptop by participating in the "Saylov Bilidoni" online test contest for students and young people

The Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education has launched the online test competition "Election Knower" for students and youth.

The online competition is divided into 3 stages. will take place:

  • 1st stage: October 9-11;
  • 2nd stage: October 13-15;
  • 3rd stage: will be held on October 19.

For whom: All students studying in republican higher education institutions can participate in the contest.

Prizes:

  • 1 laptop for 1st place winner;
  • 2 2nd place winners a tablet;
  • 3 3rd place winners will be awarded with smartphone gifts.

Registration: Students participating in the contest Log in to http://sb.bimm.uz and register using their JSHIR (personal identification number).
