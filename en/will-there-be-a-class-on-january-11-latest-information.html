Tashkent

From January 11 in general education schools in the capital, educational processes will begin in stages, depending on their conditions and readiness to work.

According to the mayor's office, which announced this at 22:47, the school management will decide when classes will start in agreement with the parents' council.

These two paragraphs of the mayor's statement contradict each other: it was said that classes will start on January 11, but it is not clear in which schools they will start. not included - how can parents decide to start classes on January 11, after all, January 10 is almost over.

Surkhondarya

At first, at 9:41 p.m., it was announced that classes would start tomorrow, that is, on January 11 in the traditional way in Surkhondarya region.

Time At 10:32 p.m., an additional message was given: classes will not start in schools in mountainous, remote areas of the region.

The list of schools located in mountainous, remote areas will be formed by district administrations, and the start date of traditional classes in these schools will be announced.

Khorazm

Khorazm schools also have classes. It will start from January 11.

The regional administration, which informed about this at 12:10, explained this decision with the forecast of "Uzgidromet". According to him, the air temperature in the region is not expected to drop sharply on January 11-18.

The regional governor's office announced this at 12:34 on January 10.

In the evening, regional governor Shuhrat Abdurahmanov held a meeting on this issue. It was reported that the officials will be busy with improving the conditions of schools in the evening. Abdurahmanov himself is in the 10th school in Andijan city on the night of January 11.

Bukhara

The 3rd quarter starts on January 11 in all secondary schools in Bukhara region. This was announced on January 10 at 5:04 p.m. by the Regional Ministry of Education and Culture.

Samarkand

From a total of 1,255 general education schools in Samarkand region, classes will begin on January 11 in 950 schools.

Due to the difficulty of traveling by transport, the distance, and the limited possibility of providing the necessary heat when the temperature drops sharply, 97 classes in the school will start from January 13, and in 208 schools from January 18. It was announced that classes will start from January 11 in all 13 higher education institutions of the region. 71 out of 73 secondary special vocational education centers will start their educational processes from January 11, 2 from January 13.

The Samarkand regional hokimity, which reported on the above at 9:23 p.m., has not yet clarified which schools will be opened.

Kashkadarya region

The regional hokimity On January 10 at 10:07 p.m., it was reported that the teaching process in the educational institutions of the region will start gradually from January 11. According to the official report, "gradually" refers to the weather conditions in the region and the conditions in the hostels of higher education institutions. In particular, the educational processes in the schools of mountainous and remote villages will start from January 16. Education in special boarding schools for the disabled under the management of the Department of Public Education will begin on January 18.

In specialized schools and boarding schools under the Presidential Educational Institutions Agency, education will begin on January 16.

Holiday in all kindergartens in the region will last until January 17, and they will start their activities on January 18.

Karakalpakstan Republic

Classes in schools in Karakalpakstan will start on Thursday, January 12. There are no exceptions for now.

This was reported by the Ministry of Public Education of the republic at 18:21.
