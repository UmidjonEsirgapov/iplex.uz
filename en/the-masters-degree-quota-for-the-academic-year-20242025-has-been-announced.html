The master's degree quota for the academic year 2024/2025 has been announced

In the 2024/2025 academic year, the distribution of parameters for admission to master's degrees in republican higher education institutions by majors and languages of instruction has been approved. This quota creates great opportunities for the applicants.

Total quota

Total 17,500 seats are available.

On the basis of government grant

9,365 seats are allotted on the basis of government grant. Of these:

  • 8,092 places for the Uzbek language,
  • 1,273 places for the Russian language.

On the basis of the contract

On the basis of the contract, 8,135 places have been allocated. Among them:

  • 6,539 places in the Uzbek language,
  • 1,596 places in the Russian language.

Distribution by languages of instruction

Detailed information about the places and specialties allocated for education on the basis of a state grant and contract in the Uzbek and Russian languages.
