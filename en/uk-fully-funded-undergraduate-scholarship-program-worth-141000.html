About the university and the grant program:

University of Strathclyde is one of the best public universities in Glasgow. The university aims to expand its reach outside the university territory to make the world more educated, prosperous, healthy, fair and safe.

The main goal of the grant program allocated by the university is to provide financial support to international and national students.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must first be admitted to one of the following undergraduate courses at the University:
    • Biomedical Engineering,
    • Prosthetics,
    • Orthotics.
  • Candidates must study full-time according to the accepted program;

Application process:

  • First, students must apply for one of the above bachelor programs through the university portal HERE and complete the application process. Admitted students are also automatically considered for grant opportunities.

Required documents:

  • Diploma and its annex, educational certificates received in previous education;
  • Letters of recommendation;
  • Copy of your passport;
  • Copy of visa (upon receipt).

Entry requirement:

Students must have at least an upper-second class honors degree in a relevant physical or biological science, science, mathematics or engineering.

English language requirement:

Students must have a minimum 6.5 score in the IELTS Academic test and no part Not less than 5.5.

Benefits of the grant:

The University will provide £100,000 (approximately US$141,000) towards tuition fees, living expenses, tuition and equipment over four years to selected undergraduate students.
