Uzbekistan still uses old techniques in education - foreign expert

Expert from the United Arab Emirates Ahmed al-Bastaki expressed his opinion on the development of the education system of Uzbekistan. In an interview with the website Kun.uz, the head of the UAE national schools organization spoke about the aspects that should be paid attention to in this regard.

"The state system is using old technologies, it is necessary to adapt them to the times. Educational programs should be updated," al-Bastaki said.

According to his opinion, Uzbekistan still uses old techniques in education. In this process, it is necessary to update the curriculum.

"Because the world has advanced a lot. For example, you need to think about artificial intelligence. Because the young generation needs to know more about the future. We need to find answers to the questions of what they will do and where they will work in 10 years," said the representative of the UAE in an interview with journalists. — Read on infoedu.uz
