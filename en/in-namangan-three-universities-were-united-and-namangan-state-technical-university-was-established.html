In Namangan, three universities were united and Namangan State Technical University was established

From the 2024/2025 academic year, Namangan State Technical University graduates will be awarded a diploma.

Namangan Engineering-Technology Institute, Namangan Engineering-Construction Institute and Namangan Textile Industry Institute are being merged to form Namangan State Technical University. This decision was adopted in order to bring the training of highly qualified personnel in the field of engineering and technology to a new level.

The main tasks of the new university

  • Training of highly qualified specialists in the fields of engineering, construction, textile, information technology, programming, production and service.
  • Training of personnel suitable for the needs of the region and development of scientific and pedagogical activities.
  • Advanced engineering to organize a school without the status of a legal entity and start its activities.

New opportunities for students

  • Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year:
    • Graduation qualification theses and master's dissertations are prepared based on the problems of the employing enterprises.
    • Graduation theses and dissertations are defended in enterprises.
  • Until the academic year 2026/2027:
    • At least two bachelor's courses and three master's specialties will be internationally accredited.
  • From 2025:
    • Large industrial enterprises will be attached to the University as industrial partners.

About students and graduates

All students studying at NamMT, NamMQI and NamTSI continue their studies at the University. Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, graduates will be awarded a Namangan State Technical University diploma. — Read on infoedu.uz
