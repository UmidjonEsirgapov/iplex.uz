Applications (notifications) for admission to higher military educational institutions will be accepted from February 1 to March 31 (also on this day). The following documents are submitted to the defense department by the candidates who expressed their desire to study:

  • application (notice);
  • certificate of registration or military ID of the conscript;
  • biography written by hand or on the computer by the candidate;
  • questionnaire;
  • from the place of study, work or service (if (if he is studying, working or serving) a description and reference;
  • a letter of consent from his parents to enroll in OHTM (for minors);
  • 6 photos of 3x4 cm size (without headgear and corners) and 4 photos of 4.5x6cm size (without headgear);
  • Privileges for admission to OHTM documents confirming the right to use (original and copy).

The following documents are requested by the departments of defense affairs independently from the relevant state bodies and organizations, including through the "Electronic Government" system interdepartmental integration platform:

  • a copy of an identity document;
  • a copy of a birth certificate;
  • information (general secondary, secondary special, primary professional, secondary professional, secondary special professional) documents (original and copy) and a copy of the rating book (grade sheet).

When submitting documents, candidates must show the registration certificate or military ID of the conscript in person.
