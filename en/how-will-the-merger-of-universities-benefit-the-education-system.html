What will be the result of the reorganization of HEIs?

In recent days, big changes have been made in the higher education system of Uzbekistan. A number of decisions were made on the establishment of new universities and the reorganization of existing HEIs. Among them:

  • Cyber ​​Security University was established.
  • International University of Turkish States is planned to be opened.
  • Universities in Fergana, Namangan, Nukus and Bukhara are being merged. This serves to improve the quality of the higher education system and optimize the management system. What are the benefits of university mergers? This will reduce the number of rectors, vice-rectors and other administrative positions, and it will be possible to direct funds to the quality of education. Large universities will have the opportunity to engage in more scientific and research work.

    ✔ Resources will be distributed effectively.Large universities will have the opportunity to fully use modern laboratories, information resource centers, educational technologies. This creates more scientific and practical opportunities for students and professors.

    ✔ The quality of education increases.In many developed countries of the world, large universities are distinguished by high scientific potential. The unification of Uzbekistan's universities is also expected to increase the quality of education and opportunities for students.

    What changes may occur in the education system in the future?

    If the number of universities is reduced and transformed into large universities, this will help make the educational system of Uzbekistan competitive in the global arena. Large HEIs:

    • More support for scientific projects,
    • Creating wide opportunities for students,
    • Expanding international cooperation,
    • Can increase the competitiveness of graduates in the labor market.
