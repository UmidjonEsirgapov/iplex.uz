"Red Bull Basement" international competition for students

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
It is not necessary to be an entrepreneur or an inventor to participate in the competition - all students who have the citizenship of Uzbekistan can participate.
The video must be uploaded to https://basement.redbull.com.
The team should consist of one or two people. possible.

"Red Bull Basement" is a major international event of "Red Bull" company, its national partner is IT Park. Within the framework of the competition, young students - participants must present interesting, creative and, most importantly, technological ideas that can change the world for the better.

The goal - to unite students and encourage them to make constructive changes and have a positive impact on the world now and in the future.

You don't have to be an entrepreneur or inventor to participate in the competition - all students with Uzbek citizenship must participate. possible.

The most interesting point, will be the international final stage, where the finalist teams will present their ideas to the international jury. At all stages of the project, participants will have the opportunity to make useful contacts, receive expert advice and much more.

To become a participant in the competition:

1. You need to come up with an original idea and shoot an interesting 60-second video. Video must be in English or Russian with English subtitles. Video must be uploaded to https://basement.redbull.com. The quality of the video is not the main thing: the most important thing is your idea. A team can consist of one or two people. Team members must be students over 18 years old and be able to speak English fluently.

2. Eat votes that are only visible to the participant. Once applications close, the organizers will announce a 3-day voting process. Community feedback will be one of the criteria for future jury ideas, so you need to show that the world needs your idea and get the support of friends, family and acquaintances.

3. The best works will be accepted by the national jury. The jury will determine the national winner according to the following criteria: appropriateness of the idea, its level of influence, creativity and the number of public votes collected at the application stage.

4. Give shape to your idea. In particular, the finalists of the national stage will receive a workplace with experienced mentors in their chosen field, individual training, and also prepare for an international meeting.

Each idea will be considered - you can propose ways to solve acute problems that affect the life of the student campus or help improve the life of the student community throughout the country. A technological solution can be both the basis of your idea and a part of it. The topics are only limited by your imagination, but you can start by thinking about the following topics: body and mind, careers, fighting water pollution, fighting climate change, education, people's rights and opportunities, energy sources, smart cities.

The winning team of the 2021 International Final will have the special resources needed to realize their idea.

More information in the link: https://basement.redbull.com/ru-ru — Source
