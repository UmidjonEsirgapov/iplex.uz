Tashkent is a city of bread and at the same time a city of students. You will see students hurrying at every step: to class, to work... It is not easy to earn money while studying. A student needs a lot of money for rent, travel expenses, food, etc. Thanks to the parents who sent their only child to a distant city to study. The state does not look at it, it gives a scholarship of 517,000 soums per month on average.

So, is it easy to study and live in Tashkent? Is the state scholarship enough? What are the student expenses? Khabar.uz questioned young people.

- How much money does a student spend in a month?

Most of the students we questioned said that their monthly expenses reach 1-1.5 million soums on average. Students from the capital city spend around 200-500 thousand soums per month.

- Do you think that the one-month stipend given to students by the state is enough to cover their expenses?
