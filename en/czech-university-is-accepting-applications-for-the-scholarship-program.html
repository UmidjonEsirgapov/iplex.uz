Charles University of the Czech Republic is accepting applications for the scholarship program.

Bachelor, master and PhD candidates from all over the world can apply.

Financial in the grant application it is necessary to state the need for assistance. The required application form is here.

The application should also include a 1,000-word letter of motivation and two letters of recommendation about the expected results of studying at Charles University.

All submitted documents must be written in English or accompanied by certified translations.

In addition to the documents submitted by applicants for the scholarship, see the scholarships the exit board takes into account the results of the candidates' previous studies.

Admitted students will be awarded a one-time scholarship of approximately 3,000 euros to support their living expenses.

The grant will also be reevaluated annually and students will be able to reapply for the following academic year.

Applications must be submitted by April 30 of this year via the link can be sent. The application fee is about 35 euros.
