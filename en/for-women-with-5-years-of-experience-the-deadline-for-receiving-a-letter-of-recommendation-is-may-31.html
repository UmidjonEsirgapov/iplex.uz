Preferential admission to higher education for women with 5 years of experience

The government of Uzbekistan is creating an opportunity to study in higher education for women with at least 5 years of experience and no higher education. They are admitted to state higher education institutions within special admission parameters, i.e. under contract based on 500 quota.

To use this privilege, applicants must have a recommendation letter from the Family and Women's Committee.

Terms and conditions for receiving a recommendation

  • Application deadline: May 31, 2025 (also on this day)

  • Application address: Applicants should apply to district (city) women's departments in the area where they are on the permanent or temporary list

Recommendation criteria:

To receive a recommendation, it is necessary to meet the following three criteria at the same time:

• Have a specialty in secondary special or professional education • Have not yet received higher education or are not currently studying • Have at least 5 years of work experience in their specialty

About letters of recommendation from previous years

  • Women who received letters of recommendation in 2023 and 2024, but did not get admission do not need to apply again

  • The period of letters of recommendation received in 2022 has expired, a new letter of recommendation must be obtained

  • For those who entered the study, the previous recommendation letter is considered burned

Submitted documents will be reviewed by working groups from June 1-7. After that, a decision is made to grant or reject the recommendation.

Note: The recommendation does not add to the exam scores, it only gives the right to participate in the preferential admission quota. — Official Data
