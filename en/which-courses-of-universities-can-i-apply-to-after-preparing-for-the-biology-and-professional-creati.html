Which courses of universities can I apply to after preparing for the biology and professional (creative) exams?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the statement No. 11 of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated December 19, 2019, from the 2020/2021 academic year, a test corresponding to the bachelor's degree programs of the republic's higher education institutions will be given. the set of subjects, the number of test tasks, and the evaluation criteria have been approved.
It contains the name of the subjects, the number of test tasks, evaluation criteria (points for each correct answer) and the maximum number of points.
Including biology and professional (creative) exams, applicants who are preparing for entrance exams to higher educational institutions can apply for the following educational areas: Physical education and sports management Higher Oliy nursing work Psychology (sport) Organization and management of sports events

With statement No. 11 of December 19, 2019 of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan, a set of subjects for which a test will be given in accordance with the baccalaureate educational directions of the republic's higher education institutions from the 2020/2021 academic year. the number of assignments and evaluation criteria were approved. It contains the name of the subjects, the number of test items, the evaluation criteria (points for each correct answer) and the maximum number of points.

Including biology and professional (creative) exams, applicants preparing for entrance exams to higher education institutions can apply for the following educational areas:

  • Physical education and sports management
  • Graduate Nursing
  • Psychology (Sports)
  • Organization and Management of Sports Events
