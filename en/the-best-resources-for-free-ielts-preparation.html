The best resources to prepare for the IELTS exam for free

The IELTS (International English Language Testing System) exam is one of the most popular and reliable language tests in the world. It is used to assess your level of international language proficiency in academic studies, work or immigration processes. The preparation process for this exam requires a serious approach, so choosing free and effective resources is very important. Below are the 10 best free resources for preparing for all sections of the IELTS exam.

1. Elc.polyu.edu.hk

Resource containing all basic information about IELTS exam Understand the format of the exam, provide tips and exercises for each section.

2. Splendid-speaking.com

A great resource for

Speaking! There are interviews with high-scoring IELTS students, helpful tips, and exercises based on real conversations.

3. Takeielts.britishcouncil.org

is

the official site of the British Council and provides the complete IELTS exam format, exam strategies, free practice tests and advice.

4. Ielts.allearsenglish.com

Site with comprehensive IELTS material and exercises. Independent preparation resources are available for each section.

5. Netlanguages.com

A site that provides free practice exercises to prepare for the IELTS exam. A useful platform for strengthening language skills.

6. Ieltsforfree.com

This site has free exercises and full tests for every section of the IELTS exam. Provides an opportunity to try the exam in real conditions.

7. Examenglish.com

A platform that allows you to prepare not only for IELTS, but also for other international exams such as TOEFL, TOEIC, CAE and FCE.

8. Itests.com

Allows registered users to take free IELTS tests and analyze the results.

9. Ielts-exam.net

One of the largest and most trusted free IELTS resources. Provides independent preparation with many test materials and exercises.

10. Scribd.com

provides the best free books for IELTS preparation to download or read online.

Conclusion

Choosing the right resources for IELTS preparation is important. With the 10 free resources above, you can master the Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking sections.
