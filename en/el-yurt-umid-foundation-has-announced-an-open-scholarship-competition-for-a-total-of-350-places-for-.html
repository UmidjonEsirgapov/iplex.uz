El-Yurt Umid Foundation has announced an open scholarship competition for a total of 350 places for the 2021-2022 academic year

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The purpose of the program: "El-yurt umidi" fund is the most priority areas for the Republic of Uzbekistan, including: Space technologies; Nanotechnologies, IT and digitization; Health care in the fields of artificial intelligence, atomic and solar energy and other directions of using renewable sources of energy; Pharmaceuticals; Telemedicine; Virology; Microbiology; Electrical power in immunology and other fields; Oil and gas; Petrochemicals; Chemistry; Rational use of natural resources in the areas of mechanical engineering, automobile, electrical engineering, textile and product standardization in the priority branches of metallurgy; Wide implementation of water-saving technologies; In the important sectors of the agricultural sector, such as horticulture, vegetable growing, cattle breeding, poultry farming; as well as encouraging deserving candidates with open scholarships for 350 places in other priority fields and specialties in our republic.
If you have a high intellectual potential, modern and creative thinking, and have set yourself the goal of bringing innovative ideas and technologies for the science of the country and developing it on a digital basis - this opportunity is for you!

Details of the grant program:

"El-yurt umidi" fund 2021-2022 in the most prestigious foreign countries:

- 100

 scholarships for the undergraduate education program,

- 150

 scholarships for the master's education program,

- 100 scholarships for the doctoral education program are planned. if you set yourself the goal of introducing technologies and developing it on a digital basis - this is your opportunity!

  • under master's programs - under 23 years old and over 40, as well as those who previously studied at a master's degree abroad in a similar specialty;
  • under doctoral programs - under 25 years old and over 45, as well as those who previously received a PhD or doctor of science degree;
  • training and internship programs - under 25 years old and over 50 years old, as well as those who have studied abroad for the last three years on the basis of one of the Foundation's programs;
  • those who have less than 2 years of work experience, including teaching experience, in the specialty chosen for study abroad;
  • work in elected bodies of state power those who are driving;
  • who have convictions for serious or very serious crimes not completed or those who have not had their convictions removed.

    • Admission process:

    Documents of candidates from July 20 to August 20, 2021 representatives of all regions of the republic based on a uniform procedure and equal opportunity, online and electronic admissions are accepted.

    The selection stages will last from May 1 - May 20, 2021.

    The fund will cover the following expenses of the scholarship holders:

    • - tuition fees;
    • - obtaining a visa;
    • - vehicle expenses. (both ways, at most once a year);
    • - living, food and transportation expenses in a foreign country;
    • - purchasing necessary educational and scientific literature;
    • - publishing an article at the request of a foreign educational or scientific institution;
    • - copying scientific articles;
    • - subscribing to an electronic database of articles and scientific literature;
    • - medical insurance;
    • - Other expenses according to the decision of the board of trustees of the fund.
    Candidates for education abroad submit the following documents:
    • reference-lens (questionnaire) of the appropriate sample;
    • indicate the form and direction of education an application in the name of the chairman of the commission in the prescribed form;
    • 2 photographs in the prescribed form;
    • a copy of diplomas confirming that he graduated from a higher education institution, as well as a scientific degree (candidate of science, PhD and doctor of science), scientific title (senior researcher, associate professor, professor);
    • copy of passport;
    • medical certificate of the prescribed format;
    • valid national or international certificate determining the level of knowledge of a foreign language (National qualification certificate, IELTS, TOEFL, etc.);
    • certificate of no criminal record.
