General Information:

One of the most prestigious universities in Germany, the University of Groningen has announced its fully funded Eric Bluemink Fund grant program for all students admitted to the University of Groningen. If you have been accepted and you meet the requirements of this grant, the university will inform you that you are a candidate for this grant.

  • Academic indicators should be high (undergraduate and school grades are also important) and letters of recommendation should be well written;
  • For this grant program, you are asked to be fluent in English;
  • Candidate should be healthy;
  • Must not receive financial support other than this grant or receive other grants;

    • Note: This grant is provided for 1-2 years for the master's degree.

    Benefits of the grant:

    • This grant covers all costs: contract money, international tickets are provided free of charge, costs for educational materials and books, medical insurance costs are all covered.

    Note: This grant is only available to a limited number of students due to high demand.
