There are various discounts on dormitory fees for students, which are determined by the Council of Higher Education Institutions. Students can be partially or completely exempted from the dormitory fee.

The following students are completely exempted from the dormitory fee:

  • Students with 1-2 groups of disabilities according to eye diseases;
  • Chinese orphans (both parents died) and orphans (one of the parents died);
  • Children of orphanages;
  • Students in need of social protection.

Also, the following students can get a 50 percent discount on the dormitory fee:

  • Students with group 2 disabilities according to general diseases;
  • For the chairman of the student council, the floor captain and the dormitory library who are performing their duties in an exemplary manner. responsible students.
