All universities are comprehensively studied

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
All higher education institutions of the republic will be comprehensively studied by the working group headed by the Minister of Higher and Secondary Special Education Abdugadir Tashkulov and the Head of the State Inspectorate for Quality Control of Education Ulugbek Toshkenboyev.
Year 2022 In order to ensure the implementation of the tasks given at the meeting held on January 28 under the leadership of President Sh. Mirziyoyev, higher education institutions will be critically and analytically studied on the basis of 23 criteria. criteria such as the degree to which the educational process is organized, distance education, credit-module system, the conditions created in student residences, effective mechanisms for combating corruption and ensuring transparency are introduced. Reforms in higher education, integration of science, science and production, activity of information resource centers, practical support to educational institutions granted financial and academic independence, scientific potential, the degree to which the educational process is organized, distance education, credit-module system, conditions created in student residences, implementation of effective mechanisms for combating corruption and ensuring transparency are focused on.

Goalto set the educational process of higher education institutions in the right direction, to improve the quality of education, to effectively organize scientific and innovative activities and increase their competitiveness, to raise the educational process to a new level in terms of quality through the full introduction of digital technologies. proposals are prepared.
