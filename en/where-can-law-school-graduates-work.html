Where can law school graduates work?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Graduates of the legal technical school are awarded a diploma of the state model on secondary special professional education, indicating the level of competence in the directions, professions and specialties of secondary special professional education in accordance with the state educational standards.
Graduated from the legal technical school The student can take part in the competition for vacant positions within the number of general staff units of the public authorities and organizations, including: the Ministry of Justice of the Republic of Uzbekistan and its regional structures, regional bodies of the Bureau of Compulsory Enforcement under the Prosecutor General's Office, notary offices, advocacy structures and similar offices, in accordance with the requirements of the qualification requirements.
