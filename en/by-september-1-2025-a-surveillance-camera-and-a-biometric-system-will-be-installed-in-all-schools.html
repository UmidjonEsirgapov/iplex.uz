By September 1, 2025, a surveillance camera and a biometric system will be installed in all schools

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
In order to ensure the safety of the territory of general secondary education institutions of Uzbekistan and to constantly monitor the attendance of students, the "Safe School" system is being introduced. Funding sources related to the installation of surveillance cameras and biometric identification systems in secondary educational institutions will be determined. As the first stage of this project, it is planned to introduce the Safe School system in at least 130 schools by January 1, 2024. "Safe school" system is being introduced in order to provide and constantly monitor student attendance. This measure will be implemented within the framework of the national program for the development of public education in 2022-2026. Initially, funding sources will be identified for the installation of surveillance cameras and biometric identification systems in general secondary education institutions. As the first stage of this project, it is planned to introduce the Safe School system in at least 130 schools by January 1, 2024.
