Lesson distribution rules and mistakes of primary school teachers

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Lesson distribution of primary school teachers In many schools, the following mistake was made in the distribution of lessons. and they gave the leadership of the class to another teacher and divided the lessons among themselves.
However, in paragraph 23 of the joint decision of the Ministry of Justice registered with the number 3271 on June 30, 2020, the basic subjects in primary classes; it is said that reading, mother tongue, mathematics and class management should be given to only one teacher, and these subjects, class management should be given to only one primary school teacher. That is, in the primary classes, the mother tongue and reading literacy lesson was given to one teacher, the mathematics lesson to another teacher, and the class management to another teacher, and the lessons were divided among themselves. in paragraph 23 of the joint decision registered with basic subjects in primary grades; It is said that reading, mother tongue, mathematics and class management should be given to only one teacher and these subjects, class management should be given to only one primary school teacher. In addition, for elementary school students, different teachers can have a negative impact on the learning and learning process of students. However, it is also necessary to take into account the education of students and the fact that one day there will be an inspection.

Paragraph 27 of the above joint decision No. 3271 contains legal responsibility for those who are guilty of violating the requirements of the statute. — Read on infoedu.uz
