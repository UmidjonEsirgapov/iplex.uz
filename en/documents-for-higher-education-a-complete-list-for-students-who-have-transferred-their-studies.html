What documents should a student who passed the transfer exam submit to the university?

The process of submitting documents to the university begins for students who successfully passed the transfer exam. A contact letter is issued by the higher education institution, which clearly indicates what documents should be brought from the student. Below you can familiarize yourself with the main documents.

Documents that the student must submit to the university:

  1. The original copy of the diploma or certificate.
  2. The original copy of the transcript or rating book.
  3. Academic reference.
  4. According to admission to the 1st year extract from the order.
  5. Extract from the order about transfer from course to course.
  6. Extract from the order about exclusion from the ranks of students.
  7. University accreditation documents (certificate or license).

These documents are ready for each student after that, it is required to submit to the university. This process is an important step to successfully continue your studies. — Read on infoedu.uz
