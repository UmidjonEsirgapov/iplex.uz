Students included in the youth register will be paid the contract money of up to 16.5 million soums

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to paragraph 35 of the Cabinet of Ministers Resolution No. 312 (7.06.2022) and Appendix 8 of this regulation, the following categories: Child of a family included in the Iron Register; Children of women included in the women's register; A child of a family enrolled in the Unified Register of Social Protection, who is studying at a professional or higher education institution; A student brought up in an orphanage or children's town; Low-income student-student deprived of parental care, placed under guardianship; A child of a low-income family with at least one of the family members having a group I or both group II disabilities, who is studying at a professional or higher education institution; A student with a disability or health impairment; It is intended to be paid to a student whose family is low-income.
Educating in professional educational organizations in our Republic, as well as in branches of state, non-state higher education organizations and foreign higher education organizations operating in the Republic of Uzbekistan: full-time, part-time and evening bachelor's degrees; A part of the annual expenses for studying on the basis of a fee-contract will be paid to those who study full-time master's degree.
Payment of these expenses is carried out according to the scheme in the amount of up to 50% of the fee-contract, but not exceeding 50 times of the BHM (16,500,000 soums) based on the positive recommendation of the youth leader.
