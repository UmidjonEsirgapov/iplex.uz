Certificates giving preference to the English language have been announced

According to Decision No. 3515 (29.05.2024) of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the Ministry of Preschool and School Education, national and international recognition of the English language the list of received certificates has been approved.

According to this decision, the following certificates are considered to be preferred in undergraduate education:

  • National certificate – B2 level
  • IELTS – 5.5 and above
  • TOEFL IBT – 72 and above
  • FCE (First Certificate in English) – 160 points and above

Applicants with these certificates are exempt from the English language entrance exam.
