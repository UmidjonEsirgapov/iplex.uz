How many Uzbeks have received a national certificate so far?

As of September 1, 2024, "Determining the level of knowledge of general education subjects" on the my.gov.uz portal a total of 57,174 applications were received. 37,116 of those who applied managed to get a national certificate.

The most applied subjects:

  1. Uzbek language and literature: 17,316 applications
  2. Chemistry: 13,446 applications
  3. Mathematics: 8 272 applications

Fergana region is the leader in obtaining certificates by region (8,034 applications, 5,216 certificates), followed by Tashkent (6,667 applications, 3,996 certificates) and Bukhara region (5,725 applications, 3,916 certificates).

The general results show the high interest in this assessment system and certificate applicants are exempted from the relevant subjects from entrance exams to higher education institutions. — Official Data
