From 1940 to today, the number of higher education institutions has increased significantly. This shows the attention and potential of the country in the field of education.

Figures and facts:

  • 1940/1941 academic year: There are only 30 higher educational institutions in Uzbekistan, where 19,061 students studied. The war period had a negative impact on the education system, and the number of students decreased significantly.

  • 1991 (the eve of independence): The number of higher education institutions reached 41. During this period:

    1. 3 universities,
    2. 15 pedagogical institutes,
    3. 7 technical institutes,
    4. 5 medical institutes,
    5. 3 economic institutes,
    6. 4 agricultural institutes,
    7. 3 art institutes,
    8. 1 sports institute operated.

  • Year 2002: The number of higher education institutions reached 61, and 207,200 students studied in them.

  • 2023/2024 academic year: The number of higher education institutions in Uzbekistan reached 213. Of these:

    • 116 are state higher education institutions,
    • 67 are non-state institutions,
    • 30 are foreign higher education institutions.
