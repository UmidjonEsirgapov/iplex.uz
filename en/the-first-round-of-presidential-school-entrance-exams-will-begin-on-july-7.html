The first round of presidential school entrance exams will begin on July 7

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Information about the time and location of the exam will be sent to the phone numbers entered by the applicant on the ariza.piima.uz site via SMS. 60 minutes will be given to complete the tasks.

Information about the time and location of the exam will be notified by SMS to the phone numbers entered by the applicant on the ariza.piima.uz site.

The first round of entrance exams for presidential schools 30 tasks (in the form of a test) will be held in mathematics. Applicants will be given 60 minutes to complete the tasks.

Note: Applicants will not be given additional time to mark the answer sheet of the entrance exam apart from the specified time (60 minutes) for completing the tasks.

For information: the exams of the city of Tashkent and the Tashkent region will be held in Tashkent.
