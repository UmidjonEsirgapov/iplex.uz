A big change: now the grant is given for one year only

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Although some applicants have high scores, depending on the quota number of the higher education institution they applied for, they may not be admitted.
According to the new regulation, an applicant who has scored high scores within the framework of a state grant allocated to a specific field will be admitted to the higher education institution of his choice. Regardless, it is recommended for a state grant.
That is, young people who get high scores will not be denied admission.
