When a student expelled from an educational institution?

Students can be expelled from a higher education institution for various reasons. These cases are clearly defined in the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

On the initiative of the student

  • He writes an application and leaves according to his own will.

  • In connection with the transfer of studies to another higher education institution.

For reasons related to health

  • If, based on the conclusion of the medical commission, his health prevents him from studying.

Violation of academic discipline and absenteeism

  • Violation of academic discipline and non-compliance with internal procedures.

  • Absence of more than 74 hours of classes in one semester without an excuse.

Failure to fulfill the terms of payment

  • Failure to make the specified payment on time by students studying on a fee-contract basis.

Legal circumstances

  • Deprivation of liberty of the student by court decision.

  • Death of the student.

Right to academic leave

In some cases, a student has the right to take academic leave without being expelled. Academic leave may be granted for the following reasons:

Pregnancy and childbirth

  • Due to pregnancy, childbirth or child care.

Military service or health recovery

  • Completion of military service.

  • Time to recover health. if necessary.

Academic leave is granted based on the procedure approved by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

Academic indebtedness and dropping out of the course

Students who fail to master subjects are considered academically indebted. In such a case:

  • The student is removed from the course by order of the rector of the higher education institution.

  • He can continue his studies from the semester of the lower course on the basis of a payment contract.

Conclusion

There are clear legal reasons for the expulsion of the student from the higher education institution. These cases can be voluntary, disciplinary or financial reasons. Academic debt is also an important factor. Every student should take these conditions seriously. — Read on infoedu.uz
