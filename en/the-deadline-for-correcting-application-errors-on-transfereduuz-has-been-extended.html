The deadline for correcting application errors on Transfer.edu.uz has been extended

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
For the 2024/2025 academic year, citizens who made technical errors in the applications sent through the transfer.edu.uz platform during the process of transfer and restoration of inter-state higher education students' studies are given the opportunity to correct their mistakes.
This opportunity It is valid until 17:00 on August 15, 2024.
During this period, citizens can revise their applications, correct errors and replace documents with better quality copies.

In the process of transfer and restoration of inter-state student studies for the 2024/2025 school year. Citizens who made technical mistakes in the applications sent through the transfer.edu.uz platform are given the opportunity to correct their mistakes. This opportunity is valid until 5:00 p.m. on August 15, 2024. During this period, citizens can revise their applications, correct errors, and replace documents with better copies. After August 15 at 17:00, the process of eliminating technical deficiencies will be stopped, and there will be no opportunity to submit new applications.

This opportunity will help students to timely correct technical errors that occurred during the application process and facilitate the process of transferring their studies for them. — Read on infoedu.uz
