The official website of the State Test Center has posted a list of recommended textbooks and educational literature for preparing for the entrance exams to higher education institutions, and it does not include textbooks created on the basis of the newly introduced curriculum for the 6th, 7th and 10th grades by the Ministry of Public Education in the 2022-2023 academic year.

The state testing center explains that each test must be in accordance with the state educational standards and science programs in the curriculum. According to the principles of testology, the test tasks used in qualifying exams cannot be formed on the basis of curricula not mastered by the examinees.
