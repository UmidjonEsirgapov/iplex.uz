Can teachers teach two subjects?

According to the President's national program for the development of public education in 2022-2026, starting from the 2022/2023 school year, teachers will have the opportunity to teach two subjects. This procedure was introduced by providing additional specialization for closely related fields of study.

Which fields can teach two subjects?

In the following 4 bachelor's fields, the possibility of providing teachers with basic and additional specialization (qualification) has been introduced:

  1. Mathematics and informatics - additional informatics subject as a teacher.
  2. Pedagogy — as a teacher of supplementary education.
  3. Biology — as a teacher of additional natural sciences.
  4. Special pedagogy: sign language pedagogy — as an additional sign language interpreter.
