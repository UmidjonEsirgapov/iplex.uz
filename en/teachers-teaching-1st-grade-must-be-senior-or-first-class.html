The requirements set for teachers who teach 1st grade students in the new school year are as follows:

  1. Teachers who teach 1st grade must be senior or first class. This requirement is important to provide quality education to students.
  2. Teacher's speech should be fluent, able to think creatively, and meet the requirements of the time. These qualities increase students' interest in lessons and help them to be successful in the educational process.
  3. A teacher taking the 1st grade should have at least 10 years of experience. Experienced teachers will quickly and effectively solve the problems that arise during the lesson.
  4. The 1st grade teacher should know by heart the terms related to mathematics and computer science given in the new generation textbooks in English. This is an important factor in increasing students' interest in modern sciences.
  5. The 1st grade teacher should have a one-year educational project and teach the 1st grade based on this project. The project-based educational method helps students to gain deeper knowledge.
  6. The 1st grade teacher must be a creative teacher who can work on a project and encourage his students to be creative. This will develop students' creative and independent thinking abilities.

These requirements are important for providing quality education in schools and for the comprehensive development of students. — Official Data
