General Information:

The IMF Youth Fellowship Program 2021: The IMF Youth Fellowship Program 2021 is one of the best virtual learning opportunities. The program is aimed at empowering young people who are in the most difficult situation affected by the pandemic. This fellowship program allows young participants to gain experience from the IMF's expert team.

Program benefits:

  • Participants receive a certificate of completion of the IMF Youth Fellowship Program;
  • Participants become part of the IMF's network of experts, scholars and leaders;
  • Participants have access to annual meetings, sessions and events
  • Candidates will participate in a two-day virtual seminar supervised by IMF experts.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Candidate must be 20-32 years old;
  • Candidate social activism, engagement with civil society organizations, entrepreneurship, participation in startups, content creators, vloggers, bloggers. should have experience as freelance media contributors or journalists;
  • Candidate should have knowledge in international studies, economics and development, climate change, inequality and digital economy;
  • Students and graduates can also apply, but they should have studied international relations, journalism, economics or related subjects;
  • Candidate should be fluent in English required;

Program registration process:

  • Candidates must apply online through the application form;
  • To proceed with the application, the candidate must enter all the required information;
  • Read carefully before submitting the application;

Registration deadline: September 24, 2021.
