As a result of the language certificate requirement, HEIs lost 206 billion 435 million soums in 3 years

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovations is conducting a regulatory impact assessment (RIA) of the decision PQ-5117 of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated May 19, 2021.
According to this decision, the Ministry completed TSTB report was published.
According to it, financial losses for higher education institutions due to the fact that the quotas allocated for master's degrees remained open as a result of the language certificate requirement amounted to a total of 206 billion 435 million soums in the period 2021-2023.
