International University of Turkic States will be established

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Decision of the President on the establishment of the International University of the Turkish States (PQ-24, 25.01.2025)
International cooperation: the university is established under the auspices of the Republican Council of Higher Education and the Council of Higher Education of Turkey
Financial conditions: Education at the University is organized on the basis of a fee-contract, admission parameters and the amount of the fee-contract are determined by the University. This university operates as a non-governmental higher education organization in order to train modern personnel in accordance with international standards.

Basic information

  • Educational areas: The university has higher education in engineering, construction, logistics, information technology and programming, healthcare, agriculture, food industry, economic and social spheres. specialists are trained.

  • International cooperation: The university is organized under the auspices of the Republican Council of Higher Education and the Council of Higher Education of Turkey.

  • Address: Tashkent city, Uchtepa district, Kichik Halka Motorway Street, 3 and 3a, located in the buildings and facilities of Tashkent State University of Economics and Tashkent Tax Technical College. Free use of the buildings is provided.

  • Financial conditions: Education at the university is organized on a fee-contract basis, the admission parameters and fee-contract amount are determined by the University.

The purpose of the university

International University of Turkish States is not only to train personnel with modern knowledge and skills, but also cultural, scientific and economic between Turkic countries. serves to strengthen relations.
