This year, the payment-contract sums will not be increased

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Fee-contract amounts will not be increased in 2025. According to the officials of the Ministry of Higher Education, fee-contract amounts will not be increased in higher education institutions this academic year.
From a legal point of view, how will it be?
Conclusion: In 2025, the fee-contract sums for students will remain unchanged.

The fee-contract sums will not be increased in 2025

According to the information given by the officials of the Ministry of Higher Education, the fee-contract sums will not be increased in the current academic year. So, students will continue their studies in the amounts approved last year.

What will happen from a legal point of view?

In the decision of the State Admissions Commission in 2024, there was a sentence: "Starting from the 2024-2025 academic year, the payment-contract amounts should be approved in the following order". Therefore, since the new amounts have not been approved, the amounts set last year will remain in effect.

Issue of universities with financial independence

Higher educational institutions granted financial independence have the right to increase the contract amounts or leave them as they are. But most likely, they will not increase the fee-contract amounts either (there may be only some exceptions).

Conclusion

In 2025, the fee-contract amounts for students will remain unchanged. This provides financial comfort for students and parents. Online contracts will be live soon and will reflect the exact amounts approved last year. — Official Data
