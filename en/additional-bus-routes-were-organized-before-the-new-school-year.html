Additional bus routes were organized before the new school year

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In connection with the beginning of the 2023-2024 academic year, additional bus services have been launched in the following directions for the timely arrival of students: Urganch-Tashkent, Khiva, Tashkent, Bukhara, Tashkent, Tashkent, Gijduvon, Tashkent, Karakol, Tashkent-Shofirkon. Additional bus services will be launched from Vobkent, Jondor and Olot districts until September 3, 2023.
Bus tickets can be purchased at the bus station in the city of Urganch, at the ticket booth at the bus station in Bukhara, or electronically (https://avtoticket.uz/).
