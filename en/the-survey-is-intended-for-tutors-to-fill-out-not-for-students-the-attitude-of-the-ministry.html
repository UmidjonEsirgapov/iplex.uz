"The survey is intended for tutors to fill out, not for students" - the attitude of the ministry

Brief content:
A few years ago, the letter sent by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation to the heads of HEIs caused objections.
Kun.uz contacted the press service of the ministry to get information about this questionnaire. According to the report, this questionnaire is called "Student's socio-psychological passport" and it is filled out by tutors and psychologists. "Are there emotional and behavioral disturbances?" "Is he aggressive in dealing with others?" The survey with such items caused many objections. Kun.uz received a comment on the situation from the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

A survey titled "Student's Social-Psychological Passport" was circulated on social networks. One of the student channels says that it is given to students to fill out. Questionnaire "Do you have emotional and behavioral disorders?" "Is he aggressive in dealing with others?"

Some time ago, the letter sent by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation to the heads of higher education institutions caused objections. the students were assigned the task of connecting the managers and employees of the ministry, higher education institutions, and organizing individual interviews with their parents. began to express an active opinion about the situation.

Kun.uz contacted the press service of the ministry to get information about this questionnaire.

According to the information provided by the press service, this questionnaire is called "Student's social-psychological passport" and it is filled out by tutors and psychologists. is considered The questions are not directed directly at the student, but are intended for a third party," answered Nazokat Abdukunduzova, head of the press service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

According to the information provided by the press service, by the decision of the president in 2017, a psychologist service was established in HEIs on a separate staff basis.

"Prevention of various unpleasant situations and tragedies related to suicide in higher education institutions. psychologists organize systematic work in order to assess the mental and psychological condition of students, to diagnose their problems and pains, and to establish the necessary psychodiagnostic, treatment-prophylactic work on this basis. By identifying the root causes of long-term depression, chronic depression, chronic low mood, difficulties in handling, personal and social psychological problems, it becomes important to turn the life, fate, and future of young people towards themselves, by eliminating them. — Read on infoedu.uz
