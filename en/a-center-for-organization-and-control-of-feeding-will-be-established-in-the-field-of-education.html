A center for organization and control of feeding will be established in the field of education

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to this decision, a number of changes will be made in order to improve the organization and management of healthy meals in state preschool educational institutions.
Implementation deadlines: the system of healthy feeding will be fully implemented in all state kindergartens by July 1, 2025.

On January 6, 2025, the Cabinet of Ministers adopted a decision "On measures to improve the order of healthy feeding in state preschool educational institutions." According to this decision, a number of changes will be introduced in order to improve the organization and management of healthy feeding in state preschool educational institutions.

Main news:

  1. Transition to the outsourcing system:

    • The system of feeding by the method of outsourcing will be introduced step by step in state preschool educational institutions.
    • With entrepreneurs. Contracts will be drawn up.

  2. Introduction of the "Uniform Seasonal Menu":

    • Management of food costs will be managed through the "Kindergarten" information system.
    • Educators will be fed according to the same seasonal menu.
      • "Educational catering organization and control center" will be established, its tasks will be:
        • Coordination of healthy catering;
        • Digitalization of processes;
        • Organization of catering on the basis of outsourcing through public procurement.

Implementation deadlines:

  • Until July 1, 2025, a healthy feeding system will be fully implemented in all state kindergartens.
  • Agreements will be signed with entrepreneurs on food delivery and service.
