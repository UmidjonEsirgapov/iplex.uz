Instead of "Fine art and drawing" subject "Art" can be passed

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
It is proposed to introduce the subject of "Art" instead of the subject of "Fine Art and Drawing" in schools of creativity in Uzbekistan.
President Shavkat Mirziyoyev learned about it yesterday, on March 28, with plans for the development and improvement of the quality of school education. it was announced in the presentation.
In the subject of "Art", not only painting, but painting, crafts, cinematography, sculpture and design skills are taught.

It is proposed to introduce the subject of "Art" instead of the subject of "Visual Art and Drawing" in creative schools in Uzbekistan.

About this yesterday, On March 28, President Shavkat Mirziyoyev met with President Shavkat Mirziyoyev at the presentation of plans for the development and improvement of the quality of school education. The task of conveying the heritage of philanthropy and theater to young people was set.
