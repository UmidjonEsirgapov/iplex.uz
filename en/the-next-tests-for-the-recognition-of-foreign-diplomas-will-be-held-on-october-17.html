The next tests on the recognition (nostrification) of documents about education in foreign countries will be held on October 17, 2024.

The list of applicants scheduled to take part in the test, the subjects that will take the test and the region of the test can be found by clicking on this link (my.gov.uz website or registration through State Service Centers through the application number submitted during the transition).

Applicants must come to the building where the exam will be held without delay with an identity document (passport or ID card).

Note: Applicants for various reasons can change the region of taking the test or the language of taking the test through the address https://t.me/NostrifikaBBA_bot (application number, first name, last name and initials citing) can apply.
