One private university license was revoked

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:

Decision of the Fergana Interdistrict Economic Court on July 17, 2023 by decision, the license No. 053546 issued to the limited liability company "VALLEY INNOVATIVE UNIVERSITY" on December 13, 2022 for the provision of non-state educational services was canceled.

The screening of private universities has begun. This is reported by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

By the decision of the Fergana Inter-district Economic Court dated July 17, 2023, the license No. 053546 issued to the limited liability company "VALLEY INNOVATIVE UNIVERSITY" on December 13, 2022 for the provision of non-state educational services is canceled.

It was found that he committed a number of gross violations in compliance with general and special license requirements and conditions in the field of non-state education. In particular, — Details here

  • there are no documents confirming that the buildings and facilities of this higher education organization designated for use specified in the license belong to the founders based on property rights or lease agreements;
  • The HEMIS information system of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation does not include correct and complete information about students and teaching staff;
  • the rector's advisor state unit dealing with youth issues and spiritual and educational work has not been introduced in agreement with the competent state body;
  • the bank account of the educational organization does not have 150,000 US dollars or its equivalent;
  • 60 electronic libraries equipped with computer equipment and 20 information resource centers equipped with computer equipment, scanners and copiers have not been established;
  • it was found that students are not provided with educational literature (textbooks, study guides) in each subject.
