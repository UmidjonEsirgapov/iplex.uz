Teachers' working hours at the school depend on their workload and this is clearly defined in the school's internal labor regulations. According to Article 174 of the Labor Code, the working order is determined by agreement with the trade union or the representative body of employees. Also, the working hours of teachers are determined based on their work rate and workload.

For example, teachers who work 1 rate (20 hours in upper grades, 18 hours in elementary grades) must work 36 hours per week. If a teacher teaches for 10 hours, he is not required to stay at school all day. The working time is determined based on the pedagogical load.
