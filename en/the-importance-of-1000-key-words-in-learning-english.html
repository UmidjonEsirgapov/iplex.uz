1000 Basic English Words: A Basic Step for Students

Know the 1000 Most Used Words in Learning English very important. These words are the foundation of language learning and allow students to strengthen their reading, speaking and translation skills.

Why are these words important?

The so-called "1000 Key Words" list is taken from The Reading Teacher's Book of Lists and these words offer the following benefits:

  • Most frequently used words: The words in the list make up 50-65% of English texts.
  • Accelerates language learning: Students who know these words will understand texts better and develop speaking skills.
  • Practical use: For use in conversations and written texts convenient.

List content

The list is divided into 10 parts, each containing 100 words. For example:

  • The first 100 words: words such as "the", "of", "and", "to", "in";
  • The second 100 words: "over", "new", "sound", "take", etc.;
  • In this way, all 1000 words are used in many practical situations.

How to use them?

  1. Every day, learn a few words from the list and try to use them in sentences.
  2. Read simple texts with these words and practice translating.
  3. Repeat the words over and over to improve your pronunciation and writing.
