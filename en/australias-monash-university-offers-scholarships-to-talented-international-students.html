Australia's Monash University offers scholarships to talented international students

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Monash University scholarship is open to international students, Masters and PhD students.
• Students studying at Monash University can also apply for the scholarship, but priority is given to students who are just entering Monash University.

General Information:

Location: Melbourne, Australia

Monash University in Australia offers scholarships to talented international students. Every year, 31 students can get the opportunity to study for free at Monash University. The Monash University scholarship is open to international students, masters and doctoral students.

Students receive a scholarship of $10,000 annually for full-time study (48 credits). The stipend is paid during the entire period of the student's studies in the program. If the student has above average (70%) and participates in at least 3 events dedicated to promoting the scholarship or the university, the scholarship will be extended to the following year. be.

• Must not be an Australian citizen.

• Students studying at Monash University can also apply for a scholarship, but priority is given to students who are just entering Monash University .

The student must be enrolled in one of the full-time undergraduate, graduate, or doctoral programs. Get your student's acceptance letter, then submit a separate scholarship application. You don't need to submit additional documents to study the scholarship, only an application is enough!

You can start studying at Monash University from 1 semester and 2 semesters.

Application deadline:

If the student wants to start studying in the first semester: Deadline: October 15, November 15, January 15.

If a student wants to start studying in the second semester: Date: March 15, April 15, May 15.
