Permission was granted to build a private kindergarten on vacant school grounds

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On additional measures to improve the quality and efficiency of preschool education" was adopted. it is allowed to start the activities of non-governmental preschool education organizations.
From the 2026-2027 academic year, the procedure for issuing electronic certificates to children who have graduated from preparatory groups for school will be introduced.
Also, the preschool education program will be improved based on international standards. "Decree on additional measures to increase" was adopted

According to the decree, starting from January 1, 2025, non-state pre-school education organizations will be allowed to operate on vacant lands of public general secondary education institutions on the basis of public-private partnership.

Starting from the 2026-2027 academic year, the procedure for issuing an electronic certificate to children who have completed preparatory school groups will be introduced. Also, the preschool education program will be improved based on international standards. — Official Data

The main measures to be implemented based on the decree:

  • Until August 1, 2025: the state program of preschool education will be improved based on international standards, and school preparation groups and primary education programs will be interconnected.
  • 2025 From January 1: to establish at least one primary preschool education organization in all districts and train their deputy directors as qualified trainers.
  • From March 1, 2025: mobile training services (4 buses) will be introduced for pedagogues in remote areas.
  • 2025-2026 academic year from: dual education will be launched in state higher education institutions in the field of preschool education
