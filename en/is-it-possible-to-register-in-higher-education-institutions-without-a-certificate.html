Is it possible to register in a higher education institution without a certificate?

Important news for graduates of this year

Among many applicants, "Can I register without a certificate?" the question arises. This is especially relevant for young people who have just finished the 11th grade, but have not yet received their certificate.

Is it possible to register before the certificate is issued?

✅ Yes, it is possible. If you are a graduate of a comprehensive school, academic lyceum or vocational school in 2025, you do not need a certificate. Because when registering through my.uzbmb.uz, the information about the educational institution is automatically integrated - that is, you are not required to upload documents.

Who can use this privilege?

  • 2025 graduates of general education schools

  • Vocational schools graduates

  • Current year graduates of academic lyceums

In what cases is a certificate needed?

This convenience is not available for applicants who graduated from school in previous years. They will have to enter certificate information manually during registration, upload a copy of the document if necessary.

Conclusion

If you are a graduate of the current year, you can easily register at a higher education institution without waiting for a certificate to be issued. The system automatically receives information about you from the information base of the ministries. This will save you time. — Official Data
