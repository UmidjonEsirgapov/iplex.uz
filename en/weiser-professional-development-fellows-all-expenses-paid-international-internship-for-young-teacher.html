General Information:

Weiser Professional Development Fellows: The Ronald and Eileen Weiser Center for young foreign teachers or researchers from the entire Eurasian region in the United States of America in cooperation with the University of Michigan. "Weiser Professional Development Fellows" announced the grant program. Program participants are invited to spend 4-6 weeks on short research training at the University of Michigan.

Citizens of the following countries may register for the program: Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Georgia, Kaliningrad (Russia), Kazakhstan, Kosovo, Kyrgyzstan, Moldova, North Macedonia, Serbia, Slovak Republic, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Ukraine and Uzbekistan.

Important: Selected candidates will be asked to carry out their own research or new projects in the field of education in cooperation with the University of Michigan.

Benefits of the program:

  • Travel expenses will be covered;
  • Accommodation will be provided;
  • Medical insurance expenses will be covered;

Required documents for the program:

  • CV;
  • PhD diploma;
  • Copy of passport;
  • Personal statement of no more than 2 pages ((a) about the research and project you plan to do at the University of Michigan b) How you plan to benefit your university through this internship program);
  • Michigan an offer letter from the university (a letter received via email is also accepted);
  • Recommendation letter issued by the university;
  • TOEFL language certificate (if available);

⏰ Deadline: September 15, 2021
