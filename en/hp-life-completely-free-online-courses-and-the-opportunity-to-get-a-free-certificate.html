About "HP LIFE" in brief: HP LIFE is a global learning program organized online and offline through Learning Equality's Kolibri platform.
HP LIFE is a global training program for the future of business startups or growth, workforce acquisition and better employment. is a completely free course for all people who want to learn and develop IT and business skills.

HP LIFE is a absolutely free course for all people around the world who want to start or grow their own business, acquire and better employ their workforce, learn and develop IT and business skills for the future. Another advantage is that the courses are conducted in seven world languages ​​(English is also available). HP LIFE is part of the HP Foundation program.

Registration for courses:

Registration for courses is very simple. There are no requirements for it, just good internet and a good communication tool to connect to it and that's it! You can join the courses by taking the following steps:

  1. Visit the official website from here and choose one of the available courses according to your interest;
  2. After choosing a course, you can find the available courses in that field;
  3. You can choose any course offered and join the course for free;
  4. Your chosen course register with your personal e-mail address and confirm it by the message received in your e-mail, and you can start the course right away.
