70 percent bonus: For whom and under what conditions?

Question: Will a grade below 86 be downgraded?

Answer: No, this is incorrect. Negative decisions will not be made against pedagogues who scored less than 86 points. A 70 percent bonus is given as an additional incentive only to pedagogues who have scored 86 points and above.

Question: Can pedagogues with a lifetime category participate in the certification?

Answer: Yes, pedagogues with a lifetime category can also participate in an extraordinary certification at their discretion.
