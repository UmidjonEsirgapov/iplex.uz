The school principal who forced teachers to vote was fined 34 million soums

The principal of the 18th school in the Romitan district of Bukhara province forced teachers to vote for the project under the "Initiative Budget" it was confirmed that he was involved in the gathering. This was reported by the National Commission for Combating Human Trafficking and Decent Work Issues.

In accordance with Part 2 of Article 51 of the Administrative Responsibility Code, an administrative fine in the amount of 100 times the BHM (34 million soums) was applied to the director.

It is known that in Romitan The teachers of the 18th school were forced to go door-to-door and collect votes for the improvement of the technical base of the school.

In the end, this project received 1654 votes and was not among the winners. The school lacked only 28 votes to win.

For information, in the "Initiative Budget" forcing subordinates to collect votes is classified as forced labor.

According to Article 5 of the Labor Code, forced labor is prohibited. According to Article 51 of the Labor Code, officials who commit forced labor will be fined 50-100 BHM. If the same act is committed repeatedly or against a minor, criminal liability is established according to Article 148-2 of the Criminal Code. — Official Data
