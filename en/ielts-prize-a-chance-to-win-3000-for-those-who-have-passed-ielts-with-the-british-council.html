The consulate announces an IELTS PRIZE grant worth 3000£ to help 7 lucky participants who have passed the IELTS exam recognized by the universities of the world with British Council to realize their dream of studying abroad.

For participants. requirements:

  • be over 18 years old
  • be a citizen of one of the 10 countries listed (Uzbekistan is on this list)
  • have passed the IELTS exam between 01.05.2020-15.09.2021 through the British Council
  • Achieving an IELTS score of 5.5 and above and being one of the examination requirements of the university receiving the certificate
  • Beginning studies at the university in the 2021-2022 academic year
  • Submit a letter of acceptance from the university by September 30, 2022
