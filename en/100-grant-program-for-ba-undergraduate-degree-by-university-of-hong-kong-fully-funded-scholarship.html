Requirements for candidates: IELTS score of at least 6.5 or TOEFL iBT 79 or equivalent international language certificate.
General Information. Kong-University has announced "Entrance Scholarship Scheme" grant programs in order to financially support foreign students who have high academic performance and have been admitted to this university. requirements:
  • Must have an IELTS score of at least 6.5 or TOEFL iBT 79 or an equivalent international language certificate.
  • Must have completed 12 years of education (primary and secondary/secondary special education) or have completed it by the time the scholarship begins.

interested parties: Entrance Scholarship Scheme program for students admitted to the university provides 3 types of grants to students:

  • Top Scholarship- this covers the contract fee, room and living expenses (approximately 23,000 USD);
  • Full Tuition Scholarship-Includes an annual contract fee (approximately 18,000 US dollars);
  • Half Tuition Scholarship-Includes a half-year contract fee (approximately 9,000 US dollars);

Important: Candidates do not apply for these grants separately. the grant program is given according to the student's preferences and academic performance. The academic performance of the student receiving this grant is required to be GPA 3.4 and higher. Also, these indicators should not fall in order to keep the grant for the next year. Admission to the City University of Hong Kong-university starts in the last week of September and continues until November 15th.
