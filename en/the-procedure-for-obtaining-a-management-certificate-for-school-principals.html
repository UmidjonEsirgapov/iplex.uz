Directors of secondary schools are required to work with a management certificate. According to it, directors must be certified according to the following conditions and procedures:

  1. Candidate requirements:

    • Must have at least 5 years of experience.
    • Must have a bachelor's degree.

  2. Application:

    • Candidates must apply every month. Applications will be submitted by the 5th.
    • Selected candidates will be trained in training courses.

  3. Payment:

    • 1 BHM (330 thousand soums) must be paid for the certificate.

  4. Examination:

    • To get a certificate, candidates must pass an exam consisting of 25 tests, cases and practical tasks.
    • The maximum score of the exam is 100 points.
    • Those who get at least 70 points can get a certificate.

Note: This system is scheduled to start on January 1, 2024.
