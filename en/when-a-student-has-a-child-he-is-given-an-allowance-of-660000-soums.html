When a student has a child, he is given an allowance of 660,000 soums

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
On the basis of the regulation "On the procedure for assigning and paying benefits under the state social insurance", the following rules are established: For working women, as well as in higher, secondary specialized, vocational educational institutions, graduate, postgraduate, clinical residency, doctoral studies the one-time allowance given to women studying when a child is born is paid from the appropriate place of work or study.
A student submits an application to the OTM no later than six months from the day of the child's birth, and it is mandatory to pay him the allowance set by the management no later than 10 days.
Currently, the amount of the allowance is twice the amount of the basic calculation (660,000 soums).

On the basis of the regulation "On the procedure for assigning and paying benefits under the state social insurance", the following provisions are established:

One-time allowance given to working women, as well as to women studying in higher, secondary special, vocational educational institutions, master's degree, post-graduate studies, clinical residency, doctoral studies, separated from production, upon the birth of a child. is paid from the place of work or study, respectively.

The student submits the application to the OTM no later than six months from the day of the child's birth, and it is mandatory to pay him the allowance determined by the management no later than 10 days later. Currently, the amount of the allowance is 2 times the amount of the basic calculation (660,000 soums).

To receive the allowance, the following documents must be submitted:

  • Application to the Rector of Higher Education Institution;
  • Copy of the passport;
  • Copy of the birth certificate.
