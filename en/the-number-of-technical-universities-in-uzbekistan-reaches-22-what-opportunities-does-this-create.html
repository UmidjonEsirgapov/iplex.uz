The number of technical universities in Uzbekistan reaches 22: What opportunities does this create?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Plans and goals: Increasing the scientific potential: in 2025, the scientific potential will be 55%

On January 22, President Shavkat Mirziyoyev got acquainted with a presentation on the priority tasks in the field of higher education and science. At this meeting, a number of plans for the development of the higher education system for 2025 were defined.

Plans and goals:

  1. Increasing the scientific potential:
    • Increasing the scientific potential to 55% in 2025.
    • Upgrading the qualifications of 5 thousand pedagogues abroad.
  2. Advanced engineering schools:
    • Increase their number to 25.
    • Increase the value of projects 10 times in cooperation with industrial enterprises.
  3. Technical universities:
    • Nukus, Bukhara, Fergana. and Namangan cities, new technical universities will be established.
    • A total of 22 technical universities will be established by enlarging 35 educational institutions.

Engineering schools:

Plans to open 17 new engineering schools were also discussed at the meeting. These schools are aimed at training personnel that meet the requirements of modern industry.
