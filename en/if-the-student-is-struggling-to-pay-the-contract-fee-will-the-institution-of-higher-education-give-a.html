If the student is struggling to pay the contract fee, will the institution of higher education give a discount?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Study on a fee-contract basis is optional, and the relevant regulatory and legal documents do not provide for the granting of benefits for this category of students by the higher education institution to ease payment conditions.
Currently, students are paid on a fee-contract basis. one of the acceptable ways to finance one's studies is an educational loan.
There are a number of benefits for low-income people under educational loans allocated by bank institutions to students studying on a payment-contract basis: interest-free educational loans are given to orphans, those brought up in "Mehribonlik houses" and disabled people of groups I and II; For students from low-income families, 50 percent of the interest added on the loan is to be paid by the district Employment Assistance Centers; The amount of taxable wages and other income of citizens that is directed to education in higher educational institutions of our republic (for their own studies or for the studies of their children under the age of 26) is not subject to income tax from individuals. the educational institution does not plan to provide benefits for easing payment conditions.
