The suspect who escaped from Jizzakh to Tashkent used the phone of a student. After that, the student followed him on the instructions of the Ministry of Internal Affairs and helped arrest the suspect.

The person who committed a serious crime in Jizzakh was arrested in Tashkent. Mirzabek Elmurodov, a 2nd-level student of the University of World Languages ​​of Uzbekistan, helped the IIB officers to arrest him. This is reported by the IIB information service of Jizzakh region.

It is reported that on April 19, 2024, a person living in Nebo'sa neighborhood of Zomin district of Jizzakh region stabbed a man living in Akbulok neighborhood of Zarbdar district and hid from the scene of the incident. As a result, the victim died in the hospital.

After that, the IIB officers launched a quick search to find the suspect. Footage from surveillance cameras was studied. Also, the places where he could hide were inspected.

During these processes, it was found that the man suspected of murder called his wife. When IIB officers contacted the number used by the man, another young man, not the suspect, answered.

The young man told the IIB officers that he agreed when a citizen asked to use his phone while walking down the street. The IIB officers told the young man to follow him.

"While talking to his wife, the citizen said that he had committed a crime and asked about the victim's condition. 10 minutes later, the IIB officers of the Jizzakh region called me and told me that the person who used my phone had been murdered and asked me to follow him. I followed him. We caught the criminal with the help of the IIB officers of Tashkent," says Mirzabek.

It is noted that the ceremony of awarding Mirzabek Elmurodov was held at the University of World Languages of Uzbekistan. It was attended by the head of the IIB of Jizzakh region, Ghairat Kadirov, the university management, teachers and students.

The student was awarded 5 million soums, souvenirs and a letter of thanks to his parents. — Read on infoedu.uz
