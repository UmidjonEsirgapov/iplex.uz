The Pomodoro method, created by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s, is one of the most effective time management methods today. In this article, you will learn whether this method is suitable for your work or study and how to apply it.

What is Pomodoro?

Tomato is a method of efficient use of time, maximum attention to the assigned task. carefully designed to achieve without excessive mental pressure. If you want to achieve a specific goal in a short period of time without excessive mental and physical strain, then this method is for you.

The process is very simple: you divide the time you want to spend on the specified project into short, 25-minute "periods", and after each such period, you take a small, 5-minute break. That is, you work for 25 minutes and rest for 5 minutes. Every 25 minutes is called "pomodoro".

The word is Italian for tomato, and the method was named after Francesco Cirillo, who originally used a tomato-shaped kitchen clock. Later it became the name of the whole process. After every 4 pomodoros, take a longer, 15-20 minute break and write down your progress and shortcomings.

Where is the efficiency?

  • According to research, the biggest obstacle to completing a project is lack of self-control. Through the above method, you need to complete small tasks in a short time. This is much easier and easier to control than the usual hard work.
  • Small details that disturb and distract you in the work process actually take more time and attention than you think. With the tomato method, you can also control external distractions. When you set the timer for 25 minutes, even that little time will remind you not to be distracted by other external factors. A 5-minute break after each "pomodoro" will allow you to focus on your work again.
  • This method teaches you to value time. You will also learn more about your capabilities through this process. As a result of a few days of training, you will know exactly how many "pomodoros" you need to complete a certain task. In this case, each "pomodoro" is a new opportunity, you can correct the shortcomings and mistakes made in the previous one and increase efficiency even more.

The above method may not suit your work style or work process itself. But in many cases, it can help you complete daily tasks and lighten your to-do list. Your smartphone or any type of time display device is suitable for the timer.
