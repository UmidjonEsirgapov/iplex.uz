Scholarship "Meridian" is an ejegodnaya scholarship, prisujdaemaya luchshim studentam Universiteta SIUE. Stipendiaty Meridian poluchayut four-year salary for education, accommodation and food. To apply for a Meridian scholarship, you must submit the following information by December 1, 2021:

  • Apply for SIUE in the fall semester;
  • Fill out the additional online profile form;
  • Otpravte ofitsialnye trantskripti sredney schkoly.

Requirements:

  • The candidate has excellent grades (3.5 points on a 4-point scale);
  • Podat vysheukazannoe zayavlenie i dokumenti;
  • Chtoby poexat v Soedinennye Shtaty, vy doljny oplatit svoi sobstvennye skhody, takie kak visa i meditsinskaya straxovka.

Poluchateli stipendii poluchayut chetyre goda besplatnogo obucheniya v Universitete Yuzhnogo Illinois Edwardsvilleya v SShA, a takje skhody na zzhivanie i pitanie.

Drobnee o program ZDES.

S dopolnitelnymi proprosami mojete obratitsya po adresu admissions@siue.edu.
