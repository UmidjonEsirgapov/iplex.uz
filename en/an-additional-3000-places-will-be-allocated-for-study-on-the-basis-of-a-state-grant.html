An additional 3,000 places will be allocated for study on the basis of a state grant

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
By Presidential Decree (PF-107-No., 03.07.2023)
amendments and additions were made to some regulatory legal documents. In the 2023/2024 academic year, an additional 3,000 places will be allocated on the basis of a state grant for young people who have graduated from secondary special and professional educational organizations.
In this, the need for highly educated personnel of the sectors and industries will be determined, and proposals for higher education institutions and educational areas will be submitted to the State Commission for Admission to Educational Organizations of the Republic of Uzbekistan. It will be submitted until July 20, 2023.
