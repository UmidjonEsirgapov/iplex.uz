But calculators are allowed at the Olympiads. What is the reason for these 2 differences? The question was answered by the officials of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency.

The reason: related to the level of difficulty of the questions and the types of questions.

The questions in the entrance exams are of 3 levels: easy, medium and difficult and consist of closed tests. Numbers in the tests can be calculated without a calculator.

At the Olympics, there will also be written work, and the time is limited. The level of questions is difficult, and the numbers involved are also difficult. That's why they are given a calculator. — Source
