Entrance tests for military-academic lyceums of the Temurbek school have been announced

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Entrance tests to "School of Temurbeks", "Young border guards" and Military-academic lyceums named after Jaloliddin Manguberdi will be held on July 27, 2024 in the 2nd shift and at 3:00 p.m.
Applicants must come to the area where the test will be held with an identity document and a permit to participate in the test no later than 14:00.
A permit to participate in the test will be distributed by the military-academic lyceum where the applicant submitted the application. Military-academic lyceum entrance tests will be held on July 27, 2024 in the 2nd shift and will start at 15:00. Applicants should arrive at the test area with an identity document and a permit to participate in the test no later than 14:00.

Permission to participate in the test is distributed by the military-academic lyceum where the applicant submitted the document. Obtaining this permit is important for applicants, because they will not be admitted to the test without a permit and identity document.

All necessary measures have been taken in order to conduct the entrance tests for military-academic lyceums in a fair and transparent manner. Applicants must follow all instructions for this process to be successful. — Read on infoedu.uz
