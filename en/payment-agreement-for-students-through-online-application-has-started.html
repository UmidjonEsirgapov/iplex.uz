From August 22, an online application for a payment contract was created for students

From August 22, all students (including 1st courses) payment for the new academic year had the opportunity to submit an online application for a contract. This was reported by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovations.

"For this, register on the kontrakt.edu.uz system and send an online application without too much hassle," the ministry said. The applications are approved by the admission commission of the higher education institution, and a payment contract with a QR code is created.

You are requested to download the approved contract and make the specified payments on time.
