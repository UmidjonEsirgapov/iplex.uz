Special assistance in testing for disabled applicants

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
State commission statement stated that applicants with physical disabilities and visual impairments will be facilitated in coloring the answer sheet when taking tests.
On July 18, 2024, in the 1st shift, Bukhara Region Applicants with disabilities in groups 45-46 took tests in building 3 (basketball gym of the Youth Sports Complex).
This process was widely discussed on social networks, as a video about the assistance to the applicant was circulated. it is decided to make it easier for the applicants to fill in the answer sheet when taking the tests.

On July 18, 2024, on the 1st shift, applicants with disabilities in groups 45-46 took tests in the 3rd building (basketball gym of the Youth Sports Complex) of the Bukhara region. This process was widely discussed on social networks, as a video about how the applicant was being helped was spread.

H.Khaydarov, an applicant from group 45, asked for help in filling out the answer sheet due to his visual impairment. This applicant was given practical assistance by the group invigilator in marking the answer sheet. This assistance was provided with the consent of all applicants under the supervision of the employees of the Knowledge Assessment Agency and group supervisors.
