Applicants Attention!

Today is the last day to choose a course! Until August 10 at 11:59 p.m., applicants must choose a course suitable for the complex of subjects they have chosen.

During this period, applicants who have not chosen a course cannot participate in the competition, i.e. they cannot enroll. Regardless of how many points they have earned, their results will be cancelled.

You also have the option to change your course selection until 23:59 today. Don't forget to take advantage of this opportunity! — Read on infoedu.uz
