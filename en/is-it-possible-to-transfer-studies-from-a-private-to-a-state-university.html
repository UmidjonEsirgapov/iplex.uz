Is it possible to transfer studies from a private university to a state university?

Students studying in a private or foreign HEI have the opportunity to transfer studies to a state higher education institution. However, this process is carried out in accordance with certain procedures and requirements.

Deadline and platform of study transfer

According to the procedure established by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation of the Republic of Uzbekistan:

  • Applications are accepted online from July 15 to August 5.

  • For this It is necessary to enter the my.uzbmb.uz platform and register.

  • When applying, a transcript (zachotka) file is uploaded to the system in pdf or jpg format.

Testing and transfer criteria

  • August 15-20 The possibility of transferring studies is determined through a special test.

  • Based on the results of the test, a decision is made to accept or reject the student.

In what cases is it possible to transfer studies?

✅ In the same or related fields

  • Participation in the student test

  • It is allowed to transfer studies based on points.

✅ In unrelated fields

  • No test is required.

  • The student continues his studies based on a graded payment-contract.

Important notes

  • When transferring from a private or foreign higher education institution to a public university, official documents must be complete.

  • Payment conditions and the compatibility of the field of study should be carefully studied.

Conclusion

It is possible to transfer studies from a private university to a state university, but this is done within a certain period of time and based on a specific procedure. Students must prepare their documents correctly, pass the necessary tests and agree to the terms.
