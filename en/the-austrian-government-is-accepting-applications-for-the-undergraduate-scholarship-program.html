The Austrian government is accepting applications for the undergraduate grant program.

Candidates from all countries can apply, but the age limit should not exceed 35 years.

Only three fields of application can be applied within the program:

  • hotel management;
  • tourism management;
  • entrepreneurship or teacher training module.

All applications must be sent directly to the institute by post. the application form can be obtained here or from Austrian embassies and consulates.

When considering applications, the following will be taken into account:

  • the form is filled out correctly and clearly;
  • the presence of all the necessary information and the attachment of all the necessary documents;
  • the inclusion of a working e-mail address, telephone and fax numbers - because if these are not clearly entered, the application may be canceled;
  • show with facts why he applied for the scholarship.

Late applications will not be considered and all requirements for admission must be submitted. Only relevant applications will be considered.

Accepted students will be exempted from tuition fees and provided with a monthly stipend of 205 euros.

In addition, medical insurance, food and travel expenses will be covered.

Applications can be sent until March 31 of this year. More information here. — Official Data
