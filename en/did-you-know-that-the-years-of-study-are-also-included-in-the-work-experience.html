Did you know that the years of study are also included in the work experience?

Student years are not only a period of education, but can also affect the future pension calculation. This rule, which many people do not know, but which is important, is defined by the Law "On State Pension Provision of Citizens". According to Article 37, Clause "e" of this law, full-time education at the following levels of education is added to the length of service:

  • Higher educational institutions (undergraduate)
  • Master's degree and clinical residency
  • Trainee-researcher institute
  • Senior researcher-researcher institute
  • Basic doctoral studies
  • Doctoral study
  • Periods of study abroad

This means that if you have completed these stages of education full-time, these years will be added to the work experience when you retire.
