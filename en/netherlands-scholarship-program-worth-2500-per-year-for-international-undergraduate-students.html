About the grant program in brief:

The

Hanze Minerva Scholarship grant program is intended for candidates from countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) who intend to study at the bachelor level of the Academy of Arts at Hanze University of Applied Sciences. This grant program is provided as financial support for the living expenses of the winners in the Netherlands for a period of four years.

Benefits of the grant:

  • The amount of the Hanze Minerva scholarship is 2,500 euros per academic year.
  • The grant consists of one payment per academic year and is awarded to selected students in November of each year;

Note: The Hanze Minerva grant program is a financial aid for living expenses and cannot be used to reduce tuition fees!

Requirements:

  • Citizenship of candidates outside the European Economic Area;
  • Being admitted to one of the following bachelor programs of the Minerva Academy of Arts from September 2021;
    • Design
    • Fine Arts

Note: The scholarship is awarded based on motivation and financial need.

Application Procedure:
If you wish to receive a scholarship, you must first be admitted to one of the programs mentioned above. For information on how to apply, visit here.
As soon as you are accepted to the undergraduate program, you will also be invited to apply for the Hanze Minerva Scholarship and will receive an email from the admissions office with application instructions. The Hanze Minerva Scholarship is awarded only from the first year of the program and mainly for the nominal duration of the program: four years. Additional information about the procedure and payment of the scholarship will be sent to you by e-mail after your selection.

The deadline for applying for the grant - June 1, 2021.
