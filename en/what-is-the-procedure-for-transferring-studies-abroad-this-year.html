What is the procedure for transferring studies abroad this year?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
It is allowed to transfer studies to Uzbekistan state higher education institutions from foreign higher education institutions of a suitable or similar field, including universities included in the TOP-1000 list.
What exams are required for transferring education?
Conclusion: In 2025, the transfer of studies from abroad to Uzbek higher education institutions is strictly regulated, and it is possible to take an exam and transfer only in appropriate directions. the procedure for students to transfer their studies to the state higher education institutions of Uzbekistan in 2025 was determined based on the President's decision PQ-279. On the basis of this decision, strict criteria and requirements have been introduced for transferring studies.

Which higher education institutions abroad can one transfer studies to?

Only foreign higher education institutions of similar or similar fields, including top-1000 universities, are allowed to transfer studies to state higher education institutions of Uzbekistan.

Transferring studies to disproportionate or unrelated fields is strictly prohibited.

What exams are required when transferring studies?

Professional (creative) exams

Students in fields that require special talent (for example, art, sports, journalism) professional or creative exam

Tests

In all other areas, tests are conducted based on 3 compulsory subjects. This procedure is organized in the same way as the undergraduate admission system.

Passing scores and publication procedure

Passing scores for tests and professional exams:

  • Approved by July 15 every year;

  • Evaluation of knowledge and skills agency. (former DTM) will be announced on the official website;

  • Scores are determined by the ministries and agencies under the jurisdiction of higher education institutions.

What about those who scored below the passing score?

Students who scored below the passing score in the exams can be admitted to study on the basis of a differentiated fee-contract. This allows for continued study under the contract, but cannot be transferred without an examination or for good reason.

Important note

For transferring studies from abroad, no good reason will be considered. All students are evaluated on the basis of the exam.

Conclusion

In 2025, the transfer of studies from abroad to HEIs of Uzbekistan is strictly regulated, and it is possible to take an exam and transfer only to suitable courses. This system, introduced on the basis of Presidential Decree No. 279, aims to ensure a fair and transparent election. — Source
