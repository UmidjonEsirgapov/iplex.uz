Science Adventures: Women and Glaciers in Central Asia Expedition 2022
Are you passionate about art, science and nature and seek to understand the world around you?

On our adventure would you like to join and explore the wild together?

Do you want to meet interesting, young, like-minded women and develop your personal and professional skills?

Then join our 2022 expedition Science Adventure: Women and Glaciers in Central Asia!

During this 10-day expedition you will explore Ala-Archa National Park in Kyrgyzstan and explore its beautiful glaciers and environment. You will be part of a diverse group of young women (ages 18-25) from all over Central Asia and practice scientific methods, artistic observation and mountaineering in nature. You will discuss thematic issues related to climate change and high mountain ecosystems and explore them in groups by developing your own scientific projects. After the expedition, two public events will allow you to share your discoveries with scientists and experts in related fields: a post-expedition event in Kyrgyzstan and an international online event. The program is designed to encourage critical thinking, curiosity and self-expression of all kinds!

On this trip you will be accompanied by experienced all-female instructors and a mountain guide.

Requirements: The expedition is free and provides all necessary equipment. You don't need any climbing experience or scientific knowledge; We only care about your interest and motivation!

Program dates: On August 15, 2022, we will meet in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan, and together we will go to Ala-Archa National Park. We will return to Bishkek for the last days of the program, which will end on 25.08.2022.

Who can apply: The program is open to all young women between the ages of 18 and 25 who want to study and research from Central Asian countries: Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan. The expedition will be conducted in a bilingual Russian-English format, therefore sufficient knowledge of English or Russian is required for active communication with all participants.

We are looking for diverse young people with different views, interests and personalities. That's why we don't just select participants based on their individual applications, but create a cohesive team by balancing understanding, experience and energy. If you've never been on an adventure like this before, don't worry; We are not looking for experienced scientists and climbers, we only ask for motivation and interest! Unfortunately, the number of places is limited, so hurry to fill out the form and let us know about your interest! Please answer the questions on the application in as much detail as possible and explain in detail why you think Science Adventures: Women and Glaciers in Central Asia is the right program for you.

Apply now.
The application form is open from April 15 to May 15 2022.
