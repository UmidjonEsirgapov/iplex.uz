Brief program:

Bali International Student Festival 2021 is a "Post-Pandemic World" i.e. lifestyle during the pandemic and the changes made to the education system and new panels discussion online conference dedicated to The conference will discuss the importance of each of us in the global society and our role.

Requirements for participants:

Program participants:

  • Be at least 18 years old;
  • Be a student (graduates or young activists can also participate);
  • Good English
  • Should try to make positive use of the experience gained.

The beneficial aspects of the program:

The program allows participants to:

  • Discover the culture of Bali and Indonesia;
  • Present their presentation or idea to the public at an international conference;
  • A number of opportunities such as obtaining a certificate of participation creates opportunities.

The main requirement of the program:

Candidates should write a motivation letter about the big topic of "Post-Pandemic World" and about their place in society that motivates them to participate in the program.
