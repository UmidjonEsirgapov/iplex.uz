Erik Blumbeng Scholarship Program at the University of Groningen (Netherlands)

Date: December 1, 2020

University of Groningen (University of Groningen) ranks 128th among the best universities in the world and third among the universities in the Netherlands according to the QS World University Rankings 2021.

Eric Blumbeng Scholarship Program provides financial support to students for all master programs (MSc/MA/LL.M.) at the university.

Scholarship It is paid for 1 or 2 years and includes the following fees:
• Tuition
• Living expenses
• International travel and book fees
• Medical insurance expenses.

To apply for the scholarship, you must be accepted to the master's level of the university.
