Admission to the Tashkent branch of the University of Economics and Technology of Turkey has begun

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
During the visit of the President of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev to Turkey in February, a branch of the University of Economics and Technologies of Turkey, which provides non-state educational services, was established in Tashkent in accordance with the cooperation agreement signed between the University of Economics and Technology of Turkey and the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Uzbekistan.
Turkey's branch of the University of Economics and Technology in Tashkent will accept the first students in the 2020-2021 academic year.
✅ The branch will start its activities on September 14, 2020 with the following two educational areas: Electronic commerce and technology management; International business and entrepreneurship.

During the visit of the President of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev to Turkey in February, in accordance with the cooperation agreement signed between the University of Economics and Technology of Turkey and the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Uzbekistan, a branch of the University of Economics and Technology of Turkey, providing non-state educational services, was established in Tashkent.

A branch of the University of Economics and Technology of Turkey in Tashkent It will accept the first students in the academic year 2020-2021. The branch will start its activities on September 14, 2020 with the following two educational areas: Electronic commerce and technology management; International business and entrepreneurship. Applications will be accepted until July 30, 2020. The entrance exam will be held on August 15, 2020.
