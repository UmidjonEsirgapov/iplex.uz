How is the level of difficulty of the questions of the state tests distributed?

As every year, the questions presented to the applicants in the 2025 tests will be made on the basis of exact percentages according to the level of complexity. This procedure ensures fair and equal evaluation of test results.

Levels of questions in percent

Test questions are divided into the following three categories:

  • 15-25% easy questions (first level)

  • 50-70% questions of medium complexity (secondary level)

  • 15-25% difficult questions (tertiary level)

These relative percentages allow you to comprehensively test the knowledge level of test-takers.

Practical example - 30 questions from a specialist subject

If you are taking a test from a specialist subject and Given 30 questions, their level of difficulty is approximately as follows:

  • 5-8 easy questions

  • 15-21 medium questions

  • 5-8 difficult questions

These proportions reflect the overall balance of test questions. provides and serves to assess the comprehensive readiness of applicants.

Conclusion

The level of difficulty of the test questions does not change from year to year and is based on a certain template. This allows applicants to accurately plan their preparation, allocate time correctly, and work on questions of varying complexity.
