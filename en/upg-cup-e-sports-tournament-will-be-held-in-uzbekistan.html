General information:

On April 24-25 the 3rd season of the republican cybersport online tournament "UPG CUP" organized by the UPG company in the discipline of CS:GO in cooperation with IT Park, FRAG.GG and the Cybersport Association of Uzbekistan will be held.

The total prize pool of the tournament is 6,000,000 soums.

Tournament Rules:

Unlike the first two seasons, in season 3 of the tournament, a division system has been introduced for three qualifiers:
– Stage #1: the third division (DIVISION 3), suitable for beginners with an overall level of 1-4;
– Stage #2: the second division (DIVISION 2) with an average playing level, overall for players with a team level of 5-7;
– Stage #3 – First division (DIVISION 1) for teams with a high level of play, with a total team level of 8-10.
