The appeal of the ministry on the extension of the winter vacation was announced

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Dear professors and teachers, dear parents and students!
Classes will start on January 18.
In order to create favorable conditions for students to return to their educational institutions, air and railway The deadline for a 50 percent discount on the ticket price has been extended until January 18, 2023.

Dear professors, dear parents and students!

According to the Hydrometeorological Service Center, since January 10, during the period of sharp drop in air temperature, seasonal flu among the population, especially in educational institutions in order to prevent a sharp increase in the disease, the winter vacation period for students of higher and professional educational institutions and academic lyceums has been extended until January 18, 2023.

Classes will start on January 18.

During this period, the current salary payments to pedagogues and other employees of educational organizations will be maintained. Also, online seminars on preparation of scientific articles and spiritual-educational events will be held for students on holidays.

In order to create favorable conditions for students to return to their educational institutions, the deadline for granting a 50% discount on air and railway tickets has been extended until January 18, 2023.

Most importantly, purchased before January 9 air and railway tickets will be exchanged free of charge for the relevant period. In addition, in connection with the extension of the vacation, additional seats (flights) will be allocated on the air and railways.

The lessons scheduled during this period will be intensively taught during the academic year.

Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation
