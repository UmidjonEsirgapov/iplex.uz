What opportunities does the B1 level certificate offer to applicants?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
It is possible to submit a document with a B1 certificate to courses where foreign language is the 1st subject. According to the new decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, important changes have been made to the procedure for admission to some educational courses with a foreign language subject.
The amount of points awarded for the B1 certificate New in accordance with the regulations, applicants with a B1 level certificate will be awarded 75% of the maximum score in foreign language.
The procedure for holders of B2 and higher certificates, the current procedure for applicants with national or international certificates of B2, C1 or higher levels has been preserved.

You can apply for courses in which foreign language is the 1st subject with a B1 certificate

Ministers Based on the new decision of the court, important changes were made to the procedure of admission to some educational courses in which foreign language is available. Now, applicants have the opportunity to apply with a level B1 national or internationally recognized language certificate.

Content of the new decision of the Cabinet of Ministers

According to this decision, admission to the fields of study foreign language is designated as the 1st subject in the subject complex is carried out on the basis of a certificate confirming the level of knowledge of a foreign language.

At admission applicable certificate requirements

The applicant is required to have a national or internationally recognized certificate of at least B1 level in a foreign language. If this certificate is available, it is not required to take a test in foreign language.

Amount of points awarded for B1 certificate

According to the new regulation, applicants with a B1 level certificate will be awarded 75% of the maximum points in foreign language.

What is the advantage of B1 certificate?

Through this change:

  • The opportunities for applicants with B1 level have been expanded;

  • It is possible to get points without taking a test;

  • Incentive to learn a foreign language has increased.

Procedure for holders of B2 and higher certificates

Applicants with B2, C1 or higher national or international certificates order is preserved.

How many points are awarded for B2 level and above?

  • 100% of the maximum points are awarded;

  • No foreign language test is required;

  • The same conditions as the previous order apply.

The previous and new order the difference between

Previous procedure

  • Only B2 and higher certificate holders received 100% points;

  • B1 certificate was not taken into account in the admission process.

New procedure

  • B1 certificate — 75% points;

  • B2 and above — 100% points;

  • The procedure is valid only when the foreign language is 1st subject.

Conclusion

This decision of the Cabinet of Ministers created a great opportunity for applicants. Now, when applying for educational courses where foreign language is the 1st subject, a B1 level certificate is sufficient and it provides 75% of the maximum score.
