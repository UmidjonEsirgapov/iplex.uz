Grant distribution among students: New procedure and evaluation criteria

Redistribution of grant places these days started

According to the news of 2025, the process of redistribution of places among students based on grants has begun in higher education institutions. Now, students studying on the basis of a contract can transfer to the grant basis, and those studying on a grant can enter a contract.

Evaluation criteria for grant redistribution

Academic mastery: 80 points
