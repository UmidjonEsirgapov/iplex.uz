A 66-year-old father applied to a private university

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
66-year-old father applied to non-state university Yuldashev Sohibjon Komiljonovich, born in 1958 in Kuva district of Fergana region, applied to UBS non-state higher education institution in Namangan.
News of "University of Business Science" According to him, the future student of the university has been contributing to the development of cultural heritage in his region for several years.
In order to continue this activity, Sohibjon's father applied to the university's "Tourism and Hospitality" department. Yuldashev Sohibjon Komiljonovich, who was born in the district, submitted an application to the UBS non-state higher education institution in Namangan.

"University of Business Science" reports that the future student of the university has been making a great contribution to the development of cultural heritage in his region for several years. In order to continue this activity, the owner applied to the university's "Tourism and Hospitality" department.

"The purpose of entering the university is to strengthen the protection of cultural heritage in Uzbekistan and develop children's tourism," - says the future student. — Official Data
