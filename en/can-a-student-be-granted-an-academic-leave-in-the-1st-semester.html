Can a student be granted an academic leave in the 1st semester?

An academic leave is granted to a student only in cases established by law. According to the regulation approved by Cabinet of Ministers decision No. 344 dated 03.06.2021, academic leave can be granted to a student in the following cases:

  • For military service;
  • To restore health;
  • For pregnancy and childbirth;
  • For child care.

According to Clause 3 of this regulation, academic leave is granted in any part of the academic year, including 1st semester, if one of the above circumstances exists. academic leave can also be granted. The student is advised to contact the District Justice Department in this matter — Source
