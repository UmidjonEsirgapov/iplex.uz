Applications are now being accepted for the Elevate Training Program for Women in Artificial Intelligence Technology

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
It is open to women and girls aged 22 and above, and applications must be submitted by March 31st.
Photo: Spot Google Cloud and the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for a large-scale free AI training for women "Elevate" is launching an educational program.
This is reported by the press service of "IT Park".

Women and girls aged 22 and older can participate in it, and applications should be sent by March 31.

Photo: Spot

"Google Cloud" together with the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is launching Elevate, an extensive free AI training program. This is reported by the "IT Park" press service.

The main goal and mission of the program is to expand the opportunities for women to build and continue careers in artificial intelligence technologies by reducing the gender gap in STEM fields.

The training will be held in English, in a hybrid format, with the support of the Google Cloud team.

Participants can be students and graduates of higher education institutions in the fields of STEM, information management, university graduates in the fields of business management and business informatics, as well as women and girls aged 22 and older living in developing countries.

Participants are required to know English, be computer literate, and have access to a computer and the Internet.

Training is conducted in two directions:

1.Technical competencies are divided into 3 more directions depending on the interests and experience of the program participant (Google Cloud Architecture, Data Engineering, Machine Learning and Data Processing).

2. Business competencies are a common focus for all (introduction to digital transformation, corporate security of cloud environment, etc.).

Applications for participation in the program are accepted through the link until March 31, 2023.
