A grant allocated to TOP 100 Universities of the world

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
Young people who are admitted to the 100 most prestigious universities of the world on a grant basis will be allocated funds from the "Hope of the World" fund without additional exams.
Admission to the 500 most prestigious universities of the world on the basis of a grant transportation and accommodation expenses of the young people will be fully covered. In this regard, the "El-Yurt Umid" fund and the Youth Affairs Agency will develop a relevant draft decision in this regard by December 2023.

The funds of the "El-Yurt Umid" fund will be given to young people admitted to the 100 most prestigious universities of the world on grants. allocated without additional exams.

Transportation and accommodation expenses of young people admitted to the world's 500 most prestigious universities on the basis of grants are fully covered.

In this regard By December 2023, the "El-Yurt Umid" fund and the Youth Affairs Agency will develop a draft of the relevant decision.

The basis: Presidential Decree (PQ-300, 11.09.2023) — Source
