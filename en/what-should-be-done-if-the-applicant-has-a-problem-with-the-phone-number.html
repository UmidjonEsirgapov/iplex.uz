What should be done if the applicant has a problem with the phone number?

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Applicants may have problems with their phone number in 2 different situations when applying to the OTM.
In the 1st situation: Applicants who lost their phone number registered on the my.uzbmb.uz site before the start of admission should register with a new phone number. possible.
In this case, he should contact the regional offices of the Knowledge Assessment Agency.

The applicant may have a problem with the phone number in 2 different situations when submitting an application to OTM.

In case 1:

Applicants who have lost their phone number registered on the my.uzbmb.uz site before the start of admission can register using a new phone number. When the applicant registers using a new phone number and enters his personal identification number (JSHSHIR), all his data will be attached to the new phone number.

In case 2:

The applicant who lost his registered phone number after the start of admission, i.e. after June 20, cannot change it himself. In this case, he should contact the regional offices of the Knowledge Assessment Agency. The agency employee will change the phone number to the applicant.
