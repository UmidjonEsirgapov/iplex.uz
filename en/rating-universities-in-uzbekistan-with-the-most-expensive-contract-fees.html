Today, the number of higher education institutions in Uzbekistan is increasing year by year. In today's rating, we will look at universities in Uzbekistan with the highest fee-contract amount.

1. Akfa University. Akfa University in Tashkent was established in 2019 on the basis of the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On the establishment of Akfa International University in Tashkent" and the initiative of the Ministry of Health, Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education and "Akfa Medline" LLC. Applicants who want to study at the university are required to have a certificate of graduation from a school or secondary special educational institution, as well as IELTS or TOEFL certificates. This process included 2-year medical training courses and 4-year medical course programs. Akfa University was founded in cooperation with South Korea's Ganjeon University. The total amount of the contract in the medical department of the university is 58,000,000 (fifty-eight million) soums per year / 29,000,000 (twenty-nine million) soums per semester.

The cost of studying in other areas available at Akfa is cheaper than this, and medicine has taken the first place in the rating because it is the most expensive.

2. MGIMO Tashkent branch (Moskovskiy gosudarstvennyy institut mejdunarodnyx otnosheniy). The foundation stone of the institute, which started its operation in September 2019, was laid at the meeting of the advisor to the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, rector of the University of World Economy and Diplomacy, Abdujabbor Abduvahidov, and vice-rector of the Moscow State Institute of International Relations, Artyom Malgin, held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in February this year.

The institute has 6 programs for bachelor's and master's level students, educational grants for the best applicants, preparatory programs for entering studies, and other programs. and three semesters for undergraduate students. The amount of the payment contract is 52,230,000 soums.

3. Inha University. Inha University in Tashkent was established in 2014 in cooperation with Korea's Inha University. Inha University is one of Korea's leading universities in the field of information technology. Today, more than 1,500 students are studying in two main areas of the university. These are the areas of computer and information engineering and logistics.


Photo: "Inha"

Inha University in Tashkent gives 10 active and talented students the opportunity to study for free at Inha University's summer school in Korea every year. The amount of one-year contract for studying at the university is 30 million soums.

4. Tashkent International University of Westminster. TXVU was established in 2002 in cooperation with the University of London Westminster, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of Uzbekistan and the "Talent" fund. The university offers Preparatory, SIFS, Bachelors, Masters and Professional Certificate courses, with Bachelors in Commercial Law, Business Information Technology, Finance, Economics and Finance and Business Administration. All these courses lead to a UK Honors Bachelor's degree. The amount of the payment contract for the bachelor's degree is almost 30 million soums. (The fee for the master's degree is 44,991,000 soums).

5. Management Development Institute of Singapore (MDIS). According to the relevant decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, MDIS was established in order to train highly qualified specialists in undergraduate and graduate programs in accordance with the international requirements for the quality of higher education, as well as national goals and objectives and the national program of personnel training of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

The total number of educational programs offered by the university is 15 undergraduate programs and 5 graduate programs, and the total number of students at the Higher Education Institution is more than 5100. The amount of the payment contract is 28,800,000 soums.

6. Polytechnic University of Turin. This educational institution, established in cooperation with the Polytechnic University of Turin in Turin, Italy, is located in Almazor district of Tashkent city. The main goal of the university is to provide qualified personnel to the high industries based on high technology. There are faculties of BTA in information technology and production, industrial-civil construction and architecture, power engineering, mechanical engineering technology. The amount of the one-year contract is 28,500,000 soums. Puchon University in Tashkent.

 Puchon University (Republic of Korea) was founded in April 1958, currently more than 8,000 students are studying at this university. There are two educational centers of Puchon University in the Republic of Korea, but this is its first foreign branch.

The educational process at the university is carried out on the basis of the curriculum and standards of Puchon University, taking into account the requirements of the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan. The teachers and professors are made up of the best specialists of Puchon University (Republic of Korea) and the Republic of Uzbekistan. The amount of the payment contract corresponds to this - 28,250,000 soums per year. 8. Webster University in Tashkent. Today, in addition to the center in Tashkent, in the territory of Uzbekistan, TDIU, UzDJTU, TATU, based on joint programs, the training of personnel for master's degree has been started since last year. The amount of the payment contract is 27,400,000 soums.

9. Yodju Technical Institute. On July 24, 2018, Yodju Technical Institute in the city of Tashkent received the status of an institution providing higher education services in the Republic of Uzbekistan according to the state license provided by the State Inspectorate of Education Quality Control under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Currently, the institute offers education in the following areas: architecture and urban planning, construction, alternative energy, tourism, business management, international economic relations, international marketing, primary education, preschool education, Korean language philology, clothing design, beauty aesthetics, accounting, banking, finance, English language education, information systems engineering and elevator engineering. The one-year contract amount is 26 million soums.
