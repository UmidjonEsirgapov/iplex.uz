Women who want to study in 5 fields in colleges and technical schools receive education on the basis of grants

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The draft decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On measures to support women who wish to acquire professions and specialties in colleges and technical schools" has been announced. women who wish to study in colleges and technical schools in professions and specialties in the construction, transport, communal, agricultural and service sectors will receive education on the basis of a full state grant, and their expenses will be fully covered by the state budget funds.
"On support measures" was published. It is stated that, starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, women who wish to study in colleges and technical schools in professions and specialties in the fields of construction, transport, utility, agriculture and service will receive a full state grant. receive education and their expenses are fully covered by the state budget funds.
