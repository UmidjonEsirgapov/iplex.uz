Republican scientific-practical conference on "Philological issues - from the perspective of young researchers".

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Uzbek language and literature teaching methodology.
a) Uzbek language teaching methodology; b) methodology of teaching Uzbek literature. Articles will be accepted until November 10, 2021 at the following telegram addresses: Branch of Linguistics: https://t.me/konferensiya_tilshunoslik Branch of Literary Studies: https://t.me/konferensiya_adabiyotshunoslik Branch of Applied Philology: https://t.me/konferensiya_amaliy_filologiya Uzbek Branch of teaching language and literature: https://t.me/konferensiya_metodika Branch of theory and practice of translation: https://t.me/konferensiya_tarjimashunoslik The organizing committee has the right to sort and select the materials. On November 15, 2021 it is planned to hold a scientific-practical conference of republican students (undergraduates) on the topic of "Philological issues - from the perspective of young researchers". Linguistics.
  • a) language history;
  • b) language theory;
  • c) modern Uzbek literary language; Turkic languages.

II branch. Literary studies.

  • a) history of Uzbek literature;
  • b) modern literary process (poetry, prose, dramaturgy);
  • c) folklore studies;
  • d) literature of Turkic peoples; children's literature

III branch. Translation studies.

  • a) translation theory;
  • b) translation practice.

IV branch. Methods of teaching Uzbek language and literature.

  1. a) methods of teaching Uzbek language;
  2. b) methods of teaching Uzbek literature.

According to the decisions of the board of the conference affiliates, in the directions "Article on the most relevant topic", "Best scientifically based article", "Most diligent young specialist" Recognized students are awarded with a special certificate.

Articles are accepted in Uzbek, Russian, and English languages. It is possible to participate in the conference in absentia.

All authors will receive a certificate that their article has been published.

Articles will be accepted until November 10, 2021 at the following telegram addresses:

  1. Linguistics branch: https://t.me/konferensiya_tilshunoslik
  2. Literary studies branch: https://t.me/konferensiya_adabiyotshunoslik
  3. Department of Applied Philology: https://t.me/konferensiya_amaliy_filologiya
  4. Department of Teaching Uzbek Language and Literature: https://t.me/konferensiya_metodika
  5. Department of Theory and Practice of Translation: https://t.me/konferensiya_tarjimashunoslik

The organizing committee has the right to sort and select the submitted materials. Materials not sent on request will not be accepted.

Address of the organizing committee: ToshTO'DAU, Department of Scientific Research and Scientific Pedagogical Personnel Training, Room 423.
