About the program in brief:

The Einstein Fellowship 2022 Program Einstein Forum and Daimler & Funded by the Benz Foundation, this program is intended to provide the opportunity to German and foreign young scientists who stand out from others with their diligence to carry out scientific project work in areas other than the ones they have previously worked in. The purpose of the program is to support young universalists who, like Albert Einstein, are distinguished by their outstanding achievements in a particular field of science.

Partners of the program:

The community will cover the living expenses of the winners for 5-6 months at Einstein's summer garden house in Caputh (Brandenburg), not far from the Potsdam and Berlin institutes, and A stipend of 10,000 euros and covers travel expenses.

Requirements:

Candidates must be no older than 35 years old and have a higher education in social and humanities or natural sciences. The following documents are required to apply for the program:

  • Curriculum vitae (CV);
  • Two page project proposal (Project Proposal)
  • 2 letters of recommendation

NOTE: The Project Proposal submitted for the program MUST NOT BE DISSERTATION RESEARCH previously written by the candidate. The submitted project proposal is required to cover a completely new topic in content.

All documents should be sent by May 15, 2021 to the following address:

  • fellowship@einsteinforum.de
