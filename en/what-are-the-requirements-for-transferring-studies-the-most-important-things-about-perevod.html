In the summer season, along with the entrance exams and master's exams, another major event is held - the transfer exams. In recent years, the number of applicants who want to transfer studies from foreign and non-state HEIs to state HEIs and local HEIs is increasing. In 2016, a total of 7,500 applications were received to transfer studies from one higher education institution to another or from abroad to a local university, and by 2021, this number will increase 22 times to 170,000. was confirmed. Starting from 2022, it will be allowed to transfer studies from local higher education institutions only in 2 cases:

  • when a student applies to transfer his studies to a higher education institution in the area where his spouse permanently lives in connection with starting a family;
  • in the event that employees of state bodies change their place of work, his spouse or minor child studies when applying for transfer, on the basis of the recommendation of the head of the relevant ministry (department);

Examinations for transfer of studies from foreign and non-state HEIs have been tightened and passing scores have been increased. In particular, the entrance exams were conducted by the former State Testing Center - the current Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency, and the professional (creative) exams were taken by the HEIs. For example, for the last academic year, the passing score for transferring studies to medical universities was set at 129 points. The passing scores differ depending on the courses.

The maximum possible score for all higher education institutions was 189 points, the same as in the undergraduate exams.

This It is expected that the passing scores of this year will be higher than last year. Abduqodir Tashkulov, the former minister of higher and secondary special education, said in his speech a year ago that the passing scores for those transferring their studies in 2023 will be the same as the scores obtained by the applicants a year ago. If the information provided by the former minister is implemented, the passing scores for those who transfer to law universities this year may be higher than 180 points.

However, the exact passing scores for this year have not been announced.

When does the acceptance of applications begin?

Applications for transferring studies are accepted from July 15 to August 5 every year. The exams are organized in the same way as the entrance exams for the bachelor's degree.
