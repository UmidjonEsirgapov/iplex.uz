It was found that there are Uzbek students who have never visited the country at the university in Kyrgyzstan

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
59 students are active in documents, but not in practice According to the publication "24.kg", it was found that 59 foreign students who were officially considered students at the university did not enter Kyrgyzstan.
Of them: 30 - citizens of Uzbekistan 20 - 9 citizens of Kazakhstan - 9 citizens of Russia All of them were expelled from the university.
A criminal case has been initiated.

The State Committee for National Security of Kyrgyzstan exposed a major service fraud at Kyrgyz State University named after Ishenali Arabayev in Bishkek.

In the course of investigations, it was found that officially admitted foreign students not only did not attend classes, but did not come to Kyrgyzstan at all.

59 students are active in documents, but in practice no

According to the publication "24.kg", it was found that 59 foreign students who were officially considered as students at the university did not enter Kyrgyzstan.

Of them:

  • 30 are citizens of Uzbekistan

  • 20 are Kazakhstan. citizen

  • 9 people are Russian citizens

All of them were expelled from the university.

A criminal case was initiated

A criminal case was initiated based on the Article of the Criminal Code "forgery of service". Investigations are ongoing and it is being determined which teachers or administrative staff of the university are involved in this violation.

Conclusion

This case of service fraud found in Kyrgyzstan has once again shown the need to strengthen control over the process of admission of foreign students. Although the authorities have not yet completed the investigations, strict measures are expected to be taken against the violations at the university. — Source
