Now, when applying for studies, students will be able to specify their need for accommodation

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The draft decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On measures to improve the system of providing housing for students of higher education institutions" was published. A simplified procedure for covering a part of the rent payment from the state budget will be introduced.
The Agency for the Evaluation of Knowledge and Qualifications will create an opportunity for applicants to determine their need for accommodation during the process of applying to study at State higher education institutions starting from the 2023-2024 academic year. The project of the decision "On measures to improve the provision system" was published.

According to it,

  • starting from September 1, 2023, a simplified procedure for covering a part of the rent for students of state higher education institutions from the state budget will be introduced. Starting from the 2023-2024 academic year, applicants will have the opportunity to determine their need for student accommodation during the process of applying for admission to state higher education institutions.

Starting from the academic year, students will apply for accommodation in a dormitory only through state service centers or the Unified Interactive State Services Portal of the Republic of Uzbekistan (hereinafter - YAIDXP).

— Official Data
