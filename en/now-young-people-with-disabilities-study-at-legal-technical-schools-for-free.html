Now, young people with disabilities study at legal technical schools for free

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Young people with disabilities now study for free in legal technical schools. Decision No. 640 was adopted.
Measures to support children with disabilities are being determined with the additions to the decision.
In particular, a discount of 50% of the service fee for children with disabilities, such as the issuance of a certificate in general education subjects, is now being determined.

Young people with legal disabilities are now study for free in technical schools

Resolution No. 640 of the Cabinet of Ministers "On amendments and additions to certain decisions of the Government of the Republic of Uzbekistan in relation to material support for persons in need of social protection and improvement of the system of social protection of children with disabilities" was adopted. measures to support children with disabilities are defined with additions. In particular, a discount of 50% of the fee for services such as general education certificates for children with disabilities is now set. In addition, they and graduates of family orphanages are admitted to legal technical schools on the basis of a state grant under special admission parameters.
