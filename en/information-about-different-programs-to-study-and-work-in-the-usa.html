The most popular programs to study and work in the USA.

1. Work&travel. It is the most popular and popular program for students. Full-time students of the higher educational institution can participate in it. The agency will find an American employer for a summer student (3-4 months), but you can also find him yourself. The student is then interviewed and receives a J1 visa.

2. FLEX. Exchange program for students of grades 9-11. It stands for Future Leaders Exchange, an exchange program for future leaders. Fluency in English, willingness to live with an American family and study in an American school is required. It is not known exactly where the participants will go to study and their number is limited, so it is necessary to prepare well.

3. Global UGRAD. Offers a one-semester study program to talented undergraduate students from around the world. During the semester, students can engage in social work, professional development, and cultural acquisition in the United States. UGRAD is a fully funded exchange program that includes a monthly stipend for student travel/return to the US, university tuition, room and board, health insurance, required books, and incidentals. Participants live in student housing with their American peers and are required to participate in twenty hours of community service.

4.Fulbright. The program has various options, but most importantly, it is funded by the US government. Students, graduates, scholars, teachers, and staff of international departments may be eligible for an educational program or internship in the United States based on the results of a long selection process and a rigorous selection process.

5. Opportunity funding. A grant covering the cost of admission to a US university. The government supports and provides financial assistance to international students who are eligible for scholarships. That is, accommodation, study or other expenses are not covered, only there is help with admission. In addition, this program is not available in all educational institutions of the country.

6.Camp USA. participants of this program Work & They will receive the same J1 visa as the Travel participant. But students can work only in the summer camp.

7. AU Pair. Very popular program for J1 visa. They go there to work as nannies for an American family. The problem is that contestants usually apply and wait until some family from any state chooses them. That is, it is not possible to choose the day of travel, as well as the place of work.

8. MAST international. The program is designed for students of higher education institutions of agriculture, because they work on the farm and also study in the state of Minnesota. In general, this is a J1 visa, but the specialization is much narrower - agriculture. According to this program, they will spend from 3 to 18 months in America and even have the opportunity to work in a winery. — More information
