General information:

Deakin University is one of the most prestigious universities located in the state of Victoria, Australia. This university was founded in 1974. Deakin University, one of Australia's leading higher education institutions, provides world-class programs and the opportunity to acquire international knowledge and skills supported by modern digital communications. Currently, this university is announcing a grant program for international students. The scholarship is for three years for Master's degree and Ph.D. for four years.

Benefits:

  • Tax-free stipend of $28,900 per year for 2022;
  • Relocation allowance of $500 to $1,500 (single family) for students moving interstate or overseas to study at Deakin;
  • For international students only: Health care benefits with a student visa.
  • 3 years stipend for PhD and 4 years tuition waiver;
  • 2 years stipend and 2 years tuition fee for Masters level researchers;
