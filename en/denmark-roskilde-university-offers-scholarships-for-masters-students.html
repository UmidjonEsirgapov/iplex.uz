Denmark: Roskilde University offers scholarships for Masters students

Date: January 15, 2022.

Country: Denmark, Roskilde.

Roskilde University was created to innovate the traditional education system, as well as to offer new methods of education and learning.

The university offers scholarships to talented students. Only candidates applying for the fall semester of 2021 can receive the scholarship.

The scholarship is provided for 22 months (study period according to the program). Students receive a stipend of 7,800 Danish kroner per month (this stipend is considered sufficient for rent payments in Denmark).

Candidates can receive a scholarship for the following programs:

· Master in Communication Studies and Cultural Encounters;

· Master in Cultural Encounters and Communication Studies;

· Master in International Public Administration and Politics;

· Master in International Development Studies and Global Studies; Master in Mathematical Physical Modeling Master in Spatial Designs and Society Master in Nordic Urban Planning Studies

· good knowledge of English language (IELTS: 6.5 or TOEFL iBT 83);

· knowledge of Danish language (if the candidate enters the study program in Danish).

Candidates must apply for master's degree and attach the following documents to it:

· letter of motivation;

· biography

· candidates also need to pay a fee of 165 € for registration.
