Contract fees are non-refundable for all graduate students.

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
On the basis of Government Decision (No. 447, 15.08.2022)
contract fees of all women studying at the master's level of state HEIs are paid from the state budget funds without the condition of refund
This procedure also applies to the Academy of Public Administration under the President, the Academy of the General Prosecutor's Office, the Higher School of Business and Entrepreneurship under the Ministry of Economy and Finance, and the Academy of Banking and Finance.

On the basis of the government decision (No. 447, 15.08.2022), the contract fees of all women studying at the master's level of state higher education institutions will be refunded. unconditionally reimbursed from the state budget funds.

This procedure also applies to the Academy of Public Administration under the President, the Academy of the General Prosecutor's Office, the Higher School of Business and Entrepreneurship under the Ministry of Economy and Finance, and the Academy of Banking and Finance.

❗️For this purpose, the applicant must pay the full amount of the contract to the rector (branch director) Applications will be submitted by September 15.

⛔️ Not applicable:

- to applications submitted after September 15;

- to differentiated fee-contract payments;

- to joint education.

Applications will be considered in 3 working days and a list will be formed.

The summarized list of applicants will be sent to the relevant ministries and agencies according to the departmental affiliation of the Higher Education Institution in order to cover the payment contracts within 5 working days.

The order of the rector (branch director) of the relevant Higher Education Institution will be accepted until October 15 of the current academic year. — Source
