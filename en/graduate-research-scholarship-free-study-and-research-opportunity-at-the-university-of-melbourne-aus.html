Graduate Research Scholarship is organized by the University of Melbourne, Australia, and allows students of all majors to study at this university for free at the master's and doctoral stages. About 600 candidates will be awarded this grant. Duration of the program:

  • 2 years for the Master’s level
  • 4 years for the Doctoral level

Benefits of the program:

  • Free from contract payment during the program
  • Master students receive up to 2 years and PhD students receive up to 3.5 years of living expenses $31,200 per year (this amount may change each year depending on various circumstances (such as illness, pregnancy and childcare))
  • For students moving from outside Australia $3000 provided
  • Medical insurance coverage for international students

Applicant requirements:

  • Candidate must have applied to and met the entry requirements for a University of Melbourne research MSc or PhD, or be currently enrolled in a research MSc or PhD at the University of Melbourne

Apply to the program procedure:

  • If you are a freshman and apply for a graduate research course before the application deadline, you will automatically be accepted for a Graduate Research Scholarship.
  • If you are a master's or doctoral student who has been deferred for scholarship review, you must submit an online application form by October 31st for next year's scholarship.
