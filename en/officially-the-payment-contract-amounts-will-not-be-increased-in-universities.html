Officially: the payment-contract amounts will not be increased in universities

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
As a result of the special attention paid by the head of our state, Shavkat Mirziyoyev, to the education system of our country, the fee-contract amounts for the 2023/2024 academic year in all areas will not be increased.
Relief for students and their parents and taking into account their support and the population's ability to pay, as an exception, the cost of teaching students at all levels on a fee-contract basis for the 2023/2024 academic year will be maintained at the basic fee-contract cost for the 2022/2023 academic year in state higher education institutions, including universities with financial independence.
At the same time In addition, the amounts of differentiated payment-contracts for the 2023/2024 academic year will remain unchanged at the amounts set for the previous academic year.

As a result of the special attention paid by our head of state Shavkat Mirziyoyev to the education system of our country, the amount of payment-contracts will not be increased in all areas for the 2023/2024 academic year. For the 2022/2023 academic year, it will be kept in the specified amounts.

Alternatively, the amounts of differentiated fee-contracts for the academic year 2023/2024 will remain the same as the amounts set for the previous academic year. from the beginning of the academic year, it was allowed to draw up contracts, as well as to accept students who paid 50% of the cost of one year's education.

Also, another relief - a 10% discount is given to applicants who paid the approved differentiated fee-contract value in full by October 15, 2023. — Source
