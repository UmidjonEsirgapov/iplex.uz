The Deanship of Graduate Studies at King Fahd University : QS 2022 is located in Saudi Arabia, which ranks 163i in the world ranking. King Fahd University offers fully funded MS and PhD scholarships to support young people who have international high potential and are strongly interested in natural sciences, engineering, and business fields for master's and doctoral degrees.

Online admission for this program 2 times per year (09.08.2021 the first spring admission; 01.25.2022 fall admission.)

Required documents for online admission:

  • Passport copy;
  • Bachelor diploma for master's level or master's degree Required for PhD;
  • 1-page essay (write with emphasis on your goals for research and future profession (Statement of Purpose));
  • 2 Recommendations;
  • Accomplishments at the undergraduate or graduate level;
  • GMAT (for international MBA courses);
  • CV;
  • Passport size picture;

Documents that may be needed for additional grant:

  • TOEFL (min. 68 iBT for master’s degree and 79 iBT for doctoral degree) or IELTS score (min. is 6.0 for master’s degree and 6.5 for doctoral degree);

Benefits of the program:

  • The amount of the contract is fully covered;
  • Accommodation is provided with separate textbooks;
  • Discounts are available for meals in the university canteen;

The deadline for registering to the program is 20.09.2021!
