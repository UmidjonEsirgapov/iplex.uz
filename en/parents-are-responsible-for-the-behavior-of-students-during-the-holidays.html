Parents are responsible for the behavior of students during the holidays

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Responsibility for the behavior of students outside the educational institution during the school holidays is placed directly on the parents.
On June 27, 2023, according to order No. 190 of the Minister of Preschool and School Education, from the territory of the educational institution outside, including during the holidays, parents are responsible for the behavior of students according to the law.
In this order, class leaders or deputy principals of the school are not held responsible.

According to these legal grounds, parents should pay special attention to their children's health, spiritual and moral development.
