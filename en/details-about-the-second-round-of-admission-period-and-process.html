The second stage of admission for applicants who participated in the entrance tests to higher and professional educational institutions is from July 27, 2024 It will last until August 10. During these 15 days, applicants must choose higher education institutions and educational fields.

How is the admission process carried out?

  1. Access to the platform: The applicant enters the my.uzbmb.uz platform, clicks the "Use service" button in the "Admission to higher and professional educational institutions" section and goes to the "Select HEI" section
  2. Selection priority and targeted admission: The applicant first makes his choice according to "Selection priority" and "Targeted admission".
  3. Choosing educational fields: up to five higher education institutions and educational fields with the form of education corresponding to the set of subjects and the language of education that the applicant passed the test chooses together. These directions can be selected within one higher education institution or different higher education institutions.
  4. Save and confirm: After selecting the education directions and pressing the "Save" button, an SMS message will be sent to the applicant's phone number to confirm the information. The data is confirmed by entering the code in the SMS message into the program.
  5. Record form: After the data is confirmed, the "Applicant record form" is created and can be downloaded.

Possibility to change the data

Applicants can change their data and register they can re-download the form.
