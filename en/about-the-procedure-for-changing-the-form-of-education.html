Changing the form of education in higher education institutions

If a student wants to change the form of education (full-time, part-time, evening, distance), this issue is resolved directly at the university where he is studying. Important aspects:

    Considered on the basis of the application.

  • The capabilities of the higher education institution (audience capacity, number of teachers, etc.) are taken into account.

Transfer by changing the form of education in state higher education institutions

Moving from one state higher education institution to another

In this case, it is allowed to change the form of education and transfer to a higher education institution of another state only on the basis of the conclusion of the Ministry. Permissible cases:

  1. If the marriage is concluded and the spouse lives in another region.

  2. If the civil servant moves to another region for work and it is necessary to transfer the education of the spouse or child (on the basis of a letter from the relevant ministry).

Transition from non-state or foreign HEIs to state HEIs

In this case, changing the form of education is carried out in the following order:

  • The student must have received enough points from the tests.

  • Change the form of education, taking into account the material and technical base of the state HEIs transferring studies.

Changing the form of education in a non-state higher education institution

If a student wants to change the form of education in a non-state higher education institution, this situation is considered by the internal commission of the university and a decision is made.

Conclusion

The procedure for changing the form of education depends on various factors. and the consent of the relevant organization or university is required in each case. The most important thing is that any change is made on a legal basis and within the available possibilities.
