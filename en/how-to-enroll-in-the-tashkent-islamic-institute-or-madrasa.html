How to enroll in the Tashkent Islamic Institute or madrasa?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Admission to the Tashkent Islamic Institute named after Imam Bukhari under the jurisdiction of the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan and other religious educational institutions is carried out by passing social examinations and tests. Applicants with the highest scores in social examinations and tests will be admitted to study on the basis of payment-contracts according to the admission quotas approved by the relevant decision of the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan.

In this process, a competition will be held for all applicants on the basis of equal rights and uniform admission rules, and applicants who have scored the highest points in social exams and tests will be admitted to study on the basis of a fee-contract according to the admission quotas approved by the relevant decision of the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan. — Read on infoedu.uz
