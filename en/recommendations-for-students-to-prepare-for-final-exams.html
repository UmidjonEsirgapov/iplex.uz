Winter vacation is over and final exams are approaching. Follow these helpful tips to prepare yourself as much as possible for this important process: — Read on infoedu.uz

1. Make a clear plan

  • Make a daily or weekly program for each subject.
  • Try to cover all topics on time.

2. Study the study material in parts

  • Don't try to study too much at once.
  • It is more effective to read the material in small parts.

3. Don't forget to revise

  • Write short notes after each topic and revise them.
  • Repetition makes it easier to remember.

4. Do practical exercises

  • Solve old exam questions, try working in groups with other students.

5. Pay attention to rest and sleep

  • Take breaks after long study sessions.
  • Reading in the morning or during the day is more beneficial than studying at night.

6. Manage stress

  • Try to keep a high mood when exams are approaching.
  • Avoid negative thoughts and control your mental state.

7. Eat right

  • Nutritious food and regular physical activity gives energy.
  • It helps to improve concentration.

8. Use additional resources

  • Ask teachers for help, use online resources or additional lessons.

9. Keep a positive attitude

  • Be confident and positive about assignments.

10. Create a comfortable environment

  • Work in a light and quiet environment to read books and textbooks.
