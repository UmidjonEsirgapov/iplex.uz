National certificate exams in the subject of mother tongue and literature will be held in all regions on January 29, 2023, reports the State Testing Center.

Tests will be held in the subjects of Uzbek language and literature, Russian language and literature, and Karakalpak language and literature, starting at 10:00. Applicants will be given 45 tasks and 3 hours to complete the answer sheet in the test.

According to the test result, a certificate valid for 3 years will be presented to those who at least 60% of the highest score.
