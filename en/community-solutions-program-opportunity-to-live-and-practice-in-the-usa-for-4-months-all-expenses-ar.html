General Information:

Community Solutions Program 2022: This program aimed at identifying and improving the problems through this program, based on the retraining of qualified young personnel from all over the world and the further development of their leadership skills and the creation of positive changes in their societies after their return to their countries. Candidates selected within the framework of this program will undergo 4 months of training in the USA. There is a specific list of countries where this program is valid (Uzbekistan is also on the list).

The benefits of the program:

  • Participants are provided with J-1 visa assistance;
  • The scholarship covers the travel expenses of the participants from their cities to the USA;
  • A monthly allowance for the participants for housing, meals and other living expenses
  • Participant accident and sickness insurance is covered;

Requirements for candidates:

  • Candidate must be 25-38 years old (as of January 1, 2022);
  • Candidates must have two years of experience in team development;
  • Applicant must be current must not be a student or currently participating in research projects conducted in the US;
  • Language certificates are requested IELTS/TOEFL (if semi-finalist);
  • Candidate must be able to visit the US for four months from August to December 2022;
  • Applicants must return to their home country for at least two years after the end of the program;
  • Applicant must not be a current IREX employee or must not be a close relative as an IREX employee.
