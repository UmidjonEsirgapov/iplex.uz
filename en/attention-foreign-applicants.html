Attention foreign applicants!

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Applicants who are currently abroad and have submitted online applications for admission for the 2020/21 academic year must return to Uzbekistan at least 3 weeks before the entrance exams, it was announced at AOKA on June 25 at the briefing.
According to statement No. 1 of the State Commission, applicants from abroad should arrive at least three weeks before the start of the tests.
After they have fully fulfilled the quarantine requirements, they will be included in the tests based on the conclusions presented by the Sanitary-Epidemiological and Peace Agency.

Currently abroad, Applicants who submitted online applications for admission for the 2020/21 academic year must return to Uzbekistan at least 3 weeks before the entrance exams, it was reported at a briefing held at AOKA on June 25.

According to Statement No. 1 of the State Commission, applicants from abroad should arrive at least three weeks before the start of the tests.

They must meet the quarantine requirements. after completing them, they will be included in the tests based on the conclusions presented by the Agency for Sanitation, Epidemiology and Peace.

According to the explanation given by the State Testing Center to Kun.uz regarding this procedure, the applicant will not be able to participate in the entrance exams if he does not return to Uzbekistan within three weeks before the exam, or if the virus is detected in him during quarantine. — Read on infoedu.uz
