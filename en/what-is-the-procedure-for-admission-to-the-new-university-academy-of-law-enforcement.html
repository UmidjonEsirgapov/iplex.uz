What is the procedure for admission to the New University - Academy of Law Enforcement?

About the expected changes in the educational system:

Brief content:
A new Law Enforcement Academy was established in Uzbekistan by the relevant decree of our President.
A new Law Enforcement Academy has been established in Uzbekistan.

Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, a two-level system of higher education in investigative activities and other areas of law enforcement will be organized in the academy - bachelor's and master's degrees.

Bachelor's education is full-time education.

The press service of the Prosecutor General's Office informed "Khabar.uz" that the duration of education at the Academy for a bachelor's program is 3 years, and for a master's program in the field of investigation - 1 year. Graduates are awarded diplomas of the state model confirming that they have completed the appropriate stage of higher legal education.

When admitted to the Academy, certain categories of applicants are not eligible for admission to other higher education institutions of the republic, as provided for in the legislative documents.

The annual admission quotas of the Academy in the field of investigative activity are up to 250 students under the bachelor's program.

Investigative activity employees of inquiry and preliminary investigation bodies with a higher legal education can be trained in the master's degree program, and the admission quota is up to 50.

The admission quotas for other specialties of full-time and part-time master's degrees currently available at the Academy are determined by the Prosecutor General's Office together with the Ministry of Economic Development and Poverty Alleviation.

The Academy's master's degree in the field of investigation Training of employees of authorized state bodies enrolled in full-time studies, as well as employees of prosecutor's offices enrolled in full-time master's studies in other specialties, is carried out free of charge, and their current position and average monthly salary are maintained during the study period.
