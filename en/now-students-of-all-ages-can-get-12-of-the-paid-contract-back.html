Now students of all ages can get 12% of the paid contract back

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
From August 1, 2023, payments made by taxpayers for their children's education in educational institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan, regardless of age, will be included in the category of non-taxable income.
This procedure applies to all stages of education - before school, will be introduced into the school and higher education system.
Presidential Decree According to the current regulations, only 12 percent of the income tax could be recovered from the contract payment made to a child under the age of 26.

Starting from August 1, 2023, taxpayers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, regardless of their age Payments for education in educational institutions are included in the category of non-taxable income. Presidential decree

According to the current regulation, income tax could be recovered only from the contract payment of 12 percent to a child under 26 years of age. Now there is no age limit.
