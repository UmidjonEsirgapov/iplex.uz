What are the main reasons for not being able to get a student visa? What are the most common reasons why an F-1 visa application is rejected by the US Embassy?

Happy Schools Blog recently shared information about this. Based on interviews with more than 100 people who did not get a student visa, it tells about the 10 most common reasons for not getting an F-1 visa for universities in the United States. Let's list these reasons one by one below:

1. Not knowing English well;
2. Low GRE score;
3. Low TOEFL/IELTS score;
4. Low academic performance;
5. Failing some courses and exams;
6. High TOEFL score and low GRE score (TOEFL score may be fake);
7. Failure to fully answer questions in the interview;
8. Forged documents (forgery of financial and bank documents, grades, diplomas and scores);
9. Self-doubt;
10. Trying to cheat in an interview.
