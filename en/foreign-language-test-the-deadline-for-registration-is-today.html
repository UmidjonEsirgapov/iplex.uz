Register for tests of the level of knowledge of a foreign language ends

Registration period and payment

Please be aware that on January 31 of this year, registration for English, German, French, Arabic, Turkish, Korean (B2; C1), Pashto (B2; C1) and Urdu (B2; C1) foreign language proficiency tests will be completed.

The payment for the test must be made by February 1. Taking this into account, it is important not to delay the registration.

Choosing the date and region of the tests

The tests are scheduled to be held on February 21-22, 2026. Exams are organized in January-May in order to determine the level of knowledge of a foreign language.

During the registration process, applicants will have the opportunity to choose an area that is convenient for them and take part in the tests in that area.

Registration procedure

Applicants for the test through the site my.gov.uz They can register online.

According to the earlier changes made by the Cabinet of Ministers to the procedure for admission to bachelor's degrees of higher education institutions:

  • Regardless of whether a foreign language is the first or second subject, a B1 certificate is also accepted.

  • The maximum set of this subject 75 percent of the score will be awarded.

Important notes

  • Registration last day is January 31.

  • Please make payment before February 1 to avoid delay.

  • Area you have a choice, so choose a region that is convenient for you.

  • Exams will be held only for applicants registered through my.gov.uz.

Conclusion

Registration for foreign language proficiency tests is coming to an end. Applicants should not miss the last day and make the payment on time. The tests will be held on February 21-22, 2026 and all information is provided online through the my.gov.uz site. — Official Data
