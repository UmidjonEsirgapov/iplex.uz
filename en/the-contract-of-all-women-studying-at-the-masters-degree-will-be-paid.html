The contract of all women studying at the master's degree will be paid

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The state will cover the contract money of women studying at the master's degree. In accordance with the presidential decree, the contract money of all women studying at the master's level in state higher education institutions will be paid by the state in full and without the condition of return.
Contract How will the payment be covered?
So, the contract money of women studying at the master's degree will be paid by the state. the women's contract money will be paid by the state in full and without the condition of return. This privilege is an important part of the family and women's support policy.

How will the contract fee be paid?

  • Every year before September 15 female graduate students submit an application in the name of the rector of the higher education institution where they are studying.

  • The higher education institution collects the applications and sends them to the relevant ministry.

  • Until October 15, the rector will issue an order and the contract amount will be reimbursed by the state.

⚠️ If the application is submitted after September 15, the contract fee will not be reimbursed.

Requirement of a foreign language certificate

A foreign language certificate is mandatory when entering the master's program. is:

  • For philology education areas - at least C1 level,

  • For other education areas - at least B2 level.

Does it also apply to evening and distance education?

According to the current legislation, only full-time There is no limit to the type of education that will be covered. According to the presidential decree and government decisions, master's degree includes all forms - full-time, evening and distance education. So, in any form, the contract money of the women studying at the master's degree is also paid by the state.

Conclusion

This privilege introduced by the state greatly helps to increase the education and scientific potential of women.
