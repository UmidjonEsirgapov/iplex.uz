From July 1, 2025, pensions and allowances will be increased by 10%

According to the presidential decree, starting from July 1, 2025, a number of payments will be increased by 10% in order to strengthen the social protection of the population. These changes apply to pensions, benefits and financial assistance payments.

List of increased payments:

  • Pensions

  • Benefits

  • Child benefits for low-income families

  • Financial assistance payments

The new amounts are as follows:

  • Minimum old-age pension — 918,000 soums

  • Disability pension — 1,012,000 soums

  • Child allowance:

    • One child up to 0-3 years — 360,000 soums

    • One child up to 3-18 years — 275,000 soums

    • Additional for the second child — 165,000 soums

    • For the third and subsequent children — 110,000 Soums

  • Financial assistance (per month) — 420,000 soums

Salaries and scholarships will be increased from August 1, 2025
