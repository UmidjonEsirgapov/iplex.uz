General Information:

"Global peace Chain" proposed a unique idea to increase the potential of young people within the framework of the 21-Youth World Cup program. This fully funded International Youth World Championship is a recognition of the contribution, struggle and achievements of the UN in achieving the Sustainable Development Goals. One of the main goals of Global Peace Chain is to show to the world the passion for modern youth poverty alleviation, gender equality, healthcare, art, technology, as well as freedom and justice promotion by gathering extraordinarily high-potential young people in Turkey. Candidates have the right to participate in the contest as an ordinary participant or a delegate. The competition will be held on October 27-29 in Istanbul, Turkey.

Program benefits:

  • $10,000 prize money to the winner of the competition;
  • $1,000 prize money to the 2 place winners in each direction;
  • Furthermore, 50 quarter-final participants will be reimbursed for their travel expenses to Turkey;
  • 15 fully-reimbursed grants for delegates who have places available;
  • 25 partially reimbursable grant programs for delegates;
  • Opportunity to participate in international conferences in Turkey;
  • Gold, silver, bronze and valuable gifts and diplomas will be awarded;

Note: There is a $40 fee for delegate registration and a $30 fee for regular attendees;

Required documents and admission process:

  • Register online here;
  • Good quality photo;
  • Pictures taken while doing 3 exercises;
  • A 5-second video of someone doing an exercise;
  • 5-10 seconds of you tube should be appreciated;
