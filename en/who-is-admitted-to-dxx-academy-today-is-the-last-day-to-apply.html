Who is admitted to DXX Academy? Today is the last day to apply

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to the Regulation approved by the Government Decision (No. 241, 27.04.2021), students who are deemed fit to study at the DXX Academy in the field of bachelor's education, according to their health, are not less than 170 cm tall and in the prescribed manner Candidates of the following age limits (male citizens of Uzbekistan) who successfully passed the competition are accepted: 17 to 23 years old, including those who will turn 17 in the year of admission, graduates of general secondary, secondary special and professional educational institutions; Reservists who are between the ages of 18 and 25, including those who will turn 18 in the year of enrollment, who do not have officer military ranks, have general secondary, secondary specialized and professional education; Military servicemen between the ages of 18 and 25 who have general secondary, secondary specialized and professional education and are serving in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan on a fixed-term and contract basis; 1st and 2nd year (in the year of admission) students of higher education institutions of the republic who are under 26 years old and have expressed their desire to study at the Academy in the field of undergraduate education.
In this case, the candidates who have successfully passed all stages of selection will start studying at the Academy from the first year.
