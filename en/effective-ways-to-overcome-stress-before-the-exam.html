Effective ways to overcome stress before the exam

The exam process is exciting for many. Some try to study until the last minute, while others get nervous and lose concentration. The following recommendations will help to reduce stress before the exam and prepare effectively.

Important aspects of preparation

  • Stop preparing 17-18 hours before the exam.As a result of studying with great pressure in the last minutes, the information will be remembered less. Experience shows that reading too much close to an exam will reduce your ability to remember.
  • Avoid sloppy studying.Swimming through the material before an exam will only lead to confusion. This forces you to rely only on luck during the test or oral answer process.
  • Prepare the brain for relaxation.Excessive exhaustion of the brain before the exam will have a negative effect on the results. Therefore, the last minutes should be devoted not only to preparation, but also to rest.
  • Useful activities to reduce stress
  • Take a walk and take a shower.Walking outside, breathing fresh air and taking a warm shower calms the nervous system.
  • Relieve stress through laughter.Watch comedy or humorous programs. reduces stress, improves sleep and normalizes appetite. This is one of the scientifically proven methods.
  • Don't forget to get enough sleep.Sufficient sleep before the exam is important for good brain function. Avoiding coffee, chocolate, cola and strong tea in the afternoon improves sleep quality.
  • Psychological preparation and visualization techniques
  • Prepare the exam process in your mind.Try to create the following scene in your imagination:

  • You entered the exam hall.
  • Ticket or test paper.
  • You calmly start to answer.
  • The supervisors are satisfied with your knowledge.
  • You finally got the result you wanted.

Repeating this technique over and over prepares you mentally and reduces excitement.

  • Eating properly is also important.Dinner is rich in carbohydrates. it is recommended to choose dishes. Protein-rich products should be consumed less. Drinking a glass of warm water with honey before going to bed helps to strengthen the nervous system.

Conclusion

It is not enough to prepare for the exam based on knowledge alone. Thinking, visualization and relaxing the body also affect the results. These recommendations will help reduce stress before the exam and increase the effectiveness of preparation. — More information
