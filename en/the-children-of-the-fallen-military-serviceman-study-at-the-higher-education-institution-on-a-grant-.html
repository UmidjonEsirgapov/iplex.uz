The children of the fallen military serviceman study at the Higher Education Institution on a grant basis

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The children of the fallen military servicemen will be provided with education on the basis of a state grant.
Parents of a fallen military serviceman are given the right to use railway and air passenger transport twice a year free of charge.

Children of a fallen military serviceman are provided with education in higher education institutions on the basis of a state grant

According to the decree of the President, from June 1, 2023, the children of a fallen military serviceman can go to school free education in educational institutions, as well as additional quotas of education in professional and higher education institutions on the basis of state grants.

The parents of a fallen military serviceman are given the right to use railway and air passenger transport twice a year for free. — More information
