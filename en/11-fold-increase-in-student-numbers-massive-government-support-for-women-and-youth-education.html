Uzbekistan's higher education system has seen sharp growth and systematic expansion in recent years. In his speech, the President touched on main achievements and strategic directions in this regard.

The scope of higher education is expanding

  • In the past, at least 10 applicants fought for 1 higher education place, now:

    • The number of universities has increased by 3 times;

    • The coverage level in the system has reached 42%.

The number of female students has increased 11 times

  • Women's contracts are covered by the state as a result, the number of female students increased 11 times.

  • The president said in this regard:

    "If we educate one girl, we will educate the whole family. If the family is educated, the whole society will be educated."

Education opportunities abroad is expanding

  • In the last 25 years, 800 young people have been sent to study abroad at the expense of the state,

  • In the last 7 years, through the "El-Yurt Umid" fund, this figure has increased 3 times.

The state's new commitment: to support students abroad

    • Provide encouragement and support,
