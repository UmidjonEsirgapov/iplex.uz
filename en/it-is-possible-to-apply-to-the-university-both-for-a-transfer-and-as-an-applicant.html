It is possible to apply to the university both for a transfer and as an applicant

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
A student who wants to transfer his studies can also submit a document to the university as an applicant.
For this, between June 15 and July 15, apply to a higher education institution as an applicant through the website my.uzbmb.uz and it is enough to enter the diploma or certificate number on the site.
If he submitted this document to his university, if he does not know the number, he may enter the wrong number.

He has the opportunity to submit an application for "perevod" through the relevant website during the admission process for transferring studies starting on July 15. It is enough to upload the transcript.

Since the test for the applicant and the "passengers" will not be held at the same time, he can easily enter the exam and after passing it successfully, he will have to choose one according to his wishes.

It is not forbidden to apply for two different processes at the same time, and this will not cause any problems for the student. — Read on infoedu.uz
