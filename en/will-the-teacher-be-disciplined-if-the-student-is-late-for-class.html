If the student is late for class, is the teacher subject to disciplinary punishment?

According to the orders of the Ministry of Public Education No. 206 and 393 , the main responsibility of teachers one of their tasks is to monitor student attendance and record this information in the class log.

However, it is not their responsibility to ensure that the student attends the class. If the student is late for class or does not come at all, the teacher will not be subject to disciplinary action. According to Article 47 of the Code of Administrative Responsibility, if a student is late or does not come to class without an excuse, his parents will be fined 1 to 5 times the amount of the basic calculation. up to bari, i.e. from 330 thousand soums to 1 million 650 thousand soums. — Source
