Separate classes were organized for students interested in the teaching profession - President

This year 14 schools are pedagogues. Separate classes were organized for 10th-11th graders who are interested in the profession.

From the next academic year, this experience will be introduced in 60 more schools, including 10 specialized schools.

In the future, if the students who graduated from these classes enter universities in this direction, they will be admitted to the school starting from the 2nd year. an opportunity will be created to improve their professional skills. Specially gifted students are also paid a monthly allowance for attending classes. Our president also proposed to establish a scholarship named after Abdulla Avloni for talented students studying in pedagogic universities.
