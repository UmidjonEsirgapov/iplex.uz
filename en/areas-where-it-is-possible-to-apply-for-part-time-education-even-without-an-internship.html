Areas where it is possible to apply for part-time education even without an internship

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
It is allowed to apply for part-time education even if there is no internship in some pedagogical fields. According to the statement of the State Admission Commission, any applicants can apply for part-time studies in the following fields: Pedagogy; Pre-school education; History; Physical education and sports in preschool and primary education; National idea, foundations of spirituality and legal education.
At least 5 years of work experience related to pedagogical activity is not required to apply for part-time study of these educational areas.
