Correspondence education is organized at the master's degree of the higher business school

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The draft order of the Minister of Economic Development and Poverty Alleviation "On approval of the procedure for organizing part-time education at the Master's degree of the Higher School of Business and Entrepreneurship" was announced.
External education uses distance learning technology.
The admission of students to extramural education is carried out in accordance with the regulation on the procedure for admission to the master's degree of the Higher School of Business and Entrepreneurship and its organization, approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 460 of July 30, 2020.

The Minister of Economic Development and Poverty Alleviation The draft of the order "On approval of the procedure for the organization of part-time education at the higher school of business and entrepreneurship" was published.

The order was developed in the framework of the implementation of the Law "On Education", the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On measures to organize the activities of the higher school of business and entrepreneurship", and Business and entrepreneurship determines the procedure for the organization of education in the higher school without separating it from production in master's specialties.

Part-time education in the higher school is organized in accordance with the admission quotas approved by the relevant order of the Ministry of Economic Development and Poverty Alleviation.

Part-time education is carried out in correspondence form using distance education technology.

The procedure only applies to higher school master's specialties will be implemented.

The admission of students to extramural education is carried out in accordance with the regulation on the procedure for admission to the master's degree of the Higher School of Business and Entrepreneurship and its organization, approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated July 30, 2020 No. 460.

The period of study in extramural education:

    5A230302 – Master of Business Administration (MBA) 2 years; 5A230203 – Project management (PM) 1.5 years.

    External education students are provided with educational literature and educational materials by the higher school and create the necessary conditions for learning.

    External education students use all educational equipment, auditoriums, information resource centers (library) and educational support structures of the higher school free of charge.

    Exclusion of external education students from the audience and resuming studies is carried out in accordance with the regulation on the procedure for transferring, resuming and expelling students of higher education institutions, approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated June 20, 2017 No. 393. however, it is not allowed to transfer from one specialization of part-time education to another. — Details here
