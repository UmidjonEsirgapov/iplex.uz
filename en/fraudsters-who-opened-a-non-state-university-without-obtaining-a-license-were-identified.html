Fraudsters who opened a non-state university without obtaining a license were identified

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
During the pre-investigation investigation carried out by the Department of the General Prosecutor's Office in cooperation with the employees of the Regional State Security Service, citizens A.A. (indicted in absentia for the crime of fraud and wanted) and Sh.A.
conspired with each other to establish a non-state university in the form of LLC in 2022, obtained a total of 152.9 million soums from 18 citizens who expressed their desire to study at this institution, and used it for their own needs
Similarly, in the pre-investigation investigation conducted by the Department's Denov district department on the protection of the rights and interests of citizens, citizen H.M. (guilty was announced in absentia for the crime of fraud and a search was announced) and Sh.A. It was found that 18 citizens who colluded to establish a non-state university in the form of LLC in 2022 and wanted to study at this institution obtained a total of 152.9 million soums and used them for their own needs. H.M. was found to have fraudulently obtained a total of 15,300 US dollars in exchange for transferring the studies of 21 students studying in the neighboring Republic of Tajikistan to higher educational institutions in the Republic of Uzbekistan with the help of his acquaintances working in high positions.
