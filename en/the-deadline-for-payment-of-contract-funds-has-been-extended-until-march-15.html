The deadline for payment of contract funds has been extended until March 15

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the relevant decision of the State Commission, in the 2022/2023 academic year, it is recommended for students to study in professional educational institutions (college, technical institute) on the basis of a fee-contract for bachelor and master's degrees of higher education institutions, including a differentiated fee-contract. The deadline for transfer of tuition fees for applicants, transferred (replaced) and reinstated students and formalization of admission to the ranks of students is March 15, 2023. Also, by the decision, the payment of those who paid their contract after January 20, 2023 will be taken into account.
This is reported by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.
