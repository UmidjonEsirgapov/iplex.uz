General Information:

Central European University Scholarship in Hungary and Austria : For the 2022-2023 academic year in Hungary and Austria for undergraduate, graduate and doctoral levels Central European University Scholarship(CEU) grant programs have been announced. There are no admission fees, and IELTS/TOEFL certificates are not required to know the same language.

The grant includes food, accommodation, medical insurance expenses:

  • CEU Master’s Excellence Scholarship – 1,000 EUR/month
  • CEU Master’s Distinction Scholarship – 750 EUR/month
  • CEU Doctoral Scholarships: 1,350 EUR/Month
  • 100% contract fees paid;
  • Medical insurance;
  • Stipends for living expenses;

Required documents

  • Education certificate (high school diploma, university or college diploma/certificate or equivalent; all in English (translation
  • Proof of citizenship (e.g. passport);
  • Brief CV;
  • Medical certificate;
  • Citizens, citizens or permanent residents of the United States applying to the university's medical program must submit the results of the Medical College Admission Test (MCAT);
  • IELTS/TOEFL language certificate (if available), English proficiency from another university degree;

Eligibility criteria

  • Representatives of all nationalities can apply;
  • Students must have at least 12 years of education for admission to the bachelor program;
  • Applicants must have a bachelor's/bachelor's degree to apply for the master's program;
  • Candidates for admission to the doctoral program must have a master's degree and must have bachelor degrees/transcripts;
  • Students must have a good academic record;
  • Language requirements : If you have a level of English proficiency, you can be exempted and your English language proficiency letter will be accepted;
