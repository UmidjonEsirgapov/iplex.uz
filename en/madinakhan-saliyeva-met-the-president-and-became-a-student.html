Madinakhan Soliyeva, who spoke with President Shavkat Mirziyoyev at the "Yangi Uzbekistan" massif in Fergana, became a student of the Fergana University of World Languages.

Madinakhan Soliyeva Fergana came to the city to pass the exam and lived in a rented house. It turned out that he lives in Zinasha MFY in Uzbekistan district.

During the President's trip to Fergana, Madina Khan was lucky enough to meet the President while he was walking in the alley with his nephew.

According to Madina Khan's words:

"My nephew and I were walking in the alley. Wait. The head of state visited and talked with the citizens. I told the president about my wishes.
