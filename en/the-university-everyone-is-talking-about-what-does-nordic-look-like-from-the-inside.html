Nordic University is not just a place of study, but an educational bridge connecting Uzbekistan and Europe. Today we take a look at this university from the inside, which has become one of the most discussed private higher education institutions among young people and parents in recent years, based on interviews and real experiences. the door to education - Nordic

Nordic University implements double degree, joint degree and exchange programs in cooperation with prestigious universities of countries such as Finland, USA, Italy, South Korea and Malaysia. Students have the opportunity to study in cooperation with Karelian University of Finland, Trent University of Italy, Rowan and Lawrence universities in the USA.

Work & Travel: Student's dream is now a reality

Every year, the university sends hundreds of students to Finland through the "work and travel" program. The participants of the program will not only travel around Europe, but also earn up to 3,000-4,000 euros and have the opportunity to pay for the contract themselves. This experience is an important step not only for financial, but also for personal development.

Student life full of clubs, festivals and performances

Extracurricular time at Nordic is also rich: every day of the week is full of clubs and activities. Speaking club, personal development club, acting classes, even performances staged by students - this makes the university a center not only for education, but also for creative development.

Finnish model and international teachers

The main idea of the university is to introduce the Finnish model of education in Uzbekistan, and for this purpose, classes are held in English, Finnish and Russian. will go Lessons are based on interactive, open dialogue, and students can freely express their opinion.

Terms and prices

Teaching is available in full-time and part-time forms. The cheapest contract price is 11 million soums (primary education), and the most expensive one is 22 million soums (industrial management, IT fields). The university has three campuses, modern auditoriums and study opportunities in various fields.
