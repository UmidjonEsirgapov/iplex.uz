Attention of applicants who want to become a lawyer with a double international diploma

We have a great opportunity for you. We are doing it!

✅ Tashkent State University of Law and Yanka Kupala State University of Grodno (GrDU, Republic of Belarus) have introduced a joint educational program at the bachelor's level in private international law and are starting to accept applicants.

Documents for admission will be accepted by GrSU online from July 20 to August 10, 2020.

Submit documents by e-mail education@grsu.by or get more information on GrSU and TDYU official websites, as well as by phone (71) 233-13-95.

Please note: joint program applicants submit the document separately, not through the State Test Center. At the same time, this general procedure does not prohibit the parallel submission of documents.

The exam is conducted through an online interview on knowledge of the Russian language and motivation to study in the field of law. The study is conducted in Russian and partly in English. — Source
