DAAD EPOS Scholarship is a grant program to study International Health in Germany.

Application deadline: October 15, 2021

This International Health Scholarship program is one of the master's courses supported by the EPOS scholarship program of the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD). The program will take place at Charite University Medical Center in Berlin, Germany.

The grant covers:

  • educational expenses;
  • living expenses.

Requirements:

  • Candidate must be a citizen of one of the developing countries (Uzbekistan have);
  • 2 years of experience in the health sector;
  • English language proficiency.

Document submission process:

  • Online registration and application filling;
  • CV (curriculum vitae);
  • Motivational letter;
  • Research plan;
  • Recommendation letter from the current supervisor;
  • Reference from the place of work;
  • Document confirming language proficiency (IELTS 6/TOEFL 80);
  • Diploma and its grades (with translation).
