Miami University in the United States has announced its "International Scholarship" - up to 100% grant programs for those who want to study at the bachelor's level. Get acquainted with the directions of this university in detail here!

Benefits of the grant:

  • Those admitted to the program can be provided with a scholarship from $1,500 to $12,000 . However, other types, such as living expenses, air tickets, are not covered;

Requirements for the candidate:

  • The candidate must have graduated from school/college/lyceum with high grades;
  • Activity and achievements in social life will be taken into account;
  • Language level must be very good (at least IELTS 6.5 , TOEFL ( iBT 80));
  • If the candidate has SAT, ACT certificates, they can send them voluntarily;

Important: The deadline is December 1, 2021. There is no separate registration for this grant program, only candidates admitted to this university who meet the above requirements will become direct participants of the competition!
