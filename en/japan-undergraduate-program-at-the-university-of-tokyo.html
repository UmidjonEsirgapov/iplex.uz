Undergraduate program at the University of Tokyo

Where: Japan

Date: March 24, 2021

Place of study: University of Tokyo, Tokyo, Japan

Education period: 2 years

The Global Science Course Undergraduate student transfer program is designed to develop cultural connections among young promising students and promote the next generation of leaders. Students study together with students of the University of Tokyo, classes are conducted in English, participate in scientific projects, and work in the world's best laboratories. This is a great opportunity for science students to broaden their horizons and gain unforgettable experiences and impressions.

The university provides a monthly stipend of 150,000 yen to scholarship participants. This will be enough to cover tuition and living expenses. The university provides accommodation, students are not charged rent, only utility bills. Faculty of Science teachers help foreign students adjust to Tokyo. Students also take part in Japanese language courses, which helps to overcome the problem of language proficiency.

Requirements:
• completed the 2nd year of the bachelor's degree (62 credits);
• have a completed secondary education (in a country other than Japan);
• knowledge of the English language (TOEFL iBT — 80, TOEFL PBT — 550, IELTS — 6.5, Cambridge English: Advanced(CAE) — S) is required.

The application is submitted online with the following documents:

- personal information about the student;

- information about education (copies of the certificate of complete secondary education, transcript with grades, letter confirming admission to the university, summary of subjects studied at the university description);

- English language test (TOEFL iBT - 80, TOEFL PBT - 550, IELTS - 6.5, Cambridge English: Advanced(CAE) - C);

- letter of motivation;

- additional information (academic achievements, work experience, volunteer activities);

- contact information of 2 teachers for recommendation;

- copy of passport;

- declaration;

- registration fee (about $90).
