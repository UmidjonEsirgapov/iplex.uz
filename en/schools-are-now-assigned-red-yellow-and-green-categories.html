Schools are assigned "red", "yellow" and "green" categories

A new procedure is being introduced in the schools of Uzbekistan in order to fight against crime and strengthen prevention. Now each school is divided into categories based on the level of criminality of students.

What categories are there?

According to the new regulation, schools are classified as follows:

  • "Green" category - no crime by school students during the reporting period. not committed.

  • "Yellow" category - if 1-3 crimes were committed during the reporting period.

  • "Red" category - if more than 3 crimes were committed during the reporting period.

This system is in schools. It is being introduced with the aim of strengthening preventive work, ensuring social security and crime prevention. Appropriate measures will be taken based on the results of the category. ‚Äî Official Data
