Can applicants take the test with a driver's license?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Summary:
The Agency for the Evaluation of Knowledge and Skills said that it is necessary to come to the exam with a passport (ID card).
However, in fact, applicants can enter the tests with the following identity documents: Biometric passport (green color); ID card; Passport for going abroad (red color); Driver's license (only in the new form); military ID card for military personnel (not valid for those who do not work in the military).
The above are official documents confirming identity in the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan, and you can come to the test with any of them. But, actuallyapplicants can enter the tests with the following identity documents:
  • Biometric passport (green color);
  • ID card;
  • Exit passport (red color);
  • Driving license (only in new form);
  • Military card for military personnel (not valid for those who do not work in the military).

    • The above are official identity documents in the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan and you can come to the tests with any of them. Just don't forget to get applicant's permit along with the document.
