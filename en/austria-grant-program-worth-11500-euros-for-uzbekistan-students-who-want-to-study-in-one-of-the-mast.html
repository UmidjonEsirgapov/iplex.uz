Brief about the university and grant program:

Vienna University of Technology - founded in 1815, is one of the largest public universities in Vienna, Austria. It has a wide international and local reputation in the fields of pedagogy and scientific research, and is a university that organizes education based on innovative projects. The CASPIAN Scholarships grant program announced by this university provides a scholarship with a total value of 11,500 euros to excellent and talented students from the countries of Azerbaijan, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Turkmenistan, Iran and Russia for their master's degree education.

General details of the grant:

  • Applications for this program are accepted only from citizens of the countries of Azerbaijan, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Turkmenistan, Iran and Russia;
  • The grant provides a scholarship with a total value of 11,500 euros in the Engineering Management department of the university offers for talented students who want to study at the master's level. The proposed grant is paid to the university for students' tuition fees;
  • The grant is offered to candidates who have completed their bachelor's degree with excellent grades.

Application process:

  1. Candidates must first here the application form for study at the master's degree in Engineering Management. must fill out and submit other necessary documents;
  2. Candidates will be able to submit an application for the grant program upon admission;
  3. CV, passport copy (in JPEG format) and grade transcripts are required along with the completed application form for the grant program;
  4. Candidates must have at least B2 level international IELTS 5.5, TOEFL iBT 87, CAE 45 or must provide one of FCE 160 certificates.

End of admission: April 30, 2021
