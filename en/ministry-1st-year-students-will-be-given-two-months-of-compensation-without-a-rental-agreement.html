Ministry: 1st year students will be given two months of compensation without a rental agreement

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation announced that this year the approach to providing housing for students has changed and a new digitized system has been introduced. according to this, centers have been established to find and place rental apartments for students who do not have a dormitory in higher education institutions. This was discussed at the organized press tour.

According to the report, centers have been established to find and place rented apartments for students who do not have a dormitory in higher education institutions.

Help for students:

  • Students who cannot find a place in a dormitory can be accommodated in rented apartments with the help of special centers in HEIs. It is possible.
  • When the lease agreement is presented, aid money will be allocated for students living in Tashkent city in the amount of one times the BHM (currently 340 thousand soums), and in the regions 0.5 times (170 thousand soums). There are a total of 338 student residences in 112 universities in Uzbekistan, their capacity is 127,792 places. Projects on the construction of 86 new student residences with an additional 46,252 beds are being implemented throughout the country. At the moment, 67 new student residences with 34,320 beds are being built.
