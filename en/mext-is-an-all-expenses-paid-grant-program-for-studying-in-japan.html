General Information:

Japan MEXT Scholarship announces its 2022-2023 season. Scholarships are awarded for doctoral and master's degree programs in all disciplines taught at the University of Tokyo.

Benefits:

  • For masters - 144,000 yen monthly scholarship;
  • For doctoral students - 145,000 yen monthly scholarship.(Awarded based on 2021 data);
  • Expenses for coming to Japan;
  • Exempt from the contract fee;

Cancellation of the program is possible DETAILS

Who can apply:

  • Bachelor or master with excellent grades;
  • Have at least B2 in English or N2 in Japanese who are;
  • Born after October 2, 1987;
  • Mentally and physically healthy persons and others.

Application process:

  • Download and fill out the application form;
  • Study and research plan (LIST);
  • Diploma grades and translation;
  • Copy of passport;
  • Document confirming language proficiency;
  • Recommendation;
  • Brief information from graduation work;
  • 3.5x4.5cm photo for document;
  • Reference from last place of study.
