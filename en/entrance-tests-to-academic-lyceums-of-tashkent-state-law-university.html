Tests for admission to academic lyceums of Tashkent State Law University for the 2024-2025 academic year will be held on July 27, 2024 at 09:00.

Tests law and foreign language subjects. and intellectual ability level (IQ).

Applicants will be given a total of 60 test tasks, 20 of which are from each subject, and 2 hours will be allocated to work on them.

The maximum score that can be obtained in the tests is 126 points, and the following points will be given for each correct answer:

  • law subject — out of 3.1 points (total 62 points)
  • foreign language subject — out of 2.1 points (total 42 points)
  • in IQ — out of 1.1 points (total 22 points)

To participate in the test, applicants must obtain a permit from the Knowledge Assessment Agency can be obtained from the site lycey.uzbmb.uz.

Applicants should come to the area where the test will be held with identification document and permission to participate in the test no later than 08:00.
