Football development in Uzbekistan: President's decision and new opportunities

Legionaires club activities are underway put

On August 21, 2025, the President's decision PQ-262 established important measures to increase the digitalization and popularization of football in Uzbekistan. According to the decision, the "Legionaires club" will be established under the Football Association of Uzbekistan.

Highly qualified Uzbek football players and football specialists who are working or have worked abroad will be attracted to this club.

What opportunities will be created for the Legioner club?

  • Football academies and schools in the republic will provide club members with attached.

  • Empty land plots of state sports educational organizations will be given to members of the "Legionaires Club" on the basis of public-private partnership.

  • These land plots will be used for the establishment of non-state football educational organizations and football clubs.

The process of digitizing football academies

From January 1, 2026:

  • Football academies and schools, including sports schools with football sections, will be gradually connected to the information system.

  • On-line observation of training sessions, selection of players, and their medical and physical examination. digital monitoring system will be introduced.

Conclusion

The new decision not only creates wide opportunities for Uzbek legionnaire players, but it is also a big step for the development of football academies and clubs, modernization of infrastructure and training of young players at a professional level. — Read on infoedu.uz
