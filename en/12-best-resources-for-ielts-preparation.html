12 Best Resources for IELTS Preparation

IELTS is one of the most popular English language exams. The results of this test are accepted in the UK, Australia, European countries and almost all English-speaking universities in the world.

1) Elc.polyu.edu.hk - on this site you can familiarize yourself with the IELTS tasks and get many useful tips for preparing for each part of the exam.

2) Splendid-speaking.com - Interview with students who have passed the test.

3) Takeielts.britishcouncil.org - The British Council international organization conducts the IELTS exam in all countries of the world. On this site you will get a lot of useful information, presented in the form of tips and short articles.

4) Examenglish.com - On this site you can find many useful tests to help you practice before taking the IELTS. All tasks are given in the format of exam tasks.

5) ielts.allearsenglish.com - Another great site for IELTS preparation, which has a wide variety of tasks given in the exam.

6) Examword.com - This resource is not intended to practice speaking skills, but to improve your vocabulary.



7) Netlanguages.com - Here you can take several free practice tests.

8) Ieltsforfree.com - here you can take the whole IELTS test or practice individual parts.

9) Examenglish.com - on this site you can prepare not only for IELTS, but also for TOEFL, TOEIC, CAE, FCE, KET, PET exams.

10) Itests.com - registration free tests will be provided for you after you pass. You can also follow the process of exam preparation on the site.

11) Ielts-exam.net is one of the largest IELTS exam preparation sites. Here you will find all the information you need for each part of the exam.

12) Scribd.com - here you can find scanned IELTS exam preparation books.
