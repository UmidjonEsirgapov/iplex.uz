Special scholarships are paid to excellent female students from poor families

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Special scholarships will be paid to excellent female students from poor families. Starting from 2025, important benefits will be introduced in Uzbekistan for children of families in need of social protection.
From June 1, 2025: "Red" families will receive excellent grades. A special stipend will be assigned to female students who have studied.
Cost expenses will be fully covered. Also, children of poor families who study with "excellent" grades: can spend free time in camps within the system of the Federation of Trade Unions of Uzbekistan.

A special stipend will be paid to excellent female students from poor families

From 2025 Important benefits for children of families in need of social protection will be introduced in Uzbekistan. According to the presidential decree, educated and aspiring girls of "red" families are now given additional incentives.

Who are the "red" families?

The "red" category means families with a high level of poverty and deemed by the state to be in need of social assistance. A number of benefits have been established for the children of families included in this category.

How will the special scholarship be paid?

Starting from June 1, 2025:

  • A special scholarship will be awarded to student girls of the "Red" category who studied with excellent grades.

  • The amount of this scholarship is - It is set as 2 times the basic scholarship.

  • Payments are made on a monthly basis and the student's grades are regularly monitored.

Resident expenses are fully covered

Also, children of poor families who study with "excellent" grades:

  • They can rest free of charge in the camps of the Federation of Trade Unions of Uzbekistan.

  • All expenses related to recreation and health care are fully covered by the state.

Conclusion

The new order not only ensures social equality, but also provides educated and hardworking female students with material and serves for moral stimulation. Through this initiative, talented young people will be supported and more opportunities will be created for them. — Official Data
