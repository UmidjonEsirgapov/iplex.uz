  • A new university in Termiz:

Currently, in Surkhandarya there is the Termiz Institute of Engineering and Technology and the Termiz Institute of Agrotechnologies and Innovative Development. By expanding them, it is planned to establish the Termiz State University of Engineering and Agro-Technology.

Its activity will begin from the 2024/25 academic year. The university prepares personnel for the fields of engineering, architecture and design, information technologies, healthcare, economy and finance, and international relations.
