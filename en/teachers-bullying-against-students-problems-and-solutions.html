Understanding the Psychological State of Teachers

Videos of teachers being rude to students are going viral. Watching these different videos, you want to make the teacher do something in your mind, but you can't solve this problem. To understand this situation, it is very important to take into account the psychological condition of teachers.

Economic Problems

First of all, economic problems arise. Today, teaching is one of the lowest paid professions. A lot of money problems creates a nervous state in a person.

Social Problems

Secondly, social problems arise for teachers. The majority of teachers are women, and their mental state corresponds to the state of women in our society. In their living conditions, there is a lot of pressure on them: the whims of in-laws, family problems, and society's self-determination. These make a woman aggressive and become a big problem for teachers.

Political Problems

Thirdly, political problems form the field of teaching. The teaching profession has fallen to the lowest level. In such a situation, teachers are forced to follow formalism and this is an obstacle to the increase of the quality of education.

Solution Ways

There are several ways to eliminate these factors:

  • Study teachers with better salaries and working conditions and increase their motivation.
  • Responsible decision-making for social problems and personal problems and appeal with emotions.
  • Providing a wide range of psychological support for teachers.
  • Supporting teachers' own legal decisions to overcome political problems.
  • Increasing investment in scientific research for teachers and trying to improve the quality of education.
