MEXT Scholarship for prospective undergraduates and postgraduates in the field of engineering

The Japanese government will is offering scholarships to international students for master's and postgraduate programs in engineering at Hokkaido University's ye3 program. The scholarship covers tuition fees, transportation costs, and provides a monthly stipend of 144,000 JPY for undergraduates and 145,000 JPY for postgraduates.

A minimum of IELTS 6.0 in English is required for undergraduate (or graduate) studies.

Deadline: May 5, 2020 January
