Yunusabad District Criminal Court February 10, 2025 "ISFT" 3rd stage student N.Kh. of the institute was sentenced to 10 days of administrative imprisonment. He was held responsible for distributing a video in which he insulted the residents of Tashkent on social media.

Details of the incident

  • On February 4, 2025 on Instagram and Telegram networks, N.H. insulted the residents of Tashkent with shameful words spread.
  • He was summoned to come to the Department of Internal Affairs, but he did not comply with this request.
  • After that, a Administrative violation report was drawn up against him.

Comment of the female student

N.X. stated the following in the court:

  • "I actually posted the video on my Instagram page a month ago and deleted it after 5 minutes"
  • "My page is closed, strangers cannot see it"
  • "I posted this video out of anger because the taxi driver insulted me"
  • “Some bloggers cut and distributed only the offensive parts of the video”

Court decision

  • He was found guilty under Article 183 of the Code of Administrative Responsibility (Petty hooliganism).
  • 10 days of administrative imprisonment was imposed.
