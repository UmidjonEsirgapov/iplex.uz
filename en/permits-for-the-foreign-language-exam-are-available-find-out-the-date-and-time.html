Turkish language exam: Permits and important information

The dates of the next exams for determining the level of proficiency in a foreign language (Turkish language)** have been announced. Permits are available for applicants through my.gov.uz.

Exam dates:

  • Written part: January 12, 2025;
  • Speaking skills: between January 13-22, 2025.

Important for applicants:

  1. Examination place and time: You can view and download the permit in the "My applications" section (my.gov.uz).
  2. Arrival time: Applicants are required to come to the test at least 1 hour before the scheduled time with identity document (passport or ID card)

Additional information:

  • Exam locations and geolocation: https://telegra.ph/Chet-tili-01-09.
