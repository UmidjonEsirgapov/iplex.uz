Applicants who have not chosen a course by August 8 will not be accepted

Important note for applicants: the period for choosing courses of higher education institutions (HEIs) continues until August 8 (this day). This date has a direct impact on whether applicants will be candidates for studentship or not.

Why is the period of choosing a course important?

Every year, in the process of entering higher educational institutions, applicants must pass the test, as well as the stage of choosing a course. At this stage:

  • The applicant can choose 5 directions according to his wishes;

  • Priority (grant or contract) is determined for each direction;

  • The process of recommending the student is carried out based on the selection results.

If the direction is not chosen within the specified period, what will happen? will be?

If the applicant does not choose a course by August 8, he/she will not be admitted to HEIs, regardless of the test results. That is:

  • He will lose the opportunity to become a student;

  • His documents will be automatically rejected;

  • He will have to resubmit next year.
