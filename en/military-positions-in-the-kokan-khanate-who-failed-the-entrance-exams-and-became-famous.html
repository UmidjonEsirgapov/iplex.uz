Famous military positions in the Kokan Khanate

One of the most common subjects of history in entrance exams is Military structure in the Kokan Khanate and positions in it. Below, we will explain to you the list of historically important military positions that often fall in the tests.

Main military positions

1. Mingboshi

The position of supreme commander. He was the commander of the army and directly led the military campaigns.

2. Koshbegi

The governor of Belik who has his own army. He had great political and military influence.

3. Batirboshi

An official responsible for the state of military affairs in Belik.

4. Valiy Naib

Artillery, that is, the military commander who led the gunners.

5. Kalabaon

A person in charge of military fortresses. The construction, repair and defense of the castle was under his control.

6. Korboshi

Head of weapons production workshops and warehouses.

7. Yovar

Amiri is the head of the Lashkar's special unit. Completed secret and special assignments.

8. Topchiboshi

Head of artillery (artillery unit) in the Kokan Khanate.

9. Toxabo

Head of an independent military unit with its own clan.

10. Ponsadboshi

A commander who led an army of 500 soldiers.

Moral and auxiliary positions

11. Special guards

A special group that encouraged the soldiers during the battle and was a moral support.

12. Qazi soldier

A person who declared jihad on behalf of the khan before the start of the battle, responsible for the price of booty and the morale of the soldier.

13. Kazi Rais

He implemented the fatwas issued by the military mufti.

14. Group of 5

He was in every department during the military operations. He was engaged in evacuating the wounded and burying the dead.
