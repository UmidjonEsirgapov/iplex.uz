    Named state scholarships which course given to students?

    Named state scholarships are awarded to students of the last two years of undergraduate studies. For example, in 4-year studies, 3-4 courses, and in 6-year studies 5-6 courses are assigned to students.

    • The number of state scholarships for the next academic year is determined by the Cabinet of Ministers at the end of the current academic year according to the presentation of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

    criteria?

    The following criteria must be fulfilled for the appointment of scholarships:

    • excellent studies and participation in scientific (creative) work;
    • knowledge of languages - the state language and one foreign language (except for the state languages of the Commonwealth of Independent States), and in special cases, one more foreign language in addition to the field of study at the bachelor's degree. knowledge of the language; For persons who are not citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan, additional knowledge of the language of study is required;

    Students awarded scholarships are given special certificates and handed over in a ceremonial situation at the students' place of study.

    What documents should be submitted?

    Applicants for the state scholarship submit the following to the academic council for preliminary review:

    • excerpt from the test book;
    • recommendation letter from the dean's office, department;
    • opinion of the scientific (creative) supervisor;
    • scientific copy of (creative) works
    • copy of scientific (creative) achievements (diploma, certificate, thank you note, etc.)
    • reference of the results of the interview of the department of languages.

    The student submits the documents to the Department of Working with Gifted Students at the university. All documents are submitted in a binder or in a folder. The name of the state scholarship, applicant's surname, name and patronymic, faculty, course, group are indicated on the cover. The university where the student is studying may require additional documents based on its requirements. On the basis of the decision of the scientific council, the administration of the Higher Education Institution sends a recommendation to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education about the appointment of a scholarship. lx_elem_comment">In this case, the rector (director) of the higher education institution is responsible for the correctness of the candidates' documents and their timely and correct entry into the electronic system "stipendiya.edu.uz". reviews nomination letters once a year.     What are the benefits for scholarship students?

    • Students who have received state scholarships are accepted on the basis of a grant within one year after completing the bachelor's degree, without entrance exams for the master's degree in the relevant specialty. (Small comment: Named state scholarship holders will not be admitted to specialties that are not related to the master's degree without an exam. If the scholarship holder wants to apply for a non-related specialty of the master's degree, then they will not be given an advantage and can submit to the exam on general grounds. Basis: the Regulation approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 393 dated 20.06.2017 Clause 7.)
    • Students who have been awarded state scholarships have priority in being included in groups of young people who are sent abroad to continue their master's studies within one year after graduation.

    NOTE: Students will be suspended by the state during the academic year for not mastering daily lessons, violating academic discipline and academic ethics. deprivation of scholarships can be carried out by the orders of relevant ministries and agencies according to the submissions of state higher education institutions.

    NOTE: Applicants who want to apply for state scholarships should pay special attention to the fact that the main requirement for candidates is scientific (creative) works . Starting from the first course of the bachelor's degree, the student should choose a certain direction and a scientific (creative) mentor. Also, it is advisable to carefully prepare scientific (creative) works in the direction of the selected topic. A student's eligibility for a state scholarship is mainly determined by his academic work.
