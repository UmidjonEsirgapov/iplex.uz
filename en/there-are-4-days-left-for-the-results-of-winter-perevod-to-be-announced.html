Winter perevod results 2026: February 10 - The deadline is approaching!

It's an exciting time for thousands of students who want to transfer their studies to higher education institutions. According to the new regulation approved by Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated September 13, 2025 No. 578, there are only a few days left until the final answers to spring semester (winter semester) applications are published.

From January 21 Until February 10: When will the Perevod results be released?

Many students are troubled by one question: "Why haven't the results been released yet?". According to the regulation, the result of the applications will be notified to the citizens in the period from January 21 to February 10.

Term and legal grounds

According to the current regulations, transfer of studies the process is based on transparency and strict deadlines. February 10 is the last day for declaration of results. So, only 4 days are left to find out the final answers.

How to check the results of the winter school?

Students can check the status of their applications remotely without much hassle. For this, it is recommended to perform the following steps:

  1. Monitoring the status of applications sent through the unified interactive state services portal (my.gov.uz).

  2. Access to the personal cabinet through the official platforms and bots of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

  3. With the admission commission of the higher education institution to which studies are transferred contact.

What to do after the result?

If your application for transfer of studies is decided positively, you should go to the relevant OTM within the specified period, take steps to issue an order and join the course. — More information
