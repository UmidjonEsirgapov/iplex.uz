The Excellence Scholarship & Opportunity Program (ESOP): In Switzerland, ETH Zurich University offers the best students enrolled in one of the university's master's programs to participate in one of the scholarship programs. The scholarship covers full tuition fees and living expenses.

Benefits of the program:

• Excellence Scholarship & Stock Options Program (ESOP). The scholarship covers the accommodation fee and tuition grant (11,000 CHF=$12,000 per semester) and all additional university fees.

• Master's Scholarship Program: The scholarship consists of partial tuition fee (CHF 6,000=$6,500 per semester) and accommodation. The university can allocate an additional 1500 - 3000 CHF per semester or offer the student an assistant position with a monthly salary of 600 CHF from the second semester.

The student receives a scholarship from the master's stage and the scholarship is paid for the entire period of study in the master's program.

Requirements for candidates:

1. Scholarships are available for both ETH University students and foreign students;

2. Only students who have earned a bachelor's degree and are among the top 10% of their students can apply for the scholarship;

3. You can apply for both scholarship programs and admission to ETH University at the same time;

Documents:

• an attachment from the undergraduate grade book;

• an attached letter;

• proposal of a thesis topic;

• two letters of recommendation;

Admission to the program opens on November 1 until December 15. You can send all questions to studienfinanzierung@sts.ethz.ch.
