UNIL - the famous University of Lausanne in Switzerland announces that it has started accepting international students for the master's degree starting in 2022 on the basis of a grant. Every year, the 10 most deserving students will be exempted from the University of Lausanne contract (excluding the mandatory 80 CHF per semester) and will be provided with a monthly stipend. And students are required to study well and master the given activities to a perfect level. The following fields cannot apply to the program:

  • Master from the School of Medicine;
  • Master of education;
  • Master of Law from the Universities of Zurich and Lausanne;
  • Master of criminal law, magistracy specialty;
  • Master of Science in Physical Education and Sports Didactics;
  • Master of Science in Health sciences;
  • All MASs (PhD programs).

IMPORTANT! Applications for master's scholarships for law are accepted from students studying in such fields as "international and comparative law" and "legal theory".

Requirements:

  1. The candidate must not be a Swiss citizen and meet the UNIL University admission requirements;
  2. Having completed a bachelor's degree;
  3. Having shown himself in all aspects during his studies and having excellent grades;
  4. Language certificate (at least B2 for French, at least B2 for English C1). Detailed information about the required documents is HERE.

    IMPORTANT The monthly stipend provided is not enough to live in Switzerland. in addition, students must independently cover the compulsory fee paid per semester (CHF 80=$87).
