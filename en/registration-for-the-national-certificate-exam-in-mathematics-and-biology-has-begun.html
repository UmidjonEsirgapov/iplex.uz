Registration for the national certificate exam in mathematics and biology has begun

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Registration of applicants for the national certificate in mathematics and biology has begun. According to the Agency for the Evaluation of Knowledge and Skills, on January 30, 2023, the registration of applicants for the tests to assess the level of knowledge in mathematics and biology has begun.
Registration my.dtm.uz is carried out through the "National certificate" section of the website and will last until February 8 (payment of 450,000 soums on February 9). registration of applicants for the national certificate in biological sciences has begun (payment of 450,000 soums will last until February 9). The test will be held on February 19, 2023 (Sunday) and will be held in Uzbek, Russian, and Karakalpak languages. At the time of registration, applicants choose one of Uzbek, Russian, and Karakalpak languages ​​as the language of the test. Also, applicants can choose a region that is convenient for them and take part in the test in this region. The test will be organized in all regions and applicants will be registered based on the capacity of the places where the test will be held.

If one region is full, it will be possible to change the testing region and participate in the test in another region. — Source
