General Information:

Oxford University is offering Clarendon Scholarships for academically outstanding young people who wish to study at the Master's and Doctoral level in the United Kingdom. Oxford Scholarship It has been given by the Clarendon Foundation since 2001, the year the foundation was founded. 150 scholarships are allocated annually for foreign students.

The advantages of the grant:

  • 100% of the tuition fee for the master's and PhD degree at Oxford University is covered;
  • A scholarship for living expenses of about $15,000 per year;
  • Researchers are members of the Clarendon Scholars Association.
  • Researchers are given the opportunity to use cultural places and historical places such as symposiums, theaters;

Requirements for candidates:

  • All citizens can apply regardless of nationality, religion and race;
  • Bachelor grades must be very good, i.e. GPA of at least 3.7 out of 4.0 must be;
  • Candidate must have been admitted to a Master's or PhD program at the University of Oxford;
  • All interested candidates must first apply for admission to the University's Master's or PhD program;
  • After applying for admission, the applicant will be automatically selected for a scholarship;

Note: Once you are registered, the Clarendon Foundation will contact you by email and send you an offer. You must accept the offer letter;

Required Documents:

  • Personal Statement (Personal Statement);
  • Research proposal (if applicable)
  • Awards, certificates, etc.
  • Academic grades and diploma;
