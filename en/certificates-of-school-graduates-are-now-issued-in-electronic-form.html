The procedure for issuing electronic certificates of general secondary education has been established. This is reported by the Legal Information Channel.

Graduates are awarded the following types of certificates:

  • certificate of basic secondary education (grade 9);
  • excellent certificate of basic secondary education (grade 9);
  • general secondary education certificate (11th grade);
  • certificate of general secondary education (with silver medal) (11th grade);
  • excellent general secondary education certificate (with gold medal) (11th grade);
  • specialized school for children with mental developmental disabilities (boarding school) graduation certificate.

The certificate is prepared in Uzbek and English languages. Ordering and creating a certificate is done through the "Electronic Certificate" information system. In this case, relevant information about the graduate is entered into the platform by December 1 of each year, and an order is formed.

Certificates are issued to graduates by June 20 of each year. Graduates can download the certificate from the electronic platform by entering the information about the identity documents for free.

From the end of the 2022/2023 academic year, the information about the graduates will be entered into the "Electronic Certificate" information system. — Official Data
