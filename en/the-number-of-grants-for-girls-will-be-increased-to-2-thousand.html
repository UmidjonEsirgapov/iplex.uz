The number of grants for girls will be increased to 2 thousand

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In Uzbekistan, special attention will be paid to increasing the scope and quality of higher education.
President Shavkat Mirziyoyev, in his address to the Oliy Majlis on December 29, stated that starting from 2021, the number of state grants allocated to higher education will be at least 25 percent
"We will double the number of grants for girls from needy families for admission to higher educational institutions to 2,000.

Special attention will be paid to increasing the scope and quality of higher education in Uzbekistan. President Shavkat Mirziyoyev in his Address to the Oliy Majlis on December 29 He announced that starting from 2021, the number of state grants for higher education will be increased by at least 25%. Special scholarships will be introduced for girls who are studying with excellent grades and need social protection," said Shavkat Mirziyoyev. Currently, young people strive to enter the most prestigious universities, but there is no competition between universities to attract educated and talented young people. Therefore, the system of issuing state orders for the training of necessary specialists will also be established for private universities," said the head of state.

In order to strengthen the unity between universities and the lower levels of the education system, 65 academic lyceums will be transferred to the management of higher educational institutions. Also, 187 technical schools are affiliated universities and branches in their fields. will be attached to enterprises.

"In the new year, 30 leading universities in our country will be given the right to develop educational programs, admission quotas and independently solve financial issues," added Shavkat Mirziyoyev. — Source
