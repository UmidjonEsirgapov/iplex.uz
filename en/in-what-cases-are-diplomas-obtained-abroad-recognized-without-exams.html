In what cases are diplomas obtained abroad recognized without exams?

In what cases are diplomas obtained abroad recognized without exams?
Any diploma obtained abroad must be recognized in Uzbekistan by nostrification (recognition of foreign diplomas at the state level).

In what cases are diplomas obtained abroad recognized without exams?

For any diploma obtained abroad to be recognized in Uzbekistan, nostrification (recognition of foreign diplomas at the state level) must be done. This process is carried out based on Cabinet regulations.

Only, diplomas of HEIs established on the basis of intergovernmental agreements are excluded (Westminster, MDIS, Turin, Inxa, etc.).

Nostrification of foreign diplomas is carried out on the basis of an exam, as well as without an exam.

Starting from March 1, 2019, the following diplomas of individuals issued in foreign countries are recognized and nostrified directly (without special tests): — More information

  • citizens of Uzbekistan, foreign citizens and stateless persons who studied in foreign educational institutions with separate divisions (branches) and joint educational institutions operating on the territory of Uzbekistan;
  • QS, THE, ARWU of those who studied in foreign HEIs ranked in the TOP-1000 in the rating (previously it was TOP-500);
  • citizens who received education at the expense of quotas (grants) allocated by foreign countries for targeted training of specialists based on the referrals of Uzbekistan's ministries and agencies;
  • to foreign educational institutions until January 1, 1992 of citizens enrolled;
  • full members of the European Association for Quality Assurance in Higher Education - citizens who have studied in educational institutions accredited by authorized organizations, some exceptions are approved by the Cabinet of Ministers.
