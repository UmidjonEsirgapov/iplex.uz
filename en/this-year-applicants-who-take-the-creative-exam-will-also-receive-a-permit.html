This year, applicants who take the creative exam will also receive a permit

This year, it has been introduced to issue separate permits for applicants who take professional (creative) exams.

In addition to information about the applicant, the permit will contain information about the exam. That is, it will clearly indicate the professional (creative) exam:

  • direction;
  • name of the higher education institution;
  • address;
  • date and time.

Permits are currently being created on the site my.uzbmb.uz. After the process is completed, the applicants will be able to download from their personal cabinet.

The Ministry announced that additional information on the date of the exams will be issued soon.
