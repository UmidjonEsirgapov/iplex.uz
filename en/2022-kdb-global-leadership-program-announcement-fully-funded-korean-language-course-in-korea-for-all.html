2022 KDB Global Leadership Program Announcement: Fully Funded Korean Language Course in Korea for All Level Students

Today's top news in education is:

Summary:
General Information: The KDB Global Leadership Program: Presently invites students with high academic performance and leadership skills to a 10-week (2 weeks in Korea) online language course.
Apply to this program in any major. undergraduate, master's and doctoral students can participate.
Note: All documents must be submitted in English or Korean; If they are not in English, they must be notarized.

General information:

The KDB Global Leadership Program: Currently invites students with high academic performance and leadership skills to 10-week (2 weeks in Korea) online language courses. Undergraduate, graduate and doctoral students studying in any field can participate in this program.

Benefits:

  • The course is completely free;
  • Candidates are provided with free materials for the course;
  • The whole course is 10 weeks long, and 8 weeks are online and 2 weeks are offline in Korea (for 2 weeks of offline training, all expenses are fully covered: food, air tickets and accommodation place);
  • 'Excellent Trainee' that is, the best participants will be awarded with additional prizes;

Requirements:

  • Be a citizen of Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Philippines or Vietnam;
  • Bachelor's degree, master's degree in one of the above countries and be studying at the doctoral level;
  • Knowledge of English or Korean language at least B2 level (certificate is not mandatory for application);

Note: A total of 40 students will be selected;

The process of submitting documents:

  • Register HERE;
  • And upload the following necessary documents to the site it is necessary; a) This application form must be downloaded and filled in; b) Student certificate; c) University grades; d) Passport copy; e) Language certificates (if any); f) Letter of recommendation (in this case, you will leave the email of the person giving the recommendation and they will send the application form for the recommendation to your teacher, and your teacher should send it back by email);
  • Candidates who have successfully passed the document submission stage will be invited to an online interview. The interview will be held between May 19 and 27.

Note: All documents must be submitted in English or Korean; If they are not in English, they must be notarized. Also, all of them must be uploaded in PDF format. The result of the papers will be announced on 13th May 2022 and the result of the final interview on 3rd June.

You can get complete and detailed information with all the information through the official page of this program. For additional questions and information, write to klp@snu.ac.kr.
