The Academy of Nurses will be established in Tashkent - draft of the Government's decision

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to the project of the government decision, the Academy of Nursing, which is being established on the basis of the Technical College of Public Health named after Chilanzor Abu Ali Ibn Sina, is considered a state higher education institution. In order to improve the personnel training system and fundamentally transform the nursing education system, the following will be introduced: "General practice nurse", "Patronage nurse", "Medical social work", "Medical preventive work" employees will be trained for two-year primary medical institutions; "Specialized nurses" are trained for three years.
They are trained in the fields of "Paramedic work", "Nursing work", "Functional and laboratory diagnostic work", "Dentistry work", "Pharmacy" according to their specialties.
