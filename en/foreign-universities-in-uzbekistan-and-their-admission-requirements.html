Conditions of admission: To be admitted to the first stage of TTPU, applicants must have a certificate of IELTS not less than 4.5 (academic) points, and for the second stage, this requirement is 5.5 points. Candidates also have to pass an entrance exam. Entrance exams are in the form of a test, the level of difficulty is at the level of the general secondary education curriculum of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

The test consists of the following:

  • Mathematics: 18 questions (time: 40 minutes)
  • Reading comprehension: 6 questions (time: 12 minutes)
  • Logic: 6 questions (time: 12 minutes)
  • Physics: 12 questions (time: 26 minutes)

All questions will be in English. You can find detailed information on the university website.
