Graduates who have not received a passport (ID card) cannot apply to the OTM.

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
More than 122,000 (31 percent) graduates who have reached the age of 16, included in the electronic database of graduates attestat.uzedu.uz, have not received a passport.
Passport (ID card) for graduates to apply to HEIs important.
"Documents of applicants to higher education institutions are accepted online through the website of the State Test Center abitur.dtm.uz.

According to the Ministry of Public Education, more than 390 thousand students will graduate from schools this academic year. The electronic database of graduates included in the attestat.uzedu.uz website is 122 who have turned 16 more than a thousand (31 percent) graduates did not receive a passport.

Passport (ID card) is important for graduates to apply to higher education institutions.

"Documents of applicants to higher education institutions are accepted online through the website of the State Test Center abitur.dtm.uz. Applicants no longer need to rush to higher education institutions and stand in long queues as before.

In order to submit an application online, it is required to enter the passport (ID card) information of the graduates (personal identification number of a natural person - JSHSHIR). Therefore, graduates who have reached the age of 16 must get a passport (ID card) on time.

In order to make the process more convenient for graduates, the attestat.uzedu.uz electronic database has been integrated with the DTM abitur.dtm.uz website, says the ministry.

The ministry requires parents to hand over passports to school graduates at the time of school graduation, and school administrations to obtain passports for graduates entered their information on the attestat.uzedu.uz site and asked them to help them later submit documents to the OTM. — Source
