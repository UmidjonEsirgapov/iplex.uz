Japan's Honjo International Foundation is accepting applications for the grant program.

Candidates from all over the world can apply for master's and PhD. Almost all academic fields and specializations are available for this.

Candidates can apply directly to the fund without being pre-selected by the university.

Candidates up to 30 years old for master's degree and up to 35 years old for doctoral studies are accepted.

For this, the following documents must be sent:

  • picture;
  • two-minute video about research plan;
  • academic transcript;
  • research proposal;
  • recommendation.

Candidates are required to speak Japanese. Applicants whose documents are accepted will be interviewed in Japanese.

Accepted students will receive the following scholarships:

  • Approximately $1,600 per month for a 1-2 year degree;
  • $1,400 per month for a 3-year degree;
  • A month for a 4- to 5-year degree. $1200.

Applications can be sent through the link until April 30 of this year. — Official Data
