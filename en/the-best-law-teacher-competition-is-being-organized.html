The "Best Law Teacher" competition will be organized is being carried out

According to the decision of the Ministry of Justice and the Ministry of Preschool and School Education, from the 2024/2025 academic year, the "Best Law Teacher" republic competition will be held for pedagogues who teach the subject of "Fundamentals of State and Law" in general secondary schools.

The competition is in 3 stages will be held:

  1. District (city) stage - teachers will participate in tests via the Internet. According to test results, 20 teachers who scored the highest points will go to the next stage.

  2. Regional stage - will be held at the level of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, Tashkent city and regions.

  3. Republic stage is the final stage of the competition.

The competition is evaluated according to 2 conditions and each participant can score up to 40 points:

  1. Field knowledge - up to 10 points.
  2. Teacher's presentation - up to 30 points.

Winners of the Republic stage in the following amount money prize will be awarded:

  1. 1st place - 30 million soums.
  2. 2nd place - 20 million soums.
  3. 3rd place - 10 million soums.
