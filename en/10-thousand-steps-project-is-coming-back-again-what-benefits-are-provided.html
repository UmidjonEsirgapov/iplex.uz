The "10 thousand steps" project is returning again: benefits and news

The "10 thousand steps" project is being revived in Uzbekistan. The draft of the presidential decree has been submitted for public discussion and it is planned to relaunch this initiative from June 1, 2025.

The status and benefits of "Eco-active citizen"

According to the new decree, a special platform will be launched within the nationwide movement of One Million Green Families. This platform grants the status of "Eco-active citizen" to citizens who walk at least 10,000 steps per day. Those who have this status can use the following privileges:

  • 10% discount on government services
  • Discounts for consumer loans in banks with a state share of more than 50%
  • The status of "green family" for families with all adult members who have received the status of "Ecoactive citizen" will be given
  • Families that have received the status of "Green Family" will participate in the lottery and have the opportunity to win one of 14 electric cars every year

Previous experience of the project

This project has been implemented before.

  • In 2021 3000 per day to encourage walking Soum fee was introduced.
  • In January 2022, the payment system was canceled and it was promised to be replaced by a system of 100 million soum prizes and a thousand bicycles as gifts.
  • Three months later, this incentive system was completely canceled.

Now however, through the new Presidential decree, the "10 thousand steps" project is returning with a new incentive system.

"Cleanest neighborhood" and "Greenest neighborhood" competition

Also, "Cleanest neighborhood" and "Greenest neighborhood" contests between neighborhoods with at least half of the population receiving the status of "green family" will be held. 500 million soums will be allocated to the fund of winning neighborhoods and will be used for the following purposes:

  • Building children's playgrounds
  • Creating a green garden
  • Building waste collection areas and installing containers

Conclusion

Walking is not only beneficial for health, but also brings financial benefits. Through the new "Ecoactive citizen" system, it is expected that the population will be more involved in environmental improvement and development of a healthy lifestyle. — More information
