Applications for transferring studies from foreign and non-state HEIs have begun

Applications for transferring students' studies from the following higher education organizations to suitable and related educational areas of state higher education institutions are online admission has begun:

  • From higher education organizations operating in foreign countries (including those that occupy one of the first 1,000 places in internationally recognized rankings);
  • Non-state higher education organizations in Uzbekistan;
  • Uzbekistan branches of foreign higher education organizations.

Study from foreign countries Transfer applications will be accepted from July 15 to August 5 through the "Study transfer (foreign and non-state)" section of the my.uzbmb.uz site.

It is not allowed to transfer students' studies from higher education organizations operating in foreign countries to non-equivalent educational areas of state HEIs.
