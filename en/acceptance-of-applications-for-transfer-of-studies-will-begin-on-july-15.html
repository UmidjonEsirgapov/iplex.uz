Acceptance of applications for transfer of studies will begin on July 15

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Receipt of applications for transfer of education will begin on July 15. This was announced by the press secretary of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation at a briefing. From July 15, the process of transfer of studies will begin in higher education institutions.
Applications for transfer of studies will be accepted through the site of the Agency for Evaluation of Knowledge my.uzbmb.uz until August 5.

Acceptance of applications for transfer of studies will begin from July 15

About this, the press of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation secretary informed at the briefing.

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 393 dated 20.06.2017, from July 15, the procedures for transfer of studies in HEIs will begin.

Applications for transfer of studies will be accepted through the website of the Knowledge Assessment Agency my.uzbmb.uz until August 5.

Uzbekistan students of foreign and private higher education institutions also take an exam to transfer to state higher education institutions.

Those who do not get a passing score can be accepted with a "super contract". — Source
