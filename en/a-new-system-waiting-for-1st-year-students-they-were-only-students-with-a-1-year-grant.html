A new system waiting for 1st year students: they were only students with a 1-year grant

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:

New procedure for educational grants from the 2024/2025 academic year

Bachelor and master's degree admissions from the 2024/2025 academic year students will receive an educational grant for 1 academic year only. In order to get a grant in the following courses, students will have to meet new criteria.

Educational grant for the 1st year

  • Educational grant is distributed in the first year according to the state order quota among students who scored high marks in the entrance exams.

  • Students who won the grant are exempted from contract payment for one year.

Grant in the 2nd year and beyond

  • In the second year and above, the educational grant is redistributed between high academic rating and socially active students.

  • The grant is funded in a differentiated manner, that is, only those who have achieved high results will get it.

Terms of getting a scholarship

  • The scholarship is awarded only to students who have won a full education grant.

  • The scholarship is not provided for students who have received a partial grant.

Social-stimulating grants

  • Students in need of social protection or with high social activity are provided with a state grant for the full study period.

  • Students who receive this type of grant are exempted from contracts and other expenses.

Conclusion

According to the new system, educational grants are now guaranteed for only 1 year. In order to maintain or regain the grant at the next stages, students need to achieve high learning rates and be socially active.
