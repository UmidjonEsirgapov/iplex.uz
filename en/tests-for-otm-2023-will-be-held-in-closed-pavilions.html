Tests for OTM 2023 will be held in closed pavilions

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Under the leadership of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation Ibrahim Abdurahmanov, a meeting was held on systemic problems and their solutions in the activity of the Agency for the Assessment of Knowledge and Skills under the Ministry. The work being carried out in connection with the organization of tests for admission to higher education institutions was discussed.
It is noted that due to the lack of separate buildings for conducting tests, most of the tests conducted in educational institutions are held on days off (Saturday and Sunday) in order not to interfere with the educational process.

A meeting was held under the leadership of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, Ibrahim Abdurahmanov, dedicated to the systemic problems and their solutions in the activities of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency under the Ministry.

It is noted that due to the lack of separate buildings for conducting tests, most of the tests are being conducted in educational institutions on days off (Saturday and Sunday) in order not to interfere with the educational process. drawings were prepared and approved in agreement with local administrations and agencies.

26,520 test items from 15 general education subjects and 2,400 test items from related languages (Kazakh, Kyrgyz, Tajik, Turkmen) were prepared and examined. About 4,000 of the 8,956 applicants who registered as candidates for group supervision among the general public have been selected and contracts are being formalized with them.

For information, due to the coronavirus pandemic, in 2020-2022, for 3 consecutive years, tests were held in open places, including stadiums and avenues. — Read on infoedu.uz
