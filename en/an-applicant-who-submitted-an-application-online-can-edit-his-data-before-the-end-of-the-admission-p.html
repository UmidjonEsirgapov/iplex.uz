An applicant who submitted an application online can edit his data before the end of the admission period

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Until the end of the registration period, applicants can change their address of permanent residence, educational institution where they have completed registration, privileges and chosen fields of study, upload a copy of a foreign language certificate. until the end of the transfer period, i.e. until July 20.
Therefore, this year's graduates cannot change information about educational institutions during registration," said Dyorbek Kochkharov.

Until the end of the registration period, applicants must provide their address of permanent residence, completed education can change the institution, preferences and chosen educational areas, upload a copy of a foreign language certificate.

At an online briefing held at AOKA, the head of the State Test Center Department, Dyorbek Kochkarov, informed about admission issues. They have the opportunity to edit the information they have entered until July 20.

«In this case, the applicant can change the address of permanent residence, the educational institution he graduated from, benefits and the chosen fields of study. However, the information about the educational institutions of the current year's graduates is obtained from the relevant ministries, that is, the information about the graduates of secondary schools is obtained from the Ministry of Public Education, and the information about the graduates of academic lyceums and vocational colleges is obtained from the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education. can't change", Diorbek Kochkarov said.

It is noted that applicants who have IELTS or other international certificates in foreign languages after applying online can upload them before the end of the admission period. — Official Data
