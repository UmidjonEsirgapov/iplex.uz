In Uzbekistan, the payment contract money of some students is paid by the state. This was announced in the decision of the Government adopted in June 2022.

According to the decision, a special list of them was created, and the contract money of the students enrolled in the following cases will be paid:

  • A student raised in an orphanage or an orphanage;
  • A child whose family is included in the "Temir Daftar";
  • low-income student-student deprived of parental care, placed under guardianship;
  • child of women included in the "Women's register";
  • child of a low-income family with at least one of the family members having a group I or both group II disabilities studying at a professional or higher educational institution;
  • professional or A child studying at a higher education institution;
  • a student with a disability or a health problem;
  • a student whose family is low-income.

Also. A part of the contract amount is also paid to students studying in professional educational organizations and studying in branches of state, non-state higher education organizations and foreign higher education institutions. In these cases, it is necessary for the student to study full-time, part-time and evening bachelor's and master's courses.

Based on the positive recommendation of the youth leader, these costs are paid in accordance with the scheme in the amount of up to 50% of the payment contract, but not exceeding 50 times of the BHM (16.5 million soums).

Part of the costs of the payment contract are covered. in some cases it does not apply. If a student is studying in the second and subsequent specializations, in joint education programs and on the basis of a differentiated fee-contract, his fee-contract will not be covered. — Read on infoedu.uz
