"Children should be educated based on their ability after the 7th grade" - Alisher Kadyrov

The leader of the "National Revival" faction, Alisher Kadyrov, in his next election program, wants to put forward the proposal to educate children in schools from the seventh grade according to their abilities.

In his opinion, children up to the 7th grade after receiving general, fundamental knowledge, their potential for science, athletic ability, interest in entrepreneurship and other abilities will become visible. That is, it is suggested that the child spend more time not in all areas, but in the area in which he wants to work as an expert.
