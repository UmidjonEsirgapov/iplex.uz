According to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, For the 2020/2021 academic year, applications (documents) are being accepted online from citizens who wish to transfer (restore) their studies to higher education institutions of the republic.

Most of the received documents are returned for re-registration for the following reasons:

  • a copy of the passport (with the page listed on the address of permanent residence) in full or not uploaded in good quality (the permanent residence address of the student is one of the substantiating reasons when considering applications for transferring zero studies);
  • a copy of a document in the prescribed form (academic certificate or rating book or transcript) about studying (studying) at a higher education institution (academic certificate or rating book or transcript) is not uploaded in full or in quality;
  • the name of the higher education institution or the field of study not fully entered;
  • registered for transfer to a non-existent higher education institution or educational form or field of study.

Citizens registering for transfer (restoration) of studies are requested to take into account the above circumstances.

Registered for transfer (restoration) of studies but their applications Citizens rejected by the program are asked to re-register by eliminating the noted deficiencies in a short period of time.

At the same time, registered citizens are requested to log in to their personal offices (using the login and password during registration) and constantly monitor the status of their applications.

It is reminded: The last day of registration is August 5, 2020. — Source
