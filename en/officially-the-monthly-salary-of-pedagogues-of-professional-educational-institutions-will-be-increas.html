Officially: the monthly salary of pedagogues of professional educational institutions will be increased

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The decree of the President "On the state program for the implementation of the development strategy of New Uzbekistan for the years 2022-2026 in the "year of human attention and quality education"" was signed.
According to the state program, the professional education system in order to train qualified middle-level personnel who meet the requirements of the development, labor market and employers: starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, it is envisaged that state grants will be allocated to colleges and technical schools in the form of dual education.
The following is also specified in this document: with the involvement of foreign experts, 1 model professional educational institutions will be established that fully meet the requirements of the European vocational education and training quality assurance system; ➖ Starting from April 1, 2023: ✔️ basic tariff rates of pedagogues of professional educational institutions will correspond to basic tariff rates of pedagogues of general secondary education institutions; ✔️ basic tariff rates of professional education institutions with the first and second qualification category, higher education without qualification category and industrial education masters without higher education correspond to the basic tariff rates of the teacher of the first and second qualification category of the general secondary education institution, teacher with higher education and teacher with secondary special education
