Is it possible for students studying on a state grant to re-enter the state grant?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to it: the second and subsequent higher education is carried out on the basis of a fee-contract, and a student admitted to the relevant stage of higher education on the basis of a state grant will not be allocated a state grant for this stage of education for the second time. The provision on the second and subsequent higher education in this paragraph of the Decree is new no, this was also in the Law "On Education".
That is, now a student admitted to the relevant stage of higher education on the basis of a state grant will not be allocated a state grant for this stage of education for the second time.
