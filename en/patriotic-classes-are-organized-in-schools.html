Patriotic classes are organized in schools

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Now, starting from the 9th grade of each school in Uzbekistan, one "Young Guardsmen" patriotic class will be organized.
The head of the National Guard Information Service Davron Jumanazarov informed about this. education in the spirit of patriotism According to him, as part of measures aimed at educating young students in the spirit of military patriotism, a joint decision of the Ministry of Pre-school and School Education "On measures to organize the activities of specialized classes of "Young Guardsmen" in general secondary schools" was prepared. patriotism classes of "Young Guardsmen" will be organized one at a time. This was reported by Davron Jumanazarov, head of the National Guard Information Service.

The goal is to educate young people in the spirit of military patriotism

According to him, as part of the measures aimed at educating young students in the spirit of military patriotism, the Ministry of Preschool and School Education is taking measures to organize the activities of the "Young Guardsmen" specialized classes in general secondary schools.

There were classes in 56 schools

Until now, there are 265 classes of "Young Guardsmen" in 56 schools in 4 schools in each region under the jurisdiction of the National Guard. Now, based on the decision, one "Young Guardsman" patriotic class will be organized in each school. In the 2025/2026 academic year, 2,536

schools will start their military patriotism classes. — Official Data
