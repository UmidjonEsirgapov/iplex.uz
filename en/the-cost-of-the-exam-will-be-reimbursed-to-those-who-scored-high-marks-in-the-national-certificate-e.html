The cost of the exam will be reimbursed to those who scored high marks in the national certificate exam for the Uzbek language

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
From January 1, 2025, it is planned to reimburse the expenses of the youth who obtained a high score (level) in the national certificate exams in Uzbek language and literature at the expense of the State Youth Policy Support Fund.
January 1, 2024 By December, the necessary documents and forms for providing public service will be prepared and agreed with the Ministry of Justice.
A technical document will be developed for the introduction of electronic public service through the Unified Interactive Public Service Portal (YIDXP) and will be submitted to the Ministry of Digital Technologies for approval. It is planned to cover the costs of taking the exam for young people who have received a score (level) from January 1, 2025. The relevant draft of the Cabinet of Ministers' decision on this was announced.

According to this decision:

  • Youth Affairs Agency will fully cover the expenses of taking the exam to young people who have obtained a high score (level) in the national certificate exams in the subject of Uzbek language and literature.
  • Until December 1, 2024 the necessary documents and forms to provide public service prepared and coordinated with the Ministry of Justice.
  • A technical document for the introduction of electronic public services through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal (YIDXP) will be developed and submitted to the Ministry of Digital Technologies for approval.
  • The Ministry of Digital Technologies will launch a service that allows for automatic review of applications through the YIDXP and ensure its integration with relevant ministries and agencies.

In addition, From December 1, 2024, together with the Ministry of Justice, training seminars on the application of the administrative regulation approved by this decision will be organized with the participation of the employees of the state service centers. Reimbursement of the costs of taking this exam will further increase the interest of young people in learning and lay the foundation for their future success. — Source
