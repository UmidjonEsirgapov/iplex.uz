A great opportunity for poor families: Preferential admission to kindergarten has been introduced!

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
From January 1, 2025, children from poor families will be admitted to state preschool education organizations on a preferential basis.
Wider coverage of preschool education services and increase well-being.
Conclusion This initiative will help low-income families provides an opportunity for their children to receive quality education.

Starting from January 1, 2025, children from poor families will be admitted to public preschool education organizations on a preferential basis. This initiative is implemented within the "From Poverty to Prosperity" program.

The purpose of the program

  • To help the children of low-income families to get an education.
  • To expand the coverage of preschool education services and increase well-being.
  • To ensure social equality by improving the quality of education.

How are benefits implemented?

  • Special quotas are allocated in state preschool educational organizations.
  • The admission process is carried out on the basis of documents confirming the social status.
  • The program focuses on the well-being of the family during admission to kindergartens.

Conclusion

This initiative is aimed at low-income families. enables their children to get quality education. This social assistance is an important part of the state's strategy to reduce poverty. — Source
