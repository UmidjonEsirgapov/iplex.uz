How long do students who have been expelled have to resume their studies?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
How long should the expelled students resume their studies?
The period of resuming the studies of expelled students is defined as follows: within 5 years - by order of the rector; in the period from 5 to 10 years - based on the decision of the relevant ministry or office; For a period of more than 10 years - by the decision of the interdepartmental commission.
This procedure is based on the regulation approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated June 20, 2017 No. 393.
