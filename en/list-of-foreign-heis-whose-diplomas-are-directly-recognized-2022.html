The list of foreign HEIs whose diplomas are recognized directly (without special tests) has been approved

The State Inspection of the Quality of Education and Higher and Secondary Special by the joint decision of the Ministry of Education No. 01/qq- and 6-q/q of February 14, 2022, the higher education institutions included in the first 1000 list of higher education institutions in the ranking of higher education institutions announced by the internationally recognized Quacquarelli Symonds World University Rankings, Times Nearer Education, Academic Ranking of World Universities organizations in 2022 approved.

The list was compiled based on the ranking of the following international organizations:

  • ARWU – The Academic Ranking of world Universities;
  • THE – Times Higher Education;
  • QS – Quacquarelli Symonds World University Rankings.

It should be noted that the Cabinet of Ministers In accordance with the decision No. 620 of June 24, 2019 "On improving the procedure for the recognition of documents on education in foreign countries", diplomas obtained in foreign higher education institutions included in this list, that is, in the top 1000 in the rating of internationally recognized organizations (QS, THE, ARWU) recognized directly (without special tests).
