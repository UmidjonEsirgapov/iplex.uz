The application process for student housing will begin on August 1

For the 2025/2026 academic year, students who wish to be accommodated in student residences (TTJ) can start applying from August 1, 2025. This process is carried out on the basis of the regulation approved by Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 376 of August 14, 2023.

Priority for privileged students

According to the regulation, at the first stage of the application process privilege and priority students will be placed in TTJ first. Students who meet one of the following criteria are included in the privileged category:

  • Categories in need of social protection (orphans, people with disabilities, etc.);

  • Children of families with many children;

  • Students living in areas where accommodation is needed and other criteria.
