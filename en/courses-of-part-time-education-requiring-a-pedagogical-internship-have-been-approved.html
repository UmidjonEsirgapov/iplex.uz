External education courses requiring pedagogical experience have been approved

With the relevant statement of the State Commission, applicants with at least 5 years of experience in teaching activities in the school education system are admitted to the form of external education. The list of routes has been confirmed. These are:

  • Elementary Education
  • Fine Arts and Engineering Graphics
  • Music Education
  • Physical Education
  • Technology Education

At least five years of work experience The information about the pedagogic staff who were and are currently working is obtained from the Ministry of Preschool and School Education. For information, according to the Presidential Decree No. PQ-289 (21.06.2022), students with at least five years of experience in pedagogical activity in the educational system can study in the field of pedagogy in the form of part-time education. It is decided to accept persons who have been and are working in educational organizations during the registration period. — More information
