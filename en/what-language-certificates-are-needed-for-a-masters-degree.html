What language certificates are needed for a master's degree?

From the 2023/2024 academic year, the list of nationally and internationally recognized certificates assessing the level of knowledge of foreign languages required for applying for master's and doctoral degrees. What are the changes?

List of nationally and internationally recognized certificates assessing the level of knowledge of foreign languages required for applying for master's and doctoral degrees starting from the 2023/2024 academic year

What are the changes?

The new list includes English, Italian and one change in Turkish.

Removed:

  • From English - Cambridge Assessment English Linguaskill
  • From Italian - Accademia Italiana di Lingua (AIL)
  • From Turkish - İstanbul Aydın Üniversitesi Türkçe Öğretimi Uygulama ve Araştırma Merkezi (Aydin TÖMER)

Added:

  • English - International Test of English Proficiency (iTEP) Academic-Plus

Linguaskill, AIL and Aydin TÖMER certificates obtained before the approval of the new list (March 23, 2023) and which have not expired will be taken into account for admission in the 2023/2024 academic year.
