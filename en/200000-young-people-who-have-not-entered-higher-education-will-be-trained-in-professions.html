200,000 young people who have not entered higher education will be trained in professions

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to this document, starting from the 2026/2027 academic year, the system of directing young people who do not want to enter higher education to technical schools will be expanded.
According to the plan, 200 people who do not want to enter higher education About a thousand students are scheduled to be trained in professions.
"Vocational Development Platform" will be launched. An opportunity to independently learn more than 100 professions will be created.

The draft of the Decree on the State Program for the implementation of the reform programs in priority areas and the strategy of "Uzbekistan - 2030" in the year "Neighborhood Development and Upliftment of Society" was posted on the discussion portal. The project defines important measures aimed at guiding young people to professions and ensuring their employment.

According to this document, starting from the 2026/2027 academic year, the system of directing young people who do not want to enter higher education to technical schools will be expanded.

Measures to guide 11th grade graduates to professions

Students with a low interest in higher education will be identified

According to the draft decree, among the 11th grade graduates, students with a low desire to enter higher education will be identified. Based on the interests and opportunities of young people, it is planned to guide them to a profession.

"Profession Selection Week" events will be held

"Profession Selection Week" will be organized in cooperation with schools and neighborhood youth leaders to familiarize students with the professions and specialties taught in technical schools. During these events, students will be given detailed information about the advantages, opportunities, and demand in the labor market of various professions. According to the plan, about 200,000 students who do not want to enter higher education will be trained in professions.

"Professional development platform" will be launched

The opportunity to independently learn more than 100 professions will be created

In order to establish a system of independent acquisition and certification of professions, "Professional development platform" will be launched this year. Through this platform, citizens will have the opportunity to get independent knowledge of more than 100 professions.

Modern educational content is provided

The platform includes educational materials, video lessons and simulation exercises. This will help users learn the secrets of the profession in a practical way.

A certificate will be issued at the end of independent education

The system of issuing certificates to citizens who have completed independent study of professions will be introduced. These certificates serve to increase the competitiveness of young people in the labor market.

Conclusion

The project of the new Decree is aimed at directing young people to professions, ensuring their employment and expanding the opportunities to acquire modern professions. It is expected that hundreds of thousands of young people will acquire a profession and enter the labor market by directing them to technical schools and launching the "Professional Development Platform". — Source
