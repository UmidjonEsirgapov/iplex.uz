General Information:

Technische Universität Berlin has announced a grant program for summer courses. It was awarded to young people who have high academic performance, are socially active, and who can fully demonstrate that they are worthy of this grant. This university has gathered well-known qualified teachers, such as professors of the American National Academies, two National Science Medal winners and 10 Nobel Prize winners. This grant is intended for 1 candidate only,

Benefits of the grant:

  • The candidate's tuition fees are fully covered;
  • International knowledge and cultural exchange will also make international friends;

Note: The grant only covers the course fee. Does not cover travel expenses and accommodation.

Requirements:

  • Must be living outside of Germany;
  • Candidate must be registered HERE at the time of application;
  • Also upload required documents in PDF format;

Note : This grant available only for offline courses. Applicants for this grant do not need to pay 60 euros.
