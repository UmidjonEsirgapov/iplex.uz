Admission without examination for those who have successfully completed the technical school

Those who have successfully completed the technical school are admitted to the OTM without an exam only by interview. They start studying from the 2nd course at once. Currently, the admission for applicants of this category is ongoing (source).

What is the interview?

At the interview, the applicant will be asked a total of 4 questions in 4 directions (source):

  • Understanding the nature and importance of the reforms being implemented in the country related to his field.
  • Choosing the field of undergraduate education motivation.
  • Personal and professional characteristics.
  • Knowledge and professional skills in the chosen field of undergraduate education.
