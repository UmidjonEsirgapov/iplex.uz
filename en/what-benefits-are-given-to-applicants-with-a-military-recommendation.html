What benefits are given to applicants with a military recommendation?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The letter of recommendation of the command of the military unit is given to military personnel who have served in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan and achieved high results. in addition, separate admission indicators are determined.
- Separate admission indicators are determined for each form of education (day, evening, part-time) of each higher education institution.
- Applicants with a letter of recommendation will be able to participate in the competition within the framework of separate admission indicators and have the opportunity to study at a higher education institution.

The letter of recommendation of the command of the military unit is given to military personnel who have completed military service in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan and achieved high results

Citizens who have the letter of recommendation will have the following privileges:

✅ In addition to the admission parameters for those entering higher educational institutions (except military educational institutions) separate admission indicators are determined.

- Separate admission indicators are determined for each form of education (day, evening, part-time) of each higher education institution.

- Applicants with a letter of recommendation will be able to participate in the competition within the framework of separate admission indicators and have the opportunity to enter a higher education institution.

- By participating in the competition within the framework of individual admission indicators Applicants who did not get admission will be able to enroll in the general competition.

✅ For higher military educational institutions, applicants will be given additional points in the amount of 50 percent of their points (for example, if an applicant scores 120 points in the test, 60 points will be added to it).

The validity period of the recommendation is three year.

The basis: the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-1765 of May 31, 2012. The regulation approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 681 of August 17, 2019
