New undergraduate courses will be opened at the University of Oriental Studies

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Starting from the 2020/2021 academic year, Tashkent State University of Oriental Studies will add 4 new undergraduate courses. new curricula have been developed.
In these curricula, according to YeCTS (European Credit Transfer System), it is determined that students will acquire 240 credits at the bachelor's level and 120 credits at the master's level. field of education will be added.

From the 2020/2021 academic year, 4 new educational fields will be added at the Tashkent State University of Oriental Studies. Gulchehra Rikhsiyeva, rector of the University of Oriental Studies, informed about this at a briefing held in the conference hall of AOKA:

According to the Presidential Decree No. PQ-4680 of April 16, 2020, the Tashkent State Institute of Oriental Studies was granted the status of "university". based on experiences, 4 new educational areas will be added to the bachelor's degree. Including:

Textual studies and literary source studies;
Journalism: international journalism;

Starting from the 2020/2021 academic year, new curricula have been developed in order to transfer the educational process of students of the first stage to the credit-module system. In these curricula, according to the European Credit Transfer System (ECTS), students will acquire 240 credits at the bachelor's level and 120 credits at the master's level. databases of test materials were created in cooperation. — Details here
