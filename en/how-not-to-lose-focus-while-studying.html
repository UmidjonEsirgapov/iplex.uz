A little Everyone is probably distracted by the phone or lost in thought after preparing a lesson. But this leads to a decrease in the quality of mastering and loss of time. Better not to get distracted.

How?

Find an atmosphere where you won't be distracted. Someone prepares a lesson better in a very quiet room, someone in a crowded library.

Put distractions away. If you are distracted by your phone, put it out of sight, not next to you while you are studying.

Make a plan. Determine how much you need to finish, how many minutes of break are useful for you.

Create an agenda. Regularly preparing lessons at the same time will help you not to be distracted by unnecessary things.

Don't forget to take breaks. Getting some fresh air or relaxing in some other way will help you to resume your studies in a better way. — Read on infoedu.uz
