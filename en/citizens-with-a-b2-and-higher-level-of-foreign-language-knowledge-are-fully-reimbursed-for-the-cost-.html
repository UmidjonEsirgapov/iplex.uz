Citizens with a B2 and higher level of foreign language knowledge are fully reimbursed for the cost of obtaining international IT certificates

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Decision of the President "On measures to increase the coverage and quality of digital services and digital transformation of sectors, sectors and regions" (No. PQ-162, 24.05.2023)
was adopted. According to the report of the Legal Information Channel, the decision with, among other things, the following was approved: a list of 300 priority projects to be implemented in 2023-2024 within the framework of the "Digital Government" and "Digital Territory" programs; A list of 95 services and services that will be introduced on the portal first in the framework of increasing the number of services provided on the Unified Interactive State Services Portal to 570 by the end of 2023; A list of 47 types of information that will be canceled from residents and entrepreneurs from December 1, 2023; list of reminders and notices sent to residents and entrepreneurs on the status of using services.
From June 1, 2023, the part of projects implemented by republican and local executive authorities on the basis of external credit and non-profit aid funds directed to digital components will be compulsorily agreed with the Ministry of Digital Technologies.
