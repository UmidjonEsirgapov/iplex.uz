Social services for children with disabilities are developing further. According to the President's decision PQ-41, a day care service will be launched. This service will be implemented step by step from March 1, 2025. Phases of the service: from March 1, 2025 - in the Republic of Karakalpakstan, Andijan, Kashkadarya, Samarkand, Surkhandarya, Tashkent regions and the city of Tashkent. 2025 Until October 1 - in the rest of the republic.

This initiative is organized on the basis of public-private partnership and is intended for children with disabilities from 3 to 18 years old.

Terms of use of the service

  • Parents with the "Inson" social services center social must conclude a contract.
  • Children are accepted based on the conclusion of the Psychological-medical-pedagogical commission.

Subsidy system for entrepreneurs

From March 1, 2025, the following subsidy procedure will be introduced in order to support businessmen providing day care:

  • 25% of the basic calculation amount for
  • children between 3 and 7 years;
  • 27% subsidy for children between 7 and 18 years
    •  will be allocated.
  • In regional centers, cities of Tashkent and Nukus the subsidy will be increased by 1.1 coefficient.
  • Families included in the registry of poor families will be paid an additional 30 percent of funds.

Conclusion

Day care service for children with disabilities will improve their quality of life and provide comfort to parents. The new subsidy system allows to expand these services and support entrepreneurs. — Source
