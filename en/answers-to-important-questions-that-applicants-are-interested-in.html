Important questions and answers for applicants

We have collected the main questions that applicants face during the summer admission process and their reliable answers. The following information was prepared on the basis of the admission process of 2025.

Deadline for submitting documents to HEIs

Question: When does the application to higher education institutions end?

Answer: June 25, 2025 at 23:59.

This date is the only one for all public and private HEIs in Uzbekistan. is considered It is recommended to submit your application without delay.

Test fee for those who graduated from school in previous years

Question: How much will the test fee be for those who graduated from school in previous years?

Answer: 170,000 soums.

Graduates of 2025 will not pay the test fee - this service is for them. free of charge.

When should the test payment be made?

Question: When should the payment be made?

Answer: until 11:59 p.m. on June 25.

Applicants who have not paid the test fee will not be allowed to take the test.

Date of undergraduate exams

Question: When do the undergraduate entrance exams start?

Answer: They start on July 14 and end on July 28.

Exams can be in one-shift or two-shift format.

Time to apply for master’s degree

Question: When to apply for master’s degree will begin?

Answer: It will last from July 1 to July 30.

The whole process is carried out through the my.uzbmb.uz portal.

Perevod and renewal period

Question: When does the acceptance of applications for perevod begin?

Answer: from July 15 to August 5.

Applications for returning from abroad, internal transportation or resuming studies are submitted during this period.

Room and rent during vacation

Question: Will the rent be paid in July-August?

Answer: No, the dormitories will be closed. The rent is paid only during the academic period.

So, during the holidays, neither the hostel is open nor is the fee charged.

The admission process for 2025 is based on fixed dates. It is important for the applicant to submit applications without delay, make payments on time and check all the processes on official sites.
