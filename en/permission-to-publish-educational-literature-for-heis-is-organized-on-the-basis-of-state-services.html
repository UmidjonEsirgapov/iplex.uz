Permission to publish educational literature for HEIs is organized on the basis of state services

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The project of the government decision "On approval of the administrative regulation on the organization of permission to publish educational literature for higher education institutions on the basis of state services" was announced. the administrative regulation of presentation is approved.
It contains the following: the procedure for organizing permission to publish educational literature prepared in accordance with state educational standards, qualification requirements, curriculum and science programs for educational organizations on the basis of public service; the procedure for drawing up documents for examination of educational literature and permission for publication; issuance of an electronic QR-code publication permit for educational literature that is allowed to be printed; contractual payment to specialists participating in the process of examination of educational literature; charging a fee for using the public service in the amount of three times the amount of the basic calculation; the grounds for refusal to provide public services are determined.
