An offer was made to adopt the "Educational Code" in Uzbekistan

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Uzbekistan proposed to adopt the "Educational Code" Yesterday, August 28, at a press conference held with the participation of the Minister of Preschool and School Education Hilola Umarova, an offer was made to develop the "Educational Code".
In this code, the minister It was noted that the qualification requirements for all employees, from the teacher to the school teacher, and their general work activities should be clearly defined. Hilola Umarova also supported this proposal and put forward the idea of forming a team together and bringing similar proposals together.
