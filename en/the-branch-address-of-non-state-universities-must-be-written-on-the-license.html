The branch address of non-state universities must be written on the license

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
Why should the branch address be on the license?
Universities can provide educational services only at the address specified in the license.
This procedure is in place to prevent deception of applicants and parents and to control the quality of education.
In particular, the address where educational activities are carried out must be clearly indicated in the license of each university.

Why should the address of the branch be in the license?

  • Before issuing a license, the compliance of the building and infrastructure at this address with the requirements and conditions of the decision is studied.

  • Universities only may provide educational services at the address specified in the license.

  • If a legal branch is opened, its address must be reflected in the license.

Difference between admission office and branch:

  • Some non-state universities admit in other regions offices can open.

  • These offices serve only to submit documents and give advice.

  • However, this does not mean that education will be conducted in that area.

What should be done?

If a representative of a non-state university tells you in another region if it says that we have a branch:

  • Check the address section of the license;

  • Remember that formal education can be provided only through the addresses specified in the license.
