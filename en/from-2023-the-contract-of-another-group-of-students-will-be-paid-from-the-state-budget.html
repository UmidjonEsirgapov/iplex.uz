From 2023, the contract of another group of students will be paid from the state budget

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The contract of some students studying in joint education programs will be paid from the state budget According to the Presidential Decree No. The payment contract for the period of study of the students studying in the joint educational programs of the institutions of higher education institutions is covered by the state budget funds.
This is mentioned by the channel "Education control". a competition is held among students; A contract will be drawn up with the students who won the competition, providing for at least three years of work in state pre-school, general secondary, special secondary, professional and higher educational institutions. Pursuant to the decision PQ-289, starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, students studying in the joint educational programs of higher education institutions that train pedagogues will be covered by the state budget for the period of study at partner higher education institutions.
