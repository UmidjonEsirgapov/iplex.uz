For the category of successful graduates of the technical school, at the end of the academic semesters, the mastering grades "5 (excellent)" and "4 (good)", but mastering Graduates with at least 50 percent (at least 85 percent for medical institutions of higher education) of grades "5 (excellent)" are included.

This procedure will be implemented starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, within two years from the year the graduate graduated from the technical school.

Those who do not belong to the above mentioned category and who are more than two years after graduating from the technical school when they express their desire to study, they are admitted to undergraduate courses of higher education institutions according to the generally established procedure.

The list of courses is approved by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

Enrollment is based on a fee-contract .

Graduates every year from July 5 Until July 25, register online through the official website of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

For this, graduates are required to

  • a copy of a passport or ID card,
  • a certificate of graduation from a technical school
  • and upload a copy of the application to it:

Online The documents of the registered graduates uploaded to the system are examined by the admission commissions of higher education institutions every year until August 5. The results of the study are communicated to the applicants through their personal offices. The interview process for admission of graduates to higher education institutions is held every year. From August 10 to August 25, will be held in the period determined by the higher educational institution.
