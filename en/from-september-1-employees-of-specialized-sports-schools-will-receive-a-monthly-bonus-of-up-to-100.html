Starting September 1, 2024, employees of specialized sports schools will receive up to 100% monthly bonus will be paid. This was established by the Presidential Decree and adopted on June 22. This decree is aimed at supporting the development of specialized sports schools.

Details of the decree

According to the decree, until September 1, 2024:

  • Teaching a second foreign language: Teaching a second foreign language will be introduced in specialized sports schools. This is an important step aimed at improving students' language skills and ensuring their success in the international arena.
  • Establishment of gymnasiums: At least 3 of the most exemplary state pre-school educational organizations will be selected from each district and city and gymnasiums will be organized in them. This is the environment created for the development of physical education of young children.

Educational process in specialized sports schools

In specialized sports schools:

  • Class size: The educational process is carried out in classes with the number of students from 12 to 24.
  • School size: Total number of students the limited number is set to 168.

Participation in competitions

Students-athletes of specialized sports schools directly participate in the competitions of the Championship and Cup of Uzbekistan as a separate team. This serves to increase their success in professional sports and ensure their competitiveness. — Official Data
