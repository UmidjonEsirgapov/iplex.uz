Tests for transfer from foreign higher education institutions branches and non-state (private) universities to state universities in Uzbekistan will be held between August 20-25.

The evaluation criteria in 2023 will be as follows There will be:

In 3 subjects that are mandatory for all test takers:

Mother language (Uzbek, Russian or Karakalpak) - 10 questions.

  • 1.1 points for each.
  • Maximum score - 11 points.

Mathematics - 10 question.

  • 1.1 points for each.
  • Maximum score - 11 points.

Uzbekistan history - 10 questions.

  • 1.1 points for each.
  • Maximum score - 11 points.

Total possible score - 33 points.

2 major subjects suitable for undergraduate education:

1st subject - 30 questions.

  • 3.1 points for each.
  • Maximum score - 93 points.

2nd subject - 30 questions question.

  • 2.1 points for each.
  • Maximum score - points.

Total possible points - 156 points.

In general, a total of 90 tests in 5 subjects for those who want to transfer their studies in the academic year 2023/2024 task is given. The maximum possible score is 10 points — Source
