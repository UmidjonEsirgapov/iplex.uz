To determine the level of foreign language knowledge tests will be held from January to May 2025. The tests are conducted in different languages and are carried out on the following dates.

The main stages of the tests:

  • Registration: done through the my.gov.uz portal.
  • Ability to choose a region: Applicants choose a region convenient for them for the tests and participate in the exam at this place.
  • Exam types and languages: Tests in different languages are organized every month. Exams include assessment of written and oral skills.

Table for specific months

  • Tests: January 12, January 18-19.
  • Languages: English, German, Arabic, Korean, etc.
