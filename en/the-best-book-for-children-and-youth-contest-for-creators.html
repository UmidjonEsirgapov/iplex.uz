"The best book for children and youth" contest for creators

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The competition is organized by the Youth Affairs Agency of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Information and Mass Communications Agency under the Presidential Administration of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, the Writers' Union of Uzbekistan. Legal entities engaged in past publishing activities and authors of books officially published in the Republic of Uzbekistan can participate.
The following additional nominations will be introduced as part of the competition: "The best translated work for children and young people" (translated into Uzbek); "Best Book Design"; "Best editorial work"; "The book with the largest number". The competition is organized by the Youth Affairs Agency of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Information and Mass Communications Agency under the Presidential Administration of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, and the Writers' Union of Uzbekistan. business legal entities and authors of books officially published in the Republic of Uzbekistan can participate.

The competition is held within 3 age categories (under 6 years, 7-11 years, 12-18 years)
in the following main nominations:

  • "The best literary and artistic book";
  • "The best scientific and popular book".

The following additional nominations will be introduced as part of the competition:

  • "The best translated work for children and young people" (translated
    to Uzbek);
  • "The best book design";
  • "The best editorial work";
  • "The book with the largest number".

A statuette and a special diploma will be awarded to the authors of the book that won in the main nominations (if deceased, to their legal heirs), as well as:

  • for the first place - 100 million soums;
  • for the second place - 75 million soums;
  • for the third place - 50 million soums will be awarded.
