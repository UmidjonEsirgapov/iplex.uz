A 12-year-old student in Tashkent got 8.0 points in IELTS

It is not easy for any young person to show a high result in the International English Language Testing System (IELTS) exam. However, Aziza Khikmatullayeva, a student of school 260 in the Yunusabad district of Tashkent, was only 12 years old IELTS score 8.0 and obtained C1 level.

What were the results?

Aziza noted the following results:

  • Listening (Listening): 9.0
  • Reading: 8.5
  • Writing: 7.0
  • Speaking: 7.0
  • Overall band score: 8.0
  • CEFR level: C1

These results It is a great achievement for a young student, reflecting the level of excellent knowledge of a foreign language. Scoring 8.0 points in IELTS is enough to apply to many international universities and allows participation in prestigious grant programs.
