Amount of super contract for the new academic year messages are spreading on various telegram channels that it has not yet been confirmed. The most important questions and answers about the super-contract have been collected by our editors here.

What is the price of the super-contract this year?

The amount of the basic payment-contract for applicants whose scores in the entrance exams to higher education institutions do not reach the limit of the approved admission parameters is determined annually by the statement of the State Commission. However, the amount of the super-contract for the 2024-2025 academic year has not yet been confirmed and announced.

How many times will the super contract be paid to the differentiated contract?

The amount of the differentiated payment-contract was determined for the applicants whose scores in the 2023/2024 academic year did not reach the limit of admission parameters up to 4.05 points. This amount has not yet been determined for this year's admission.

For example, if the last contract score in the chosen field of the applicant was 123, and you could not enter with 122.5 points, you will be able to enter this field by paying a super-contract in the amount of 1.5 times the specified contract.

Is an education loan granted for a super-contract?

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, the educational loan is not allocated for the implementation of the differentiated payment contract and other one-time payments equal to its minimum amount.
