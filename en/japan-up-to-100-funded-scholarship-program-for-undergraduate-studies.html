Japan: Up to 100% funded scholarship program for undergraduate studies

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
About the university and the grant program: Meiji University is one of Japan's leading universities established in January 1881.
The Meiji University Special Grant for Privately Financed International Students grant program provided by the university provides financial support to international students.
The program is for international students from all countries.

About the university and the scholarship program:

Meiji University is one of the leading universities in Japan, founded in January 1881. Meiji is a famous university in Japan, which made a great contribution to the development of Japan as a modern independent country by training leading personnel.

Meiji University Special Grant for Privately Financed International Students

Who is the program for?

  • The program is for international students from all countries.

For which fields of study:

The grant program is valid for all undergraduate programs offered by the university.

Application process:

Students must first apply and be admitted to one of the university's undergraduate programs HERE. It is not necessary to submit a separate application for the scholarship, the candidacy of admitted students will be considered automatically.

Required documents:

  • Motivational letter;
  • School, college or high school diploma and applications;
  • Resume;
  • Personal statement about purpose (Statement of purpose);
  • Two letters of recommendation.

Language requirement:

  • Students must have a good level of written and spoken English.

Program benefits:

  • Subsidy of 50% or 100% of tuition fees for successful students at Meiji University
