Preference for master's degree for those who have an international accounting certificate

On the basis of the Presidential Decree dated February 24, 2020 (No. PQ-4611), an important privilege was established for applicants with international certificates in the field of accounting and auditing. Now they will be able to enter masters without tests.

Who can use this benefit?

Applicants with the following international accounting certifications:

  • Certified International Professional Accountant (CIPA)

  • Certified Diploma accountant (ACCA)

  • Certified Public Accountant (CPA)

  • Diploma in International Financial Reporting (DipIFR)

have this right, they are "Accounting" and "Audit" Master's majors are accepted for study without submitting the privilege of admission on the basis of a payment contract.

Duration and procedure of document submission

Applicants must submit documents for master's degree before July 30, 2025 (also on this day) via the https://magistr.edu.uz platform.

When submitting documents pay attention to the following:

  • The certificate must be valid and recognized.

  • Application must be submitted within the specified specialties.

  • Preferential admission is valid only on the basis of payment-contract.
