76,400 applicants take the test in one day

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Entrance tests will be held in 72 buildings across the republic.
In this: 38,220 people in 1,274 groups in one shift; 76,440 applicants in 2,548 groups will take part in the test in one day (in two shifts).
Tests will begin in all regions on August 1 and will last from 11 to 13 days, depending on the number of applicants in the regions and the capacity of the buildings.
