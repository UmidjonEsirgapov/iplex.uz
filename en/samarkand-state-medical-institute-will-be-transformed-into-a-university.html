The following are defined as the main tasks and directions of activity of the university:

  • training, retraining and upgrading of highly qualified medical personnel in the system of continuous medical-professional education;
  • directing the educational process to the formation of practical skills, wide introduction of advanced pedagogical technologies, educational programs and innovative teaching-methodical materials based on international educational standards, creating necessary conditions for ensuring the harmony of theoretical knowledge and practice in the operation of clinics and educational bases;
  • to focus fundamental, practical and innovative scientific-research works on the solution of existing problems in medical practice, to ensure the organic connection of medical education, science, innovation and the results of scientific research;
  • in the framework of cooperation with foreign medical higher education institutions, to introduce and develop programs for the academic mobility of students, professors and researchers, as well as joint educational programs;
  • to regularly increase the scientific potential of the university, to ensure that professors-teachers and researchers publish articles in prestigious international scientific journals;
  • organize international scientific-practical conferences, seminars and symposia on discussing current issues in the field of medicine in cooperation with republican and foreign higher education and scientific-research institutions and staff customers;
  • Modernization and strengthening of the material and technical base of the university, simulation center, educational and scientific-research establishing laboratories and equipping them with modern equipment;
  • To regularly study the needs and problems of the health care system of Samarkand region and neighboring regions, to establish a cooperative activity between the health care system and non-governmental non-profit organizations in order to eliminate them within the scope of their authority.

Students studying at the Samarkand State Medical Institute continue their studies at the University in the appropriate bachelor's education areas and master's specialties, and the students of the graduate course are awarded a state model University diploma according to the established procedure.
