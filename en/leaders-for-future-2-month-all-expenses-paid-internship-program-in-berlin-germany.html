"Leaders for Future" - this 2-month internship program focuses on gathering all the curious, science-hungry, leaders of the world under one team and strengthening networking among them. Candidates can receive full or partial grants in this internship.

The types of grants awarded:

  • The full scholarship-this full scholarship covers administrative expenses, insurance, housing expenses, and additional money is also given;
  • The partial scholarhsip-plus-this full scholarship covers administrative expenses, 50% of insurance, housing costs are covered, and additional money is also given;
  • The partial scholarship: this full grant administrative costs, insurance, housing costs are fully covered by the candidate himself, in addition, additional money is also given;

Requirements for candidates:

  • under 35 years of age be;
  • All citizens can apply;
  • Representatives of all fields;
  • English or German language must be at least B 1 level;
  • Be independent, courageous, a leader and an inspiration;

Required documents:

  • CV of no more than one page (Example for);

Procedure of admission to the program:

  • Fill out the online application between 01.02.2022 and 21.02.2022;
  • In the second stage, there will be a live stream on You tube and special tasks will be given to the candidates;
  • In the last stage 3, one-on-one interview
  • The final results will be announced by April 1st;

For all kinds of questions, contact application@copernicusberlin.de, — Official Data
