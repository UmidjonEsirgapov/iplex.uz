The procedure for obtaining the second and further education was approved

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The regulation on the procedure for obtaining second and further education in institutions of higher education was approved. will be announced.

Government Decision (No. 606, 24.09.2021) approved the regulation on the procedure for obtaining second and further education in higher education institutions. can receive the second and subsequent higher education.

Admission parameters for the second and subsequent higher education are determined by the Higher Education Council.

Applicants register for admission through a special electronic system developed by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education every year from July 10 to August 10 (also on this day).

Admission procedure (entrance exam form, evaluation criteria, examination dates) are published on the official website of Higher Education Institution by July 1 every year.

✅ Admission results are announced by Higher Education Institution by August 30 every year.

Study in secondary and subsequent higher education starts from the 2nd year. In this case, the duration of study is determined to be one year less than the period specified for the relevant bachelor's education courses.

❌ The regulation does not apply to higher military educational institutions.

This procedure will come into force on May 1, 2022.
