Starting from the new academic year, these expenses of entrepreneurs who train school, college, and technical school students for professions and work will be deducted from the tax base. Income tax and social tax for students are set at 1 percent.

New opportunities for girls to get an education and earn an income

The head of our state has put forward new initiatives to improve the education, income and general conditions of girls. These initiatives include a number of measures aimed at improving the lives of girls.

Construction of social houses

One-room social houses will be built for girls who are in dire circumstances and need housing. These houses allow girls to live for free for 3 years until they can get a job and stand on their own feet. Mayors were instructed to develop a model project of social houses and start construction.

Possibilities of remote work and education

Distance work:

  • Girls who cannot go to work due to their health will be provided with remote work in their apartment. Through this, they will have the opportunity to work at home.

Distance education:

  • Students with children under the age of three will be given the opportunity to receive distance education. This allows girls to continue their education and take care of their children together.
