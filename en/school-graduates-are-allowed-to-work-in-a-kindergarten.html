School graduates are allowed to work in a kindergarten

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Starting from the 2024/2025 school year, some school graduates will be allowed to work as teacher assistants in kindergartens.
Graduates who have successfully completed these classes will be given a certificate, a state model certificate, This certificate will be given to them as teacher assistants in preschool educational organizations gives the right to work.
Only: students who have studied in the 10-11th grade in a pedagogical class, have completed training and practice determined by the program, and have successfully passed the final assessment will be given a certificate.

Can school graduates work in a kindergarten?

Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, some school graduates it is allowed to work as a teacher's assistant in kindergartens. However, this opportunity applies only to students studying in a pedagogical class.

What is a pedagogical class?

According to the government decision, starting from the 2024/2025 academic year:

  • Republic of Karakalpakstan,

  • Tashkent city

  • and in each region one school

in 10-11 grades special pedagogical classes preparing for the profession of pedagogue will be introduced as an experiment.

What rights are given to those who have completed the pedagogical class?

For graduates who have successfully completed these classes:

  • A certificate is issued,

  • A state model certificate is handed over,

  • This certificate entitles them to work as teaching assistants in preschool educational organizations.

Through this, young people who have graduated from school can start their professional career early and gain experience in the field of pedagogy.

Who will be awarded the certificate?

Only:

  • Students who studied in the 10-11th grade in a pedagogical class,

  • Completed the training and practice set on the basis of the program,

  • A certificate will be awarded to students who have successfully passed the final assessment.

Conclusion

A new door of opportunity has been opened for schoolchildren studying in the pedagogical direction. Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, graduates will have the right to work as teacher's assistants in kindergartens through pedagogical classes that are being introduced as an experiment. This not only gives young people early work experience, but is also a thorough preparation for those who are genuinely interested in the field of education. — Read on infoedu.uz
