Higher education coverage will reach at least 50 percent

Brief content:
Until 2030, it is planned to double the average salary of kindergarten and school teachers.

It is planned to double the average salary of kindergarten and school teachers until 2030. The goal of including 10 HEIs in the top thousand of the world's most prestigious universities was also set.

President Shavkat Mirziyoyev approved the strategy of Uzbekistan until 2030. It envisages the implementation of the following tasks in the education system: — Official Data

  • providing 100% of kindergartens with clean drinking water and modern sanitation and hygiene infrastructure;
  • organizing free buses for students of 715 schools located in remote and remote areas;
  • creating 1,000 multimedia programs for tablets based on new generation textbooks;
  • attract 500 foreign experts who are "language owners" to educational organizations every year;
  • double the average salary of pedagogues of preschool educational organizations and general secondary educational institutions;
  • increase the number of non-state general secondary education organizations to 1,000, increase the share of students studying in them by 3 times;
  • Each year starting from 2024, conclude contracts for the construction of at least 100 general secondary education institutions based on public-private partnership;
  • Raise the level of coverage of young people with higher education to at least 50%;
  • Achieve inclusion of 10 higher education institutions in the rating of the world's most prestigious top-1000 universities;
  • Additional Construction of 120,000-bed educational buildings and 150,000-bed student residences;
  • allocation of 3 trillion soums for fundamental research.
