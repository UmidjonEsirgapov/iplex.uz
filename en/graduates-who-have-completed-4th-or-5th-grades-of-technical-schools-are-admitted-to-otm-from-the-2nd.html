Graduates who have completed 4th or 5th grades of technical schools are admitted to OTM from the 2nd year based on an interview.

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the government decision No. 17 on January 11, 2022, the procedure for accepting graduates who have successfully completed technical schools to continue their studies from the 2nd stage of higher education institutions without entrance exams to the bachelor's education programs suitable for their field, through an interview
In order to be recommended for a graduate interview, during the entire period of study at the technical school, at the end of the academic semesters, the mastering grades are "5 (excellent)" and "4 (good)", but at least 50 percent of the mastering grades (at least 85 percent for medical institutions of higher education) are required to be "5 (excellent)".
Enrollment based on a payment contract.
