From the 2025/2026 academic year, correspondence education will be suspended

On the basis of Presidential Decree No. PF-76 of May 5, 2025, important changes are being implemented in the education system of Uzbekistan. Now correspondence education will be canceled in all educational institutions.

Correspondence education will be officially closed

According to the presidential decree, starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, state and non-state HEIs will not accept new students in the form of correspondence education. This decision was made in order to improve the quality of education, strengthen control and speed up the transition to modern forms of education.

What will happen to students who are studying outside?

Students who are currently studying in part-time education will continue their studies. They attend classes based on the established curriculum and become full-fledged diploma holders.

What forms are introduced instead of extramural education?

Evening education

A convenient opportunity for those who want to study after work. Classes are usually held in the evening hours.

Distance education

Enables distance learning through digital technologies. It is especially convenient for those who live in another area or are employed.

Approved full-time (part-time) education

This form is specially developed for employed citizens. Classes are held on certain days of the week. Specific regulations and procedures will be introduced after they are developed.

Procedure regarding admission quotas

Admission to alternative forms of education is defined as a condition not to exceed 50% of the daytime quota. This serves to maintain the quality of education and the balance of resources.

Conclusion

From 2025, an important change in the education system of Uzbekistan is observed - the closure of extramural education. This creates a basis for the introduction of high-quality, modern and flexible forms of education. Students and applicants can now continue their education through evening, distance or part-time forms of education. — Official Data
