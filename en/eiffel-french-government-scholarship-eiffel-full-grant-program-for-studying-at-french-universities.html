General Information:

Eiffel French Government Scholarship: The Eiffel Scholarship program was developed by the Ministry of Europe and Foreign Affairs and is one of the fully funded grants to French higher education institutions for the best foreign students to study at the master's and doctoral levels.

Important dates:

  • Opening of applications: September 27, 2021;
  • Ending date: January 7, 2022;
  • Declaration of results: April 4th week, 2022;

Admission Criteria: Europe and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs appoint experts to select scholarship holders for the Eiffel Program. Each expert - research professor has the right to supervise research on the doctoral component and. Scholarships are awarded for a non-renewable period of 4 years. Get acquainted with the list of appointed jury for 2021.

Amount of the grant: All expenses are fully covered:

  • Full tuition fee (for master's degree);
  • Living expenses 1,031 euros per month for master's degree and 1400 euros for doctoral degree;
  • Per month Scholarship in the amount of 150 euros - for master's degree;
  • International tickets to return from your country to France (for master's degree and doctoral degree;
  • Cultural events;
  • Housing allowance;

You can apply for the Eiffel grant in the following directions:

  • Engineering sciences;
  • Exact sciences;
  • Mathematics;
  • Physics;
  • Chemistry;
  • Life sciences;
  • Nano- and biotechnology;
  • Sciences of the universe;
  • Environmental sciences;
  • Information and communication science and technology;
  • Law and political sciences;

Selection criteria:

  • Only foreign citizens are eligible to apply for Eiffel scholarships for international students;
  • The age requirement is up to 25 years for a master’s degree;
  • Up to 30 years for a doctorate;
  • In many universities you can be admitted without IELTS and TOEFL;
