What benefits are given to the winners of the Republic competition on fine arts?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The order of the Ministry of Culture of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan of the Secondary Special Vocational Education Center dated May 3, 2017 No. 10-Q/Q and 34/QQ "Procedure for holding republican contests of creative youth" on" In accordance with the regulations, a republican competition for fine arts will be held.
Competitions will be held among children's music and art schools and specialized music-art boarding schools, secondary special and vocational educational institutions (specialized music academic lyceums of the republic).
The Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan In accordance with the Decision No. 393 of July 20, 2017 "On the approval of the regulations on the procedure for admission to higher education institutions, the transfer, reinstatement and expulsion of students", the winners of the republican competition on fine arts of the Academy of Fine Arts, based on the presentation of the Academy of Fine Arts, according to the decision of the State Commission for Higher Education are admitted to institutions, appropriate educational fields without competitive entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam) on the basis of state grants. In accordance with the Regulation "On the procedure for holding republican contests of creative youth" approved by the decision No. 34/QQ, a republican contest in visual arts will be held. Contests will be held in children's music and art schools and specialized music-art boarding schools, secondary special vocational education institutions (specialized music academic lyceums of the republic).

According to the Resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the approval of the regulations on the procedure for admission to higher education institutions, transfer, restoration and expulsion of students" of July 20, 2017, the republican competition of fine arts of the Academy of Fine Arts The winners of the Academy of Arts will be accepted by the decision of the State Commission on the basis of state grants without entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exams, written exams) in the relevant higher education institutions, suitable educational areas. — Official Data
