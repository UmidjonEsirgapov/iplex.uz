Be careful when using public transport

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Get acquainted with the etiquette and safety rules below: 1.
Take your bags and backpacks in your hands, try not to disturb people with them.
This is not only etiquette, but a necessity. requires culture. Check out the etiquette and safety rules below:

1. Save yourself and others from discomfort

  • Do not try to get inside if the subway or bus is full. This will cause inconvenience to children, the elderly and others inside.
  • Take your bags and backpacks, try not to disturb people with them.

2. Don't forget respect and civility

  • Give space to the elderly, women and young children. This is not only a courtesy, but a necessity.
  • Do not speak loudly in transport or talk on the phone - it may disturb others.

3. Observe personal hygiene and manners

  • Avoid using perfumes or deodorants that have a strong smell. This can make others uncomfortable.
  • Control your emotions. If someone speaks rudely to you, do not escalate the situation and leave peacefully.

4. Keep your mood positive

  • Always try to be cheerful and positive. This will have a good effect not only on you, but also on those around you.
