The system of training young people for professions and foreign languages ​​will be improved

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Accepted.
According to it, a purposeful training center (Center) for teaching citizens (young people) foreign languages and working in foreign countries will be established. training courses will be organized.

Government Decision (No. 213, 04/17/2024) "On measures to improve the system of training young people to professions (specialties) and foreign languages" was adopted.

According to it, Citizens A target center (Center) will be established for teaching (young) foreign languages and preparing them for work in foreign countries.

Training courses on profession (specialty) and teaching foreign languages will be organized in professional educational institutions. Organization of selection processes by employers of citizens of Uzbekistan who wish to work abroad has been defined as one of the main tasks of the Center.

Graduates who have successfully completed training courses are given a document of the state-approved model.

Training of personnel in training courses is carried out on a fee-contract basis in accordance with educational programs of up to one year.

As part of the training courses, learners are taught English, German, Japanese, Korean, Arabic and other foreign languages that are in high demand.

In the event that learners enrolled in professional educational institutions study a foreign language independently and present an international or equivalent certificate, the expenses spent by the learner for studying a foreign language are reimbursed. 50 percent, but not exceeding 10 times of the base calculation amount, will be covered from the state budget.

It is required to obtain an internationally recognized certificate of at least B2 level in foreign language for citizens who will be employed in a foreign country, and at least B1 level in German language for citizens going abroad on the basis of "Ausbildung" (dual education).

Pedagogical personnel involved in training courses are paid a monthly bonus of up to 100% of their monthly salary based on their performance. — Details here
