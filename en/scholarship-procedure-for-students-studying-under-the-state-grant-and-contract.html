Scholarship procedure for students studying under the state grant and contract

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
For students studying on the basis of a fee-contract. 576 soums will be able to receive a scholarship.
Students who have paid the scholarship contract will receive a guaranteed scholarship of 517,880 soums even if they get 3 or 4 grades in the session.

With the beginning of the new academic year, many questions about student scholarships will arise. This issue is especially relevant for first-year students. Below you can find the most important information about student scholarships.

1. For students studying on the basis of a state grant

  • All students studying on the basis of a grant receive a basic stipend of 517,880 soums.
  • If a student gets an "excellent" (5) grade in all subjects at the end of the semester (a rating of 86 points and above), he will receive an increase of 20% of the basic stipend form – 621,456 soums will be assigned.
  • If gets a "satisfactory" (3) grade from 30% or more subjects (the rating is less than 71 points), the scholarship will not be paid during the next semester.
  • If he/she does not cover all subjects with 5 grades, but 30% or more if he does not get 3 marks in more subjects, he will be awarded a simple scholarship of 517,880 soums.

2. For students studying on a fee-contract basis

  • If a student has paid a contract with a scholarship, he will receive a monthly basic stipend of 517,880 soums.
  • If a student has paid a contract without a scholarship, he will not receive a scholarship.

All subjects To students studying on the basis of a contract with a grade of "5":

  • Those who paid a contract with a scholarship - will receive 621,456 soums (517,880 + 103,576).
  • Those who paid a contract without a scholarship - 103,576 soums.

For this reason, even if students studying under a contract without a scholarship get "5" marks in all subjects, they will have the opportunity to receive a scholarship of 103,576 soums.

  • Students who pay a contract with a scholarship even if they get 3 or 4 marks in the session, they will receive 517 A guaranteed scholarship of 880 soums will be paid.

3. Additional scholarship for certain categories of students

For students with group 1 and 2 disabilities:

  • Regardless of the mastery rate, 50% of the basic scholarship - 776,820 soums is assigned.

Orphans and those deprived of parental care for students:

  • Students who are fully funded by the state and do not have debts from subjects will receive a scholarship of 776,820 soums.

Source: Resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 59 of January 31, 2020 — Official Data
