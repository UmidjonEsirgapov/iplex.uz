Basic Information:

The Chinese Government Scholarships program funded by the Chinese Ministry of Education has been announced. The Chinese Scholarship Council will start accepting online applications for this grant from all international students for undergraduate, graduate, doctoral and student exchange programs from November 1, 2021. The CSC scholarship program offers fully funded education to foreign students at its prestigious universities. A big bonus is that foreign students don't have to take the Gaokao entrance exam, which is mandatory for Chinese universities and is considered the toughest entrance exam in the world. This scholarship is valid for all countries of the world. To apply, the candidate must select a study program, fill out the application and attach the necessary documents.

Requirements for candidates:

  • For all foreign nationals (Chinese nationals are not eligible)
  • A school/college/high school diploma is required for undergraduate and a 4-year bachelor’s degree for master’s;
  • For undergraduate level the candidate must not be more than 25 years old, and not more than 35 years old for master;
  • Candidates must not have won any grant from the Chinese government before;

Note: This program includes 3 types of grants:

  1. Chinese CSC Scholarship Category A : Fully Funded- this grant includes the following includes: a) Full contract amount b) Registration expenses c) Accommodation provided d) Medical insurance e) Monthly stipend ($350-500);
  2. Chinese CSC Scholarship Category B: Partially Funded: This grant includes: will receive: a) Full contract amount b) Registration costs c) Housing provided d)Medical Insurance;
  3. Chinese CSC Scholarship Category C: Nearly Funded: a) Partially covers contract amount;

Program admission process:

  • Chinese Government Scholarship Program You can register online for this grant program. You choose your personal information and 3 Chinese universities of your choice;
  • After registration, you will be asked about the code of the selected universities (the code will be available on the internet) and the type of grant;
  • Then you will upload all your documents in one PDF format; download the online application form for the course;
  • Upload the application form of the Chinese Government Scholarship grant program together with the online application of your chosen university to the website;
  • Submit all the documents requested by your chosen university to your university;
  • The results will be announced on the website of the Chinese Government Scholarship grant program, after which you will prepare the necessary documents for the visa;

Note : Application for the program will start on November 1, 2021 and will end in April 2022;
