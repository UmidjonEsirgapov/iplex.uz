When does "Perevod" start for the 2023/2024 academic year?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Applications for transferring studies from abroad will be accepted from July 15 to August 5.
Transferring studies from accredited higher education institutions of foreign countries to higher education institutions of Uzbekistan State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of Uzbekistan
Required documents: copy of application rating book or academic certificate copy of passport Exams will be conducted in the form of a test (from 5 subjects) or a professional (creative) exam (test from 3 mandatory subjects) depending on the direction.

Applications for transfer of studies from abroad are accepted from July 15 to August 5.

The transfer of studies from accredited higher education institutions of foreign countries to higher education institutions of Uzbekistan is carried out by the decision of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of Uzbekistan.

Required documents:

  • application
  • copy of rating book or academic reference
  • copy of passport

Depending on the direction of the exams a test (of 5 subjects) or a professional (creative) exam (a test of 3 subjects is mandatory).

The result of the special test is presented to the State Commission, and the citizen is transferred to the educational groups specified by the decision of the State Commission on the basis of a fee-contract.
