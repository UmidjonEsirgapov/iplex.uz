Today in Uzbekistan interest in learning, scientific activities and reading among young people is increasing significantly. Numbers and specific examples show how this process is effective.

The number of young people with scientific degrees has increased dramatically

The interest in science among young people has increased, the number of scientists with scientific degrees has reached 4 thousand 357, which is almost 2 times more than last year. Such an increase indicates that a new generation is forming in the field of science.

Parviz Tuksanov - showed maximum results in the SAT exam

Interest in foreign languages has also increased. This is proven by the fact that Parviz Tuksanov scored 1600 points on the SAT exam. This is the highest result in the world. President Shavkat Mirziyoyev emphasized this achievement and expressed his gratitude to Parviz's family members and teachers.

Book-loving has become a mass movement

Based on the idea of ​​"Book-loving nation", a culture of reading is being formed among young people. The number of participants of the traditional Yosh Kitabkhan competition has increased 6 times and reached 3 million.

More than 1 million young people have joined the ranks of readers through the "Mutolaa" project created by active young people. These projects are evaluated as an effective means of instilling love for books.
