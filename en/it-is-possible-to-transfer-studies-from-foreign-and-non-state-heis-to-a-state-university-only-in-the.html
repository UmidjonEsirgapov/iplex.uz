It is possible to transfer studies from foreign and non-state HEIs to a state university only in the summer

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
In the winter period, transfer of studies is allowed only for state HEIs for 2 valid reasons.
In the winter period, it is allowed to transfer studies only for state HEIs for 2 good reasons: ➖ in order to transfer studies to a higher education institution in the area where the spouse permanently lives due to the student starting a family; ➖ employees of state bodies to transfer the education of their spouse or minor child in case of a change of workplace (based on the recommendation of the head of the relevant ministry). transfer is organized only in July-August in accordance with the current regulations.

In the winter period, transfer of studies is allowed only for state higher education institutions for 2 good reasons:

➖ to move the student to a higher education institution in the area where his/her spouse permanently lives in connection with starting a family;

➖ in case the employees of state bodies change their place of work to transfer the studies of a spouse or minor child (on the basis of the recommendation of the head of the relevant ministry (office).

Applications to transfer studies for valid reasons will be accepted by regional working groups from December 20 to January 20. — Official Data
