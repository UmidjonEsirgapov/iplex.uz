According to it, there are 43 types of benefits in the entrance exams.

As they are grouped separately on the official website of the Education and Skills Assessment Agency, the benefits differ from each other:

– Benefits that are the basis for recommending a student based on an additional state grant without an exam

– Benefits that are the basis for recommending a student based on a basic payment contract without an exam;

- Privileges based on being exempted from the exam in the relevant subject and giving a certain amount of points;

- Privileges that are the basis for awarding additional points compared to the accumulated points;

- Privileges that are the basis for participating in the competition within the additional quota;

- Additional privileges.

  • Children of military personnel

Preferences that are the basis for recommending students on the basis of a basic fee-contract without an exam
