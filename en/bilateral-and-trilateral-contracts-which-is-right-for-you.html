Students are faced with the item "Choose the type of contract" when applying online through the kontakt.edu.uz site to receive a payment-contract. In this section, students are asked to choose either a two-way contract or a three-way contract. Many applicants received questions in this regard. In this article, we will give you complete information on how to make this choice.

What is a bilateral agreement?

A bilateral agreement is concluded between a Higher Education Institution (HEI) and an applicant. This type of contract should be chosen if the applicant wants to pay for the contract or super-contract himself.

When to choose a bilateral contract:

  • The applicant makes the contract payment from his own funds.
  • If he wants to get an educational loan from the bank.
  • If the payment is made from the monthly salary from the parents' office.

In these cases, it is not necessary to get a tripartite agreement and only a bilateral agreement is sufficient.

What is a tripartite agreement?

A tripartite agreement is ETM, applicant and another sponsoring organization is made between This type of contract should be chosen if a sponsoring organization wants to pay the contract or super-contract money, not the applicant himself.

When to choose a tripartite contract:

  • The sponsoring organization (for example, an enterprise or fund) covers the applicant's contract fee.
  • The condition of working for that organization later
  • Input of STIR numbers of the sponsoring organization is required.

Considerations when choosing the type of contract

The correct choice of the type of contract is important for speeding up the application process and clearly defining future financial obligations.
