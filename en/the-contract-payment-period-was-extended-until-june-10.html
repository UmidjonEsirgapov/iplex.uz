The contract payment period was extended until June 10

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The deadline for transferring tuition fees for certain categories of students and formalizing admission to the ranks of students is June 10, 2023. (also on the same day).
This period applies to the following who are studying in bachelor's education and master's specialties: Applicants recommended for studentship on the basis of a fee-contract, including a differentiated fee-contract (1st courses); The reading is copied (copied and restored); and for reinstated students. Also, the deadline for applicants recommended to study at colleges and technical institutes (students of the 1st stage) has been extended until June 10 (the payment of those who paid their contract after March 15, 2023 is also taken into account). taking into account the appeals of parents and rectors of higher education institutions, the deadline for payment of tuition fees for certain categories of students and formalization of admission to the ranks of students was set as June 10, 2023 (the same day).
