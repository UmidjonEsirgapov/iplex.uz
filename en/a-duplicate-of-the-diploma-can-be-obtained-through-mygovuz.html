The service "Obtain a duplicate diploma of higher, secondary special, vocational and professional education" has been launched on the portal of the single interactive state services.

Compatriots whose diplomas have been damaged or lost can get a duplicate You can apply online through my.gov.uz and easily receive the document at any address.

That is, you do not need to go to the authorized body to get the result of the service. The document will be delivered through the courier service of "Uzbekistan Post".

We remind you that the duplicate has the same legal force as the original.
