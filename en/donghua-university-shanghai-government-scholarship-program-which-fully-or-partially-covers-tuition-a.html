Donghua University has been designated to host international students under the Shanghai Government Grant Program since 2006. Since then, DHU has been accepting 40 students from all over the world for bachelor, master or doctorate stages with the Shanghai government grant scholarship. must apply for one. You can see the educational programs offered by the university through the following links:

  • Bachelor's programs
  • Master's and doctoral programs

For students who have received a full grant, the following will be covered (Type A):

  1. Tuition contract fee fully covered
  2. Free accommodation (from university dormitory)
  3. Monthly stipend (for undergraduate programs: 2500 CNY; for master programs: 3000 CNY; for doctoral programs: 3500 CNY)
  4. Medical insurance and protection program costs

The following will be covered for students who have won a partial grant (Type B):

  1. Tuition contract fee will be fully covered
  2. Medical insurance and protection program costs

Requirements:

  • Applicant candidate must be healthy and not a citizen of China;
  • Applying to doctoral programs must be under 40 years old and have a master's degree;
  • Applying to master's programs 35 Must be younger than and have a bachelor degree;
  • Must be 25 younger than when applying for undergraduate programs and a high school (college or high school) graduate;
  • Highly active in academics and extracurricular activities be, but must not have been awarded with other scholarships provided by the Chinese government, institutions or organizations.

Admission to the program process:

  • First, check whether DHU University does not offer the academic program you want to study, and even if it does, check whether it is provided with SGS scholarships.
  • Before applying, DHU University, online application for the SGS grant program on this site to save your information in our scholarship database.
  • Then, to submit your application to one of DHU's offered programs, complete DHU's online application.
  • Submit copies of the required documents and pay the application fee.

Application deadline: March 31, 2021 (only for autumn semester)
