Internship start time: February 1, 2021.

Country: France, Paris.

The Paris office of the German Marshall Fund (USA) is looking for an intern for a period of 6 months. The intern will have the opportunity to gain practical experience at a leading think tank, as well as participate in political research in the field of transatlantic relations.

Requirements for candidates:

· candidates must be in a master's program (international relations/European politics/political science) or graduate;

· fluent in English and French. knowledge;

· creativity, entrepreneurship and responsibility;

· organizational skills, time management skills;

· ability to work in an international environment;

· ability to work individually and in a team;

· organization of projects, software planning, as well as research work experience;

· work experience or interest in foreign policy, diplomacy, transatlantic relations;

· Knowledge of European and American domestic and foreign policy, as well as transatlantic relations is preferred.

The intern will work directly with security and defense, European programs, as well as small projects on various issues related to French, European and US politics.
