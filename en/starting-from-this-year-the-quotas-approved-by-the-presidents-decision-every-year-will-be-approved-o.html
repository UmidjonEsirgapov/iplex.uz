Starting from this year, the quotas approved by the President's decision every year will be approved only for the state grant

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
This is determined by the Presidential Decree No. 279 (15.06.2022)
The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation reports that starting from this year, the quotas approved by the Presidential Decree every year are only for state grants. according to it, starting from the 2023/2024 academic year: parameters of the state order for admission to state higher education institutions (quota) will be approved only according to the state grant; The admission parameters based on payment-contract are approved by the decision of the Supervisory Boards of HEIs with financial independence, the rest in agreement with the relevant ministries and agencies, and are published annually on the official website of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation by June 20.

This is Presidential Decree No. 279 (15.06.2022) starting from:

State order parameters (quota) for admission to state higher education institutions are approved only based on a state grant;

Admission parameters based on a payment contract are determined by the decision of Supervisory Boards of HEIs with financial independence. approved in consultation with ministries and agencies and published annually on the official website of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation by June 20.
