France: scholarships for postgraduate studies

The most relevant news in the field of education today is:

Summary:
The Emile Boutmy Scholarships grant program has been established to provide financial support to foreign students studying/planning to study at the Master's level of the University of Science Po, France.
The last date of application for the Émile Boutmy Scholarship is 2022 November 30.
Requirements: Must have completed the bachelor's degree and been admitted to the master's degree at Science Po University; The candidate must have good grades and be socially active and talented; Must be fluent in English.

The Emile Boutmy Scholarships grant program is organized to provide financial support to foreign students studying/planning to study at the Master's stage of France's Science Po University. Socially and academically active students can participate in the program.

The deadline for applications for the Émile Boutmy Scholarship is November 30, 2022.

Benefits:

  • Participants receive €12,000 per year to pay the tuition fee.

Requirements:

  • Must have completed the bachelor's degree and been accepted to the master's degree at Science Po University;
