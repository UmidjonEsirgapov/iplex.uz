New procedures for entrance exams to colleges and technical schools of the Republic of Uzbekistan evaluation criteria were defined. This information will help the applicants to prepare and help them to understand the testing process more clearly.

Compulsory Subjects Test

For each test-taker, 3 compulsory subject tests are set. These subjects and evaluation criteria are as follows:

  1. Mother Language (Uzbek, Russian or Karakalpak Language)

    • Number of questions: 10
    • Points for each question: 1.1 points
    • Maximum points: 11 points

  2. Mathematics

    • Number of questions: 10
    • Points for each question: 1.1 points
    • Maximum points: 11 points

  3. History of Uzbekistan

    • Questions number: 10
    • Points for each question: 1.1 points
    • Maximum points: 11 points

Total possible points for compulsory subjects: 33 points.

Test of Subjects Suitable for Profession and Specialization Test

2 additional subjects are specified for the test corresponding to professions and specialties. These subjects and their assessment criteria are as follows:

  1. Subject 1

    • Number of questions: 30
    • Points for each question: 3.1 points
    • Maximum points: 93 points

  2. Subject 2

    • Number of questions: 30
    • Points for each question: 2.1 points
    • Maximum points: 63 points

Total points that can be collected in subjects suitable for professions and specialties: 156 points.

Total Points

Total points that can be collected in all subjects:

  • Maximum points in compulsory subjects: 33 points
  • Maximum points in subjects suitable for profession and specialty: 156 points
  • Total maximum points: 189 points

Registration to Colleges and Technical Schools

Registration to colleges and technical schools for entrants is carried out through the site my.uzbmb.uz until June 25. This process will help students prepare for their application and preparation process.
