Foreign students studying or planning to study in Japan for a doctorate or masters from around the world can apply for this scholarship. There are 2 application seasons:

  • For the spring season - from September 1 to October 31;
  • For the fall season - from April 1 to April 30.

For the spring season, there is a grant for 10 selected candidates . DETAILS.

Benefits:

  • 200,000 yen per month for 1 year or 2 years;
  • 180,000 yen per month for 3 years;
  • 150,000 yen per month for 4 or 5 years. Repayment period once started, it cannot be changed. Those who have studied for less than one year cannot apply.
  • A travel grant will be provided to cover the expenses of attending the international conference.

Requirements:

  1. Candidates must not be Japanese citizens;
    2. Students who are studying at a Japanese university in 2022 or have been accepted before April 2022;
  2. Students studying for a professional doctorate cannot apply. However, a student who submits a research plan has the right to apply.
  3. Must have at least 12 months of study;
  4. A student applying for a doctoral program must be at least 35 years old, and a student applying for a master's program must not be younger than 30 years old;
  5. Must have a level of conversational Japanese and submit all documents in Japanese.

  • online application submission (open from September 1);
  • Applicant's undergraduate and graduate (if applicable) grades (translation in Japanese);
  • Research objective (in Japanese);
  • Recommendation letter from academic supervisor;
