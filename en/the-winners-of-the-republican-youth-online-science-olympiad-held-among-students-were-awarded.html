OLIYGOH.UZ team on June 13, 2021, in order to support talented young people and further develop the intellectual potential of young people, the "Youth Online Science Olympiad" was organized among young people on the scale of the republic. taking into account, keeping up with the times, in order to serve to improve the legal literacy of young people, test questions on the subjects of English (Elementary, Intermediate, Advanced levels), Law (Constitutional Law), Computer Knowledge (General Computer Literacy) and IQ (Logical Questions) given 60 questions were presented to each of the participants, and they were given 1 hour to determine the answers to the questions.

A total of more than 1200 talented students and youths from different parts of our Republic participated in the Olympiad and fought for the victory.

The strongest among the participating candidates were selected and our youth who won Awarded with cash prizes and special QR-code DIPLOMAS:

Special certificates were presented to all candidates who participated in the Olympiad.

The main goal of this Olympiad, organized by the OLIYGOH.UZ team, is to support talented young people and further develop the intellectual potential of young people.

This Olympiad It is the 1st Olympiad organized by the OLIYGOH.UZ team, and the next Olympiads are planned to be held ONLINE and OFFLINE on a large scale in a special spirit.
