Hokimites will pay the contract of female students

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Hokimities will pay the contract of female students Every year 150 female students from each region will be paid contract money by the state hokimitas. single mothers serve as a great help to their daughters.
Distribution of reserved seats ✔️ 40 seats for first-year students ✔️ 100 seats for second-year and higher-level female students Who can use this privilege? 150 female students from one region will be paid contract money by the state governments. This initiative serves as a great help to girls who are in need of social protection, orphans, orphans and single mothers.

Distribution of seats

✔️ 40 seats for first-year students ✔️ 100 seats for second-year and above female students

Who can use this benefit is it possible?

Main categories:

  • Girls from low-income families (according to the Unified Register of Social Protection)

  • Student girls who are orphans or deprived of parental care

  • Women who are raising minor children alone, widowed women daughters

In what cases is the contract not paid?

  • For students studying in the second specialty

  • For girls studying in foreign or private higher educational institutions in Uzbekistan

Conclusion

This privilege offered by the Hokimites is a great supporting force in the education of female students in need of social protection. Applicants who meet the specified criteria will have the opportunity to submit an application and cover the contract fee from the state. — More information
