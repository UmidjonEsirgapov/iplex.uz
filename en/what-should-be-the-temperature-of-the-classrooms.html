What should be the temperature of the classrooms?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In accordance with labor protection requirements, the air temperature during training in educational institutions should be as follows: For light physical work - at least 21°C.
For heavy physical work - at least 16°C.
Physics for laboratories, it is recommended that the temperature be in the range of 18°C - 21°C, and the relative humidity of the air should be 40 - 60%.
