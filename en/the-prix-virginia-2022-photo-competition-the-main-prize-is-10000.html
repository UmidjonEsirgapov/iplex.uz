The Prix virginia 2022 photo competition - the main prize is €10,000

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
– The annual Prix Virginia is open to all women photographers, regardless of nationality or subject matter; - Work done for commercial advertising or as an editorial assignment for an article will not be accepted; - Submitted photos must not have been published or exhibited in France before.
Once all documents and photos are ready, you can register here.
- Applications close May 7, 2022, 23:59 (Paris time).- November 02, 2022: official award ceremony

General information:

The next "Prix Virginia 2022" photo competition has been announced for all women and girls interested in photography, regardless of religion, race, and age. This competition has been held since 2012, it takes place twice a year. The submitted photos must not have been previously presented at the French exhibition.

Benefits:

- The Sylvia S. Association will award €10,000 to the winner;

What types of photos are accepted and for whom?
works made as an assignment will not be accepted;

– Submitted photographs must not have been published or exhibited in France until now. However, the artist may have already exhibited other works in France;

– Photographers may exhibit their work directly or by contacting other professional experts (photo editors, gallery owners, photo agents, etc.);

– Award to finance the completion or exhibition of photographic works not intended;

–Application is absolutely free;

– Any application that does not meet the pre-defined conditions will not be considered by the jury;

Required documents:

  • CV/Resume;
  • Submitted work a short letter written by the candidate describing his creative and artistic dimension (in PDF format);
  • Candidates are strongly recommended to submit not a single picture, but a whole collection of work (up to 12-18 pictures) (in jpg format). Each photo must be in jpeg format, 72 dpi and 2 MB (or Mb);

Note: Each photo must show the photo number, name of the candidate, title and date of the photo in its title, for example: 01_NameFirstName_PhotoTitle. All documents and registration must be in English or French.

Once all documents and photos are ready, you can register here.

Important: Here you can familiarize yourself with the winning artworks of previous years . For all kinds of detailed information, including exactly what form of photos and what aspects will be taken into account during evaluation, and such questions, please contact here.

Important dates:

– January 13, 2022 to May 7, 2022: applications are accepted.

– May 7, 2022, 23:59 (Paris time) applications close.
– November 02, 2022: the official award ceremony will take place. Winners will be announced a few days in advance during an online live audience.
