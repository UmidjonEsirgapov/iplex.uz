An opportunity for students to do an internship at the Max Planck Institute in Germany

Max Planck Institute offers talented and knowledgeable bachelor, master and doctoral students to do internship at this university. The internship is in the form of a research program. Interns conduct research with graduate students and professors of the Max Planck Institute and find solutions to various problems.

Undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral students in informatics and technology around the world can apply for the program. Students are selected on a competitive basis.

The program is available throughout the year, but interns prefer summer programs.

The program usually lasts 12-14 months .

Benefits:

  • Participants receive a monthly stipend;
  • With free accommodation and transportation to the internship location.

Requirements:

  • The candidate must be a bachelor (completed at least the 3rd year), studying at the master's or doctoral level or have recently completed it;
  • Must be interested in the work carried out by the Max Planck Institute;
  • Must have sufficient knowledge of Computer Science;
  • A candidate must have excellent grades.

Application

Applications to the program are accepted online. Candidates should register through this link and submit the necessary documents.

It is recommended to apply for the program 4 months before the start of the internship.
