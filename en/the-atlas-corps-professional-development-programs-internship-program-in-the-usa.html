General information:

Atlas Corps of America a non-profit organization at the same time promoting the capacity building of students studying in higher education as one of the most successful ways to solve social problems. announced a 12-18-month internship grant program for candidates who have completed the bachelor's degree.
Benefits of the grant:
- monthly stipend to cover basic expenses (accommodation, meals, public transport, mobile phone costs);
- payment for air tickets (return); organizing related international and domestic trips.

Approximate size of monthly scholarships by city:
Boston: $1,874, Chicago: $1,657, New York: $1,693, San Francisco: $2,150, Washington, DC: $1,657.

Grant applicants:

  • Having a bachelor's degree or equivalent;
  • Age between 22 and 35 years old, candidates must be at least 35 at the time of receiving the scholarship; must be of age (scholarship holder must not be 36 years old before the end of the program);
  • Have experience in the non-profit sector (2 to 10 years);
  • Fluent command of spoken and written English;
  • Commitment to return home after the program ends;
  • Do not have US citizenship (residence);

Enrollment in the program process:

  • Candidates must register from this page and upload at least a copy of their bachelor's degree and 2 letters of recommendation;
  • Candidates invited to the second stage
  • Candidates who passed the interview stage are considered semi-finalists for the Atlas Corps Fellowship or the Virtual Leadership Institute, and according to the interests of the candidates, they are invited to participate in these programs;
  • At the last stage, the organization announces the desired positions for them and submits the semi-finalists who are eligible for it. will be interviewed;
  • Furthermore shortlisted candidates will be sent to the Atlas Corps headquarters where candidates can undergo qualification;
