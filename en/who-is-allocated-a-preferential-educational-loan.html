What is the interest rate of the preferential educational loan?

Educational loan by commercial banks is allocated to the borrower at the main rate of the Central Bank (14 percent).

In this case, if the main rate of the Central Bank is reduced, the interest rate on the education loan will decrease proportionally. In case of an increase in the main rate of the Central Bank, the interest rate on the education loan will remain unchanged.
