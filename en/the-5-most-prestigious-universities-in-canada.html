Originally controlled by the Church of England, the University of Toronto was the first university established in the colonized territory of Upper Canada. It is organized as a collegiate system like the prestigious universities of Great Britain. The University of Toronto ranks 1st in Canada in terms of academic performance.

Thus, the university is the first among universities in Canada to have its own academic publishing house, the first Faculty of Forestry in the country. It became the first university to have a budget of more than 1 billion Canadian dollars (586 million pounds). This university is home to many prestigious academic endeavors, including the Toronto School of Literature and Communication Theory and stem cell therapy research. The University of Toronto offers more than 700 undergraduate and 200 graduate degree programs and is recognized as one of the best medical institutions in the world.
