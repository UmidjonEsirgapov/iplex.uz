The period of entrance exams for the 2020-2021 academic year is scheduled to begin on August 1. However, how long and how long the current situation will continue may also affect the test start date.

During an online briefing held at the National Press Center, Farhod Mirzayev, head of the DTM department, gave information about the news regarding the organization of entrance exams this year.

Farhod Mirzayev said that the entrance exams are scheduled to begin on August 1, but the exams may be delayed depending on the quarantine situation.

"The period of entrance exams for the 2020-2021 academic year is scheduled to begin on August 1. However, how long and how the current situation will continue may also affect the timing of the test. In this, the duration of quarantine restrictions and the registration rate of applicants are taken into account," said the DTM representative at an online briefing.

In the briefing, information was also given about the preparation of the premises where the exam will be held. It was reported that a maximum of 754 applicants will be accommodated in each building in one shift, and the buildings will be disinfected 4 times a day.

In addition, information was provided about the process of selecting supervisors for the exams. It is planned to select 3,000 supervisors before the exam begins," Farhod Mirzayev noted.

Earlier, Deputy Prime Minister Behzod Musayev announced that this year's entrance exams are scheduled to begin on August 1. — Source
