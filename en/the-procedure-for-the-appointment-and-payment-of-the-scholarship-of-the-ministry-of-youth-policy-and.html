The procedure for the appointment and payment of the scholarship of the Ministry of Youth Policy and Sports was established

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The regulation on the procedure for appointing and paying the scholarship of the Ministry of Youth Policy and Sports by the Ministry of Justice has been registered with the state register.
The scholarship is awarded in the form of a monthly payment to athletes who are part of the main or reserve national sports teams of the Republic of Uzbekistan in the sports included in the program of the Olympic and Paralympic, Asian and Para-Asian Games and who meet the specified criteria.

According to the information service of the Ministry, this Regulation defines the procedure for appointing and paying the scholarship of the Ministry of Youth Policy and Sports of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

The scholarship is awarded in the form of monthly payment to athletes who are included in the main or reserve team of the sports national teams of the Republic of Uzbekistan and meet the specified criteria in sports included in the program of the Olympic and Paralympic, Asian and Para-Asian Games.

The scholarship is appointed by the Scholarship Commission established in the Ministry of Youth Policy and Sports of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Republican Centers for the Development of Sports and the National Paralympic Committee of Uzbekistan draw up a list of candidates for scholarships from the sports national teams based on the established criteria by the 5th day of the first month of each quarter and submit it to the Scholarship Commission.

The Scholarship Commission submits the list of scholarship candidates and their examines the documents confirming the results achieved in the competitions within 15 days.

At the meeting of the scholarship commission, a decision is made to award and pay a scholarship or to refuse to award a scholarship by a general vote.

The scholarship is paid in the amount of 3 times the minimum monthly salary for a period of one year.

The scholarship is for military personnel and students will also be appointed for the winners of the world championships. — Source
