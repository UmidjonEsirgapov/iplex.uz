Parents who do not send their children to school can pay a fine

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to the law, non-fulfilment of obligations to raise and educate minor children by parents or their substitutes causes a fine from 340,000 soums to 1,700,000 soums.
Also, preventing children from receiving compulsory general secondary education, secondary special, vocational education by parents or their substitutes will result in a fine of 10 to 15 times the BHM (from 3 million 340 thousand to 5 million 100 thousand soums).
