Holders of this certificate can earn additional points or bonuses. Below are the main benefits of national certification.

Teacher benefits

If a teacher scores 80% or higher on the national certification exam, a 20% bonus will be added to his/her monthly salary.

Benefits for applicants

If an applicant scores 86% or higher, he/she will receive the following benefits obtains:

  • In the case of the subject of the 1st block - 93 points
  • In the case of the subject of the 2nd block - 63 points
  • In the case of the compulsory subject (10 tests) - 11 points

If the result is from 60% to 85%, proportional points will be given in relation to the percentage. For example, those who have a result of 80% the applicant will be awarded 80% of 93 points (74.4 points).

Discount for compulsory subject

If the mother tongue is a compulsory subject and the applicant scores 60% or higher in the national certificate examination, he will be awarded a maximum of 11 points for the compulsory mother tongue subject.
