KhushnudbekUz channel announces an educational grant for students studying under a contract

General Information:

The https://t.me/khushnudbek channel announces an educational grant for contract students

The main goal of the project is to invest in education, provide financial support to needy students for their education, and create a culture of patronage in society. This educational grant program is the next educational grant in the Metsenat.uz project.

https://t.me/khushnudbek In the educational grant introduced on behalf of the Telegram channel, we intend to pay 3 million soums to 35 students, totaling 105 million soums.

Information: the funds allocated for this grant are covered by advertisements published on the Khushnudbek.Uz channel.

Young people who wish to register for the program can APPLY ONLINE.

Information about the educational grant:

For which higher education institutions: For all state, non-state, religious educational institutions;
For which stage: all stages of baccalaureate;
For which form of education: full-time, part-time and evening;
What aspects are given priority: high mastery rate, in need of social protection, orphans and true orphans, disabilities students.;
Quota number: 35 (10 of which are TDYUU students);
Education grant amount: 3 million soums (105 million soums in total);
Last day of application submission: October 31. — Official Data
