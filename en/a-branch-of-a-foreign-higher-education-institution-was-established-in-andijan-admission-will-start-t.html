A branch of "Collegium Humanum" Warsaw University of Management was established in Andijan.

The branch is a non-governmental higher education organization. Education at the branch is carried out in the form of full-time education, in English, Russian and Uzbek languages, in accordance with the curricula and subject programs approved by the Warsaw University of Management, on a fee-contract basis. The annual admission parameters of the branch Not less than 10% of the amount will be allocated for the admission of applicants (Based on a branch grant) from among talented children of families in need of social protection, and talented and high-achieving students will be given a discount of up to 50% compared to the fee-contract amount.
