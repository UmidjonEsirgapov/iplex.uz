68 cases of grade falsification among technical school graduates were identified

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:

This is reported by the press service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

From August 22, the study of the veracity of the data of the technical college graduates who were accepted to study at OTM on the basis of an interview began. According to it, 68 cases of changing grades "3" and "4" to "5" and violating the law were identified. This is reported by the press service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

As of August 22, information on more than 2,000 technical school graduates recommended to study at higher educational institutions has been studied. In it, 68 cases of falsification of grades, i.e., changing grades "3" and "4" to "5" and contrary to the requirements of the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On approving the regulation on the procedure for admission of graduates who have successfully completed technical schools to undergraduate courses of higher educational institutions on the basis of interview" were identified and formalized in the appropriate manner.
