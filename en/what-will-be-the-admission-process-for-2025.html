What will be the admission process for 2025?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
This system allows applicants to choose a higher education institution based on the test results.
2nd stage: choosing a higher educational institution and direction. Period: 15 days after the end of the tests. Chooses the form of education.
Applicants will have 15 days to think about the results of the test.

According to the presidential decree, the 2025 admission processes will be carried out in two stages according to the principle of "first test, then selection". This system allows applicants to choose a higher education institution based on test results.

Step 1: Registration for tests

Duration: June 5 - June 25 (also on this day)

Applicants only choose:

  • Test subject complex
  • Language of education
  • Teaching area
  • If there is a creative or professional exam, the state HEI that will give this exam

Important!

  • In the first stage, a higher education institution is not selected.
  • "I "I applied to the Law University" or "I applied to the Medical Academy" will not be used in the first stage.
  • Tests will be held in July-August.

2nd stage: selection of vocational training and direction

Term: 15 days after the end of the tests

will have 15 days to review.

  • During this time, an opportunity will be created to strategically plan the selection and choose the most optimal option.

    • Opportunities for those who could not get into the study

    • College or technical school with the score can choose.
    • After the announcement of the results of admission to OTM, they will have the opportunity to apply to colleges or technical schools within another 10 days.

    Conclusion

    Starting from 2025, admission to higher education will be carried out in two stages:

    1. In the first stage, a test
    2. At the second stage, the applicant chooses a higher education institution and a field depending on the test results.
