Detailed information about education loan

You can get detailed information about all questions related to education loan below. Read full information about the terms of education loan for students, interest rates, loan repayment period and to whom it is granted.

How much is the loan?

For 1st year students up to the amount of a one-year contract educational loan is allocated. For students of the second year and above, if the average indicator of learning subjects is below 3, the educational loan is allocated in the amount of 80% of the contract amount. (With the exception of children of families included in the unified register of social protection.)

How long should it be returned?

The loan is required to be returned for a period of 7 years starting from the seventh month after the end of the study period (3, 4 or 5 years).

What is the interest rate for the loan?

  • For young men: in the amount of the main rate of the Central Bank (now 13.5%, as of September 10, 2024).
  • For girls: No interest (0%).

Note: If the main rate of the Central Bank decreases, the loan percentage will also decrease, but if it increases, the interest rate will not increase.

Who is given an education loan?

  • Daytime only. to students studying at the bachelor's or master's level in the form of education.
  • To students who entered or transferred studies on the basis of a fee-contract.

In what cases are loans not granted?

  • Educational credit is not allocated for super-contract, part-time, evening, distance and special correspondence forms of education.

Which higher education institutions are granted?

Educational loans are allocated to state, non-state or foreign higher education institutions in Uzbekistan, as well as professional educational organizations and their branches.

Educational loans are not granted to students of higher education institutions located abroad or private higher education institutions not connected to the HEMIS program.

Is a guarantee required?

The borrower can get a loan with a guarantor through family members or third parties who have a permanent source of income. It is not necessary to be a relative.

Is the education loan granted to foreign citizens as well?

No, the education loan is allocated only to citizens of Uzbekistan. Foreign citizens can get loans from banks on commercial terms.

What documents are required?

The following documents are submitted to the bank:

  • Application,
  • Agreement (contract) between the student and the educational institution,
  • Documents on loan repayment,
  • Special for families entered into the single register of social protection reference.

How long will the application be processed?

The application will be processed within 5 working days and the loan agreement will be signed. If the application is rejected, the student will receive a response letter explaining the reason. The borrower can return the education loan before the terms specified in the contract.
