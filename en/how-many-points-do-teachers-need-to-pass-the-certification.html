The criteria for the points set for successful attestation of pedagogic personnel were announced. According to the results of the attestation, the professional level of the teacher is determined and he is assigned to the appropriate category.

Criteria of attestation points

  • Higher category – 80 points and above
  • First category – 70 points and above
  • Second category – 60 points and above
  • Expert category – 55–59 points

If the teacher shows results less than 55 points, he is considered not certified and is not suitable for the position of specialist (teacher with higher education)

Also, pedagogical personnel engaged in extra-ordinary attestation may be exempt from this requirement.

What will be the impact of the attestation results?

Teachers who have successfully passed the attestation:

✔ Will have the opportunity to obtain a higher position and category.

✔ Salary and overtime uses the incentive system.

✔ Recognized as a qualified pedagogue in the educational system.
