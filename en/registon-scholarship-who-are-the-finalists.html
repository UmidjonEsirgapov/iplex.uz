Registon Scholarship: Who Are the Finalists?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The "REGISTON SCHOLARSHIP" competition for students announced by the Registan educational project continues. and 581 participants who showed the highest results at this stage won the ticket to the finals.
According to the selection instructions and regulations, the candidates who showed the highest results in the finals will have the opportunity to participate.

On the scale of the republic, announced by the Registon education project for students "REGISTON "STIPENDIYASI" competition continues. On February 20, the 1st stage of the scholarship competition was attended by tens of thousands of talented students from different regions of our Republic, and 2211 candidates who showed the best results won a ticket to the next stage. (See the results)

✅ On February 27, 2nd stage exams were held among the candidates who passed to the second stage of the competition, and 581 participants who showed the highest results in this stage won the final ticket. (View results)

All participating candidates can view their results and download special certificates of the competition (Download certificates).

Who will participate in the final stage of the competition?

The competition According to instructions and regulations, the candidates with the highest results in the final will have the opportunity to participate. The organizers will send a special sms-notification to these participants.

How will the final stage take place?

Third stage: This is the stage of submission of documents, candidates participating in the scholarship competition will submit documents in the order specified through their personal profiles.

Candidates selected from the exams will submit the following documents online. Through a special platform window, they submit:

  • identity document;
  • reference from the place of study (verified through my.gov.uz or verified by the dean's office);
  • recommendation letter from the dean's office or department for the Registon scholarship;
  • Motivational letter (why they are in the "Registon scholarship" competition A special motivational letter written about what they consider worthy of winning and their goals. The letter can be submitted in Uzbek, Russian and English languages);
  • Achievements in the competition and various projects (in the 2021-2022 academic years);
  • CV (reference)
  • Published scientific works, patents, inventions ((in academic years 2021-2022) if available);
  • International examination certificates (if available).

All documents are submitted online through the platform. Candidates can submit the documents until March 10.

After the candidates download the documents, the total marks will be calculated and the results will be announced within 2 weeks.

  • For the documents: total 90 points will be given. In addition, the grades received by the participants in the previous two stages will be summarized and the final results will be published.

Incentive for the scholarship winners

The absolute winners of the scholarship competition will be solemnly awarded with a special certificate of "Registon scholarship holder", souvenirs, cash prizes in the special event hall of the "REGISTON" educational center. will be awarded.
