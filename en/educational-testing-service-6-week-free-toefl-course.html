Brief about the course and the TOEFL exam:

Educational Testing Service is offering a 6-week free TOEFL course in cooperation with the edx.org online education platform.

To everyone It is known that the TOEFL test is the most reliable and widely recognized exam that determines the level of English language proficiency in the world. This exam has helped millions of people achieve their dreams of studying, working or living abroad. More than 10,000 universities, government agencies, and organizations around the world accept TOEFL scores to make important decisions.

This course is highly interactive, using video materials, sample questions with explanations, short quizzes, and collaborative discussion windows. You will also have access to real test materials from past tests. During the weeks covering Speaking and Writing, all course participants can get essential tips for their future exams. In addition, free resources and discounted test preparation will be provided during the course.

What can be learned during the course?

  • How to improve your English language skills;
  • Lessons on reading, listening, speaking and writing in the four parts of the TOEFL test;
  • Useful tips for preparing for the TOEFL test;
  • Getting a job based on the results of the TOEFL test. information on how to apply for tuition, visas, and scholarships;
  • How to manage your time during the test;
  • Additional resources for the TOEFL test;
  • How to register for the TOEFL test.
