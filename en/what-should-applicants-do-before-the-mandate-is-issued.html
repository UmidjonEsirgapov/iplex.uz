What should applicants do until the mandate is issued?

Waiting for the mandate response is not only waiting, but also a period of careful preparation for the future student life. Below we will give you practical recommendations for successful implementation of this process.

1️⃣ Prepare your documents

Organize your passport, diploma or certificate, existing certificates and other necessary documents. Keep your documents in electronic and paper form.

2️⃣ Be ready for different options

Opportunities outside of the major field

  • In addition to your chosen field, consider other OTM or professional opportunities.

  • Compare grant or contract options and determine which one is financially convenient.

3️⃣ Make a financial plan

Estimate expenses in advance

  • Estimate the costs of rent, dorms or living in a house.

  • Create a budget for transportation, food and study materials.

4️⃣ Gather information about the new place

  • Explore the area where OTM is located on the map.

  • Find out nearby hostels, rental houses and transport routes.

5️⃣ Continue working on yourself

  • Consolidate your basic subjects, take additional tests.

  • Foreign languages, IT or other enroll in useful courses.

Conclusion
