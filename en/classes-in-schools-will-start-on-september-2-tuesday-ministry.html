More than 738 thousand first graders will be given "President's gift"

More than 738 thousand will enter first grade this school year. the state pays great attention to the child. A special "President's gift", consisting of 12 educational tools, will be ceremonially handed over to each student.

Contents of "President's gift"

The gift set is designed for students to start their school life comfortably and efficiently. It consists of the following educational tools:

  • Notebooks

  • Pens and pens

  • Paints

  • Drawing tools

  • Bag and other necessary educational equipment

Event importance

Such attention increases the desire of the young generation to learn, and serves as a great support for parents. At the same time, it ensures that every child enters the school with the same opportunity.

Conclusion

The "President's gift" is not only financial support for first graders, but also a symbolic gift that strengthens their love for learning. This initiative is important in increasing the interest of the young generation in education. — Read on infoedu.uz
