Contract Payment is Tax Free: Information on Benefits

There is a tax exemption benefit when payment for education is made. Below are the terms and conditions for using this benefit.

Who can use the tax benefit?

  • When you pay for yourself.
  • When you make a payment for your children.
  • When you pay a contract for your spouse under the age of 26.

Payment methods

Through your workplace:

  • You can transfer the payment to the school directly from your salary. In this case, the amount of the payment is excluded from the amount considered as income tax.

Independent payment:

  • If you make the payment in cash, non-cash or electronic form, you will be able to recover the amount of tax paid in advance by submitting a declaration to the tax authorities at the end of the year.
