What are the terms and benefits of the preferential education loan?

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Preferential education loan to students who are citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan recommended or accepted to study in the form of full-time education on the basis of a payment contract in all higher, secondary special, professional educational organizations operating in the Republic of Uzbekistan, as well as in joint educational programs
Family members of the borrower who have a permanent source of income (parents, brothers, sisters, husband or wife) or other third parties can participate as joint borrowers in the educational loan agreement.

Preferential education loan All higher, secondary special, professional educational organizations operating in the Republic of Uzbekistan, as well as students who are citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan recommended or accepted to study in the form of full-time education on a fee-contract basis in joint educational programs.

To the permanent source of income of the borrower. family members (parents, brothers, sisters, husband or wife) or other third parties can participate as joint borrowers in the educational loan agreement. For 1st-year students, this period can last up to 11 years and 7 months.

The amount of the educational loan and the interest rate

The loan is given in the amount specified in the payment contract between the student and the educational organization. The loan is provided at the main rate of the Central Bank (14 percent), but if the rate is reduced, the interest rate will be reduced accordingly.
