Changes introduced to the training system from 2025

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
From January 1, 2025, updates will be introduced to the training system for pedagogues.
Calendar of training plans pedagogues who received online training in 2024: Specialists with secondary and higher education (trainee teachers), validity period 1 the remaining first and higher-class teachers are scheduled for 2025.
Courses held once every 5 years to improve the knowledge and skills of teachers will serve to develop their professional skills. These changes are aimed at streamlining and improving the efficiency of the professional development process.

Main changes of professional development

  • Course duration: Professional development courses will be changed to 1 month.
  • Duration of retraining: Educators are set to participate in professional development courses once in 5 years.

Professional development plans calendar

Pedagogues with online training in 2024:

  • Secondary specialized and higher educated specialists (trainee teachers), first and higher category teachers with 1 year of validity remaining are planned for 2025.
  • Second category teachers and remaining first and higher category teachers for 2026

Pedagogues who received offline training in 2024:

  • Professionals with secondary and higher education (trainee teachers) are planned for 2027.
  • Second and first category teachers are planned for 2028.
  • Higher category teachers, as well as the category All remaining teachers will be scheduled for 2029.

The goal of the system

  • Updating the knowledge and skills of teachers based on modern requirements.
  • Regulating and improving the efficiency of the training process.
  • Regularly updating the qualification requirements for the categories and positions of teachers.

Conclusion
