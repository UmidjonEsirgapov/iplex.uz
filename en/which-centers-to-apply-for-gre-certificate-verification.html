Official centers

The GRE test centers approved by ETS in Uzbekistan are:

  1. Knowledge And Skills Assessment Center (Tashkent)Address: INHA University, 9 Ziyolilar street, main building, room 101, TashkentCenter code: STN20720A

  2. Micros Training CenterAddress: 86 Kichik Beshagach street, Tashkent

  3. STARS International University (Tashkent)Address: 2, Buyuk Ipak Yoli street, Mirzo Ulugbek district, TashkentCenter code: STN20757A

Official centers in the regions

The following regional centers of STARS International University also officially conduct the GRE exam:

  • Nukus city: 23 Karakalpagistan Street, Uzbekistan Youth Evening Building, 2nd floor
  • Opposite city: Islam Karimov Street, 288, Youth Union of Uzbekistan building, 1st floor

Official procedure for confirmation of results

If you take the exam at these official centers, the GRE results will be submitted to the Ministry of Preschool and School Education for viewing. With DI code (1282), the results will be visible to the ministry.
