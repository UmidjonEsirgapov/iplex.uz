Admission to correspondence and distance education in pedagogical fields has been suspended

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to the presidential decree No. PF-73, adopted on April 28, 2025, the admission to the form of correspondence education for pedagogic education has been completely suspended.
This change also applies to the form of distance education. applies.
All non-state higher education institutions can organize teaching only in the form of day or evening education.

According to the presidential decree No. PF-73 adopted on April 28, 2025, full admission to the form of external education for pedagogical education fields stopped. This change also applies to the form of distance education.

What has changed?

  • Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, pedagogical courses will not be accepted in correspondence and distance education forms.

  • Only day and evening education. forms are allowed to accept applicants.

  • This requirement applies equally to state and non-state universities.

How does it affect non-state higher education institutions?

  • Non-state higher education institutions that previously received a license for distance education can no longer accept applicants in this field either.

  • All non-state HEIs can organize education only in the form of day or evening education.

Now applicants who want to study in pedagogical fields only day or evening can apply for education. Admission for external and remote forms is strictly prohibited. This innovation aims to improve the quality, practical teaching and formation of a sustainable qualification system in pedagogical education. — Read on infoedu.uz
