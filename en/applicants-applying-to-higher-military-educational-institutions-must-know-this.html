About entrance tests to higher military educational institutions announcement

Entrance tests to higher military educational institutions (OHTM) will be held at the same time as tests conducted to higher educational institutions of the republic. For this reason, applicants who have submitted documents to OHTM should pay attention to the following when registering at state higher and professional educational institutions:

Focusing on the set of subjects

Applicants should choose subjects that correspond to the set of subjects in the relevant educational direction of OHTM. This is important for successful participation in the entrance exam.

Compatibility of the range of subjects

Applicants must match the range of subjects in the educational field of the OHTM to which they have applied and the range of subjects they choose when registering to state higher and professional educational institutions.

Consequences of choosing the wrong range of subjects

If the applicant is in the OHTM if he chooses a set of subjects that does not correspond to his relevant field, he will not be able to participate in the selection for admission to OHTM. This may cause the applicant to fail in the admission process.
