The National Statistics Committee scholarship competition has been announced: who can participate?

In order to identify talented students, support them, and create a pool of promising personnel for the National Statistics Committee, the "National Statistics Committee Scholarship" competition has been announced. This competition is an important opportunity for educated and active young people studying in higher educational institutions of Uzbekistan.

Who can participate in the competition?

Students studying full-time at higher educational institutions in the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan can participate in the competition.

Which majors can students participate in?

students studying in the fields of education can apply for the competition:

  • statistics;

  • economics;

  • finance;

  • information technology;

  • data science and analysis (Data Science & Analytics).

For which stage students?

Students of the following stages will participate in the competition:

  • Bachelor 3-4 stage students;

  • Master stage students.

At what stages is the competition held?

"National statistics committee's scholarship" competition is organized in 3 stages.

Stage 1: Submission of documents and selection

At this stage, documents submitted by candidates are reviewed and selection is made.

Stage 2: Tests

Candidates who have successfully passed the selection will participate in tests in the following areas:

  • statistics;

  • economics;

  • mathematics;

  • analytical thinking and IQ;

  • English language.

Stage 3: Interview

At the last stage, candidates are interviewed by the members of the selection committee.

Note: Candidates who have successfully passed stage 1 will be contacted and invited to stage 2.

What is the procedure for submitting documents?

Candidates should submit documents in PDF format only.

Date and address for document submission

  • Deadline: February 15, 2026, until 18:00

  • E-mail: sto1@stat.uz

Documents to be submitted

The following documents are required from candidates:

  • reference (CV, with contact information);

  • motivation letter;

  • academic rating indicators (academic reference);

  • documents on knowledge of foreign languages (if available);

  • documents on scientific research works (if available);

  • documents confirming achievements and social activity (if available).

Stipend amount and number of winners

According to the results of the competition, 10 winners will be determined. The winners will be paid a scholarship in the amount of 3 times the BHM during the second half of the 2025-2026 academic year (February-June). This competition provides not only a financial incentive, but also an opportunity to gain access to important experience and human resources for working in the state statistics system in the future.
