In this letter, it is strictly determined to make overtime payments for native language and history teachers from January 1, 2025.

According to the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated February 21, 2024 PF-37, from January 1, 2025 starting from 2018, teachers who teach mother tongue, literature, and history in general secondary education institutions and who have a national certificate issued by the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency will be paid an additional monthly bonus of 50 percent.

In this letter, the implementation of additional payments for native language and history teachers is strictly defined from January 1, 2025.

These changes are aimed at further appreciating the qualifications and work of native language and history teachers. This decision creates an incentive to improve the quality of education and active participation of pedagogues in the national assessment system. — Official Data
