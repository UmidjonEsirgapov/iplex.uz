Entrance tests to professional educational institutions

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Entry tests for professional educational institutions (college and technical school) for the 2023-2024 academic year will be held on September 26-29, 2023 in 2 shifts. from 08:00 to 11:00; It will be held in the 2nd shift from 15:00 to 18:00.
The test will be conducted in 2 subjects suitable for profession and specialty and 3 subjects that are mandatory for all test takers (mother tongue, mathematics, history of Uzbekistan).

For the 2023-2024 academic year, professional educational institutions (college and technical school) entrance tests will be held in 2 shifts on September 26-29, 2023. Tests will be held:

  • 1️⃣-shift from 08:00 to 11:00;
  • 2️⃣-shift from 15:00 to 18:00.

Testing 2 subjects suitable for profession and specialty and 3 subjects compulsory for all test-takers (mother tongue, mathematics, Uzbekistan) history).

In it, applicants are given a total of 90 test tasks, 30 from specialized subjects, 10 from compulsory subjects, and 3 hours to work on them.

The maximum points that can be collected in the tests are 189 points, and for each correct answer:

  • in the 1st specialty subject - 3.1 of points (total 93 points);
  • in the 2nd specialization subject — 2.1 points (total 63 points);
  • compulsory subjects — 1.1 points (total 33 points).

Applicants can download the permission to participate in the test from the website of the Knowledge Assessment Agency my.uzbmb.uz.

Applicants can take the test. they are asked to come to the venue at least 1 hour before the scheduled time with an identity document and a permit to participate in the test. — Official Data
