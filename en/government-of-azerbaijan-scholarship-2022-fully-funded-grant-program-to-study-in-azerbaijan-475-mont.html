Government of Azerbaijan Scholarship 2022 : Fully funded grant program to study in Azerbaijan-$475 monthly scholarship

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
General information: Announcement of fully funded grant programs by the government of Azerbaijan for citizens of this country (including Uzbekistan) to study at one of the most prestigious universities in Azerbaijan at all levels (undergraduate, graduate, doctoral, general medicine/residency)
Visa and registration fees are also exempted.

(undergraduate, graduate, doctoral, general medicine/residency) has announced fully funded grant programs for citizens of these countries (including Uzbekistan). This grant is provided to a total of more than 40 students every year.

Benefits of the grant:

  • Full contract for bachelor, master, doctorate and other courses.
  • Expenses for international round-trip tickets are also covered.
  • Monthly stipend for food, accommodation and utilities (800 AZN= $475).
  • Medical insurance expenses.
  • Visa and registration expenses are also exempted.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Applicants for bachelor programs must be under 25 years old.
  • Applicants for master programs must be under 30 years old
  • Applicants for doctoral programs must be under 40 years old

Required documents list:

  • Completed application form;
  • Diplomas or transcripts from previous high school or university studies;
  • Copy of passport;
  • Document on general health status (including HIV/AIDS, Hepatitis B and C tests);
  • CV/Resume;
  • Motivation letter;
  • A document confirming the English language (not mandatory);

Note: before applying to the program, you should first get the document submission instructions, list of eligible countries, list of universities in Azerbaijan, as well as answers to frequently asked questions from here possible.
