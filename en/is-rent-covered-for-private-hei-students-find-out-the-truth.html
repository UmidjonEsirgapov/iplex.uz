Questions related to student rent are common. Are students studying in private higher education institutions able to use this benefit or not? In this article, you will find answers to these questions.

Are there benefits for students of private higher education institutions?

Unfortunately, rent is not reimbursed for students studying in private higher education institutions. Only students studying in full-time state higher education institutions can use this privilege. This does not include students studying in higher education programs and faculties of foreign cooperation.

For which students is the rent reimbursed?

According to the current regulations, 60 percent of the rent expenses of students studying in state higher education institutions, studying on the basis of a state grant or payment contract, and living on the basis of the right to rent It will be covered from the State budget of the Republic of Uzbekistan. There is no difference between students studying on the basis of a contract or studying on a grant - both have privileges.

How are students selected?

Special commissions in higher education institutions select students who will be reimbursed for rent. These commissions consider the social and economic status of each student when selecting them. — More information
