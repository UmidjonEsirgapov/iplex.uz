We chose "Registon"! Because...

During our visit to the Andijan branch, we were accompanied by the head of the branch Jasurbek Yusupov. This branch is located opposite the train station in Andijan city, near the Babur statue in the area called "Yangibozor" in the local language.

Our Andijon branch is established in a brick building that will make everyone jealous. Just like prestigious universities abroad and our own Westminster! It is convenient in every way, next to the train station, near the market, the bus station...

The first floor consists of a reception hall. There are 20 rooms on the second floor, 17 of which are study rooms, and the other three are the director's room, kitchen and library. Today, more than 25 professional teachers work in our educational center. 17 of them are English language teachers, and the rest are teachers who prepare applicants. Such a small team has been teaching about 500 students!

Like other branches, the Andijan branch mainly specializes in the English language, that is, English is taught in an in-depth manner compared to other subjects. Our English language teachers and other teachers who prepare applicants are real owners and experts in their field. They are considered to be professional teachers who graduated from Turkish high schools, studied in foreign countries, or at least gained their qualifications abroad. You will also immerse yourself in the debates and learning processes here! The atmosphere in our educational center does not leave students interested in studying and learning. We were especially interested in the unique design of the rooms and the fact that they were named after famous cities. A teacher who wants to start a new job first talks with the HR manager and then with the supervisor. Students take the exam 4-5 times until they finish one book. Exams are divided into internal and external exams. In addition, monthly mock tests are also organized regularly. Exams are designed to be as similar as possible to DTM practice tests. The purpose of this is to prepare students mentally and psychologically.

Our attention was drawn to the fact that a coworking zone and classrooms have been organized in the basement of the building in our Fergona branch, and even one square meter of land has been used effectively. Fergana branch of "Registon" today has about 100 employees and more than 2200 students. These numbers continue to grow. Despite the fact that this branch has been opened for 1 year, about 80 IELTS results have been issued. The highest score was 7.5. Our teachers intend to record more than 200 results by the end of the year. In addition, more than 400 students will be trained in applicant subjects, and more than 100 of them will pass this year.

The good thing is that "Registon" educational center has cooperation with "IDP IELTS". The IELTS Mock zone on the third floor was organized in cooperation with "IDP IELTS". This makes it much easier for residents of Fergana to pass IELTS exams. Fergana residents don't need to go to Tashkent to take IELTS, they can easily take the exam at the "Registan LC" branch in Fergana!

The capacity of the Fergana branch is designed for 3,500 people! Support zones can accommodate 100 people on average. Support teachers work with students in 2 shifts, from morning to evening. The main goal is to strengthen knowledge, to master the subjects perfectly, to analyze with support what they did not understand in the lessons, and to record good results.

Our Fergana branch has a number of requirements for hiring support teachers and main teachers. Here, assistant teachers are divided into two: assistant teachers working in the coworking zone on the lower floor with Beginner, Elementary, Pre-intermediate levels and assistant teachers working in the upper coworking zone with Intermediate, IELTS levels. We attach them to the lead mentor before hiring them. We have leading teachers from all disciplines, first they try and recommend to us, then we conduct an interview with that person. In the end, they give us a lesson, and then we train them based on our special programs. We observe, if there is a staff who really has pedagogic skills, who knows his field perfectly and corresponds to our values, we will certainly accept them into our team!

Also, there are some lazy students, their motivation "dies" in the summer and they drop out with various excuses. We launched the "Exam King" project in order to keep them interested in studying.
