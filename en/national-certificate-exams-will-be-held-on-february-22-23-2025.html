National Certificate Exams will be held on February 22-23, 2025

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
Necessary requirements for the exam Applicants must come with a passport or ID card.
1st shift: must pass the examination and settle down by 08:30. Candidates who are qualified can take part in the exam on February 22-23, 2025.

On February 22-23, 2025 (Saturday, Sunday), national certificate exam in mathematics, physics, history and geography will be held. Applicants who want to take part in the exam can download the permit from my.gov.uz website.

Necessary requirements for the exam

  • Applicants must come with a passport or ID card.
  • 1st shift: from to 08:30 inspection
  • 2nd Shift: must be screened and seated by 2:00 p.m.

Test Times

  • Applicant screening for 1st shift at 07:30 starts, and the test starts at 09:00.
  • Checking of applicants for the 2nd shift starts at 13:00, and the test starts at 14:30.

Procedure for getting a permit

Applicants The permit can be downloaded from the "My Applications" section of the my.gov.uz site.

Conclusion

Applicants who want to obtain a national certificate in mathematics, physics, history and geography can take part in the exam on February 22-23, 2025. It is recommended to come to the exam on time and get the permit in advance. — Read on infoedu.uz
