Important note on the Emaktab.uz (Kundalik.com) platform (newly changed rules)

The Ministry of Public Education has corrected the electronic journal "Kundalik" and important instructions for timely filling. According to it, teachers are required to put a mark on the class completed schedule immediately after the lesson.

Also, grading of lessons should be done until 21:00 on the same day for lessons of the 1st shift, and until 13:00 the next day for lessons of the 2nd shift.

During the evaluation process, in the classroom at least 30 percent (one third) of students should be evaluated. Failure to post marks in the electronic journal on time will be considered as absenteeism by the instructor and will not be paid for the lesson.

Starting April 15, 2023, editing of grades posted in the journal will only be allowed within 72 hours. After this period, grades will be frozen and cannot be changed.
