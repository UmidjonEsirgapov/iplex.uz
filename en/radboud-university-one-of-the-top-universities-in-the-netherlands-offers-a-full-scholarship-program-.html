Radboud University, one of the top universities in the Netherlands, offers a full scholarship program for master's degree

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Available for master's programs in almost all fields and specializations, including: social sciences, arts, medicine, law, etc.
Students initially admitted to the master's level can then apply for the Radboud grant program; Deadline: The deadline to apply for the Radboud University Netherlands 2022 Scholarship for International Students is March 1, 2022.
Application Process: The application process is online.

General Information:

Radboud University is one of the top universities in Europe located in the Netherlands. has announced its grant program for all citizens of countries outside the European Union who want to study at the master's level. The total number of grants is 37. In this university, subjects in all directions are taught entirely in English. Available for almost all fields and majors, including: Master's programs in social sciences, arts, medicine, law, and more. All students of any nationality are eligible to apply for a Radboud University scholarship in the Netherlands.

Benefits of the grant:

Requirements for candidates:

  • Only citizens of countries outside the European Union can apply;
  • Academic performance at the undergraduate level must be excellent;
  • Bachelor degree must be obtained outside the Netherlands;
  • Candidate Radboud must have been admitted to the master's level of the university;

Required documents:

  • Passport copy;
  • Academic performance (transcript grades);
  • Motivation letter;
  • 2 letters of recommendation;
  • Resume/CV;

Note: Admission to a master's program and receiving a Radboud scholarship are two separate processes, successful acceptance to a master's program does not guarantee selection for a scholarship. Students initially admitted to the Master's program can then apply for the Radboud Scholarship Program;

Deadline:

The deadline for international students to apply for the Radboud University Netherlands 2022 Scholarship is 1-March 2022.
