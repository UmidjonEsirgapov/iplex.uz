Can the applicant not take the entrance exam if he gets a national certificate?

Of course it will! Applicants can take the exam for a certificate in the following subjects and earn points in advance:

  1. Mathematics
  2. Biology
  3. Chemistry,
  4. Physics
  5. Native language and literature (Russian, Karakalpak)
  6. History
  7. Geography

Note: Other it is not possible to get a national certificate from the subjects at the same time (except for foreign languages), but it can be added in the coming years.

For example, if an applicant applying to a medical university takes a certificate from chemistry, biology, only 3 subjects from the mandatory block will be tested in the exam.

Otherwise, if the applicant who wants to enter the pedagogy course with the main block subject of History and Mother Language and Literature succeeds in obtaining a national certificate in History and Mother Language, then he will receive both the main and compulsory subject points in advance. In the entrance exam, only mandatory block tests from Mathematics are used! If he does not want to work in the mandatory block of mathematics, he can withdraw the national certificate from this subject.
