7 mistakes made by applicants who have just started their preparation

1. Procrastination

At the beginning of the school year, time seems to pass by. There is an opinion that it is possible to start preparing for exams later. But if you start work not today, but in a month, preparation becomes more difficult. If you start after half a year, you'll feel like you haven't learned anything in so long, seeing that there are so many topics to learn.

2. Overconfidence

There is no guarantee that even students who consistently study for excellent grades will get high results on tests. Your exam performance depends on how well you master the subject, how well you can solve examples and problems, and how well you remember information.

3. Excessive saving of money

Independent preparation for the exam without any expenses often does not give results. Do not believe people who say that they will teach you for free or for a very small amount, you will learn through free video lessons on Telegram. The demand for qualified teachers is always high. Rely on courses from experienced teachers.

4. Allocating time for other activities

To achieve good results in exams, separate your most important goals for yourself. You can spend time doing one thing and another without knowing how much you are losing.

5. Preparing until the sillage is dry

Preparing without rest and sleep can also have a negative effect on your nervous system and your psychological state. To successfully prepare for the exam, you need to know how to distribute everything correctly. It is very important to have time to meet friends and have fun.

6. Being jealous of other people's success

It is not a good thing to compare yourself with others. Everyone has their own learning speed and learning ability. Focus on yourself and your results and achievements.

7. Lack of confidence in your own achievement

When you ignore the results you are achieving and underestimate the value of your daily work, fear and insecurity surround you. In addition, your ability to acquire knowledge and remember will decrease. — Official Data
