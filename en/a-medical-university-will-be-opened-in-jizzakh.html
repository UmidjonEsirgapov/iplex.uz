According to the press service of the Ministry of Health, the Jizzakh branch of the Samarkand State Medical University and the Regional Children's Diagnostic Center equipped with the most modern equipment will be established in the city of Jizzakh.

The main tasks of this branch are the training of qualified medical personnel in the region, the development of qualification requirements, curricula and science programs based on the application of the advanced experience of foreign higher education institutions. to ensure the coherence of research results;

- to introduce and develop academic mobility programs of students, professors and researchers as well as joint education programs within the framework of cooperation with foreign medical higher education institutions;

- to increase the potential of the branch's scientific and pedagogical staff by regularly sending talented young graduates to republican and foreign higher education institutions and research centers for master's and doctoral studies;

- In order to increase the regional and international prestige of the branch, to expand the educational opportunities for foreign citizens and to implement advanced international experience.
