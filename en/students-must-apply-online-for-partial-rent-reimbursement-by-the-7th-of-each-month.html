Procedure for submitting an application for reimbursement of rent payments for students

The possibility of partial reimbursement of rent payments by the state is a great help for students. Online application is required by the 7th of every month to avail this benefit. Below is the step-by-step procedure and required documents.

Online application procedure

1. Registration and access to the office

The student must register in the kontrakt.edu.uz information system and enter his personal office.

2. Section selection

In the cabinet, the section "Rental payments" is selected.

3. Submit application

Press the button "Send application" and the questionnaire will be officially sent.

Requirement of documents

For first-year students

For September and October of the current academic year, lease agreement is not required.

Second-year students and above for

In order to cover rent payments, there must be a rental contract.

Rental contract accounting service: ijara.soliq.uz

Legal basis

This procedure is carried out based on Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 605 of September 24, 2021. (Source: lex.uz). Although the first months are exempted from the contract for students of the 1st year, it is mandatory to have a contract for those in the upper year. — Read on infoedu.uz
