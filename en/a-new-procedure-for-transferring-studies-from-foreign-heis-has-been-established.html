A new procedure for transferring studies from foreign HEIs has been established

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The Ministry of Justice announced a new procedure for transferring students' studies from July 15, 2021.
The order of these processes is as follows: - study from higher education institutions in foreign countries (included in the international list of 1,000) When transferring to the appropriate and similar directions of the state higher education institution in Uzbekistan: - professional (creative) exams in directions requiring special talent, tests in other directions are held; - passing scores for tests and professional exams are determined by July 25 of this year, and then every year until July 15, and are announced in the mass media; - students who scored lower than the passing score are admitted to study on the basis of a differentiated payment-contract; - the results of tests and professional exams are published on the official websites of DTM and OTM; - it is not allowed to transfer studies from higher education institutions in foreign countries to the corresponding and unrelated directions of the state HEIs in Uzbekistan. class="newsView-images articlePage-Slider-height" data-src="/_nuxt/img/b078bb7.jpg">
