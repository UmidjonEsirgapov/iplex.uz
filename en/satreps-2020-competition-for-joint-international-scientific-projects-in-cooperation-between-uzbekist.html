Republic of Uzbekistan The coordinators of the Ministry of Innovative Development and the SATREPS-2020 project "The Project for Development of Innovative Climate Resilient Technologies for Monitoring and Controlling of Water Use Efficiency and Impact of Salinization on Crop Productivity and Livelihood in the Aral Sea region" ("The Project for the Development of New Technologies for Monitoring and Controlling the Effective Use of Water Resources to Combat Salinity in the Island Region and Increase Land Use Productivity") announce a competition among research groups of Uzbekistan.

This project envisages the establishment of a climate change observation center, meteorological monitoring, as well as the introduction of new technologies in agriculture to combat salinity in order to raise the level of well-being of local communities in the Aral Sea region under conditions of limited water supply and increase land productivity.

Details about this competition and its categories are provided here.

Each the participation of several scientific teams in the project has advantages. DETAILS Including,

  • Monthly allowance for project members;
  • Travel expenses;
  • Payment for equipment needed in the project;
  • Payment for other expenses of the scientific project.

Scientific projects participating in the competition until November 30, 2021 via grant.mininnovation.uz e-mail It is accepted in electronic form through the "Submission of scientific projects to the competition" page of the platform.

In the process of registering research project documents through the electronic platform grant.mininnovation.uz, it is necessary to upload the pdf/doc electronic form of the initial documents:

  • Letter of support for the project from the Japanese university of the participant of SATREPS-2020;
  • Letter of recommendation signed by the head of the executive body (pdf);
    Implementation extract from the decision of the scientific (scientific and technical) council of the promoting organization (pdf);
  • Text of the project (signed and sealed, in pdf and doc/docx versions).
