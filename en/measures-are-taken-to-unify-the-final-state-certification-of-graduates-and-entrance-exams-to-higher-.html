Measures are taken to unify the final state certification of graduates and entrance exams to higher education institutions and introduce a single exam.

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
It has been established: this year graduates of general education institutions will pass tests in three subjects - mother tongue (Uzbek, Russian or Karakalpak language), mathematics and history of Uzbekistan (compulsory subjects) as the final state certification exam; the results of the test on compulsory subjects are included in the general education document (certificate, diploma) of the graduates of the general education institution; graduates of general education institutions who have expressed their desire to enter higher education institutions, according to their personal choice, voluntarily take tests in subjects suitable for bachelor's education; Starting from 2022, those who graduated from educational institutions in previous years will submit tests in subjects and compulsory subjects suitable for bachelor's education courses within the specified periods; tests are carried out by the State Test Center (State Test Center) under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan; the results of the tests (the number of correct answers in the subject) will be published on the official website of the State Test Center the day after the test; the results of the tests are taken into account in accordance with the evaluation criteria in the selection for admission to higher education institutions; graduates of general education institutions and those who have graduated from educational institutions in previous years (applicants) who have expressed a desire to enter higher educational institutions are given the right to participate in competitions in educational fields with the same language of instruction and the same set of subjects; the applicant is given an electronic certificate of the applicant valid for 3 years in the subject for which the test was submitted.
The applicant is given the right to retake the test in this subject in the prescribed manner before the validity period of the electronic certificate expires; the result of the test of the applicants in compulsory subjects is taken into account when enrolling in educational courses that require special talent and are carried out through professional (creative) exams without entrance tests; the set of subjects for which the test will be given, the number of test tasks, and the exact dates of the tests are determined by the State Commission for admission to educational institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan; Ministries and agencies that have general education institutions under their jurisdiction ensure that information about graduates of general education institutions, including graduates of general education institutions who have expressed a desire to enter higher education institutions, is included in the special program of the State Test Center by March 1 of each year; Applicants who have a national certificate issued by the State Test Center on the level of knowledge of the subject, as well as an international certificate on the subject of a foreign language, have the right to be given the maximum score in this subject without exams.
The Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Ministry of Public Education, the State Test Center, the State Inspectorate for the Quality of Education under the Cabinet of Ministers and the ministries and agencies with higher education institutions together: submit to the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan within two months proposals regarding the composition of subjects and evaluation criteria for entrance tests to higher educational institutions in the fields of education (undergraduate education); Every year, on the basis of the analysis of the results of the test and admission to higher educational institutions, educational institutions monitor the quality of educational programs and graduates.

President's The draft of the decision "On unification of the final state attestation of graduates and entrance exams to higher education institutions and measures to introduce a single exam" was posted for discussion.
