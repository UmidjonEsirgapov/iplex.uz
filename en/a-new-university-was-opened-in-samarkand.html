A new university was opened in Samarkand

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The Presidential Decree "On fundamental improvement of the personnel training system in the field of veterinary medicine and animal husbandry" (No. PQ-187, 31.03.2022)
was adopted According to the decision, on the basis of the Samarkand State Institute of Veterinary Medicine the University of Veterinary Medicine, Animal Husbandry and Biotechnology was established.
In the Institute of Irrigation and Agro-Technology of Karshi, from the 2022/2023 academic year, personnel will be trained in the field of "Livestock Processing Technology". (PQ-187, 31.03.2022) was adopted

According to the decision, Samarkand State University of Veterinary Medicine, Animal Husbandry and Biotechnology was established on the basis of the Samarkand Institute of Veterinary Medicine. educated personnel will be trained.

The Board of Directors of the University, approved by the decision, determines the duration of education for educational areas and specialties.

The University operates in the system of the State Committee for the Development of Veterinary Medicine and Animal Husbandry.

In order to ensure the harmony of education with practice, Technological Park of Veterinary Clinic and Disease Diagnostics was organized at the University.

When employees with 5-year experience in the fieldare recommended to study at the university based on a recommendation, the fee-contract amount is equal to the fee-contract amount (without stipend) in the field of full-time education.

On the basis of the Qamashi service technical school, the Qamashi veterinary medicine technical school was established.

From the 2022/2023 academic year, a separate quota will be allocated from the "El-yurt umidi" fund for veterinary and biotechnology courses. — Details here
