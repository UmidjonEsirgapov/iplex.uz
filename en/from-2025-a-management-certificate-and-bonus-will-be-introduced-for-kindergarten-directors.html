From 2025, a management certificate and bonus will be introduced for kindergarten directors

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
From 2025, kindergarten directors will also receive a management certificate, and from September, a 30 percent increase will be introduced. Cabinet of Ministers Decision No. 531 of August 27, 2024 on the procedure for issuing a management certificate to directors of preschool education organizations
According to this decision, starting from September 2024, a 50% bonus will be paid to school principals who have a management certificate, and 30% to deputy principals. will be paid.

From 2025, kindergarten directors will also receive a management certificate, and from September, a 30 percent increase will be introduced

Decision No. 531 of the Cabinet of Ministers dated August 27, 2024 on the procedure for issuing management certificates to directors of preschool education organizations was signed.

Previous news as we have already mentioned, by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers 681-50n of December 25, 2023, the procedure for training candidates for the position of director of public general secondary educational institutions in management training courses and issuing them with a management certificate was established. According to this decision, starting from September 2024, a 50 percent bonus will be paid to school directors who have a management certificate, and 30 percent to deputy directors.

Starting from January 1, 2025, the same procedure will be applied to the directors of preschool education organizations. In this regard, starting from September 1, 2025, a 30 percent bonus will be paid to kindergarten directors who have a management certificate. Also, the pool of candidates for the position of kindergarten director will be formed from among those who have a management certificate.
