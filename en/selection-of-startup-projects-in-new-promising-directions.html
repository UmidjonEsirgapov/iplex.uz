Selection of startup projects in new promising directions

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
Terms for accepting startup projects: Applications are accepted from November 1 to November 15; Participation of 1 participant with 1 project; The maximum period of creation of finished products, software products, services and technologies on the basis of startup projects should not exceed 2 years; Startup project initiator - to be one of the founders of the startup project (in the case of a company, to be one of the main shareholders).
Expected results of project implementation - targeted and effective use of allocated funds, quality and time of implementation of activities, organization of production of products that require import substitution and export-oriented scientific basis; The decision of the Ministry of Innovative Development of the Republic of Uzbekistan regarding the winners of the contest is final and will not be revised.
In this case, the original copies of the following documents should be sent to e-mail in PDF format: application signed by the author of the startup project; Startup project passport; Cost estimate (.xls/.xlsx); Startup project business text (with signature and seal, .pdf and .doc/.docx); Presentation of the startup project (10 slides) and complete coverage of the project content in the form of a tablet (information prepared in the form of a video of no more than 5 minutes is approved); Preliminary technical expertise on the fact that the presented projects strictly followed the competition requirements, the reliability and authenticity of the project materials, that copying (plagiarism) was not allowed, that they were formed based on the requirements, as well as that the important results obtained at the end of the startup project were fully based in accordance with the research design, and that the requested funds were correctly calculated in accordance with the amount of financing specified in the conditions of the competition. will be held.

General Information:

Procedure for approval, financing and implementation of startup projects approved by the Decision No. 133 of the Ministry of Innovative Development of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated March 9, 2020 on" in accordance with Article 7 of the Regulation announces a selection of startup projects in new promising areas at the level of the republic.

Conditions for accepting startup projects:

  • Applications are accepted from November 1 to November 15;
  • 1 participant with 1 project;
  • The maximum period of time for creating ready-made products, software products, services and technologies based on startup projects should not exceed 2 years;
  • The initiator of the startup project should be one of the founders of the startup project (in the case of a company, he should be one of the main shareholders).

Conditions for holding the competition of startup projects:

  • In the competition, all areas of a wide range of production-oriented applications aimed at creating and implementing promising start-up projects are accepted. They must be based on innovations and technologies.
  • Applications can be submitted by legal entities and individuals.
  • The priority areas for project implementation are defined as:
  • Copper industry;
  • Viticulture;
  • Silk and wool industry;
  • Biotechnology and medicine;
  • Renewable energy;
  • Agriculture;
  • Information technologies.

The following are taken into account when evaluating the project:

  • Innovativeness of the project - new technologies and (or) process organization that have not been used before in the republic. the degree of use of the principles, in which the project envisages the production of a new product (in the presence of an intellectual property object), the creation of a new type of service, which has not been produced before in the republic; is approved);
  • Provision of specialists, scientific-technical
    and production possibilities of the project - formation of a team of qualified specialists, availability of necessary equipment and infrastructure of scientific objects;
  • Investment attractiveness of the project is a set of favorable investment
    and innovative conditions that bring additional profit and reduce the risk of invested funds. The investment attractiveness of the project is determined by its compliance with the long-term prospects of the country's development, the value of the project, the consideration of risks, self-recovery and profitability; market size, target audience, main channels of communication/sales, competitive environment, etc.
  • Expected results of the project implementation - targeted and effective use of allocated funds, quality and deadlines of implementation of activities, organization of production of products that require import substitution
    and export-oriented scientific basis;
  • The decision of the Ministry of Innovative Development of the Republic of Uzbekistan regarding the competition winners is firm and

Projects submitted to the competition are accepted by e-mail startup@mininnovation.uz .

The original copies of the following documents should be sent to the e-mail in PDF format:

  • Application signed by the author of the startup project;
  • Startup project passport;
  • Expenses estimate (.xls/.xlsx);
  • Business text of the startup project (with signature and seal, .pdf
    and .doc/.docx);
  • Presentation of the startup project (10 slides) and full coverage of the project content in the form of a tablet (information prepared in the form of a video not exceeding 5 minutes is approved);

Presented projects in accordance with the competition requirements The preliminary technical examination is carried out on the fact that the project materials are strictly followed, the reliability and truthfulness of the project materials, that copying (plagiarism) is not allowed, that they are formed based on the requirements, as well as that the important results obtained at the end of the project are fully based in accordance with the research design, and that the requested funds are correctly calculated in accordance with the amount of funding specified in the tender conditions.

  • Projects that have not passed the initial technical examination are not recommended for the next stage of the competition!

Projects submitted in the following cases may not be recommended for participation in the competition:

  • The startup project does not have an innovative nature in content;
  • The goals and objectives of the startup project are not clarified;
  • The startup project has a solution to the problem presented.
  • The start-up projects submitted to the competition were not winners in previous competitions;
  • If the monthly salary is more than one-fifth of the total value of the project;
  • If the funds allocated for the implementation of the start-up projects are spent on the reconstruction and repair-construction of buildings, as well as the purchase of motor vehicles.

In the presented start-up projects The heads of the start-up project, the heads of the organization implementing this project, and the responsible employees of the relevant high-ranking organizations are responsible for the accuracy and reliability of the information and the results of the startup projects by the competition winners, the full and timely implementation of the expected results set in the tasks of the competition programs.
