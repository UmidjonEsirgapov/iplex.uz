A student from Fergana became an ambassador of the Microsoft Learn Student program

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
Dilafruz Zhoraboyeva, a 11th-grade student of the 10th school of Fergana region, became the ambassador of the Microsoft Learn Student program for young people.
IT Ladies Uzbekistan informs about this.
Now Dilafruz Acts as the face of the Microsoft company in Uzbekistan.

Dilafruz Joraboyeva, a student of the 11th grade of the 10th school of the Fergana region, became the ambassador of the Microsoft Learn Student program for young people. This is reported by IT Ladies Uzbekistan.

Now Dilafruz works as the face of Microsoft in Uzbekistan. This program is a global project that selects educated, active and aspiring young people in the field of technology.

Dilafruz's entry into the program was not easy. He was nominated due to his good technical knowledge and was required to fulfill the conditions and tasks given by the company for 1 year. One of them was lessons and assignments, which included 30 modules on artificial intelligence.

Dilafruz is currently studying and interning for Yandex, and if he passes the tests, he will have the opportunity to join big projects. She is also a participant in Microsoft Developers Community Uzbekistan, Sado24, and .NET Documentation Uzbekistan projects.

Dilafruz was among 46 women and girls in the field of IT and was included in the top 12 teams of female sports programmers in Uzbekistan. — Source
