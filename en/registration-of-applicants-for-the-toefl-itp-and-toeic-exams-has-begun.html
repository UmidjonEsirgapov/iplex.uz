Registration of applicants for the TOEFL ITP and TOEIC exams has begun

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Online registration of applicants for TOEFL ITP and TOEIC exams has begun by the Scientific-Educational Application Center under the Agency for the Assessment of Knowledge and Skills. It will last until April 18.
Exams will be held electronically on a computer on April 20 in the building of the Knowledge Assessment Agency (former State Test Center) in Tashkent.

Online registration of applicants for the TOEFL ITP and TOEIC exams has begun by the Scientific and Educational Practical Center under the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency. Exams will be held electronically on April 20 in the building of the Knowledge Assessment Agency (former State Test Center) in Tashkent.
