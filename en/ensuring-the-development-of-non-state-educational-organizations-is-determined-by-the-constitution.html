Ensuring the development of non-state educational organizations is determined by the Constitution

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The draft of the Constitutional Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the Constitution of the Republic of Uzbekistan" was announced. ensures the development of non-governmental educational organizations", it is said.
The determination of the issue of ensuring the development of non-governmental educational organizations at the level of the Constitution eliminates the assumptions that it may be closed or disappear later by a decision.

The draft of the Constitutional Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the Constitution of the Republic of Uzbekistan" was announced.

Notably, Article 50 of the Draft Constitutional Law states, "The state ensures the development of the continuing education system, its various types and forms, and state and non-state educational organizations."

In particular, Article 16 of the Draft Constitutional Law stipulates that "No law or other regulatory legal document may contradict the principles and norms of the Constitution". — Source
