Is it legal to recruit teachers?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Is it possible to put teachers on duty?
On the job duties of teachers of general secondary education institutions, approved in accordance with the order of the Minister of Preschool and School Education No. 565 dated December 20, 2023 duty does not exist.
According to Article 115 of the Labor Code, the employer does not have the right to demand the employee to perform work that is not specified in the employment contract.

Can teachers be put on duty?

Decision of the Minister of Preschool and School Education dated December 20, 2023 According to order No. 565, there is no duty of a teacher according to the job duties of employees of general secondary educational institutions. Therefore, employer's forcing teachers to be on duty does not have a legal basis.

According to Article 115 of the Labor Code, the employer has no right to demand from the employee to perform work that is not specified in the employment contract. Also, the employer cannot require the employee to perform actions that are against the law or violate the rights of the employee.

In such cases, according to Article 49 of the Code of Administrative Responsibility, an official may be fined for violating labor laws. For this reason, it is illegal to attract teachers to school duty. — Read on infoedu.uz
