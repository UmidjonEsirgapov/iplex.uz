As you know, in order to support talented young people and increase the level of English literacy of young people, the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform team is holding a 3-level English Olympiad in cooperation with the Ministry of Innovative Development, the Republican Council of Young Scientists and the "REGISTON" educational center.

The 1st stage of the Olympiad was held on the educational platform OLIYGOH.UZ on July 11, and a total of more than 9 thousand young people from our Republic participated, and the best candidates won a ticket to the next stage. All the candidates who participated can see their results through "Personal cabinet" or the Olympiad window and download special certificates of participation in the Olympiad.

The 2nd stage of the Olympiad will be held on OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform July 18 from thousands of talented young people from our republic. 1000 best candidates received the right to participate.

Who will participate in the final stage of the Olympics?

According to the instructions and regulations of the Olympics, 169 (one hundred and sixty-nine participants) who showed the best results in the 3rd stage (final stage) won a ticket. List of candidates.

How will the final stage take place?

  • Participants of the final stage of the Olympiad will be specially tested in the auditorium of the "REGISTON" training center, where special exams will be held.
  • Time: July 25, 08:00
  • In order to ensure objectivity in the final stage, the exam will be recorded by video cameras and broadcast online on the YouTube platform.
  • Appeals from candidates will be accepted until July 26 at 4:00 p.m.

Address: "REGISTON" training center (Tashkent city, Chilonzor district, Shukhrat intersection, Registan Academy building: Google map)

At this stage, the absolute winners of the Olympiad will be determined and the winners will be solemnly awarded on July 27.

For information, the winners of the Olympiad will receive special awards and souvenirs. prizes, diplomas with QR codes of the I, II and III levels will be awarded:

1st place winner: Laptop computer and souvenir books worth 200 thousand soums

2nd place winner: Samsung A21 mobile phone and souvenir books worth 100 thousand soums

3rd place to the owner: Xiaomi 9 A mobile phone and souvenir books worth 100,000 soums

OLIYGOH.UZ team and "REGISTON" educational center wish the participants of the final stage of the Olympiad good luck!
