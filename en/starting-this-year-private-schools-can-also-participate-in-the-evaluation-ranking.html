Starting this year, private schools can also participate in the evaluation ranking

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
As you know, based on the relevant decision of the Government, the ranking of general education institutions in districts (cities) and the Republic of Karakalpakstan, regions and Tashkent city is being compiled. The knowledge of 9th grade students will be evaluated, and a social survey will be conducted among teachers based on the methodology of the international TALIS evaluation program.
This year, in order to bring innovation to these processes, it is planned to evaluate the activities of non-governmental educational organizations operating in our country. The ranking of general education institutions in the city is being compiled.

In this process, the knowledge of the 9th grade students in 10 percent schools, selected by drawing lots, will be evaluated, and a social survey will be conducted among teachers based on the methodology of the international TALIS evaluation program. In this regard, we ask non-governmental educational organizations that wish to participate in the national rating to register via the link https://forms.gle/tAhZ5EbfbiZBLURS7.
