ClimateScience Olympiad

Check out the ClimateScience Olympiad! Run by the London-based charity Climate Science, the Olympics is a global competition for young people aged 12 to 25 to empower you in your work on climate change. It allows you to develop critical thinking, teamwork, problem solving and leadership skills.

Benefits:

By participating, you can win $15,000 in prize money with your teammates and the opportunity to speak at the United Nations Climate Summit COP27.

Participants age:

Anyone between the ages of 12 and 25 can participate individually or as a team. Qualifiers last only 25 minutes online, so you can join from anywhere in the world!

We encourage you to visit the website and participate in CS Olympic Qualifiers today. We hope that many of you will try to take advantage of this opportunity.
