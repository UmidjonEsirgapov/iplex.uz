Can winter vacation be extended?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
On January 9-11, precipitation (rain, snow) will fall in most of the republic.
On January 10-15, there will be anomalous cold weather across the republic.
Think about extending the vacation period for educational institutions. It will be necessary to see."

Former deputy Rasul Kusherbayev writes on his Telegram channel:

"Anomalous cold is coming. On January 9-11, precipitation (rain, snow) will fall in a large part of the republic. On January 10-15, there will be anomalous cold weather in the republic. The temperature drops to 12-17 degrees at night, 5-8 degrees during the day, 17-22 degrees at night in the north, 25-27 degrees in Ustyurt and desert regions, 7-10 degrees during the day, 5-10 degrees at night in the south and Fergana Valley, and 2-5 degrees during the day. Therefore, it is necessary to take measures to ensure continuous heating of houses and workplaces.

It is also necessary to consider the issue of extending the vacation period for educational institutions."

According to reports on social networks, it is said that many higher education institutions have extended the winter vacation period. Official information was given on the telegram channel of the press secretary of the Special Minister of Higher and Secondary Education that this statement is not true, but This post has now been deleted. — Read on infoedu.uz
