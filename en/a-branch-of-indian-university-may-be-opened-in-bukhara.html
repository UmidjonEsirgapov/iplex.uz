A branch of Indian University may be opened in Bukhara

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The Embassy of Uzbekistan in India organized an online dialogue between the administration of Bukhara region and Jaipur Manipal University in the state of Rajasthan, India. noted their readiness to establish close cooperation within the framework of development and mutual student exchange programs.
At the same time, the Indian side expressed its desire to visit Bukhara with a group of Manipal University management, teachers, students and businessmen from the state of Rajasthan in order to continue the negotiations. An online dialogue was organized between Jaipur Manipal University in the state of Rajasthan.

In it, the head of the international cooperation department of Jaipur Manipal University, Ajay Sharma, noted the readiness of the university to open a department in Bukhara region, to establish close cooperation within the framework of teacher training and mutual student exchange programs.
