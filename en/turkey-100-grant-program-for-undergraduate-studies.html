Acıbadem University is a private, non-profit organization established in 2007 and located in Istanbul, Turkey.

The grant program offered by the university is intended to support international students who intend to study at the bachelor's level.

Who is the grant program for?

  • Citizens of all ages can participate in the program.

Document submission process:

  • Candidates for the grant must first choose one of the medical fields suitable for them HERE and submit an online application for it HERE required;
  • Admitted students will be automatically considered for the grant.

Required documents:

  • School or college/high school diploma;
  • Curriculum vitae;
  • Two letters of recommendation;
  • One photograph;
  • Passport copy;

Note: Please refer to the application form of your chosen course for other documents that may be required in addition to the above documents!

Admission requirement:

Candidates are required to score at least 149 on the GRE or 450 on the GMAT to be admitted to the university.

Language requirement:

one of the following language certificates is required:

  • YDS – 85
  • TOEFL IBT – 80 (Test) / 20 TWE
  • CAE – C
  • PTE – 67

Benefits of the grant:

Candidates awarded the grant are 100% exempted from the tuition fee. Fees for training courses can range from 3,500 to 27,000 USD.
