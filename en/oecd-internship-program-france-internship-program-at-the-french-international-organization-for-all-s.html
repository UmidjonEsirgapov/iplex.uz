General information:

OECD(Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development) international organization is accepting applications for its OECD Internship Program. This program is designed for high-potential, in-depth students studying at the bachelor's (BA) and master's (MA) and doctoral (PhD) levels. The purpose of the program is to help successful candidates improve their analytical and technical skills.

Students from all countries can apply for the program. The internship program can be 1-6 months, depending on the student's wishes and chosen direction. The internship program will take place in Paris, France.

Students admitted to the program can work 40 hours a week (Monday to Friday) and do an internship for 1-6 months in one of the following areas:

  • Audit
  • Communication, mass media and public relations
  • Translation

Benefits of the program:

  • Monthly stipend in the amount of 700 euros.
  • Opportunity to practice in France for 1-6 months.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Candidate (BA) must be studying at the bachelor's degree, master's (MA) or doctoral (PhD) level.
  • The candidate must be fluent in English or French (knowledge of other languages used in the OECD organization is preferred).
  • The candidate must have good accounting and information technology (IT) skills.
  • In addition, the candidate must be able to work in a multinational international environment and communicate and document. must have preparation skills.

You can read more about the requirements for candidates.

Application for the program:

  1. An online application is submitted and the necessary information is filled in.
  2. Required documents:
  • Passport copy
  • Cover letter
  • Reference from the educational institution you are studying
  • Medical insurance
  • Visa
  • French bank account number (after official admission to OECD)

Program admission process:

Admission to the program is announced 2 times during the year. For 2021, you are the 2nd season. You can register for (End date 01.09.2021) (the 1st admission was until 01.03.2021). Due to the large number of applications for the program, only those who have been accepted will be notified that they have passed to the next stage. Unaccepted applicants may reapply for the March 1, 2022 and September 1, 2022 seasons. — More information
