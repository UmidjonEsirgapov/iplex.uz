How many years should extramural education be?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
This regulation is still in force.
According to the seventh paragraph of the regulation, the period of study in part-time (special part-time) education: part-time — at least 5 years; in a special extramural form - at least 3 years; part-time on the basis of the second and subsequent higher education - at least 3 years.

This regulation is still in force. Duration of study in (special extramural) education:
