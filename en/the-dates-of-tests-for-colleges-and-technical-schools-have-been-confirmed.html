Entry tests for professional educational institutions (college and technical school) for the 2023-2024 academic year will be held in 2 shifts on September 26-29, 2023. In this case, tests are held:

  • in the 1st shift from 08:00 to 11:00;
  • in the 2nd shift from 15:00 to 18:00.

The test is conducted in 5 subjects. 3 hours will be allotted for the test.
