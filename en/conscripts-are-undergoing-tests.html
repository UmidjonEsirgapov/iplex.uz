Composition and distribution of test questions

The test questions intended for long-term military servicemen consist of a total of 100 questions, which are divided into the following directions:

Combat training questions

40 of the test questions are about combat training. was created and aimed at determining the practical and theoretical knowledge of military personnel.

Spiritual-educational training questions

20 questions were allocated to assess the level of spiritual-educational training. These questions serve to determine the military servicemen's patriotism, military discipline, and moral views.

Questions on exact sciences

10 questions from mathematics and physics are included in the tests and are aimed at assessing the analytical and technical thinking ability of military servicemen.

Logical test questions

The remaining 20 questions are designed to determine the level of logical thinking. These questions will help assess the quick decision-making skills of military personnel.
