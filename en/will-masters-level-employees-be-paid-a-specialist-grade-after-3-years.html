Will Master's level employees be paid a specialist grade after 3 years?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Will an employee with a master's degree be paid in the specialist category after 3 years?
According to Cabinet of Ministers decision No. 572 of September 17, 2021, new recruitment at the master's level who has not previously passed attestation It is noted that the second qualification category (senior teacher position) will be given to the pedagogic personnel who have graduated from higher education institutions or to the pedagogic personnel who have entered work for the first time after graduating from higher education institutions.

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 572 dated September 17, 2021, the second qualification category (senior teacher position) will be given to newly hired pedagogic staff who have not previously passed attestation or to pedagogic staff who have graduated from higher education institutions and entered work for the first time before being recruited for attestation.

According to this rule, after completing the master's degree and entering the job, the above category will be paid until the attestation.

In the second paragraph of Clause 4 of the Regulation, newly hired pedagogues who have not previously passed attestation or pedagogues who have been employed for the first time after graduating from higher education organizations will be paid after three years of professional activity. then the next mandatory attestation will be carried out. This provision is also contained in Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 392, effective in 2020.
