The procedure for admission of candidates to higher military educational institutions is approved

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The draft of the President's decision "On the approval of the regulation on the selection of candidates for higher military educational institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan, admission, expulsion and reinstatement of cadets" was published.
Selecting candidates for higher military educational institutions, according to the regulation on the procedure for admission, expulsion and reinstatement of cadets, the following selection measures are carried out step by step in order to qualitatively form the contingent of cadets of higher military educational institutions: military-professional direction; initial selection; tests.
Candidates who did not successfully pass one stage will not be allowed to the next stage.

Draft of the President's decision "On approval of the regulation on the selection, admission, expulsion and reinstatement of cadets to the higher military educational institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan" announcement .

According to the regulation on the selection of candidates for higher military educational institutions, admission, expulsion and reinstatement of cadets, the following selection measures are carried out step by step in order to qualitatively complete the contingent of cadets of higher military educational institutions:

  • military-professional direction;
  • initially selection;
  • tests.

Candidates who did not successfully pass one stage will not be allowed to the next stage.

Admission to higher military educational institutions is carried out on the basis of the uniform rules of selection, equal rights and admission.

Candidates for admission to higher military educational institutions are approved by the presidential decree on the procedure for military service of citizens of Uzbekistan. It is necessary to meet the requirements specified in the regulation.

Candidates for admission to the Military Institute of Information and Communication Technologies and Communication of the Ministry of Defense must comply with the requirements of the President's decision "On measures to further improve the system of officer training in the field of information and communication technologies and communication" and candidates for admission to the Military Medical Academy of the Armed Forces must meet the requirements of the President's "Establishment of a qualitatively new system of personnel training in the field of military medicine"

Candidates must be informed in writing about their financial responsibility in the event that their parents, before entering higher military educational institutions, are released to the reserve due to failure to fulfill the requirements of the initial contract after graduation, are terminated before the contract expires, or are deprived of liberty for a crime committed by the leadership of higher military educational institutions.

In order to organize high-quality admissions to higher military educational institutions, the higher military educational institutions admission commission is established in accordance with the procedure established by the interested ministries and agencies that have higher military educational institutions.

The admission commission is headed by the head of the higher military educational institutions. The Admission Commission will include experts from relevant structural structures of ministries and agencies.

The Admission Commission will operate from March 10 to September 1 of the admission year. — Read on infoedu.uz
