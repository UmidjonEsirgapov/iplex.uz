Information about admission processes and majors of Public Security University

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
The University prepares highly educated personnel for the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the National Guard - in the areas of public security, road safety and the implementation of passport system requirements, as well as for the Armed Forces and other law enforcement agencies.
Professionals are trained at the University. a two-stage system of higher education, including bachelor's degree and master's degree, will be introduced into the personnel training process.
Medical examination of the candidate is carried out by the medical commission of the district defense department.

"On measures to further improve the system of training professional personnel in the field of public safety" (No. PQ-5077, 15.04.2021) was adopted in order to prevent any threats to the peace and tranquility of our country and to improve the training system of highly qualified specialists in the field of combating crime based on advanced international standards, as well as to increase the personnel capacity of law enforcement agencies and the Armed Forces.

By decision of the National Guard Military-Technical Institute was reorganized as Public Security University of the Republic of Uzbekistan and designated as a base higher military education and scientific-research institution that carries out targeted training of qualified personnel.

In the university, for the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the National Guard, it is necessary to maintain public order in the field of public security, road safety and the implementation of passport system requirements. in the directions, highly educated personnel are prepared for the Armed Forces and other law enforcement agencies.

In particular,

  • military diplomacy;
  • jurisprudence and economics;
  • engineering-technical;
  • educational-psychological;
  • tactical command;

and for the state and economic management bodies that have their own security structures -

  • organization and implementation of security activities

are trained in the directions.

Those who want to apply to the university can apply to the departments of territorial defense. After that, the study of the application is conducted, interviews are organized and preliminary selection events are held with the candidates.

Medical examination of the candidate is carried out by the medical commission of the district defense department. Candidates found fit during the medical examination will undergo individual psychological and general physical tests at the next stage.

After that, final medical examinations will be organized. Candidates who have passed this examination will be put to the test. Those who successfully pass the tests will be admitted to the university.

From the 2021/2022 academic year, the university will introduce a bachelor's and master's degree system.

The admission quota is up to 300 undergraduate students and up to 100 students for part-time education. — Source
