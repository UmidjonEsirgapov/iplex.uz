35% of the student contract fee can be paid by the Youth Union

According to the regulation "On the procedure for financial support of students who are active members of the Youth Union of Uzbekistan", 35% of the contract of students studying in higher education institutions on a fee-contract basis is paid by the Union can be paid by.

Who will receive financial assistance?

✅ Students studying full-time at the bachelor and master stages

✅ Students admitted to bachelor's degree based on the decision of the State Commission on an additional payment-contract from the second year starting

Students can use this opportunity if they actively participate in the activities of the primary organization of the Youth Union in higher education institutions.

Conditions for financial aid

  • Students must pay 65% of the contract within the specified period.
  • After that, The Union will cover the remaining 35 percent.

Cases where financial support is not provided

❌ Students studying in the form of second specialization, part-time and evening education

❌ After completing military service, admitted to study with a recommendation and already supported by the Union students
