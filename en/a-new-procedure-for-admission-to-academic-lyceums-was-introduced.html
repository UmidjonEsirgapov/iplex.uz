A new regulation on the procedure for admission, transfer, reinstatement and expulsion to academic lyceums was introduced in the Republic of Uzbekistan. This procedure was approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 320 "On measures to improve the procedures for the admission of students to academic lyceums, their transfer, restoration, and suspension of studies."

  • Registration:

    • Applicants register through the Unified interactive state services portal and the official website of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.
    • Registration is carried out through the my.edu.uz site and free registration is available from June 10 to July 10 every year.

    1. Choosing a major:

    • At the time of registration, applicants have the opportunity to choose one suitable major from up to three academic lyceums with the same language of instruction.

    1. Tests:

    • Applicants take part in entrance tests organized in the region of their choice (the Republic of Karakalpakstan, the regional center or in Tashkent).

    Admission parameters and tests

    The parameters for admission and tests are approved by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation every year:

    • Admission Parameters: Every year by June 1st, in agreement with the Ministry of Economy and Finance, parameters for admission to academic lyceums are approved.
    • Tests: Every year by November 1st for the next academic year, a set of subjects, the number of test items, the number of test tasks, evaluation criteria and a test to determine the level of logical thinking (IQ) should be prepared for the next academic year. the list of literature is approved.

    Transfer, reinstatement and suspension of studies

    The new procedure is not limited to admission, but also covers the processes of transfer, reinstatement and suspension of studies. Clear rules and requirements are also set for these processes, creating transparent and fair conditions for each student. — Read on infoedu.uz
