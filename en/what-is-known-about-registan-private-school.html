Evaluation Center

In schools, pedagogues have different approaches to evaluating students, some teachers rate children higher and some lower them. Registon private school has an assessment center, where general exams in basic subjects are held every quarter. During one quarter, exam questions are prepared by pedagogues who are not school employees, and exams are taken without the participation of school employees. The result will be publicly announced, the students with good results will be encouraged, and the students with low scores will be given additional retraining lessons for a month. service
