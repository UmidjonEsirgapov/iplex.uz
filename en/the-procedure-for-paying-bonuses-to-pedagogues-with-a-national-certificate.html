The procedure for paying bonuses to pedagogues with a national certificate

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
According to the decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 134 dated May 11, 2022, the procedure for paying bonuses to pedagogues who have a national certificate in general education subjects is established.
A national certificate that meets the requirements of international standards 50% bonus will be paid to pedagogues.
Until January 1, 2025, the bonus for these certificates will be 50%, and from January 1, 2025, 20% will be paid. according to the decree, the procedure for paying bonuses to pedagogues with a national certificate in general education subjects is established. With these certificates, pedagogues are paid a bonus of 20 percent if they get at least a B+ grade from Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency.

Subjects with bonus:

  • Mother language and literature
  • History
  • Geography
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Mathematics
  • Physics

From September 1, 2022, bonuses will be paid in the following order:

  • Teachers with an internationally recognized certificate will be paid a bonus of 50 percent.
  • With a national certificate that meets the requirements of international standards pedagogues are also paid a 50 percent bonus. Authorized international organizations must confirm that the national certificate meets international standards.

Until January 1, 2025, the premium for these certificates will be 50 percent, and starting from January 1, 2025, 20 percent will be paid. — Read on infoedu.uz

  • Pedagogues with several certificates will be paid a bonus according to the certificate that gives them the right to receive a higher bonus.
