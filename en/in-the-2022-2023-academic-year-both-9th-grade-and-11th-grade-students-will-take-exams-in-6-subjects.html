In the 2022-2023 academic year, both 9th grade and 11th grade students will take exams in 6 subjects.

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Order No. 46 of the Minister of Preschool and School Education dated March 6, 2023 "On the organization and conduct of the final state certification of students in general secondary education institutions in the 2022-2023 school year" according to the following subjects, final control exams will be held: for 9th grade: Mother tongue and literature (written, essay); Mathematics (written); Uzbek language - for general secondary educational institutions where education is not conducted in Uzbek / Russian language - for general secondary educational institutions where education is not conducted in Russian (oral); History (oral); Physical education (practical); According to the student's choice* (oral).
For grades 11: Mother tongue and literature (written-essay); Mathematics (written); Uzbek language (for general secondary educational institutions where education is not conducted in Uzbek language) / Educational institution selection** (written and oral) Foreign language (written and oral); History of Uzbekistan (oral); According to the student's choice* (oral).
