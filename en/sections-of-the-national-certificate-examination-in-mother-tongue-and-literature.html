Exams are organized on the basis of state educational standards and curricula. Below we look at the main sections of the exam and what they contain.

Exam Sections:

  1. Phonetics:

    • The phonetics section examines sounds and their properties. Students should know the types of sounds, sound changes and their importance in pronunciation.

  2. Graphics:

    • This section covers the writing system, letters and their graphic representation. Students need to have knowledge of graphic symbols and their use.

  3. Orthography:

    • Orthography section deals with the rules of correct writing. In the exam, attention is paid to the correct spelling of words and spelling rules.

  4. Orthopeia:

    • Orthopeia section introduces the rules of correct pronunciation. Students should know how to pronounce words and pronounce sounds correctly.

  5. Lexicology:

    • The department of lexicology deals with the study of words and their meanings. The exam will have questions on vocabulary and word meanings.

  6. Phraseology:

    • Phraseology section covers phraseology and their usage. Students should know the meanings of these compounds and their use in context.

  7. Grammar:

    • Grammar section includes knowledge of word groups, sentence structure and syntax rules. In the exam, there will be questions related to the correct structure of sentences and words. Students should know different text styles and their proper use.

  8. Literary History:

    • Literary History section provides information about the development history of literature and important literary works. The exam will have questions about famous writers and their works.

  9. Literary Theory:

    • This section covers the main theoretical concepts and terms of literature. Students should know literary genres and their characteristics.
