A new university will be established in Tashkent region

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Approval of the proposal of the State Inspectorate for Quality Control of Education under the Cabinet of Ministers, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, the Tashkent Regional Government and "International School of Finance Technology and Science" Limited Liability Company to establish the International School of Finance and Technology (ISFT) University
Taking into account the needs of the republic, the university prepares highly qualified personnel in international accounting and auditing, business management, financial analysis, financial risk management and other fields.

The proposal of the State Inspectorate for Quality Control of Education under the Cabinet of Ministers, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, the Tashkent Regional Government and the International School of Finance Technology and Science Limited Liability Company to establish the International School of Finance and Technology (ISFT) University can be approved.

Graduates will receive a diploma of the state model of higher education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, and graduates of the ASSA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants) international program, organized with foreign partners, will receive an Oxford Brookes University diploma.

In accordance with the development plans of the university, branches of the university may be established in other regions of the republic by the founder.

The following are the main tasks and areas of activity of the university.

  • training of highly qualified personnel in international accounting and auditing, business management, financial analysis, financial risk management and other fields, taking into account the needs of the real sectors of the republic's economy;
  • implementing extensive cooperation with leading foreign higher education and research institutions, involving highly qualified local and foreign specialists, including compatriots working abroad;
  • Uzbekistan's state enterprises and other implementation of experience exchange programs based on improving the qualifications of employees of enterprises, cooperation with advanced scientific and research institutions abroad;
  • introducing modern pedagogical and information and communication technologies into the educational process;
  • implementing scientific and research activities.

The university is organized at the address of 2 Oliygoh Street, Oliygoh Street, Citizens' Assembly, Kibray District, Tashkent Region. — Source
