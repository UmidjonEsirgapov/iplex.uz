New Uzbekistan University hosted a ceremony to award the winners of the "Yandex Uzbekistan Coding and Math Contest 2025" passed. This prestigious competition was held between students of higher education institutions and talented IT specialists in Uzbekistan.

Participants demonstrated their knowledge and potential in the following areas:

  • Programming and algorithms

  • Statistics and probabilities. theory

  • Mathematical analysis

  • Linear algebra

The competition was organized as a two-stage online test. Test tasks at each stage are in English and are developed by international experts. Participation was absolutely free for everyone.

Winners determined based on points were awarded valuable gifts. They were presented with:

  • Cash prizes

  • Modern gadgets

  • Yandex Plus promo codes

. The total prize fund was 65 million soums.

The awarding ceremony not only marked the end of the competition, but also became an experience sharing space for participants, organizers and partners.
