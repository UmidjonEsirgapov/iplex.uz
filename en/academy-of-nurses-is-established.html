Academy of nurses is established

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Decision of the President (No. PQ-197, 20.06.2023)
was adopted.
According to the decision, the following will be established from the 2023/2024 academic year: Locksmith Abu Ali Academy of Nursing on the basis of Public Health College named after ibn Sino; Abu Ali Ibn Sina Public Health Colleges based on 27 medical colleges; Also, the following will be introduced: training of specialists in the field of "highly qualified nurse" bachelor's education with a duration of 4 years of study at the Medical Higher Education Institution; admission parameters based on the offers of technical schools and, additionally, the admission procedure on the basis of a differentiated payment-contract; the national system of 2-stage (fundamental and clinical-practical skills) evaluation of the knowledge of medical students of the state HEIs and their qualification confirmation.
  • Nursing Academy on the basis of Public Health College named after Chilanzor Abu Ali ibn Sina;
  • Public Health Colleges named after Abu Ali ibn Sina on the basis of 27 medical colleges;
