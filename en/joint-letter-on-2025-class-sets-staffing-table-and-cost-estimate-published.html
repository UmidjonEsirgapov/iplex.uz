Ministry of Preschool and School Education and Ministry of Economy and Finance A joint letter was published on the formation of class sets, tariff lists, staff tables and cost estimates for 2025. Regulation of this process is carried out on the basis of Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 722 of October 31, 2024.

Main steps in the process

Formation of class sets

  • General education schools and newly established classes for the 2024-2025 academic year based on the standards
  • Students in poorly equipped classes will be reassigned, and measures will be taken to redistribute students in classes with reduced contingents. Date: December 13.

List of pedagogues and leading staff

  • Lists of teaching staff, leading staff, candidates of science and doctors with national and international certificates are created in a single electronic system (erp.maktab.uz). Deadline: December 16.

Formation of tariff lists

  • Tariff lists of general education schools will be correctly formed and approved. Deadline: December 18.

Table of States

  • The table of states is formed based on the norms established by the legislation, approved by the economic and financial authorities and sent to the ministry. Deadline: December 20.

Expenditure estimate

  • Expenditure estimate for 2025 will be formulated and sent to the ministry. Deadline: December 23.

Optimization of budget funds

  • Special attention is paid to the saving of budget funds and the optimization of inefficient expenses during the approval of class composition and staff schedules.

Conclusion
