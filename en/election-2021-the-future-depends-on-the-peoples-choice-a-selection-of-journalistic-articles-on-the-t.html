Youth Affairs Agency and the Youth Union of Uzbekistan "Election - 2021: The future depends on the people's choice!" a contest of journalistic articles is being held on the topic.

Young people between the ages of 18 and 30 can participate in the contest. Articles will be accepted in electronic form from September 28 to October 10 via the following telegram bot address: @maqolalar_tanlovibot.

Prizes: According to the results of the competition, the 3 participants with the highest scores will be declared winners.

  • A diploma and a modern notebook for the 1st place;
  • Diploma and a modern smartphone for 2nd place;
  • A diploma and a set of books will be presented for 3rd place.

The purpose and tasks of the contest:

The purpose of the contest is to increase the socio-political and legal knowledge of young people and to help realize their creative potential.

The tasks of the contest are to encourage the active participation of young people in political processes in our country. to increase, to expand the ranks of young people who think independently, who have a high spirituality, a firm outlook on life, a broad outlook and deep knowledge, who are able to spend all their strength, knowledge and potential in the interests of the people, and who are able to take responsibility for the future of the country.

Note:

Participants under the age of 30 can participate;

- Do not make mistakes when registering;

- When registering, write full and correct answers to all questions using only the Latin alphabet;

- The articles should reflect the participation of young people in political processes today and the role of elections in the lives of young people.

The winners of the competition are determined by the group of experts in a 20-point system, based on the following criteria:

  • The presence of spelling and grammatical errors in the article - 5 points;
  • The methodological accuracy of the article - 5 points;
  • Complete disclosure of the topic and idea in the article - 5 points;
  • The article is marked effectiveness and originality - 5 points.

The idea of the submitted articles should not conflict with legal and regulatory documents.
