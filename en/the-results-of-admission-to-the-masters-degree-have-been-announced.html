For the 2023-2024 academic year, registered online through the electronic system for admission to master's specialties of state higher education institutions The applications of 20,054 citizens were fully reviewed. This was reported by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

According to it, 9 thousand 74 of the registered applicants were rejected due to the fact that their documents did not meet the specified requirements. 10,980 applicants who were at the required level took part in the competition. According to the latest results, 6,393 of them were recommended to study. Among them:

• state grant - 3,238;

• payment-contract - 3,155.

Applicants can find out the results through the official electronic system from their personal office.

For information, 2,160 of the applicants recommended to study on the basis of payment-contract are women. Based on the President's decision, the fee-contract amount of the women admitted to the master's level of higher education institutions on the basis of the basic fee-contract will be covered from the state budget funds. — Source
