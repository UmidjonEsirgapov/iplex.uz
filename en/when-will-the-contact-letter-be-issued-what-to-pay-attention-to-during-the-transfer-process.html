When will the contact letter be issued? What to pay attention to in the transfer process

The next step for applicants who have scored enough points in the transfer exams is getting a contact letter. In this process, applicants are recommended to follow the telegram channel of the university to which their studies are recommended to transfer.

The reason is that the universities announce when they will issue the contact letter on their official pages. From this date, applicants will have to go to the university to receive the contact letter.

After receiving the contact letter, the applicant must submit the required documents completely and on time to the university to which he transferred his studies. Otherwise, his "perevodi" may be rejected.

This process determines the student's next step in higher education, so it is important to follow all the processes carefully. — Read on infoedu.uz
