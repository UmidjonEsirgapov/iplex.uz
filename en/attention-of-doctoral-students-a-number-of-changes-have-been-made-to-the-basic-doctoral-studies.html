According to the decision No. 362 adopted by the Government on June 12, a number of innovations were introduced to the procedure for expulsion from basic doctoral studies and doctoral studies. These changes are aimed at increasing the discipline and quality of doctoral students' scientific activities.

Cases of expulsion based on documents

On his own application

If the doctoral student does not want to continue his studies, he can leave the doctoral program based on his written application.

Due to his health

With his health confirmed by the medical commission if there are related problems, the doctoral student will be expelled from studies.

Failure to fulfill individual plans

If the doctoral student does not fulfill the scientific plan assigned to him, this situation is considered a serious disciplinary error.

❌ Doctoral students who do not fulfill individual plans will be expelled by the decision of the collegial body and again cannot be restored.

Expulsion for organizational and disciplinary reasons

Violation of internal procedures

If the doctoral student has violated the internal procedures of the institution three or more times, he will be expelled on this basis.

Imprisonment by court order

If If the doctoral student is convicted and sentenced to imprisonment, he will be expelled.

Failure to submit a report

Failure to submit annual or interim reports within the specified periods is also grounds for expulsion.

Dissertation requirements are violated

Violation of the rules during the preparation and defense of the dissertation will have serious consequences for the doctoral student.

Conclusion

The new decision aims to strengthen responsibility for the doctoral process, increase the efficiency of scientific activity and train quality personnel. Doctoral students must carry out their plans clearly and responsibly, strictly follow the established procedures. — More information
