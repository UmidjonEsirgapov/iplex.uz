Is it possible to pay OTM contract fees in 4 installments?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Answer: Yes, it is possible to pay in 4 installments.
100% installment: must be paid by May 1, 2025.
Note: The possibility of paying in installments also applies to the super-contract

Is it possible to pay the contract in 4 installments?

Answer: Yes, it is possible to pay in 4 installments. Students of higher education institutions (HEI) are allowed to pay their annual tuition fees in 4 equal installments.

Terms are divided as follows:

  • 25% part: Those recommended for the 1st year will pay by September 15, and students of the second and subsequent stages will pay by October 1.
  • 50% part: by January 1, 2025.
  • 75% part: by March 1, 2025.
  • 100% part: must be paid by May 1, 2025.

Extension of payment periods is also possible. The authority to extend the initial payment period for 1st-year students is given to the State Admissions Commission, and for the rest of the stages, the HET Rector makes a decision on extending the period.

If you send an application through the kontrakt.edu.uz website and it is approved, you can pay in 4 parts and get information about the terms. However, this procedure is only recommended for state higher education institutions with financial independence. But super-contract students need to pay 50% first.
