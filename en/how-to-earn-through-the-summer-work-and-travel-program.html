Real opportunities for International Nordic University students

Education at the International Nordic University is not only study in the auditorium, but also practice, foreign experience and one of the first steps towards financial independence. In particular, the Summer "Work and Travel" program offered by the university allows students to work abroad during the summer vacation and gain great experience and income.

What is the program and how does it work?

"Work and Travel" is an international internship program that provides students with temporary work in foreign countries during the summer vacation. In this regard, the International Nordic University has established official cooperation with several international companies.

Main features:

  • Program duration: 2.5-3 months (mainly between June and August)
  • Areas of work: service, warehouse-logistics, tourism, production, technical service
  • Jobs: restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, warehouses, entertainment venues

    Who can participate?

    The conditions for participation for Nordic students are simple and open:

    • Having completed at least the 1st stage at the university
    • Passport valid for travel to European or Asian countries have
    • CEFR or IELTS (minimum B1) certificate (for some countries)
    • Selection based on interview and selection

    All organizational processes are carried out through the International Relations Department of the university. What are the benefits of the program?

    • Foreign language practice - developing English, Korean or Turkish in a permanent foreign environment
