Applications for transfer of studies are being accepted

Applications for transfer of studies are accepted until January 20, 2025. To implement this process, 13 higher education institutions formed regional working groups. Working groups work every day until January 20:

  • Working hours: 9:00 to 17:00
  • Saturday: 9:00 to 15:00
  • Sunday: day off

List of documents

For application documents are required:

  • Application for transfer of studies (provided by the regional working group and filled out on the spot);
  • A copy of the student's rating book or academic reference or transcript (one is enough);
  • Copy of passport;
  • Documents confirming the good reason.

Good reasons for the transfer of studies

  1. If the student wants to transfer his studies to the OTM in the area where his spouse lives permanently due to starting a family.
  2. If the employee of the state bodies wants to transfer his spouse's or minor child's studies in the event of a change of workplace. In this case, the recommendation of the head of the relevant ministry (department) is required.
