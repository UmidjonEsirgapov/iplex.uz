Admission to higher military educational institutions will start from February 1, 2026

From February 1, 2026, the acceptance of applications from candidates who expressed their desire to study at higher military educational institutions under the system of the Ministry of Defense of the Republic of Uzbekistan will officially begin.

Where and in what order will applications be accepted?

According to the current procedure, the applications of the candidates are accepted through the district (city) Departments of Defense Affairs. It is necessary to strictly follow the requirements and deadlines during the document submission process.

Important requirements for candidates

Candidates should pay attention to the following when submitting an application:

  • specified documents must be completed and completed correctly;

  • applications must be submitted only within the specified deadlines;

  • be prepared for the requirements of the selection stages.

The possibility of submitting applications online will be created

In order to make it easier for candidates, the Ministry of Defense plans to introduce a special system of online acceptance of applications through an electronic platform.

About the electronic platform

Additional information on the launch of this platform and the procedure for using it will be available later through official sources. will be announced.

How will the process of admission to higher military educational institutions be carried out?

The processes of admission to higher military educational institutions will be organized step by step. The results of the competition will be delivered to the candidates in the prescribed manner.

Results of the competition

The results of the competition will be announced in an open and transparent manner, and the candidates will be notified based on the established procedure.

Conclusion

The start of accepting applications for admission to higher military educational institutions from February 1, 2026 is an important opportunity for young people who want to receive military education.
