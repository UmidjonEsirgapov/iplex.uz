Admission for the 2020-2021 academic year at the Faculty of Medicine named after Ibn Sina and the Higher Schools of Qualified Medical Services opened on the basis of the Bukhara State Medical Institute

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Higher School of Qualified Medical Services (training of auxiliary personnel in the field of health) - duration of education - 3 years, quota 120 people in the following directions: - Emergency medicine - Physiotherapy - Anesthesiology University of Health Sciences (Istanbul Turkey) Bukhara State Medical Institute named after Abu Ali Ibn Sina The educational process at the Faculty of Medicine and Higher School of Qualified Medical Services named after Ibn Sina is conducted in Turkish and Uzbek languages on the basis of the curriculum and programs of the University of Health Sciences in accordance with the state educational standards of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
Enrollment and attestation of graduates is established by the University of Health Sciences (Istanbul, Turkey) in accordance with the educational standards of the Ministry of Health of the Republic of Uzbekistan. is carried out in accordance with the regulations.
Education is carried out on a fee-contract basis.

The Faculty of Medicine named after Ibn Sina and Higher Schools of Qualified Medical Services, opened by the University of Health Sciences (Istanbul Turkey) on the basis of the Bukhara State Medical Institute named after Abu Ali Ibn Sina, announces admission for the 2020-2021 academic year.

In 1903 Founded University of Health Sciences (Istanbul, Turkey) is one of the leading medical universities in Turkey. The university includes 14 faculties, 5 institutes, 5 medical schools and about 20 research centers. The 2 faculties mentioned above started their activities in Bukhara in 2019. Currently, 131 students are studying at the Faculty of Medicine and Higher School of Qualified Medical Services named after Ibn Sina.

The main task of the University of Health Sciences (Istanbul, Turkey) is to train advanced specialists of their profession who can meet international standards and national principles of the health care system, and at the same time provide quality medical services to the population and improve the quality of life of society. is to implement scientific and practical research.

Training of highly specialized personnel is carried out on the basis of the following educational programs:

1. "Medicine" bachelor's degree (Medical work), study period - 6 years, quota - 80 people.

2. Higher school of qualified medical services (training of auxiliary personnel in the field of health care) - duration of education - 3 years, 120 people according to quota areas:

- First aid

- Physiotherapy

- Anesthesiology

The Faculty of Medicine named after Ibn Sina, opened by the University of Health Sciences (Istanbul, Turkey) on the basis of the Bukhara State Medical Institute named after Abu Ali ibn Sina and the educational process at the Higher School of Qualified Medical Services is carried out in Turkish and Uzbek languages based on the curriculum and programs of the University of Health Sciences aligned to the state educational standards of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

The training of specialists is organized on the basis of specific orders of the Ministry of Health of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the private sector.

Study address - Bukhara region.

Education is carried out on a fee-contract basis.

Educated by pedagogues of the University of Health Sciences and the Bukhara State Medical Institute named after Abu Ali Ibn Sina.

The possibility of paying a scholarship is being considered for applicants with the highest scores.
