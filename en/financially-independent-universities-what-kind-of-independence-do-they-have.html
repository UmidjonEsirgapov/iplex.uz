LIST of state higher education institutions granted financial independence

President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "State higher education decision on measures to grant financial independence to institutions was adopted.

üî∞ According to the decision, from January 1, 2022 financial independence was granted to 35 leading state higher education institutions, including on the following:

  • on the basis of payment-contract, taking into account the demand in the market of educational services determining the cost of education;
  • determining and extending the payment terms of students' fee-contract funds;
  • allocating scholarships and grants for students at the expense of their own funds.

Also, 10 powers of the monitoring councils established in state higher education institutions granted financial independence were determined by the decision.

In addition, higher education institutions granted financial independence educational institutions were given a number of privileges.

Medical higher education institutions were allowed to spend necessary funds from their extra-budgetary funds to strengthen the material and technical base of their clinics, as well as to carry out capital and current repair work.
