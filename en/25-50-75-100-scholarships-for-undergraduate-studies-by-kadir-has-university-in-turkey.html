Requirements for candidates:

  • For international students who have completed high school or are in their final year of high school;
  • Also, citizens of Turkey or any other country who have obtained citizenship of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus are also eligible;
  • Citizens of Turkey or the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus who have completed the last three years of high school abroad can participate;
  • Their school grades must be excellent;

Privileges:

  • Bachelor students receive 25%, 50%, 75% and 100% of the tuition fee as a scholarship. will be exempted.
  • A separate scholarship application is not required. Eligible students are automatically considered for the scholarship;
  • Selection is based on student achievement and academic performance;
  • There is also the option of a full scholarship for students;
  • Students with a GPA of at least 1.8 are eligible for scholarship extension or may lose their scholarship;

Required documents:

  • Passport copy;
  • CV/résumé;
  • Two references;
  • Statement of interest/purpose;
  • Academic transcripts of all courses;
  • Turkish language proficiency certificate/examination result;
  • English language proficiency certificate/examination result;
  • Bachelor diploma or graduation diploma;
  • Passport at the time of application if not, the student can submit a copy of the citizenship certificate;
