To receive an educational loan, the borrower shall submit the following documents to a commercial bank:

a) an application for granting an educational loan;

b) an agreement (contract) between the student and the institution of higher education on the basis of a payment contract drawn up according to the established procedure. The fixed term of study and the amount of payment for one year should be specified in the contract. In the case of changes in the amount of tuition fees in subsequent years, relevant changes will be made to the contracts, the higher education institution will inform the bank in writing about this within ten days, and then the necessary changes will be made to the loan agreement between the borrower, the bank and the higher education institution; a reference;

d) true orphans who have the right to receive preferential education loans, those brought up in "Mehribonlik houses", persons with disabilities of groups I and II from childhood, and those from low-income families shall provide relevant supporting documents.

By submitting the above documents to the bank, the borrower at the same time, he shows his passport in person. The bank has the right to take the necessary extracts and copies of the passport and other submitted documents.
