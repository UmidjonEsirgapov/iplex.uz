Global Korea Scholarship is a grant program that provides students with the opportunity to study their undergraduate degree in South Korea. During the program, students study in Korea for free and travel and living expenses are covered, as well as free accommodation and food. The program lasts from 4 to 6 years for those who want to study at the bachelor's level, in which the 1st year is taught only in Korean.

Embassy Track - grants for 125 students from 67 countries each year;

University Track - grants for a total of 60 natural science and engineering students from 72 countries

Requirements:

  • Candidates and their parents must not be Korean citizens;
  • Must be under 25 years of age, born after March 1, 1996;
  • Must have graduated from school or college/high school with honors by March 1, 2021;
  • Must be medically fit.

Learn more about requirements here.

Benefits:

  • Economy class air ticket for travel;
  • KRW 200,000 for accommodation;
  • KRW 900,000 per month scholarship;
  • KRW 100,000 per month for Korean language proficiency (Topic 5 or 6);
  • KRW 100,000 upon degree completion;
  • KRW 1,000,000 for language learning;
  • KRW 5,000,000 for contract fee;
  • Medical insurance costs are covered.

Required documents:

  • Filled application form;
  • Personal statement/Personal Statement;
  • Study plan;
  • Recommendation;
  • Medical certificate;
  • Passport copy
  • School or document confirming graduation from college/high school and its grades;
  • Certificate confirming language proficiency.

All documents must be in English or Korean

NOTE! Application dates vary by university and department. If you have any questions about applying, contact the university or department of your choice.
