Graduates are awarded 6 types of certificates

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
6 types of electronic certificates will be issued to graduates Based on the decision of the Government No. 44 of January 31, 2023, the regulation on the procedure for issuing electronic certificates of general secondary education was approved.
According to the regulation, the following 6 types Certificates have been introduced: Certificate of Basic Secondary Education (grade 9); Excellent certificate of basic secondary education (grade 9); General secondary education certificate (grade 11); Certificate of general secondary education (with silver medal) (grade 11); Certificate of general secondary education with honors (with gold medal) (11th grade); Certificate of completion of a school/boarding school for children with mental retardation.
How does the electronic system work?

6 types of electronic certificates are issued to graduates

On the basis of Government Decision No. 44 dated January 31, 2023 Issuance of electronic certificates of general secondary education The regulation on the procedure was approved. Based on this regulation, several types of certificates are issued to graduates.

What are the types of documents?

According to the regulation, the following 6 types of certificates have been introduced:

  • Certificate of basic secondary education (grade 9);

  • Certificate of excellent basic secondary education (grade 9);

  • Certificate of general secondary education (grade 11);

  • Certificate of general secondary education (with silver medal) (Grade 11);

  • Certificate of general secondary education with honors (with gold medal) (Grade 11);

  • Certificate of completion of a school/boarding school for children with mental disabilities.

How does the electronic system work?

Certificates are issued in Uzbek and English and created using the "Electronic Certificate" information system.

Information entry and order period

  • The information about graduates is entered into the platform before December 1 every year.

  • On the basis of orders, June 20 up to electronic certificates are prepared.

How to download?

Graduates can download free of charge from the electronic platform by entering identity document information.

Conclusion

The new system will more accurately reflect the level of knowledge of graduates at the general and basic secondary education levels. and serves to recognize them internationally. The digitized system makes the process more transparent and convenient. — Official Data
