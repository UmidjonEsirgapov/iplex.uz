Bukhara State Medical Institute will be reorganized as a university and a branch will be opened in Navoi

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The draft of the presidential decree "On the establishment of the Bukhara State Medical University and further improvement of the personnel training system in the field" was announced. The Navoi branch of the Bukhara State Medical University will be established on the basis of the buildings of the Navoi Abu Ali ibn Sino Technical School of Public Health, located at 1 Ainiy Street, and the Children's and Adolescent Football Academy, located at 7-Daha, I.Karimov Shoh Street, Navoi City. The 1st-2nd level students of the Medical Faculty of the Pedagogical Institute in the field of "Treatment Work" and "Pediatrics Work" will be transferred to the Branch.

The draft of the Presidential Decree "On the establishment of the Bukhara State Medical University and further improvement of the personnel training system in the field" was announced.
