A branch of TATU will be established in Tashkent region

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
From the 2021-2022 academic year, a branch of the Tashkent University of Information Technologies will be opened in the Tashkent region.
The national system of certification of teachers of this subject will be introduced by March 1, 2021.
From September 1, 2021, digital skills teachers of these subjects of specialized schools who have the appropriate certificate on the level of acquisition will be paid a monthly bonus in the amount of 50 percent of the BHM. This was determined in the decision of the President of Uzbekistan "On measures to further improve the education system in the field of information technologies, develop scientific research and integrate them with the IT industry".

Accordingly, 14 basic schools will be established in this direction this year, and a total of 205 specialized schools will be established in the republic in 2021-2023.

From the 2020/2021 academic year:

• integration of "Informatics and Information Technologies" subject in schools with the curriculum of the "One Million Programmers" project

• within the framework of this project, a one-time reward system will be introduced for the 100 best "Informatics and Information Technology" subject teachers of schools who have successfully completed training courses in the amount of 20 times the BHM. Teachers of this subject of specialized schools who have the appropriate certificate are paid a monthly bonus in the amount of 50 percent of BHM.
