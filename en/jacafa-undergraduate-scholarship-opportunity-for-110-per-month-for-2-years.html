JACAFA: Undergraduate Scholarship Opportunity for $110 per month for 2 years!

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
This program is open to undergraduate students studying at universities in Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Azerbaijan and Georgia.
Those who wish to apply to the program must submit the following documents: Completed online application form.
A copy of the document proving that you are currently a student (student ID card/card).

Applications are being accepted for the JACAFA The Nippon Foundation Scholarship Program scholarship program, which was established to provide financial support to talented and leadership students. This scholarship is intended for students studying full-time in the fields of social sciences, computer engineering, IT engineering, and local agricultural development.

This program is open to undergraduate students studying at universities in Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Azerbaijan, and Georgia.

Who is the grant for?

For 2nd-year undergraduate students

Link for more information

Benefits of the grant:

The winners will have the following opportunities:

  • A scholarship of 110 USD per month will be provided for 2 academic years (2023-2025). The scholarship is intended to be used for the student's study expenses and training.
  • The program will organize various events in the countries of Central Asia, and the winners will have the opportunity to participate in these events.
  • Those who have successfully completed the program can participate in the JACAFA Japan Visit Program competition. Selected participants will travel to Japan, all expenses covered.
  • Program graduates will have the opportunity to receive grants of up to $10,000 per year for small-scale projects.

Requirements:

The requirements for candidates are as follows:

  1. Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan or Georgia It is necessary to be a citizen.
  2. For Uzbekistan: only students studying in higher education institutions located in Tashkent can apply (note: the official announcement did not mention this separately. Last year, it was not mentioned separately in the official announcement, but when students of higher educational institutions located in regions other than Tashkent city applied, they were told that only students of higher education institutions in Tashkent could apply, and their application was not accepted. This request is foundationjacafa@gmail.com you can check it personally by writing a message to e-mail).
  3. The candidate's grade point average (GPA) must be at least 3 on a 4-point system and this indicator must not decrease even after the grant is awarded. This indicator is equal to 75 points in a 100-point system and 3.75 points in a 5-point system.
  4. The candidate must have good knowledge of the English language and have an IELTS, TOEFL, TOEIC certificate or the experience of completing courses taught in English at a university.
  5. The age of the candidate must not be older than 23 years at the time of the scholarship (October 2023).
  6. The candidate must be a student and about to start the 3rd year of the bachelor's degree. That is, students who will graduate from the 2nd year in the summer of 2023 can apply for this program.
  7. The candidate must be studying full-time in social sciences, computer engineering, IT engineering, and local agricultural development or related fields.

Program admission process:

Applications in Uzbekistan time Accepted until April 15, 2023. Those who wish to apply for the program must submit the following documents:

  1. Completed online application form. The application can be completed and submitted HERE.
  2. Copy of passport to go abroad.
  3. Certificate of no conviction.
  4. Copy of proof of current student status (student card/card).
  5. Scanned copy of university grades.
  6. Recommendation from at least 2 university teachers.
  7. Essay in English. The essay should be about the candidate, revealing who he is as a person, including information about the candidate's education and upbringing, greatest achievements, experiences and people that have influenced his academic and professional growth, why he chose the field he is currently studying.

All documents other than the online application should be sent to the email address foundationjacafa@gmail.com

Interviews will be held in May and June 2023 with those who have successfully passed the document submission stage. Interviews can be done online.

The final results will be announced in July 2023.
