Is it possible to reschedule the certification test?

Question: My brother works as a teacher in a school and has paid for the class. But their wedding took place on November 6-7. Therefore, is it possible to postpone the test to another day or get a refund?

Answer: The procedure for the certification of teaching staff is based on the regulation approved by Cabinet of Ministers Resolution No. 572 of September 17, 2021. According to Clause 45 of this regulation, if pedagogic employees fail to come to the tests for a valid reason, the test will be postponed.

The following are considered valid reasons:

  • Employee's temporary incapacity for work;
  • State or public duties
  • Participation in national and international competitions;
  • Death of a close relative;
  • Being on a foreign trip;
  • Other reasons specified in the reference of the authorized bodies or the management of the educational organization.

Therefore, the teacher A wedding ceremony can be considered as a valid reason, and the test can be moved to another day based on the relevant certificate from the school director. For those who do not come to the qualification test for a good reason, the tests will be held on the day specified by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.

The fee will not be collected again, that is, if the test is moved, you will not be required to pay again. — Official Data
