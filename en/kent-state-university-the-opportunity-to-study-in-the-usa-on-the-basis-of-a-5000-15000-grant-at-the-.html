Kent State University, one of the top universities in the United States, has announced its grants for foreign undergraduate students. Apply for spring 2022 undergraduate admission and win a $5,000-$15,000 grant. The grant is intended for foreign students of the university. The purpose of the scholarship is to provide financial assistance to foreign students to study in the USA. The scholarship can be received for study in any undergraduate program of the university.

Application deadline: October 1, 2020

Benefits of the program:

  • $ 5,000 - $ 15,000 grant is allocated for contract payments;

Requirements for candidates:

1. Scholarships may be awarded to foreign applicants studying in one of Kent State University's undergraduate programs;

2. GPA not less than 3.0 on a 4-point scale;

3. Admission to an undergraduate program at Kent State University;

4. Excellent knowledge of English is required (TOEFL - 525 (71 - Internet -test), MELAB - not less than 75, or PTE Academic score - 48).

There is no separate application for a grant, foreign students are automatically considered as scholarship applicants if they enter the university before the deadline.
