Transcript problem when transferring studies: Why are students struggling?

"Perevod" season has begun

Now the season of "perevod" - i.e. transferring studies to another higher education institution - has begun in the higher education system. Every year, thousands of students, especially young people studying abroad or in private universities, try to transfer their studies to state universities.

Transcript is one of the main documents

One of the most important documents when transferring studies is transcript. Through it, information such as the student's grades, list of subjects, studied semesters is determined. The online application submitted to DTM is also based on this document.

Problem: There are HEIs that do not provide transcripts

These days, some private universities are accused of not providing transcripts to their students, or of setting various conditions in return for providing them. This situation is causing hundreds of students to wander. Although the right to transfer education is defined in the law, artificial obstacles are created in practice.

Why do students want to transfer?

First of all, the university management should ask itself this question: why do students leave this school? want to leave?

In most cases, there are 2 reasons for this:

1. The quality of education is very low

The student does not find it meaningful to stay in that institution. The lessons are of poor quality, there are no qualified teachers, they are taught only for diplomas, not knowledge.

2. Excessive demands

Demands are very high in the university: internal exams are difficult, it is difficult to get grades, acquaintances do not pass. Those who cannot cope with this situation will want to move to another place.

In the first case, the university should improve the quality of education in order to maintain its reputation. In the second case, if the student wants to transfer due to strong requirements, the university itself is probably ready to give up.

A call to action

If you are also having problems with getting a transcript, do not be indifferent. Write about it in the comments or relevant sources. This is not only in your interest, but in the interest of thousands of students.

Conclusion

The transfer of studies is the right of every student, but this process should be legal, fair and transparent. HEIs should not blame students instead of solving their internal problems.
