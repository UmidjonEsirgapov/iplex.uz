Student scholarships from the 2025/2026 academic year increased

According to the President's decision of June 2, 2025, starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, scholarships for students of state higher education institutions were increased by 10%. At the same time, it was announced that contract prices will not change. This news was a great support for students.

Stipend amounts

State scholarship

The amount of state scholarship awarded to students is now set at 2,410,695 soums.

Named state scholarships

Students studying in various fields The amount of named state scholarships for named state scholarships is 1,607,130 soums.

Basic scholarship

The amount of basic scholarship for students studying in higher education institutions is 569,670 soums.

Scholarships for disabled and orphan students

Stipends of 854,505 soums were allocated for those in need of social protection, including disabled students and orphans.

Conclusion

The increase of scholarships from the 2025/2026 academic year will serve to improve the financial support of students and increase their motivation to study. The most gratifying thing is that the increase in the amount of scholarships without increasing the contract prices is a great relief for students. — Official Data
