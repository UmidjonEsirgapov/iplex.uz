Conditions for admission to the educational courses "Mirzo Ulugbek's Successors".

With the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers (19.05.2023 No. 206), we informed about the organization of preparatory training courses "Mirzo Ulugbek successors" whose listeners (students) will be provided with a monthly stipend, one hot meal and the necessary computers.

Selection processes for these training courses conditions:

In the first year of the competition, students of state HEIs who have successfully completed the 2nd year of the bachelor's degree (by all forms of education) (without academic debt) will participate.

The process of accepting talented listeners to training courses is carried out in the following three stages every year in June-August:

1st stage - submitting an online application and IQ test (online test). (Evaluated on a 100-point system)

Candidates fill out an online application for participation in the competition through the dscience.uz website of the Ulugbek Fund for Supporting Talented Youth.

A total of 300 candidates with the highest scores will be transferred to the 2nd stage.

2nd stage - exam. It is evaluated on a 100-point system based on the following criteria:

  • use of information and communication technologies (beginning);
  • fundamentals of algorithms and programming;
  • logical questions and problems;
  • logical problems in mathematics;
  • solving problems of mathematical statistics;
  • solving examples from algebra;
  • equations solving;
  • complex numbers and operations on them.

120 candidates with the highest results are recommended to participate in the 3rd stage.

3rd stage - interview. (Evaluated on a 100-point system)

In the interview, questions will be asked based on the following criteria:

Understanding the essence and importance of comprehensive reforms in the field of digitalization in the Republic (the presence of imagination and thinking about reforms in electronic government, digital economy, digital education, telecommunications and other fields.

Information about the winners of the third stage of the competition will be announced by the organizers within ten working days. — Official Data
