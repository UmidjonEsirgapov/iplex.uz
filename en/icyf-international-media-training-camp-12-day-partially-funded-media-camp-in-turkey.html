ICYF INTERNATIONAL MEDIA TRAINING CAMP: 12-day partially funded media camp in Turkey

About the expected changes in the education system:

Summary:
Encourage youth media participation in various communities.
Note: June 4 - 9 for boys.
Note: Only selected participants will be notified of the status of their applications.

General information:

In cooperation with the Ministry of Youth and Sports of the Republic of Turkey and the Islamic Cooperation Youth Forum (ICYF), ICYF International Media Training Camp will be held on June 4-16, 2022, which will allow young people from OIC member states to learn journalism, digital and social media issues is organized.

This event will include:

  • Giving young journalists an opportunity to understand and evaluate the credibility of digital media.
  • Participants will share their personal experiences with the media and how mainstream media inaccuracy can sometimes cause problems for smaller communities. to share the issue.
  • Encourage the participation of youth media in various communities.
  • Improve media work directions and strategies that reflect the social realities of the participants.
  • Strengthen youth's connection with heritage, language, culture and self-awareness through the media.

Note: For boys, June 4 - 9. For young women, it will be held on June 11-16;

Requirements for candidates:

  • A citizen of an OIC member state (including Uzbekistan) or a representative of the Muslim community in a non-OIC country;
  • Ability to communicate in English;
  • Between 18–30;
  • Strong media background (study, work experience or interest);
  • Participated in/or coordinated a media related project;
  • To participate in the camp program passionate and responsible;

Important:

The Selection Committee evaluates applications based on eligibility and criteria. If the committee decides on additional information, it may schedule an online interview as the final stage of the selection.

Selected participants will receive an email with details of the camp program. Due to the tight schedule, it will not be possible to notify those who are not selected. General information about the results of the competition will be announced through the ICYF website and social media accounts.

Note: Only selected participants will be notified of the status of their applications.

Benefits:

  • Host organizers local transportation (from the airport/bus station to the camping area and from the camping area to the airport/bus station) and covers medical and travel insurance. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey assists applicants in the process of obtaining a visa. Participants are responsible for their own visa costs.
    • Participants who fully participate will be awarded a certificate issued by ICYF and program partners.
