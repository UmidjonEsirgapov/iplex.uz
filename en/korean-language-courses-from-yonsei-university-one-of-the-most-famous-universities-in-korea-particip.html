Korean language courses from Yonsei University, one of the most famous universities in Korea

Who is it for? Beginner and for those who want to learn spoken Korean

Participation is absolutely free

This course is designed for beginners who are familiar with the Hangul alphabet of the Korean language. With the help of this course, students will acquire the necessary knowledge for daily communication in Korean.

The course consists of six modules, and each module consists of five blocks. Each block contains vocabulary, grammar rules and sentences, oral conversation practice, video clips, quizzes, a workbook and a glossary of necessary words.
