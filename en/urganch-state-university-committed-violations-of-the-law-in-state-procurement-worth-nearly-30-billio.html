Urganch State University committed violations of the law in state procurement worth nearly 30 billion soums - Competition Committee

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Urganch State University violated the law in state procurement worth nearly 30 billion soums - the Competition Committee The Competition Committee declared that the tender for the construction of the student accommodation building of Urganch State University was against the law
Urganch State University placed the object in the national information system "Transparent Construction" under the wrong category, the participation of construction organizations in other regions in the tender was limited. violated the law worth about 30 billion soums - Competition Committee

Competition Committee found the tender sale for the construction of the student accommodation building of Urganch State University to be against the law. According to the press service of the Competition Committee, this sale did not meet the legal requirements.

In the investigation carried out on the basis of the Khorezm region prosecutor's office, the tender for the construction of a 7-story, 600-bed student accommodation building based on public-private partnership with an initial price of 29.7 billion soums violations were detected.

Urganch State University placed the object in the national information system "Transparent construction" according to the wrong category, so the participation of construction organizations in other regions in the tender was limited.
