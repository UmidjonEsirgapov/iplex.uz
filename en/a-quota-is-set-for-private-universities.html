will also be given to non-state HEIs

According to the decision PQ-200, which will come into force on January 1, 2025, non-state higher education organizations will also be allocated a quota for the training of personnel based on the state order. In this case, the quotas are determined based on the position of these educational institutions in the rating.

Rating is carried out by educational fields

Now higher educational institutions are evaluated not only in general, but also in the section of educational fields based on the rating. This will encourage the development of HEIs with high quality indicators in each field.

What changes will this bring?

  • Transparent and competitive distribution of state orders

  • Healthy competition among non-governmental HEIs will increase in terms of quality

  • Choice opportunities for applicants expands

  • The specialist training system in the field of education will improve

Conclusion

From 2025, a new era will begin for non-governmental higher education organizations. The state order is given not only to state higher education institutions, but also to non-state educational institutions that have a high place in the rating.
