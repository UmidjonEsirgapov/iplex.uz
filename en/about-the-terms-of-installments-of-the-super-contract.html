About the terms of installments of the super-contract

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
Many are asking when we should pay the remaining half if we pay 50%.
That is: 75% - by April 1; 100% - until July 1.
However, this 10% discount does not apply to a regular contract and to a super-contract on a scale of up to 4 points.

According to the rule, those who want to study in a super-contract must first pay 50% in order to receive an order on student admission. Those who entered into a normal contract can pay 25%.

Usually they ask to pay half of the super-contract by September 15. However, due to the late confirmation of the contract and super-contract amounts and the late start of the application, this period should be extended (also in the case of a regular contract). They extend this term every year.

Many people are asking when we should pay the other half if we pay 50%.

The remaining 50% should be paid within the period set by the Rectors of Higher Education Institutions. Usually, these terms are the same as regular contract terms. That is:

  • 75% part - until April 1st;
  • 100% part - until July 1st.

We remind you that if you cover the super-contract 100% by October 15th, you will get a 10% discount. But this 10% discount does not apply to a regular contract and a super-contract on a scale of up to 4 points. — More information
