The fee for participation in the 2025 higher education entrance exams and study transfer tests has been increased. Now applicants and students who want to transfer their studies will pay 187,500 soums.

Why was the fee increased?

The fee is 50% of the basic calculation amount (BHM). Last year:

  • BHM — 340,000 soums

  • Payment amount — 170,000 soums

And this year:

  • BHM — 375,000 Soums

  • Amount of payment — 187,500 soums

Who is exempt from the test fee?

Applicants in the following categories are exempted from the test fee:

  • Graduating school this year applicants

  • Persons with disabilities

  • Children of orphanages

  • Graduates of Children's Town

  • Persons listed in the "Unified Register of Social Protection" and their children

  • In the "Youth Register" young people on the list

Which tests does it apply to?

This fee:

  • Bachelor entrance tests

  • Applies to transfer tests.
