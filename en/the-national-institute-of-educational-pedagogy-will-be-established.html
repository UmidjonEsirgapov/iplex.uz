The National Institute of Educational Pedagogy will be established

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
The National Institute of Pedagogical Education will be established on the basis of the Research Institute of Pedagogical Sciences of Uzbekistan named after Kori-Niyozi. The Institute will establish a Scientific Council for 5 specialties.

On the basis of the Research Institute of Pedagogical Sciences of Uzbekistan named after Kori-Niyozi, a "National Institute of Pedagogy" will be established. This new institute was created in order to strengthen the education and training system and develop pedagogical activities.

The main tasks of the institute

The tasks of the "National Institute of Education Pedagogy" include:

  1. Strengthening the cooperation of "neighborhood - parent - school":

    • The institute helps to strengthen the interaction between the neighborhood, parents and school. gives It is aimed at increasing efficiency by applying a collective approach to the education of children.

  2. Creation of educational literature:

    • The institute develops and publishes educational literature for parents and children. These literatures help to improve the upbringing of children in the family and school environment.

  3. Scientific research of the educational views of the Jadids:

    • The advanced views of the Jadids on education and upbringing are studied through scientific research and communicated to the general public. This allows integration of historical experiences into the modern education system.

Scientific Council and personnel training

The Institute will establish a Scientific Council for 5 specialties. This council supervises scientific research works and ensures their quality.

In addition, training of qualified personnel is established through master's and doctoral programs. This serves to train specialists with deep knowledge in pedagogical activity. — Read on infoedu.uz
