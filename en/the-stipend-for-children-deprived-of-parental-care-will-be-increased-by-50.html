The stipend for children deprived of parental care will be increased by 50%

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Additional benefits are provided by the state for children deprived of parental care.
According to the new legislation, orphans and children deprived of parental care are admitted to state kindergartens and schools without waiting.
Legislative documents (source, document) basically, children of this category will have the following privileges: Orphans studying in technical schools, colleges, academic lyceums or higher education institutions will receive a subsidy in the amount of 1,700,000 soums per academic year. According to the new legislation, orphans and children deprived of parental care are admitted to public kindergartens and schools without waiting.

According to the legal documents (source, document), children of this category will have the following privileges:

  • Technical school, college, academic lyceum or higher education orphans studying in institutions receive a subsidy in the amount of 1 million 700 thousand soums every academic year. This fund will be allocated for the purchase of educational, scientific and fiction literature (books).
  • The stipend will be paid with a 50% increase for orphans studying in state higher education institutions.

These changes are aimed at expanding educational opportunities and strengthening social protection. — Read on infoedu.uz
