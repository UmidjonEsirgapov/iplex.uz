Win a 4 week IELTS English scholarship: Partially funded grant program for a 4 week IELTS course in Malta - $2000 grant

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
General information: A grant program for general English and IELTS preparation courses in Malta has been announced for all English language learners.
This course is conducted in English.

General information:

For all young people learning English, a grant program for General English and IELTS preparation courses in Malta has been announced. The 4-week language school in Malta will provide a total cost of $2,000. This course is conducted in English.

The 4-week scholarship includes:

  • General English lessons;
  • IELTS preparation lessons;
  • All course study materials, placement test and certificate;
  • Accommodation in a shared apartment;
  • Pick up from the airport to the language school and back from the language school escorted transport to the airport;
  • Free use of the full course;
  • Student discount card for local shops and restaurants;
  • Use of the school swimming pool (weather permitting;
  • Monday welcome night with Maltese food;
  • Free SIM card;
  • In the dormitory free use of air conditioning;
  • free use of Netflix, DVDs and reading books;

Important: The total cost of these above services is $2000 and all of these will be provided free of charge to the winning candidates. Requirements:

  • Citizens of any country can apply;
  • The age limit is 18 years and older;
  • Family members of Maltalingua LC employees are not eligible;

Document submission process:

  • Initially, give the buyer your full name and surname. email and answer one question "Why do you want to improve your English";
  • The winners of the competition will be announced on the social pages;
