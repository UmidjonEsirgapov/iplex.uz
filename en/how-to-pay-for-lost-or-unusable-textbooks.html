According to the current legislation, lost or unusable textbooks and instructional manuals are paid by the student's parents, as well as their substitutes or teachers in the following manner:

  • in the form of textbooks and instructional manuals;
  • In primary documents (in the invoice, waybill) in the amount of the value indicated:
  • for each textbook and teaching methodical manuals of the first grade students - in the amount of one time;
  • for each textbook and teaching methodical manuals of the 2nd-4th grade students - in the amount of twice it;
  • for each textbook and teaching method of the 5th-9th grade students for methodological manuals - in the amount of four times.

P.S.: From this academic year, the rent paid by parents for school textbooks was canceled and paid from the state budget. It is still unknown where the lost or damaged textbooks will be reimbursed. — Source
