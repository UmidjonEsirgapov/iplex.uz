The procedure for issuing recommendations to women for admission based on an additional state grant has changed

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The procedure for giving recommendations to women to participate in the competition has changed as part of the admission indicators based on the additional state grant.
The Ministry of Neighborhood and Family Support informs about this.
Given to women from needy families. letters of recommendation are sent to district (city) neighborhood and family support departments, and district (city) neighborhood and family support departments ensure that the letter of recommendation is delivered to applicants by June 15 every year. changed. This is reported by the Ministry of Neighborhood and Family Support.

On June 3, 2021, the Cabinet of Ministers approved the regulation "On the approval of the regulation on the procedure for recommending women to participate in the competition and organizing their admission to higher education institutions within the framework of admission indicators based on additional state grants" 2020 Decision No. 338 on amendments and additions to Decision No. 402 of June 23 was adopted. The information system "Unified Register of Social Protection" of the off-budget Pension Fund is connected with the interdepartmental integration platform of the "Electronic Government" system, and the level of need of the family is determined through the information about female children of the families included in the "Iron Register" and "Women's Register" and women included in the "Youth Register".

  • in order to receive a recommendation, women from needy families apply electronically to the district (city) neighborhood and family support departments of their place of permanent residence or temporary residence;
  • documents of women from needy families applying for a recommendation are accepted annually from May 30 to June 8;
  • information and confirmation of one of the relevant criteria Electronic documents (birth, marriage registration, death, etc.) are obtained through the "Unified Register of Social Security" information system of the Ministry of Finance and the "Electronic Government" interdepartmental integration platform;
  • A message about the recommendation or rejection of a recommendation is sent to the mobile phone number or email address of the applicants.

    • The list of women in needy families to whom recommendations have been issued is published on the official website of the Ministry of Neighborhood and Family Support.

    The recommendation is issued by the Ministry of Neighborhood and Family Support in the form of an electronic document based on the order of the district (city) neighborhood and family support departments issued according to the final minutes of the meeting of the working group

    Recommendations for participating in the competition are issued for one year as part of the admission parameters based on additional state grants to higher education institutions.
