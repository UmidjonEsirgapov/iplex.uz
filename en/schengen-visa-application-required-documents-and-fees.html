Schengen visa: application, required documents and fees

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
Visa application can be submitted in advance, no more than 6 months before the start of the planned visit.
How long will it take?
Types of visa fees: Schengen visa fee for adults: 80 euro.

When to apply?

Visa application can be submitted in advance, no more than 6 months before the planned visit. As a rule, it is advisable to submit the application at least 15 days before the intended visit, taking into account the deadlines of the diplomatic mission.

How long will it take? In some cases, when additional verification of the application is necessary, the period is extended up to a maximum of 30 days. In exceptional cases, if additional documents are required, the period can be extended up to a maximum of 45 days.

What documents are submitted: — Source

  • Filled visa application form.
  • International travel passport.
  • Personal photo (photo requirements).
  • A document showing the purpose of the trip.
  • Medical insurance (coverage amount is at least 30 thousand euros)
  • Depending on the situation, the duration of the trip, accommodation, round-trip flight ticket reservations and other documents may be canceled.

Types of visa fees:

  • Schengen visa fee for adults: 80 euros.
  • For children aged 6-12: 40 euros.
  • Free for children under 6 years old. In addition, there may be a change in prices for mutual agreement between countries and exceptional cases. For more information, contact the official representative office, embassies and consulates.
