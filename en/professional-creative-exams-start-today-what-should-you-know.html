Professional (creative) exams start today: what should you know?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Information about the address, date and time of the exam will be shown.
Applicants currently taking professional/creative exams can download permits from their personal offices.
Permits to enter tests. It is being formed on the site my.uzbmb.uz.

From today, professional (creative) exams for entering higher and professional educational institutions will begin. After the entrance exams held in July of this year, professional (creative) exams for transfer of students from foreign and non-governmental higher education organizations in foreign countries, as well as in Uzbekistan, to areas of state higher education institutions that require special talents, are scheduled to be held on August 16-17. According to the presidential decree, this year higher and professional entrance exams to educational institutions are scheduled to be held in July-August. These exams are held in 53 higher education institutions in fields that require special talent, such as sports, art, and journalism.

In order to enter creative exams, applicants are required to clearly indicate their phone numbers and address information when applying, as information about exam dates will be sent to them by email. It is also important for applicants to follow the official websites of higher educational institutions.

Where to get permits?

The permit is created in the applicant's personal office on the site my.uzbmb.uz. It will contain information about the location, date and time of the exam. Currently, applicants for professional/creative exams can download permits from their personal offices.

Permits for entering tests are also being created on the site my.uzbmb.uz. After the process is completed, applicants can download their admit card.

Examination cannot be late. The exact start time of the exams will be indicated on the applicant's admit card. Therefore, applicants are requested to arrive at the time and place specified in the permit one hour before the start of the exam.

Is it possible to take the test in another region?

No, applicants must take the exam in the region specified during online registration. Because the test materials are prepared only for the region specified by it and it is not possible to replace it.

In addition, with this decree, the practice of distributing the parameters of the state order in the section of state HEIs will be canceled from the 2024-2025 academic year. The parameters are confirmed in the form of full-time education for undergraduate education and master's specialties. — Source
