General Information:

A forum under the name "Young Leaders of Russia-Central Asian Countries" will be held in the Russian city of Omsk between November 16-19.

The purpose of the forum-Russia, It is aimed at helping to further develop friendly and professional relations between young people with the relevant state organizations of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan.

Forum organizers: Federal Agency for Youth Affairs, Ministry of Youth Affairs, Physical Education and Sports of Omsk Region, as well as the Project Bureau for International Cooperation of Youth in the Russia-Central Asia Region .

Working language of the forum: Russian.

Advantages of the forum:

Accommodation, food and all transportation costs within the city in the Omsk region are covered by the organizers. However- The payment for the flight to Omsk is made by the participant or the organizers sending the participant.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Age: citizens between 18 - 35;
  • Citizens of Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan;
  • Submit an online application here and fill in the necessary items.

Directions:

  • Promoting career guidance and aspirations of young people;
  • Development of international and interregional cooperation;
  • Formation of the identity of the Russian language, the Russian nation development of unity, intercultural and interreligious dialogue.
