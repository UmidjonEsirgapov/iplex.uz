General Information:

Currently, Qatar University has announced the Qatar University Scholarship grant program for the 2022 spring semester. This grant program is valid for representatives of all nationalities who wish to study at all master's and doctoral levels. This university is located in Doha, the capital city of Qatar, and ranked first in the country.

Benefits of the grant:

  • Fully exempted from contract fees;
  • Provided with accommodation;
  • Exempted from fees for training courses and textbooks.
  • Annual return air tickets are covered;

Eligibility criteria:

  • Bachelor or master degree;
  • Citizens of all countries and Qatari students can apply;
  • The requirements for the program vary according to your chosen field;
  • For admission additional requirements can be checked here;

The following areas are available:

  • Education;
  • Economics and Business;
  • Engineering;
  • Law;
  • Medicine;
  • Pharmaceuticals;
  • Sharia and Islamic studies;

The last date to apply for 2022 Qatar University Scholarships for international students is October 21, 2021!
