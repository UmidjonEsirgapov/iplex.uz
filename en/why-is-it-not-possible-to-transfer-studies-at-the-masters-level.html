Why is it not possible to transfer studies at the master's level?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Why is it not possible to transfer studies at the master's level?
In accordance with Article 232 of the Regulation on the procedure for transfer, restoration and expulsion of students of higher education institutions, at the master's level of foreign higher education institutions It is not allowed to transfer the studies of students to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
The main reason for this is that, as specified in the Master's Degree Regulations approved by the Cabinet of Ministers' decision No. 36 dated March 2, 2015, master's thesis topics and academic supervisors are approved for master's degree students in the first two months of the first academic year.

Why is it not possible to transfer studies at the master's level?

In accordance with Article 232 of the Regulation on the procedure for transfer, restoration and expulsion of students of higher education institutions, students studying at the master's level of foreign higher education institutions can transfer their studies to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan. transfer is not allowed.

The main reason for this is the Master's thesis topic and scientific supervisors are approved for master's degree students in the first two months of the first academic year, as specified in the Master's Degree Regulation approved by the Cabinet of Ministers' decision No. 36 of March 2, 2015. The process of preparation of master's thesis starts from the first semester of the first year.

By transferring studies, it becomes impossible to fully implement this process at the level of the newly established requirements. Therefore, it is considered inappropriate to transfer studies at the master's level. — Read on infoedu.uz
