It will be possible to transfer studies from abroad on the basis of a grant

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
It will be allowed to transfer the studies of young people studying abroad to universities in Uzbekistan on the basis of a grant - this was announced by presidential candidate Shavkat Mirziyoyev. The coverage of higher education in Karakalpakstan will be increased to 50 percent next year.
For this purpose, the practice of transferring the studies of young Uzbeks studying abroad to local universities on the basis of a state grant will be established. presidential candidate Shavkat Mirziyoyev informed about it.

The head of state said at a meeting with the voters of Karakalpakstan that the coverage of higher education in Karakalpakstan will reach 50% next year. For this purpose, the process of transferring the studies of young Uzbeks studying abroad to local universities on the basis of a state grant will be launched. — Source
