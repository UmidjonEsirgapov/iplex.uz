The final stage of the Republican "English Science Olympiad" was held

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
According to the instructions and regulations of the Olympiad, 169 (one hundred and sixty-nine) participants who showed the best results won a ticket to the 3rd stage (final stage). ACADEMY and Registan Chilanzar branches.
This organized Olympiad is one of the first Olympiads organized in cooperation between the REGISTON training center and the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform, and it is planned to hold SCIENCE Olympiads that will cover millions of young people starting from the new academic year.

You know, Support and In order to improve the level of English literacy of young people, a 3-level English Olympiad was held on a national scale in cooperation with the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform and the "REGISTON" educational center! Thousands of young people with a thirst for knowledge from different regions of Uzbekistan participated in this Olympiad.

The first and second stages of the Olympiad were organized on the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform. The 1st stage of the Olympiad was held on July 11, and more than 9,000 young people from our Republic participated, and the best candidates qualified for the next stage. The 2nd stage of the Olympiad on July 18 1000 best candidates selected from thousands of talented young people from our republic received the right to participate.

According to the instructions and regulations of the Olympiad, 169 (one hundred and sixty nine) participants who showed the best results in the 3rd stage (final stage) they got the ticket. The final stage of the Olympiad started today July 25 at 09:00.

It was held at Registan Chilanzar branches.

Olympic participants tested their speaking skills from 09:00 to 13:00 spent. The speaking exam was conducted by the most influential expert teachers and native speakers of our Republic.

The main exam of the Olympiad started at 14:00 and lasted until 17:00. To ensure impartiality and full transparency, the main examination was recorded by video cameras and broadcast live online for everyone on the YouTube platform.

The results of the exam will be announced on July 26, the results will be delivered to the participants in the form of sms notifications, and on July 27, the most worthy absolute winners will be solemnly awarded.

For your information, special awards, souvenirs, I, II and III level QR-coded diplomas will be awarded to the winners of the Olympiad:

To the winner of 1st place: Laptop computer and souvenir books worth 200 thousand soums

To the winner of 2nd place: Samsung A21 mobile phone and souvenir books worth 100 thousand soums

To the winner of 3rd place: Xiaomi 9A mobile phone and worth 100 thousand soums commemorative books worth soums

As a reminder, all candidates who participated can view their results and download special certificates of participation in the Olympiad through the "Personal Cabinet" on the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform or the first window of the Olympiad. educational center and OLIYGOH.UZeducational platform is one of the first Olympiads, and it is planned to hold the SCIENCE Olympiad, which will cover millions of young people from the new academic year.
