Applicant registration procedure

Online registration dates

Employees who have worked in the system for more than 5 years can register online from August 1 to August 15, 2025 (also on this day) through the my.uzbmb.uz website. necessary.

Procedure of acceptance based on interview

Interview with applicants is carried out on the basis of the regulation approved by Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 675 (04.11.2021). According to the results of the interview, they are recommended to study on the basis of differentiated payment-contract.
