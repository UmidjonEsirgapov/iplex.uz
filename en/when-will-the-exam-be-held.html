When will the exam be held?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
If it is canceled on August 15, it will be held after August 15, if it is canceled on September 1, it will be held after September 1.
- If the applicant is quarantined for some reasons, then what will be?
Answer: if he is in quarantine on his test day, he can take an additional test that will be completed 4 days after the completion of all tests.

Based on the current situation, the time of the exam will be postponed depending on the situation. If necessary, it can be held after September 1.

"An offer has been made to postpone the exam. When quarantine restrictions are lifted, the test will be held then. If it is canceled on August 15, it will be held after August 15, if it is canceled on September 1, it will be held after September 1.

❌ Exams the venues have not yet been confirmed. We will inform you after confirmation. The exam will not be held in the pavilion that has been converted into a stationary one.

The places selected for the tests will definitely be a large-capacity facility," said the head of the State Test Center, Barno Rajabov.

Answers to many questions:

What will happen if the applicant has a high body temperature?

Answer: Those with a fever will not be tested, for them a re-test will be conducted after 4 days after all the testing procedures have been completed.

- If the applicant is diagnosed with coronavirus, what will they do?

Answer: if until the day of the test if the relevant organization provides information about his recovery, he will be tested. If not, he will not be admitted to the test.

- What happens if the applicant is quarantined for some reason?

Answer: if he is quarantined on the day of his test, he can take an additional test 4 days after the completion of all tests. If he is in quarantine on the day of the additional test, he will miss the test.
