ECU (Edith Cowan University) offers a fully funded grant program to all those who intend to continue their PhD and conduct research at this university. Those admitted to the program are exempted from the contract payment during the academic year and are provided with an annual scholarship.

The program lasts up to 3 years, depending on the chosen field. Those who want to participate can apply starting from September 20. Benefits:

  • Exemption from the study contract;
  • An annual stipend of $29,000 is provided;
  • Travel and medical insurance expenses are covered.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must have completed a master's degree;
  • Admission to ECU's doctoral program;
