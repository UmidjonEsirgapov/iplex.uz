On what topics are the tests prepared?

In the entrance exams for the bachelor education courses of higher education institutions, test questions are prepared on the basis of specific topics in various subjects. Below is the compulsory subject test question distribution.

Compulsory subject test topics

1. Native language (Uzbek):

  • Orthography and punctuation - 40%
  • Lexicology, methodology - 40%
  • Grammar - 20%

2. Mother tongue (Russian):

  • Phonetics - 10%
  • Vocabulary - 10%
  • Grammar - 40%
  • Orthography - 40%

3. Mother tongue (Korkalpak):

  • Phonetics – 10%
  • Vocabulary – 20%
  • Grammar – 40%
  • Orthography – 30%

4. Mathematics:

  • Numbers and operations on them - 40%
  • Percentage, proportion and equation - 30%
  • Geometry - 30%

5. History of Uzbekistan:

  • Ancient and medieval history - 35-45%
  • Historical period at the beginning of the 16th-20th centuries - 35-45%
  • History of the Soviet era and the period of independence - 20-25%
