Is admission to the master's degree based on the diploma score or the certificate level?

So, the explanation of the Ministry was as follows: first of all, there will be a selection based on the level of the language certificate specified in the law according to the mastery score in the diploma; after that, if there are free places in the quota, applicants with a certificate below the specified level will be sorted and accepted again according to the diploma score.
As an example, let's say that the following 4 applicants applied to the non-philology course: Ali - diploma score 82, certificate C1; Nigora - 87, B2; Vali - 80, B1; Sabohat - 88, A2.

The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation has decided to continue the winter admission to the master's degree from December 20 to January 10. exceptionally language proficiency certificate level was not requested. That is, regardless of the certificate level, one can be admitted to the master's degree program, but in the future, he must obtain the required degree before graduation, otherwise, the master's degree will not be awarded. Actually, the level required by the law: ➖ for philological fields - C1;➖ for all other fields - B2.
