Contract sum of 16.5 million soums will be paid to the students

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Half of his contract will be 20 million soums, but not 20 million soums, but up to 50 times of BHM will be covered, that is, up to 16 million 500 thousand soums.
Since his contract does not exceed 50 times of BHM these 10 million soums will be fully reimbursed.
Therefore, the application will be submitted to the department of youth affairs.

Up to 50 percent of the contract, but in an amount not exceeding 50 times of the BHM will be reimbursed.

For example:

  • Student A's contract amount 40 million soums. Half of his contract will be 20 million soums, but not 20 million soums, but up to 50 times of BHM will be covered, i.e. up to 16 million 500 thousand soums.

  • Student B's contract is 20 million soums. Half of his contract is 10 million soums. This 10 million soums will be fully covered as his contract does not exceed 50 times the BHM. Anyone studying in Uzbekistan, including state, private, foreign higher education institutions, technical schools, colleges, can use the privilege under the contract.

    In addition, this privilege is given to those studying not only at the bachelor's level, but at the master's level. It should be a full-time, part-time and evening bachelor's degree, and a full-time master's degree.

    Will the super contract also be paid?

    No, this benefit does not apply to those studying in the super contract or the second major.

    Who should I contact?

    The contract will be paid at the expense of the Yoshlar Daftari fund. The fund is managed by the Trustee Council headed by the governor in districts (cities). The working body of the council is the district (city) youth affairs department. Therefore, the application is submitted to the Department of Youth Affairs. A copy of the contract issued by OTM must be attached to the application. There is no specific deadline for applying, you can apply at any time. — More information
