Graduates must get an ID card!

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Otherwise, you will not be able to study. It is known that in order to submit relevant documents for admission to higher education institutions, applicants must enter their passport (ID card) data into the ABITUR electronic program. the Ministry of Preschool and School Education writes that it is necessary to obtain a passport (ID card).
In order to make the process more convenient for graduates, the electronic database of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education has been integrated with the ABITUR electronic program of the Agency for the Assessment of Knowledge and Skills. Applicants must enter their passport (ID card) data into the ABITUR electronic program in order to submit relevant documents online for admission to higher education institutions. Therefore, we remind you that every graduate who has reached the age of 16 needs to get a passport (ID card) on time, writes the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.

In order to make the process more convenient for graduates, the electronic database of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education has been integrated with the ABITUR electronic program of the Agency for the Assessment of Knowledge and Skills.
