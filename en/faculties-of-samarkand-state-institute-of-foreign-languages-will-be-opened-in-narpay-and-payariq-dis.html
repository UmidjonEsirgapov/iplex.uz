Faculties of Samarkand State Institute of Foreign Languages ​​will be opened in Narpay and Payariq districts

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Samarkand foreign language institute branches will be opened in two districts of Samarkand region.
The press service of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education informs about this. "Foreign language in pre-school and primary education" (English) and "Foreign language and literature: English" faculties of the Samarkand State Institute of Foreign Languages will be opened at the service technical school and economy and service vocational colleges in Payariq district. This is reported by the press service of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

According to the order of President Shavkat Mirziyoyev, now in the service technical school in the Narpay district of Samarkand region and the economy and service vocational colleges in the Payariq district of the Samarkand State Institute of Foreign Languages "Foreign language in preschool and primary education" (English) and "Foreign language and literature: English" faculties will be opened.

It is noted that on April 17, responsible leaders of the field went to Narpay and Payariq districts. They defined the tasks related to the quality of education, the repair of the educational building, the creation of necessary opportunities for students to receive modern education, and other tasks to be carried out. — Read on infoedu.uz
