The deadline for submitting documents to specialized schools and boarding schools and exam subjects have been announced

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was this:

Brief content:
Parents of students who expressed their desire to participate in the selection of specialized schools and boarding schools submit the following documents in electronic form through the official website of the Agency (http://www.piima.uz/) in June of this year: a photo of the applicant taken during the last 2 months; identity document information of the applicant and his parents (legal representatives); the contact phone number will be entered into the platform.
This was announced by the head of the agency's information service Jorabek Jangirov at a briefing.
According to the information provided by the agency's press secretary, the entrance exams to these institutions will be held in the form of a test in the following subjects: In the field of specific sciences: mathematics (logical thinking); physics (for 7th grade and above); English language; In the field of natural sciences: biology; chemistry (for grades 8 and up); mathematics; English language.
