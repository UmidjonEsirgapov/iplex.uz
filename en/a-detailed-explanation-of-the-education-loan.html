Full information about education loan

Education loan is a financial aid given by the government to ease the cost of education for students. It is offered to full-time undergraduate and graduate students studying on a fee-contract basis.

Educational loan conditions

  • Loan amount: No limit

  • Repayment period: up to 7 years

  • Interest rate:

Who can use the education loan?

  • State and private university students

  • To study on the basis of a super-contract

  • In the second major, distance or special part-time education

  • To study in foreign HEIs

List of necessary documents

  • Application

  • Student and the agreement (contract) between the educational institution

  • Documents on the provision of loan repayment

  • Certificate confirming inclusion in the "Uniform Register of Social Protection" (if any)

  • Additional documents specified in the bank's policy
