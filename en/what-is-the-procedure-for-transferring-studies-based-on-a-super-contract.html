What is the procedure for transferring studies based on a super-contract?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Applicants who want to transfer their studies on the basis of an extended contract, submit an online application through the website kontakt.edu.uz.
After that, after the "approval" is given by the university, a contract payment agreement is formed on the site.
Then After that, you will pay 50% of the increased contract amount and you will be admitted to a higher education institution.

Applicants who want to transfer their studies based on the increased contract, submit an online application through the website kontakt.edu.uz.

Then, after the "confirmation" by the university, a contract payment agreement will be formed on the site.

then you will pay 50% of the increased contract amount and you will be admitted to a higher education institution. The university will send you a contact letter. According to the contact letter, you will bring the necessary documents from your previous educational institution and submit them to the personnel department (student personnel).
