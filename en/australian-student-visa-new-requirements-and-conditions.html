Australia is one of the leading countries for accepting international students, and in recent years, the interest of young people from Uzbekistan to study in this country is increasing. However, from 2024, important changes to the Australian student visa have been introduced.

Main changes

1. English language requirements have been increased

Now the requirements for IELTS results for obtaining a student visa have changed as follows:

  • The minimum IELTS level for bachelor programs has been increased to 6.0 instead of 5.5.
  • For master programs it was previously 6.0 the requirement should now be at least 6.5 degrees.

2. The age limit has been reduced

  • The maximum age limit for students applying for master's programs has been reduced from 50 years to 35 years.

3. Changes in the duration of the visa

  • The duration of the visa for the Master's coursework has been set at 2 years.
  • The visa period for the research master and PhD students has been set at 3 years.

4. "Genuine Student Test" will be introduced

Students coming to Australia must prove that they are studying only. Through the new Genuine Student Test, candidates who want to work more than to study will be identified and the chances of them being granted a visa will decrease.

New opportunities for those who want to work

New opportunities are being created for those who want to come to work in Australia. Through the new visas, programs oriented to the labor market are expanding, which creates a convenient opportunity for those who want to get a job.

Conclusion

Applicants who want to study in Australia should take into account the new visa requirements. English language requirements have been strengthened, the age limit has been lowered and the visa period has been clearly defined. Therefore, those who are planning to apply to Australian universities should take these changes into account. — Read on infoedu.uz
