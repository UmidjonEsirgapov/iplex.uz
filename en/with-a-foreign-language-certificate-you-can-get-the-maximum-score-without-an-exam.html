With a foreign language certificate, you can get the maximum score without an exam

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Foreign language certificates with maximum points in the process of admission to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan, it is possible to receive maximum points in foreign language.
If the applicant has B2 level from the following national or international certificates, he is not required to take an exam in foreign language and with maximum points
Recognized certificates English: National certificate – B2, C1 TOEFL IBT – 72+ Cambridge Assessment English (FCE, CAE, CPE) – FCE (160+), CAE (180+), CPE (200+) IELTS (Academic) – 5.5+ French: National certificate – B2, C1 DELF – B2 DALF – C1, C2 German: National certificate – B2, C1 Goethe-Zertifikat – B2, C1, C2 TestDAF – TDN 3 (B2.1), TDN 4 (B2.2-C1.1), TDN 5 (C1.2) DSD II – B2, C1 Spanish: National certificate – B2, C1 DELE – B2, C1, C2 SIELE – B2, C1, C2 Italian, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Turkish and there are similar national and international certificates for other languages, which exempt from the foreign language exam if they are at the B2 level or higher. If the applicant has the B2 level of the following national or international certificates, he is not required to take an exam in the subject of a foreign language and will be evaluated with the maximum score.
