Writers of the Future offers the writers who create in the science-fantasy direction an opportunity to win a cash prize by participating in this competition. the competition is held every 3 months.

The winners will be awarded as follows:

  • 1st place - $1000;
  • 2nd place - $750;
  • 3rd place - $500;

DETAILS. In addition, each year, the best artist among the 4 season winners has the opportunity to win an additional $5,000.

Requirements:

  • Creative work must be original, not plagiarized or published elsewhere;
  • Creative work must not exceed 17,000 words. condition;
  • The name and surname of the writer should not be written in the creative work.
  • When submitting the application, a reference letter with separate personal information should be placed. Winners will be notified by phone or email. Therefore, you are asked to be careful when entering data.
