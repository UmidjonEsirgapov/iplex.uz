Another scholarship will be established for students

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Stipends in the amount of 3 times of BHM per month for students who have paid their contract in full, and 6 times of BHM per month for students receiving education on the basis of state grants.
Decision of the President (No. PQ-174, 31.05.2023)
According to: From September 1, 2023, the Minister of Ecology, Environmental Protection and Climate Change scholarship will be established for the most talented students of higher education institutions in ecology, environmental protection, forestry, "green" economy and hydrometeorology and other areas of high demand.
From September 1, the scholarship of the Minister of Ecology, Environmental Protection and Climate Change will be established for the most talented students of higher education institutions in ecology, environmental protection, forestry, "green" economy, hydrometeorology and other areas of high demand. 3 times of BHM (990 thousand soums) per month, scholarships of 6 times of BHM (1 million 980 thousand soums) per month are awarded to students receiving education on the basis of a state grant.
