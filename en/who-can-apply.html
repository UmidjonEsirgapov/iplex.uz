Those who have completed the 11th grade, those who have graduated from lyceums and colleges, even citizens who are currently studying or have graduated from higher educational institutions have the right to apply. Below we provide detailed information on who can apply for the bachelor's degree.

1. Graduates of General Secondary Education (Grade 11)

All applicants who have successfully completed Grade 11 with a certificate are eligible to apply. This category includes students who have completed all secondary schools. It should not be forgotten that those who have completed the 10th grade cannot apply - only a certificate based on the 11th grade is required.

2. Graduates of professional and secondary special education

Applicants who have graduated from academic lyceum, vocational college and technical schools are also eligible to apply. Graduates of this category can apply to the OTM of their choice based on their diploma.

3. Those who are currently studying at a vocational college and technical school

Students who are currently studying at a professional educational institution, but have not yet graduated, can also apply based on their 11th grade certificate. In this case, they will have the opportunity to continue their studies or start studying at a new HEI.

4. Students currently studying in higher education

Students currently studying in higher education institutions in Uzbekistan or abroad can also apply. They can try again to enter the bachelor's degree through their diplomas from schools, high schools or colleges. If accepted, he will choose where to continue his studies.

5. Individuals who have completed higher educational institutions and have higher education

Citizens who have previously received higher education have the opportunity to re-enter the bachelor's level. They have the right to apply for any new course regardless of their existing diplomas.
