The requirement of B2 certificate for foreign language teachers has been strictly defined

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The requirement for the B2 certificate for foreign language teachers has been strictly defined in accordance with the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers on May 19, 2021, to work in general secondary, secondary special and professional educational institutions until the 2025/2026 academic year. All foreign language teachers must have a national or equivalent international certificate of at least B2 level.
Note from the Ministry The Ministry of Preschool and School Education strongly reminds all foreign language teachers of this requirement and states that it is necessary to obtain a certificate by the 2025/2026 academic year. Improving the quality of education and students' knowledge level Ensuring that teachers meet international standards Certification period All foreign language teachers must obtain a B2 level national or international certificate by the 2025/2026 academic year. According to the decision of May 19, 2021, until the 2025/2026 academic year, all foreign language teachers working in general secondary, secondary special and professional educational institutions must have at least a B2 level national or equivalent international certificate.

Note from the Ministry

The Ministry of preschool and school education strongly reminds all foreign language teachers of this requirement, says that it is necessary to obtain a certificate by the 2025/2026 school year.

The importance of the B2 certificate requirement

  • Improving the qualifications of foreign language teachers
  • Education quality and improve students' knowledge level
  • Ensure that teachers meet international standards

Certificate period

All foreign language teachers should obtain a B2 level national or international certificate by 2025/2026 school year. In case of non-fulfillment of this requirement, measures may be taken affecting the work of teachers. — Source
