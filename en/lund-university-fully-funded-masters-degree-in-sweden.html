General Information:

The Lund University school of Economics and Management in Sweden has announced the fully funded The Söderberg Scholarship . This grant includes 100% contract money. Only citizens of countries outside the European Union can apply for this grant program. Citizens of European Union countries are not eligible to apply;

  • You must choose management as your main choice for the master's degree during the application process.
  • You must also pay a one-time fee to complete the application;

    • Note: To apply for The Söderberg Scholarship grant program, candidates must first apply for the university You must have applied for the management course as the 1st choice. Then, after submitting and paying the admission fee, you will be asked to fill out the LU Global scholarship application form.

    Eligibility criteria:

    Having high academic performance and showing strong interest in this scholarship and future management Candidates who can demonstrate how important the knowledge gained in the field of study is to their future work will be accepted.

    Benefits of the grant:

    • You will be exempted from the full contract fee:
    • However, other expenses (food, living) will not be covered.
