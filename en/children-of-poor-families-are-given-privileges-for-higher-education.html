Additional opportunities in education for children of low-income families

Now additional conditions are being created for children of low-income families to get higher education. This initiative is aimed at ensuring social equality and expanding access to education.

What benefits have been introduced for access to education?

✔ Educational loan is provided without interest - an interest-free educational loan is provided for the children of low-income families to have the opportunity to study on the basis of a contract.

✔ Additional grant is provided - state grant places will be expanded for talented young people, but with limited financial opportunities.

✔ Part of the contract fee will be covered - financial support will be provided by the state and part of the tuition fee will be covered.

These benefits will create a great opportunity for children of low-income families who want to enter higher education. — More information
