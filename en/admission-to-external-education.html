Admission to external education

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Admission of students to extramural (special extramural) education: in extramural form - general secondary (on the basis of grades 10-11), secondary special, who have an appropriate document on professional education and have successfully passed tests (entrance exams); in a special part-time form - only in the relevant or related field of education, having at least three years of practical work experience (pedagogical experience for pedagogic fields) in the field, profession, and position in accordance with the secondary special, vocational education and field of education, having a letter of recommendation issued by the relevant ministries and agencies, having successfully passed the tests (entrance exams or professional interview); The second and subsequent higher education is carried out in correspondence form - at the expense of applicants who have a higher education (if they graduated from a foreign higher education institution, their document of higher education must be recognized (nostrified) in the prescribed manner) and who successfully passed tests (entrance exams or professional interviews).
External (special correspondence) admission of students to education is carried out in accordance with the regulation on the procedure for admission of students to the bachelor's degree of higher educational institutions, approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 393 of June 20, 2017. It is carried out in accordance with the regulation on the procedure for conducting entrance tests for admission to bachelor's degrees of higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan, approved by the decision No. 261 of April 3, 2018; Admission to the part-time education of the bachelor's degree of higher education institutions is carried out on the basis of selection through entrance exams (professional (creative) exam, written exam) held in higher education institutions according to the procedure specified in Appendix 5 to this Regulation.
Part-time (special correspondence) education in higher education institutions is organized in accordance with the admission quotas approved by the decision.
External (special correspondence) education is conducted in the following forms: external form, special correspondence form, correspondence form of second and subsequent higher education.
External admission of students to (special part-time) education:
  • part-time - general secondary (on the basis of grades 10-11), secondary special, having an appropriate document on vocational education and successfully passing tests (entrance exams);
  • in a special correspondence form - only in the relevant or similar field of secondary special, vocational education and field of study, having at least three years of practical work experience (pedagogical experience for pedagogic fields), having a letter of recommendation issued by relevant ministries and agencies, successful in tests (entrance exams or professional interview) passed;
  • second and subsequent higher education in the correspondence form - has a higher education (if he graduated from a foreign higher education institution, his higher education document must be recognized (nostrified) in the prescribed manner) and successfully passed tests (entrance exams or professional interview) at the expense of applicants

External duration of study in (special correspondence) education:
  • correspondence - at least 5 years;
  • special correspondence - at least 3 years;
  • second and subsequent higher education correspondence - at least 3 years regardless of the difference in the subjects in the curricula, the bachelor's degree begins with the 2nd year of education.
Expulsion of extramural (special extramural) students from the ranks of students and resumption of their studies approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 393 of June 20, 2017 is carried out based on the regulation on the procedure for transferring, restoring and expelling students from studies.
