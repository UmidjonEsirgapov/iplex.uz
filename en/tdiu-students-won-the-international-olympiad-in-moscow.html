TDIU students won the international Olympiad in Moscow

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
A university team consisting of professors and teachers of the Department of "Accounting" and students of the Faculty of "Accounting and Audit" of the Tashkent State University of Economics took place at the People's Friendship University of Russia named after Patrice Lumumba on March 27-29, 2024 Participated in the international Olympiad "Accounting around us".
According to the information service of the university, TDIU students won the 1st place in the individual competition and the 3rd place in the team competition.
It is known that 89 teams from 67 universities and a total of 524 students participated in the international Olympiad.
