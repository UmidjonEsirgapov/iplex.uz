Illegal circumstances related to admission to higher education institutions were exposed - DXX

As a result of quick actions conducted by the State Security Service (DXX) in cooperation with the Ministry of Internal Affairs, several illegal admissions to higher education institutions were carried out. the circumstances were exposed.

A person living in the Yashnabad district of Tashkent city, born in 1986, was caught with physical evidence when he received 22 thousand US dollars in return for enrolling a resident of the Ortachirchik district of the Tashkent region in the MIA Academy through his official acquaintances.

Kashkadarya in the case observed in the region, the specialist of the Karshi University of Innovative Education promised to enroll 3 citizens in the Karshi branch of the Tashkent University of Information Technologies through his acquaintances at this higher educational institution for 4 thousand US dollars each, for a total of 12 thousand US dollars, from the 2nd year. He was caught while receiving 11,700 US dollars in a joint operation conducted by the State Security Service and the regional offices of the Department under the General Prosecutor's Office.

In a joint operation conducted by the employees of the State Security Service for the Tashkent Region in cooperation with the prosecutor's office in Chinoz District, a resident of Qamashi District, Kashkadarya Region, born in 1987 person was caught with physical evidence when he demanded 10,000 US dollars and received 5,000 US dollars in exchange for enrolling a citizen living in Boyovut district of Sirdarya region to Tashkent State Pedagogical University. and was caught while receiving US$3,500 from a resident of Almalyk city in exchange for admission to a sports university.

In 1990, during a quick event held in cooperation with the Khorezm Regional Department of the State Security Service and the Department of the General Prosecutor's Office, Dean of the Faculty of Urganch State University, a resident of Gurlan district, in 1990 2600 US dollars and 2.5 mln. In another case, the head of the department of the Urganch specialized art school demanded 4 thousand USD to a citizen living in Bogot district with the help of his acquaintances to get a pass to study at the Uzbek National Music Art Institute named after Yunus Rajabi and to help him in the practical exam. However, he was caught when he received the requested amount of money.

Criminal cases have been opened against all of these persons. — Read on infoedu.uz
