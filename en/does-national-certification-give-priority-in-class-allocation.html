Does national certification give priority in class allocation?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The national certificate gives priority in the distribution of lessons.
In this case, clauses 15 and 16 of the regulation "On the procedure for the completion of classes in general education institutions and the formation of tariff lists" (list number 3271)
According to paragraph 15, teachers with international certificates in foreign language and STEAM subjects, including TESOL Level 5, CEFR B2, IELTS 6 and higher certificates and other certificates determined by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, are listed in the 3rd level. In this case, clauses 15 and 16 of the regulation (list number 3271) "On the procedure for the completion of classes in general education institutions and the formation of tariff lists" are used.

According to clause 15, teachers who have international certificates in foreign language and STEAM subjects, including TESOL Level 5, CEFR B2, IELTS 6 and higher certificates and other certificates determined by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan are listed in Level 3.

In accordance with Clause 16, priority order is taken into account in the distribution of lessons, that is, teachers with national or international certificates have the right to receive more lessons than other teachers. — Source
