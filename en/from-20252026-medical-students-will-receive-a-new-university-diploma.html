From 2025/2026, medical students will receive a new university diploma

About the expected changes in the educational system:

Brief content:
The new university is called Tashkent State Medical University.
It will be formed on the basis of the following higher educational institutions: Tashkent Medical Academy Tashkent Pediatric Medical Institute Tashkent State Dental Institute The main tasks of the university are the training of highly educated specialists and scientific personnel for the medical field, their retraining and advanced training.
This decision is an important step aimed at increasing the quality of medical education, bringing it closer to international standards, and strengthening integration with practice.

According to the President's decision "On measures to fundamentally improve the personnel training system in the field of medicine", a new university is being established in the city of Tashkent, which combines higher medical education institutions.

New university - Tashkent It is called the State Medical University. It is formed on the basis of the following higher educational institutions:

  • Tashkent Medical Academy

  • Tashkent Pediatric Medical Institute

  • Tashkent State Dental Institute

The main tasks of the university are to train highly educated specialists and scientific personnel for the medical field, to be a leading center for training and professional development.

What will be the issue of the diploma?

Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, all students studying at these institutions will continue their studies at the new University. Students of the graduate course will be awarded with a diploma of Tashkent State Medical University.

Clinical practice and international groups

For new university students, specialized medical centers and medical institutions in the city of Tashkent will be designated as clinical practice bases. This will serve to increase the quality of practice.

From the 2025/2026 academic year, separate groups taught in foreign languages will be introduced at the master's level.

Structure and financial resources

A fund formed on the basis of sponsorship donations will be established within the university structure. This fund will be directed to the development of Tashkent State Medical University.
