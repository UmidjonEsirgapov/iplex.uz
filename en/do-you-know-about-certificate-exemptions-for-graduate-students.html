Preferences for persons with disabilities when entering a master's degree

Education facilities are being created for persons with disabilities in the Republic of Uzbekistan. In particular, a new procedure was introduced in which a foreign language certificate is not required for admission to the master's degree.

Who is it for?

This privilege is intended for persons with visual, hearing and speech disabilities. Such persons are exempted from presenting a foreign language certificate when enrolling in public higher education institutions:

  • Master's majors;
  • Post-higher education majors.

Note

This procedure is not for all persons with disabilities. Only persons belonging to the above three categories have the right to use this privilege.
