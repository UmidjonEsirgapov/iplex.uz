Dates and shifts of tests

It is known that in the 2024-2025 academic year, the tests for admission to higher and professional educational institutions will begin from July 14.

2 tests per day. organized in shifts:

  • 1st shift starts at 08:00
  • 2nd shift starts at 15:00

Applicants are required to arrive at the designated area at least one and a half hours before the start of the tests.

Permits and participation requirements

Registered online to participate in the tests Applicants are issued "Applicant Permit" via my.uzbmb.uz website.

As there are a few days left before the start of the tests, we ask applicants to download and print their "Applicant Permit".

Rules for participation in tests

  • Applicants participates in the test at the building at the address specified in the permit, on the date and shift indicated.
  • Applicants are not allowed to participate in the test in another area, day and shift.
  • Applicants must come to the test with passport (ID card) and applicant permit no later than the specified time.
  • Copy of passport or other documents Applicants who come with or late will not be admitted to the tests.
