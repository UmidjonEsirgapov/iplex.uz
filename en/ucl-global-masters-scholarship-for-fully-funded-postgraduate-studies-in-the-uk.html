General Information:

UCL Global Masters Scholarship: This scholarship is academically good for nationals of any developing country in the world. offers international students with outstanding qualifications to study at the University College.

Eligibility requirements:

Candidates can be from any country outside the UK but must meet all of the following criteria:

  • be eligible to pay the overseas tuition fee;
  • come from a lower income family background
  • must have applied for full-time Masters courses at University College London in 2022/23.

*Guidelines on how to determine UCL household income can be found on this important information page. Also see detailed admissions requirements here.

Benefits:

  • Up to 70 scholarships available in 2022/23.
  • The value of the scholarship is £15,000.
  • The scholarship is for one year only.
  • UCL Global Masters Scholarship Other Scholarships, Studentships, Awards or may be held alongside scholarships.
  • Grant recipients must notify the UCL Office of Student Funding immediately of any other funding awarded for a UCL Master's degree before or after the award of the UCL Global Masters Scholarship.
  • The grant is primarily applied to the recipient's tuition fees, with the remainder being paid to the recipient in three equal installments during the academic year

Important: The grant is awarded based on financial need and on the recommendation of the student finance department. The results will be announced at the end of June.
