Some young people will be given another 1 million soums for learning a language in addition to the cost of the exam!

Today's most relevant news in the field of education is this:

Summary:
According to the decision: source Decision content: In addition to compensating the costs of taking the exam, young people who have obtained a certificate of at least C1 or equivalent in English, German, French, Korean, Chinese and Japanese languages ​​under the international examination systems with the cost of taking the examination are covered. A one-time financial incentive system will be introduced in the amount of 3 times the BHM (1 million 20 thousand soums).
Additional relief: from September 1, 2024 to January 1, 2027, the points to be collected by young people in order to cover the costs of taking exams in the international examination systems for German, French, Korean, Chinese and Japanese languages will be introduced. the minimum indicator (of the certificate level) will be reduced by one point.
This decision will introduce an additional incentive system for young people to learn foreign languages and will greatly help them improve their language skills.
