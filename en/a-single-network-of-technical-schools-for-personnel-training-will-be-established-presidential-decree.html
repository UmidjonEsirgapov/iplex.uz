A single network of technical schools for personnel training will be established - Presidential Decree

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:

On October 16, 2024, the Presidential Decree "On measures to further improve the system of training qualified personnel in vocational education and introduce international education programs" (PF-158) was adopted. According to this decree, a single network of technical schools for personnel training will be established in Uzbekistan.

Current types of vocational education organizations will be accepted through technical schools and personnel training will be launched at all levels of professional education.

The main directions in technical schools:

  • Training It is organized in accordance with international standards and Uzbekistan's national qualification system.
  • Elementary, secondary and secondary special vocational education programs, as well as professional development and short-term training courses are organized.
  • Primary vocational education programs It is implemented on the basis of a state grant for students who have completed the 9th grade.
  • Secondary and secondary special vocational education programs are launched on the basis of a state grant and payment-contract.
  • Based on the modules and credits acquired by graduates of secondary special vocational education. it is allowed to continue studying at the appropriate stage of the bachelor's degree.

This new system is aimed at improving the quality of professional education and creating educational opportunities for the youth of Uzbekistan that meet modern, international requirements. — Source
