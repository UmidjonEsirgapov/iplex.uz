Admission to academic lyceums has been completed

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:

According to the final information, a total of 33,876 students have registered online for academic lyceums. 62% of them are boys, 38% are girls.

For information, this year a quota of total 14,534 admissions to lyceums has been set.

Note: According to the Presidential Decree (PQ-200, 03.07.2023), in the admission of students to academic lyceums the preliminary selection stage based on the written exam has been canceled. Admission is carried out through tests conducted by the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency.

Additional information on the exact dates of the tests will be provided.
