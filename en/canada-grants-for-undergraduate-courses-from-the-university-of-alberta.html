Brief about the university and the grant program:

University of Alberta (U of A or UAlberta) is a public research university located in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. It was founded in 1908. It ranks #131st in the ranking of world universities published by THE (Times Higher Education).

The university plans to provide the following grants for outstanding international students who intend to study at the bachelor's level:

  • Entrance Scholarships (each applicant will be considered directly) - This grant is available for undergraduate and graduate students;
  • International Student Scholarships: Value: $9,000 is available for students who have obtained a student visa to study one of the undergraduate courses.
  • University of Alberta Gold Standard Scholarship: Value: Awarded up to $6,000 based on the average admission value to the top 5% of students in each faculty;
  • University of Alberta Master’s Entrance Scholarship: Stipend: $17,500 for outstanding domestic and international students to study at one of the university’s master’s programs (an additional scholarship of $10,000 may be given to international students to help them pay their tuition fees);
