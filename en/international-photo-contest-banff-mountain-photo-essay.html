International Photo Contest - Banff mountain photo essay

Deadline: May 2, 2022.

Entries are now being accepted for the 2022 Banff Photo Essay Contest. The theme of the competition is the mountain. The goal of the competition is to show stories and stories about the mountains through a series of photographs. Photographers have the opportunity to showcase images of events related to mountain culture, adventure, nature, sports and the environment. A series of shots that convey a compelling story or message will be accepted - each shot must have a strong meaning and relate to other photos in the series.

Prize: $2,000.

Requirements:
-Essay must contain 3-5 images.
-Advice for photographers: only images relevant to the story in the essay. should be. Unnecessary photos can spoil the impression of the entire photo series. Photos must be taken in 2020-2022 (within the last 2 years).
Essays must be submitted by May 2, 2022.
Both professional photographers and amateur photographers over the age of 18 can participate in the contest.
