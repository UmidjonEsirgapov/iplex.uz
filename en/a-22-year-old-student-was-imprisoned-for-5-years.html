A 22-year-old student was imprisoned for 5 years

Brief content:
S.R., a 4th-year student of Tashkent State Technical University, born in 2001, sent an audio song (nashida) with fanatical ideas copied by "VK music bot" to an acquaintance through his Telegram application page.
January 30 at the open court session of the Almazor district court on criminal cases, the criminal case against S.R., who was charged with paragraph "g" of Article 244-1, part 3 of the Criminal Code, was considered. "The religious song distributed by S. R. is mixed with the idea of fanaticism, and it is indicated that it is prohibited to import, prepare and distribute such religious material on the territory of our country," the message says. He sent an audio song with his ideas to an acquaintance.

On January 30, at the open court session of the Almazor district court on criminal cases, the criminal case against S.R., who was charged with paragraph "g" of Article 244-1, part 3 of the Criminal Code, was considered.

"The Committee on Religious Affairs under the Cabinet of Ministers according to the conclusion of IP-1218 dated July 18, 2022, the religious song (nashida) distributed by S.R. with a religious content is based on the idea of fanaticism, and it is indicated that the importation, preparation and distribution of such religious material to the territory of our country is prohibited.

S. R. was imprisoned for 5 years. It is noted that the verdict of the court of first instance has not entered into legal force, and in case of dissatisfaction with this sentence, an appeal (protest) can be filed within the procedure and time limit established by the law. — Read on infoedu.uz
