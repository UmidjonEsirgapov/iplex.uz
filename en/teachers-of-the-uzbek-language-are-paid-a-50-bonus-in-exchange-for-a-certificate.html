Teachers of the Uzbek language are paid a 50% bonus in exchange for a certificate

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the decree of the President, starting from February 1, 2025, a new financial incentive will be introduced to the teachers of the Uzbek language (the state language) working in the preschool and general secondary schools of the Ministry of School Education.
Who has this bonus? will be?
Pedagogues teaching Uzbek language and literature; Those who received a certificate in the national assessment system of the Agency for the Assessment of Knowledge and Skills.

According to the presidential decree, from February 1, 2025, a new financial incentive is in effect for teachers of the Uzbek language (state language) working in general secondary schools of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education

Who will receive this award?

  • Pedagogues teaching Uzbek language and literature;

  • Those who have received a certificate in the national evaluation system of the Agency for the Assessment of Knowledge and Skills.

Congratulations to teachers who have fulfilled these conditions. a monthly bonus of 50 percent of the rate is paid.

What system does it work on?

  • The bonus is given every month;

  • The activity must be within the school;

  • The certificate is required to be obtained based on the national evaluation system.

Why is it important?

This decision was made in order to improve the qualifications of Uzbek language teachers, stimulate the certification system and further improve the quality of education. It is intended to create decent incentive for teachers. — Source
