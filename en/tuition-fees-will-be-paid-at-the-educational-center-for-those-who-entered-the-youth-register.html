Tuition fees will be paid at the educational center for those who entered the "Youth Register".

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In this, 1 BHM - 330 thousand soms will be paid every month for up to 6 months to study science, sports, and culture.
In addition, up to 6 months, up to 4 BHM - 1.3 million soums will be allocated for young people to acquire modern professions, learn information technologies, and teach them foreign languages. 1 BHM - 330 thousand soums will be paid. In this case, the recommendation of the youth leader of the neighborhood is necessary.
