Interest-free loans are given to girls

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the President No. PQ-323 dated July 18, 2022, from 2022, preferential interest-free education loans will be given to women studying under a payment contract.
Women only pay the main part of the education loan.
The main part of the education loan is repaid for 7 years starting from the seventh month (i.e. six months) after the end of the student's official study period.

President's decree of July 18, 2022 According to the decision No. PQ-323, from 2022, preferential interest-free educational loans will be given to women studying under a payment contract. to students of educational institutions (technical school, vocational college, etc.).

Which form of education is given?

  • Daytime;
  • External;
  • Evening.

Not given to distance education ❌

14% are given So, why is it called interest-free?

True, the education loan is actually given at a rate of 14%. But women do not pay this percentage, the state pays. More specifically, it is covered by the Education Loan Financing Fund under the Ministry of Finance. Women only repay the principal portion of the student loan.

When does repayment begin?

The principal portion of the student loan is repaid over a period of 7 years, beginning in the seventh month (i.e. six months) after the end of the student's formal study period. For example, if an education loan of 7 million soums is taken, after graduation, 83 thousand soums will be paid monthly for 7 years after 6 months.

What documents are needed?

  • application to the bank;
  • identity document;
  • with the educational institution. payment-contract agreement;
  • a document on the provision of education loan repayment for family members with a permanent source of income and other third parties as joint borrowers.

No collateral and guarantees are required for education loans allocated to women of families included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection".
