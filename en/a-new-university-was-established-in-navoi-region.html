A new university was established in Navoi region

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The presidential decree "On the establishment of Navoi State University of Mining and Technologies" (PQ-186, 31.03.2022)
was adopted.
By the decision, Navoi State Mining Institute Navoi State Mining and Technology was reorganized as a university of technologies.

The Presidential Decree "On the establishment of the Navoi State University of Mining and Technologies" (No. PQ-186, 31.03.2022) was adopted.

By the decision, the Navoi State Mining Institute was reorganized into the Navoi State University of Mining and Technologies.

In the university, the fields of industry, technology, engineering highly educated specialists and scientific-pedagogical personnel are prepared.

According to the decision, the following were established at the University:

  • Nukus Mining Institute;
  • Navoiy scientific-technological industrial park;

In the 2022/2023 academic year:

  • Karmana Polytechnic Technical College;
  • Ghozgon Polytechnic Technical College.

Training of personnel in the fields of industry, mining, metallurgy, mechanical engineering, construction, energy, electrical engineering, and service is underway at the technical schools of the university.

University senior students will be hired through a paid system, including steps will be taken to introduce a payment system for internships.

From the 2023/2024 academic year, the university will start training personnel based on joint educational programs in the field of technology. — More information
