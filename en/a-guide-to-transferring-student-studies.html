A guide to transferring student studies

On June 13, the Ministry of Higher and Special Secondary Education announced that the transfer of students will be done online, through the transfer.edu.uz site. Applications are accepted from July 15 to August 5.

It is recommended to prepare the necessary documents before applying: copy of passport, academic reference or rating book or transcript in electronic format jpg, jpeg, png, pdf is required. In addition, it is possible to upload a copy of the additional supporting documents.

Oliygoh.uz provides a guide on transferring studies to another HEI or faculty.

Start registration

To start the application process, the user enters transfer.edu.uz and clicks the "Register" button.

Phone number, email address and secret password are set on the registration page that appears. The «Send» button is pressed.

Then, a special confirmation SMS code will be sent to the indicated phone number, which must be entered within five minutes.

The registration process begins in the "Application submission" section. In the "Target" section, select "Move Reading". In the citizenship section, the user selects the desired country, for example, "Citizen of the Republic of Uzbekistan" was selected in the photo. Information is filled based on the passport of a citizen of the Republic of Uzbekistan: series, number and JSHSHIR.

JSHSHIR number sample. Photo: Uzimei.uz

Second stage. Personal data

Based on the entered passport data, the system automatically determines the user's last name, first name, first name, gender and date of birth.

Enter the country, region, district and address where the user lives.

Third stage. Educational information

Sample of filling out information about education.

At this stage, the user enters information about the educational institution where he is currently studying: the country where the institution is located, name, form of education, type of education, year of study, educational direction, course. Click the "Next" button to go to the next step.

Fourth step. Transfer of study

At the last stage, the educational institution to which the transfer is intended is indicated.

The educational institution to which the transfer is intended must also correspond to the direction and specialization of the HEI currently being studied.

Note for those who plan to transfer studies: in which directions it is possible to transfer and in which ones it is not possible.

The target educational institution is selected in the "Educational institution" section, the sample in the photo is Tashkent University of Information Technologies. The form of education, type of education, direction (faculty, example - computer engineering) is selected. Then, the reason for the transfer of studies is indicated.

Moving only the faculty within one educational institution.

In order to transfer only the faculty within one institution, the same higher education institution and the faculty to be transferred are indicated in the "Education information" and "Study transfer" sections.

Submission of documents

After entering the data, the necessary documents must be submitted: passport in electronic format jpg, jpeg, png, pdf copy, academic reference or rating book or transcript is required. In addition, it is possible to upload a copy of the additional documents that are the basis for the transfer, based on which the transfer committee can make a decision. id="67933" src="https://www.gazeta.uz/media/img/2020/07/EXiJTU15956109556718_b.jpg"/>

When the files are uploaded, a check mark is placed in the "I have familiarized myself with this transfer procedure" item.

Registration completed. The application is located in the "My application" section. If the user discovers an error in the application or wants to start from the beginning, it can be done in the "Edit Application" section.

NOTE: Applications are accepted until the fifth of August. From August 5th to August 30th, applications will be considered and citizens will be sent a response letter.
