The payment contract money of students up to 16.5 million soums will be paid

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Students included in the youth register will be paid the contract money of up to 16.5 million soums according to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 312 (7.06.2022), paragraph 35 and Appendix 8 of this regulation, the following categories: A child of the family included in the Iron Notebook; Children of women included in the women's register; A child of a family enrolled in the Unified Register of Social Protection, who is studying at a professional or higher education institution; A student brought up in an orphanage or children's town; Low-income student-student deprived of parental care, placed under guardianship; A child of a low-income family with at least one of the family members having a group I or both group II disabilities, who is studying at a professional or higher education institution; A student with a disability or health impairment; It is intended to be paid to a student whose family has a low income. ➡️ part of the annual expenses for studying on the basis of a fee-contract will be paid to those who are studying in the full-time course of the master's degree.
Payment of these expenses is carried out according to the scheme in the amount of up to 50% of the fee-contract, but not more than 50 times of the BHM (16,500,000 soums) based on the positive recommendation of the youth leader.
