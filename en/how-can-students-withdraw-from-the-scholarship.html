How can students withdraw from the scholarship?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Today, the amount of scholarships for students of higher education institutions of Uzbekistan is set at 517,880 soums.
This amount is given to all students studying on a state grant or paying a scholarship contract. if he falls into the following state of mastery, he may be separated from the scholarship: Mastery level: mastery of 30% of subjects and less (that is, getting grade "3" in 30% of the final examinations). This amount is given to all students studying on a state grant or paying a scholarship contract. However, there are cases of separation from the scholarship and it is important to be aware of them.

Possibility of receiving an additional scholarship

If a student receives only excellent (i.e. "5") grades in all subjects during the semester and shows a result of 86 points or higher in the final examination, an additional payment of 20% will be made to the scholarship for 6 months. As a result, the student can receive a scholarship of 621,456 soums.

Reasons for withdrawal from the scholarship

If the student falls into the following situation regarding mastering subjects during the semester, he can be separated from the scholarship:

  • Level of mastering: Mastering 30% of subjects and less (i.e. final examinations getting grade "3" in 30%).
    • For example, if 6 subjects are taken during one semester, getting a grade of "3" in at least 2 of them will cause the student to lose the scholarship.

Conclusion

In order not to be separated from the scholarship, the student should try to pass the final exams with the highest possible grades. Good performance allows not only to keep the scholarship, but also to get an additional scholarship. — Read on infoedu.uz
