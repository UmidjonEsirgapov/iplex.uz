Can a student study and work at the same time?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Legal foundations Regulation on Higher Education (Article 50): Students studying in the form of full-time education are allowed to work in higher education institutions or other organizations on the basis of an employment contract during their free time (in the afternoon).

Yes, a student can work in paid jobs based on an employment contract. there is no requirement or prohibition. — Details here

Legal foundations

  • Regulation on higher education (paragraph 50): Students studying in the form of full-time education are allowed to work in higher education institutions or other organizations based on an employment contract during their free time (in the afternoon). (Source)
  • Labor Code (Article 248): The employer must create the necessary conditions for employees studying in educational institutions to carry out work together with education.

Can students teach at school?

  • Government Decision No. 646 (15.10.2021): 3rd-year students studying in the fields of pedagogy in full-time, evening and part-time departments have the right to teach in schools. (Source)
  • Class hours: Students may work in schools during their free time, but class hours must not interfere with the student's main learning process.

Conditions according to legal requirements

  • Employers must provide flexible working conditions for students to combine education and work.
  • Higher cooperation is established between educational institutions and employers, prioritizing the student's study process. Pedagogical students are given the opportunity to teach in schools starting from the 3rd year. This helps students to strengthen their theoretical knowledge with practice.
