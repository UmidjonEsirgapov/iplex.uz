From September 1, tuition fees for a language certificate will be reimbursed

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
These changes are intended for English, German, French, Korean, Chinese and Japanese languages, and the minimum score obtained from them will also be reduced. changes and additions have been made.
Starting from September 1, young people who have obtained at least C1 and equivalent level from the international examination systems will be reimbursed for up to 6 months of study expenses in an amount not exceeding 3 times the amount of the basic calculation. From September 1, tuition fees for obtaining a language certificate will be reimbursed. These changes are intended for English, German, French, Korean, Chinese and Japanese languages, and the minimum score obtained from them will also be reduced.

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, due to the improvement of the system of training young people in foreign languages, changes and additions were made to some decisions of the government. Starting from September 1, young people who have obtained at least C1 and its equivalent level from international examination systems will be reimbursed in an amount not exceeding 3 times of the basic calculation amount for up to 6 months of study expenses. one-time fees are allocated.

Compensable languages and exam systems:

  • English: IELTS
  • German: Test DAF, Goethe-Zertifikat
  • French: DELF/DALF, TCF
  • Korean: TOPIK I-II
  • Chinese language: HSK
  • Japanese language: JLPT

From September 1 of this year to 2027, the minimum number of points required to cover the cost of taking the exam for these languages will be reduced by one point.

Compensation conditions:

  • Applications are accepted through the State Service Centers and the Unified Portal.
  • At least C1 and equivalent level international certificate must be applied within 180 calendar days from the date of receiving it.
  • The validity and correctness of the exam result indicated in the certificate must be confirmed by the Youth Affairs Agency.
  • It is necessary to attach a bilateral contract between the student and the educational center.

If the application is on behalf of the youth under the age of 16, a birth certificate is attached to the application submitted by the parents.

In addition to the application requesting compensation for the exam, it is also possible to apply for the cost of education. However, individual applications for tuition reimbursement will not be considered. — Details here
