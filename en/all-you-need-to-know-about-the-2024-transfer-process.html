All you need to know about the 2024 transfer process

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Processes of transfer of study (perevod) in higher education institutions began on July 15.
Applications for transfer of studies of applicants and students will continue until August 5.
This process is the following two platforms It is carried out through: transfer.edu.uz - to transfer from one state higher education institution to another. Applicants and students' applications for transfer of studies will continue until August 5. This process is carried out through the following two platforms:
  1. transfer.edu.uz — for transfer from one state HEI to another.
  2. my.uzbmb.uz — for transfer from foreign and non-state HEIs operating in foreign countries or in the republic to state HEIs.

Permitted categories for transferring studies:

  1. If a student gets married, he can be transferred to the higher education institution of his spouse's place of residence.
  2. When a civil servant changes his place of work, his child or spouse can transfer his studies with a recommendation letter from the relevant supervisor.

For foreign and private higher education institution students:

Uzbekistan students of foreign and private HEIs will also have to take the exam to transfer to the state HEI.
