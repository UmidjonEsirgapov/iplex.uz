Here, a year of preparation, study and tests are over. And now, one of the most exciting periods in the life of applicants - DTM mandate results are only 2 days away. These days are usually spent with anticipation, excitement and various questions: "Will I pass?", "Where was I accepted?", "What if not?"

We will tell you about psychological preparation, plan of next steps and backup options before the mandate is announced.

1. Mental Preparedness - The Key to Self-Control

Regardless of the outcome when the mandate comes, stay positive. Stress and anxiety lead to making bad decisions.

  • Give yourself rest, sleep well.
  • Evaluate the situation coolly when the result comes out.
  • Remember: this is not the only chance in your life.

2. If the result is good - your steps should be prepared

If you have transferred to the OTM of your dreams:

  • Prepare your documents in advance (certificate/diploma, passport, photo).
  • Find out the working hours of the admissions committee.
  • If there is a contract, pay on time make a plan to implement.
