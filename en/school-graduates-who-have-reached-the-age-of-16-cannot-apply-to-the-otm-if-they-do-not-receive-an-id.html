School graduates who have reached the age of 16 cannot apply to the OTM if they do not receive an ID card

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
School graduates who have reached the age of 16 should receive an ID card on time - MMTV According to the Ministry of Preschool and School Education, in order for applicants to enter the OTM and submit relevant documents online, it is necessary to enter the ID card information of graduates into the ABITUR electronic program.
"Your child's using your constitutional right, we ask you to ensure that your children who turn 16 receive an ID card on time, and we ask the school administration to enter the ID card information of the graduates at e.shahodadnoma.uz/ and provide them with the necessary assistance in submitting their documents to the OTM in the future.
