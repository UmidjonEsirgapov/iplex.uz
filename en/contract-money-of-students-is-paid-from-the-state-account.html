Students' contract money is paid at the expense of the state: Privilege and application procedure

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, children of families included in the "Iron Register" and youth included in the "Youth Register" in any higher education institution of Uzbekistan, including state, private and foreign HEIs, technical schools and colleges. if he is studying, he can use the privilege under the contract. This contract is paid at the expense of the "Yoshlar daftari" fund.

The fund is managed by the Trustee's Council headed by the mayor in each district (city). Applications are submitted to the district (city) youth affairs department, and a copy of the contract issued by the higher education institution must be attached to the application. There is no fixed deadline for applying, so you can apply at any time.

Before applying, consult and inform the local youth leader, because after submitting the application, your application will be sent to the youth leader within 3 working days. The youth leader will issue a recommendation or denial of contract coverage within 5 business days. If the recommendation is refused, this decision must be justified.

If the youth leader does not consider the application within 5 working days or rejects it without reason, the Board of Trustees may transfer contract money on its own initiative. After the council makes a decision, within 3 working days, the money will be transferred to the account of the higher education institution. The application must be considered within 11 business days in general.
