311 thousand quotas for technical schools: 205 thousand places were allocated on the basis of grants

Brief content:
By order of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, the admission parameters for technical schools in the 2025/2026 academic year have been officially approved.
General admission quotas: On the basis of a state grant: 205,729 students On the basis of a fee-contract: 105,280 students. Also, special attention was paid to 14 technical schools where international education programs were introduced - they were set to admit 1,680 students on the basis of a state grant.

Higher by the order of the Minister of Education, Science and Innovation, the admission parameters for technical schools in the 2025/2026 academic year were officially approved. According to it, a total of 311,009 applicants have been admitted to 599 technical schools. Admission is carried out in full-time, evening, part-time and dual education, in 370 professions and specialties.

General admission quotas:

  • On the basis of a state grant: 205,729 people

  • On the basis of payment-contract: 105,280 people

Also, special attention was paid to 14 technical schools where international education programs were introduced - they were set to receive 1,680 students based on a state grant.

Admission classes section:

For 9th grade graduates:

  • 515 technical schools

  • Total 139,245 quotas, on the basis of a full state grant

For 11th grade graduates:

  • 549 technical schools

  • A total of 171,764 quotas, of which 66,484 are based on a state grant
