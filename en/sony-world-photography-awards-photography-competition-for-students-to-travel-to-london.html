Sony Photography Awards was established to encourage students studying photography. The task of the contestants is to submit a photo or a series of photos associated with a small text on a certain topic. Photos can be taken in any style, on any device, as long as they are related to the theme and the main message.

10 winning participants will be able to participate in an exhibition in London.

Benefits:

  • 10 winners will participate in an exhibition in London and all expenses will be covered by the organization;
  • The works of the winners will be displayed at the exhibition.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must be between 18-30 years old and be studying photography;
  • The presented works must have been photographed between June 1 and November 30, 2021. necessary;

In order to submit documents, it is recommended to familiarize yourself with listed educational institutions first. If not in the list, online registration is required.

To participate in this competition, you need to open a personal profile. You can learn more about how to apply here.

For further applications: panagiotis@worldphoto.org
