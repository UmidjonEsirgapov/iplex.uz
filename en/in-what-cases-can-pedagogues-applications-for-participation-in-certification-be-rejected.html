In what cases can pedagogues' applications to participate in the certification be rejected?
According to paragraph 26 of the relevant decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan (No. 572 of 17.09.2021), in the following cases, the public service center or the provision of public services may be refused by the competent ministries: if the questionnaire is not confirmed by the pedagogic staff with an electronic digital signature or other means of signature identification or by SMS-notification; It became known that false information or forged documents were submitted by teaching staff; Incomplete submission of questionnaire and attached documents; Non-payment of the fee set for the provision of public service for attestation (with the exception of cases of mandatory attestation of pedagogues). Is it possible?

According to paragraph 26 of the relevant Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan (No. 572 of 17.09.2021), the provision of public services by the center of public services or authorized ministries may be refused in the following cases:

  • Educational personnel use digital signatures of questionnaires or other means of signature identification. Not confirmed by SMS-notification;
  • It became known that incorrect information or forged documents were submitted by the pedagogue personnel;
  • Incomplete submission of the questionnaire and the documents attached to it;
  • The fee set for the provision of the state service for attestation unpaid (with the exception of the cases of the next mandatory certification of the teaching staff).

It is not allowed to refuse the provision of state services on other grounds than those specified in this clause. These conditions are important for successful certification of teaching staff. — Source
