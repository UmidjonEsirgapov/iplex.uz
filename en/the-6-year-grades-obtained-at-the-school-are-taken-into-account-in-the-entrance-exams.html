The 6-year grades obtained at the school are taken into account in the entrance exams

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
On November 6, 2020, the Presidential Decree No. PF-6108 "On measures to develop the fields of education and science in the period of new development of Uzbekistan" was adopted.
According to the decree, Starting from the 2020-2023 academic year, the average score of the last 6 years of compulsory 11-year education will be included in the certificate (diploma appendix) of graduates of general secondary, secondary special educational institutions and vocational schools, and this indicator will be taken into account in the entrance exams to higher education institutions.
General secondary, secondary special education The procedure for taking into account the grades of graduates of institutions and vocational schools in the entrance exams to higher education organizations will be developed by the State Test Center together with the State Inspection of Quality of Education, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education and the Ministry of Public Education by June 1, 2021, and will be submitted to the Cabinet of Ministers.
