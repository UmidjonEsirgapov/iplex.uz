On January 8, by the Government's decision, the regulation on the organization of paid internships for students of higher education organizations at enterprises of economic sectors was approved.

According to it, the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation conducts a competition among students at least once a year in order to organize a paid production internship.

500 places are allocated for internships (45 for the city of Tashkent, 35 for the Republic of Karakalpakstan and the regions). himself, by mail or electronically, to the higher education institution where he is studying.

To participate in the competition, a student must:

● be studying at the graduate level;

● the field of study (specialty) must correspond to the field of activity in the network enterprise;

● be a resident of Uzbekistan.

The higher education organization submits the information about the students who applied to the Special Commission with a referral letter within 5 working days.

The Special Commission will review these documents within 5 working days from the date of receipt and make a decision on whether the student will participate in the competition or reject it.

In 10 working days after the final decision, information on the results of the competition will be sent to the Ministry's official website.

Internship is carried out within the period from 1 month to 3 hours in the period specified in the curriculum.

The trial period for the student's internship is not set.

Expenses for the internship of the winning students will be reimbursed by the Special Commission within 15 days after the final decision is made. the tax is transferred to the appropriate account number.

The regulation does not apply to military and militarized higher education organizations. — Source
