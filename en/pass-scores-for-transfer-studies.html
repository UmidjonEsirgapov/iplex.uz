Transfer of studies from abroad and non-state HEIs to state HEIs: Passing points

For students studying in foreign or non-state higher education institutions to state HEIs the transfer process is carried out annually on the basis of tests in August. The passing points for these test processes are determined separately for each field of study.

How are the transfer tests conducted?

  • Tests are held only in August.

  • Students take exams in subjects appropriate to their field.

  • Minimum passing points are set for each field of study.

  • Transferring studies is allowed only to HEIs approved by the state.
