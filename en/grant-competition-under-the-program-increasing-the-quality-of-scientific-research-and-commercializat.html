Grant competition under the program "Increasing the quality of scientific research and commercialization" with the participation of the World Bank

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In the framework of the 1st component of the project "Modernization of the National Innovation System of Uzbekistan" "Increasing the quality of scientific research and commercialization", the Ministry of Innovative Development of the Republic of Uzbekistan announces that in October of this year a competition for scientific research grants will be announced under the program "Increasing the quality of scientific research" and the scientific community is preparing for this competition. invites you to participate in it.
The program for improving the quality of scientific research The "Program for improving the quality of scientific research" directed to finance research projects submitted by researchers, approves joint research proposals of local and international researchers (including Uzbek scientists abroad), proposals of young researchers aimed at further enriching the country's future research potential. The selection is made according to two main criteria, including: (i) in terms of scientific achievements proposals and (ii) proposals that are of social or economic importance and serve to solve some social problems.
The program for improving the quality of scientific research provides two types of grants: grants for young scientists (Junior research grant) and grants for senior researchers (Senior research grant). within the framework of the Ministry of Innovative Development of the Republic of Uzbekistan announces the announcement of a competition for scientific research grants under the program "Increasing the quality of scientific research" in October of this year and asks the scientific community to prepare for this competition and participate in it. scientists) approves joint research proposals, proposals of young researchers aimed at further enriching the future research potential of the country. The selection is made according to two main criteria, which include: (i) proposals given in terms of scientific achievements and (ii) proposals that are of social or economic importance and serve to solve certain social problems. grants (Junior research grant) and grants for senior research workers (Senior research grant). Grants are allocated in the equivalent of $200,000 and $600,000 , respectively, for a period not exceeding 24 months for each research group.

An additional period may be granted to joint international projects. The program may also include the preliminary research necessary to apply for international research funds that meet the country's requirements.
