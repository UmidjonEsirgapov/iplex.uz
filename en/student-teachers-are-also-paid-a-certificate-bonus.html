Students who work as teachers are also paid a certificate bonus

Student-teachers who have received a national certificate are also paid a certificate bonus in the same way as other teachers. Because they are students, no special rules apply to them.

So, in addition to applicants, the certificate can also benefit students studying in ped fields.
