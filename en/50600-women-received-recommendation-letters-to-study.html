50,600 women received recommendation letters to study

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The Family and Women's Committee announced whether or not to give recommendations to the phones of women who applied for 2,000 grant positions and 500 contract quota. This year, 38,700 needy women received recommendations
They will work for the 2,000 grant quota.
Those who did not get the required points for this grant will participate in the general competition along with everyone else.

The Family and Women's Committee of women who applied for recommendations for 2,000 grant places and 500 contracts quota said that a message was sent to her phone about whether or not to issue a recommendation letter

This year 38,700 women in need received a recommendation letter. They work for 2 thousand grant quota. All those who did not get the required points for this grant will participate in the general competition.

For the additional 500 contract quota allocated for 5-year trainees, 11,900 people received recommendations.

Currently, the recommendation information is being entered into the electronic database.
