Admission to master's degree in the 2021/2022 academic year is carried out in two stages

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The President signed the decision "On the parameters of the state order for admission to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan in the 2021/2022 academic year." Admission to the master's (clinical residency) specialties of higher educational institutions is carried out in two stages.
In this, in the first stage, entrance exams in subjects suitable for the specialty chosen by the state higher education institution, and in the second stage, foreign language tests are conducted by the State Testing Center; applicants with an appropriate national or international certificate in a foreign language for admission to a master's degree (clinical residency) are exempted from passing a foreign language test at the second stage and are given the maximum score in this subject; the results of examinations in subjects suitable for the specialty of magistracy (clinical residency) are submitted by the state higher education institution to the State Test Center through the interdepartmental electronic information exchange system within three days after the examination and are published on the official website of this institution; Information about the applicant's foreign language test scores will be published on the official website of the State Test Center the day after the tests, as well as the results of the master's degree (clinical residency) selection within a week after the tests have been completed.

President signed the decision "On parameters of the state order for admission to educational institutions".

Accordingly, in the 2021/2022 academic year: — Official Data

  • admission to master's degree (clinical residency) specialties of state higher education institutions will be carried out in two stages. In this case, in the first stage, entrance exams in subjects suitable for the specialty selected by the state higher education institution, and in the second stage, tests in foreign languages are held by the State Testing Center;
  • applicants with a national or international certificate of the appropriate level in foreign languages for admission to the master's degree (clinical residency) are exempted from passing the test in a foreign language in the second stage, and they are given the maximum score in this subject;
  • results of examinations in subjects suitable for the master's degree (clinical residency) within three days after the examination are submitted by the state higher education institution to the State Test Center through the interdepartmental electronic information exchange system and published on the official website of this institution; The results of the selection for the admission to the residency program will be published on the official website of the State Testing Center within a week after the tests have been completed.
