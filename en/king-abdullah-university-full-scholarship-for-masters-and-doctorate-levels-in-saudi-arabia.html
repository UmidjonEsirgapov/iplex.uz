General Information:

One of the most prestigious universities in Saudi Arabia "King Abdullah University of Science and Technology" has announced fully funded grant programs for master's and doctoral degrees.

requirements and necessary documents:

  • IELTS at least 6.5 or TOEFL 79 (language certificate is not required if the candidate studied at the undergraduate level in one of the countries such as America, Canada, Great Britain, Ireland, Australia and New Zealand);
  • University grades;
  • CV/Resume;
  • Indicating the reason for studying at this university statement of purpose (Statement of purpose);
  • 3 letters of recommendation;
  • GRE certificate (not required, but may increase the chance of winning a grant);

Benefits of this grant: All accepted students receive KAUST scholarship benefits. KAUST scholarship benefits include:

  • Full tuition fees
  • A monthly living stipend will be provided (between $20,000-$30,000 annually, depending on the destination);
  • Housing will be provided;
  • Medical and dental care expenses will be covered;
