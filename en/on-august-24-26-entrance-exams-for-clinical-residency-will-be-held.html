Clinical residency exams will start tomorrow: Addresses and locations

On August 24-26, clinical residency exams will be held for specialized medical institutions, higher medical educational institutions and private clinics authorized to train clinical residents.

According to the Ministry of Health, Entrance to tests starts at 7:00. Candidates must arrive at the exam locations at least 1 hour before the start of the exam with a permit and an original copy of their identity document downloaded from the tmbm.ssv.uz website.

We remind you that there is a way to hold an additional selection for applicants who did not apply for the entrance exams, who were late or did not come to the exam.

Exams will be held at the following addresses:

For the Republic of Karakalpakstan:

The building of the Regional Department of the Center for the Evaluation of the Qualifications of Medical and Pharmaceutical Employees of the Republic of Karakalpakstan.

The Republic of Karakalpakstan, Nukus, Amir Temur Street, 185 (formerly M. Gorky Street).
