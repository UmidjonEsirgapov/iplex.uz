Which certificate will give you a percentage discount? Full list

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Certificate bonuses for English language teachers have been approved. According to the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 134, it is established to pay an additional salary bonus to teachers of foreign languages who have national or international certificates.
How many percent for which certificate below? a premium is indicated.
Certificate premiums National certificates Foreign language proficiency test (Multi-level) – 20% premium International certificates (50% premium is paid) IELTS – 50% TOEFL iBT/ITP – 50% TKT + B2 certificate – 50% TESOL/TESL/TEFL + B2 certificate – 50% CEQS – 50% Certificates obtained before August 1, 2023, validly registered Linguaskill - 50% Aptis - 50% iTEP - 50% CELTA/DELTA - 50% Important notes The TOEFL Home Edition certificate does not give the right to receive a bonus. According to Decree No. 134, it is established to pay foreign language teachers an additional monthly salary if they have national or international certificates. Below is shown how much percentage bonus is paid for which certificate.

Amount of bonus on the certificate

National certificates

  • Foreign language proficiency test (Multi-level) - 20% bonus

International certificates (50% bonus paid)

  • IELTS – 50%
  • TOEFL iBT/ITP – 50%
  • TKT + B2 certificate – 50%
  • TESOL/TESL/TEFL + B2 certificate – 50%
  • CEQS – 50%

Acquired before August 1, 2023, valid registered certificates — Source

  • Linguaskill – 50%
  • Aptis – 50%
  • iTEP – 50%
  • CELTA/DELTA – 50%

Important notes

  • TOEFL Home Edition certificate does not give the right to receive a discount.
  • TESOL/TESL/TEFL certificates will be considered only if they meet the requirements of the Ministry.
  • If the teacher has more than one certificate at the same time, he will receive an additional fee only for the certificate with the highest percentage.
