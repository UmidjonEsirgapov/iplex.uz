Types of grants to be selected when applying

1️⃣ Full or partial grant

Students with a GPA (average rating score) higher than 3.5 can apply for this grant.

2️⃣ Additional state grant

Only "red" or "yellow" in the unified register of social protection category students and those with a GPA above 4.0.

3️⃣ OTM (higher education institution) grant

Students with a GPA between 3.3 and 4.0 can apply for the OTM grant.

The following cannot apply:

  • Those enrolled on the basis of a preferential or targeted grant

  • Those who study in the form of part-time, evening and distance education
