Applications are accepted from December 20 to January 20 (also on the same day).

Not everyone can apply for the winter "perevod". Only those with 2 good reasons are allowed.

The following are good reasons:

1. in order to move the student's studies to the higher education institution in the area where the spouse permanently lives due to starting a family;

2. employees of state bodies to transfer the education of their spouse or minor child in case of a change of workplace (based on the recommendation of the head of the relevant ministry (department)).

The following documents are submitted to the regional working groups:

➖ application for transferring education (the application form is issued by the regional working group and filled out by the student on the spot);
➖ copies of the student's rating book or academic reference or transcript in the prescribed form (one is enough);
➖ a copy of the passport;
➖ the relevant documents justifying the reasons mentioned above.

Applications for the restoration of studies (except for relocation) are submitted directly to the educational institution where the student studied.
