Women's contract money will be paid

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the relevant decision of the Cabinet of Ministers (No. 447 dated 15.08.2022), the contract fees of all women studying at the master's level will be reimbursed without the condition of return. apply to the rector of the higher education institution where they are studying.
Applicants' applications are collected and sent to the ministry to which the higher education institution belongs.

According to the relevant decision of the Cabinet of Ministers (No. 447 dated 15.08.2022), the contract fees of all women studying at the master's level are non-refundable

Applicants apply before September 15 of each year to the rector of the higher education institution they are studying.

The order of the rector (branch director) of the relevant higher education institution will be accepted by October 15. — Source
