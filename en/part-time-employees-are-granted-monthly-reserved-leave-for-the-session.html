Part-time employees are granted monthly reserved leave for the session

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Employees studying in evening or part-time education in higher education institutions, technical schools or colleges are given educational leave for participation in laboratory-examination sessions in the following periods with the average salary preserved: Studying in evening education in higher education institutions 1-2 level students - at least 20 days, at technical schools or colleges - at least 10 days, and those studying part-time in higher education institutions and technical schools or colleges - at least 30 days; At least 30 days for those studying in the 3rd-4th stages of evening education in higher education institutions or higher, at least 20 days in technical schools or colleges, and at least 40 calendar days for those studying part-time in higher educational institutions, technical schools or colleges. employees are given educational holidays for participating in laboratory-examination sessions in the following periods with the average salary preserved:
