The scholarship competition of the Ministry of Digital Technologies will be held

It can be attended by students of higher education institutions of the ministry system and students of TATU, its branches, lyceums and technical schools.

Photo: Yevgeniy Sorochin / Spot

This year, the contest "Scholarship of the Ministry of Digital Technologies" will be held. The press service of the ministry informed about it.

The total fund of the competition is equal to 717 million soums, in which students who have completed the 1-2-3 stages of undergraduate education of the following HEIs in the ministry system will be able to participate:

  • Inha University in Tashkent;
  • Amity University in Tashkent;
  • IT-Park University digital university.

Also, students and pupils of the Tashkent University of Information Technologies named after Muhammad al-Khorazmi, its branches, lyceums, technical schools can also participate.

The ministerial scholarship quota is distributed among educational institutions. From 1.5 million soums, 600,000 soums per month will be given to students in the amount of 2 times the BHM for 10 months.

  • the level of knowledge of foreign languages (B1,B2,C1);
  • the presence of an international IT certificate;
  • participation in hackathon or IT-competitions;
  • the presence of scientific, creative and innovative work;
  • the mastery level (GPA).

    • The two semester GPA must be 76% or higher.
