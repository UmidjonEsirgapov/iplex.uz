Amendments have been made to the government's decision "On the introduction of the national test system for assessing the level of knowledge in general education subjects".

According to it, the registration of applicants for national certificate exams in general education subjects is carried out through the Unified interactive state services portal (my.gov.uz site).

90 percent of the prescribed fee is collected from the applicant because the registration for participation in the national certificate exam is done through the my.gov.uz site.

To persons registered in the information system of the "Unified Register of Social Protection", Also, a 50 percent discount is applied to persons with disabilities of groups I and II.

The test results are calculated based on the international methodology - Rash model. According to it:

A+ grade for a student with 70 points;

A grade for a student with 65-69.9 points;

B+ grade for a 60-64.9 grade student;

B grade for a 55-59.9 grade student;

B grade for a 50-54.9 grade student

C+ level;

An applicant who has obtained 46 - 49.9 points - C level.

An applicant who has received an A+, A level certificate will be given the maximum score in this subject in the entrance tests for the bachelor's degree of higher education institutions.

An applicant who has received a B+, B, C+, C level certificate will be given proportional points relative to the maximum score. the highest score is awarded. — Official Data
