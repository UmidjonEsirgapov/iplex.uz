Change in the procedure of payment of bonuses to teachers: the problems were solved by the President's decision

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to Khalqtaliminfo.uz journalist Ulugbek Polatov, in some districts of Fergana region, the payment of overtime paid to teachers from the director's fund in June-August was considered a financial mistake, and these funds were returned from the teachers.
This situation was intended to be implemented contrary to Article 269 of the Labor Code.
However, with the intervention of the Ministry of Pre-School and School Education, this problem found a positive solution. payment of bonuses paid to teachers from the headmaster's fund in June-August was found to be a financial mistake, and efforts were made to recover these funds from teachers. This situation was intended to be implemented in violation of Article 269 of the Labor Code.

However, with the intervention of the Preschool and School Education Ministry, this problem found a positive solution. Nevertheless, similar cases were observed in the city of Shahrisabz, Kashkadarya region.

One of the reasons for the origin of this problem is due to the fact that according to Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 823 of September 30, 2019, premiums should be paid only during the academic year. The academic year lasts from September 2 to May 25, so the auditors concluded that the bonus should not be paid in June-August.

However, with the 3rd annex of the President's Decision PQ-346, this issue was resolved, and the bonuses should be paid from September 1 to August 31 payment procedure has been introduced. — Details here
