Rotary Yoneyama is a program designed for international students who want to apply to a university in Japan.

The grant supports talented international students studying or doing research at universities in Japan.

The amount of the program is 15.

Useful parties:

  • Undergraduate students - 100,000 yen;
  • For master and doctoral students - 140,000 yen;
  • Economy class air tickets to Japan (not more than 400,000 yen) are provided.

Requirements:

  • Must not be Japanese citizens;
  • Candidate must be born after April 1, 1976 (not older than 45 years old);
  • Must be proficient in Japanese (JPLT N4 test);
  • Applicants who have received the Rotary Yoneyama Scholarship or any other scholarship are welcome to apply.
  • Fellows must have a desire to learn, a clear goal for the future, respect for cultural differences, the ability to express themselves and communicate with others, good mental and physical health.

Documents:

  • Application;
  • Copy of application for admission to a university in Japan;
  • Research plan (Masters and PhD students only) for);
  • Essay on "Purpose of Studying in Japan";
  • Recommendation;
  • Also, a copy of the university admission letter (can be submitted after the application deadline for the scholarship);
  • The result of the Japanese language test.
