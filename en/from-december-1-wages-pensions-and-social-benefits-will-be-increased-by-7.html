From December 1, wages, pensions and social benefits will be increased by 7%

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
UzA reports that according to the Presidential Decree (No. 196, 17.11.2023), wages, pensions and social benefits are being increased by 7% from December 1 of this year, that is: the basic amount of pension calculation is 372 thousand soums; the minimum age pension is 725,000 soums; the minimum amount of labor remuneration is set at 1,500,000 soums.
As a result of the presidential decree, the amount of pensions and allowances of 4.5 million people will increase, and the monthly salaries of employees of all budget organizations will be increased by 7% from December. It is set at 340 thousand soums.

According to the Presidential Decree (No. 196, 17.11.2023), wages, pensions and social benefits are being increased by 7% starting from December 1 this year, i.e.:

It should be noted that the base calculation amount has changed by 3% (not 7%) from December 1 and is set at 340,000 soums. In general, wages, pensions and social benefits increased by a total of 14.5% during the year, while the amount of the base calculation was less than that, i.e. 13%. — Source
