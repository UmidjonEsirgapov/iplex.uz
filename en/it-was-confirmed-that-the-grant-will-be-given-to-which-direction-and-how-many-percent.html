Approved criteria of the share of the grant

According to the new decision, up to a maximum of 50% of the total quota for each bachelor's education field is a state grant

For example:

This takes into account the region where each HEI is located and its socio-economic needs:

  • Grant places for universities located in rural areas are relatively more

  • The number of grant places may be limited in highly competitive fields.
