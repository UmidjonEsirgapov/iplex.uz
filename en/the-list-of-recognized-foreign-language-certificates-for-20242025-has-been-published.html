Applicants with a B2 level foreign language certificate will be given maximum points

on May 29, 2024 Ministry of Education and Ministry of Justice According to Decision No. 3515 approved by Decision No. 3515, the list of certificates indicating the level of knowledge of a foreign language has been updated for applicants applying for the bachelor's degree.

Now applicants with a B2 level certificate will receive a maximum score in the subject of a foreign language - 3.1 points. This allows them to get the highest grade in the same subject without taking the exam.

In what cases is the certificate mandatory?

According to the new regulation:

  • If in the course chosen by the applicant, foreign language is indicated as the 1st subject, only those who have a certificate can apply for this course.

  • In other cases, if a certificate is available, it is possible to get maximum score without passing the exam.

Recognized language and list of certificates

The following foreign language national and international certificates are at B2 and above, the maximum score is considered:

English language:

  • National certificate

  • TOEFL iBT: 72+

  • Cambridge Assessment English: FCE (B2), CAE (C1), CPE (C2)

  • DALF: C1 or C2

German:

  • Goethe-Certificate

  • TestDaF, DSD II

Spanish:

  • DELE, SIELE

Arabic, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Turkish** and Pashto, Dari, Urdu, Hindi, Uyghur, Persian national and international certificates of level B2 are also recognized.

The full list of certificate names and scoring criteria is in the official document

This decision is an important step aimed at recognizing the foreign language proficiency of applicants and freeing them from the burden of the exam. Those who do not have a document will have to take the exam in the usual way. — Read on infoedu.uz
