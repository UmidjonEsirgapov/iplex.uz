A new wage system will be introduced for employees of secondary special and vocational education

The draft of the Government's decision "On the introduction of an improved system of payment for the work of employees of secondary special and professional educational organizations" was published on the discussion portal. Within the framework of the project, it is planned to equalize the system of remuneration for the work of teachers, psychologists and managers to employees in other educational systems.

The main changes that will be implemented from September 1, 2025

The basic tariff rates of teachers will be equalized

  • The tariff rates of teachers of vocational education organizations of general secondary educational institutions

  • Academic lyceum teachers receive salaries based on the conditions of employees of specialized schools.

Salary for teachers with scientific degrees and titles at the level of higher education

  • Salary of teachers with the title of candidate of science, PhD, DSc, associate professor or professor of higher education

The salaries of psychologists and leaders are also equalized

  • The basic tariff rates of psychologists are equal to those of general secondary education psychologists.

  • The tariff rates for the directorate (principal, executive director, deputy director) are equal to the heads of general secondary educational institutions.

From 2026, increases will be introduced based on KPI

Incentive system through the director fund

From January 1, 2026:

  • For the director and executive director - up to 20% compared to the base rate,

  • Director for deputies - up to 10% monthly bonus is planned.

These bonuses are determined based on the performance of the organization's leaders (KPI).
