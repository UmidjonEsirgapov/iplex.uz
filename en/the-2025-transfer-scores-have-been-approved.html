The 2025 transfer scores have been approved

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
For the academic year 2025/2026, the transfer marks have been approved based on the relevant order of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation for the academic year 2025/2026 from foreign and non-governmental higher education institutions of the republic. The minimum passing scores for special tests for transferring studies to higher education institutions have been officially approved.
These scores are formed annually based on the proposals of relevant ministries and agencies.
The only change compared to 2024 was observed in the field of law: Field of Law: in 2024 — 140 points in 2025 — 165 points This change means that the competition in this area has increased and the quality requirements have increased.

Passing points for transferring studies for the 2025/2026 academic year have been approved

Based on the relevant order of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation for the 2025/2026 academic year The minimum passing scores for the special tests for the transfer of studies from foreign and non-state higher education institutions to the state higher education institutions of the republic have been officially approved. This process was carried out in the same order for 2025.

In which directions there is a change?

The only change compared to 2024 was observed in the law direction:

  • Law direction: in 2024 — 140 points In 2025 - 165 points

This change means that competition in this direction has increased and quality requirements have increased.

What about other directions?

Last year's scores for all other directions remained unchanged. Therefore, applicants can continue their preparation based on the standards set in previous years.

Conclusion

The most important news for students who plan to transfer their studies for the 2025/2026 academic year is increased passing score in the field of law. Stability is maintained in other areas.
