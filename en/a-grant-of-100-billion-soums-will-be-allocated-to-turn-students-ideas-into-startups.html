The best start-ups are supported in the universities of Uzbekistan

Wide opportunities are created for the youth of Uzbekistan to realize their ideas. The "Best Creative Idea" contest held annually among universities and other initiatives are aimed at supporting innovative projects of young people.

"Best Creative Idea" Contest and Grants

The "Best Creative Idea" contest is held annually for the best startup ideas of university students. The winners of the competition will be awarded a grant of up to 300 million soums from the universities' own funds.

RnD center for prototype creation and testing

A special RnD center will be established by the Innovation Agency and the IT-park to turn the startup into a prototype and test it. This center provides a scientific and technical base and necessary conditions for start-ups.

Recovery of prototype costs

Up to $50,000 of prototype creation costs for the best projects will be covered by the Youth Venture Fund. This will ease the financial burden for young entrepreneurs.

"Startup-expo" is a field of new opportunities

Every year, the exhibition "Startup-expo" is organized, in which investors, large enterprises and startup initiators participate. This platform makes it possible to establish new partnerships and attract investment in projects.

Conclusion

These opportunities created for the youth of Uzbekistan serve to develop the startup ecosystem and implement innovative ideas. Grants, a prototype center and exhibitions open a wide path for students to successfully implement their projects. — More information
