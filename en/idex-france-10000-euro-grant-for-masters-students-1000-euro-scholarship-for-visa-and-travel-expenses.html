Brief about the university and the grant program:

Université Paris-Saclay is a university founded in 2014 and since 2019 has its own technical college in Saclay, France and has a university. This university ranks #178 in the ranking of international universities published by THE (Times Higher Education). Through the INTERNATIONAL MASTER'S SCHOLARSHIPS PROGRAM IDEX grant program offered by the university, it aims to promote the increase of international students' access to master's programs offered by the university and to facilitate the process of joining the university of highly qualified international students, especially for those who want to develop.

process:

  1. Selected students will be automatically emailed a link to the online application form. After receiving this e-mail, students who wish to apply must complete the online application form and indicate the names of two representatives (academic director, professor, internship coordinator ...) who are willing to provide a letter of recommendation for the candidate (required). They are asked to complete and submit an online recommendation letter before the grant application deadline. The candidate is automatically notified when each online recommendation is submitted.
  2. If both recommendations are submitted, the application for the grant program is considered complete. Do not forget that the candidate will not receive a message confirming that the application has been filled out. It is up to the candidate to ensure that both letters of recommendation are completed and submitted by the deadline specified in the invitation email.

Note: Incomplete applications or those received after the deadline will be automatically rejected.

Eligibility Criteria:

Candidates who meet the following requirements for the program may apply:

  • Candidates Must have been accepted to the Université Paris-Saclay master's program by one of the following institutions: AgroParisTech, CentraleSupelec, ENS Paris-Saclay, INSTN-CEA, IOGS, UEVE, UPSaclay, UVSQ. Among these students, only those who meet one of the following criteria can apply:
    • International students who are under 30 years old at the time of application, who have recently arrived in France;
    • Foreign students who have lived in France for less than one year, previously or currently studying in a training course or internship that does not offer a certificate;
    • Foreign students who have lived in France for less than one year, if they are studying in language courses (FFL type);
    • Students who previously lived in France, during their studies, as part of the mobility program (for example, Erasmus Mundus joint master's degrees, exchange program ...).

Benefits of the grant:

  • The amount of the Université Paris-Saclay grant is 10,000 euros per year. This amount is paid by the Université Paris-Saclay during the academic year and for 10 consecutive months (September to June).
  • A maximum of 1,000 euros may be awarded for travel and visa expenses, depending on the candidate's country of origin.
  • Winners will receive their scholarship and travel expenses only after they arrive in France and start their accepted program.

Deadline for receiving applications: May 16, 2021;
Announcement of results: end of June 2021.
