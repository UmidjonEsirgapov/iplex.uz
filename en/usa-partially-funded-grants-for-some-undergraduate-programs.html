About the university and the grant program in brief:

National Louis University is a private university with a main campus in Chicago and its purpose is to give students the opportunity to achieve success through their own support.

The International Opportunity Scholarships grant program announced by the university for the 2021-2022 academic year provides financial assistance to talented students. Applications for this grant are accepted from all international applicants, because the purpose of the grant is to make higher education abroad easier and more convenient for students.

For which programs the grant is valid:

International Opportunity Scholarships grant program is offered for the following undergraduate programs:

  • Culinary Arts/Baking and Pastry
  • Hospitality Management

Benefits of the grant:

All students who apply to National Louis University will be provided with a grant from 10% to 50%, which is intended to be used for tuition fees under the International Opportunity Scholarships grant program.

Application process:

  1. Applicants must first go to the university's online portal register and apply for the courses of their choice according to the university's instructions here;
  2. In addition to good knowledge of the English language, candidates are required to have international English language proficiency certificates such as IELTS, TOEFL or PTE;
