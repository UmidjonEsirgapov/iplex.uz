Overtime pay for teachers and other employees payment procedure

In order to encourage the work of pedagogues, psychologists and library employees in the new academic year, overtime is determined monthly. Overtime payments are calculated based on the efficiency of employees' work, the quality of work with students, and annual final scores.

How is the overtime determined?

Overtime is determined at the beginning of each academic year, based on the results of the previous year's activities. Payments are made from the Fund and can be up to 40% of the basic tariff rate.

Teacher bonus points and percentages

The number of bonus points for teachers is determined based on the following rating system:

Conclusion

The bonus system is an important tool for appreciating the work of pedagogic and other educational staff and achieving quality education. By constantly monitoring the performance and evaluating it based on the point system, the possibility of getting a promotion increases. This, in turn, encourages teachers, psychologists and librarians and serves to increase the quality of education. — Official Data
