When will the entrance exams to sports schools named after Bahadir Jalolov and Oksana Chusovitina begin?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The first round of entrance exams to the sports school specialized in boxing named after Bahadir Jalolov and the sports school specialized in gymnastics named after Oksana Chusovitina will be held on August 27-29 this year.
Boxing named after Bahadir Jalolov For applicants applying to a specialized sports school, the exam will be held at the following address: State University of Physical Education and Sports of Uzbekistan (Tashkent region, Chirchik city, 19, "Sportchilar" street). school (Tashkent city, Mirabad district, Sarikol street, destination: 175 general education school).
