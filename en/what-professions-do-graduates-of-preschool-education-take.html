Educator positions in preschool educational institutions

The preschool education system is an important stage in the all-round development of children and preparation for social life. Several types of educators work in state and non-state institutions.

1. Educator of preschool educational institutions

The holder of this position involves children up to 3-7 years old in the educational process. They are involved in the formation of speech, thinking, physical and social skills of children.

2. Educators of specialized preschools

Educators of this type work with children with disabilities or developmental limitations. They provide education based on special methods, taking into account the individual needs of each child.

3. Family educator

Family educator - educates preschool children in small groups, usually at home. This type of activity is often found in the non-governmental sector and provides an individual approach to the child.

4. Caregiver of an orphanage

Educators of an orphanage are specialists who provide love, care and education to orphaned children. They play an important role in stabilizing the psychological state of children.

Conclusion

The preschool education system is the foundation of education of the future generation. Educators of various fields play an important role in the comprehensive development of children.
