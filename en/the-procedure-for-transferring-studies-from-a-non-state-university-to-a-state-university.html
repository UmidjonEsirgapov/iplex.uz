The procedure for transferring studies from a non-state university to a state university

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
If I am studying at a bachelor's level in a non-state higher education institution in Uzbekistan (for example, Kokand University) or a branch of a foreign higher education institution (Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent), I can transfer my studies to a state higher education institution Is it possible?
In this case, what is the procedure for transfer?
✅ ANSWER: If you register to transfer your studies from a non-state higher education institution in the Republic of Uzbekistan or a branch of a foreign higher education institution in our republic to a state higher education institution in the same or similar field, a special test (test) in the subjects of entrance to this field of education without a supervisor and according to the result of a special test, the issue of transferring your studies will be considered.

If I am studying at a bachelor's level at a non-state higher education institution in Uzbekistan (for example, Kokand University) or a branch of a foreign higher education institution (Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent), can I transfer my studies to a state higher education institution? In this case, what is the procedure for transfer? and depending on the result of the special test, the issue of transferring your studies will be considered. If you register to transfer your studies to a non-cognate educational field of a state higher education institution, you will need to make a one-time payment in the minimum amount of the differentiated payment contract established for this educational field.
