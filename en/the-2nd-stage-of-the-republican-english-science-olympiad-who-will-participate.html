The 2nd stage of the Republican "English Science Olympiad". Who will participate?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The 1st stage of the Olympiad was held on the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform for 1 hour from 20:00 to 21:00.
According to the instructions and regulations of the Olympiad, 1000 people who were selected from the 1st stage for the second stage (thousand) participants passed and had the opportunity to participate in the 2nd stage of the Olympiad.
How will the second stage take place?

As you know, in order to support talented young people and increase the level of English literacy of young people, the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform team organized by the Ministry of Innovative Development, Republic of Youth The Council of Scientists is also holding a 3-stage English Olympiad in cooperation with the "REGISTON" educational center.

The 1st stage of the Olympiad lasted for 1 hour from 20:00 to 21:00 on the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform. A total of more than 9 thousand young people from our Republic participated in the 1st stage of the Olympiad. All participating candidates can view their results and download special certificates of participation in the Olympiad through "Personal cabinet" or Olympiad window.

Who will participate in the 2nd stage of the Olympiad?

According to the instructions and regulations of the Olympiad, 1000 (thousand) participants selected from the 1st stage went to the second stage and had the opportunity to participate in the 2nd stage of the Olympiad.

How will the second stage take place?

Second stage (online form) Candidates participating in the Olympiad will be tested on OLIYGOH.UZ platform in the form of a test on General grammar, Reading and Listening skills: 40 test questions on General grammar, Reading and Listening skills. (Intermediate-advanced levels). Candidates who successfully passed the 2nd stage will go to the 3rd stage.*

Alloted time: 60 minutes

The address of the second stage: OLIYGOH.UZ platform

  • 100 (one hundred) participants selected for the third stage from the 2nd stage

The 3rd stage of the Olympiad will be held on July 25between the selected candidates in the "REGISTON" training center.

For information, the winners of the Olympiad will be awarded with special prizes, souvenirs, diplomas with QR codes of I, II and III levels:

To the 1st place winner: Laptop computer and souvenir books worth 200 thousand soums

To the 2nd place winner: Samsung A21 mobile phone and souvenir books worth 100 thousand soums

To the 3rd place winner: Xiaomi 9 A mobile phone and the value of 100 thousand soums souvenir books

OLIYGOH.UZ team and "REGISTON" educational center wish the participants of the 2nd stage of the Olympiad good luck!
