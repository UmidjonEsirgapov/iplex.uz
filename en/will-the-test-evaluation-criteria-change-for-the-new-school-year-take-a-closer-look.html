Higher The Ministry of Education, Science and Innovation has approved the procedure of tests and evaluation criteria for admission to undergraduate courses for the 2025/2026 academic year. This year's criteria have remained the same as last year.

Compulsory subjects and distribution of points

For each applicant, the following 3 compulsory subjects test tasks are set:

  • Native language (Uzbek, Russian or Karakalpak) – 10 questions

  • Mathematics – 10 questions

  • History of Uzbekistan – 10 questions

These 30 questions are evaluated with 1.1 points each. General maximum score – 33 points.

Tests in specialized subjects

According to the field of study, each applicant will take a test in 2 subjects:

  • 1st subject – 30 questions, 3.1 points each (total 93.0 points)

  • Science 2 – 30 questions, 2.1 points each (total 63.0 points)

A total of 60 questions will be asked from this specialty block and the maximum score for them is 156 possible.

General test procedure and maximum score

A total of 90 questions (30 compulsory, 60 special) will be given to applicants for the new academic year. It will be possible to score 189 points in total.

This evaluation system serves to determine the level of knowledge of applicants fairly and accurately. — Official Data
