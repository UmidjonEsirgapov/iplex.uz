According to the changes made to the regulations approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, students of higher education organizations can submit applications for transferring (restoring) their studies for the spring semester every year on January 10-20.

Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 578 in accordance with the regulations approved by the Ministry of Education, the deadline for accepting applications for transferring (restoring) the studies of students of higher education organizations before the spring semester has been changed. According to the new rules, applications are submitted every year from January 10 to January 20 (also on January 20).

Applications can be submitted in the following forms:

  • via the official transfer.edu.uz website of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation;
  • online through the unified interactive state services portal - my.gov.uz;
  • organized by order of the minister can also be accepted through regional working groups.

The following documents are required for the application:

  • a copy of the rating book, academic reference or transcript;
  • documents confirming the reasons for transferring (restoring) studies.

At the same time, some restrictions are also defined. In particular, applications for "transfer" from one higher education institution to another will not be accepted before the spring semester. Also, it is not allowed to transfer studies to the 1st semester of the 1st stage (1st year) (except in exceptional cases).

It is noted that the difference in subjects should not exceed 4, and the GPA should be at least 2.4. will be considered until February (also on February 10). The results will be notified to the applicants in the prescribed manner. — Source
