Conscription? Look at your phone, maybe it has already arrived!

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
This new system is being implemented as a test.
How electronic call works. Registration: registration as a user of the My.gov.uz mobile application is required. simplifies communication.

The procedure for notifying citizens (those conscripted and forced into military service) electronically to the defense affairs bodies through the My.gov.uz mobile application has been introduced. This new system is being implemented in a test mode.

How the electronic summons works

  • Registration: Registration as a user of the My.gov.uz mobile application is required.
  • Receive notifications: Electronic summons notices will be displayed in the "Notifications" section of the application.
  • The paper form is saved: This system was launched without canceling the paper summons.

Advantages of this system

  • Convenience: Citizens receive information about the summons in electronic form on time.
  • Economics: Enables quick and efficient organization of the process
  • Interdependence: Simplifies communication between defense agencies and citizens.

Note

Electronic summons notices are implemented together with paper summonses. This procedure has been implemented in cooperation with the Ministry of Defense on a trial basis.

Conclusion

The electronic call system is another important step in the implementation of modern technologies in the fields of education, security and military service. Citizens will have the opportunity to make processes much easier using the my.gov.uz application. — More information
