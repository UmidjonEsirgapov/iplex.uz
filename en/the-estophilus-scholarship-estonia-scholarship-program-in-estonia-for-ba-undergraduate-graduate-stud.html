The Estophilus scholarship (Estonia): The Estophilus scholarship for students of the final stage of bachelor's degree (BA), master's degree (MA) and doctoral degree (PhD) in Estonia for a period of 1-5 months to do an internship in the fields of their choice. a grant is being allocated. Knowledge of the Estonian language is certainly good but not mandatory, the research can be conducted in English;

Benefits of the grant:

  • 500 euros will be allocated for living expenses;
  • Funds will also be allocated for travel expenses;

How much money will be allocated for travel expenses depends on the distance between your country and the state of Estonia depends on:

1-499 km

180 euros

500-1999 km

275 euros

2000-2999 km

360 euro

3000-3999 km

530 euro

Admission to the program:

Submit an online application here (the application can be completed in English or Estonian);

Required documents:

  • Register online;
  • Study plan;
  • Curriculum vitae (CV);
  • Reference from the educational institution;
  • A letter from the Estonian scientific supervisor agreeing to work with the researcher and a letter indicating the importance of the research for Estonia;

Selection process:

  • Quality of the application;
  • The relevance of the candidate's specialization and research plan;
  • The candidate's research work and its importance for Estonia;

Note: Results will be announced 45 days after sending online applications;

Deadlines for applying to the program:

  • Spring admission: 01.04-02.05.2021;
  • Autumn admission starts from September 1, 2021 ;

For additional questions, contact Tiiu.Tuisk@harno.ee!
