"September 7th syndrome" is a condition observed in children after the start of school

"September 7th syndrome" is a set of symptoms of fatigue, mental disturbance and, in some cases, illness in children after the first week of school. This phenomenon is especially common in 1st graders, as well as in 4-5 and 9-10th grades.

Why does the "September 7 syndrome" occur?

Regime change

During the school period, waking up early in the morning, sitting for a long time in classes, homework and new responsibilities are a child's responsibility. increases stress in the body.

Difference between summer vacation and school

A child who is used to freedom, more games and walks in the summer, suddenly returns to strict order. This causes mental and physical difficulties.

How does the syndrome manifest itself?

  • Physical symptoms: headache or sore throat, upset stomach, fever.

  • Emotional changes: fatigue, apathy, rapid mood swings.

  • Changes in behavior: not talking about school, giving up hobbies, sleep and appetite disturbances.

Duration of adaptation

  • Primary classes (1st grade) — 1-2 months.

  • Middle and upper classes — approx. 1 month.

Useful tips for parents

1. Start the routine in advance

From the summer months, gradually prepare the child for the school routine: get up early, eat regularly and observe sleep.

2. Provide quality sleep

Put the child to bed earlier and limit the use of gadgets (no more than 30 minutes).

3. Leave additional training for later

In September, focus only on the main lessons, clubs can be started later.

4. Increase physical activity

Outdoor walks, family games reduce stress and restore energy.

5. Emotional support

Parental love, attention and understanding are more important than grades.

6. Healthy diet

Products rich in magnesium and vitamins - eggs, fish, dairy products, nuts, bananas and dried fruits are good for the child's health.

7. Consult a specialist

If the child has serious health problems, it is necessary to consult a pediatrician.

Conclusion

"September 7 syndrome" is a natural, temporary and usually not dangerous condition. The most important thing is that parents monitor the child's physical and mental condition, support him with the right regime, healthy food, rest and love. Then it will be easier to adapt to school life. — Official Data
