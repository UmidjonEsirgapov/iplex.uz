The "Delete account" function has been added to the online applicant registration system.

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The "Delete account" function has been added to the online applicant registration system (abitur.dtm.uz site).
This function is located in the "Personal information" section.
Account Deleted applicants can re-register until July 20.

To delete the account, it is necessary to perform the following steps:

  • Step 1: Enter the password and click the delete account button.
  • Step 2: Enter the SMS code received on the phone number and confirm it.

After completing these steps, the account is considered completely deleted.

When the account is deleted:

  • All user data will be deleted from the system;
  • The applicant will lose the right to participate in the entrance exams for the 2020-2021 academic year.

The applicants who deleted the account should re-register by July 20

Also, if the applicants enter their privileges incorrectly, they can enter the privileges section and delete or change their privileges.

We remind you that the applicants have the opportunity to change their permanent residence address, the educational institution they graduated from, their privileges, and their chosen educational fields until the end of the registration period (July 20).
