Can a student study and work?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Yes, a student can work in paid jobs based on an employment contract.
There is no legal restriction for this.
A student who is studying in the form of full-time education according to Article 50 of the Regulation on Higher Education during free time from classes (in the afternoon) it is allowed to work in a higher education institution and in other organizations under an employment contract.

There is no legal limit for this.

According to Article 50 of the Regulation on Higher Education, a full-time student is allowed to work in a higher education institution or in other organizations during his spare time (in the afternoon).

According to Article 248 of the current Labor Code, the employer must create the necessary conditions for employees studying in educational institutions to combine work with education.
