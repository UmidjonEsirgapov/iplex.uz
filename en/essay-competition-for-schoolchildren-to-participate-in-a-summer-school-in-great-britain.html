Essay competition for schoolchildren to participate in a summer school in Great Britain

Useful information for applicants and students:

NOTE!

The Immerse Education Essay Competition is an essay competition for school students between the ages of 13 and 18 in their chosen field. The winners of the competition can get a 100% grant to participate in a 2-week summer school organized at the universities of Oxford and Cambridge in Great Britain.

Duration of the program: 2 weeks - July-August 2022

Young people belonging to the following group can participate in the competition (as of July-August 2022 according to)

  • Participants aged 13-15;
  • Participants aged 16-18;

Privileges:

  • The 1st place winners will participate in a 2-week summer school for free - 10 people;
  • The next best candidates will participate in this summer school will receive a 70% grant for their tuition fees.
  • All participants will be provided with 13 days of free accommodation at Oxford or Cambridge University;
  • Free breakfast and dinner, all will be provided with a separate room;
  • Can participate in 40-hour classes from Oxford and Cambridge University professors;
  • 2 excursions to the city of Oxford;
  • Insurance;
  • You will get a free certificate and other opportunities.

NOTE! Participants must cover their own travel expenses.

To participate in the competition, go through the online list and post your essays.

Results will be announced on February 1, 2022.
