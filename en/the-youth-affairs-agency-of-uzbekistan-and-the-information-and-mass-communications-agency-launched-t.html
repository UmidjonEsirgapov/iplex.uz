General information:

Uzbekistan Youth Affairs Agency and Information and Mass Communications Agency in cooperation on the world wide web In order to increase quality information in Uzbek language and adequately encourage talented students Wiki Scholarship marathon has been launched.

The marathon will be held between May and December 2022. The expected result of the marathon is to increase the number of Uzbek Wikipedia articles.

Main directions for writing articles:

  1. Natural sciences and science;
  2. History;
  3. Culture and art;
  4. Philosophy;
  5. Technology and technologies;
  6. Human and society;
  7. Games and sports;
  8. Geography;

The marathon will last from May 20-00:00 (GMT+5) to December 2000:00 (GMT+5) 2022. The most actively participating participants will be awarded a one-time scholarship of the following amount: 50,000,000 soums; 40,000,000 soums; 30,000,000 soums; strong top five 20,000,000 soums;

  • strong top ten 10,000,000 soums;

    • How are the most actively participating participants evaluated;

    1. Only the articles included in the "Fountain" by the judges count as one;
    2. The rest go to the "Fountain" "0" is given for the articles that are not included and created by the referees;
    3. One point is given for each article that meets the requirements of the marathon;
    4. Judges have the right to give additional points, taking into account the quality of the articles;
    5. If methodological, orthographic errors, poor translation require serious revision, they have the right to reduce by 1 point;
