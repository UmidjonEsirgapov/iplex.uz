For International Participants S5+1 Network Cup 2021 application (for participants residing outside of Kyrgyzstan)
The C5+1 Youth Council is pleased to announce the “Network Cup 2021” C5+1 YC annual regional debate tournament, sponsored by the US Embassy in the Kyrgyz Republic.

C5+1 YC "Network Cup 2021" aims to bring together young leaders of Central Asian countries and Afghanistan to discuss regional issues:
- Global counter-terrorism and security;
- Business competitiveness in Central Asia;
- Development of transport corridor;
- Environmental problems and climate change.

When: December 4-5, 2021
Where: Bilimkana American School (Chingiza Aytmatova St. #1a), Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan

For whom: High school students, college/lyceum and university students for;
Language: English ;
Format: offline

Note: The annual regional debate tournament C5+1 YC “Network Cup 2021” aims to teach public speaking, debate skills and critical thinking to young leaders.

The event will be held in the format of debate format, public speaking, motion analysis, analytical speaking, logic, argumentation, research and debate preparation training seminars during the weeks of November 27 - December 2, 2021, before the debate tournament via Zoom (for registered applicants only). The personal debate tournament will be held on December 4-5, 2021 at the Scientific American School in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan.

Terms of selection:
- Participants must be university or lyceum students; - Citizens of Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Afghanistan.
- All selected participants must attend BOTH days (Saturday and Sunday) between 9:00 AM and 6:30 PM;

How to apply:







  • All candidates who wish to participate must fill out the application form.

Participants from Central Asia residing outside of Kyrgyzstan can register here;

Important: Application to participate does not guarantee delivery. In case of a large number of applications, the organizers will select the finalists based on the quality of the application, the experience of the debate and the diversity of the participants (based on countries and genders). Therefore, we recommend that you fill out your application accordingly;
- Participants can apply as a team or individually;
- All selected participants will be sent a request to confirm their participation by e-mail;


Note: Flight and accommodation will be provided for participants from Central Asian countries residing outside Kyrgyzstan and in Kyrgyz regions.

Registration deadline:

  • Central Asian participants residing outside Kyrgyzstan: November 27, 2021 (23:59 Bishkek time);
  • Participants residing in Kyrgyzstan: November 30, 2021 (23:59 Bishkek time).
