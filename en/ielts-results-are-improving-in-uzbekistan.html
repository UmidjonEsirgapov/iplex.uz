IELTS results are improving in Uzbekistan

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:

The number of young people who know English is increasing

Among the young people of Uzbekistan, the number of high scorers in the IELTS exam is increasing every year. According to the results of 2024:

  • The number of people with 8.5 and 9.0 points increased from 14 people in 2020 to 332 people.
  • This means a 23.7 times increase compared to 2021.

General indicators for 2024:

  • 52,706 young Uzbeks took the IELTS exam
  • The number of those who got the highest score is between 6.0 and 6.5
  • The number of those who scored 7.5 and above has increased significantly
