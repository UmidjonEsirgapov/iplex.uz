A discount is not offered to those who have up to 4 points in the super-contract payment

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

However, this discount applies to applicants who scored more than 56.7 points, but did not reach the entrance score of 4.05 points and dropped out.
We remind you that the scale of the super-contract up to 4 points is divided as follows: For applicants who did not reach the passing score of 1.05 points - the amount of the basic payment-contract 1.5 times; from 1.06 points to 2.05 points - 2 times; from 2.06 points to 3.05 points - 2.5 times; From 3.06 points to 4.05 points - 3 times.

In the amount of 10 percent for applicants who paid the differentiated (super) payment-contract value in full this year until October 15, 2024 discount is given. This discount does not apply to a regular (basic) contract.

However, this discount applies to applicants who scored more than 56.7 points, but fell short of the entrance score 4.05 points and dropped out. Discounts are not applied to those who are on the scale of up to 4 points.

We remind you that the scale of the super-contract up to 4 points is divided as follows:

  • For applicants who do not reach the passing score of 1.05 points - 1.5 times the amount of the basic payment-contract;
  • 1.06 from points to 2.05 points — 2 times;
  • from 2.06 points to 3.05 points — 2.5 times;
  • from 3.06 points to 4.05 points — 3 times.

Applicants in this category, although 100% payment and there is no discount for them. Therefore, it is possible to pay 50% of the contract and pay the rest in installments during the academic year.
