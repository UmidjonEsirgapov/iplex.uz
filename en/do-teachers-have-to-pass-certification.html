Do teachers have to pass certification?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Are pedagogues obliged to undergo certification?
According to Clause 4 of the Regulation of the Cabinet of Ministers "On the procedure for certification of pedagogic staff of preschool, general secondary, secondary special, professional and extracurricular educational organizations" Mandatory attestation will be held in the 5th year. Also, based on Article 6 of the regulation: teaching staff with 25 years or more of work experience in pedagogical activity; Pedagogical employees who have no more than 5 years left before retirement due to age will not be involved in the next mandatory attestation. According to clause 4 of the regulation, mandatory attestation is conducted in the 5th year.

Also, according to clause 6 of the regulation:

  • pedagogical staff with 25 years of work experience in pedagogical activity and more;
  • more than 5 years for retirement. Pedagogical employees who have not completed the period will not be involved in the next mandatory attestation.

Article 8 is filled with the sub-item "ye" based on the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated December 29, 2023 No. Pedagogists who have no more than 5 years to retire will be given their current qualification category (position). Therefore, if a pedagogue has 30 years of experience in the field, he will not be involved in mandatory certification and his category will be retained.
