Acceptance of state grants will be increased by 25%, grants allocated to daughters of needy families will be doubled - Oliy Majlis.

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Starting from the 2021-2022 academic year, the acceptance rate for the state grant to higher education institutions will be increased by 25%.
This is determined in the amendment to the parameters of the state budget for 2021.
On June 8, the Oliy Majlis Legislation The House of Representatives considered and adopted the draft law on amendments to the Law on the State Budget for 2021.

Starting from the 2021-2022 academic year, the acceptance rates for state grants to higher education institutions will be increased by 25%. This is defined in the amendments to the parameters of the state budget for 2021.

On June 8, the Legislative Chamber of the Oliy Majlis reviewed and adopted the draft law on amendments to the Law on the State Budget for 2021. This is reported by the press service of the chamber.

According to the document, starting from the 2021-2022 academic year, the acceptance rates of state grants to higher education institutions will be increased by 25%, the grants allocated to girls in needy families will be doubled, and their number will reach 2 thousand.

Also, the bill provides for primary and specialized medical care in remote areas it is planned to involve private hospitals in screening and treatment activities for the needy population, and to establish a subsidy for them. Additional funds are allocated from the budget and strengthening of the material and technical base of institutions for early detection and treatment of endocrine diseases is planned.

According to the source, the changes made to the budget will serve to develop family entrepreneurship, ensure employment of the population, provide financial support for youth and women's initiatives, and improve internal roads and drinking water supply. — Source
