To determine the best educational brands, "Mfaktor" business school, EduOn.uz and OLIYGOH.UZ announces "EDU-BRANDING" public competition in cooperation with educational platforms.

Organizers of the competition:

  • EduOn.uz and OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform.

Competition purpose:

  • To further increase the potential of private education in Uzbekistan, to support the best projects in the field of education and to bring them to the general public.

Who is this competition for?

  • For all public and private schools in the city of Tashkent, regions and Karakalpakstan.

Competition winners:

  • Candidates with the highest number of votes from each category are considered the winners;
  • The winners will be ceremonially awarded with special awards, souvenirs, a special statuette (transparent cup) of the "Best Education Brand" and diplomas with a QR code;
  • The award ceremony will be held in the special events building of "Mfaktor" business school;
  • Participate more actively in the competition and get the name of the brand of the year;
  • Great gifts and prizes await the winners.

15 nominations in the competition:

  • The best IT school;
  • The best private university;
  • The best educational center (sciences);
  • The best foreign language teaching center;
  • The best educational publishing house and bookstore;
  • The best consulting center (education abroad);
  • The best speaker working in the field of education.

Choice calendar:

  • From February 1 to March 1.

How is voting in the contest?

  • Candidates are evaluated by voting through a platform specially organized for the contest:
  1. Registration;
  2. Nomination selection;
  3. Vote for an existing candidate.
