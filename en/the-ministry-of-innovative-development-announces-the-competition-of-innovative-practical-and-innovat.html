The Ministry of Innovative Development announces the competition of innovative practical and innovative projects "Women Scholars".

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Scientific projects submitted to the competition will be accepted only in electronic form until May 2, 2022 through www.grant.mininnovation.uz - the only intellectual electronic platform of state scientific programs.
In the course of registration of scientific project documents through the electronic platform grant.mininnovation.uz, the original copies of the following documents in pdf/doc form must be uploaded to the electronic platform: reference letter (.pdf) signed by the head of the executive organization; extract from the decision of the scientific (scientific and technical) council of the executive organization (.pdf); practical or innovative project text (pdf and .doc/.docx with signature and seal).

General information:

The main goal of the competition is to attract women to science in our republic, to comprehensively present new ideas and developments of women engaged in scientific activities in various branches and fields of science and education and economy. support and encouragement, based on the results of initiative practical and innovative projects, the creation of scientific voluminous products and innovative technologies aimed at solving the existing problems, real needs and regional problems of economic sectors and social spheres. Until May 2, 2022, it will be accepted only in electronic form.

The composition of the project executors should consist only of women, the average age of the scientific team should not exceed 45 years, and the scientific team should include young scientists, talented students and graduate students

The Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education should participate in the competition. Women with degrees working in research and higher education institutions of the Ministry of Health, Ministry of Public Education and other organizations engaged in scientific activity can participate. product, new development, technology) it should be noted (justified) that it meets the following minimum requirements:

  • aimed at providing a scientific solution to an existing problem in a specific economic sector or a specific social sphere (annual demand/need should be indicated in specific figures);
  • the presence of a comparative advantage in comparison with existing/foreign (imported) analogues (must be specified in clear numbers);
  • the presence of a clear economic (social/practical) effect from commercialization/implementation.

Applicable and innovative projects expected to be completed within one year are accepted for the competition. Scientific projects, which are intended to be completed for more than one year, will not undergo preliminary technical expertise and will not be reviewed.

Only one project can be submitted to the competition by the project leader. If the head of the project is actually carrying out a scientific project, it is not allowed to be the head of two projects of the same type at the same time.

During the registration of scientific project documents through the grant.mininnovation.uz electronic platform, the following documents in pdf/doc form must be uploaded to the electronic platform:

  • referral letter (.pdf) signed by the head of the executive organization;
  • excerpt from the decision of the scientific (scientific and technical) board of the executive organization (.pdf);
  • the text of the practical or innovative project (pdf and .doc/.docx with a signature and seal).

The requirements of the competition have been strictly observed by the presented projects, the reliability of the project materials and A preliminary technical examination will be conducted to ensure that the scientific project has not been carried out before, that it is based on the requirements of the competition, and that the important scientific results obtained at the end of the project are fully based in accordance with the research design, and that the requested funds have been correctly calculated in accordance with the amount of funding specified in the competition conditions.

Note: Information about the results of the review of the projects submitted to the competition at each stage is provided through the interactive service provided in the personal offices of the project managers on the grant.mininnovation.uz electronic platform.
