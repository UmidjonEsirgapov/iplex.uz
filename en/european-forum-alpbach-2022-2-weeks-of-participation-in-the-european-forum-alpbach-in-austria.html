European Forum Alpbach 2022: 2 weeks of participation in the European Forum Alpbach in Austria

General information:

Every year Alpbach European Forum announces grant programs for hundreds of outstanding young talents from all over the world. This grant program also allows students, young professionals to come to Alpbach and participate in a unique program of scientific and artistic seminars, a high-level conference program and various social and cultural events. This year's Alpbach 2022 European Forum will be held from August 21 to September 2, 2022. Workshops last two weeks during the forum. They will be held in the morning. Scholarship holders can choose two workshops, each of which they attend for one week. There will be an interaction of scientific, creative and skill-oriented workshops, panels, workshops and afternoon plenary debates.

Note: Candidates must attend the Albach opening forum on August 20.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Not have participated in this forum before;
  • English language certificate minimum B2 or IELTS 5.5 (other types of proof of language proficiency at this level are also accepted);
  • Be between 18-30 years old;
  • Citizens of all nationalities can apply;
  • Candidates to participate in the Alpbach 2022 European Forum from August 20 to September 2, 2022 must be ready;
  • Applications are mainly accepted from people who are socially active and who think holistically about society. Active people who have participated in public service, volunteering, political or non-profit work and similar social projects are encouraged to apply.

Benefits:

  • Accommodation expenses are fully covered;
  • Funds are also provided for daily food expenses;
  • Also, participation in the Alpbach European Forum. expenses are also fully covered;

Note: However, the participants must cover the travel expenses to Austria themselves.

Required documents:

  • A document proving your English B2 level specified in the section "Knowledge of the English language";
  • The most recent diploma (in English, German or French);
  • SV/Resume;
  • Motivational letter (learn more about how to write it here);

Important dates:

  • Reception started on March 15;
  • Applications will be accepted until March 31;
  • Results will be announced in May;
  • Agreement at the end of May will be signed;
  • General preparations will be carried out in June-August;
  • Arrive in Austria on August 20;
  • Return on September 2;

Note: Prepare your motivation statement before starting to fill out the application form. Do not include personal information such as your name or gender in the essay.
