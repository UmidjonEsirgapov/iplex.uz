The amount of the fee set for the entrance exam to higher education institutions this year is 170,000 soums. However, not all applicants are required to make this payment. The category of fee-exempt applicants is clearly defined in the official documents.

Check out the non-fee list of applicants below:

1. Graduates who graduated in the same year Applicants graduating from school in the year of the exam are exempted from payment.

2. Persons with disabilities No examination fee is required for applicants with confirmed disability.

3. Children of orphanages Children of state-sponsored orphanages are exempt from the examination fee.

4. Children's town graduates Applicants brought up in children's towns in Uzbekistan also take part in the exam free of charge.

5. Individuals and their children registered in the "Unified Register of Social Protection" Citizens and their children registered in this system participate free of charge. 6. Young people who are listed in the "Youth Register" under this social project will also be exempted from the payment of 170 thousand soums.
