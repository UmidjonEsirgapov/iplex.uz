A science Olympiad center will be established

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On measures to further improve the system of working with talented youth" was adopted.
From 2025, the Center will: form a base of talented students who have recorded high results in Olympiads among 5-8th grade students, and target preparation of them for international Olympiads will be launched; Curriculums, literature, methodical recommendations, manuals and magazines are created for each discipline; Necessary conditions are created for the continuous professional development of trainers-teachers who prepare students for science Olympiads, they are taught new methods; The practice of holding joint meetings of the national national teams with the national teams of foreign countries for Science Olympiads will be established; Seminars on overcoming mental pressure and motivational training are organized for students at international Olympiads; "Intellectual camps" will be organized for talented young people.

The decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On measures to further improve the system of working with talented young people" was adopted.

According to it, a Science Olympiad Center will be established under the Specialized Educational Institutions Agency in the form of a state institution.

One of the main activities of the center is to identify and select young people who are capable and interested in deep study of sciences, and to create the necessary conditions for further development of their talent. purposeful preparation of them for international Olympiads will be launched;

  • Study programs, literature, methodical recommendations, manuals and magazines will be created for each discipline;
  • Necessary conditions will be created for the continuous professional development of trainers-teachers who prepare students for science Olympiads, they will be taught new methods;
  • National teams of science Olympiads will cooperate with national teams of foreign countries. the practice of holding joint meetings will be established;
  • Seminars and motivational trainings will be organized for students on overcoming mental pressure at international Olympiads;
  • "intellectual camps" will be organized for talented young people.

    • From January 1, 2025:

    • The procedure for encouraging the leader of the national teams participating in the prestigious international Olympiads will be introduced based on the results of the team;
    • The leader of the national teams will be awarded a one-time cash prize in the amount of 35, 25, 15 times the base calculation amount for each gold, silver, bronze medal won in the prestigious international Olympiads; Prizes and gifts paid to participating students, their teachers and national team leaders are exempt from income tax;
    • Candidates who are invited to participate in international Olympiads are provided with free food and lodging at the expense of the State budget during the training sessions.

    Until May 1, 2025:

    • Main Olympics a system of awarding medals to the winners of the republican stage will be introduced.
