Financially independent universities: Grants of up to 1 billion soums are allocated annually

Higher education organizations with financial independence allocate up to 1 billion soums of grants annually in order to support talented students in need of social protection. These grants open up great opportunities for students.

To whom the grant is awarded?

The grant of the higher education organization is provided only to certain categories of students. Their list is approved by the National Agency of Social Protection and the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

Grant amount according to GPA

Based on the student's GPA (average rating indicator), the amount of the grant is determined as follows:

  • 3.3 - 3.39 — **20%** of the payment contract amount

  • 3.4 – 3.49 — 30%

  • 3.5 – 3.59 — 40%

  • 3.6 – 3.69 — 50%

  • 3.7 – 3.79 — 60%

  • 3.8 – 3.89 — 70%

  • 3.9 – 3.99 — 80%

Application Deadline

Applicants They must register through the official website of their higher education institution before July 20 every year. And the results will be announced until September 5.
