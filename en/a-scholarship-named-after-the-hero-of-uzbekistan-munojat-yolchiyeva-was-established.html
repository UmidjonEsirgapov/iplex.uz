A scholarship named after the Hero of Uzbekistan Munojat Yolchiyeva was established

About the expected changes in the education system:

For the students studying at the Uzbek National Institute of Music named after Yunus Rajabi, a true devotee of our art, Uzbekistan A scholarship named after her heroine Munojat Yolchiyeva was established. It is intended to be awarded to undergraduate students who have completed the 2nd year of the Institute of Music and master's students who have completed the 1st year, and the candidate will be selected based on the competition. The applicant's documents will be accepted by the Scientific Department of the Institute. The documents submitted for participation in the competition will be submitted to the Selection Commission within a week after the end of the competition period, and experts will be appointed to review the documents by the decision of the commission.

Experts will be appointed with the consent of the members of the Selection Commission and professors and teachers of the Institute or, if necessary, experts recruited from other organizations. approved on the basis of the order and submitted for the payment of the scholarship. According to the submission of the faculty or department regarding scholarship holders, that they have violated the rules of the internal procedure of the institute or allowed laziness in their scientific activities, the grant of the scholarship will be stopped by the decision of the Council.

We remind you that the materials submitted for participation in the competition will not be returned. — Read on infoedu.uz
