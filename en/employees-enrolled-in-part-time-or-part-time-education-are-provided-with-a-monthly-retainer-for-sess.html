Employees enrolled in part-time or part-time education are provided with a monthly retainer for session submissions

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Employees studying in evening or part-time education in higher educational institutions, technical schools or colleges will be given educational leave for participation in laboratory-examination sessions in the following periods with the average salary preserved: 1st-2nd year students studying in HEIs - at least 20 days, at technical schools or colleges - at least 10 days, and for those studying part-time in higher education institutions and technical schools or colleges - at least 30 days; For those studying in the 3rd-4th year of higher education in higher education institutions - at least 30 days, in technical institutes or colleges - at least 20 days, and for those studying part-time education in higher educational institutions and technical institutes or colleges - at least 40 calendar days. To participate in laboratory-examination sessions, the following periods of study leave are given, while keeping the average salary:

At least 20 days for students of the 1st-2nd year studying in the evening education at the higher educational institution, at least 10 days at the technical school or colleges, and at least 30 days for those studying part-time in the higher education institution and technical school or colleges;

Evening at the higher education institution; those studying in the 3rd-4th year or higher - at least 30 days, at technical schools or colleges - at least 20 days, and at least 40 calendar days for those studying part-time in higher education institutions and technical schools or colleges. — Details here
