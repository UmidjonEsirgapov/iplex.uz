Republic-wide Registon education project in cooperation with OLIYGOH.UZ team announces the competition "Registon IELTS Grant". It is planned to train 1000 young people throughout the Republic for free through this Grant.

Registon IELTS Grant is a Registon education project that is not indifferent to the future of Uzbekistan's education and the team of OLIYGOH.UZ is one of the largest platforms in the field of education. is the next social project developed by , through which the most talented young people will be educated free of charge and duly rewarded.

The main goal of the competition - To encourage the education of young people studying on the scale of the Republic in modern ways, to support talented young people, to improve the English language of young people. is to further develop the education sector of Uzbekistan by increasing the level of literacy and financial incentives.

What opportunities will the winners of the Registon IELTS Grant contest have?

  • Registon IELTS Grant winner certificate and souvenirs;
  • Preparation for the IELTS exam at one of the branches of the Registon training center the opportunity to study the course absolutely free of charge for 4 months (based on the calendar month);
  • the opportunity to use Registan's special IELTS materials and books for free;
  • the opportunity to use the official materials of "Registan IELTS Premium Mock Exam" for free ;
  • the opportunity to free participate in the "Registan IELTS Premium Mock Exam" exams;
  • Registan the opportunity to use the "Support teacher" service of the educational center for free;
  • online lessons on a special platform, the opportunity to take additional lessons from IELTS 8.0+ mentors;
  • Also, special QR-code certificates will be given to all candidates who participated in the competition.

Organization and conduct of the competition Registon education project's is carried out by a special commission consisting of international class teachers and OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform experts.

For information, Registan Education Project is one of the largest private educational organizations in Uzbekistan, with a network of 31 schools throughout the Republic. includes large projects such as educational center, Registan IT school, Registan IT company, Registan private publishing house, Registan private school. In the near future, the non-state university "Registan University" is about to start its activities. Currently, 20 thousandyoung people are studying in the Registon educational center network. In its 9-year history, more than 70 thousand young people have successfully completed the center.

Selection stages

The Registon IELTS Scholarship competition consists of 2 stages. The stages of the competition are evaluated on the basis of the general evaluation system and the most suitable participants are selected. Through the OLIYGOH.UZ platform, republican online exam will be held.

  • In the online exam, 50 test questions will be given at different levels of the English language (listening, reading, vocabulary, grammar). Ends at 12:00 on October 23rd (Note: At the same time as the start of the 1st phase of the project, tens of thousands of participants started the exam on the platform, and as a result, there was a huge load on the server of the platform system. Due to this, many candidates experienced interruptions in accessing their personal accounts on the platform. Therefore, candidates who could not participate in the 1st stage exam we have extended the time of this stage.)
  • All candidates will take part in the online test lasting 40 minutes within the specified time interval. The special test platform of the competition will be closed on October 23 at 12:00. After the first stage, the mandate of general results will be formed and the candidates with the highest results in the branch section will be distributed within the quotas, and a total of 3 thousand candidates will qualify for the next stage. From stage 1 The minimum result of passing to the 2nd stage is 80 percent (40 correct answers and above).
  • A special SMS message and a message through the Personal Cabinet will be sent to the candidates who have passed to the second stage.
