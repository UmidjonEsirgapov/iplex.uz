Transfer of studies from non-state universities to state universities without exams

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
On the basis of tests for other educational fields.
According to this decision, a one-time payment equal to the minimum amount of the fee-contract classified from non-state universities to unrelated educational fields of state universities without an exam studies can be transferred.
Note: This decision creates new opportunities for students.

According to the Presidential Decree (15.06.2022, PQ-279), it is possible to transfer studies from non-state (private) and foreign higher education institutions operating in the Republic to state higher education institutions in two different ways:

  1. Transfer to compatible and related educational areas:

  • Professional (creative) exams in educational areas that require special talent.
  • On the basis of tests in other educational areas.

  1. To non-accompanying educational areas transfer:

  • It is carried out on the basis of a one-time payment equal to the minimum amount of the differentiated fee-contract established for the relevant bachelor's education field.

According to this decision, from non-state universities to non-affiliated educational fields of the state universities, it is equal to the minimum amount of the differentiated fee-contract it is possible to transfer studies without an exam on the basis of a one-time payment.
