In 2023, at the expense of 7,500 graduates, it is planned to reduce the vacancy of family doctors by 90%, and the vacancies of narrow specialists by 70%.

Also, starting from this year, at least 500 young, talented pedagogues and directors of colleges and technical schools will receive qualifications abroad.

Furthermore, training of highly qualified nurses in higher educational institutions is carried out on the basis of 4-year international training programs. Students study for 2 years in Uzbekistan and 2 years abroad.

From the 2023/2024 academic year, subjects such as "nanotechnology", "medical genetics", "IT-technology" will be included in the curricula of undergraduate, graduate and clinical residency programs in medical universities, and educational processes will be organized with the involvement of foreign pedagogues.

A new two-stage system for assessment of students' theoretical and practical knowledge and skills will be introduced.

Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, the personnel training system in colleges and technical schools will be launched in completely new procedures and directions. According to it: — Official Data

  • general practice in colleges for primary level institutions training of nurses is carried out on the basis of a 2-year program;
  • specialized nurses are trained in technical schools on the basis of a 3-year program;
  • from February 2023, on the basis of the Medical College of the 2nd Republic, the Saudi Arabian "Al Habib Academy" for training doctors and nurses will be established.
  • In cooperation with the University of Health Sciences of Turkey, Nurses will be trained Academy's activities will be launched.
  • most importantly, from 2023 international joint education programs will be introduced in every college and technical school, the report says.
