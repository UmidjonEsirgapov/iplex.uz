General Information:

The application process for the 2022 Youth Peace Summit Forum in Turkey has begun. The Forum is fully funded by the Alliance for Humanity (AH). This forum, which is expected to be held in Antalya, aims to unite more than 150 young people from all over the world. This forum will be held for three days from July 16 to July 19, 2022.

The program aims to empower participants to use their leadership skills through various programs on "Peace and Diplomacy" to promote peace and unity. Students from all over the world can apply for this summit. 1Young people aged 6-40.

For whom: Students and young professionals;

Where: Antalya, Turkey;

Number of participants: 150 people (10 on the basis of fully funded grants. 25 on the basis of partially funded grants and more The remaining 115 accept self-funded participants);

Grant types: there are fully funded and partially funded types;

Deadline: July 17, 2022;

Benefits:

  • Full funded grant: all expenses are covered, including airfare, visa costs, accommodation expenses, food and also includes expenses for attending various parties;
  • Partially funded grant: includes accommodation expenses, food and expenses for various parties;

Requirements for candidates:

  • Youth between the ages of 16-40:
  • Must be fully vaccinated with a vaccine acceptable to Turkey;
  • Socially active, strong-willed youth;

Note: There is a registration fee of $30. To register, click here do it. The results will be announced on June 20; Interviews with selected candidates will be held on June 22-23.
