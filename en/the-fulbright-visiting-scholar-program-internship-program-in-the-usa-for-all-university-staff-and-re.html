The Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program: The Fulbright program for invited scholars provides an opportunity for experienced scientists from Uzbekistan to conduct scientific research at US universities for a period of 3-5 months.

The main benefit of this grant program intended purpose:

  • To train teachers mainly for young teachers and thereby increase the potential of higher education;
  • To develop long-term cooperation and friendship between scientists of Uzbekistan and the USA; Requirements for candidates:
  • Must be a citizen of Uzbekistan or a permanent resident with the right to obtain a valid passport issued by Uzbekistan and reside in this country for the entire duration of the application and selection process.
  • Must have a doctoral degree or an alternative professional qualification;

Possible areas of research:

  • Research may be conducted in all academic areas except medical sciences directly related to patient care.
  • Possible areas include public health and health system management;

Note: Soon in the US special preference is given to applicants without extensive experience.

Benefits of the grant:

  • International flight tickets are fully covered (to and from America);
  • Full financial support in the USA:
  • Insurance costs are covered;

Application for the program and necessary documents;

  • Apply online through this page;
  • A research plan to be carried out in the US;
  • Cv;
  • 2 references;
  • A letter of support from your university and an invitation from the US host universities (these are not mandatory but preferred);

Note: the application deadline is 20 November 2021;

Important: Only candidates who have advanced to the next stage will be interviewed. The last stage will take place in the summer of 2022;

Selection criteria:

  • Knowledge of the English language at a sufficiently high level to carry out the proposed research project in the USA (the level of English language proficiency will be checked in the second round of the selection process, during a personal interview); It must demonstrate its practical value for the institution in the USA;
  • The candidate must return to his country after the program ends and continue teaching, and be able to further develop and use the knowledge and skills acquired during the program during teaching;

Important:

  • Incomplete applications will not be considered;
  • The Fulbright Program reserves the right to verify any information provided in the application documents. In cases where inconsistency with the actual situation is found, the applicant's participation in the Fulbright competition will be suspended;
  • No clear reason is given for the selection or non-selection of candidates;
