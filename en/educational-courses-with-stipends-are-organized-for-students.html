In Uzbekistan, educational courses will be organized for students with a monthly stipend (1 million 320 thousand soums).

Training courses are organized in the following directions:

  • use of information technologies and communications (beginner);
  • mathematical statistics;
  • fundamentals of algorithms;
  • data processing;
  • programming languages (Phthon);
  • English;
  • fundamentals of decision-making.

Also, training courses can be organized in teamwork and other areas. In the first academic year, at least 60 students are accepted, and in the following years, the number of students will be increased depending on the need.

Education is organized for a period of 34 weeks in accordance with the curriculum based on "Blending learning - mixed educational technology". students who have graduated (with no academic debt) can participate.
