An independent exam to become a doctor has been introduced in Uzbekistan

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
According to the order of the President, the personnel training system in the medical field is being fundamentally reformed in Uzbekistan.
This innovation will be mandatory for students, doctors and nurses.
Why is this change important?

President according to the task, the personnel training system in the field of medicine is being fundamentally reformed in Uzbekistan. One of the most important changes is the introduction of an independent final exam. This news will be mandatory for students, doctors and nurses.

What will happen now?

  • Medical university graduates will take an independent exam to receive a diploma.

  • University teachers cannot participate in the exam process, evaluation It will be completely independent and transparent.

  • Doctors and nurse practitioners will also have to take an exam in order to upgrade their qualifications or be appointed.

Why is this change important?

Through this system:

  • To the medical field really knowledgeable and qualified specialists will enter;

  • impartiality and fairness in exams will be ensured;

  • The quality level of medical universities will increase.

The new system will make obtaining a medical diploma more responsible, based on real knowledge turns it into a process. — Source
