What level of nationally and internationally recognized certificates are required?

The following CEFR level language certificates are required when applying for a master's degree:

C1 level fields required

A language certificate at least C1 level is required when applying for the following majors:

  • Philology (foreign language fields)

  • International relations

    • Fields requiring level B2

    A language certificate of at least level B2 is required for the following graduate fields:

    • Economics

    • Law

    • Management

    • Finance

    • Computer Science (some international programs)

    • Sociology

    • Psychology

    Recommendation: B2 level is considered the main requirement for many fields, so get this level if possible.

    B1 level is enough. fields

    B1 level language certificate is also accepted for the following fields:

    • Pedagogy

    • Agronomy

    • Construction

    • Natural sciences (biology, chemistry, physics)

    • Technical areas (electronics, automation, etc.)

    • Geography

    Important: Some HEIs set their own requirements. Therefore, it is recommended to check the official page of the chosen HEI.

    Summary

    The language certificate level differs depending on the field of study. C1 is sufficient for high-demand philological and international fields, B2 is for economic and legal fields, and B1 is sufficient for many technical and pedagogical fields.
