A second foreign language is introduced in schools

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the order of the Ministry of School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, teaching a second foreign language will be gradually introduced in the general secondary education system.
In addition to this innovation, orientation to business and profession will also be integrated into the educational process.
What does the new procedure include?

According to the order of the Ministry of School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, the teaching of a second foreign language will be gradually introduced in the general secondary education system. Along with this innovation, business and career orientation will also be integrated into the educational process.

What will the new order include?

  • A second foreign language will be introduced for high school students (exact stages will be determined later);

  • It will include language teaching. It is conducted together with practical profession and business skills;

  • Through this approach, students are expected to be prepared for the labor market and become specialists who can speak many languages.

What is the goal?

Through this decision:

  • The level of language knowledge and communicative potential increases;

  • School graduates become professionally ready;

  • Students are not only prepared for university, but also acquire the skills to work independently in life.
