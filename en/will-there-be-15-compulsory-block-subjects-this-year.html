Will there be 15 compulsory block subjects this year?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Will the test be given from 10 compulsory subject blocks or 15? It is known that due to the quarantine, the number of questions was reduced, and actually there were 15 compulsory block subjects reduced to 10.
