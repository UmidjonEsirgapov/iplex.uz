Special scholarships and benefits for excellent student girls from poor families

Starting in 2025, the President according to the decree, a number of new opportunities are introduced for children of poor families. This procedure serves to provide financial support to young learners and further increase their interest in studying.

Special scholarship procedure

Who can receive it?

  • Children from poor families are included in the "red" category;

  • Female students who study only for "excellent" grades.

Amount to be paid

Starting from June 1, 2025, female students of this category will receive a special scholarship in the amount of 2 times the basic scholarship.

Additional benefits

Opportunity to rest and health

Excellent children of poor families Federation of Trade Unions of Uzbekistan will have the right to free recreation and health care in the system's recreation centers.

Recovery of costs

All costs of this process will be fully covered by the state.

Conclusion

This decree creates great opportunities for children of poor families, especially excellent female students. They not only receive a double stipend, but also are completely exempted from health care and recreation expenses.
