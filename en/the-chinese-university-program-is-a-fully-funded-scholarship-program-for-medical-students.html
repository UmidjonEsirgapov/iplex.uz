The Chinese University Program is a fully funded scholarship program for medical students

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Applicants for the Master of Professional Medicine must have a HSK 4 Chinese Language Certificate with a score of 180 or higher and hold an MBBS or BDS degree.
Terms of Scholarships Masters Programs: 3 years; Preparatory course: 1 year if there is no HSK 4 certificate; Note: (1) For those who do not meet the entry requirements of the Chinese language proficiency level and wish to take remedial Chinese courses before majoring in Chinese, they must also provide the HSK certificate when applying.

General Information:

China Ministry of Education The "Chinese University Program" full scholarship established in 2008 aims to promote the Chinese higher education brand on an international basis. Dalian Medical University, one of the most prestigious medical universities in China, is currently accepting international students through this grant program. Applicants must not be Chinese citizens in good health.

2. Hold a bachelor's degree or higher and be under 35 years of age (as of September 1, 2022)

3. If the applicants are immigrants from China, they must have a foreign passport for at least 4 years and have lived abroad for 2 years.

4. Applicants for the Master of Professional Medicine must have a HSK 4 Chinese Language Certificate with a score of 180 or above and hold an MBBS or BDS degree.

5. Academic Master applicants must have a TOFEL (above 80) or IELTS (above 5.5) English language proficiency certificate. For those who cannot provide the above-mentioned standard English language test results, they can provide proof of having studied at a previous educational institution where the language of instruction is English, and are required to take an additional oral or written English language test organized by Dalian Medical University.

6. English-speaking applicants may be exempted from providing the above English language certificate.

Terms of Scholarships

Master's programs: 3 years;

Preparatory course: 1 year without HSK 4 certificate;

Note:

(1) Those who have not met the entry requirements of the Chinese language proficiency level and the basic Chinese language those wishing to take remedial Chinese courses before graduation must also provide an HSK certificate when applying. Only by passing the required HSK examination in the first year and meeting the entry requirements for the relevant study programs can they begin their main studies. Those who pass the screening may continue to apply for or enjoy the scholarship in the following academic year. Those who fail the examination will be suspended or disqualified from receiving the scholarship.

(3) The scholarship covers both basic education and the Chinese language course.

Benefits of the scholarship:

  • Tuition fees are waived;
  • Monthly stipend for living expenses - RMB 700;
  • Food for-RMB 300;
  • Insurance expenses will be covered;

Note: However, the student must cover other types of travel and accommodation expenses.
