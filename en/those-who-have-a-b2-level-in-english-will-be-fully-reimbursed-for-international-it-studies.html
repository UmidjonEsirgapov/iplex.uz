Those who have a B2 level in English will be fully reimbursed for international IT studies

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
It is known that in paragraph 1 of the decision PQ-51 dated February 1, 2023 of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On additional measures to support the training of qualified specialists in the field of digitization" the study and the procedure for covering up to 60% of the costs of obtaining these certificates and the procedure for fully covering the costs of obtaining international IT certificates for young people who have a B2 and higher level certificate in English were established.
For this purpose, the administrative regulation on the provision of public services on covering the costs of obtaining international IT certificates for young people was approved.
In this regard, youth international Submits an application electronically through the Unified interactive state services portal (my.gov.uz) for covering the costs of obtaining IT certificates.

The costs of obtaining and studying international IT certificates of a professional level for young people with a B2 and higher level of English will be fully covered

Cabinet of Ministers "Young people with international IT certificates Decision No. 95 dated February 17, 2024 "On additional measures to encourage specialists" was adopted.

It is known that the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On additional measures to support the training of qualified specialists in the field of digitization" No. PQ-51 dated February 1, 2023 In paragraph 1, the procedure for covering up to 60% of the costs of studying and obtaining these certificates for young people with international IT certificates and the procedure for fully covering the costs of obtaining international IT certificates for young people with a B2 and higher level certificate in English was established. The administrative regulation of public service provision on reimbursement was approved. According to it, starting from March 1:

The procedure for reimbursement of up to 60% of the expenses spent by young people with international IT-certificates on studying and obtaining these certificates was established. The procedure for obtaining international IT certificates at a professional level and fully covering the costs of study was established.

In this case, young people submit an electronic application for reimbursement of the costs of obtaining international IT certificates through the Unified interactive state services portal (my.gov.uz). the list is approved based on the list of international IT certificates formed annually by the Global Knowledge company and other internationally influential organizations, after studying the needs. — Official Data
