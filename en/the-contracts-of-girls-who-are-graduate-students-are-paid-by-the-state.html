The contracts of girls who are graduate students are paid by the state

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
In the video selection of the President, information was given about a number of wide opportunities for women in the education system.
According to it: starting from the new academic year, the contract money of girls studying at the master's degree will be fully covered from the budget (200 billion soums for 23 thousand girls). Every year, 50 girls are sent to prestigious foreign universities for bachelor's degree and 10 for master's degree. The educational contract of 150 girls who have lost their father or mother (total of 2.1 thousand) will be paid from the local budget. a target quota will be allocated.
For this purpose, at least 50% of the quota for women to become students will be allocated for specific sciences, technical and legal fields.
