Opportunity to earn income from practice! Special program for 500 students!

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
About practice: Duration: Students can participate in production practice at enterprises of economic sectors for 1 to 3 months. 500 places Payment conditions: Successful students will receive a monthly salary in the amount of 2 times the BHM (680 thousand soums).
This procedure is mainly designed for students of the graduate course, and only students who have successfully passed the competition can use this opportunity.

Internship fees only selected graduate course interns applies to students. This was announced in January based on the approved procedure.

About the practice:

  • Duration: Students can participate in production practice at enterprises of economic sectors for 1 to 3 months.
  • Based on the selection: The Ministry of Higher Education holds a competition among students every year.
  • Separated seats:
    • For the city of Tashkent: 45
    • For the Republic of Karakalpakstan and regions: 35 each
    • Total: 500 seats

Terms of payment:

  • Successful in the selection passed students receive a monthly salary in the amount of 2 times the BHM (680 thousand soums).
  • The salary is paid by the student's internship company, and funds are transferred from the Ministry of Higher Education to the company.
  • Enterprises may also provide additional incentives to students at their request.
