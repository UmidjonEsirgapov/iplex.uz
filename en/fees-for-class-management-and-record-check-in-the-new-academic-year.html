Class Management Fees

Class management fees are determined by the number of students in the class. These payments are paid as a percentage of the minimum salary for teachers (1,050,000 soums). Below is the amount of fees depending on the number of students in the class:

  • Up to 15 students: 26.4% (277,200 soums)
  • 16-20 students: 31.6% (331,800 soums)
  • 21-25 students student: 36.9% (387,450 soums)
  • 26-30 students: 42.1% (442,050 soums)
  • 31 and older students: 52.8% (554,400 soums)

These fees designed to make teachers more involved in classroom management and to give them additional motivation.

Payments for checking notebooks and written works

Teachers also get additional payments for checking notebooks and written works. These payments are made in the following order depending on the number of students in the class:

  • Up to 15 students: 8.8% (92,400 soums)
