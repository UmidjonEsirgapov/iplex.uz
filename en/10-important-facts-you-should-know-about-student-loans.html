10 important facts about education loan

Education loan aims to ease the cost of education, but it has its own terms and conditions. Here are 10 basic things you should know about education loans:

Preferential interest rates:

  • Women and girls are allocated education loans without interest.
  • Boys are given loans at the main rate of the Central Bank (13.5%).

Who is it for?

  • The loan is allocated to public and non-public higher education students.
  • Starting this year, it is only valid for full-time students.

Impact of grades:

  • average grade of students above the second year If it is lower than "3", they will be given an education loan only in the amount of 80% of the contract.

Dynamics of interest:

  • If the main rate of the Central Bank decreases, the percentage of the education loan will also decrease. But if the rate is increased, the interest will remain the same.

No loan for Supercontract:

  • Students admitted to study on the basis of "Supercontract" are not granted an education loan.

Preferential repayment period:

  • 7 years from the 7th month after the end of formal studies. is paid for the term. For example, a student studying in the 1st year can fully repay the loan in 11 years and 7 months.

Guarantee condition:

  • For children of families included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection", no collateral and guarantor is required.

Can be obtained annually:

  • Educational loan is available every academic year.

Online applications:

  • Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, applications for educational loans will be submitted through the "Educational Loan" information system (talim-krediti.mf.uz) and my.gov.uz.

Temporary military service:

  • Students who have completed military service and returned within a specified period have the opportunity to recover on the basis of a state grant.

Conclusion

Educational loans are aimed at easing the cost of education, but the rules related to it should be carefully observed. If you also want to use the education loan, consider these conditions and submit the application on time. — More information
