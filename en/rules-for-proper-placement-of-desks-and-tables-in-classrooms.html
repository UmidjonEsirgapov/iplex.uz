How to place desks and tables in classrooms?

Correct placement of desks and tables and chairs in classrooms plays an important role in increasing educational efficiency and creating a comfortable environment for students. Below are main rules for placing desks and tables and chairs:

1️⃣ Distance between rows of two-seater tables should be at least 60 cm. This ensures free movement of students.

2️⃣ The distance between the rows of desks and the outer wall should be 50-70 cm. This will help to provide proper light and air for students.

3️⃣ The distance between the rows of desks and the inner wall, fence or cabinet should be at least 50 cm. This ensures the safety of students.

4️⃣ The distance between the last desk and the wall should be 70 cm. This area allows easy movement for students.

5️⃣ The distance between the blackboard and the first desk should be 240 cm. This is necessary for students to see the blackboard correctly and effectively understand the lessons.

6️⃣ The distance between the blackboard and the last desk should not exceed 860 cm. This provides a comfortable distance for the eyes.

7️⃣ The distance between the lower side of the board and the floor should be 70-90 cm. This makes it easier for teachers and students to use the blackboard.

Conclusion

Following these rules will help create ergonomic conditions in classrooms. Properly arranged desks and tables make the learning process more efficient and comfortable. — Read on infoedu.uz
