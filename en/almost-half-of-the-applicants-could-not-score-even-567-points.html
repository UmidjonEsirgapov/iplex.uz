Almost half of the applicants could not score even 56.7 points

According to open data on the mandat.uzbmb.uz website, 888 thousand 472 applicants took the test this year.

439 thousand 337 applicants (49.4%) failed to score 56.7 points, which is the minimum passing score. So, they left the fight before the mandate. Because those who scored less than 56.7 points cannot enter even if there is a place in the quota.

The number of applicants who scored more than 56.7 is 449 thousand. (50.6%).

Compared to last year, our situation has slightly improved. Last year, 51.2% percent, more precisely, 550,000 applicants could not score even 56.7 points, this year this indicator fell to 49.4%.

In 2023, the worst result was recorded in Syrdaro region (54.9%), and the best result was recorded in Tashkent city (40.8%), just like last year (see the table above).

8 more than 50 percent of applicants in the region scored below 56.7 points. Last year there were 9 regions, this year Namangan region improved the result and dropped below 50%.
