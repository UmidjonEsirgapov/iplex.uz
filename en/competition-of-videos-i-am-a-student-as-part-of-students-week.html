Competition of videos "I am a student" as part of "Students' Week"!

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Youth Affairs Agency of Fergana region announces a competition of videos from 1 minute to 3 minutes long, between students of higher education institutions called "I am a student", reflecting the life of students.
The competition is online. will be held.
Requirements for the competition of videos depicting the life of students called "I am a student": students of any higher education institution of Fergana region can participate in the competition; A high-quality video with a duration of 1 to 3 minutes and no more than 50 megabytes is sent to Mentalabaman_bot; Interesting events in the life of students, the reaction of the participant to the conditions created for students in the educational institution should be reflected in the video.

Youth Affairs Agency of Fergana Region Announce the competition of videos between the students of higher education institutions, which reflect the life of students called "I am a student", lasting from from 1 minute to 3 minutes

The contest will be held online .

Requirements for the contest of video clips depicting the life of students called "I am a student":

  • Students of any higher education institution of the Fergana region can participate in the contest;
  • The duration should be from 1 to 3 minutes. A high-quality video of no more than 50 megabytes is sent to Mentalabaman_bot;
  • Interesting events in the life of students, the participant's reaction to the conditions created for students in the educational institution should be reflected in the video.

The creativity of the approach to the topics presented in the video will be given special attention.

The following prizes will be awarded to the winners of the competition:

  • 1st place modern tablet;
  • 2nd place electric oven;
  • 3rd place Thermopot.

Videos sent to the competition are evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • originality of the idea;
  • level of quality and approach;
  • compliance with the competition requirements.


The selection will be evaluated by the organizing committee consisting of experts in this field.
Note. Slide show videos will not be accepted.

Videos will be accepted from November 9 to November 14 at 11:59 p.m. Source: oliygoh.uz. — Official Data
