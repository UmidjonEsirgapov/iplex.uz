Is it possible to hire students for the position of psychologist?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the regulation approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 275 of December 21, 2005, persons studying in the pedagogic fields of universities are to be hired as key workers in schools to carry out pedagogic work without interfering with their studies from the 3rd year. It is possible.
However, students are not allowed to work as psychologists.
Qualification requirements of psychologists are specified in the "Qualification descriptions of the main positions of general secondary school (boarding school) employees" approved by the order of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education No. 247 of August 16, 2023.

Qualification requirements for the position of psychologist

A citizen who expressed a desire to work as a psychologist must have at least a bachelor's degree in psychology or higher education and completed a retraining course in additional psychology. And students do not meet these requirements, because they are persons with incomplete higher education.
