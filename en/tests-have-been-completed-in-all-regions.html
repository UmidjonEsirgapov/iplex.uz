Tests have been completed in all regions

Useful information for entrants and students:

Brief content:
It is known that on August 1, 2023, entrance tests for the undergraduate educational programs of higher education institutions of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, regions and Tashkent city for the 2023-2024 academic year began.
On August 13, all tests in the regions were completed.
