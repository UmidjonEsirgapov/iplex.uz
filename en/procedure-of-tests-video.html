Dates and shifts of the tests

In the 2024-2025 academic year, entrance exams to higher and professional educational institutions will begin on July 14. Tests are organized in 2 shifts per day:

  • 1st shift: 08:00
  • 2nd shift: 15:00

Applicants are required to arrive at the designated area at least one and a half hours before the start of the tests.

Permits

Applicants They should download the "Applicant permit" from my.uzbmb.uz. It is also necessary to print the permit.

Rules for coming to the test

  • It is necessary to come to the test with a passport (ID card) and applicant permit.
  • Applicants who come with a copy of their passport or other documents, as well as late applicants, will not be admitted to the test.
