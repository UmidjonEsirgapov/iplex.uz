To participate in the GTL scholarship competition, the following documents should be sent electronically (in the form of scanned copies) to scholarship@gtl.uz with the subject of the letter as "GTL scholarship": Reference from the place of study; Completed application form for participation in the contest; A letter of motivation in English, Russian or Uzbek (at the request of the applicant); Copies of patents or other documents confirming achievements in the field of science (if any); Copies of certificates/recommendations on participation in various projects/internships (if any); Consent to the processing of personal data, filled and signed by the Applicant in the form specified in Appendix 2 of these Rules; Certificates or documents confirming the level of knowledge of foreign languages ​​(if any); DETAILS about the procedure of the competition Privileges: The GTL Scholarship is awarded annually to the winners of the scholarship for one academic year.

Another scholarship program has been announced in the patronage project.

Name of the scholarship: Uzbekistan GTL Limited Liability Company scholarship program

Uzbekistan GTL limited liability company "Uzbekistan GTL" limited liability company under the jurisdiction of "Uzbekneftgaz" JSC announces III scholarship program for the academic year 2021-2022 in order to expand the personnel training program for the synthetic liquid fuel production plant, to provide material and social support to talented students.

The 10-month amount of the scholarship for students studying at the final stage of bachelor's and master's degrees will be paid monthly for 7 months.

How many people will be given: 12

Who is the scholarship for?

  • The candidate should not be older than 30 years old;
  • Bachelors and 1-2 year master's students studying in full-time form of higher education institutions can participate.

DETAILS about the educational direction of the participants

How to submit an application for participation in the competition and what is required?

To participate in the GTL scholarship competition, the following documents must be submitted in electronic form (scanned copies) in the form), indicating the subject of the letter as "GTL scholarship" and sending it to the e-mail address scholarship@gtl.uz:

  • A reference from the place of study;
  • A completed application form for participation in the competition;
  • A letter of motivation in English, Russian or Uzbek (at the request of the applicant);
  • Copies of patents or other documents confirming achievements in the field of science (if any) if any);
  • Certificates of participation in various projects / internships / copies of recommendations (if any);
  • Consent to the processing of personal data, filled and signed by the Applicant in the form indicated in Appendix 2 of these Rules;
  • Certificates or documents confirming the level of knowledge of foreign languages (if available);

About the competition procedure DETAILS

Benefits:

  • The GTL Scholarship is awarded annually to scholarship holders of the competition for one academic year.
  • The appointment of GTL scholarships is approved by the decision of Hayat, which consists of the company's management and responsible specialists.
  • During the academic year, the company conducts a monitoring of the progress of the scholarship holders receiving the GTL scholarship every quarter to determine whether they are in accordance with it.
  • GTL scholarship holders are included in the company's personnel reserve.
  • will provide practical assistance in industrial or pre-diploma practice.

Documents will be accepted until October 15. After that, the winners will be determined through an internal exam.
