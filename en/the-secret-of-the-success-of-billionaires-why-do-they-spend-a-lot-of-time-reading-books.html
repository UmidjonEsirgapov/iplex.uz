Why should you read a lot of books?

The most successful people in the world always emphasize the importance of reading. They read books every day or every week and expand their knowledge. Numerous studies and experiments confirm that reading books can lead to success in life and work.

What books do successful people read?

  • Bill Gates, the founder of Microsoft, reads 50 books a year. This means reading 1 book a week.
  • Engineer, writer and inventor Elon Musk claims that he reads a lot of books. When he was young, he read several hours a day.
  • Mark Zuckerberg, founder of Facebook, spends time reading one book every 2 weeks. This means 24 books a year.
  • The world's largest investor Warren Buffett spends 80% of his free time reading. Before starting his business, he read 500 pages of books a day.

It is not difficult to see the connection between the success of these people, their passion for learning and the number of books they mastered. They consume 20-50 times more information than an ordinary person, so you should not be surprised at their intelligence.

The excuse of not having time to read a book

Many people think that "I don't have time to read a book". But research shows that the modern person spends 608 hours on social media and 1642 hours on YouTube, movies and TV a year. For a total of 2250 hours, more time is spent on mostly useless material. By spending 20-50% of this time reading books, you can increase your knowledge and achieve success in life.
