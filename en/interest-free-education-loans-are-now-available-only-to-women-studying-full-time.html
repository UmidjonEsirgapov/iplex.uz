Interest-free education loans are now available only to women studying full-time

Brief content:
By the Decree of the President (PF-82 of 27.05.2024), the decision PQ-323 of July 18, 2022 was amended.
According to it, now only day education A preferential interest-free educational loan will be given to studying women.
Which form of education will it be given?

By the Decree of the President (PF-82 dated 27.05.2024) decision PQ-323 of July 18, 2022 was amended. According to it, preferential interest-free education loans are given to females studying only in the form of full-time education.

Who will receive the loan?

  • Students of all higher education institutions in Uzbekistan (branches of public, private and foreign HEIs);
  • Secondary special and professional to students of educational institutions (technicum, vocational college, etc.).

Which form of education is given?

  • Daytime;

It is not given to distance education.

If it is given at 14%, why is the percentage it is said?

True, the education loan is actually given at a rate of 14 percent. But women do not pay this percentage, the state pays.

When does the repayment start?

The main part of the education loan is returned for 7 years, starting from the seventh month after the end of the student's official study period (that is, six months).

What documents are needed?

  • to the bank application;
  • identity document;
  • payment-contract agreement with the educational institution;
  • a document on the provision of education loan repayment for family members who have a permanent source of income and other third parties as joint borrowers.
