What are the changes in 2021 regarding obtaining a visa to study in the United States?

International students studying in US universities in 2021 the spring semester is going to be continued online as well, writes the grant channel for Bizli. So, due to the coronavirus pandemic, the rule adopted in March 2020 will be valid this year as well. This was reported by the spokesperson of the US Immigration and Customs Enforcement agency.

Before the pandemic, foreigners enrolled in US educational institutions were required to be physically present in America in order to maintain their student visa status. Since the beginning of the pandemic, they have been granted the right to enter the United States even from abroad.

The 2021 schedule has not yet been officially announced, but international educational organizations and educational institutions are informing foreigners that the schedule has not changed.

How will this affect current students? Foreign nationals who have received it can continue their studies online in the United States or in their own country. Their current immigration status will remain until the end of their studies. Even if the classes are held entirely online, they can return to the US at any time.

Those admitted to the university after March 9, 2020 can take all courses online from their home country, but in order to be admitted to the US, at least one class must be taken at the university, i.e. in a classroom.

What will the new students do?

Enrollees for the spring semester of 2021 may take classes 100 percent online if they are already in the United States.

International enrollees for this year's spring semester will not be able to enter the United States if they are taking their education entirely online. At least one class must be taken face-to-face at the university to enter the US, which is to obtain a visa.

If the university is offering all classes entirely online, foreign students will not issue the F-20 form needed to apply for a visa. So, in this case, you will not be granted a visa.
