Who is temporarily exempt from professional certification?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
By the relevant decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan (12.02.2024 No. 87), the following pedagogues are exempted from professional certification for a certain period: Pregnant women, women on pregnancy or maternity leave, during their vacation and after starting work for one year; Women with children under the age of three who are engaged in pedagogical activity (until the child turns three years old); Pedagogical staff undergoing long-term treatment (for more than three months) during the period of treatment and for six months after starting work; Pedagogical personnel sent to study, retraining and advanced training courses for three months or longer in the republic and a foreign country, for six months after they start working.
