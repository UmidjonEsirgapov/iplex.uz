How long after receiving the contact letter should I submit the documents?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Students who have been recommended to transfer their studies and received a contact letter must submit their documents to the educational institution to which they are transferring within 10 working days or within the period specified by the university.
Reason: Decision No. 393 of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated June 20, 2017

Students who have been recommended to transfer their studies and received a contact letter should submit their documents to the educational institution being transferred within 10 working days or within a period determined by the university.

If the student does not submit the personal summary folder within the specified period, his application to transfer or resume his studies will be rejected.
