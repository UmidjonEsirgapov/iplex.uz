General information:

Private research institute established in 2009 in Saudi Arabia. The university offers research and graduate education programs in English. In terms of research, KAUST ranks 16th in the world. The KAUST internship program is open to all international students from all over the world. Currently accepting applications for the KAUST VSRP Fully Funded Internship Program in Saudi Arabia. This internship program can be applied by 3-4 undergraduate students, master's and doctoral students studying in fields such as food, art, engineering, computer, business and water. Every year, 130 students are selected from more than 150 current projects from all over the world. fees;

  • Medical insurance;
  • Research;
  • Social activities;
  • Cultural initiatives;
  • Accommodation (private dormitory);
  • Monthly salary $1000;
