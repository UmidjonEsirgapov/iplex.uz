All-expenses-paid grant for summer courses in the Netherlands

In order to strengthen the knowledge of international law students and encourage them, the prestigious Netherlands The Hague Academy of International Law organized summer and winter courses for students.

These courses are held in English or French. You can learn more about the courses here.

15% of the program participants will be able to receive financial support from this academy. Scholarship recipients will have the following opportunities:

  • Free study in these courses;
  • 350 euros for travel expenses;
  • 550 euros for accommodation;

Requirements:

  • Citizens of any country can participate in the program;
  • Candidates are bachelors. must have completed his/her degree in the field of international law or be studying in the 3rd year;
  • Must be fluent in English or French;
  • Must have strong interest and knowledge to participate in the program.
