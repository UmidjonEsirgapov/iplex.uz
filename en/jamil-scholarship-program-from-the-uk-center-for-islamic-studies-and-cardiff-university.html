The Center for Islamic Studies in the UK has started accepting applications for the Jameel Scholarship at Cardiff University.

Established in 2005, the Islamic Center in the UK promotes a better understanding of Islam and the lives of Muslims in Britain through high-quality education and research.

Its work has been critical to the plight of Muslims in contemporary Britain. solves issues. The Center provides unique training and research opportunities for those seeking employment in a range of academic and non-academic careers.

The Jameel Scholarships are awarded to outstanding students at Cardiff University - those who have the intellect and determination to apply their knowledge to the benefit of Muslim communities in the UK.

MA Scholarships.
Four Masters for the academic year 2022/2023 scholarship is allocated. These scholarships are available to study on the Islam in Modern Britain (MA) program and consist of full UK tuition fees, an annual stipend of £15,896 and a £1,000 stipend towards your research costs. From 2022/2023, the scholarship is now also available for part-time study. Those taking part in this program will be awarded a stipend and research payment over two years.

PhD Fellowship.
One PhD Fellowship will be available from September 2022.

Research proposals will be made on topics that clearly match the research interests and expertise of UK Islamic Center staff. Proposals must demonstrate outstanding academic excellence, potential and relevance to Muslims in the UK. The three-year PhD scholarship covers full tuition fees for the UK Religious Studies programme, an annual stipend of £15,896 and £2,500 per year towards research costs.

Application:
By 13 June 2022 Religious and Theological Studies (PhD) or Islam in Contemporary Britain (MA) programs, apply online directly to the university.

If you wish to apply for a scholarship, please complete the Jameel Scholarship application form by June 13, 2022.
