Students of 10-11th grade can choose the subjects they want

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, new educational programs will be gradually implemented in general secondary educational institutions.
On the basis of the new curriculum in which 14 compulsory subjects in grades 10-11 have been improved, students will be given the opportunity to choose 8 compulsory subjects and 2 optional subjects (chemistry-biology, mathematics, physics, history-law, mother tongue and literature-foreign language). new educational programs will be put into practice in general secondary educational institutions step by step.
