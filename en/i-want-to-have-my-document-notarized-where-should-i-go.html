There are 2 different methods of nostrification:

  • Method 1: You can get it independently online through the single interactive state services portal (https://my.gov.uz/oz). In this case, the directory will be ready in about 10 working days.

To do this:

  • You enter the portal (https://my.gov.uz/oz).
  • You enter the system through OneID (if OneID) by pressing the login button. If you don't have OneID, you will register.
  • Then you will select the Education section from the list of services on the portal.
  • You will enter the diploma verification section.
  • You will click on the service use button and enter the necessary information.
  • You will make the payment (through Upay, Payme or Click applications). The amount of payment is equal to 2.7 of BHM (as of April 20, 2023, 810,000 soums) for individuals.

Method 2. Through the State Services Center. In this case, you can apply to any State Service Center that is closest and convenient to you with the necessary documents.

What documents are needed:

  • A document on education in foreign countries and a copy of its annex:
  • A document on education in foreign countries and its annex translated into the official language of the Republic of Uzbekistan notarized copy.
