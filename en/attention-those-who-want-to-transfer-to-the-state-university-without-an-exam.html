Is it possible to transfer studies from a non-state higher education institution to a state university without an exam? there is good news for students studying at a higher educational institution: they can now transfer their studies to state universities without exams. This opportunity is implemented on the basis of a super contract.

Basis: In accordance with the Presidential Decree

The right to transfer education is provided on the basis of Presidential Decree No. PQ-279 of June 15, 2022. According to paragraph 7 "g" of the decision, students who want to transfer from a non-state higher education institution to a field that is not compatible with a state higher education institution can study on the basis of a differentiated payment contract (super contract). will be done. In this case, the applicant sends a request through his personal cabinet and attaches the necessary documents.

Important: The text that must be written so that the application is not rejected

In order for the working group to understand your conscious desire to transfer studies based on the super contract and not to reject the application, write the following text in the Reason for transfer of studies section of the cabinet:

I (applicant's F.I.Sh) want to transfer my studies from the non-state higher education institution (the name of the HEI to which the applicant is applying) to the educational field that is not compatible with my current field of study. I agree to study on the basis of a one-time payment equal to the minimum amount of the differentiated payment-contract in accordance with paragraph 7, subparagraph "g" of the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-279 of June 15, 2022. I am fully acquainted with the above decision of the President. Taking this into account, I ask you to consider my application based on my consent and give practical help.
