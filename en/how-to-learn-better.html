When learning something, don't try to learn everything in one sitting.

It is more effective if you break the process into small intervals, such as 40-50 minutes. Continuous work has been scientifically proven to cause brain fatigue, and if you don't give it a break, it simply stops receiving information. And this can lead to burnout.
