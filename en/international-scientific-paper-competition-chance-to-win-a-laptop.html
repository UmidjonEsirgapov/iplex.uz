International Scientific Paper Competition: Chance to Win a Laptop

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
The competition will be held in two areas: · The best scientific article (CA) on Central Asia: The article will raise questions about the impact of ozone layer depletion in Central Asia and make practical recommendations for prevention and prevention of ozone layer depletion, including the role of women and girls in this regard; · Overall Best Research Paper: The paper raises questions about the global impact of ozone depletion and makes practical recommendations for mitigating and preventing ozone depletion, including the role of women and girls; · Two (2) winners (one male and one female) will be awarded for each category.

General information:

Uzbekistan State Committee for Ecology and Environmental Protection and UN Development programs announced the selection of scientific articles on "Ozone layer and life on Earth". This contest is open to students of all levels (Bachelor's, Master's, Doctorate) of all accredited universities around the world. All articles are accepted in English and Russian languages.

Requirements for candidates:

  • This competition is open to students of all levels of accredited universities around the world (i.e. undergraduate, graduate, doctoral).

The competition is two-way. will be held:

· Best Scientific Paper (CA) on Central Asia: The paper raises questions about the effects of ozone layer depletion in Central Asia and provides practical recommendations for prevention and prevention of ozone layer depletion, including the role of women and girls in this regard;

· Best Scientific Paper Overall: questions are raised about the global impact of ozone depletion and practical recommendations for mitigating and preventing ozone depletion, including the role of women and girls;

· Two (2) winners (one male and one female) will be awarded in each category. "Winners in each direction will be awarded a laptop "

All finished articles, Appendices and electronic versions of scientific articles, including a link to the article published on web platforms or a scanned copy, will be sent to ozon.o3.uz@gmail.com by August 1, 2022, according to the specified form. The list of participants will be drawn up by the Working Group based on the applications received at ozon.o3.uz@gmail.com;

The purpose of the competition:

· to increase the awareness of the population about the ozone layer of the Earth;

· The importance of the ozone layer in the life of all living organisms (plants and animals) and the consequences of the depletion of the ozone layer show;

· Increasing the role of women in protecting the ozone layer;

· Increasing environmental awareness and responsibility in protecting the ozone layer.

IMPORTANT: To submit documents, first fill out this application form .

The following criteria for evaluating scientific articles of the international competition is used.

1. An article is useful to decision makers and the public, in that it defines a problem or problem and identifies factors related to it, which help create a clear understanding of what needs to be decided and can influence choices between alternatives. - 20 points

2. An article supports/contradicts an existing theory or helps develop a new concept or theory. - 10 points

3. The article raises new important issues, including the gender aspect in the protection of the ozone layer. - 10 points

4. The article is written according to the generally accepted rules for scientific articles:

- The scientist's article should be up to 8000 words, excluding the list of used literature

- The scientist's article should be published on web platforms or scientific publications

- citing scientific sources - 10 points

5. Follows the method rigorously and clearly explains why the data supports the findings - 10 points

6. The material is connected with previous research in this or a related field, which helps to better understand the arguments of the article - 10 points

7. The work tells the story, is well written and understandable, the arguments are logical and internally consistent - 10 points

8. Relevance of title and content - 10 points

9. Analysis of the indicated issue was carried out - 10 points
