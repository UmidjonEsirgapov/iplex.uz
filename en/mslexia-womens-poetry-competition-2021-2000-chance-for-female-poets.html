Mslexia Women's Poetry Competition 2021 - £2000 chance for female poets

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Mslexia Women’s Poetry Competition 2021- Organized by Misslexia, an opportunity for all female poets to win up to £2000 with their poems written on any topic.
Prizes: 1st place - £2,000; 2nd place - £500; 3rd place - £250; £250 for the best unpublished poem.
Poems of the above 4 poets and an additional 6 will be published on the Mislexia website.

Mslexia Women's Poetry Competition 2021 - Organized by Mislexia for all women poets to win up to £2000 with their poems written on any topic

Awards:

  • For 1st place - £2,000;
  • For 2nd place - £500;
  • For 3rd place - £250;
  • For best unpublished poem - £250. The poet can submit up to 3 poems.

    Requirements:

    • The poem can be in English on any topic and of any length;
    • The poem must be written by the poet herself and must not be the work of another author;
    • The poem must not have been published before;
