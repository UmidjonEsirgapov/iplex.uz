Excellence scholarships: a fully funded grant program for studying at the master's level in Italy in all areas

The opportunity to study at the most prestigious universities in Italy at the master's level has been created for all foreign students through the grant program "Excellence Scholarship"

. This grant is awarded to a total of 155 students. This grant is offered only to students admitted to the master's programs of the University of Milan

Requirements for candidates:

  • Citizens of any foreign country can apply;
  • Be able to be admitted to one of the master's courses of the University of Milan;
  • At least a B2 certificate is required for English-language programs;

Benefits:

  • A total of 155 grants are available, of which 100 are fully funded grants that cover all costs associated with education. The remaining 55 grant holders will receive 6,000 euros to cover university-related expenses;

Note: There are no separate applications for this grant, it is enough if you have been accepted to this Milan University. You will be among the candidates. Now, whether or not you will be awarded a grant from among these candidates will be evaluated based on your undergraduate grades (20) as well as your CV (10). Total score is 30. Applicants must score above 21.
