A branch of the Italian University may be opened in Bukhara

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The Embassy of Uzbekistan in Italy organized the next video conference on the establishment of a student campus (branch) of Luigi Vanvitelli University of Campania (UniCampania) in Bukhara. and heads of universities, vice-chancellors and professors of Italian universities, as well as the honorary consul of Uzbekistan in the Campania and Molise regions, Vittorio Giorgi, took part.
According to him, the materials presented by "UniCampania" were studied, and currently, based on them, a quadrilateral agreement was signed between Higher and secondary special education, the Ministry of Health, Bukhara regional government and "UniCampania", as well as education a "road map" is being prepared for the implementation of the project.
This proposal was supported by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

Establishment of a student campus (branch) of Luigi Vanvitelli University of Campania (UniCampania) in Bukhara by the Embassy of Uzbekistan in Italy the next video conference was organized

The meeting was attended by the heads of the Higher and Secondary Special Education, Health Ministries of Uzbekistan, Bukhara Regional Government and Universities, vice-chancellors and professors of the Italian University, as well as Vittorio Giorgi, Honorary Consul of Uzbekistan in the Campania and Molise regions.

Uzbekistan Ambassador Otabek Akbarov thanked the leadership of UniCampania for organizing a visit to this university at the end of March, for making a detailed presentation about the university, and for the proposal of Italian representatives to establish a student campus in Bukhara. The ambassador expressed his readiness to organize a visit of UniCampania management to Uzbekistan in May-June to discuss all organizational issues of this project and to sign the relevant contract.

Deputy Minister of Higher and Secondary Special Education Uzokboy Begimkulov welcomed UniCampania's readiness to establish its campus in Bukhara region. He noted that this project can greatly contribute to the development of mutual relations in the fields of education and culture. According to him, the materials presented by "UniCampania" are being studied, and currently, on the basis of them, a four-way agreement is being prepared between the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, the Ministry of Health, the Bukhara Regional Government and "UniCampania", as well as a "roadmap" for the implementation of the educational project. Gianfranco Nicoletti's greetings to the participants. He also stated that he is ready to start practical work to establish a branch of the Italian University in Bukhara soon.

In particular, the "UniCampania" student campus envisages the training of personnel for bachelor's and master's programs in four areas: construction sciences and technology (engineering), design, nursing and architecture. Education is carried out on the basis of the Italian educational program with the involvement of Italian teachers. Graduates will have diplomas recognized in Italy and Europe.

The representative of the rector, professor Sergio Minucci, said that it will take some time to create a full-fledged branch of the university in Bukhara, and in order to start the educational process as soon as possible, he suggested starting courses in parallel in September of this year in the form of a branch of the University of Campania named after Luigi Vanvitelli, based on the existing universities in the city.

This proposal was supported by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education. As the first stage of mutual cooperation, it was stated that it is possible to organize joint educational programs in the field of engineering, architecture and construction at the Bukhara Engineering-Technical University, and on the basis of the Bukhara State Medical Institute in the field of medicine. "All the necessary conditions are being created for the placement and operation of the student campus to start joint activities," he said.

Rector of Bukhara Engineering-Technical University Nusrat Barakayev and Rector of Bukhara State Medical Institute Shuhrat Teshayev made a brief presentation about the educational institutions and expressed their opinions on the directions of cooperation with UniCampania. — Official Data
