Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Driving school expenses will be reimbursed for young people included in the "Youth Register" According to the legislation of Uzbekistan, young people included in the "Youth Register" will be provided with the opportunity to cover the costs of studying at a driving school.
This assistance is intended to support the development of driving skills of young people.
Cost reimbursement procedure Reimbursed amount: up to 1,320,000 soums (4 times the BHM).

The expenses of driving school are reimbursed to young people included in the "Youth register". This assistance is aimed at supporting the development of young people's driving skills.

Procedure of reimbursement of expenses

  • Reimbursable amount: up to 1 million 320 thousand soums (4 times of BHM).
  • Documents: copy of application and driving school contract.
  • Review period: applications are considered within 8 days.

What is required?

  1. Apply to the youth affairs department of the district or city.
  2. The name of the organization, STIR, course name, monthly payment amount and term must be specified in the training course contract.

Who can apply?

This opportunity is available to young people who are included in the "Youth register" and want to study at a driving school. intended. — Official Data
