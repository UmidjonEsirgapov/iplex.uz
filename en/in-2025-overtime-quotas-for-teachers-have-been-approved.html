In 2025, overtime quotas for teachers have been approved

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The amount of general quotas has been set for the payment of a monthly bonus to a total of 1,500 pedagogues across the Republic.
Distribution by educational areas Quotas have been set for pedagogues in general secondary educational institutions in the following areas: Primary education pedagogues - a total of 314 people; Pedagogues of exact and natural sciences - a total of 318 people; Foreign language pedagogues - 230 in total; Pedagogues of social and philological sciences - a total of 318 people; Pedagogues of applied sciences - a total of 220 people.
This is one of the important measures aimed at increasing the financial incentives of teachers.

Regional quotas for qualified pedagogues in 2025 have been announced

Order of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education No. 408 of December 20, 2024 mainly approved the quotas for 2025 for pedagogic personnel with monthly bonuses. This decision was implemented in order to encourage qualified pedagogues working in general secondary education and specialized educational institutions across the republic. Of these:

  • 1,400 were allocated to general secondary educational institutions,
  • 100 were allocated to specialized educational institutions.

Distribution by educational areas

Quotas were set for pedagogues in general secondary educational institutions in the following areas:

  • Pedagogues of primary education - a total of 314;
  • Pedagogues of concrete and natural sciences - a total of 318;
  • Pedagogues of foreign languages - a total of 230;
  • Pedagogues of social and philological sciences - a total of 318;
  • Pedagogues of applied sciences - a total of 220 people.

A total of 100 quotas were approved for specialized educational institutions, and they were distributed according to the directions of exact and natural sciences, foreign languages and social sciences.

amount of incentive

According to the results of the competition, qualified pedagogues who have the highest score will receive a monthly bonus in the amount of 5.5 times the minimum wage. This is one of the important measures aimed at increasing the financial incentive of teachers.
