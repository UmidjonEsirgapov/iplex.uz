Additional admissions for master's degrees have begun: an opportunity for those who have not been recommended for studentship

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
With the relevant statement of the State Commission, for the 2024/2025 academic year, applicants who have a foreign language certificate of the required level in the admission process to master's majors of higher education institutions of the republic, and who are not recommended for studentship within the admission quota, will receive a basic fee-contract amount determined by higher education institutions. It was allowed to accept on the basis of a payment-contract no more than 2 times.
Applicants who expressed their desire to study on the basis of additional admission should directly apply to the admission committee of the higher education institution.
