General information:

In 2021, the summer "Ibrat Language Camp" organized by the initiative of the Youth Affairs Agency of Uzbekistan has started accepting applications for the 1st season of 2022 (to be held in Bostonliq district, Tashkent region) for 2022.

These languages The camp can be attended by young people of all nationalities, regardless of their social background, from 16 to 25 years of age, who can speak English at least B1 level. Requirements for candidates: Participants must be between 16-25 years of age; English language proficiency at least B1 level is required. will be done. A certificate or a document proving it is not required;

  • They should also have critical thinking skills and be socially active;

    • Benefits;

    • The possibility of free participation in this 1-week camp (all expenses are covered, including food and accommodation, as well as transportation from Tashkent to Bostanlik travel expenses will also be covered);
    • Because the 1st season of this camp is specialized in languages, language skills training and seminars;
    • Meetings with experienced entrepreneurs;
    • Various types of sports training and various activities will be held during the event;

    Registration process (admission process consists of 2 stages consists of):

    • Registration for the "Ibrat" language camp has started for the 1st stage. Stage 1 will take place in the form of a test. After pressing the start test button on the platform, 20 minutes are allotted for the test. A total of 10 minutes is given for critical thinking questions, 5 minutes for each of the 2 questions. Note: the test on the platform is opened only every 2 hours (10:00, 12:00, 14:00, 16:00, 18:00, 20:00). You can enter and work on the test only during the specified hours. Apply here to register.
    • Step 2 Young people who have scored the highest points from the 1st stage will now be interviewed online by the organizers (in English), and 250-300 candidates who have scored higher than this interview will get the opportunity to participate in this camp.
