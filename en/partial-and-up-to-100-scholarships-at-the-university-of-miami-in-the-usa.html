The University of Miami in the United States offers its "International Scholarship", from a minimum of $1,000 to a full 100%, for those who wish to study at the undergraduate level. grant programs were announced. Get acquainted with the directions of this university in detail here!

Benefits of the grant:

  • Those admitted to the program can be provided with a scholarship from $1,500 to $12,000 . However, other types, such as living expenses, travel tickets, are not covered;

Requirements for the candidate:

  • The candidate must have graduated from school/college/lyceum with high grades;
  • Activity and achievements in social life are taken into account;
  • Language skills must be very good (At least IELTS 6.5, TOEFL (IBT 80));
  • If the candidate has SAT, ACT certificates, they can send them voluntarily;

Important: The deadline is December 1, 2022. There is no separate registration for this grant program, only candidates admitted to this university, if they meet the above requirements, will become direct participants of the competition!
