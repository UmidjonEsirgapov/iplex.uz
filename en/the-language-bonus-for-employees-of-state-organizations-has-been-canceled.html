The language bonus for employees of state organizations has been canceled

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Previous situation: starting from January 1, 2022, up to 20% overtime was paid to employees of state organizations with national and international certificates in foreign languages.
Conclusion This decision is based on the previously valid foreign language certificates for employees of state organizations. will stop the practice of bonus payment.
This change was implemented in order to use the budget funds more effectively.

According to the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 455 adopted on December 25, 2024, the payment of a bonus of up to 20% in addition to the position salary to employees of state organizations with national and international certificates in foreign languages is canceled

The content of the decision

  • Repealed provision: Clause 7, paragraph 3 of the decision No. PQ-5117 of May 19, 2021 was deleted.
  • Previous situation: Starting from January 1, 2022, positions for employees of state organizations with national and international certificates in foreign languages up to 20% salary bonus was paid.
  • New change: starting January 1, 2025, this bonus will no longer be paid.

Impact of the decision

  • Effect on employees: Overtime payments for employees of all levels of government agencies are canceled
  • Financial result: It will be possible to save budget funds and direct them to other priority areas.

Conclusion

This decision stops the practice of premium payment based on foreign language certificates for employees of state organizations. This change was made in order to use the budget funds more effectively. — More information
