About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
On April 6, 2023, Cabinet of Ministers Resolution No. 143 "On measures to determine the procedure for admission to higher education and post-higher education at the Law Enforcement Academy of the Republic of Uzbekistan"
In this decision, it is determined that the registration of candidates for the "Investigative activity" undergraduate education of the Academy will be carried out by the Knowledge Assessment Agency.
For this reason, the "Law Enforcement Academy of the Republic of Uzbekistan" section was opened on the website of the Knowledge Assessment Agency, my.uzbmb.uz, and an opportunity was created to register applicants.
