Many people have a habit of leaving important things in life until the last minute. This is called procrastination. Here are examples of this situation in our lives and ways to overcome it.

The habit of doing things at the last minute

  • Exam preparation: Even if we have 6 months, we start the preparation in the last 1 week.
  • Homework: Even if we have 1 week, we have to do it in the last 30 minutes. We will try.
  • Thesis: Although we have 1 year to write, we will finish it in the last 1 month.

This is because of the natural tendency of our brain to minimize the time needed to reach the goal. In this situation, we consider only 10 days to be enough, not 10 months. But we are forced to work under a lot of pressure in such a short time.

How to overcome "Procrastination"

  1. Planning: Get into the habit of breaking each task into small parts and doing it according to daily plans.
  2. Time management: Instead of working on a big project at the last minute, constantly do small tasks. more productive.
  3. Reduce stress: Reduce stress and improve work quality by planning your time properly.

Conclusion

No matter how productive it may seem, working at the last minute has long-term negative consequences. Planning work on time not only saves time, but also reduces stress.

Plan your life and put aside "procrastination" - this is the most correct way to success. — More information
