Silk Roads Youth Research Grant: $10,000 grant for research on the Great Silk Road!

Under the program, UNESCO is awarding grants to 12 research projects exploring the shared heritage and history of the Great Silk Road.

For whom:
For researchers under 35 years of age.

For applicants requirements:
-Must be studying at the master's or PhD level;
-The research project can be submitted by one researcher or a team of several researchers;
-The proposed project must be able to provide important conclusions for better understanding of the general heritage and history of the Great Silk Road, and have the potential to achieve concrete results.

The project must be submitted in English!

Opportunities for winners:
Each of the 12 winners will receive a $10,000 grant to carry out their research.

Registration period: Until May 31, 2022.
