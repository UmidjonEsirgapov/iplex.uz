The National Guard Institute was reorganized under a different name

About the expected changes in the education system:

Presidential Decree (No. PQ-5077, 15.04.2021) "On measures to further improve the system of training professional personnel in the field of public safety" was adopted.

According to the decision, the Military-Technical Institute of the National Guard It was reorganized as the University of Public Security.

The university prepares qualified personnel for the following:

For the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the National Guard - implementation of public order, traffic safety and passport system requirements;
For the Armed Forces and law enforcement agencies - in military diplomacy, jurisprudence and economics, as well as engineering-technical, educational-psychological and command-tactical activities;
guarding for bodies with structures - in all areas of the field of security.

From the 2021/2022 academic year, the university will introduce a bachelor's and master's system.

The admission quota is up to 300 cadets for the bachelor's degree, and up to 100 trainees for part-time education.
