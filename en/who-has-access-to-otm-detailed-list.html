Graduates of Temurbek School

Graduates who graduated from the "Temurbek School" with an honors diploma are given the right to enter higher military or specialized institutions of higher education according to the relevance of the "Temurbek School" ministries and agencies without tests, based on the results of successful military-professional selection events.

Winners of the International Olympiads
