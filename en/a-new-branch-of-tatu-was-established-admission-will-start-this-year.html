A new branch of TATU was established. Admission will start this year

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:

was adopted According to the decision, the following were established: Nurafshan Branch; "Digital Library" at the University Information Resource Center.
Application and study of programming, robotics, "Internet of Things", and artificial intelligence technologies in order to improve digital skills in all strata of the population is one of the main tasks of the Branch. (No. 288, 07.05.2021) was adopted
