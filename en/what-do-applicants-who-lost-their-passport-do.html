Applicants who have lost their passport for various reasons can easily take the test by getting a new ID card.

Even if the passport series and number on the permit are different, this will not be a problem. The main thing should be the identity document of the applicant. In addition, even if the series and number change when receiving a new passport, the JShSIR (identification) number of each person will not change, they will identify it through that. It's easy to get ID cards now, it only takes about 1 day, it's not as long as before.

Note: dear applicants, keep your passport or ID card safe. If it is not necessary, do not use it until the day of the test, let it stand in a clear place.
