What professions can graduates of the software engineering branch take?

About the expected changes in the educational system:

Brief content:
Graduates of the software engineering department can occupy the following professions: Professional automated and computer systems mathematical and software; Automated design systems; Software of information processing, microprocessor systems; Processes of design, adjustment, production and implementation of information and communication systems and technical and software tools; Formation of electronic document circulation and information exchange in databases of state interactive services electronic government systems; Development of software for forming electronic document circulation and information exchange in databases in electronic government systems; They will have the right to work as a junior researcher in research organizations

Graduates of software engineering can take the following professions:

  • Mathematical and software support of professional automated and computer systems;
  • Automated design systems;
  • Information processing, software of microprocessor systems;
  • Processes of design, configuration, production and implementation of technical and software tools of information-communication systems and technologies;
  • Formation of information exchange in electronic document circulation and databases of state interactive services in electronic government systems;
  • Electronic document circulation in electronic government systems and information in databases development of software for exchange formation;
