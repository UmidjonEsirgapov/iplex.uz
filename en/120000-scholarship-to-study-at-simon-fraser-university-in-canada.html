Canada's famous Simon Fraser University offers scholarships to students who have demonstrated high achievement and good results in both academic and extracurricular activities. This scholarship is open to Canadian and international students enrolled in any undergraduate program for the fall semester.

The amount of the grant is $120,000. This amount is enough to pay for tuition, compulsory university fees and living in Canada. The amount of the scholarship varies depending on the type of grant, the details are given here.

You can get detailed information about tuition fees here.

Requirements:

  • Citizens of any country can participate in the program;
  • For the grant, you must first be admitted to the bachelor's level of this university;
  • Admission after completion, candidates must show at least 90% of these courses or score 31 IB points;
  • Must be fluent in English.

Note! Before applying for a scholarship, you must register with the university.

Applications for the scholarship will be accepted until December 15, 2021, and related documents will be accepted until January 31, 2022.
