General Information:

Computer Science Internships in Germany: Institutes of Informatics (Saarbrücken), Software Systems (Saarbrucken and Kaiserslautern) and Security and Privacy (Bochum) offer research internships in all areas of computer science.

Benefits:

  • All practitioners provided with a monthly stipend;
  • Free (shared) housing;
  • Travel expenses to and from the internship institute are also covered.
  • Internships usually last from 12 to 14 weeks, but may be longer.
  • Max Planck Institutes are internationally renowned research with world-class professors in the full spectrum of computer science. offers a community.
  • Interns will gain direct hands-on experience in conducting advanced research in computer science. Successful practitioners often present at the best scientific conferences. An internship at the Max Planck Institute is a great way to start your future research career;

Who can participate:

  • Interns from around the world with a background in computer science (standard 3-year undergraduate computer science students before the start of the internship) and exceptional Bachelors, Masters and PhD students with an interest in research in the field .
  • Representatives of the field who have the same background and are interested in academic computer science research can also apply.
  • Students looking for bachelor's or master's thesis projects can also apply. If the student's university allows, the internship project can be turned into a dissertation.
  • Internships are also a great opportunity for doctoral students to learn about computer science research before starting a research career.
  • Candidates with different academic backgrounds and majors are also welcome to participate, such as social sciences, economics, law, humanities, etc.

Important dates:

  • Apply by December 1 for internships starting in May and August;
  • Apply at least 4 months before the intended start date for internships starting at any other time of the year.

  • Max Planck Institute for Informatics (Saarbrücken)
  • Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (Kaiserslautern and Saarbrücken)
  • Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy (Bochum)
