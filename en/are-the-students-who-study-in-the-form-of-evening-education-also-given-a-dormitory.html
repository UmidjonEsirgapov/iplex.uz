Are the students who study in the form of evening education also given a dormitory?

Dormitory for students studying in the form of evening education issue

Many people are interested in the dormitory issue for students studying in the form of evening education. According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 376 dated August 14, 2023, students studying in full-time education will be provided with accommodation first. Students studying in other forms of education cannot be accommodated in accommodation until they are fully accommodated.

Evening education students can also be accommodated if there is space left

If there is a vacancy in student accommodation after full-time students are accommodated, students studying in evening education will also be provided with a dormitory.

Priority for students belonging to privileged category

If a student studying in evening education falls into a defined category of preferences, then he has the right to priority in the matter of dormitory. Such students are the following:

  • children of "Mehribanlik houses", orphans and students deprived of parental care;

  • children of families in need of social protection included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection", "Iron Register", "Women's Register" and "Youth Register";

  • Those with group I and II disabilities. students;

  • Students from the same family who are studying in full-time education on the basis of two or more contracts;

  • Students who won international and republican science Olympiads, examinations;

  • Students who won at the national stage of "Zakovat";

  • Students who are council chairpersons or floor captains in student housing.

Conclusion

So, students studying in the form of evening education are provided with a dormitory only in two cases:

  1. If there is space left after the placement of day education students.

  2. If the student belongs to the privileged category and has the right to priority. — Official Data
