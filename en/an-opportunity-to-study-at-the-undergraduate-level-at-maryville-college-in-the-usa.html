General Information:

International Diversity Scholarship: Application for the Diversity Scholarship is available only through the MaryVille Portal (MVP). A complete application is required for admission.

Application Deadline: January 15

Maryville College offers one full scholarship to a freshman each academic year. This scholarship is the most prestigious scholarship for international students. It covers the full cost of tuition for each academic year and can be extended up to four years if the student meets the requirements (maintains F-1 student status, serves two hours per week at the Global Partnership Center, and achieves an overall 3.0 GPA ). must demonstrate sufficient funds to cover the remaining expenses.

  • How can you help Maryville College? prepare a portfolio of your skills;

  • Must live on campus during the four years of study;

Explain your plan for sharing your culture on the Maryville College campus as part of our Cultural Ambassadors program.
