The head of our state decided to establish "Creative Industrial Parks" in "Barkamol Avlod" centers in each region. These parks receive benefits provided for IT park residents.

Key aspects of "Creative industrial parks"

Benefits:

  • Creative industrial parks receive benefits provided for IT park residents.

Organization:

  • Creative industrial parks are universities and large manufacturers
  • These parks will be a platform for communication between young entrepreneurs, science and business.

Financial support:

  • The fund will provide loans of up to 100 million soums for three years to startups of young people here.
  • Young entrepreneurs will be given preferential loans of 100 million soums for business expansion and turnover.
  • 10 million dollars will be allocated this year for the development of creative industrial parks.

Conference and legislation

Fourth World Conference on Creative Economy:

  • The Fourth World Conference on Creative Economy will be held in Tashkent on October 2-4.
  • The law on the development of the creative industry is expected to be adopted by the day of the event.
