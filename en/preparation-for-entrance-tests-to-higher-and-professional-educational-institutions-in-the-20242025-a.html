Preparation for entrance tests to higher and professional educational institutions in the 2024/2025 academic year

A total of 894,279 applicants were registered this year, of which 410,415 (46%) were graduates of the current year, and 483,864 (54%) were applicants who graduated in previous years.
This year, 489,118 applicants (60.7%) were exempted from the fee for participation in the test.
In addition: 30,637 applicants have a foreign language certificate, 20,684 applicants have a national certificate in general education subjects.

Registration process

Online registration of applicants for the

2024/2025 academic year started on June 5 and ended on June 25. Registration of applicants was carried out online through the Uniform interactive state services portal (my.gov.uz) and the website of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency (my.uzbmb.uz).

A total of 894,279 applicants were registered this year, of which 410,415 (46%) were graduates of the current year, 483,864 (54%) were applicants who graduated in previous years. 805,688 applicants who fully met the registration requirements were allowed to participate in the tests (in 2023 - 948,486 people).

Statistics of applicants

Of the applicants scheduled to participate in the tests, 422,400 are women. (52.4 %), 383 288 are men (47.6 %).

This year 489 118 (60.7 %) applicants are exempted from the prescribed fee to participate in the test.

Territorial statistics

This year, the most by the region of permanent residence applicants:

  • Samarkand region: 95,979 people
  • Kashkadarya region: 92,636 people
  • Fergana region: 79,808 people

Special talent and military education fields

Of the applicants, 46,244 people expressed their desire to study in educational fields requiring special talent, 12,851 people expressed their desire to study in higher military educational institutions.

Certificates

At registration 22,632 1 applicant uploaded an international certificate from a foreign language, of which 20,063 were validated, 2,569 certificates were not validated.

In addition:

  • 30,637 applicants from a foreign language,
  • 20,684 applicants has a national certificate in general education subjects.

Preparation for tests

This year, buildings are optimized for high-quality organization and conduct of tests, and currently 73 closed-type large-capacity buildings are being prepared for tests. It is planned that 1,176 groups, 35,280 applicants and 70,560 applicants will take part in the test in one shift, and the tests will be held for 12 days. 2,690 groups of supervisors were selected by conducting. In addition, 139 sign language interpreters will be recruited to provide practical assistance to applicants with speech and hearing impairments.

New test tasks

More than 40,000 new test tasks from 15 (29 languages) subjects of general education used in the tests have been created and examined.

Contact center

To answer the questions of applicants and their parents, 1195 short digital contact center ("Call center") provides continuous service.
