The procedure for conducting the final state certification in secondary educational institutions was approved

Brief content:
According to it, the final state certification of 9th grade students: in the subject of mother tongue and literature - written; From the subject of the state language (for classes where the language of instruction is not Uzbek) - written; Mathematics - written; History of Uzbekistan - written/oral; The subject of physical education will be held in a practical form. Graduation exams (except physical education) from May 26 to June 15, 2024, in particular: for graduates of 9th grade: Mother tongue and literature - on May 27; Mathematics - on May 30; For 11th grades: Mother tongue and literature - May 29; Mathematics - will be held on June 1.
For information: in the 11th grade, one of the optional (variant) subjects is chosen according to the student's application one month before the final state attestation. examination forms were approved.

By order of the Minister of Pre-school and School Education, the list of subjects and examination forms for final state certification in general secondary education institutions, and the list of internationally recognized certificates assessing the level of knowledge of foreign languages were approved. from the subject of literature - written;

  • from the subject of the state language (for classes where the language of instruction is not Uzbek) - written;
  • from the subject of mathematics - written;
  • from the subject of the history of Uzbekistan - written/oral;
  • from the subject of physical education - conducted in practical form.

    • 11th grade students final state certification:

    • In the subject of mother tongue and literature - written (essay);
    • In the subject of mathematics - written;
    • In the subject of the Uzbek language (for general secondary educational institutions where education is not conducted in Uzbek, based on the choice of the educational institution) - written and oral;
    • In the subject of a foreign language - written/oral;
    • Uzbekistan history — written/oral;
    • (according to the student's choice) chemistry, physics, foundations of state law, geography — will be conducted in written form.

    Graduation exams (except physical education) from May 26 to June 15, 2024, specifically:

    For 9th grade graduates:

    • Mother language and literature - May 27;
    • Mathematics - May 30;

    For 11th grades:

    • Mother language and literature - May 29;
    • Mathematics - June 1

    Physical education exams (for students of the 9th grade) will be held on May 1-6, 2024 under the supervision of qualified medical personnel.

    Students who have a state model certificate of knowledge of general education and a national or equivalent internationally recognized certificate of at least B1 level in foreign language subjects are exempt from the exam.
