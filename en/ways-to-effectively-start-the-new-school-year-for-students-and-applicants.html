Tips for a successful start of the new academic year: For students and applicants

With the beginning of the new academic year, it is time for students and applicants to take steps towards successful studies and achieving their goals. Below are some important tips for an effective start to the new academic year.

1. Set Goals

Clarify your academic and personal goals for success throughout the school year. Goals guide you and help you stay motivated even when you face challenges. It's also helpful to update your goals at each stage.

2. Plan Your Time

Finding a balance between productive study and relaxation is essential. Plan your tasks by creating an Agenda. You will increase your efficiency by properly allocating study and rest time for each day.

3. Be active

Actively participating in classes and joining study groups will not only help you learn better, but also make new friends. You can further expand your knowledge by active participation.

4. If you don't know, ask for help. Ask for help from teachers, tutors or friends. It helps to strengthen your knowledge and overcome difficulties.

5. Take care of yourself

Good sleep, proper nutrition and rest are important factors for your overall health. Giving yourself enough attention will help you improve your study performance. Don't forget to take care of yourself every day!
