The education of children of low-income families is financially supported

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
By the decision of the government, the regulation on measures to financially support the study of children of low-income families in groups of extended day in general education institutions has been approved. In the groups whose children's day in general education institutions is extended, the parents' payment for child support is covered by the following funds, respectively: Women's Support Fund included in the "Women's Register"; Youth support fund included in the "Youth Register"; "Generosity and Support" fund. District (city) public education departments annually: identify children of low-income families studying in schools until August 1; Forms a list of children from low-income families who wish to study in extended day groups of general education institutions and are not exempted from paying fees until August 5; The district (city) neighborhood and family support department, the Youth Affairs Agency department, the "Mahalla" charity public fund department will come up with the lists by August 10 and submit the lists to the district (city) council of people's deputies for approval. regulation on measures was approved.

The children of families found to be low-income through the information system of the "Unified Register of Social Protection" will be reimbursed by the following funds: support fund;

  • Fund to support young people included in the "Youth register";
  • "Generosity and support" fund.

    • District (city) public education departments every year:

    • Identifies children of low-income families who are studying in schools until August 1;
    • Formulates a list of children of low-income families who wish to study in extended day groups of general education institutions and are not exempted from paying fees by August 5;
    • The lists are brought to the district (city) council by August 10 with the department of district (city) neighborhood and family support, the department of the Youth Affairs Agency, the department of the "Mahalla" charity public fund, and the people's deputies for approval.

    The general educational institution shall enter into a contract with the parents of the relevant students (or their substitutes) within 3 days from the date of receipt of the board's decision. Contracts are concluded for one academic year.

    The payment is covered for each child in the amount of 25% of the base calculation amount (up to 61,250 soums) for one month during the academic year. — Official Data
