International scholarship program "Schwarzman Scholarship"

Where: Tsinghua University, Beijing, China
For whom: Students aged 18 to 29 or graduates with a bachelor's degree
Deadline: September 20, 2022

This annual scholarship was launched in 2016 by American financier Stephen Schwarzman

The purpose of the program: To cultivate business, political, and civil society leaders who will be a bridge of mutual solidarity between China and other countries.

Candidates must go through 2 stages: online application and direct interview. At the same time, they must be fluent in English and have a high level of academic research.

The scholarship covers:
- tuition fees;
- accommodation and meals;
- transportation to and from Beijing;
- necessary textbooks and materials;
- Lenovo laptop;
- health insurance;
- $4,000 for personal needs. scholarship.
