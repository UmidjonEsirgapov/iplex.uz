Keystone Scholarships 2021: Scholarship program with a total value of USD 22,000

Important news for students and applicants.

Grant Brief:

This grant program is provided by Keystone Academic Solutions. For the 2021-2022 academic year, it is intended to be offered to international candidates who wish to study at the bachelor's or master's level.

For whom:

For international students who are over 18 years of age and plan to study full-time in a bachelor's or master's program.

Benefits of the program:

This program Provides a total of $22,000 in scholarships for the 2021 academic year and distributes that value to program winners as follows:

  • Four undergraduate students will each receive $2,000 worth of Keystone Scholarships;
  • Two graduate students will receive 10,000 USD, and second will be awarded USD 4,000.
  • The above amounts will be paid directly to the university for the winners' study program.

How to apply for the grant:

  1. This grant to apply for the program, first register on the StudentHub portal of the Keystone Academic Solutions website by completing the online application form here;
  2. Submit your information to at least one of the universities recommended by the portal;
  3. Log in to the StudentHub portal and apply for a Keystone scholarship by May 31, 2021;
  4. Recommended by the portal Send an admission letter from one of the universities to Keystone Academic Solutions.
  5. Get one of the six scholarships that will help finance your studies at the university!
