The acceptance of articles for the next - 5th issue of the Nordic" scientific-practical electronic magazine operating under Nordic International University has begun. This opportunity serves as an important space for researchers, young scientists and students who want to present the results of their scientific research to the general public.

With its scientific-practical orientation, "Nordik" magazine creates a space for the exchange of theoretical knowledge and practical experience for researchers, practitioners and scientific-researchers at the national and regional levels. The main goal of the publication is to disseminate qualitative research to the public, to introduce the works of young scientists and experts in the field of practice to a wide audience. The journal provides practical assistance in writing articles in accordance with the requirements of IMRAD, and published scientific works are published on the journal's official website and social networks. Each author will receive a special certificate.

The magazine accepts articles in the following areas: economy, pedagogy, psychology, philosophy, history and philology. The unique thing is that the journal "Nordik" is indexed in CrossRef, DOI, ORCID and Google Scholar systems, which creates a wide opportunity for international recognition of scientific articles.

The cost of publishing an article is set at 400,000 soums.

Conclusion. Open admission for the 5th issue of "Nordik" magazine is a convenient platform for young scientists, graduate students and scientific staff to formally present their research, refer to international indexes and reach a wide audience. Authors must submit articles within the specified period and closely follow the requirements on the journal's website.
