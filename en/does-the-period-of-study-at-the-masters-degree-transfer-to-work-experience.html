Does the period of study at the master's degree transfer to work experience?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
In the Republic of Uzbekistan, issues related to work experience are regulated by the Law "On state pension provision of citizens". Full-time study at the institute, the institute of senior researcher-researchers, basic doctoral training, doctoral training, and clinical residency, including studying abroad, are counted as types of work activities that are included in the work experience.
Also, if during the study the student worked somewhere officially, this period should also be included in the work experience.
