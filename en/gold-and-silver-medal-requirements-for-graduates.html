Gold and Silver Medal Requirements for Graduates

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
Among school graduates, the opportunity to receive gold and silver medals is given only to students who studied with excellent grades.
The gold medal is awarded to graduates who fulfill the following conditions: having completed the 9th grade with an excellent certificate
Obtaining a silver medal has slightly simpler conditions than a gold medal: it is enough to have excellent grades in the 10th and 11th grades. This award approved by the Ministry of Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan was introduced to stimulate the academic achievements and activity of students.

Who will be awarded the gold medal?

The gold medal will be awarded to graduates who have fulfilled the following conditions:

  • Graduated 9th grade with an excellent certificate
  • Quarterly, annual and final grades in all subjects of the 10th and 11th grades must be excellent.
  • Must have exemplary behavior and be active in school community affairs.

Who will be awarded the Silver Medal?

Silver getting a medal has slightly simpler conditions than the gold medal:

it is enough to have excellent annual grades in the 10th and 11th grade.

Who will award the medals?

The process of awarding gold and silver medals is under the full control of the school administration, and the principal of this school is responsible for the fair distribution of awards.

Conclusion

Gold and silver medals are awarded only to graduates who studied with excellent grades, disciplined and showed activity. These awards recognize achievement during school and enhance future opportunities. — Official Data
