Academic important news for applicants planning to enter lyceums: the structure of the test and evaluation criteria for the 2025/2026 academic year have been announced. In this test, students will be tested on two subjects suitable for the chosen field and IQ test.

Exam structure and evaluation system

There are total 60 questions for all test takers, which are distributed as follows:

  • Subject 1: 20 tests (each one 3.1 points, total 62 points)
  • Subject 2: 20 tests (3.1 points each, total 62 points)
  • IQ test: 20 tests (1.1 points each, total 22 points)
  • Maximum points: 146
