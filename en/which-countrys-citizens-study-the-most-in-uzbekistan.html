Foreigners studying in Uzbekistan India is the leader in the number of students

Based on the data from the beginning of the 2023-2024 academic year, the Statistics Agency announced that the largest number of Indian citizens are studying in higher education institutions in Uzbekistan. According to the data, the number of students from India is 5,733, and this figure is much higher than the citizens of other countries.

Foreign students are distributed by country according to the following indicators:

  • India – 5,733
  • Pakistan – 1,358
  • Turkmenistan – 904 people
  • Russian Federation – 750 people
  • Kazakhstan – 537 people
  • Tajikistan – 285 people
  • Kyrgyz Republic – 285 people
  • Afghanistan – 196 people
  • China – 182 people
  • Republic of Korea – 176 people
  • Canada – 124 people
  • Others – 315 people

The increase in the number of foreign students in higher education institutions in Uzbekistan is the result of the country's education system strengthening its competitiveness at the international level, creating opportunities for new market and academic cooperation.
