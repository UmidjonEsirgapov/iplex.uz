The age limit of 26 years for withdrawing 12% of the contract fee has been abolished

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Changes in the procedure for the return of 12% of the contract payment. introduced.
What was the previous procedure?
This means that regardless of how old the student is, his parents or close relatives can use a 12% tax credit on the amount paid for the contract fee.

Changes in the procedure for returning 12% of the contract fee

According to the decision of the Government of the Republic of Uzbekistan According to the law, important changes were made to the procedure for personal income tax exemption for funds used for education in professional and higher education organizations.

What was the previous procedure?

Previously, when parents or close relatives made contract payments, if the student was under 26 years of age, from the contract payment 12 percent income tax was not calculated. This amount would be refunded and education costs would be slightly reduced.

What does the new regime include?

Now the age limit of 26 has been abolished. This means that regardless of how old the student is, he/she can use the 12% tax credit on the amount paid by his/her parents or close relatives for the contract fee.
