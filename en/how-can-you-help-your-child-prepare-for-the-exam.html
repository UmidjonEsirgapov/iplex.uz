Don't let him suffer too much.
  1. Do not increase your child's anxiety on the eve of the exam - this can have a negative effect on the test result. The excitement of parents is always transferred to children;
  2. Make your child happy, praise him for his good deeds;
  3. Increase your child's self-confidence, because the more children are afraid of failure, the more likely they are to make mistakes;
  4. Pay attention to how your child feels, no one but you will notice his extra tiredness, does not notice the deterioration of his health and cannot prevent it;
  5. Control your child's study mode! Don't let him torture himself too much. Make sure that you always alternate study and rest;
  6. Set aside a comfortable place for classes at home! Make sure that no one disturbs him at home;
  7. Introduce your child to exam preparation techniques. Teach your child to understand the gist of the exam without memorizing all the information; Get him a watch;
  8. Your child should get enough sleep before the exam. Do not worry your child by talking about how many points he can get in the exam and do not criticize him after the exam! Inculcate in him the idea that scores cannot measure his real potential.
