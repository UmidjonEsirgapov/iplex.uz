Participate in the Republican "English Science Olympiad". The winners will be awarded with special prizes, souvenirs and diplomas with a QR code.

Brief content:
In order to support talented young people and increase the level of English literacy of young people, the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform team organized a 3-level English Olympiad in cooperation with the Ministry of Innovative Development, the Council of Young Scientists of the Republic, and the "REGISTON" educational center. in progress!
Alloted time: 1 hour 20 minutes Venue: "REGISTON" educational center The third qualifying round will be held in the auditorium of the "REGISTON" educational center, where special exams will be held.
Also, special QR-code certificates will be given to all candidates who participated in the Olympiad in their "Personal Cabinet" on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.

In order to support talented young people and increase the level of English literacy of young people, the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform team is organizing a 3-level English Olympiad on a national scale in cooperation with the Ministry of Innovative Development, the Republican Council of Young Scientists, and the "REGISTON" educational center!

Participate, test your knowledge and get special prizes.

The winners will be awarded with special prizes, souvenirs, I, II and III level QR-coded diplomas, and all those who participated in the Olympiad will be given special certificates.

How to register?

Applicants who want to participate in the Olympiad register independently from the system of the OLIYGOH.UZ platform and meet the conditions of the Olympiad in advance they get to know each other. Olympiad participants should pay attention to filling in their personal information correctly and accurately during registration. A special code will be sent to each candidate's phone number during the registration process at the Olympics. This code will activate the participant's "Personal Cabinet".

Olympiad stages and questions

The Olympiad will be held in 3 stages:

First stage (Online form) Candidates participating in the Olympiad will take a test on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform English grammar, vocabulary (dictionary). and "Reading" skills will be tested:

Exam type: 40 test questions on English grammar, vocabulary (Vocabulary) and "Reading" skills (Elementary-intermediate levels)

Time: 1st stage of the Olympiad on July 11 at Starts at 20:00 and closes to everyone at 21:00. Candidates registered during this period are required to participate in the Olympiad. Candidates who successfully passed the 1st stage will go to the 2nd stage.*

Alloted time: 40 minutes

The address of the first stage: OLIYGOH.UZ platform

* 1000 (one thousand) participants selected from the 1st stage will go to the second stage

Second stage (Online form) Candidates participating in the Olympiad will be tested on General grammar, "Reading" and "Listening" skills in the form of a test on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform:

Exam type: 40 test questions on General grammar, "Reading" and "Listening" skills (Intermediate-advanced levels)

Time: The 2nd stage of the Olympiad will start on July 18 at 8:00 p.m. and close for everyone at 9:30 p.m. Candidates registered during this period are required to participate in the Olympiad. Candidates who successfully passed the 2nd stage will go to the 3rd stage.**

Alloted time: 60 minutes

The address of the second stage: OLIYGOH.UZ platform

** 100 (one hundred) participants selected from the 2nd stage for the third stage

Third stage (Offline form) Candidates participating in the Olympiad will be tested on the skills of "Speaking" and "Writing" in the auditorium of special exams of"REGISTON" educational center:

Type of exam: "Speaking" and "Writing" skills evaluation, writing a motivational essay

Time: The 3rd stage of the Olympiad will be held on July 25 between the selected candidates at the "REGISTON" educational center.

Alloted time: 1 hour 20 minutes

Location: "REGISTON" educational center

The third qualifying round will be held in the auditorium of"REGISTON" educational center where special exams are held.

Olympiad participants

Students of general education schools, academic lyceums, professional educational institutions and students of higher educational institutions can participate in this Olympiad.

Olympiad encouraging the winners

The young people who took part in the Olympiad and won prizes will be given special QR-code diplomas of the 1st, 2nd, 3rd level as follows:

To the winner of the 1st place: A laptop computer and souvenir books worth 200 thousand soums

2nd place to the owner: Samsung A21 mobile phone and souvenir books worth 100 thousand soums.

Special awards and diplomas will be awarded to the winners of the Olympiad in a ceremony at the "Registon" educational center.

Also, special QR-code certificates will be given to all candidates who participated in the Olympiad online through their "Personal cabinet" on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.

OLIYGOH.UZ team and "REGISTON" educational center wishes good luck to all participants of the Olympiad!
