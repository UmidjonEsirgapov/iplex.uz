On January 31, the bodies of 4 people were found in one of the apartments located on Sayilgoh Street in Jizzakh city. 2 of them were students. The press secretary of the Prosecutor General's Office, Hayat Shamsutdinov, reports this.

According to the primary information of experts, the heating system installed in the apartment where the incident occurred is against the rules of natural gas use and fire safety.
