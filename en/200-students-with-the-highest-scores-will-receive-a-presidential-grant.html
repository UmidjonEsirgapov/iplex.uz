200 students with the highest scores will receive a Presidential grant

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
The 200 students with the highest scores in the full-time exams will receive a Presidential grant. Even if he does not get an "excellent" grade, he will receive a regular scholarship instead of a presidential grant scholarship in the next semester.

The 200 students who scored the highest points in the full-time exams will receive the Presidential grant.

The maximum score that can be scored in the exams is 189 points. Even if the student is admitted to the contract, if he is considered worthy of the President's grant, he will study on the basis of the grant! But even if the student fails to complete any subject with an "excellent" grade, he will receive a regular scholarship instead of a presidential grant scholarship in the next semester. If he closes the next semester with an "excellent" grade, the student will continue to receive the grant scholarship. The amount of this scholarship is the same as that of the President's scholarship.

Students who have received a grant scholarship continuously until graduation will be admitted to the master's degree in their field without an exam based on the grant. — Source
