Payment options for studying at the university!

You can pay for studying at the University of Management and Future Technologies by one of the following three options:

1) By yourself or your parents can pay;

2) An enterprise, organization or firm can order and pay for your education;

3) You can get a preferential education loan through the bank

Conditions for obtaining a preferential education loan

To whom the loan is granted: Citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan in full-time, part-time and evening forms of the university has the right to receive a loan for study.

Loan term: repayable within 7 years after the end of the study period.

Loan amount: the loan is given equal to the amount specified in the university contract.

Loan percentage and fee: 14% for all applicants, no interest (0%) for women
