List of documents for those recommended for studentship

Applicants recommended for studentship to higher education institutions should prepare and submit the following documents within the specified period.

Main documents

  • Application to the Rector (written in OTM)

  • Copy of passport or ID card (indicated in original)

  • Original copy of diploma or certificate (with application)

    • Certificate of language proficiency (if available) original

  • 6 photos of size 3.5x4.5

Additional documents for those admitted to study on the basis of privilege

  • The original of the document granting the privilege

  • Applicant record sheet at the time of registration

Additional documents and arrangement

  • Collection folder of documents (folder skorosshivatel)

  • Envelope in A5 format

  • ❗️ Documents must be submitted in a color printed copy

Conclusion

The student admission process is one of the important stages. By submitting the above documents in full on time, applicants will simplify the admission process and avoid problems. — Source
