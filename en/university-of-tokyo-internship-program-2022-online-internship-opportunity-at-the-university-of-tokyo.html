General Information:

All natural sciences for all nationalities regardless of religion, race and background ( a natural science or related field) or a related field) fully funded online internship (UTRIP Online 2022) opportunity at the University of Tokyo for undergraduate and graduate students.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Applicants must be currently studying at the undergraduate or graduate level;
  • Must be studying at a university outside of Japan;
  • Must be studying in one of the natural sciences or related fields (chemistry, biology, physics, etc.);
  • If applicants' first language is not English, they must provide a certificate of proficiency in any English language test such as IELTS, TOEFL, etc. Expired certificates will also be accepted if proven valid.
  • Applicants must attend all activities planned by UTRIP.
  • These online internships must be attended and completed on time;

Advantages of applying for a fully funded online internship:

  • Interns will be able to carry out research projects at an international level. will learn from experts;
  • Opportunity to hold online meetings with professional researchers in Japan;
  • Candidates who successfully complete the internship will receive a certificate that they completed the internship at a Japanese university;

Required documents:

  • Scanned copies of all academic transcripts;
  • Resume/CV;
  • Certificate of English Language;
  • Transcript in English with grading system ();
  • Recommendation;
