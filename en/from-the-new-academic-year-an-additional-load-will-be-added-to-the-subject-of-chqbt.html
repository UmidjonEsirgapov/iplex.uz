Primary training before the draft (ChQBT) new changes are being introduced. Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, this subject will be taught two hours per week. For grades 10-11, an additional hour is added per week. Also, according to the decision "On measures to develop and popularize shooting sports" signed by the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan on November 4, 2024, the school students will have the opportunity to use military ranges.

According to this decision, starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, practical training on "Tactical-technical characteristics of firearms and pneumatic sports weapons" will be introduced within the scope of the CSBT subject. Now, schoolchildren will have practical experience in shooting weapons.

The following opportunities will be created in the organization of training:

  • General secondary educational institutions can use the educational and material base of military units and military ranges attached to them.
  • Practical training will be held at military ranges and specially equipped places.
  • Schoolchildren will have the opportunity to gain experience in working with weapons.

These changes will increase the military training of students, and their physical and intellectual development.
