Students who failed the Perevod exam now have two options to continue their studies.

Option 1: Continue studying at your current university.

Option 2: Transfer studies on the basis of a super-contract.

Super-contract fees are divided into two categories:

Category 1: Students who do not reach the passing score of 4.05 points

To students of this category The price of a super-contract is determined on the basis of the following scale:

  • Those who do not reach 1.05 points - 1.5 times the amount of the normal payment contract;
  • From 1.06 points to 2.05 points - 2 times;
  • From 2.06 points to 3.05 points - 2.5 times;
  • From 3.06 points Up to 4.05 points - 3 times.

For example, if 1.5 points are not enough in mathematics, the applicant pays 2 times for the super-contract. If the price of a regular contract is 7,400,000 soums, then the amount of the super-contract will be 14.8 million soums (2x7,400,000).
