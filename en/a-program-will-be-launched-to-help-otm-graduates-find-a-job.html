A program will be launched to help graduates of higher education institutions find a job

On the basis of the President's decision On additional measures to support the employment of graduates of higher education institutions" (No. PQ-61, 14.02.2025), the program "Step into the future" will be implemented.

This program is being introduced in order to provide practical assistance to graduates of higher education institutions in finding a job, as well as to help students create their own business projects.

The following are planned as part of the program:

✅ Support for business ideas

  • Graduates with their own business ideas the opportunity to fully form and finance the project will be created for the students of the first stage.

✅ Competitions for graduates and students

  • Open competitions in various fields will be organized and the winners will be helped to get a job.

✅ Employers' needs

  • Requirements for qualified personnel are formed by large companies and organizations and a system of training graduates suitable for work is established. will be trained.
  • An opportunity will be created for internships at production facilities and participation in short-term training courses abroad.

This program aims to ensure the employment of graduates of higher education and support their professional development. — Official Data
