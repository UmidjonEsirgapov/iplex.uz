Reading a book is important for improving everyone's knowledge, broadening the scope of thinking and quickly absorbing information. Each person can choose the method of reading depending on the purpose and context. Below are the 7 most effective methods:

1. Speed ​​reading

This method is used to quickly understand the main content of the book. Mainly, it is used to get acquainted with a new book, to understand the main ideas.

2. Deep reading

A method of reading slowly and carefully to fully understand, analyze, and reflect the essence of a text. Important for scientific books and complex literature.

3. Skimming

Looking through the text quickly to find the main points, to highlight important points. It is used in the process of research and information seeking.

4. Reading for specific information

Saves time by reading only the necessary sections when searching for a specific word, date or fact.

5. Active reading

Helps to master the book deeply by asking questions, marking important points, and writing a synopsis during reading. An effective way to remember and use the information read.

6. Reading by listening

Reading through audiobooks or audio materials. Convenient to get information while on the road, doing sports or busy with work.

7. Re-reading

Reading a book several times allows you to deepen your understanding and memorization. Useful for complex books or classic works.

Choosing each method according to your purpose and type of book will increase the efficiency of book reading. — Official Data
