Terms of study leave for employees studying in the evening and part-time

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Is it possible to give more study leave to employees studying in the evening or part-time?
Study leave periods for these employees are determined as follows: First- and second-year students studying in the form of evening education in higher education institutions at least 20 calendar days, at least 30 calendar days for those studying in the third and higher years; At least 10-20 calendar days for those studying in the form of evening education in secondary special, vocational education organizations; Study leave for at least 30-40 calendar days is given to those studying part-time.
These guarantees and compensations apply to employees studying in the evening or part-time, that is, they can be given more study leave, if this is provided for in other legal documents on labor.

Is it possible to give more educational leave to employees studying in the evening or part-time?

According to Article 385 of the Labor Code, certain guarantees and compensations are provided to the employees who are studying in the form of evening or part-time education. The periods of study leave for these employees are determined as follows:

  • at least 20 calendar days for first and second year students studying in the form of evening education in higher education organizations, at least 30 calendar days for those studying in third and higher years;
  • in secondary special, vocational education organizations those who study in the form of evening education are given at least 10-20 calendar days;
  • those who study in the form of part-time education are given a study leave for a period of at least 30-40 calendar days.

At the same time, the duration of these leave periods is specified in the Labour Legislation or other legal documents. can be more.

These guarantees and compensations apply to employees studying in the form of evening or part-time education, that is, they can be granted more study leave, if this is provided for in other legal documents on labor.

For those studying in the form of full-time or distance education

The above guarantees and compensations do not apply to employees who are studying in the form of full-time or distance education. In such cases, special guarantees can be provided in the internal local documents of the enterprise or other legal documents.
