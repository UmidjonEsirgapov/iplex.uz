Is the privilege also granted to those who study at a private university?

Yes, children of families included in the "Iron Register" and young people included in the "Youth Register" can use the privilege according to the contract if they are studying in any of Uzbekistan, including state, private, foreign HEIs, technical institutes, colleges.

In addition, this privilege is given not only to bachelors, but also to those studying at the master's level. In this case, the bachelor's degree should be full-time, part-time and evening, and the master's degree should be full-time only.

How much of the contract will be covered?

Up to 50% of the contract, but not more than 50 times the BHM, will be covered.

For example, the contract amount of student A is 40 million soums, his half of the contract will be 20 million soums, but not 20 million soums, but up to 50 times the BHM will be reimbursed, i.e. up to 16 million 500 thousand soums.

Is the "super contract" also paid?

No, this privilege does not apply to those studying in the "super" contract or in the second specialty.

Who should apply?

The contract is paid at the expense of the "Youth Notebook" fund. The fund is managed by the Board of Trustees headed by the mayor in districts (cities). The working body of the council is the district (city) youth affairs department.

For this reason, the application is submitted to the youth affairs department.

A copy of the contract issued by the Ministry of Education and Culture should be attached to the application.

There is no specific deadline for submitting the application, it can be submitted at any time.

How long will the application be considered?

Before submitting the application, consult with the local youth leader and It's better to inform.

The reason is that within 3 working days after you apply, your application will be sent to the youth leader in your neighborhood for consideration.

The youth leader will send a recommendation or denial of contract coverage within 5 working days. If the recommendation is refused, it must be justified.

If the youth leader does not consider the application within 5 working days or refuses to issue a recommendation without reason, the Board of Trustees may transfer the contract money on its own initiative.

The Board of Trustees will transfer it to the OTM account within 3 working days after the decision to cover the contract.

Then the application should be reviewed in 11 working days.
