7th grade student from Uzbekistan international English won a prize in the Olympiad

On September 23-26, 2024, the final stage of the 12th International English Language Olympiad organized by the "Global Hippo Association" was held in Assisi, Italy. Students from Uzbekistan took part in this prestigious competition and demonstrated their knowledge.

Over 88,000 students from 63 countries took part in the qualifying stages of the competition, while 4,500 schoolchildren from Uzbekistan took part. Mirabror Rajabboyev, a 7th-grade student from Surkhandarya region, took the proud 3rd place in the international Olympiad in the Hippo-2 group. The organizers awarded all participants with an international English language certificate, medals and valuable gifts. Also, letters of thanks were presented on behalf of the Embassy of Uzbekistan to the students and their teachers who defended the honor of Uzbekistan.

It should be noted that in 2025, the European stage of this Olympiad is planned to be held in Venice, the Asian stage in Samarkand, and the final stage in Sicily.
