When will the salary of teachers-pedagogues exceed 1000 US dollars?

A question was asked during the press conference about when the salary of teachers-pedagogues will exceed 1000 US dollars.

Hilola Umarova responded to this question and noted that she is working with the Ministry of Finance on the issue of increasing the monthly salary of teachers. He also noted that today there are teachers who earn more than 1000 US dollars.

For information, the average monthly salary of teachers today is 3.4 million soums and this amount has increased by 13.3% compared to the corresponding period of 2023. The salary of teachers of higher class, who receive bonuses from the director's fund and have internationally recognized certificates, reaches 10 million soums.

The monthly bonus from the minister's fund is 5.7 million soums. This money is paid to teachers who transparently passed the tests and there is no limit to the number of teachers. Teachers who go to work in remote areas will receive a bonus of 3.7 million soums, and those who won the international Olympiad and are currently working as teachers will receive up to 4.1 million soums.
