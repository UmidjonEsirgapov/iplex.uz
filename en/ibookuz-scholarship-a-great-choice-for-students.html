iBook.uz scholarship: a great choice for students!

Quota for the scholarship:

How to register will be held?

Applicants who wish to participate in the "iBook.uz Scholarship" competition register independently from the OLIYGOH.UZ platform system and familiarize themselves with the test conditions of the competition in advance. Contestants should pay attention to filling in their personal information correctly and accurately during registration. A special code will be sent to each candidate's phone number during the registration process. This code activates the participant's "Personal cabinet".

Selection stages

Taking into account the interests of young people, the selection stages are evaluated and sorted through a general evaluation system. The competition is organized in three stages.

The first stage

The first stage (in the form of a test) candidates participating in the competition will take an online exam at the national level through the OLIYGOH.UZ platform .

Type of exam: IQ (logical thinking) 40 test questions.

Duration: Stage 1 of the competition starts on April 24 at 18:00 and the exam window closes for everyone at 22:00. All candidates registered during this period can participate in the exam for 40 minutes. After the completion of the 1st stage, the general results will be formed and 2 thousand (two thousand) participants will be transferred to the 2nd stage.

Second stage (in the form of a test-examination) Candidates who passed the 2nd stage of the competition will take an online exam in the form of a test on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform, and the candidates with the highest score will receive a ticket to the final stage.

Exam type: 40 test questions compiled from 7 books recommended by the selection commission. This stage questions are aimed at full thinking, and participants take an exam from selected books.

Currently, the most widely read among young people (TOP bestseller) and works on personal development have been selected. The list of books was recommended by the special commission of the competition and specialists of the iBook.uz bookstore network. List of books:

— Lolazor (Murod Muhammad Dost)

— Factfulness (Hans Rosling)

— Laws of victory (Bodo Schaefer)

— Labyrinth (Franz Kafka)

— Goodbye, Homeland (Azam Hashemi)

— Resurrection (Lev Tolstoy)

— Selected Works (Mahmudhoja Behbudi)

Time: The 2nd stage of the competition starts on May 15 at 6:00 p.m. and the exam window closes for everyone at 10:00 p.m. During this time, all candidates can participate in the exam for 40 minutes. After the completion of the 2nd stage, the mandate of the overall results will be formed and the 200 (two hundred) participants who will get the highest result will get a ticket to the final (3rd stage).

Third stage

Third stage: This stage is the most important stage of the competition, and candidates who have qualified for the final stage of the "Yuksalish" leadership academy They will be tested in the center located in the city of Tashkent.

Time: The 3rd stage of the competition will take place on May 29 among the shortlisted candidates.

At this stage, the participants will be based on one of the books recommended by the special selection commission (personal review comment) they write. List of books:

— The Old Man and the Sea (Ernest Hemingway)

— The Archway (Paulo Coelho)

— The Day of the Century (Chingiz Aitmatov)

Reviews 100 points is evaluated. In the review, candidates should be able to analyze the book in its entirety. Candidates' independent opinion, consistency and fluency of the opinions written in the review, as well as the originality of the conclusion are of particular importance.

Note: Candidates who have received a ticket to the final stage must submit personal documents through a special examination window of the OLIYGOH.UZ platform. Candidates submit the following documents online through a special platform window:

— identity document;— reference from the place of study (reference confirmed via my.gov.uz or approved by the dean's office);— CV (reference).

The results of the selection will be announced within 2 weeks.

The general results are mainly determined by the decision of the special commission. Based on the decisions made by the competition commission, the list of prize-winning students is approved. Special commission The special commission of the competition is made up of experts from the "iBook.uz" chain of bookstores, qualified teachers of the "Yuksalish" leadership academy, and OLIYGOH.UZ team members. The organization, holding and award ceremonies of the competition are carried out by a special commission.

Awarding ceremony

The winners of the scholarship competition will be solemnly awarded in the special events hall of the "iBook.uz" bookstore chain.

Also, the candidates who participated in the competition Special QR-code certificates will be given online to everyone. Certificates will be sent to participants through "Personal cabinet" on OLIYGOH.UZ platform.
