Another university started working in Uzbekistan

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Samarkand International University of Technology has started its activities from this month.
In the university, professors and teachers are mostly foreign professors and teachers of SXTU Professor Josef Sapienza (Italy), Director of the Language Center Professor Annamarie Basson (JAR), Director of the Information Technology Center Professor Muhammad Gouse Galeti (India), Director of the Preparatory School Professor Ivy Silverman (USA) and Dean of Student Affairs Professor Dorota Sylvia Majewicz (Poland) will teach.
For information, 35 students were admitted to the courses of Entrepreneurship and Management and Information Technology in the spring semester.

Samarkand International University of Technology has started its activities from this month. Currently, the first 35 students are participating in the classes.

Currently, students are studying in the fields of "Entrepreneurship and Management" and "Information Technologies". It is planned to start accepting students in such fields as oil and gas, chemistry, mining, electrical engineering, geological engineering from the autumn semester. It was reported that the first vice-rector of SXTU Professor Joseph Sapienza (Italy), the director of the language center Professor Annamarie Basson (JAR), the director of the information technology center Professor Muhammad Gouse Galeti (India), the director of the preparatory school Professor Ivy Silverman (USA) and the dean of student affairs Professor Dorota Sylvia Majewicz (Poland) will teach at the university. It was announced that the "Korea College of Technology and Culture" was founded under the leadership of Hwang Sung Don, the director of the university network. In order to give new meaning to people's lives, the company awarded a grant to 3 students who achieved the highest results in the entrance exams and needed social assistance.

For information, 35 students were admitted to the courses of Entrepreneurship and Management and Information Technologies in the spring semester of the university. In the fall semester, it is planned to open such courses as oil and gas engineering, chemical engineering, mining, geology and geological engineering, and electrical engineering for students.

We remind you that February 4, 2022 is the day when Samarkand International University of Technology was officially registered in the state register and acquired legal status. This year marks one year of this date. In this regard, the university team decided to celebrate February 4 as the day the university officially started its activities. — Source
