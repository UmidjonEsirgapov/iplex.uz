A new procedure for teachers to receive a bonus from the ministerial fund

Based on the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, important changes have been made in the system of attestation, professional certification and promotion of teachers. Now the procedure for receiving allowances from the minister's fund has been simplified and determined on the basis of fair criteria.

News about the minister's fund:

  • No longer a recommendation from the regional council is required for monthly allowances.

  • All subject teachers, including MTT (pre-school education) educators can also participate part-time and claim a bonus. An additional fee of up to 70% is paid.

  • This bonus is given to those who pass both stages of the competency tests and score 86 and above.
