How is the National Certificate in General Education used?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
A national certificate will be presented to applicants who have scored at least 60% of the highest score.
Applicants with a certificate will be awarded the following points in the specialty subject when entering HEIs: from 60% to 86% — proportional score; 86 percent and above - the maximum score.
In this case, the applicant chooses whether to use the certificate for the specialty subject or not: if he chooses to use it - the applicant will not participate in the test of this subject and will be given the corresponding (maximum or proportional) score; if the use of the certificate is refused, the points on the certificate will not be taken into account and the applicant will participate in the test of this subject.
