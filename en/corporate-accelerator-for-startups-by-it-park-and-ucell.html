IT Park in cooperation with Ucell is organizing an accelerator for startups that want to implement their projects and develop them to the level of a full-fledged company.

The accelerator offers startups the following opportunities provides:

  • Industry expertise by Ucell highly qualified specialists, as well as advanced entrepreneurs in Uzbekistan;
  • Technical base by Ucell as support;
  • Wide choices for cooperation;
  • Personal tracker that serves as an assistant in startup development;
  • Invaluable networking with the help of strong communities and absolutely free programs

What are the criteria for selecting teams to participate in the accelerator?

  • The originality of the idea;
  • The advantage of the product;
  • How easy it is to implement the idea using existing technologies;
  • Availability of the necessary professional skills of team members.
