What is the deadline for applying for student rent reimbursement?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:

Students must submit an online application for reimbursement of rent by the 7th of each month. Decision 605 of the Cabinet of Ministers dated September 24, 2021 is the basis for this process.

The application process consists of specific steps:

  1. registration from the kontrakt.edu.uz information system and access to the personal cabinet.
  2. In the personal cabinet, "Rent payments" select section.
  3. Send an application using the button "Send application".

First-year students can apply without a rental agreement for the months of September and October. A rental agreement is required for Sophomores and above. There is a special service for accounting of rental contracts: ijara.soliq.uz. — Source
