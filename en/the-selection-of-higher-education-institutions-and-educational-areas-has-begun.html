Choice of higher education institutions and education courses has begun

Entrance to higher and professional educational institutions applicants who participated in the tests should choose higher educational institutions and educational fields for 15 days from July 27 to August 10, 2024.

For this, my.uzbmb.uz platform "Higher and professional" enter the "admission to educational institutions" section, press the "Use the service" button and go to the "OET selection" section.

At first, the applicant makes his choice according to "Selection priority" and "Targeted admission".

After that, the subjects for which the applicant passed the test chooses up to five higher education institutions and educational fields that match the complex and language of education along with the form of education.

Educational fields can be selected within one higher educational institution or within different higher educational institutions.

After selecting educational fields and clicking the "Save" button an SMS message is sent to the phone number of the applicant to confirm the data. The data is confirmed by entering the code in the SMS message into the program.

After the data is confirmed, the "Applicant registration form" is created. The record sheet will reflect the priority of the selection chosen by the applicant, the forms of education, higher education institutions and educational directions. By clicking the download button, the applicant can download the registration form.

Applicants can change their information and download the registration form again before the end of the period of selection of educational areas.

The results of the final selection of applicants recommended to the studentship or not within a week after the selection of educational areas is completely completed. will be announced.
