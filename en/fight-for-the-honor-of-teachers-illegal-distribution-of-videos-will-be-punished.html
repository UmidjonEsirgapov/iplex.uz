Taking videos of teachers and distributing them on social networks is against the law and causes administrative, criminal and civil liability.

Administrative liability

  • According to Article 46-2 of the Code of Administrative Liability, citizens shall be fined 3-5 times the amount of the base calculation for unlawful collection, distribution or use of personal information.
  • Officials shall be fined the amount of the base calculation A fine in the amount of 5-10 times is set.

Criminal liability

  • If this violation is repeated within a year after the application of administrative liability, criminal liability is determined in accordance with Article 141-2 of the Criminal Code.

Civil liability and moral damage

  • Civil Code According to Article 1021, a teacher can apply to the court for moral damage caused to him and demand the recovery of the amount of damage.
  • Moral damage must be compensated when the teacher's reputation, value, and work honor are damaged.
  • The amount of damage is determined by the court based on the physical and moral suffering of the victim and the actions of the culprit.
