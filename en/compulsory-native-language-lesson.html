A simple life hack that will help you prepare for the mandatory native language even while sitting on the Internet.

There are many tests to find correctly or incorrectly written words in the mandatory native language. Therefore, while you are studying and writing on the Internet for fun (as if you are writing an essay at school), write incorrectly:

"When are we going to wawlu yeyw", not "When are we going to eat shashlik?"
"I have something to do with a girl, get my coat", not "Girls, I have news. I bought a new dress"

This will help you remember the correct spelling of words faster. IELTS recommends that you write something in English to improve your writing. But you don't make mistakes when you write in English, it's the same thing.
