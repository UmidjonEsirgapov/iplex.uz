Learn more about student loans

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
If the main rate increases, then the loan percentage will not change (i.e. will not increase).
If it is better to go to the bank, then you can also consider this option.
  • Legal basis: Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On measures to regulate the allocation of educational loans for study in higher education institutions on a fee-contract basis" (#527, 18.08.2021)
  • Loan amount: in the amount of the payment-contract agreement concluded between the student and the educational institution (no limit)
  • Repayment period: for a period of 7 years starting from the seventh month after the end of the study period (3, 4 or 5-year period)
  • Interest: for boys - in the amount of the main rate of the Central Bank (currently 14% - as of August 23, 2023), for girls - without interest (0%);
  • If the main rate changes: if the main rate of the Central Bank decreases, then the loan percentage will also decrease. If the main rate increases, then the loan percentage will not change (i.e. will not increase).
  • To whom it is given:
    • full-time, part-time and evening education
    • bachelor or master
    • students enrolled on a fee-contract basis
    • or transferred their studies

Educational credit is not given for the super-contract or for the second specialty, distance, special correspondence forms.

For which HEIs: educational credit in Uzbekistan, state, non-state or foreign

Higher; branches

✅ or given to students studying in a joint program.

Educational loans are not granted for studying at HEIs located abroad. — More information

  • Guarantee: family members of the borrower who have a permanent source of income (parents, brothers, sisters, husband or wife) or other third parties are joint borrowers in the educational loan agreement can participate as a recipient (that is, it is not necessary to be a relative).
  • Citizenship: education credit is given only to citizens of Uzbekistan. Banks themselves can give loans to foreign citizens and stateless persons on commercial terms.
  • Required documents: the following documents are submitted to the bank:
    • application;
    • agreement (contract) between the student and the educational institution;
    • a document on provision of loan repayment. This does not require a pledge or guarantee for the education loan allocated to the children of families included in the "Single Register of Social Protection";
    • a certificate confirming the inclusion in the "Single Register of Social Protection" (if any);
    • other documents specified in the internal documents (policy) of the bank, including information and documents related to the joint borrower.
  • Application processing time: the application must be processed and the loan agreement signed within 5 working days. If it is rejected, the student will receive a response letter stating the reason.
  • Premature repayment: the borrower has the right to repay the educational loan before the terms specified in the contract.
  • When and how to apply: you can apply for an educational loan today. You don't need to go to the bank for this, you can also send an application through https://talim-krediti.uz. If you prefer to go to the bank, then you can also consider this option.
