Singapore International Graduate Award (SINGA) for Ph.D. applications are being accepted for the program.

Candidates from all countries can apply in biomedicine, computing and information, engineering and technology, and physics.

SINGA is mainly intended for international students studying in Singapore for the first time.

Singaporeans, permanent residents of the country and international students studying in higher education institutions are not eligible.

The project will be conducted in English in the laboratory of Singaporean research institutes chosen by the candidates.

Successfully completed students will be awarded a Ph.D. degree.

Accepted students will be exempted from tuition fees and will receive a monthly stipend of $1500−1800.

In addition, approx. $1,200 will cover flight expenses.

Candidates must submit a passport, photo, bachelor's and master's diploma, 2 letters of recommendation, GRE scores depending on the university, and language certificates.

Applications can be sent through the link until June 1st.
