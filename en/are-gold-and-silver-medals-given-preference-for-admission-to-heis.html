Are gold and silver medals given preference for admission to HEIs?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Awarding talented graduates of schools with gold and silver medals does not provide privileges for entering higher education institutions. The regulation "On the procedure for awarding talented graduates of secondary educational institutions and academic lyceums with gold and silver medals" does not provide for the granting of privileges to students awarded with gold and silver medals.
According to this regulation, gold and silver medals are awarded to graduates who have studied with excellent grades, have exemplary behavior and are active in public affairs.
