Vistula University: Full scholarships to study in Poland

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:

General information:

One of the most prestigious universities in Poland, Vistula University and Vistula School of Hospitality, who wants to study at bachelor and master levels with high academic performance. provides up to 100% grants to support young people financially. All citizens can apply for this grant. On average, 70 foreign students are offered this grant.

Grant amount: 100% of the contract fee is exempted for the 1st academic year.

Requirements for candidates:

  • For all foreign students (Polish students cannot apply);
  • Having high academic performance. must have;
  • Language certificate at least - IELTS 6.0;

Admission Process;

  • Register online;
  • You must pay the registration fee;
  • Register for undergraduate or graduate courses taught in English and Get an acceptance letter for March 2022 courses;

Deadline: January 15, 2022 for March 2022 studies;

August 20, 2022 for October 2022 studies;
