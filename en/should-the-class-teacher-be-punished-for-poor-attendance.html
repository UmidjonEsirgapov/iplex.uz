According to the order of the Minister of Preschool and School Education No. 565 of December 20, 2023, among the teacher's job duties, the obligation to check students' attendance and enter it into the class journal is defined. However, among these obligations, the task of ensuring that students are in the classroom is not specified.

Therefore, it is wrong to discipline the head of the class for low attendance of students. In this case, the disciplinary punishment is disproportionate to the actions of the teacher, and according to Articles 301 and 313 of the Labor Code of the new version, the teacher can apply to the District Justice Department or the State Labor Law Inspector.

Action can be taken against parents in case of students not coming to class.
