The Ministry of National Education has presented a new educational model for pre-school, primary and secondary schools starting from the 2024-2025 academic year.

The Ministry of National Education of Turkey has announced significant changes in history textbooks. The new curriculum Instead of the term "Central Asia" the term "Turkestan" is used. Experts said that this innovation is aimed at forming a sense of Turkish nationalism and patriotism in the young generation. He explained the need to strengthen the unity of the Turkish world under the slogan "Our unity is our strength". It is necessary to ensure the strong unity and solidarity of the world of our nation. As the Republic of Turkey, we will continue to work towards the peace, prosperity and security of the Turkish world. We will work together and spread the concept of the Turkish Age to the Organization of Turkic States in order to turn the next period into a Turkish era, he said. In this context, "Central Asia" will replace the old terms "Turkestan" and "Relocation and Resettlement Law".
