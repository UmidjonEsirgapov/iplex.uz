The University of Dayton, located in the United States, is accepting applications for undergraduate courses starting in the spring semester of 2021 and the grant program allocated for these courses.

Who is the grant for?

Those who want to study in the undergraduate course for;

Benefits of the grant:

The total value of the grant is US$105,390 to cover the 4-year tuition fee. About tuition fee (see “cost” section);

Requirements:

  1. Candidate must have graduated high school, high school or college with good grades. Scholarships are awarded to candidates with the highest marks;
  2. A candidate must have a good knowledge of English and (depending on the field of application) at least TOEFL 70-80 or IELTS 6.0-6.5 certificates. Details;
  3. Some courses may have special admission requirements. It is advisable for you to familiarize yourself with the details of the course you are interested in;

Program admission process:

  1. First you need to apply for the bachelor's course. Documents are to be submitted here by 14 December 2021;

Original or official copies of documents submitted when applying for an undergraduate course must be sent to the university by post. Due to COVID-19, electronic copies of documents may be sent to goglobal@udayton.edu for now.

Required documents:

  • Application and essay;
  • Recommendation;
  • High school, high school or college transcript or diploma and grades (official copy);
  • Certificate of English language test;
  • "Letter of financial support" and "bank statement" - documents confirming the candidate's financial status. Here you can find some videos about the application process.
