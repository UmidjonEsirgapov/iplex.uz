In social networks, information is spreading that higher education institutions are asking students for various documents. The Ministry of Justice announced that it is against the law to demand some of them.

In particular, the following documents have been canceled from residents:

  • a copy of an identity document;
  • a photograph of a person in the prescribed format;
  • a secondary special, professional (vocational) educational institution or a general secondary educational institution by an individual. (on the basis of 11th grade) information (documents) confirming the completion of education;
  • information (documents) confirming registration as a low-income family or family member;
  • information confirming the registration of tax-paying natural persons in the state tax service bodies (STIR);
  • applicants with privileges for admission to higher education institutions information about the document confirming the right.

In addition, the request for these documents and information is considered a violation of the requirements of the legislation in the field of public services, and the state agencies that requested them will be held accountable.

In case of such documents, it is said that citizens can apply to the Ministry of Justice and regional justice bodies. — Official Data
