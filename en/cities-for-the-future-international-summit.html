Cities for the future international summit
The international youth summit is a dialogue with the participation of students aimed at stimulating discussions and innovative solutions on various socio-economic issues in developing countries. The dialogue attempts to find solutions to these problems, and participants receive recognition and rewards for advancing their proposals. The purpose of the event is to encourage young people, to instill in them the spirit and sense of thinking to find creative solutions to various problems facing their country and the world today, to combine their concerns with the necessary skills and create effective solutions. Entries will be evaluated and six entries will be shortlisted for the final round, where they will have to give a detailed 10-15 minute presentation of their proposal live. Video recordings of the presentation are welcome from those facing time zone issues.

Sequence:
a) Phase 1 - Deadline: April 15, 2022.

Candidates/teams interested in the field submit their idea or proposal. These presentations will be evaluated and the top 6 entries will be selected for the final stage.

b) Stage 2 - Final Event: 30th April 2022.

Shortlisted candidates should give a 15-20 minute presentation giving a more detailed overview of their idea/proposal.

THEME:
Theme of this year's Youth Summit "Cities for the Future".

JOB REQUIREMENTS:
1st stage: written applications.

a. The work should be between 500 and 750 words.
b. Must be written in English.
c. When writing a text, it is necessary to refer to and cite all sources correctly, observing the consistent style of citation.
d. The text must be the original work of the entrant(s). No plagiarism is allowed.
e. Originality of ideas and an analytical approach are preferred over descriptive essays. Please avoid overly descriptive words and try to justify your ideas.

Step 2: Presentations.

a. The presentation should consist of a maximum of 20 slides (excluding title and reference slides).
b. The allotted time for the presentation is 15-20 minutes.
c. The presentation must be in English.
d. All sources should be cited (in-text) and properly cited at the end of the presentation.
e. Submissions must be the original work of the entrant(s). No plagiarism is allowed.

NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS PER GROUP:
Participants may register in groups of a maximum of 3 people. They may also register separately.

ELIGIBILITY:
Participants must be enrolled in an undergraduate program or higher in any field of study.

Cost of Participation:
The event is free to all.

REGISTRATION DEADLINE DEADLINE:
Deadline for submissions is April 15, 2022.

PRIZES:
• First Place Prize – $1,000
• Second Place Prize – $500
• Third Place Prize – $250
