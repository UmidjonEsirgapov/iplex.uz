Starting from the new academic year, the certificate will be mandatory for employment

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
It is known that according to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated May 19, 2021 No. 312, from the academic year 2022/2023, candidates who are being recruited for the position of foreign language teacher of state educational institutions must have at least B2 level national or equivalent having an international certificate of the appropriate level.
Last year, in order to ensure the continuity of the process of teaching foreign languages in schools, the Government issued an order not to hire foreign language teachers who do not have a national or international certificate of B2 level, which will come into force from the 2023/2024 academic year.
Taking into account the above, Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, a B2 level certificate is required from new foreign language teachers.
Candidates for employment should have at least a B2 level national certificate or an equivalent international certificate.

Taking into account the above, starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, new foreign language teachers are required to have a B2 level certificate.

We remind you that from the 2024/2025 academic year any foreign language teacher in a school is required to have a B2 level certificate — Source
