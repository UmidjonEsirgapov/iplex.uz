Transfer Test Appeal: Applications are now being accepted

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Appeal applications have been accepted in connection with the transfer tests. It has started.
This process will continue until August 27, and applications will be accepted from applicants online through the my.uzbmb.uz site, through a personal office. started

The process of accepting appeal applications for those who are dissatisfied with the results of the test conducted on the transfer of students from foreign and non-state higher education institutions to state higher education institutions has begun. This process will continue until August 27, and applications will be accepted online from applicants via the my.uzbmb.uz site, through a personal cabinet.

To submit an appeal application, the applicant must have participated in the test and not been excluded from the test. After the application is submitted, the applicant will be given a notification indicating the date and time of its review.

The applicant, at the time specified in the notification, must come to the Knowledge Assessment Agency with a document confirming his identity, a notification and a permit.

Through this process, students can check their knowledge once again and get a fair evaluation. — More information
