✅ A major financial crime was exposed in Andijan State University. It is said that the person who has been working as the chief accountant of the university for 27 years, the rector and 11 other officials have looted a total of 53.6 billion soums of state funds. The chief accountant, the rector and other officials who worked for 27 years colluded and embezzled state funds.

How were 53.6 billion soums stolen?

They embezzled student scholarships, contract money, incentive funds, dormitory fees and other resources in different ways. For this, even signatures and other documents of the students were forged.

Documents were forged on behalf of the students

According to the documents, the students were not aware of this. Fake signatures were put on their behalf, money was taken in cash and looted.
