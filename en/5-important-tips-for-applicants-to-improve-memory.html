Memorizing and recalling information is an important process for applicants. Effective learning requires memory consolidation. Below are 5 important recommendations for improving memory.

1. Review what you've read

Remember new information within 24 hours to remember it quickly. It is recommended to plan the repetition interval as follows:

  • First repetition in 24 hours
  • Second repetition in 3-4 days
  • Reinforcement in 1 week and 1 month

2. Write an outline

Handwritten information settles better in the brain. pretty writing of important places, use of diagrams and color drawings help to remember knowledge better.

3. Explain what you've learned to others

Whether someone is listening or not, explaining to others speeds up the learning process. Thus, you organize the information and help keep it in the brain's long-term memory.

4. Eat right

Eat products that improve brain activity:

  • Walnuts - the most useful food for strengthening memory
  • Fish - Omega-3 activates brain cells
  • Greens - improves blood circulation
  • Eggs - rich in vitamins that improve brain functions
  • Dark chocolate - helps to increase concentration

5. Don't forget to get enough sleep

During sleep the brain organizes the learned information. 7-8 hours of good quality sleep a day is necessary to strengthen memory and retain what has been learned.

Conclusion

For effective learning, regular repetition, writing, explanation, proper nutrition and enough sleep are important. Follow these tips and your knowledge will be solid! — Read on infoedu.uz
