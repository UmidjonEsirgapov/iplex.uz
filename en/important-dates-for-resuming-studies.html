Important note for students who want to resume their studies!

Students who want to resume their studies for the spring semester can submit an application from December 20 to January 20.

Application review period

Submitted applications will be reviewed from January 21 to January 31 and a relevant decision will be made on this basis.

Conditions for resuming studies

Resuming studies is mainly carried out on the basis of a payment contract. However, the following categories of students can be reinstated on the basis of a state grant:

  • Bereaved and Group I and II disabled;
  • Conducted military service, if they have submitted an application for return to studies within 3 years after discharge from the reserve;
  • Students who were sent to study abroad by the order of the state and returned within the specified period.

Don't forget the important dates!

If you want to resume your studies, do not miss the January 20 deadline for submitting an application! — Source
