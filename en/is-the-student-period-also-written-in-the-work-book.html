Is the student period also written in the work book?

The employer should write information about recruitment, transfer to another permanent job and termination of the employment contract in the labor record.

I heard that I am currently working, so should I get the work book from my office or from my place of study?"

Nodira Tosheva, legal consultant of Yunusabad District Employment Assistance Center, answered khabar.uz reporters:

- According to the second part of Article 81 of the Labor Code, the work book is the main document confirming the length of service of an employee. The employer must keep a labor record for all employees who have worked at the enterprise for more than five days, with the exception of those who work on a temporary basis. At the employee's request, entries about periods of temporary work and temporary transfer to another job are included in the work book. The grounds (reasons) for the termination of the employment contract are not recorded in the employment record.

Also, in accordance with Part 2, Clause 2 of the Instruction No. 402 "On the Procedure for Keeping Employment Records", records of secondary, secondary special and higher education are only based on relevant documents (certificate, certificate, diploma)

Based on the above, your employment record will be maintained by the enterprise, institution or organization where you are currently working, and after you graduate from a higher education institution, information about your 4-year student period will also be included in your diploma. — Read on infoedu.uz
