Digital University of Japan was established in Tashkent

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Decision No. 762 of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan on December 2, 2020 "On the establishment of the Digital University of Japan in Tashkent" was adopted.
The university prepares personnel in the form of full-time and distance education for undergraduate education.

Decision No. 762 of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan on December 2, 2020 "On the establishment of the Japanese digital university in Tashkent" was adopted.

Training of competitive personnel with higher education in ICT, software development, management of information systems and computer networks, and others were defined as the main tasks and areas of activity of the university.

According to the decision, the Digital University of Japan in Tashkent is considered a non-state higher education institution. taking into account the requirements of the state education standards, it is carried out on a fee-contract basis in Japanese and Uzbek according to the curricula and science programs approved in agreement with the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

The parameters of admission to the university in the academic year 2020/2021 and subsequent academic years are determined in agreement with the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education. in the field of education - from the next academic year.

Each year, no less than 10 applicants from families in need of social protection who are capable, talented and have high learning rates are admitted to study free of charge (on the basis of a university grant). Diplomas of the Republic of the established model are issued.

The university is located on the first floor of the 3rd educational building of the State University of World Languages of Uzbekistan at the address of 21-a Kichik Khalka Yoli street, Tashkent city, Uchtepa district. — Read on infoedu.uz
