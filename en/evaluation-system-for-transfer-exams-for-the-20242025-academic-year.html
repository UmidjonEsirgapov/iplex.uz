Evaluation system for transfer exams for the 2024/2025 academic year

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:

Evaluation criteria for students applying to transfer in the 2024/2025 academic year were set as follows:

On 3 subjects that are mandatory for all test takers:

  1. Native language (Uzbek, Russian or Karakalpak language) - 10 questions. 1.1 points for each. The maximum score is 11 points.
  2. Mathematics - 10 questions. 1.1 points for each. The maximum score is 11 points.
  3. History of Uzbekistan - 10 questions. 1.1 points for each. The maximum score is 11 points.

The total possible score is 33 points.

In 2 major subjects suitable for the undergraduate education:

  1. 1st subject - 30 questions. 3.1 points for each. The maximum score is 93 points.
  2. Subject 2 - 30 questions. 2.1 points for each. The maximum score is 63 points.

The total possible score is 156 points.

In general, in the 2024/2025 academic year, a total of 90 test tasks in 5 subjects will be given to those who request to transfer their studies. The maximum possible score is 189 points. — Official Data
