University and Scholarship Program Brief:

Florida International University is a public research university in the state of Miami, one of the most prestigious universities in the United States, with a high is one of the leaders in quality teaching, modern research, creative activities and collaboration with local and global communities.

If your academic achievements are high and you want to be rewarded for it, Florida International The university announces the First-Year merit awards for International Students grant program for students like you. This grant is provided by the university for the academic year 2021-2022.

The following candidates can apply for the grant program:

  • Eligible countries: Citizens of all countries can apply for the grant;
  • Covered programs or subjects: All undergraduate subjects offered by FIU;
  • Admission Eligibility Criteria: Applicants must be FIU first-year students.

Application Process:

  1. Application Submission: Applicants must first complete an online application to the university here. After receiving the acceptance letter from the university, the candidates can also apply for the grant program in the following order:
  • Log in to your personal portal at the university - MyFIU account;
  • From there, select the 'Student Tools' section;
  • From there, select the 'Student E-Forms' section;
  • From there, select the ’Office of Scholarships’ section;
  • Finally, select the ’merit awards Appeal Form’ section from there.

Required documents:

Candidates must submit the following documents to the university when applying:

  • Source of income (proof of financing to study and live in the US):
  • High school, college/high school diplomas and applications;
  • Motivation letter;
  • Essay response on the topic presented;
  • Two letters of recommendation;
  • Resume.

Specific requirements:

Candidates are required to have the following minimum GPA and relevant academic performance:

  • GPA of 4.0,
  • SAT score of 1280 – 1370 or ACT score of 27- 30.

Candidates are required to submit one of the following language certificates:

  • TOEFL iBT – 80
  • TOEFL PBT – 550
  • IELTS – 6.5

Scholarship Benefits:

Scholarships are available for eligible international students in the following 2 categories:

  • Presidential Premier – Candidates with an overall 4.0 GPA and 1370 SAT or 30 ACT will receive tuition waivers 100% tuition waiver and a school supplies stipend;
  • Ambassador Premier – Applicants with an overall 4.0 GPA and 1280 SAT or 27 ACT will receive a 75% tuition waiver and a school supplies stipend.

Application Deadline: 2021 May 5.
