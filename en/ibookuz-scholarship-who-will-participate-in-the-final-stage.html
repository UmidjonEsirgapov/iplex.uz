iBook.uz scholarship: who will participate in the final stage?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
On April 24, the 1st stage of this competition was held.
✅ On May 15, the 2nd stage exams were held among the candidates who passed to the second stage of the competition, and 211 participants who showed the highest results in this stage were sent to the finals.
For information, the chain of bookstores "iBook.uz" is one of the largest bookstores operating in our republic, and currently delivers more than a thousand types of books to our reading compatriots. The "iBook.uz scholarship" competition for students, announced by the initiative of "iBook.uz" and the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform, continues. On April 24, the 1st stage of this competition took place. Tens of thousands of talented students from different regions of our Republic took part in the 1st stage of the scholarship competition, and 2389 candidates who showed the best results (23 years of work and more) received a ticket to the next stage (see the results).

✅ On May 15, second-stage exams were held among the candidates who passed to the second stage of the competition, and 211 participants who showed the highest results in this stage won the final ticket. (View results)

All participating candidates can view their results and download special certificates of the competition (Download certificates).

Who will participate in the final stage of the competition?

The competition According to instructions and regulations, the candidates with the highest results in the final will have the opportunity to participate. The organizers will send a special SMS-notification to these participants.

How will the final stage take place?

Third stage: This stage is the most important stage of the competition, candidates who have qualified for the final stage will be tested at the testing center of the "Yuksalish" leadership academy in Tashkent.

Time: The 3rd stage of the competition will be held on May 29 among the selected candidates.

At this stage, the participants will write a review (personal review) based on one of the books recommended by the special commission of the competition. List of books:

— The Old Man and the Sea (Ernest Hemingway)—The Archway (Paulo Coelho)— The Day of the Century (Chingiz Aitmatov)

Reviews are evaluated on a 100 point system. In the review, candidates should be able to analyze the book in its entirety. Candidates' independent opinion, consistency and fluency of the opinions written in the review, as well as the originality of the conclusion are of particular importance.

Note: Candidates who have received a ticket to the final stage must submit personal documents through a special examination window of the OLIYGOH.UZ platform. Candidates submit the following documents online through a special platform window:

— identity document;— reference from the place of study (reference confirmed via my.gov.uz or approved by the dean's office);— CV (reference).

The results of the selection will be announced within 2 weeks.

The general results are mainly determined by the decision of the special commission. Based on the decisions made by the competition commission, the list of prize-winning students is approved. Special commission The special commission of the competition is made up of experts from the "iBook.uz" chain of bookstores, qualified teachers of the "Yuksalish" leadership academy, and OLIYGOH.UZ team members. The organization, holding and award ceremonies of the competition are carried out by a special commission.

Awarding ceremony

The winners of the scholarship competition will be solemnly awarded in the special events hall of the "iBook.uz" bookstore chain.

Also, the candidates who participated in the competition Special QR-code certificates will be given online to everyone. Certificates will be sent to participants through "Personal cabinet" on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.
