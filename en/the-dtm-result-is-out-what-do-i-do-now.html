What if the DTM score is not enough?

Admission to the International Nordic University does not depend only on the DTM score. Applicants are admitted as a student 100% legally and officially through the following ways:

  • Through DTM score (if required)
  • Through internal university test
  • Based on previous academic information (high school, college)
  • Through selection and interviews (bachelor and master for)

Applicants with low scores on the DTM test also have the opportunity to study at Nordic.

Admission process: simple and fast

Applying at the International Nordic University takes no more than 10 minutes. All processes are carried out through the online platform:

Steps of document submission:

  1. Enter the site https://oliygoh.uz/nordik
  2. Fill out the application form
  3. Upload a copy of the passport, diploma or certificate
  4. Choose the direction
  5. Confirm the document - that's it!

You will be contacted by university operators within 1 business day after registration.
