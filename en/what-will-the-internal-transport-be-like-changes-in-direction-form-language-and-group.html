Complete information about all types of internal transfer

Internal transfer is the study within one higher education institution means transfer to another course, form, language or group. Below is detailed information about all types of internal transport and how to implement them.

1. Changing the field of study

✅ Transfer to suitable and related fields

  • Application submission: from July 15 to August 5

  • Platform: transfer.edu.uz

  • Decision: OTM issued by the responsible commission

⚠️ Transfer to non-corresponding courses

  • Application submission: via transfer.edu.uz

  • Decision: reviewed by the State Commission

2. Changing the form of education

(between full-time, part-time, evening, distance forms)

  • Application: online via transfer.edu.uz

  • Conclusion: Responsible higher education commission

3. Changing the language of education

(Uzbek, Russian, Karakalpak and other groups)

  • Application: submitted to the dean's office in offline form

  • Decision: made by the dean's office

4. Change group

(from one group to another group)

  • Application: submitted to dean's office in offline form

  • Decision: by dean's office

5. Resumption of studies

(to own course or similar course)

  • Application: completely online through transfer.edu.uz

  • Decision: by the responsible commission of higher education institutions — Read on infoedu.uz
