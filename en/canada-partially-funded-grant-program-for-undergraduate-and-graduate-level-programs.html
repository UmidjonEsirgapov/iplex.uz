University and Program Brief:

The University of Winnipeg (UWinnipeg, UW) is a public research university in Manitoba, Canada, offering undergraduate programs in the arts, business and economics, education, science and kinesiology, and applied health, as well as several graduate programs.

The University of Winnipeg President's Scholarship for World Leaders (for International Students) The scholarship program is intended to be offered to international students admitted to one of the university's bachelor's and master's degree programs for the 1st academic year.

Benefits of the grant:

  • The winners of The University of Winnipeg President's Scholarship for World Leaders (for International Students) grant program will be awarded 5,000 Canadian dollars and this money will cover their tuition fees.

Requirements:

  1. Candidates must be international students;
  2. Candidates must have 80% or higher in their diploma;
  3. Candidates must be accepted into one of the undergraduate or graduate programs offered by the university;
  4. Candidates must demonstrate leadership

Application process:

  • Candidates must apply and be accepted into one of the undergraduate or graduate programs offered by the university;
  • Then download and complete the online grant application form here;

Required documents:

  • CV;
  • Personal statement of 250-300 words;
  • Two letters of reference showing your active participation in extracurricular/volunteer activities;

All required documents in one PDF file to the following email address should be sent to:

  • awards@uwinnipeg.ca

Deadline is June 1, 2021
