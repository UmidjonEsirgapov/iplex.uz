Plastic bags may be banned in Uzbekistan from 2027

A national environmental protection program draft is being developed in Uzbekistan. According to this program, the production, import and use of plastic bags may be completely banned from 2027. Currently, the project is publicly discussed.

Environmental alternatives to plastic bags

The proposed national program envisages the gradual reduction of plastic bags and encouraging the use of biodegradable environmental goods instead. It is planned to:

  • Reduce the volume of plastic waste,
  • Populate biodegradable packaging materials,
  • Improve the ecological environment.

Chemical content restrictions

The draft program proposes to introduce restrictions on the chemical content of not only plastic bags, but also other plastic products does. Also:

  • Regulations for the use of recycled plastic in packaging will be established;
  • Restrictions on the use of harmful chemicals will be strengthened.

Conclusion

This initiative is aimed at ensuring environmental stability, keeping the environment clean and reducing plastic waste. The introduction of biodegradable alternative products instead of plastic bags can be one of the important steps of Uzbekistan towards environmental reforms. — Source
