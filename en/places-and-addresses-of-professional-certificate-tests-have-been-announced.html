In these processes, a list of places where professional certification tests for pedagogues will be held has been announced.

1. Republic of Karakalpakstan - National Center for Training Teachers in New Methods (Ye. Alakoz Street 54, Nukus, destination: next to the MEGA NUKUS shopping complex);

2. Andijan region - National center for training teachers in new methods (Andijan city, Istiqlal street 8, destination: former Leninsky street);

3. Bukhara region - Kogon district specialized school ("Choloki" MFY, Qasri Orifon settlement, house 10, destination: Bakhovuddin Naqishbandi Shrine);

4. Jizzakh region - the 4th general education school in the city of Jizzakh (Jizzakh city, Kimyogar neighborhood, Toqimachilar street 45, destination: regional eye disease hospital);

5. Kashkadarya Region - National Center for Training Teachers in New Methods (address: Olimlar Street 2, Karshi City, destination: Karshi State University);

6. Navoi region - Navoi city Barkamol Avlod children's school (Aloka street 6, Navoi city, destination: at the back of Navoi shopping center (SUM);

7. Namangan region - 15th general secondary school in Namangan city (Namangan city, Ghishtkoprik MFY, Uychi street, house 329-A, destination - Chorsu);

8. Samarkand region - National center for training teachers in new methods (Samarkand city, Hasan Obiddinov street, 7-u.)

9. Syrdarya Region - Regional Children's School of the Perfect Generation (Guliston city, Shodlik MFY, Navoi street 55, destination: Guliston city recreation park);

10. Surkhandarya region - general secondary school 26 in Termiz city, (Termiz city, Kattabog MFY, Farovon massif, district 2, address 7, destination: Yashil Dunya market);

11. Fergana region - 28th general secondary school in Fergana city (Fergana city, "Istiqlol" MFY, A. Qadiri street -13A, destination "Chery" car showroom);

12. Tashkent region - Center for assessment of knowledge and skills of teachers (Tashkent city, Beruniy street 83-A, destination: metro station "Tinchlik", the old building of "IMZO");

13. Khorezm region – 36th general education school in Urganch district (Urganch district Mevazor MFY Kuvonch street 51, address: Urganch district administration);

14. Tashkent city - Center for assessment of knowledge and skills of pedagogues (Tashkent city, 83-A-Beruni Street, destination: "Tinchlik" metro station, old building of "IMZO" company).
