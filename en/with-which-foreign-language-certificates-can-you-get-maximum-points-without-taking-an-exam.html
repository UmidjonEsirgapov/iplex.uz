With which foreign language certificates can you get maximum points without taking an exam?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The list of national and international certificates that give the opportunity to get the maximum score in the subject of a foreign language in the entrance exams to higher education institutions has been published. certificates give the maximum score?

The list of national and international certificates that gives the opportunity to get the maximum score in the subject of foreign language in the entrance exams to higher education institutions has been announced. Applicants with this certificate do not take a foreign language exam and are evaluated with maximum points in this subject.

Which certificates give maximum points?

If an applicant has a certificate of knowledge of a foreign language at the B2 level, he will receive maximum points without taking an exam. The following certificates are officially recognized:

English language:

  • National certificate (B2 and C1)
  • TOEFL iBT (72-94 – B2, 95-113 – C1, 114-120 – C2)
  • Cambridge Assessment English
    • FCE (160-179 – B2)
    • CAE (180-199 – C1)
    • CPE (200-220 – C2)
  • IELTS Academic (5.5-6.5 – B2, 7.0-8.0 – C1, 8.5-9.0 – C2)

French:

  • National Certificate (B2, C1)
  • DELF (B2)
  • DALF (C1, C2)

German:

  • National Certificate (B2, C1, C2)
  • Goethe-Certificate (B2, C1, C2)
  • TestDaF
    • TDN 3 (B2.1)
    • TDN 4 (B2.2 – C1.1)
    • TDN 5 (C1.2)
  • DSD II (B2, C1)

Spanish:

  • National Certificate (B2, C1)
  • DELE (B2, C1, C2)
  • SIELE (B2, C1, C2)

Italian, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Turkish and other languages:

  • National certificates (B2, C1)
  • International certificates:
    • HSK (4 – B2, 5 – C1, 6 – C2)
    • TOPIK II (Level 4 – B2, Level 5 C1, Level 6 – C2)
    • Turkish Proficiency Test (TYS) (B2, C1)
    • ATI Tanal Al Arabi (B2, C1, C2)

Conclusion

If the applicant has B2 or B2 of the above national or international certificates if he has a higher degree than that, he does not take the exam in the subject of a foreign language and receives the maximum score. Therefore, obtaining this certificate before the exam gives a great advantage to reduce the exam load and achieve maximum results. — Read on infoedu.uz
