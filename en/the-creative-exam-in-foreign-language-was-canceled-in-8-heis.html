In 2024, the creative exam in foreign language has been canceled in the higher education institutions (HEIs) on this list, and a test exam will be held like other HEIs with foreign language courses.

The creative exam has been canceled in the following HEIs:

  1. Uzbekistan State University of World Languages
  2. Samarkand State Institute of Foreign Languages
  3. Tashkent State University of Oriental Studies
  4. Tashkent State Pedagogical University
  5. Kokan State Pedagogical Institute
  6. Nukus State Pedagogical Institute
  7. Andijan State Institute of Foreign Languages
  8. Namangan State Foreign Languages institute

Starting from 2024, a test exam will be held instead of a creative exam in a foreign language in the undergraduate education programs of these educational institutions that train pedagogues in foreign languages.
