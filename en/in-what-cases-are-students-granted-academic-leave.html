In what cases are students granted academic leave?

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
According to the law, academic leave can be granted to students of higher education institutions of Uzbekistan in the following cases: - to complete military service; - to restore health; - for pregnancy and childbirth; - to take care of children.
Academic leave in any part of the academic year, for the following periods - for military service - for the duration of military service; - for health recovery - for a period of 12 months; - for pregnancy and childbirth, as well as childcare - for the duration of pregnancy and childbirth, as well as childcare leave.
Students can be granted academic leave 2 times during their studies (up to 3 times for students of medical educational institutions).
