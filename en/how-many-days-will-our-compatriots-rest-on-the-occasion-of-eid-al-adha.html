How many days will our compatriots rest on the occasion of Eid al-Adha?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The day of Eid al-Adha is expected to fall on June 16 or June 17 this year, on the tenth day of Zul Hijjah according to the Hijri calendar.
If Eid falls on June 16, the holidays will be as follows: June 15, Saturday — a holiday for 5-day students, a working day for 6-day students; June 16, Sunday - Eid al-Adha; June 17, Monday is a day off for everyone (according to Article 208 of the Labor Code, if the holiday falls on a Sunday, the day off is moved to Monday); Tuesday, June 18 is an additional day off for everyone (according to the President's decree No. PF-213 dated 12.22.2023).
