As the exam approaches, many people have one question: how to prepare for all subjects? In fact, to achieve this, you only need to follow 3 important steps.

1. Admit the truth

The first stage of exam preparation is self-analysis.

  • Which subjects are you strong in?

  • And which ones should you admit you are not ready for.

  • How long before the exam? calculate the rest.

For example, you have 3 months. Write down the strengths and weaknesses of each subject. By doing this, you will organize the confusion in your mind and it will be easier for you to make a plan.

2. Make a clear plan

A plan is the key to success. Plan each day:

  • Let's say you need to study 1000 pages in subject 1 and 500 pages in subject 2.

  • To master in 3 months, it is enough to read 12 pages of subject 1 and 6 pages of subject 2 every day.

  • Last leave a week to repeat.

This method will prepare you thoroughly for all subjects.

3. Don't forget to adapt to the plan

Even the most ideal plan should be resilient to life's circumstances. For example, if you cannot follow your plan due to illness or other reasons:

  • If you have a plan, you know exactly where you are behind.

  • Calculating how many pages you need to reach, you can distribute the remaining time properly.

In this way, you have enough time for the exam in any case. you will be able to prepare.
