Brief about the grant program:

LifeAsapa Foundation is now offering the LifeAsapa Undergraduate Student Scholarship grant program to outstanding international students for the academic year 2021-2022. The scholarship program is offered as a support to students who want to study in a technical bachelor's program at one of the partner universities in Denmark.

Benefits of the scholarship:

Usually, the scholarship is offered to the winners in the following three forms:

  1. A grant of USD 10,000 and a monthly stipend of USD 2,000 for the first year of the undergraduate course;
  2. Grant money in the amount of 50% of the fee for the three-year undergraduate course;
  3. Grant money in the amount of 100% of the fee for the full period of the undergraduate course.

Requirements for candidates:

Candidates who meet the following requirements can apply for the grant program:

  • Candidates are citizens of the United States and Canada or this must not be permanent residents of the countries;
  • Must have received a letter of acceptance from a partner university to study on an undergraduate program at the university of their choice;
  • Graduated with excellent grades, for example:
    • 4 points in GCE A levels;
    • A total of 98% in an approved Foundation programme;
    • International Baccalaureate (IB) 44 points in the program.

Required documents:

  1. CV (you can prepare a CV according to European standards through this link);
  2. Grant application - you must fill it out online here;
  3. Motivational letter (up to 1000 words);
  4. English proof of proficiency, written by someone you know who is a native English speaker;
  5. Bachelor's degree or equivalent and transcript of grades;
  6. GRE, TOEFL or IELTS scores.

You must send all the documents requested above in one PDF FILE to the following email address:

  • scholarship@lifeasapa.com

Deadline: December 31, 2021
