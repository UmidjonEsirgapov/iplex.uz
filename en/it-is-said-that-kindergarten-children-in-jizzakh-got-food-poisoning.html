News spread on social networks that children were poisoned by food in a non-governmental preschool education organization in Pakhtakor district of Jizzakh region.

Provincial prosecutor's office investigated the situation. As it turned out, on August 2, the body temperature of girls named 4-year-old I.F. and 5-year-old S.T. raised in the kindergarten. The children were taken to the hospital by their parents and diagnosed with food poisoning. After that, the children were admitted to the Department of Infectious Diseases.

On the same day, the remaining 14 children being educated in the kindergarten were observed by doctors and the necessary procedures were performed. Since they did not complain about their health, they were sent home.

Also, during the epidemiological investigation, samples were taken from the food products given to the children during breakfast.

It is noted that the Pakhtakor district prosecutor's office is currently conducting an investigation into the situation. A legal decision will be made by the prosecutor's office based on the results of the appropriate examination. — Source
