How are scholarships redistributed? A new procedure for students

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
According to the decision of the Government of March 10, 2025, the procedure for the redistribution of grants in higher education institutions was officially approved. will be passed.
Academic mastery - the maximum GPA of 80 points will be multiplied by 16.

According to the Government decision of March 10, 2025, The procedure for redistribution of grants in higher education institutions was officially approved. Now in order to get an education grant, students are selected not only based on GPA or social status, but based on clear assessment criteria.

The main requirements for the redistribution of grants:

  • The student's GPA must be 3.5 and above.

  • GPA Students with a score below 3.5 cannot participate in the competition.

  • Students register through the official site and upload documents confirming their achievements.

Evaluation criteria:

1. Academic performance — maximum 80 points

GPA is multiplied by 16. For example, if GPA is 3.8: 3.8 × 16 = 60.8 points.

2. Social activity — maximum 20 points

The following activities of the student are evaluated by a special commission in a point system (based on a total of 100 points, divided into 5):

  • Book reading culture

  • Participation in "5 important initiatives"

  • Volunteer work

  • Science Olympiads

  • Visit to the theater/museums

  • Sports and healthy lifestyle

  • Full participation in educational classes

  • Observance of academic discipline

  • Participation in "Enlightenment classes" and others

Steps of the process:

Grants are redistributed in 6 stages:

  1. Student registers

  2. Academic and social activity is evaluated

  3. Results are submitted to the commission

  4. A special committee will formulate the results

  5. The board will take a decision

  6. The results will be announced by August 25

What happens if there is a tie?

If two or more students have the same score collects:

  • GPA prevails first

  • If GPA is also equal, a test of specialized subjects will be conducted. — Official Data
