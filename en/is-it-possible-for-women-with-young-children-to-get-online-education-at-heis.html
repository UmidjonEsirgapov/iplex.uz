Can women with young children study online at higher education institutions?

According to paragraph 11 of the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 344 of June 3, 2021, women with young children can, according to their wishes, study at a higher education institution remotely or they can transfer to the external form. According to this decision, based on the student's application, the rector or director of the higher education institution issues an order, in which the student is transferred to online education if there is a distance form in the field of education.

Also, if the student due to health cannot attend classes for more than a month or due to pregnancy and childbirth in related cases, he has the right to receive an academic leave based on an application.

Reasons for switching to online education:

  1. If an academic leave is necessary to restore health
  2. Pregnancy and childbirth
  3. Possibility to continue part-time or remote based on an academic leave

According to these conditions, if the higher education institution has distance education, women with young children can use the right to receive online education. — Official Data
