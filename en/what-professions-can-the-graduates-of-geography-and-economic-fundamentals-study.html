What professions can the graduates of geography and economic fundamentals study?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Graduates of geography and economic foundations of knowledge can work in the following areas of professional activity: carry out pedagogical activities as teachers of the subjects of geography, economic knowledge and entrepreneurship in general secondary education in accordance with the professional standard of a general secondary school teacher; Pedagogical activities as teachers of geography, basics of economic knowledge and basics of entrepreneurship in secondary special and professional education; Engage in scientific-pedagogical research works in the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan and branch scientific-research institutes and as an independent researcher; Management of the educational institution in accordance with the professional standard of the director of the general secondary school; Laboratory assistant in general secondary school, professional educational institutions, higher educational institutions; The head of the cabinet.

Graduates of geography and economic fundamentals can work in the following areas of professional activity:

  • In accordance with the professional standard of general secondary education school teacher, teachers of geography, economic fundamentals and entrepreneurship in general secondary education
  • Pedagogical activities as teachers of geography, fundamentals of economic knowledge and business basics in secondary special and professional education;
  • Engage in scientific-pedagogical research works at the Academy of Sciences and network research institutes of the Republic of Uzbekistan and as an independent researcher;
  • General secondary school director managing an educational institution according to the professional standard;
  • Laboratory assistant in general secondary school, professional educational institutions, higher education institutions;
  • Cabinet head.
