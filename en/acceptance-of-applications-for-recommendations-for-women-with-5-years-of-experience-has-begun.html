Acceptance of applications for recommendations for women with 5 years of experience has begun

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Applicants must submit the following documents to the working groups of the district (city) where they are permanently residing or temporarily registered with the application until June 1 of this year (also on this day): a copy of a citizen's passport or ID card; extract from the labor record (electronic labor record); diploma in secondary special or professional education.
Women who receive this recommendation, if they fail, will have one more chance and can enter the contract study through one of the 500 places among all failed recommendations.
To receive a recommendation, applicants must meet all of the following criteria: secondary special or to have a specialty in professional education; that he has not received or is not receiving higher education before receiving the letter of recommendation; having at least 5 years of work experience in the specialty of secondary special or professional education.

Women who have at least 5 years of work experience in their specialty, but do not have a higher education, will be admitted to state higher education institutions on a fee-contract basis within the framework of special admission parameters (500 quotas in total).

This women who want to use the privilege must have a letter of recommendation from the Family and Women's Committee.

Applicants must submit the following documents to the working groups of the district (city) where they are temporarily registered with an application for permanent residence or temporary registration:

  • a copy of a citizen's passport or ID card;
  • work an extract from the notebook (electronic work book);
  • diploma of secondary special or professional education.

THIS IS IMPORTANT: just because I received a recommendation, does not mean that I entered the study. You also need to get a high enough score for this. If the women who received this letter of recommendation fail to study, they will have one more chance and can enter the contract through one of the 500 places among all those who have failed the letter of recommendation.

NOTE: Women who received a letter of recommendation in 2022 and 2023, but could not enter the study, do not need to apply again. The letter of recommendation obtained in the last 2 years is still valid, and they can apply with it.
