A paid internship system will be introduced for students

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Acceptance of the Presidential Decree (No. PQ-300, September 11, 2023) on measures to implement the "Uzbekistan - 2030" strategy in 2023 in a timely manner.

According to it, the task of developing a draft decision of the Cabinet of Ministers on introducing a paid internship system in state organizations for students included in the "Youth Register" by November 2023.

Measures for quality and timely implementation of the "Uzbekistan - 2030" strategy in 2023 on" Presidential Decree (No. PQ-300, 11.09.2023) was adopted.

According to it, by November 2023, the task of developing a draft decision of the Cabinet of Ministers on introducing a system of paid internships in state organizations for students included in the "Youth Register" was set.

This decision provides for the following will be held:

Allocation of subsidy during the internship of the students included in the "Youth Register" in state organizations;

Implementing the internship based on the internship program in accordance with the curricula of the state higher education institutions;
