1. First of all, start with fundamental (basic) knowledge. Then you will not suffer in the future. These are: Faith, Physics, Arithmetic, Biology.

2. The main weapon of the learner is Why? - Always ask this question. Searching for the answer will help you gain a deeper understanding of the topic.

3. More important than knowledge: Anti-knowledge. That is, knowing what you don't know. As your knowledge increases, so does your anti-knowledge.

4. The biggest mistake of a learner is thinking: "I don't need to write, I will remember."

5. If you can't explain something in 5 sentences or less, you don't understand it. Those who can explain the simplest are those who understand the subject best.

6. Example of normal learning speed: You must find your thoughts about this topic 1 year ago funny.

7. Books, lectures, videos are a great source of knowledge. But the most important thing is that the knowledge that is not found everywhere and is very useful is spread by word of mouth.
