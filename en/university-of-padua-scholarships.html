University of Padua Scholarships

University of Padua undergraduate or graduate degree in Padua entirely in English offers 50 scholarships to highly talented prospective students. Only candidates with the highest achievements and school/academic grades can win the scholarship.

Grant amount: tuition fee waiver + €8,000 stipend per academic year.

Duration:

Maximum duration of the Padua International Excellence Scholarship:

-2 years for Master’s candidates;
-3 years for Bachelor’s candidates.
After the above period, students must cover their own expenses as well as tuition fees until graduation

Who can apply: highly talented prospective international students from around the world who wish to study at the University of Padua for a bachelor's or master's degree entirely in English.

How to apply: Your application may be considered for the selection process if you meet the following requirements.

-Do not hold Italian citizenship. (except for dual citizenship, including Italian citizenship);
-Possess a non-Italian foreign high school diploma (for bachelor or single-level applicants) or a non-Italian bachelor degree (for master applicants) that allows admission to the Italian university system;
-Not reside in Italy;
-Apply to one or more of the above-mentioned degree programs taught entirely in English at the University of Padua. Please note that you can apply to up to 3 programs.

If you meet these requirements, your application will automatically be considered for the selection process. No separate application or additional special documents other than those required to apply for the selected degree program are submitted.

Selection Criteria: The Academic Committee of each degree program compiles a list of eligible applicants and awards scholarships to the most deserving candidates based on their academic achievement and previous academic performance.

Selection Results: Scholarships are mainly for the first application period. allocated for candidates who submitted; as an exception, the Scientific Committees may decide to award the scholarship to eligible candidates of the second application period.

The results of the scholarship competition will be announced in accordance with the application deadline on this site.
