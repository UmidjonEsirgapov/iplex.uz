Is there a 12% refund when paying for a student contract?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to Article 378, paragraph 16 of the Tax Code, wages and other incomes that are to be taxed for education in professional and higher educational institutions of Uzbekistan (education of one's own children or children under 26 years of age or husband (wife)) is not taxed.
So, income tax is not deducted from the funds paid for the following 3 categories of contracts: If a citizen transfers to his own contract; If parents transfer to the contract of their child under the age of 26; If a husband or wife transfers to the contract of a spouse under the age of 26.
This privilege applies to which educational institutions: students of professional education (vocational schools, colleges and technical institutes); to students of higher education.

According to paragraph 16 of Article 378 of the Tax Code, taxes are not imposed on wages and other incomes that should be taxed for education in professional and higher education organizations of Uzbekistan (education of one's own or children under 26 years of age or husband (wife)).

So, the following Income tax is not deducted from funds paid for contracts of 3 categories:

  1. If a citizen transfers to his own contract;
  2. Parents transfer to the contract of a child under 26 years old;
  3. If a husband or wife transfers to the contract of a spouse under 26 years of age.

Which benefit is this? applied to educational institutions:

  1. students of professional education (vocational schools, colleges and technical institutes);
  2. students of higher education.

Tax discount is provided when payment is made from:

  • own funds;
  • given to an individual by commercial banks educational loans;
  • funds (loans) provided by legal entities to individuals with the condition of repayment.

❗️If the payment is not from the salary, it is possible to use the tax deduction by submitting the annual income declaration.

Note: other than the above 3 categories, for example, brothers, sisters, son-in-law or daughter-in-law or other persons no benefits apply if the contract is paid. — Source
