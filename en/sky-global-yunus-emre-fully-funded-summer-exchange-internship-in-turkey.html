Sky Global & Yunus Emre Summer Exchange Program and Internship in Turkey
Country: Turkey.
Duration: 1 month (July 18, 2022 to August 19, 2022).
Financial coverage: fully funded.
For which countries: all nationalities.
Application deadline: April 30, 2022.

Financial coverage:
As mentioned above, the Sky Global International Internship Program in Turkey is a fully funded internship. All expenses covered:

-Travel expenses;
-Accommodation;
-Food;
-Monthly stipend of 3300 Turkish lira;
-City transportation expenses;
-No application fee.

Requirements:
1. Ability to read, write and speak English fluently. (CEFR standards - level C1).
2. For those who are currently studying at a higher education institution or a master's degree in one of the following fields of study:
a. Aerospace Engineering.
b. Aeronautical Engineering.
c. Mechanical engineering.
d. Electronic Engineering.
e. Computer engineering.

3. Possess 3.00/4.00 CGPA/GNO or equivalent score on the date of application.

4. There should be no restrictions on traveling to the Republic of Turkey.

5. Be over 18 years old.

Required documents:
Applicants must submit the following documents via email:
-Application form (completed and signed);
-CV (.pdf document);
-One page personal statement;
-English language certificate (.pdf document);
-Scanned copy of passport (.png, .jpg or .pdf document);
-Transcript (.pdf document);

Sky Global & To apply for the Yunus Emre 2022 Summer Exchange and Internship Program in Turkey, all documents mentioned above must be sent to skyglobal@yee.org.tr.

Once the application is verified and meets the official requirements, applicants will be notified whether they have been approved or not.
