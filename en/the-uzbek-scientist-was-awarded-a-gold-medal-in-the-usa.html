The Uzbek scientist was awarded a gold medal in the USA

On July 26-28 this year, the Silicon Valley International Invention Festival - "The Silicon Valley International Invention Festival (SVIIF)" was held in Santa Clara, California, USA.

Uzbekistan Inventor Women's Center reports that this international festival is held in order to study and encourage achievements in the field of world science and technology and new technologies being created.

SVIIF 2024 - more than 350 inventors from more than 30 countries will participate in the festival "SVIIF 2024". participated with their inventions and innovative developments in the fields.

It is noteworthy that for the first time in this international festival of inventors of the Silicon Valley, a female scientist from Uzbekistan, head of the Department of Metrology, technical regulation, standardization and certification of the Tashkent State Technical University, Parahat Matyakubova, an expert of the Center of Women Inventors of Uzbekistan, participated.

She presented her scientific work and achieved results on her invention on "Capacitive moisture meter for liquid products and pool materials" at the competition organized within the festival.

This invention of Uzbek scientist, doctor of technical sciences, professor Parahat Matyakubova was highly appreciated by international experts.

Our compatriot in the competition organized within the festival. His patent for a device for controlling the humidity of grain products and solid dispersed materials "SVIIF-2024" - in the "The Finals" nomination "Inventor Specialist Special Prize" and a gold medal.

The results of the invention were introduced to enterprises processing grain products, and high economic efficiency was achieved due to the expansion of functional capabilities. — Official Data
