Basic information:

NewsPrice organization to support the projects of all entrepreneurs and journalists who have a new project but need financial support. A cash prize program in the amount of $20,000 USD has been announced. The average amount of the grant is $2,000-2,500. A part of the grant will be paid after the initial review and approval of the projects. The remaining 2nd part will be paid after the complete publication. Mainly, the projects must meet these criteria.

Who can participate?

  • For journalists in Eastern Europe and Central Asia;
  • Must have at least 3 years of experience in the field of journalism;
  • Working in non-governmental and commercial organizations or working from home as a freelancer (hired worker) in publishing houses, writing associations all experienced editors, reporters and journalists can participate;

Registration: You can register Here. Try to answer all questions as clearly as possible. If you have any questions, you can contact This Email.

Note: All expenses must be shown in your project plan based on accurate accounts because the grant will be allocated accordingly. However, this grant does not include the costs of office rent, office equipment and other types of items.

The deadline for submitting projects December 1, 2021.
