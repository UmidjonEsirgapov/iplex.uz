Brief about the university and the grant program:

The University of Glasgow (Glasgow University; lat. Universitatis Glasguensis) is a state-sponsored research university. The university is offering 70 International Leadership Scholarships for international students for all graduate programs in the academic year 2021-2022. The scholarship is awarded as a discount on tuition fees.
