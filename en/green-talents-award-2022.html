Are you working on innovative solutions for a sustainable future? Do you want to contribute to the implementation of the UN Sustainable Development Goals as a young researcher?

Green Talents Award 2022 The Green Talents Award has started accepting applications for 2022! Share your innovative ideas with a strong focus on sustainable development and build long-term collaborations with German research institutes and international researchers.

Requirements:
-Enrolled in a Master/PhD program or hold a Master/PhD degree;
-Have an emphasis on sustainable development and an interdisciplinary approach in any area of sustainable development research required;
-Professional work experience no more than 3 years;
-Fluent knowledge of English;
-Non-German citizenship and non-resident in Germany.

The research internship is fully funded by the German Federal Ministry of Education and Research (BMBF).

Application deadline: May 24, 2022.
