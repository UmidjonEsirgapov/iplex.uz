Free buses will be introduced for schoolchildren in Kashkadarya and Surkhandarya

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
From September 1 of this year, free bus transportation for schoolchildren will be launched in Kashkadarya and Surkhandarya regions. Starting from September 1, free bus services will be launched in Kashkadarya and Surkhondarya regions as an experiment for students attending general secondary schools from remote areas on the basis of outsourcing.
This initiative is aimed at facilitating the access of schoolchildren to education and increasing their interest in studying

From September 1 of this year, free bus transportation for schoolchildren will be launched in Kashkadarya and Surkhandarya regions

President of the Republic of Uzbekistan 2024 According to Decree No. PF-37 of February 21, from September 1, 2024 in Kashkadarya and Surkhondarya regions, free bus transportation will be launched on the basis of outsourcing for students attending general secondary education schools from remote areas as a test.

According to the decree, this work Kashkadarya Region Governorship (M. Azimov) and Surkhandarya Region Governorship (U. Kasimov) are responsible for implementation.
