Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
When will part-time students go to study?
Students admitted to part-time education must also go to study from September.

When will the students of part-time education go to study?

Students admitted to part-time education should also go to study from September. The reason is that some documents are required to issue an order on their admission to study.

The following documents must be prepared and submitted:

  • The original document of completion of general secondary education (college, lyceum diploma or school certificate)
  • Enrollment record
  • eight 3×4 personal photos
  • Military ID or certificate of registration
  • Original documents confirming the existence of privileges (if the privilege exists)
  • Original copies if using national or international certificates
  • "Case" folder
