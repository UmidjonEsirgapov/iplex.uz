Brief content:
Can graduates of private universities apply for master's degrees?
They have the right to apply for fields that are compatible or similar to the field of study they studied as a bachelor.
Graduates of unrelated fields Those who studied for a bachelor's degree, which is not included in the list of related educational fields, can only apply for a master's degree in the field they graduated from. They have the right to apply for fields corresponding or related to the field of study they studied as a bachelor.

Possibility to apply for a field of study

If the graduate's bachelor's degree is shown in the list of fields of related study, he can choose one of the other suitable fields on the list for the master's degree. can apply.

Graduates of non-cognate fields

Those who studied in a bachelor's field that is not on the list of cognate educational fields can apply for a master's degree only in the field they graduated from.

Note: cognate fields have not been confirmed yet
