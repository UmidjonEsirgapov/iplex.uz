What is the procedure for transferring studies from foreign HEIs to Uzbekistan HEIs?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Transferring studies from accredited higher education institutions of foreign countries to higher education institutions of Uzbekistan is carried out by the decision of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of Uzbekistan. a copy of the rating book or academic reference; A copy of the passport.
In this case, citizens of Uzbekistan who have expressed a desire to transfer their studies will submit a special test (in the form of a test or a written work or a professional (creative) exam determined by the commission) in the commissions established in the existing ministries and departments of the system of higher education institutions of Uzbekistan. It is carried out by the decision of the State Commission on admission to educational institutions.

✅ A citizen who expresses a desire to transfer his studies submits the following documents:

  • an application;
  • a copy of a rating book or an academic reference;
  • a copy of a passport.

In this case, citizens of Uzbekistan who express a desire to transfer their studies in the system of higher educational institutions, he submits a special test (in the form of a test or a written work or a professional (creative) exam specified by the commission) in the commissions established by the existing ministries and agencies.

The result of the special test is submitted to the State Commission, and the citizen is transferred to the educational groups indicated by the decision of the State Commission on a fee-contract basis. it is not allowed to transfer students' studies to higher education institutions of Uzbekistan. — Read on infoedu.uz
