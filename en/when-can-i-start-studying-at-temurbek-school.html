When can I start studying at "Temurbek school"?

The most relevant news in the field of education today is:

Brief content:
Applicants who have completed the 9th grade and have a certificate are admitted to the "Temurbek school". "School" accepts applicants who have completed the 9th grade and have a certificate. The study period is 2 years.

Students are admitted on a merit basis. It consists of 3 exams: preliminary selection, military-vocational selection and tests. They will also undergo a medical examination.

Dictation will be taken from them, and a one-on-one interview will be held. At the end, tests are taken by the Knowledge Assessment Agency within the framework of the 5-9th grade textbooks. Those who pass all of them will be admitted to study. — Source
