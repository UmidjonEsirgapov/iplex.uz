Who will pay the rent? Also to those who are studying privately?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
State higher education students, regardless of whether they study on the basis of a state grant or a fee-contract, students studying in the form of full-time education can use this privilege.
Students of private higher education institutions or those studying in foreign cooperation programs cannot use this opportunity.

Will rent be reimbursed to private students?

Unfortunately, no. According to the current regulations, students studying in private higher education institutions cannot use the rent reimbursement privilege. This privilege is intended for only public higher education institutions students.

Which students are reimbursed for rent?

State higher education institutions students

Students studying full-time, regardless of whether they study on the basis of a state grant or fee-contract, use this privilege

Programs with foreign cooperation are an exception

Students studying in joint educational programs and faculties of foreign partner HEIs are not included in this list.

Amount and source of reimbursement

60 percent of the number of students living on the basis of the right to rent is reimbursed from the state budget.

Is it also given to those who study on a grant?

Yes, students who study on the basis of a grant are also reimbursed for rent.

How are students selected?

Students to be reimbursed for rent are selected by special commissions under the Higher Education Institution.

Conclusion

Reimbursement of rent valid only for students studying in full-time education at state HEIs. Students of private HEIs or those studying in foreign cooperation programs cannot use this opportunity.
