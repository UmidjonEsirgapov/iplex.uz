The Latvian government offers scholarships for citizens of Uzbekistan

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The Embassy of the Republic of Uzbekistan in Latvia established cooperation relations with the Ministry of Science and Education of this country, and it was reported that active work is currently being carried out to attract grant funds to the fields of education and research.
The Latvian Government's Education The State Development Agency plans to allocate scholarships and grants for academic staff, teachers and students of Uzbekistan's higher education institutions for the 2021/2022 academic year.
Term: April 1, 2021.

Cooperation between the Embassy of the Republic of Uzbekistan in Latvia and the Ministry of Science and Education of this country It was announced that active efforts are being made to attract grants to education and research.

The State Agency for the Development of Education of the Government of Latvia plans to allocate scholarships and grants for academic staff, teachers and students of Uzbekistan's higher educational institutions for the 2021/2022 school year.

Date: April 1, 2021.

Country: Latvia.

These scholarships and grants are provided under 3 programs:

  • Scholarships for Studies
  • Latvian fellowships for Research
  • Latvian Scholarships for Summer Schools
