"Labor Law Expert" competition is being held among school, college, and lyceum students.

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
General Information: The Federation of Trade Unions of Uzbekistan is holding the contest "Knowledge of Labor Law" among all vocational colleges, academic lyceums and 10th-11th grade students of general secondary education.
Participation in the contest Young people who express their desire must answer the test questions related to labor legislation through the site http://tanlov.kasaba.uz by October 16.
20 participants who successfully passed the selection stage will participate in the Republican stage, which will be held in the form of oral questions and answers.
