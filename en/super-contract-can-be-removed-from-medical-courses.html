"Super-contract" can be removed from medical courses

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Minister of Health Amrillo Inoyatov announced that a proposal on canceling education with a "super-contract" in medical fields has been developed and submitted to the government.
According to the minister, medical education is a complete transformation.
A student who has passed the examination of fundamental sciences will be trained in clinical sciences in 4-5-6 courses and then take the exam a second time.

The Minister of Health Amrillo Inoyatov announced that a proposal on canceling education with a "super-contract" in medical fields has been developed and submitted to the government.

According to the minister, medical education will be a complete transformation. Here:

"The student now passes the exam in two stages. After the end of the third year, he passes the first exam in fundamental sciences. This exam is not taken by the teachers of the same higher education institution, but by [representatives] of a completely different organization. A student who passed the exam in fundamental sciences is trained in clinical sciences in 4-5-6 years and then takes the exam a second time. it will be allowed to work after obtaining a full international level". — Read on infoedu.uz
