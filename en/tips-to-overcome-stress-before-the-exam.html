Tips to overcome stress before the exam!

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Everyone reacts to the exam differently.
Visualize the exam process with the help of psychological techniques: You entered the auditorium where the exam is being held, selected a ticket, sat down and calmly completed the tasks without any difficulties; keep in mind: they are showing that they are satisfied with your answer, and at the end of the exam, they gave you the grade you want. ber" should work. Everyone reacts to the exam in a different way.

Therefore, he will think of ways to fight against the stress that occurs at this time. What we want to present to you below are practical and reliable methods.

  • It is recommended to complete the exam preparation 17-18 hours before . The productivity of reading a book with great pressure in the last hours is much lower. Experience shows that the more you read when you are very close to the test of knowledge, the less you will remember.
  • It is not recommended to look at the study materials indiscriminately when life or death is about to be decided. As a result, you have to rely only on luck during the exam.
  • It is more useful to devote the rest of the time to preparing the body, which is tired of mental work, for the upcoming test.
  • Before the exams, it is advisable to engage in any distracting and relaxing activities, such as going for an evening walk, taking a shower. It is very useful to watch a comedy or a humorous show to "relieve the burden of the brain".
  • In addition to helping to overcome stress, laughter stimulates the appetite, increases work capacity, normalizes sleep and improves the ability to think and imagine in an unnatural way. This has been scientifically proven.
  • Before the exam, you must sleep enough to satisfy the needs of the body!
 To sleep without difficulty, it is enough to exclude products such as coffee, chocolate, cola, tea from the diet in the afternoon.

Use your imagination. Visualize the exam process with the help of psychological techniques:

You entered the auditorium where the exam is being held, chose a ticket, sat down and calmly began to complete the tasks without any difficulties.

Try to recreate these scenes in your memory many times and believe that it will happen in real life.

It is also important to have a good dinner before the exam in order to sleep well. At this time, it is necessary to eat carbohydrate foods as much as possible, and it is better to refrain from products containing a large amount of protein. If you drink a teaspoon of honey in a glass of hot water 30-40 minutes before going to sleep, the nervous system will be strengthened. — More information
