Educational credit was canceled for additional admission to the master's degree

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Starting from 2025, educational loans will not be allocated for students enrolled in the master's level of state higher education institutions according to additional admission parameters.
This change is in the President's decision on the state budget for 2025
The main changes Students who entered the master's degree on the basis of additional admission will pay for their education entirely from their own funds.

Starting from 2025, students who entered the master's degree stage of state higher education institutions according to the parameters of additional admission will not be allocated an educational loan. This change is defined in the President's decision on the state budget for 2025. Main changes Students who enter the master's program based on additional admission will pay for their education entirely from their own funds.

  • This change applies only to students in additional enrollment, the education credit will be preserved for students enrolled in the main enrollment parameters.

    • Impact on students

    This decision will increase the financial burden for students enrolled in additional enrollment. Students should take this change into account when planning educational expenses.

    Conclusion

    This decision was adopted within the framework of the 2025 state budget and is aimed at revising the procedure for issuing educational loans. Applicants who are planning to enter the master's degree on the basis of additional admission should evaluate their financial capabilities in advance. — Read on infoedu.uz
