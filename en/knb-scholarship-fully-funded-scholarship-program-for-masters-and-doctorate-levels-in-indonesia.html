About the scholarship and the university in brief:

Beasiswa Kemitraan Negara Berkembang or popularly known as the KNB grant program, this program is a prestigious grant program offered by the government of the Republic of Indonesia to talented international students from developing countries. Administered by the Directorate General of Higher Education of the Ministry of Education and Culture, this scholarship offers potential students the opportunity to study the life of the world's most culturally diverse nation and obtain bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees at one of 23 renowned universities in Indonesia. It ranks #301-350 among Asian universities. If you have a desire to study at such a prestigious university for free, then hurry up!

Duration of the grant:

The duration of the grant includes the selected study program and a period of 1 year (2 semesters) of Bahasa Indonesia language study. It is offered for a 3-year duration (2 semesters of Bahasa Indonesia language course + 4 semesters of Master's degree) for Master's programs and 4 years of duration (2 semesters of Bahasa Indonesia language course + 6 semesters of Doctoral level).

Note: All academic programs (lectures and theses/dissertations) are conducted in Indonesian and therefore students complete the language course in the first year.

General details of the grant:

  • Country: Indonesia;
  • University: Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology;
  • Grant Type: International Full Grant;
  • Benefits:
    • Full Tuition Reimbursement (paid directly to the University)
    • Living Expense Stipend
    • Research and Book Fees (selected major course
    • Medical insurance
    • International flight ticket fee for departure and arrival
    • Immigration fee
  • Duration: Selected study program + 2-semester language course;
  • For whom: In one of the graduate and doctoral stages of Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology For international students from developing countries who want to study.

General requirements for candidates:

  • The maximum age for applying for a grant is 35 years old;
  • The candidate must have a bachelor’s or master’s degree approved by the relevant Ministry of Education and Culture;
  • The candidate must have a minimum TOEFL iBT score of 80 or must provide IELTS 6.0 score certificate (or other equivalent English language test certificates);
  • Candidate must have GPA of 3.25;
  • Candidate must complete online application form;
  • Before departure to Indonesia, winning candidates must sign the declaration letter and scholarship agreement prepared by KNB scholarship management

Note: In the process of applying for the scholarship program, the candidate is required to receive a letter from the Indonesian Embassy confirming his/her official recommendation for candidacy.
