Science Po University in Paris offers the Emile Boutmy Scholarship for undergraduate and graduate studies. Educated students who want to study in France and who meet 100% of the university requirements can participate in the program.

Benefits:

  • The amount of the scholarship for a bachelor's degree is from 3,600 euros to 13,000 euros for a 3-year study;
  • For a master's degree - for a 2-year study. 12,200 euros. The amount of the scholarship depends on the achievements and income of the family.

Application deadline:

Bachelor's degree - mid-February 2022

Master's degree - November 30, 2021

Requirements:

  • Candidates must not be EU citizens;
  • Be accepted to Science Po University and be applying for the program for the first time;
  • Have completed school or college/high school for Bachelors;
  • Have completed Bachelors for Masters;
  • Have good grades;
  • Must be fluent in English.
