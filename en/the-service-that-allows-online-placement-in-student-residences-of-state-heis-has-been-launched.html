The service that allows online placement in student residences of state HEIs has been launched

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
All applicants recommended for admission in the 2023/2024 academic year can submit an electronic application for placement in the student accommodation of state HEIs through the State Services Center or the Unified Interactive State Services Portal - my.gov.uz.
We remind you that this service It will be available after the announcement of the final results (mandate) of the entrance tests to the state higher education institutions for the 2023/2024 academic year.
Therefore, the information about students of the 1st stage on the platform will be updated based on the results of the 2023 mandate.
