The state order will now be formed through a special platform

With Decision No. 49 of January 29, 2025 of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, "Regulation on the procedure for forming a state order for highly educated personnel" was approved. According to the new regulation, from 2025/2026 academic year in higher education institutions the state order will be formed through a special electronic platform.

How will the state order be determined?

On the basis of the new system labor market demand will be analyzed, and the need for personnel training will be updated every year. The state order is formulated on the basis of a 4-year forecast, but changes in the labor market are taken into account, and corrections are made every year.
