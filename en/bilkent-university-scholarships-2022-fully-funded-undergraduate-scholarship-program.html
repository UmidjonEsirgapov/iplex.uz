Bilkent University Scholarships 2022: Fully funded undergraduate scholarship program

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Brief about the university and grant: International students from all countries can apply for the Bilkent University Scholarships 2022 grant.
Requirements for candidates: Citizens of all countries, including citizens of Turkey, can apply for a grant; Undergraduate students are required to have a minimum GPA of 2.80 / 4.00 when applying.

About the University and Scholarship:

International students from all countries are eligible to apply for Bilkent University Scholarships 2022. Bilkent University offers many academic programs and courses to study. The university provides education opportunities for approximately 12,500 students at undergraduate, graduate and higher level courses. The Bilkent University Scholarships 2021 grant program is the second most valuable grant for students of all levels. Grants in the amount of 20%, 40, 60, 80%, 100% are provided for the tuition fee of the students who won this grant. For students who win 100% grant, a grant for dormitory service may also be allocated.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Citizens of all countries including citizens of Turkey can apply for the grant;
  • Bachelor graduates must have at least 2.80 / 4.00 GPA when applying.
  • If you are a citizen of Turkey: ALES score required, <55 (quantitative)
  • TOEFL IBT (minimum 87), IELTS (average of at least 6.5 and no less than 5.5 in each section) or Bilkent COPE (minimum C, it will be possible to apply at the university itself).
  • SAT requirement: Reasoning Test-"Math" and "Critical Reading" at least 900/1600; Subject Test_must get at least 500 out of 800 points in at least 2 sections;

Required documents:

  • Scientific degree (if received);
  • Diploma or certificate and transcript of grades;
  • Motivation letter;
  • Two letter of recommendation;
  • CV and other additional documents may be required depending on your chosen field.

Application process:

To apply for the grant program, you must first be admitted to one of the university courses. All course applications will be considered for this grant program. The grant will be provided if the candidates fulfill the requirements for the grant program. You can apply for Bilkent University courses online using the following links. There is also an application fee of $30.

  • Bachelor's courses
  • Master's and doctoral courses

Expiration date: July 4, 2022
