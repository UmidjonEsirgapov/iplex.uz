What course students receive the Presidential State Scholarship? Procedure for receiving the Presidential Scholarship

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
State scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan are awarded to students of state higher education institutions (undergraduate and graduate) who are citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan according to the results of the competition held by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
How to submit documents for the Presidential State Scholarship Is it necessary?
The appointment of state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan to students of state higher education institutions is carried out by the decision of the board of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan. special attention is paid to encouraging young people, involving them in active creative activities, supporting talented pupils, students and graduate students, as well as giving appropriate awards. and state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan are being appointed for postgraduate students. In which areas are scholarships awarded?

State scholarship quota of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan (eleven) in each of the following educational areas one scholarship will be allocated separately for undergraduate and graduate studies:
  • agriculture and water management;
  • technology and informatics;
  • health and social welfare;
  • social-humanities and social sciences;
  • natural sciences;
  • business and management;
  • pedagogy;
  • culture, art and sports;
  • foreign languages;
  • law and international relations;
  • journalism.
This list may be amended and supplemented by the Cabinet of Ministers at the beginning of each academic year, based on the expediency of changing the priorities in educational areas, based on the recommendation of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

What criteria are the scholarships awarded to? The following criteria must be met for the appointment of state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan:

  • combination of general education training with research (creative works);
  • participation in conferences (competitions) in the fields;
  • the presence of at least two published works (works);
  • Knowledge of languages - the state language and one foreign language (except for the state languages of the Commonwealth of Independent States), and in the case of a bachelor's course of study or a master's degree in a foreign language, knowledge of one more foreign language is required;
  • Knowledge of the history of Uzbekistan.

How are knowledge of these criteria determined?

  • Requirement levels for knowledge of languages, information technologies, and the history of Uzbekistan are determined by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
  • Applicants for the state scholarship of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan take special tests at the State Test Center (by region) under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan to determine their level of knowledge in these subjects.
  • The center submits the final reports together with the test results to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

    How is the sequence of document review carried out?

    • Nomination of candidates for the state scholarship of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, after public discussion of the candidates' success in studies and scientific (creative) activities, the state university scientific councils of educational institutions.
    • Scientific councils through secret voting, with a simple majority vote, make decisions to nominate candidates for state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
    • Scientific councils decisions It is the basis for ministries and institutions to send a recommendation to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan on the appointment of state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
    • Appointment of state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan to students of state higher education institutions is carried out by the decision of the board of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
    • The level of knowledge and scientific (creative) preparation of applicants according to the established criteria is thoroughly examined before the appointment of scholarships.
    • For this purpose, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan:
      - special competition commission creates;
      - creates groups of experts in order to assess the level of educational and scientific (creative) preparation of applicants;
      - organizes examination commissions in information technologies, history of Uzbekistan, foreign languages together with the state test center, develops test assignments;
      - defines the work order of the selection commission and expert groups;
      - defines the terms of the selection commission meetings.
    • The selection commission conducts the final selection of applicants by secret voting and at least two-thirds of the commission members participating in voting for the applicant and his job When voting, the student candidate is recommended for the appointment of state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan. It will come into force after approval by the board of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic.
    • The selection commission has the right to recommend the candidates who did not receive the state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, but have the best performance for other state scholarships.
    • The decision of the board of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan is the basis for the preparation of orders by state higher education institutions on the appointment and payment of state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan to students.
    • State scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan are awarded to students every year, for one academic year, starting from September 1, with strict observance of the established amounts.

    When will the Ministry review?

    The Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan issues recommendations for the appointment of scholarships once a year The period of receiving documents is August 1-15, and the review period is September 10-25. Students who have received state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan will receive a certificate of "Holder of the President's Scholarship" at a meeting of the board of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

    What are the benefits for scholarship students?

    • Bachelor's degree students who are holders of state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, after graduating from a higher education institution, go to a master's degree for two years on the basis of a state grant. It is accepted based on the admission quota for education at the expense of the State budget of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
    • Students who have state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan retain the right to be sent abroad to continue their studies at a master's degree (for bachelor-scholarships) within two years after graduation.
