The procedure for admitting students to "Courage schools" has been determined

2025 Starting from July 1, Regulation on the procedure for admitting students to "Courage Schools" and organizing the educational process has been registered with the Ministry of Justice (list number: No. 3636).

Get acquainted with the main procedures and conditions established below based on this regulation.

Procedure for admission to classes of Courage

  • Students are admitted to Courage classes starting from 5th grade.

  • Each class consists of 26 students.

  • Admission is based on the annual quota.

Study period and level of education

  • The duration of study in courage classes is 7 years.

  • Certificate of basic secondary education for those who completed the 9th grade, and general secondary education certificate for those who completed the 11th grade.

How will the entrance exams be conducted?

  • Applicants will take part in tests in July.

  • Tests will be held in the following areas:

    • Physical training

    • Sciences. tests in the field (based on specified subjects)

Conclusion

"Schools of Courage" are aimed at selecting talented, physically and mentally prepared students and educating them as disciplined, patriotic and mature specialists for 7 years. The new regulation has clearly defined the process and created a clear and understandable direction for parents and candidates. — Official Data
