Monday will be a day off

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
For the first time, the day of rest will be moved to Monday as the holiday falls on Sunday. In accordance with Article 208 of the Labor Code of the new version: "October 1 - Day of Teachers and Coaches", which is considered a non-working holiday, will be Sunday - a day off in 2023 due to its validity, this day off will be moved to Monday, October 2, 2023.
In this case, the days off will not be moved when performing work that cannot be stopped on holidays that cannot be stopped due to production-technical and organizational conditions (existing uninterrupted production, daily service to the population, shift work, etc.).
