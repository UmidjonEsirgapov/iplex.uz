More than 193,000 applicants were recommended for the 2023-2024 academic year.

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the results of the entrance tests for the undergraduate education courses of higher education institutions for the 2023-2024 academic year, 193,421 applicants were recommended to students.
Of these: 42,655 — state grant; 150,766 people were recommended for studentship on the basis of a fee-contract.
Distribution of applicants recommended for studentship by forms of education: Full-time form of education - 129,927 people; Extramural education — 49,852 people; Evening education — 10,752 people; Form of distance education - 2,890 people.

According to the results of the entrance tests for the bachelor education courses of higher education institutions for the 2023-2024 academic year, 193,421 applicants were recommended to students. Of these:

  • 42,655 are state grants;
  • 150,766 are recommended for studentship on the basis of a fee-contract.

Distribution of applicants recommended for studentship according to forms of education:

  • Form of full-time education - 129,927 persons;
  • Form of external education - 49,852 people;
  • Form of evening education - 10,752 people;
  • Form of distance education - 2,890 people.

According to the results of the competition: — Official Data

  • based on the state grant, the average score is 119.3 points;
  • on the basis of the payment contract, the average score was 93.3 points.
