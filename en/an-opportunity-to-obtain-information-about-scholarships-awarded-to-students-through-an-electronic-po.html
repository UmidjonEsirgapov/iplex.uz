An opportunity to obtain information about scholarships awarded to students through an electronic portal has been created

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
This service is intended only for students who are currently studying.
The information service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation informs that by entering the my.gov.uz portal, selecting the "Stipend information" service and the desired period from the "Education" section. it is possible to get information about scholarships calculated for this period.
In addition, students can use this service through the HEMIS information system.

This service is intended only for students who are currently studying.

According to the information service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, enter the my.gov.uz portal and select the "Education" section By selecting the "Scholarship information" service and the desired period, you can get information about the scholarships calculated for this period.

Students can also use this service through the HEMIS information system.

This service is intended only for students who are currently studying. Scholarship information is not available for those who have graduated or are not students.
