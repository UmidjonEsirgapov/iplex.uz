Officially: The base contract amount has been increased

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The amount of the basic contract has been increased. The fee contract for 2023 students has not been increased, but the contract prices for the 2024/2025 academic year have been approved and the prices have clearly increased.
For comparison, last year if the contract for the economics field was 9.1 million soums, this year it is 10.5 million soums.
By the way, these approved prices do not have much effect on higher education institutions given financial independence.
