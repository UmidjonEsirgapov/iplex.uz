"Director's Scholarship" was introduced in the school in Tashkent

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In order to support educated and talented students, "Director's scholarship" was introduced in general education school No. 100 located in the Yakkasaray district of Tashkent.
This scholarship is valid for 1st-11th grade students studying in the 2024-2025 school year.

In order to support educated and talented students in school #100 located in Yakkasaray district of Tashkent city, "Director scholarship" was introduced. This was reported by the Information Service of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education of Tashkent city. This scholarship is valid for students of grades 1-11 studying in the 2024-2025 school year. Azizakhan Tojibayeva, a student of the 2nd "A" class, and Asaliya Ergasheva, a student of the 9th "B" class, were chosen as the first scholarship holders. — Details here
