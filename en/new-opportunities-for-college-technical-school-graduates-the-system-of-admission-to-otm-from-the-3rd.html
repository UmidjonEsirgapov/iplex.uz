New opportunities for college-technical school graduates: the system of admission to OTM from the 3rd year will be introduced

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Important changes are expected in the education system of Uzbekistan.
This will create new opportunities for professional growth by combining work experience and higher education.
This innovation will open the door to great opportunities for technical and college students and make the education system more effective.

Important changes are expected in the education system of Uzbekistan. Now graduates of colleges and technical schools will be able to continue their studies directly in higher education institutions, even from the 3rd year.

A new system of transition from college and technical school to university

Based on this system, programs of colleges and technical schools will be combined with universities:

  • 1-2 year educational programs, textbooks and knowledge assessment criteria are the same as in universities.
  • A credit-module system is introduced in technical schools as well.
  • The credits earned by students are recognized by universities.

This gives students Through the 2+2 or 2+3 system, it creates the opportunity to continue studying from the 2nd or 3rd year of the universities.

The path to higher education will also be opened for working professionals

A new system will also be established for those who want to continue their career in production after graduating from a technical college:

  • Students who studied in the field of working profession can take an exam after two or three years of work and gain experience and get a qualification equivalent to "bachelor".

This combines work experience and higher education, creating new opportunities for professional growth.

Advantages of a new direction in education

  • The system of transfer to OTM will be simplified - college and technical school graduates will save time to transfer to the university.
  • Knowledge will be recognized at the expense of the credit system - students will not need to re-learn what they have previously acquired at the university.
  • Opportunity to obtain a bachelor's degree with work experience - it will be easier for experienced professionals to obtain higher education.

This news opens the door of great opportunities for technical school and college students and serves to make the education system more effective. — Official Data
