Contest: A story contest with a total prize pool of 15 million soums has been announced

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief contents:

Stories submitted for participation in the contest are selected by a panel of famous writers, poets and literary scholars.
"Audience RECOGNITION" NOMINATION In addition to the above three places, the author of the story whose story is the basis for the most watched video product during a month, based on the period during which the story was read on air, will be recognized as the winner in the "Audience Recognition" nomination and will be awarded with a cash prize of 5 million soums.

CONTEST DETAILS:

  • Name of the contest: "Stories we don't know"
  • Country: Uzbekistan
  • For whom: For candidates of all ages

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE IN THE CONTEST?

This contest depends on age and gender regardless, all professional and amateur creators can participate.

CONSIDERATIONS FOR THE CONTEST:

  • the story submitted for the competition must be based on real life events and characters (it should not be entirely a product of fiction);
  • it should not have been published in any mass media before;
  • "It's hard to believe this story... But it's true!" reflect the opinion;
  • the story should be written on A4 format paper, with 1.5 line spacing, Times New Roman 12 font size and should not exceed 5 pages;
  • the text of the story should not be edited, free of spelling and stylistic errors;
  • the name of the Word file containing the story should be the author's name and surname
  • the last page of the story should contain the author's name, address, phone number and brief information about himself.

SENDING ADDRESS:

  • Stories until June 30, 2021 at 12:00 p.m. should be sent to the e-mail address vozlimaqolalar@gmail.com.

Note: Stories that do not meet the above conditions will not be considered by the selection committee. One author can participate with more than one story.

HOW WILL THE WINNERS OF THE CONTEST BE DETERMINED?

The stories submitted for participation in the contest will be determined by a panel of well-known writers, poets and literary scholars.

WHERE WILL THE COMPETITION RESULTS BE ANNOUNCED?

Competition results will be announced on Abdukarim Mirzayev's official telegram page (http://t.me/Abdukarim_mirzayev_tv) and YouTube social pages (https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCQJ6VyqVtkRMjgMK3WmNZNw?sub_confirmation=1).

HOW WILL THE WINNERS OF THE CONTEST BE DETERMINED:

1st place - 5 million soums (1 winner)
2nd place - 3 million soums (1 winner)
3rd place - 2 million soums (1 winner)

Also, for each story deemed worthy of broadcast A fee of 300,000 soums will be paid.

" AUDIENCE RECOGNITION" NOMINATION

In addition to the above three places, the author of the story whose story is the basis for the most watched video production during a month, based on the period of time the story was read on air, will be recognized as the winner in the "Audience Recognition" category and will be awarded with a cash prize of 5 million soums.
