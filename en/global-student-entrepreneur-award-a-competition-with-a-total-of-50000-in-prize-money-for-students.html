Global Student Entrepreneur Award: a competition with a total of $50,000 in prize money for students

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Applications are being accepted for participation in The 2021-2022 GSEA competition for student entrepreneurship organized by the Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO).
This competition is intended for students who are active in business.
The competition is an exclusive opportunity for student-entrepreneurs to make the "right" connections, find missing resources and expand their business.

Applications are being accepted for participation in The 2021-2022 GSEA Competition, an international student entrepreneurship competition organized by the Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO). This competition is intended for students who are active in business.

The competition is an exclusive opportunity for student-entrepreneurs to make the "right" contacts, find missing resources and expand their business.

Privileges:

  • 1st place - $25,000 and all travel expenses will be covered;
  • 2nd place - $10,000
  • 3rd place - $5,000 and other benefits. Read more here.

Who can participate:

  • You must be enrolled as an undergraduate or graduate student at a university/college for the current academic year at the time of application;
  • You must be the owner, founder or controlling shareholder of your student business;
  • Your student business has operated for at least six consecutive years prior to application. must have been in business for a month;
  • Your business must have generated $500 in revenue or invested $1,000 at the time of application;
  • You must not have been a finalist in last year's GSEA Global Finals;
  • Eligibility limit is 30 years old
