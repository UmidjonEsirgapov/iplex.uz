President's grant: Opportunity for the 200 students with the highest scores

The 200 students who scored the highest scores in the full-time entrance exams will be awarded with the President's grant. This grant fully finances the student's studies, despite the fact that he was accepted on the basis of a contract.

Examination scores and grant conditions

  • In entrance exams, maximum score - 189 points can be obtained.

  • 200 applicants with the highest scores receive a Presidential grant

  • A student admitted to the contract, if he is higher in the ranking, will study on the basis of the President's grant.
