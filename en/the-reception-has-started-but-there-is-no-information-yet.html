Admission has started, but there is no information yet: Applicants remain unanswered

It has been 17 days since admission processes for higher education institutions for the 2025/2026 academic year have started. However, the information and explanations most needed by the applicants have not yet been delivered to the public.

Slow activity of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation

Usually every year from the beginning of the admission process:

  • what documents are needed;

    • answers to the most frequently asked questions by applicants;

    Important information such as

  • notes about the test and the selection procedure;
