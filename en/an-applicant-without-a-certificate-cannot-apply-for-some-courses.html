An applicant without a certificate cannot apply for some courses

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
From 2025/2026, it is not possible to submit a document without a certificate to some fields. According to the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan, starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, a certificate confirming the level of knowledge of a foreign language is mandatory for submitting documents to some educational fields.
This change was introduced on the basis of Presidential Decree PF-81 dated 24.05.2024.
The following certificates meet the requirements: IELTS / TOEFL (English) TestDaF / DSD / Goethe (German) DELF / DALF (French) Certificates issued by the National Testing Center Summary from the 2025/2026 academic year starting from 2025/2026, applicants who want to apply for educational courses in which a foreign language is designated as the 1st subject in the subject complex must present a certificate approved in a mandatory manner. certificate confirming the level of knowledge of a foreign language is mandatory to apply for the courses. This change was introduced on the basis of the Presidential Decree PF-81, 05/24/2024.

New requirements for fields where foreign language is designated as the first subject

If foreign language is designated as the first subject in your chosen field of study, the test will not be conducted for this subject. Instead, the applicant must provide a national or international certificate.

This requirement applies to the following languages:

  • English

  • German

  • French

  • And other recognized foreign languages languages

If "English language" is clearly indicated, a certificate of this language is required

If "English language" is clearly indicated as the 1st subject in the set of subjects, the applicant must provide a certificate of English language. A certificate in German or another language is not valid in this case.

The language of the certificate is not important for courses where the 1st subject is "Foreign language".

If the 1st subject in your chosen field of study is generally indicated as "foreign language", then you can submit a certified national or international certificate in any foreign language.

National and What are the international certificates?

The following certificates are eligible:

  • IELTS / TOEFL (English)

  • TestDaF / DSD / Goethe (German)

  • DELF / DALF (French)

  • Certificates issued by the National Testing Center

Conclusion

Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, applicants who want to apply for educational courses in which a foreign language is designated as the 1st subject in the subject complex must submit a compulsorily approved certificate.
