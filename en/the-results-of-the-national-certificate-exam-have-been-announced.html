Evaluation criteria

Test results according to the international methodology - Calculated on the basis of Rush model, the following evaluation system was introduced:

  • 70+ points – A+ grade
  • 65 – 69.9 points – A grade
  • 60 – 64.9 points – B+ grade
  • 55 – 59.9 points – B level
  • 50 – 54.9 points – C+ level
  • 46 – 49.9 points – C level

Certificate benefits

Certificate holders in OTM bachelor entrance tests obtains the following privileges:

  • Certificate of A+ and A level - maximum score
