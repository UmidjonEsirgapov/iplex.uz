The procedure for paying 50% of the rent for students living in a rented accommodation

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The President signed the decree "On additional measures to support the entrepreneurial activity and employment of young people, their social protection and meaningful organization of free time". including 60% of the students of state higher educational institutions who live on the basis of the right to rent in a house not owned by their parents (legal representatives), 50% of the monthly rent paid by them is reimbursed from the State budget. the reimbursable part of the rent payment is reimbursable primarily for children of needy families, exemplary and active students; Other students who live on the basis of the right to rent can be reimbursed by higher education institutions from their extra-budgetary funds.

The President signed the decree "On additional measures to support the entrepreneurial activity and employment of young people, their social protection and meaningful organization of free time".

From May 1, 2021, 60% of the students of state higher education institutions who live on the basis of the right to rent in a house that is not covered by a dormitory and does not belong to them, including their parents (legal representatives), will be reimbursed 50% of the monthly rent paid by them from the State budget. In this case: — Source

  • the reimbursed part of the rent should not exceed 1 times the base calculation amount in Tashkent city, and 0.5 times the base calculation amount in the rest of the regions;
  • the reimbursed part of the rent payment is primarily reimbursed for children of needy families, exemplary and active students;
  • part of the monthly rent may be reimbursed by higher education institutions from their extra-budgetary funds to other students who live on the basis of the right to rent.
