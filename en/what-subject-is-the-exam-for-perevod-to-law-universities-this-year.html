What subject is the exam for "perevod" to law universities this year?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In the study transfer exam, the same subjects will be tested in the subjects that were taken in the previous year's admission.
For example, in the 2022/2023 admission to legal departments, the history and English language exam will be taken.
Not from the subject of jurisprudence.
