83 applicants were excluded from the transfer tests - the agency

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Tests were held on August 19-20, 2024 in the Republic of Karakalpakstan, regions and Tashkent city to transfer student studies from foreign and non-state higher education institutions to state HEIs.
Education Evaluation Agency reported that 40,897 applicants took part in the tests. 2,902 of them did not take part in the tests, and 83 were excluded from the tests due to violation of the rules. On August 19-20, 2024, tests were held in the Republic of Karakalpakstan, regions and Tashkent city. According to the Education Evaluation Agency, 40,897 applicants took part in the tests. Of these, 2,902 applicants did not take part in the tests, and 83 applicants were excluded from the tests due to violating the rules.

Also, it was reported that the results of the test conducted on August 20 were announced. Applicants can find out their results in the "Study transfer (foreign and non-state) Result" section on the my.uzbmb.uz site by entering their passport series and number and personal identification number.
