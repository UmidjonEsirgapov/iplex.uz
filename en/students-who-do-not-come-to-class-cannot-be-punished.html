It is not allowed to punish students who do not come to class

It is prohibited by law to punish students who do not come to class and violate the internal procedures of the school. This was reported by the head of the Ministry of Public Education.

Teachers cannot punish students who have not prepared lessons or who violate the internal rules of the school. However, the student's behavior can be discussed in the presence of his parents. If the student continues his behavior, he will be included in the internal list of the school. It is legally prohibited to apply direct punishment to students.

The parents of a student who is not prepared for class should be notified through the head of the class. Parents can also be informed about the student's unpreparedness through the Kundalik.com platform. — Source
