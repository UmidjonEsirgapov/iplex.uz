Who can teach Natural Science, Informatics and Education in primary grades?

According to the order of the Minister of Preschool and School Education, "Primary Education" teachers in primary grades "Natural Science" (Science)", "Education" and "Informatics and Information Technologies" subjects.

Also, Teachers of "Geography", "Biology" and "Physics" in 5th-6th grades have "Science" subject, "History", "Idea of National Independence and Law" teachers are allowed to teach the subject of "Education" in grades 5-11.
