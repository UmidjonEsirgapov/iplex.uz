The targeted admission procedure has changed: priority is given to education and medical fields

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Territorial needs are the main criterion for targeted admission. According to the updated regulation, in the process of increasing the parameters of targeted admission, the real needs for highly educated personnel in the fields of education and healthcare in remote and remote areas are taken into account. it is planned to increase the level of providing the necessary specialists for the organizations.
The content of the quadrilateral contracts has been clarified. The conditions of mandatory production are clearly defined. significant changes were made to the regulations defining the procedure for admission to bachelor's degrees of higher education institutions. These innovations are aimed at taking into account the real needs of the regions when increasing the targeted admission parameters.

Regional needs are the main criteria for targeted admission

According to the updated regulation, in the process of increasing the targeted admission parameters, first of all, the real needs for highly educated personnel in the fields of education and healthcare in remote and remote areas are taken into account.

  • school education institutions,

  • it is planned to increase the level of providing necessary specialists for medical organizations.

Special attention to the education and health sectors

The problem of shortage of personnel for school education

The need for qualified teaching staff is high in remote areas. and the new procedure will gradually eliminate this problem.

Lack of specialists in the healthcare system

There is a shortage of doctors and medical specialists in regional hospitals and medical institutions. It is planned to strengthen this system with the necessary personnel through targeted admission.

The content of the four-way contracts has been clarified

The conditions of mandatory work are clearly defined

The content of the four-way contracts concluded with the applicants recommended for the studentship within the framework of the targeted admission has been further improved.

Now in the contracts:

  • in which region or organization the graduate will work,

  • mandatory period of work,

  • the rights and obligations of the parties are clearly and clearly indicated.

What will be the selection procedure for applicants?

Targeted and general admission stages

Targeted admission parameters are separated applicants who have chosen the fields of study:

  1. Initially — within the framework of targeted admission,

  2. Then — participate in the selection according to the parameters of general admission.

If the applicant is recommended for studentship within the scope of targeted admission, he will not participate in the general admission competition.

Allocated quotas for the 2025/2026 academic year

For information, 626 targeted quotas for the school education sector have been allocated for the 2025/2026 academic year across the republic.

Conclusion

The introduced changes serve to make the targeted admission system more effective.
