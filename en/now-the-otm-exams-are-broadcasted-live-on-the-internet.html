Now the OTM exams are broadcasted live on the Internet

Brief content:
Bachelor and master exams of higher education institutions will now be broadcast live on the Internet. the list of socially important information (33 items) was approved.

The decree approved the list of socially significant information (33 items) that should be posted as open information by all state bodies and organizations.

According to the decree, state bodies and organizations must submit a report on their activities every year:

➖ Post it on their websites by March 1 and discuss it with the public until March 10

➖ will hold press conferences to discuss the report with the participation of the general public by April 1.

✅ From July 1, 2021, information on the following will be posted on the Open Information Portal (Portal) and on the websites of state bodies and organizations and will be updated quarterly:

● implemented state purchases;
● official expenses of individuals on business trips and welcoming guests from abroad;
● tax and customs benefits and preferences with the documents of the President, the Cabinet of Ministers;
● their annual expenditure estimate and its implementation.

According to the decree, the procedure for live broadcasting of the following exams will be introduced on the Internet:

● Bachelor and Master exams of Higher Education Institutions;
● exams related to the transfer of studies;
● nostrification tests.

Until the 2021/2022 academic year, the web platform "imtihan.uz" will be launched, which will provide live streaming of exams on the Internet and is open to all users.
