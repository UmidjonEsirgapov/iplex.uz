About the university and the scholarship program:

Satakunta University of Applied Sciences is an industrial higher education institution for the study of applied sciences. and takes the 29th place in the whole country. It is a university that aims to produce skills tailored to meet the modern and multifaceted needs of working life in the entire Satakunta region of Finland and the surrounding areas.

Grant provided by the University program provides financial support to international students.

Who is the program for?

  • The program is for international students from all countries outside the European Union.

For which fields of study:

The grant program is valid for all undergraduate programs offered by the university.

Application process:

Students must first apply and be admitted to one of the university's undergraduate programs HERE. It is not necessary to submit a separate application for the scholarship, the candidacy of admitted students will be considered automatically.

Required documents:

Candidates must submit the following documents to the university:

  • Photo (passport size);
  • Motivation letter;
  • School, college/lyceum diploma and applications;
  • Passport copy.

Language requirement:

Students must be fluent in Finnish. They must know basic Finnish language skills (level A2).

End of admission:

  • November 15, 2021

Benefits of the program:

The winning students will receive 100% of their tuition fees annually by the university.
