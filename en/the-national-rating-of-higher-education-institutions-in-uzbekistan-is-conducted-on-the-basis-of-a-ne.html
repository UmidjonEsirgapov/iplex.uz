The new procedure for conducting the national rating of higher education organizations is under discussion

The draft regulation "On approval of the regulation on the procedure for conducting the national rating of higher education organizations" was posted on the discussion portal. This document is aimed at establishing a single mechanism for evaluating and comparing the quality of higher education in the country.

Which higher education organizations participate in the national rating?

Requirements for rating participants

According to the draft regulation, regardless of the organizational and legal form:

  • at least 3 years after its establishment. full;

  • higher education organizations with graduates can participate in the national rating.

Organizations excluded from the rating

The following institutions do not participate in the national rating:

  • higher military and militarized;

  • specialized higher education institutions;

  • scientific research organizations.

A separate national rating may be conducted based on international ratings

Evaluation based on QS and THE ratings

Globally recognized:

  • Quacquarelli Symonds (QS),

  • The Times Higher Education (THE) national rating may be conducted separately for the higher education institutions that occupy the highest places in Uzbekistan.

How are the places determined? place is determined based on sequence.

What sources are the national ranking data formed on?

Main information sources

National ranking data is formed through a special platform from the following sources:

  • information systems of relevant ministries and agencies;

  • higher official data of educational organizations;

  • international databases.

Requirements for data verification

On a special platform:

  • data source;

  • the methods of their verification should be clearly indicated. condition.

Obligation of higher education organizations to provide information

Important deadlines

  • Information must be entered into a special platform every year until February 1.

  • Information not entered within the specified period will not be taken into account in the rating.

Who is responsible?

The head of the higher education organization is personally responsible for the:

  • reasonableness,

  • reliability,

  • timely submission of the entered information.

How is the nationwide survey conducted?

Survey participants

The nationwide survey is conducted annually until June 1 and includes:

  • undergraduate and graduate students;

  • professors;

  • Employers who hire graduates will participate.

Requirement for the level of participation

Results of the survey:

  • if at least 20% of students and professors participate will be taken into account.

If it is below the specified level, the survey points are not given based on indicators.

Which areas are evaluated in the nationwide survey?

During the survey, the following are taken into account:

  • quality of education;

  • spiritual-educational environment;

  • scientific-creative and sports activities conditions for;

  • knowledge and skills of graduates.

Higher education organizations are divided into categories

National rating is maintained in the following categories depending on the contingent of higher education organizations:

  • social-humanitarian sciences;

  • applied sciences;

  • medical sciences;

  • higher education organizations in the direction of creativity and sports.

Conclusion

The draft of the new regulation makes the national ranking of higher education organizations open, transparent and It is expected that the clear definition of rating criteria, data verification and nationwide survey mechanism will be an important impetus to improve the quality of higher education. — Source
