Students have the opportunity to study without leaving work

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Dual education system will be introduced in higher education by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 14 of January 16, 2025, document "On measures to organize dual education in the higher education system" approved.
How will the dual education system be organized?
This regulation: Coordinating theoretical and practical training, establishing cooperation mechanisms between higher education institutions and production enterprises will serve such purposes as improving the practical skills of students. The document "On measures to organize dual education in the higher education system" was approved by the decision of the court on January 16, 2025 No. 14. they will have the opportunity to study without being separated from work.

The main directions of the dual education system

  • "Professional" dual education system will be introduced.
  • At the first stage, it will be used for 3rd and 4th year students of the Tashkent Textile and Light Industry Institute.
  • Practical training of students is organized in cooperation with enterprises.
  • On the basis of dual education, the salary of up to 2 million soums for the period of work of students in enterprises is covered by the State Employment Assistance Fund.

How is the system of dual education organized?

  • Each By June 1, a list of enterprises that accept students in the form of dual education will be determined and published. Every year, by September 1, students accepted to study in the form of dual education will be attached to enterprises and relevant contracts will be drawn up. is constantly monitored.

The importance of the dual education system

With this decision The regulation on the procedure for the organization of dual education in the higher education system was approved. This regulation:

  • Coordination of theoretical and practical training
  • Determining cooperation mechanisms between higher education institutions and production enterprises
  • Improve students' practical skills.

Conclusion

Students through the dual education system He will have the opportunity to gain practical experience along with theoretical knowledge and to directly participate in the production process. This serves to increase the competitiveness of higher education graduates in the labor market. — Source
