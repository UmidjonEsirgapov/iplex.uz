Cambridge Learning Center 400 million soums "Sergelida Science Center" announces an educational grant

In connection with the opening of a new branch of the Cambridge educational center in Sergeli, we are announcing 50 grants for young people who live permanently in Sergeli, have lost their breadwinners or have disabilities.

Through this grant 50 students will have the opportunity to study English for free at the "Cambridge" educational center for 1 year.

To participate in the grant, you need to write a motivational letter about yourself and leave it here.

The grant consists of 2 parts. will be:
Part 1- Questionnaire. All participants must enter their personal information and send us a motivational letter.
Part 2 - Interview. The best of the applications will be selected and invited for an interview, and 50 of them will be selected as winners.

Important dates of the grant:
1. Forms are accepted from October 24 to November 1. 2. Questionnaires will be analyzed from November 1 to November 7 3. Interviews will take place from November 8 to November 18 4. Courses will start from November 22.

Note: To participate in the project, one must permanently reside in the Sergeli district.
