Who can apply as an applicant?

Legal blogger Khushnudbek answered this question: the following can apply for the stage:

those who have completed the 11th grade of general secondary education.

This includes all schools, including presidential and creative schools, specialized and military schools, lyceums, gymnasiums, vocational schools, and so on. The most important thing is to have a certificate based on the 11th grade, it is not possible to study as a student after completing the 10th grade;

Those who graduated from a professional and secondary special education organization.

This includes academic lyceums, vocational colleges and technical schools. Students studying in higher education.

In case of enrollment, he chooses where he will study, if he wants to continue his current studentship, if he wants, he can study in the new place he entered. But please, don't apply just for the sake of the game, because if you enter and then drop out, you will have reserved one applicant's place in vain, and then no one will be accepted for the vacant place. they can apply for the bachelor's degree. They are just like today's students in school, high school, college, etc. they apply for studies with their diplomas, not with a university diploma.
