The diploma of Tashkent State Technical University is recognized all over the world

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
On October 2-6, 2023, the compliance of the quality of education at Tashkent State Technical University with world standards and criteria was assessed by the German organization ASIIN. 60712100-Metallurgy Bachelor's degree courses 70710601-Electrical supply (industrial enterprises and cities) 70712101-Metallurgy (by types of metals) master's specialties were successfully passed international accreditation for a period of 5 years.
It should be noted that the certificate of international accreditation guarantees the quality of education and services, their compliance with international requirements, and the diploma of higher education for these programs is recognized all over the world.
