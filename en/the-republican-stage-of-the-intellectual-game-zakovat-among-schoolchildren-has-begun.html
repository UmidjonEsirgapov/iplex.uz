The Republican stage of the intellectual game "Zakovat" among schoolchildren has begun

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
On October 20, the Republican stage of the intellectual game "Zakovat" among the students of secondary schools of Uzbekistan began. a total of 42 teams and 294 students are competing for the victory. The winning team will be admitted to higher education institutions on a preferential basis and will be encouraged with a "Zakovat" scholarship for 1 academic year. started This was reported by the information service of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.
