General information:

The US Embassy in Tashkent is accepting applications for participation in the Sports Visitor program funded by the Department of Sports Diplomacy of the US State Department. The program is designed for women self-defense instructors from all regions of Uzbekistan. The U.S. Embassy in Tashkent is accepting applications for participation in the "Sports Visitor" program funded by the Department of Sports Diplomacy of the U.S. State Department. The two-week Sports Visitor program provides selected candidates with the opportunity to travel to the United States, participate in interdisciplinary self-defense training programs that promote women's rights and empowerment in the United States, and participate in internships.

Note: The program is open to female self-defense instructors from all regions of Uzbekistan.

Applicable to requirements:

  • be over 21 years old before the start of the program (July 2022);
  • Citizen of Uzbekistan currently residing in the regions of Uzbekistan, including the city of Tashkent and the Republic of Karakalpakstan;
  • Perfect knowledge of Uzbek, English or Russian languages (knowledge of English is not required);
  • in good physical and mental condition;
  • gender promotes an American vision of equality and a world free of sexual violence;
  • regular participant in professional sporting events in his neighborhood;
  • two years of professional experience in self-defense/martial arts, including boxing, kickboxing, karate, wrestling, sambo, judo, taekwondo, muay thai, etc.;
  • at least 50 hours of self-defense does / has martial arts training;
  • belongs to a sports club, gym or other local sports organization and/or its instructors;
  • with or without the financial support of the US Embassy, is ready or willing to create self-defense courses for women and girls in its territory;
  • July-August 2022 has the opportunity to visit the US for two weeks in months;
  • has the right to obtain and maintain a US exchange visa (J-1) required for the program;
  • the program is open to female self-defense instructors who are required to return to Uzbekistan immediately upon completion.

The following individuals are in the Sports Visitor program Participation is not eligible:

  • US citizens and permanent residents of the United States;
  • Persons currently living outside of Uzbekistan;
  • Candidates who have previously participated in this program;
  • Men.

To participate in the Sports Visitor program in one of the following languages online applicationyou can fill out:

  • Fill out the application in English;
  • Fill out the application in Uzbek;
  • Fill out the application in Russian;

Note: Only shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview

Last date for submission of applications: Online you must submit the application no later than April 10, 2022 at 18:00 (Uzbekistan local time). — Source
