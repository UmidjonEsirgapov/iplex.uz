Mayor's assistants are admitted to study on the basis of a state grant

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The President signed the decision "On the measures to organize the activities of assistant governors on the issues of developing entrepreneurship in the neighborhood, ensuring population employment and reducing poverty." According to the recommendation of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, governors of the regions and the city of Tashkent, the practice of accepting non-resident assistant governors to study on the basis of an interview in the form of correspondence education based on the recommendation of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Karakalpakstan and the mayors of the city of Tashkent. deputy for entrepreneurship issues, employment inspector of district (city) employment assistance centers, reception offices of the prime minister in districts and cities for consideration of entrepreneurs' appeals are organized at the expense of state units.

Starting from the 2022/2023 academic year, assistant governors who do not have a higher education will be admitted to study in economic education areas of state higher education institutions on the recommendation of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, governors of the regions and the city of Tashkent, on the basis of a state grant based on an interview.

Accordingly, the staff units of the governor's assistants and the staff of the centers will be established at the expense of the optimization of the staff units of the deputy chairman of the neighborhood assembly for improvement, real estate and entrepreneurship issues, employment inspector of the district (city) employment assistance centers, and reception offices of the prime minister in the districts and cities for considering the applications of entrepreneurs.
