Students will be directed to sports based on their physiological condition

According to Government Decision No. 447 of July 17, 2025 mainly, the system of promoting a healthy lifestyle and specializing in sports in general secondary schools will be gradually improved. Now, students are directed to sports taking into account their physiological and anthropometric condition.

Medical examinations will enter a new stage

Students will undergo medical examinations every year. During these examinations:

  • Physiological parameters (heart rate, blood pressure, etc.)

  • Anthropometric measurements (height, weight, body mass index)

are determined and their tendency to sports is assessed. These data are entered into the "Electronic Health" information system.

Direction to sports through the "ERP-school" system

Based on the collected data, through the "ERP-school" information system, each student:

  • Physical ability for sports

  • Interest level

  • Healthy sport

is determined and recorded in the system. This allows teachers and parents to be properly guided.

Educational seminars for nurses will be organized

Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, school nurses will be able to:

  • Actually take anthropometric measurements

  • Analyze the physiological state Special training-seminars are held every year on

  • Entering data into the electronic system

. This will serve to improve the qualifications of specialists.

Conclusion

According to the new decision, students will now be directed to sports based on their health, physical performance and interests. This not only improves children's health, but also increases interest in professional sports. Through electronic systems, this process is transparent and accurate. — More information
