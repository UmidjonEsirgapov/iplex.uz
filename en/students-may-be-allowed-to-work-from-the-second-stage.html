Students may be allowed to work from the second stage

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Photo: "Khabar.uz" The draft of the government's decision "On measures to create opportunities for students of higher education institutions to work in their specialty" was placed for discussion. on the basis of the certificate issued by the head of the higher education institution, it is allowed to work during free time from studying in the specialty; - a certificate is not required when hiring students undergoing paid production practice and paid internships; - students are allowed to work in their specialty on the basis of a certificate only during the period of study at a higher education institution. Also, the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation is tasked with developing and approving the procedure for issuing a certificate that gives students the right to work in their specialty within two months: a list of educational areas for which a certificate is issued; from which stage the certificate is issued according to educational directions; Circumstances to be taken into account when issuing a certificate. The draft decision was discussed.

According to it:

- students of higher education institutions are allowed to work during their free time from their studies on the basis of a certificate issued by the head of higher educational institution, starting from the second level (from the second year courses in some educational fields);

- students undergoing paid production practice and paid internship certificate is not required for employment;

- students are allowed to work in their specialty based on a certificate only during their studies at a higher education institution.

In addition, the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation is tasked with developing and approving the procedure for issuing a certificate that gives students the right to work in their specialty within two months: — More information

  • list of educational areas for which a certificate will be issued;
  • from which stage the certificate will be issued according to educational areas;
  • circumstances taken into account when issuing a certificate.
