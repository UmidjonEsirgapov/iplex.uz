Aarhus School of Architecture has organized the annual Photo Of The Year competition to financially encourage students in the field of architecture. This year's photo contest will accept photos on the theme Architectural Landscapes.

Awards:

  • 1st place: €5,000;
  • 2nd place: €2,000;
  • 3rd place: €1,000.

Participation in the competition is absolutely free. Applicants must upload 5 photos when submitting an application. The size of the photos should not exceed 20mb.

To participate in the contest, please register and upload your photos.

The deadline for submitting an application to participate in the contest is October 18. — Source
