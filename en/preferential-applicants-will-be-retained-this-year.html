Preferential applicants will be retained this year

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the decree of the President, applicants who have a privilege that is the basis for recommending a studentship on the basis of an additional state grant without an exam, based on the decision of the State Commission, regardless of the form of education, will receive an additional state grant for the first bachelor's course of study chosen in the framework of the privilege during registration.

According to the decree of the President, applicants who have a privilege that is the basis for recommending to students on the basis of an additional state grant without an examination, based on the decision of the State Commission, regardless of the form of education, in the scope of the privilege is valid during registration on the basis of an additional state grant will be admitted to study. — Official Data
