In some educational institutions, the direction "Art of Askiya and curiosity" will be opened

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
According to the Presidential Decree "On Measures for the Further Development of National Askiya and Hobby Art in the Republic" (No. PQ-222, 11.07.2023)
: Famous creators in the field of Askiya and hobby art vocational schools will be established; Creators who have created their own skill school in accordance with the procedure specified in this decision will receive an additional incentive in the amount of 5 times the minimum wage (4,900,000 soums) per month through the main workplace; Skill schools are given the right to use the facilities of the institution where they operate for free, and a separate room is allocated to them by this institution.
From the 2024/2025 academic year, the "Askiya and curiosity art" course will be opened at the Karim Zaripov Republican Pop and Circus College and the Fergana Specialized Art School.
