Entrance exams to higher and professional educational institutions are held in July-August

Brief content:

at the briefing, the head of the information service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation Nazokat Abdukunduzova gave information on the questions from citizens regarding the conduct of entrance exams to higher and professional educational institutions for the 2024/2025 academic year and the procedure for admission to master's degrees. and entrance exams to professional educational institutions are scheduled to be held in July-August. Accordingly, it was confirmed that the professional/creative exams will be held in 53 higher education institutions on July 5-19. That is, from tomorrow, professional/creative exams in areas requiring special talents, such as sports, art, and journalism, will begin from tomorrow.

After the date of the tests conducted by the Education Evaluation Agency is officially confirmed, additional information will be provided through the official pages of the ministry.

Where to get the permits to enter the exam. will it be?

The permit is created in the personal office of the applicant on the site my.uzbmb.uz. The applicant's permit will contain information on the location, date and time of the examinations. Currently, applicants for professional/creative exams can download permits from their personal offices.

Permits for entering tests are also being created on the site my.uzbmb.uz. After the process is completed, applicants will be able to download from their personal account.

How to get a permit if the registered phone SIM card is lost?

Permits can be downloaded from the applicant's personal account at my.uzbmb.uz. In this case, the phone number in the online registration can be entered in the place of login and the password they set can enter the personal cabinet and upload the permit. If the telephone SIM card is lost and the password is forgotten, it is recommended to do the following:

  • Go to the telecommunications company and restore the registered number (renew the SIM card);
  • Or if this is not possible, when applying to the Knowledge Assessment Agency and its regional departments with an identity document, change it to another phone number requested by the applicant.

Are late applicants allowed to enter the exam?

It is not allowed to be late for the exam. The exact start time of the exams will be written on the applicant's permit. Therefore, applicants are asked to arrive at the time and place specified in the permit one hour before the start of the exam.

Can an applicant who is in another region on the day of the test for some reason, take the test in that region?

No, the applicant can take part in the exam organized in that region, whichever region he specified during online registration. Therefore, the test materials for this applicant will be reserved for the region he has specified and it is not possible to change it.

Is it possible to choose up to 5 courses?

Yes, this year, after the final results of the entrance exams, applicants will have the opportunity to choose up to 5 courses according to their specialty. For this, they will be given 15 days after the completion of the tests. During this period, applicants choose a higher education institution, a bachelor's course of study and a form of education.

How are students admitted to the master's degree?

According to the established procedure, the applicant's admission to the master's degree in state higher educational institutions is based on the average score of the bachelor's degree and the level of knowledge of a foreign language. or based on an international certificate score. In this case, the sequence of points is strictly followed and no additional exam is held.

When will the result of admission to the master's degree be announced?

Admission to the master's degree is carried out by the State Commission, strictly observing the sequence of points within the set admission quotas. The decision of the State Commission on admission to the magistracy of higher education institutions is scheduled to be approved by August 20.

According to it, professional (creative) exams and tests for other educational areas will be held in the areas of education that require a special talent from the student in order to transfer them to suitable and similar educational areas. In this case, professional (creative) exams are conducted by the relevant higher education institution, and tests are conducted by the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency.

Passing scores for tests and professional (creative) exams are determined by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and the ministries and agencies that have higher education institutions in their system by July 15 of each year, and Knowledge and Skills will be announced on the official website of the evaluation agency.

Students who scored below the passing score will be admitted to study on the basis of a differentiated fee-contract in accordance with the procedure for admission to the bachelor's degree.

Transfer to appropriate and unrelated (i.e., unrelated) educational areas of the state higher education institutions in accordance with the procedure established for the relevant undergraduate education. is made on the basis of a one-time payment equal to the minimum amount of the differentiated payment-contract. That is, the minimum amount of the differentiated fee-contract is determined annually by the State Commission, and it can be from 8 to 25 times the amount of the basic fee-contract, depending on the direction.
