About the University and Grant Program:

Founded in 1991, Khazar University is a private university in Baku, Azerbaijan. This university has a total of 6 campuses, 2 of which are located throughout Azerbaijan, and the other 4 are in Baku. The Scholarship grant program covers the full tuition fees of international students and gives them the opportunity to study at Khazar University for free. The scholarship does not include living, travel and other expenses. Initially, the grant is granted for one year with the condition that it can be extended depending on the student's academic performance.

Who can apply for the grant program?

For the program to be considered suitable, the candidates must:

  • Be a citizen of any country other than Azerbaijan;
  • Apply for admission to one of the master's or doctoral programs.
  • Demonstrated excellent academic performance and high achievement in previous study levels;
  • Demonstrated academic talent and strong motivation in the chosen field of study.

1. Complete your application:

Once you have identified your program, download the application form for your chosen study level (given at the very end of the article) and complete it according to the application instructions.

2. Submit your application:

Applications should be sent directly to the Office of International Relations and sent to international admissions@khazar.org or by post with all required documents.
