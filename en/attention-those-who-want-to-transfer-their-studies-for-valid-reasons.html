Based on the above decision, citizens can apply to regional working groups for the following valid reasons:

The following documents are presented to regional working groups:

  • on transfer of education application (the application form is issued by the regional working group and filled out by the student on the spot);

  • a copy of the passport;
  • relevant documents justifying the above-mentioned good reasons.
