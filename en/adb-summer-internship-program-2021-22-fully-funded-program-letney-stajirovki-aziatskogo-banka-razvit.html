ADB Summer Internship Program 2021-22: fully funded program letney stajirovki Aziatskogo banka razvitiya

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
ADB Summer Internship - eto programma stajirovki na polnyy rabochiy den dlya inostrannyx studentsov.
Stajirovka nachnetsya v junye 2022 goda i prodlitsya 8 nedel.
Trebovaniya k kandidam: Uchitsya na stepen magistra ili doctorate and university odnoy iz stran-uchastnits Aziatskogo banka razvitiya; Obuchenie v sphere, neposredstvenno svyazannoy s deyatelnostyu Aziatskogo banka razvitiya; Free English language; Professional experience in the data area.

ADB Summer Internship is a full-time internship program for foreign students. Citizens of the country of Aziatskogo banka razvitiya imeyut pravo podat zayavku na programmu stajirovki. В настоящее время в Азиатский развития входят 68 стран, в том числе Узбезки. Eta internship program is not prednaznachena dlya studentsov bacalariata.

Trebovaniya k kandidam:

  • Uchitsya na stapen magistra ili doktor v universitete odnoy iz stran-uchastnits Aziatskogo banka razvitiya;
  • Buchenie v sfere, neporedstvenno svyazannoy s yatelnostyu Aziatskogo banka razvitiya;
  • Free English language;
  • Professional experience in data area.

Prodolzhitelnost programmy i process podachi zayavki:

  1. Podayte zayavku online zdes
  2. 8 weeks

Krayin srok podachi zayavok na programma stajirovki 2021:

First stream: from July 15 to September 15, 2021;

Second stream: from December 1, 2021. po January 31, 2022.

Kandidaty podayut zayavki cherez Career and Employment System(ACES). All documents (resumé and essay) must be sent to ACES for job applications. Zayavki, podannye pozje ukazannogo roka, rassmatrivatsya ne budut. Vam takje neobhodimo budet vypolnit zadanie, ukazannoe v zayavlenii. O naznachenii budet obyavleno na ofitsialnom site Aziatskogo banka razvitiya. Candidaty imeyut vozmojnost vybirat zadaniya, iskhodya iz svoix interesov i potensiala.
