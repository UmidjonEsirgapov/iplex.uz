In addition to the State Test Center, the Educational Inspection was completed. What about?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education and the Ministry of Innovative Development are merging into one ministry - the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation; The Ministry of Pre-School Education and the Ministry of Public Education are being merged into one ministry - the Ministry of Pre-School and School Education.
The State Inspection of Education Quality under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan will be abolished.
The tasks and functions of this organization will be divided between the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and the Ministries of Pre-School and School Education.
  • The Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education and the Ministry of Innovative Development are being merged into one ministry - the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation;

  • The Ministry of Preschool Education and the Ministry of Public Education are also being merged into one ministry - the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.

  • The State Inspectorate for Quality Control of Education under the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan will be abolished.

The tasks and functions of this organization will be divided between the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and the Ministries of Preschool and School Education.
