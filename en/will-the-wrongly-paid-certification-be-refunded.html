❓ I sent an application by choosing an extraordinary type of attestation to get a category. I had also made the specified amount of payments. I chose the wrong type of certification. My application was rejected as "you are submitting it in the next compulsory attestation". Can I get my paid fee back?

✅ Answer: According to Clause 25 of the Regulation, at any stage where incorrect information or forged documents are found to be submitted, the process of receiving and attesting the documents of the teaching staff will be stopped, the submitted documents and the paid fee will not be returned.

Submitted by the teaching staff Determining whether a document is fake is made on the basis of the information available in the competent ministries.

If it is not possible to determine the authenticity of the documents submitted by the teaching staff based on the information available in the competent ministries, the competent ministries will send a questionnaire to the relevant authorized organizations.

If it is found that the documents submitted by the teaching staff are fake, it will be submitted to the appropriate law enforcement agencies by the competent ministries to take action in accordance with the legislation.

Incorrect selection of the type of attestation is also considered as providing incorrect information. — Source
