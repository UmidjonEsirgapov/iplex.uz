About the conference:

Future News Worldwide is a partnership program between the British Council and the world's leading media organizations. The main goal of the program is to help young people develop a broad range of journalism skills and demonstrate the UK's global leadership in training young journalists and media workers.

Who is the program for:

The program is open to undergraduate and graduate students aged 18-25 and graduates who have completed their studies on or after July 1, 2019. It is not important for candidates to be studying or have studied journalism, but it is important that they have a broad vision of the media and a passion for working in this field in the future.

  • Having an IELTS 6.5 or equivalent language certificate or the ability to communicate in English at this level;
  • Not previously attended Future NEWS or Future News Worldwide conferences in Edinburgh or London in person;
  • Strong interest in the field of journalism.
  • At the online conference on either 14th or 15th July 2021 to be able to attend.
