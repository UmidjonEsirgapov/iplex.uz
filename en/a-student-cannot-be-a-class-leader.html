A student cannot be a class leader According to the order of the Minister of Preschool and School Education No. 190 dated June 27, 2023 (source), class leaders are appointed and released by the director of the educational institution from among the teachers.
The class leader is as follows. selected based on the following criteria: Has at least 3 years of teaching experience; Teachers who work as the main worker in the school (teachers who work on an hourly (including substitute) basis in general education institutions cannot lead a class); Has the ability to manage; Able to take a responsible approach when working with students.
Class leadership in primary grades is assigned to teachers assigned to the primary grade.
