The American Embassy announces a free "First Lessons in Media" course for everyone.

It is very important today to be able to correctly evaluate the information we receive. If we develop the ability to correctly analyze data, we can distinguish between reliable and fake, useful and useless. This course known as "First Lessons in Media" will help you in forming these skills.
In the online course "First Lessons in Media" you will be able to correctly evaluate information and notice hidden advertisements in them, distinguish between opinions and facts, identify fake news and "hype" information and create correct information.

Duration:

The course "First lessons on media" will be held online 2 times a week for 1 month through the "Zoom" platform.

The lessons will be conducted in Uzbek and will start on November 1.
