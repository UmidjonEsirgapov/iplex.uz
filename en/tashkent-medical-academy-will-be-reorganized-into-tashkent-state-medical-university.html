Tashkent Medical Academy will be reorganized into Tashkent State Medical University

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The information service of the Ministry of Health informs that the draft decision of the President "On the establishment of the Tashkent State Medical University and the further improvement of the personnel training system in the field" It states: the basis for the training, retraining and improvement of the qualifications of highly educated specialists and scientific personnel for the medical field of the university is a higher education institution; the university is the legal successor of the Tashkent Medical Academy in terms of its rights, obligations and contracts, including international contracts; Students studying at the Tashkent Medical Academy continue their studies at the university in the appropriate bachelor's and master's specialties, and the students of the graduate course are awarded a university diploma of the state model according to the established procedure; the university is the founder of the university clinic, academic lyceum, research institute of experimental medicine, and their activities are coordinated by the university; constituent structures of the university are public institutions and have private treasury accounts; The heads of the structural structures of the university are appointed and dismissed by the Minister of Health according to the presentation of the university council.
