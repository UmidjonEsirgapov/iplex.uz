Central Asian Youth Talks and Model OSCE: 5-day all-expenses-paid summit in Tajikistan

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The Central Asian Youth Talks and Model OSCE - an event for the exchange of knowledge and experience for 25 active young people from Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan.
At the event, active young people with their ideas and achievements to share, gain mutual knowledge and develop their communities.
Participants of the event will discuss issues related to youth cooperation in the Central Asian region, strengthening youth communication and developing network platforms for the future development of the region.

The Central Asian Youth Talks and Model OSCE - Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Knowledge and experience exchange event for 25 active young people of Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan. At the event, active young people can share their ideas and achievements, learn from each other and conduct regional discussions to develop their communities.

Participants of the event will discuss issues related to youth cooperation in the Central Asian region, strengthening youth communication and developing network platforms for the future development of the region.

Within the program, young people will also participate in the Model OSCE, while at the same time on Security issues. They get acquainted with the comprehensive approach of the OSCE, discuss existing problems and develop negotiation and constructive communication skills with the help of the OSCE in solving them.

Duration of the program: October 29 - November 2

Benefits:

  • Participants attend the event for free;
  • The event is in a "traditional" format. if held, accommodation, meals and transportation will be covered by the organizers;
  • International travel expenses will be financed by the organizers, but the participants must bear the cost of traveling to and from cities in their own countries.

Who can participate:

  • The candidate must be 18-25 years old and be from Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Be a citizen of Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan;
  • Must be fluent in English and Russian;
  • Must have participated in youth summits and models at various levels and share experiences with others.

To participate, fill out this application form and send it to POID-Election@osce.org together with a copy of your biometric passport.

The final results will be announced by October 14.

For more information: POID-Election@osce.org
