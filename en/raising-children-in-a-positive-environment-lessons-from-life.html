Children what do they learn from life?

Children's attitude to life is formed through the lessons they learn from the environment and their upbringing. Below we explain how children learn from different situations.

What do children learn in a negative environment?

  • If you criticize a child all the time, he will learn to hate.
  • If a child lives in conflict, he will learn to be aggressive.
  • If you laugh at a child, he shuts down.
  • If you scold a child, he learns to live with guilt.

What do children learn in a positive environment?

  • If a child lives in resilience he learns to accept others.
  • If a child lives in rightness and honesty, he learns to be just.
  • If a child lives in security, he learns to trust others.
  • If a child If you support, he will learn to value himself.
  • If a child lives in understanding and friendship, he will learn to find love.
