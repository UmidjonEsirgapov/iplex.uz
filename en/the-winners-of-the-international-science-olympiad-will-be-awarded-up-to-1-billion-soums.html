The winners of the International Science Olympiad will be awarded up to 1 billion soums

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the new decree of the President, the prize money paid to students who win international science Olympiads will be increased by 5 times.
This decision will come into force on June 1, 2025.
Now the 5 most prestigious international science Olympiads (IMO — mathematics, IBO — biology, IChO — chemistry, IPhO — physics, IOI — informatics) winners or prize-winners will receive a one-time cash prize in the following amount: 1st place: 937,500,000 soums (2,500 times) 2nd place: 562,500,000 soums (1,500 times) 3rd place: 375,000,000 soums (1,000 times). These indicators are increased by 5 times compared to the previous amounts. This decision will come into force on June 1, 2025.

Currently, the winners or prize-winners of the 5 most prestigious international science Olympiads (IMO - Mathematics, IBO - Biology, IChO - Chemistry, IPhO - Physics, IOI - Informatics) will receive a one-time cash prize in the following amount:

These indicators are 5 times increased compared to the previously valid amounts.

The decree is intended to stimulate students' interest in science and intellectual development, and increase the influence of Uzbekistan in the international arena It is planned.
