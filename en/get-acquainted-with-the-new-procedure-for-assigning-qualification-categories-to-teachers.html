Get acquainted with the new procedure for assigning qualification categories to teachers

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
63 vk.pdf On December 29, 2023, the Cabinet of Ministers issued a resolution on "Measures to improve the procedure for certification of pedagogic personnel of preschool, general secondary, secondary special, professional and extracurricular educational organizations" Decision No. 692 "On Amendments and Additions to Decision No. 572 of September 17, 2021" was approved.
According to it: from January 1, 2024, a new procedure for assigning the qualification category to pedagogic staff of state preschool, general secondary and extracurricular educational organizations will be introduced.
