Consideration of appeals has been completed

Starting from August 22 of this year, admission to and study at HEIs appeals of applicants who are dissatisfied with the results of the transfer test were considered by the Appeals Commission. The appeal process was completely completed on September 10, 2024.

A total of 4,055 applicants' appeals were planned to be considered by the appeal commission. 831 of them (20.5%) directly participated in the process, and the remaining 3,224 did not participate in the process.

This year, for the first time in Nukus, a mobile Appeals Commission was established by the Knowledge Assessment Agency to consider the complaints of applicants from Karakalpakstan.
