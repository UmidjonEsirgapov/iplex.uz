Brief about the grant program:

The main goal of the Curtin Merit-based international awards grant program presented by Curtin University is excellence and is to encourage talented international students. The scholarship is offered to international students who wish to study at Curtin University's Dubai branch.

Benefits of the scholarship:

  • Up to 100% grant for tuition fees.

Who can apply for the scholarship?

  • To study at the university’s foundation, bachelor’s and master’s levels. Citizens of all countries can apply for the program.

Application process:

  1. Candidates should apply here to study one of the following courses offered at the university's foundation, bachelor's and master's levels;
  2. Then, apply here for the grant program offered by the university.

Required documents (for admission to training courses):

  • Diploma (if not in English with its notarized translation);
  • Diploma application (if not in English with its notarized translation);
  • Passport copy;
  • Language certificate - IELTS 6.5 (each section 6.0 (must not be less than) or other equivalent international language certificates.

Candidates have the opportunity to study at the following university foundation, bachelor and master levels:
