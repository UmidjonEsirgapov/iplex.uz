Have you dreamed of working in the heart of the European Union? This is your chance. Applications for the Schuman Internship at the European Parliament are now open.

The European Parliament offers various vacancies for interns in the Secretariat. The goal of the internship is to help young citizens gain the necessary experience.

The duration of the internship is 5 months. Each candidate can apply for 3 vacancies.

Benefits:

  • Participants will do internships in prestigious parliaments for 5 months;
  • Work with strong experts in an international environment;
  • Interns working in Brussels and Luxembourg can receive 1339 euros per month.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must be at least 18 years old;
  • Have completed at least the 3rd year of a bachelor’s degree;
  • Have a perfect knowledge of one of the EU languages;
  • Have not worked in EU institutions for more than 2 months;
  • Have not engaged in research activities during the last 6 should be.
