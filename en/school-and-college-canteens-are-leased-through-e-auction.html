School and college kitchens will be rented through E-auction

Canteens of educational institutions are now planned to be rented in a more open and transparent way - through the E-auction platform. This opens up new opportunities for entrepreneurs offering kitchen services.

Who can participate in the auction?

In order to participate in the auction, an entrepreneur must meet the following requirements:

1. Must be state registered

The lessee must be registered as a legal entity or an individual entrepreneur.

2. At least 6 months of experience

Entrepreneur must have worked in the catering industry for at least 6 months. This is an important experience in effective kitchen management.

3. Possession of special equipment

The tenant must have special equipment necessary for the organization of kitchen activities.

4. Absence of debt

The entrepreneur should not have any debt under lease or other contracts concluded with the state, as well as any debt regarding tax payments.

How will the auction take place?

The leasing process will be carried out openly through the E-auction electronic platform. Entrepreneurs can participate online by submitting the prescribed documents.
