The deadline for the payment of contract funds has been extended until December 1

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the relevant statement of the State Admission Commission, the deadline for making payments for undergraduate and graduate students of higher education institutions in the 2024/2025 academic year has been extended until December 1.
Admission for the following and The deadline for making payments is set: Applicants who expressed their desire to study on the basis of a basic or differentiated payment contract (stage 1); Applicants recommended for studentship on additional admission quotas allocated for young people from Karakalpakstan on the basis of Decree No. PF-213; Students whose studies have been transferred or resumed.
Terms of payment: Those recommended to study within the parameters of admission pay at least 25% of the basic fee-contract amount; Those who want to study on the basis of a differentiated payment contract (super) must pay at least 50% of the annual fee.

According to the relevant statement of the State Admissions Commission, the payment deadline for undergraduate and graduate students of higher education institutions in the 2024/2025 academic year is December 1. extended. The deadline for admission and payment is set for the following:

  • Applicants who expressed their desire to study on the basis of a basic or differentiated fee-contract (stage 1);
  • Recommended for additional admission quotas for young people from Karakalpakstan based on Decree No. PF-213 applicants;
  • Students whose studies have been transferred or reinstated.

Payment conditions:

  • Those recommended to study within the admission parameters must pay at least 25 percent of the amount of the basic fee-contract;
  • Study on the basis of the graded fee-contract (super) applicants must pay at least 50 percent of the annual fee.

Note: The right to extend the payment period for the second and higher stages belongs to the head of the higher education institution. — Read on infoedu.uz
