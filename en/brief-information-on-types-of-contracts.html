Brief information on types of contracts

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
A bilateral contract is concluded between the higher education institution and the applicant.
Also, if you want to get an educational loan from a bank or pay from your parents' office, it is enough to get a bilateral contract.
If possible. if the contract or super-contract money is to be paid not by the applicant himself, but by a sponsoring organization (perhaps there may be a condition to make it to that organization later), then it is necessary to select the clause called a tripartite contract.

When students apply online through the kontakt.edu.uz site for the purpose of receiving a payment contract, there is a clause called "Choose the type of contract". In this case, you are asked to choose a 2-way or 3-way contract. Many questions came from the applicants asking for an explanation on this.

It is very simple.

Bilateral contract is concluded between the HEI and the applicant. In other words, if you want to pay the contract or super-contract money from the applicant himself, you should select this item.

In addition, if you want to get an education loan from the bank or pay from your parents' salary, it is enough to get a bilateral contract. will be established between a sponsoring organization. If a sponsoring organization wants to pay the money for the contract or super-contract, not the applicant himself (perhaps there may be a condition to work for that organization later), then you should choose a clause called a tripartite contract. When choosing three-way, it is necessary to enter the STIR numbers of the sponsoring company (not the university you are studying in). They should get a bilateral contract. — Details here
