Dear girls, UniSat — Launch your dreams program invites you to a new international project.

The doors of the world's most advanced technological universities will be opened for girls participating in the UniSat program. You will be in a team with NASA professors and engineers, Russian Space Research Agency, Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan scientists.

Unisat is a unique opportunity for girls of Uzbekistan to find their way, change their lives and try their hand at science and advanced technologies.

What is the UniSat program?

UniSat is A joint program of UNICEF Uzbekistan, Tech4impact NGO and the Ministry of Innovative Development of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

UniSat mission is to develop knowledge and skills in girls to work in a team, perform in public, manage time effectively, and improve social adaptation. This is a program to popularize science, space technologies, STEM-education and attract the attention of the general public to the issue of gender in technological fields among the advanced youth of Uzbekistan. No special knowledge or skills are required.

What will you learn?

  • Team building and time planning;
  • Introduction to initial satellite design and 3D modeling;
  • Software integration and hardware assembly;
  • Engineering for UniSat nanosatellite design and development. approach;
  • European Space Industry Standard (ECSS) used by UNISAT;
  • Data analysis and many other interesting subjects

You will gain knowledge about printed circuit boards, electrical engineering, space engineering, Internet of Things and wireless communication.

You will make your own flight plan and fly your nanosatellite from the field in Uzbekistan to the air navigation services. fly under the supervision of experts. A live broadcast of this grand event will be held for all space enthusiasts around the world.
