Is a Linguaskill certificate considered for applicants?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 395, when entering HEIs, applicants with a national or international certificate of level B2 or higher in a foreign language will be given maximum points in this subject.
The relevant statement of the State Commission established the following procedure: from the 2023-2024 academic year, the Cambridge Assessment English Linguaskill international certificate will be taken into account only for applicants who have passed the exam in officially registered exam centers of the Cambridge Assessment English organization under the direct supervision of the organization's representatives.

Ministers In accordance with the decision of the Court No. 395, applicants with a national or international certificate of level B2 or higher in a foreign language are given the maximum points for this subject when entering higher education institutions. statement established the following procedure:

For admission to state HEIs starting from the 2023-2024 academic year, the Cambridge Assessment English Linguaskill international certificate will be taken into account only for those applicants who have passed the exam in officially registered examination centers of the Cambridge Assessment English organization under the direct supervision of the organization's representatives.

Therefore, the Linguaskill certificate is an official exam. Maximum points will be given to the applicants who have passed the exam with direct participation in the centers. Certificates obtained online without going to the exam centers will not be taken into account. — Official Data
