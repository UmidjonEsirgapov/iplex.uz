Average salary in Uzbekistan in 2024: regional analysis

In 2024, average monthly salary in Uzbekistan was 5 million 357 thousand soums. This indicator shows a significant increase compared to last year. Salaries vary by region, and there is a large gap between the highest and lowest monthly incomes.

Highest salary indicators

  • Tashkent city - 9 million 72 thousand soums (21.4% increase);
  • Navoi region - 6 million 766 thousand soums (15.0% growth).

These regions are distinguished by their economic opportunities and industrial potential. Tashkent city is a major business and service center, while Navoi region is the leader with the development of mining and heavy industry.

The lowest wage indicators

  • Kashkadarya region - 3 million 785 thousand soums;
  • Surkhandarya region - 3 million 810 thousand soums.

These regions are lower in terms of the rate of economic development and are associated with an economy based on agriculture.

General analysis by regions

Average wage indicators vary between regions:

  • Tashkent region - 5 million 97 thousand soums (12.2% increase);
  • Namangan region – 3 million 869 thousand soums (16.5% increase);
  • Andijan region – 4 million 616 thousand soums (14.9% increase).
