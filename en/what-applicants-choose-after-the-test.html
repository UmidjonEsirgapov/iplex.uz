What will applicants choose after the test?

After passing the test, an important stage for applicants begins - this is direction, form of education and priority is a choice. Below we provide detailed information about each stage.

1. It is possible to choose up to five courses

There is freedom in the selection of HEIs and courses

The applicant can choose any Higher Education Institution (HEI) and the courses based on the complex of disciplines corresponding to it. A maximum of 5 directions are selected.

2. Choosing the form of education

Daytime, evening or remote - you choose

During the selection, you can indicate daytime, evening or distance learning. It is also allowed to mix and match these forms in several directions. For example:

  • Direction 1: Daytime

  • Direction 2: Daytime

  • Direction 3: Daytime

  • Direction 4: Evening

  • Direction 5: Remote

3. Prioritization

Which is more important, the direction or the grant?

You choose the priority:

  • If only the state grant is important to you, you will give priority to the grant.

  • If your goal is to enter the first chosen direction if there is (even on the basis of a contract), then the priority is given to the direction.

This choice is an important factor that affects the results!

Conclusion

When the test result comes out, the applicant should not only wait for the result, but also learn to make the right choice. The right direction, appropriate form of education and reasonable prioritization directly affect the future study and professional path. — Read on infoedu.uz
