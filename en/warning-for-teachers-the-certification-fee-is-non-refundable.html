Warning for teachers: the certification fee is non-refundable

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
It is necessary to be careful when submitting documents for certification for teaching staff.
According to the regulation approved by the government's decision No. 572 of September 17, 2021, in some cases, the fees paid for certification It is not refundable.
The money paid is not refundable in the following cases: Relinquished attestation; Failed to defend the applicable qualification category; Failed to pass certification; Provided false information or forged documents; He submitted a document out of turn and actually had to take part in the next (mandatory) attestation.
