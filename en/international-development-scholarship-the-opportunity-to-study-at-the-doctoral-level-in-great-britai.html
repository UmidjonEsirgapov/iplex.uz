General information:

International Development Scholarship: For young people who are currently studying at the master's level and want to continue their doctoral studies in England, the opportunity to study on a grant basis at the University of East Anglia, one of the most prestigious universities in Great Britain.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Students must be currently enrolled in a full-time Master's degree;
  • Candidates from any country may apply;
  • They must pay international admission fees;
  • The scholarships are open to international and EU students;
  • Proficiency in English is required;
  • In addition, they are currently enrolled in any full-time Master's degree course at the School of International Development must be or be studying;

University of East Anglia Scholarship offers the following benefits:

  • Full tuition fee waiver;
  • Students receive €18,500 contract fee;
  • Furthermore, students will have the opportunity to do a placement or internship at the host institution;
  • This is a good opportunity to study in England;

Application process:

  • Firstly, students must apply to any master course from the website and be accepted;
  • Students must receive an offer letter from the University of East Anglia before applying;
  • Then Complete this online application form;
  • The written form should detail how the program will help them achieve their goals and how their learning will help the institution. The written statement should not exceed 250 words;
  • The deadline for submitting the grant form is May 31, 2022;
