Attention of applicants who are 16 years old - do not forget to get an ID card!

Why is an ID card important?

This year, the application process for higher education institutions will be only online. To apply online, you must have a biometric passport or ID card. At the moment, biometric passports are not issued, so documents are accepted based only on an ID card.

Statistics: how many graduates have not yet received an ID card?

According to the Ministry of Preschool and School Education, 23 thousand (5.1%) of the 16-year-old graduates graduating from school in 2025 still have an ID card. did not receive. This may lead to the lack of important documents for applicants.

What problems does not getting an ID card cause?

An applicant without an ID card:

  • Can not submit an application to the OTM

  • He will have problems getting a permit for exams

  • During official registration will be delayed

When to get an ID card?

It is recommended to complete the process of obtaining an ID card before the beginning of the exams and admission stages. Queues at passport stations get longer as the application deadline approaches.

Conclusion

If you or your child is a school graduate who has turned 16, don't delay getting an ID card. This is necessary not only for applying to OTM, but also for all further official processes. Act in time! — Source
