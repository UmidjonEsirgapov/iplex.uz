Suggestions for improving the quality of education were considered

President Shavkat Mirziyoyev got acquainted with the presentation of proposals on improving the quality of pre-school and school education, improving the system of pedagogues' professional development. A number of new initiatives to improve the quality of education and support pedagogues were discussed at this event. Therefore, it is important to improve the qualifications of pedagogues and encourage them. The President emphasized the need to introduce advanced technologies and innovations to improve the quality of education.

Attestation and new evaluation system

A new attestation system was introduced, and more than 190,000 teachers participated in it. Through this system, the category of 51,000 teachers was increased. This made it possible to increase the number of highly qualified pedagogues in schools.

From September 2025, a proposal was made to increase the monthly salary of teachers with the highest and first qualification categories. This is an important step aimed at increasing the financial motivation of teachers.

Training and new programs

It was decided to develop special training programs for teachers who did not pass certification or had insufficient experience. For this purpose, it is planned to establish a system of training of teachers and pedagogues of kindergartens and schools every 5 years at the National Research Institute named after Abdulla Avloni.

11 pedagogic colleges in the regions were transferred to the training centers, and the task of attracting trainers from abroad was also set. It was also said that kindergartens and schools, technical schools, and "Barkamol Avlod" school will be established on the basis of pedagogical colleges that are working at low capacity. As a result of this system, students' mastery increased from 53% to 59%. From the new academic year, this system will be used in 1,000 more schools, and 182 specialized and 500 experimental schools will be added to them.

New methods and equipment

270 schools will be equipped with interactive whiteboards, 365 will be equipped with computer classrooms. This will help to make the educational process more effective.

Strengthening the education and training system

The proposal to establish the National Institute of Education Pedagogy on the basis of the Research Institute of Pedagogical Sciences of Uzbekistan named after Kori-Niyozi was discussed. The new institute will perform tasks such as strengthening "neighborhood-parent-school" cooperation, creating educational literature for parents and children, and scientific research of the educational views of the Jadids.

Sports schools and international styles

President Bahadir Jalolov and Oksana Chusovitina also touched on the issues of introducing international styles and improving the quality of training in sports schools.

New initiatives and incentive system

The head of our state gave instructions on improving the quality of teacher training, fair evaluation and organization of incentive system. These initiatives help to further develop the education system and create a more effective environment for students. — Official Data
