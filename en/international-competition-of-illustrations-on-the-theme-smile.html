MediBang Japan is holding an international smile photo contest. Anyone can participate in the contest.

Pictures with a smiling theme are accepted.

Requirements:

・Anyone can participate in the contest, regardless of whether they are professional or amateur artists.
・Contestants can submit several works.
・Only original works should be submitted.
・You can enter your artwork that you have previously posted on social networks such as Twitter. You can also post your contest artwork on social media during the participation period.

Entries are accepted through the contest website, please follow the written instructions for uploading work.
