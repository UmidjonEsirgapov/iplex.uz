Does a diploma from a private university really not pass?

Many people say "a diploma from a private university does not pass". But before believing such statements, it is necessary to check the legal basis. Below we provide information on how to proceed step by step.

1. Ask an official for a reason

If someone tells you that a private university diploma will not pass, first ask him:

  • Clarify what law or decision he is talking about,

  • Which normative legal document he is relying on.

2. Apply in writing

Of course, in most cases there is no legal basis for such a statement. Therefore, you should apply in writing to that manager or responsible person.

➡️ According to the law, an answer to your application should be returned within 15 days.

3. If there is no answer - the second stage

In most cases, this question is not answered. If there is no legal basis in the given answer, then such a claim is untrue.

4. Request an official letter from the university

Ask your private university to issue an official letter. Through this, you will have a two-way approach to the issue and will have the opportunity to prove it with documents.

5. The right to appeal to the court

If you are not given a reasonable answer despite all the appeals, you can appeal to the court. Because in such a case, the leader is abusing his duty and should be held accountable.

Conclusion
