United People Global (UPG) is a community that inspires people and empowers them to stabilize the world. This can be done in various ways, in particular by contributing to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

The UPG Sustainability Leadership program mobilizes people and organizations to support citizen leadership in sustainable development. UPG unites people who want to make an impact by taking positive action.

Benefits:

  • Selected candidates will participate in UPG Sustainability Leaders online classes for free;
  • The best participants will have the opportunity to conduct training on Hurricane Island, USA;
  • All expenses of the organizers - training materials, accommodation, meals, covers domestic transportation, international flights.

Requirements:

  • All candidates between 18-35 years of age can participate, regardless of nationality, gender, race;
  • Must be fluent in English;
  • Must be motivated, able to overcome difficulties, think differently from others.

Pay attention to the following when filling out the application.

  • Applications must be filled out online; applications sent to UPG by post, fax or e-mail will not be accepted;
  • Questions marked with a red asterisk must be answered;
  • Individuals may submit only one application. If a candidate submits two applications using different email addresses, they will be disqualified.
