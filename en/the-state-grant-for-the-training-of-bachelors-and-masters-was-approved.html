The state grant for the training of bachelors and masters was approved

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Parameters of the state grant for the years 2025-2029 have been approved. Parameters of the state order for admission to state higher education institutions by Presidential Decree No. PF-43 of July 18, 2025 approved.
Quotas for the baccalaureate stage (full-time education) In accordance with the order, the following grant places were determined for the 2025/2026–2028/2029 academic years: 2025/2026 academic year - 35,500 2026/2027 academic year - 36,023 2027/2028 academic year - 36,460 2028/2029 academic year - 36,761 quotas for the Master's level (full-time education) The following grant places are allocated at the Master's level: 2025/2026 academic year - 9,922 2026/2027 academic year - 10 191 2027/2028 academic year - 10,477 2028/2029 academic year - 10,784 grants allocated to special category applicants. The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation submits proposals for distribution to the State Commission in the following directions: Grants allocated to foreign and non-state HEIs 1,320 grants - foreign It will be allocated for HEIs, branches and non-governmental HEIs. In particular, the allocation of separate grant quotas for women, foreign citizens and non-governmental HEIs will serve to create equal opportunities in education. The parameters of the state order for admission to state higher education institutions were approved by the decree No. PF-43 of July 18.

Quotas for the undergraduate stage (full-time education)

According to the decree, the following grant for the academic years 2025/2026–2028/2029 places were determined:

  • 2025/2026 academic year – 35,500

  • 2026/2027 academic year – 36,023

  • 2027/2028 academic year - 36,460

  • 2028/2029 academic year - 36,761

Quotas for the master's degree (full-time education)

At the master's degree, the following grant places are allocated:

  • 2025/2026 academic year – 9,922

  • 2026/2027 academic year – 10,191

  • 2027/2028 academic year – 10,477

  • 2028/2029 academic year – 10,784 grants

Grants allocated for special categories of applicants

The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation submits proposals for allocation to the State Commission in the following directions:

Foreign and non-state HEIs grants

  • 1,320 grants are allocated for foreign higher education institutions, branches and non-state higher education institutions.

Grants for young citizens abroad

  • 30 grants - for young citizens living abroad based on special educational directions

Separate quotas for women

  • 4,000 grants - will be distributed in agreement with the Family and Women's Committee.

Conclusion

This order promotes openness and clear planning in admission to higher education. and determined the directions of the education policy for the coming years. The allocation of special grant quotas, especially for women, foreign nationals and non-governmental HEIs, will serve to create equal opportunities in education. — Official Data
