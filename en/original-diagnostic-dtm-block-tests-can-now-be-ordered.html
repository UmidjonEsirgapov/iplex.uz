The Agency for the Evaluation of Knowledge and Skills has launched diagnostic tests based on sample test tasks in the format of entrance tests for undergraduate courses of higher education institutions. This is reported by the agency's Telegram channel.

Through diagnostic tests, applicants can test their knowledge and gain skills in working with test materials (book of test tasks and answer sheets). Diagnostic test materials are delivered to all regions every week, and applicants can pick up test materials from the agency's regional representatives. Applicants who wish to use this service can order diagnostic test materials through the my.dtm.uz site. For more information, visit the oliygoh.uz site. — Official Data
