The recommendation letter of the applicant who received a recommendation letter last year, but did not enter the OTM, is not valid this year either

Brief content:
The head of the Department of the Family and Women's Committee, Oghiloy Orozova, informed about it. In the regulation on the procedure for admitting women to state higher education institutions, it is stipulated that the recommendation is valid for admission to a higher education institution only once within three years from the date of issuance. they can use it without any obstacles.

The recommendation is valid only once for admission to a higher education institution within 3 years from the date of issuance.

The recommendation letter of the applicant who received a recommendation last year but did not enter the OTM is valid this year as well. The head of the Family and Women's Committee, Ogiloy Orozova, informed about this.

It is noted that in the regulation "On the procedure for admitting women with at least five years of work experience in their specialty to the state higher education institutions" approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers, only once during the three-year period from the date of issuance of the recommendation it is determined to be valid for admission to a higher education institution. Accordingly, the recommendation letter of the applicant who received a recommendation letter last year, but was not able to enter the OTM, will be valid this year as well.
