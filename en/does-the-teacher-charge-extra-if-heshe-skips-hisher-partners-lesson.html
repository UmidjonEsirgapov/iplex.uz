Does the teacher charge extra if he/she skips his/her partner's lesson?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the new regulation, if one of the groups of subjects taught separately is left without a teacher and the class is combined, the additional teacher will not be paid extra.
According to the previous regulation, the teacher in such cases it was compulsory to join the class, but now this obligation has been cancelled.
