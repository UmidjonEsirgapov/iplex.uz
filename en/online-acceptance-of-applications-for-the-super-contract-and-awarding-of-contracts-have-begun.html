Online acceptance of applications for the super-contract and awarding of contracts have begun

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Online acceptance of applications for the super-contract and awarding of contracts have begun. Super-contract fees have been set for applicants whose scores do not reach the limit of admission quotas under the approved payment-contract.
Payment amounts are calculated based on the following ratios: less than 1.05 for applicants - the fee is 1.5 times; For those who do not reach 1.06 to 2.05 points - the payment is 2.0 times higher; For those who do not reach 2.06 to 3.05 points - the payment is 2.5 times; For those who do not reach 3.06 to 4.05 points, the fee is 3.0 times.
The minimum amount of the fee-contract for applicants of this category is calculated as follows: the cost of training according to the basic fee-contract approved by the State Commission (without stipend) is used in higher education institutions; In the higher education institutions granted financial independence, the fees for the 2024/2025 academic year are equal to the payment-contract amounts that they have determined (without scholarships) for the 2024/2025 academic year. super-contract fees were set for applicants who did not reach the limit of admission quotas.
