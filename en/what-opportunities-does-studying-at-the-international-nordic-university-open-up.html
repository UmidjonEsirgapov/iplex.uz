What does the university offer you?

  1. International education is a double degree system. Students studying in the Nordics study in the 2+2 or 3+1 format in cooperation with universities in Europe, the USA and Korea, and two countries. has the opportunity to get a diploma.
  2. Work & Travel experience. University students have the opportunity to work abroad during the summer internship. Through this, the student not only gains language and experience, but also earns an average of 3000-5000 US dollars.
