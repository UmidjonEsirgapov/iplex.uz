Important announcement for applicants: Registration from June 5-25

Only subject complex, language of instruction and test area will be selected

Applicants who want to take part in the 2025 tests from June 5 to June 25 (including (even on June 25) must register. When registering, applicants choose only the following:

  • The complex of subjects for which the test will be taken

  • The language of instruction

  • The region of taking the test

Special order for professional (creative) test takers

If if there is a professional or creative exam in the field chosen by the applicant, then he can choose any state higher education institution and pass this exam. For example:

If you want to enter the University of Journalism and Mass Communications of Uzbekistan, you can take the creative exam in Bukhara, Andijan or another region.

Special convenience for Sokh and Denov districts

  • Test takers from Sokh district do not need to go to Fergana city.

  • Applicants from Denov and nearby areas will be exempted from going to Termiz city.

Separate testing places have been allocated for these districts.

Payment procedure: For those who graduated in previous years

Applicants who graduated from school in previous years will pay 187,500 soums to participate in the test.

  • Payment must be made by June 25!

  • If the payment is late - it will not be included in the test!

Opportunity for those who change their mind

If the applicant wants to change his choice, until June 25 Has the right to re-apply. No changes will be made after June 25.

Exam date

Tests are scheduled to be held in July-August. Exact dates will be announced by the State Testing Center.

Summary

June 5-25 are important dates for applicants. During this period, it is necessary to make a choice for registration, subject complex, language of education, test area and creative exams.
