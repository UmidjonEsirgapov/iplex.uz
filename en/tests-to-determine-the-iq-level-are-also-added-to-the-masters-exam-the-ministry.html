Tests to determine the IQ level are also added to the master's exam - the ministry

Brief content:
Due to the strengthening of quarantine measures and the spread of the coronavirus in Uzbekistan, the entrance exams to higher education institutions are delayed. Press secretary Farhod Babashev gave information about the procedure for conducting master's exams.

Due to the strengthening of quarantine measures and the spread of the coronavirus in Uzbekistan, the entrance exams to higher education institutions are delayed. This situation does not exclude the entrance exams for the master's degree.

The press secretary of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education Farhod Babashev gave information about the procedure for conducting the master's degree exams. But he could not say anything about the exam dates.

"Entrance exams will be in the form of a test in a specialty subject and a foreign language. In addition to the specialty subject studied at the undergraduate level, the specialty subject exam will also include tests aimed at determining the scientific and pedagogical ability and IQ level. The entrance exams were planned to begin on August 3. As you all know, the pandemic is making changes to our plans. therefore, the time of admission exams for the master's degree is determined by the State Commission," said Babashev. — Official Data
