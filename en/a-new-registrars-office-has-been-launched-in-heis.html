A new "Registrar's office" has been launched in HEIs

According to the relevant decision of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, higher The activity of the "Registrar's Office" has been launched in the education system starting from the 2024-2025 academic year. This new initiative is aimed at more effective management of the educational process in higher education institutions and improvement of student services.

The functional tasks of the Registrar's Office are carried out by the following departments: academic department, deanships, accounting, marketing and practice department, scientific department, Department of International Relations. These departments transfer their functions to the registrar's office.

Works carried out in the registrar's office:

  • Storage of personal documents of students to the archive
  • Formation and regular updating of the registrar's database
  • Maintenance of the transcript and inclusion of students' grades in it
  • Graduates' diplomas registration and presentation
  • Keeping statistics on the student contingent

It is also planned to fill the "Registrar's office" with qualified employees. Among these employees, priority is given to persons who have graduated from a foreign higher education institution or have experience in foreign higher education institutions.

In addition, in cooperation with the Center for the Development of Higher Education, it is planned to organize seminars and trainings and economic studies for the employees of the "Registrar's Office". will also be accepted at this registrar's office.
