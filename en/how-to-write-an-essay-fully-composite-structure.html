Correct compositional device of essay writing

It is important to maintain a clear and logical structure when writing an essay. Below is a standard composition device for writing an essay.

1. Introduction

  • General sentence - introduction to the topic and creation of context.
  • Paraphrasing the text of the assignment - expressing the main question or problem in your own words.
  • Thesis - the main idea presented during the essay.

2. Main part

The main part of the essay consists of three parts, each part provides specific arguments and examples.

1st-party point of view

In this part, first-party representatives give their opinion on the problem.

  • First, ...
  • Secondly, ...
  • Thirdly, ...
  • For example, ...

2nd side view

In this part, opposing parties present their arguments.

  • First, ...
  • Besides, ...
  • Moreover, ...
  • Including, ...

Personal Opinion

The essay writer expresses his personal point of view.

  • In my opinion, ...
  • Because, ...

3. Conclusion

  • Summary of the main ideas - express written arguments in a final form.
  • Propose a solution to the problem - express your solutions and recommendations on the topic.

The essay written on the basis of this structure is logical, coherent and understandable, which increases the possibility of high evaluation. — Source
