New school etiquette 2026: Basic requirements for students

Republic of Uzbekistan Preschool and according to the latest order of the Ministry of School Education, new model rules on the behavior of students in general secondary educational institutions and their behavior on the school territory were approved. These rules serve to ensure discipline and a safe environment in the educational institution.

Dress and preparation requirements in the school building and area

According to the new regulations, the appearance and equipment of students reflects their responsibility for education.

Clothes and personal hygiene

Students must come to school 10-15 minutes before the start of classes, in a special uniform specified in the charter of the institution. In this case, it was strictly determined that the clothes should be clean and neat, and the hair should be kept in order.

Choosing educational materials and bags

The use of a bag suitable for the age and physical condition of the student is required to fit the textbooks and educational materials necessary for all subjects in the lesson schedule.

Maintaining school property and cleanliness

Maintaining the infrastructure of the educational institution is the duty of every student. The following are specially emphasized in the new rules:

  • Maintenance of cleanliness: All rooms, stairs and corridors, sports and activity halls should always be kept clean, waste should be thrown only in specially marked containers.

  • No damage to property: It is strictly forbidden to damage school equipment (desks, tables, chairs, blackboards), computer equipment, laboratory equipment, and flora and fauna on the school grounds.

Norms of interaction and social etiquette

In order to improve the moral and moral environment, new requirements have been introduced for relations between students.

Fight against violence and bullying

Students should be protected from any kind of violence, among other things, they must refrain from fights, insults, threats and ridicule. Putting an end to cyberbullying (harassment on the Internet), which is especially a modern problem, is an important part of the new rules.

Respect for teachers and guests

Respect for adults and respect for minors in an educational institution. principle should be given priority. Saying hello when meeting teachers and guests, maintaining peace in the corridors is the main sign of a cultured student.
