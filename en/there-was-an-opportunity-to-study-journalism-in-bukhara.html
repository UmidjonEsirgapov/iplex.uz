There was an opportunity to study journalism in Bukhara

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Those who want to become specialists in the field of journalism had the opportunity to study at the Bukhara State University.
According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, new directions at the Bukhara State University - "Print mass media" and "Public relations" baccalaureate programs were opened.
Acceptance of 25 students is planned for each course (5 grants, 20 contracts). According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, new directions - "Print media" and "Public relations" bachelor's degrees were opened at Bukhara State University. It is planned to admit 25 students in each direction (5 grants, 20 contracts).

Studies are organized on the basis of curricula and science programs created by scientists of the University of Journalism and Mass Communications of Uzbekistan, which is considered the leading higher education institution in the country in the field of journalism. In accordance with the requirements of the journalism profession, special attention is paid to journalistic practice during studies. That is, during the entire semester, students are required to participate in the work of regional, city, district newspapers, radio and television, and press centers, and provide their performances. well informed. Today, the same tradition can be continued by training specialists in the field of journalism.
