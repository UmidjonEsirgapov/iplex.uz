2024: Interest-free education loan scheme for women

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Decision No. PQ-323 dated July 18, 2022 was amended by the Decree of the President (No. PF-82 dated 27.05.2024) on the procedure for allocating interest-free educational loans for women.
according to which, now only women studying in the form of full-time education are granted preferential interest-free educational loans.
Who will be given the loan?

Procedure of allocating interest-free educational loans for women

Presidential Decree (No. PF-82 dated 27.05.2024) Decision No. PQ-323 of July 18, 2022 was amended. According to it, preferential interest-free education loans are given to women who are currently studying only in the form of full-time education.

To whom is the loan granted?

  • Students of all higher education institutions in Uzbekistan (state, private and foreign higher education institutions branches);
  • Secondary special and professional education institutions's students (technical school, vocational college, etc.).

Educational loans are given only for full-time education!

Why is it called interest-free? But the women do not pay this interest, the state pays.

When does the payment start?

The main part of the education loan is returned for 7 years starting from the seventh month (i.e. six months) after the end of the student's official study period.

What documents are needed?

  • Application to the bank;
  • Identity document;
  • Payment-contract agreement with the educational institution;
  • A document on the provision of repayment of the education loan by the student's family members who have a permanent source of income and another third party as a joint borrower.

Women of families included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection" collaterals and guarantees are not required for unsecured education loans. — Read on infoedu.uz
