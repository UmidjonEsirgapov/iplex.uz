Applications are being accepted for the Friedrich Ebert Foundation: Scholarship for International Students (FES) scholarships provided within the framework of the DAAD grant program to study in Germany. These grants are awarded to foreign students to study and conduct research in Germany at all levels. Scholarships are awarded for all fields except medicine.

For those who wish to study in bachelor, research, master and PhD programs

Before applying for the scholarship program, submit an application to one of the German public universities or a state-recognized higher education institution. You can submit an application for the FES grant after you receive the notification that you have been accepted to study.

Although applications for the FES grant program are accepted until November 30, 2020, the dates of admission to courses in German higher education institutions may vary, and admission to some HEIs may close earlier.
