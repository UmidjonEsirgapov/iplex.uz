In fact, any exam is full of emotions and exciting. Gulmira Boyuzakova, a teacher of the Tashkent State Pedagogical University, candidate of psychology, shared recommendations on how to curb excitement during the exam, which is important for the applicant (source: kun.uz). even if mastered, exam time excitement can become an enemy. — Read on infoedu.uz

Concentration

  • After completing all the formalities like filling the title sheet, try to concentrate and forget about your surroundings. Only the text of the tasks and the hours should be available to you.
  • Before entering the answer, read the question twice and make sure you understand what is being asked of you.

Starting easy

  • Start solving the test by answering the questions that do not leave you in doubt. Don't dwell on long-winded questions. Then you will be much calmer, the burden will be lightened. The brain begins to work more clearly.

Do not rush and read the task to the end

  • Do not rush to understand the condition of the task from the first words. This increases the chances of making mistakes even on the easiest questions.

Think only about the current task

  • Forget everything before when studying for a new task. The tasks in the tests are not related to each other, so the rules and formulas you used before will prevent you from solving the new task correctly. Treat a new question as a new opportunity.

Eliminate

  • If you don't know the right answer, successively eliminate the answers that you are sure are wrong. This narrows the choice between options.

Review

  • Take the time to review the tasks you have completed, at least take time to glance and see mistakes.

Guess

  • If you are not sure of the exact answer, choose an option intuitively. Sometimes you have to trust your intuition.
