Who can apply for secondary and further higher education?

According to the Government Decree (No. 606, 24.09.2021) on the procedure for obtaining secondary and further education in institutions of higher education: persons with a bachelor's degree (diploma specialist) with higher education they can receive second and subsequent higher education in the fields of bachelor's education.

Admission parameters for second and subsequent higher education are determined by the Higher Education Council.

Applicants register for admission through a special electronic system developed by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education every year from July 10 to August 10 (the same day).

Admission procedure (entrance exam form, evaluation criteria, exam dates) is published on the official website of Higher Education Institution by July 1 every year.

Admission results are announced by Higher Education Institution by August 30 every year. In this case, the length of study is determined to be one year less than the period specified for the relevant undergraduate education.

The regulation does not apply to higher military educational institutions. — Read on infoedu.uz
