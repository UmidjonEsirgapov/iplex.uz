Methodical days of teachers are introduced as "Professional development days".

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:

Starting from January 1, 2025, instead of methodological days of teachers in general secondary education institutions, "Professional development days" and "Professional development" hours" is put into practice. This initiative is being introduced in order to improve the quality of education and direct teachers to continuous professional development.

Main changes

  • Professional development hour: Introduced in all general secondary education institutions.
  • Professional development day: Organized in elementary schools.
  • Appointment of mentor-teachers: Every qualified teachers of the same subject are designated as mentors.
  • Additional incentives: Mentor-teachers are paid an additional 20% bonus compared to the position salary.

Goal

  • To improve the quality of education: To encourage the exchange of experience among teachers and the use of modern teaching methods.
  • Professional development: Regular improvement of teachers' professional skills.
  • Development of the mentoring system: Appointment of qualified pedagogues as an example to others and additional support for them.

Conclusion

Through "Professional development days" and "Professional development hours", the professional potential of teachers increases. This is one of the important steps not only for the teachers themselves, but also for improving the quality of the general education system. — More information
