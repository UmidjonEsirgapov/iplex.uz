How are the results of the certificate calculated?

The certificate is issued for a period of 3 years to those who have scored at least 60% of the highest points in the test.

The results of the test will be published on the official website of the State Test Center no later than twenty working days from the date of their completion.

Applicants who have a certificate will be given a graded (proportional) score in relation to the maximum score in this subject in the tests for admission to the bachelor's degree of a higher educational institution in accordance with the percentage of mastery (60 and above) in the certificate.

That is, the subject in which the certificate was obtained

For example, if an applicant with a certificate in mathematics has 60 percent mastery in the certificate, and the place of mathematics in the subject complex is the first (3.2 points for each correct answer, a maximum of 96 points for 30 test tasks), then 60 percent is 57.6 points. is (3.2*30=96 points, 96*60/100=57.6). If the applicant's mastery of the certificate is 80 percent, it is 76.8 points (3.2*30=96 points, 96*80/100=76.8).

The applicant who has obtained a positive result (60 percent and above) from the test will be given an electronic form within a week from the day of the announcement of the test results. The applicant can download the certificate through his personal cabinet.

How can I register to participate in the test for the purpose of obtaining a certificate?

The applicant can register to participate in the test for the purpose of obtaining a certificate by submitting an electronic application through the "Registration system of applicants' data and applications" (my.dtm.uz site).

How do I register? May I know?

After entering the necessary documents for registration, a personal cabinet will be created for the applicant on the official website of the State Test Center.

After making the payment, the applicant is registered and a public offer contract is concluded with the State Test Center.

How is the payment for registration made?

The payment is made by the applicant. in the amount of 1.5 times of the calculated amount, it will be carried out within three days from the date of submission of the application through current electronic payment systems. The applicant can find out the information about the payment through his personal office.

How can I find out the place and date of the test?

The applicant can get the information about the date and place of the test through his personal office.

How can I know the results of the test?

The results of the tests are completed. It will be announced on the official website of the State Test Center no later than 20 (twenty) working days from the date of the test.

How many points can I get a certificate?

To get a certificate, it is necessary to get at least 60% of the highest points on the test result.
