Director of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency Majid Karimov announced at the next session of the International Press Club that this year's entrance exams to higher education institutions:

They will be held in two shifts in closed buildings equipped with a cooling system.

Each shift About 45,000 applicants will take the exam.

Exams will be broadcast online via the Internet and television.

Identification systems, in particular, fingerprint and FaceControl (face recognition), will prevent unauthorized access.

The results of the applicants who passed the test will be sent to the personal cabinet by the evening of the next day of the exam.

The final mandate it will be announced within a week after the end of the exams.
