2022 International Wildbird Photo Competition - Photo competition for bird photos organized by the Bird and Nature Festival. This competition will be held online and all candidates who are interested in photography can participate. Photos are accepted in 6 directions:

  • Bird portrait - portrait of birds
  • Bursting colors - brightly colored birds
  • Bird in action - flying birds
  • Birds social relationships - the relationship of birds with people
  • Artistic vision of bird - drawing of birds appearance
  • Young talent (for 15-17 years-old) - youth talents

Awards:

  • Grand-Prix award -€1500
  • 1st Prize in each category - €800
  • Nomination "Young photographers" - €500 and other additional prizes are available. DETAILS

IMPORTANT All winners will attend the awards ceremony in France and receive full travel expenses.

Files will be submitted as follows:
• JPEG format
• borderless, framed or signed
• 3:2 aspect ratio
• exactly 3500 pixels on the longest side (should be about 2.333 pixels on the shortest side)
