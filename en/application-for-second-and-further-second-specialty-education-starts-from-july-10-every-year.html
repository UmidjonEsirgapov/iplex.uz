Application for second and further (second specialty) education starts from July 10 every year

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:

approved the regulation on the procedure for obtaining second and further education in higher education institutions.
Admission results until August 30 every year. It will be announced by the Higher Education Institution.

Government Resolution (No. 606, 24.09.2021) approved the regulation on the procedure for obtaining second and further education in higher education institutions. According to this regulation, persons with a bachelor's degree (diploma specialist) with higher education can receive second and subsequent higher education in the fields of bachelor's education.

Admission procedure:

  • The form of the entrance exam, evaluation criteria and exam dates are announced annually on the official website of the higher education institution (HEI) by July 1.
  • Admission results are announced by the HEI by August 30 of each year.

Study conditions and duration

  • Educational stage: Studying in the second and subsequent higher education starts from the 2nd year.
  • Duration: The duration of study is determined to be one year less than the period established for the relevant bachelor's education courses.

Special conditions

  • Application: Higher regulations does not apply to military educational institutions.
  • Admission parameters: Admission parameters for the second and subsequent higher education are determined by the Higher Education Council.
  • Information about quotas and exams: You can find out full information about admission quotas and exams through the higher education institution you want to study.

The opportunity to receive secondary and subsequent higher education expands the opportunity for students to acquire additional knowledge and skills in their fields.
