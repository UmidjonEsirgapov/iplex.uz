Now admission to academic lyceums is carried out through tests conducted by DTM

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, special attention will be paid to identifying talented young people, selecting them for academic lyceums, teaching them based on in-depth educational programs. highly qualified pedagogues will be involved in the classes and the necessary conditions will be created for it.
Starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, the registration of applicants to academic lyceums will be carried out through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal and the website of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education. the presidential decision "on improvement measures" was announced.

According to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, special attention will be paid to identifying talented young people, selecting them for academic lyceums, and teaching them based on in-depth educational programs.

Also, in academic lyceums, hours of in-depth subjects will be increased at the expense of hours of general education subjects, and their share will be at least 50%.

In order to ensure the continuity of educational stages, the subjects taught repeatedly in similar educational directions of HEIs will be unified with the relevant subjects in the curriculum of academic lyceums.

Unified subjects during the period of study in academic lyceums mastering these subjects is optional for students who have mastered it and are accepted as students in higher educational institutions.

Starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, registration of applicants to academic lyceums will be carried out through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal and the website of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

Starting from the next academic year, academic lyceums in the selection of talented young people, the State Testing Center conducts tests on 2 subjects suitable for the chosen direction and tests that determine the level of intellectual ability (IQ). — Official Data
