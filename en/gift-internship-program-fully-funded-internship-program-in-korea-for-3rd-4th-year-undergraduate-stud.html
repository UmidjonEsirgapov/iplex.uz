GIFT Internship program: Fully funded internship program in Korea for 3rd-4th year undergraduate students

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
General Information: Graduate Institute of Ferrous & Energy Materials Technology GIFT: This grant is one of the fully funded programs, and all 3rd and 4th year students who are interested in GIFT laboratory field and ferrous metallurgy technology can apply for this program.
This internship program lasts 4 weeks at Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH).
Program benefits: Free Accommodation is provided; Transportation costs are fully covered; Travel expenses for foreign candidates are also fully covered; A monthly stipend is also allocated for daily expenses (500,000 KRW ~ 420$); List of required documents: Application form; Your university grades must be in English (including GPA); Personal statement; Language certificate evaluating your knowledge of the English language (IELTS or TOEFL): Important dates: Twice a year: Summer admission is held until the second Friday of May and winter admission is held until the last Friday of November; The results of the summer admission will be announced in the 1st week of June and the results of the winter admission will be announced in the last week of December; Note: You are requested to send all the above-mentioned documents to this email enmei@postech.ac.kr.

General Information:

Graduate Institute of Ferrous & Energy Materials Technology GIFT: This grant is one of the fully funded programs, and for this program, all 3rd and 4th year students interested in GIFT laboratory field and ferrous metallurgy technology can apply. This internship program lasts for 4 weeks at Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH).

Program benefits:

  • Free accommodation is provided;
  • Transportation expenses are fully covered;
  • Travel expenses for foreign candidates are also fully covered;
  • A monthly stipend is provided for daily expenses. (500,000 KRW ~ 420$);

List of Required Documents:

  • Application form;
  • Your university grades must be in English (GPA must also be shown);
  • Personal statement;
  • Language assessing your English language proficiency certificate (IELTS or TOEFL):

Important dates:

  • Twice a year: Summer admission is held until the second Friday of May and winter admission is held until the last Friday of November; the results of the admission will be announced in the last week of December;

Note: You are requested to send all the above-mentioned documents to this email enmei@postech.ac.kr . — More information
