Academy of Nurses will be established in Uzbekistan

Useful information for entrants and students:

Summary:
On the order of the head of our state, in order to fundamentally improve the system of training, upgrading and retraining of medical personnel, the Academy of Nursing will be established in Tashkent in cooperation with the Turkish University of Health Sciences. during the previous dialogue, an agreement was reached on this.
Training of nurses in the academy, which will start its activities from the new academic year, will be carried out by professors and teachers of the University of Health Sciences based on international educational standards based on a 4-year curriculum. in order to fundamentally improve, a Nursing Academy will be established in Tashkent in cooperation with the University of Health Sciences of Turkey. src="https://static.oliygoh.uz/uploads/4/1100__CWWHcEumNijZyVR4FZZvWNsChuv9-jFC.png"/> According to it, two years of education will be conducted in Uzbekistan and two years in Turkey. Also, in the dialogue between the Ministry of Health of Uzbekistan and the leadership of the University of Health Sciences of Turkey, a 4-year joint program will be launched at the bachelor's level in the educational direction "Medical Management" in order to train leading personnel for the medical system of our country. In this project, students will study in Turkey for two years.
