An International Agricultural University will be established in Uzbekistan

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
This was announced on April 27, 2022 in the presence of President Shavkat Mirziyoyev at a meeting on the issues of further development of education in the agricultural sector and the progress of projects in the field of agriculture in Andijan, Jizzakh, Namangan, Samarkand, It was indicated that agricultural service centers should be established in Fergana and Khorezm regions, and more than 100 services should be established.
Also, the President noted the need for the new International Agricultural University to start its activities from the 2022-2023 academic year. 200 students will be trained.
