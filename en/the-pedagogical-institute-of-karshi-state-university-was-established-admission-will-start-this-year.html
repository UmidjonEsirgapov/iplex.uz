The Pedagogical Institute of Karshi State University was established. Admission will start this year

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the decision, the Pedagogical Institute of Karshi State University was established on the basis of bachelor's degrees and master's specialties of the field of pedagogical education of Tashkent State Pedagogical University and the Shahrisabz branch of Tashkent State Pedagogical University.
Education and teaching methods in the field of pedagogy, the training of professional highly educated personnel with the skills to use modern pedagogical technologies in the educational process and others were defined as the main tasks of the institute.
Starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, admissions to undergraduate educational courses in the field of pedagogy and related master's degrees in the field of pedagogic education in the opposite state university will be suspended, and this undergraduate educational course and related students studying for master's degrees will continue their education at the university until they graduate.

Pedagogical Institute of Karshi State University was established. Admission will start this year

✅ Government decision "On measures to organize the activities of the Pedagogical Institute of the opposite state university" was adopted. In accordance with the decision, the Pedagogical Institute of Karshi State University was established on the basis of the undergraduate courses and master's degrees of the Shahrisabz branch of Tashkent State Pedagogical University and Karshi State University in the field of pedagogical education.

Students studying in the relevant master's majors will continue their education at the university until they graduate.

The number of admissions of students to the institute for bachelor's degrees and master's majors is determined based on admission parameters to the higher education institutions of the republic, which are approved in the prescribed manner every year.
