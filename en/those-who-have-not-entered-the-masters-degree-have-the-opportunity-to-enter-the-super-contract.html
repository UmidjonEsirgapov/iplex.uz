Those who have not entered the master's degree have the opportunity to enter the super-contract

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the relevant decision of the State Commission, in the current academic year, only those who have S1, V2 national or equivalent international certificate for master's degree in state higher education institutions and submitted application, but did not become a student within the quota, did not exceed 1.5 times the amount of the basic fee-contract the opportunity to study at the master's degree was created on the basis of payment.
This was reported by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.
Those who expressed their desire to study can apply to the admission commissions of higher education institutions.
