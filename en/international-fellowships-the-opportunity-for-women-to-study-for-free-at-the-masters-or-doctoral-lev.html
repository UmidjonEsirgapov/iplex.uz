International Fellowships Established in 1917, the program is a full-time master or doctoral study in the US. Aims to support women who are not US citizens or permanent residents, and intended to return to their country of origin.

Benefits:

For Master's/early professional students $18,000
For Ph.D. $20,000
For postdocs: $30,000

Additionally, those accepted into the program will receive the following benefits:

  • All educational expenses will be covered
  • Living expenses will be fully funded
