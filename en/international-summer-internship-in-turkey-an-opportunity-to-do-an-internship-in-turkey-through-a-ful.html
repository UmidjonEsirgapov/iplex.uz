General information:

International Local Authorities Internship Program: is an international internship program organized annually by the Union of Municipalities of Turkey since 2012 to provide participants with training in local government through instruction, practical examples and site visits, including social events and trips to various cities in Turkey.

LOCALINTERNational has been providing an international learning environment for a wide range of participants from around the world, with a particular focus on European, Asian and Middle Eastern countries, to explore, interact and see best practices in municipal practice. The main goal of this internship program is to train young people as future leaders and introduce them to international experience. Including, all expenses such as international travel expenses, food, accommodation will be fully financed. The training program will take place between August 21-27, 2022.

During the program, you will gain knowledge and skills in the following areas:

· Basic values of local government bodies;

· The importance of local democracy and participation;

· Socio-cultural aspects of local government bodies activities;

· Global and regional organizations of local governments;

· Local governments and sustainable development goals;

· Local authorities in Turkey;

· CV preparation and interview techniques;

Requirements for candidates:

  • Good knowledge of English (IELTS/TOEFL certificates are not required);
  • This 2018 graduates or 1st-year master's degree students;
  • Also, students studying at the last stage of the bachelor's degree or 1st-year master's degree students in the following fields:

- Law;

- Economy;

- Urban/regional planning;

- Sociology;

- Statistics;

- Communication;

- Political Science;

- International Relations;

- Administrative Sciences;

- other similar fields;
