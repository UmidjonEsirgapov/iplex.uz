Weinstein JAMS International Fellowship: $20,000 scholarship for an internship in the United States! provides an opportunity to study US dispute resolution processes and practices to assist in dispute resolution.

The JAMS Foundation provides scholarships of up to $20,000 to support projects nominated by scholarship applicants. This program is designed for candidates who have experience and dedication to the field of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) and who are seeking training, development and access to services in their home country and beyond.
