Work as a waiter

At first glance, the best and most interesting option. Customer service isn't hard work, you get to talk to interesting people, and they can tip you. Only here, there are a couple of disadvantages that you should know in advance:

  • This option is more for part-time students (because you have to work during the day). Yes, you can get a job at a night club to work after school, but when do you get time off? You can get a job for such jobs, for a certain time, by agreement. And you come to work at the agreed time, you leave at the agreed time.
  • To work in a good club or in a good restaurant, you need experience. If you don't have such experience, you should look for a more casual restaurant.

It can be said that the advantage is the work space and the possibility to work on a flexible schedule (where you work according to your schedule). When many people come to the cafe, there is no time to sit down. In such conditions, after a few hours your legs will start to get very tired. In some cafes and restaurants, you can even forget about the tip. even if they give very little, people are ashamed to take it.

There may be various clients, they may leave without paying for what they ate and drank, as if they were drunk, they may complain about you, in such cases, they may hang the entire account books of the client on your neck, etc. So, in any case, you can earn 30,000-100,000 soums a day on average. Maybe even more. You can calculate how much it will be per month and how much money you need for your needs.
