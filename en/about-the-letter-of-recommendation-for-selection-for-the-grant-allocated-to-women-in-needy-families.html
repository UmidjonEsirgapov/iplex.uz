About the letter of recommendation for selection for the grant allocated to women in needy families

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Women from needy families who wish to receive higher education on the basis of a grant are recommended for participation in the competition based on the following criteria: women from low-income families. means a family with a monthly average total income of no more than 0.527 times (516 thousand soums) of the minimum wage (980,000 soums) during the period; girls in need of social protection who are brought up in single-parent families, i.e., whose father or mother or both of them have died; women in need of social protection whose husband has died and who are raising their minor child alone; women in needy families with children with disabilities; daughters of single women in need of social protection who are raising two or more children and living separately (on rent) from other relatives; women from needy families where one or both of their parents have first or second group disabilities.
The recommendation is given to all applicants from needy families who meet the above criteria.

For women from needy families who want to receive higher education on the basis of a grant, the recommendation is given based on the following criteria:

low women from wealthy families. In this case, a low-income family is defined as a family with an average monthly income of no more than 0.527 times (516 thousand soums) of the minimum wage (980,000 soums) for each member of the family during the period when the total income is determined; girls in need of social protection;

women in need of social protection whose spouse has died, who are raising a minor child alone;

women in needy families with children with disabilities;

daughters of single women in need of social protection who are raising two or more children, living separately from other relatives (on rent);

one or both of their parents women from needy families with disabilities of the first or second group.

The recommendation is given to all applicant women from needy families who meet the above criteria.

To receive a recommendation, women from needy families apply electronically to the district (city) family and women's departments of their place of permanent residence or temporary residence.
