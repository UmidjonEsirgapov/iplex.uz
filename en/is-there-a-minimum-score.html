Is there a minimum score?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
That is, applicants have to fight not only with others through their knowledge, but first of all with themselves. will be.

A minimum score limit is set in the tests every year. That is, applicants must fight not only with others, but also with themselves through their knowledge. If the applicant does not get the minimum score, even if there is a place in the quota, he will not be accepted as a student.

This year, the minimum requirement is the same as in previous years:

For a payment contract - 56.7 points;

For a state grant - 68 points.

What does this mean?
