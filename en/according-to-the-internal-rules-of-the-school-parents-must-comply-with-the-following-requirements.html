According to the internal rules of the school, parents must comply with the following requirements

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the order of the Minister of Public Education No. 246 dated July 28, 2022, the following requirements are set for parents. - to respect the honor and dignity of the teacher, not to interfere in the lesson process, not to insult the pedagogues; - to control the child's compliance with the requirements of the internal order and rules of etiquette of the educational institution during the lesson; - to ensure the child's full participation in classes (attendance) and workbooks for subjects and all other equipment necessary for education; - to ensure that the child comes to the institution with textbooks and educational materials; - not to hinder your child's education, to arrive at the educational institution on time, to meaningfully organize your child's free time after school; - to communicate with the head of the class and subject teachers regarding the child's behavior and attendance; - informs the educational institution (head of the class) when his child may not come to classes, stating the reasons; - participation in parents' meetings, discussions and various spiritual and educational events held in the institution based on the proposal of the educational institution.
