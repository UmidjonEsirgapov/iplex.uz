There are 41 types of privileges for admission

2022 of the State Admission Commission Based on statement No. 10 of December 29, the official list of privileges for entering higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan was approved. Privileges are granted depending on applicant's social status, professional achievements or belonging to a special category under the law.

What are the types of privileges?

1. Admission on the basis of an additional state grant without examination (15 benefits) For example:

  • Children of those killed in military service

  • Winners of the International Olympiad

  • Winners of the "Zulfia", "Nihol", "Brave Boy" awards

2. Acceptance on the basis of basic fee-contract without examination (4 privileges) For example:

  • Graduates of presidential schools, specialized schools

  • Successfully graduated technical school

3. Exemption from the exam in a certain subject (5 privileges) For example:

  • Maximum points for those who have a foreign language certificate

  • Winners of the science Olympiads of the Republic

4. Benefits based on additional points (2 benefits) For example:

  • Graduates of "Temurbek School"

  • Graduates of Military Music Academic Lyceum

5. Participation in the competition within the additional quota (12 privileges) For example:

  • Applicants with disabilities

  • Real orphans

  • Children of military, internal affairs, customs, guards personnel

6. Other additional benefits (3 benefits) For example:

  • Winners of the intellectual game "Zakovvat"

  • Graduates of the Technical College of Justice
