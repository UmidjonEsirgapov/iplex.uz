Queensland University of Technology, located in Australia, offers a grant program to foreign students who want to study in the field of education. This university is one of the most prestigious universities located in Brisbane, Australia.

The Faculty of Education offers scholarships of $5,000 per semester to foreign students enrolled in one of the master's (coursework) programs. The scholarship is available for 1 student enrolled in the first semester and 1 student enrolled in the second semester.

The fee varies for different programs, so for some programs the scholarship is enough to cover the full tuition fee, while for other programs the scholarship may cover only a part.

Requirements:

  • Must not be an Australian citizen;
  • Must be accepted into one of the Master's (coursework) programs of the Faculty of Education;
  • Must begin studies in the next semester;
  • Must have excellent grades, excellent command of the English language.

Required documents:

  • Bachelor's degree from previous institution and grades;
  • Motivation letter;
  • CV;
  • Recommendation

These documents should be sent to edu.development@qut.edu.au by February 15, 2022.
