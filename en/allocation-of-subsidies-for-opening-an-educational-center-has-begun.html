Allocation of subsidies for opening an educational center has begun

In September of last year, by the decision of the government, the procedure for allocating subsidies to non-state educational organizations teaching foreign languages in villages was approved.

This service can be funded through the Single portal. Applications must be sent by the 10th of the first month of each quarter.

Applications for subsidies for opening an educational center are still being accepted.

This type of subsidy is allocated for the following purposes:

  • equipment of buildings and classrooms with modern information and communication technologies, furniture, equipment, technical means of teaching and other equipment;
  • necessary. to provide textbooks, teaching-methodical literature and other library-informational resources and tools, to pay for the work of pedagogues involved in the organization of the educational process.

For this, the following documents are required:

  • a business plan for 2 years;
  • contracts concluded on the supply of products or the performance of works, services;
  • labor contracts concluded with teaching staff;
  • certificates of teaching staff confirming the level of knowledge of a foreign language;
  • lease agreement of buildings or classrooms used in the educational process (for non-governmental educational organizations operating on the basis of real estate lease).
