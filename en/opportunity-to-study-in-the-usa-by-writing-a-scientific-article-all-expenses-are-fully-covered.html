Human Rights Essay Award is an annual competition founded by the American University Washington College of Law aimed at encouraging scientific work on international human rights law.

The competition is open to English and Scientific articles written in Spanish are accepted.

The article must be related to the following topic: CLIMATE CHANGE AND HUMAN RIGHTS: IMPACTS, RESPONSIBILITIES, AND OPPORTUNITIES

The owners of the 2 best written articles will receive the following benefits:

  • 3 organized by the Academy can study for free at the weekly Program of Advanced Studies on Human Rights and Humanitarian Law;
  • Travel expenses to Washington will be covered;
  • Accommodation will be provided in a university dormitory;
  • Financial support will be provided for living expenses;
  • The best scientific articles may also be published in American University International Reviews;
  • A diploma or certificate will be awarded at the end of the program.

Requirements:

  • All lawyers, regardless of nationality, can participate in the program;
  • The candidate must have a bachelor's degree in the field of law;
  • Must have a strong interest in the field of international human rights;
  • The scholarly article must cover the topic extensively;
  • First in this program the winners will not be able to participate in this year's competition

Applications for the program will be accepted from December 1.

The deadline for receiving applications: January 31, 2022.
