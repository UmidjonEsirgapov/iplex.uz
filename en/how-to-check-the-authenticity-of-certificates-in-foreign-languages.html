How to Check the Authenticity of Certificates in Foreign Languages?

Certificates assessing the level of foreign language skills often play a major role in salary and promotion. However, it is important to know how to check their authenticity. In this article, we will consider how to check IELTS, TOEFL, APTIS and other international and national certificates.

1. IELTS Certificate Verification

IELTS certificate can be verified through the following addresses:

  • Official Site: IELTS TRF Verification. This service is only available for official organizations.
  • IDP Certificates: If your IELTS certificate is issued by IDP, you can check it by IDP IELTS result checker.

2. National Certificate (Multi-level) Verification

The following methods are available to verify national certificates issued by the Education Assessment Agency:

  • Official Site: National Certificate Verification. The authenticity of the certificate can also be determined by scanning the QR code on the certificate.
  • Mobile Applications: Certificates uploaded via app.uzbmb.uz or app.dtm.uz are also valid.

3. Checking the APTIS Certificate

There are two ways to check the APTIS certificate:

  • Email: by sending an official letter to the British Council's email address info@britishcouncil.uz with the certificate holder's name, date of receipt and a special code.
  • Official Website: If the APTIS certificate is issued by the ASSESSMENT CENTER if issued, you can check it through the APTIS certificate verification site.

4. Checking the LINGUASKILL Certificate

Important points when checking the LINGUASKILL certificate:

  • Certificates should be issued only by examination centers recognized in Uzbekistan (with codes UZ050, UZ026, UZ004, UZ059).
  • You can confirm the validity of the certificate through the LINGUASKILL results verification site.

5. TOEFL IBT Certificate Check

TOEFL IBT Certificate Check:

  • Official Site: TOEFL Results Check. It is done by registering an official organization and sending a request.

6. Verification of TOEFL ITP Certificate

Verification of TOEFL ITP certificate:

  • Email: can be verified by sending copies of the certificate to the e-mail address tests-security@etsglobal.org.

7. TESOL and TEFL Certification Review

TESOL and TEFL certifications are awarded by many organizations. To determine their authenticity:

  • Call Center of the Ministry: It is advisable to contact the 1006 call center and clarify the authenticity of the certificate.

8. iTEP Certificate Verification

iTEP certificate verification:

  • Official Site: iTEP certificate verification site.

Additional Verification Methods

The following methods can be used to identify fake certificates:

  • Check for errors in the design and fonts of the certificate.
  • Make sure that the QR code is directed to a trusted site.

Conclusion

It is important to use official sources and tools to check foreign language certificates. There are appropriate verification methods for each certificate, and by using them correctly, you can determine the authenticity of the certificate. — Source
