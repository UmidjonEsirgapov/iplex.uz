Who is a professional programmer?

A person who can embody the following characteristics can be called a professional programmer:

  • Knows a programming language perfectly;
  • Can find solutions to problems with little code;
  • One of the most modern programming methods uses;
  • Aware of programming and language news and trends;
  • Works on himself every day and does not fall behind the times;
  • Able to work in a team and help others;
  • During his career he has created several dozen programs;
  • Able to work with several problems at the same time;
  • Easy to use other programming languages in his field learns;
  • Can solve any problem through programming.

How many years can you learn?

In fact, programming is a profession that consists of constant research and learning. So, if you love this profession, start learning regardless of how many years it takes. The more you work, the higher your experience will be. As your knowledge increases, your programming skills will change from simple to complex.

Only you decide how many years it will take you to become a perfect and mature programmer. No one can tell you the exact time. It all depends on you. Work hard, practice logical thinking, try to gain more experience. In no time, you will become the perfect programmer you want!

Can I become a programmer?
