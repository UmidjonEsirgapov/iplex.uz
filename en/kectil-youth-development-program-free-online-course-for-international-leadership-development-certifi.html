Kectil Youth Leadership Programme 2022

The Kectil Youth Leadership Program offers the following opportunities for educated and active young people who are passionate about leadership:

  • Several weeks of modules led by experts and key youth leaders to address current issues facing young people around the world.
  • Fair government; keeping the peace; entrepreneurship and innovation; environment; the role of men and women in society; mental health; unemployment; early pregnancy and women's reproductive health; corruption in society; and more.
  • Opportunity to join community organizations for social progress;
  • Meet and interact with talented young people from all over the world;
  • Make lifelong global friends committed to advancing society
  • Not a political, religious or cultural organization; overcomes any uncertainty;
  • Certificates awarded at the end of the program.

Mode: online

Duration: 1 year

Benefits Aspects:

  • Participants are awarded after the training is completed
  • Reputable team opportunity to be a role model for leaders and their country.

Requirements:

  • 17 to 27 years old;
  • Citizen of developing and slow developing countries;
  • Having a passion for leadership;
  • Proficient in English.
