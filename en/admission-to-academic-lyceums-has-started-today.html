Admission to academic lyceums for the 2025/2026 academic year has begun

From June 10, the process of submitting documents to academic lyceums for graduates of the 9th grade of general education schools this year began online through the my.edu.uz portal. This process will continue until July 10.

Document submission procedure

Applicants must prepare the following documents during online registration and upload them to the system in electronic form (in PDF or JPEG format):

  • 1 color photograph measuring 3.5x4.5 cm;

  • Documents confirming the right to preferential access (if any if):

    • Science Olympiad winner diplomas;

    • National or international language and science certificates. View the list of benefits

Choice of field and subject

At the time of registration, applicants:

  • Can choose up to three academic lyceums (the language of instruction must be the same);

  • Which foreign language will determine the entrance test.

Tests

Applicants participate in entrance tests organized in the region of their choice. The tests will be conducted based on the direction and language chosen by the applicant.

Conclusion

The process of admission to academic lyceums for the 2025/2026 academic year has officially begun. Through online registration, applicants can easily and quickly complete the process of application, course and subject selection. Documents must be submitted by July 10. — Source
