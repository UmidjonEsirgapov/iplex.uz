Grants and the opportunity to study abroad are being created for children of poor families

On February 14, 2024, an open dialogue with young people was held in the Koksaroy residence with the participation of President Shavkat Mirziyoyev. It was noted that education is the most effective way to reduce poverty. For this purpose, the following concessions will be introduced in Uzbekistan for children from low-income families to receive higher education: An additional grant will be allocated to a part of the contract fee. is covered by the state

  • Interest-free education loan is offered

    • Through this approach, necessary conditions are created for at least one child in every low-income family to have a higher education.

    "Bright talent" project: Opportunity to study abroad

    As another important initiative, the "Bright Talent" project will be launched. This program:

    • Identify young people in need of social protection from the neighborhood

    • Evaluate their knowledge and talent

    • Choose a foreign university suitable for their competence

    • To study abroad through preparatory courses send

    Every year 1 thousand talented young people will have the opportunity to study and live in TOP-100 higher education institutions of the world. All their expenses will be covered by the state.

    Within the initiative, those who have studied abroad will be included in the National Personnel Reserve and they will be recruited for employment in state organizations.

    Today, more than 1,500 Uzbek students are studying at the world's leading universities. The President assessed this situation as "unprecedented reality in the history of Uzbekistan". And new projects serve to increase these numbers even more. — Official Data
