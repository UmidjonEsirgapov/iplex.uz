Second stage (in the form of a test-examination) Candidates participating in the competition will take an online exam in the form of a test on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform, and the candidates with the highest scores will be admitted to the final stage.

Exam type: 25 test questions on IQ (logical analysis)

Duration: The 2nd stage of the competition will start on September 27th at 20:00 and the exam window will be closed for everyone at 22:00. All candidates registered during this period are required to participate in the exam for 30 minutes.
