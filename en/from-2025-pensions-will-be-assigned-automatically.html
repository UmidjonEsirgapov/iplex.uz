Pensions are automatically assigned for mature women. In this process, it is not necessary to submit any documents, because the necessary information is obtained from the electronic databases of the relevant state agencies.

How does the process work?

All the information necessary for assigning pensions to citizens is obtained from the electronic databases of the Ministry of Justice, the Ministry of Employment, and the Tax Committee. This includes:

  • Citizen's age and gender
  • Work experience and salary information
  • Amount of social tax paid
  • Family composition (spouse and children)

What comfort does it provide to citizens?

  • It is not necessary to collect and submit documents
  • Pension is assigned automatically
  • It is not required to go to the pension fund
  • The citizen will be informed about the pension appointment via SMS
