From next year, 14 pedagogical courses will not be accepted for part-time education

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, admission to correspondence education in 14 educational areas of higher education institutions that train pedagogical staff will not be carried out.
Except for the 14 areas mentioned in this decision. Persons who have at least five years of pedagogical work experience in the education system are admitted to study in the field of pedagogy by correspondence education.
In this regard, the fact that persons who wish to study in the field of pedagogy by correspondence education in the form of correspondence education have at least five years of pedagogical work experience in the educational system is based on the information in the "Uniform National Labor System" interagency software and hardware complex.

Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, admission to correspondence education in 14 educational directions of higher education institutions training pedagogical personnel will not be carried out. This was determined in the President's decision on the development of higher education institutions that train pedagogues.

In addition to the 14 directions mentioned in this decision, persons with at least five years of experience in pedagogical activities in the educational system are admitted to study in the field of pedagogy by correspondence.

The fact that the persons who want to study in the form of part-time education have at least five years of pedagogical work experience in the educational system is determined based on the information in the interdepartmental software-hardware complex "Uniform National Labor System".
