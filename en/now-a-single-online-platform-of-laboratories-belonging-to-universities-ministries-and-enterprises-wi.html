Now a single online platform of laboratories belonging to universities, ministries and enterprises will be created

A new initiative was put forward by the head of our state to create favorable conditions for education and scientific research. Now a single online platform of laboratories belonging to universities, ministries and enterprises will be created. Through this platform, it will be possible to get complete information about what kind of equipment is available in which laboratory, what tests they can do and what capacity they work.

Advantages of a single online platform

  • Information about laboratories: Complete information about what equipment is available in which laboratory, what tests they can conduct and their capacity.
  • Scientific opportunities for research: The equipment, components and chemicals needed for the research of young scientists are exempted from duty. This will serve to further develop scientific research.

Importance of the new system

The new online platform will create great opportunities for young scientists and researchers. Through this platform, they will be able to find the necessary equipment and resources, and organize their scientific research effectively. In addition, duty-free equipment and chemicals will reduce the cost of scientific research and ease the work of researchers. — Read on infoedu.uz
