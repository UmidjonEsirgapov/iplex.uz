About the university and the grant in brief:

The Australian National University (ANU) offers 600 scholarship places to international and domestic students each year to study in master's and doctoral programs. It is one of the most highly rated scholarship programs in the world. ANU is a national university and it was established in 1946 in Canberra, the capital of Australia. This university ranks #59 in the ranking of world universities announced by THE (Times Higher Education).
