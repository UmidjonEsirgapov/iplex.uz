Amount of monthly fees for class management and notebook checking

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Payments for class leadership and notebook checking are determined as a percentage of the minimum salary (1,155,000 soums) for teachers.

Payments for class leadership

Depending on the number of students in the class as follows an additional fee is paid in the amounts:

  • Up to 15 people – 26.4% (304,490 soums)
  • 16-20 people – 31.6% (364,980 soums)
  • 21-25 people – 36.9% (426,195 soums)
  • 26-30 people – 42.1% (486,255 soums)
  • More than 31 – 52.8% (609,840 soums)

Notebook fees for checking

Teachers are paid for checking notebooks and written works in the following order:

  • If there are up to 15 students – 8.8% (104,640 soums)
  • If there are more than 15 – 17.6% (203 280 soums)

These additional payments are aimed at properly encouraging the work of teachers and improving the quality of education. — Read on infoedu.uz
