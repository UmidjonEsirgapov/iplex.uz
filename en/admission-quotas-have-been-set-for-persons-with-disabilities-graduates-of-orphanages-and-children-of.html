Admission quotas have been set for persons with disabilities, graduates of orphanages and children of Ministry of Internal Affairs

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Statement of the State Commission No. 1 approved the distribution of admission rates based on an additional 2% state grant for persons with disabilities in the section of higher education institutions and forms of education. admission rates based on an additional 1% state grant for orphans are distributed according to higher education institutions and forms of education.
According to it, a total of 875 additional quotas were allocated across the republic for the children of the employees of the internal affairs bodies.

For persons with disabilities, graduates of the Mercy House and Children's Town, and children of the employees of the internal affairs bodies additional admission rates have been approved.

The distribution of admission rates based on an additional 2 percent state grant for persons with disabilities in the section of higher education institutions and forms of education has been approved by the statement of the State Commission No. 1.

According to it, a total of 3,155 additional quotas have been allocated for persons with disabilities across the republic. In terms of forms of education:

  • for daytime education - 2,270;
  • for external education - 754;
  • for evening education - 131.

Admission based on an additional 1% state grant for real orphans who are graduates of the Kindergarten and Children's Town indicators are distributed by HEIs and forms of education.

A total of 1,580 additional quotas were allocated for those belonging to this category across the country. In terms of forms of education:

  • for daytime education - 1,137;
  • for extramural education - 377;
  • for evening education - 66.

In addition, additional admission indicators for children of internal affairs officers were approved.

Crime prevention and The distribution of additional 3 percent state grant admission rates for the children of the current employees of the internal affairs bodies who made a worthy contribution to the fight against crime and achieved high results in their service or academic activities among higher education institutions was approved. According to it, a total of 875 additional quotas were allocated across the country for the children of the employees of the internal affairs bodies.
