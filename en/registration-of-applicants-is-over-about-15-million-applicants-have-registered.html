We inform you that the online registration of applicants to the undergraduate courses of higher education institutions for the 2020-2021 academic year has officially ended.

Registration will be from June 20 to July 20 in all regions at the official State Test Center website and through the Unified interactive state services portal, and in 8 districts of our republic through State Service Centers.

According to the State Test Center, the total number of registered applicants was 1,484,085. Therefore, 536,144 (36.13%) graduates this year.
