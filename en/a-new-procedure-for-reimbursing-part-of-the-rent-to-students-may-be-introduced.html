A new procedure for reimbursing part of the rent to students may be introduced

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In particular, it is determined that a part of the monthly rent payment to applicants will be paid in the amount of one time of BHM in Tashkent, and half of BHM in other regions, regardless of the amount of the lease agreement. The administrative regulations for the introduction of the service are set to be approved. At the same time, the Knowledge and Qualifications Assessment Agency will create an opportunity for applicants to determine their need for student accommodation in the process of applying for admission to state higher education institutions starting from the 2023-2024 academic year. and in the regions it is decided to pay half the BHM.

Photo: Yevgeniy Sorochin / Spot

From September 1, a simplified procedure for covering part of the rent to students from the State budget may be introduced. In this regard, the government has discussed the draft law.

In particular, a part of the monthly rent payment to the applicants, regardless of the amount of the rental agreement, is determined to be one time of the BHM in Tashkent, and half of the BHM in other regions.

When registering two or more students in the rental agreement the area of the apartment should not be less than 12 square meters for each student.

Part of the monthly rent payment will be covered for September and October for September and October students who are not enrolled in TTJ and come from the regions.

For more than 60% of the students living on the basis of the right to rent, but the rent payments are covered from the state budget in accordance with the procedure established by this decision, a part of the monthly rent payment may be covered by extra-budgetary funds based on the criteria and procedure approved by this higher education institution.

The document specifies the following:

  • dormitories development of a register for keeping accounts;
  • priority placement of students in need of social protection;
  • creating the possibility of making electronic applications and payments through a single portal.

It is allowed to record residential rental contracts with students in the state tax service authorities in electronic form using remote biometric identification (Face-ID) technology.

From the 2023-2024 academic year, placement of higher education students in dormitories will be online.

According to this, it is decided to approve the administrative regulations for the introduction of the free electronic service provided through the DXM and the Single portal. applicants will have the opportunity to define their need for student housing in the process of submitting an application to study at state higher education institutions.

From the 2023-2024 academic year, students will apply for dormitory accommodation only through DXM or the Single portal.

In this case, the commission will not be collected.
