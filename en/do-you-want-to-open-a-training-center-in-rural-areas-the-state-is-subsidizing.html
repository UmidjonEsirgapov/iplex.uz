The opportunity to receive subsidies for "Organization of foreign language teaching activities in rural areas" continues for entrepreneurs of the Republic of Uzbekistan. Applicants can apply for a subsidy until January 10, 2024.

What will the subsidy be allocated to?

  • Equipping: Equipping the building and classrooms with modern technologies, furniture and other technical tools.
  • Educational resources: Necessary textbooks, teaching methods provision of literature, library and information resources.
  • Teachers' work: Paying for the work of pedagogues involved in the educational process.

Required documents

  • Business plan: 2-year business plan.
  • Economic contracts: Product delivery or service contracts.
  • Agreements with teaching staff: Labor contracts with teachers.
  • Foreign language certificate: Documents confirming the level of language knowledge of teaching staff.
  • Rental contract: Lease agreement for the building or classrooms used in the educational process.

How to provide the service will be increased?

Applicants can use the subsidy allocation service through the my.gov.uz portal. Use of the service is absolutely free.

Conclusion

This subsidy creates an important opportunity for entrepreneurs to develop foreign language teaching activities and create a quality educational environment in rural areas. Prepare the necessary documents on time and apply for a subsidy! — More information
