General information:

In order to support active, dynamic Bachelor 3-4 stage, Master and PhD students, teachers and young researchers The National Scholarship Program of the Slovak Republic has announced a grant program. Citizens of all countries other than Slovakia can apply.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Students studying at universities outside of Slovakia can apply;
  • Must have studied at universities for at least 2.5 years or are studying at the master's level, or young teachers and researchers at a university can apply. possible;

Duration: 4-10 months for students; 1-10 months for PhD students; 1-10 months for young research scientists;

Advantages of the grant:

  • For undergraduate and graduate students-400 euros;
  • For PhD students-734 euros;
  • Teachers and young researchers -1050 euro;
  • Accommodation and food are also provided;
  • Travel expenses are also covered depending on the distance between you and Slovakia:

a) if 0-350km 0 euros; b) 250 euros for 350-2000 km; c) 500 euros for 2000-7000 km; d) For countries with a distance of 7,000 and more - 1,000 euros;

Required documents for the grant:

  • CV/Resume;
  • Motivational letter;
  • Detailed study program- it is necessary to indicate information such as the fields of study in Slovakia and the duration of stay in Slovakia;
  • 2 letters of recommendation from current university professors;
  • Diploma or all semester grades;
  • Record of your studies at the university;
  • In order to win this grant, you must first receive an acceptance letter for this grant from the universities in Slovakia;

Program admission process:

  • Register online through this site;

Selection criteria:

‣ The selection committee decides who will be awarded a scholarship within the NSP. The Committee is appointed by the Minister of Education, Science, Research and Sports of the Slovak Republic. The committee's decisions are made based on the documents attached to the applicants' online application.

Application deadline: April 30, 16:00 CET;
