If I transfer, will I lose the grant?

Many students studying in higher education transfer to another higher education institution for various reasons. wants to transfer ("perevod") his studies. Clear information about this process is especially important for students studying on the basis of a state grant.

Can a student studying on the basis of a grant transfer to another higher education institution?

Yes, it is possible. But this is done only under certain conditions. If a student studying on the basis of a state grant wants to transfer his studies to another higher education institution, then:

  • The direction to which he wants to transfer his studies must be suitable or similar.

  • Only students studying on the basis of a grant are allowed to transfer to a grant upon application.

  • For this, there must be an empty grant slot in the new HEI.

  • It must be within the state quotas.

This procedure has been approved by an official government source.

Who will be given priority?

  • Blind or for students with disabilities, transfer of studies on the basis of a grant is carried out preferentially.

  • Although the availability of a grant place is important for them, it is allowed based on their social status.

Conclusion

If you are studying on the basis of a grant and want to transfer to another OTM, it is absolutely possible.
