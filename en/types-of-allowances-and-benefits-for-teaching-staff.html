What kind of bonuses are given to teachers? How many percentages are they given?

There are several types of bonuses given to teaching staff. We have summarized some of them in this article.

Incentives given to exemplary employees of general secondary education institutions at the expense of the director's fund

These bonuses are assigned in the amount of from 10% to 40% compared to the basic tariff rates of pedagogue, psychologist and library staff. Its procedure was approved by Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 823 dated September 30, 2019.

10 percent monthly bonus paid to the heads of methodical associations in the fields of science

In order to further improve the quality of the educational process in the educational institution, the base rate of the heads of methodical associations in the fields of science 10% monthly bonus will be given to the teachers.

Up to 40% bonuses will be paid to school management and teachers who have achieved positive results in providing quality education

Up to 40% bonuses will be paid to teachers and management of schools where a new system of teaching and knowledge assessment has been introduced.

Leader and teacher bonuses based on points:

  • First 5%: 40%
  • Next 10%: 30%
  • Next 15%: 20%
  • For the rest: 10%

Incentives given to internationally recognized or nationally certified subject teachers

International certificate holders receive a 50 percent bonus, national certificate holders 20 percent.

Incentives given to qualified teaching staff working in general secondary education institutions at the expense of the ministerial fund

Qualified pedagogic personnel are given bonuses in the amount of 5.5 times the minimum salary.

Teachers who trained Olympiad winners are given monthly bonuses in the amount of 100-150%

Teachers trained by international Olympiad winners get 150% monthly, republic stage winners will receive 100 percent bonuses.

Incentives for teachers working in remote areas

The following bonuses are paid depending on length of service:

  • 6 months to 3 years: 10 percent
  • 3-5 years: 20 percent
  • up to 5-10 years: 30 percent
  • up to 10-15 years: 40 percent
  • over 15 years: 50 percent

15 percent bonus for female physical education teachers working in rural areas

Physical education teachers in rural areas receive a 15% bonus.

Additional bonus for inclusive education classroom teachers

Inclusive education classroom teachers receive bonuses from 10% to 40%.

For holders of a prestigious international rating or PhD degree. bonuses

These pedagogues receive a salary increase of from 15% to 75%.

Additional bonuses for school principals and deputy directors with a management certificate

A 50% bonus for principals with a management certificate, 30% bonus for deputies.

Additional bonuses for teachers and principals who trained winners of the International Olympiad
