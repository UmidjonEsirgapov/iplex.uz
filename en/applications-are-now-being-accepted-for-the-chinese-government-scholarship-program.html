Applications are now being accepted for the Chinese Government Scholarship Program

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Candidates from all over the world for bachelor's, master's and PhD degrees can apply for programs taught in Chinese or English.
In particular, applicants for programs taught in Chinese must provide a Chinese Language Proficiency Test (HSK) certificate.
In English and applicants for the taught programs must submit IELTS or TOEFL certificates.

Candidates for bachelor's, master's and PhD degrees from all over the world can participate.

Photo: Forbes

The Chinese government is accepting applications for the scholarship program.

Bachelor, master and PhD candidates from all over the world can apply for programs taught in Chinese or English. You can find the program and university you are interested in using this link.

All candidates are required to provide language certificates for scholarship applications.

In particular, applicants for Chinese-taught programs must provide a Chinese Language Proficiency Test (HSK) certificate.

Applicants for English-taught programs must provide IELTS or TOEFL certificates.

Students who have received a grant are exempted from tuition fees, insurance costs ($117) are covered, and additional funds are allocated for a place in a university dormitory or for living.

Students are given a monthly stipend as follows:

  • For a bachelor's degree - $366 per month;
  • For a master's degree - $440 per month;
  • Doctoral studies for $513 per month.

A candidate must fill out an application form, upload a copy of their passport, send a notarized diploma, academic transcript, curriculum proposal, and a letter of recommendation.
