Application for accommodation in student accommodation will start from August 1

According to the relevant decision of the Cabinet of Ministers (No. 376 dated 14.08.2023), application for accommodation in student accommodation from the 2024-2025 academic year will continue from August 1 until the end of the academic year.

Processes are carried out by submitting a free electronic application through state service centers and Uniform interactive state services portal (my.gov.uz).

According to this, residential rental contracts concluded with students can be registered online at the state tax service authorities remotely through the personal office of the taxpayer through biometric identification (Face-ID) technology.

When considering applications, the following categories of students are admitted to student accommodation on the basis of priority:

  • children of "Mehribanlik houses", orphans and students deprived of parental care;
  • children of families in need of social protection included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection", "Temir Daftar" and "Women's Daftar". and students who are in the "Youth Register";
  • Students with group I and II disabilities;
  • Children of families from the same family who are studying at the bachelor's level in the form of full-time education on the basis of two or more fee-contracts;
  • International Science Olympiads, Republican Science Olympiads held by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation of the Republic of Uzbekistan students who won;
  • Students who won the republican stage of the "Student of the Year" and "Student Theater Studios" contests;
  • Students who won the republican stage of the intellectual game "Zakovat" held among students of higher education institutions;
  • The chairman of the student council, floor captain in the student residence students.

It should be noted that no fee is charged for providing public service when submitting an electronic application and making payments through state service centers or YIDXP. — More information
