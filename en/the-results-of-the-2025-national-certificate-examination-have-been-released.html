2025 national certificate exam results announced

January 25-26 results of national certificate exam in chemistry and biology announced. Candidates who participated in the exam can find out their results and download the certificate electronically through the "My applications" section on the my.gov.uz site.

Evaluation criteria

The test results are calculated based on the international Rash model, and the accumulated points are evaluated as follows:

  • 70+ points – A+ level
  • 65 – 69.9 points – A level
  • 60 – 64.9 points – B+ level
  • 55 – 59.9 points – B level
  • 50 – 54.9 points – C+ level
  • 46 – 49.9 points – C level

Certificate benefits

Certificate holders obtain the following benefits in the relevant subject in HET undergraduate entrance tests:

  • A+ and A level certificate – maximum score
  • B+, B, C+ and C certificate – proportional score

Also, students who have a certificate in academic lyceum entrance tests will be awarded maximum score in the relevant subject. — Official Data
