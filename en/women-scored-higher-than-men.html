Women scored higher than men

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
If we pay attention to the scores of applicants in the test, it can be seen that women are recommended for studentship with a higher score than men.
The average passing score of applicants recommended for studentship based on the state grant: for men - 112.4 score; for women - 125.1 points; On the basis of payment contract: in men - 88.9 points; among women - it was 98.5 points.
This difference in the average passing scores of men and women is related to the fields of study chosen by them during registration and their passing scores.
