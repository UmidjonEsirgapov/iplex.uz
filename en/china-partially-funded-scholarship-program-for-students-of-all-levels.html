About the university and the grant program:

South China Normal University is a comprehensive university ranked 60th in China and 881st globally. The main goal of the university is to develop a unique and diverse campus culture and create a rich learning environment for learning and scientific research. was established in order to attract students to study at the universities of Guangdong province in undergraduate, graduate and doctoral level courses.

Eligibility requirements for the grant:

Students intending to apply for the grant program are required to fulfill and meet the following conditions:

  • Candidates are Chinese citizens in good health. must be;

  • Candidates must be enrolled in undergraduate or graduate studies;

  • Undergraduate students must have a high school or higher education diploma and be under 30 years old;

  • Masters the applicant must have a bachelor's degree and be under 35 years old;

  • Doctoral applicants must have a master's degree and be under 40 years old;

Grant amount:

One-time grants are provided to the awardees in the amount indicated below

  • Undergraduate - 10,000 Chinese Yuan (about 1,567 USD);
  • Master - 20,000 Chinese Yuan (about 3,134 USD);
  • Doctoral - 30,000 Chinese Yuan (about 4,701 USD)

This scholarship winners will be given priority consideration in the scholarship application at the next stage.

Application process:

Candidates will be eligible for scholarship evaluation only after being accepted by SCNU University.

  • Step 1: Register, submit and print the application by accessing the University Admissions Office HERE;
  • Step 2: Download the scholarship application form, fill it out, print it and sign it.
  • Step 3: Mail your documents to the SCNU admissions office before the application deadline. The mailing addresses are as follows:
  • Room A104, School of International Culture, South China Normal University, No. 55 Zhongshan Avenue, West, Guangzhou, P. R. China, Postal Code: 510631
    Tel: 0086-20-85215350
    Email: 20161043@m.scnu.edu.cn, hscic7@scnu.edu.cn
