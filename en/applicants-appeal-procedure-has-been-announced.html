Applicants' appeal procedure has been announced

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Dyorbek Kochkharov, the responsible employee of the State Test Center, gave information about filing an appeal on June 25 at AOKA.
If the applicant's written answers are not confirmed, the appeal commission will decide to leave the test results unchanged and refuse to satisfy the appeal.

Applicants who are dissatisfied with the test results can appeal in writing or electronically to the appeal commission established at the State Test Center within the period specified by the State Commission. has the right to file a complaint.

On the basis of his application, the applicant will be notified of the review of the appeal.

Diorbek Kochkarov, the official of the State Test Center, informed about the filing of the appeal on June 25 at AOKA. the applicant is required to come with a permit.

During the appeal process, the applicant will be presented with test materials completed by him. On the basis of the submitted materials, the applicant will in writing state his answers to each of the test tasks considered incorrect.

The appeal will be considered by the appeal commission only in the presence of the applicant based on his written answers. If the applicant's written answers are not confirmed, the appeals commission will make a decision to leave the test results unchanged and refuse to satisfy the appeal.

Additionally, the acceptance of appeals from applicants began on August 20. This year, due to the pandemic, the entrance exam period will be longer than before. Applications for appeal this year will be accepted within 10 days after the tests have been fully completed.

Additional information will be provided by DTM.
