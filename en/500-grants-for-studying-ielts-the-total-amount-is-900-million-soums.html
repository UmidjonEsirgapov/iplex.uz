Brief content:
The Registon education project announces the "Registon IELTS Grant" contest for the strongest young people in cooperation with the OLIYGOH.UZ team. one of the platforms is the next social project developed by the OLIYGOH.UZ team, through which the most talented young people are provided with free education and appropriate rewards.
Competition stages The competition "Registon IELTS Grant" consists of 2 stages. In cooperation with OLIYGOH.UZ team, announces the "Registon IELTS Grant" competition. Through this Grant, it is planned to educate 500 young people across the Republic absolutely free of charge (quota allocation below).

Registon IELTS Grant is the next social project developed by the team of OLIYGOH.UZ, which is not indifferent to the future of Uzbekistan's education, and one of the largest platforms. Free education of the most talented young people and appropriate rewards are provided. The main goal of the competition is to encourage the education of young people studying in the Republic in modern ways, to support talented young people, to improve their English literacy level, and to further develop the education sector of Uzbekistan through financial incentives. consists of development.

The winners of the Registon IELTS Grant competition will have the following opportunities:

  • the opportunity to study the IELTS level course for 3 months completely free of charge at the branches of the "Registon" educational center (based on the calendar month);
  • "Registon IELTS Grant" winner's certificate, a special Grant, as well as will receive souvenirs and special prizes;
  • Opportunity to use special IELTS materials of the center for free;
  • Opportunity to participate in the "Registan IELTS PREMIUM Mock exam" exams for free;
  • Opportunity to use official materials of "Registan IELTS PREMIUM Mock exam" for free;
  • Free use of the "Support teacher" service of the "Registon" educational center;
  • Also, special QR-code certificates will be given to all candidates who participated in the competition.

      Stages of selection

      "Registon IELTS Grant" selection consists of 2 stages. The stages of the competition are evaluated based on the general evaluation system and the most worthy participants are selected.

      The first stage

      The first stage (in the form of a test) Participating candidates will take an online exam through the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.

      Type of exam: 50 test questions on different levels of the English language.

      Time: The 1st stage of the competition will start on February 12 at 19:00 and At 23:00, the exam window closes for everyone.

    All candidates registered during this period can participate in the exam for 40 minutes. After the completion of the 1st stage, the general results mandate will be formed and the participants who will get the result of the exam above 80% (40 correct answers and above) will get a ticket to the 2nd stage.

    Second stage:

    Second stage: This stage is the most important stage of the "Registon IELTS Grant" contest, and the participants will pass a special written exam at this stage.

    Time and location: The 2nd stage of the competition will be held on February 19between the selected candidates in the building of the special exams of the "Registon" training center in the city of Tashkent and in the regions (addresses in the regions are below).

    ✅ Strict notes for the participants:

    1) Read the instructions thoroughly before the start of the competition;

    2) Before participating in the competition, familiarize yourself with all the information about "Registon" educational center , its branches, their locations ;

    3) In which branch of the "Registon" educational center Make sure you can read. Because whichever branch you choose in the 1st stage exam, the final exam will be held in this branch and the grant will be distributed within the branch of your choice. Therefore, it is recommended that you read the information carefully.

    After 10 days after the final stage, the general results of the participants will be checked transparently by a special commission and the results will be announced. It should not be forgotten that the scores of the final stage are the final decisive result. At this stage, the most suitable candidates will be announced as the absolute winners of the competition.
