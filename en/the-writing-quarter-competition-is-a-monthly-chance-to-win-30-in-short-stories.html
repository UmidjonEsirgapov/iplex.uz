The Writing Quarter offers a chance for writers aged 18 and over to win a $30 cash prize for their short story written on any topic. The contest is held monthly, and stories are accepted until the last day of each month.

Benefits:

  • The winner will be awarded $30;
  • May publish their story on The Writing Quarter.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must be over 18 years old;
  • Send a story of 1000-3000 words on any topic;
  • The stories must not have been published anywhere before and must be written by oneself;
  • The first page of the story and the email sent indicate how many words the story consists of.
  • Stories should be in Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx), Rich Text Format (.rtf), and OpenOffice/NeoOffice (.odt), Courier or Times New Roman, 12 formats.

Stories info@writingquarter.com.au will be sent to this address. The results will be announced at the end of next month and the winner will be paid via PayPal.

Participation in the competition is absolutely free. Each participant can only apply once per month.

For more applications and offers: https://writingquarter.com.au/contact-us/ — Official Data
