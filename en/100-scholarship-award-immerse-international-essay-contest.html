General Information:

Essay Contest "Immerse Education" gives school/high school/college students aged 13-18 from any country in the world the opportunity to submit essay answers to a pre-set question in the subject of their choice. 10 Winners will receive 100% Scholarship, second place will also be awarded.

Important dates:

  • Application for the program opens on 16.03.2022;
  • Application for the program ends on 31.08.2022;
  • All results will be announced on 01.10.2022

Note: Essays are changed according to 2 categories. That is, for 13-15-year-olds and for 16-18-year-old students , find out the essay topic here (list of essay topics).

Benefits of participating in the program:

  • 1st place winners (10 people) will receive a 100% grant. i.e. will receive a 100% discount on the cost of attending a 2-week summer school at Oxford or Cambridge universities.
  • 2nd place winners will receive a 70% discount for this summer school.
  • Typically, about 7% of candidates will receive a scholarship to participate in the Immerse program;
  • Travel expenses will not be reimbursed;

The summer school provides the following:

– 13-night accommodation at the central college of Cambridge or Oxford University;
–Breakfast and dinner in the College Hall;
–Accommodation in a private dormitory;
–40 hours of academic tutoring by Oxford University and Cambridge University tutors;
–Study and lecture materials;
- Access to the Mentor Program and support materials;
- All excursions – Formal dinner at Cambridge colleges;
- Comprehensive insurance;
- Travel to Oxford incl. 2 coach tours;
- Graduation ceremony and certificate;
- Tutor's written evaluation;
