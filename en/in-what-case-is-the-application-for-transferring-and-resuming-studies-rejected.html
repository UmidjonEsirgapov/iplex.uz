In what cases is the application for transfer and renewal of studies rejected?

When transferring from a foreign higher education institution, the applicant takes either a test or a creative exam, depending on the direction. Passing scores are published annually on the website of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency by July 15.

Only 2 categories of students are allowed to transfer from one higher education institution to another in Uzbekistan:

  • If a student gets married, to the higher education institution in the place of residence of the spouse;
  • If a civil servant changes his or her place of work, the child or spouse can transfer his studies with the recommendation letter of the relevant leader.

Transferring studies is not allowed in the following cases:

  • those studying in non-accredited higher education institutions of foreign countries or their branches; to those who have studied;
  • To those who have studied in the centers, departments, counseling centers, etc. of higher education institutions;
  • To transfer studies to the first semester of the first year without completing the first semester (with the exception of cases where the student is given an academic leave due to illness, cases of transfer when the studied field of study is not available);
  • Higher if the educational institution does not have a suitable (cognate) field of study (specialty) or form of education;
  • When the differences between the general professional and specialty (specialty) subjects in the model curricula exceed 4;
  • When those recommended to study on the basis of a payment contract do not make the payment within the specified period;
  • The student's personal collection when the volume (academic certificate) is not submitted within the specified time;
  • Transfer to a higher education institution based on a state grant and there are no vacancies within the number of students (admission quotas) based on a state grant;
  • When the capacity of the material and technical base of the higher education institution is limited; to another form of education, to another higher education institution;
  • From evening (shift) education to part-time (special extramural) education and from one field of education of evening (shift) education to another field of education;
  • From part-time (special part-time) education to full-time education and part-time (special part-time) education from one educational field to another to the field of education;
  • From one form of education to a form of special part-time education and from a form of special correspondence education to another form of education;
  • If the student has been accepted on the basis of a mutual agreement of Uzbekistan and foreign higher education institutions and studied or is studying on the basis of joint education programs (this joint program itself is excluded).

Study transfer is carried out on the following platforms:

  1. transfer.edu.uz – for transfer from one state higher education institution to another state higher education institution;
  2. my.uzbmb.uz – for transfer from a foreign or non-state HEI to a state HEI.
