The minimum passing score for entrance exams has remained unchanged

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The minimum passing score in the entrance exams remained unchanged According to the relevant statement of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the minimum passing score for the state grant to higher educational institutions of the republic is 68.0 points (36% of the maximum 189 points). The minimum passing score for the payment contract was set at 56.7 points (30% of the maximum 189 points). Earlier, at a press conference held on May 8, the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation announced that the minimum passing score of 56.7 for entering higher education institutions is planned to be gradually increased from 2025.
In the interview given to the TV channel "Uzbekiston24" by the Minister, the passing scores for the 2024-2025 academic year are: for healthcare, law, business and management - 75.6 points; for other directions - 66.5 points.
