Youth Green Camp: 2-day camp in Kyrgyzstan

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
High school and university students from all countries of Central Asia and Afghanistan will have the opportunity to learn from the most powerful leading experts, discuss regional ecological and environmental issues. transportation and accommodation; participation in a two-day camp in Norin, Kyrgyzstan; Get advice from powerful experts in finding solutions to the environmental and ecological problems of Central Asia and Afghanistan; To get a micro-grant for the implementation of the best projects given as a solution to environmental problems.

Youth Council on November 27-28, Youth Green Camp, organized by November 27-28 in Norin, Kyrgyzstan. established for the purpose. Students of secondary education institutions and universities from all countries of Central Asia and Afghanistan will have the opportunity to learn from the most powerful leading experts and discuss regional ecological and environmental problems. At the same time, participants of "Youth Green Camp" can win micro-grants to present their solutions to "environmental and ecological problems in Central Asia and Afghanistan" and to implement them.

Who can participate?

  • Secondary education or university students from Central Asia and Afghanistan who are interested in the subject of ecology;
  • Secondary education or university students from the Norin region of Kyrgyzstan;
  • Bishkek, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Afghanistan, Kazakhstan secondary education and university students students.

Note! All candidates must be able to speak English fluently.

Benefits:

  • Participants will receive free domestic transportation and accommodation;
  • Participation in a two-day camp in Norin, Kyrgyzstan;
  • Environmental and ecological solutions in Central Asia and Afghanistan. get advice from strong experts in finding;
  • Get a micro-grant to implement the best projects given as a solution to environmental problems.
