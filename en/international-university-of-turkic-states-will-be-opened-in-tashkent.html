According to the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the International University of Turkic States will be established in Tashkent. This higher education institution operates on the basis of the programs of the prestigious universities of Turkey and is considered a non-governmental educational organization. Students are taught by leading professors and teachers in accordance with modern educational standards.

LocationThis university is located in the buildings of Tashkent State University of Economics and Tashkent Tax Technical College. Also, Tashkent Technical College of Economics and Industry and Tashkent Technical College of Taxation will be merged and replaced by Tashkent Technical College of Economics and Taxation.

The new university is expected to improve the quality of education in the country and develop international cooperation. — Read on infoedu.uz
