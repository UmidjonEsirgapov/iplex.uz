Brief about the university and the grant program:

State University of New York at Oswego (SUNY Oswego or Oswego State) is a local university with campuses in Oswego and New York. It has two campuses: the historic lakefront campus in Oswego and the Metro Center in Syracuse, New York.

At the same time, the university invites international students to submit international applications for Destination Oswego Scholarships for International Students for the 2021-2022 academic year. All international students are eligible to apply for this grant program for SUNY Oswego undergraduate students.

Grant Details:

  • University or Organization: State University of New York at Oswego;
  • Course Level: Undergraduate;
  • Grant Amount: $6,000-$9,000 per year (renewable for 4 years
  • Application Procedure: Online
  • Grant Type: International

Who Can Apply?

  • Eligible Countries: Students from all countries can apply for this grant;
  • Level and Courses of Study: All undergraduate courses offered by SUNY Oswego University;
  • Eligibility Criteria: Admission to the Scholarship Program applicants must first be accepted into one of the university's undergraduate programs;
  • Applicants must be international students.

Application Procedure:

Applicants must first apply to any undergraduate course selected by SUNY Oswego from the Common Application (here) or the SUNY Application (here via) must be registered through. For this, the following documents are required:

  • Candidates must submit bachelor's degree diplomas and applications if they have studied in school and college or previously in a bachelor's program;
  • Candidates must have a GPA of 5.0;
  • Candidates are required to have the following language certificates:
    • TOEFL-at least 71,
    • IELTS 6.0,
    • 55 in PTE.
