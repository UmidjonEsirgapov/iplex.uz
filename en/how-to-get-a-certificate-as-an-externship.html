Procedure for obtaining a certificate through an external service

For various reasons, you could not receive general secondary education Citizens have the opportunity to obtain a certificate on an externship basis. This process was introduced in order to ensure the right to education and support citizens.

Conditions for obtaining a certificate in the external procedure

Citizens can apply for a certificate according to the following requirements:

  1. Online registration: Every year from March 1 to May 1 Single interactive state services portal (my.gov.uz) it is necessary to register through.
  2. Participation in exams: It is required to participate in the exams held in May-June at external schools.
  3. Obtaining a certificate: Citizens who successfully pass the exams will be given a certificate of general secondary education.

Exam preparation and application process

  1. Visit the single interactive portal of state services (my.gov.uz).
  2. Register and fill out the application for external exams.
  3. Take the exams on the specified dates.
