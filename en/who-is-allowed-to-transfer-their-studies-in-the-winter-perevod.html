Who is allowed to transfer their studies in the winter "perevod"?

Is it possible to transfer studies abroad in the winter? What good reasons should there be? Is the internal transfer allowed?

Applications for the winter transfer are accepted from December 20 to January 20 (also on the same day).

Not everyone can apply for the winter transfer. Only those who have 2 valid reasons are allowed.

The following are considered valid reasons:

1. To transfer the student's studies to the higher education institution in the area where the spouse permanently lives in connection with starting a family;

2. To transfer the studies of his/her spouse or minor child in the event of a change of workplace of employees of state bodies (respective based on the recommendation of the head of the ministry (department).

The following documents are submitted to the regional working groups:

  • an application for transfer of studies (the application form is provided by the regional working group and filled out by the student on the spot);
  • copies of the student's rating book or academic reference or transcript in the prescribed form (one is enough);
  • passport copy;
  • relevant documents justifying the reasons mentioned above.

Applications for resuming studies (with the exception of relocation) are submitted directly to the higher educational institution where the student studied, regardless of how many years have passed since the student was expelled.
