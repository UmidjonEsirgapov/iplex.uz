Ability to re-edit rejected graduate applications

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
On August 6-8, it is possible to edit rejected applications for admission to the master's degree program for the academic year 2024/2025.
Rejected applications: As a result of reviewing applications, many applications were rejected for various reasons.
The reasons are as follows: The selected specialty is not suitable or compatible with the field of education of the graduate. August 6-8 Re-editing: applicants who sent an application from the magistr.edu.uz system between July 1-30 and whose application was rejected due to deficiencies have been given the opportunity to edit. The applications of the applicants who registered online through the magistr.edu.uz electronic system were considered.

Rejected applications:

As a result of the application review, many applications were rejected for various reasons. The reasons are as follows: — Read on infoedu.uz

  • The selected specialty is not suitable or related to the field of study of the graduate
  • Another document was uploaded instead of the requested document or it was not completely uploaded
  • The quality of the uploaded documents
  • Other technical reasons

Reediting applications:

  • Edit date: August 6-8
  • Re-editing possibility: The possibility of editing was created for applicants who sent an application from the magistr.edu.uz system between July 1-30 and whose application was rejected due to deficiencies.
  • Important note: It is not recommended to edit applications rejected due to the fact that the relevant national or international certificate of foreign language proficiency is not up to the required level.

After the editing period:

  • After this period, editing will not be allowed and applications that have not been corrected will not be considered.
