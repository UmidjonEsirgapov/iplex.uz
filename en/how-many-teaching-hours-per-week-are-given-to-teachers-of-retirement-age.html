How many hours of classes should be given to pedagogues of retirement age?

According to Article 4 of the Labor Code of the Republic of Uzbekistan, everyone has equal opportunities to exercise and protect labor rights. Also, discrimination in the field of work and training is prohibited.

This means, the age of the teacher is not the main factor in the distribution of lessons. Teaching hours are given to the teacher based on the quality of education and the results he has achieved. According to the procedure for the completion of classes and formation of tariff lists in general educational institutions, lesson hours are distributed based on priority sequence.

There is no rule that teachers of retirement age should be given fewer lessons. If a retired teacher has high performance and good teaching quality, he/she can be given more teaching hours. On the contrary, if the results are low, the class hours may be reduced. — Source
