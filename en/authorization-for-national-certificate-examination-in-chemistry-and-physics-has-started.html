Permissions for the national certificate exam in chemistry and physics, which will be held on April 2 (Sunday), 2023, have begun. This is reported by the Agency for Assessment of Knowledge and Skills.

Applicants participating in the test can obtain the permit from the "National Certificate" section of the my.uzbmb.uz site (https://my.uzbmb.uz/national-cert). The permit specifies when and where applicants will take part in the test, and applicants are asked to arrive before the specified time. — Read on infoedu.uz
