No, it is possible to register and take an exam even without a letter of recommendation. Women do not need to wait for a letter of recommendation to enter OTM. Below is a detailed explanation:

  1. Recommendation Integration:

    • The recommendation for women is directly integrated into the platform of the Agency for Knowledge and Skills Assessment (Uzbmb). This means that information for women who have received a letter of recommendation will be automatically displayed on the relevant platform of the agency.

  2. Registration and Selection of Subjects:

    • Women can register on the my.uzbmb.uz site and choose a set of subjects without waiting for a letter of recommendation. This will allow them to continue the preparation process for the entrance exams without delay.

  3. Recommendation Information Update:

    • Information about women who received a recommendation by the Family and Women's Committee is updated every weekend and displayed on the my.uzbmb.uz site. This allows students to register and move towards their goals, even if there is a delay in the recommendation process.
