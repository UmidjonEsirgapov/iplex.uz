Alta Scuola Politecnica offers fully funded scholarships for postgraduate studies in all architecture-related fields. All courses are conducted in English. The courses are primarily devoted to common problems and solutions faced by any engineering, architecture and design project. Courses will consist of lectures, practical classes and group discussions.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in architecture and related field;
  • Knowledge of English;
  • Having new ideas and innovation;
  • Sufficient knowledge and competence in their field.

The grant includes:

  • study contract;
  • transportation expenses;
  • accommodation and food expenses;
  • expenses for scientific projects.
