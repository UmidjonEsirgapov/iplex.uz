France: €15,000 Master's Degree MBA Scholarship Program for Women

Important news about higher education institutions:

About the university and the grant program: ESCP (French: École supérieure de commerce de Paris) is a private, state-recognized economic school.

Deadline: This program has 2 intakes this summer.

About the University and the grant program:

ESCP (French: École supérieure de commerce de Paris) is a private, state-recognized economics school. The university has six campuses in Berlin, London, Madrid, Paris, Turin and Warsaw. The university offers transnational bachelor, master, doctoral and MBA programs, as well as seminars for additional training of managers.

Who is the grant program for:

  • Citizens of all countries can apply for the program.

Application process:

  • Candidates must first apply online HERE for admission to the MBA program. After that, candidates should fill in the application form for the grant program HERE and send it to the following address:
    • kfisanotti@escp.eu

Required documents:

Candidates will be asked for the following documents by the university during the online registration process. Here you can apply:

  • Professional letter of recommendation from the workplace showing your commitment to the development of women in business;
  • Students must have a bachelor's degree in International Management to be accepted into ESCP University's MBA program.
  • GMAT or GRE or Tage Mage certificate; if not, these certificates must be provided by the university must pass the organized internal ESCP test;
  • Language certificates IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL are required, but if these certificates are not available, they must pass the university's internal admission test;
  • Bachelor's grade transcripts;
  • Application fee is 180 euros;
  • Then after receiving these documents, the second stage will be an online Skype interview with the selected candidates. final responses will arrive within 3 weeks.
  • You can learn more here;

Benefits of the grant:

Successfully selected international applicants will receive a grant of €15,000 (approx. USD 15,774) to study MBA at ESCP University. Hereyou can listen to 5 podcasts prepared by the university on how to win a grant.
