14 legal technical schools have been established in our republic.

They train personnel in the following specialties:

  • state legal activity;
  • legal service;
  • judicial activity.

Those who have graduated from general secondary education (grade 11) or secondary special vocational education institution can apply to legal technical schools.

Applicants' documents for admission are accepted online through the Unified interactive state services portal (my.gov.uz) or the official website of the State Testing Center.

Admission of citizens of Uzbekistan to legal technical schools is carried out through tests conducted by the State Testing Center.

The set of subjects, evaluation criteria, and the number of test tasks are in accordance with the requirements for admission to the TDYUU bachelor's degree.

Foreign citizens are accepted as a result of an interview organized by legal technical schools.

Applicants are accepted to study on the basis of a state order, payment contract, including differentiated payment contract.

The duration of study is 2 years. — Official Data
