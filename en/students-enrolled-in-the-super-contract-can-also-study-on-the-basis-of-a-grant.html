Students enrolled in the "Super-contract" can also study on the basis of a grant

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Students enrolled in the "Super-contract" can also study on the basis of a grant. According to the Decree of the President No. PF-81 issued on May 24, 2024, educational grants for students admitted to bachelor's and master's programs starting from the 2024/2025 academic year is allocated for one academic year.
In this process, students with high mastery in the 1st year can study on the basis of a grant from the following year.
For information, the regulations of this procedure must be approved by the relevant decision of the government.
