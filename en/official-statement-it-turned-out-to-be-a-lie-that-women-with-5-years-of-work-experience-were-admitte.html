Official statement: it turned out to be a lie that women with 5 years of work experience were admitted to study without an exam

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Women with 5 years of work experience will take the exam as well, only they can use an additional privilege. These days, there are unfounded reports on social networks that women with 5 years of work experience will be admitted to universities without an exam.
This is wrong. The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation informs the following regarding the interpreted messages.
In the 2023/2024 academic year, women with at least 5 years of work experience will be admitted to study based on the results of the entrance exams in accordance with this regulation. can only use the additional privilege

These days, unfounded reports are spreading on social networks that women with 5 years of work experience are admitted to higher educational institutions without an exam. Regarding these misinterpreted messages, the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation informs the following.

There is no procedure for admitting women with at least 5 years of work experience to higher education institutions without exams.

Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 322 of June 9, 2022 According to (https://lex.uz/docs/-6056269), the procedure for admitting women with at least five years of work experience to state higher education institutions has been approved. 500 additional admission parameters based on the basic payment contract were allocated in the section of higher education institutions.

In order to participate in the selections for the allocated admission parameters, women of this category must first have an appropriate letter of recommendation from the Family and Women's Committee. 500 will take part in the competition based on the basis of the basic payment-contract additional special admission parameters, according to the sequence of points.

In the 2023/2024 academic year, women with at least 5 years of work experience will be admitted to study based on the results of the entrance exams in accordance with this procedure. — More information
