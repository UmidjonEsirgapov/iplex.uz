A new rector was appointed to the Islamic Institute

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
On January 4, 2023, an extended meeting of the Supreme Committee of the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan was held.
Jalaluddin Hamrokulov / Photo: muslim.uz Uygun Gafurov, who worked as the rector of the Tashkent Islamic Institute, was appointed to the position of deputy.
Uygun Gafurov / Photo: muslim.uz The head of the department of the International Islamic Academy of Uzbekistan, doctor, professor, Muhammadolim Muhammadsiddikov, was appointed to the post of rector of this institute. extended meeting was held. It was attended by the members of the Supreme Council, the leadership of the Religious Office, clerics, specialists and mudarris.

Photo: muslim.uz

Organizational issues were discussed at the meeting. Since the deputy chairman of the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan, Ibrahim Domla Inomov, moved to another job, the imam-khatib of the "Novza" mosque in Tashkent, Jalaluddin Domla Hamrokulov, was appointed to the post of deputy chairman.

Jalaliddin Hamrokulov / Photo: muslim.uz

Uygun Gafurov, who worked as the rector of the Tashkent Islamic Institute, was appointed the deputy chairman for youth affairs in the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan.
