New University of Uzbekistan was founded

Important news related to higher educational institutions:

Brief content:
On June 23, President Shavkat Mirziyoyev got acquainted with a presentation on the organization of the educational process at the New Uzbekistan University. the head of the university gave instructions on organizing the admission and educational process at the level of international standards, effective integration of science, education and production.

On June 23, President Shavkat Mirziyoyev got acquainted with a presentation on the organization of the educational process at the New Uzbekistan University.

Educated and intelligent people ensure the development and future of every country. In Uzbekistan, which has set great goals for itself in all fields, attention is being paid to the quality of education and education of educated personnel at the level of state policy.

Presidential schools and specialized schools were established in all regions. Along with determining the knowledge and potential of young people and directing them in the right way, it also created a great wave of aspirations among our people. called This year, a new generation of such young people is graduating from schools. Now, in order to educate them in our country and create the most modern conditions for young people, the New Uzbekistan University is being founded on the initiative of the President.

This university will be established as a state higher education institution. Its main tasks are to train promising personnel with high professional knowledge and skills based on the educational programs of prestigious foreign educational institutions, to develop the intellectual abilities of students, and to create a favorable environment for this purpose. For example, Cambridge University conducts entrance exams. Curriculum and programs are developed together with the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and the Technical University of Munich. They also help to control the quality of education, fill laboratories and libraries.

The university has 5 faculties - engineering, natural sciences, management, agro-technologies and information technologies. will be. Lessons are conducted in Uzbek and English. The credit-module system has been introduced, and studies are carried out on the basis of a state grant and a payment contract.

The educational building and campuses of this educational institution also meet modern, international BREEAM certificates. The first academic year begins from September this year. Until the construction of a new building, classes will be temporarily held in the vacant buildings of the University of World Economy and Diplomacy.

Based on the needs of economic sectors, conducting scientific researches and widely involving students in scientific and innovative activities are among the main tasks of the university. For this purpose, cooperation with the world's leading universities and scientific research institutions will be established, and a scientific journal will be established. gave instructions on effective integration of education and production. It is important that ministries, prestigious institutions and large companies enter the university's board of trustees. It is necessary for them to express their needs, to integrate into the educational process, to invest in the future specialist in advance", - said Shavkat Mirziyoyev.

Our President approved the specified measures and signed the decision on the establishment of the New University of Uzbekistan.
