IT Girls 2023 is a unique opportunity for girls and women to learn valuable technology skills and start a successful career in IT

The program is specially designed for girls between the ages of 14 and 34 and provides free training in fullstack, web and mobile programming.

"IT Girls 2023" program will start on March 20 and will last for three months.

Lessons will be held offline, with individual attention to each participant.

The program is open to IELTS certificate holders who have scored at least 7+ points, which guarantees that the participants are well prepared to communicate in English in the global IT industry.
