To which areas of the university can I apply after studying history and foreign languages?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the statement No. 11 of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated December 19, 2019, from the 2020/2021 academic year, a test corresponding to the bachelor's degree programs of the republic's higher education institutions will be given. the set of subjects, the number of test items and the evaluation criteria have been approved.
It contains the name of the subjects, the number of test items, evaluation criteria (points for each correct answer) and the maximum number of points.
