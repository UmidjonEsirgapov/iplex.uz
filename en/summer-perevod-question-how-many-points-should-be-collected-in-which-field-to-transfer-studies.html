Summer "perevod" question: How many points should be collected in which field to transfer studies?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In the 2022-2023 academic year, according to the relevant order of the Minister of Higher and Secondary Special Education, students from higher education institutions in foreign countries, non-state and foreign (branch) higher education institutions operating in our republic will be transferred to state higher education institutions. the passing scores for transfer have been confirmed.
Get to know the passing scores: We remind you that according to the President's Decree No. 268 "On Measures for the Implementation of Administrative Reforms of New Uzbekistan", the tasks and functions of the State Inspectorate for Quality Control of Education under the Cabinet of Ministers are respectively "Ministry of Preschool and School Education" and "Higher Education, Science and "Innovation Ministry".
This, in turn, means that the issue of transferring studies to non-state universities may become more complicated.
