It was determined that 22 employees of the Ministry of Higher Education are engaged in business activities

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
22 employees of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation were found to be engaged in entrepreneurial activities. Cases of employees of state institutions and organizations engaged in entrepreneurial activities were investigated by the Anti-Corruption Agency.
In particular: Higher education, science and innovations It was found out that 22 employees of the Ministry of Energy are founders of 23 business entities. 7 employees of the Ministry of Energy were found to be founders of 8 business entities. 22 employees of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation were found to be engaged in entrepreneurial activities.

The Anti-corruption Agency investigated the cases of employees of state institutions and organizations engaging in business activities. According to the investigations, it was found that the employees of several ministries and agencies were registered as founders of business entities. In particular: 22 employees of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation were found to be founders of 23 business entities.

  • In the Ministry of Ecology, Environmental Protection and Climate Change 72 employees have been working in 79 business entities.

    • The ministries critically discussed the submissions and issued warning letters to the employees. According to the agency, the cases of violations have now been removed.
