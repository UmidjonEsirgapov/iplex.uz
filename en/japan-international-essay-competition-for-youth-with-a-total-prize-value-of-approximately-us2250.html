Japan: International Essay Competition for Youth with a total prize value of approximately US$2,250

Important news for students and applicants.

Summary:
To submit your essay online, you must go HERE to the online registration page and complete the necessary steps.
Applications sent by post or email will not be accepted.
Additions: 1st through 3rd prize winners online will be invited to the winners' meeting. It also aims to encourage society to learn from young minds and think about how each of us can make a difference in the world.

Participation procedure:

1.

Essays can be submitted by candidates under 25 years of age (until June 15, 2021) in one of the following age categories:

  • a) Children (under 14);
  • B) Youth (15 to 25 under age).
2.

Essays must be 700 words or less in English or French, or 1600 characters or less in Japanese. Essays must be written with your name and the title of the essay at the top of the first page.

There are no specific rules regarding formatting (font style, size, line spacing, etc.). But essays must be submitted in MS Word (DOC / DOCX) or PDF format.

* Your name and essay title are not included in the word count limit.
3.

Essays must be submitted online. To submit your essay online, you must go to the online registration page HERE and complete the required steps. Applications by mail or email will not be accepted.

Teachers and youth directors may submit a set of essays from their class or group by creating a "school / organization account" on the online registration page.

* Only one application per person will be accepted.
* The organization cannot confirm receipt of essays.
4.

Essays must be original and unpublished. Plagiarized essays will be rejected.
5.

Essays must be written by one person. Co-authored essays will not be accepted.
6.

Copyright of the submitted essays is reserved to the organizer.

Prize value:

The following prizes are "Children" and Awarded in "Youth" categories:

  • 1st prize: Certificate, prize money 100,000 YEN (approx. USD 900) and gift ... 1 participant;
  • Second prize: Certificate, prize 50,000 YEN (approx. USD 450) and gift ... 3 1 participant;
  • 3rd prize: Certificate and gift ... 5 participants;
  • Honorary certificate: Certificate and gift ... 25 participants.

Extras:

  • Award winners 1 to 3 will be invited to an online Winners meeting.
  • Additional awards (Best School Award, School Promotion Award) will be awarded as appropriate.
  • All winners will be announced on this website on October 31, 2021 (Japan time).
  • Certificates and prizes will be mailed to winners in December 2021.
