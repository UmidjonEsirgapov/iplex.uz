Australia: Applications are now being accepted for the David Myers Research Scholarship.

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
Applications are being accepted for the David Myers Research Scholarship grant program, which is awarded for study in master's and doctoral courses at La Trobe University, located in Australia.
It is not necessary to apply separately to win the David Myers Research Scholarship grant.
To win the grant, you must be a La Trobe University PhD or Research Master. It is necessary to prepare an application for the courses.

La Trobe University in Australia is accepting applications for the David Myers Research Scholarship grant program for studying in master's and doctoral courses.

Grants are awarded to the most outstanding students admitted to research master's and PhD courses at this university.

A grant of 8,000 Australian dollars (approximately 5,556 US dollars) is given to the awarded student to pay for study materials and contract fees. there may be internal requirements. Find out about all the other requirements for admission and to win the scholarship by choosing the courses you need from the list here.

It is not necessary to apply separately to win the David Myers Research Scholarship. It is enough if you submit documents to master's or doctoral courses. If you are accepted and your performance is higher than other students, you will be awarded a grant.

Applications will be accepted until September 30, 2020.

In order to win a grant, you need to prepare an application for La Trobe University PhD or Research Master courses.

Once the application form is completed and agreed with a research supervisor working at La Trobe University, send your application to grs@latrobe.edu.au.
