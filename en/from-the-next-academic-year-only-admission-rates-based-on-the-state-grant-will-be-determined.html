From the next academic year, only admission rates based on the state grant will be determined

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the President's decision, by December 1, 2020, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, together with interested ministries and agencies, will: - determine admission indicators based on state grants only in the parameters of the state order for admission to higher education institutions; - independent determination of admission rates on the basis of payment-contract by higher education institutions.
In this case, the admission rates on the basis of payment-contract should not be less than the amount determined by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan; - submits justified proposals to the Government on the education of talented students in non-governmental higher education organizations on the basis of state grants.
