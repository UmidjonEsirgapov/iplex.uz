What are the validity periods of category certificates for educators?

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
The term of the qualification category of pedagogic staff in schools is 5 years.
This period is approved by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 572, and according to it, pedagogic personnel must undergo the next mandatory certification every 5 years.
If a pedagogue if he retires, his qualification category is considered to be lowered.

The term of qualification category of teaching staff in schools is 5 years. This term is approved by Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 572, and according to it, pedagogues must pass the next compulsory certification every 5 years. According to paragraph 6 of the decision No. 572, pedagogues who have reached retirement age will not be involved in the next attestation. Regardless of the expiration of the attestation period, their current qualification category will be preserved and they will continue to receive wages according to this category. — Official Data
