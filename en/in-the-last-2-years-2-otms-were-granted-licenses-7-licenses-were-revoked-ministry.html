During the past 2 years, the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation granted licenses to 2 higher education institutions, the licenses of 7 were revoked. This was announced at a briefing with the participation of the press secretary of the ministry Nazokat Abdukunduzova. During 2024, 412 applications for non-state educational services were submitted through the "License" information system, of which 55 were positively evaluated and relevant licenses were issued. during the briefing, information was also given about the organizations that did not eliminate the shortcomings identified during the studies conducted in non-state higher education organizations. As a result, the licenses of 6 universities were revoked in 2023 and 1 in 2024. As a result, about 8,000 students of non-governmental higher education organizations whose licenses were revoked were transferred to other universities, and not a single student's problem was left unsolved.
