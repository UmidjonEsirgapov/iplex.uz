Who can apply as an applicant?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The important thing is that you must have a certificate based on the 11th grade, it is not possible to study as a student after completing the 10th grade; 2.
Persons who have already received higher education can easily apply for the bachelor's degree again.
They, like the current students of school, high school, college, etc.

So, the following can apply for the bachelor's degree of HEIs:

1. Those who have completed the 11th grade of general secondary education.

This includes all schools, including presidential and creative schools, specialized and military schools, lyceums, gymnasiums, and so on. The most important thing is to have a certificate based on the 11th grade, it is not possible to study as a student after completing the 10th grade;

2. Graduates of secondary special and vocational educational institutions.

This includes academic lyceums, vocational colleges, vocational schools and technical schools. It must also be completed and a diploma awarded. Someone may have studied for 3 years, someone for 2 years;

3. Students studying in higher education.

This includes those who are currently studying in public or non-governmental higher education institutions in Uzbekistan, as well as abroad, in schools, high schools, colleges, etc. they can apply for studies with their diplomas. In case of admission, he chooses where he will study, if he wants, he can continue his current studentship, if he wants, he can study at the new place he entered.

Students' transfer to study does not cause any problems (for example, expulsion).

4. Those with higher education who have completed higher education institutions.

People who have already received higher education can easily re-apply for the bachelor's degree. They are just like today's students in school, high school, college, etc. they apply for studies with their diplomas, not with a university diploma. Another aspect here is that according to the current legislation, those who have received higher education once (regardless of whether they studied on a grant or contract) become a student for the second time only on the basis of a fee-contract. That is, they cannot apply for a grant, and even if they do, they will only study under a contract.

Persons from the above 4 categories can easily apply as applicants. — Read on infoedu.uz
