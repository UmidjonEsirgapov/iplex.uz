Bill Gates to young people 11 life tips

One of the most successful people in the world - Bill Gates is in constant communication with young people and tries to shape their views on education, work and life. Here are 11 important life tips that he gave to young people:

  1. Life is not an exhibition. Use it, manage your life and make it work.

  2. The world will judge you not by your self-esteem, but by what you do. Show results first.

  3. It is difficult to build an independent life without finishing school. Invest in science.

  4. Is your teacher demanding? Your boss will be even more demanding. Start preparing from school.

  5. Don't despise ordinary work. This is an opportunity. Don't look down on work.

  6. Did you make a mistake? Don't blame others. Draw conclusions and move forward.

  7. Your life is a responsibility to your parents. Thank them, appreciate them.

  8. There are not as many opportunities in life as in school. Cherish every confidence.

  9. Exams in life do not warn you in advance. Always be ready. be.

  10. Life on TV is not real. Let the heroes inspire you, but there is no success without hard work.

  11. Success is not free. It is earned with time, hard work and patience. — Source
