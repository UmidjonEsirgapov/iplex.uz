The dormitory allocation for students of the 2nd year and above will begin on August 15

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The first allocation for placement in student accommodation for students of the 2nd year and above will begin on August 15. The initial allocation will be made among the applications received from August 1 to August 15 of this year. the following are admitted to residences: pupils of "Mehribanlik Houses", orphans and students deprived of parental care; Children of families in need of social protection included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection", "Iron Register" and **"Women's Register"**, as well as students in the **"Youth Register"**; Students with group I and II disabilities; Children of families from one family studying bachelor's degree in the form of full-time education on the basis of two or more payment contracts; International science Olympiads, students who won the national science Olympiads held by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation; Students who won the republican stage of the "Student of the Year" and "Student Theater Studios" contests; Students who won the republican stage of the "Zakovat" intellectual game held among students of higher education institutions; Students who are the president of the student council, the floor captain in the student residence.
After that, they will be given to others based on the order of priorities.
