Transferring student studies: 5 answers to 5 important questions

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Transferring studies in mutually compatible and similar directions within one higher education institution is carried out by order of the rector.
Applications for transferring studies are submitted annually from July 15 to August 5.
In the following cases applications for transfer of study may be rejected: those who studied at foreign HEIs or their branches that do not have accreditation; to those who studied in a form of education other than the existing form of education; To those who have studied in the centers, departments, training centers, etc. of the Higher Education Institution; Those who want to transfer their studies before completing the 1st semester of the 1st year; When there is no suitable educational direction or form at the higher education institution, when the differences in subjects exceed 4; when contract students do not pay the fee on time; if the student's personal collection volume is not submitted on time; if there are no vacant places for relocation with a grant; When the material and technical base of HEIs is limited; when those admitted for the purpose want to study in another educational field, in another higher education institution; from evening education to part-time education and from one field of education to another field of evening education; from extramural education to full-time education and from one field of education to another field of extramural education; from one form of education to a form of special correspondence education and from a form of special correspondence education to another form of education; if the student has been accepted and studied on the basis of joint educational programs based on the mutual agreement of Uzbekistan and foreign higher education institutions. Who will transfer the students' studies?

Students can transfer their studies to compatible and similar fields and specialties, or unrelated fields and incompatible specialties.

Transferring studies in mutually compatible and similar fields within the same higher education institution is carried out by the order of the rector.

The transfer of studies in the following cases is carried out by the decision of the ministry under which the higher education institution is subordinate:

  • to another higher education institution affiliated to the same ministry in the same and similar directions (for example, transfer from the National University to the Technical University, both universities are affiliated to the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation);
  • within the framework of different ministries in the same and similar directions from one HEI to another HEI (for example, from the Tashkent State Law University under the Ministry of Justice to the University of World Economy and Diplomacy under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs).

In the following cases, the transfer of studies is carried out by the decision of the State Commission (the composition of the commission is here):

  • from foreign HEIs to HEIs of Uzbekistan;
  • in any case to non-similar educational areas and unsuitable majors.

When are applications submitted?

Applications for transfer of studies are submitted annually from July 15 to August 5.

Applications are reviewed from August 5 to August 30 of each year.

Application for transfer of permanent residence address of the student is submitted annually It is issued from December 20 to January 20 of the following year.

In this case, applications are reviewed annually from January 25 to February 15.

To whom are applications submitted?

If the transfer of studies is within one HEI, the application is submitted to this HEI, in all other cases, the application is submitted to the Regional Working Group organized by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation will be given.

Regional working groups have been adopted summarizes the documents and submits them to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

Study transfer is carried out free of charge.

What documents are submitted?

The following documents are submitted for transfer of studies:

  • an application indicating the field of study, language and form of the desired transfer;
  • copies of rating book or academic reference;
  • copy of passport or ID.

When the application is considered and transfer of studies is allowed, the student is placed in classes by order of the rector of the higher education institution, and the higher education institution where the student previously studied will send a questionnaire to send his personal collection folder. a collective folder is sent or given to the student himself.

Only students studying on a grant can apply for transfer of studies on the basis of a grant, and grant places must be available in the current year's admission quota of the HEI to which the application is made.

Those who study on the basis of a grant in the quota allocated to the blind and disabled will be transferred on the basis of a grant.

Those who want to transfer their studies from foreign HEIs to Uzbekistan HEIs submit a special test (in the form of a test or written work or a professional (creative) exam).
