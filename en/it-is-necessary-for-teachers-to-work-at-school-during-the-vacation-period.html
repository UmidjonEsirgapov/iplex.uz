According to the legislation of Uzbekistan, teaching staff in general secondary education institutions must work on the basis of the annual work plan even during the vacation period. This requirement is important for the more effective organization of pedagogical activity.

Requirements based on the Labor Code

  • According to Article 114 of the Labor Code, the employee must perform tasks according to the established work schedule or employment contract.
  • Article 125 of the Labor Code stipulates the obligation of the employer to keep track of working hours and formalize it.
  • Article 154 of the Labor Code

The main directions of the annual work plan of teachers

  • Planning and carrying out the educational process.
  • Organizing individual classes with students.
  • Providing methodical support of the educational process.
  • Students' abilities development.
  • Organizing pedagogical activities and working on oneself.

Tasks to be performed during the vacation period

  • Performing methodical and organizational aspects of the educational process in accordance with the annual work plan.
  • Increasing their knowledge level through individual work with students.
  • Performing training and analytical work necessary for quality organization of the educational process increase.

Conclusion

Teachers' work at school during the vacation period should be carried out in accordance with the requirements of the annual work plan and employment contract. This activity is important for improving the quality of the educational process and effectively organizing pedagogical processes. — Official Data
