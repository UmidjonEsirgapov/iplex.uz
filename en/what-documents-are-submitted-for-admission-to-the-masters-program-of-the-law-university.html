What documents are submitted for admission to the Master's program of the Law University?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Starting from the 2020/2021 academic year, applicants for admission to the master's degree program of the Tashkent State University of Law will be introduced to the registration procedure through the website of the Unified Interactive State Services of the Republic of Uzbekistan or the website of the State Test Center.
it is strictly forbidden to make changes and additions to the information after the expiry of the term.
On the basis of the results of the entrance exams, the original documents of the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan (hereinafter referred to as the State Commission) on the recommendation of the admission of the applicants to the TDUU master's degree are adopted within three working days copies are submitted by the applicant to the TDUU admission committee.

In order to ensure the implementation of the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On additional measures for the fundamental improvement of legal education and science in the Republic of Uzbekistan" No. a draft decision was developed.

Based on this draft decision, the process of submitting documents for admission to the master's degree at the Tashkent State University of Law is detailed below.

From the 2020/2021 academic year, applicants for admission to the master's degree at the Tashkent State University of Law can register through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal of the Republic of Uzbekistan or the website of the State Test Center

Applicants to study at TDUU master's degree register through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal of the Republic of Uzbekistan or the official website of the State Test Center.

To register, the applicant electronically enters the following information in the original copy of the documents through the special program of the State Test Center:

  • passport series, number, personal identification number of an individual;
  • the name, series, number of the educational institution where the diploma of higher education (or an extract from the diploma - for those who graduated from the higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan with a state grant) was issued;
  • the series, number, time of issuance of documents confirming a certain privilege (advanced international test systems on the level of foreign language proficiency (TOYeFL, IYeLS, Sambridge Qualifications, TestDAF, Goethe English Certificates, TSF, etc.), a document on the awarding of state scholarships by the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan or named) - if available;
  • on the basis of the recommendation of the first head of the relevant state body, the recommendation of the first head of the relevant state body, signed in the established order and formalized on the letterhead of the state body series, number, date of issue;
  • series, number, date of issue of the recommendation of the first supervisor, signed in the established order for pedagogues and supervisors with a basic higher legal education, who have worked at legal technical schools for at least three years and formalized on the form of the legal technical school.

When registration is carried out through the portal of the Unified Interactive State Services or the website of the State Test Center, the data is uploaded by the applicant independently and the documents and information The applicant is personally responsible for its truthfulness and correctness.

After the registration deadline, it is strictly forbidden to make changes and additions to the information.

Daily information about the number of registered applicants is posted by the admission commission on the website of TDUU and DTM in terms of specialties, form of education and language.

Based on the results of the entrance exams, the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan (hereinafter referred to as the State Commission) has adopted a decision on the recommendation of applicants for admission to the TDUU master's degree, within three working days, the original copies of the commission documents will be submitted by the applicant to the TDUU admission.
