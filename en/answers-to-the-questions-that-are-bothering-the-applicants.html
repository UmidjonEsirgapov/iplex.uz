Come to the test in a white shirt or something similar. is it necessary?

✅ ANSWER: no, it is not necessary. There are no clothing restrictions. Come in comfortable clothes of a color and style (for example, sportswear). And we hope that you will have the sense not to come in shorts, slippers, and a T-shirt within the framework of culture.

What documents should you bring to the test?

✅ ANSWER: To enter the tests, it is enough to have a passport (ID card) and an "Applicant's permit" printed on paper.

What color pen should you bring to the test? need?

✅ ANSWER: No way! You don't need to bring a pen to the tests, they won't put it anyway. A pen, blank paper to use as a draft and water will be provided inside.

Is it allowed to bring a watch to the test?

✅ ANSWER: Yes, it is allowed. The only main condition is that your watch should be a simple mechanical watch. Electronics and smart-watches are not allowed.

✅ ANSWER: no, it will not be refunded. 165,000 soums received from the graduates of the previous year was for entering the test. This money will not be returned to those who did not enroll. — Official Data
