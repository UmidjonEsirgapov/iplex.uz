Brief about the scholarship program:

The Singapore International Graduate Award (abbreviated as SINGA) is a program in collaboration with the Singapore State Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), National University of Singapore (NUS) and Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). The preparation for the offered doctoral courses is conducted in English in a laboratory of the candidate's choice, in one of the A*STAR research institutes, as well as the above-mentioned universities of NTU, NUS or SUTD.

Students are supervised by renowned and world-renowned researchers in these laboratories. After successful completion of the courses, students are awarded a doctorate degree by NTU, NUS or SUTD.

Fields of research:

The research areas within the doctoral program are mainly divided into two categories:

  • Biomedical Sciences;
  • Physical Science and Engineering.

Requirements for candidates:

All international students who are passionate about research and achieving high academic results are welcome to apply for this program and have the following requirements:

  • Must have good written and spoken English skills. Possession of a language certificate is optional Not mandatory for the grant, but universities offering the course may require it;
  • Having good report marks from academic assessors;

Note: The above eligibility criteria are not exhaustive. During the evaluation process, A*STAR may apply additional selection criteria based on applicable grant award procedures. These regulations are subject to change from time to time without notice.

Benefits of the grant:

The grant supports doctoral studies of up to 4 years and funds:

  • Course fees;
  • S$2,000 per month (approx. US$1,486) scholarship and this scholarship amount can be increased to S$2,500 (approx. US$1,858) after passing the qualifying examination;
  • A one-time S$1,500 (approx. US$1,115) air ticket stipend;
  • A one-time S$1,000 accommodation allowance (approx. 743 USD);

Required documents:

  1. Scanned copy of passport;
  2. Passport size photograph;
  3. Undergraduate academic transcripts, diploma or certificate letter or/and graduate academic transcripts, diploma or certificate letter;
  4. 2 letters of recommendation.
