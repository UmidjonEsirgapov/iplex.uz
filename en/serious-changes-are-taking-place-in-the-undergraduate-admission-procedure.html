On May 6 of this year, in the presence of President Shavkat Mirziyoyev, at a meeting dedicated to the issues of improving the system of admission to higher education institutions and the formation of state orders, it was proposed to introduce the following reforms: On the formation of state orders (grant quotas): the parameters of the state order the practice of distribution in the section of universities will be abolished, and the procedure of approval will be introduced in the section of bachelor's courses and master's specialties; the state order for social and humanitarian directions will be reduced, and these quotas will be distributed among engineering, medicine, exact and natural sciences as a priority; Starting from 2025, the parameters of the state order will be approved for a period of 4 years (actually 1 year) in accordance with the forecast of the training and demand for highly educated personnel; on the basis of the material and technical base of universities, the composition of professors and teachers, forms the general admission parameters for the next academic year (not divided into grants and contracts) for undergraduate majors and master's majors; On the basis of the state order, the grant places are made in accordance with the selection of the applicants according to the sequence of points accumulated in the republic for each direction.
