Nurafshan State University will be opened for fifteen thousand students

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the information provided by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, during the visit of President Shavkat Mirziyoyev to the city of Nurafshon this year, the officials were given the task of starting the Nurafshon State University for 15,000 students in the next academic year, involving foreign experts.
The latest information on this news was also confirmed by Kun.uz Online.
Education at the new university will be organized on the basis of the credit-module system from the 2023/24 academic year.
