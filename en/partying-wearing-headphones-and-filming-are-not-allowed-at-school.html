According to the rules approved by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, it is now prohibited to hold birthday parties in the school grounds. This measure was introduced in order to strengthen discipline and improve the quality of education in educational institutions.

Prohibited situations:

  • Holding a birthday party on the school premises

  • Wearing headphones during class

  • Posting audio or video materials that degrade the reputation of teachers and students on social networks

  • Violence or harassment against others

What happens if the rule is broken?

  1. The student's parents in writing will be warned

  2. In repeated cases:

    • The student will be included in the institution's internal control list

    • Parents will be called to school

    • A preventive interview will be held with the participation of an inspector-psychologist

These rules are mandatory for all general secondary education institutions and their compliance is under control. — Source
