A "Chess Scholarship" will be established for pupils and students

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Schoolchildren and students who win these competitions will be encouraged with a "Chess scholarship" in the amount of five times the base calculation amount every month throughout the year.
Decision of the President (08.05.2023 PQ-150) basically, "Chess scholarship" will be established from January 1, 2024.
Systematic holding of "School League", "Academic Lyceum League", "Professional Educational Institutions League" and "Student League" competitions in general secondary, secondary special, professional and higher educational institutions in three stages every year.

Pupils and students who win these competitions will be encouraged with a "Chess scholarship" in the amount of five times the basic amount every month during the year. Starting from January 1, 2024, the "Chess Scholarship" will be established.
