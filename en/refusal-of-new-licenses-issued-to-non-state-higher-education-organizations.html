Rejection of licenses issued to non-state higher education organizations

August 29, 2025 from 06:19 A technical failure was observed in the license.gov.uz information system until 09:53. Therefore, during the review of the applications of some non-governmental higher education organizations, positive conclusions were formed based on an error by the system.

This situation was recorded as a technical error, and its causes are currently being thoroughly studied together with the relevant agencies. Additional information on the final results will be announced to the public.

Which educational organizations are not permitted?

The following non-governmental higher education organizations are not granted permission for new educational directions, specialties, forms of education and activity locations:

  • "University of Business and Science" LLC - for distance education;

  • "Asia International University" LLC - for some distance education and a new activity location in the city of Urganch, Khorezm region;

  • "Economics and Pedagogical University" NTM - medical courses and master's specialties, distance education forms and Surkhandarya region, to the new business location in Denov district;

  • "Sharq-University" LLC - for banking, accounting, evening and distance education;

  • "Profi University" LLC - for jurisprudence, distance forms and all master's degrees.

Important warning

The information announced by these organizations on the above directions and forms of education is unfounded.

Conclusion

Due to a technical failure, incorrect information was formed in the licenses of some higher education organizations. The Ministry is eliminating such errors and urges citizens to trust only the information provided by official sources. — Source
