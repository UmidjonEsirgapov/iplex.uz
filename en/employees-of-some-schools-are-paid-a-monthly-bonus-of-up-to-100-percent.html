Employees of some schools are paid a monthly bonus of up to 100 percent

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
From September 1, 2024, the procedure for paying employees of specialized sports schools up to 100% monthly bonus will be introduced.
Procedures to be implemented in specialized sports schools According to the decree, until September 1, 2024: Optimized training plans for specialized sports schools will be developed.

From September 1, 2024, up to 100 percent of the monthly salary for employees of specialized sports schools the procedure for paying premiums will be introduced. This was determined in the decree of the President "On additional measures for the organization of sports schools specialized in boxing and gymnastics in Tashkent" adopted on June 22 of this year.

Procedures introduced in specialized sports schools

According to the decree, until September 1, 2024:

  • Optimized curricula for specialized sports schools will be developed. Special attention will be paid to the in-depth teaching of the history of Uzbekistan and the English language, as well as the teaching of generalized natural science and sports psychology in modern methods.
  • Teach a second foreign language in specialized sports schools. rooms are organized.

In accordance with the decree, in specialized sports schools

  • Educational process is organized according to special educational programs within the framework of grades 5-11.
  • International and local trainer-selectors are involved in admission processes.
  • Educational process Number of students It is carried out in classes from 12 to 24 people.
  • The total limited number of students is set to 168.

Skills classes and competitions

Every year from the 2024/2025 school year, skills classes will be held with the participation of local and foreign masters of sports, trainers and famous athletes. According to the decree, student-athletes of specialized sports schools will directly participate in the competitions of the championship and cup of Uzbekistan as a separate team. — Source
