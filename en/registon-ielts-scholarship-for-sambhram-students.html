1. General information

The contest "Registon IELTS Grant - for Sambhram students" is a special educational project organized in cooperation between the Registon training center and Sambhram University. is to select talented and aspiring young people and encourage them with free studies and certificates.

The selection consists of two stages:

  • 1st stage - an online test in English, conducted through the oliygoh.uz platform. All participants participate in this phase. Students who score 60% and above will go to the next stage of the competition.

  • 2nd stage is a Mock IELTS exam which will be conducted offline for selected students. At this stage, the winners will be determined based on the final scores.

At the end of the competition:

  • 5 students with the highest scores will be offered 1 month of free study at the Registan educational center;

  • The next 10 students will receive a "Certificate of Winner"

  • All participants who took part in the competition will be awarded with a "Certificate of Participation".

The organization and evaluation of the competition will be carried out by a commission with the participation of teachers of the Registon educational center and representatives of Sambhram University.

2. The purpose of the contest

The main purpose of the contest "Registon IELTS Grant - for Sambhram Students" is to determine the knowledge and skills of the English language among Sambhram University students, to increase their motivation to work on themselves, and to reward the most talented and active students.

The following tasks are also performed through the contest:

  • Increasing awareness of IELTS exam among students and assessing their preparation skills;

  • Stimulating interest in learning English in depth;

  • Creating opportunities for students to test their knowledge levels and get to know the real IELTS environment;

  • Talented expanding their educational opportunities by identifying students and getting free education at the Registon educational center;

  • enriching student portfolios through certificates and motivating them to study at the next levels.

The competition is not only a competition, but also a platform that encourages to work on oneself, increases competitiveness in education and leads to individual achievements.
