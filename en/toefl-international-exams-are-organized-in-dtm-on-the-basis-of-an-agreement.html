"TOEFL" international exams are organized in DTM on the basis of an agreement

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
This was announced at a press conference held in AOKA with the participation of officials of the State Test Center.
Report and analysis of tests conducted by the State Test Center in AOKA in 2020-2021 to assess the level of knowledge and mastery of a foreign language and other issues related to the field "TOEFL ITP", "TOEIC", "TOEFL IBT" international exams conducted by the international educational organization ETS (Educational Testing Service) will be organized on the basis of a contract at the State Test Center from the second quarter of 2021," said the head of the DTM department. This was announced at a press conference held at AOKA with the participation of officials of the State Test Center.

A press conference was held on the report and analysis of the tests conducted by the State Test Center in 2020-2021 to assess the level of foreign language knowledge and acquisition, as well as other issues related to the field. provided information about the planned works.

"It is planned to implement a new format multi-level test system for foreign languages by the end of 2021.

This multi-level test system will be compatible with the international education system exams and will have a single format containing B1-B2-C1 levels of the CEFR scale. In this, new evaluation criteria will be developed that can evaluate all skills.

From the second quarter of 2021, it is planned to organize the international exams "TOEFL ITP", "TOEIC", "TOEFL IBT" held by the international educational organization ETS (Educational Testing Service) at the State Test Center on the basis of a contract," said the head of the DTM department. — Read on infoedu.uz
