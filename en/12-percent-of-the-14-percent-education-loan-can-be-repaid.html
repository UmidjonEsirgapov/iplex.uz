There is a tax benefit for persons who pay the contract of a student studying in higher education institutions on a fee-contract basis. In this case, 12% income tax is not charged on the money spent on the payment-contract amount.

✔️This benefit can be used even if an educational loan has been received.

For example:

  • You received an educational loan at 14% for the value of a one-year contract. Then you can get a 12% income tax refund from the tax authorities on the funds used to pay off this loan.

12% income tax can also be refunded on contract payments made by cash, plastic or money transfer. — Official Data
