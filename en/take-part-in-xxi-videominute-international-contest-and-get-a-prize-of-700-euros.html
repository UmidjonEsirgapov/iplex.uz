XXI VIDEOMINUTE INTERNATIONAL COMPETITION (2021-2022)
Dpt's cultural field Universidad de Zaragoza Department of Culture and Social Affairs, in collaboration with Cinemaremagnum Servicios Audiovisuales , in the field of video creation and animation, according to the following rules In order to encourage creativity, XXI Videominute international competition has been announced.

Requirements for candidates:

All those who want to participate in the competition can participate individually or in groups, if they submit works with a maximum duration of 1 minute (60 seconds), including a credit name.

Works that exceed this deadline can be accepted.

Works submitted in other editions of the competition will not be accepted.

Note: The topic of Videominuto is a free competition, artistic/documentary and animated works can be submitted.

Trailers, teasers and advertising spots will not be accepted under any circumstances.

IMPORTANT: All submitted in a language other than Spanish works must besubtitled inSpanish or English.

The Participant will in any case be responsible for any claim related to the violation of the image, data protection, honor and privacy rights of third parties.

The organization reserves the right to reject proposals that may be considered offensive, offensive or in violation of the law.

Submit a video:

Online only: Click here to complete the form and upload your video

Deadline:

The registration period starts on 29 October 2021 at 15:00 and remains in Spain March 23, 2022 ends at 18:00.

Evaluation criteria:

Jury members are selected by Dpt. Nationally and internationally renowned video creators and experts include the University of Zaragoza Department of Culture and Social Affairs. This jury will finalize any shortcomings or doubts that may arise in the selection process with their experience and opinion.

The jury reserves the right not to award the awards if any work is not deemed worthy. The decisions made by the jury are final.

The best 25 works will be selected for the finals and this condition of the finalist will be communicated to the respective authors.

The awarding of prizes and the prediction of the winners will be communicated to all interested parties and will be announced through common means of communication.

Awards:

Winners will be awarded with a diploma and the following prizes:

  • Best INTERNATIONAL VIDEOMINUTE 2022 prize : 700€
  • Prize for the best SPAIN VIDEOMINUTE 2022 : 700€
  • Best ARAGON VIDEOMINUTE 2022 prize : 700€ Note: These prizes are subject to tax according to law

Important: Winners grant the Universidad de Zaragoza the right to record, collect, reproduce and broadcast the submitted works, retaining their rights as creators of their works.
