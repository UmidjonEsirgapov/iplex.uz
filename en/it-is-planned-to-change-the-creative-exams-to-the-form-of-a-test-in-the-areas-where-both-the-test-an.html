It is expected that creative exams will be canceled this year in 13 directions where both tests and creative exams are taken for admission to higher education institutions.

According to the representative of the ministry, there will be no exams for applicants in this direction. They take a test in two subjects.

«The first subject is clear. As the second subject for the test, the subject of block 3, which was included in the test process last year, will be given. For example, last year the 3rd subject was English, but this year it will be the second subject," said Khalikov.

He added that only the order for creative subjects will remain unchanged.
