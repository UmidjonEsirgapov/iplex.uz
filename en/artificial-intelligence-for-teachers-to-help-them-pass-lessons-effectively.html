Also, the foreign adviser of the rector of the institute, Islam Hassan Mahmoud Hassan Elhadary recommended a number of artificial intelligence tools that will help teachers to conduct lessons effectively. Including:

– Century (for lesson planning)

– Gradescope (for grading automation)

– Dreambox (customized learning platform for math tutoring)

– Edpuzzle (for turning videos into interactive lessons, integrating quizzes and discussions)

– Quizlet (for summarizing lessons and conducting quizzes, adapting to the learning speed and needs of students)

– Diffit (an assistant teacher platform to remind teachers of messages and information)
