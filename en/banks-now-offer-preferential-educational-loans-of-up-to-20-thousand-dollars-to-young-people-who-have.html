Education is an investment in the future: great opportunities for young people who have entered the top-300 universities

The President at a meeting with young people He noted that the noble idea of "Education is an investment of the future" is becoming a great movement in our society. Recently, the activities of the El-Yurt Umid Foundation have been improved and new procedures have been introduced to support young people.

Now, young people who have entered the top 300 universities will be provided with convenient opportunities to cover their study and living expenses.

Quotas for STEM and driver fields will be increased

Special attention to STEM and other important areas

According to the new regulation, the quotas allocated to driver sectors such as STEM fields - science, technology, engineering and mathematics, as well as energy, transport, urbanization, architecture, construction, agriculture and water management will be increased several times.

This will encourage young people to take up modern and promising professions and contribute to the development of the country.

Preferential education up to 20 thousand dollars loan

New financial support from banks

Uzbekistan banks now provide preferential educational loans of up to 20,000 dollars to young people who have entered the top-300 universities. This loan serves to ease the costs of education and living for young people.

Preferential loans are planned to be provided for young people on favorable terms and for a long period of time.

These new procedures aimed at supporting young people will help them get quality education in the future, become mature specialists and contribute to the development of the country.
