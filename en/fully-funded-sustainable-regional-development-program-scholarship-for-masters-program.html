Sustainable Territorial Development Program Scholarship

Deadline: April 26, 2022.

Applications are now being accepted for the new STeDe (Sustainable Territorial Development) Masters Scholarships for students from non-EU countries. Studies will begin in September 2022. The organizers offer 20 scholarships.

STeDe offers students a 2-year master's program (120 credit hours). The whole course and study takes place in 7 universities (each student studies in 3 different countries):

1st semester - Università di Padova (Italy)

2nd semester - K.U. Leuven (Belgium)

3rd semester - Université Paris (France)

4th semester - at Panthéon-Sorbonne (France) or another European university.

Stipend amount: €24,000 - for the entire master's program (stipend paid monthly). Students can also apply for a grant to cover transportation costs.

Required documents:

1) Copy of passport or ID.

2) Proof of residence.

3) Diploma(s) and other award certificate(s) + copy of diploma supplement IF applicable.

4) Copy of transcript.

5) 2 references (reference letter) copy.

6) CV (Europass format ONLY, download the template).

7) Personal statement to be completed in English (download the template).

8) Language diplomas or certificates (if any).

All documents must be submitted in *.pdf format.
