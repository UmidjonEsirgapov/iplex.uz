Individuals can now get a remote refund of 12% income tax directed to the "contract" payment. This process is conveniently done through the Tax mobile application.

How to get a tax refund?

Download the Tax mobile application:

  • Download the application from the Tax mobile application page.

Prepare the necessary documents:

  • Scan the contract and payment documents in one PDF file.

Submit the declaration:

  • Select the "Tax credit refund" service through the application.
  • Send the declaration by attaching the contract and payment documents üìé.
  • Information about your salary and other income will be generated automatically.

Submit an overpayment refund application:

  • After sending the declaration, select the "Overpayment refund" service in the Cashback and benefits section and submit the application via Face ID. confirm:
    • Verify your identity through the application Face ID.

    Advantages of this service

    • It is carried out remotely: No need to go to the tax authorities.
