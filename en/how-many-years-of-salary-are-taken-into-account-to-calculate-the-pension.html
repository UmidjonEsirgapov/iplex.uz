According to clause 78 of the regulation "On the procedure for appointing and paying state pensions", the monthly salary for any five consecutive years during the last ten years of work is taken into account when calculating the pension
Irrespective of the existence of breaks in work, this period is considered the basis for the appointment of a pension.
