Loewe foundation announces the 15th season of this competition for talented and innovative craftsmen. By participating in the contest, they will receive €5,000 and the opportunity to participate with their work in an exhibition to be held in Seoul, South Korea in the spring of 2022.

Requirements:

  • Candidates 18 to be older than and professionally engaged in crafts;
  • All works can express an artistic purpose in addition to technical skills; be original, hand-made/partially innovative;
  • Works should be created in the last 5 years and should not be gwinner at any exhibition before;
  • Works related to pottery, bookbinding, enamel, jewelry, lacquer, metalwork, tanning, textiles, glasswork, carpentry or other applied arts;
  • 2 to 5 pictures or optionally a video of the work;
  • Brief information about the development (should not exceed 200 words);

You can participate in the competition individually or as a group. More information HERE.

Required documents and information for participation in the competition:

  • Name of the participant/group, country, place and date of birth, telephone number, e-mail and postal address;
  • Brief biography of the participant;
  • Participant's 5 best-designed combined portfolios;
  • Size (mm, cm, m), what raw materials it is made of, weight, category and estimated value (in euros, for insurance purposes);
  • Passport copy;
  • Brief information about the development (must not exceed 200 words);
  • 2 to 5 pictures of works. Photos should be in jpg or png format, 800x600 pixels, 2MB in size;
  • 1-minute video of the development taken in various forms (optional).

All information and documents for the competition must be submitted in English. The results of the competition will be announced in the spring of 2022.
