"Viki Loves Uzbekistan" marathon organized by LLC "Diplomat International School" and Wikischool Uzbekistan!

Write articles about Uzbekistan from November 1 to November 30 a marathon will be held. Any registered participant in the marathon can take part, but only school and high school students can apply for prizes. The winners will be awarded with certificates and valuable gifts.

Prizes:

Among school and lyceum students:

  • 1st place — 3,000,000 soums and a certificate;
  • 2nd place — 2,000,000 soums and certificate;
  • 3rd place — 1,000,000 soums and a certificate.

Rules:

  • Any user registered on Wikipedia can participate in the marathon;
  • Only school and high school students can apply for prizes in the marathon;
  • Participants who have written at least 25 articles in the marathon can apply for prizes;

More information about the rules for the article can be obtained here.

Evaluation procedure:

  • Only articles submitted to Fountain will be evaluated by judges.
  • Articles created by judges and included in Fountain will be given 0 points.
  • Each contest article that meets the requirements of the marathon is evaluated for 1 point.
