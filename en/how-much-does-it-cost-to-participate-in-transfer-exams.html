How much does it cost to participate in transfer exams?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:

Currently, BHM is 340,000 soums, and half of it is 170,000 soums.

To participate in the transfer of studies exams, they must pay half the amount of the basic calculation amount.

Currently, BHM is 340,000 soums, and half of it is 170,000 soums.

Those who fall into the following categories are exempt from payment:

  • persons with disabilities;
  • persons on full state support (graduates of "Mehribonlik" House and Children's Town)
  • persons registered in the information system of the "Unified Register of Social Protection" and their children;
  • youth included in the "Youth Register"

Earlier, it was reported that the acceptance of applications for transfer of studies will start from July 15 — Official Data
