10 most effective resources for IELTS exam preparation

Working with good resources is very important to master English and get a high score in the IELTS exam. Below we introduce 10 free and reliable resources for you to effectively prepare for each section of the IELTS exam.

1. Elc.polyu.edu.hkThis site has detailed information, tips and exercises for all sections of the IELTS exam. It helps in better understanding of the exam format.

2. Splendid-speaking.comContains interviews and advice with successful students. It is especially useful for preparing for the speaking section.

3. Takeielts.britishcouncil.org This site provided by the British Council is the official and trusted source for IELTS. Offers a variety of articles, tips and quizzes.

4. ielts.allearsenglish.comThis resource is full of IELTS exercises and is very useful for improving your English through practice. There are exercises for each section.

5. Netlanguages.com provides free practice sessions. Very useful for strengthening your knowledge.

6. Ieltsforfree.comHere are free and complete IELTS exam tests. You can practice each section separately.

7. Examenglish.comis a universal site where you can prepare for other exams such as TOEFL, TOEIC, CAE, FCE in addition to IELTS.

8. Itests.com offers free IELTS practice tests after registration. You can test your knowledge in real exam conditions.

9. Ielts-exam.netThis is one of the largest IELTS sites and provides many free tests and exercises.

10. You can view and download scanned IELTS preparation books for free at Scribd.com. Get more knowledge by reading the best books.
