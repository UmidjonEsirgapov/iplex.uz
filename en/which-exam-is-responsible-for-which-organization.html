Which organization is responsible for which exam?

According to the information published by the Education Evaluation Agency, various exams held in Uzbekistan and their responsible organizations are defined as follows.

Professional (creative) entrance exams for the bachelor's degree

These exams are organized by higher education institutions (HEIs).

Admission to master's specialties

The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and higher education institutions are responsible for the admission process to the master's degree.

Admission to clinical residency

Clinical residency exams in the field of medicine are conducted by the Ministry of Health and Medical Higher Education Institutions.

Admission to the second specialty

The task of submitting documents and organizing exams for the second specialty is also the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovations and HEIs are responsible.

Transfer between state HEIs

The issue of transferring students from one HEI to another is carried out by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and HEIs.

Admission to Islamic educational institutions

Islamic educational institutions and admission processes to madrasas are organized by the Office of Muslims of Uzbekistan and relevant educational institutions.

Certification of pedagogues of secondary special and professional educational organizations

The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation is responsible for this process.

Certification of pedagogues of preschool, general secondary and out-of-school educational organizations

Such attestations are carried out by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.
