What are the minimum passing scores for applicants?

Important news for applicants who want to enter state higher education institutions — the minimum thresholds for passing scores have been announced. These scores are approved by the State Admission Commission every year and serve as the basis for participation in the competition.

Current minimum passing scores for 2025

According to the relevant statement of the State Admission Commission, the following minimum passing scores are set for admission to higher education institutions for the 2025/2026 academic year:

  • ​​​​ According to the state grant – 68.0 points (This is equal to 36% of the maximum 189.0 points)

  • ✅ According to the payment contract – 56.7 points (This is equal to 30% of the maximum 189.0 points)

Who is in the competition can't participate?

We remind you that if the applicant does not get the specified minimum passing score, he cannot participate in the competition for higher education institutions. Therefore, these scores are not only an official indicator, but also a determining criterion.

Are these scores the previous year's indicators?

Yes, these minimum passing scores were approved last year and are not expected to change this year. That is, according to the current situation, these points remain valid.

Conclusion

It is very important for applicants to be aware of the minimum passing points in order not to miss the opportunity to participate in the competition. In 2025, the requirements of 68 points for the grant and 56.7 points for the contract remain. Every point is important in order not to miss the chance! — Source
