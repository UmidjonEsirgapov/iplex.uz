Registration of candidates for the State Security Service Academy has begun

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
In the 2024-2025 academic year, the registration of applicants to the Academy of the State Security Service of the Republic of Uzbekistan named after Amir Temur has begun for the selection of candidates for the full-time undergraduate education.
The Academy of the State Security Service Applicants who wish to participate in the candidate selection tests can register online through the my.uzbmb.uz site until November 10 of this year.
The selection of candidates is carried out in accordance with the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 241 of April 27, 2021, and the candidates must meet the requirements specified in paragraph 3 of the regulation approved by this decision.
