We invite everyone who is implementing good projects all over the world to participate in the "We are together" International competition organized by Russia.

The purpose of the award is to reward citizens and organizations for their outstanding contribution to solving global humanitarian problems.

This season's competition is dedicated to contributing to the fight against the spread of COVID-19. You can participate in the competition as a group or individually.

Benefits:

  • The winners of the nomination will be awarded $500,000.
  • The costs of the award ceremony held in Moscow will be covered

Nominations:

  • "Person of the Year"
  • "Education and Technology"
  • "Medicine"
  • "Business"
  • "Personal contribution of a citizen"
