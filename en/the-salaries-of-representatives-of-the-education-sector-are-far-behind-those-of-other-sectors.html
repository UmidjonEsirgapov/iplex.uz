The salaries of representatives of the education sector are far behind those of other sectors

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
The average salary increase of teachers from 2020 to 2024 was only 1,545,000 soums. and the average monthly salary in these areas is 14 million 488.8 thousand soums.
The highest figure is in the field of higher education (8 million 115.6 thousand soums), while the salary of employees in the preschool education system is significantly lower, and their monthly salary is 1 million 874.5 thousand soums on average.

Statistics Agency has published statistical data on the industries with the highest and lowest salaries as of January-September 2024. In the third quarter of 2024, the average monthly salary of Uzbeks will be 5.1 million soums, and this indicator reflects positive growth in many areas. However, for many representatives in the field of education, this increase remains at a much lower level.

According to the data, the average salary of employees in the field of education is 3 million 484.7 thousand soums. According to this indicator, the education sector has not risen from the bottom in recent years. The average salary increase of teachers from 2020 to 2024 was only 1 million 545 thousand soums. There is also a gap between salaries in the main branches of education. The highest indicator is in the field of higher education (8 million 115.6 thousand soums), while the salary of employees in the preschool education system is significantly lower, their average monthly salary is 1 million 874.5 thousand soums.
