On December 1, 2024, national certificate exams in mathematics, physics, history and geography were held. The results of these exams were announced on December 25. Appeal applications were accepted and decisions were announced for applicants who were dissatisfied with the results.

Appeal process

  1. Acceptance of applications:

    • Appeal applications of applicants were accepted within five working days after the announcement of the exam results.

  2. Review of applications:

    • All applications were carefully considered by the appeal commission.

  3. Decision announcement:

    • The decision of the appeal commission was placed in the personal cabinets of the applicants.

Instructions for viewing the results

  • Applicants should go to the "My applications" section of the my.gov.uz site.
  • In this section, you can familiarize yourself with the decisions of the Appeals Commission.

Conclusion
