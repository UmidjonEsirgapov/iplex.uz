Tests and selection process for the 2025/2026 academic year

Tests for applicants for the 2025/2026 academic year will be held on July 14-28 in two shifts a day. After the tests are completed, the applicants will have the opportunity to choose higher education institutions and courses knowing their scores.

Tests first, then the selection

First of all, applicants will test their knowledge by taking tests. For example, applicants who wish to apply to the field of jurisprudence will take a test in law, foreign language and compulsory subjects.

After the tests are completed, the applicants will know their points and depending on the results, they will choose which higher education institution and which field to apply for.

15 days will be given for the selection

After the tests are fully completed, the applicants A period of 15 days is given to make a choice. This period begins on July 29, and the applicant chooses a higher education institution, a bachelor's field of study and a form of education based on his scores.
