Uzbeks get free education in 5 areas on the Coursera platform

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Courses will be given with subtitles in Uzbek.
Through this, courses that are interesting and important for young people can be selected and free of charge. young people receive free education in 5 areas.

Courses are given with Uzbek subtitles. Also, the country will buy 10,000 licenses that give the right to use all the content of the platform. src="https://www.spot.uz/media/img/2022/10/Zgsmt216666929019473_l.jpg"/>Photo: Shutterstock. They can participate in online courses on this platform in 3 main areas - Frontend, Python course, IT-English.

After that, in November, "IT Park" conducted a survey on the selection of 3 courses on the "Coursera" platform.

Through this, courses that are interesting and important for young people can be selected and it is determined that they can participate for free.

According to the agreement with the "Coursera" company, within the second stage of the "One million Uzbek programmers" project, young people from Uzbekistan will receive free education in 5 areas. "Spot" reporter reports about it.

These five courses include:

  • AI Business;
  • Google Cloud;
  • Data Science;
  • Career Brand;
  • Coding.

"Through this, young people will be able to obtain international IT certificates and the program will be divided into two. free and paid courses. The free part will be completely free for students and young people," says Rustam Karimjonov, Deputy Minister of Digital Technologies.

Uzbekistan will purchase 10,000 licenses for the use of all content of the platform in 2023.

As a result, 12,000 Uzbeks will receive professional courses. It will be possible to participate in the programs of 275 international universities and companies.
