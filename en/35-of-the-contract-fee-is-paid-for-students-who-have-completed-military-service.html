35% of the contract fee is paid for students who have completed military service

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
An important privilege has been introduced for young people who have completed military service in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
Uzbekistan Youth Union will cover 35 percent of the contract amount for students studying on a contract basis.
Armed Forces of Uzbekistan those who have completed military service; ✅ Those who have a letter of recommendation from the command of military units; ✅ Students admitted to the higher educational institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan on the basis of a fee-contract. Uzbekistan Youth Union will cover 35% of the fee-contract amount for students studying on the basis of a contract.

Who can use this benefit?

​​​​​​ ✅ Those who have recommendation from the command of military units; ✅ Students admitted to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan on a fee-contract basis.

Application procedure

  • Applications are accepted by the leaders of primary organizations of higher education institutions.

  • Preferences are given only on the basis of official recommendation and application.

Why is it important?

This benefit:

  • Support students' studies;
