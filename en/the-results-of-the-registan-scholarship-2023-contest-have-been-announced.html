"REGISTON SCHOLARSHIP - 2023" competition for students was announced. Candidates who took part in the competition tested their knowledge in a 3-stage examination system for 3 months.

— After the announcement of the scholarship competition, this project reached hundreds of thousands young people, and more than 82,000 students registered for this scholarship.

— The competition At the 1st stage, more than 12,000 talented students from different regions of our Republic took part in the competitive exam, and 1,071 candidates who showed the best results won the ticket to the next stage. (View results) All candidates who participated in the first stage can download the special certificates of the competition through the "Personal Cabinet" (Download certificates).

— Among the candidates who passed the second stage of the competition, the 2nd stage exams were held and 209 participants showed the best results in this stage. received a ticket to the final (See the results)

— According to the competition instructions and regulations, the 3rd stage (final) was held among the best performing participants. The third stage is the stage of submitting documents, candidates participating in the scholarship competition submitted the documents specified in the competition regulations in the specified order through the platform's special examination window. The special commission of the competition examined these documents for 4 working weeks.

The composition of the commission:

Chairman:

  • Tulovov Avazbek — the founder of the Registan education project.

The commission members:

  • professional teachers of the Registon educational center of the international level consisting of 10 people;
  • Isakhanov Ja'farkhan — OLIYGOH.UZ one of the founders of the platform, winner of the Navoi State Scholarship, scholarship holder of the Republic of Scientific Research, finalist of many state and international grants;
  • Talibdjonov Botirjon — director of the Registon educational project "HR department", coordinator of social projects;
  • Hamidjonov Shavkat — one of the founders of the OLIYGOH.UZ platform, laureate of international CDAC, ACTS grants , founder of many international startups.
