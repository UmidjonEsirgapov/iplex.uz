In the state HEIs Contract prices for the 2024/2025 academic year have been approved

Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation has announced the prices of tuition for the 2024/2025 academic year at the bachelor and master's levels on a fee-contract basis. Prices are divided into full-time, external, distance, evening and second specialization forms.

Contract prices in section of directions (basic, without scholarship)

I. Humanitarian field

  • Pedagogy – full-time: 7,400,000 soums, part-time: 8,623,000 soums

  • Art – full-time: 8,950,000, part-time: 10,330,000

  • Philology and translation theory – full-time: 8,150,000, part-time: 9,444,000

  • World politics – full-time: 11,250,000, external: 12,856,000

II. Social sphere, economy and law

  • Sociology, psychology, journalism – daytime: 7,400,000

  • Economy and international relations – daytime: 10,500,000 – 11,250,000

  • Law – daytime: 11,250,000

III. Production and technical field

  • Engineering – full-time: 7,400,000

  • Computer technology, communication – 8,150,000 – 10,500,000

  • Architecture – 8,950,000

IV. Agriculture and water economy

  • Agriculture, veterinary - daytime: 7 400 000

V. Health care and social security

  • Medicine – full-time: 10,500,000, part-time: 12,030,000

  • Social security – 7,400,000

VI. Service sector

  • Sports, transport, environmental protection – 7 400 000 – 8 150 000

Note:

  • These prices are basic (without stipend). A 12-month stipend will be added to the specified amount for studying with a scholarship.
