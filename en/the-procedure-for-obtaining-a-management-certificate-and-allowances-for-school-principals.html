The procedure for obtaining a management certificate and allowances for school principals

1. What is the importance of management certificate?

Management certificate is a document that gives the right to work as a director of a public school. With the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 681 dated 25.12.2023, a reserve of candidates with a management certificate will be formed by the education departments of the Republic of Karakalpakstan, regions and Tashkent city.

2. How long is the management certificate?

School principals are required to obtain a management certificate every 5 years.

3. Are there bonuses for specialists with management certificates?

From September 1, 2024, salary increases will be paid to managers with management certificates:

  • School principals — 50 percent;
  • Deputy principals — 30 percent.

4. How long is the training course for obtaining a certificate?

Training courses last 144 hours and are taught in one month. The courses consist of theoretical, practical training and school practice.

5. Under what circumstances is a candidate expelled from the course?

A candidate is expelled in the following circumstances:

  • Disciplinary or internal procedure violations.
  • Unexcused absences of 18 hours or more.
  • For medical reasons (based on a medical certificate).
  • Due to a court order or death.

6. Will the position and salary of directors be kept during the study?

During the study, the directors will keep the position and average salary at the main place of work. Also, in accordance with the legislation, the business trip expenses of directors sent to participate in training courses will be reimbursed.

7. What is the procedure for passing the final exam after graduation?

The final exams are held directly with the participation of the candidates. The exam consists of 25 tests, cases and practical assignments, and a total of 100 points is awarded. Candidates who have scored at least 70 points will be entitled to receive a certificate.

8. Is there an additional opportunity for those who could not take the exam for some reason?

Candidates who could not take the exam due to good reasons (illness or death of a close relative) will be admitted to the next exam after applying to the Center and submitting supporting documents. — More information
