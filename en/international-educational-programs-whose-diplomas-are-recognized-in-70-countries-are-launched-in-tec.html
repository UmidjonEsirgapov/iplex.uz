International education programs whose diplomas will be recognized in 70 countries will be launched in technical schools

President Shavkat Mirziyoyev on September 24 professional got acquainted with the presentation of proposals on the development of education and the reform of the national qualification system. Now, international education programs will be launched in technical schools.

Initially, as an experiment, 14 technical schools will introduce the English BTEC program. Graduates are awarded a diploma recognized in 70 countries and have access to more than 200 foreign universities. Depending on the results, this experience can be gradually applied to other technical schools. It is also important to formulate the admission process based on the requirements of employers. For this purpose, an electronic platform has been created and all ministries and networks will be connected to it. Educational programs of technical schools are put into practice after coordination with industrial sectors, business associations, large employers and associations. — Read on infoedu.uz
