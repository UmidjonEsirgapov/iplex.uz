These applicants are admitted to legal technical schools without exams

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
According to the law, the following applicants are admitted to the legal technical schools of the Ministry of Justice on a fee-contract basis without entrance exams, except for the admission parameters: graduates who graduated from the academic high school of the Tashkent State University of Law, with a percentage of 56 and higher; entrants who participated in the tests of the "jurisprudence" educational field of the Republic Higher Education Institution for admission to study this year and were not recommended to study, but scored a passing score in the tests of legal technical schools; entrants with a B2 or higher state qualification certificate or international certificates confirming the level of knowledge of a foreign language.
Applicants of this category can apply to the legal technical school with an application for admission to study on a fee-contract basis until September 25 of this year (on the same day).

According to the law, the following entrants to the legal technical schools of the Ministry of Justice without entrance exams, in addition to admission parameters, accepted on a fee-contract basis:

  • Graduates who graduated from the academic high school of the Tashkent State University of Law, with a percentage rate of 56% and above;
  • participating in the tests for admission in the current year in the "jurisprudence" field of the republican Higher Education Institution. Entrants who are not recommended to study, but have passed the tests at law technical schools;
  • entrants with a state qualification certificate of B2 and higher level or international certificates confirming the level of foreign language knowledge.

Applicants of this category can apply to the legal technical school with an application for admission to study on a fee-contract basis until September 25 of this year (also on this day). — Read on infoedu.uz
