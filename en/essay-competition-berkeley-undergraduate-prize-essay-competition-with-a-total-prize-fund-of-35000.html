Berkeley Undergraduate Prize by the Department of Architecture at the University of California to encourage the study of architecture as a social art at the Berkeley College of Environmental Design established. Each year, a theme important to understanding the interaction of people and the built world is selected to become the focus of the essay contest.

Students enrolled in any accredited undergraduate program in the study of architecture must submit a 500-word essay in English, the essay must respond to the contest theme. Qualified students may apply in groups of two, paired with students from relevant arts and social science programs. Selected students will advance to the semi-finals and will be required to write an essay of 2500 words. At this stage, the best 5 or 8 works are selected and submitted to the jury.
