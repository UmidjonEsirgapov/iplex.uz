Preparatory work is underway to organize entrance exams in the form of traditional paper-based tests.

Director of the State Test Center Majid Karimov said at a briefing held at AOKA 70 buildings have been completed so far. In the buildings, 25,200 applicants in 840 groups can take exams at the same time. Exams will be held in the usual form, i.e. in large-capacity buildings, in compliance with sanitary and hygienic rules. Even if the exam procedure changes, educational institutions will be informed by the decision of the State Commission on Admission," said Majid Karimiov.

He said that preparations are being made to organize the entrance exams in the form of traditional paper-based tests. So far, 70 buildings have been completed. In the buildings, 25,200 applicants in 840 groups can take the exam at the same time. More than 671,000 applicants took part in the unified state exams organized in Russia. In these countries, the exam was conducted in the traditional paper format," said the official of the Ministry of Education, Culture and Sports, referring to the situation in other countries.

In addition, Majid Karimov also answered the question about the delay of the entrance exams.

"The test is conducted only for admission to a higher education institution. Even if the test is delayed, it is possible to start classes for higher courses at OTM earlier, and for first courses from October or November. The same is happening in some countries. For example, Japan and South Korea have postponed the test to the end of the year. The decision will be made by the State Commission in consultation with relevant ministries and organizations," said the director of the State Test Center.
