State universities will be privatized

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
In the concept of development of the higher education system of the Republic of Uzbekistan until 2030, expropriation and privatization of some state higher education institutions (with the exception of higher military educational institutions) while retaining at least 51 percent of the state share as an experiment, introducing the principles of corporate governance in them
In addition, the existing capacity of institute status universities will be gradually increased and changed to university status.
