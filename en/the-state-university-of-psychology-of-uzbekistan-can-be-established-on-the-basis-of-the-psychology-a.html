The State University of Psychology of Uzbekistan can be established on the basis of the "Psychology" and "Sociology" departments of the National University of Uzbekistan.

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Uzbekistan State University of Psychology can be established on the basis of the departments of "Psychology" and "Sociology" of the National University of Uzbekistan. the following will be implemented step by step: - opening centers providing psychological services to the population working at the expense of the economy at higher educational institutions in 14 regions of the republic; — to increase the admission parameters for the training of highly educated personnel in the field of psychology by 30 percent per year; - introducing the position of family psychologist in the structure of district (city) neighborhoods, etc.

Uzbekistan State University of Psychology can be established on the basis of the "Psychology" and "Sociology" departments of the National University of Uzbekistan. The draft of the relevant decision of the President in this regard was discussed.

According to the draft document, starting from 2021, the following will be implemented step by step:

- opening centers providing psychological services to the population working at the expense of the economy at higher educational institutions in 14 regions of the republic;

- admission for the training of highly educated personnel in the field of psychology to increase its parameters by 30 percent per year;

- to introduce the position of family psychologist in the structure of district (city) neighborhoods, etc.

In addition, it is established to establish the activities of the Research Institute of Psychology, which operates as a legal entity within the Uzbekistan State University of Psychology. Targeted admission parameters will be formed for regions with a high need for educated psychologists.

According to it, contracts reflecting the relevant obligations will be signed with students who have been purposefully admitted to study by December 20 of the relevant admission year, and the practice of students who have been purposefully admitted will be organized in these state organizations. — More information
