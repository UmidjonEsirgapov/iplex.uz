Termiz State University of Engineering and Agrotechnology was established

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-265 of July 18, 2024, the Termiz Institute of Engineering and Technology and the Termiz Institute of Agro-Technology and Innovative Development were merged to form the Termiz State University of Engineering and Agro-Technology.
The main tasks of the university: training specialists and scientific-pedagogical personnel in the fields of engineering, agriculture, production and service.
School of advanced engineering: the activity of the school of advanced engineering will be launched at the university from the academic year 2024/2025.

2024 of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan According to the decision PQ-265 dated July 18, the Termiz Institute of Engineering and Technology and the Termiz Institute of Agro-Technology and Innovative Development were merged, and on their basis, the Termiz State University of Engineering and Agro-Technology was established. training.

School of advanced engineering:

  • From the 2024/2025 academic year, the activity of School of advanced engineering will be launched at the University. This school is connected with large production enterprises as an industrial partner.

Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year:

  • Professor-teachers and doctoral students will be sent to leading higher education and research organizations of foreign countries for scientific internships and professional development. special groups for talented students will be established for foreign language training.

International cooperation:

  • The university will have the right to form its own educational programs based on the program of a foreign higher education organization that is in the top 300 among higher education institutions in the current ranking of internationally recognized organizations.

Students and diplomas:

  • All students studying at the Termiz Institute of Engineering and Technology and the Termiz Institute of Agrotechnology and Innovative Development will continue their studies at the newly established University.
  • University diplomas will be awarded to students graduating from the 2024/2025 academic year.
