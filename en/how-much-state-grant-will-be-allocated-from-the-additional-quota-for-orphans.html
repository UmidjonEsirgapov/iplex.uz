How much state grant will be allocated from the additional quota for orphans?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the decision of our President PQ-4296 "On additional measures to further strengthen the guarantees of children's rights": from the total number of admissions to higher education institutions, admission to genuine orphans who are graduates of the "Mehribonlik" Children's Home and Town on the basis of an additional 1% of the state grant quota will be allocated.
In addition, a full review of the direct appeal of children left without parental care to the state body is guaranteed.
