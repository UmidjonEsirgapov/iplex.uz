Vice-Chancellor’s International High Achievers Scholarships (VCIHAS)

Scholarship Information:

CDU (Charles Darwin University) offers a limited number of scholarships for academic excellence and high-achieving international students. not transferable to a semester, course or year;

Benefits:

The grant covers 50% or 25% of the tuition fee for a full-time undergraduate or graduate program.

Eligibility criteria:

-Applicant studying in the 2nd semester of 2022 must apply to start;
-Candidates must meet the University's academic and English language requirements for their chosen course;
-Applicant must have at least 95% overall marks in their highest academic degree;
-Applicant must be a tuition-paying international student;
-Applicant must be eligible for a scholarship other than an Australian or foreign sponsoring agency must not have.

Selection criteria:

-Highest academic grade point average;
-Supporting essay (maximum 600 words) showing why you should be awarded the scholarship.

Duration:

The duration of the scholarship is the minimum time during which the course can be completed or one of the following until:

-The maximum value of the grant expires;
-You complete your course;
-You no longer meet the scholarship's continued eligibility.

Continued eligibility:

To maintain the grant, the recipient must meet the following requirements:

-Continue to be an international student;
-Eligible for each semester pay the remainder of the tuition fees (the portion not covered by the scholarship) on or before the due date (first day of study);
-Maintain satisfactory results with a minimum 5.3 GPA each semester and no failing grade in any subject;
-Maintain a full 40 credit points (0.5 EFTSL) per semester or reduce study pressure, be allowed to take a semester off

How to apply:

Step 1: Apply to study at CDU.

Step 2: Apply separately for the VCIHAS scholarship.

Next?

You will be notified of the outcome of your application by email within one month of the deadline.
