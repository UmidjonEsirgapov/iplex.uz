Scholarship program for studying at the University of Hamburg, Germany

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
The Universität Hamburg offers a grant program to financially support international students.
The maximum funding period is 12 months.
The application deadline is December 1, 2021.

Universität Hamburg offers a grant program to financially support international students.

Usually, the grant is given for 6 months. The maximum amount of the grant is 720 Euros per month.

Each grant amount is calculated individually according to the criteria set out in the Federal Study Aid Act (Bundesgesetz über individualuelle Förderung der Ausbildung, BAföG).

If the training is not completed within 6 months, an extension of the funding period may be requested. The maximum funding period is 12 months.

Requirements:

  • The candidate must not be a German citizen;
  • Students who are not part of the state loan program (BAföG);
  • The family situation must be in need of financial support.

Required documents:

  • CV/Resume;
  • Reference stating the reason for the grant;
  • List of all grades showing academic performance;
  • Recommendation.
