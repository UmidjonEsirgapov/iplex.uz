Study in Turkey on a Grant! (undergraduate, graduate, postgraduate)

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Turkish Government Turkey Bursary scholarship program for international students.
Applications are now open for Turkish government scholarships for the 2021 - 2022 academic year.
Scholarships are available to students who wish to study at universities in Turkey around the world.

Turkey Government Scholarship Program for International Students.

Applications for Turkish Government Scholarships for the 2021-2022 academic year are currently open. Scholarships are awarded to students from different parts of the world who want to study at universities in Turkey.

All programs of accredited universities in Turkey participate in the scholarship program.

Date: February 22, 2021

Grant amount. The scholarship pays the following:

  • monthly financial support: for undergraduate students - 700 Turkish lira; Masters students - 950 Turkish lira; graduate students - 1400 Turkish lira; researchers - 3000 Turkish lira;
  • living in a student residence;
  • tuition and university fees;
  • medical insurance;
  • Turkish language courses;
  • travel to Turkey and back before the beginning of the studies, and back home at the end of the studies. The student buys a ticket and will be refunded.

If you have any questions, you can send an email to info@turkiyeburslari.org.
