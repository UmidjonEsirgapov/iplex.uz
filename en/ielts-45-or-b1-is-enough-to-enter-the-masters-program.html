IELTS 4.5 or B1 is enough to enter the master's program!

Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, a foreign language certificate is required to enter the master's and doctoral programs. According to the relevant decision of the President (source), the minimum required levels in various fields are defined as follows:

  • Foreign language fields - at least C1 level
  • Humanities, biological and related sciences, mathematics and statistics, business and management, law, service, social and educational fields - at least B2 level
  • Other fields, as well as culture and sports specialties - at least B1 level (equivalent to IELTS 4.5 level)

This means that for some fields, B1 level in a foreign language is sufficient for entering a master's degree.

Why this important?

  • Students' level of language skills will be increased
  • The opportunity to participate in international scientific research will expand
  • Entry with a certificate instead of a foreign language exam will be created
