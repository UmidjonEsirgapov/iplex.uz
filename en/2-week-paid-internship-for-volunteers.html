Great opportunity to volunteer . One of the most beneficial activities for your CV and future career today is volunteering. A new project by the Association of Volunteers of Uzbekistan lasting 2 weeks. Now you can not only participate in volunteering as a volunteer candidate, but also earn up to 1 million. the first week will be studies, the next week will be practice. During volunteering, short interviews will be conducted with residents of Yashnabad district. Activities will be taught in week 1.

For whom: For all young people interested in volunteering. As far as possible, distance learning students should apply.

Requirements:

1. Participating actively in practice within the specified period of time (in Yashnabad district, Tashkent);

2. Being over 20 years old;

3. To be mobile, that is, to move around the area easily.

Where: Yashnabad district, Tashkent

When: October 20-27 + November 1-7
