Alisher Kadirov, the leader of the "Milliy Tikalnish" party, expressed his strong opinions regarding the activities of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education. He criticized that, although necessary funds have been allocated to the education system, the Ministry is not doing enough effective work. Kadyrov noted that there are no visible changes in kindergartens and schools.

As part of criticism, Alisher Kadyrov cited as an example the news that one of the teachers involved in hard labor in Andijan had a broken arm. He said that educators and teachers should work tirelessly to keep their jobs and prove their qualifications to employers. Kadyrov emphasized that such measures should serve to increase the quality of education.

Also, he emphasized the need for systematic work to be carried out today in order to see real results in the education system. For example, out of 126,000 educators working in kindergartens today, only 48,000 have higher education. He said that it is necessary to change this situation and improve the qualifications of educators.

Kadyrov criticized the ministry, questioned the fact that the deputies were making mistakes when they saw the situation on the ground, and emphasized that the ministry's activities should be explained to the people's representatives. He also mentioned that Minister Umarova has never presented her report to the Parliament.

"We should not deceive ourselves based on statistics in education and training, this is nothing but a betrayal of the future!" - said Alisher Kadirov. — Details here
