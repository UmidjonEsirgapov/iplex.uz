General Information:

VinUniversity is an excellent, private, non-profit university established in Vietnam currently offering scholarships to international students from 11 countries presented. For this academic year, 3 types of grants have been announced by this university:

  1. VinUniversity up to 100% scholarship: covers 90% - 100% of the full tuition fees of students studying at VinUniversity. 100% of the tuition fee and ~$1,500 scholarship for living expenses are allocated to exceptionally talented students;
  2. VinUniversity Need-sensitive International Fellowship program covers 50-80% of the students' tuition fees for the entire period of study;
  3. 35% subsidy from Vingroup: For the academic year 2022-2023, all accepted students will receive a 35% tuition fee subsidy from Vingroup.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Candidates who have graduated from any high school in the world with acceptable grades;
  • Minimum IELTS 6.5, (each section scores below 6.0 should not be);
  • Application fee waived for international students.

Note: students interested in majors such as technology, health and service may now apply to one of 3 colleges;

College of Business Administration

  • Bachelor of Business Administration;
  • Hospitality Management Bachelor of Science;

College of Health Sciences

  • Doctor of Medicine;
  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing;

College of Engineering and Computer Science:

  • Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Computer Science

Deadline: 22/04/2022
