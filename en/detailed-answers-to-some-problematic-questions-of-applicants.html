Question: Candidates for the OTM can apply for three more bachelor's degrees according to the sequence of subjects. Can he choose any form of education (daytime, evening, part-time)? Or will they submit documents only for full-time education? Answer: According to the statement of the State Commission No. 1, candidates are given the right to choose up to five undergraduate courses of non-military higher education institutions that have the same set of subjects and the same sequence of subjects that will be tested for admission to higher military educational institutions.

Candidates can choose up to five bachelor's education courses in any one of the three forms of education (full-time, evening, part-time).
