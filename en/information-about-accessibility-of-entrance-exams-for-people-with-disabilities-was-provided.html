Information about accessibility of entrance exams for people with disabilities was provided

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

The state testing center provided information about facilities for persons with disabilities during the entrance exams.

"All conditions are provided to ensure the participation of persons with disabilities in the test process. If the applicant comes with a companion, he will be allowed to move with his companion. Observers and employees of the State Test Center will provide the necessary assistance to unaccompanied applicants with disabilities," the Kun.uz reporter quoted Farhod Mirzayev, head of the State Test Center, as saying.
