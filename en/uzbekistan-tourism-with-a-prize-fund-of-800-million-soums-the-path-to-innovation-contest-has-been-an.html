Uzbekistan: Tourism with a prize fund of 800 million soums. The Path to innovation contest has been announced!

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
"Tourism.
The Path to innovation" project will start the second wave!
The Path to innovation" competition will support the tourism sector in Uzbekistan in accordance with the decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "Primary measures to reduce the negative impact of the coronavirus pandemic" was organized in order to support startups and the tourism ecosystem, as well as to attract high-tech products for the development of the country's domestic tourism and youth initiatives. announced the start of the second wave of the tourism competition "Tourism. The Path to innovation" in cooperation with the Ministry of Communications Development. In it, participants must present startup projects that solve problems in the field of tourism.

Who can participate in the competition?

  • Everyone can participate in the competition.

Stakeholders of the competition:

  • The winners are startup projects and innovative business ideas in the field of tourism, including primarily for tourists. information programs and services aimed at creating convenience are selected from among the participants of the competition. The prize fund of the second wave is 800,000,000 soums.

Procedure of the competition:

The competition will be held in two stages:

Stage 1: from June 11 to June 30, 2021, startup projects of the applicant

Stage 2: By July 31, 2021, the author (applicant) of startup projects selected according to the results of the 1st stage will present their projects before the commission and will be evaluated based on the results of the presentation.

We remind you that 218 applications for participation in the first stage were received, among them:

  • Tashkent: 95
  • Samarkand: 33
  • Bukhara: 16
  • Fergana: 15
  • Jizzakh: 19
  • Kashkadarya: 11

At the same time, Surkhandarya, Navoi, Andijan, Khorezm and Applications were received from other regions of Uzbekistan. The average age of the applicants was 29 years. At the same time, men made up 70% and women 30%, respectively.

The winners of the first wave won a total of 700,000,000 soums.

The purpose of the competition:

"Tourism. The Path to innovation" competition of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan In accordance with the decree "Primary measures to reduce the negative impact of the coronavirus pandemic", it was organized in order to attract high-tech products to support the tourism industry in Uzbekistan, to develop startups and the tourism ecosystem, as well as the domestic tourism of the country and the development of youth initiatives in the field of tourism.

"Tourism. The Path to Innovation" is a competition of startups aimed at supporting the tourism industry of our country, as well as introducing innovations in the field of tourism, increasing its tourist potential.

Registration period:

Registration for participation in the competition will last from May 10 to June 10.

  • Get acquainted with the terms of the competition through the following link: https://clck.ru/RM7ZF
