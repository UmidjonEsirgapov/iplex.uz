In preschool education of Uzbekistan, children's mathematical skills have reached 94%

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
Until this time, there was no special tool for quality assessment of the preschool education system in Uzbekistan.
The MELQO international tool is used all over the world to measure the quality of early childhood care and education.
According to the evaluation results: the results of the assessment of mathematical skills reached an average of 94%.

From October 9, 2023, the first stage of preschool education quality assessment based on the MELQO international tool began in all regions of Uzbekistan. Until that time, there was no special tool for quality assessment of the preschool education system in Uzbekistan.

Within the framework of the World Bank project, in order to improve this situation, it was decided to evaluate preschool education based on the MELQO evaluation system. The MELQO International Tool is used around the world to measure the quality of early childhood care and education. The results of the research provide accurate information about the development of children and the quality of the educational environment.

In 2023, 100 preschool education organizations were selected across the country using a random sampling method, and their evaluation and data collection was carried out by certified experts. The second stage will start in 2024, and 250 preschool education organizations will be selected for evaluation.

Evaluation results

Recently, a seminar dedicated to the early development of children in Uzbekistan and improvement of state education was held at the National Institute of Pedagogical Skills named after A. Avloni. It announced the evaluation results presented by Hiroshi Saeki, Senior Economist of the World Bank and Co-author of the MELQO Evaluation Report.

According to the evaluation results:

  • The results of the evaluation of children's early mathematical skills reached an average of 94%.
  • Growth and preparation It was noted that early literacy skills of children in the groups are higher than 77%.

Educational-methodical supply

It was specially noted that 90% of pre-school education organizations that participated in the evaluation process are fully provided with educational-methodical and didactic literature. This indicator is an important factor in improving the quality of the educational process.

Other results of the evaluation program

The following works were carried out within the framework of the evaluation program that continued in 2024:

  • MELQO international evaluation tool State requirements for the development of primary and preschool children in Uzbekistan and "Ilk Kadam" state education program.
  • All materials were digitized and translated into Uzbek, Karakalpak and Russian languages.
  • Criteria for selecting 250 state pre-school educational organizations were developed. region — 182 (76%).
