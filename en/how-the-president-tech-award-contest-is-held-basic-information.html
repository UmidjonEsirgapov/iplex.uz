How the President Tech Award contest is held. Basic information

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:

Awarding the winners The competition is divided into main and special areas, and the total prize fund is $1 million.

The total fund for the presidential award in February is $1 The regulation on the procedure for conducting the "President Tech Award" competition was approved.

After that, the director of "IT Park" Farhod Ibragimov commented on the "President Tech Award" competition and announced that foreign companies will be involved in the evaluation of the projects.

Technopark explained the process of conducting the competition, who evaluates the startups and how the prize pool will be distributed.

All stages of the "President Tech Award" were developed on the example of "mGovAward" and other international competitions.

The competition is aimed at identifying start-up projects in the field of digital technologies and is divided into main and special directions.

The main directions are as follows:

  • technologies based on artificial intelligence;
  • digital in the social sphere technologies;
  • technologies in the field of information and cyber security;
  • entrepreneurial and financial technologies;
  • computer and mobile games.

The Hackathon Grand Prix, which envisages the creation of a digital solution within 72 hours, will become a special direction.

All stages of the competition will be held on a special portal and you can register through OneID will be. All information about the competition will be posted on the site.

Participants will also interact with the organizers of the President Tech Award through this platform.

On the portal, participants will be able to gather as a team, choose their direction and submit an application.

At the same time, information about whether to advance to the next stage or not will also be available through a special platform.

Project evaluation process

The winners of the main areas of the competition are determined by public voting, and the winners of the hackathon (special area) are determined by an independent company.

That is, the entire evaluation process is carried out by a special independent company selected through a tender.

It is with start-ups, venture funds. must have experience in operations, business development, technical process, IT.

The company involves specialists and experts from various fields related to the startup industry, and ensures that the judges evaluate the projects impartially and fairly. Voting will also take place on the portal.

The first stage will be held online, without the participation of teams, only on the basis of completed applications.

The 10 best projects in each main direction will be determined.

A total of 50 projects will go to the next stage - "Demo Day", where teams will present their projects to experts offline for several days.

A special section - a hackathon - is planned for "Demo Day". In this, participants will have to develop a product aimed at solving a specific problem within 72 hours.

Participants are allowed to participate in one of the main directions and hackathon at the same time.

Awarding the winners

The competition is divided into main and special directions, general prize fund $1 mln.

Only one winning team will be determined at the hackathon and a cash prize of $100,000will be allocated.

The prize fund for each category of the main direction will be distributed as follows:

  • 1st place - $100,000;
  • 2nd place - $50 thousand;
  • 3rd place - $30 thousand.

The process takes longer in the main directions. All teams will first receive 50% of the announced prize fund.

After that, the KPI system will be introduced and based on the results, 25% 2nd and 3rd tranches will be given. Interest is paid within one year.

If the team does not achieve certain results and growth is not observed, the next tranches will not be paid, because the competition is focused on the implementation of projects, that is, projects and their implementation will be financed.

What is being determined?

Works on the creation of a special platform under the domain awards.gov.uz are coming to an end. The platform is scheduled to be launched at the end of April.

Applications for the President Tech Award will start on May 1 and end on July 1. The subsequent processes are organized as follows:

  • preliminary application process — from July 1 to July 25
  • online evaluation of shortlisted projects — from September 5 to October 5;
  • offline evaluation (“Demo day”) — from November 5 to November 10;
  • identification and awarding of winners — From November 20 to December 25.

Young people under 30 can participate in the competition. Also, only Uzbeks can participate in the main directions.

In the special (hackathon) direction, foreign citizens and stateless persons who are employees of resident companies of "IT Park" can also participate. — Source
