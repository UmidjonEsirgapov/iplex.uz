Vocational training costs for members of poor families are reimbursed based on the voucher system

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
On the basis of the President's decision, a new system - the voucher system - will be introduced to cover the costs of vocational training from 2025.
Basic rules of the voucher system Allocation of scholarships: starting from February 1, 2025, vocational training lasting more than 3 months persons with disabilities admitted to the courses and unemployed members of families from the register of poor families will be entitled to receive a scholarship.
Voucher system will be introduced: from May 1, 2025, a voucher system will be introduced to cover the costs of vocational training of the unemployed population, including members of poor families.

Based on the President's decision From 2025, a new system will be introduced to cover the costs of vocational training - the voucher system. This system is being launched in order to improve the skills and ensure the employment of members of poor families. Basic rules of the voucher system. Persons with disabilities and unemployed members of families from the registry of poor families admitted to vocational courses lasting more than a month will be entitled to receive a scholarship.

Voucher system will be introduced:

  • Starting May 1, 2025 unemployed population, including poor families a voucher system will be introduced to cover the costs of vocational training of its members.
  • This system is aimed at effectively managing the costs of vocational training of the population and supporting them.

Test phase:

  • Starting from February 1, 2025, the system will be implemented primarily in Samarkand region and It will be tested in Tashkent.
  • Based on the test results, a draft government decision will be prepared to introduce the system in all regions by April 1, 2025.

Significance of the decision

This decision was adopted in order to develop the professional skills of members of poor families, integrate them into the labor market, and provide economic support. done With the voucher system, every citizen will have the opportunity to develop their profession.
