DAAD Summer Courses - a program for foreign undergraduate or graduate students to learn the German language perfectly and to familiarize themselves with German culture. During this program, students are provided with free learning of the German language as well as a one-time scholarship and funds for travel expenses. Language courses are usually held every year in June-November and last at least 18 days and at most 5 weeks. Each week includes at least 25 hours of lessons. Candidates can apply from September 15 to December 1.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must be at least 18 years old and have completed at least 2 academic years of bachelor's degree;
  • Study at a university in a country other than Germany;
  • Know the German language at least at A2 level and show a document proving it (no more than 2 years since the certificates were obtained);
  • Has previously attended DAAD summer courses, 2 years have passed since then;
  • Must have good grades.

For more information on the requirements click HERE You can find it.

Benefits:

  • One-time scholarship in the amount of 1061 euros;
  • Funds for travel expenses to Germany;
  • free participation in the summer course;
  • Health and other insurance payment;
  • are provided with accommodation expenses.

IMPORTANT. Due to pandemic restrictions, this year's courses will be conducted online.
To apply, first register online and fill in the required information. Detailed information about the necessary documents and the application process is HERE.

For more information, you can contact the portal service at +49 (0228) 882-8888 or e-mail portal@daad.de.
