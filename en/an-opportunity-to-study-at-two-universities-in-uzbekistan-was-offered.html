An opportunity to study at two universities in Uzbekistan was offered

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
What is the current situation?
According to the current arrangement: An applicant can submit original documents to only one OTM; Registration to another university through the HEMIS system is blocked; This situation limits the applicant's ability to obtain two diplomas at the same time.
According to the proposal of Senator Bahrom Abdullayev: Students should be given the right to study at the same time in state and private HEIs (or in two private HEIs); This practice exists in countries such as the USA, Europe, Russia and Kazakhstan; If this system is introduced in Uzbekistan, students will have more opportunities, and the potential of personnel will increase. Senator Bahrom Abdullayev sent an official request to the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation in this matter.

What is the current situation?

According to the current regulations:

  • An applicant can submit original documents to only one OTM;

  • through the HEMIS system registration at another university will be blocked;

  • This situation limits the applicant's opportunity to receive two diplomas at the same time.

What changes are being proposed?

According to the proposal of Senator Bahrom Abdullayev:

  • To students the right to study in public and private higher education institutions (or in two private higher education institutions) should be given at the same time;

  • This practice exists in countries such as the USA, Europe, Russia and Kazakhstan;

  • If this system is introduced in Uzbekistan, students will have more opportunities, and staff potential increases.

Why is it important?

  • Students will have the opportunity to acquire theoretical knowledge in one direction, and practical skills in another direction;

  • Entrepreneurship, IT, pedagogy or other fields can be taken together
