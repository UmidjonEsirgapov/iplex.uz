Graduates of this year's school will receive certificates electronically

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Those graduating from school in 2023 will not receive a certificate in a paper version.
Where and how to get an electronic certificate?
A graduate certificate is free of charge by entering passport (or ID card or birth certificate) information on the e-shahodatnoma.uz platform. downloads.

Those graduating from school in 2023 will not receive a paper certificate.

Where and how to get an electronic certificate? The graduate downloads the certificate for free from the e-shahodatnoma.uz platform by entering passport (or ID card or birth certificate) information. If the certificate is lost, it is possible to download it again for free.

Graduates of a school in a remote village where the Internet does not penetrate are given certificates in the state of being downloaded and printed on paper.

If there is a mistake in the certificate, the graduate applies to the school where he studied. If the error is justified, a new certificate will be issued. — Source
