Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, a two-stage state examination system will be introduced in all state and non-state medical higher education institutions. According to this new system, students will go through the following stages:

  • Stage 1: final state exams will be held in fundamental subjects.
  • Stage 2: Exams in clinical sciences will be given.

Students who have successfully passed the preliminary exams will be able to transfer from course to course. Clinical knowledge tests allow the student to work as a doctor in practice.
