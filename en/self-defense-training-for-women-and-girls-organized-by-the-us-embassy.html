Self-defense training for women and girls

The U.S. Embassy in Uzbekistan, in cooperation with professional Fudokan Karate-Do instructors - graduates of the US international sports exchange program - aims to develop the skills and self-confidence necessary for girls and women to defend themselves. is starting the final round of the self-defense training program in Tashkent.
Trainings will be held for three months in a modern gym supported by the Makro supermarket chain, starting from November 2021. Information on the exact start date of the training will be sent to all selected participants. Two age groups are appointed, the number of participants in each group does not exceed 30.

  • Group A: girls between 13 and 18 years old - Monday, Wednesday and Friday from 15:00 to 16:00;
  • Group B: women over 18 years old - Monday, Wednesday and Friday hours From 21:00 to 22:00.

Participants will learn a series of techniques and exercises to protect themselves from physical abuse, maintain good physical condition and calmness. The physical fitness program is complemented by mental health training led by a professional psychologist - also a graduate of a US international exchange program.

In addition to self-defense skills, participants learn conflict resolution, open communication, and stress management.

The training is designed for girls and women over the age of 13, regardless of fitness level or previous training experience.
