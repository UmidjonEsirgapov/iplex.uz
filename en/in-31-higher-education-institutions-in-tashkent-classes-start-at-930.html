In 31 higher education institutions in Tashkent, classes start at 9:30

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The starting time of classes in a number of higher education institutions in Tashkent will change.
According to the decision taken by the Ministry of Transport, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education and the Tashkent city administration, additional measures will be taken to properly distribute the flow of passengers in the metro during rush hours after the work schedule is optimized in the relevant educational institutions. This decision was made in order to reduce congestion in the metro.

From January 9, the starting time of the lesson process will be changed in 31 educational institutions located near the metro stations in Tashkent. This was reported by the press service of the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education.

It was reported that this decision was made in order to properly distribute the flow of passengers in the capital's metro, to make it easier for students to reach their classes on time.
