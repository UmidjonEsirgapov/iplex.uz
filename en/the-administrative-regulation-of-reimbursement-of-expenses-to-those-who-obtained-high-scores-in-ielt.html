The administrative regulation of reimbursement of expenses to those who obtained high scores in IELTS and other international exams was approved

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the administrative regulation on fully covering the costs of taking exams for young people who have obtained high scores (levels) according to international examination systems has been approved - Oliygoh.uz centers or through YAIDXP (my.gov.uz).
When the authenticity of the certificate is confirmed, the compensation funds will be transferred by the Agency to Xalq bank within 15 days and an SMS message will be sent to the applicant.
If the authenticity of the certificate is not confirmed, an SMS message will be sent to the applicant.

Received a high score (level) according to the international examination systems by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan The administrative regulation for fully covering the expenses of taking exams for young people has been approved - Oliygoh.uz

According to the regulations, young people who have obtained high scores in international exams apply to state service centers or through YAIDXP (my.gov.uz) within 180 days after receiving the certificate to cover the costs of the exam.

The Agency will verify the applicant's certificate in 15 days.

If it is not possible to determine the authenticity of the certificate through verification, the Agency Sends a request to a foreign organization asking for confirmation of the validity of a special certificate within 30 days.

When the validity of the certificate is confirmed within 15 days, the compensation funds will be transferred by the Agency to Xalq Bank and an SMS will be sent to the applicant. and receive compensation payments.
