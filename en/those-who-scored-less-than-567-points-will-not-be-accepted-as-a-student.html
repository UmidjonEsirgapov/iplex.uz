Those who scored less than 56.7 points will not be accepted as a student

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
In the admission process of 2024, the minimum passing scores for admission to higher education institutions were determined.
Minimum passing scores: Minimum passing score for the state grant: 68.0 points.
Minimum for the payment contract passing score: 56.7 points.

In the 2024 admission process, the minimum passing score for admission to higher education institutions was determined. Applicants who score less than 56.7 points will not be students, even if they reach the allocated quota places.

Minimum passing points:

  • Minimum passing point for the state grant: 68.0 points. This is 36% of the maximum 189 points.
  • Minimum passing score for the payment contract: 56.7 points. This is 30% of the maximum 189 points.

These criteria determine the minimum requirements of applicants in the student admission process. Therefore, applicants should check their scores and evaluate their chances of admission. — More information
