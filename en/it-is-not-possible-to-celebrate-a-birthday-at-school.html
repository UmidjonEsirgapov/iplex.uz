It is not allowed to celebrate birthdays at school

According to the established rules, it is strictly forbidden for students to celebrate birthdays on the territory of the educational institution (source). This rule was introduced in order not to interfere with the educational process and to maintain the internal order of the school.

List of prohibited actions

Not only the birthday, but also the following situations are prohibited on the school premises:

  • ✖️ using headphones during the lesson;

  • ✖️ audio or audio that lowers the reputation of the teacher and students.

  • ✖️ violence against others.

What measures will be taken in case of violation of the rules?

If the student does not follow these rules, the following punishment will be applied:

  1. A written warning will be given to the parents.

  2. If the rule violation is repeated, the student will be put on the internal control list of the school.

  3. Parents will be called to the school and an inspector-psychologist will talk with them.

Conclusion

School is a place of education and training. For this reason, birthday celebrations, any actions that interfere with the course of the lesson or are contrary to etiquette are strictly prohibited. Every student and parent must follow the rules and contribute to maintaining a healthy and disciplined school environment. — Official Data
