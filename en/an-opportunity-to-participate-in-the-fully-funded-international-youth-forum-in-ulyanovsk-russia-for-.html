An opportunity to participate in the fully funded International Youth Forum in Ulyanovsk, Russia for all active youth

It will be held offline on November 1-3; Where?
Ulyanovsk, Russian Federation; Official language of the program: the Forum is conducted in Russian; Note: All expenses are covered; The forum is held with the support of the Ministry of Science and Higher Education of the Russian Federation, the National Council of Russian Youth and Children's Associations and the main organization for work with youth of the CIS member states (RTU MIREA).

General Information:

Be a member of the first International Youth Forum of the CIS member states!

The event is dedicated to the year of science and technology in the Russian Federation, and 100 participants - representatives from the countries of the Commonwealth of Independent States (including Uzbekistan) actively participating in the promotion and implementation of the SDG program will gather.

When? It will be held offline on November 1-3;

Where? In the city of Ulyanovsk, Russian Federation;

Official program language: The forum will be conducted in Russian;

Note: All expenses will be covered;

The forum will be held with the support of the Ministry of Science and Higher Education of the Russian Federation, the National Council of Russian Youth and Children's Associations, and the Main Organization for Work with Youth of the CIS Member States (RTU MIREA).

All interested candidates must fill out an online application in Russian until October 15, 2021 23:59 Moscow time.
