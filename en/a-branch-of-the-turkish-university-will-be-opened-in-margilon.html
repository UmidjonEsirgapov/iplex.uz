A branch of the Turkish University will be opened in Margilon

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
A branch of Turkey's Bursa Uludag University will be opened in Margilan. It was announced during an online video conference meeting with the representatives of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry, Dunyo Agency reports.
In order to develop comprehensive relations, it is planned to organize an online video conference with the Bursa Chamber of Commerce and Industry and the regional administration of the Fergana Region of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry in the coming days, as well as to hold the next extended video conference with the participation of the leaders of our regions.

A branch of Turkey's Bursa Uludag University will be opened in Margilan. This was announced during a meeting held in the form of an online video conference with the participation of representatives of the Fergana regional government, the Investment and Foreign Trade Department, the Chamber of Commerce and Industry, the Bursa regional government of the Republic of Turkey, the vice-rector of Bursa Uludag University, and the representatives of the Bursa Chamber of Commerce and Industry.

Bursa Chamber of Commerce and Industry and the Chamber of Commerce and Industry Fergana It has been planned to organize an online video conference with the regional administration of the region in the near future, and to hold the next extended video conference with the participation of the leaders of our regions.
