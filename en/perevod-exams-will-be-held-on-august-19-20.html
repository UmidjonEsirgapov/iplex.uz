For "Perevod" exams the dates have been officially announced. Tests for the transfer of students' studies from HEIs in foreign countries, as well as from foreign and non-governmental HEIs in Uzbekistan to state HEIs will be held on August 19-20. Tests will be organized in two shifts: 1st shift: from 08:00 to 11:00.

  • 2nd shift: from 15:00 to 18:00

    • A total of 90 test tasks will be given to applicants in 2 specialties and 3 compulsory subjects. There will be 30 test items for each subject in specialized subjects, and 10 in compulsory subjects. Applicants will be given 3 hours to work through all the questions and copy the answers to their sheet.

    In order to participate in the test, applicants must obtain a permit from the my.uzbmb.uz site of the Knowledge Assessment Agency. When entering the tests, it is required to arrive at the time specified in the permit, as well as to bring the passport (ID card) and the permit with them. — Read on infoedu.uz
