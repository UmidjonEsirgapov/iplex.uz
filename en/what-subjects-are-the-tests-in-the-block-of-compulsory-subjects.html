How to prepare for compulsory subjects?

Fortunately, State the average distribution of the test tasks used in the entrance tests for the bachelor's education courses of higher education institutions by department was announced by the test center. This information will help you prepare.

According to the distribution, compulsory subjects are structured as follows:

1⃣ Native language (Uzbek):— Orthography and punctuation — 40%;— Lexicology, methodology — 40%;— Grammar — 20%.

2⃣ Native language (Russian):— Phonetics — 10%;— Vocabulary — 10%;— Grammar — 40%;— Orthography — 40%.

3⃣ Native language (Karabakh):— Phonetics — 10%;— Vocabulary — 20%;— Grammar — 40%;— Orthography — 30%.

4⃣ Mathematics:— Numbers and operations on them — 40%;— Percent, proportion and equation — 30%;— Geometry — 30%.

5⃣ History of Uzbekistan:— History of the ancient and middle ages — 35-45%;— The historical period of the early 16th-20th centuries — 35-45%;— History of the Soviet era and the period of independence — 20-25%.
