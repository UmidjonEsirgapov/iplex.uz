Financially independent non-governmental organizations cannot participate in the "Initiative budget"

Amendments and additions to the process of "Initiative budget" by the Senate Council of the Oli Majlis of the Republic of Uzbekistan entered. According to this decision, higher education institutions that are financially independent are prohibited from participating in this process.

Main points of the decision

Participation of financially independent HEIs is limited

  • Financially independent higher education institutions cannot participate in "Initiative budget" processes from now on.

Additional restrictions on the use of funds:

  • Purchase of vehicles, public safety equipment (radar, surveillance cameras, traffic lights) and furniture for leaders is prohibited.
  • These restrictions are aimed at ensuring the effective use of funds and greater attention to social issues.

The purpose of the decision

The decision aims to allocate funds effectively and direct them to social needs. In this way, the resources of the state budget will be used more rationally for the welfare of the people. — Read on infoedu.uz
