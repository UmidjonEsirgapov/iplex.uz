MEXT (UR) - Awarded to international students recommended by APU. This scholarship was established by the Japanese Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology (MEXT) in order to increase international competitiveness and encourage the active exchange of foreign students in Japanese universities. During the program, participants study at APU University without contract fees. In addition, travel expenses to Japan will be covered and students will be provided with a monthly stipend.

Requirements:

  • Be admitted to APU University;
  • The candidate should not be a Japanese citizen and the country of residence should have diplomatic relations with Japan;
  • After April 2, 1987 must be born;
  • GPA must be at least 2.30 on a 3.00 scale;

More information HERE.

  • Expenses for travel (between home country and APU) will be reimbursed.
