General Information:

c: Yale University, one of the most prestigious universities in the USA, has announced a 2-week summer school program to improve cultural exchange. More than 2000 young people from more than 150 countries of the world are accepted. Up to 100% scholarships can be allocated to support financially disadvantaged youth, and $3 million USD has been allocated for this purpose.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Study in school during the 10-11th grade or the last 2 years of college/high school;
  • Candidates must be at least 16 years old. (until July 19, 2022);
  • Must not have participated in this program before;

Note: The deadline for the 1st stage admission process is November 3, 2021. A one-time application fee of $60 is required. The final results will be announced on January 5, 2022. The deadline for the 2nd stage admission process is January 10, 2022. A one-time application fee of $75 is required. Final results will be announced on March 15, 2022.

Required documents:

  • List of classes;
  • One 400-word essay and one 200-word essay (Names will be displayed at that time);
  • Certificates/diplomas;
  • 2 letters of recommendation;
  • Language certificates: TOEFL or Duolingo (if applicable);
  • Candidates who wish to receive financial assistance must also fill out the "Fee Waiver" section;
