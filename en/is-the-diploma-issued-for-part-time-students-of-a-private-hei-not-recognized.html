Is the diploma issued for part-time students of a private HEI not recognized?

Useful information for entrants and students:

Brief content:
In social networks, messages with the content "diplomas issued for part-time students of private HEIs are not recognized." According to the decision PQ-289 dated June 22, 2022, starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, persons with at least five years of experience in pedagogical activities in the educational system will be admitted to study in the fields of education in the field of pedagogy in the form of part-time education, including; primary education; fine arts and engineering graphics; music education; physical culture; it is not possible to be admitted to technological education without a 5-year internship.

In social networks, messages with the content "diplomas issued for part-time students of private HEIs are not recognized." Actually, the matter has been misinterpreted, and we would like to inform you of the following regarding the subject.

It is known that in accordance with the decision PQ-289 of June 22, 2022, starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, education in the field of pedagogy will be subject to external education persons with at least five years of work experience related to pedagogical activities in the educational system are accepted, including;

  • primary education;
  • fine arts and engineering graphics;
  • music education;
  • physical culture;
  • admission to the fields of technological education without a 5-year internship. It is not possible.

Any applicant can apply for all other fields of education other than the five bachelor's degrees listed above, and there is no legal restriction on this. diplomas in accordance with Article 31 of the Law "On Education" are accepted on the territory of the Republic on the same level as the diplomas of state HEIs. — Official Data
