New educational system Academic lyceums: Starting from the new academic year, the "International Baccalaureate" and "A-level" programs will be introduced as an experiment in a total of 73 academic lyceums under universities.

The importance of the "fourth industrial revolution" professions for rapid development was noted by the head of our country. In this regard, a new system will be introduced in order to increase the interest of young people in professions related to physics, chemistry, biology, technology, engineering, mathematics (STEM). "A-level" programs will be introduced.

  • Students who have received a certificate based on these programs will be admitted to that university without an exam, on the basis of a contract.

    • Preparation for foreign universities

    Our young people are taking the "SAT" exam in mathematics, English and scientific thinking in order to study in foreign universities. Pupils who scored above 75% in these tests win grants to the most prestigious foreign universities.
