In what case is the teacher not paid for not posting the grade on time on the Emaktab.uz platform?

The most relevant news in the field of education today is:

Summary:
Teachers may not be paid for the lesson as a result of not completing these tasks on time.

According to the rules set by the Ministry of Public Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan, teachers are required to set the results of the lesson on time on the Emaktab.uz platform. Teachers may not be paid for the lesson as a result of not completing these tasks on time.

Main requirements

Evaluation of students:

  • 30% of the students who attended the lesson must be evaluated.
  • The note "Lesson passed" must be marked on the platform.

Terms of evaluation:

  • For 1-shift schools: Lesson evaluation should be done from 9:00 to 21:00.
  • For 2-shift schools: Evaluation should be done by 1:00 p.m. the day after the current lesson.

Evaluation and notes are not specified:

  • Failure to mark or grade students on time will result in the teacher being considered absent.
  • The teacher will not be paid for this lesson.

Responsibilities of the classroom teacher

  • Entering daily attendance in the "Daily Journal".
  • Using the following symbols:
    • s: Absence of class without any reason.
    • dq: Absence of class with reason.
    • k: Absence of class due to illness.
    • kq: Tardy to class.
    • m: Chronic absence of class.
    • ch: Going outside the country without permission.
    • q: Not getting an education because of parental opposition.
    • ns: Not coming to class because of lack of parental control.
    • tq: Not coming to class because of getting married.

Science teachers duties

  • Specifying attendance of students online at the beginning of the lesson.
  • s: Specifying absenteeism without any reason.
  • kq: Specifying lateness to class (can be evaluated).

Additional requirement for the class leader

Name and surname of students in class logs must be entered in a script appropriate to the language of education (Cyrillic or Latin).

Conclusion

If teachers do not:

  • do the assessment on time,
  • do not put a note "Lesson passed",

they will not be paid for this lesson. These rules are aimed at ensuring orderly and high-quality organization of classes. — Source
