Student housing application process has begun!

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:

The process of sending applications for student housing began on August 1!

According to the regulations approved by Cabinet of Ministers Resolution No. 376 (08/14/2023), students can send their applications through the my.gov.uz portal starting from August 1.

According to the regulations, initially applicants with privileges and priorities will be admitted to student accommodation. After that, for students on the general list, it will be distributed according to the number of remaining places in the dormitory, based on the queue:

  • For 1st year students 55 - 65 percent;
  • For 2nd year students 15 - 20 percent;
  • For 3rd year students and above 20 - 25 percent will be allocated.

Applications will be accepted from August 1 until the end of the academic year.
