What documents should a student who passed the transfer exam submit to the university?

Brief content:
Students who have successfully passed the transfer exams will be given a contact letter by the university, and it will be specified in this document what documents should be brought: Original copy of diploma or certificate; Original copy of transcript or rating book; Academic reference; Extract from the order on admission to the 1st course; Extract from the order on transfer from one course to another; Extract from the order on expulsion from the ranks of students; Accreditation (certificate or license) documents of the university
