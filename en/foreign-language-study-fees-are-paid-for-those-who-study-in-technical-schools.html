Foreign language study fees are paid for those who study in technical schools

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to the new decision of the government, concessions are given to students of professional educational institutions who want to learn a foreign language. in this case, 50 percent of the expenses spent by the learner on studying a foreign language, but the part not exceeding 10 times the basic calculation amount, will be reimbursed from the State budget funds.

According to it, in the event that students enrolled in professional educational institutions learn a foreign language independently and present an international or equivalent certificate, 50 percent of the expenses spent by the student on studying a foreign language, but not more than 10 times the basic calculation amount, will be reimbursed from the state budget.
