In recent years, the development of non-state higher education institutions has increased significantly in Uzbekistan. However, some private universities have been able to continue their activities, while others are facing financial difficulties.

The advantage of universities with professional management

The stability of private universities depends on several important factors:

  • The influence of important persons - Large investors, entrepreneurs and experienced in the field of education. contribution of specialists to the development of the university.
  • Sufficient number of students - The student base plays an important role in ensuring a stable flow of income.
  • Professional management system - A system run by experienced managers ensures the long-term stability of the university.
  • Financial literacy - Proper management of the university's funds and effective planning is important.
  • Targeted cost estimates - Financial resources of the university should be targeted.
  • Management by experts in the field - Not only business approach, but quality of education must be prioritized.

Due to these factors, sustainable private universities have a high probability of a long future.

Why some private universities may be closed?

Due to increasing competition in the current education market, universities operating without experience and without a plan are facing financial difficulties. This can be caused by the following factors:

  • Area experts are not in the management - The people who manage the system do not have enough experience in the field of education.
  • Lack of financial and investment strategy - Inability to ensure the balance of income and expenses.
  • Decline in the number of students - Attract new students due to lack of demand and confidence
  • Weak quality control – Failure to improve programs and neglect the quality of education.
  • Inability to withstand competition – Inability to strengthen its position against public and other strong private universities.
