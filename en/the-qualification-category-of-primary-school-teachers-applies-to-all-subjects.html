The qualification category of primary school teachers applies to all subjects

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
The qualification category of primary school teachers applies to all the subjects they teach. It is conducted as a summary of the main academic subjects taught in primary grades (mother tongue and literature, mathematics, education, natural sciences, technology, etc.).
Therefore, the qualification category given for primary education is applied to all academic subjects taught in primary grades, including informatics and educational sciences.

The qualification category of primary school teachers is applied to all subjects they teach

In the field of primary education Since the diploma of higher education does not indicate the name of academic subjects, qualification category tests of primary education teachers are conducted summarizing the main academic subjects taught in primary grades (mother tongue and literature, mathematics, education, natural sciences, technology, etc.).

Therefore, the primary education category of qualification is applied to all academic subjects they teach in primary grades, including informatics and education subjects.
