What is needed to open a kindergarten on the basis of public-private partnership?

Possibilities of opening a kindergarten on the basis of public-private partnership in Uzbekistan were approved in accordance with the Presidential Decree of April 5, 2018. This decision clearly defines the procedures and requirements for public-private partnership projects. If you want to open a private preschool, you do not need to participate in the competition. The implementation of the project is carried out based on the review of the documents submitted by the initiative of the private partner.

List of documents

The following documents are required to open a garden:

  • Application - a written request about the project initiative;
  • Certificate of registration of a legal entity;
  • The right to a plot of land confirmation document;
  • Document confirming the right to buildings;
  • Conclusion on fire safety - confirmation of the compliance of the building with fire control authorities;
  • Conclusion of sanitary-epidemiological control on hygiene rules;
  • Documents confirming the information of teaching staff - higher or about secondary special pedagogical information.

Application review period

The application of the private partner will be reviewed by the public partner within 15 days. If the project is found suitable, the parties will conclude an agreement within 7 days.

Project implementation procedure

  1. The private partner will submit the application and documents.
  2. The state partner will study the documents and make a decision on the feasibility of the project.
  3. If the decision is positive, the agreement between the state partner and the private partner

Public-private partnership projects are important for effective and sustainable development, especially in the field of education. — Official Data
