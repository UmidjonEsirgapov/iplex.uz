General Information:

Apple Entrepreneur Camp for Female Founders: An opportunity for female entrepreneurs to connect directly with Apple and discuss their business development prospects.

Eligible to participate in this online program participants get the following free of charge:

  • A chance to receive face-to-face recommendations for your program from Apple engineers;
  • A chance to receive support from Apple company managers for at least 1 year;
  • A chance to become a 1-year free member of the "Apple Developer" program;
  • Also with confident leaders at the "Apple Entrepreneur Camp" opportunity to exchange ideas and learn knowledge;

Note: Everything will be done online;

Requirements for candidates (Applications can be submitted by the candidate who created the program in 1 group with up to 3 candidates). Your organization must have the following;

  • There must be a female manager in the organization;
  • A female expert in Swift and Objective-C programming;
  • You must have a ready-made program or program you are developing;
  • All team members must be over 18 years old and have good English speaking skills;

  • The documents you prepared will be kept for up to 1 year;

    • Your applications will be checked based on the following criteria:

    • Through the answers you gave in the online application that you filled in;
    • Through the development strategy for the apple platform created using the latest apple technologies;
    • The unique creativity of your created program will be evaluated;
