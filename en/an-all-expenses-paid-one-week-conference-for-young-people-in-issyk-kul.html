An all-expenses-paid one-week conference for young people in Issyk-Kul

Important news related to higher education institutions:

At the conference, simulation exercises based on the Helsinki Final Act of 1975
In addition, the participants will participate in international briefings, training and interactive sessions on negotiations and diplomacy, they will be trained by international and local experts and will have the opportunity to meet with the staff of the OSCE (OSCE-Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe) program in Bishkek.

Model OSCE-2021 Model OSCE Applications are being accepted for the conference to be held in Issyk-Kul from September 27 to October 2. Simulation exercises based on the Helsinki Final Act of 1975 will be held at the conference. In addition, participants will participate in international briefings, training and interactive sessions on negotiation and diplomacy, they will be taught by international and local experts and will have the opportunity to meet with the staff of the OSCE (OSCE-Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe) program in Bishkek.

Benefits:

  • All expenses are covered by the event organizers;
  • They will have the opportunity to participate in the conference for free.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must not be older than 30 years old;
  • Be a citizen of one of the participating countries of the OSCE (Uzbekistan is on the list);
  • Proficient in English;
  • during the event The opportunity to be in Kyrgyzstan;
  • Participation of master students, doctoral students) and young researchers is also encouraged.

For additional questions and applications: youth-poib@osce.org

Results will be announced on September 20. — Official Data
