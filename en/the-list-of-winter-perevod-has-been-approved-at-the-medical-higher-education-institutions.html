The list of those recommended to transfer their studies to HEIs under the Ministry of Health has been approved based on valid reasons, the Press Service of the Ministry of Health reported.

President In accordance with paragraph 7 of the decision PQ-279, the applications of citizens who want to transfer their studies for 2 good reasons were considered.

On this basis, a list of students recommended to transfer their studies to the higher education institutions of the Ministry of Health in the spring semester of the 2022/2023 academic year was formed. There are 76 of them.

We remind you that the rector of the higher education institution is personally responsible for determining the educational plans and programs of the student whose studies are being transferred, and accordingly, the educational course. Responsibility for the correct formalization and legality of the submitted documents on the transfer and resettlement of students' studies, it is stated in the message, rests with medical institutions of higher education.
