National Chiao Tung University, which ranks 427th in the world and 4th in the country, has announced the grant program of NCTU Taiwan Scholarship Program. This program is not only one of the most popular grants, but also quite easy to get.

Program Brief:

  1. Country: Taiwan;
  2. University: National Chia Tong University;
  3. Course Level: Undergraduate, Master, Doctoral;
  4. Financial Support: Fully Funded;
  5. Deadline: September 30, 2021;

Duration of courses:

  • (BA) undergraduate programs-4-6 years;
  • (MA) Master's programs-1-4 years;
  • Doctoral programs-2-7 years;

Candidates are as follows It is necessary to apply for the fields:

  • Human resources and scientific sciences;
  • Engineering;
  • Biology and technological sciences;
  • Fundamentals of management;
  • Artificial intelligence and electric energy;
  • Law;

Full master's degree here and familiarize yourself with the programs of the doctoral level!

Benefits of the grant:

  • For the undergraduate level: a monthly stipend of $21,000 Taiwan dollars ($700 USD);
  • For the master's level students: a) The contract amount is fully covered; b) The university provides assistance in research works; c) A monthly stipend of $22,000 Taiwan Dollars ($733 US Dollars);

Requirements for candidates:

  • To be a foreign citizen;
  • To be physically and mentally healthy;
  • The candidate is a holder of another grant while he/she is the holder of this grant.
  • All submitted documents must be authentic and the application process must be completed without errors;

Required Documents:

  • Register online;
  • The most recent diploma/certificate and grade book with an official seal; together;
  • 2 letters of recommendation;
  • Passport copy;
  • Study plan;
  • Certificate of language proficiency;
