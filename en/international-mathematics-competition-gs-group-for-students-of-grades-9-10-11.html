Are you in grades 9-11? Do you like mathematics and dream of getting higher technical education at a prestigious university? Do you not live in the capital (not in Moscow or St. Petersburg)? Then join the VIII International Mathematical Competition GS group "Ya reshayu" Test your knowledge, get the opportunity to introduce yourself to the whole country and start thinking about your future at one of the country's leading universities.

Students of grades 9-11 from non-capital cities of Russia (except Moscow and St. Petersburg), near and far foreign countries to participate in the competition.

Prizes:

  • Annual special grant for winners among 11th-grade students who entered the university in a specialty required by Technopolis GS - 15,000 rubles;
  • Electronic equipment and the opportunity to enter the Physics-Mathematics Lyceum named after the President No. 239 without an exam - 9-10 grades for;
  • Special guests - an opportunity to talk with famous mathematicians, experts in the field of high technologies, employees who have served in the field of science and education.
The competition will be held in 3 stages:
  • 1st online tour: September 06 - November 08, 2021;
  • 2nd online tour: November 16 - December 01, 2021;
  • 3rd full-time (final) round in Technopolis GS innovation cluster: January 29-30, 2022
