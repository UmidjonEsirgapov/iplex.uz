Who will receive a 4-year grant?

A full 4-year grant will be awarded only to the winners of the following 5 international science Olympiads:

  • IMO - International Mathematics Olympiad

  • IPhO - International Physics Olympiad

  • IChO - International Chemistry Olympiad

  • IBO - International Biology Olympiad

  • IOI - International Informatics Olympiad

Students who have a gold, silver or bronze medal in these Olympiads are directly accepted to the state grant without taking a test. A 4-year grant is guaranteed.
