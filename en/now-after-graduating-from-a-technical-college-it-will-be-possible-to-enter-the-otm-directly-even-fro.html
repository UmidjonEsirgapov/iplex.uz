Now, after graduating from a technical college, it will be possible to enter the OTM directly even from the 3rd year!

About the expected changes in the educational system:

Brief content:
From the new academic year, training in technical schools will be launched on the basis of a new system that ensures the integrity of the "Academic" and "Working profession" educational components. criteria and requirements for teachers are the same as universities; A "credit system" will also be introduced in technical schools, and the "credits" collected for mastering the subject will be recognized in universities.
That is, students who have accumulated enough "credits" for two years at the technical college will have the opportunity to continue their education in the second or third year of the universities based on the "2+2" or "2+3" programs.

Starting from the new academic year, training in technical schools will be launched on the basis of a new system that ensures the integrity of the "Academic" and "Working profession" components of education.

In this:

  • the educational programs, textbooks, knowledge evaluation criteria and requirements for teachers in the 1st-2nd year of the technical college will be the same as in the universities;
  • the "credit system" will be introduced in the technical schools, and the "credits" collected for mastering the subject will be recognized in the universities.

That is, students who have accumulated enough "credits" for two years at the technical college will have the opportunity to continue their education in the second or third year of universities based on the "2+2" or "2+3" programs. Now, after working for two or three years and gaining experience, those who studied at the college-technicum can get a qualification equal to "bachelor". — Official Data
