Phystech.International: an opportunity for schoolchildren to participate in the International Online Olympiad and become a student of the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:

Phystech.International - From August 9 to October 10, 2021, the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology (State University) with the support of the Federal Agency of the Commonwealth of Independent States will hold the international Olympiad "Phystech.International" for school students in physics, mathematics and biology. High school students can participate in the Olympiad in one to three subjects. The Olympiad will be held in 2 stages.

Benefits:

  • Additional points for admission to the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology;
  • Opportunity to participate in the final stage of the Phystech Olympiad;
  • The winners and prize-winners of the Phystech Olympiad will receive a quota for free education in Russia;
  • To the Rossotrudnichestvo offices of the organizing committee of the Olympiad sends letters of recommendation for winners and prize-winners, which increases the chance of getting a quota for free education at MFTI.

Who can participate:

  • Citizens of all countries can participate;
  • Students of 9th, 10th and 11th grades.
