In Uzbekistan, admission to the master's degree program takes place twice a year

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Admission to state HEIs is organized 2 times a year.
In this case: admission in August is based on the state grant and payment-contract basis approved for the next academic year, as well as payment-contract admission parameters approved by the supervisory boards of higher education institutions granted financial independence; Admission in January is carried out in accordance with the parameters of admission based on the results of the initial admission and the additional payment-contract approved by the State Commission. The admission in August is based on the approved state grant and fee-contract for the next academic year and in accordance with the fee-contract admission parameters approved by the supervisory boards of higher education institutions granted financial independence;
