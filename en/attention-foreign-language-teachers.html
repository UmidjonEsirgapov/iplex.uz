Attention foreign language teachers!

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 312 of May 19, 2021 "On measures to effectively organize the popularization of learning foreign languages": until the 2024/2025 academic year, general secondary, It is established that all foreign language teachers working in secondary special and professional educational institutions must have a national or equivalent international certificate of at least B2 level. Based on this requirement, the Ministry of Preschool and School Education states that all foreign language teachers must have at least B2 national or international certificates by the 2024/2025 academic year. reminds.
