What are the employment benefits for graduates of educational institutions?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated May 24, 2017, from January 1, 2018: in order to increase the interest of employers in employing graduates, the established rate of the single social payment will be increased to 50% in the first year after their employment, and in the second and third years It will be reduced to 25 percent.
Those employed in budget organizations, monopolistic organizations, organizations with a state share in the authorized fund (authorized capital) of more than 50 percent will be excluded; students of colleges, academic lyceums are given an individual tax payer number and registered with the state tax authorities as a tax payer without an electronic digital signature (ERI); Also, in accordance with Article 84 of the Labor Code of the Republic of Uzbekistan, it is noted that the initial probationary periods are not specified in the labor contracts concluded with graduates of secondary special and vocational educational institutions and higher education institutions who are entering work for the first time within three years. If the employment contract with the graduates of higher educational institutions who received education under grants and who entered the job for the first time within three years from the date of graduation from the relevant educational institution is canceled at the initiative of the employer before the expiration of three years from the date of its conclusion, the employer must notify the local labor body about this.
