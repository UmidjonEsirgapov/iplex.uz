The requirement for a foreign language certificate for the master's degree will decrease

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The following procedure will be established: the certificate requirement will be canceled for students who completed the educational program in a foreign language; depending on the nature of the field, the requirement for a certificate in the fields of culture, art, sports, education, health, production, engineering, agriculture, veterinary medicine, architecture and construction, physics and natural sciences, and transport services will be lowered by one level - to level B1. the B2 level is maintained in the fields of exact sciences, biology, mathematics, law, journalism, information and communication technologies and economics.
