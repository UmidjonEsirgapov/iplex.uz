Decree "On additional measures to further increase efficiency" (PF-152, 30.09.2024) was adopted. This decree includes a number of changes aimed at further developing the preschool education system and improving children's learning and cognitive skills.

According to the decree, the following measures will be implemented:

  • Development of cognitive, psychomotor and social skills in children of small and middle groups
 will be launched.
  • Old and Educational and methodological materials aimed at learning, forming communicative and creative competences will be developed for the children of preparatory groups for school.

    • Starting from the 2026/2027 academic year:

    • Kindergartens will introduce the practice of keeping a child's development map in the Preschool education management information system
    • Children who have completed preparatory groups for school will be given an electronic certificate of completion of one-year school preparation based on their development map.

    Until August 1, 2025:

    • Electronic resources, audio-visual products and educational materials developed within the framework of the state educational program of preschool education. It will be placed in preschool education management information system and free and free access will be created.
    • A personal office for parents will be created and information on the child's development map will be placed in it.
