Justus & Louise van Effen Excellence Scholarships 2022-2023: In order to financially support high-potential students who want to study at the master's level at the TU Delft University in the Netherlands, Justus & The Louise van Effen Excellence Scholarships program has been announced. This program includes fully funded grants, including full contract fees, as well as an annual stipend of 30,000 euros for living expenses. The application fee is 100 euros.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Be admitted to a 2-year master's degree at TU Delfts University;
  • Bachelor diploma grades should be good (GPA 80% and above);
  • Language certificate (TOEFL iBT 90 each at least 21 in each section or IELTS 6.5 at least 6.0 in each section or CEFR at least C1 or C2);
  • Language certificates are not required if the candidate is a citizen of Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, England or the USA;

Required Documents:

  • Bachelor's Diploma;
  • Language certificate;
  • CV (max 3 pages);
  • Notebook;
  • Motivational letter;
  • 2 letters of recommendation;
  • Portfolio (if available)
