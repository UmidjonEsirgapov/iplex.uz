Teachers of mother tongue, literature and history are paid a 50 percent bonus every month

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Teachers of mother tongue, literature and history will be given a 50 percent bonus every month. According to the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. Pedagogical personnel who have the appropriate level certificate obtained in the national evaluation system of the Agency for the Evaluation of Knowledge and Skills under the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation in the subjects of mother tongue and literature and history in secondary educational institutions and who teach these subjects will be paid an additional monthly bonus of 50 percent compared to their tariff rate. Ministry of Education.
This change is being implemented in order to further encourage the work of pedagogues and improve the quality of the educational process.

President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PF-37 dated February 21, 2024 According to the decree, starting from January 1, 2025, in the general secondary education institutions under the Ministry of Education, in the general secondary education institutions of the Ministry of Education, in the subjects of mother tongue, literature, and history, teachers who have the appropriate level of certificate obtained in the national evaluation system of the Agency for the Evaluation of Knowledge and Skills under the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, and who teach these subjects, will be paid according to their tariff rate. An additional monthly bonus of 50 percent will be paid.
