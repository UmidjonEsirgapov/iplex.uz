The results of the category exam are calculated as follows:

First stage

Determining the level of knowledge of the teacher's specialty subject and professional standard:

  • Educators are given 40 test tasks.
  • Each correctly completed test task is evaluated with 2 points.
  • A total of 80 points are given for tests.

Second stage

Determining pedagogical skills:

  • Using advanced pedagogical methods and technologies ("case study" method, project method, cooperative teaching, "practical game", interactive educational method, etc.) 10 tasks will be given.
  • Each correct answer will be evaluated with 2 points.
  • A total of 20 points will be given.

Total points

Both steps will be performed simultaneously by computer programs. A total of 100 points can be collected:

  • First stage: 80 points
  • Second stage: 20 points

Conclusion

The certification process consists of a two-stage test aimed at determining the level of knowledge and pedagogical skills of pedagogues. At each stage, points are collected for correct answers and total results are calculated. — Official Data
