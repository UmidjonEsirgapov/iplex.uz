How is a student's application for academic leave considered? A document substantiating the reason for taking an academic leave is attached to the application (a summons to call up a student for military service or a report from a medical institution where the student is under constant medical supervision or a conclusion from a medical institution where a sick family member is under constant medical supervision). It indicates the start and end date of academic leave. — More information

  • Granting academic leave to a student is not considered to be his expulsion from a higher education institution.
