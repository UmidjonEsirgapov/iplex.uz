Registration for the National Certificate in History and Geography has been announced

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Before the entrance exams for the 2023-2024 academic year, the National Certificate Exams in History and Geography will be held. Test day: May 21.
What privileges will holders of the national certificate have?

Before the entrance exams of the 2023-2024 academic year, the national certificate exams in the subjects of history and geography will be held.

  • Registration: From April 24 to May 7;
  • Day of tests: May 21.

What privileges will national certificate holders have?

Applicants who have scored at least 60% of the highest points according to the test results will be presented with an electronic certificate. Applicants with a certificate are given the following points in the field of specialization when entering HEIs:

  • From 60 percent to 86 percent - a graded (proportional) point compared to the maximum point;
  • 86 percent and above - the maximum point.

In addition, an applicant with a national certificate is given the highest point in the relevant compulsory subject. — Official Data
