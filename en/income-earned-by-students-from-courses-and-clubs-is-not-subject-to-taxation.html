Income earned by students from courses and clubs is not subject to taxation

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to Article 369, Part 1, Clause 9 of the Tax Code of the Republic of Uzbekistan, students' income from training courses and clubs is not included in the total income and is not taxed.
In this case, pupils and students studying in the territory of professional and higher educational institutions do not pay tax for the income earned as a result of the following activities: - organization of paid training courses; — holding sports clubs; - provision of other educational services.
