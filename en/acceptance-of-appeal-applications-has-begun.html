Appeals have begun to be accepted from applicants who are dissatisfied with the results of the entrance tests of higher education institutions.

Appeal applications are being accepted onlinethrough the my.uzbmb.uz site (in the applicant's personal cabinet) and will last until August 31 (10 days).

In order to file an appeal, the applicant must have participated in the test and not been excluded from the test.

The applicant who filed an appeal will be given a notice indicating the date and time of its review.

The applicant must come to the Knowledge Assessment Agency at the time specified in the notice with an identity document, a notice and an applicant's permit.

The review of appeals will begin on August 24, 2023. — Source
