It is known that according to Decree No. 81 of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated May 24, 2024, starting with the admission of the 2025/2026 academic year, subjects Admission to the fields of study in which a foreign language is the first subject in the complex is carried out on the basis of a national or international certificate that determines the level of foreign language knowledge.

In the current 2024/2025 academic year, the main part of those recommended for full-time study in the fields of education in the complex of subjects in which a foreign language is the first subject is made up of applicants with a foreign language certificate.
