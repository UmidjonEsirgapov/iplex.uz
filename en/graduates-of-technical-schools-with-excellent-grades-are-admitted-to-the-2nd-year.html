Admission to the 2nd course for technical school graduates procedure

According to the government decision (source), the procedure for admission of graduates who have successfully completed technical schools on the basis of an interview to the 2nd year of higher education institutions bachelor's studies is established.

Registration procedure

Applicants of this category from July 5, 2024 Online registration is being done through the Unified Education Services portal of the Ministry of Education, Science and Innovation my.edu.uz (source). During registration, the applicant's information is automatically generated on the platform.

The registration process will continue until July 25 of this year.

Institutions excluded from the Regulation

For information, this Regulation applies to higher military, higher paramilitary educational institutions, Tashkent State Law University, foreign law schools operating in Uzbekistan. and does not apply to non-governmental higher education organizations and their branches. — Official Data
