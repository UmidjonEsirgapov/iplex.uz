If the first-year students stay in the apartment, will they also be paid the rent?

Useful information for entrants and students:

Brief content:
When students admitted to full-time education of the first level in the new academic year stay in a rented apartment, part of the rent will be paid to them. instead, a part of the monthly rent payment to students will be made regardless of the rent amount in the city of Tashkent - one times the amount of the basic calculation (330 thousand soums), in other regions - half the amount of the basic calculation (165 thousand soums).
