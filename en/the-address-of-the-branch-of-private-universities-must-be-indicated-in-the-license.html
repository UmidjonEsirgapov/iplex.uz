The address of the branch of private universities must be indicated in the license!

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 80 dated February 21, 2022, non-state higher education institutions have the right to provide education only at the address specified in the license.

What does the exact indication of the address in the license mean?

Each non-state higher education organization must indicate the exact address of its operation in its license. This requirement is related to the following reasons:

  • Educational buildings and areas must meet the established standards
  • Before issuing a license, official inspections are carried out
  • For branches to operate legally, a separate address must be indicated on the license

If the university has opened a legal branch, the address of the branch must be clearly written in its license.

What does the presence of admission offices in other regions mean?

Some private universities may open admission offices in other regions for the convenience of citizens. However, this does not mean that you have the right to teach in that province.

  • Admission offices are only for submitting documents and providing information
  • The educational process is carried out only at the address indicated on the license

If a university tells you that it has a branch in another province, list the operating address on the license it is recommended to check.

Conclusion

In order for the branches of private universities to be legal, their addresses must be clearly indicated in the license. All students are advised to check the information about the place of study and the branch from official sources. — More information
