Applications for the second wave of the President Tech Award competition, with a total prize fund of 1 million dollars, have started!

Participation in the exciting competition and don't miss a unique opportunity to bring your ideas to life! If you are ready to try your hand and create advanced IT-solutions, this opportunity is for you!

The competition is divided into main and special directions.

The main direction of the competition is held in the following categories:

  1. Technologies based on artificial intelligence;
  2. Digital in the social sphere technologies;
  3. MicroSaaS and AdTech;
  4. Computer and mobile games;
  5. Entrepreneurship and financial technologies.

The special course consists of a Grand Prix Hackathon, which involves creating a digital solution within 72 hoursh.

Prize fund remains unchanged — 1st, 2nd, 3rd place winners in each of the 5 categories will be determined and receive the following prizes:

  • 1st place - $100,000
  • 2nd place - $50,000
  • 3rd place - $30,000
