Entrance exams will be facilitated for applicants with disabilities

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Admission to universities in defectology, speech therapy, deaf pedagogy, oligophrenopedagogy will be based on a full grant. based on the decision, it is planned to provide 475 needy persons with modern foreign prosthetic-orthopedic items (foot, hip and lower leg prostheses, and foot apparatus) free of charge and to expand the list of prosthetic-orthopedic and rehabilitation items provided to needy persons with disabilities by July 1. admission to universities is based on a full grant. People with disabilities can use state services without queues, they can enter official receptions without queues. With the new decision, these and other facilities are provided to persons with disabilities.

Photo: AP

Information and materials about the admission procedure and test for applicants with disabilities from the 2023/2024 academic year in a convenient format, it is planned to show them with videos, sign language or subtitles.

This is defined in the Presidential Decree "On additional measures to improve the system of support for persons with disabilities" signed on February 27. "defectology" master's degrees, including speech therapy, deaf pedagogy, and oligophrenopedagogy, admission to universities is carried out on the basis of a full state grant. Also, with this document, people of this category are allowed to use public services without queue and to receive officials in government offices. Privileges for access are also provided.

In the "Legal Aid" information system, a system will be introduced to help the lawyers provide free professional legal assistance voluntarily to the disabled. free meals will be introduced.

The procedure for requiring a medical report to issue a certificate for prosthetics-orthopedics and rehabilitation items will be canceled.

In addition, based on the decision, 475 needy persons will be provided free of charge with modern foreign prosthetics-orthopedics (foot, hip, lower leg prostheses, and leg apparatus) during 2023, and prosthetics-orthopetics will be provided to needy persons with disabilities until July 1. and it is planned to expand the list of rehabilitation items. It is planned to systematically provide citizens with 6 additional tools (currently 18 in the current list), such as an electric wheelchair, a smart watch with special functions, a braille writing device, a voice thermometer and tonometer, complex summer orthopedic shoes. — Read on infoedu.uz
