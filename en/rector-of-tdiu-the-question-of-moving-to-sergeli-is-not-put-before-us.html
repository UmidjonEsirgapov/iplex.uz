Rector of TDIU: "The question of moving to Sergeli is not put before us"

In an interview with Kun.uz, Rector of the University of Economics Tolkin Teshaboyev explained why the financial and fiscal institutes were added to the University of Economics, from the new academic year answered the questions about where students will start their studies and what will be the fate of the current buildings of HEIs.

By the presidential decree of February 7, 2024, the Institute of Finance and Fiscal Economics joined the Tashkent State University and became the only The University of Economics was established. According to the decision, starting from the 2023/24 academic year, graduate students studying at the Institute of Finance and Fiscal will receive a new university diploma. This will transfer the students of the 2nd and 3rd year of the three higher education institutions to the appropriate educational fields.

Earlier, in November 2023, the press service of the Tashkent city administration announced that there is no possibility of the transfer of the Tashkent State University of Economics and the Institute of Finance to the Sergeli district.

Rector of the University of Economics Tolkin. Teshaboyev informed Kun.uz about these and other issues.

According to his words, rumors about the transfer of universities to Sergeli district are groundless. A total of 13 objects of the three HEIs will remain under the reorganized Tashkent State University of Economics.

"Many people ask me when they will move to Sergeli. It is clearly stated in the decision that all buildings will remain under the control of the university. The question of moving to Sergeli has not been put before us," the rector said.

He added that the Ministries of Economy and Finance It is designated as the supervisory board of TDIU. "All our activities are carried out in coordination with the supervisory board," said Tolqin Teshaboyev.

Student distribution

According to the rector of TDIU, next year, students of Finance and Fiscal Institutes studying economics will come and study at the University of Economics; Finance and banking students will be transferred to the former Financial Institute. Also, tax students will continue to study at the former Fiscal Institute.

"Students should not suffer from this distribution of faculties. They should study in one building on the same day. Now they will not have to study for one hour in another building and another hour in another building. This decision was made both in social direction and taking into account travel expenses," he said. Teshaboyev.

When does the new academic year start?

Students will be admitted to the new university from the 2024/2025 academic year. In this case, admission to study on the basis of a state grant is determined within the parameters of the state order for admission to state higher education institutions. The educational process is conducted in the state language, English and other foreign languages.

According to the rector, including 3 universities, more than 44,000 students are studying.

"This is the largest higher education institution in the republic. I think there are many applicants in economics, accounting, and taxation. We will determine next year's quotas in agreement with the Ministry of Economy. I hope that the demand will increase", said Teshaboyev.

Where will students study in the new academic year?

According to the decision, an experienced design organization will be involved until the beginning of the 2024/2025 academic year to build a modern campus based on the existing areas and buildings of the University of Economics. A master plan will be developed.

According to the Rector, this campus will be built on the vacant areas of the current University of Economics. The area of the university on Islam Karimov Street will not be disturbed. Of course, a new building will be built in the vacant areas in agreement with the Ministry of Construction," he explained. Also, the decision envisages the construction of a multi-functional multi-storey building, which will meet international standards, in the area of 183 Almazor Street, Chilonzor District, Tashkent, including educational auditoriums, co-working centers, an IT laboratory, a business incubator and a conference hall. At the same time, this According to Teshaboyev, a dormitory for students of the former Financial Institute will be built in this area, due to the demolition of the old buildings in the area.

"With the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation we came to build a student residence. The student should not be in need of social protection. And it was done in order to spend as little as possible on road expenses. The biggest problem is providing students with TTJ. Apartment prices are increasing in Tashkent, which creates difficulties for students," said the rector of the university.

The reason for the merger of universities

The rector also explained the reason for the merger of 3 universities. There were faculties.

“For example, accounting, tax, etc. were also in Finance, Fiscal, Economy. They are taught by various methods. The origin of the decision was caused by the issue of unification of returned subjects. Now, by combining all of them, a single system and, of course, new classifiers will be introduced into the educational process, new directions will be opened," Teshaboyev said. — Details here
