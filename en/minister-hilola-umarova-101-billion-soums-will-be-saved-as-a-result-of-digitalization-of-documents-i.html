Minister Hilola Umarova: 10.1 billion soums will be saved as a result of digitalization of documents in schools

As you know, every year the documents kept in educational institutions are transferred to electronic form. According to the Minister of Preschool and School Education Hilola Umarova, starting from the 2024-2025 academic year, educational and pedagogical documents in schools will be fully digitized. This process not only creates convenience, but also significant savings.

As a result of the transition to electronic form, the following changes will take place:

  • on the "emaktab.uz" platform, the electronic evaluation table will be created automatically and parents will be able to download it from the system.
  • Teachers and school management time is saved, each teacher saves 75 hours per year.
  • In the process of school administration, paperwork is eliminated and efficiency increases.
  • 232 tons of paper and logistics costs are saved.
  • According to the state budget, 10.1 billion annually Soum funds will be saved.
