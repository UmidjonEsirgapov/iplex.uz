How are the certificate results calculated?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The results of the test will be published on the official website of the State Test Center no later than twenty working days from the date of their completion. in the admission tests, the highest score is given to the applicant who scored 86 percent and above, which is classified (proportional) in relation to the maximum score of this subject.
For example, an applicant with a certificate in mathematics has a mastery of 60 percent in the certificate, the place of mathematics in the complex of subjects is the first (3.2 points for each correct answer, maximum for 30 test tasks 96 points), then 60% of it is 57.6 points (3.2*30=96 points, 96*60/100=57.6).

The certificate is issued for a period of 3 years to persons who have scored at least 60% of the highest points according to the results of the test. will be published on the official website of the State Test Center no later than 1.

In this case, the applicant who scored from 60% to 85% of the highest score in accordance with the mastery percentage in the certificate will be graded in relation to the maximum score of this subject (proportionately) and 86% and above. the highest score is given to the applicant.

In other words, a certain percentage of the maximum score is given depending on the subject from which the certificate is obtained in the set of subjects.

For example, the mastery of the applicant with a certificate in mathematics is 60%, and the first place of mathematics in the set of subjects (for each correct answer 3.2 points, a maximum of 96 points for 30 test tasks), then 60% of it is 57.6 points (3.2*30=96 points, 96*60/100=57.6). If the applicant's mastery of the certificate is 80%, it will be 76.8 points (3.2*30=96 points, 96*80/100=76.8).
