As part of the "Future scientist" competition, the "First step for women's business" competition is announced

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

General information:

Talented and talented In order to support the results and initiatives of the innovative activities of women, women and young scientists by the state, to create the necessary conditions for their scientific research, and to attract potential young people to science, the Youth Academy announced the contest "The First Step for Women's Business" as part of the "Future Scientist" contest. The "Future Scientist" contest will be held in accordance with the requirements specified in the regulation on the procedure for holding it. 10 billion soums have been allocated for this competition from the Science Funding and Innovation Support Fund.

Applications for business projects of young scientists in new promising areas are accepted within the framework of the competition "First Step for Women's Business". applications are accepted. Business project is a new business project that is characterized by the application of innovative developments that create a new product or service type in conditions of high risk of implementation, scope and rapid growth.

Conditions of the submitted business project; not to exceed 40 years of age, regardless of the level, rank, position and workplace;

  • 1 team participates in 1 project (the number of team members should not exceed 5 people);
  • The amount of funds requested for the implementation of the assigned business project is from the size of the Regulation approved by the Cabinet of Ministers decision No. 222 dated April 19, 2021

    • In the framework of the competition, proposals for business projects will be accepted HERE until June 1, 2022 at 18:00.
