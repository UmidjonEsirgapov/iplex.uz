About the applicants who apply to the OTM without tests

Who are the applicants who are exempt from the tests?

Currently, in 2025, a total of 678 applicants are national corresponding to the range of subjects. or was exempted from tests due to having an international certificate. For them, a permit has not been created, that is, they will not take part in the test.

These applicants were evaluated based on the following points depending on the certificate:

The distribution of points is as follows:

  • 189 points — 361 applicants

  • between 180-189 points — 207 people

  • between 170-180 points — 57 people

  • between 160-170 points — 46 people

  • 150-160 points range — 3 people

  • 126 points (takes a creative test) — 3 people

  • 96 points (takes a creative test) — 1 person

In what order will they participate in the competition?

After these tests are completed, second-stage selection process begins for applicants. At this stage, they have the right to specify the following for 15 days:

  • ✅ Higher educational institution

  • ✅ Undergraduate education

  • ✅ Form of education (day, evening, part-time)

  • ✅ Selection priority

Important note!

⚠️ Applicants who do not use the above option within the specified period cannot participate in the higher education selection and are not recommended to be a student.

Conclusion

This option is a great advantage for applicants who are applying to OTM without tests in 2025 admission. However, it is necessary to strictly follow the established procedure. Those who are exempted from the test based on the certificate should not miss the selection stage, otherwise they will lose the right to use the privilege. — Official Data
