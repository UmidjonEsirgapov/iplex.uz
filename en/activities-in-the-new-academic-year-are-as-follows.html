List of thematic months in schools

In order to educate students in the spirit of spirituality, ethics and patriotism in the educational system of Uzbekistan, various events are held throughout the year. thematic months are held. Below are the topics for each month.

September - Independence Month

Various events are organized in order to glorify the glory of the independence of the Republic of Uzbekistan, to increase the sense of national pride and loyalty to the Motherland among young people.

October - the month of honoring teachers and coaches

Paying tribute to the teachers of students shows that the work of pedagogues is appreciated.

November - Tolerance Month

Interethnic harmony, religious tolerance and human values are promoted.

December - Legal Culture Improvement Month

Works are carried out to provide students with legal knowledge and respect for the law.

January - Patriotic month

Through military-patriotic events, competitions and meetings, love for the Motherland is strengthened among young people.

February - the month of Great Scholars

The life and scientific heritage of great Uzbek scholars will be studied.

March - the Month of National Values

Continuously, national traditions, events will be held to promote traditions and values.

April is the month of studying the heritage of great ancestors

Works will be carried out to convey the rich cultural and historical heritage left by our ancestors to the students.

May - the month of memory and appreciation

Remembrance and memorial events will be organized for ancestors who sacrificed their lives for the freedom of Uzbekistan.

Conclusion

Thematic months play an important role in the education of schoolchildren. Through them, our youth will grow up in the spirit of national pride, tolerance, legal culture, loyalty to the Motherland and respect for the rich historical heritage. — Details here
