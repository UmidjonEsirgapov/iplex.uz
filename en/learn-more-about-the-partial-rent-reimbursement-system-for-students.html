Learn more about the partial rent reimbursement system for students

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Complete information about the system of reimbursing part of the rent payments to students 1️⃣ To whom the rent payments will be reimbursed? In order to receive compensation, it is necessary to have a legal rental contract registered on the website ijara.soliq.uz.

Full information about the system of reimbursing part of the rent payments to students

1️⃣ To whom the rent payments are reimbursed?

  • The student must not be covered by a dormitory. If a student rents a dormitory, compensation will not be given.

  • The student must live on rent in a house that does not belong to him or his parents.

  • Applicable only to students of public higher education institutions, not to students of private HEIs.

  • Citizen of Uzbekistan or a person living permanently in the republic must be, does not apply to foreign citizens.

  • Students studying in the form of full-time education are covered, except for evening, part-time, distance and joint education programs. For example, it is not given to those who are registered in Samarkand and study at a university in Samarkand.

  • Compensation is also provided to those who study on the basis of a state grant and contract.

2️⃣ How much compensation is given?

  • The full amount of the base calculation amount for each month in Tashkent is 412 000 soums.

  • In other regions, compensation is provided in the amount of 206,000 soums.

  • 1st-year students can get without a contract in September and October, but a contract is required starting from November.

  • If two or more students live in the same residence, they must all be listed in the contract.

  • The rent income of the landlords is not taxed (Tax Code, Article 378).

4️⃣ Who will be compensated in the first place?

  • Students in need of social assistance.

  • Active students who set an example.

  • Note: The limit of reimbursement of rent payments in state HEIs covers 60% of the number of students. If the budget limit is reached, additional funding can be provided by the university for the remaining students.

5️⃣ How to submit an application?

  • The application must be submitted online by the 7th of every month.

  • Register in the Kontrakt.edu.uz system and enter your personal office.

  • "Rent" fees" section is selected and the "Send application" button is clicked.

6️⃣ Reasons for rejection

  • If a fake document is submitted, the address is incorrect or the application is not submitted within the deadline, it will be rejected.

  • Students who have forged contracts or provided false information will be registered and compensated.

  • Rejected applications can be resubmitted after the deficiencies have been corrected.

7️⃣ Conditions for termination of compensation

  • If the student goes on academic leave, leaves the course or is expelled, the payment will be stopped.

  • 3 working days if the lease contract is canceled or the address changes. It is mandatory to report within.

Conclusion

The system of covering part of the rent payments to students serves as financial support for students studying in higher education. This program is intended for students of public higher education institutions, and students can receive compensation for the rented house on the basis of a legal contract. Applications are submitted online and priority is given to students in need of social assistance and active students. Correct submission of documents is very important for the correct and fair functioning of the system. — Details here
