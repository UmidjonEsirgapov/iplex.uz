It is suggested to spend 4-5 days a week in evening education

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The draft decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On measures to organize part-time and evening education in professional educational institutions" has been submitted to the discussion portal for regulatory and legal documents. postponed.
According to it: - until the end of the academic semester, part-time education is carried out in the form of independent, including distance education; - the student is called to the educational institution at least once for a training session based on the approved schedule during each semester; - distance learning in part-time and evening education is carried out without leaving the students' main workplace; - studying in the evening education will consist of 4-5 days a week.
