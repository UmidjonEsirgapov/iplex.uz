Privileges for access to OTM increased by one more

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-369 dated October 25, 2024, - observance of the rules of etiquette for students of state comprehensive schools, Presidential schools, creative schools, specialized schools and boarding schools (hereinafter - schools). Education in the spirit of loyalty to the motherland and strong self-confidence, helping students with low academic, discipline and physical performance to achieve high results; - creation of favorable conditions for meaningful spending of free time of students in schools; - "Support of the Motherland" detachments consisting of 20 students of grades 10-11 will be formed in schools under the leadership of a teacher of primary education until convocation, and their activities will be coordinated by the "Support of the Motherland" movement; - One member of the "Support of the Motherland" detachment who actively participated in the activities of the "Support of the Motherland" movement and graduated from schools with excellent grades will be given a preferential recommendation for admission to higher military educational institutions and higher paramilitary educational institutions of the ministries and agencies that are part of the Armed Forces in accordance with the selection procedure determined by the Ministry of Defense; - Members of the "Support of the Motherland" detachment, who have the relevant recommendation letters of the "Support of the Fatherland" movement, have a privilege in the form of 5% of the points they have scored according to the results of tests or individual final interviews when entering the relevant educational fields of higher military educational institutions of ministries and agencies that are part of the Armed Forces, as well as higher paramilitary educational institutions.

According to the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-369 of October 25, 2024,

- students of state general education schools, Presidential schools, creative schools, specialized schools and boarding schools (hereinafter referred to as schools) must observe the rules of etiquette, loyalty to the Motherland and self-confidence educating in the spirit, helping students with low academic performance, discipline and physical performance to achieve high results;

– creating favorable conditions for students to meaningfully spend their free time in schools;

– based on the number of classes under the guidance of a pre-service teacher, the composition 20 10-11 grades "Support of the Motherland" detachments consisting of students will be formed and their activities will be coordinated by the "Support of the Motherland" movement;

- one member of each "Support of the Motherland" squad who actively participated in the activities of the "Support of the Motherland" movement and graduated from school with excellent grades will be included in the Armed Forces in accordance with the selection procedure determined by the Ministry of Defense preferential recommendation for admission to higher military educational institutions and higher paramilitary educational institutions of incoming ministries and departments is given;

- members of the "Support of the Fatherland" detachment who have the relevant recommendation letters of the "Support of the Fatherland" movement to study in the relevant educational fields of higher military educational institutions and higher paramilitary educational institutions of the ministries and agencies that are part of the Armed Forces according to the results of entrance tests or individual final interviews, they will receive a bonus in the form of 5% of additional points. — Source
