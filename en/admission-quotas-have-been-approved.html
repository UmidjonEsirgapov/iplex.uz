Admission quotas have been approved

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The President signed the decision "On the parameters of the state order for admission to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan in the 2021/2022 academic year". From the beginning of the 2021/2022 academic year, the state order for the training of personnel on the basis of a state grant will be formed for foreign higher education organizations and their branches operating in Uzbekistan, as well as for non-state higher education organizations. formed based on the need for educated personnel; A tripartite contract (recruitment client-student-higher education organization) will be concluded with applicants who scored the highest points in the entrance exams to branches and non-state higher education organizations and who expressed their desire to work in the staffing system for at least 5 years after graduation; if the applicants who scored the highest points in the entrance exams to branches and non-governmental higher education organizations do not express their desire to sign a contract with the personnel customer, the right to conclude a contract is given to the next applicant in accordance with the sequence of points scored in the entrance exams; The educational expenses of the students admitted to study on the basis of the state order are covered by the personnel orderers from the funds provided for these purposes in the parameters of the state budget.
