It is a private university based on advanced educational achievements of Northern European countries. On August 8, 2022, the university started its activities based on license number 034528.

What tasks does the International Nordic University set for itself?

  • XNU serves as a modern and innovative platform for Finland and other developed Nordic countries in Uzbekistan for human capital and business development, education, startups and international projects cooperation center.
  • Preparation of competitive specialists for the international and local labor market, short-term courses that serve to improve their skills in specific professions.
  • In cooperation with Finland's Karelia University of Applied Sciences, it creates conditions for students to study in joint educational programs.
  • To study the areas of interest and abilities of students, to divide them into groups, to ensure participation in start-up projects, contests of innovative ideas, and intellectual debates.
  • Organizes special master classes and seminar-trainings for students with the participation of foreign experts and professors.
  • Supporting the publication of articles by talented students in scientific journals available in Scopus/Web of Science databases through the mentor-student tradition, etc.
