On June 17, 2020, the Cabinet of Ministers Decision No. 389 "On measures to establish a branch of the University of Economics and Technology of Turkey in Tashkent" was adopted.

According to the decision, a branch of the University of Economics and Technology of Turkey, which provides non-state educational services, was established in Tashkent. It will start from the 2020/2021 academic year.

The branch is considered a non-governmental higher education organization, and its main tasks are to expand the commercialization of scientific research results, attract investments for the implementation of innovative ideas of students, etc.

The graduates of the branch are awarded a university diploma, which is recognized as a document of higher education in Uzbekistan.
