Minister of Preschool and School Education Hilola Umarova revealed the reason why winners of science Olympiads and sports competitions are encouraged based on two different standards

Preschool and school At a press conference, Minister of Education Hilola Umarova answered blogger Khushnudbek Khudoyberdiyev's question that winners of science Olympiads and sports competitions are encouraged on the basis of two different standards, and this issue is being discussed on social networks. previously, students who won science olympiads had the privilege to study in higher education institutions. However, with the decision of the President in 2019, a system of incentives for students who won science olympiads, in particular world olympiads, was established.

According to the decision, the winner of the gold medal in the international IT, mathematics, physics, chemistry and biology science olympiads will be awarded BHM 500 times.

The Minister also compared sports competitions and science Olympiads and noted the following: "In many cases, sports competitions and science Olympiads are held at the same time. But one thing should be paid attention to: science Olympiads are held every year, while Olympiads are held every 4 years."
