Admission to pedagogic universities can be done through a two-stage exam

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the President No. PQ-290 of June 21, 2022, in the 2024/2025 academic year, as a pilot test, the pedagogical education fields of Tashkent State Pedagogical University named after Nizami (requiring special talent) admission processes are carried out in two stages, including the first stage - in the form of a preliminary exam, and the second stage - through entrance tests.
Here: preliminary exams and entrance tests are conducted by the State Testing Center (currently called the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency); as part of the preliminary examination, applicants' aptitude for the teaching profession is studied; Only applicants who have successfully passed the preliminary exam will be allowed to participate in the entrance test.
By January 1, 2024, a temporary procedure for admission to pedagogic education courses of the University will be developed and approved. In the 2024/2025 academic year, as a pilot test, the admission process to pedagogical education courses of Nizamiy Tashkent State Pedagogical University (with the exception of education courses requiring special talent) will be carried out in two stages, including the first stage — in the form of a preliminary exam, and the second stage — through entrance tests.

In this:

  • preliminary exams and entrance tests are conducted by the State Testing Center (currently known as the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency);
  • applicants' aptitude for the teaching profession is studied within the framework of the preliminary exam;
  • only applicants who have successfully passed the preliminary exam are allowed to participate in the entrance tests.

Until January 1, 2024, the University's pedagogy a temporary procedure for admission to educational courses will be developed and approved.

Also, based on the results of the pilot test, the decision stipulates the task of submitting a proposal to the Cabinet of Ministers to implement the procedure of conducting entrance exams for pedagogic educational courses in two stages to other higher educational institutions that prepare pedagogic personnel. — More information
