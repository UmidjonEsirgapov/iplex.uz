A system of referral to sports through an electronic module will be introduced

Until August 15, 2025, a special electronic module will be launched. Through this module, the physiological and anthropometric conditions of each student are analyzed, and suitable sports are recommended for them.

Medical examination is carried out in central polyclinics

Before the specified period, each student undergoes an in-depth medical examination in regional central polyclinics. This process plays an important role in the orientation to sports and serves to study the health status of students in depth.

Special educational seminars will be held for school nurses

Special seminars for nurses working in all schools will be organized starting from the 2025/2026 academic year. Through these seminars, they will acquire the knowledge and skills to obtain, analyze and give appropriate referrals to students' health.
