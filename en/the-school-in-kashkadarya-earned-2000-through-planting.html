The school in Kashkadarya earned $2,000 through planting

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The school in the Koson district of Kashkadarya region earned $2,000 through seedlings.
There are 8,000 seedlings ready for sale.
In 2023, the school will earn more than $2,000 through seedlings. In addition, the 28th school in the district has more than 13,000 fruit and ornamental trees.

The school in the Koson district of Kashkadarya region earned $2,000 through planting. This was reported by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.

It is said that landscaping works were carried out in the framework of "Green Space" in the Koson district of Kashkadarya region. In particular, the 15th, 28th, 77th schools in the district had the opportunity to grow fruit and ornamental trees and sell them to local residents to earn income. It should be noted that the garden of school 77 has 18 thousand trees. Of these, 8,000 seedlings are ready for sale. In 2023, the school earned more than 2,000 US dollars through plantings. School 28 has more than 13,000 fruit and ornamental trees. This school has also been engaged in germination and nursery cultivation for many years, and its annual additional income is on average 2500 US dollars. It has become a tradition to encourage talented students and teachers and coaches through earned income. — Official Data
