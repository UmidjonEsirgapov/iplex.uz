5 effective ways to strengthen memory for applicants

Memory is the most important component of a successful educational process. It is especially important for applicants to remember a large amount of information and be able to use it in exams. Check out the 5 most effective memory-building tips below.

1. Repeat what you read

  • In order for the newly learned information to be remembered quickly, it is important to repeat it within 24 hours.
  • For several days try and repeat it again.
  • Regular repetition helps the information to be retained for a long time.

2. Write a synopsis

  • Writing by hand is one of the most effective memorizing methods for the brain.
  • Write down the important parts separately, use colored pencils.
  • Rewriting in a way that is understandable to you will help you deeper understand the topic.

3. Explain what you have learned to others

  • Explaining the topic to your friends or yourself is an effective way to remember information.
  • Through this method, the brain consolidates important information and it becomes easier to remember them faster.

4. Eat right

  • In order to strengthen your memory, you should eat products that are good for the brain.
  • Nuts, fish, eggs, greens and dark chocolate improve brain activity.

5. Don't forget to get enough sleep

  • 7-8 hours of quality sleep at night improves brain activity.
  • During sleep the brain organizes learned information and consolidates it.
  • Study without sleep reduces efficiency.

Conclusion

To strengthen memory It is important to repeat, write, explain, eat and sleep properly. If you regularly use these methods, your ability to remember will increase significantly and you can effectively prepare for exams. — Source
