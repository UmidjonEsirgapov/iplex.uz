Brief about the university and the program:

Cambridge University in Great Britain is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities founded in 1209. The MBA program for which the grant is offered is one of the world's leading business programs taught at one of the world's most prestigious universities. The Boustany MBA Cambridge Scholarship grant program is awarded every two years for a one-year course taught at the University of Cambridge, Judge Business School.

Requirements for candidates:

  • All international students can apply for the grant program;
  • Candidates must have a high academic background;
  • All candidates can apply for the Cambridge University MBA scholarship after receiving an offer letter from the university itself;
  • Candidates must have a master's degree in business.

Benefits of the grant:

The grant will fund:

  • Financial support of £30,000 (a grant of approximately US$41,200) towards tuition fees,
  • Internship-related travel and accommodation expenses.

This grant program is awarded for MBA program courses starting in Fall 2022.

Application process:

Before applying for the grant program, please apply here for the University of Cambridge MBA program. After that, you can send the following documents:

  • CV (with photo);
  • Acceptance letter from the university (for MBA);
  • GMAT certificate;

you can send it to the following email address:

  • admissions@boustany-foundation.org

Application deadline: May 15, 2022.
