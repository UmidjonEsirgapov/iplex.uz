Registan educational grant. Final stage

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The final stage of the competition will be held on October 24 in one of the auditoriums of the "REGISTON" educational center, where special exams are held.
There will be 100 grant winners. In addition to receiving special prizes, souvenirs, books, manuals and QR-code diplomas issued by "Regbooks" publishing house, the winners of the prizes will be encouraged as follows: 1st place winners: "REGISTON" educational center will receive a grant voucher for 6 months of free education.
Grant place - 7 for 10 directions and the number of grants is 70; Note: Grant vouchers are used to study at the "REGISTON" educational center in order to encourage the education of talented young people.

As you know, "REGISTON" educational center supports talented young people and encourages the education of young people with the OLIYGOH.UZ team. in cooperation with the Republic announced the Grant competition with a total prize pool of 100 million soums.

Who will participate in the Final stage of the competition?

How will the final stage take place?

The final stage of the competition will be held on October 24 "REGISTON" school The exam will be held in one of the center's auditoriums where special exams are held. The final stage will be held for the youth of the valley and Syrdarya region in one of the branches of the "REGISTON" educational center in Tashkent, and for young people living in other regions of our republic, in the regional branches located in the Samarkand region. will pass. Each finalist will receive a separate text message with the address, time and region of the exam.
