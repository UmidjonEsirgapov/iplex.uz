Good news for applicants: 5 for some areas the requirement of one-year experience has been canceled

According to the new admission procedure, now 5 years of pedagogical work experience is not required to apply for part-time education in some educational fields. This change is a great opportunity for applicants and creates a foundation for more students to get higher education.

Fields that do not require 5 years of work experience

Five years of work experience is no longer required to apply for part-time study in the following fields of study:

  • Pedagogy

  • Preschool education

  • History

  • Physical education and sports in preschool and primary education

  • National idea, spiritual foundations and legal education

This change has pedagogical experience It is a great opportunity for young people who are not yet interested in these fields.

Fields requiring 5 years of work experience

In the following fields, 5 years of pedagogical work is still required to enter the part-time department:

  • Primary education

  • Fine arts and engineering graphics

  • Music education

  • Physical culture

  • Technological education

Also applies to non-state universities

❗️This procedure Activity in the Republic of Uzbekistan also applies to all licensed private higher education institutions. So, now it is possible to use this opportunity when applying to non-state HEIs.
