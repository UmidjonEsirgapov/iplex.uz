Applications are being accepted for the competition "Mirzo Ulugbek's Successors".

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Admission to the Republican competition "Successors of Mirzo Ulugbek" continues. A great opportunity for young people interested in science and innovation!
Those who want to participate in the competition can register on vorislar.uz until February 25, 2025 or contact the officials of the higher education institution where they are studying.
