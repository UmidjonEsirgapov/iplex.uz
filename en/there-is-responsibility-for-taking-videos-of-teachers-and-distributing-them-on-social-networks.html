Additional liability: If the teacher sues in a civil court for moral damages, he may be required to pay additional damages in addition to the above fines. According to the second part of Article 1021 of the Civil Code, if the damage was caused due to the dissemination of information that insults the honor, dignity and professional reputation, moral damage is determined to be compensated regardless of the fault of the person who caused it. the obligation to compensate for damages is placed on the persons who spill their dignity and business reputation.
