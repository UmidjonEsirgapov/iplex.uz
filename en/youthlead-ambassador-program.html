YP2LE is a USAID-funded effort to create and disseminate evidence of positive youth development.

“Youth Lead The Ambassadorprogram is an 18-month training and leadership opportunity for young leaders who have demonstrated success in mobilizing other youth to make a positive impact in their community. Ambassadors play an important role in communicating the benefits of YouthLead to national, regional and local change-making groups, and in raising awareness of the YouthLead platform as a resource and networking center for leaders.

As part of the program, YouthLead Ambassadors develop and implement leadership projects on important issues affecting young people. These include youth-led learning activities that contribute to knowledge, skills development, local activism and peer networking.

Ambassadors are selected from thousands of candidates through an open application process. Graduates of the Ambassador program will join the global alumni network of former YouthLead Ambassadors and Peer Advisors.

Program duration:

May 2022 - October 2023 (18 months).

Ambassador duties:

To act as a Youth Leader Ambassador, you must meet the following criteria:

-Be between the ages of 18-30 as of January 1, 2022.
-Interest in building and practicing your leadership skills, working collaboratively with young people from different backgrounds and experiences, and developing professional relationships.
-Good written and spoken English (please prepare your application in English).
-Strong communication and interpersonal skills (English language).
-Be able to work online comfortably on Google (Google Meets), WhatsApp, Zoom or other online work and meeting platforms.
-Have regular access to the Internet to participate in online meetings (e.g. Zoom) and communicate with other Ambassadors and the YouthLead team using online tools (e.g. WhatsApp, Google and Google Meets).

Ambassadors from late May 2022 to October 2023 they will work remotely (online) for 18 months until the end. Members are expected to communicate regularly with their peers and the YP2LE/YouthLead team via email or other online collaboration tools and platforms.

Ambassadors receive a small honorarium for successfully completing their duties.
