General information:

The ANSO grant program, which is considered one of the prestigious grant programs by the Chinese government to support young people and improve their scientific skills, has been given since 2019. This grant is offered annually to 200 master's degree and 300 doctoral degree students to study at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) or Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS).

Benefits of the grant:

  • Full tuition reimbursement
  • Monthly stipend: a) For Master's degree: $475 per month, up to 36 months; b) PhD: $1,000 or $1,200 per month (depending on passing the qualifying examination organized by USTC/UCAS), up to 48 months;
  • Medical insurance;
  • Application fees are also covered;
  • Travel expenses from home country to China are covered;

Note: Education at universities in the process of receiving, if the mastering indicators of the grant holder decrease: — Official Data

• Termination of the scholarship;
• This may lead to consequences such as suspension of studies in the master's/doctoral program;
Requirements for candidates:
• Not having Chinese citizenship;
• Fluency in English or Chinese knowledge;
• For Master's applicants: born after January 1, 1992;
• For PhD applicants: born after January 1, 1987;
• Meet the admission criteria for USTC/UCAS international students;
• Not perform other assignments during the term of the scholarship.
Application Process:
The ANSO Scholarship can be applied for from November 1, 2021 (exact dates may be slightly different for USTC and UCAS, check their websites for details).
