Organize your child's free time in a meaningful way. According to the order No. 246 dated July 28, a number of requirements are set for parents in accordance with the internal rules of the school. Below are the main requirements that parents must follow:

Requirements for parents:

  1. Get to know the rules of the internal order

      • Know the rules of the internal order of the educational institution and follow them.

  2. Respect the teacher do

      • Respect the honor and dignity of teachers, do not interfere in the lesson process and do not insult pedagogues.

  3. Supervise your child

      • Supervise your child's compliance with school internal rules and etiquette.

  4. Ensure your child's full participation in classes

      • Ensure your child's regular attendance in classes, as well as educational materials, workbooks and necessary educational equipment.
