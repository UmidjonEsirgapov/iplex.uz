Online admission to schools in city and regional centers has started for the districts

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
This process will begin on August 16, from 2:00 p.m.
To apply, citizens should register on the my.maktab.uz website of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.
Steps of registration and application submission on this website is shown in sequence, which makes the process easier.

Now citizens with permanent residence in districts will have the opportunity to apply online for the 1st grade of general secondary education schools located in Tashkent city and regional centers and with vacancies. This process will start on August 16, from 14:00. To apply, citizens should register on the website of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education my.maktab.uz. The registration and application process is step-by-step on this site, which makes the process easy.

The online admission process simplifies school admission and provides great convenience for parents living in districts. You can also complete the registration process step by step using the instructions provided on the site. — Read on infoedu.uz
