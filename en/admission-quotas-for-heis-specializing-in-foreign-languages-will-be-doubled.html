Admission quotas for HEIs specializing in foreign languages ​​will be doubled

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In the 2021/22 academic year, the parameters of admission to HEIs specialized in teaching foreign languages will be increased by 2 times, and the admission rates based on state grants will be increased by 3 times.
Also, according to the decision, starting from the 2022/23 academic year, graduates of HEIs specializing in teaching foreign languages will, in addition to passing the state certification exams, take exams in national or other leading international assessment systems that are equivalent to it. 100%, and the acceptance rates based on the state grant will be increased by 3 times.

This was determined in the presidential decree on popularizing the study of foreign languages.

Also, according to the decree, from the 2022/23 academic year, graduates of HEIs specializing in teaching foreign languages, in addition to passing state attestation exams, will also be required to take national or equivalent leading international evaluation systems.

In this case, the expenses of the graduates will be borne by the Higher Education Institution.

Starting from the 2022/23 academic year, the procedure for the participation of persons with a national or equivalent international certificate in philology majors will be introduced in the competitions for master's degrees and post-higher education specialties of Higher Education Institutions. or an international certificate of the corresponding level, and for non-philological subjects, a national certificate of the B2 level or an international certificate of the corresponding level is required. — Read on infoedu.uz
