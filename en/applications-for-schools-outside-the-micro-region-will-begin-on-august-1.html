Applications for schools outside the micro-region will begin on August 1

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
You can submit an application for admission to 1st grade to schools located outside the micro-region through the my.maktab.uz platform.
Applications are accepted in the order in which they are sent. after that, the previously sent application for the micro-region will be automatically transferred to the main school.

Applications for schools outside the micro-region will start tomorrow - the ministry

As the Ministry of Pre-school and School Education has informed, sending applications for admission to the 1st grade to schools outside the micro-region will start tomorrow - August 1 at 11:00.

Through the my.maktab.uz platform, you can submit an application for admission to the 1st grade to schools located outside the micro-region.

Applications are accepted in the order of the time they are sent. After the applications are received in accordance with the specified quota, the admission processes are automatically stopped.

Once the child is admitted to a school outside the micro-region then, the previously submitted application for the micro-region will be automatically transferred to the main school. — More information
