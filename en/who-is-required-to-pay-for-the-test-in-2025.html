Fees and benefits for the 2025 entrance exam

According to the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan, applicants must pay for participation in entrance exams. This fee is determined annually on the basis of base calculation amount (BHM).

For 2025, the following procedure has been introduced:

  • BHM amount: 340,000 soums

  • Test fee: half of BHM – 170,000 soums

This payment is regulated on the basis of the Regulation on Education and is mandatory for the following applicants:

Those who must pay:

  • Persons who graduated from school, high school or college in 2024 and earlier

  • According to the legislation, applicants belonging to the following social protection categories are exempted from the test fee of 170,000 soums:

    • Graduates of 2025 - those graduating from school, lyceum or college this year

    • Group 1, 2 or 3 Citizens with disabilities (with appropriate conclusions) register (IHYR)"** and their children

    Important: To use the privilege, all documents must be uploaded to the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency (former DTM) portal.
