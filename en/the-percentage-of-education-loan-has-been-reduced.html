The percentage of education loan has been reduced

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Educational loan percentage reduced On July 25, the Central Bank made a decision to reduce the main interest rate by 0.5% per year to 13.5%. will decrease.
Before the beginning of the 2024/2025 academic year, it will be possible to submit applications for educational loans through the "Educational Loan" information system of the Ministry of Economy and Finance (talim-krediti.mf.uz) and the Unified interactive state services portal. made a decision to reduce the main rate by 0.5 percent per year to 13.5 percent. If the basic rate increases, then the loan percentage will not change, that is, it will not increase.

For information, now the education loan is allocated for boys in the amount of the Central Bank's basic rate (13.5%) for girls - without interest (0%).

It should be noted that according to the presidential decree Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, educational loans will be allocated from the funds of the Educational Loan Funding Fund only to full-time students. In the case of less than "3", a maximum of 80 percent of the payment-contract amount is allocated (except for children of families included in the "Single Register of Social Protection"). "Management of Higher Education Processes" (HEMIS) must be available in the information system.

Higher education organizations that have their own software must integrate their information system with the relevant information systems of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation or use them in order to receive an educational loan.
