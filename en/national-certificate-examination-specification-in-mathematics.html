TEST OPTION DETAIL FOR THE NATIONAL TEST SYSTEM IN MATHEMATICS

The purpose of this test option detail within the framework of the national test system for assessing the level of knowledge of general education subjects, the format of the test option used for determining and certifying the level of knowledge of students in mathematics is to determine the format (tasks, type, time frame), the content of the subject, the levels of cognitive skills, the requirements for the level of preparation of students and the evaluation criteria.

I. BASIC REGULATORY DOCUMENTS FOR DEVELOPING TEST TASKS

  • Decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On measures to improve the quality of education and develop scientific research in the field of mathematics" dated May 7, 2020 PQ - No. 4708;
  • of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan Resolution No. 646 of October 12, 2020 "On the introduction of the national test system for assessing the knowledge of general education subjects";
  • Current state educational standard and curriculum for mathematics.II. SECTIONS COVERED

Based on the state education standard and curriculum, the following sections of mathematics are covered:

  1. Numbers and operations
  2. Algebraic transformations
  3. Equations and inequalities
  4. Functions
  5. Basics of mathematical analysis
  6. Geometry
  7. Collection, considerations, data analysis, combinatorics, probability theory and modeling

III. TYPES OF TEST TASKS

  • Y-1 is a closed test task with an alternative answer that has one correct answer.
  • Y-2 is a closed test task with an alternative answer that requires adaptation.
  • O is an open test task that requires a short answer (divided into items a and b).

IV. MATHEMATICS TEST OPTION FORMAT

VIII. EVALUATION CRITERIA

  1. Evaluation criteria of the closed test item (Y-1) with an alternative answer with one correct answer: a) if the specified answer is correct, 1.3 or 2.2 points depending on the level of difficulty;b) if the specified answer is incorrect, 0 points are given.
  2. A closed test with alternative answers that requires adaptation evaluation criterion of assignment (Y-2): a) if the specified answer is correct, 2.2 points; b) if the specified answer is incorrect, 0 points are given.
  3. Evaluation criterion of the open test task (O) requiring a short answer (divided into parts a and b): a) if only the answer in part a is correct, 1.5 points; b) if only b if the answer in part 1 is correct, 1.7 points; c) if the answers in parts a and b are correct, 3.2 points; d) if the answers in parts a and b are incorrect, 0 points are given.*Note: Additions, changes and corrections may be made to this document based on the approval result, relevant decisions and orders of competent state organizations.
