Process of admission to academic lyceums

1. Registration platform:

  • Applicants to academic lyceums are registered through the Unified interactive state services portal and the official website of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.
  • The registration process is carried out for free every year from June 10 to July 10 through the my.edu.uz platform.

2. Selection of course:

  • At the time of registration, applicants are given the opportunity to choose one of the courses of up to three academic lyceums with the same language of instruction.

3. Entrance tests:

  • The applicant participates in the entrance tests organized in the region of his choice, that is, in the Republic of Karakalpakstan, the regional center or in the city of Tashkent.
