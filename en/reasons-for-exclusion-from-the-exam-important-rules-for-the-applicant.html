In what cases will the applicant be excluded from the exam?

By the relevant decision of the Cabinet of Ministers (No. 261 dated 03.04.2018), the procedure for conducting entrance tests for admission to bachelor's degrees of higher education institutions is established.

According to it, in the following cases, the applicant will be excluded from the testing process and the results will not be issued:

  • When entering the area or building where the tests are held, if he tries to bring in a microcalculator, dictionary, table, book, mobile phone, computer, telecommunication devices and other tools, erasers, or uses them during the testing;
  • hands it to the applicant or helps them;
  • talks to each other during the tests;
  • takes the test materials out of the building, tears the page of the book of test tasks;
  • refuses to hand over the test materials after the end of the time allotted for the tests;
  • in the course of the tests, it is found that the applicant did not follow the procedures and rules through video surveillance.
