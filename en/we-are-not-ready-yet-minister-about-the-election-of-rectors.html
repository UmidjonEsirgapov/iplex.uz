Is there no plan to elect rectors in higher education institutions, is this not being considered? The reporter of Kun.uz addressed this question to the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation Kungirotboy Sharipov. The issue of electing rectors is included in the "Uzbekistan-2030" strategy. It has not yet been decided. If such a decision is made, of course, in most countries, the rectors will be elected for 5 years, the minister replied.

"Now, right now, I don't think we are ready. It takes time. You know, we have our own mentality. We can do it when public control is well established," he said.

To the journalist's clarifying question about the exact time of electing the rectors, Sharipov answered again that "time is needed."

"We should work in this way, for example, if we do our work as we wish, our system will improve if we perform the tasks set before us with quality. At this moment, the day we achieve this will be their public council or scientific council, and it will be right if [rectors] are elected by them," said the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

Kongirotboy To Sharipov: "Why are non-state higher education institutions not granted a license for the field of jurisprudence, is there some kind of artificial barrier?" the question was also asked. Sharipov said that there is an artificial barrier here.
