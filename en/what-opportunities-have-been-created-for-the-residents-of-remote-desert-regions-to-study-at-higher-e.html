What opportunities have been created for the residents of remote desert regions to study at higher educational institutions?

Important news related to higher educational institutions:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated April 25, 2017 "On additional measures to further improve the living standards of the population in remote desert districts of Navoi region" PQ-2925: Admission to educational institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan from the 2017-2018 academic year to the State Commission for admission to higher education institutions in Navoi, Bukhara, Samarkand regions, applicants who have a score of 68 or higher according to the results of the tests, who have been living in remote desert districts for at least three years, then between the relevant regional administration and the higher education institution in the budget organizations of these regions on the basis of the contract, the right to preferential admission is given on the basis of an additional grant with the condition of working for at least 4 years. Starting from the 2017-2018 academic year, the State Commission for Admission to higher education institutions in the regions of Navoi, Bukhara, Samarkand will receive 68 or more points according to the results of tests for admission to higher education institutions, applicants who have been living in remote desert districts for at least three years, and then the budget of these regions according to the specialty organizations are given the right to preferential admission on the basis of an additional grant on the condition of working for at least 4 years.
