ATTENTION APPLICANTS! Applicants who have not paid for the tests within the specified period are considered unregistered and cannot participate in the tests.

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The online registration system of applicants (site abitur.dtm.uz) is integrated into the unified billing system, processes such as creating invoices for payment and checking payments are carried out online.
The program generates invoices based on the category of applicants.
Applicants who have not paid for the tests within the specified period are considered unregistered and cannot participate in the tests.

It is known that applicants who graduated from educational institutions in the years prior to the academic year in which the tests are organized will pay half the amount of the basic calculation amount to participate in the tests.

Applicants online the registration system (abitur.dtm.uz site) is integrated into the single billing system, processes such as creating an invoice for payment, checking payments are carried out online.

In the program, an invoice is generated based on the category of applicants. That is, an invoice will be created for the applicants who have to make the payment, and the invoice will not be created for the applicants who do not make the payment.

After registration, the entrants who have received an invoice along with a note sheet in their personal office should make the payments until the end of the registration period (July 20).

Payment can be made through electronic payment systems such as Click, Payme, UPAY, Paynet or bank cash registers.
