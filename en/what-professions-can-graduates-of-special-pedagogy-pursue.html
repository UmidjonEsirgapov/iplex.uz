What professions can graduates of special pedagogy pursue?

Special pedagogy (defectology) is a necessary pedagogy for working with healthy and disabled children or with limited opportunities It is one of the important areas that train personnel. Graduates of this course have the opportunity to work in various institutions in the modern education system.

Main types of special pedagogy

Special pedagogy includes the following 4 areas:

1. Oligophrenopedagogy

Prepare specialists working with children with mental retardation.

2. Deaf pedagogy

Direction of personnel engaged in education and upbringing of children with hearing impairment.

3. Visual pedagogy

Teaches special educational methods for children with visual impairments.

4. Speech therapy

Discipline dealing with identification, correction and development of speech-impaired children.
