The Berlin House of Representatives offers undergraduate scholarships to young students mainly from the USA, Germany, France, Great Britain and the Soviet Union. "Study Foundation of the Berlin House of Representatives " grant program has been announced.

Requirements for admission to the program:

  • Must be citizens of the above-mentioned countries;
  • Candidates must be at least 25-35 years old;
  • Candidates must have completed their research they should also upload their plans to the site;
  • During the application process, the candidate must have already received an acceptance letter from a research institution in Berlin;
  • After the research is completed, the candidate must return to his country;
  • Candidates must be participating in scientific research even if they have completed higher education, candidates must be socially active;

Grant amount:

  • For master and doctoral students - 1100 euros;
  • For postdoctoral researchers, 1630 euros will be allocated;

These monthly stipends are given including accommodation expenses and travel expenses. Any other costs are covered by the candidate;

Required documents for the admission process:

  • Curriculum vitae (Cv);
  • Motivation letter;
  • Reference from the academic supervisor in Berlin;
  • Research plan;
  • 2 letters of recommendation;
  • Copy of diplomas and grades;
  • You can include copies of any additional certificates and diplomas;

For additional information and questions, call -0049-30-2325 2005- these numbers or send an email to studienstiftung@parlament-berlin.de!
