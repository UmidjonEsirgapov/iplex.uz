General Information:

Harbin Institute of Technology Scholarship 2022: The Chinese government has announced a scholarship program for all foreign nationals who want to study at Harbin Institute of Technology in China on the basis of a fully funded scholarship. This university was ranked 260th in the 2021 Qs ranking. The duration is 2-3 years for a master's degree and 3-4 years for a doctoral degree.

    Eligibility criteria for Chinese Government Scholarship 2022 consists of:

    • Candidates must not be Chinese nationals;
    • International students who have studied outside of China or former graduates of Chinese universities;
    • Masters applicants must have a bachelor's degree and be under 35 years old'
    • Doctoral applicants must have a master's degree and be under 40 years old;
    • Academic performance will be considered separately;
    • Candidates should have good ability in scientific research.

    Benefits of the grant program:

    • Exemption from registration fee, tuition fee, accommodation fee;
    • Comprehensive medical 800 RMB ~$125 USD per year for insurance;
    • Master degree candidate: stipend 3000 RMB ~$470 USD per month;
    • PhD candidate: 3500 RMB~$550 USD per month stipend.

    List of Required Documents:

    • Application form for Chinese government scholarship ( duplicate ). The CSC Online Application System is available at: . Grant type : B. Code : 10213;
    • Higher level certificates. Original notarized pre-degree certificate in English or Chinese;
    • Transcripts;
    • Two letters of recommendation from professors or associate professors in English or Chinese;
    • Copy of passport (valid for at least one year);
    • Copy of foreigner's physical examination form;
    • Study or research plan (at least 800 words in English or Chinese). The format can be downloaded from the official website: http://studyathit.hit.edu.cn/en/academic/program/view?id=17;
    • Certificate of no criminal record;
    • Letter of acceptance (optional).
