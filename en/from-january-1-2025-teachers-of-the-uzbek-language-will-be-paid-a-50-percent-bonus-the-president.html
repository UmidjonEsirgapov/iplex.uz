From January 1, 2025, teachers of the Uzbek language will be paid a 50 percent bonus - the President

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
President Shavkat Mirziyoyev sent congratulations on the occasion of the Uzbek language holiday and the 35th anniversary of the granting of the state language status to the Uzbek language.
From January 1, 2025, teachers of the Uzbek language who have a national certificate in secondary schools will be paid a bonus in the amount of 50% of their monthly salary. congratulated on the 35th anniversary of the award. In the congratulation, the wide-scale work carried out to develop the state language, increase its prestige and position, and promote it internationally was highlighted.
