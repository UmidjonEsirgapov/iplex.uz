In order to submit relevant documents for admission to higher education institutions, applicants must enter their passport (ID card) information into the ABITUR electronic program. Without this document, it is not possible to submit an electronic application for admission to the OTM.

When submitting an application to the OTM, only an ID card (passport) and a certificate are needed. It is enough to enter the information from these documents into the database.

Previously, the Ministry of School and Preschool Education reminded that 23,000 (6%) of more than 390,000 school-leaving students who have reached the age of 16 have not yet received a passport (ID card). it is necessary to contact the Migration and Citizenship Departments and the State Services Center of the authorities. — More information
