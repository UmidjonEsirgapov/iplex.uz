General Information:

For young people who want to study at the bachelor's, master's and doctoral levels of Stanford University, one of the world's top universities. a grant has been allocated.

  • Duration of the bachelor's degree: 4 years;
  • Master's and doctoral studies: 2,3,4,5 years depending on the direction;

Benefits of the grant (for the bachelor's degree):

  • The full amount of the contract is covered (if the candidate's family income is $150,000 US if less than USD);
  • The contract amount is covered by the cost of accommodation and educational materials (if the candidate's family income is less than $65,000 USD);

The benefits of the grant (for the master's degree):

  • The full contract amount and related expenses are covered;
  • Knight Hennessy Scholarship - all expenses including living expenses, books, school supplies, transportation costs and other expenses are covered;
  • One-way air tickets are provided in economy class;

Requirements for candidates:

  • All nationals can apply;
