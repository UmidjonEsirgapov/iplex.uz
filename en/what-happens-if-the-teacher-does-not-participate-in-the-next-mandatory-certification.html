What happens if the teacher does not participate in the next mandatory certification?

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
The regulations approved by the government decree specify the measures to be taken for non-participation in attestation based on justified or unexcused reasons. (e.g., temporarily traveling in a foreign country, death of a close relative, other reasons specified in the certificate of the authorized state body or heads of educational organizations), their certification will be postponed to another period.

In the regulations approved by the government decision, the measures to be taken for non-participation in the attestation based on reasonable or unexcused reasons.

Educators in the attestation for reasonable (temporary inability to work, performing state or public duties, participating in republican and international competitions, temporarily in a foreign country travel, death of a close relative, other grounds specified in the certificate of the authorized state body or heads of educational organizations), their attestation will be postponed to another period. qualification category (position) will be lowered by one level.

Pedagogical personnel in the position of specialist (highly educated teacher) who did not participate in the attestation for no good reason is considered not to have passed the attestation, and the employer may cancel the employment contract concluded with him due to the insufficient qualifications of the employee in accordance with the procedure established by the labor legislation. has the right to cancel.

The qualification category (position) of pedagogues who participate in extraordinary attestation at their own discretion and fail to pass the attestation will not be lowered in their qualification category (position) and they will be paid according to the qualification category (position) that was in effect before participating in the extraordinary attestation. — Official Data
