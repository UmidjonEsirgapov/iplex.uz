Registan Scholarship: Awards, Special Certificate and Souvenirs

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Scholarship organizers: "REGISTON" training center and OLIYGOH.UZ team Partners: Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education Registration deadline: February 20 Who is the competition for?
How to register will be held?
Winners of the scholarship competition will be ceremoniously awarded with a special certificate of "Registon scholarship holder", souvenirs, cash prizes in the special event hall of the "REGISTON" educational center. "REGISTON SCHOLARSHIP" was established for students in the Republic in cooperation with the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education and the educational platform OLIYGOH.UZ. Our main goal is to further develop the education sector of Uzbekistan by supporting talented young people in modern ways.

The winners of the scholarship are awarded with a special certificate "Registon scholarship holder", souvenirs, monetary gains. Also, all the candidates who participated in the competition will receive special QR-code certificates.

Participate, test your knowledge and get special prizes.

Stipend organizers:

  • REGISTON training center and OLIYGOH.UZ team

Partners:

  • Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education

Registration deadline:

  • February 20

Who is the contest for?

  • For all undergraduate students studying in our republic

Prizes for winners:

  • Scholarship winners Special certificate "Holder of Registon scholarship", will be awarded with souvenirs and cash prizes. Stipends are awarded starting from April 1, for a period of 3 months, with strict observance of the specified amount. The total amount of the scholarship is 6 times of the base calculation amount, and this amount is paid in 3 months.

  • In the current academic year, the initiatives and proposals of the students are studied by the special commission of the competition, and from the following academic years, the number of scholarship winners and the amount of scholarships awarded to them will be increased.

Quota for the scholarship:

  • The number of Registon scholarship winners is 30 students this academic year.

How to register? They register on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform system and familiarize themselves with the conditions of the selection test in advance. Contestants should pay attention to filling in their personal information correctly and accurately during registration. A special code will be sent to each candidate's phone number during the registration process. This code will activate the participant's "Personal Cabinet".

Selection stages

Taking into account the interests of young people, the selection stages will be evaluated and sorted through the general evaluation system.

Exam type: 40 test questions on the English language (elementary-intermediate). The correct answer to each test question will be evaluated from 1.25 points.

Time: The 1st stage of the scholarship competition will start on February 20 at 19:00 and the exam window will be closed for everyone at 22:00. All candidates registered during this period can participate in the exam for 40 minutes. After the completion of the 1st stage, the general results mandate will be formed, and 2000 (two thousand) participants with the highest results will be transferred to the 2nd stage. take an online exam in the form of a test on the platform, and the candidates with the highest score will go to the final stage.

Type of exam: 40 test questions on IQ (logical analysis). The correct answer to each test question is evaluated out of 1.5 points.

Duration: The 2nd stage of the competition starts on February 27 at 19:00 and the exam window closes for everyone at 22:00. All candidates who have registered during this period can participate in the exam for 40 minutes they can do. After the completion of the 2nd stage, the mandate of the general results will be formed, and the 500 (five hundred) participants with the highest results will qualify for the finals (3rd stage). submit documents in the prescribed manner through their profiles.

Candidates selected from the exams submit the following documents online through a special platform window:

  • identity document;
  • reference from the place of study (confirmed via my.gov.uz or confirmed by the dean);
  • recommendation from the dean's office or department;
  • Motivational letter;
  • Competition and achievements in various projects;
  • CV (reference)
  • Published scientific works, patent, inventions (if available);
  • International examination certificates (if available).

All documents are provided online through the platform.

After the candidates have downloaded the documents, the total marks will be calculated and the results will be announced within 2 weeks.

How will the exams be graded?

The total marks will be calculated as follows:

  • For the English language exam: 50 marks
  • For IQ (logical analysis) exam: 60 points
  • For documents: 90 points

A total of 200 points can be collected.

Winners of the scholarship competition in the hall of special events of "REGISTON" training center with a special certificate of "Registon scholarship holder", souvenirs, cash prizes will be ceremoniously awarded.

Also, special QR-code certificates will be given online to all candidates who participated in the competition. Certificates will be sent to the participants on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform through "Personal cabinet".

"REGISTON" educational center and the OLIYGOH.UZ team wish good luck to all participants of the contest!

For information, "REGISTON" educational center is the largest in our republic. is one of the educational centers, currently there are 15 branches of the center throughout the Republic. About 30,000 young people studied at this center for 7 years.
