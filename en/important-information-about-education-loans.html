Important information about education loan (2024/2025)

Education loan in Uzbekistan is a state-supported financial tool to ease the cost of education for students. Below, familiarize yourself with the main changes and conditions that came into force from the 2024/2025 academic year.

1. Preferential education loan interest

  • Preferential education loan for women is provided without interest.

  • The interest rate for boys is equal to the main rate of the Central Bank (14%).

2. Who can get it

  • The loan is allocated to state and non-state higher education students in Uzbekistan.

  • Only full-time students can use it.

3. Grades and amount of payment

  • 2nd year and above students, if the average grade is lower than "3", the loan is up to 80% of the payment contract.

4. Change in interest rate

  • If the central bank rate decreases - the loan percentage will also decrease.

  • If the rate rises - the loan percentage will not change.

5. Periodicity of receiving

  • Educational loan can be received every year.

6. To whom it is not granted

  • Students studying on the basis of "Supercontract" cannot use the preferential education loan.

7. Payment period

  • Payment begins 7 months after the end of official studies.

  • The total payment period is 7 years, that is, for a 1st year student, it is possible to pay up to 11 years 7 months.

8. Bail requirements

  • For children of families included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection", no bail and surety is required.

9. Application procedure

  • Starting from 2024/2025, applications will be submitted through:

    • "Educational loan" information system - talim-krediti.mf.uz

    • my.gov.uz portal.

Conclusion

Education loan is a great opportunity to continue your education. Favorable terms, a long-term payment plan and no interest rate increase provide financial relief to students. Knowing the new rules for the 2024/2025 academic year will help you use the loan correctly. — More information
