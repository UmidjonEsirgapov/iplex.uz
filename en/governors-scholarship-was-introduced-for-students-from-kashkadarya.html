Hokim Scholarship is awarded to students of state HEIs located in Kashkadarya region. The amount is 3 BHM, that is 990 thousand soums. It is paid in 1 academic year from September to June.

A competition for the scholarship is held, in which the applicant's activity and achievements are evaluated, and an interview is held. A total of 14 students will receive scholarships.
