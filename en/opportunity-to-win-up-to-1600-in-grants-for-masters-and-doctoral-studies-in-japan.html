General information:

Applications to the Honjo International Scholarship Foundation of Japan, which offers scholarships of varying amounts for students who have obtained the opportunity to study at universities in Japan for master's degrees and above. A total of 5 students will be awarded this grant.

Requirements for candidates:

  • All nationals can apply (Japanese cannot apply);
  • The candidate must have been accepted to a master's degree or doctoral degree at one of the universities in Japan;
  • The age limit for the master's degree. 30 years old and 35 years old for doctoral studies;
  • Candidates should be socially active and interested in international relations between the two countries and participate in various similar activities during their studies;
  • Knowledge of the Japanese language, even at the elementary level, is required, and interviews will be conducted in Japanese;
  • The student must not have graduated for more than 1 year. required;

Learn more about the requirements HERE;

Benefits

  • 2,000,000 yen per month (average $1,591,000) per month for programs that may last 1-2 years;
  • per month for programs that may last 3 years A stipend of 180,000 yen (Average $1,451) is provided;
  • A stipend of 150,000 yen per month is provided for programs that may last 4-5 years;

Important: a) Applications are accepted from April 1 to April 30; b) At the beginning of July, candidates will be given information about the interview; c) The list of all winners will be announced at the end of July;

Application process:

  • Firstly register HERE;
  • Then you will be asked to upload these documents to the portal;
  • Upload undergraduate grades for master's degree and master's grades for doctoral degree in English or Japanese requested;
  • The research plan must be submitted in Japanese using the special form shown HERE; If you cannot write in Japanese, you must translate it;
  • Letter of recommendation from academic supervisors;
  • Document on admission to study; If you haven't received your acceptance letter yet, you should enter an estimated date when you will receive it. As soon as you receive it, you should send it to them by email;
  • The documents will be reviewed by the admissions committee and selected candidates will be invited for an interview;

Note: All documents must be uploaded in PDF format;
