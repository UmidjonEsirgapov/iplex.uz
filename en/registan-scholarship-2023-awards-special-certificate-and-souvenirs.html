The main purpose of the contest is to further develop the education sector of Uzbekistan through the promotion of education of young people studying on the republican scale, support of talented young people, and financial assistance.

Special commission

Organization and holding of the contest Registon It is carried out by a special commission consisting of international teachers of the educational project and specialists of the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform. including about 30 major projects such as "Registon" training center, Registan IT school, "Registon" private publishing house, Registan International School. Who is the competition for?

  • For all undergraduate students studying in state and non-state universities in our republic.

Important note: students who want to participate in this scholarship project must have a score of 6 or higher in the international IELTS exam or a National Certificate of Foreign Language (English) B2 and higher degrees are required.

This scholarship is aimed at financial stimulation of knowledgeable students who have thoroughly studied foreign languages, and does not have the character of giving additional privileges in studies.

Participate in the competition, test your knowledge and become the owner of the most prestigious prizes.

Prizes for the winners

  • The winners of the scholarship will be awarded with a special certificate of "Registon scholarship holder", souvenirs, monetary gains. The total amount of the scholarship is 2 times of the base calculation amount, and the scholarship includes a one-time payment amount.
  • The number of Registon scholarship winners is set for 50 students in the current academic year.
  • Also, all the candidates who participated in the competition will receive a special QR-code. certificates will be given.

In the current academic year, the initiatives and proposals of the students will be studied by the special commission of the competition, and from the following academic years, the number of scholarship winners and the amount of scholarships will be increased.

How to register?

  • "REGISTON SCHOLARSHIP" Applicants who wish to participate in the competition register via @Registon_stipendiya_bot and activate the special "Personal Cabinet" of the competition on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform. Contestants should pay attention to filling in their personal information correctly and accurately during registration.

Stages of selection

"REGISTON SCHOLARSHIP" selection consists of 3 stages. The selection stages are conducted based on the general evaluation system and the most worthy participants are selected.

First stage

First stage (in the form of a test) Candidates participating in the competition will take an online exam through the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.

Exam type: 50 test questions on different levels of the English language.

Time: The 1st stage of the competition will start on April 30 at 6:00 p.m. and at 11:00 p.m. closes the exam window for All candidates registered during this period can participate in the exam for 40 minutes. After the completion of the 1st stage, general results mandate will be formed and 1,000 (thousand) participants will get a ticket to the 2nd stage.

Second stage

Type of exam: Written exam (Written exam questions will be announced at the time of the exam through a special platform).

The shortlisted candidates, together with the written exam, will submit the following documents online through the platform:

  • Identity document (passport or ID);
  • International IELTS or foreign language National certificate that determines the level of knowledge.

Duration: In the 2nd stage of the competition, all documents are submitted by the candidates through the platform on May 6 from 09:00 to May 9 at 20:00. After the end of the second stage, within 10 days, the document collections will be examined by experts, the general results of the participants will be checked by a special commission, and the results will be announced. After the completion of the 2nd stage, the mandate of the overall results will be formed, and 200 (two hundred) participants with the highest results will get a ticket to the finals (3rd stage).

Third stage

Third stage: This is the stage of submitting documents, candidates participating in the scholarship competition submit documents in the prescribed manner through the "Personal Cabinet" created on the "OLIYGOH.UZ" platform.

Candidates selected from the exams submit the following documents online through a special platform window:

  • A reference from the place of study (confirmed via my.gov.uz or a reference issued by the dean's office);
  • Recommendation letter from the dean's office or the department;
  • Selection and achievements in various projects;
  • CV (reference)
  • Published scientific works, patent, inventions (if any);
  • International exam certificates (if available: SAT, GMAT, etc.).

Time: In the 3rd stage of the competition, all documents are submitted by candidates through the platform on May 29 at 09:00 to June 1 at until 20:00 will be submitted online (*Due to technical work on the platform, minor changes have been made to the timings of the 3rd stage).

All documents will be submitted online through the platform.

After the candidates download the document collections, they will be checked by a special commission within 14 days and the results will be announced.

Note: the scores of the final round are the final decisive result. At this stage, the most worthy candidates will be announced as absolute winners of the competition.

The general results will be determined based on the decision of a special commission, the list of students who won the competition prize will be approved.
