Summer School in Romania.

Full and partial scholarships are available.

An international summer school held annually in Bucharest brings together students from all over the world to discuss various topics in the field of economics. The school will be held on August 14-28, 2022. Together with speakers and professors from leading universities and companies from around the world, more than 40 participants will spend 2 weeks, including one additional weekend, at the campus of the Bucharest University of Economic Studies.

This year's theme is Fintech: challenges in financial services.

Bucharest Summer School is an international cultural and academic event with the main objective of promoting common academic values and focusing on economic issues and related fields.

To apply:
- Fill out the application form;
- Resume;
- Motivation letter.

Deadline: June 2, 2022.
