Basic tariff rates for pedagogues and managers of secondary special education and professional education organizations were announced

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The basic tariff rates of pedagogues and managers of secondary special education and professional education organizations have been increased in accordance with the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PF-108 dated August 12, 2024 "On increasing the amount of wages, pensions and allowances". Starting from October 1, 2024, the salaries of pedagogues and medical workers working in budget organizations have been increased.
This information was officially announced by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and the Ministry of Poverty Alleviation and Employment.
The salaries of other employees working in budget organizations increased by 10%.

According to the Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated August 12, 2024 No. PF-108 "On increasing the amount of wages, pensions and allowances", starting from October 1, 2024, the salary of pedagogues and medical workers working in budget organizations will be increased. fees have been increased. According to the new regulation, the following changes were made for pedagogues and other employees in budget organizations:

  • Salaries for pedagogical employees working in budget organizations were increased by 15%. This applies to all pedagogues working in pre-school, general education, secondary special, professional educational institutions, "Muruvat" boarding houses, "Mehribonlik" houses and children's sanatoriums. the minimum amount of payment is 1,155,000 soums per month, and the basic calculation amount is 375,000 soums.

    As a result of the salary increase, the basic tariff rates of secondary special education and professional education (academic lyceums, vocational colleges) pedagogues and managers were determined based on new tables.
