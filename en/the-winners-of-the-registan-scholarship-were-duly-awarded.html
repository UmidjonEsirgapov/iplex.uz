The winners of the Registan scholarship were duly awarded

As you know, in order to support and financially encourage talented young people studying in our Republic, the "REGISTON SCHOLARSHIP" for students is being offered in cooperation with the Registon Education Project, the Ministry of Higher Education and the OLIYGOH.UZ platform the competition was announced. The candidates who took part in the competition tested their knowledge in the examination system consisting of 3 stages for 2 months. The 1st stage of the scholarship competition was held on February 20. they won the ticket. (View Results) All candidates who participated in the first stage can download special certificates of the competition through the "Personal Cabinet" (Download Certificates).

— On February 27, 2nd stage exams were held among the candidates who passed to the second stage of the competition, and at this stage 581 participants who showed the highest results won the ticket to the final. (View results)

— According to the competition instructions and regulations, the 3rd stage (final) stage was held among the best participants . The third stage is the stage of submitting documents, candidates participating in the scholarship competition submitted the documents specified in the competition regulations in the specified order through the platform's special examination window. The special selection commission checked these documents for 2 working weeks. (See the results)

Today, April 24, the absolute winners of the scholarship contest were solemnly awarded with a special certificate of "Registon scholarship holder", souvenirs, monetary gains in the special event hall of the "REGISTON" educational center (gallery of the awarding ceremony below)

The results of the candidates were evaluated based on the following requirements 90-point evaluation system based on the following requirements:

  • From the place of study to the student a letter of recommendation from the dean's office or department for the Registon scholarship. In the recommendation, the information provided by the official representative of the university about the student;
  • Motivational letter (a special motivational letter written about why they consider themselves worthy of winning the "Registon Scholarship" competition and their goals). The letter was accepted in any of the Uzbek, Russian and English languages) In the letter, the candidate's possession of independent opinion, spelling errors, consistency and fluency of the written thoughts, as well as the originality of the conclusion were checked with special attention. examined by specialists.
  • Student's social activity in the 2021-2022 academic years, as well as published scientific works, patents, inventions
  • Not only scientific work or achievements, but also international exam certificates obtained by the student; document, reference from the place of study (verified via my.gov.uz or verified by the dean's office), CV (reference) were also studied and commission members, summarizing the results, announce the following candidates as "Registon scholarship" winners:
