You can get a reference from the place of study instantly through the my.gov.uz portal!

We know that in necessary cases, students are required to provide a reference from the place of study to the airline or railway cashiers, the defense department, the bank and other places in order to get a job. Usually it was given in the dean's offices.

It is possible to get this certificate easily via the Internet.

"Get a certificate from the place of study" service:

— absolutely free;
— no electronic signature is required;
— fully automated (without human factor);
— download the certificate in PDF form and documents from the personal office there is an opportunity to save it in the repository.

❗️This is important! Documents received through the single portal have legal force and state bodies have no right to refuse to accept such documents, this is strictly prohibited. — Official Data
