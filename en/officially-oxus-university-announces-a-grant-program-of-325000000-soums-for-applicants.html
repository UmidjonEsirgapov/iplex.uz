About the University

"Oxus University" operates in Tashkent based on the license (№153214, 31.10.2023) provided by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

Oxus University it is not just a new private university, but a modern higher education institution providing education based on international standards. The university campus, located on an area of ​​3 hectares, offers modern equipment, green space and a comfortable learning environment. In the current academic year, the university has announced admissions in 4 faculties and 7 fields. About the program

Oxus University, which aims to provide education based on international standards, announces the Oxus University Grant Program competition with a total value of 325 million soums for the 2023-2024 academic year.

The main purpose of the competition - to encourage the education of school graduates and applicants, to support talented young people and to financially encourage them.

What opportunities will the winners of the competition have?

Oxus University grant program winners will have the following opportunities:

  1. Oxus University grant certificate of winning;
  2. The opportunity to study at the university for 1 year on the basis of a grant in the chosen field (if the student has a high learning rate during 1 year, the grant may be retained in future years);
  3. The opportunity to participate in various practical projects organized by the university;
  4. Opportunity to practice in prestigious companies after 2 courses in conjunction with studying at the university;
  5. Opportunity to participate in student exchange programs at famous universities in Great Britain, Japan, USA, Germany and Malaysia;
  6. Commemorative gifts and special awards;
  7. A special certificate with a QR code will be presented to all candidates who participated in the grant competition.

The organization and conduct of the competition will be carried out by a special commission consisting of Oxus University's international class teachers and OLIYGOH.UZeducation platform experts.

For information, you can get all the information, opportunities, faculties and directions about Oxus University from the oxusuniversity.uz web page.

Register for the contest transition

  • Applicants who wish to participate in the Oxus University grant contest register via @Oxus_grant_bot and activate the contest's special "Personal cabinet" on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform. they should pay attention to the fact that they are being filled in. Last date of registration: November 26. Selection stages. stage
    • At this stage, the candidates participating in the competition will take republic-wide online exam through OLIYGOH.UZ platform.
    • In the online exam, 50 test questions will be given on IQ and different levels of English (listening, reading, vocabulary, grammar).
    • Phase 1 will start at 17:00 on November 26 and end at 23:00.
    • All the candidates will participate in the online test lasting 40 minutes within the specified time interval. The special test platform of the competition will be closed on November 26 at 11:00 p.m. After the completion of the first stage, the mandate of the general results will be formed, and the total of 500 candidates with the highest results will receive a ticket. To pass to the second stage, candidates must score at least 70 percent (35 correct answers and above) .
    • For candidates who passed to the second stage, a special SMS notification and a message through the Personal Cabinet will be sent.

    Second stage

    • Second stage: University exam. This stage is the most important stage of the competition, and the participants will pass a special exam at this stage.
    • Time and place: The 2nd stage of the competition will be held on November 29 between the selected candidates in the building of special exams of Oxus University. Candidates will participate in IQ and English test conducted offline by Oxus University. A special SMS notification will be sent to the candidates on the time of the final stage.
    • For 3 days after the end of the final stage, the general results of the participants will be checked by the special commission of the competition and the general results will be announced. Based on the decisions made by the selection committee, the list of grant winners is approved.
    • The candidates found most worthy at this stage are announced as the absolute winners of the competition and are recommended to the university for studentship on the basis of a grant.

    Note: Candidates who have been admitted to the final stage before coming to the university OLIYGOH.UZ platform must submit personal documents through a special examination window. Candidates submit the following documents online through a special platform window:

    — international exam certificates (if available: IELTS, SAT);

    — achievements in various contests, Olympiads, competitions and projects;
