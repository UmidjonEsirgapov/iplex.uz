General Information:

Russian Government Scholarship-The Russian Government Scholarship is a unique opportunity for talented students. The Russian government has announced the fifth Open Doors International Olympiad. The goal of this Olympiad is to select the best students in the world and attract them to Russia for higher education and research. The "Open Doors" scholarship allows Olympiad winners to choose one of 500 higher educational institutions of Russia, including 21 leading university participants of 5-100 projects. This year's "Open Doors" Olympiad includes 14 disciplines, which are mainly related to science. The Russian Government scholarship for international students is for young people who want to study at the master's and doctorate levels.

Participation in the global Olympiad is mandatory for students who have excellent academic performance and leadership qualities and who want to study in Russia for free. Fortunately, there are 14 disciplines as last year, and what is new is that PhD students can apply in any of these disciplines. Last year, PhD students applied for only 6 fields.

Benefits of the grant:

  • The tuition fee is paid by the Russian Federation;
  • The winner can enter any Russian university that offers the discipline in which he won the Olympiad;
  • If you were not selected for last year's Olympiad, you can apply again this year is possible;

Requirements:

  • All international or foreign students can apply for master’s and doctorate;
  • The applicant must have a bachelor’s degree in the master’s field and a master’s or specialist degree similar to the doctoral thesis in the doctoral field;
  • Our compatriots living abroad can also apply possible;
  • There is no gender discrimination in this Olympiad;
  • Any foreign citizen can apply regardless of race, nationality, color or gender;
  • The participant must be fluent in English or Russian.

Application deadline:

  • Register for the Russian Government Scholarship 2022 the last date is December 10, 2021!
