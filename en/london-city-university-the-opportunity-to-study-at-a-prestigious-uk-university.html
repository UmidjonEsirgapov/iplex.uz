International Excellence Scholarships It is a grant program that provides the opportunity to study at the master's level at London City University in Great Britain.

Educated, versatile young people from abroad can participate in the program and continue their master's degree in London. Unfortunately, grants are not available for these areas:

  • MSc Speech and Language Therapy
  • MSc Adult and Mental Health Nursing.

Requirements:

  • The candidate must be an international student;
  • Bachelor’s degree must be completed;
  • Meet the requirements of the master’s course of this university and

Benefits:

  • The amount of the scholarship can range from £1,000 to £5,000 depending on the candidate's academic performance.

To participate in the program, candidates must submit an online application and write a personal statement of 500 words.
