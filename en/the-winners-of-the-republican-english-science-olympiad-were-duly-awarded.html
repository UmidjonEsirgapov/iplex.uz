The winners of the Republican "English Science Olympiad" were duly awarded

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
169 (one hundred and sixty-nine) participants who showed the best results in the crucial 3rd stage (final stage) of the Olympiad won a ticket and fought for the victory.
Participants of the Olympiad in the final stage Speaking their skills were tested from 09:00 to 13:00.
Special certificates and souvenirs were given to all the candidates who participated in the Olympiad.

In order to support talented young people and improve the level of English literacy of young people, OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform and "REGISTON" educational center in cooperation with the Ministry of Innovative Development, the Council of Young Scientists of the Republic organized a 3-stage "English Science Olympiad" on the scale of the Republic. Thousands of young people with a thirst for knowledge from different regions of Uzbekistan took part in this Olympiad.

A total of 17 thousand 800 students of general education schools, academic lyceums, professional educational institutions and students of higher educational institutions from different parts of our Republic registered for the Olympiad and competed for the winner.

The first and second Olympiads stages were organized on the OLIYGOH.UZ educational platform. The 1st stage of the Olympiad was held on July 11, and more than 9,000 young people from our Republic participated, and the best candidates qualified for the next stage. The 2nd stage of the Olympiad on July 18 1000 best candidates selected from thousands of talented young people of our republic took part and the strongest ones won the ticket to the final stage. nine) participants won the ticket and fought for the victory.

The final stage of the Olympiad was held on July 25 at "REGISTON" school. It was tested in the auditorium of the center's "Registan Academy" and "Registan Chilanzar" branches, where special exams are held. At the final stage, the participants tested their English speaking, writing, reading and listening skills. They conducted the exam from 09:00 to 13:00. The speaking exam was conducted by the most influential expert teachers and native speakers of our republic.

The main exam of the Olympiad started at 14:00 and It lasted until 17:00. To ensure impartiality and full transparency, the main exam was recorded by video cameras and broadcast live online for everyone on the YouTube platform.

The strongest among the participating candidates were selected, the results of the Olympiad were delivered to the participants in the form of sms notifications, and on July 27, the most worthy absolute winners were solemnly awarded in the special event hall of the "REGISTON" educational center.

For information, special awards were given to the Olympiad winners, souvenirs, DIPLOMAS with QR-codes of I, II and III levels were handed over. It was found to be Azodbek Botiraliev, a student of the department, resident of Andijan. The winner of the Olympiad was given a laptop computer, a 1st-level diploma with a QR code, souvenir books and other gifts from "REGISTON" educational center

Muhammadjonova Latofat, a student of Westminster University, living in Tashkent, was awarded the 3rd place, and the winner received a Xiaomi 9A mobile phone. 2nd level diploma with QR code, souvenir books and other gifts were handed out.
