The first deputy minister of preschool education was appointed to a new position

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the relevant decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Usman Sharifkhodjayev, who was the first deputy minister of preschool and school education, was appointed deputy director of the Social Protection Agency under the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
About this information service of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education informed.
Today, the observation ceremony of Usman Sharifkhodjayev was held at the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.

According to the relevant decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Usman Sharifkhodjayev, who served as the first deputy minister of preschool and school education, is the director of the Social Protection Agency under the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan. was appointed deputy. This was reported by the information service of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.

Today in the Ministry of Preschool and School Education Usman Sharifkhodjayev was observed. The team of the ministry wished him great success in his future career.

In his speech, Sharifkhodjayev expressed gratitude for the work carried out in cooperation with the team of the ministry. — Source
