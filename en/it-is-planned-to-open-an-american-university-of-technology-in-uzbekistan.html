It is planned to open an American University of Technology in Uzbekistan in cooperation with Arizona State University (USA). This was discussed at the meeting of President of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev with the founder and chairman of Cintana educational company Douglas Becker in New York on September 18.

Cintana was established in cooperation with Arizona State University, operates in 30 countries of the world, and is engaged in improving the quality of education in universities. It is planned to start training personnel in areas such as conservation, engineering, architecture and design.

"The head of our state approved these plans and expressed his readiness to create favorable conditions for the implementation of projects in the field of education of Uzbekistan," the press service of the president said. — Read on infoedu.uz
