"Mirzo Ulugbek" online school was established for remote and mountainous areas

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Mirzo Ulugbek online school was established by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education in cooperation with the Fund for Supporting Talented Youth "Mirzo Ulugbek".
These are schools No. 7 of Jizzakh region, Forish district, No. 70 of Koshrabad district of Samarkand region, and No. 62 of Denov district of Surkhandarya region. "Ulugbek" online school was established.

As part of the online school, 3 schools in mountainous and remote areas were selected as a test. These are schools No. 7 of Jizzakh region, Forish district, No. 70 of Koshrabod district of Samarkand region, and No. 62 of Denov district of Surkhandarya region. In these schools, high school students receive additional education in mathematics and English.

The purpose of establishing an online school is to improve the quality of education in remote areas and mountainous areas and to support talented young people.

If this project gives positive results during the testing process, it can be implemented in educational institutions in other mountainous and remote areas of the Republic. — Source
