How to color the applicant answer sheet?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
If the answer sheet does not belong to you or the subjects in the book of test tasks do not match, inform the group supervisor.
After checking the answer sheet and the book of test tasks, write your information and sign it.
Test test after the start, open the book of test tasks within the specified time and mark the answers you think are correct on the answer sheet.

Before the start of the test

Before the start of the tests, check that your information is entered correctly.

also check that the sheet belongs to you.

Then, familiarize yourself with the group of subjects where you will take the test.

Check whether the set of subjects in the book of test tasks matches the subjects specified in the permit.

If the answer sheet does not belong to you or the subjects in the book of test tasks do not match, notify the group supervisor

After that, check the correctness of the book of test questions:

  • there should be 30 test questions in each subject;
  • There should be 4 alternative answer options in each test;
  • the good print of the book of test questions done.

If it is found that there is a defect in the book of test tasks, inform the supervisor of the group. Once the tests have started, the invigilators will not consider objections.

After checking that the answer sheet and test book are free of defects, write your information and sign.

Book of test tasks and last name, first name, and patronymic should be written and signed on the answer sheet.

After entering personal data, the book of test tasks number should be written and colored on the answer sheet.

After that, The option number will be painted on the answer sheet. src="https://www.gazeta.uz/media/img/2020/07/cDFmOx15936007656406_b.jpg"/>

It is forbidden to work with the test materials until the tests start.

After the test starts

Within the specified time open the book of test tasks and find the correct one yourself mark the answers on the answer sheet. In doing so, pay attention to mark the circles corresponding to the number of the test task.

Applicants are personally responsible for correctly filling out the answer sheet.

Applicants use the book of test tasks as a draft possible.

Note

During the test, use the distributed blue ballpoint pen.

Mark only one answer to each test item.

Mark the circles correctly.

Applicants will be excluded from tests in the following cases:

  • if they use a microcalculator, dictionary, table, book, mobile phone, computer, telecommunication devices and other tools, or use erasers;
  • passes the test materials to another applicant or helps them;
  • talks to each other during the tests;
  • takes the test materials out of the building, tears the page of the test task book;
  • refuses to hand over the test materials after the time allotted for the tests.

Applicants' rights and responsibilities:

  • if errors are observed in the test tasks, he informs the group supervisor about it and writes it in the objection form.
  • after the time set for the test, the applicant hands over the test materials to the group supervisor in complete condition and leaves the building.
  • the applicant who does not hand over the test materials at the specified time is considered excluded from the test, and the results according to his answer sheet will not be issued.

If the applicant is dissatisfied with the test result, he can in writing submit an application to the appeal commission.
