According to the new regulation, if a foreign citizen does not register in the country for 60 days, he must apply for a visa or leave the country during this time. Citizens who have completed the 60-day period and left Kyrgyzstan will be allowed to enter after another 60 days.

The period of stay of foreign citizens in the country is calculated in calendar days from the day of crossing the border at 00:01.

Calculation is carried out by the automated system of the External Migration Accounting System (TMEBT). In the event that it is not possible to enter the system or the system does not work, the calculation will be carried out by the authorized body of the state by calculating the date of entry/exit in the valid document of the foreign citizen.

In this regard, students staying in the territory of this country, compatriots working in various fields are recommended to comply with the requirements of this decision adopted by Kyrgyzstan.
