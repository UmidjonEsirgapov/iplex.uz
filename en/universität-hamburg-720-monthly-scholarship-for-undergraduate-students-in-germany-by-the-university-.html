General Information:

Hamburg University, one of the most prestigious universities in Germany, has announced its 200-720 euro scholarship programs for 6 months in order to financially support international students admitted to the undergraduate level.

  • Be admitted to the University of Hamburg;
  • Not be a German citizen;
  • Not possible for BAföG federal students;
  • Prepared for or accepted for the final exam;
  • Not currently receiving any financial aid.

The amount of the grant:
