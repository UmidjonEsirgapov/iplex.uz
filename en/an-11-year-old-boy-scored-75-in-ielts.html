An 11-year-old 4th-grade student in Uzbekistan won the IELTS international certificate with 7.5 points. This was reported by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education.

It is noted that Abdullah Egamberdiyev, a student of the 4th "B" grade of general education school No. 147 in Mirabad district, scored 7.5 points in English and obtained the IELTS international certificate.

For information, IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is an international system for assessing English language proficiency. 900 test centers organize and conduct IELTS in 130 countries.

Academic IELTS is conducted 48 times a year. IELTS score is evaluated on a scale from 0 to 9. — Source
