Currently, education loans are only given to full-time students

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Relevant decree of the President about this was published in UzA.
Until now, education credit could be obtained by those studying part-time and evening education. if the average mastery rate is lower than "3", the educational loan is allocated in the amount of maximum 80% of the payment contract amount (with the exception of children of families included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection").

Also, now for students of the second year and higher, if the average indicator of learning subjects is below "3", the educational loan is allocated in the amount of a maximum of 80% of the payment-contract amount (except for children of families included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection").

And for the first years, it is allocated in the amount of a one-year contract. — Read on infoedu.uz
