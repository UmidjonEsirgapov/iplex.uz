Students can work legally from the 3rd year

According to Decision No. 646 dated October 15, 2021 of the Government of the Republic of Uzbekistan, 3rd year students studying pedagogy were given the right to teach in schools. This opportunity also applies to full-time, evening and part-time students.

Who can work in schools?

  • 3rd-year students studying pedagogy at public and non-state higher education institutions
  • In the day, evening and part-time department studying students

The purpose of this decision

This procedure:

  • Reducing the shortage of qualified pedagogic personnel in schools
  • Creating opportunities for students to gain practical experience during their studies
  • Early involvement of new pedagogues in the school system
