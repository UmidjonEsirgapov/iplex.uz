What will the exam be like?

Creative exam questions from a foreign language will be B1 level. Tasks are given in 4 sections in a total of 2 hours and 45 minutes:

  • Section 1: Listening comprehension (45 minutes / 5 parts / 30 questions);
  • Section 2: Reading (60 minutes / 5 parts / 30 questions);
  • Section 3: Writing (45 minutes / 2 assignments / 30 points);
  • Section 4: Speaking (15 minutes / 3 parts / 30 points).

Note: Admission to HEIs with a foreign language creative exam ends on July 10 (admission to other HEIs until July 20). When choosing courses, the applicant can choose only those that are subject to a creative exam.
