40% of this year's graduates Failed to score 56.7 points

The results of this year's graduates in the entrance tests have been announced by the Education and Skills Assessment Agency.

According to it, a total of 378,574 11th grade graduates participated in this year's tests. 226,151 of them, i.e. 59.74%, were able to score 30% of the maximum score, i.e. 56.7 points and more, while 152,114 could not record this result.
