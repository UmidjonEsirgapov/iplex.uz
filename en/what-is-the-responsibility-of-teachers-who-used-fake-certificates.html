What is the responsibility for teachers who use fake certificates?

It is known that, in order to incentivize teachers, pedagogues who have a certificate of the appropriate level in general education subjects the system of paying additional monthly premiums has been introduced. Also, pedagogues with qualification category receive an additional fee. These additional fees are paid on the basis of a certificate of qualification. Consequences of using fake certificates

Using a fake certificate not only damages the teacher's reputation, but such actions lead to liability under the Criminal Code of the Republic of Uzbekistan

  • According to Article 228 of the Criminal Code, for forgery and sale of documents is punishable by a fine of fifty to one hundred times the basic calculation amount or up to three years of correctional work.
  • If the document is used knowing that it is fake, twenty five to fifty times the basic calculation amount a fine or up to two years of imprisonment is applied.

It should be noted that the owner of the certificate is personally responsible for the authenticity of any certificate presented by teachers. If a fake certificate is detected, the employment contract may be terminated. — Read on infoedu.uz
