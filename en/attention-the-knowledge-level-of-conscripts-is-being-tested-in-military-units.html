Military recommendation tests are being conducted for conscripts

Tests have begun for soldiers who are serving in the Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan to receive preferential admission to higher educational institutions. These processes are transparently organized based on the current legislation and established strict principles.

Participating in the tests units

According to the established schedule, these days the soldiers on duty in the following formations are testing their knowledge:

  • DXX "Falcon" international airport security brigade;

  • Conscripts of the guard troops of the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

These tests determine the extent to which military servicemen have mastered combat and spiritual-educational training programs during their service.

Test question structure and directions

The tests are aimed at a comprehensive assessment of the intellectual potential and professional skills of military servicemen. There are a total of 100 questions and they are divided into the following subjects and directions:

Professional and spiritual block:

  • Combat training: 40 questions;

  • Spiritual-educational training: 20 questions.

General education and logic block:

  • Mathematics: 10 questions;

  • Physics: 10 questions;

  • Logic: 20 questions.

Formalization of the results and issuance of recommendations

After the tests are completed, the results report will be issued in the prescribed manner. Based on the accumulated points, relevant military organizations will provide recommendations giving military servicemen a privilege to enter higher education institutions.
