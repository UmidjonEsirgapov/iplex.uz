1st-year students can start studying at higher education institutions in October

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
According to Majid Karimov, if the epidemiological situation does not improve, the issue of postponing the entrance exams to the Higher Education Institution cannot be predicted at this time. announced that the procedure for conducting entrance exams for undergraduate education in educational institutions has not changed.

According to the words of Majid Karimov, if the epidemiological situation is not corrected, the issue of postponing the OTM entrance exams cannot be predicted at this time. Higher courses can be started earlier. 1st courses can be started in October. The republican special commission will make a decision on the basis of proposals, depending on the situation," said the head of DTM.

According to his note, it is not possible to admit students to OTM without tests. The final conclusion will be announced by the decision of the commission.

It should be noted that after the press conference held at the Information and Mass Communications Agency on Wednesday, July 8, about the information of the special commission of the Republic on strengthening quarantine measures in the country, various sources reported that entrance exams for undergraduate education of state higher education institutions may be online. However, the State Test Center rejected these reports and announced that the procedure for conducting entrance exams for undergraduate courses of state higher education institutions has not changed. — Source
