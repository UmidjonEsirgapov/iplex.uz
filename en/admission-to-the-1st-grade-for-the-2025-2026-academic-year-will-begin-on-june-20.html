Admission to the 1st grade for the 2025-2026 school year: Start date and procedure

For the 2025-2026 academic year, the process of admission to the 1st grade is carried out in accordance with the regulations in 2 stages. The procedures are carried out electronically through the my.maktab.uz platform.

Stages of admission to the 1st grade

Stage 1: June 20 - July 31

At this stage, children are admitted only to schools in their micro-region. Parents choose the school attached to their region through the electronic platform and submit an application.

Phase 2: August 1 - August 15

In this phase, it is possible to apply to schools outside the micro-region as well.
