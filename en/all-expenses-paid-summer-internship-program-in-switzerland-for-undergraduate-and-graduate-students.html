Student Summer Research Fellowship program The Faculty of Computer Science is offering a new summer research program to undergraduate and graduate students.

Research can be conducted in the following areas:

  • Theory and Algorithms
  • Network Systems and Parallel Computing
  • Pervasive Computing and Cyberphysical Systems
  • Information and System Security
  • Data Management and Machine Learning
  • Programming Languages and Software Engineering
  • Visual calculation
Students will have the opportunity to get to know the wonderful culture and beautiful mountains of Switzerland in their free time.

Once the candidate has been accepted into the research program, the organizers will help him obtain a visa and help him find accommodation. The participant of the program receives 1750CHF per month for housing and basic expenses. The organizers will also pay for travel and visa expenses. Students may receive a travel allowance at the beginning of the program.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must be at least 3rd year undergraduate or graduate studies;
  • Students must study in a program related to computer science (computer science, electrical engineering, mathematics, physics, and other programs);
  • Must be fluent in English;
  • Must be able to be in Switzerland in July and August.
