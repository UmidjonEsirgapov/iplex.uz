About the university and the grant program:

Established in 1958, Zhejiang A&F University, formerly known as Zhejiang Forestry University, is a provincial university. This university offers undergraduate and graduate courses in the fields of agriculture, science, technology, literature, commerce, law, economics and medicine.

The grant program provided by the university provides financial assistance to deserving students who intend to study in all courses offered by the university.

Who can apply for the grant program?

  • All countries citizens;

  • For Degree Students, applicants must not be more than 30 years old;

  • Applicants must be a foreign citizen with a valid foreign passport, in good health and friendly to China;

  • Candidates must have a criminal record or university rules and

  • Candidates must be fluent in Chinese or have relevant certifications such as HSK;

  • Candidates must be at the top of their class or offer a transcript of the appropriate level.

Language requirements:

  • If Chinese language proficiency does not meet the requirements, applicants may come to the university to study Chinese or take the Hanyu ShuiPing KaoShi, an internationally recognized Chinese language proficiency test.

Application process:

  • To be eligible for the scholarship, candidates must apply and be admitted to the university program of their choice HERE.

Required documents:

  1. Notarized copy of applicant's previous diplomas issued by the applicant's home country;
  2. Copy of passport;
  3. Medical summary
  4. Letter of admission to one of the university courses;
  5. HSK certificate (if available);
  6. Documents showing additional achievements (if available).

The application process has not yet started for this year. The admission process will begin on May 22, 2021 and will continue until June 23.
