Pay IT Forward provides free programming classes for young people interested in programming. The number of scholarships is 10.

Purpose:
In our daily life, we often see courses, training centers and paid online classes that teach programming. We see that the prices of some courses are set from 500,000 soums to 10,000,000 soums. It is clear that such prices are not suitable for everyone. And our Pay IT Forward team believes that programming can be learned for free, and we want to teach it to others. The goal of our team is to unite aspiring people interested in programming and expand the ranks of professional level programmers.

Study procedure and rules

  1. Teachers in the team give students certain tasks and materials. The students, in turn, must deliver the tasks on time.
  2. During daily short meetings, everyone tells what they have learned and what they will do today. At the end of the week, a longer meeting will be held to analyze the week. expected.
  3. Since members live in different places, all classes will be online.
  4. The requirement for completing our course is entering a job.
  5. You go to the end of the course junior (starter) You will become a frontend developer. Main programming language: JavaScript.

Requirements to join the team:

Minimum:

  1. You are interested in programming, diligent, patient and able to teach continuously (at least 2-3 hours a day);
  2. Be able to think logically and find a critical solution to the problem;
  3. Know the English language at least intermediate;
  4. You must have modern computer suitable for programming, there should be no problems with internet and electricity;
  5. Having basic knowledge to use a computer:
  • Create folders and files;
  • Install new programs;
  • Working with the browser;
  • Search for information on Google;
  • Check e-mail and write letters;
  • Membership, access and exit from websites.

6. And the most important requirement: after you graduate with us, you should help our team closely and find at least 3 students and teach them programming for free under the same conditions.

Additional requirements (optional):

  1. In Uzbek write fluently and without mistakes.
  2. Having tried writing code in some programming language.
  3. Knowledge of the Russian language.
