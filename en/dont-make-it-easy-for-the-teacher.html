Don't make it easy for the teacher...

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Statistics presented in the scientific work of a Russian scientist Bitter truths about teachers: Teachers spend 79% of their time on their feet; On average, they have to speak loudly or very loudly for 3.5-4 hours a day; Nominally, a teacher's working day lasts 6-7 hours, but its actual duration is 10-12 hours; 80 percent of teachers suffer from a partial lack of vitamins, because the salary and work schedule do not allow for proper nutrition; 2/3 of teachers are under constant stress.
The highest level of stress is among principals and their deputies, above average - among teachers of concrete subjects, linguistic subjects, and primary school teachers; On average, 85% of the teaching staff are women, which means that in addition to the burden of education, they are also responsible for household chores and child rearing; 50 percent of teachers sleep less than six hours instead of eight hours a day; Only 14% of teachers have the opportunity to do morning exercises; Every fifth teacher eats only twice a day, every second - three times, while four or five light meals a day are considered correct; 80 percent of teachers cannot spend time at a sanatorium or by the sea, in fact, it is this kind of vacation that fully restores health.
