What action will be taken if the student disrespects the teacher during the lesson?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Disrespecting, insulting or humiliating the teacher during school classes is strictly prohibited by law and such actions lead to certain measures. Punitive measures are applied based on the second part of Article 47 of the Code of Education.
Types of liability and punishment: If a violation is committed by a student: Parents will be warned or fined. such actions lead to certain measures.

Legal basis of the violation:

Code of conduct:

  • Article 15 of the code of conduct approved in accordance with the order of the Minister of Preschool and School Education dated August 31, 2023 No. 282:
  • To students discrimination of human personality and dignity, insult or violence is prohibited.

Parental responsibility:

  • In such cases, against the student's parents or legal representative according to the second part of Article 47 of the Code of Administrative Responsibility punitive measures are applied.

Types of responsibility and punishment:

  1. If a student commits a violation:
    • Parents are given a warning or fined.
  2. If the violation is repeated:
    • From 5 times the basic calculation amount A fine of up to 10 times can be imposed.

Measures to be implemented:

  • Documents about this violation will be issued by the school management.
  • An interview will be held with the student's parents.
  • If necessary, the case will be sent to law enforcement agencies.

Conclusion:

Disrespecting the teacher during the lesson is against not only the internal procedures of the school, but also the law, and this situation leads to the administrative responsibility of the parents.
