International Nordic University with what is international!?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The "Finland Education Night" was held at the International Nordic University.
In addition, 2 visits of Finnish youth were organized to our country.
With the support of the International Nordic University, the Republic of Finland on Education influential experts are involved in the activities of educational advisers of regional governors.

1. The International Nordic University (INU) was opened in strategic cooperation with the world's leading Finnish companies. Karelian University of Applied Sciences, Nokia multinational corporation, ISKU company, Helsingin Suomalainen Yhteistskoulu (SYK) general education school, Solar Finland and Finnish Water Forum are operating in official cooperation with dozens of Finnish companies.

2. The International Nordic University opened a big way to international opportunities and signed agreements on the implementation of work and travel for full-time and part-time students in the Nordic countries such as Finland, Norway, Denmark on the basis of the "Work and travel" program.

3. On the basis of a joint program with Karelia UAS, which is among the 1000 in Finland, the training of international specialists in "International business" and "Industrial management" was launched. This provides students with the opportunity to study in Finland or acquire Finnish education while staying in Uzbekistan and receive an international diploma (double degree).

4. It is possible to study at XNU and study in developed countries such as Finland, Italy, America, Japan and Korea on the basis of the student exchange program. Undergraduate and graduate studies are available at the University of Trento, which ranks 457th in the list of 1,400 Italian universities in the world.

5. The International Nordic University supports students to work in leading companies in foreign partner countries, to do internships or to implement their own business start-up projects after the bachelor's degree.

6. The International Nordic University fulfilled its role as an international platform and held the first "Uzbekistan-Finland International Business Forum". The forum was attended by the ministries and agencies of the two countries, the world's leading Finnish companies - Nokia, Reka Cables, FinnPartnership, Honka, Kone, Suokone, more than 30 company managers and experts, Finland's KAMK University of Applied Sciences, and media representatives. At the prestigious conference, B2B and B2G meetings of Finnish and local business representatives were organized, mutual cooperation agreements were signed, and more than 10 agreements on promising future projects were reached. 7. The International Nordic University organized 4 educational trips to Finland for foreign and local pedagogues. On the basis of the university, with the participation of Finnish education experts, online and offline seminars are held for representatives of the education sector of our country. 5 seasons of international educational courses were held on the topic "Education in Finland: digital infrastructure in schools and teaching methods within the international PISA study" with the participation of more than 100 Kazakh pedagogues, school principals. With the participation of Finnish experts, international skills are being formed among industry activists.

8. "Finland Education Night" was held at the International Nordic University. An agreement was signed between the International Nordic University and the Karelian University of Applied Sciences, and the Memorandum of Understanding was signed between the National Education Agency of Finland and the representative office of UNICEF in Uzbekistan. "Finnish Language and Culture Center" named after Mikayel Agrikola was launched at the university.

9. KNU is creating conditions for Uzbek students to study in Finland, creating a thorough foundation for competitive personnel training and introduction of advanced Finnish technologies to leading industries. Also, 2 visits of Finnish youth to our country were organized. The visits of the director and students of the famous Helsingin Suomalainen Yhteiskoulu, i.e. Finnish-Helsinki joint educational school, to our country and the university became the basis for the exchange of new knowledge, experience and values.

10. XNU contributes to the exchange of experience between Uzbek-Finnish jurists. A group of lawyers from Uzbekistan visited the Finnish Ministry of Justice, the Prosecutor General's Office and other law enforcement organizations. Also, XNU, in cooperation with the Rule of law center of the University of Helsinki, Tashkent State Law University, held an international scientific and practical conference on the topic "Ensuring the rule of law: Finland's experience" and the experiences of this legal state were widely studied.

11. With the support of the International Nordic University, influential experts in the field of education of the Republic of Finland are involved in the activity of educational advisers of regional governors. Petri Lunaskopri, a professor of the University of Savonia, an expert in the implementation of ICT in education, with 25 years of experience in the development and implementation of new curricula in Finnish schools, was appointed as an educational adviser to the governor of Kashkadarya region. Hanne Koli, an educational designer and educational consultant with more than 25 years of experience in retraining teachers and developing education in Finland and Europe, works as an educational consultant to the governor of Navoi region. In the schools of the Tashkent region, an educational consultant actively participates with his valuable advice on the implementation of the Finnish experience. 00;
