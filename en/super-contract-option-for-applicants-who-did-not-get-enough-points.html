Super-contract possibility

Unable to collect enough points applicants can study on the basis of a super-contract according to their wishes.

The amount of the super-contract is as follows:

  • from 0.1 points to 1.0 points if it is not enough, it pays 1.5 times of the regular contract
  • from 1.1 points - to 2.0 points of the regular contract Pays 2 times
  • From 2.1 points to 3.0 points pays 2.5 times of the regular contract if it is lacking
  • From 3.1 points to 4.0 points pays 3 times of the regular contract

Minimum payment contract amount:

If no more than 4.0 points are reached and he has scored 75.6 points for health, law, business and management areas, 66.5 points and more for other areas, he will pay the minimum amount of the differentiated fee contract. This amount can be set from 8 times to 25 times the base contract depending on the directions. — Source
