Important changes have taken place in the education system of Uzbekistan. Now, more financial support opportunities are being created by the state for students who have entered the universities that are ranked high in the international rankings. allocated without exams. This will significantly increase the opportunities of talented young people to study in the most prestigious educational institutions.

Also, international transportation and accommodation expenses will be covered without exams for those who have received grants to universities in the TOP-300. This decision was announced in official sources on May 6, 2025.

Special benefits for graduates of presidential schools

Under the new system, additional benefits were introduced for graduates of Presidential schools. Scholarships are awarded to graduates of presidential schools who are accepted to bachelor's degrees in TOP-20 universities on the basis of a 50% grant.

Thanks to these benefits, talented young people of our country are getting more opportunities to study at internationally recognized educational institutions.
