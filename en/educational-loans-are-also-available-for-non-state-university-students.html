This is contrary to the state policy of the President. In accordance with Resolution No. 323 of 2022 (source), educational loans are allocated to students studying full-time in all private, foreign and state higher and professional educational organizations operating in the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Educational loans are also allocated to non-state university students! "We don't give educational loans to private universities" is giving wrong information. This is contrary to the state policy
