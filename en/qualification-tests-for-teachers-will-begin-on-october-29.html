Qualification tests for teachers will begin on October 29

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The certification of pedagogues in the system of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education will begin on October 29, 2024.
A total of 80 points will be awarded for the tests; In the second stage, 10 tasks will be given on the application of advanced pedagogical methods and technologies ("case study" method, project method, cooperative teaching, "practical game", interactive educational method, etc.) in order to determine pedagogical skills. Certification of pedagogues will begin on October 29, 2024. Qualification tests will be conducted in the form of a real-time test (including physical education and foreign language subjects). to complete tests:

  • For mathematics, informatics and information technology, chemistry, biology, physics — 120 minutes;

  • For all other subjects — 90 minutes time is allotted.

Qualification tests:

  • First at the stage - 40 tests (35 from the specialty, 5 from the pedagogue's professional standards) are given to determine the level of knowledge in the specialty and professional standards. Each correctly performed test is evaluated with 2 points. A total of 80 points will be given for the tests;

  • In the second stage, 10 tasks will be given on the application of advanced pedagogical methods and technologies ("case study" method, project method, cooperative teaching, "practical game", interactive educational method, etc.) in order to determine pedagogical skill. Each correct answer is evaluated with 2 points, and a total of 20 points is given.
