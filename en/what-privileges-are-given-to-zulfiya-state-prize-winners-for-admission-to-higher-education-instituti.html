On support of proposals for the establishment of the Zulfiya State Award of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Pursuant to Decree No. PF-2326 of June 10, 1999 and Cabinet of Ministers Decision No. 393 of June 20, 2017, the Zulfiya State Prize is awarded to talented girls between the ages of fourteen and thirty who have made special achievements in education, science, literature, culture, art, sports, and community activities.

Winners of the Zulfiya State Awards are admitted to higher educational institutions in their fields of study without competitive entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exams, written exams) on the basis of state grants.
