If the foreign language certificate is found to be fake, the applicant will be excluded from the exam

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The state testing center reported that applicants make mistakes when entering foreign language preference documents when applying online. allows: inclusion of certificates that do not serve as a basis for granting privileges (certificates issued by educational centers or non-international certificates); increase the level of knowledge of the language (enter the B1 level certificate as B2); incomplete or incorrect entry of the certificate series and number; entering expired certificates; wrongly uploading a certificate picture (another picture).

The state testing center reported that applicants make mistakes when entering foreign language preferential documents when applying online.

DTM writes that applicants with certificates from foreign languages must enter the foreign language in which the certificate was issued, the type of certificate, the level of language proficiency, the document series and number, the date the certificate was issued, and the photo of the certificate.

"This information is very important in checking and confirming the authenticity of certificates. Therefore, applicants are requested to be careful when entering this information and enter the information correctly. In terms of languages, 33 of them correspond to English, 22 to Korean, 17 to Chinese, and 1 to Arabic," writes DTM.

Applicants make the following mistakes when entering these certificates:

  • entering certificates that are not a basis for granting privileges (certificates issued by educational centers or non-international certificates);
  • increasing the level of language knowledge (inputting a B1 level certificate as B2);
  • incomplete or incorrect entry of the certificate series and number;
  • input of expired certificates;
  • wrongly uploading the certificate picture (another picture).

If the state test center detects the forgery of the documents submitted by the applicant, then the applicant will be excluded from the entrance exams (if admitted as a student, from the ranks of students of a higher education institution) and to report this to the law enforcement authorities in the prescribed manner.

For information: for all undergraduate courses in which a foreign language is included in the complex of tests (professional (creative) exam), applicants with a foreign language certificate of level B2 and higher will be given the maximum score for this subject. — Details here
