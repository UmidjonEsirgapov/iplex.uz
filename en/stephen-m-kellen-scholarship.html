Steven M. Kellen Scholarship is an award-based scholarship designed to attract the most talented and deserving students to the Dual BA program between Sciences Po and Freie Universität Berlin.

It enables promising young people to study law, economics, history, political science and sociology, invites them to Europe, provides the opportunity to speak at least three languages and learn about German and French cultures.

The Stephen M. Kellen Scholarship covers two years of tuition at Sciences Po, plus $6,000 per year in tuition and $7,000 per year for the two years at Sciences Po's Franco-German European Campus in Nancy.

ELIGIBILITY:
The candidate must be admitted to Sciences Po's joint undergraduate program with Freie Universität Berlin.

The grant is awarded to a student who meets the values promoted by Stephen M. Kellen:

-High academic performance - academic achievement.
-Worldview.
-Social goals - desire to serve others.

Applicants should submit the following to scholarship@usscpo.org by June 15, 2022 (3:00 p.m. ET):

-Online application form.
-Personal statement. Describe your motivation for studying in Berlin (350 words, in English).
-Essay (1500 words, in English).

The selection committee consists of representatives from Sciences Po and the Sciences Po American Foundation.
