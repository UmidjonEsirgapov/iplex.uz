Uzbek language and literature teachers are paid a 50 percent bonus

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
International Science Olympiad: International Science Olympiad on the Uzbek language will be held.
Winners will be accepted to study on the basis of a state grant in the field of Uzbek language philology.
The President stated: The meeting President Shavkat Mirziyoyev emphasized that it would be fair to pay the teachers of Uzbek language and literature the same amount as the teachers of other subjects who have international certificates. This decision was announced by President Shavkat Mirziyoyev at an extended meeting of the Republican Council of Spirituality and Enlightenment.

New measures:

  • Increase payments: Teachers of the Uzbek language and literature with a national certificate will be paid a 50 percent bonus.
  • International Science olympiad: International science olympiad on the Uzbek language will be held. The winners will be accepted to study on the basis of a state grant in the field of Uzbek language philology.

The President stated:

At the meeting, President Shavkat Mirziyoyev said that it would be fair for teachers of the Uzbek language and literature to be paid no less than teachers of other subjects with international certificates. noted.

Olympiad and grant opportunities:

  • International Science Olympiad: An international science Olympiad on the Uzbek language will be organized. This Olympiad creates a great opportunity for those interested in the philology of the Uzbek language.
  • State grant: The winners of the Olympiad are admitted to universities on the basis of a state grant in the field of philology of the Uzbek language.

These measures are implemented in order to improve the qualifications of teachers of the Uzbek language and literature and give them appropriate incentives. — Source
