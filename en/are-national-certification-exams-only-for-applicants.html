Currently, many questions are being asked by many people about who can participate in the national certificate exam, and whether this exam is only for applicants. It is organized on the basis of the decision No. 646 dated October 12, 2020.

This decision does not contain a restriction that only applicants can participate in the national certificate exams. That is, everyone can take this exam.

In other words, anyone who expresses a desire to test their knowledge, as in foreign language exams, can take part in the national certificate exam in general education subjects and obtain the appropriate certificate.

One of the most frequently asked questions about this exam is related to the granting of bonuses to teachers with national certificates.
