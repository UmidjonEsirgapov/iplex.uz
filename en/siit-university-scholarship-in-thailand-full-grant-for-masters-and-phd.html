Sirindhorn International Institute of Technology(SIIT): The Sirindhorn Institute of Technology in Thailand has announced a fully funded grant program for international students for master's and doctoral degrees. No admission fee is required. The master's degree lasts 2 years, and the doctoral degree lasts 3 years. Language certificates (IELTS/TOEFL) are not mandatory.

Benefits of the grant:

  • The full contract fee is waived;
  • $305 for living expenses;
  • Selected air tickets are covered (Economy class);
  • Visa expenses, transportation expenses $ 305 USD;
  • Medical insurance is also covered by the grant;

You can apply for the following areas:

  • Electrical engineering/Energy technology and management;
  • Engineering;
  • Natural sciences, technology management
  • Management- industrial engineering, Logistics;
  • IT technology;
  • Biochemical engineering;
  • Chemical engineering:
  • Computer engineering
  • Engineering management;
  • Logistics;
  • Management technology;

Requirements for candidates:

  • To be a foreign national (Thai nationals cannot participate here;
  • 2 letters of recommendation (1 from an academic place of study);
  • To be mentally and physically healthy;
