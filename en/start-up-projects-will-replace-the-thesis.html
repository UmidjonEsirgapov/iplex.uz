Start-up projects will replace the thesis

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the president's decision, starting from this year, a startup project or a rationalization work suitable for a student's field of study will be protected as a diploma thesis or a master's thesis.

This means that if a student has a start-up project, he does not have to sit down and write a 60-page diploma. So, if he shows his knowledge in practice rather than in words, he will get his points. — More information
