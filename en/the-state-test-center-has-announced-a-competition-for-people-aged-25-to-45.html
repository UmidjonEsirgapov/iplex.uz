The State Test Center has announced a competition for people aged 25 to 45

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency is announcing a competition for candidates who want to participate as supervisors in the tests organized by the agency in 2023.
The selection is carried out in 3 stages: in the 1st stage, the candidates' compliance with the requirements set by the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency will be studied.
At the 2nd stage, candidates in the regions will be trained in a training course, and at the end of the course, a final inspection will be conducted, and based on its results, applicants will be awarded a certificate.

The selection is carried out in 3 stages:

In the 1️⃣-stage the compliance of the candidates with the established requirements is studied by the Agency for the Assessment of Knowledge and Skills.

In the 2️⃣-stage in the regions, candidates are trained in a training course, and at the end of the course, a final inspection is conducted, and applicants are awarded a certificate based on its results.

In the 3️⃣-stage Knowledge and the regional departments of the Agency for the Evaluation of Qualifications will conduct an interview with candidates with a certificate, and based on the results of the interview, a contract will be concluded with the candidates recommended for the supervision of the group. For this, applicants must register from January 23 to February 23, 2023 through the "Competition for Group Supervision" section of the my.dtm.uz site.

The message explains that students, tutors, directors and teachers of educational centers cannot participate in the competition. — Official Data
