One of the top universities in Milan, Italy - Milano-Bicocca University is accepting applications for the International Economics grant program, which fully covers contract fees for the master's degree.

Requirements for the candidate:

  • Bachelor's degree is required or must be obtained by December 23 of this year;
  • One of these language certificates is required (BEC Vantage; BEC Higher; CAE; City&Guilds Pitman Qualifications; CPE; FCE; Academic IELTS; ETS-TOEFL; ETS-TOIEC )(language certificate is not required if the undergraduate education is in English);

Note: Citizens of non-EU countries are requested to submit online application here before registering on this site.

Admission period:

  • Admission for all students is from April 6 to September 14;

after all documents are submitted then the candidates will be interviewed. Interview Deadlines:

  • May 7th 2021, 9 a.m. CET;
  • June 7th 2021, 2 p.m. CET;
  • July 1st 2021, 9 a.m. CET;
  • July 23rd 2021, 2 p.m. CET;
  • September 6th 2021, 9 a.m. CET;
  • September 17th 2021, 2 p.m. CET;
  • September 20th 2021, 9 p.m. CET;
  • September 21st 2021, 2 p.m. CET;

Due to Covid19, all interviews will be conducted online. For this via Skype, contact this email.

To register, visit this web page of the university and enter the following information:

  • Personal information;
  • Abode;
  • Telephone number and e-mail;

After that, the candidate will be given a username and password, and together with these, they will upload the following documents needed through this site:

  • ID or Passport;
  • 300x400 pixel size photo;
  • Bachelor's diploma (translated into Italian or English);
  • CV;
  • Language certificates and any other language additional certificates specifying qualifications may also be placed;
