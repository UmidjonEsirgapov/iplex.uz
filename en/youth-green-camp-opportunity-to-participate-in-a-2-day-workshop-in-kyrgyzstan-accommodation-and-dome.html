Youth Green Camp: opportunity to participate in a 2-day workshop in Kyrgyzstan; Accommodation and domestic transportation expenses are covered

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
General Information: C5+1 Youth Council is pleased to announce the start of the application process for the Youth Green Camp.
High school and university students from all countries of Central Asia and Afghanistan (residing in Kyrgyzstan) They will have the opportunity to learn from the leading experts in Afghanistan, to discuss regional ecological and ecological problems.
Benefits: Candidates will have the opportunity to participate in this 2-day camp for free; Domestic transportation and accommodation expenses are also covered. You can register through this link: Registration deadline: November 23, 2021, 23:59 (Bishkek time); The final results will be notified on November 24, 2021.

General Information:

C5+1 Youth Council is pleased to announce that the application process for the Youth Green Camp has begun. It consists of seminars on raising awareness of environmental and ecological problems in Afghanistan. High school and university students from all countries of Central Asia and Afghanistan (residents of Kyrgyzstan) will have the opportunity to learn from leading experts in Central Asia and Afghanistan, discuss regional environmental and environmental issues. In addition, the participants of the Youth Green Camp will have the opportunity to present their solutions on the topic of "Environmental and ecological problems in Central Asia and Afghanistan" and win micro-grants to implement their solutions.

Benefits:

  • Candidates will have the opportunity to attend this 2-day camp for free;
  • Internal transportation and accommodation expenses will be covered.
    You can register using this link:

Registration deadline: November 23, 2021, 23:59 (Bishkek time);
Final results will be notified on November 24, 2021.

Location: Naryn, Kyrgyzstan (Aska Hotel)
Date: November 27-28, 2021
Format: offline

For candidates requirements:

  • Designed for English-speaking high school and university students
  • Citizens of Central Asia (Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan, Tajikistan) and Afghanistan;
  • Students of secondary schools and universities in the Naryn region of Kyrgyzstan;
  • Residents of Bishkek Lyceum and university students from Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan;

Note: Participants from Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan residing in Bishkek will be provided with transportation and accommodation, they will have to go to Norin region of Kyrgyzstan to participate in the Youth Green Camp.
