Apply online for the test

Online registration of applicants for tests to determine the level of foreign language knowledge starting from March 2022 in accordance with the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated February 16, 2022 No. 73 Unified interactive public services portal (YIDXP - my.gov.uz site).

This service is located in the "Education" section of the my.gov.uz site (Figure 1).

To register for foreign language exams online, select "Determination of foreign language proficiency" service from "Education" section (Figure 2).

In this, the user is provided with the most necessary information related to this service, such as the legal basis of service provision, service price, contact information (Figure 3).

The "Use service" button is clicked to start registration.

Registration consists of 4 steps, the 1st step is familiarization with the public offer and acceptance of the terms of the offer (Figure 4).

In step 2, the information about the applicant is filled (Figure 5).

In the 3rd step, the information about the test is filled in (Figure 6).

In this, the applicant chooses the Republic of Karakalpakstan , provinces and Tashkent as the testing area, and he chooses one of them. in the region can participate in the test.

After filling in this information, the applicant is asked to check the information again in order to avoid errors (Figure 7). If the data is correct, the "Send" button is clicked (Figure 8).

The 4th step is to make a payment, which shows the information about the application and the payment information (Figure 9).

Clicking the “Payment Information” button will display all information related to the payment. Here you can download the payment receipt or cancel the application.

The payment amount is 1.5 times of the basic calculation amount. In this case, since the registration for public service is done through YIDXP, the applicant will be charged 90% of the specified payment amount. Currently (as of March 9, 2022) the basic calculation amount is 270,000 soums, so the test fee is 364,500 soums.

Payment through electronic payment systems such as Click, Payme, UPAY or bank cash desks (14 to the invoice number) is possible.

When payment is made through electronic payment systems, select "State Services Agency" from the "State Services" section and enter the 14-digit invoice number shown on the receipt.

Also, make the payment to "Invoice it can also be done through the "pay" button.

After the payment is made, a record will be created with information about the applicant and the test. This application must be downloaded.

Applicants will be informed about the time and place of the test through the my.gov.uz website 3 days before the test.
