Complete information about the super-contract (year 2025)

Many applicants who did not get admission have questions about the super-contract. Below we have summarized the most important information about the super-contract procedures and prices for 2025.

‼️ Attention: The super-contract prices and procedures for 2025 have not yet been confirmed. More information will be provided upon confirmation. You can see the prices for 2024 through this link.

Super-contract amount for under 4 points

Super-contract prices are determined depending on how many points you scored below the passing score:

  • Under 1 point is 1.5 times of the base contract. (For example, if the contract is 10 million soums, the super-contract will be 15 million soums).

  • 1.01 - up to 2 points - 2 times.

  • 2.01 - up to 3 points - 2.5 times.

  • Up to 3.01 - 4 points - 3 times.

Are ordinary contract and super-contract paid together?

No. In the first year, only the super-contract is paid. Starting from the second year, a regular contract will be paid.

How to apply

  • When will it start? — In September, when studies start (last year it started on September 2).

  • Where will it be submitted? — Only online. There is no need to go to the ATM, wait in line, or beg.

  • Whoever submits first gets in? - No. Applicants are ranked according to their scores, priority is given to those with higher scores.

Is it possible to super-contract to another direction?

This year's order is not yet known. Last year, super-contracting was allowed only in the first chosen field.

Super-contract options

Study in any five fields

Last year, it was possible to choose one of five fields and study on the basis of a super-contract. This year's rules have not yet been approved.

Obtaining education loans

State education loans are not issued for super-contracts. But banks can offer commercial loans. After the 2nd year, it is possible to get an education loan.

Possibility to transfer to a grant

Those studying in the super-contract also have the right to transfer to a grant if they learn well after the 1st year.

Payment in installments

The amount of the super-contract can be paid in 4 parts.
