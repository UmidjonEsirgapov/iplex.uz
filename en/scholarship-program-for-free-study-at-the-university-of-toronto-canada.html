The Lester B. Pearson International Scholarships - The Lester B. Pearson International Scholarships - is a grant program established by the University of Toronto in Canada to support international students. The scholarship program is designed for students who are active and talented in all aspects, and who are recognized as leaders in their school. The candidate's impact on the life of his school and community and the contribution he can make to the future global community are important for admission to the program.

Benefits:

  • The opportunity to study at the university without contract fees;
  • All books and money for emergencies. stipend;
  • Free accommodation will be provided

Requirements:

  • Candidates must not be Canadian citizens
  • Be in their last year of school or graduate after June 2021 be;
  • Be accepted to the University of Toronto and must begin studies in September 2022.

Document submission process:

  • A letter of recommendation from the candidate's school for admission to study at the University of Toronto;
  • Apply to the University of Toronto and be accepted;
  • After being admitted to the University then will be able to apply for this grant

The deadline for applying to the university is December 14, 2021.

The deadline for applying for the grant is January 17, 2022.
