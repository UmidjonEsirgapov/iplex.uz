The results of the Registan scholarship have been announced

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

"REGISTON SCHOLARSHIP" competition for students was announced in cooperation with the OLIYGOH.UZ platform. Candidates who took part in the competition tested their knowledge in the exam system consisting of 3 stages for 2 months. The 1st stage of the scholarship competition was held on February 20. they won the ticket. (View results) All candidates who participated in the first stage can download special certificates of the competition through "Personal cabinet" (Download certificates).

— On February 27, 2nd stage exams were held among the candidates who passed to the second stage of the competition, and those who showed the highest results in this stage 581 participants qualified for the final. (View results)

— According to the competition instructions and regulations, the 3rd stage (final) stage was held between the best performing participants . The third stage is the stage of submitting documents, candidates participating in the scholarship competition submitted the documents specified in the competition regulations in the specified order through the platform's special examination window. The special commission of the competition

checked these documents for 2 working weeks.

The composition of the commission:

Chairman:

  • Tulovov Avazbek — the founder of the Registon educational project, the director of the REGISTON" training center.

Members of the commission:

  • professional teachers of the Registon educational center of the international level, consisting of 10 people; finalist of international grants;
  • Talibjonov Botirjon — director of the Registon educational project "HR Department", coordinator of social projects;
  • Hamidjonov Shavkat — OLIYGOH.UZ is one of the founders of the platform, laureate of international CDAC, ACTS grants, founder of many international startups.

The results of the candidates were evaluated by the selection committee based on the 90-point evaluation system based on the following requirements:

  • Recommendation letter from the deanery or department for the Registan scholarship awarded to the student from the place of study. Information about the student provided by the official representative of the university in the recommendation;
  • Motivational letter (a special motivational letter written about why they consider themselves worthy of winning the "Registon scholarship" contest and goals set for them. The letter is accepted in any of the Uzbek, Russian and English languages) Independent thinking, spelling errors, consistency and fluency of written thoughts, as well as the originality of the conclusion were checked with special attention. social activity during the academic years, as well as published scientific works, patents, inventions
  • Not only scientific work or achievements, but also international level exam certificates were studied;
  • Also, the accuracy of the documents sent by students, proof of identity, reference from the place of study (confirmed via my.gov.uz or confirmed by the dean's office) references), CVs (reference books) were also examined and members of the commission, summarizing the results, announced the following candidates as "Registon Scholarship" winners:
