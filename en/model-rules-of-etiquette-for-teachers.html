Model rules of etiquette for teachers

The following requirements for pedagogues according to the improved model code of conduct for general secondary education institutions approved by the Ministry of Preschool and School Education on August 31, 2023 placed:

1. Responsibility and respect for students

  • Do not use physical force or psychological pressure.
  • Do not discuss personal information of students in front of classmates or other people.
  • Do not stalk the student in public.

2. Cooperation with parents and management

  • Provide information about students who have gone abroad to the management of the institution in a timely manner.
  • Do not engage in personal enmity with the student's parents or legal representatives.

3. Protection of information and interests

  • Not to use information about the educational institution in order not to damage its interests and reputation or for personal purposes.
  • To refrain from actions that are against the interests of the educational institution and prevent such situations.

4. Immediate response to situations and events

  • In case of any unpleasant situation or incident, immediately inform the management of the institution.

5. Promotion of a healthy lifestyle

  • Widely promotion of a healthy lifestyle among young people.
  • Warning against harmful habits, religious extremist influences and foreign influences of "popular culture".

6. Personal example and friendly attitude

  • Being an example to students, showing a personal example and being friendly with them.

7. Explaining the rules to students

  • Wide explanation of the rules of etiquette to students in the "Class Hour" held every Friday.

Basic: Order No. 282 of August 31, 2023. — Source
