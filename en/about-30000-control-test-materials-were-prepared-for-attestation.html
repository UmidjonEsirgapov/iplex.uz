According to the information given at the press conference of the Ministry of Pre-school and School Education:

Teacher certification is held at least once a year in two stages and in the 100 point system.

In the 1st stage - the level of knowledge of the pedagogue's specialty subject and professional standard (80 points),

In the 2nd stage - the pedagogical skill (20 points) is evaluated.

Both stages are conducted on a computer in real time (online).

Three out of 42 subjects. (Uzbek, Russian, Karakalpak) about 30,000 control test materials have been prepared.

Today, 243,541 pedagogues have submitted online applications to participate in the certification, and more than 202,000 of them are expected to participate.

Qualification tests will begin on April 15 of this year. It is planned to be held in April and May in all regions. For this purpose, 1,900 computers are ready in large-capacity buildings located in regional centers. — Official Data
