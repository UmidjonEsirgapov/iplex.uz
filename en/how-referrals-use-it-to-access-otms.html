How do those who receive a recommendation use it to enter the OTM?

The recommendation letter provided by the Women's Committee is one of the important privileges for entering higher education institutions for educated and active girls in need of social protection. Below we provide detailed information on how to use this letter of recommendation.

The application period has ended - what happens now?

As you know, the process of submitting an application for a letter of recommendation ended on June 10. Now the Women's Committee will review the applications and determine the applicants who should be given a recommendation. This process is also carried out on the basis of special criteria.

How to find out if a recommendation has been issued?

The committee will send a reply to all applicants who submitted an application via SMS or another official channel - that is, you will be informed whether you have received a recommendation or not. In addition, the Committee submits a list of those who have received a recommendation to the Knowledge Management Center (BMB).

How will the recommendation be reflected in the document submission process?

If you have received a recommendation:

  • When you submit a document through the Abituriyent.uz platform or another official system, about the recommendation the information will be displayed automatically.

  • You do not need to upload any documents, because the system automatically recognizes the presence of your recommendation letter.

What benefits does it give?

The recommendation allows applicants admission to state HEIs based on an additional quota. This makes the competition in the competition much easier.
