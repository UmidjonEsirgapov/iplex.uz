Amendments and additions to the new regulations of class leaders

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
By order of the Minister of Preschool and School Education No. 190 dated June 27, 2023, the new position instruction of class leaders has been adopted.
New regulation: This paragraph has been deleted, grades 1-4 and It is not written about the appointment of a single head of class for grades 5-11.
The new job instruction helps to increase the efficiency of planning and implementation of the educational work of class heads through the methodical association of "Class Heads". accepted. This new regulation canceled the previous orders:
  • Order of the Minister of Public Education No. 180 of July 30, 2020
  • Order No. 41 of February 22, 2021
  • Order No. 52 of March 1, 2021

Major changes

Appointment of a single classroom teacher

  • Previous statute: In grades 1-4 and in grades 5-11, there were clauses for the appointment of a single classroom teacher.
  • New statute: This clause has been deleted, for grades 1-4 and grades 5-11 It is not written about the appointment of a single class leader.

Evaluation of the performance of class leaders

  • Previous regulation: It was written that the performance of class leaders will be evaluated in January and June and posted in the teachers' room.
  • New regulation: This chapter has been deleted.

New position instruction

"Class leaders" methodological association

With the new job instructions, the "Class leaders" methodological association is created and all class leaders are members of it. The methodological association performs the following tasks:

  • Planning the annual work of class leaders
  • Helping to organize and conduct educational activities
  • Increasing their efficiency
  • Coordinating their work
  • Publicizing work experiences

Conclusion

The new regulation is aimed at improving the activities of class leaders. The new job instruction will help to increase the effectiveness of planning and implementation of the educational work of class leaders through the methodical association of "Class leaders". — Read on infoedu.uz
