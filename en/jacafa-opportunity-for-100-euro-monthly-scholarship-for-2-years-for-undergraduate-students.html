General Information:

JACAFA Foundation (Japan Central Asia Friends and Associates Foundation) & The Nippon Foundation: This program is a grant program for undergraduate students to receive a monthly scholarship of €100 for 2 years and can be applied by 2nd-year students studying almost any field (social sciences, economics, finance, management, computer engineering, and computer science). This program is available to candidates for 2 years (valid from October 2022 to June 2024).

  • The opportunity to win up to 10,000 dollars is provided for small projects;
  • At the end of the program, candidates can get the opportunity to participate in the JACAFA JAPAN Visit Program;

    • Requirements for candidates:

    • Be a citizen of Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Kazakhstan or Azerbaijan
    • Grade average (GPA) must be at least 3 on a 4-point system. In the local universities of Uzbekistan, this indicator is equal to 75 points on a 100-point system, 3.75 points on a 5-point system;
    • Good knowledge of the English language is required, as well as IELTS, TOEFL, TOEIC (CEFR is also accepted) certificate or experience of completing courses taught in English at the university;
    • Age at the time the scholarship is awarded (2022 October) must not be more than 23 years old;
    • Students who will complete the 2nd year in the summer of 2022 can apply for this program;
    • The candidate must be studying full-time in one of the fields of social sciences, economics, finance, management, computer engineering and computer science.
