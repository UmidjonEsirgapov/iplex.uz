A branch of the Italian University was established in Tashkent

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
A branch of the Italian University of Pisa is being established in Tashkent.
Highly qualified experts in geology are trained in the branch, researches are conducted in the field of geology and their results are put into practice. 24.11.2021) was adopted.

According to the decision, a branch of the Italian University of Pisa was established in Tashkent.

Highly qualified experts in geology are trained in the branch, research in the field of geology is conducted and their results are put into practice.

Education in the branch is full-time from the 2022/2023 academic year.

in the form of Uzbek, English and Russian languages, it is carried out on the basis of a contract.

Graduates of the branch are awarded a diploma of the University of Pisa.

Admission parameters are determined based on the need for specialists.

Educational activity in the branch begins after the license is issued in the manner prescribed by the State Inspection of Education Quality under the Cabinet of Ministers.

The University of Pisa (Università di Pisa) is located in Pisa, Italy, and is one of the oldest educational institutions in Europe. Its history dates back to the 11th century. The institution received the status of a university on September 3, 1343. There are 11 faculties and more than 57,000 students. — Read on infoedu.uz
