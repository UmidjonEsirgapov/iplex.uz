The main aspects of the new system Admission based on selection: Candidates for teaching are accepted on the basis of competition.

Candidates accepted for the first time will work as teacher-trainees for 1 year and will have to receive a professional certificate at the end of the year. In the future, this system will be gradually introduced for all teachers.

The main aspects of the new system

  • Admission based on selection: Teacher candidates are accepted based on selection.
  • Internship period: Candidates who are accepted for the first time work as teacher-trainees for 1 year.
  • Professional certificate: By the end of the year, the trainees will have to get a professional certificate.
  • Step-by-step implementation: This system will be implemented step-by-step for all teachers in the future.
