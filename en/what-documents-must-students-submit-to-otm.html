What documents must students submit to OTM?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Certificate of language proficiency (if any) — the original copy of the certificate confirming the student's knowledge of a foreign language.
Application for student housing — If the student needs housing and has documents confirming that the conditions are severe or poor.
Privilege document for those recommended for studentship on the basis of - Students with privileges are required to have the original document confirming the privilege (if available).

First-year students admitted to higher education institutions must submit the following documents to the educational institution: — Official Data

  1. Application to the Rector - written application to confirm the student's admission.
  2. Copy of passport (with the original indicated) — Copy of the student's identity document.
  3. Diploma or certificate (with an attachment) — Original copy of the document confirming completion of school or secondary special education.
  4. Certificate of language proficiency (if any) — Original copy of the certificate confirming the student's knowledge of a foreign language.
  5. 3.5×4.5 size photo — 12 photos are required.
  6. Document folder (folder skorosshivatel) — Special folder for collecting all documents together.
  7. Envelope in A5 format — Envelope for documents.
  8. Application for student accommodation — If the student needs accommodation and if they have documents confirming that their circumstances are difficult or low-income.
  9. Document for those recommended for studentship on the basis of privilege — The original document confirming the privilege is required from eligible students (if available). There may be changes in the list of documents this year, so it is recommended to contact the admissions committee before submitting the documents.
