Why do you need PhD and Doctor of Science degrees?

PhD (Doctor of Philosophy) and Doctor of Science degrees are important for post-higher education research and academic activity in Uzbekistan. A PhD degree provides the basis for starting scientific research and becoming a university teacher. In order to obtain this degree, it is required to complete a basic doctoral course and defend a dissertation.

The degree of Doctor of Science means gaining extensive experience in scientific research and conducting in-depth scientific research in a specific field of science. Doctors of science will have the opportunity to advance in academic positions and take scientific leadership. — Source
