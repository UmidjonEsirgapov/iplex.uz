Now kindergarten leaders and pedagogues will also be financially encouraged

President's 2024 According to Decree No. PF-152 dated September 30, a number of new changes and financial incentives will be introduced in the preschool education system starting from 2025. This decree creates additional opportunities for managers and pedagogues of preschool education organizations.

From January 1, 2025:

  • The position of deputy director of preschool education organization will be introduced.
  • At least one primary preschool education organization will be established in all districts and cities of the republic.
  • In all pre-school education organizations, "Methodical skill hour", and "Methodical skill day" will be gradually introduced in basic preschool education organizations.
  • Deputy directors working as trainers will receive an additional bonus in the amount of 20% of their salary. will be paid.

From March 1, 2025:

  • Mobile training services will be introduced on a pilot basis for pedagogues of preschool educational organizations located in remote and remote areas of the republic.

Incentives for leaders and pedagogues:

At the expense of the fund of the Minister of Preschool and School Education It is allowed to preschool educational organizations to encourage the leadership and pedagogic personnel. It is aimed at materially supporting the employees of the preschool education system and improving their performance.

Inclusive education:

From January 1, 2025, inclusive education groups will be opened in one general type of state preschool education organizations in each district (city). This is a step aimed at expanding the educational opportunities of children. — Source
