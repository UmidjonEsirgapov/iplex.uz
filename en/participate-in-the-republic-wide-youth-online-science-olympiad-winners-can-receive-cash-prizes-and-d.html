Participate in the Republic-wide "Youth Online Science Olympiad". Winners can receive cash prizes and diplomas with a special QR code.

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
On June 13, the OLIYGOH.UZ team is organizing an online Science Olympiad among young people on the scale of the Republic!
The number of questions is 15. Certificates with a QR code will be handed out.

OLIYGOH.UZ team is organizing Science Olympiad online among young people on June 13 in the republic! Participate, test your knowledge and receive cash prizes and diplomas with a special QR code.

In order to support talented young people and develop their intellectual potential, the OLIYGOH.UZ team is organizing the Republic-wide "Youth Online Science Olympiad".

The winners will receive cash prizes, can get diplomas and certificates with a special QR code, and special certificates will be given to all the participating candidates. (optional)">

Registration.

  • Young people who want to participate in the Olympics can register on OLIYGOH.UZ website until June 13th. Registration is done by opening "Personal cabinet". When filling in the information, make sure that the information is correct and accurate. A code will be sent to each candidate's phone number during the registration process at the Olympiad.
  • Registration is done through OLIYGOH.UZ website

Completing tests and tasks.

  • Test tasks from subjects consist of 60 questions and to work on test questions 60 minutes time is given. one minute is allotted for each question

Which subjects are Olympiad questions made from?

  • OLIYGOH.UZ team takes into account the interests of all young people and aims to keep up with the times. With the desire to serve in the way of increasing legal literacy, the Republic announces that the Youth Science Olympiad will be based on the following subjects:

- English (Elementary, Intermediate, Advanced levels). Number of questions - 15

- Law (Constitutional law). Number of questions - 15

- Computer knowledge (General computer literacy). Number of questions - 15

- IQ (Logical questions). The number of questions is 15

Olympiad will be held in the form of test. The total number of questions is 60.

Olympiad participants

First of all, this Olympiad is intended for students. If you are not a student (student, applicant or any interested person) it is not a problem. You have the opportunity to participate in this Olympiad

Alloted time

  • 60 minutes will be allotted for online test of subjects. The Olympiad starts at 19:00 and closes for everyone at 20:00. During this period, candidates are required to participate in the Olympiad.

Olympic-winners encouraged

  • Young people who took part in the Olympiad and won prizes 1, 2, 3rd level special QR-coded diplomas will be awarded and diplomas will be presented to them online
  • Olympic winners will be financially encouraged in the total amount of 1.5 million (one million five hundred thousand) soums:
  1. For a place - 700 thousand soums
  2. For a place - 500 thousand Soums
  3. For a place - 300 thousand soums

*If several candidates occupy the same place, the prize money will be divided equally among the candidates.

Special QR-coded certificates will be given to all candidates who participated in the Olympiad.

All participants of the OLIYGOH.UZ team good luck!
