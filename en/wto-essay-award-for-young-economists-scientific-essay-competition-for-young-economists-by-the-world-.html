The World Trade Organization announces the WTO Essay Award for young Economists! required
Requirements for participants:
✅ The candidate must be studying at the PhD level or have a PhD degree
✅ If the candidate is over 30 years old, it must not be more than 2 years since he received his PhD
✅ The essay must analyze issues related to trade policy and international trade cooperation.
✅ Essay size No more than 15,000 words


Opportunities for winners:

- $5,400 for the best essay author;
- Participation in the award ceremony in the Netherlands on September 8-10, 2022;
- All expenses are covered by the organizers;
- The winning essay in the competition is in the WTO Working Paper Series will be published.
