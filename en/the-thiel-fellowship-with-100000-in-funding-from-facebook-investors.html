The Thiel Fellowship, with $100,000 in funding from Facebook investors

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Facebook investors established The Thiel Fellowship program in order to support young people with strong knowledge and big ideas.
Benefits: Program participants will be given $100,000 to implement their ideas for 2 years. There is an opportunity to participate in the program while staying in their own country. Extraordinary talented young people who want to start a big project instead of studying at school can apply for the program at any time of the year.

Benefits:

  • Participants of the program will be given $100,000to implement their ideas for 2 years
  • It is possible to participate in the program while staying in one's own country.

Requirements to participate in the program:

  • Citizens of any country around the world can participate;
  • Candidates must not be older than 22 years old and have a good command of English;
  • To participate, they must give up school or college education and have a strong idea. It's a must.

Projects in any field are supported.
