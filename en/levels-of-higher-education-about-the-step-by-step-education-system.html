Levels of higher education: About the step-by-step education system

Uzbekistan's higher education system consists of several stages, and each stage gives students the opportunity to develop specific knowledge and skills.

1️⃣ Bachelor

  • Duration: 4-6 years
  • Content: Students get the basic theoretical and practical knowledge of the specialty.
  • After completion: Bachelor's diploma is awarded. This document allows you to enter a master's degree or start work in your field.

2️⃣ Master's Degree

  • Duration: 1-3 years
  • Content: Students will gain deeper knowledge in a specialized field and conduct scientific research.
  • After completion: A Master's degree will be awarded. This degree provides the opportunity to enter a doctoral program or to work as a highly qualified specialist.

3️⃣ Doctorate

  • Duration: 3 years (PhD or DSc programs)
  • Content: Students conduct scientific research, develop new ideas and theories.
  • Upon completion: Degree (PhD or DSc) is awarded. This degree gives you the opportunity to become a professor in educational institutions or work in research centers.

Important Note

Strong training and knowledge are required to succeed at each stage. It is necessary to fulfill specific requirements to move to the next stages.
