IDEX masters program: the opportunity to study in France on the basis of a full grant.

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
How to apply: 1) Selected students will automatically be sent a link to the online application form by email. After receiving this email, students who wish to apply can fill out the online application form and provide the names of two references who are ready to submit a reference to the candidate (academic director, professor, internship coordinator ...) (mandatory).
They are asked to complete and submit the form before the closing date of the scholarship call. Each reference will automatically notify the candidate after submitting the form.
Requirements: Students accepted to the Master's program of the University of Paris-Saclay by one of the following institutions: AgroParisTech, CentraleSupelec, ENS Paris-Saclay, INSTN-CEA, IOGS, UEVE, UPSaclay, UVSQ.

General information:

Université Paris-Saclay aims to provide international students with access to Master’s programs offered by their faculties, constituent institutes and related universities and to facilitate the admission of highly qualified international students to the University, especially those who wish to develop. To become a recipient of this grant, you must first be admitted to this university.

Important deadlines:

-Deadline for Master’s application: May 7, 2022;
- After selection, scholarship application deadline: May 14, 2022;
- Acceptance of recommendations after selection and call termination date: May 18, 2022; - Announcement of results: end June 2022;

Note: Admission to the Master's program does not automatically entitle students to scholarships. Only students who have received an email from Paris-Saclay University inviting them to apply for the Paris-Saclay University IDEX Scholarship can apply.

How to apply:

1) Selected students will be automatically sent a link to the online application form by email. they must indicate the names of two references (mandatory) that they are ready to submit. .

2) Each of the two references provided by the candidate will be emailed a link to the online recommendation form. They are requested to complete and submit the form before the closing date of the scholarship call.The candidate will be automatically notified after each reference submits the form.

3) The scholarship application file is considered complete when both reference forms are submitted by two references.


Please note: the candidate will not receive a confirmation that the application has been completed.
must ensure the completeness of the reference and submit the form by the deadline specified in the invitation e-mail.

Requirements:

Students admitted to the master's program of the University of Paris-Saclay by one of the following institutions: AgroParisTech, CentraleSupelec, ENS Paris-Saclay, INSTN-CEA, IOGS, UEVE, UPSaclay, UVSQ. Among these students, only those who meet one of the following criteria can apply:
- Newly arrived international students aged 30 or younger during the selection year.
- Foreign national students who have lived in France for less than one year, who were previously or currently enrolled in training courses or internships that do not lead to certification.
- Foreign national students who have lived in France for less than a year, language lessons (FFL type).


Note: Admission to the master's program does not automatically give students the right to receive a scholarship.

Choosing the winners, evaluation criteria:

-The selection of the winners is within the framework of a jury consisting of experts from the faculties, constituent institutes and relevant universities of Paris-Saclay University.

Evaluation the following criteria are taken into account:
- Academic degree;
- Personal project;
- In particular, for students enrolled in the M2 program: written motivation to continue the dissertation in the laboratory of the University of Paris-Saclay.

Benefits:

  • The amount of the Université Paris-Saclay scholarship is €10,000 per year. Paid by Université Paris-Saclay during the academic year and for 10 consecutive months (from September to June).
  • A maximum of 1000 euros for travel and visa expenses may also be awarded depending on the candidate's country of origin.
  • Laureates will receive their stipend and travel expenses only upon arrival in France and registration in the program they are accepted for.

Note: First, before applying for this grant, please study in detail through the official website.
