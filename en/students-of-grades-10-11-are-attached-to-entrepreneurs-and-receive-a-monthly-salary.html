10-11th graders will be attached to entrepreneurs and paid monthly

Vocational and vocational education in schools of Uzbekistan A new system for the development of education is being introduced. Starting from 2025, students of grades 10-11 will be directed to a profession according to their talents and interests and be attached to business entities.

This procedure was approved based on the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers on determining the procedure for mutual cooperation between schools and business entities.

How does the new system work?

  • Students will be directed to a profession based on their interests and talents
  • Students assigned to entrepreneurs will be paid monthly during the internship
  • By the end of the 2024/2025 academic year, school graduates will take a practical exam
  • Procedure of issuing electronic vocational certificate to graduates will be introduced

These changes will help young people to gain real experience during their studies, choose a future profession and be competitive in the labor market. — Details here
