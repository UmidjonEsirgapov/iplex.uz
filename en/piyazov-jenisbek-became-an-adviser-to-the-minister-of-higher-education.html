Piyazov Jenisbek became an adviser to the Minister of Higher Education

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The position of adviser on the development of culture and art of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation was introduced on a public basis.
Jenisbek Piyazov was assured that he will be an expert in ensuring the socialization of young people in the higher and professional education system, meaningful organization of their free time, realizing the talents and abilities of young students in the field of art, culture, and music within the framework of the "Five Important Initiatives", and actively involving them in circles and clubs in this direction.
