The beginning of a new academic year is not only a timetable and notebooks. It means new opportunities, new goals and a new stage. For every student and applicant, this period can be a life-defining turning point.

With the following tips, you can take yourself to a whole new level in 2025.

1. Dare to renew yourself

Summer vacation may have left you a little "slow". At the beginning of the new year, try to introduce a new approach to your life:

  • new habits (morning jogging, reading, writing in English diary),
  • new routine (disciplined sleep and lesson plan),
  • new motivation (envisioning the future profession).

This seemingly simple actions will set you apart from others.

2. Not only knowledge, but also skills are needed

The grades you get at school are important, but skills are more important in real life:

  • time management,
  • quick decision-making,
  • effective use of online platforms.
