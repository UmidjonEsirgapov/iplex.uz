During the first 5 months of 2024, 359 schoolchildren were involved in road traffic accidents. As a result of these accidents, 65 students died, and 294 students received various injuries. These situations cause great concern for schoolchildren and their parents.

In 2023, 1,794 accidents involving children occurred, in which 1,568 children were injured and 263 children died. These numbers show that there are serious problems with road safety in our country.

It is necessary to take a number of measures to prevent such incidents. First of all, it is necessary to teach students the rules of the road and monitor their practical application. Parents and school administration should also pay attention to the traffic safety of students.

The following measures can be taken to reduce the number of accidents and ensure the safety of children:

  1. Teach traffic rules: Regularly teach children traffic rules at school and at home.
  2. Strengthen control: Near school and home strengthen police control on roads.
  3. Improving infrastructure: improving road signs and sidewalks near schools.
  4. Cooperation with parents: ensuring safe movement of children together with parents.
