A branch of Mukhtar Avezov University will be opened in Tashkent

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
On March 9, the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation of the Republic of Uzbekistan Kongirotboy Sharipov, the Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of Kazakhstan to Uzbekistan Beybut Atamkulov and the Mayor of Chirchik Davron Khidoyatov of South Kazakhstan University named after M.Avezov inspected the ongoing work on opening a branch in the city of Chirchik.
For information: M.Avezov University is included in the list of internationally recognized TOP-1000 universities and has been occupying the 482nd place for the past two years.
