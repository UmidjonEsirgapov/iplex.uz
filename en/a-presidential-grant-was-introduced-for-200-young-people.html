A Presidential grant was introduced for 200 young people

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The distribution of the quota allocated for the presidential grant by educational fields is approved by the state commission for admission to educational institutions every year. (No. PQ-5172, 06.07.2011) was adopted.

According to the decision, from the 2021/2022 academic year, the Republic of Uzbekistan for 200 young people who scored the highest points in entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam) to the full-time education form of bachelor of higher education institutions of the state The President's grant has been introduced.

The distribution of the quota allocated for the Presidential grant by field of education is approved by the State Commission for Admission to Educational Institutions every year.

✅ The applicant in the last place according to the Presidential grant quota allocated for a specific field of education and all citizens with the same points as him will be eligible for the Presidential grant.

❌ The points added to the score obtained by the entrance exam results of applicants in the category of additional points for admission to OTM are not taken into account when determining candidates for the Presidential grant.

Applicants for the Presidential grant are admitted to study on the basis of a state grant. It is determined based on the selection of students who have made progress in the fields of education, spiritual education, and scientific research. — Read on infoedu.uz

At the end of the academic semester, the scholarship for the President's grant will be reassigned when the mastery rate in all subjects is only "excellent".
