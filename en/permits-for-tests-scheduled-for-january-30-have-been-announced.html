Nostrification: details of the next tests of 2025

On January 30, 2025, the next tests will be held on the recognition (nostrification) of documents on higher education abroad. The list of applicants scheduled to take part in these tests and other details can be found in the following sources:

  • through the State Service Centers;
  • by the application number on the my.gov.uz site;
  • Or the PDF document above.

Important information about the tests:

  • The test will be held places: Defined in accordance with the regions;
  • Time: Exact time is indicated for each participant based on the established schedule;
  • Required documents: It is required to bring an identity document (passport or ID card) to the tests.

Changes in the region and language: If the applicant changes the place of taking the test or the language of the test if they want to enter, they can apply for Nostrification through the Telegram bot. The following information should be included in the application:

  • Application number;
  • Name, surname and first name.

Note: Applicants must come to the tests before the scheduled time. Test takers will not be given additional tests. — More information
