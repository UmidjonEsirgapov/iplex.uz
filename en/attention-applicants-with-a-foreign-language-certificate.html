It is known that applicants with certificates from foreign languages enter certificate information into the system during registration.

Currently, the State Test Center is checking the authenticity of certificates based on this information and assigning them 3 different statuses:

- When the certificate is included in the program - "Checking".

- When it is determined that the certificate is valid and meets the requirements for granting the privilege - "Approved".

- If the validity of the certificate is not confirmed or does not meet the requirements for granting the privilege - "Not approved".

In this case, for applicants whose certificate is not approved, the reason why the certificate was not approved

To date, more than 6,000 certificates have been checked by the State Testing Center, and more than 450 certificates have been found not to meet the requirements.

⚠️ Fake certificates are also being detected during the inspection process, applicants who falsified certificates will not be allowed to take the tests and will be dealt with according to the law.
