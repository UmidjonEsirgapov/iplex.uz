Japan: Fully and partially funded scholarship program to study at Kyoto University

Summary:
KUAS - Japan's Kyoto University offers the SUPER KUAS-E and KUAS-E grants to those who wish to study engineering at the bachelor's degree. possible.
Requirements: Candidates must have completed 12 years of compulsory education; Language certificate (IELTS 5.5/ TOEFLiBT 70); Must have excellent grades in physics and mathematics. Application: Applications are accepted online. provides grants. Those who want to study in the field of engineering can apply and be exempted from university contract fees and provided with a scholarship.

Benefits:

Students admitted to the program will be exempted from university fees for 4 years under the SUPER KUAS-E grant and will be provided with a stipend of JPY 1,200,000 per year. According to KUAS-E, 30%-50%-100% of the contract fee is exempted.

Requirements:

  • Candidates must have completed 12 years of compulsory education;
  • Language certificate (IELTS 5.5/TOEFLiBT 70);
  • Physics and must have excellent grades in mathematics.

Recipients of both grants must meet the mastery standards during the academic year. Otherwise, the grant may become invalid.
