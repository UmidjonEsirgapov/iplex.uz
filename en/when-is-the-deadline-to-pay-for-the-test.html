Important information on making payments during the registration process for entrance tests for higher education institutions is given below. It is important to know the terms and conditions of payment for participation in the tests.

Terms of payment for the test

Graduates of the current year

Entrance to the test is free for applicants who graduated from an educational institution this year. These applicants are exempted from paying for the test.

Graduates of previous years

Applicants who graduated from the educational institution in previous years must pay 170 thousand soums to take the test. This amount is equal to half the "minimalka" (minimum monthly salary).

Payment deadline

Applicants who have not made the payment are considered unregistered and will not be allowed to take the tests. Payment can be made in installments, but the full amount must be paid by June 25.

Payment can also be made on June 25, but payment must be made by 23:59.

Precautions

It is recommended not to leave the payment until the last date. The reason is that there is a risk of not being able to make a payment due to unexpected preventive works or server loads in the bank and online payment systems.

Persons exempted from payment

The following groups are exempted from payment:

  • Those who graduated from an educational institution this year.
  • Alumni of the Charity House and Children's Towns.
  • Persons on full state support.
  • Persons with disabilities.
  • Persons included in the single social protection register and their children.
  • Young people included in the "Youth register".

These persons have the right to participate in the entrance exams without payment. — Source
