What is the procedure for receiving benefits for dismissed employees?

Brief content:
In Uzbekistan, information was provided on the circumstances in which severance pay is granted to an employee.
According to it, in accordance with Article 173 of the Labor Code, severance pay is paid in the following cases when the employment contract is terminated: at the initiative of the employer, the contract with the exception of cancellation on grounds related to the employee's culpable actions (inaction); in the following cases that do not depend on the will of the parties: When the employee is called to military or alternative service; When the employee who previously performed the same job is reinstated to the previous job (for example, if the employee is fired illegally, the court reinstates him); Upon entry into legal force of the court decision on liquidation of the organization or termination of the activity of an individual entrepreneur who is an employer; When a deputy of the Legislative Chamber, as well as a member of the Senate who worked in the Senate on a permanent basis, returns to his previous position (work) due to the expiration of the term of office or the dissolution of the Legislative Chamber and the Senate; Exceptions are cases where the employer, who is a natural person, is recognized as incompetent in paragraphs 1-4.
Among these cases, the established rules of employment are violated due to the fault of the employee (concealment of the court verdict on deprivation of the right to occupy certain positions or engage in certain activities, submission of false documents, etc.) or cases where the actions of the employee prevent the continuation of individual labor relations in accordance with the law.

Information was provided regarding the circumstances in which severance pay is granted to an employee in Uzbekistan.

According to it, in accordance with Article 173 of the Labor Code, when an employment contract is terminated, severance pay is paid in the following cases:

  • at the initiative of the employer, the contract is the fault of the employee. unless canceled on the grounds related to his actions (inaction);
  • in the following cases that do not depend on the will of the parties:
  1. When the employee is called up for military or alternative service;
  2. When the employee who previously performed the same job is reinstated (for example, if the employee is fired illegally, the court reinstates him);
  3. Dissolution of the organization or work when the decision of the court to terminate the individual entrepreneur activity of the employer enters into legal force;
  4. When the term of office of the deputy of the Legislative Chamber, as well as the member of the Senate who worked on a permanent basis in the Senate has expired or he returns to his previous position (work) due to the dissolution of the Legislative Chamber and the Senate;
  5. violation of the established rules on employment, if it is not possible to eliminate the committed violation and it prevents the continuation of the work;
  6. circumstances have arisen that prevent the continuation of labor relations in accordance with the law (the employee has been recognized as completely incapable of work according to the medical report issued in the prescribed manner, as well as the right to use state secrets has been revoked, if if the work being performed requires the right to use it, he has been deprived of a permit or license to perform certain work, etc.). others) or the actions of the employee lead to the occurrence of circumstances that prevent the continuation of individual labor relations in accordance with the law. or in connection with the lack of suitable work at the employer;
  7. in connection with the employee's refusal to move to another place together with the employer;
  8. in connection with the employee's refusal to continue working due to the change of ownership of the organization, its reorganization, departmental affiliation (subordination) change.

    • from 50 percent for employees with up to 3 years of service;
    • for 3 to 5 years of service - from 75 percent;
