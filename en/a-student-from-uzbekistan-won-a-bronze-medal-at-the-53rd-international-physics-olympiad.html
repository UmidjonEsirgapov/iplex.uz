A student of the Presidential School won a bronze medal at the International Physics Olympiad Javahir Sadullayev, a 10th-grade student of the Presidential School in Nukus, won a bronze medal at the 54th International Physics Olympiad held in Isfahan, Iran.
It should be noted that this It is the 4th bronze medal of Uzbek students at the International Physics Olympiad.
Members of our national team have been participating in this prestigious competition since 2015, and to date Umidjon Hafizov (2018), Rail Khashayev (2021) and Javahir Sultanov (2023) have also become bronze medalists.
