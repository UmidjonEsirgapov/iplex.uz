From the next academic year, all higher education institutions will independently determine the amount of the contract

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
In accordance with the Presidential Decree "On the parameters of the state order for admission to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan in the 2020/2021 academic year", the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education together with the relevant ministries and agencies by December 1, 2020 up to: - determining admission indicators based on state grants only in the parameters of the state order for admission to higher education institutions; - independent determination of admission indicators based on payment-contract by higher education institutions.
In this case, admission indicators based on payment-contract should not be less than the amount determined by the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan; - training talented students in non-governmental higher education organizations on the basis of a state grant; - submits reasoned proposals to the Government on the introduction of the procedure for passing tests on the subject of the mother tongue (Uzbek, Russian or Karakalpak language), mathematics and the history of Uzbekistan, along with professional (creative) exams for admission to undergraduate courses of education in which special talent is required.
