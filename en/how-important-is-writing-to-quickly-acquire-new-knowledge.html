How important is writing to quickly acquire new knowledge?

Write by hand According to research, writing by hand makes the brain stronger activates and helps to remember information better.

Many people think that new information can only be learned by reading or listening. But writing is one of the most effective ways of fixing information in the brain. According to research, knowledge written down is remembered for a long time and it is easier to repeat later.

Why is it important to write?

  1. The brain remembers betterIn the process of writing, the topic settles deeper in the brain, because we do not just read or listen, but we independently process the information.

  2. Thoughts fall into orderWriting makes it easier to get a deeper understanding of a topic and distinguish the main points. And this is very useful for remembering complex information.

  3. Repetition becomes easierLater revision of written information and recall is more effective. Things you read may be forgotten over time, but information written down is always at hand.

  4. FocusWriting makes us not distracted and helps us focus only on important information. This is especially useful when listening to a lesson or lecture.

How to write more effectively?

  • Write by handAccording to research, handwriting makes the brain more active and helps to remember information better.

  • Write in your own wordsIf you rewrite what you understand in your own words, you will understand the topic more deeply.

  • Write down important pointsHighlighting key words, expressing them in the form of headings or drawings will speed up the process of remembering.

  • Make short summariesWrite down the main points on each topic briefly is a very effective way to remember later.

Conclusion

In order to absorb new information faster and remember it for a long time, it is necessary to form the habit of writing. This is a simple, but extremely powerful method, which significantly increases efficiency in the educational process. — Read on infoedu.uz
