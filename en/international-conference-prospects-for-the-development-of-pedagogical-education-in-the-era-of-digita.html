About the conference

The purpose of the conference is to discuss modern perspectives of pedagogical education in the conditions of digital transformation, to discuss foreign and domestic experiences, to produce practical recommendations and to introduce innovative approaches in the field of education. The event brings together international and local experts, scientists, teachers and representatives of education management.

Scientific directions (conference affiliates)

  • Quality education is a guarantee of sustainable development: foreign experience and local practice.

  • Artificial intelligence and the transformation of human relations: personal success and development perspectives.

  • From inclusive education to inclusive society: strategies, experiences and modern educational technologies.

  • Using digital technologies in preschool education: strategic approaches, innovations and perspectives.

  • Current problems and solutions in language and literature education. (The official list of directions and a detailed description are given on the pages of ASR/Nordic).

Who can participate

Teachers, academic staff, masters and doctoral students, preschool education leaders, methodologists, researchers and practitioners in the field of pedagogy and educational technologies can participate in the conference. (Participants are required to submit an article/thesis on a topic appropriate to their category.)
