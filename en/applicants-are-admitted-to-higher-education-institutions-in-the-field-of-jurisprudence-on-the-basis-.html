Applicants are admitted to higher education institutions in the field of "jurisprudence" on the basis of an interview

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Applicants are now admitted to higher education institutions in the field of "jurisprudence" on the basis of an interview.
According to this decision, academic lyceums of Tashkent State Law University will now be established on the basis of legal technical schools.
It has been confirmed that their talented graduates will be admitted to higher educational institutions in the field of "jurisprudence" without tests on the basis of interviews.

Now applicants to higher education institutions in the field of "jurisprudence" adopted on the basis of discussion

The decision of the Cabinet of Ministers "On the introduction of a continuous system of legal education in state educational institutions and measures to increase the legal culture and literacy of minors" was announced. According to this decision, academic lyceums of Tashkent State Law University will be established on the basis of legal technical schools. It was confirmed that their talented graduates will be admitted to higher education institutions in the field of "jurisprudence" based on an interview without tests.

Also, the following points are defined in the decision:

  • The system of "school-lyceum-technical school-university" in the training of legal personnel through the organization of "Law class" and in-depth training of legal sciences in schools. Implementation
  • Continuous improvement of the professional knowledge and skills of law teachers through the special platform of the Ministry of Justice and establishment of a system of their methodical support
  • Holding the science Olympiad "Law Scholar" among students, the "Best Law Teacher" competition among law teachers
  • Aimed at the independent acquisition of legal knowledge by school students creation of visual training manuals, videos, computer games and mobile applications

Through this decision, it is aimed to make legal education more effective and continuous. The interview-based admission system serves to increase the quality of legal education. — Official Data
