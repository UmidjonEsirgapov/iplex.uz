Germany: Scholarship program with up to 50% funding from Aachen University for Master's degree programs

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The grant is awarded to high-performing students who want to study in one of the master's programs at the University of Aachen in Germany.
International students of all countries can apply for the grant.
Benefits of the grant: The scholarship program can cover up to 50% of the tuition fee; Requirements: To be eligible to apply for the scholarship program, candidates must be admitted to one of the following master's programs: MSc.

Brief about the university and the scholarship program:

RWTH Aachen University is one of the best public universities located in Aachen, Germany. It ranks #145 in the ranking of international universities published by QS Global World Rankings.

The High Potential Students presented by the University Grant's grant program provides financial incentives to high-performing international students. The scholarship is awarded to high-performing students who wish to study in one of the master's programs at the University of Aachen, Germany. It should be noted that all international students with high academic performance can apply for this grant.

Who can apply for the grant?

  • All international students can apply for the grant.

Benefits of the grant:

  • The grant program covers up to 50% of the tuition fee. may grant;

Requirements:

To be eligible to apply for the grant program, candidates must be accepted into one of the following graduate programs:

  • MSc. Computer-Aided Conception and Production in Mechanical Engineering;
  • MSc. Management and Engineering in Computer-Aided Mechanical Engineering;
  • MSc. Management and Engineering in Structural Engineering and Risk Management of Industrial Facilities;
  • MSc. Management and Engineering in Production Systems;
  • MSc. Networked Production Engineering;
  • MSc. Robotic Systems Engineering;
  • MSc. Textile Engineering.

Note: Candidates must not be receiving financial support from any other grant programs and must not be studying in any other programs in Germany at the time of applying for this grant.

Application Process:

Candidates must first apply to one of the master programs listed above here. After that, they will be able to submit a separate online application for the grant program here.

Required documents:

  1. Filled-out application for the master's program through the RWTH online application portal;
  2. Bachelor's diploma obtained with high marks;
  3. The candidate is among the top 10% of bachelor's degree holders in his field. admission certificate or alternatively a GPA of 1.7 or higher (if transferred to the German grading system);
  4. Motivation letter (500 words).

Language requirements:

Candidates must provide one of the following English language proficiency certificates:

  • TOEFL PBT – 550
  • TOEFL IBT – 90
  • IELTS – 5.5
  • DSH Certificate – Stage 2
  • Cambridge Certificate in Advanced English (CAE) – Pass
  • German Language Diploma – Present
  • TestDaF – Level 4
  • First Certificate in English – Grade B
