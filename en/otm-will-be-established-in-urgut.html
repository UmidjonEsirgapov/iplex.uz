OTM will be established in Urgut

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the governor of Samarkand region on July 1, 2020, a branch of Samarkand State University will be established in place of the college of economics and service in Urgut district.
According to the admission commission of Samarkand State University, the branch in Urgut It will accept students from the 2021-2022 academic year.
Until then, the college allocated for the branch will be renovated and equipped in accordance with higher education standards.

According to the decision of the governor of Samarkand region on July 1, 2020, a branch of Samarkand State University will be established in place of the vocational college of economics and service in Urgut district.

According to the admission commission of Samarkand State University, the branch in Urgut will accept students from the 2021-2022 academic year. Until then, the college allocated for the branch will be renovated and equipped in accordance with the standards of higher education.

We remind you that Urgut district is the 1st in the country in terms of population and about 600,000 people live here. Despite the large population, the result of the youth of this district entering the higher education institution is the lowest in the country.

Also, according to the decision, the building and facilities where the 3rd academic lyceum of the Samarkand State University, which operated in the Urgut district, is located, will be allocated for the establishment of a school for gifted students specialized in specific sciences under the university. — More information
