Can a student also apply as an applicant while transferring studies?

Many students are in the process of transferring their studies to another university (perevod), at the same time as an applicant. he thinks about whether he can apply to another OTM. This article provides a clear answer to this question and practical advice.

Terms and procedures for applying as an applicant

If you want to transfer your studies to another university, this does not mean that you do not have the right to apply as an applicant. You can easily submit a document in the following order:

  • Duration: June 5 - June 25
  • Site: my.uzbmb.uz
  • Required document: Just enter the diploma or certificate number

If you submitted this document to your previous university and if you don't know the exact number, you can enter it roughly - it won't cause problems.

Process of applying for Perevod

In order to transfer your studies to another OTM (perevod), you need to follow these steps:

  • Beginning: from July 15
  • Submission via the site
  • Required document: It is enough to upload the transcript

These processes are independent, one does not give rise to the other.

Tests and the possibility of selection

✅ Applicants and students who are applying for the exam take the tests at different times. Therefore, it is possible to participate in both processes and participate in the exams.

If you are successful in both processes, you can choose your desired course later.

Important note

❗️It is not prohibited to apply as an applicant and through perevod at the same time, and it will not bring you any negative consequences.

❌ However, it is not possible to study in two universities at the same time. Therefore, in the end, you have to make one choice.

Conclusion

A student who wants to transfer his studies to another university can submit an application as an applicant at the same time. Both processes are carried out independently and do not interfere with each other. The important thing is that you can take advantage of both options, and if you succeed in the tests, you can ultimately choose the path that suits you best.
