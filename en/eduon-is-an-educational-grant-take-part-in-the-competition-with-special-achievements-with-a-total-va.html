How to register?

Applicants who want to participate in the independent contest "EduON - educational grant" they register on the system of the OLIYGOH.UZ platform and familiarize themselves with the conditions of the selection test in advance. Contestants should pay attention to filling in their personal information correctly and accurately during registration. A special code will be sent to each candidate's phone number during the registration process. With this code, the participant's "Personal cabinet" is activated.

Selection stages

Selection stages are evaluated and sorted through a general evaluation system, taking into account the interests of young people. This Scholarship competition is organized on the basis of the following stages, taking into account the interests of young people in education and business:

The first stage (in the form of a test-examination) the candidates participating in the competition take an online exam on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform at the national level

Type of exam: IQ (logical analysis) 30 test questions about

Duration: The 1st stage of the Olympiad will start on August 27 at 20:00 and the exam window will be closed for everyone at 21:30. All candidates registered during this period are required to take part in the exam. After the completion of the 1st stage, a general mandate will be formed, and 2000 (two thousand) participants with the highest results will be transferred to the 2nd stage.*

The address of the first stage: OLIYGOH.UZ platform

* 2000 (two thousand) participants selected from the 1st stage will go to the second stage passes

Second stage (in the form of a test-examination) Candidates participating in the competition will take an online exam in the form of a test on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform, and candidates with the highest score will receive a ticket to the final stage.

Exam type: 25 basic-level business test questions

Time: The 2nd stage of the Olympiad starts on September 3rd at 20:00 and the exam window closes for everyone at 21:30. During this time, participants are required to take part in the exam. After the end of the 2nd stage, the mandate will be formed and 300 (three hundred) participants with the highest results will get a ticket to the final (3rd stage).

Third stage (Written form) Motivational letter.

They write a special motivational letter about why they deserve to win the "EduOn - educational grant" competition and goals they set for themselves. The letter will be sent by the participants of the final stage through the specially opened FINAL window on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform until September 8, 21:00. Sent motivational letters are examined by specialists and the 100 most eligible participants will be selected at this stage and will receive special achievements. The results of the final stage will be announced on September 10 on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform.

The absolute winners of the final stage will be solemnly announced on September 12 at "Youth Forum", which will be held in the hall of special events of "Mfaktor" business school. will be awarded.
