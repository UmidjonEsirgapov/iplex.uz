The effectiveness of the educational system at the Tashkent University of Architecture and Construction will be increased

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In this system: for 1st-year students: 60% of theoretical classes are held in university auditoriums, 40% of practical classes are held in construction enterprises. strengthens it and serves to prepare personnel suitable for the modern labor market.
Dual education, orientation to the field from school and unification of science and production through the UniLab platform - these changes will be an important stage in the development of the education system of Uzbekistan.

According to the decision of the President No. PQ-209, a form of dual education will be introduced at Tashkent University of Architecture and Construction starting from the 2025/2026 academic year. In this system:

  • For 1st-year students:

    • 60% of theoretical classes are held in university auditoriums

    • 40% of practical classes are held at construction enterprises.

  • For 2-4-year students:

    • 40% of theoretical education

    • 60% of practical education is carried out in production.

This method strengthens the organic connection between education and production.

Training of personnel based on the principle of "school - technical school - university"

as an experiment from September 1, 2025 The principle of "school - technical school (lyceum) - university" will be introduced.

  • Schools No. 250 and 273 in the Yunusabad district of Tashkent city will be attached to the University.

  • Every month, the University organizes classes for students of grades 7-9 according to their interests in the field.

  • Lessons will be held in university laboratories as extracurricular activities.

The integration of science and production will be strengthened

On the basis of the new system, "University-laboratory-enterprise-ministry" ecosystem will be established. Through this ecosystem:

  • Every year, the 20 best ideas competition is held among students and researchers;

  • Selected scientific research is put into practice;

  • Their authors receive 35 times BHM will be encouraged.

Laboratories will be united through the UniLab platform

From September 1, 2025, all laboratories in the field of architecture and construction in the country will be coordinated through the UniLab platform.

This system will make it possible to manage scientific research, technological experiments and practical developments on a single platform.

Conclusion

Reforms implemented at the Tashkent University of Architecture and Construction strengthen the connection between education and practice and serve to train personnel suitable for the modern labor market.
