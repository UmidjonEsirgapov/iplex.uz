Additional opportunities are given to those who want to study in colleges and technical schools

Taking into account the requests of parents and applicants, as well as in order to support young people, Additional opportunities for admission to colleges and technical schools for the 2024/2025 academic year are provided. To do this, it is necessary to apply online or directly to the educational institution through the site my.uzbmb.uz before September 25.

The following opportunities are provided:

  • Applicants to colleges and technical schools with vacancies can choose a suitable profession based on the test results in two specializations.
  • Applicants change the language of education and choose appropriate professions and specialties of colleges and technical schools.
  • Dual education applicants are admitted directly to colleges and technical schools based on an agreement with employers.
  • Applicants who have not registered through the My.uzbmb.uz electronic platform are accepted to study on the basis of a fee-contract through written exams in the form of evening or part-time education.

Attention! For full-time education of colleges/technical institutes, it is necessary to apply online through the website my.uzbmb.uz, and for evening and part-time education, apply directly to the educational institution.

At the same time, the educational process for the first courses of colleges and technical institutes will begin on October 2, 2024. — Read on infoedu.uz
