List of examinations that may be considered in awarding a bonus from the director's fund

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In accordance with the letter of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated January 13, 2025, the following examinations, competitions and Science Olympiads: Basic Science Olympiad International Mathematics Olympiad (IMO) International Physics Olympiad (IPhO) International Chemistry Olympiad (IChO) International Biology Olympiad (IBO) International Informatics Olympiad (IOI) Al-Khorazmi International Mathematics and Informatics Olympiad Ahmad al-Farghani International Physics Olympiad European Girls Mathematics Olympiad (EGMO) Asian Physics Olympiad (APhO) and Others.
Competitions: "Captain of the Year" competition "Rainbow Scholars" competition "Zakovat" intellectual game "Festival of books" competition "Fun and clever" tournament "I'm against drugs" competition "Third renaissance-in the eyes of young people" project competition and others.
Sports competitions: Judo Ministerial Cup, Gymnastics sports competitions, wrestling competitions under the slogan "Wrestling is our national pride", "Umid Nihollar" sports competitions, Cybersports, drone, robotics competitions, etc.

According to the letter of the Ministry of Preschool and School Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated January 13, 2025, For the 2025/2026 academic year, the following examinations, competitions and events are evaluated when setting monthly increases to the basic tariff rates of teachers in general education institutions:

Sciences:

  1. Basic Science Olympiad
  2. International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO)
  3. International Physics Olympiad (IPhO)
  4. International Chemistry Olympiad (IChO)
  5. International Biology Olympiad (IBO)
  6. International Informatics Olympiad (IOI)
  7. Al-Khorazmi International Mathematics and Informatics Olympiad
  8. Ahmad al-Farghani International Physics Olympiad
  9. European Girls Mathematics Olympiad (EGMO)
  10. Asia Physics Olympiad (APhO) and others.

Competitions:

  1. Competition "Captain of the Year"
  2. Competition "Rainbow Scholars"
  3. Intellectual game "Ingenuity"
  4. Competition "Festival of Books"
  5. "Fun and clever" tournament
  6. "I am against drug addiction" competition
  7. "Third Renaissance-in the eyes of young people" project competition and others.

Sports competitions:

  1. Ministerial Cup in "Judo"
  2. "Gymnastics" sports competitions
  3. "Wrestling is our national pride" under the motto of wrestling sports competitions
  4. "Sprouts of Hope" sports competitions
  5. Cyber sports, drone, robotics competitions, etc.

For writers and talented young people:

  1. "Zulfia" state award
  2. "Five initiative Olympiad" sports competitions
  3. "Human rights" contest
  4. Competition of creative fairy tales called "Gifts of Nature" and many other competitions.

Based on the participation and results of teachers in these contests and competitions, bonuses may be set for them.

Source: Cabinet of Ministers Resolution No. 823 of September 30, 2019 — More information
