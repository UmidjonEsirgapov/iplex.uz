About the minimum score and the super-contract procedure

One of the most frequently asked questions among applicants is whether those who could not score the minimum score can also become a student through the "super-contract" can they be?

According to the rules of the previous years, if the applicant scored less than the minimum 56.7 points, he was granted the right to be a student by paying 2 times the highest contract amount set for his field.

As an example

For example, in 2022 in the field of jurisprudence, the amount of the super-contract is set at 220.7 million soums. An applicant who scored less than the minimum score had the opportunity to become a student by paying double of this amount (that is, about 441 million soums).

What is the situation in 2025?

So far, the "super-contract" procedure for this year has not been officially approved. Therefore, applicants will be able to find out the final decision after the Decision of the Cabinet is announced.

Conclusion
