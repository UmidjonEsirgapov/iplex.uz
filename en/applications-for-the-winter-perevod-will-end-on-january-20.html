Applications for the winter "perevod" will end on January 20

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Not everyone can apply for the winter "perevod".
Only those with 2 good reasons are allowed.
The following are considered good reasons: in connection with the student starting a family, to transfer his studies to the OTM in the area where his spouse lives permanently; employees of state bodies to transfer the education of their spouse or minor child in case of a change of workplace (based on the recommendation of the head of the relevant ministry (department)). Only those who have 2 good reasons are allowed.

The following are considered good reasons:

  1. to transfer the student's studies to the OTM in the area where the spouse permanently lives in connection with starting a family;
  2. to transfer the studies of the spouse or minor child in the event of a change of state employees' place of work (relevant ministry based on the recommendation of the (department) head).

Applications should be submitted to the regional working groups (addresses and phone numbers are given in the photo above).

The following documents are submitted to the regional working groups:

  • an application for transfer of studies (the application form is issued by the regional working group and filled out by the student on the spot);
  • the student's rating book or the prescribed form copies of academic references or transcripts (one is enough);
  • copy of passport;
  • relevant documents justifying the reasons mentioned above.

Applications for transferring studies from abroad or non-state HEIs in Uzbekistan to the state HEIs in Uzbekistan are currently not accepted. They only apply online and take the test during the summer months. — More information
