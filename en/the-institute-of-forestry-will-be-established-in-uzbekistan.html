The proposal of the State Forestry Committee, the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, the Ministry of Innovative Development to establish a Forestry Institute within the university on the basis of the Faculty of Forestry and Landscape Design of Tashkent State Agrarian University starting from the 2020/2021 academic year.

The following The main tasks of the institute are defined as:

  • effective use of forestry land, development and propagation of forest selection and seed production, planting, protection and use of forests, scientific research and experimental tests, and training of highly qualified personnel;
  • advanced on the basis of foreign experiences, to gradually transfer the curricula of the educational directions and specialties of the Institute to the credit-module system of education;
  • modernization of the educational laboratory base, reflection of knowledge on modern production processes in the field, high-performance and resource-saving advanced technologies in science programs; wide introduction of new interactive methods that lead to innovative and new thinking, including mechanisms that ensure students' independent research;
  • organization of training and internships for professors-teachers, scientific and scientific-pedagogical personnel in advanced foreign research and higher education institutions;
  • initiative of professors-teachers, researchers, students in creating and introducing innovative technologies and in order to increase their material interest, to establish "Small Innovative Enterprises" on the basis of the conditions of public-private partnership;
  • organization of seminars-trainings and information-consulting services for forestry leaders and experts of clusters, coordination of practice and scientific research work of bachelor, master, basic doctoral students and doctoral students;
  • participation in the selection of projects carried out within the framework of state scientific and technical programs and grants of international organizations, current topics related to the field implementation of scientific-research works;
  • development of modern agro-technological developments of forest establishment and cultivation;
  • formation of practical skills for growing seeds and seedlings of forest trees and shrubs free from diseases and pests (in vitro method), and wide application to production.

Institute of forestry It is a higher education institution within the framework of the Tashkent State Agrarian University that prepares bachelors, masters and scientific and pedagogical personnel in the relevant educational fields and specialties, taking into account the personnel needs of the fields and economic sectors.

The institute will receive the republican higher education, which is approved annually, starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, based on the personnel needs of forestry and economic sectors. In order to provide highly qualified personnel for forestry in Karakalpakstan and its regions, the institute will, based on the recommendation of the chairman of the State Committee on Forestry, offer bachelor's degree programs for foresters and their children in the relevant bachelor's education fields of higher education institutions. an additional 20 percent will be allocated to the admission quota set on the basis of the grant. — Details here
