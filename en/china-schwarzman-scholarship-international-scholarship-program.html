For whom? Students between the ages of 18 and 29 or graduates with a bachelor's degree
Deadline: September 22, 2021

This annual scholarship was established in 2016 by American financier Stephen Schwarzman and covers all expenses.

The purpose of the program: To cultivate business, political, and civil society leaders who will be a bridge of mutual solidarity between China and other countries.

Candidates must go through 2 stages: online application submission and direct interview. At the same time, they must be fluent in English and have a high level of academic research.

The scholarship covers:
- tuition fees;
- accommodation and meals;
- transportation to and from Beijing;
- necessary textbooks and materials;
- Lenovo laptop;
- health insurance;
- $4,000 for personal needs. scholarship.
