7-year interest-free education loan for women

In order to issue a preferential educational loan, the following is required: an application to a commercial bank for obtaining a preferential educational loan; identity document of the borrower; the payment-contract agreement of the educational institution; family members (parents, brothers, sisters, spouse) of the borrower who have a permanent source of income or a third party together with the borrower, a document on the provision of preferential educational loan repayment in accordance with the credit policy and internal procedure of the commercial bank. In addition, taking into account the suggestions made by many citizens, according to the decision, professional educational institutions and educational institutions Preferential educational loans are being allocated for part-time and evening students as well.
According to the decision, funds for loans in 2022 will be from the State budget and state trust funds, loans from international financial institutions and foreign government financial organizations involved in supporting the budget, issuance of sovereign international bonds and other sources not prohibited by legislation.

On July 18, the president signed the decision "On measures to support the education of women in higher, secondary special and professional educational organizations". The text of the document was published in UzA.

According to it, starting from the 2022/2023 academic year, a new system of allocating educational loans from the state budget on preferential terms will be introduced in all private, foreign and state higher, secondary special and professional educational organizations in the form of full-time, part-time and evening education.

Commercial banks allocate educational loans at a rate of 14 percent for a period of 7 years at the expense of deposit funds placed by the Ministry of Finance. In this case, reduction of interest rates of the loan will be made from the funds of the Education Loan Funding Fund of the Ministry of Finance, by paying them to commercial banks.

According to the press service of the Ministry of Finance, female students will not pay interest on the loan. Only the main part of the loan will have to be paid for 7 years, starting from the seventh month after the end of the official study period.

President Shavkat Mirziyoyev informed about this procedure during the holiday greetings held on March 8.

The following are required to formalize a preferential education loan:

  • obtain a preferential education loan application to a commercial bank;
  • document confirming the identity of the borrower;
  • payment-contract agreement of the educational institution;
  • members of the borrower's family with a permanent source of income (parents, brothers, sisters, spouse) or a third party together with the borrower in accordance with the credit policy and internal procedure of the preferential educational loan repayment of the commercial bank document on provision.

In this case, no collateral or guarantor is required for the preferential education loan allocated to the women of the families included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection".
