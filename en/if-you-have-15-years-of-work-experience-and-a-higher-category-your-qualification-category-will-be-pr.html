If you have 15 years of work experience and a higher category, your qualification category will be preserved for life

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The qualification category of high-class teachers who have 15 years of continuous work experience will be preserved for life and will not participate in attestation. Pedagogical personnel of the category (in the position of head teacher) will not be involved in the next mandatory attestations of this year.
Amendments to the Regulation on the Procedure for Certification of Pedagogical Personnel by Decision No. 425 of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated August 2, 2022 have 15 years of continuous work experience until August 2, 2022 and that it is not applied to pedagogues with a higher category (the period of obtaining a higher category is not important) and on this issue the letter of the Ministry of Public Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 03-03/1-4619 dated September 12, 2022 is still in force. the qualification category will be preserved for life and they will not participate in the attestation.

In 2022, pedagogic personnel of the higher qualification category (in the position of head teacher) who are exempted from regular mandatory attestation due to having 15 years of continuous work experience in educational organizations will not be involved in the regular mandatory attestation of this year.
