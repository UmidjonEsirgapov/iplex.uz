Most complaints about forced voting come from schools

During the voting phase, citizens have received a total of 39 complaints about cases of forced voting to the hotline number "1282". Now, in the "Open budget", the projects that have received votes through coercion will be removed from the register.

In this case, the regions with the most appeals:

  • Fergana region - 7;
  • Tashkent - 6;
  • Namangan and Jizzakh regions - 5 each.

In terms of sectors:

  • General education schools - 19;
  • Medical organizations - 16.

In a complaint received by a group of teachers of the 17th school in the city of Jizzakh, candidates who want to start a new job are first voted for an order was issued that he can submit his documents after his arrival. Also, the teachers who left the decree were informed that they will collect votes when they come to work, and then they will consider the case.

We remind you that all received appeals will be sent to the Councils of People's Deputies of the relevant district (city) for consideration. If the situation is confirmed, these projects may be removed from the voting stage or removed from the list of winners. — Read on infoedu.uz
