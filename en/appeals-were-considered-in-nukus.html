On September 6-7, 2024 in Nukus city, appeals were filed by applicants who were dissatisfied with the results of the tests for entering and transferring studies. These appeals were considered by the members of the appeal commission and the results were announced.

A total of 446 applicants were planned to participate in the appeal process. Of these, only 91 applicants (20.4%) participated in the process to express their dissatisfaction, and the remaining 355 did not participate in the process.
