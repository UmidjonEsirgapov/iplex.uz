The form of part-time education in pedagogy is closed!

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the decision PQ-289 of June 21, 2022, from the admission of the 2023/2024 academic year, part-time education of higher education institutions training pedagogical personnel in the educational directions specified in Appendix 1 to this decision admission to the form is not carried out.
Including; 60110600–Mathematics and informatics 60110700–Physics and astronomy 60110800–Chemistry 60110900–Biology 60111000–Basics of geography and economic knowledge 60111400–Uzbek language and literature 60111500–Native language and literature (by languages) 60111600-Uzbek language in foreign-language groups 60111700-Russian language in foreign-language groups 60111800-Foreign language and literature (by languages) 60112000-Military education before conscription 60112500-School management 60112600-Preschool and primary foreign language in education (by languages) 60112700-Special pedagogy (by types of activity) is not accepted for part-time education. Individuals with at least five years of experience in pedagogical activities in the educational system are accepted for part-time study in the fields of education in the field of pedagogy not specified in Appendix 1 to this decision.
The fact that persons who wish to study in the field of pedagogy by correspondence education have at least five years of pedagogical work experience in the educational system is determined based on the information in the interdepartmental software-hardware complex "Uniform National Labor System". the higher education institutions training pedagogical personnel will not be accepted for part-time education according to the educational directions indicated in the 1st appendix to this decision. Including;
  1. 60110600–Mathematics and informatics
  2. 60110700–Physics and astronomy
  3. 60110800–Chemistry
  4. 60110900–Biology
  5. 60111000–Basics of geography and economic knowledge
  6. 60111400–Uzbek language and literature
  7. 60111500–Mother language and literature (by languages)
  8. 60111600–Uzbek language in foreign language groups
  9. 60111700–Russian language in foreign language groups
  10. 60111800–Foreign language and literature (by languages)
  11. 60112000-Military education before conscription
  12. 60112500-School management
  13. 60112600-Foreign language in pre-school and primary education (by languages)
  14. 60112700-Special pedagogy (by types of activities) cannot be accepted as part-time education

Persons with at least five years of work experience related to pedagogical activities in the educational system are accepted to study in the fields of education in the field of pedagogy not specified in Annex 1 to this decision. the length of service is determined on the basis of information in the "Uniform National Labor System" interdepartmental software-hardware complex.
