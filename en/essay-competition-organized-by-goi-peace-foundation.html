"What is life?" international essay contest.

Deadline: June 15, 2022.
Participants: children and youth under 25 from around the world.
Prizes: 100,000 yen, 50,000 yen, certificates and prizes.

Goi Peace Foundation by "What is Life?" in Japan international essays are organized. Children and youth under 25 years of age from all over the world are invited to participate.

Essays on the topic "My values" are accepted.

Our values ​​determine how we think and act and shape the society in which we live. What values ​​are dear to you?

The essay should not exceed 700 words in English, French, Spanish, or German or 1,600 characters in Japanese, excluding the essay title and cover page. The essay must be original and unpublished. Multiple applications are accepted from one participant. Children under the age of 14 and young people between the ages of 15 and 25 will be evaluated separately.

Essays in DOC, DOCX or PDF format, in the form of a single file containing the author's name and title, can be uploaded from March 1 through the official page of the competition.
