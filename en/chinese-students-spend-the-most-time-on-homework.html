Chinese students spend the most time on homework

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
Chinese students spend the most time on homework among students in the world.
According to statistics, teenagers in China do homework for an average of 14 hours a week.
Why Chinese students spend a lot of time on homework?

Among the students in the world, those who spend the most time on homework are Chinese students. According to statistics, teenagers in China spend an average of 14 hours on homework per week.

Why do Chinese students spend a lot of time on homework?

  • High educational standards: Students in China prioritize academic success.
  • Family pressure: Parents they constantly monitor their children's good studies.
  • Preparation processes: More time is allocated for preparing for tests and additional activities.

Interesting fact

Students in other countries spend much less time on homework:

  • In the USA, the average is 6-8 hours per week.
  • And in Europe, this figure is 4-6 hours per week.

Conclusion

The fact that Chinese students spend a lot of time on homework shows their high sense of responsibility and serious approach to education. If your students are dying to do their homework, this information can be a good motivation for them! — Official Data
