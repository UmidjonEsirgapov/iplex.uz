Women receive education in colleges and technical schools in a number of fields on the basis of a state grant

Government Decision No. 516 (29.09.2023) "On measures to support women who wish to acquire a profession (specialty) in colleges and technical schools"
According to the decision, starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, women who wish to acquire a profession (specialty) in construction, transport, communal, agricultural and service fields will receive education on the basis of a state grant. As a distributor of budget funds from the republican budget, it is carried out within the funds allocated to the ministries and agencies that have colleges and technical institutes in their system: ➖ and starting from 2024, it is provided for in the formation of the parameters of the State budget.
