Those studying psychology can enter the Armed Forces Academy

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, the system of training officers in the field of education "Psychology (military psychologist)" was launched at the Academy of Armed Forces.
In this system, higher education institutions Candidates will be selected from among students who have successfully completed the 2nd stage of psychology education, who wish to continue their studies at the Academy of Armed Forces and do military service on the basis of a contract in the future, who are fit for military service according to their health, and who are under 26 years old.
Candidates do not have to have completed military service.

Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, the Armed Forces Academy has launched a system of training officers in the "Psychology (military psychologist)" field of study. Candidates will be selected from among the students under 26 years of age who are fit for military service due to their health and want to continue military service on the basis of a contract in the future. Applicants are not required to have completed military service.

Applicants are trained on a 2-year program and graduates are awarded a bachelor's degree. — Source
