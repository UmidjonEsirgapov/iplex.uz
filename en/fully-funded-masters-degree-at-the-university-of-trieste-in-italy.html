General information:

Currently, the International Center for Theoretical Physics (ICTP) and the University of Trieste, which is considered one of the most prestigious universities in Italy, are full of students who want to study in the field of Medical Physics. an opportunity to study under financing was created. Students will spend their first year at the ICTP and their second year at the Medical Physics Department of the Trieste Hospital. Candidates must have at least a master's degree in the above-mentioned field or a related field. 30 candidates will be accepted.

Sponsors of this program: International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), International Organization of Medical Physics (IOMP) and European Federation of Medical Physics (EFOMP) Trieste University Hospital.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Candidates must have a master's degree or equivalent in medical physics or a related field required;
  • Excellent academic university grades, research projects and professional experience in physics are required;
  • Sufficient command of the English language;
  • Applicants must be under 30 years of age;
  • In addition, applicants must be citizens of United Nations, UNESCO or IAEA member countries (including Uzbekistan);
  • Candidates should be willing to participate in all activities at ICTP in their respective fields;
