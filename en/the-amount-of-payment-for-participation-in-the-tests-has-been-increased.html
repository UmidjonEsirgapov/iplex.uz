The amount of the fee for participating in the tests has been increased

The amount of the fee for participating in the entrance exams to higher education institutions and transfer (perevod) tests in 2025 has been increased. Now applicants and "drivers" will have to pay 206,000 soums.

How much is the new fee?

  • Last year, the fee for taking part in the tests was 187,500 soums. This year, this amount is 206,000 soums. This change is primarily due to the increase of BHM (Basic calculation amount).

Dependence of BHM and payment amount

According to the current regulations, the amount of payment for participation in tests is 50% of BHM.

  • BHM in 2024 - 370,000 soums -> Payment – 187,500 soums

  • BHM in 2025 – 412,000 soums -> Fee - 206,000 soums

So, due to the increase in BHM, the fee for the test has also increased accordingly.

Who will be exempted from the test fee?

According to the current legislation, the following categories of applicants will be exempted from the fee for the test:

✅ Reading test Graduates of schools, lyceums and colleges who completed the current year

✅ People with disabilities

✅ Children of the orphanage

✅ Graduates of children's town

✅ Those registered in the "Unified Register of Social Protection" and their children

✅ Young people listed in the "Youth Register"

These benefits It is determined taking into account the social status of the applicants.
