Teachers were given the opportunity to get cars on preferential terms

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
The Prime Minister of the Republic of Uzbekistan Abdulla Aripov made a decision to create special preferential conditions for teachers and pedagogues within the framework of the "Muallim ezozda" action.
According to this decision, the teachers will be given 5 years of cars such as Malibu, Tracker, Onix, Equinox, and Captiva 5T. It is given with the condition of paying in installments.
Conditions of the promotion Cars are offered through commercial banks with an initial deposit of not more than 30%. According to this decision, cars such as Malibu, Tracker, Onix, Equinox, and Captiva 5T will be given to teachers with the condition of paying in installments for a period of 5 years. No more than 30 percent will be provided.
  • Payments will be made during a 60-month period.

    • The main purpose of the decision

    This project is aimed at supporting pedagogues working in the preschool and school education system and further increasing their status in society.

    Implementation process

    Preschool and school In the framework of this project proposed by the Ministry of Education, the necessary financial mechanisms for the purchase of cars will be organized in cooperation with "Uzavtosanoat" JSC and commercial banks.

    Expected results

    This campaign will serve to increase the role and value of pedagogues in society and will serve as an important step in improving their living standards.
