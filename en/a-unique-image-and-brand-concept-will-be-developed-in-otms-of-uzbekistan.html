A unique image and brand concept will be developed in OTMs of Uzbekistan

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
According to the new draft decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan, it is expected to develop a unique image and brand concept of each higher education institution (HEI) in the country.
Sales offices will be established for HEIs. cooperates with.
Choices and measures According to the draft decision: until November 2025, a competition will be announced for the logo, business card, anthem and visual identification of HEIs. is expected to be released. This project was published for public discussion on the regulation.adliya.uz portal.

What will be the brand and image concept of HEIs?

  • Until August 1, each HEI will develop a unique brand concept based on its historical development characteristics, social mission and contemporary requirements.

  • The logo and visual identity of HEIs will be created based on scientific, educational and innovative achievements.

  • In this framework, souvenirs, canstovar products, sportswear, toys and other paraphernalia will be produced.

Sales outlets will be established for HEIs

  • OTMs will cooperate with organizations that produce products displaying their logo and brand.

  • Each OTM is planning to establish sales outlets selling brand products.

Options and measures

According to the draft decision:

  • Until November 2025, a contest for the logo, business card, anthem and visual identity of the HEI will be announced.

  • Production of special clothing and souvenir products with the logo for students, professors and employees is set.

This initiative creates a unique brand of each HEI and popularizes it. aimed at strengthening the image of higher education institutions. — Read on infoedu.uz
