Linguaskill and Tömer certificates obtained before the new order are taken into account

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The list of relevant national or international certificates on the level of foreign language knowledge is in accordance with the decision of the State Commission and in agreement with the Agency for Popularization of Foreign Language Learning by the order of the Ministry No. 220 of June 30, 2022 signed.
It is known that in accordance with the administrative reforms, according to the decree of the President No. 269 (https://lex.uz/docs/6324756), the Agency for Popularization of Foreign Language Learning was terminated, and its respective functions were transferred to the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.
Therefore, in 2023, the Ministry According to Order No. 65 dated March 23, the list of national or equivalent international certificates to be presented for participation in contests for master's majors and post-higher education majors of higher education institutions starting from the 2023/2024 academic year has been updated.

Yesterday on the Telegram channel "Alimoff" a post of Data-Journalist Nodirbek Nortorayev with the title "This post is not related to public procurement, but related to education" was shared, in which the author published the newly approved list of foreign language certificates required to participate in the master's and doctorate competition starting from the 2023/2024 academic year by order of the Ministry No. 65 Ministers He faced the question of whether it contradicts the decision of his court No. 395 of 2019.

The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation informs the following regarding this question.

In the material, he emphasized that the Linguaskill certificate was not canceled based on paragraph 1.g of the government decision. In this case, the author ignored other legal documents related to the issue, which led to its misinterpretation.

That is, in paragraph 1.g of the decision, applicants with a national or international certificate in a foreign language are exempted from the exam. However, by Presidential Decree No. 279, additional exams for admission to master's degrees were canceled starting from the 2022/23 academic year, and according to Decision No. PQ-5117, a language certificate was set as a criterion for submitting documents. agreed upon and signed by the Ministry's Order No. 220 of June 30, 2022.

For information, according to Cabinet of Ministers Decision No. 312, the approval of the list of internationally recognized certificates in foreign languages was assigned to the Agency for Popularization of Foreign Language Learning.

It is known that according to administrative reforms According to the decree of the President No. 269 (https://lex.uz/docs/6324756), the Agency for Popularization of Foreign Language Learning was abolished, and its respective functions were transferred to the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation. Starting from the 2023/2024 academic year, the list of national or equivalent international certificates to be presented for participation in contests for master's degrees and post-higher education specialties of higher education institutions has been updated. In turn, this list is reconfirmed every year by the order of the Ministry based on the opinion of experts.

In conclusion, admission to the master's degree is carried out according to the established procedure based on the decisions of the President and the Cabinet of Ministers. We inform you that expired national or equivalent international certificates specified in the list approved by order No. 220 of June 30 will be taken into account in the selection of master's specialties of higher education institutions for the 2023/2024 academic year.
