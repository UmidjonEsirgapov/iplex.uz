General Information:

Gates Cambridge Scholarship is fully funded by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation Scholarship for young people who want to study at the Masters and PhD levels at Cambridge University, one of the Top Universities in Great Britain. 2022 announced the grant program. Students from all over the world can apply for this scholarship. A total of 90 students will receive this grant. Candidates can apply for this scholarship for all courses at the University of Cambridge. This grant fully covers all expenses.

Benefits of the grant;

  • Full cost of study at Cambridge;
  • Covers university entrance fee;
  • Stipend for living expenses (one-time, $24,300 per year);
  • 1 economy class air ticket to and from;
  • All visa expenses;
  • Child allowance for families (up to $14,000 USD for the 1st child; up to $5,900 USD for the 2nd child);
  • Additional funds (for unforeseen, unexpected situations);

Note: Every year Grants will be awarded to 90 students (about 25 for US citizens and 55 for international candidates);

Duration of the program:

  • (PhD) doctoral stages - 3,4,5,7 year courses are available;
  • Master's stage - 1,2 years

For candidates requirements:

  • Must not be a citizen of Great Britain;
  • Must have applied for master's and doctoral degrees at Cambridge University;
  • Must have high academic grades;
  • Must be able to prove with strong reasons why you are worthy of this grant;

Required Documents;

  • Online application;
  • 500-word statement(Statement);
  • Research plan for (PhD) doctoral students;
  • 2 letters of recommendation;
