Applicants for academic lyceum may be allowed to choose up to three majors

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
It will be necessary to choose one suitable direction of the academic lyceum with the same language of instruction.
This was reported by the press service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation. the procedure for selecting and admitting 9th grade graduates of general secondary schools to academic lyceums; the procedure for organizing the activities of advisory councils in academic lyceums, accepting documents, conducting exams, formalizing and announcing results; applying benefits for winners of science Olympiads, contests and those who have a certificate of mastering general education subjects; The procedure for transfer, reinstatement and expulsion of academic lyceum students is determined.

In this case, it is necessary to choose one suitable direction of the academic lyceum with the same language of instruction. The draft decision on "improvement" was discussed. This was reported by the press service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

Accordingly, when registering, applicants will be given the opportunity to choose one suitable course from up to three academic lyceums with the same language of instruction. for admission to lyceums, the winners of international and republican science olympiads, applicants with a foreign language certificate are given preferences.

According to the regulation, which is intended to be approved by the decision:

  • the procedure for selecting and admitting 9th grade graduates of general secondary education schools to academic lyceums;
  • consulting councils at academic lyceums the procedure for organizing activities, accepting documents, conducting exams, formalizing and announcing the results;
  • applying benefits for science olympiads, competition winners and those who have mastered general education subjects;
  • the procedure for transfer, reinstatement and expulsion of academic lyceum students is determined.
