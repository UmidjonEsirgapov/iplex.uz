Telegram-bot for students of "HEMIS" information system has been developed. This was reported by the press service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

Through it, students can receive the following information related to the educational process:

  • rating book and transcript;
  • lesson schedules;
  • mastery indicators;
  • tasks in subjects;
  • electronic resources of subjects.

To use the bot, first select a university and then enter the user's login and password in "HEMIS". — Details here
