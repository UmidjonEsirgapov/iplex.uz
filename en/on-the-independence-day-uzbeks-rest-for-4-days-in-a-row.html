On the Independence Day, Uzbeks will rest for 4 days in a row

According to the decree of the President, on the occasion of the Independence Day, 4 consecutive days of rest have been set for Uzbeks. On this day:

  • Saturday, August 31 — an additional day off for employees on a 6-day schedule, a regular day off for employees on a 5-day schedule;
  • Sunday, September 1 — Independence Day;
  • Monday, September 2 — a day off for all (Labor Code According to Article 208, the day off will be moved to Monday due to the fact that the holiday falls on a Sunday);
  • Tuesday, September 3 is an additional day off for everyone.

At the same time, the new academic year begins on the following dates:

  • In schools - September 4;
  • In academic lyceums — September 4;
  • In vocational schools — September 4;
  • For second and higher years of colleges and technical schools — September 4;
  • In higher education institutions (for all courses) — September 9.

Since the admission for the 1st courses of colleges and technical schools is still ongoing, the start date of the new academic year is not yet clear. More information on this will be provided later. — Official Data
