The requirement of a foreign language certificate may be waived for admission to graduate and post-secondary education.

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Decision PQ-5117 dated May 19, 2021 by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On measures to bring the popularization of foreign language learning to a qualitatively new level" evaluation (TSTB) is being conducted.
Within the framework of TSTB, the impact on the population and educational institutions of the mandatory requirement that only persons with a language proficiency certificate participate in competitions for master's and post-higher education specialties and the payment of additional bonuses to teachers of foreign languages with such a certificate is being evaluated.
According to the results of the evaluation, it is intended Proposals for changing or canceling requirements and procedures that have not been achieved and are inefficient or ineffective will be developed. Decision No. PQ-5117 is undergoing a regulatory impact assessment (TSTB).
