Why is it not possible to transfer master's studies abroad to Uzbekistan?

Why is it not possible to transfer studies at the master's level abroad? In accordance with Article 232, it is established that it is not allowed to transfer the studies of students studying at the master's level of foreign higher education institutions to the higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Why is it not possible to transfer studies at the master's level abroad to our country?

Official answer from the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education:

- In accordance with paragraph 232 of the Regulation on the procedure for transferring, restoring and expelling students of higher education institutions It has been established that it is not allowed to transfer the studies of students studying at the master's level of higher education institutions to higher education institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

According to the Regulation on Master's Degree, approved by the Cabinet of Ministers' decision No. 36 of March 2, 2015, master's thesis topics and their attached scientific supervisors for master's degree students in the first two months of the first academic year will be approved.

The process of preparation of master's theses of master's students starts from the first semester of the first year. As a result of the transfer of studies of master's students, it is not possible for them to prepare master's dissertations at the level of the newly established requirements for the rest of the term.
