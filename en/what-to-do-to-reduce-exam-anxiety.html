Exam anxiety is a problem that many applicants and students face. Anxiety can negatively affect performance, so overcoming it is critical to success. Check out the 7 most effective tips for reducing exam stress below.

1. Prepare in advance

  • Good preparation is the key to confidence!
  • Plan and study all the topics before the exam.
  • Last minute preparation increases stress, so work according to the plan.

2. Use relaxation techniques

  • Do breathing exercises before the exam:
    • Take a deep breath and exhale slowly and repeat 5-6 times.
    • This method reduces nervousness and helps to concentrate.

3. Think positive

  • "I can do it!" - say this phrase to yourself.
  • Negative thoughts increase excitement, so believe in your own strength.
  • Success-oriented thinking improves your results.

4. Get enough sleep

  • Study late at night impedes the effective functioning of the brain.
  • 7-8 hours of quality sleep is necessary for good results on the exam day.
  • Insomnia reduces attention and memory.

5. Eat healthy

  • Have a light but nutritious breakfast before the exam.
  • Instead of fast food or sweets choose the following products:
    • Nuts, fish, eggs, dark chocolate - good for the brain.
    • Fruits and greens - gives energy.

6. Allocate time properly

  • Take your time, pay attention to each question.
  • Solving easy questions first will help you to allocate time effectively.
  • Coming back to difficult questions at the end will reduce stress.

7. Don't forget to drink water

  • Water helps the brain work and increases concentration.
  • Dehydration can increase fatigue and nervousness.
  • Drink a little water before and during the exam.

Conclusion

The exam is just a test! The secret to success is good preparation, stress management and following the right habits. Using these 7 tips, you can overcome exam anxiety and achieve high results. — Source
