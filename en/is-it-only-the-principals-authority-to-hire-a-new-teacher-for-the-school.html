Is it only the principal's authority to hire a new teacher for the school?

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The process of hiring a new teacher to the school is under the authority of the school director, who exercises this authority based on the Labor Code of the Republic of Uzbekistan. Regardless, it is the employer.
Article 24 stipulates that the employer has the right to conclude and cancel individual employment contracts in accordance with the law. The relevant articles of the Labor Code are explained as follows:

The right to employment based on the Labor Code

  1. According to Article 19 of the Labor Code, all organizations - regardless of the form of ownership - are employers. On this basis, schools are also considered employers' organizations.
  2. Article 24 specifies that, in accordance with the law, the right to conclude and cancel individual labor contracts belongs to the employer.
  3. Article 46 defines the recognition of an authorized person of the employer as the head of the organization. the grounds mean that recruitment of a new teacher to the school and his dismissal is under the authority of the school principal, who has the right to independently carry out this process. The director has the following rights when hiring employees:
    • Independent decision-making: The school director makes a decision taking into account the experience and skills of the teacher he hires.
    • Conclusion and termination of employment contracts: The director has the right to conclude or terminate employment contracts with teachers.
