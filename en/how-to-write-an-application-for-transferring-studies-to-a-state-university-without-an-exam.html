From non-state HEIs transfer of studies to a state university without an exam - on the basis of a super contract

Based on the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated June 15, 2022, PQ-279, it is now possible to transfer studies from non-state higher education institutions to state universities without an exam on the basis of a super contract. This procedure is opening new doors for many students.

Register online via my.uzbmb.uz website

The transfer process is carried out via https://my.uzbmb.uz website. Applicants can create a personal cabinet on this platform, upload the necessary documents and submit an application.

Important note so that your application is not rejected

Note to be written in the field "Reason for transfer of study"

During the application review, the Working Group must make sure that your decision is made consciously. In particular, those who have chosen an unrelated (i.e., a different field) field of study should write the following text completely and clearly in the "Reason for transfer of studies" section:

I (the applicant's F.I.Sh) to the field of study that I (the applicant's F.I.Sh) is studying in the non-state higher education institution (the name of the HEI to which the applicant is applying) I would like to transfer my studies to an unrelated field of study. I agree to study on the basis of a one-time payment equal to the minimum amount of the differentiated payment-contract in accordance with paragraph 7, subparagraph "g" of the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. PQ-279 of June 15, 2022. I am fully acquainted with the above decision of the President. With this in mind, I ask you to consider my application based on my consent and give practical help.

Why is this comment important?

Just by writing this text, you can prevent your application from being rejected. This means that you have applied on the basis of legal knowledge and consent.
