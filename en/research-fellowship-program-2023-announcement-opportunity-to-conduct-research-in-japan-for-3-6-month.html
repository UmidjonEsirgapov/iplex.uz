General Information:

Research Fellowship Program 2023 Announcement: Completed a Ph.D. for candidates up to 3-6 months to conduct research

Requirements for candidates:

● Applicants must receive an invitation (acceptance letter) from the host institution in Japan before applying.
● Candidates must have a Ph.D. (Doctoral Degree).
● Applicants must be 49 years of age or younger at the time of application.
● Applicants must be proficient in English or Japanese.
● Applicants must have no past or present experience of being in Japan (except for short-term stays such as sightseeing or conferences)
● Applicants must have an occupation in their home country, should return there and contribute to the development of their countries after the scholarship period.

Fields of research available: Research fields such as science, engineering and medicine are given priority.

Note: Research is for three to six months between April 2023 and March 2024 In your application form, indicate in detail how long you will stay and the month of its beginning and end. (For example, six months from September 2023 to February 2024.). On average, 10 candidates are selected every year. Also, don't forget to get an acceptance letter from the host institution before applying.

Grants are only available to successful applicants and are intended for necessary living expenses and research activities in Japan. This scholarship is not transferable under any circumstances:

Benefits of the grant:

120,000 yen will be awarded to cover a portion of initial living expenses in Japan.
Round-trip economy class air tickets will be provided between the nearest international airport from the recipient's residence and Tokyo, Japan.
Medical treatment and overseas travel insurance are provided.
MIF invites scholarship recipients to participate in a study tour during the scholarship to deepen their understanding of Japan.
