More than 11,000 applied for the master's degree

Until July 30 Admission to the master's degree of higher education institutions will continue. So far, 11,149 applicants have applied for the master's degree.

This year, 17,100 quotas were allocated for the master's degree. If another 6,000 applicants apply, the quota will be paid.

The Minister of Higher Education stated that this year admission to the master's degree will be held only once - in the summer.

According to the decree of the President, the Admissions Commission must allow the winter admission only when the summer admission quota is not filled. — Official Data
