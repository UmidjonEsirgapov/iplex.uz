Another new faculty was established at the Andijan Mechanical Engineering Institute.

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Based on the agreement between the Andijan Institute of Mechanical Engineering and the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, another faculty, the Faculty of Technological Process Management and Computer Systems, was established at the institute. Education is given to students of the departments "Informatics and information technologies (by networks and sectors)", "Automation and control of technological processes and production", "Mechatronics and robotics", "Intelligent engineering systems". and based on the agreement of the Ministry of Secondary Special Education, another faculty, Technological Processes Management and Computer Systems Faculty, was established at the institute.

Now, starting from the new 2020/2021 academic year, the faculty will have "Informatics and information technologies (by networks and sectors)", "Technological processes and production automation and management" in the section of the Departments of Automation of Mechanical Engineering and Information Technologies. Students of "Mechatronics and robotics", "Intelligent engineering systems" are taught. The number of students at the faculty is 422 for the 2020/2021 academic year.
