Opportunity to win a modern smartphone by participating in the video contest organized by the Central Bank

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
Do you like making videos and want your work to be as useful as possible for everyone?
Participate in the Central Bank's youth video contest on the topic "Benefits of Online Savings".
The best creative works will receive valuable gifts from the Central Bank. will get: 1st place - smartphone; 2nd place - smart watch; 3rd place - silver coin of the Central Bank For whom: The competition is held in two age categories: 1st category - 7-14 years old; 2 categories - 15-25 years old.

The Central Bank announces a video competition among young people on "Benefits of online savings" within the framework of "World Savings Day 2021".

Are you between 7 and 25 years old? Do you like making videos and want your work to be as useful as possible for everyone? Do you know how to save money and know all about bank deposits? Then this opportunity is for you!

Participate in the competition of videos of the Central Bank among young people on the topic "Advantages of online savings". Tell everyone exactly why this investment tool is the safest and most profitable!

The best creative works will win valuable gifts from the Central Bank:

  • 1st place - a smartphone;
  • 2nd place - a smart watch;
  • 3rd place - a silver coin of the Central Bank

For whom: Two choices held by age category:

  • Category 1 - 7-14 years old;
  • Category 2 - 15-25 years old.

So, to be a winner:

  1. Make an informative video up to 1 minute on the topic "Online savings benefits";
  2. Download and fill out the application form for participation in the competition via this link;
  3. Send the application and video to the e-mail address konkurs.cbu@gmail.com by October 26 of this year (it is necessary to indicate "WSD video competition" in the e-mail subject);
  4. It is necessary to follow the announcements on Finlit.uz social pages

Applications and jobs will be accepted from October 11 to October 26, 2021.
