Fully funded DAAD Helmut-Schmidt Program

The DAAD Helmut-Schmidt-Program supports future leaders of developing countries who want to promote democracy and social justice in their countries.

Level: master's degree.
Benefits:
- DAAD scholarship holders are exempted from paying tuition fees.
- Scholarships for postgraduate courses in German higher education institutions (€931 per month).
- Health insurance costs covered in Germany.
- Adequate travel allowances for travel within Germany and relevant developing countries.
- Study and research grant.
- Rent subsidies and family allowances, if necessary.
- German language course in Germany lasting up to 6 months. Participation is mandatory.
