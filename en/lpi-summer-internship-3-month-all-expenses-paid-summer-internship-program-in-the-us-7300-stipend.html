General Information:

"LPI Summer Internship in the United States 2022": Applications are currently being accepted for a fully funded LPI Summer Internship in Houston, USA. All citizens can apply for this 10-week internship program.

Fields of internship:

Physics or natural sciences, engineering, computer science or mathematics The probability of admission is higher for students studying in the fields, but all students from all other fields can apply.

The advantages of LPI summer internship:

The LPI summer internship in the USA is a fully funded internship. All expenses covered.

  • All interns receive a stipend of up to $7,300 to cover program-related expenses;
  • Airfare provided;
  • Up to $500 in travel expenses awarded to international interns;
  • Home -accommodation and living expenses are covered.

Eligibility criteria:

  • All local and international students can apply.
  • Students must be willing to be a 10-week intern.
  • Students from all fields can apply, but mathematics, computer science, natural or physical sciences, engineering Preference will be given to students majoring in science.
  • Candidates will be considered without discrimination based on gender, age, color, disability or religion.
  • IELTS/TOEFL language certificates are required but not required if you obtain a certificate/reference of your English language proficiency from an English professor at your university.

Note:The application process is online. You must complete an online application and upload documents. To apply visit the official LPI USA internship website .
