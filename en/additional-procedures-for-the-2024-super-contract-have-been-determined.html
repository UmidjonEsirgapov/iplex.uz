Additional procedures for the 2024 "super" contract have been determined

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the decision of the State Commission, applicants who participated in the selection based on the results of the entrance exams and were not recommended for the first bachelor's education can be admitted to study on the basis of the "Super" contract. will be allowed to study on a fee-contract basis, but for this the score must be lower than the contract passing score.
The amount of the super contract is calculated on the basis of the differentiated fee-contract values approved by the State Commission.

According to the decision of the State Commission, by participating in the competition based on the results of the entrance exams, the first undergraduate education Applicants who are not recommended for the course can be admitted to study on the basis of a "Super" contract. In this case, the applicant will be allowed to study in the first educational field of his choice on the basis of a fee-contract, but the score obtained for this must be lower than the contract passing score.

Super contract amount is calculated based on the differentiated fee-contract values ​​approved by the State Commission. Applicants who did not participate in the competition or were not recommended for study will also have the opportunity to study in one of their fields on the basis of a differentiated contract.

A 10% discount will be provided to applicants who have fully paid the super contract. Those who have made 50% or more of the contract payment by October 15, 2024 will be accepted as students. — Official Data
