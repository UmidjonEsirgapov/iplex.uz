The chance to win £1,000 by writing short stories - prizes are up for grabs for 20 people

The 2022 Bristol Short Story Prize is now accepting applications.

20 stories will be shortlisted and published by Tangent Books in the Bristol Short Story Prize Anthology Volume 15. The winner and 2 runners-up will be shortlisted and announced at an awards ceremony in October 2022.

Prizes:

1st place £1000, 2nd place £500, 3rd place £250. The remaining shortlisted writers will be awarded a further 17 prizes of £100. All shortlisted writers will receive 2 free copies of the anthology. Prizes will be sent to any writer who is unable to attend the awards ceremony.

There is also a Sansom Award, with a prize winner of £100.

Eligibility for nominations:

The competition is open to all published and unpublished writers. Any citizen can apply. All participants over the age of 16 are eligible to participate. A story can be submitted in any genre, there are no restrictions.

Note: You can also submit finished work online or by mail.

Entries must be unpublished, the maximum word count is 4000 words , there is no minimum word count. Stories can be on any topic or subject, and any style or genre is welcome. The number of stories is not limited, one candidate can submit several stories. You have to pay £9 per story. But 250 people can join for free. Learn more about Nu. No email inquiry or other form of application is required to take advantage of the free entry option.
