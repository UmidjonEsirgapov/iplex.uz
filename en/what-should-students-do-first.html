What should students do first?

üéâ First of all, congratulations on becoming a student! Studentship is a new stage in your life, a period of opportunities and responsibilities. Below are specific recommendations for your first steps.

1. Get to know the HEI closely

  • Following official sources Each higher education institution (HEI) has its own official website and Telegram channel. Follow all the news and announcements by visiting the website of the higher education institution where you are a student.

  • Location and infrastructure Get information about the location of the higher education institution, faculty buildings, libraries, laboratories and sports facilities. This will help you adapt faster.

2. Find out about your field of study

  • Subjects and grading system Find out which subjects will be covered during your studies, the amount of credit for each subject and how the grading system works.

  • Get to know the curriculum If you know in advance about the sequence of subjects and practical work in the curriculum, the semester will be less difficult.

3. Make a personal study plan

  • Don't lose motivation Maintain the level of study at the entrance exam, try not to indulge.

  • Time management Make a weekly or monthly study schedule. It will help you to be effective in mastering subjects.

4. Get ready for an independent life

  • Sense of responsibility Now you are an independent person. You have to manage your money, your time and your daily activities.

  • Don't be afraid of the adjustment period If you are studying away from home, it will teach you independent life skills. Independence means new opportunities.

Conclusion

In order to get your student life right, first gather complete information about your higher education institution and your major, make your personal study plan and prepare for independent life.
