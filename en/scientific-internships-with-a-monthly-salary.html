Research Internships (year-round)
Students pursuing or holding a bachelor's or master's degree in natural or computer science, mathematics, or other related disciplines may apply for a research intern position at ISTA. A scientific intern can be appointed for a period of up to 1 year. Candidates are selected by the head of the research group (professor or associate professor) and the appointment is made subject to the availability of sufficient funds for the salary of the research intern and the official approval of the Department of Academic Affairs. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis and those interested in an independent research intern position at ISTA should contact the relevant group leader directly.

Only complete applications will be considered, including 1) the candidate's CV 2) a short motivation letter outlining why their profile and research interests match the group's research topics. Incomplete and/or irrelevant applications will not be considered.

ISTA offers scientific interns a minimum salary of €1,119 per month.

ISTA does not charge any application fees at any stage, including scientific internships.

Requirements:
Enthusiasm and interest in the research topic of the applying group is essential. A relevant background is a must, while transferable/relevant research experience or technical skills would be an advantage.

Apply:
First, you will identify which research group you are most interested in working with. For information about available research groups, please see this overview of available research groups.

Once you have found a research group that you would like to work for, please email the research group leader directly and do not forget to attach the following:

-A brief introduction about yourself and motivation for working with them;
-Attached CV (in PDF format), relevant experience, educational history;
-Possible dates for your internship
If the head of the research group wants to accept you as a research intern in his research group, he or his assistant will contact you about the next steps.

Please note: not all research groups can accept interns. Before applying, candidates should carefully review the research groups' web pages to ensure that their profile and research interests match the groups' research topics.

Applications are accepted on an ongoing basis and those interested in an independent research trainee position at ISTA should contact the relevant group leader directly. — More information
