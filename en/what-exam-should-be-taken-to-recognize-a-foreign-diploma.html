Nostrification process: Registration and examination procedure

In Uzbekistan The process of recognition of foreign diplomas (nostrification) is officially regulated and is carried out through the my.gov.uz portal or Government service centers.

Registration methods

There are two ways to register for nostrification:

  1. Through My.gov.uz site
  2. Government Services Center (DXM)

Required documents

üìå For online registration:

  • Diploma obtained in foreign countries (translated into Uzbek and notarized PDF form)
  • Diploma application (translated into Uzbek and notarized in PDF format)

üìå When applying through the State Services Center:

  • Passport
  • Original copy of diploma and application
  • Translated into Uzbek and notarized copy of diploma and application

Terms of payment and review of documents

For nostrification, a payment of 3 times the basic calculation amount (BHM) is required (1,125,000 soums).

Documents are considered within 10 working days, if there are no deficiencies in the documents, the applicant is taken to the exam access permit will be granted.

Exam procedure and evaluation system

üìå Tests:

  • Consists of 50 questions
  • Each question is given 2 minutes
  • A total of 100 minutes is required to complete the test

üìå Evaluation criteria:

  • Each correct answer is evaluated with 2 points
  • At least 56 points are required to pass the exam
  • If the applicant scores less than 56 points, the diploma will not be recognized

üìå Resubmission conditions:

  • The applicant who failed the test can re-register
  • For the re-examination, a payment of 2 times the BHM will be made (750,000 soums)
