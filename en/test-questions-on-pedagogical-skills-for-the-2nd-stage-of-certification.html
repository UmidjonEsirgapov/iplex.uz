For pedagogues preparing for the 2nd stage of certification, pedagogical skill test questions will help. The following questions are important for strengthening pedagogical knowledge and improving skills.

Samples of test questions

  1. At what stage of the technological design of the educational process is pre-diagnosis of the students' knowledge level carried out?

    • Answer: 3

  2. How many stages does the technological design of the educational process consist of?

    • Answer: 3

  3. In how many stages was "Educational technology" formed?

    • Answer: 3

  4. Which creativity of the teacher is aimed at creating new theories of social importance?

    • Answer: Creative creativity

  5. What is an individual psychological feature such as methodologically correct organization of training sessions or educational activities?

    • Answer: Constructive ability

  6. Individual characteristic of a teacher that allows understanding the nature and laws of the educational process?

    • Answer: Understanding ability

  7. What are the didactic goals of pedagogical technology?

    • Answer: Developmental goal

  8. What are the common approaches to organizing the teaching process?

    • Answer: Individual, group, frontal

  9. What are the stages of the structure of educational activities?

    • Answer: The stage of purposeful, controlling, checking, monitoring actions

  10. What is the individual psychological characteristic of teachers as a specialist, which ensures the ability to organize educational and educational processes in accordance with pedagogical requirements and effectively manage a group of students?

    • Answer: Organization ability

Importance of these tests

  • Consolidation of knowledge: Tests the basic theoretical knowledge necessary in the pedagogical process.
  • Preparation for practice: Questions increase preparation for practical situations encountered in the educational process.
  • Professional development: Successful participation in the next stages of certification. creates a basis for achievement.
