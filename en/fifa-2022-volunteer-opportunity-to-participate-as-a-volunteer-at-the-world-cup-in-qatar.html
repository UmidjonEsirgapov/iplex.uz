General Information:

FIFA 2022 QATAR: Calling for volunteers from all over the world for the 2022 World Cup in Qatar. It is expected to be the largest volunteering activity in the history of Qatar.

You do not need to be a citizen or resident of Qatar. In total, more than 20,000 volunteers are expected to be received. Volunteers will go through an appropriate selection process.

A visa is not required to enter Qatar. Qatar provides visas on arrival to more than 90 countries. This is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to be a part of the biggest sporting event in the world. You'll also gain new skills and invaluable experience that will benefit you for years to come.

Who can participate: Anyone aged 16 and over regardless of religion, race, or social background can participate.

All volunteers must go through a recruitment process that includes the following steps:

  • Click here register;
  • Post an introduction video;
  • Online interview;
  • You will receive a role offer based on the exact area in which you volunteer;
  • Online training;
  • And you may be accepted after other types of qualifying stages;

Who can participate: Candidates will be selected based on their communication, leadership, language skills, teamwork skills;

The following expenses will be covered;

  • Use of all internal andtransportation throughout Qatar is absolutely free;
  • Meals: one meal will be provided for every 8-hour session. If you work more than 8 hours in 1 day, you can get 2 meals;

Note: 1 day off is given every seven days; Deadline: As soon as possible
