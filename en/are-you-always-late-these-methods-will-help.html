Procrastination is caused by poor time management. You can arrive on time and create an efficient schedule through the following methods.

1. Plan aheadMake plans for each day or week. Estimate how long it will take to complete each task and avoid overspending.

2. Get up earlyIt is important to form the habit of getting up early in order to finish your daily work on time and avoid unnecessary rush situations in the morning.

3. Take time into accountAllow extra time for unexpected delays when setting off or going to an appointment. If the journey takes 30 minutes, leave at least 45 minutes before.

4. Limit the use of electronic devicesSpending too much time on the phone and computer is one of the main causes of procrastination. Avoid spending time on social networks without control, try to use them only when necessary.

5. PrioritizeOrder your daily tasks in order of importance. Do the most important things first so that your plans don't get derailed.

Follow these tips to beat the habit of procrastination and manage your time effectively. — Official Data
