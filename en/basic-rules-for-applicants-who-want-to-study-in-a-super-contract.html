Basic rules for applicants who want to study in a super-contract

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Applicants who did not pass the main selection in the tests can be admitted to study with a super-contract (differentiated contract payment). It depends.
Applicants who fall short of more than 4.05 points, but do not score less than 56.7 points, can become students by paying a certain amount of the basic contract. can be admitted to study with a super-contract (differentiated contract fee).

According to the law, the differentiated contract fee depends on the amount of the student's score compared to the entrance score.

Including:

  • applicants who do not reach 1.05 points - 1.5 times the basic fee-contract amount;
  • 1.06 those who have less than 2.05 points - 2 times the basic payment-contract amount;
  • Those who have not reached 2.06 to 3.05 points - 2.5 times the basic payment-contract amount;
  • Those who have not reached 3.06 to 4.05 points - pay 3 times the basic payment-contract amount.

Applicants who fall short of more than 4.05 points, but do not score less than 56.7 points, can become students by paying a certain amount of the base contract amount. This amount varies depending on the directions.

In 2022, super-contract prices were calculated based on the amount of contract money without scholarships.

It should be noted that the contract amounts for the new academic year have not been confirmed. Once the contract amount is confirmed, the super-contract money will also be confirmed. Then the applicant can apply for a super-contract to the institution of higher education he wants to study. — Read on infoedu.uz
