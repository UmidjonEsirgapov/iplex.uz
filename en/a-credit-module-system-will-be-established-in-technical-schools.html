A credit-module system will be established in technical schools

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Government Decree (No. 648, 07.12.2023) approved the regulation on the procedure for organizing the educational process in the credit-module system in technical schools.

According to the regulation, the educational process in the credit-module system in technical schools is organized in accordance with the European model of credit transfer and accumulation (ECTS).

The current credit-module system educational programs are determined by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation.

The duration of the academic year is determined based on the educational program. The number of credits to be accumulated by the student during the academic period includes the mandatory and optional subjects specified in the curriculum.

The student must accumulate at least 60 credits in one academic year. The size of the training load in technical schools is determined based on the curriculum.

In the credit-module system, 1 credit is equal to the training load of an average of 25-30 academic hours.

In this case, the student needs to master the specified amount of training load in order to collect the relevant credits in a particular subject.

The training load is divided into an hour of lecture and an hour of independent study. is divided. The educational program for the academic year is developed by the student, as a rule, taking into account the accumulation of 30 credits in one semester and 60 credits in one academic year.

On the initiative of students with academic debts, the transfer of existing academic debts is organized on a paid basis during the vacation at the technical school. — Official Data
