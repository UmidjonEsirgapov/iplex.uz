Registration for professional certification is ongoing

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Registration and sending of applications for obtaining a professional certificate continues. Trainee-teachers who were hired for the first time as pedagogues in general secondary education organizations and pedagogic personnel who do not have the appropriate category by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 87 dated February 12, 2024 will be involved in professional certification tests.
From July 25 of this year, applications for participation in extraordinary professional certification tests will be accepted through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal and state service centers.

Registration and application for professional certification will continue

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 87 dated February 12, 2024, in general secondary education organizations intern teachers who have been hired for the first time in the teaching profession and teaching staff who do not have the appropriate category will be involved in professional certification tests.

From July 25 of this year, through the Unified interactive state services portal and state service centers, extraordinary applications for participation in professional certification tests are accepted.

Sending applications will continue until August 10, 2024 (the same day).

For information: the certificates of 4,896 trainee teachers who successfully passed the professional certification tests held in March-June of this year have been sent to their personal offices.

We remind you: trainee teachers who could not participate in the control test due to good reasons and failed to pass the control test for the first time (scored less than 56 points) in the professional certification tests held in March-June of this year can apply. — Source
