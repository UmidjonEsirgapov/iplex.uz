How will dual education be introduced?

Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, in the higher education system, gradual education based on dual education will be introduced and the "Professional" dual education system will be established.

Initially, a dual education system will be introduced for 3rd and 4th year students of Tashkent Textile and Light Industry Institute. Students undertake practical training in enterprises, this process is coordinated by the higher education institution and the head of practice.

There is also an important incentive mechanism for students working on the basis of dual education:

  • Salary up to 2 million soums for the period of working in enterprises. part Employment assistance is covered by the funds of the state fund.

Organizational processes:

  • Every year by June 1 a list of enterprises accepting students in the form of dual education is published.
  • By September 1 on the assignment of students to enterprises. a contract is signed between the enterprise, student and higher education institution.
  • Conditions for students are constantly monitored in enterprises where dual education is established.

In addition, with this decision, the regulation on the procedure for organizing dual education was approved. This regulation defines the mechanisms of organization of the dual education process, coordination of theoretical and practical training, and cooperation between higher education institutions and production enterprises. — Source
