2025/2026 academic year undergraduate admission: Test subjects and evaluation criteria

According to the relevant order of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation, an important news regarding the tests for the 2025/2026 academic year was announced. The number of subjects to be submitted by the applicants, the amount of test tasks and the evaluation criteria have been determined.

The number and order of the subjects to be tested

In the new academic year, applicants will be tested in a total of 5 subjects. Each subject is determined according to the field chosen by the applicant.

Test subjects are divided as follows:

  • 2 compulsory subjects (mother tongue, mathematics or history)

  • 3 specialist subjects (according to the field)

Number of test items and maximum score

The number of test questions for the 2025/2026 academic year has also been left the same as last year:

  • The total number of test questions is 90

  • The maximum possible score is 189 points

This procedure is a more accurate assessment of the knowledge of applicants and a fair assessment set to achieve results.

What are the evaluation criteria?

Evaluation criteria were left based on last year's system. For each correct answer, a certain point is given. Points will not be deducted for wrong answers, which makes it convenient for applicants.

Conclusion

Tests for the 2025/2026 academic year will be held in the order of the previous year. This will provide additional clarity in the preparation process for applicants.
