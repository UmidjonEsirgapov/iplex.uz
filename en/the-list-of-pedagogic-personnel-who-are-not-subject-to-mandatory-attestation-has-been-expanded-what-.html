With the new decision of the government of Uzbekistan, important changes have been made for the teaching staff. According to the decision No. 120 dated March 23, 2023, pedagogues with 25 years or more of experience in teaching activities and less than 5 years left before retirement will be exempted from compulsory attestation. not:

  • Those who have 25 years or more of pedagogical work experience.
  • Those who have less than 5 years left before retirement due to age.

Pedagogues of this category remain with their current qualification categories (positions) and are paid according to this qualification category (position) while working in educational organizations. will be paid.

Maintenance and support of the qualification category

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 572 of September 17, 2021, pedagogues can undergo extraordinary certification at their own discretion. If they fail to pass this special certification, their qualification category (position) will not be lowered and they will continue to receive remuneration according to the current qualification category (position).

What do these changes mean?

  1. Supporting experienced educators: These changes are aimed at encouraging educators who have worked for many years. Their extensive experience and knowledge will be valued, their qualifications will not be downgraded, and they will be exempt from conditional certification.

  2. Stability and Trust: Educators can maintain their current qualifications and continue to be paid for their current positions. This gives them stability and confidence.

  3. Certification at will: Educators have the opportunity to undergo extraordinary certification at their will. This will give them the opportunity to update and develop their qualifications, but will not downgrade them in the process.
