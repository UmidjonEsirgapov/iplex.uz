✅ Answer:

Students cannot be expelled for being late to classes or not in school uniform.

If they are not included in the curriculum, they cannot be left out of class for other work. There is no provision in the regulatory documents about leaving a student who comes to school unattended during the lesson and expelling them from the lesson for being late, lack of school equipment or school uniform. The educational process in educational institutions should be based on the principles of "legality, democracy, non-discrimination and respect".
