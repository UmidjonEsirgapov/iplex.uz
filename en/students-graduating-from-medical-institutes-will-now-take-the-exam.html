Students graduating from medical institutes will now take the exam

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
This system will be mandatory for graduates who plan to work as doctors.

Starting from the 2024/2025 academic year, a new examination system will be introduced in all state and non-state medical higher education institutions. This system is mandatory for graduates planning to work as doctors.

Two-stage state exam

First stage:

  • Students pass the final state exam in fundamental sciences.
  • Students who successfully complete this stage will have the right to transfer from course to course.

Second stage:

  • Students take an exam in clinical subjects.
  • Those who have shown good results in clinical knowledge will get the right to practice and work as a doctor.

An important step for the medical profession

This new system aims to test the theoretical and practical knowledge of doctors, provide quality medical education and prepare graduates for the profession. It is intended to:

  • Students develop their theoretical knowledge,
  • Strengthen their practical skills in clinical science,
  • Gain real experience to become a qualified doctor.

Conclusion

Now medical universities graduating students must pass a two-stage state exam to work as a doctor. This new procedure serves to train qualified and quality personnel. — More information
