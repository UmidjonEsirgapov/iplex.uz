President: If we educate one girl, we will educate the whole family

The President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev expressed important thoughts about women's education and their role in society at the ceremony dedicated to International Women's Day.

President stated that a girl's education helps the whole family become enlightened. As a result, the consciousness and cultural level of the society will also increase.

Results in the education of women

  • The percentage of women in higher education has increased to 52 percent.
  • In 2023, 100 will be given to about 15,000 women studying at the master's degree. billion soums of contract money has been paid.
  • More than 6,200 women are engaged in scientific activities.
  • 25% of scientific developments are created by women scientists.

These numbers show that the participation of women in the scientific and academic sphere is increasing year by year.

A new era in women's scientific activities

This speech of President Mirziyoyev emphasizes that women are not only getting an education, but their contribution to the development of science is also increasing. In the future, it is expected that new initiatives will be implemented to support the scientific activities of women and create conditions for them. — Official Data
