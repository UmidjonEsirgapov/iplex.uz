Germany is the most convenient country for education. There are several reasons for this.

  • First of all, all German universities are ranked among the top 500 universities.
  • The contract fee for higher education in Germany is very low, most universities have no contract fee, study is completely free.
  • In Germany, not only German, but international students for education in English was introduced. But this does not mean that I will not learn the German language.
  • By coming to study in Germany, you will have the opportunity to travel all over Europe. All you have to do is apply for a student visa!
  • While studying in Germany, students are given the opportunity to work at the same time, as well as get an internship in their specialty.
  • In addition, accommodation is very cheap, food and clothes are very cheap. Student discounts are available: you can show your student ID and get discounts on the pool, movies and more. You can move freely in the city and the countryside both day and night.

Now we bring to your attention the list of free universities in Germany:

  • Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich - This institution was founded in 1472 and has been operating since then. With a large endowment of more than 1.7 billion euros every year, it is able to educate students without any tuition fees. This is great news for international students as they may be able to finance their education with free tuition.
  • Humboldt University Berlin- More than 50 Nobel laureates have studied at this university. Famous scientists such as Albert Einstein were part of the faculty here, which demonstrates its excellence in natural sciences. It is one of the best universities in the world for arts and humanities.
  • University of Mannheim- This university is relatively young and was founded in 1967. Currently, about 12,000 students study for free at this university in Germany. International students admitted to the University of Mannheim can study in the business school, humanities school, economics and law school, etc.
  • University of Cologne- Currently, it has more than 40,000 students, which is one of the largest student bodies in Germany. Therefore, the university offers many courses in different subjects.
  • RWTH Aachen University- Germany's leading technical university in engineering, science and medicine, as well as one of the most prestigious universities in Germany. Collaborates with excellent institutes such as TU Delft and ETH Zurich. International students are welcome at this great university in Germany.
  • Technical University of Munich- Another excellent technical university is Munich, competing with RWTH University in Aachen. On the 17th of 1868, Nobel Prize laureates studied at this university. For international students interested in STEM fields, consider attending this free German university.
  • University of Hamburg- Since 1919, this university has produced thousands of scientists, many of whom have enriched the lives of those around them. With an endowment of over 600 million euros, it is one of the best German universities for international students.
  • Free University of Berlin- Founded during the Cold War, the Free University of Berlin represents the freedom of West Berlin, which contrasts sharply with the lack of freedom in East Berlin. International students can study at this famous university for free!
