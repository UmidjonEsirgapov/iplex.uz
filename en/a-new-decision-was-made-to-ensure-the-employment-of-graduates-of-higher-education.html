A new decision was made to ensure the employment of graduates of higher education

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education is:

Brief content:
The President of the Republic of Uzbekistan signed the decision "On additional measures to support the employment of graduates of higher education organizations".
Financial support and resources 80 million US dollars will be allocated to JSC "Uzmilliybank" for the implementation of the program. This decision is aimed at strengthening the integration of young people into the labor market and improving the personnel training process.

The "Step to the Future" program will be launched

According to the decision, the "Step to the Future" program will be implemented in cooperation with the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and "Uzmilliybank" JSC. Within the framework of the program:

  • Development of business ideas - graduates who want to start their own business will be given financial support.
  • Conducting open competitions - competitions will be held among students and the process of providing employment will be organized.
  • Cooperation with employer organizations - supply of experts suitable for the field will be established.

Financial support and resources

Uzmilliybank JSC will be allocated 80 million US dollars to implement the program. Also:

  • 20 million US dollars will be allocated to finance student training and internship costs.
  • Students and graduates will receive employment referrals through "Step into the Future" centers.

Oversight and Enforcement

Ensuring program implementation:

  • Methodological manuals will be developed within one month.
  • The essence of the decision will be communicated to all higher education institutions.
  • The Prime Minister of the Republic of Uzbekistan is entrusted with monitoring the implementation of the decision.
