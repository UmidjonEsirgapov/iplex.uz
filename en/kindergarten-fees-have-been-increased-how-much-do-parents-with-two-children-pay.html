Kindergarten fees have been increased: how much do parents with two children pay?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Starting from January 1, 2025, parents' fees have been increased in state preschool education organizations.

Parents with two children going to kindergarten must pay 388,000 soums every month.

Starting from January 1, 2025, the parents' fee in state preschool education organizations has been increased. About this Ministers It was determined in the decision of the court of April 29, 2024.

What is the new payment procedure?

For parents who send their children to state preschool education organizations, the amount of payment is determined according to the number of children.

If only one child from one family goes to kindergarten:

  • Payment for a 9-hour group - 272 thousand soums.

If two or more children from one family go to kindergarten:

  • 194 thousand soums are paid for each child.

Example: How much is a parent with two children? will pay?

If two children from one family go to a 9-hour public kindergarten:

  • 194 thousand soums * 2 = 388 thousand soums
