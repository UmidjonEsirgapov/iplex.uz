As you know, Registon education project has announced the contest "The best essay 2022" at the national level. The competition was held on the scale of the Republic in 3 stages. Candidates who took part in the competition tested their knowledge in the examination system consisting of 3 stages for 1 month

— In the 1st stage of the competition, more than 3 thousand talented young people with IELTS 6+ or B2+ certificates from different regions of the Republic registered and 406 candidates who showed the best results won the ticket to the next stage.

— 2nd stage exams were held among the candidates who passed to the second stage of the competition, and the 105 participants who showed the best results in this stage won the final ticket (Results see)

— According to the competition instructions and regulations, the 3rd stage (final) stage was held among best performing participants . In the third stage, candidates wrote essays in special observation rooms in the order specified in the competition regulations. The special commission of the competition checked these documents for 2 working weeks.

The composition of the commission:

Chairman:

  • Tulovov Avazbek — the founder of the Registon educational project, the director of the REGISTON" training center.

  • professional teachers of the Registon educational center of the international category, consisting of 10 people;
  • Isakhanov Ja'farkhan - OLIYGOH.UZ, one of the founders of the platform, winner of the Navoi State scholarship, recipient of many republican scientific researches finalist of state and international grants;
  • Talibjonov Botirjon — director of the Registon educational project "HR department", coordinator of social projects;
  • Hamidjonov Shavkat — OLIYGOH.UZ, one of the founders of the platform, laureate of international CDAC, ACTS grants, many startups at the international level founder.
