An agreement was signed on teaching French language in kindergartens for free

Minister of Preschool and School Education Hilola Umarova held the International He participated in the 19th summit of the Organization of Francophonie. During the summit, an important agreement was reached between the minister and Natalie Lasslet, the founder of Kokoro Lingua. According to the agreement, the French language will be taught free of charge for 5-6-year-old children in 12 preschool educational organizations in Uzbekistan.

Within the framework of the agreement, it is planned to teach children French using innovative methods using the Kokoro lingua platform. This project is being implemented with the support of the Embassy of the Republic of Uzbekistan in France.

During the meetings, Hilola Umarova held talks with Olive Brandoui, the General Director of France Education International. During the negotiations, the issues of improving the skills of French language teachers in Uzbekistan, the teacher exchange program and the introduction of DELF junior and DALF tests were agreed upon. — Official Data
