Australia: Hedley Bull scholarship program

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Summary:
All foreign and domestic citizens can apply.
The grant is awarded for study in the Master of International Relations (Advanced) graduate course.
No separate application is required for the grant.

Australian National University The Graduate Studies in International Affairs announced the acceptance of applications for the Hedley Bull scholarship program.

The program is intended for students who want to study at the Master of International Relations (Advanced) graduate course taught at the university.

The grant is awarded to the student for 2 years.

The foreign student pays approximately 43,680 AUD (approximately 30,662 US dollars) per year to study in the university's master's courses.

The contract of the 4 candidates who won the grant will be fully covered.

All foreign fifty and local citizens can apply.

The candidate must be admitted to the Master of International Relations (Advanced) graduate course of the university.

All requirements: here and here.

The grant is awarded to study in the Master of International Relations (Advanced) graduate course. It is not required to submit a separate application for the grant.

At the time of applying for the study, you must also submit an Interest form for the grant.

The deadline is December 15, 2020, but it is advisable to apply for the grant from July 31 to November 11.
