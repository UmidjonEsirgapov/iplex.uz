"Google Developers" program student programmers in Uzbekistan is accepting applications from leaders for its clubs.

It is open to undergraduate and graduate students with a background in software development concepts.

Applicants should lead development clubs (Google Developers Student Club) at their universities and lead the creation of technology projects.

Also, they should provide information about important products and platforms. organizes seminars and other events.

Leaders will be able to participate in Google educational events and receive advice from the company.

In addition, in this position, they will have the opportunity to develop a community and network in the professional field.

Main requirements for candidates:

  • at least a full-time bachelor's or master's degree 1 year remaining;
  • technical knowledge in the field of programming and software development;
  • experience in organizing events or managing a team;
  • contact with the local developer community.

Candidates who want to participate in it can send an application through the link.

Registration for the countries of Central Asia, Caucasus and Turkey the transition period is until June 30.
