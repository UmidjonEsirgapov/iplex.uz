Important dates for applicants in 2025: When will the Mandate be announced?

Stages such as test procedures, selection of courses, preparation of a mandate and selection of a technical school for applicants will be carried out in the prescribed order. The following dates will play an important role in this year's admission process.

July 14-23 - Testing phase

Testing will take place between these days. Each applicant must take part in the tests on the specified day and time.

July 24 - August 7 - Course selection process

After the tests, the applicants will choose higher education courses based on their scores. This process is an approximate period, and possible changes may occur.

August 8-17 - Mandate preparation process

After the selection, mandate documents will be formed by the DTM. During this process, the official results of the applicants will be analyzed.

August 17 - Mandate will be announced (estimated)

It will be known by this date whether the applicants have been accepted or not. Although this date is estimated, official results are expected on August 17.

August 18-27 - Technical college selection stage

Applicants who could not enter higher educational institutions will be given the opportunity to choose technical schools. At this stage, applications will be accepted through the my.uzbmb.uz site.

Conclusion

2025 test processes and admission stages will be carried out in a certain order. One of the most important dates is August 17, on which the mandate is expected to be announced. Applicants should keep these dates in mind and constantly monitor official sources. — Source
