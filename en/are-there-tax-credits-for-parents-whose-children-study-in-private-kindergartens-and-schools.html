Are there tax credits for parents whose children study in private kindergartens and schools?

According to the decision of the president, tax for parents for their children studying in private kindergartens or schools. privilege is provided. That is, educational fees paid by parents for each child up to 3 million soums per month are exempted from income tax. In order to use the privilege, parents must keep receipts of fees paid to private educational institutions throughout the year. At the end of the year, they submit all receipts to the tax office along with the income declaration and get back the overpaid 12% income tax. For example, if a parent pays 3 million soums per month, it will be 36 million soums per year. At the end of the year, the tax authority will return 4 million 320 thousand soums from this amount.

If the parent is paying through his office, he can submit an application to the accounting office of the enterprise about not withholding income tax. — Source
