Denmark: Fully and partially funded grants for all stages

Important news for students and applicants.

Summary:
This grant is available to all students from all countries outside of Europe to study at the undergraduate, graduate and doctoral levels in all available disciplines.
Benefits of the grant: Full or partial tuition fee. covered; A monthly stipend will be provided for living expenses.
Documents of all accepted students will also be considered for the grant program.

Brief about the grant:

Denmark Government Scholarship grant program is a grant program funded by the Ministry of Higher Education and Science of the Danish government. This grant is intended to be provided to all students from all countries outside of Europe for studies at the bachelor's, master's and doctoral levels in all available subjects.

Benefits of the grant:

  • The study program fee is fully or partially covered;
  • A monthly stipend is provided for living expenses.

Grant application and general requirements:

No separate application is required for this grant from the Danish government. The general and basic requirement for candidates is that they must be citizens of European and non-Swiss countries. Candidates should apply for their preferred course from the 8 universities listed below. Documents of all accepted students are also considered for the grant program.

Universities:

  • IT University of Copenhagen (ITU)
  • University of Southern Denmark (SDU)
  • Copenhagen Business School (CBS)
  • Aalborg University (AAU)
  • Roskilde University (RUC)
  • Technical University of Denmark (DTU)
  • University of Copenhagen (UCPH)
  • Aarhus University (AU)

Note: The requirements for the courses offered by all the above universities vary depending on the level and course of study. To do this, first here choose your university and education field and familiarize yourself with the application form of your chosen course.

Expiration date: March 31, 2021
