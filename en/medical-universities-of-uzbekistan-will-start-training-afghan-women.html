Medical universities of Uzbekistan will start training Afghan women

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
An important step has been taken for Afghan women to study medicine in higher education institutions of Uzbekistan.
On September 9, in Doha, the capital city of Qatar, the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation of the Republic of Uzbekistan and Qatar's "Education is above everything" ("Education "Above All") Foundation signed a memorandum of understanding on the organization of training of Afghan women in the field of medicine.
Through this, Uzbekistan continues to strengthen cooperation with neighboring countries and strengthen its position in the international educational arena. On September 9, in Doha, the capital of Qatar, a memorandum was signed between the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation of the Republic of Uzbekistan and the Qatar Foundation "Education Above All". training in medical fields is planned in educational institutions. This initiative, on the one hand, is a big step for expanding the educational opportunities of Afghan women, and on the other hand, it serves to develop cooperation at the international level for the Uzbekistan education system.

Through this, Uzbekistan continues to strengthen cooperation with neighboring countries and strengthen its position in the field of international education. Such cooperation in the field of education ensures the development of knowledgeable and qualified specialists in the region. — Source
