Access to the OTM with a B1 certificate: the procedure for using the privilege

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to it, now a B1 level foreign language certificate is also accepted when applying for educational courses where a foreign language is the first subject in the subject complex.
According to the new regulation: applicants whose first subject is a foreign language can also apply with a B1 level certificate; however, the B1 certificate is awarded not the maximum score, but 75% of it. applies to all certificates that have not yet expired.

Amendments were made to the procedure for admission to OTM based on the B1 certificate

By the decision of the government, amendments were made to the Regulation on the procedure for admission to bachelor's degrees of higher education institutions. This regulation was approved by Decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 578 dated September 13, 2025, and Clause 12 of it was explained in a new version.

According to it, now, when applying for educational courses where a foreign language is the first subject in the complex of sciences, a foreign language certificate of level B1 is also accepted. .

The difference between the previous and the current procedure

What were the previous requirements?

Before this change, admission to the fields of study where a foreign language was the first subject in the subject complex was only based on a national or international certificate of at least B2 level.

What changes have been made now?

New according to the regulations:

  • applicants whose first subject is a foreign language may apply with a B1 level certificate;

  • however B1 is not the maximum score for the certificate, but 75 percent of it is awarded.

Scoring of foreign language certificates procedure

For subjects with a foreign language in the complex of subjects

  • B1 level national or international certificate — 75 percent of the specified maximum score;

  • B2 and higher level certificates — are evaluated at the specified maximum score.

Is the B1 certificate accepted even if the foreign language is a second subject?

Is the privilege for the B1 certificate maintained?

Yes. Irrespective of whether the foreign language is the first or second subject, a B1 certificate is accepted.

But:

  • B1 certificate - 75 percent of the maximum score;

  • B2 and higher certificates - the maximum score is given in full.

Are B1 certificates obtained in previous years also valid?

Does the validity period of the certificate matter?

B1 level exemption:

  • regardless of when it was obtained;

  • Applies to all certificates that have not expired.

Usually foreign language certificates are valid for 3 years. For example, if the B1 certificate obtained last year is still valid, 75 percent discount will be given. It is not required to resubmit it.

Is it possible to apply for the master's degree with a B1 certificate?

Does this change also apply to the master's degree?

No. The introduced changes apply only to the bachelor's degree.

For admission to master's and doctoral studies:

  • requirements for foreign language certificates are determined on the basis of a separate list approved by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation;

  • depending on the specialization B1, B2 or C1 level certificates are required.

Summary

According to the new regulation, B1 level foreign language certificate now provides a wider opportunity to enter the bachelor's degree. Applicants can also apply with a B1 certificate, but this certificate will be valued at 75% of the maximum score.
