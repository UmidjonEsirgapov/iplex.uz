StudentExpo-2025: job, credit and career opportunities for young professionals

Brief content:
The National Bank of Uzbekistan invites all young professionals to participate in the career fair "StudentExpo-2025". It is a comprehensive employment platform with the participation of the Chamber of Commerce and other large organizations.
Through this fair: graduates of higher education institutions are provided with jobs; Their business ideas are supported; Direct communication with major employers will be created.
