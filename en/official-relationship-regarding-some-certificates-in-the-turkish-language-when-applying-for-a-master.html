OFFICIAL RELATIONSHIP: Regarding some certificates in the Turkish language when applying for a master's degree

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation informs the following regarding appeals regarding the non-acceptance of certain certificates in the Turkish language when applying for the master's degree of higher education institutions. When international certificates are checked through the relevant official websites, some of the documents presented as proof of knowledge of the Turkish language are not certificates confirming the level of language knowledge, but rather certificates confirming having studied in language courses given by some educational centers. Therefore, applications that did not meet the specified requirements were rejected. The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation informs the following regarding appeals regarding the non-provision of applications. or international certificates of an equivalent level will be taken into account for the admission of the 2023/2024 academic year for participation in the competition in the master's specialties of higher education institutions.

When the national or international certificates of an equivalent level entered through the magistr.edu.uz platform in recent days to participate in the competition were checked through the relevant official sites, some of the documents presented as confirming knowledge of the Turkish language were not certificates confirming the level of language knowledge, but language issued by some educational centers. Certificates proving that they have studied in the courses are issued. Therefore, applications that did not meet the specified requirements were rejected.

For information, the TYS (Turkish Proficiency Test) certificate submitted by the applicants is checked through the http://belgesorgula.yee.org.tr/ platform. Certificates that are not approved as a result of the inspection will not be taken into account.

At this point, applicants are recommended to check their certificates on the relevant official sites.
