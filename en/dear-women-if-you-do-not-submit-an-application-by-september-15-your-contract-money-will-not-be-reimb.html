Dear ladies! If you do not write an application in the name of the rector or director before September 15, the state will not cover your contract fees!

On the basis of the government decision (No. 447, 15.08.2022), the contract fees of all women studying at the master's level of state higher education institutions will be covered from the state budget funds without the condition of return.

For this The applicant applies to the rector (branch director) of the educational institution of higher education in order to cover the full amount of the payment contract before September 15. Not applicable to: applications submitted after September 15; differentiated payment contract for payments;
