Procedure for certification of vocational education masters approved

Procedure for certification of production education masters from July 22, 2025 by order of the Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation formalized. This procedure is carried out on the basis of the regulation registered with the number 3635.

Professional education attestation regulation pdf.

Attestation obligation and term

According to the new regulation, production education masters of vocational education organizations undergo mandatory attestation every 5 years. It was introduced in order to constantly update the skills and knowledge of masters.

Who is involved in certification?

  • Those who have never passed certification before;

  • Newly hired masters;

  • Those who have graduated from educational organizations and are entering work for the first time.

They are involved in attestation when they have at least three years of pedagogical work experience in their specialty.

Opportunity of extraordinary attestation

Masters can pass extraordinary attestation according to their wishes. In these cases, an individual evaluation is carried out based on the specified conditions.

On the basis of what specialties is the certification conducted?

The certification is carried out on the following basis:

  • Diploma of higher or secondary special (professional) education;

  • Skills acquired in informal and informal ways (Recognition of Prior Learning);

  • Certificates and diplomas of retraining courses.

Masters are certified only in their specialty or specialty.

How is the certification process carried out?

Registration process

Masters are certified every year from August 1 They should register and submit an application through the electronic information system pedagog.edu.uz by October 1.

Attestation will be conducted in two stages

1st stage (20 points): Pedagogical skills and psychological preparation will be evaluated. Through this, the master's teaching style and skills of working with students are analyzed. 2nd stage (80 points): Theoretical and practical skills are evaluated. The master demonstrates his knowledge in his field through practical training.

Conclusion

The new certification procedure serves to stimulate the professional development of the masters of industrial education, increase the quality of education, and evaluate the modern skills of the masters. Through this system, teachers will have the opportunity to regularly update their skills and maintain their competitiveness in the labor market. — Source
