Foreign language exams to be held in the second half of 2024

Tests to determine the level of foreign language knowledge will be held in October, November and December of 2024. These tests provide an opportunity for applicants to assess their knowledge and obtain an official language degree.

Registration process

To participate in the test, applicants must register online at my.gov.uz. Applicants will be able to choose a region convenient for them and take part in tests in that region. The registration period and test dates of each test are clearly indicated in the table above.

Languages and dates

  • In October: tests will be held in English, German, French, Arabic, Italian, Persian, Korean.
  • November In December: tests in English, Arabic, Japanese, Chinese, Dari, Urdu, Pashto. In December: tests in English, German, French, Spanish, Uyghur, Hindi.
