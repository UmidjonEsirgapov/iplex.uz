How can students use the phone in higher education institutions?

Rules for the use of mobile phones by students in higher education institutions are clearly defined. Adherence to these rules is important for the effective organization of the educational process of students.

Basic rules for students

  • Phone mode: When entering the educational institution, students must switch their phones to "silent" mode.
  • During training: Turn off the phone before the start of classes and activities, put the phone in a briefcase or bag placement is required.
  • Permitted cases: Only between classes, in cases of necessity and urgency, the use of the phone is allowed.
  • Emergencies: The use of the phone is not restricted in the event of an emergency.

Prohibited cases

Students are strictly prohibited from using the phone in the following cases

  • Hanging the phone around your neck or keeping it in your pocket.
  • Connecting the phone to electrical outlets to charge it.
  • Talking or texting on the phone during training sessions.
  • Putting the phone on the desk or listening to music through headphones.
  • Showing content that promotes violence and cruelty.

Complying with these rules will help students to ensure discipline in the educational process.
