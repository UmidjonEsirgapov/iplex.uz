UWC IB Diploma program: A great opportunity for school students to study abroad for free

Today's most relevant education news is:

Summary:
General Information: A program known as UWS has been announced for school students to study abroad for free.
The UWC movement is for leaders who want to be changemakers for the better world, regardless of their socio-economic, religious, national, ethnic or cultural background. great opportunity.. If you are a passionate student, if you want to study and live with young people from different parts of the world, this program is for you.

UWC Movement is a great opportunity for leaders who want to be changers for the better world, regardless of their socioeconomic, religious, national, ethnic or cultural background. Students born between August 2006, 9-11th grade and high school students can apply. Admission to the program will be held in September - November 2021.

There are two main ways to apply for the IB diploma program at the UWC boarding school:

  • UWC national committee in your country or citizenship;
  • Through UDK Global Selection Program (GSP). You cannot apply to two UWC National Committees or both the UWC National Committee and the UWC Global Selection Program at the same time.

    Please visit the page below for more information on the key differences between the two UWC program streams.

    What's in Admission asked?
    1. Online application (essay and recommendation letter);
    2. Online test (thinking and analysis);
    3. Online video chat;
    4. Final test (exercises separately and together with other candidates);
    5. Assessment of family financial need;

    Required Documents:

    • Scanned copy of applicant's passport (front page with photo);
    • Scanned copy of academic transcript for the last full academic year (10th grade students can use general secondary education certificate);
    • Personal a scanned copy of the completed and signed consent to data processing;

    The winners will have the opportunity to study at one of 18 UWC schools around the world with all expenses covered.
