A branch of Sun Moon University of South Korea will be opened in Syrdarya

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
Syr Darya Region Governor Akmaljon Mahmudaliev received a delegation led by Sun Moon University President Son Jae-moon of South Korea. He approved the choice of the city of Gulistan.

Syr Darya Region Governor Akmaljon Mahmudaliev received the delegation led by Sun Moon University President of South Korea Son Jae Moon. This was reported by the press service of the governorship.

It was reported that during the dialogue, the effectiveness of the visit of representatives of the Syrdarya region to this university in December 2023 was noted. Son Jae-moon proposed to establish a university branch in Sirdarya. Governor of the region Akmaljon Mahmudaliyev approved the selection of the city of Gulistan for the establishment of the university.

It is noted that the university will initially offer training in five topical areas based on IT and artificial intelligence technologies. It is planned to turn the university into an educational cluster that prepares specialists ready for industry. For this purpose, educational processes will be fully integrated with production.

"At the end of the dialogue, the parties agreed to continue practical work on the proposals put forward. A memorandum of cooperation was signed," the press service of the governor's office said. — Source
