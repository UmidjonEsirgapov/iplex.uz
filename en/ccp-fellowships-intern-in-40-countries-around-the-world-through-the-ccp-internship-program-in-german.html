CCP Fellowships : Intern in 40 countries around the world through the CCP internship program in Germany

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Cross Culture programs in cooperation with SSP in Germany planned to support 120 practitioners from 46 countries of the world to complete internships every year on the basis of a full grant. acquire intercultural skills.
Note: Admission to the program opens in mid-autumn each year; Scholarships mainly allow internships in the following areas: Politics and society; Media and Culture; Human rights and peace; Sustainable development; Representatives from the following countries can participate; Algeria, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Bahrain, Bangladesh, Belarus, Bhutan, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Cuba, Egypt, Georgia, Germany, Guatemala, Haiti, India, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Jordan, Kazakhstan, Kuwait, Kyrgyzstan, Lebanon, Libya, Malaysia, Mauritania, Mexico, Morocco, Nepal, Oman, Pakistan, Palestinian Territory, Qatar, Republic of Moldova, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Sri Lanka, Sudan, Tajikistan, Tunisia, Ukraine, United Arab Emirates, Uzbekistan.

In Germany Together with SSP, Cross Culture programs planned to support 120 practitioners from 46 countries of the world for internships on a full grant basis every year. During 2-6 years in Germany or in one of the CCP partner countries, all participants will deepen their experience and gain intercultural skills. Note: Every year, admission to the program opens in mid-autumn; Scholarships mainly allow internships in the following areas: Politics and society;

  • Media and culture;
  • Human rights and peace;
  • Sustainable development;

    • Representatives from the following countries can participate; Cuba, Egypt, Georgia, Germany, Guatemala, Haiti, India, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Jordan, Kazakhstan, Kuwait, Kyrgyzstan, Lebanon, Libya, Malaysia, Mauritania, Mexico, Morocco, Nepal, Oman, Pakistan, Palestinian Territory, Qatar, Republic of Moldova, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Sri Lanka, Sudan, Tajikistan, Tunisia, Ukraine, United Arab Emirates, Uzbekistan.

    Requirements for candidates:

    • 23-45 years old
    • Must have a good level of language skills;
    • At least 2 years of work experience or 2 years of volunteer work in public works;
    • Candidate should be healthy and not have any problems with going out;

    Required Documents:

    • Cv (all information is required);
    • Motivation Letter;
    • Recommendation;
    • Photo (optional);

    Admission process:

    • Register through this link and select the fellowship section;
    • Required documents download;

    Benefits of the grant:

    • 350 euro monthly stipend for 6 months;
    • Digital exchange opportunity;
    • Free seminars with graduates from CCP countries;
