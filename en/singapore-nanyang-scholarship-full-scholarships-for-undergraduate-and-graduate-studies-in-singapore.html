General Information:

Nanyang Technological University Scholarship (NTU) - One of the best undergraduate scholarships in Singapore, the Nanyang Scholarship is awarded to first-year undergraduate students. The Nanyang Scholarship recognizes students who have achieved academically, demonstrate strong leadership potential, and have excellent academic programs. Therefore, it is important to prepare for each step of the application and interview process.

Applicants Eligibility:

  • All nationalities may apply:
  • For undergraduate admissions;
  • Successful awardees must be in full-time undergraduate study. The scholarship covers years of study at NTU.
  • Candidates with Singapore-Cambridge GCE "A" grade, Singapore Polytechnic Diploma, NUS High School Diploma, IB Diploma or Year 12 education.
  • Strong portfolio (if available);
  • Strong leadership qualities and potential;
  • University grades. GPA must be minimum 3.5 out of 5.0;

Grant Benefits:

  • Full tuition waiver;
  • $6,500 per academic year for living expenses;
  • Up to $2,000 per academic year for accommodation permit;
    (Applicable only to scholarship holders living in NTU dormitories);
  • Travel grant up to US$5000;
  • US$1750 for computer (one-off);

Application process:

Applicants must submit their application for admission to the university before applying for the scholarship.

Applicants with 12th year education:

  1. You You must enter your acceptance code before applying for this grant. The scholarship application deadline is the same as the admission deadline. Applications submitted after the closing date will not be considered.

      • A personal essay of no more than 300 words is required to complete the form. 3 topics you can write about are listed in the scholarship application.
  2. After completing the application, applicants must submit the following:
    • upload a recent passport-sized photograph (digital image).
    • submit the referee's evaluation online. The assessment should be done by your teacher, not your family or relatives. After submitting your scholarship application, you will be given a URL to send to your school teacher. If you are forwarding the link to your teacher, please let them know your NTU application number and date of birth, as they will need this information to complete the online assessment. Your teacher must complete the online evaluation form and submit it within 7 days of applying for the scholarship. If your instructor is unable to submit it online within 7 days and needs more time, please notify us as soon as possible (preferably no later than 2 weeks from the scholarship application date

Year 12 candidates:

  • All scholarship applicants will be notified of the status of their scholarship applications by email within one month of the NTU program offer being announced to successful students.
  • Interviews for grant selection will be held within one month of the NTU program offer being announced to successful students.
