HEMIS Version 1.5.9 Launched for HEIs: New Features

HEMIS system version 1.5.9 has been launched for higher education institutions

On January 31, 2026, the HEMIS information system Version 1.5.9 has been officially launched for use in higher education institutions. In the new version, a number of important innovations aimed at further improving the digitalization and management of the educational process were introduced.

The updates mainly serve to facilitate the processes of working with the evaluation system, academic groups, questionnaires, study plans, and educational orders.

Important innovations in the evaluation system

The passing threshold for current and interim controls has been set

A new parameter for the evaluation system added. Now it is possible to set a threshold for the sum of current and intermediate controls. This increases transparency and accuracy in the assessment process.

The statuses of grade correction requests have become visible

A system has been introduced that reflects the status of requests sent to correct grades according to subject records in the teacher's profile. This allows teachers to easily control the process.

Management of academic groups and the learning process

The functionality of academic groups has been improved

In the section of students and academic groups, it is possible to use subject groups to create small groups. It serves to organize lesson processes more flexibly.

Errors in the lesson schedule and subject topics have been eliminated

In the new version:

  • copying daily lessons in the lesson schedule,

  • errors in the filtering function of subject topics

completely eliminated

Opportunities to work with questionnaires and commands

Global questionnaire function introduced

Global questionnaire function was added to student and teacher profiles. This makes it possible to participate in a wide range of surveys.

Education orders functionality has been expanded

In the section of education orders:

  • allow enrolled students to attend classes,

  • assign them to academic groups

new on the order function was introduced.

Working with curricula and documents

Exporting personal records to Excel format

On the mastering and personal account page, an opportunity to export the list of personal records to Excel format was created.

The calculation of the curriculum load was clarified

Defects related to taking into account the load of inactive subjects in the total load of the curriculum have been eliminated.

Restrictions on the science syllabus have been strengthened

In the functionality of the science syllabus (program) in the curriculum:

  • the total score of control types should not exceed the maximum limit,

  • after the student is evaluated restriction of editing control types

provided. This enhances fairness and systematicity in the evaluation process.

Conclusion

HeMIS system version 1.5.9 is aimed at making the management of the educational process, assessment and working with documents more convenient and efficient in higher education institutions. New features increase transparency in the educational process and make it easier for teachers and students to use the system. — Details here
