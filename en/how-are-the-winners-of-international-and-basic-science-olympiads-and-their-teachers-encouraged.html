In what order are the winners of international and basic science Olympiads and their teachers encouraged?
According to the law, the winners of international Olympiads and their teachers are awarded one-time monetary awards in the following amounts of BHM (base calculation amount): For the 1st place (gold medal) - 500 times, for the teacher - 450 times; For the 2nd place (silver medal) - 300 times, for the teacher - 250 times; For the 3rd place (bronze medal) - 200 times, for the teacher - 150 times.
Also, the teachers of the winners of the republic stage of the main Olympiads will be awarded with one-time cash prizes in the following amounts of the base calculation amount: for the 1st place - 50 times; 2nd place - 35 times; For the 3rd place - 30 times.
