Examinations of specialized schools will be held in Tashkent

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
On July 25-26 of this year, the entrance exams for the specialized school named after Muhammad al-Khorazmi, and on July 26 for the specialized schools named after Mirza Ulug'bek and Abu Ali ibn Sina will be held in Tashkent.
We remind applicants. In addition to the set time (90 minutes) for completing the tasks, no additional time will be given to mark the answer sheet of the entrance exam.
Addresses where the exam will be held: "Uzekspomarkaz" national exhibition complex "Higher educational inter-sports complex" at the Financial Institute.
