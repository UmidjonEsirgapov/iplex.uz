"EPAM Training Center" is accepting applications for the free training program "DevOps Essentials".

Candidates from Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan and Belarus can participate in it.

The course is aimed at gaining theoretical and practical knowledge of Git and network concepts, understanding the basic syntax of Bash and Python, and teaching the main features of Linux and Windows OS.

This course has no specific start and end dates. After registration, you can access the training materials and move as you wish.

If the participants successfully complete this program and successfully complete the theory test and practical tasks, they will have the opportunity to join the second stage - DevOps Fundamentals training.

Candidates must meet the following requirements:

  • English language level A2+ (Pre-intermediate) and above;
  • Having basic knowledge of IT concepts and software skills;
  • Analytical thinking and creativity in solving non-standard problems;
  • Desire to work and develop in DevOps.

Unaccepted candidates will be able to learn the following free DevOps classes:

  • "DevOps: self-study materials";
  • "A bookshelf for a junior DevOps engineer";
  • "Why DevOps is needed and who are DevOps engineers";
  • "Fast Track VS Full Track: How to choose the right path in learning DevOps?".
