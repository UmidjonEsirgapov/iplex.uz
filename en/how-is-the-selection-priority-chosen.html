How is the selection priority chosen?

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
Priority of "Educational Area" If the applicant chooses the priority of "Educational Area", the action is as follows: Selection for each area is carried out sequentially; In each direction, selection is made on the basis of a grant first, and then a contract; The first directions are suitable for those who are important.
Which priority should be chosen?
If the main goal is to study on the basis of a grant, even in any direction, the "State grant" priority is chosen. then, one of the important stages is determining the priority of the selection. This choice has a direct impact on the basis on which you will study - state grant or payment contract and in which direction.

Types of selection priority

There are 2 types of selection priority in the DTM system:

1. "State grant" priority

If the applicant chooses the "State grant" priority, the following procedure applies:

  • In each field of study, he first participates in the selection based on the state grant;

  • If he cannot get the grant, consider the payment-contract

This method is suitable for those who want to enter any direction based on a grant.

2. "Educational field" priority

If the applicant chooses the "Educational field" priority, the following actions will be taken:

  • Selection for each field will be sequentially;

  • For each field, selection will be made first on the basis of the grant, then on the basis of the contract;

  • The first directions are suitable for those who are important.

Which priority should be chosen?

  • If the main goal is to enter studies based on a grant, regardless of the direction, then the priority "State grant" is chosen.

  • If If it is important to study in the fields of your choice, even if there is a contract, "Education field" priority is chosen.

Conclusion

The priority of choice is an important factor that determines on what basis and in which field the applicant will study. Each applicant should make a decision based on his wishes, opportunities and priorities.
