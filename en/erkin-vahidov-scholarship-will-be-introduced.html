Today, at the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek, an online creative dialogue dedicated to the work of the hero of Uzbekistan, People's Poet of Uzbekistan Erkin Vahidov was held. Creative meetings have changed the format due to quarantine. The scope of this online dialogue was wider than expected. Scientists from the state of Turkey participated in the event and gave lectures.

Students of the boarding school named after Erkin Vahidov in the city of Margilan also took part in the roundtable discussion. They read from examples of their creativity.

In an online dialogue, professor Avazjon Marahimov, rector of the National University of Uzbekistan, Vali Savashbey Yilok, professor of Haji Bayram University in Ankara, poets, writers, literary scholars - Hamidulla Boltaboyev, Nasimkhan Rahmonov, Nigora Mannopova, director of the boarding school named after Erkin Vahidov in Margilon, and young artists talked about the unique features of the poet's work. During the conversation, the Rector of UzMU, Professor Avazjon Marahimov, emphasized that the work of the poet enriches our spirituality, expands our thinking, gives pleasure to our hearts, and embodies the image of a perfect human being.

During the conversation, the rector said that UzMU will announce grants for the scientific study of Erkin Vahidov's work abroad, that all conditions will be created at UzMU for young Turkish scientists who want to do scientific research of the poet's work, that a special fund will be opened for the translation of Erkin Vahidov's work into other languages, the publication of collections of young artists who continue his traditions, Turkish and other state universities expressed the proposal and initiative of holding international conferences on the study of the poet's work with It was stated that the dialogue will be held every week and everyone can participate in it.
