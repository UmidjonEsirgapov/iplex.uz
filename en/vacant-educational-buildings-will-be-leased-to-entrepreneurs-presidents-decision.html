Educational buildings and rooms underutilized will be rented directly to entrepreneurs - President

President Shavkat Mirziyoyev, during the video selector meeting held to discuss the results of his visit to Bukhara region, educational buildings and rooms underutilized directly to entrepreneurs announced that it will be leased. This decision is aimed at solving the problem of the lack of professional personnel in the labor market. Main directions:

  • Only 2% of young people are trained in the form of dual education and cooperation with only 350 out of 30,000 enterprises in the region was noted. acceptance is less than 50%, which shows that the demand of the labor market and the interests of young people are not sufficiently taken into account in vocational training.
  • The proposal was put forward to rent educational buildings and rooms to entrepreneurs and direct the proceeds to the repair of buildings and the purchase of equipment. The only condition is to train and employ young people here.

At the same time, the task of removing restrictions on the sale of goods produced by young people in schools, colleges, technical institutes and universities on state procurement platforms was also set. — Official Data
