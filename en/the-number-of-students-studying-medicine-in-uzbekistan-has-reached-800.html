Our country has an incomparable opportunity for the development of folk medicine. The fact that more than 750 plants in our country are recommended for use in the pharmaceutical industry is a vivid example of this. This field, which has been neglected for many years, is now reaching a new stage of development. In particular, effective work is being carried out in our country on the cultivation of medicinal plants and their deep processing.

At the moment, more than 500 students are studying at the bachelor level in the field of folk medicine in all higher medical educational institutions of our country. In the next academic year, their number will increase to 800 people.

Another innovation. In the department of "Neurology and folk medicine" of the center for the development of professional qualifications of medical personnel, the training of medical personnel in this field has been launched. Phytotherapy, needle therapy, massage, hirudotherapy methods were put into practice within the scope of specializations. In public and private hospitals, many methods of folk medicine such as hijama, apitherapy, and naturopathy are effectively used. Therefore, in 2023, close cooperation relations will be established with the leading local and foreign medical institutions and health care practice centers in the field. — More information
