Foreign language certificate requirements for bachelor and master's degrees: who does the new procedure apply to?

By the decision of the government Regulation on the procedure for admission to the bachelor's degree of higher education institutions (Ministry of Ministers of September 13, 2025 Decision No. 578) a number of changes were made. These changes have raised the question among applicants whether they apply to the master's degree as well.

The new procedure is valid only for the bachelor's degree

Foreign language requirement in bachelor's programs

According to the introduced changes, admission to bachelor's programs with a foreign language in the subject complex is carried out in the following order:

  • foreign language proficiency

  • at least B1 level

  • on the basis of a national or internationally recognized certificate.

The important point is that this requirement is only for the bachelor level.

This procedure is also established for the master level. does it apply?

Has the B1 requirement for the master's degree been introduced?

No. By the government's decision, only the "Regulation on the procedure of undergraduate admission" has been amended. Therefore, the B1 level certificate requirement is not automatically applied to the master's degree. Foreign language requirements for the master's degree are regulated by separate documents. was announced, according to which:

  • foreign language requirements for entering the master's degree program are differentiated depending on the fields;

  • the only requirement that applied in previous years has been canceled.
