From the academic year of 2025, the admission procedure for doctoral studies may change

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
The order of admission to doctoral studies may change from the next academic year.
According to it, starting from 2025, admission to post-higher education institutions will be carried out according to the sum of points collected on the basis of the relevant data collected in the single electronic system of coordination of post-higher education based on the results of the applicants' scientific activity.
Data collected in the single electronic system: Specialization points - points (grades) collected in relevant subjects at the undergraduate and graduate levels of education; Points on the level of knowledge of foreign languages are classified according to the possession of a national or international certificate that determines the level of knowledge of foreign languages in the relevant fields and areas of education. The draft decree "On the introduction of a fundamentally new system of coordination and their support" was discussed.

According to it, starting from 2025, points will be collected based on the relevant data collected in the Unified Electronic System of Coordination of Post-Higher Education based on the results of scientific activity of applicants to post-higher education institutions.

Information collected in a single electronic system:

  • Points in specialized subjects - points (grades) collected in relevant subjects at the undergraduate and graduate levels of education;
  • Points in the level of knowledge of foreign languages - relevant fields of education

In the single electronic system, research proposal, purpose and expected result of the research work, letters of recommendation, published scientific articles related to the field and other related information are also gathered in the single electronic system. — Details here
