Admission to colleges and technical schools is organized twice a year, the share of the state grant is increased

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
The draft of the state program also provides for the implementation of appropriate measures to improve the system of admission of students to professional educational institutions by August 2023.
According to it: admission of students to colleges and technical institutes will be organized twice a year; by introducing the allocation of state grants based on specialization in technical schools, the share of the total state grant will be increased to 50%; the private sector's personnel needs will be determined in a systematic way.
For information, admission processes to professional educational institutions are carried out based on the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 715 of November 11, 2020.
