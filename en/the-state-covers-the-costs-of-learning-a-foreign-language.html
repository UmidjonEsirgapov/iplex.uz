Motivation is provided by the state to learn a foreign language

A mechanism of state support has been introduced for young people to learn foreign languages. Expenses for young people learning a foreign language in private educational centers are covered in the form of "2+6".

How does it work?

  • The student pays out of his own funds for the first 2 months
  • Next 6 months paid by the state

This program is aimed at expanding language learning opportunities and professional and personal development of young people.
