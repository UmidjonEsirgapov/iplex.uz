China University Scholarship

Where: Chendu, China

Education Location: University of Electronic Science and Technology of China

Deadline: March 21, 2021

The University of Electronic Science and Technology of China is offering scholarships to graduate and postgraduate students.

Scholarship Amount:
First Class Scholarship is a full scholarship that includes tuition, campus living, health insurance and living allowance. Awarded to graduate and postgraduate students.

Secondary Scholarship is a partial scholarship that covers tuition, living on campus, and health insurance. Postgraduate students only.

Third Category Scholarship —a partial scholarship that covers tuition and health insurance. Only for master's students.

Duration of study: 2 years for master's students, 3 years for postgraduate students.

The university has the opportunity to study in English and Chinese. The application is submitted online.
