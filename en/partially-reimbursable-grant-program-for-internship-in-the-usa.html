The POSCO Visiting Fellowships program invites eminent scholars and politicians to conduct policy-related research on political, security, and socioeconomic issues in Northeast Asia. The program is for scholars in the field of korean studies taught at the East-West Center, and participants are provided with a stipend and a travel ticket along with internship and research.

The deadline for applications for this program is December 31.

Apply procedure:

Candidates must submit the following:

  • Fill out the application form;
  • Cover letter indicating the research topic or project for which the candidate is applying;
  • Curriculum vitae/CV (for security reasons, please do not write personal information (for example, date of birth, passport number);
  • three scientific supervisors (refree's) full name, title, mailing address, phone and fax numbers and email address);
  • Research proposal (3 to 5 pages);
