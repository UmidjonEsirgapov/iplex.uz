Cambridge The Trust offers scholarships to international students applying to one of Cambridge University's undergraduate, graduate or doctoral programs each year.

Undergraduate students can receive a partially paid scholarship to study at the University of Cambridge. The main criteria for awarding the scholarship are academic performance and financial need. Competition for scholarships is fierce! Only students enrolled in an undergraduate program who are experiencing financial hardship may apply for a scholarship. Students cannot apply directly to the fund, only accepted students can apply for this scholarship.

85% of the grants are awarded to international master and doctorate students. The purpose of the scholarship is to provide full support to 250 foreign students, regardless of their nationality, to conduct research to obtain a doctorate degree. Students do not apply for a scholarship directly to the fund, the application for the scholarship is submitted at the same time as the application for admission to the university.

Benefits:

  • This scholarship covers up to 100% of the tuition contract fee.

Requirements:

  • Students of any country are eligible for the scholarship. can apply for it;
  • Must meet Cambridge University admission requirements and be accepted;
  • Must have high academic standing;
  • Bachelor students must apply by October 15th;
  • Masters students can apply until December 2nd.
