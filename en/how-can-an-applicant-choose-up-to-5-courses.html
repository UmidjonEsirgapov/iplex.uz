July 25 was the last day of the test exams for admission to the bachelor's degree of the state HEIs.

Today the results of the last day will be announced and the results of all applicants will be known. After that, applicants are given the opportunity to choose a field.

When applicants are given the right to choose a field, they can set the state grant as a priority.

Procedure for choosing a field

In this case, the applicant chooses a state grant as a priority:

  • If he chooses a state grant, he will first receive a state grant in all the educational fields of his choice, then selected on the basis of a payment-contract;
  • If he chooses an educational field, he first participates in a sequential selection based on a state grant and a payment-contract in each field of education.

For example, if someone says "it doesn't matter which of the five directions I enter, the main thing is that I need to enter the state grant", then he gives priority to the grant. On the other hand, if someone says, "I have to enter my first chosen field, I agree even if I sign a contract," then he gives priority to the field. — Source
