Will students be involved in training?

No. On the basis of the letter of the Ministry of Pre-school and School Education No. 03-33-3453 dated November 22, 2024:

  • Pedagogues studying at a higher education institution and simultaneously working at a school are not involved in professional development courses.

This means that if you are working at a school, but are still in higher education, you do not need to attend professional development courses.
