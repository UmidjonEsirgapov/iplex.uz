Applicants for the directorship of the technical school will be selected based on an open competition - Presidential Decree

According to the Presidential Decree, From January 1, 2026, the requirements for middle-level personnel will be formed through the "Vocational Education" platform. These changes are being implemented in order to improve the quality of the professional education system and introduce international education standards.

New procedures:

  • Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, international education programs will be introduced in technical schools.
  • Those who studied on the basis of international education programs and successfully passed the exams students will be given international certificate and state diploma.
  • From January 1, 2025, candidates for the positions of director and deputy director of technical schools will be selected through an open competition.

Maintaining a unified state policy in the vocational education system responsibility was assigned to the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovations.
