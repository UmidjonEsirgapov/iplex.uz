7 advantages of online education

Today the whole world has faced the problem of introducing online education. Due to the coronavirus pandemic, distance learning is taking place from schoolchildren to university students. Someone welcomed this with joy, someone with a critical eye. True, online education has some disadvantages, but today let's talk about its advantages.

Convenience in terms of time

You can choose online courses according to your busy schedule. You can practice anywhere, anytime. Almost all educational online platforms work on mobile devices. You can listen to a lecture with a cup of coffee in comfortable clothes. No need to spend time on the road. Such small things will save you from excessive stress. You can spend the extra time and energy on other useful and interesting things.

They do not lag behind in terms of quality

According to research, online education is more traditional than traditional indicators. they do not fall short of education in terms of quality. 77% of American university teachers believe that online education is equal to traditional education in terms of quality, and sometimes even better than it. The effectiveness of the course depends mainly on the listener: distance education requires more discipline and personal control.

Various convenient tools: presentations, tests, videos, chat, screen demonstration and the ability to watch the lesson repeatedly ensure high efficiency of education.
Organizers of online education can invite teachers and professionals from different cities and countries to lectures or workshops. This increases the reputation and quality of education and diploma.

Interesting and fun

Online educational tools help to make the lesson interesting. It is especially convenient to use for exact sciences. Interactive activities, gameplay, achievements and competitions immerse students in the learning process. In addition, communication in online classes is less formal, the student feels comfortable and less stressed, which is especially important for tests and supervision.

Affordable
All parents want to educate their children, but not everyone can afford to send them to a prestigious school or pay for private lessons. Group online classes can replace expensive tutoring services.

Suitable and convenient for everyone

Online education allows everyone to study. Some may not be able to attend school, university and courses due to illness, business trips, childbirth, remoteness of their home, physical disabilities. In such cases, it is possible to get a good education, acquire a new profession and necessary skills through the Internet.

Democratization of education
Good educational platforms allow to educate millions of students around the world. There is absolutely no question of running out of space in the class. Even if one group is formed, it is not a problem to find a new one.

Equality

Non-existence of discrimination among students based on race, religion, gender, nationality, age, clothing, appearance is another advantage of distance education. All participants can participate equally. There will be no student who talks too much and does not give others a word. Online lessons are especially good for introverts and students who spend a lot of time understanding the material. — Source
