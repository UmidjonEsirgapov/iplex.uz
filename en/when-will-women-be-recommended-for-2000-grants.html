When will women be recommended for 2000 grants?

Useful information for applicants and students:

In the framework of 2000 grants (4% concession) allocated by the state, the process of issuing recommendations to women in needy families has been announced. Applications are accepted electronically through the xotin-qizlar.uz platform.

When are applications for recommendations accepted?

  • Application submission period: from May 30 to June 8 (also accepted on this day).
  • Applications can be submitted electronically through the xotin-qizlar.uz platform.

Who will receive the recommendation?

  • Girls from low-income families
  • Girls with one or both parents dead
  • Women from needy families with disabled children
  • Single women with 2 or more children under 14 years of age to daughters
  • To girls whose parents are disabled
  • To mothers who are raising their children alone

When will recommendations be issued?

  • Applications will be considered until June 10.
  • Recommendations will be submitted electronically until June 14 will be prepared.
  • Recommendations will be delivered to all applicants by June 15.
