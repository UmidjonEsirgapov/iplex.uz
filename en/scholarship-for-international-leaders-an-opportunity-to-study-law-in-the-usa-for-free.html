Scholarship for International Leaders - established by Widener University Delaware Law School, located in the state of Delaware, USA, offers the most advanced and knowledgeable students the opportunity to pursue a master's degree in one of the following 3 areas of law without contract fees:

  • Master of Law
  • Master of Jurisprudence
  • Doctor of Juridical Science

For candidates requirements:

For those in the field of Law (Master of Law):

  • Bachelor's degree in law at a university other than the US universities
  • GPA 3.0 or equivalent must be
  • Language level - TOEFL 83 or IELTS 6.5

For Master of Jurisprudence:

  • Candidates must have completed a bachelor's degree at an international or US university
  • Grades with a grade point average of 3.3 or equivalent
  • Language level - TOEFL 83 or IELTS 6.5

Doctor of Juridical science

  • Must have completed a bachelor's degree at a university in the United States or another region that meets American standards
  • The average grade should be 3.3 points or equivalent
  • Language level - TOEFL 94 or IELTS 7

In addition, candidates should be socially active, volunteer activities, regularly participate in various events, conferences are required to have leadership and public speaking skills. More information is available at this link.

Benefits:

  • Candidates receive free graduate education during the program
  • Approximately per year for living expenses $50,000 will be provided with funds

But the program does not cover expenses such as accommodation, food, insurance, and travel, so the candidates must solve these issues themselves. Read more at this link.

The application procedure is different for each track, and the candidate must first apply separately for the courses and then separately for the grant. The application fee is $60. If you want to know more about the application process, go to this link.
