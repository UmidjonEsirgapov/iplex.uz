Admission in non-state HEIs can be twice a year

New draft decision under discussion

Government's "Improving the quality of non-state education services in the Republic The draft decision "On measures" was submitted for public discussion. According to the project, the procedure for accepting applicants in non-state higher education institutions (HEIs) can be introduced twice a year - until October 15 and until February 15.

Through this offer, applicants will be given the opportunity to get an education and be given the right to choose a more suitable time for admission.

Admission parameters and additional requirements

Agreement with the Ministry

The draft decision stipulates that non-state educational institutions must set admission parameters in agreement with the Ministry.

Compliance with state requirements

Admission of applicants, transfer of studies and certification of graduates is carried out on the basis of the requirements set for state HEIs. This serves to bring the quality of education closer to state standards.

Information system and integration

Unified management system

Information system that manages educational, scientific and administrative processes will be introduced for non-state HEIs. This system should be integrated with HEMIS - higher education process management information system.

If students are not registered in this system, they will not be awarded a diploma of the state model.

Additional license requirements

Infrastructure required

According to the decision, operating non-state HEIs 5 must have property rights of buildings and structures within a year. Otherwise, it will not be possible to obtain an additional license for the provision of educational services. This requirement does not apply to HEIs established based on the Presidential decrees.
