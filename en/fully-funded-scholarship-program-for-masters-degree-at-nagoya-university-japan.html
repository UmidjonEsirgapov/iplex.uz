Fully Funded Scholarship Program for Master's Degree at Nagoya University, Japan

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Summary:
This scholarship is awarded by the Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology of Japan and covers all the student's expenses (tuition fee, air ticket, monthly stipend of USD 1400).

Nagoya University's Japanese Government Program in Law and Political Science The master program has started accepting applications for the scholarship.

This scholarship is allocated by the Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology of Japan and covers all the student's expenses (tuition fee, air ticket, monthly stipend of 1400 US dollars).

In the program, all training sessions are conducted in English, and depending on the student's desire, Japanese language lessons are also included.

Original documents will be accepted by Nagoya University from November 25 to December 1, 2021r. In order to send documents to Japan on time, candidates are required to submit all documents to Nagoya University's representative office in Uzbekistan by November 19, 2021.

You can download the necessary documents from the following link:

https://www.law.nagoya-u.ac.jp/en/admissions_and_programs/masters.html

For application requirements and detailed information, you can contact the representative office of Nagoya University in Uzbekistan.
