Is there a discount in the super-contract for those who do not reach 4 points?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
A 10 percent discount is given to applicants who paid the full amount of the differentiated (super) payment contract this year by October 15, 2023 (❗️this discount has nothing to do with ordinary contracts).
But this discount, It applies to applicants who scored more than 56.7 points, but fell short of the entrance score by 4.05 points and dropped out.
I would like to remind you that the scale of the super-contract up to 4 points is as follows: for applicants who do not reach the passing score by 1.05 points - 1.5 times the amount of the basic payment-contract; from 1.06 points to 2.05 points - 2 times; from 2.06 points to 3.05 points - 2.5 times; From 3.06 points to 4.05 points - 3 times.

No discount is given to those with up to 4 points.
