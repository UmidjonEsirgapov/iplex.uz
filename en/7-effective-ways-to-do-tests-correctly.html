7 effective ways to do tests correctly

Not only knowledge, but also the right approach and strategy are important to achieve high results in the tests. Below you can get acquainted with 7 main methods to effectively perform exam tests.

1. Read the question and condition carefully

The question may contain additional conditions, so try to understand the question completely without jumping to the answer options. Some questions can be misleading, paying attention to the terms will prevent mistakes.

2. Answer the easy questions first

When the test starts, do the easy questions first. This allows you to:

  • Understand the structure of the exam and the complexity of the questions
  • Build confidence and avoid excessive stress.

3. Determine the answer in your mind, then look at the options

Read the question and try to find the correct answer yourself without looking at the options. Then check the answer options and compare your opinion. This method reduces the possibility of being distracted by wrong options.

4. Read all answer choices

Sometimes an answer choice may seem right but could be more correct. Therefore, all answers should be carefully considered.

5. Use the "Elimination" method

If you don't know the exact answer, reduce the range of choices by eliminating wrong answers. It will be easier to choose the most correct answer among the remaining options.

6. If there is an option "All answers are correct", pay attention

If there is an option "All answers are correct" among the answer options, and you know at least two correct answers, the probability of choosing this option is high.

7. Answer each question

In the DTM system, there is no penalty for wrong answers, i.e. no points are deducted for wrong answers. Therefore, please mark the answers to all questions. This increases your probability of finding the right answer.

Conclusion

A strategic approach is needed to pass the tests successfully. Reading the questions correctly, excluding the wrong answers and marking the answers to all the questions increases the chances of getting a high score in the exam. — Read on infoedu.uz
