An undergraduate course in e-sports will be opened. Study period is 3 years!

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Shavkat Mirziyoyev signed the decision on the development of e-sports in Uzbekistan.
According to the relevant decision of the President, from 2023 on the traditional types of e-sports: "Uzbekistan Esports Championship" republican championship in April every year; every year in December the "Cup of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan" tournament; Every year in June, the "Youth Cup" tournament is held.
Uzbekistan State University of Physical Education and Sports will open the "Sports Activity: Cybersport" undergraduate education course from the 2023-2024 academic year, and the duration of study in this course is set to 3 years.
