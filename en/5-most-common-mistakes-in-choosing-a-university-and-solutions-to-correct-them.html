5 Most Common Mistakes in Choosing a University and Solutions to Correct Them

Choosing a university is just a document. not handing over is one of the most important decisions of your life. Many applicants make small mistakes in this process, and as a result, they end up in a field that is not suitable for them or lose the opportunity altogether. Below are the 5 most common mistakes among applicants and ways to correct them. Choosing only based on the ranking

Most applicants look only at the ranking when choosing a university. Of course, the rating is important, but it is not the only criterion. Even the top-ranked university in a field you are not interested in will not satisfy you.

Identify your field of interest.

First, identify your strengths, interests, and future career goals. Then look for HEIs that are strong in that area. Also check graduate employment rates.

2. Not taking into account the financial side

Many people do not calculate the contract, accommodation, food and other expenses in advance. As a result, financial problems arise after the start of studies.

Make a spending plan.

Make a spending plan in advance. To do this, write down the contract price of your chosen university, hostel or rent fees, daily expenses and other payments you will need during the year. Another useful way is to compare contract prices and grant opportunities at different universities. For example, on educational platforms such as mentalaba.uz, you can find general information about directions, prices and scholarship conditions. This will help you plan more clearly.

3. Not preparing a backup option

It is a big risk to apply to only one university and not think about another option. As a result, if you miss one opportunity, you will have to wait a year.

Choose several options.

For example, through Mentalaba.uz, you can enter private and international partner universities in Uzbekistan without an exam with the result of DTM, and apply before the deadline. This gives you multiple logins at once.

4. View only local HEIs

Many applicants overlook study abroad opportunities. In fact, foreign universities often offer high-quality education and internationally recognized degrees.

Check out foreign opportunities.

You can get admission to universities in Turkey, Germany, Latvia, UAE, China and other countries with up to 100% grants through iApply.org. It provides not only education, but also the opportunity to learn a language and discover a new culture.

5. Limiting yourself to the test result

The idea that "it's enough if I don't pass the DTM" is a big mistake. There are many ways to get an education, you just need to be aware of them.

Expand your opportunities.

Private HEIs, foreign universities, online bachelor's programs, vocational courses - all this will give you the opportunity to get your future profession.
