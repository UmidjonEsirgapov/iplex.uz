Why is the set of subjects for entrance exams not released at the beginning of the academic year?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
The Ministry of Higher Education must announce the set of subjects that will determine which subject will be examined in the entrance exams.
Last year, this set was approved and announced at the end of December. It is said that it will be approved by and announced at least 6 months before the beginning of the exam tests.

The set of subjects that will be taken in which subject in the entrance exams should be announced by the Ministry of Higher Education.

Last year, this complex was approved and announced at the end of December.

The complex of examination subjects in law is approved by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation and is announced at least 6 months before the start of the exams.

❗️That is, "at least 6 months in advance", it is also possible to announce earlier, for example, as soon as the new school year begins in September. Applicants also start their preparation from this moment, in fact. It will be announced in the middle of the year and if subjects change in some areas, it will be difficult for the applicant.
