Pharmaceutical Technical University (Pharmaceutical Technical University) is a new state university established in cooperation with Sunderland and De Montfort Universities of Great Britain within the innovative research and production pharmaceutical cluster "Tashkent Pharma Park".

The university currently offers higher education in 3 directions is accepting applicants for the programs:

  • Course of general education in pharmaceutical sciences (Foundation in Certificate Pharmaceutical Science);
  • Course of general education in biological sciences (Foundation Certificate in Biosciences)
  • Course of general education in natural sciences (International Foundation year in Sciences)

After the general education stage, students continue their education in Biochemistry (BSc Biochemistry), Biomedicine (BSc Biomedical Science) or Pharmacy (MSci Pharmacy).

Educational processes are taught in English by professors with international qualifications and scientific experience Conducted in accordance with British higher education standards. University graduates will have diplomas from the UK universities mentioned above.

Applicants must have the following requirements:

  • secondary education certificate
  • high school or college diploma;
  • IELTS 5.5 or higher, or an equivalent international certificate.
