In 2025 What will be the evaluation criteria for transferring studies?

Tests for transferring studies from foreign higher educational institutions branches and non-state universities to state universities will be held on August 15-20. In this process, the evaluation criteria are defined as follows:

Test on compulsory subjects

All test-takers will pass the exam on the following 3 compulsory subjects:

  • Native language (Uzbek, Russian or Karakalpak language) – 10 questions.
    • 1.1 points each
    • Maximum score – 11 points
  • Mathematics – 10 questions.
    • 1.1 points each
    • Maximum score - 11 points
  • History of Uzbekistan - 10 questions.
    • Each 1.1 points
    • Maximum score – 11 points

Total maximum score – 33 points

Test of specialization subjects

Examination of 2 specialization subjects corresponding to undergraduate education submitted:

  • Science 1 – 30 questions.
    • 3.1 points each
    • Maximum score – 93 points
  • Subject 2 – 30 questions.
    • Each 2.1 points
    • Maximum points - 63 points

Total maximum points - 156 points

General calculation

Students who want to transfer their studies in the 2024/2025 academic year A total of 90 test tasks should be completed in 5 subjects. The maximum possible score is 189 points. — Source
