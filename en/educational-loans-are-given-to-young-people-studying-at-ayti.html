Educational loans will be allocated to young people studying in the field of information technologies in non-state educational organizations.

By the government decision (No. 79, 27.02.2023), education of young people in the field of information technologies at the expense of the Fund for Financing Education under the Ministry of Economy and Finance the regulation on the procedure for allocating educational loans has been approved.

According to the regulations, educational loans in the amount of up to 65 times the BHM are allocated to persons who are 14 years old and under 30 years of age with a 1-year grace period of up to 4 years. consists of: — Source

  • application;
  • a formalized agreement (contract, public offer) between the borrower and non-state educational organizations resident in the IT park on training courses in the field of information technologies;
  • a document on the provision of educational loan repayment. This does not require a pledge or guarantee for the education loan allocated to young people with children from families included in the "Single Register of Social Protection";
  • a certificate confirming inclusion in the "Single Register of Social Protection" (for young people with children from families included in the "Single Register of Social Protection");
  • other documents specified in the internal documents (policy) of the bank, including information on the joint borrower and documents.
