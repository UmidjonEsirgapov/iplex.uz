Fergana Public Health Medical Institute is being established

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
The President signed the decision "On the establishment of the Fergana Public Health Medical Institute".
The text of the document was published by UzA.
Ministry of Health, Ministry of Economic Development and Poverty Alleviation, Higher and Secondary Special of the Ministry of Education and Fergana Regional Government: establishment of the Fergana Public Health Medical Institute on the basis of the Fergana branch of the Tashkent Medical Academy; Proposals to designate the hospital of infectious diseases of Fergana region as the clinical base of the Institute were approved.

The President signed the decision "On the establishment of the Fergana Public Health Medical Institute". The text of the document was published by UzA.

The Ministry of Health, the Ministry of Economic Development and Poverty Alleviation, the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education, and the Fergana Region Hokimat:

Establishing the Fergana Public Health Medical Institute on the basis of the Fergana branch of the Tashkent Medical Academy; proposals were approved.

The following were defined as the main directions of the Institute's activity:

  • organization of training of doctors in narrow fields of specialization for health care and sanitary-epidemiological peace and public health institutions;
  • based on joint education and academic mobility programs in cooperation with leading foreign medical higher education institutions. personnel training;
  • wide implementation of advanced and distance learning technologies in the process of training specialists with modern knowledge and skills, attracting highly qualified professors and teachers, developing and implementing electronic educational resources;
  • supporting the development of scientific and pedagogical potential, actively improving pedagogical technologies and teaching methods;
  • health care, sanitary-epidemiological peace and implementation of promising innovative, practical and fundamental scientific-research works on the issues of public health, disease prevention, parasitology, microbiology, virology, development of a healthy lifestyle, as well as focusing their results on the solution of existing problems in medical practice;
  • ensuring the continuity and consistency of the programs of the attached Abu Ali Ibn Sina Public Health Technical Colleges and Medical Colleges, effectively organizing their activities, providing scientific and methodological support
  • strengthening and developing long-term cooperation in the fields of education and science with leading medical organizations and scientific research centers of foreign countries;
  • modernizing and strengthening the material and technical base, equipping the simulation center, training and research laboratories with modern equipment.

  • The institute constantly provides scientific and methodological support to teachers who conduct classes in specific subjects at Abu Ali Ibn Sina public health technical schools and medical colleges in Fergana region.
