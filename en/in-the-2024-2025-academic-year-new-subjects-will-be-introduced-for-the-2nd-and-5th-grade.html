The Ministry of Preschool and School Education approved the basic curriculum for the 2024-2025 school year. This new plan includes a number of important changes in the education system.

Introduction of new subjects

Starting from the 2024-2025 academic year, students of the 2nd grade will begin to study the subject "Informatics and information technologies". This subject aims to teach children the basics of computer technology and programming from an early age. Also, the subject "Science" is introduced for 5th grade students. Through this subject, students get acquainted with the basics of natural sciences such as biology, chemistry, physics and geography.

Teacher training

To teach these subjects, teachers of "Biology" and "Geography" subjects participate in short-term training courses. These courses give them the necessary knowledge and skills to effectively teach new subjects to students.

Lesson distribution based on the curriculum

Lesson distribution for all classes in the new school year is based on the approved basic curriculum. This plan was developed in order to provide students with modern knowledge and improve the quality of education. This plan is aimed at harmonizing the educational process with modern technologies.
