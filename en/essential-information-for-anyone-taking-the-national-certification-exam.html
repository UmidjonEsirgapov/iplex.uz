Essential information for anyone taking the National Certification Exam

REMINDER TO THE APPLICANT

1. On the day of the test, the applicant must arrive with the original identity document (civil passport) no later than the time specified in the permit.

2. An applicant whose identity is not confirmed and who arrives late will not be allowed to take the test.

3. It is strictly forbidden to bring any manuals, literature, telecommunications and technical equipment into the building and the auditorium where the tests will be held. It is forbidden to negatively influence the testing process by various behaviors: communicating with other candidates, tearing the pages of the question book, giving the question book to other candidates, continuing to write after the time allotted for the test, passing any item to another candidate. An applicant who does not comply with these requirements will be excluded from the test.

4. It is forbidden to bring outerwear, bags, folders, etc. into the audience.

5. It is forbidden to open the question book before the auditor in charge of the audience announces the start of the test.

6. It is forbidden to ask questions to the responsible observer during the test (all questions about the conduct of the tests are asked to the responsible observer before the start of the exam).

7. It is forbidden to leave the audience without the permission of the responsible observer.

8. Taking test materials out of the auditorium is prohibited.

9. If you believe that you have been given the wrong test materials, or if the test tasks are defective, immediately inform the invigilator in charge of the auditorium before the test begins.

10. Additional time will not be assigned to a candidate who has to leave the group during the test.

11. Compare the numbers of the answer sheets (test and written work), they should be the same. If the numbers are different, contact the supervisor in charge of the audience.

12. If your health deteriorates during the tests, immediately notify the supervisor in charge of the auditorium.

13. If the applicant is late or absent from the test, 50 percent of the fee will be refunded.

14. In the course of the test, the applicant is allowed to take only one general education test at a time.

RESULTS OF THE TEST TESTS

1. The certificate is issued for a period of 3 years to persons who have scored at least 60% of the highest points in the test results. 2. The results of the test will be published on the official website of the State Test Center no later than twenty working days from the date of their completion. 3. An applicant with a certificate will be given a graded (proportional) score based on the percentage of mastery (60 and above) in the certificate in the tests for admission to the bachelor's degree of a higher educational institution compared to the maximum score determined for this subject. That is, a certain percentage of the maximum score is given depending on the subject in which the certificate is obtained, depending on the place of the subject in the complex of subjects. For example, if the mastery of the certificate of the applicant with a certificate in mathematics is 60 percent, if the place of mathematics in the complex of subjects is the first (3.2 points for each correct answer, a maximum of 96 points for 30 test tasks), then it is 60 percent. It is 57.6 points (3.2*30=96 points, 96*60/100=57.6). If the applicant's mastery of the certificate is 80 percent, it is 76.8 points (3.2*30=96 points, 96*80/100=76.8).

4. The certificate will be issued in electronic form within one week from the date of announcement of the test results to the applicant who has achieved a positive result (60% and above) in the test. The applicant can download the certificate through his personal cabinet. — More information
