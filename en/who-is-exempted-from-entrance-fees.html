Who is exempted from entrance fees?

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
Graduates of academic lyceums and vocational schools graduating in 2023 do not pay fees.
In addition, graduates of certain categories are exempt from paying for tests, even if they are graduates of previous years.
Persons with disabilities, fully state-supported, included in the unified register of social protection, and their children or young people included in the "Youth Register" are also exempted from paying for the tests.
