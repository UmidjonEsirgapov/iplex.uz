English language courses based on international standards

The importance of a foreign language certificate for entering higher education institutions is increasing day by day. "Mentor" training center will closely support you not only in preparation for OTM, but also in obtaining internationally recognized certificates.

IELTS courses: Open the doors to the world

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is a way to study and work abroad, as well as in Uzbekistan is the main ticket for admission to prestigious universities (WIUT, Turin, Akfa, etc.).

IELTS classes in "Mentor" are conducted in the following order:

  • Listening: A method of understanding different accents and quickly catching key words.

  • Reading: Fast reading of texts (Skimming & Scanning) and ways to find clear answers to questions.

  • Writing: Golden rules for writing academic essays (Task 1 & Task 2).

  • Speaking: Regular Speaking Club classes for speaking freely and fluently.

CEFR courses: Privileged with national certificate be

For applicants applying to universities of Uzbekistan CEFR (level B2 or C1) certificate gives the opportunity to get the maximum score (without exam) in the subject of English language.

Mentor training center The CEFR preparatory course includes:

  • Special program according to the State Test Center (DTM) standard.

  • Systematic development of grammar and vocabulary.

  • Continuous practice tests to feel the atmosphere of a real exam.
