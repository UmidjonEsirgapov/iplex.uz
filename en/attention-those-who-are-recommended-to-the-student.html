The final results (mandate) of entrance tests to state higher education institutions for the 2025/2026 academic year have been announced. Now, applicants recommended for studentship can determine their place of study and apply for accommodation in student accommodation.

Procedure for accommodation in student accommodation

Possibilities to submit an electronic application

Those recommended for studentship can submit an electronic application for accommodation using state services. For this:

  • Apply to the State Services Center, or

  • Send an online application through the Unified Interactive State Services Portal (YIDXP) — my.gov.uz.

Registration and authorization

We remind you that the applicant is on the YIDXP portal Must be registered or authorized. After that, an electronic request is sent through the system and student housing is allocated to students accordingly.

Conclusion

The process of accommodation for students admitted to state HEIs is now much simplified. Now they can be done quickly and conveniently through the my.gov.uz portal or the State Services Center. — Read on infoedu.uz
