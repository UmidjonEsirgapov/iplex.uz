The process of admission to clinical residency (residency) in higher medical educational institutions and their branches will continue until August 20. According to the relevant order of the Minister of Health, the process of accepting documents for the 2024-2025 academic year has begun.

General information about the admission process:

  • Admission will continue: until August 20, 2024 (also on this day)
  • Admission will be carried out: Higher medical education institutions, their branches, the center for the development of professional qualifications of medical personnel, republican specialized scientific and practical medical centers and research institutes

Document submission procedure:

  • Registration: Directly and online, through the official websites of organizations
  • List of documents:
    • An application to the head of a higher education institution or a non-state medical institution, specifying the specialty of a clinical residency
    • A document on higher education or an extract from a diploma
    • Medical certificate in the form of 086-U
    • A document granting admission to study (if any)

Admission plan:

By the relevant order of the Minister of Health, "General and targeted admission plan for clinical residency (residency) in the 2024-2025 academic year in the field of higher medical educational institutions and their branches, the Center for the Development of Professional Qualifications of Medical Workers, specialized scientific and practical medical centers of the republic, research institutes" was approved.
