Eligible students for the grant:

To receive the Ball State University International Honors grant, students must have a cumulative GPA of 2.5. The scholarship will not be renewed if the GPA falls below 2.5 at the end of the spring semester of any academic year. However, the award may be reinstated upon request if the cumulative GPA rises to 2.5 at the end of the fall or summer semester. The award is for a maximum of eight undergraduate semesters.
