Global Peace Summit in Baku

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
The Global Peace Chain organization is organizing the Global Peace Summit Baku 2022 in Azerbaijan for young leaders from different countries of the world to achieve the 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals by 2030. Global Peace Chain is a world peace organization in 2018. Founded on July 2nd, headquartered in the USA. This summit provides an opportunity to share experiences/solutions on peace, social networks, international organizations, human rights issues through various sessions, talks, panel discussions and activities with international delegates. For whom? Ambitious students and young leaders aged 16-40. When: May 29, 2022 - June 1. Funding: Fully funded by organizers (15 grants), partially funded (25 grants) and self-funded (110 places). Language of communication: English. Application deadline: April 29, 2022. Details.
