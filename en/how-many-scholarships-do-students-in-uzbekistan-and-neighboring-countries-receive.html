How are stipends paid in Uzbekistan?

A basic stipend is given to students who study full-time on the basis of a state grant and a scholarship payment contract. 20% will be added to the scholarship of students who have completed the semester only "excellently" (above 86%).

However, students with 30% or more of the indicators of mastering subjects at the end of the academic semester with a "satisfactory" grade (below 71%) will not be paid a scholarship during the next semester.

The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation told Kun.uz according to the information given, the amount of the basic scholarship paid to students is 517,880 soums. A scholarship for students with an "excellent" mastery index (a rating of 86 points and above) in all subjects - 621,456 soums.
