Scholarship to study at the University of Gothenburg in Sweden

Duration: January 15, 2021
Place of study: Gothenburg, Sweden

The University of Gothenburg offers the Axel Adler Scholarship to graduate students. Citizens of EU/non-EU countries can apply for the scholarship. The scholarship is funded by the Swedish Higher Education Council and covers the full cost of a master's degree at the University of Gothenburg.

Number of scholarships: 60 for the Master of Humanities and Master of Science and 4 in the fields of art, applied and performing arts.

The amount of the scholarship. The scholarship covers the full tuition fee for the master's program. No additional financial support will be provided in the form of living and transportation expenses.

Requirements:
• Applicants must be enrolled in a graduate program at the above university by January 17, 2020.
• The scholarship will be awarded to students with excellent academic performance. Preference will be given to candidates who have chosen the University of Gothenburg.
• The registration fee must be paid by February 1, 2022.
