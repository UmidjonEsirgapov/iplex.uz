A mayor's grant for start-ups and IT specialists was launched in Khorezm

The "Mayor's Grant" contest was launched for startups and IT specialists of Khorezm region. This was reported by the "IT Park" press service.

It is aimed at supporting projects in the field of IT education, software development and export.

Candidates can send applications in the following directions:

  • science and education;
  • energy saving;
  • social-economic;
  • agriculture;
  • information technologies;
  • development of artificial intelligence technologies;
  • industry;
  • health care.

Competitions for new startups are organized in the following categories:

  • "Socially significant innovations"
  • "Technostart";
  • "Youngest Scientist";
  • "Best Innovative Project";
  • "Promising Development".

Young people who are 18 years old and under 30 years old can participate in the competition.

Candidates must fill out the prescribed application form.

The competition participant the application is evaluated according to the novelty, social significance, purposefulness and purposefulness of the business idea, as well as practical experience.

The winners will be financially encouraged as follows from the additional sources of the local budget of Khorezm region:

  • 1st place - 100 million soums;
  • 2nd place - 80 million soums;
  • 3rd place - 60 million soums.
