✅ On January 29, 2023, national certificate exams in the subject of mother tongue (Uzbek, Russian, Karakalpak) and literature were held. About 5,000 applicants took part in this test, and their written work is currently being checked, the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency reports.

Checking written work is a time-consuming process, and now efforts are being made to complete this process as soon as possible. The decision of the Cabinet of Ministers No. 646 stipulates that the results of the test will be announced within twenty working days.

Therefore, it is planned to complete the process of examination of written works by February 24 and announce the results of the test on February 24. — Official Data
