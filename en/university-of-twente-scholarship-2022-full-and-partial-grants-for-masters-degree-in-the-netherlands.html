University of Twente Scholarship 2022: Full and partial grants for master's degree in the Netherlands

The latest education news is as follows:

Summary:
The application deadline for qualified UT graduate programs starting in the 2022/2023 academic year (September) is May 1; Candidates must have your student number (studentnumber - given after entering the master's stage, we cannot apply for a grant without this number).
Candidates must not have previously completed (undergraduate) master's programs at this university; Academic IELTS 6.5 (or TOEFL iBT 90) and at least 6.0 in speaking; The University of Twente Scholarship is a scholarship for students with high academic performance.
See general information about the approximate living costs of studying at the University of Twente (excluding tuition fees).

General Information:

The University Twente Scholarship (UTS): is a European application for a master's program at the University of Twente. A scholarship program is offered to students from non-EU/EEA countries (except Uzbekistan) with high academic performance. Students will receive a scholarship from EUR 3,000 to EUR 22,000 for one year.

Requirements for candidates:

  • Initially, candidates must have received an acceptance letter to the University of Twente for master's degree.
  • Qualified UT master's degree starting in the academic year 2022/2023 (September). the deadline for applying for the programs is May 1;
  • Candidates must have your student number (studentnumber - given after entering the master's stage, we cannot apply for a grant without this number).
  • Candidates must not have previously completed (bachelor's) master's programs at this university;
  • Academic IELTS 6.5 (or TOEFL iBT 90) and speaking minimum 6.0;
  • The University of Twente scholarship is a scholarship for students with high academic performance.
  • Netherlands students cannot apply;

Scholarship amount:

  • €3,000 - €22,000 per year (about 50 scholarships available).
  • At the University of Twente see an overview of the estimated living costs of study (excluding tuition fees).
  • This scholarship is intended to cover study-related costs. The student decides how to spend the money. No expenses (such as tuition fees) will be paid on your behalf.

Note: This university mainly offers 1-year and 2-year master's programs. If you want the scholarship to be transferred to the 2nd year:

  • An average score of 7 (out of 10 points) at the end of the third quarter of the first year.
  • At the end of the third quarter of the first year, you must have received at least 50% of European credits;

Application deadline - stage 1:

  • Application form opens on 15 October 2021 at 09:00 CET
  • Application forms close on 1 February 2022 at 16:00 CET (for programs starting in September 2022)

Application deadline - Stage 2:

  • Application form opens on 1 February 2022 at 16:00 CET
  • Application form closes on 1 May 2022 at 16:00 CET (for programs starting in September 2022)

Application application:

You must first apply for a graduate program . After applying for the master's program and receiving a (conditional) acceptance letter, you can apply for a scholarship with your student number.

Please read the detailed official information before applying.
