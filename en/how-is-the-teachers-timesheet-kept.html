How is the teacher's time sheet kept?
Teachers' time sheet is usually assigned to the deputy director of academic affairs.
Based on this journal, the monthly work time sheet is filled.

How is the teacher's working time table kept?

Teachers' working time table is important for determining and correct registration of their pedagogical workload, arrival and departure times. This process is carried out on the basis of the internal procedures of the school and the established legal requirements.

Who keeps the timesheet?

Maintenance of teachers' working hours is usually entrusted to the deputy director for educational affairs. If the deputy director of educational affairs is not at work, this task is assigned to the deputy director of spiritual and educational affairs or personnel manager (inspector).

How is the time table kept? will be done. On the basis of this journal, the monthly working time registration form is filled out. Also, in cases where teachers do not come to work for a known reason, they are formalized using the signs corresponding to the sign:
  • T - on vacation
  • K - on the form of incapacity for work
  • X - on a business trip
  • M - on training
  • D - rest day
  • B is a holiday

Pedagogical employees working and non-working days are determined only on the basis of supporting documents (vacation orders, incapacity for work forms, business trip documents, etc.). filling is the responsibility of the Deputy Director of Academic Affairs. Also, if the teacher has a certificate of incapacity for work, the employee must submit this document to the personnel service on the first day of work.
