"Future scientist" competition: a competition supporting STARTAP projects of young scientists

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Today, within the framework of the "Future Scientist" competition announced by the Youth Academy, the deadline for accepting applications for start-up projects has been extended until October 10.
The purpose of the "Future Scientist" competition is to: talented and talented young scientists In order to support the innovative results and initiatives of the state administration, to create the necessary conditions for scientific research, it is all about attracting young people with potential to science.
Conditions of the submitted start-up project: The age of the project executors should not exceed 40 years, regardless of their academic level, title, position and workplace; Participation of 1 team with 1 project (the number of team members should not exceed 5 people); The amount of implementation of the commissioned start-up project should not exceed the amount of the Regulation approved by Annex 2 of the Cabinet of Ministers Resolution No. 222 dated April 19, 2021.

Youth Academy announced the 2nd season of the "Future Scientist" contest. As part of the "Future Scientist" competition announced by the Youth Academy today, the deadline for accepting applications for startup projects has been extended until October 10. The purpose of the "Future Scientist" competition is to support the innovative results and initiatives of talented and talented young scientists by the state administration, which is necessary for scientific research. in order to create conditions, it is all about attracting potential young people to science.

Applications for STARTAP projects of young scientists are accepted within the framework of the Future Scientist Competition

Startup project — innovative developments that create a new type of product or service in conditions of high risk of implementation. a new business project that is distinguished by its application, scale and rapid growth.

Conditions of the startup project to be submitted:

  1. The age of project executors should not exceed 40 regardless of their academic level, title, position and workplace;
  2. 1 team Participation with 1 project (the number of team members should not exceed 5);
  3. The amount of the assigned start-up project implementation should not exceed the amount of the Regulation approved by the Annex 2 of the Resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers dated April 19, 2021 No. 222.
  4. The maximum period of implementation of start-up projects is up to one year be;

NOTE!

  • The total amount of funds required for the implementation of a scientific idea for one applicant should not exceed 400 times the amount of the basic calculation;
  • The total amount of funds required for the implementation of a start-up project for one applicant should not exceed 3000 times the amount of the basic calculation.

Within the competition for Startup projects proposals will be accepted until October 10, 18:00 this year.
