The selection competition for state scholarships of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan will be held on December 27, 2024. This competition is aimed at encouraging and supporting the **second-level doctoral students (PhD)** who are engaged in scientific research activities in higher education institutions and scientific organizations of the republic and have achieved significant results.

Competition directions and quotas

  • Natural and exact sciences: 3 quotas
  • Social and humanitarian Science: 3 quota
  • Technology and technology: 2 quota
  • Health: 1 quota
  • Agriculture: 1 quota

About submission and participants

October 1-31, 2024 72 documents were received through the stipendiya.edu.uz portal, of which 68 applicants' documents were accepted. On December 17, candidates passed special tests on information technologies, foreign languages and Uzbekistan history. 64 out of 68 applicants took part in the tests.

Conclusion

This competition serves to increase the scientific potential of the 2nd stage basic doctoral students and to motivate them. Presidential State Scholarships are an important platform for supporting talented researchers. — Official Data
