Greater opportunities are provided to the president, creative and specialized schools

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The main aspects of the decree The Presidential decree stipulates the following: from the 2024/2025 academic year, the practice of conducting classes with the participation of academicians of the Academy of Sciences will be established in schools within the system of the Agency of Specialized Educational Institutions.
Also, this in schools: classes aimed at developing students' creativity skills are organized.
Every year, among students of general secondary educational institutions, the competition "Step to Invention" is held, which involves the creation of a new type of product or service in such areas as education, information technology, green energy, ecology, climate change, and agriculture.

This year On April 1, the President's Decree No. PF-55 "On measures to expand the network of specialized schools and further improve their activities" was signed. With this decree, we can see that the President, creativity and specialized schools have been given a bigger package of opportunities.

The main aspects of the decree

The presidential decree stipulates the following:

  • From the 2024/2025 academic year, the practice of teaching classes with the participation of academicians of the Academy of Sciences will be launched in the schools of the Agency of Specialized Educational Institutions.

Also, in these schools:

  • Lessons aimed at developing students' creativity skills are organized.

  • Students are assisted in the development of practical projects and their implementation.

  • Students are actively involved in production processes and Science will be introduced to the projects carried out in the scientific laboratories of the Academy.

  • In order to develop the innovative ideas and developments of the students, the "Young Academicians" clubs will be organized.

  • Every year, among the students of general secondary educational institutions, a new product or service in such areas as education, information technology, green energy, ecology, climate change and agriculture will be introduced. "A Step to Invention" competition will be held, which implies the creation of a type of innovation.

  • The educational process in the specialized school named after Mirzo Ulugbek will be organized according to the STEM model. "Uzbekistan" University will introduce educational programs for training students in the basics of engineering.

  • Selection for engineering programs will be made among the 6th grade graduates of general secondary educational institutions who have abilities in mathematics, logical thinking, informatics and engineering. will be integrated.
  • Graduates who have properly mastered engineering education programs will be given the right to continue their studies from the 2nd stage of the relevant bachelor's education program of "Yangi Uzbekistan" University.

  • According to the decree, from the 2024/2025 academic year, creativity and specialized in the Agency system in schools:

    • The practice of teaching subjects in a foreign language by teachers who have at least a B2 level certificate of knowledge of a foreign language is introduced.
    • Teachers who conduct classes in a foreign language in mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, natural sciences (science), informatics and information technologies are paid an additional monthly bonus in the amount of 100 percent compared to the tariff rate.
    • President the procedure of paying an additional monthly bonus of 350 percent to the local teachers of their schools will be introduced.

  • According to the decree, from the 2024/2025 academic year, a new system of educating students in the spirit of patriotism will be introduced in the schools of the Agency system:

    • Amir Temur in the 5th-8th grades, By connecting the exemplary life of our great generals such as Jalaluddin Manguberdi and Zahiruddin Muhammad Babur with the bravery of our soldiers today, trainings are organized aimed at forming the skills of the defender of the Motherland.
    • In the 9th-11th grades, military games and sports competitions, activities aimed at increasing military-patriotic feelings and preparing for military service are held.
    • Every year. "Turan Tigers" military sports competitions are held among students.
    • A model school that reflects national traditions and values corners will be organized.
    • The activity of the "Turon" theater studio will be launched as an experiment.

  • From the 2024/2025 academic year, in creative schools: — More information

    • Master classes will be held with the participation of socially and creatively active writers and public figures.
    • "Young translators" clubs
    • "Text Studies" classes are organized.
    • The practice of publishing the collections of students called "Bouquet from the Garden of Creativity" twice a year.
    • The subject of "Art" is introduced.
    • The basics of robotics are taught within the subject of "Technology".
    • Every year, a competition aimed at selecting talented young people in the field of fiction and art is held.
    • A collection of creative works of at least two students from each creative school will be published as part of the "My First Book" project.
    • At least 5 creative students from each creative school will participate in the "Zomin Seminar".
    • Each creative school It is provided with a set of 100 volumes of books called "Masterpieces of Turkish Literature".
    • Creative schools are attached to higher educational institutions and embassies of Uzbekistan in foreign countries.
