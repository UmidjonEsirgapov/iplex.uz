Students who come to the dormitory before the end of the vacation are expelled from the dormitory. The ministry said it would monitor it

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
Currently, the winter vacation has been extended, but students who came to the educational institutions from the regions for the purpose of preparing for studies or other purposeful activities can come to the dormitory early and spend the additional winter vacation effectively with their relatives in the arms of their parents. It is recommended and individual work is being done to create all the conditions for this.
It is explained that the outbreak of seasonal infectious diseases is high as a result of a sharp drop in air temperature, and measures are taken to create the necessary conditions for each student based on the real situation and according to their wishes.

Information Service of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation reports that the rectors and vice-rectors of all higher education institutions of our republic have been given instructions to create appropriate conditions for students who come to the university before the end of the extended vacation. or for other purposeful work, students who have come to educational institutions from the regions are recommended to effectively spend the additional winter vacation with their relatives and individual work is being done to create all conditions for this.  measures will be taken to create the necessary conditions.

The Minister of Higher Education, Science and Innovation has given this order to the heads of all educational institutions. Also, work is being carried out on the effective organization of students' vacations.

At this point, it is organized for students who remain in their living areas to improve their knowledge and skills through homework, reading books, and online classes. Students are requested to contact the rectorate of the higher education institution in which they are studying in any problem situations.

All these measures are being implemented in order to prevent serious damage to the health of students as a result of a sharp drop in air temperature.

At this point, the management of HEIs is requested to effectively organize an additional winter break for each student, observing the principles of humanity. finding a solution to the problem has been determined.

We remind you that this assignment is under the personal control of the minister.
