External education will be abolished: distance and part-time now education will be launched

On the basis of the Presidential decree of May 5, 2025, the form of part-time education is being phased out, and it is being replaced by more flexible and modern forms of education - distance and part-time (half-time) education.

What is part-time education?

Part-time education is an approved full-time education. is a convenient form, especially for employed citizens. In this system:

  • Lessons are organized in a shortened form;

  • Students attend classes on certain days of the week;

  • It is possible to continue work and study together.

Distance education options

Distance (online) education is organized on the basis of modern technologies and has the following advantages:

  • Ability to study from anywhere;

  • Education in parallel with work and other obligations;

  • Constant online access to resources and textbooks.

Quota restriction for alternative education

One of the important aspects is that the quota allocated to alternative forms of education in universities should not exceed 50% of full-time enrollment.

For example: If 100 full-time students are accepted for one course, a maximum of 50 part-time or remote students will be accepted.

Changes at the master's level as well

Now it is possible to study in distance or other alternative forms even at the master's level. Previously, this was done only in the daytime.

Conclusion

These changes in the education system give citizens more freedom and flexibility. This is a great opportunity for those who work, have family responsibilities, or live in remote areas.
