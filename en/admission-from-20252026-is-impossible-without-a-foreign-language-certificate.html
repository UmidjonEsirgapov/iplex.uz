Admission from 2025/2026 is impossible without a foreign language certificate!

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Certificate is mandatory for admission to education courses in which foreign language is block 1. Starting from the 2025/2026 academic year, admission to education courses where foreign language is the first subject in the subject complex is carried out on the basis of a national or international certificate confirming the level of knowledge of a foreign language. How does the new procedure work?

  • The test will not be conducted:In the areas designated as foreign language as the first subject in the subject complex, the test will not be conducted in this subject.

  • A certificate is required:Applicants must provide a certificate confirming the level of foreign language knowledge.

Why is the certificate important?

This new procedure is aimed at assessing the level of knowledge of foreign language applicants based on international and national standards and improving the quality of education. — Source
