Distribution of dormitory places for students

The dormitory places for students are distributed according to the established procedure and percentages every year. This procedure is usually carried out taking into account the needs of students.

Places allocated to first-year students

55-65 percent of dormitory places are allocated for first-year students. This is done in order to create comfortable conditions for students who are starting the new academic year.

Places allocated to second-year students

Around 15-20 percent of places are allocated for second-year students. Among them, students from remote areas and in need of social protection will have priority.

Places allocated to students of the 3rd year and above

For students of the third year and above, dormitory places will be 20-25%. This group usually includes students who are about to finish their studies and are engaged in scientific activities.

Conclusion

If you are a future 1st year student, you do not need to worry - the main places are reserved for you. Students of other courses are accommodated on a first-come, first-served basis and according to the level of need. — Official Data
