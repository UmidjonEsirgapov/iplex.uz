III quarter classes in schools will begin on January 13

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
According to the Ministry of Preschool and School Education, the III quarter classes in schools will begin on Monday, January 13, 2025.
Duration of the winter vacation Start date: 2024 December 29.
During the holiday, classes will be reorganized in order to create comfort for parents, students and teachers and to ensure the continuity of the educational process.

Duration of the winter vacation

Important notes
