Is it possible to retire 5 years before the general retirement age?

By using this right, an opportunity to retire 5 years earlier than the general retirement age is created.

In particular, specialized educational institutions for disabled children, orphanages, military academic lyceums and teachers of training colonies have the right to use such a privilege.
