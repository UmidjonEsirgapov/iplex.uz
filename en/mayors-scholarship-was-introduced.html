"Mayor's scholarship" was introduced

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
At the next session of the Council of People's Deputies of the Tashkent region, the allocation of 63 million. 950 thousand soums to be spent for the "Mayor's scholarship" for one year was put on the agenda and approved by the deputies.
According to it, in the Tashkent region In order to provide material and moral support to talented and creative young people studying in general education schools, professional and higher educational institutions, and to provide them with appropriate rewards, the Regulations of the "Hokim Scholarship" were developed. The issue of allocation of 950 thousand soums was put on the agenda and approved by the deputies.

According to it, in order to provide material and moral support to talented and creative young people studying in professional and higher educational institutions of general education schools in the Tashkent region, the Regulations of the "Hokim Scholarship" were developed.

The competition is held once a year, and the winners are divided into 3 categories (general education schools, professional and higher education institutions) are determined in 3 places among young people. In particular, a scholarship in the amount of 3 times the minimum monthly salary for the 1st place, 2 times the minimum monthly salary for the 2nd place, and 1 times the minimum monthly salary for the 3rd place will be paid for 1 year. — Details here
