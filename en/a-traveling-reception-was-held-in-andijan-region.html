In order to communicate directly with the population, to find solutions to the problems of applicants and parents, mobile receptions continue in the regions. The next event was organized at a high level in Andijan region.

2026 exams and applications are in the spotlight

On February 3, 2026 Deputy Director of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency Farhod Isamovich Mirzayev's reception was attended by citizens from different regions of the region. At the event, mainly teachers, students, school graduates and their parents received answers to their questions.

Public appeals and legal explanations

Each appeal was carefully studied during the mobile reception:

  • For entrants: Legal explanations were given on the procedure of tests and news.

  • Teachers for: National certification system and training issues were discussed.

  • Suggestions: Reasonable suggestions made by citizens were noted to be taken into account in the future activities of the Agency.

Andijan region branch of the Agency: New building and modern conditions

F.Mirzayev also got acquainted with the construction of a new building being built for the Andijan region branch of the Agency. During the construction process, the main focus is on international standards and the convenience of citizens.

Inclusive infrastructure and the concept of "Green Space"

The following aspects are particularly emphasized in the project of the new building:

  1. Conditions for persons with disabilities: Special infrastructure is being created for persons with disabilities, from entrance to the building to examination rooms.

  2. Parents waiting rooms for: Comfortable waiting areas are provided for the relatives of the examinees.

  3. Ecological approach: Greening of the area, planting of fruit and ornamental trees suitable for the local climate within the national project "Green Space" recommendations were given.

Conclusion

This mobile reception held in Andijan region served not only to solve existing problems, but also to bring updates in the field closer to the people. The newly constructed administrative building will be an important foundation for organizing quality and transparent examination processes for the youth of the region in the future. — Official Data
