How will educational credits be allocated from the 2024/2025 academic year? From the 2024/2025 academic year, the procedure for issuing educational loans to students and pupils of higher and professional educational organizations was determined on a new basis. This procedure applies only to full-time students.

To whom is the educational loan granted?

  • For students of the first stage and students of professional educational organizations - a loan is allocated in the amount of the payment-contract amount.

  • Second stage and above for students - if the average mastery index is below "3", up to 80 percent of the contract amount is given a loan.

On what conditions is it allocated?

Educational credit is granted when information is available in the following information systems:
