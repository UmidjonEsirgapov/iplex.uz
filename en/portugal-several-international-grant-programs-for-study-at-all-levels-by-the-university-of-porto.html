Portugal: Several international grant programs for study at all levels by the University of Porto

All international students, as well as Portuguese students, can apply for this grant program. Briefly:

After an evaluation of 51 research centers in Portugal, University of Porto was recognized as one of the major science centers. The University of Porto has well-known research and development centers that have focused on various fields of science and technology for centuries.

There are currently 4 grant programs offered by the University of Porto. Candidates for these programs are selected based on a variety of criteria, including academic performance, merit, and financial need. Meritorious scholarships are awarded equally to all deserving students based on their academic achievement and grades. In addition, there are some other scholarship programs where sponsors help students through financial aid.

Grant Details:

  • Country - Portugal;
  • University - University of Porto
  • Levels - Bachelor, Master and PhD;

Grant and Scholarship Programs Offered:

  • SASUP scholarships
  • Merit scholarships
  • Scientific scholarships
  • Other support programs

Who can apply:

1. SASUP scholarships:
  • In addition to Portuguese students, all international students can apply for this scholarship program. For this, candidates are required to have applied to study in one of the bachelor's and master's programs of the University of Porto;
2. Merit scholarships:
  • In addition to Portuguese students, all international students can apply for this scholarship program. For this, candidates are required to have applied for one of the bachelor's and master's programs of the University of Porto. Candidates must have high academic grades in the previous stage and be at the top of their class or course even after being selected for the grant.
3. Scientific scholarships:
  • In addition to Portuguese students, all international students can apply for this grant program. For this, candidates are required to have applied for one of the bachelor's, master's and doctoral programs of the University of Porto. It is required that the candidate is interested in conducting scientific research at the university.
4. Other support:
  • The University and its sponsoring organizations are willing to provide financial assistance to students in need and do so through several such programs. All international students can apply for this grant program in addition to Portuguese students for such support scholarships. For this, candidates are required to have applied to study in one of the bachelor's, master's and doctoral programs of the University of Porto.

Benefits of the grant:

Each of the above grant programs has specific benefits and you can get to know them in full here. The total value of the scholarships is expected to include:

  • Monthly stipend;
  • Exemption from tuition fees;
  • Reimbursement of accommodation, meals and transportation expenses.
