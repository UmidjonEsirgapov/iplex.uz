Innovative ideas screening contest for citizens aged 14-30 who live, work or study in the city of Tashkent!

You can participate in the contest in one of the following fields:

1. Production and product efficiency innovation
2. Technological innovation
3. Business model innovation
4. Marketing / Trade Innovation 5. Innovation of electrical networks

Awards:
- laptop for 1st place.
- TV for 2nd place.
- smartphone for 3rd place.
- souvenirs and a certificate for all participants in the final.

Participation in the contest applicants:
- F. I. O., residential address, year, month, day of birth;
- Brief information about their project, pictures;
- Information about where they are currently active (work, study)
should be sent to @syndzia telegram account.
