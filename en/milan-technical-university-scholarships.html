Scholarship in Italy for international students who want to study at the Faculty of Natural Sciences of the Technical University of Milan.

For students enrolled in one of the university's "Laurea Magistrale" programs Offers 3 types of scholarships:

Platinum Scholarship.
€10,000/year.+ Tuition Fee Exemption.
Gold Scholarship.
€5,000/year. + exemption from tuition fees.
Silver scholarship.
Pays tuition fees in full.

Requirements:
Having a bachelor's degree, the field of bachelor's degree must be related to the field chosen for master's degree.
The main subjects studied in bachelor's degree must be similar to the subjects of master's field

GPA must also be submitted for some countries.

Certificate of knowledge of Italian (B1) or English (TOEFL, IELTS) is required.

Duration: depends on the program, but the general deadline is May 26, 2022.
