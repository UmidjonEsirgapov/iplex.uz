A student from Namangan became a student of 18 foreign HEIs. He is awaiting responses from another 20 universities

Brief content:
Hikmatilla Khudoyberdiyev, an 11th-grade student of the 1st state specialized boarding school in Namangan, became a student of 18 foreign higher education institutions ahead of schedule.

1st state in Namangan city Hikmatilla Khudoyberdiyev, an 11th-grade student of a specialized boarding school, has become a student of 18 foreign higher education institutions before the deadline. She is waiting for an answer from 20 more universities, says "Daryo" reporter Azizbek Abduvaliyev.

Hikmatilla Khudoyberdiyev, 11th grade student of 1-DIMI in Namangan

Photo: "Daryo" / Jahangir Akramov

Hikmatilla is from the village of Jiydakapa, Namangan region He was one of the top students in the local school, but when he entered the 1st state specialized boarding school in Namangan, he noticed that his knowledge was not very good.

Our hero scored 1310 points in mathematics and English and got 18 international SAT certificates He entered foreign HEIs, including the USA's Cincinnati, Arizona, Colorado, Minnesota, Buffalo, and Switzerland's Franklin universities ahead of schedule, and received a total of $1.3 million in grants.

Hikmatilla Khudoyberdiyev. Photo: "Daryo" / Jahangir Akramov

"Waiting for an answer" the number of my universities is more than that - 20 in total. Among them are higher educational institutions that occupy leading positions in the top ratings, such as John Hopkins, Carnegie, Georgetown, and the University of Pennsylvania. When I get my results from them, I will consult with my teachers and family members and come to a clear decision," says the student.
