Master's course "Legal provision of cyber security" will be opened at the Academy of Law Enforcement

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
From the 2025/2026 academic year, the Academy of Law Enforcement of the Republic of Uzbekistan will introduce a new direction and specialties aimed at training highly qualified specialists in the field of cyber security and digital technologies.
Organization: Law Enforcement of the Republic of Uzbekistan Academy.
Faculty of Cyber Security and Digital Forensics: Until July 1, 2025, the Faculty of Cyber Security and Digital Forensics will be established within the structure of the Academy of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, which will include the departments of cyber law, exact sciences, digital technologies and information security, forensic expertise.

Decision of the President (No. 17, 22.01.2025) from the 2025/2026 academic year, a new direction and specialties aimed at training highly qualified specialists in the field of cyber security and digital technologies will be introduced at the Academy of Law Enforcement of the Republic of Uzbekistan. "Activities to combat crimes in the field of digital technologies."

  • Study period: 4 years.
  • Organization: Academy of the Ministry of Internal Affairs.
  • Master's degree:
    • Specialization name: "Legal provision of cyber security."
    • Study term: 1 year.
    • Organization: Academy of Law Enforcement of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

    • Privileges for students:

    • For undergraduates: Laureates of international, regional or republican Olympiads in mathematics, informatics and programming are accepted without a final interview after successful preliminary selection.
    • For the master's degree: Candidates with an international IT certificate in digital technologies are exempted from taking the exam in the subject "Informatics and information technologies".

    Faculty of cyber security and digital forensics:

    Until July 1, 2025 in the structure of the Academy of the Ministry of Internal Affairs:

    • Cyber law, exact sciences, digital The Faculty of Cybersecurity and Digital Criminalistics will be established, which will include the departments of technologies and information security, criminalistic expertise.

    Special training courses:

    • Continuous special training courses in the field of "Digital Forensics" will be organized remotely for investigators, investigators and prosecutors through the information and training portal of the Law Enforcement Academy.
