About the University and Scholarship Program:

Raffles University is one of the best private, multi-faculty universities in Malaysia. The university is recognized by the Malaysian Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE) to offer various programs to its students. The university is distinguished from the rest by the high quality of education.

The grant program provided by the university provides financial support for international students.

Who is the program for?

  • The program is for all national and international students.

For which fields of study:

The grant program is offered for education at the bachelor and master levels in the following programs:

  • Faculty of AI & Robotics;
  • Faculty of Social Sciences;
  • Faculty of Business;
  • Faculty of Arts & Design;
  • Faculty of Built Environment & Regenerative Design;
  • Postgraduate Programs.

Application Process:

Students must first apply HERE and be admitted to one of the university's undergraduate and graduate programs. After that, they will be able to apply for the grant program HERE.

Language students:

Candidates must have one of the following language certificates:

  • IELTS – 6.0
  • TOEFL IBT – 80
  • TOEFL PBT – 550
  • TOEFL CBT -213

Benefits of the program:

Candidates are offered a 50% discount on the study program fee to study at Raffles University Malaysia.
