iBook.uz scholarship: who will participate in the 2nd stage?

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
According to the competition instructions and regulations, 2389 participants selected from the 1st stage passed to the second stage and had the opportunity to participate in the 2nd stage of the competition. recommended by specialists of the chain of stores.
For information, the chain of bookstores "iBook.uz" is one of the largest bookstores operating in our Republic, and currently delivers more than a thousand types of books to our reading compatriots. "iBook.uz", one of the largest bookstores, and the "OLIYGOH.UZ" educational platform launched the "iBook.uz scholarship" competition for students. On April 24, the first stage of this competition took place. Tens of thousands of talented students from different regions of our Republic took part in the 1st stage of the scholarship competition, and 2389 candidates who showed the best results (23 years of work and more) received a ticket to the next stage (see the results).

All participating candidates can view their results and download special certificates of the competition (Download certificates).

Who will participate in the 2nd stage of the competition?

According to the instructions and rules of the competition, 2389 participants selected from the 1st stage went to the second stage and participated in the 2nd stage of the competition.

How will the second stage take place?

Second stage (in the form of a test) Candidates who passed the 2nd stage of the competition will take an online exam in the form of a test on the OLIYGOH.UZ platform, and candidates with the highest score will be sent to the final stage.

Exam type: 40 test questions from 7 books recommended by the Selection Committee. This stage questions are aimed at full thinking, and participants take an exam from selected books.

Currently, the most widely read among young people (TOP bestseller) and works on personal development have been selected. The list of books was recommended by the special commission of the competition and specialists of the iBook.uz bookstore network. List of books:

— Lolazor (Murod Muhammad Dost)— Factfulness (Hans Rosling)— Laws of Victory (Bodo Schaefer)— Labyrinth (Franz Kafka)— Farewell, Homeland (Azam Hashimi)— Resurrection (Lev Tolstoy)— Selected works (Mahmudhoja Behbudi)

Time: The 2nd stage of the competition starts on May 15 at 18:00 and the exam window closes for everyone at 22:00. During this period, all candidates can participate in the exam for 40 minutes. After the completion of the 2nd stage, the mandate of the overall results will be formed and the 200 (two hundred) participants who will get the highest result will get a ticket to the final (3rd stage).

Absolute winners of the scholarship competition in the hall of special events of the "iBook.uz" bookstore chain ceremoniously awarded with a certificate, souvenirs, cash prizes.
