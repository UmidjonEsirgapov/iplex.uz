Brief about the university and the grant:

GISMA (German International Graduate School of Management and Administration) is a private educational institution located in Hannover and Berlin, specialized in the field of business. The university was founded in 1999 on the initiative of companies and the state government of Lower Saxony. The university was founded together with the former chancellor of Germany, Gerhard Schröder.

Presented by the university Future Leaders Scholarships for International Studentsgrant program provides financial support for international students to study in university MBA (master's degree) program.

Grant details:

  • University or organization: GISMA Business School;
  • Course level: Master's;
  • Grant amount: 50% of tuition fees (11,500 up to EUR)
  • Application procedure: Online

Required documents:

  • Passport size photograph;
  • Passport copy;
  • Language certificate - TOEFL – 77/IELTS – 6.5 or Cambridge English – B2;
  • Motivation letter;
  • Bachelor diploma;
  • Resume;
  • Work experience (optional);
  • Attested copies of other relevant certificates/transcripts.

Application Procedure:

Candidates must apply online as per the procedure and instructions provided by the school. First, choose your MBA program here and apply online. After being admitted to study, you will need to fill out an online application for the grant program here.

Note: The process of receiving documents will continue until May 31, 2021!
