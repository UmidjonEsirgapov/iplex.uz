Admission to psychology courses will be increased by 30%

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
Admission parameters for the fields of psychology in higher education institutions of Uzbekistan will be increased by 30%.
According to the Ministry of Justice, in the 2021/2022 academic year: in the higher education system, physical sciences will be adapted to fields is taught; in-depth teaching practice will be launched in separate groups of talented students studying in the 3rd and 4th courses of biology majors of higher educational institutions of the republic; the parameters of admission based on the state order for educational courses in the field of psychology will be increased by 30%; All higher education institutions training personnel in the fields of chemistry and biology hold their own Olympiad in chemistry and biology at a multi-level (district (city) - region - higher education institution) among the students of the graduating class and accept the students who take 1-3 places to study without an exam at the expense of the funds of the higher education institution.
