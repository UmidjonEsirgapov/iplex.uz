Chevening Oxford Center for Islamic Studies (OCIS) - To learn more about the culture and civilization of Islam and modern Muslim societies. was established to encourage researchers with strong potential in this field. The program runs for 6 months and participants will have the opportunity to work with leading experts in the Islamic world, including Islamic history and classical Islamic studies and Islamic finance. In order to participate in the program, they must have planned the research they want to do in the UK.

Benefits:

  1. 6 months of research at the Oxford Islamic Center;
  2. Living expenses and round-trip tickets to Britain during the program expenses;
  3. Stipend for research-related activities;
  4. The right to participate in cultural events and programs organized by the FCDO and the Chevening Secretariat.

Requirements:

  1. To be a citizen of one of the countries listed (Uzbekistan is included);
  2. The candidate must have a doctoral degree or its equivalent;
  3. Has at least 5 years of professional and/or scientific research experience to be;
  4. Ability to lead and influence;
  5. To return to the country after the program is over;
  6. Knowledge of English at the basic level;
  7. Must be a British citizen or dual citizen
