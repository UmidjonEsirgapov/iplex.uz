"EPAM Training Center" is accepting applications for the SAP Development course.

Participation in it is free, and the classes are conducted online.

SAP is all is a business software provider that offers a variety of internal process management solutions to organizations of all sizes.

Also, participants will have access to the materials and can take them at their own pace.

Candidates English Pre-intermediate (A2) and higher knowledge and 10-20 hours a week or more are required to study.

Applications for participation must be sent through the link until June 1st.

An English language test must be taken within 3 days after registration, and it will be available in the profile.

If the candidate successfully passes the test, he will receive a link to the course.
