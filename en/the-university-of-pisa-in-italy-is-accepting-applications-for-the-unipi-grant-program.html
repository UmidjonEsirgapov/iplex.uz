The University of Pisa, Italy is accepting applications for the UNIPI grant program.

24 scholarships for international students enrolled in the English-taught Master's program or Bachelor of Management in Business and Economics

Candidates do not need to submit a separate application for the grant. All students admitted to the university will be automatically considered and will focus on:

  • CV;
  • Interview score;
  • GPA;
  • country of origin of the candidate (candidates from less economically developed countries are preferred).

    Stipends will be allocated to those admitted as follows:

    • first year of the course: 6500 euros and exemption from university fees (except 140 euros for regional taxes and 16 euros for state duty);
    • second year of the course for master's degree: 8 thousand euro and a 50% reduction in university fees (if the student has received at least 36 CFU credits);
    • second and third year of the bachelor's degree: 8 thousand euros and a 50% reduction in university fees (if the student has received at least 36 CFU credits (first year) and 96 CFU credits (second year).

    Grants each it is paid in two parts with an interval of 5 months. Grant requests for the second and third year will be evaluated at the beginning of the next academic year (September-end of October). Therefore, it is recommended to get more detailed information on each direction.
