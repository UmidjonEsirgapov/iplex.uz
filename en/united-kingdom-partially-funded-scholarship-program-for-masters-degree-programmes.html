About the university and the grant program:

University of Reading is a multifaceted higher education institution providing quality education. About 17,000 students of different backgrounds and cultures study there. It is divided into 16 academic schools and has four main campuses in the UK and overseas.

The grant program offered by the university is for international students. provides financial support for university graduate studies.

Application process:

To be a candidate for the grant program, you must first apply HERE to the university's master's program ahead of time. All successful candidates will be provided with an application form for the grant program through the university's online portal.

  • Candidates must have a bachelor's degree;
