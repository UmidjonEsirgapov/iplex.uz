"Legal classes" are organized in schools

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
"Legal classes" will be organized in schools.
Starting from the 2024-2025 academic year: the "school-lyceum-technical school-university" system will be introduced in the training of legal personnel through the establishment of a "Legal Class" in schools and in-depth training of legal subjects; admission to academic lyceums of the Tashkent State Law University will begin on the basis of legal technical schools in the Namangan and Samarkand regions.
The main tasks of establishing a "Legal Class": modern information in-depth study of the science "Fundamentals of State and Law" using technologies; Preparation of students for effective study in the next stages of education based on variable study plans in the field of "Law"; effective organization of extracurricular activities by involving employees of legal practice of law enforcement activities; preparing students to participate in school, district (city), regional and republican Olympiads and other events in the subject "Fundamentals of State and Law" on" was adopted.

Starting from the 2024-2025 academic year:

the "school - lyceum - technical school - university" system will be introduced in the training of legal personnel through the establishment of a "Legal Class" in schools and in-depth training of legal subjects; Tashkent State University of Law on the basis of legal technical schools in Namangan and Samarkand regions. admission to lyceums begins.

The main tasks of organizing a "legal class":

  • in-depth study of the science of "Basics of State and Law" using modern information technologies;
  • on the basis of varied curricula in the field of "Jurisprudence" to educate students effectively in the next stages of education training;
  • effective organization of extracurricular activities by involving law enforcement personnel;
  • preparing students to participate in school, district (city), regional and republican olympiads and other events in the subject "State and Fundamentals of Law". a system of methodological support will be established through the manuals, video clips, computer games and mobile applications are used.

    "Legal Class" is selected from among 8th-grade students interested in "State and Fundamentals of Law" and is held by the director's order based on the recommendation of the school's pedagogical council. Graduates who have successfully completed academic lyceums under the system (the final grades of each academic year during the two-year study period were "excellent" and "good") will be admitted to law technical schools directly (on a fee-contract basis) without tests starting from the 2024-2025 academic year.
