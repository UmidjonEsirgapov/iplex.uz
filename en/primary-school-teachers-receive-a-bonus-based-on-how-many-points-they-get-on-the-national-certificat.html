Primary school teachers receive a bonus based on how many points they get on the national certificate

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Elementary teachers can receive a promotion with a C or higher certification on the national certification exams.
That is, they must score at least 46 points or higher.
According to the Rush model, the national certification levels are as follows: For those who got more than 70 points - A+ grade; 65 - 69.9 points - A grade; 60 - 64.9 points - B+ level; 55 - 59.9 points - B level; 50 - 54.9 points - grade C+; 46 - 49.9 - grade C.

Elementary teachers can receive a promotion with a grade C or higher on the national certification exam. That is, they must score at least 46 points or higher.

According to the Rush model, the levels of national certification are as follows:

  • A+ grade for those who scored above 70 points;
  • 65 - 69.9 points - A grade;
  • 60 - 64.9 points - B+ grade;
  • 55 - 59.9 points - B grade;
  • 50 - 54.9 points - C+ grade;
  • 46 - 49.9 points - C degree.
