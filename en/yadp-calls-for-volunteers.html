YADP invites volunteers
Young Analysts Development Project (YADP) is an updated version of the well-known Young Leaders Development Project (YLDP).

YADP is a support for the achievement of Uzbekistan's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and decision-making of public and private sector, regional and international organizations. is an information project aimed at providing individuals with accurate information. Organizations in Uzbekistan do not have high-quality databases available, which makes it difficult for them to comprehensively analyze, evaluate and make decisions about the transfer. Thus, YADP was launched in order to develop Data Science in Uzbekistan.

The main goals of the project are as follows:

1. Raising awareness of Data Science in Uzbekistan;
2. Creating a strong community of talented young people in the field of Data Science;
3. To become one of the powerful social tools/institutions in the field of data collection and analysis in Uzbekistan;
4. Measuring statistical indicators of national BRM achievements in Uzbekistan.

If you have a passion for Data Science and want to volunteer at YADP events, you can join us by filling out the form! Volunteers' roles include sorting applications from speakers and delegates, advising and supporting teams who want to participate in the YADP competition, helping with public relations (PR) on social media, and some other organizational parts.

Besides the obvious benefits of volunteering, volunteers get to learn about Data Science, network with Data Science experts, and get certifications.
