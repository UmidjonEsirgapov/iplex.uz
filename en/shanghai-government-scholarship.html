Shanghai Government Scholarship was established in 2006 to promote the education of international students in Shanghai and encourage more international students and scholars to ECNU.

Who is it for?
Shanghai Government Scholarship East China Normal It is intended for international students applying for a bachelor's, master's or PhD program at the university.

Requirements:
1) Applicants must not be Chinese citizens and must be in good health.

2) Education level and age limit:

-Applicants for the bachelor's program must have a high school diploma and be under 25 years old.

-Applicants for the master's program must have a bachelor's degree and be under 35 years old.

-Applicants for the doctoral program must have a master's degree and be under the age of 40.

3) Students who have won a Chinese government scholarship or any scholarship provided by other organizations are not eligible to apply for SGS.

Reimbursable expenses:
1) Type A (full scholarship)

Provides tuition fees and accommodation on the campus; scholarships to provide comprehensive medical insurance and monthly living expenses (Doctoral program: 3500 yuan; Master's program: 3000 yuan; Bachelor's program: 2500 yuan)

2) Type B (partial scholarship)

Tuition fee exemption and comprehensive medical insurance are provided.
