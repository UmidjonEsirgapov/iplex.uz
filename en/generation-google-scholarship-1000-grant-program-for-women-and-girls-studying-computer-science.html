General Information:

Generation Google Scholarship: This The grant program is dedicated to women and girls who are studying in one of the bachelor's and master's degrees in computer fields or related fields, who are interested in creating programs and gaining computer-related knowledge. Selected candidates will be awarded $1,000 in prize money for the 2021-2022 academic year.

Who can apply?

  • All girls currently studying full-time at the undergraduate level in computer science or related fields during the 2021-2022 academic year;
  • Currently be a 2nd-year student at an accredited higher education institution from one of the countries in the Asia and Oceania region (all higher education institutions in Uzbekistan are also suitable);
  • Be studying computer or computer engineering or related fields;
  • Have high academic performance;
  • Your leadership ability as well as the above subjects, you must be able to demonstrate your strong interests in the fields;

Program registration and online application process:

  • Enter all information about yourself correctly here (study and place of residence and contact information);
  • CV;
  • Academic grade book/transcript;
  • Essays on 2 short given topics are required;
  • Will there be a 15-minute interview with selected candidates;
  • Offer letters will be sent to selected candidates 5-7 days before the end of admission to the program.

Note: 2 essay topics are given below and their It is required to write no more than 400 words:

  1. What is a significant challenge that you believe women in the tech industry face and how do you see yourself being part of the solution(s) to this challenge? Keep in mind that impact can happen in many ways and at different scales.
  2. What impact would receiving this scholarship have on your education? Describe any circumstances affecting your need for a scholarship and what educational goals this scholarship will enable you to accomplish.
