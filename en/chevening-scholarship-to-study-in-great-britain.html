Chevening Scholarships is a global scholarship program funded by the UK Government's Foreign Office and partner organisations. The scholarship is mainly awarded for a period of one year to obtain a master's degree. Participating countries include: Russia, Uzbekistan, Ukraine, Moldova, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Georgia and others.

Priority areas:

- Politics and international relations;

- Law and public administration;

- Economy, business and innovation;

- Climate change, environmental research and energy security;

- Science and Space;

- Journalism.

Cheve's scholarship amount:

- One month scholarship;

- Transportation expenses;

- Dissertation grant;

- Visa expenses;

- Tuition expenses.

Requirements for candidates:

1) be a citizen of one of the countries participating in the program and must return to their home country for at least 2 years after completing the Chevening scholarship program;

2) have a bachelor's degree or higher.

3) have at least 2 years of work experience (including internships, volunteering, etc.).
