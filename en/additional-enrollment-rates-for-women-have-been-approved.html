How long is the recommendation?

For women with 5 years of experience, a recommendation for a quota of 500 is issued for 3 years, and for women in need, a recommendation for a quota of 2,000 is given for 1 year. Based on this, those who received a recommendation letter as a needy woman last year must apply again.

Information about those who have a recommendation letter is entered into the applicant database of the Knowledge and Skills Assessment Agency every year by July 15.

Can one person receive both types of recommendation letters?

What is the procedure for passing the exam for those who have a recommendation?

Entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam) are carried out in accordance with the established procedure for admission to bachelor's degrees of higher education institutions.

Based on the recommendation who will be accepted for an additional grant?

Women from needy families who have a recommendation letter and have scored not less than 30 percent of the highest possible score in the entrance exams will be recommended for studentship within the admission parameters based on the additional four percent state grant, strictly following the sequence of points. — Official Data
