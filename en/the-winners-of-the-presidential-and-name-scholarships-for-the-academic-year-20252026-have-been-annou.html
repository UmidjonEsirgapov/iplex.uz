The winners of the presidential and named state scholarships for the undergraduate stage were announced

The winners of the presidential and named state scholarships were announced. The Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Innovation gave official information about this.

According to it, 101 students studying at the bachelor level of higher education institutions for the 2025/2026 academic year were awarded the winner of the Presidential State Scholarship of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

President about the winners of the state scholarship

How many students won in the 2025/2026 academic year?

According to the Ministry, for the current academic year 101 talented students were found worthy of the Presidential State Scholarship. This scholarship is considered one of the most prestigious state scholarships in the country.

Information on named state scholarships

What is the promotion procedure?

Students who participated in the competition and did not win a prize are also promoted by the head of the relevant higher education organization (OTT).

In particular:

  • Applicants for the presidential state scholarship will receive a certificate and a one-time monetary award in the amount of 3 times of the basic scholarship;

  • Applicants for named state scholarships will receive a one-time monetary award in the amount of 2 times a certificate and the basic scholarship.
