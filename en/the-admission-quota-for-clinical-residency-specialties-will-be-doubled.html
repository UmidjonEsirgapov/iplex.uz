The admission quota for clinical residency specialties will be doubled

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
The admission quota for clinical residency specialties will be doubled.
It is noted that the Ministry of Health will double the admission quota for clinical residency specialties starting from the 2021/2022 academic year.

The admission quota for clinical residency specialties will be doubled. This is defined in the draft of the president's decree "On the consistent continuation of reforms in the health care system and the creation of necessary conditions for increasing the capacity of medical workers". a new system of residency training will be introduced:

a) the second year of the clinical residency training period will be held in the branches of specialized medical centers in the regions, in the branches of the regional children and adults multidisciplinary medical centers, in the branches of the Republican Emergency Medical Center, and also a certain part will be continued at the workplace; in order to train doctors of narrow specialties, a sufficient amount of grant will be allocated from the local budget, and the graduates who received education based on this grant will be obliged to go to the respective region and work for three years;

the living expenses of doctors of narrow specialty sent to work in the region will be covered from the local budget funds. — Read on infoedu.uz
