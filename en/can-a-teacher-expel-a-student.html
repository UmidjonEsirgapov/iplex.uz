Can a teacher expel a student from class?

Approval of the Minister of Preschool and School Education dated August 31, 2023 "Approval of the improved model code of conduct of a general secondary educational institution" 282 of the Law on Education, the following norms are defined.

Teachers and employees are prohibited in the educational institution from:

  • Degrading the personality, dignity, mental, physical and other violence of team members and others;
  • Excluding students from classes without reason expulsion or non-admission;
  • keeping students in the classroom for no reason after the "exit" call;
  • conducting religious propaganda during classes;
  • coming to the educational institution while consuming alcohol;
  • consumption of alcohol and tobacco products in the territory and building of the educational institution;
  • Institution organizing various activities in the territory and building that are not related to the education of students;
  • Conducting all activities and preparations for them in educational institutions during class sessions.
