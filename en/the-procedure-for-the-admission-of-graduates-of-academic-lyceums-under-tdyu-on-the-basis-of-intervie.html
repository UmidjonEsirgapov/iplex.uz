TDYU lyceum graduates can now study without a test takes

A new procedure was introduced by the Government's decision

On the basis of the Government's decision No. 374 of June 18, 2025, the procedure for admission of talented graduates of academic lyceums under the Tashkent State University of Law to study jurisprudence without tests and based on an interview was established.

This procedure is temporary. It applies to students admitted to the lyceum in the 2023/2024 and 2024/2025 academic years.

How is the admission process carried out?

Application deadline

Graduates until June 20 of each year for admission to the bachelor of higher education institutions without a test must submit an application to a special commission.

Required documents

The following documents are attached to the application:

  • foreign language certificate (national or international);

  • documents confirming personal achievements, activity in social life.

How is the interview conducted?

Interview assessment criteria

  • The interview is held annually by a commission established by July 1.

  • 100 point system.

  • Minimum passing score – 56 points (56%).

Cases of non-admission

The applicant will not be admitted to study in the following cases:

  • in case of failure to attend the interview;

  • in case of obtaining a minimum score of less than 56% in the interview.

Recommendation and final stage

  • Successful in the interview successful applicants are recommended for bachelor's degree without a test.

  • The right of recommendation is valid only for 2 years.

  • Every year until August 10, the rectors order the admission decisions.

New procedure provides a great opportunity for talented high school graduates.
