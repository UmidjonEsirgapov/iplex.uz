You need to know the following information about the payment procedure for participating in entrance tests for higher education institutions.

Payment conditions for applicants

In 2024, applicants must pay 170 thousand soums to participate in the entrance exam. This fee is part of the exam process and gives you access.

Fee Exemptions

However, some applicants are exempt from the fee. The following groups are exempted from payment:

  1. Graduates of the current year.
  2. Graduates of orphanages and children's towns.
  3. Persons on full state support.
  4. Persons with disabilities.
  5. Persons included in the single register of social protection and their children.
  6. Young people included in the "Youth register".
