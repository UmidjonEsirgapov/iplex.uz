École Normale Supérieure: grant to study in Paris, the capital of France

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
The École Normale Supérieure is an annual scholarship to support international students, allowing foreign students to obtain higher education (Master's or ENS diploma) in the humanities, natural sciences, natural sciences or arts.
Each year the scholarship is 10 science, i.e. awarded to science students and 10 arts and humanities students.
Requirements: Candidates must be over 26 years of age; Students must not have previously applied for admission to the university as international students; Students who have lived in France for 10 months or more in recent years cannot apply for a scholarship; Students must have completed at least 1 course in an undergraduate program at a foreign university in the previous year before applying to the École Normale Supérieure; Upon admission to École Normale Supérieure, students must have completed at least 2 courses in an undergraduate program at a foreign university by September 1st.

Each year, the École Normale Supérieure is a scholarship established to support international students, offering foreign students a degree in the humanities, natural sciences, natural sciences or arts. allows you to get education (master's degree or ENS diploma).

Grant applicants must pass an exam. Candidates must be students who have completed at least 1 year of a bachelor's program at a foreign university and have excellent academic performance.

The selection will take place in 2 stages:

  • Based on the documents submitted when applying for the selected program;
  • On the basis of an oral and written exam with selected candidates in Paris.

Each scholarship is awarded to 10 science students and 10 students of arts and humanities.

Students enrolled in arts and humanities are recommended to know French well, otherwise students can take an intensive French language course. Intensive French language courses are available for science students.

Stipend amount:

  • A monthly stipend of 1000 euros is provided for 3 years;
  • Accommodation in a university dormitory is provided;
  • Air ticket expenses are partially covered.

Requirements:

  • Candidates Must be over 26 years old;
  • Students must not have previously applied for admission to the university as international students;
  • Students who have lived in France for 10 months or more in recent years cannot apply for the scholarship;
  • Students must have completed at least 1 course in a foreign university's undergraduate program in the previous year before applying to École Normale Supérieure;
  • Students must have completed at least 2 courses in a foreign university's undergraduate program on September 1st after enrolling at the École Normale Supérieure.

Exceptionally, the organizers may consider an application from a student with a non-standard education obtained outside of France.
