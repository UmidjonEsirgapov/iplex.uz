Andijan Institute of Agriculture and Agrotechnologies will be established

Useful information for applicants and students:

Brief content:
According to the decision, starting from the 2020/2021 academic year, higher schools based on the Andijan Institute of Agriculture and Agro-Technology and Agricultural Vocational Colleges transferred to the Ministry of Agriculture on the basis of the Andijan branch of the Tashkent State Agrarian University technical schools of agro-technologies will be established instead. It is also decided to introduce the position of vice-rector for issues of digitalization of agriculture and information and communication technologies and work with professional educational institutions at the Tashkent State Agrarian University and the Andijan Institute of Agriculture and Agro-Technology. (AKIS) strategy implementation was defined as one of the main tasks of the National Center.

According to the decision of the President of Uzbekistan, the Andijan Institute of Agriculture and Agrotechnologies will be established on the basis of the Andijan branch of the Tashkent State Agrarian University. This was reported by the Ministry of Justice.

According to the decision, starting from the 2020/2021 academic year, the Andijan Institute of Agriculture and Agro-Technology on the basis of the Andijan branch of the Tashkent State Agrarian University and the Agricultural Vocational Colleges transferred to the Ministry of Agriculture will replace agro-technologies with higher schools. technical schools will be established.

"Graduates who have successfully completed technical schools will have the right to continue their studies from the 2nd level in the appropriate bachelor's programs of agricultural higher education institutions without entrance exams based on an individual interview," the message says.

It is noted that starting from the 2020/2021 academic year at the Tashkent State Agrarian University A special faculty will be established for young people selected from among the talented students of higher education institutions within the framework of the implementation of the project "Innovative development of agriculture in Uzbekistan and its digital transformation". id="adfox_157805472798843542">

It is also decided to introduce the position of vice-rector in the Tashkent State Agrarian University and the Andijan Institute of Agriculture and Agro-Technology for issues of digitization of agriculture and information and communication technologies, as well as working with professional educational institutions. a 10 percent increase will be given to the monthly salaries of professors and teachers (with the exception of professors and teachers of foreign languages).
