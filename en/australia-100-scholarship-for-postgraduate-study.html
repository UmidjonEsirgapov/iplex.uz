About the university and the scholarship program:

Victoria University is a public research university located in Melbourne. The university ranked #351-400 in the ranking of international universities published by THE (Times Higher Education) in 2021, and can easily compete with world universities with its high-quality and innovative-practical education system.

And now, this university has allocated MASTER'S BY RESEARCH for international students who intend to study at the master's level. Let's look at the information prepared by the OLIYGOH.UZ team about the SCHOLARSHIPS grant program!

Who is the grant program for?

  • For international candidates from all countries.

Eligibility requirements:

  • To be able to apply for the grant program, candidates must first have a master's degree from the university (by research) must be admitted to any of the courses.

Application process for Master’s degree:

  • Candidates must register HERE on the university’s online portal and fill out an online application for their desired Master’s degree course;
  • Accepted for the grant program. students' candidacies are automatically considered.

Required documents:

  • Bachelor's diploma;
  • Diploma applications and grades (translated if not in English);
  • Copy of valid passport;
  • Medical insurance;
  • Knowledge of English at a level not lower than the following results one of the certificates:
    • IELTS – 6.5
    • TOEFL IBT – 79
    • PTE – 58-64
    • CAE – 176
    • EAP – Level 6

Note: The GPA of the candidates for the scholarship program is above average must have!

Program benefits:

Victoria University will provide 100% tuition fee waivers, up to AUD 10,000 (approx. USD 7,777) and a candidacy budget of AUD 3,350 (approx. USD 2,600) to selected candidates for the grant.

Admission deadline:

  • October 1, 2021
