If I apply to the Russian language course in foreign language groups, will there be questions from the textbooks of Russian schools?

Brief content:
This question was answered by Jamshidbek Odilov, head of the Department of Organization and Control of Tests of the Knowledge Assessment Agency: Question and Answer Question: If I submit to the Russian language course in foreign language groups, will there be questions from the textbooks of Russian schools?
Answer: Education is Russian There is a difference between the curriculum of the Russian language taught in educational institutions taught in the Uzbek language and the curriculum of the Russian language in general educational institutions taught in the Uzbek language. Therefore, the test questions for foreign language groups who have submitted to the Russian language course will be based on the curriculum of the Russian language.

Evaluation of this knowledge question Jamshidbek Odilov, the head of the Department of Organization and Control of Tests of the agency, answered:

Question and Answer

Question: If I submit to the Russian language course in foreign language groups, will there be questions from the textbooks of Russian schools?

Answer: The subject of the Russian language is taught in educational institutions where education is conducted in Russian. there is a difference between the curriculum and the curriculum of the Russian language in general education institutions where education is conducted in the Uzbek language. For this reason, test questions on the subject of the Russian language are included in foreign language groups.

Details

Curricular programs in Russian-language institutions differ from Uzbek-language institutions. Therefore, test questions for foreign language groups applying to the Russian language course will be based on the curriculum of the Russian language.

Conclusion

Applicants applying to the Russian language course in foreign language groups should not expect questions from the curriculum of Russian schools. Test questions are based on the curriculum designed for foreign language groups. — Source
