Medical faculties will be opened in HEIs in 4 regions

Today, the most relevant news in the field of education was:

Brief content:
According to the presidential decree, starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, medical faculties will be established at Karshi State University, Jizzakh State Pedagogical Institute, Navoi State Pedagogical Institute, and Gulistan State University. the representative of the Ministry of Health, Oktam Ismailov, announced at the international forum dedicated to the activities of the Ministry of Health.
In them and other medical institutions of higher education, it is decided to introduce a training system based on joint programs with leading foreign medical institutions of higher education.

According to the decree of the President, starting from the 2021/2022 academic year, based on the existing needs:

  • Karshi State University,
  • Jizzakh State Pedagogical Institute,
  • Navoi State Pedagogical Institute,
  • Gulistan State University medical faculties will be established. Ismailov informed.

    It is decided to introduce a training system based on joint programs with leading foreign medical higher education organizations in them and other medical higher education institutions.

    A training system for family physician assistants will be introduced in public health technical schools and medical colleges named after Abu Ali Ibn Sina. Based on the recommendations and proposals of the World Health Organization, the Ministry of Health will introduce a completely new system of training family physician assistants in technical schools and colleges;

    Public health technical schools and medical colleges named after Abu Ali ibn Sina will be attached to family doctor centers and family polyclinics, and their students will practice in these institutions based on their specialty. — Source
