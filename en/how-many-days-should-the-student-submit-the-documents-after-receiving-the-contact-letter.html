Deadline for submitting documents after receiving the letter of communication

Recommendation to transfer his studies Applicants who have received a communication letter must submit the relevant documents to the educational institution to which they have transferred their studies within 10 working days or within the period specified by the university.

Document submission procedure

  • The student who received the communication letter must prepare all necessary documents.

  • The documents must be submitted within 10 working days.

  • If the university sets an additional deadline, the student can submit the documents within this period.

Consequences in case of violation of the deadline

If the student's personal summary file is not submitted within the specified period, his application to transfer or resume his studies will be rejected. This prevents the student from starting his studies at a new university.

Legal basis

This procedure is established on the basis of Decision No. 393 of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated June 20, 2017.
