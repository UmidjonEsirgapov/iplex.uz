METI Government of Japan (Japan Internship Program) - is a 2-month internship program for all experienced, educated and well-behaved students or graduates in Japan. Citizens of the following countries can apply for this program (Uzbekistan is also available). A total of 200 practitioners will be selected

Admission period:

Admission to the program is usually 2 times a year:

  • For Course A, from May 4 to June 30;
  • For Course B, from May 4 to August 31;

The internship program will be held in one of the following 6 countries: Course A- India, Cambodia, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam. Course B - from home (online)

The internship program will take place in the following periods:

Course A from August 10 to October 8, 2021;

Course B from October 25 to December 22, 2021. (Approximately both courses are 35 days.

Requirements for the program:

  • Must be citizens of this country (Uzbekistan is also available);
  • Must be able to work 4 hours a day, 5 days a week;
  • Must have Japanese language certificate -N3 or know English at a business level required;

Required Documents:

  • Recommendation letter for students;
  • Diploma application for university graduates;
  • Certificates received in additional internship programs (if any);
  • Must be participating in this internship program for the first time;

Admission Process:

  • Register through the official web page;
  • If the documents are accepted at the first stage, then 2 online interviews will be held (in English or Japanese);
  • Additional documents may be requested after the candidate (Language certificates, university grades, letters of recommendation, picture etc.);

Program Benefits:

  • Daily 40,000 Yen($365) (Only for Course A);
  • Insurance (Only for Course A);
  • Great work environment and PC and Wired connection means (Course A);
  • Communication equipment is loaned: PC, Wifi, phone (Course B);
