Civil aviation pilot training school will be opened in Uzbekistan

According to the decision of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan (PQ-5100, 30.04.2021), a Training School for Civil Aviation Pilots will be established within Tashkent State Transport University together with the French National School of Civil Aviation (ENAC). and initially, on the basis of joint educational programs (double degree), personnel will be trained in "Flight eyesploitation of aircraft" bachelor education.

The amount of payment-contract for students to study at the school is determined by the Tashkent State Transport University in agreement with the French National School of Civil Aviation.
