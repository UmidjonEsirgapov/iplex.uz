Remember how they used to find tutors? Word of mouth: "The cousin of sister Marjan's neighbor teaches, they say, she prepares well for the exam." Calling, asking, doubting... And the result was like a lottery: maybe they would be lucky, maybe not.

Today, our children are facing other tests: complex curricula, entrance exams to lyceums and universities. The "Ask a Neighbor" method no longer works. Now you need not just an ordinary teacher, but a specialist with a reliable reputation and a clear price, suitable for your specific task. Most importantly, you had to find it "yesterday".

Well, the good news is that everything is about to change. Today, in Tashkent, Samarkand, Nukus and other cities, more and more parents are discovering another way - special site aggregates, where tutors are gathered in one place. It's like choosing a product from the marketplace: everything is in front of your eyes, everything is transparent, there are reviews and can be compared.

How is this approach better than the old one?

The main problem with searching through social networks or ads is the "one-sided" reality. The tutor manages his own page and can delete offensive comments. You will only see what he wants to show.

Repet.uz platforms are completely different. Comments here will last forever. You can see what other students and their parents are saying about the teacher - both good and bad. This gives a real, unpainted picture. Choosing a tutor here is based not on how he knows himself, but on the business of dozens of other families.

Also, this site is not an ordinary bulletin board. It is a tool that understands your needs. Do you need a chemistry tutor to get into medical school? Are you looking for a native language teacher for your child? Do you want to take lessons with an online teacher from another city? You set the filters - and you get a tailor-made list of suitable candidates with photos, resumes and clearly indicated tuition prices. No more calls and clarifications.

How to search?

Everything is very simple. Let's say your son needs math help before his annual test.

  1. You go to the site, select the subject "math", your city (or the "online" format).
  2. You will immediately see a list of teachers. Next to each one is its rating, lesson price and brief information about itself.
  3. You open the profile you like and read the reviews. Usually, they are very detailed: "The child mastered algebra in two months from scratch", "He explains very simply, my daughter finally understood fractions."
  4. You choose the person who best suits you and your budget and contact him directly through the site.

And if you don't have time to understand? There is an option to "Leave a request". Describe who you need ("Need a Russian language teacher for preschool children in Chilanzar district"), and you will be offered several verified candidates.

Why is it also convenient for teachers?

It is also a great help for teachers. Instead of knocking on windows and posting ads, you can create one good questionnaire - and it will always attract new readers. This is especially important for young professionals and those who live not in the capital, but in the regions.

In Uzbekistan, education has been and remains the key to future success. But today, this key must be modern and convenient. Using specialized sites to search for tutors is not only a trend, but practicality and common sense.

You save the most valuable thing - your time and nerves. Instead of hoping for luck, you'll make a conscious choice based on the same parenting experience. — Official Data
