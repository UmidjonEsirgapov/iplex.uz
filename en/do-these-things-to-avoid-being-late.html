To avoid being late:

Plan Ahead

You can have better time control by planning your tasks for each day or week in advance. Don't forget to account for the time it takes to complete each task.

Get Up Early

If you make a habit of getting up early, you will have more time to complete your daily tasks.

Time Reserve

When estimating your time to travel to any trip or meeting, add extra time reserve for travel and other unexpected delays. For example, if the journey takes 30 minutes, plan to leave 45 minutes or 1 hour in advance.

Limit Use of Electronic Devices

To avoid spending too much time on your phone or computer, try to use them only when absolutely necessary. The time spent on social networks is often more than the unplanned time.

Prioritization

Prioritize your daily tasks and focus on doing the most important things first. — Read on infoedu.uz
