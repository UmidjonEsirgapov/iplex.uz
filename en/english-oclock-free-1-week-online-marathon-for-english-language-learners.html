Free Online English Marathon - "English Clock" Marathon
This is a weekly online marathon in English:
- daily live broadcast with polyglot Andrey Gulyaev;
- practical homework on each topic + teacher's opinion;
- communication with the teacher in English + knowledge and skills by checking the level of English proficiency you will be.
Note:
During the course you will watch daily broadcasts and complete and submit assignments at your convenience. We check the submissions and provide feedback.
English friend Friend):
If you complete all tasks, you will get a bonus lesson - an English conversation with an individual teacher.
For whom:
The marathon is suitable for any English-speaking people. Especially if you've experienced at least one of the following:
  • I tried to learn several times but quickly I gave up because I didn't feel any change;
  • I taught English, but it is very difficult for me to communicate;
  • I promised myself many times to learn, but I failed I didn't get it;
  • I tried to learn by myself, but there was no motivation;
