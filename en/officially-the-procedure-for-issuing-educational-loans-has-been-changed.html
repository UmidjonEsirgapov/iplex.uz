The procedure for issuing an educational loan has been amended

According to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers dated September 19, 2024 No. 579, the procedure for issuing an educational loan has been amended. These changes will be effective from the 2024/2025 academic year.

  • For students of the second level and above, if the average indicator of mastering subjects is below "3", the educational loan will be allocated in the amount of maximum 80% of the payment-contract amount. In this case, the loan is disbursed after the first 20 percent of the loan is paid by the student.

  • If the average indicator of learning subjects is "3" and above or if there are members of the families included in the "Unified Register of Social Protection" who are studying, the education loan payment-contract amount in the amount allocated.

For the current academic year, the average indicator of mastery of subjects of the student who wants to receive an educational loan is obtained from the "Management of Higher Education Processes" (HEMIS) information system at the end of the previous academic year.

The student's mastery rate and relevant information Through the HEMIS system, and the information about the payment contract is formed automatically through the information system "Maintaining the account of services provided to students on the basis of the contract" (kontrakt.edu.uz).
