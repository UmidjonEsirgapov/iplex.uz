General Information:

The 2022 edition of the ICYF Ramadan Contest will challenge young people to capture Ramadan and share it with the world. is a platform to see how they are using the holy month of Ramadan to do good and restore faith in humanity to serve their communities. We invite Muslim youth to share their Ramadan with us in photo and/or video format in accordance with this year's theme "Ramadan is a month of self-reflection and inculcation of good values". Our goal is to encourage Muslim youth to do good deeds and meritorious deeds by serving them as exemplary young leaders and active members of their communities. Entrants can apply for one or all categories at the same time.

  • Category 1: The best image or illustration depicting the month of Ramadan, including but not limited to a selection of pictures expressing love and devotion;
  • Category 2: A full-length film or video depicting the true spirit of Ramadan, Islamic values and mutual respect. should describe the idea of giving and caring for them;

Who can participate in this contest?

  • All young people between the ages of 18-35;
  • Candidates must be citizens of countries that are members of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC countries) or countries that are considered Muslim countries;
  • Participate in the contest free;
  • Only 1 job will be accepted. It is not possible to change your work after it has been sent;
  • You must provide a short description of your work in English (where, when, and under what circumstances are the pictures or videos taken);
  • All pictures and videos taken must belong to you only;
  • ICYF employees and their relatives cannot participate in this competition;

Note: Learn about the requirements for the short film and the photo HERE . You can also apply HERE.

Important dates: Application for the competition will last from April 1, 2022 to April 25, 2022. The results will be announced by April 30.

Prizes: The winners in each category will receive a $500 cash prize. Second place candidates in each category will be awarded a cash prize of $250 and special gifts. Winners will be notified by email.
