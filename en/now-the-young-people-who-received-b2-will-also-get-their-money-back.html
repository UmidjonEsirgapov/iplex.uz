Now the young people who received B2 will also get their money back

Important news related to higher education institutions:

Brief content:
According to the new decision, examination costs will now be reimbursed for young people who have obtained a B2 level certificate for certain languages other than English.
Covered languages and conditions List of languages: German French Korean Chinese Japanese Covering conditions: From September 1, 2024 to January 1, 2027, the minimum number of points (certificate level) that young people must obtain in order to cover the costs of taking exams in international examination systems will be lowered by one step.
Opportunities for young people This new decision will further encourage young people to learn foreign languages and provide them with additional financial support for obtaining an international certificate.

According to the new decision, examination costs will be reimbursed for young people who have obtained a B2 certificate for certain languages in addition to English.

Covered languages and conditions

  • Japanese language

Conditions of coverage:

Opportunities for young people

This new decision further encourages young people to learn foreign languages and provides them with additional financial support for obtaining an international certificate. This will expand the opportunities of young people to learn foreign languages ​​and continue scientific research.
