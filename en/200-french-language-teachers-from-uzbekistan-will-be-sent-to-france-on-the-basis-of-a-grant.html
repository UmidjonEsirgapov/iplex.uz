French language teachers from Uzbekistan will be sent to France for training on the basis of a grant

on October 7, International Francophonie will be held in France The 19-summit was held. In it, Minister of Preschool and School Education of Uzbekistan Hilola Umarova met with Yann Librati, head of the University of the Francophonie in Nice. During the meeting, Mr. Librati expressed confidence that Uzbekistan will become a regional "hab" in the field of the French language. According to information, today in Uzbekistan French is taught as a foreign language in about 600 schools and as a second foreign language in about 100 schools. During the meeting, the issue of intensification of training programs and exchange of experience was discussed. The French side expressed its support for new projects in this direction.

As a result of the discussion, an agreement was signed to send 200 French language teachers from Uzbekistan to France for training on a grant basis during 2025. — Source
