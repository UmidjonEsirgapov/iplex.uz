Acceptance of applicants on the basis of a payment contract, procedure and amount of payment for studies

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Brief content:
In this case: the previous applicant on the list according to the sequence of points refused to study on the basis of an additional fee-contract or did not apply within one month from the date of the announcement of the results of the entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam) the next applicant is recommended to study on the basis of an additional fee-contract; Admission to study on the basis of an additional fee-contract is carried out according to the proposals of the higher education institution in agreement with the subordinate ministries and agencies (on admission indicators) based on the decision of the State Commission, taking into account the possibilities of higher education institutions. among the applicants who have not less than the specified minimum score and who have not reached the main admission threshold of up to 4 points, they are carried out with strict observance of the sequence of scores. Applicants who have stated that they will be granted the right to study on the condition that they pay 10 times or more the amount of the fee-contract for the first academic year. examinations (tests, professional (creative) examination, written examination) are carried out by the State Commission according to the score rating.

After the decision of the State Commission to recommend applicants for studentship on the basis of a payment-contract, contracts are drawn up between the higher education institution and legal entities or individuals in the prescribed manner.

Specified sample contract forms are issued by the higher education institution's admission commission from the day of the announcement of the applicants' recommendation to the higher education institution and are signed by the head and chief accountant in 3 copies for legal entities, and in 2 copies for individuals.

The terms of the contract and the obligations of the parties, the order of their implementation are carried out in accordance with the law.

According to the proposals of higher education institutions, based on their capabilities, selection and the amount of accumulated points, on the basis of the decision of the State Commission, applicants are admitted to study in addition to the quota established under the payment contract (next (in places called additional admission) is possible.

Proposals for additional admission are submitted by higher education institutions to the State Commission by October 15 in agreement with the subordinate ministries and agencies.

The State Commission increases the number of higher education institutions based on the opportunities, selection and the amount of accumulated points. will make a decision on the indicators of additional acceptance of applicants on the condition of a payment contract by October 20.

In this case:

  • the previous applicant on the list according to the sequence of points, refusing to study on the basis of an additional payment contract with an application or entrance exams (test tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam), if the next applicant on the list does not submit an application within one month from the date of publication of the results, it is recommended to study on the basis of an additional fee-contract; according to the indicators) based on the decision of the State Commission, taking into account the possibility of higher education institutions.

Higher education institutions strictly follow the sequence of points from among applicants whose scores are not less than the minimum score determined by the State Commission and who do not reach the main admission limits by 4 points, according to the indicators determined by the decision of the State Commission.

The increased payment-contract amount is determined in the following amounts compared to the actual payment-contract amount:

  • for applicants whose points do not reach the limit of acceptance quotas under the approved payment-contract by one point - 1.5 equal;
  • For those who do not reach 1.1 to 2 points - 2.0 times;
  • For those who do not reach 2.1 to 3 points - 2.5 times;
  • For those who do not reach 3.1 to 4 points - 3.0 times

Admission to study by the order of the rector of the applicant's higher education institution after full fulfillment of the terms of the payment-contract for these amounts for the first academic year within the terms set by the State Commission

Applicants whose results in entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam) are not less than the minimum score determined by the State Commission, within the framework of the main quotas, and who are not admitted to the additional places determined by the State Commission, and who express their desire to study, are increased by 10 times or more compared to the fee-contract amount actually established for the first academic year. The right to study is granted on the condition of payment in the amount of the payment contract.

Applicants who, according to the results of the entrance exams (tests, professional (creative) exam, written exam) scored less than the minimum score determined by the State Commission, the increased fee for the first academic year is equal to 2 times the maximum amount determined by the educational direction of the current payment contract. the right to study is given on the condition of payment.

All applicants who did not score enough points according to the results of the tests and expressed their desire to study on the basis of the increased payment contract, will be given the opportunity to choose an educational field that is less than the amount (times) of the increased payment contract. In this case, the criterion is determined by the suitability of the complex of subjects for which the applicant passed the test (place of the subjects in the block) together with his permanent place of residence or the region where the higher education institution where he submitted the application is located. by the order of the head.

Higher educational institutions submit information about applicants admitted on the basis of an additional fee-contract to the Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education and the State Test Center by November 1.
