Bilkent University of Turkey is accepting applications for the scholarship program

Important news for students and applicants has been announced.

Summary:
Candidates will be partially or completely exempted from tuition fees depending on the results.
In particular, the amount allocated for monthly stipend and accommodation is $230.
Students admitted to the university must provide original copies of all documents submitted with the application. candidates for bachelor's, master's and PhD degrees can apply. Students are exempted from tuition fees and provided with a monthly stipend.

Photo: bilkent.edu.tr

Turkey's Bilkent University is accepting applications for the scholarship program for the 2023-2024 academic year.

Candidates for bachelor's, master's and PhD degrees from all countries can apply.

Bilkent University offers scholarships in academic fields such as art, design and architecture, business administration, economics, administrative and social sciences, engineering, liberal arts and literature, science, music and performing arts, applied technology and management.

Candidates must submit documents such as SAT, GCE, IELTS (6.5) or TOEFL (87).

Also, they are evaluated according to their mastery rates, national or international exam scores, majors and other relevant academic criteria. The scholarship is allocated in five levels from 20% to 100%.

Funds for housing are also allocated to students admitted with a high score.

In particular, the funds allocated for monthly stipend and housing are $230.

Also, students are exempted from medical insurance and food costs.

The amount of scholarships awarded to students may change based on their performance during the academic year.

For example, students on residential scholarships must maintain a minimum GPA of 2.50 / 4.00.
