Video recording and distribution of teachers: About administrative and criminal liability

Useful information for applicants and students:

Summary:
Article 46-2 of the Code of Administrative Responsibility stipulates that receiving, collecting, storing, organizing, changing, using, distributing and destroying personal data must be on a legal basis.
If these rules are not followed, the following penalties defined: From 3 to 5 times the basic calculation amount for a citizen; For officials, a fine of 5 to 10 times can be imposed.
If these actions are repeated within 1 year after the application of administrative responsibility, criminal responsibility is applied (Article 141-2 of the Criminal Code). it is established that receiving, collecting, storing, organizing, changing, using, distributing and destroying data must be on a legal basis.
