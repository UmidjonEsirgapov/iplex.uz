Issuance of permits to applicants has begun

The latest news in the field of education is as follows:

Brief content:
In the 2023-2024 academic year, the "Applicant's permit" has begun to be issued to applicants who have registered online for admission to undergraduate courses of higher education institutions.
Applicants can participate in tests. "Applicant's permit" can be obtained by entering the personal identification number and passport series and number on the my.uzbmb.uz site.
Applicants must come to the test with a passport (ID card) and applicant's permit no later than the specified time. "Applicant's Permit" has been issued to applicants who have registered online for study in educational fields.
