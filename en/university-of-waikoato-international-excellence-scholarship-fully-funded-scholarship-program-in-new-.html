General information:

The Waikato University located in New Zealand has announced the University of Waikato International Excellence Scholarship 2022 grant program for international students who want to study at the undergraduate and graduate levels. The requirements for each young person who wishes to become a recipient of this scholarship are as follows:

  • All applicants must first receive an acceptance letter from the University of Waikato;
  • High GPA/grades of B+ and above;
  • Only students who have not previously studied at this university are eligible;

Application process:

  • All candidates can register and submit an application from the same site.
  • Applicants will receive an email notification of registration;
  • Pay more attention to entering all the information correctly during the registration process;

Deadline: Documents will be accepted throughout 2022.

Before correcting the document for this grant, please familiarize yourself with the detailed information on the official website of the university .
