Quotas of technical and medical fields will be increased by 25 thousand places in higher education institutions

From the 2025-2026 academic year starting in the state higher education institutions grant-based quotas in the fields of exact and natural sciences, engineering and technology, medicine will be increased to 25 thousand.

New changes in the field of education

  • New sciences of environmental protection and green economy will be introduced.
  • Special courses on ecology will be organized in the fields of natural sciences, engineering and economics.
  • Grant places will be increased gradually.

Additional opportunities from the "El-yurt Umid" fund

  • To study in the fields of ecology, environmental protection and climate change Up to 20 quotas will be allocated.
  • These scholarships create an opportunity to study at international and local higher education institutions.
