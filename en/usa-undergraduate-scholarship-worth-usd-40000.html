Brief about the university and the grant:

The University of Michigan-Dearborn University of the USA is considered one of the prestigious universities that share knowledge with international students from more than 50 countries of the world. The International Students Scholarship offered by this university provides financial support to excellent and leading international students. The total value of the scholarship is 40,000 US dollars, and 10,000 US dollars per year for 4 years will be given to the candidate who won it to study in one of the undergraduate courses at the University of Michigan-Dearborn.

Who can apply for the grant:

University of Michigan-Dearborn All international candidates admitted to one of the undergraduate courses can apply for this grant. International students must have applied and been accepted to an undergraduate course offered by the host university and obtained a student visa to study in the United States before applying for this grant. You can find the undergraduate courses offered by the university here and apply for one of them.

Requirements for candidates:

  1. Have a valid student visa to study in the USA;
  2. GPA 3.0 (on a 4.0 point system)
  3. At least 1020 (Combined) and higher SAT certificate;
  4. Being admitted to one of the undergraduate (full-time) courses at the University of Michigan-Dearborn;
  5. Language certificate - IELTS (6.5), TOEFL (IBT-80, CBT-213, PBT-550), or MELAB (76);
  6. GPA at the university must not drop below 3.0.
