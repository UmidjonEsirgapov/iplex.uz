What are the benefits for the winners of international and republican Olympiads?

About the expected changes in the education system:

Brief content:
According to the decision PQ-4306 dated May 3, 2019 of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On measures to identify talented young people and establish a continuous system of highly qualified personnel training", starting from the 2019/2020 academic year, at the republican stage of the main Olympiads The participants who took the 1st-3rd places will be presented with a certificate that gives them the right to receive the maximum score in the entrance exams to the state higher education institutions and is valid for a period of three years. Also, the applicants who took the 1st-3rd places in the following international Olympiads will be admitted to the higher education institutions without a test and without an additional exam. These are: - International Mathematics Olympiad - International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) - International Physics Olympiad - International Physics Olympiad (IPhO) - International Chemistry Olympiad - International Chemistry Olympiad (IChO) - International Biology Olympiad - International Biology Olympiad (IBO) - International Informatics Olympiad - International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI)

According to the decision PQ-4306 dated May 3, 2019 of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On measures to identify talented young people and establish a continuous system of highly qualified personnel training", starting from the 2019/2020 academic year, participants who took 1-3 places in the republican stage of the main Olympiads in the field of specialization state higher education institutions are provided with a certificate that gives the right to receive the maximum score in entrance exams and is valid for a period of three years. Also, applicants who take 1st-3rd place in the following international olympiads are admitted to higher education institutions without tests and additional exams. These are: - International Mathematical Olympiad - International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) - International Physics Olympiad (IPhO) - International Chemistry Olympiad (IChO) - International Biology Olympiad (IBO) - International Informatics Olympiad - International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) The status of the Olympiads is equal to the republican stage of the main Olympiads, and their list is determined by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers.

What does this mean?

That is, students who won all but the 5 international Olympiads listed above are not allowed to study without a test, but in whichever subject they won, only the maximum points are given.

For example, if the student who won the Mendeleev International Chemistry Olympiad, who is well known to all of us, applies to OTM, he will be given the maximum score in chemistry in the tests, but he will have to do the rest of the science blocks himself. — Read on infoedu.uz
