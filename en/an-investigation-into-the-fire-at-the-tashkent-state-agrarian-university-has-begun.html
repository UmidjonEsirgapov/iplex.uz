Criminal case related to the fire in the territory of the Tashkent State Agrarian University located in Qibray district started

On September 30, a large fire broke out in the territory of Tashkent State Agrarian University located in Qibrai district of Tashkent region. In this case, a criminal case has been initiated under Article 259, Part 2 of the Criminal Code ("Violation of fire safety regulations"), and investigations are underway. This was reported by the head of the information service of IIBB, Babur Tolaganov.

According to the press service of the university, the fire occurred in the building of the Forestry Agency on the territory of the university. However, this post was deleted shortly after. In turn, he denied reports of a fire in the building of the Forestry Agency. According to their explanation, only the roof was partially damaged due to water spraying.

According to the Ministry of Emergency Situations, the fire started at 20:10, and fire-rescue crews arrived at the scene at 20:17. The fire was contained at 21:36 and completely extinguished at 22:10. There is no information about those killed or injured as a result of the incident.

The process of extinguishing the fire was directly managed by the Minister of Emergencies, Major General A.Kh. Koldoshev. — Source
